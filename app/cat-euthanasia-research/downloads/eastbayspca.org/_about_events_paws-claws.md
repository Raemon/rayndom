# Paws & Claws [Oakland]

## June 2, 2025 \- June 6, 2025

Grade Range: entering 1st-3rd grade

A must for young animal-lovers! This session explores what it takes to care for pets of all kinds—furry, feathered, and scaled. Campers dive into the world of pet-ownership by fostering their very own pet-plushie for the week. With plenty of crafts, games, and animal interactions, this camp is guaranteed to get your tail wagging.

OAKLAND | 8323 BALDWIN ST • 9 a.m. to 3 p.m.

June 2nd-June 6th- Registration will open Feb 1
