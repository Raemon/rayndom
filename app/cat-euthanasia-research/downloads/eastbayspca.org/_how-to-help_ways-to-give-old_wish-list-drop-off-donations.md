# Wish List & Drop-Off Donations

Thank you for thinking of the animals at East Bay SPCA and donating your new and gently-used items.

Spacing: small 

## Wish List

[ Shelter Wish List ](https://www.amazon.com/gp/registry/wishlist/AZR4YYVA80QN)

Explore our Amazon Wish List to contribute crucial support for the East Bay SPCA’s shelter and foster efforts. From essential heavier equipment and nutritious food to comforting blankets, engaging toys, and general supplies like collars, your donations play a vital role in enhancing the well-being and happiness of our animals awaiting loving homes. Our Amazon Wish List provides a comprehensive list of supplies that can be delivered directly to our shelter. Your gift will be shipped directly to us.

[ ](https://eastbayspca.org/how-to-help/ways-to-give/drop-off-donations)

[ Drop Off Donations ](https://eastbayspca.org/how-to-help/ways-to-give/drop-off-donations)

Contribute to the well-being of animals in need by dropping off your generous donations at the East Bay SPCA. From pet essentials to comforting supplies, your support helps create a nurturing environment for shelter residents awaiting homes. Join us in making a positive impact, one donation at a time.

Spacing: small 

## More Ways to Give

We want to make it easy and convenient for you to make your donation. Call our development team at 510-563-4604 or learn about [more ways to give](https://eastbayspca.org/how-to-help/donate/more-ways-to-give) so that we can help create a giving plan that works for you.
