# Vaccine Clinics

## Low-Cost Vaccine Clinics for Dogs & Cats 

Our vaccine clinics provide affordable preventative care for your pets—including vaccines, microchipping, testing, and de-worming. **Walk-in availability at the vaccine clinic is on a first-come, first-served basis.** Priority will be given to those with appointments.

**Note** : These clinics are not for sick pets or general exams. Please contact your regular veterinarian or our   
Full-Service Veterinary Clinic for those needs. 

Check our event calendar for upcoming Walk-in Vaccine Clinic dates and times.

[EVENT CALENDAR](https://eastbayspca.org/about/events)

##  How to book with the Vaccine Clinic

  1. **Schedule your appointment** : 
  2.      * [Book online](https://app.cw.vet/hosted/us/eastbayspcamain), or 
     * Call us at **(510 569- 1606 )**
  3. Email your pet’s vaccine history to [clinic@eastbayspca.org](mailto:clinic@eastbayspca.org) **at least 24 hours before your appointment**



**Note:** If you are new to our clinics, making a profile ahead of your visit can decrease wait time. [Get started today.](https://eastbayspca.usw2.ezyvet.com/external/portal/main/login)

## Services & Prices 

### Vaccines for Dogs 

  * Canine Rabies: $18.50 

  * DA2PP (Distemper/Parvo): $18.50 

  * Bordetella (Kennel Cough): $18.50 

  * Lyme: $23 

  * Lepto: $23 

  * Canine Influenza: $30 per dose 




### Vaccines for Cats 

  * FVRCP: $18.50 

  * FeLV/FIV test: $47.00 

  * FeLV (Feline Leukemia): $40* 

  * Rabies (Purevax**, 1-year): $25.50 

  * Rabies (Purevax**, 3-year): $66.50 




**[Learn more about vaccines and what your pet may need](https://veterinarypartner.vin.com/default.aspx?pid=19239&id=4951406) **

###### *FeLV vaccines require a negative test within the past 3 weeks or proof of consistent annual vaccination.   
**Purevax is the #1 rabies vaccine designed specifically for cats. The vaccine protects without the need for adjuvants, additives that may present potential risks to feline patients, such as injection site reactions and chronic inflammation.
