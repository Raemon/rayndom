# Make a Tribute Gift

Consider making a tribute gift to honor, remember or celebrate a special occasion or a loved one. If you would like notification of your gift sent to the honoree or their friends or family, please complete the tribute notification form below. The East Bay SPCA will fulfill one tribute notification per gift.

If you wish to donate by mail, please [download the Donation Form](https://eastbayspca.org/wp-content/uploads/2023/10/EBSPCA_Printable_Donation_Form.pdf).
