# Pet Food Pantry

## September 16, 2025 @ 12:00 pm \- 2:00 pm

Every animal deserves nutritious food, and we are excited to announce our Pet Food Pantry is open to all pet parents who live in Alameda County.**Requirements:**

  * Live in Alameda County
  * Have a working phone number
  * Register ahead of time



For more information visit our [Pet Food Pantry](https://eastbayspca.org/resources/pet-food-pantry) page.

**Registration:** Opens 9/8 and closes 9/12 (or when full)

**Date:** Tuesday 9/16

**Location:** 8323 Baldwin Street, Oakland

**Time:** 12-2pm.

## [Register here](https://calendly.com/eastbayspcapetfoodpantry/pet-food-pantry?month=2024-05)

**If you successfully register you’ll be sure to receive food on the distribution day.**
