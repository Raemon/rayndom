# Block Party National Night Out | Oakland Housing Authority

## August 5, 2025 @ 4:00 pm \- 6:00 pm

Join us for Oakland’s 2025 National Night Out: **“Town Nights – Celebrating Safe Spaces in the Town”!**

It’s the perfect evening to reconnect with neighbors, build community spirit, and celebrate safety across Oakland. City staff, fire and police personnel, and elected officials will be stopping by neighborhood gatherings all over town!

We’re excited to join in! The **East Bay SPCA** team will be there with info, giveaways, and friendly faces. See you there!
