# Control Unleashed Group Class: Part 2

## September 14, 2025 @ 5:00 pm \- 7:00 pm

#### Sunday, September 14 from 5 p.m. to 7 p.m.

East Bay SPCA is thrilled to host local Certified Control Unleashed Instructor Cory Abshear for a 2-part workshop on pattern games.

Day 1: Sunday 9/7

Day 2: Sunday 9/14

Pattern games, a term coined by trainer Leslie McDevitt, are a set of versatile training exercises which can be used to teach anything from relaxation to calm behavior around triggers.

Signups will be directly through Cory’s organization Dog Trek Oakland, you can [read about the event and apply for slots at this link](https://www.dog-trek.net/group-classes "https://www.dog-trek.net/group-classes").
