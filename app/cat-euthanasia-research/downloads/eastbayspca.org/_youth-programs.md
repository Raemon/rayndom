# Youth Programs

Our humane education programs inspire compassion, empathy, and respect for all living things. We offer hands-on experiences for youth of all ages through camps, school programs, and volunteering at our Dublin and Oakland campuses. Additionally, our community programs span Alameda and Contra Costa counties.

[ ](https://eastbayspca.org/youth-programs/kids-teens-animal-camps/school-break-animal-camps)

[ Youth Animal Camps (ages 6-11)  ](https://eastbayspca.org/youth-programs/kids-teens-animal-camps/school-break-animal-camps)

Traditional day camp fun with an animal-loving twist! Offered during fall, winter, spring, and summer breaks.

[ ](https://eastbayspca.org/youth-programs/kids-teens-animal-camps/programs-for-teens)

[ Teen Camps (ages 12-17)  ](https://eastbayspca.org/youth-programs/kids-teens-animal-camps/programs-for-teens)

Engaging opportunities for teens to gain leadership skills, volunteer experience, and connect with animals in a camp setting.

[ ](https://eastbayspca.org/youth-programs/shelter-scouts-youth-volunteering)

[ Shelter Scouts On-site Youth Programs (ages 8-17)  ](https://eastbayspca.org/youth-programs/shelter-scouts-youth-volunteering)

Supervised, experiential learning and hands-on volunteer opportunities—perfect for earning service hours and learning about animal care.

[ ](https://eastbayspca.org/youth-programs/birthday-parties)

[ Birthday Parties (ages 6+)  ](https://eastbayspca.org/youth-programs/birthday-parties)

Celebrate your special day with us! Parties are hosted at both our Oakland and Dublin campuses and include animal-themed fun.

[ ](https://eastbayspca.org/youth-programs/school-programs)

[ Community Programs  ](https://eastbayspca.org/youth-programs/school-programs)

Go behind the scenes at our adoption centers. Includes a visit from an animal friend and an engaging presentation tailored to any age group, youth through adult.

[ ](https://eastbayspca.org/youth-programs/school-programs)

[ School Programs  ](https://eastbayspca.org/youth-programs/school-programs)

Students explore responsible pet care, animal welfare, human rights, environmental protection, and more. Aligned with CA State Standards and SEL.
