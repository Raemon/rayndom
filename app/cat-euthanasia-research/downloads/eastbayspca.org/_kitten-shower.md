# Kitten Shower!

### East Bay SPCA Foster Team invites you to be a part of this fun and informative event:

### •

### ______________________________

### When: July 12, 2025   
From: 11 a.m. to 1 p.m.   
Where: 8323 Baldwin St. in Oakland

### ______________________________

———–• See kitten bottle-feeding and care firsthand  
———–• Create enrichment for the 250+ incoming kittens  
———–• Learn all about our flexible foster program

**You can make a big difference!**

We’re in the thick of kitten season, one of the busiest times of year for our foster team. So far in 2025, we've already taken in 430 kittens — and we’re preparing for **250 more neonatal kittens next month alone!**

Join us for a fun, hands-on event as we celebrate the littlest lives and the foster families who help them thrive. 

### Unable to Attend?

  * KMR & Esbilac (kitten and puppy milk replacement)
  * Miracle Nipples
  * SnuggleSafe Heating Disks
  * Puppy Training Pads
  * Digital Food Scales



•

**Keep them safe: **Neonates have fragile immune systems — fostering keeps these vulnerable animals healthy.

**Watch them grow:** Experience the wonder of newborns opening their eyes, learning to walk, and discovering the world.

**Short commitment, big impact: **Fostering these babies for just a few weeks has a big impact on their lives. Helping them grow, socialize, and get ready for their real homes.

[sign up to foster today!](https://eastbayspca.org/how-to-help/become-volunteer-foster/foster)

Whether you're a seasoned foster or just curious, we’d love to see you there. Bring a friend and help us give every tiny kitten the chance to flourish!
