# About

East Bay SPCA is a local non-profit that is not affiliated with any national organization. We’re proud to be one of the oldest shelters in the country and one of the most innovative in our region. Find out more about our history, mission, staff and positions on the issues on these pages.

[ Career Opportunities ](https://eastbayspca.org/about/careers)

Be a part of an amazing team that’s making a difference in the lives of animals and people. Medical/dental benefits paid 100%, three percent contribution to your 401(k) account, discounts as a pet owner, and more.
