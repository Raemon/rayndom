# Behavior & Training Library

### **Recursos de comportamiento en español**

Pet behavioral issues can be overwhelming—so we’re here to help! You can read our guides below or [call our behavior & training helpline](https://eastbayspca.org/behavior-training/behavior-training-helpline).

Our goal is to help pet owners and potential adopters gain a deeper understanding of cat and dog behavior through classes, [private training](https://eastbayspca.org/behavior-training), [on-demand webinars](https://eastbayspca.org/behavior-training/webinars), our [helpline](https://eastbayspca.org/behavior-training/behavior-training-helpline), and the training guides below to ensure that people and pets live together comfortably. These guides include scientifically-based methods and best practices that will help you manage many common behavioral problems.
