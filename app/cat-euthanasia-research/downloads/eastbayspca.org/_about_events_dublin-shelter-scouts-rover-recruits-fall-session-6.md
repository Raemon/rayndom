# Rover Recruits – Fall Session 6 [Dublin]

## December 6, 2025 @ 2:00 pm \- 4:00 pm

Older youth (ages 12-17) have the chance to deepen their understanding of animal welfare through shelter tasks, animal socialization, enrichment prep, and more. Each 3-week session features new content, activities, and animal interactions. Youth are encouraged to enroll in multiple sessions to continue building skills and experience.
