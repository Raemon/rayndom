# JR Vet Camp [Oakland]

## June 30, 2025 \- July 3, 2025

Jr. Vet Camp

Grade Range: entering 10th-12th grade

Jr. Vet Camp is for students who are interested in learning more about the veterinary field in a fun, camp-like setting. Campers will observe medical procedures, meet animals, and practice the basic skills needed to help our furry friends. No experience is necessary and all supplies will be provided.

OAKLAND | 8323 BALDWIN ST • 9 a.m. to 3 p.m.

June 30th-July 3rd- Registration will open Feb 1
