# Our Sponsors & Partners

East Bay SPCA greatly appreciates the generosity of our corporate sponsors and community partners. Their valuable support of our lifesaving programs and services enables us to strengthen the human-animal bond and save more lives every day.

## Sponsors

**Central Garden & Pet**

Central Garden & Pet is a market leader in the Garden and Pet industries. For over 40 years, Central has proudly nurtured happy and healthy homes by bringing innovative and trusted solutions to its consumers and customers**.**

#### **Downtown Oakland Subaru**

Subaru believes in making the world a better place and the Subaru Love Promise is a commitment to show love and respect to customers and to work to make a positive impact in the world. Whether they live in homes or the wild, Subaru is committed to keeping all animals safe and healthy.

#### **Fresh Step – 2021 Kitten Season Care Partner**

Fresh Step has proudly supported shelter cat adoption for years. With the help of loyal customers, Fresh Step has contributed millions of dollars and millions of pounds of litter to shelter partners. As our 2021 Kitten Season Care Partner, they are supporting the East Bay SPCA in our efforts to care for the more than 1,000 kittens we expect to receive this Spring.

#### **Pet Food Express**

Pet Food Express helps California pets and their parents live happier, healthier lives together and be force for good in the greater pet community. Pet Food Express works hard to make an impact on the lives of shelter and rescue pets throughout California, through a number of programs designed to creatively ease the strain on the agencies that help them.

#### **Petco Love**

Petco Love is a nonprofit leading change for pets nationally by harnessing the power of love to make communities and pet families closer, stronger, and healthier. Since their founding in 1999 as the Petco Foundation, they’ve empowered organizations with nearly $300 million invested to date in adoption and other lifesaving efforts. And, they’ve helped find loving homes for more than 6.5 million pets in partnership with Petco and more than 4,000 organizations, like ours, nationwide.

#### **PetSmart Charities**

PetSmart Charities is committed to finding lifelong, loving homes for all pets by supporting programs and thought leadership that bring people and pets together. PetSmart Charities provides grant funding to nonprofits aligned with its mission under four key areas of support: Preventing Pet Homelessness; Helping Shelter Pets Thrive; Supporting the Bond Between People and Pets and Providing Emergency Relief and Disaster Support.
