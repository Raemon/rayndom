# Drop-Off Donations

**We Accept**| **We Do Not Accept**  
---|---  
\- Dog harnesses  
\- Leashes  
\- Toys  
\- Canned Dog & Cat Food  
\- Unopened Dog and Cat Kibble  
\- Unopened Dog and Cat treats  
\- Carriers and crates  
-Small blankets  
-Towels  
-Small Dog and Cat beds| \- Pillows, Large blankets  
\- Battery-operated toys  
\- Dog houses  
\- Fabric crates  
\- Human clothing  
\- Medications of any kind  
  
Spacing: small 

#### Where to Bring Donations

In-kind donations can be placed in our donation bins located outside of the shelter entrance or brought to the front desk during our open hours. Please note we are not currently accepting any soft goods like towels, blankets, and beds, and can only take in canned dog and cat food.

Spacing: small
