Serving the Pets & People in Our Community

We are **More Than a Shelter** , supporting the increasing and changing needs of animals and people in our community and beyond. We believe all people, regardless of their housing situation, income level, race, spoken language, or access to resources, should be able to experience the joys of the human-animal bond.
