# Livermore & San Leandro Residents

•

•

East Bay SPCA programs serve Alameda and Contra Costa counties. Through municipal contracts with the **Cities of Livermore and San Leandro** , we are able to provide enhanced services including specialized sheltering, medical care, and humane placement options for stray and abandoned animals found within these city limits.

[Partnership FAQ](https://eastbayspca.org/wp-content/uploads/2025/07/LPD-SL-Partnership_FAQs_7.1.25.pdf)

### **What animals will East Bay SPCA be caring for under this agreement?**

Stray dogs and underage, ill, or injured cats found within the City of Livermore will be cared for by East Bay SPCA. East Bay SPCA supports the [National Animal Care and Control’s position statement on management of community cats.](https://www.nacanet.org/animal-control-intake-of-free-roaming-cats/)

### **What additional services does East Bay SPCA offer the community?**

  * [**Pet Owner Support Services** :](https://eastbayspca.org/resources) Free community pantry with pet food and supplies, financial assistance for veterinary care, low or no-cost spay/neuter, temporary crisis boarding, behavioral support, and owner surrender evaluations. 




  * [**Youth Education Programs** :](https://eastbayspca.org/youth-programs) Day camps, experiential learning, volunteering, and teen leadership programs for ages 6–17. Scholarships available. 




  * [**Animal Training and Community Education Workshops** :](https://eastbayspca.org/behavior-training) Private and group training for dogs, free Behavior & Training helpline, bilingual training guides, virtual cat training, on-demand webinars, humane education presentations, and safety workshops. Scholarships available. 




  * [**Emergency Preparedness** :](https://eastbayspca.org/resources/disaster-prep-for-pets) Equipped to support temporary sheltering or rescue deployment during disasters as an American Red Cross partner, at the request of local jurisdictions. 




  * [**Volunteer and Foster Programs** :](https://eastbayspca.org/how-to-help/become-volunteer-foster) Opportunities to walk, care for, or foster animals — including weekend field trips, day hikes, and sleepovers. 




  * [**Modern Shelter Facility** :](https://eastbayspca.org/adopt) Features indoor/outdoor kennels, natural lighting, state-of-the-art ventilation, on-site veterinary care, and quarantine spaces.
