## Animal Statistics

East Bay SPCA's Animal Statistics Reports offer a comprehensive view of its efforts in animal welfare. The report provides a comprehensive overview of animal intake, adoptions, and euthanasia. Notably, it emphasizes transparency by disclosing cases of owner/guardian-requested euthanasia for animals deemed unhealthy and untreatable. The statistics showcase East Bay SPCA's dedication to compassionate care and community engagement.

[Animal Statistics Tables](https://eastbayspca.org/about/impact-financials/animal-statistics#AnimalStatistics)
