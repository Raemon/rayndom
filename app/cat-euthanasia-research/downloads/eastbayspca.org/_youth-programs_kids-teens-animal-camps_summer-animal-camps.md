# Summer Animal Camps

REGISTRATION FOR ALL CAMPS OPENS FEB 1ST AT 10 AM

**Prices for Camps**

5-day camp weeks are $465

**Grade Ranges for Camps**

Grade ranges are based on what grade your child will be entering for the 2026-27 school year. If you register your camper for a session for which they are not grade-appropriate, your camper’s enrollment will be canceled and our cancellation policy will be applied.

**Scholarship Applications**

Financial assistance is available and applications are available below each location and grade grouping.

You will have the option to select the week(s) you are interested in having your child attend after you sign in and select their age/grade. Scholarship applications will stay open until April 3rd.

Deaf/Hard of Hearing/CODA Animal Camp

Grade Range: entering 1st-6th grade

This week of camp is designed for campers who are Deaf, Hard of Hearing, or Children of Deaf Adults (CODA).

Campers will explore the world of animal welfare through innovative activities, games, and animal meet-and-greets. Whether helping out our shelter animals, or learning about proper pet care and humane training techniques—campers are sure to leave with an understanding that it is cool to care!

Humane Heroes

Grade Range: entering 4th-6th grade

Do you know a child who wants to work with animals when they grow up? Why wait! This session investigates different ways that kids can help pets in our community. Campers will explore the world of animal welfare through innovative activities, games, and animal meet-and-greets. As Humane Heroes, campers will learn important lessons on empathy, advocacy, and consent through the lens of animal care. They will assist in socializing shelter animals, creating enrichment, and making adoption ads to give our harder to adopt animals some extra love and attention while in our care. Campers will leave knowing they made a paw-sitive impact in the lives of our shelter animals.

* Cancellation Policy

Cancellation Policy 

+

-

For cancellations made 21 days or more prior to the program’s start date, we can provide you with a refund minus a $75 administrative fee to your credit card or check. Cancellations must be emailed to education@eastbayspca.org before the deadline in order to be eligible for a refund.

For cancellations made less than 21 days prior to the session’s start date, no refund will be granted.

For any illness related cancellations, due to potential or confirmed COVID symptoms, or for potential exposure self-isolation, we can provided you with full credit to your East Bay SPCA registration account balance, which can be used at a later date to either reschedule the same program or to sign up for future education programs such as our Seasonal Camps or our youth volunteer programming.

Please note, we cannot accommodate any makeup sessions or days. Transferring sessions due to illness cancellations is subject to availability.
