# Locations and Hours

We’re here for you. You’ll find contact information for our adoption centers, Theodore B. Travers Family Veterinary Clinic, and neuter/spay surgery center, as well as our holiday hours and department directory. We’re also open to conversations about donations and giving.

* Pet Medical Emergencies / Immediate Care

Pet Medical Emergencies / Immediate Care 

+

-

If your pet is having a medical emergency and needs immediate care, please contact an emergency facility near you or one of the facilities below:

  * **San Leandro:** Bay Area Veterinary Specialists at (510) 483-7387
  * **Oakland:** OakVet at (510) 879-4888
  * **Dublin:** Ironhorse Vet Care at (925) 556-1234 or SAGE (925) 574-7243
  * **San Ramon:** Veterinary Emergency Group (925) 718-7771



Download a PDF of [San Francisco Bay Area pet hospitals and vaccine clinics](https://eastbayspca.org/wp-content/uploads/2023/09/Local-Veterinary-Hospitals-updated-Dec-2020.pdf).
