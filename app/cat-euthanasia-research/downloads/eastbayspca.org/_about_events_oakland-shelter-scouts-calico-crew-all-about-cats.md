# Calico Crew – All About Cats [Oakland]

## June 29, 2025 @ 10:00 am \- 11:30 am

Our newest youth program for ages 8-11 explores animal care through games, crafts, and supervised meet-and-greets with shelter pets. Participants can join one or multiple modules. Each module will include safe, supervised, hands-on animal interaction.

**All About Cats**

Explore cat body language, learn how to safely meet a new cat, identify different breeds, create enrichment games, and learn how to care for community cats.
