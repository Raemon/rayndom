# Walk-in Vaccine Clinic

## January 13 @ 10:00 am \- 4:30 pm

# Our vaccine clinics provide affordable preventative care for your pets—including vaccines, microchipping, testing, and de-worming.

**Walk-in availability at the vaccine clinic is on a first-come, first-served basis.**

**Clinic Hours:** **Adjusted for this date:** 10 a.m. to Noon and 1:30 p.m. to 4:30 p.m.

_Typical hours: 8:30 a.m. to Noon and 1:30 p.m. to 4:30 p.m._

**Priority will be given to those with appointments.[Book Now](https://eastbayspca.usw2.ezyvet.com/external/portal/main/login)**.

**Note:** These clinics are not for sick pets or general exams. Please contact your regular veterinarian or our  
Full-Service Veterinary Clinic for those needs.

**For more information:[Vaccine Clinic](https://eastbayspca.org/vet-clinic/vaccine-clinics).**
