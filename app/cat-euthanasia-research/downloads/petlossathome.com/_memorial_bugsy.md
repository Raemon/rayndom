# In Memory of “Bugsy”

“Bugsy”

Bugsy, you will always be missed by the whole family. We are all trying to find a way to go on without you and it’s difficult. But our memories of you will get us through. At least now you are not suffering any more.

You are the best little boy there ever was-a little bundle of love wrapped in the softest fur possible. We love you so much.
