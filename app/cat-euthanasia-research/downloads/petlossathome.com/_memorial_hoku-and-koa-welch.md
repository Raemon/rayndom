# In Memory of Hoku and Koa Welch

Hoku and Koa Welch

We miss you Hoku and Koa. You filled up our hearts and our home with so much love. We want to thank Dr Allegra Liu for her compassion and care. She was kind and so helpful in making sure both dogs were comfortable till the very end. She made herself available by phone if I had any questions or I couldn’t get a hold of my vets in person and helped Koa manage her pain. She helped our family through an extremely difficult time and made the transition for us and Hoku and Koa a peacful one. We are so grateful. Thank you Dr. Liu
