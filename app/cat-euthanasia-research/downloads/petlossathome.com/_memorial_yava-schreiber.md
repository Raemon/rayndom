# In Memory of Yava Schreiber

Yava Schreiber

Yava  
8-2-2005 ~ 4-21-2015

Yava dava doo…  
Who rescued who?

On angels wings you had to fly…  
sometimes our pain – we scream out and say why?

We promised you we would be with you in your time of need…  
If you would have stayed with us, it would have been our greed

We want you by our side every day  
But God had better plans and you flew away.

We go thru your pictures of our incredible life together  
We are blessed and know our love will last forever and ever.
