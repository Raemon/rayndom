# In Memory of “Princess Leigh Bean Marie Mary Conn”

“Princess Leigh Bean Marie Mary Conn”

Princess Leigh Bean Marie Mary Conn  
4/17/2006-02/27/2020

Princess was very special to us. She enjoyed beagle rolls, begging for food, barking at other dogs, going on sniff and walks, getting pampered, swimming, sucking on stuffed animals, playing and running with her boy. We will cherish her in our hearts forever and never forget the almost 14 beautiful years she gifted to our family.
