# In Memory of Sasha

Sasha

Sweet Sasha girl, our “mama” and protector…we miss you so much but find peace in knowing that your suffering has ended. I picture you running around “like a gazelle” as you used to do and sneaking treats. I know that you are forever with us although you are no longer here. You were the best of your kind. We love you so much. Rest in peace mama.
