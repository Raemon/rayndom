# In Memory of Livy

Livy

Our sweet angel passed on April 3rd at 7:30pm. Dr. Gail Larson from Pet Loss At Home helped our sweet girl join our other beloved pets in heaven. Dr. Gail was thoughtful, considerate, and kind. Livy was always terrified of vet visits, and we were so grateful she was able to pass in the comfort of home. Thank you Pet Loss At Home.
