# In Memory of Kubrick

Kubrick

We love you so much Kubrick, our hearts ache for you. But we feel your beautiful little kitten spirit dancing in the trees and in snowflakes and in the stars. Just as your spirit will dance forever, our love for you will burn forever. Thank you for blessing us with three months of pure love and joy. We will do what we can support the fight against FIP in your honor. Our precious angel. Until we meet again at the rainbow bridge.
