# In Memory of Bailey

Bailey

This is our sweet Bailey. She loved running on the beach, jumping into the ocean waves, chasing seagulls, boating, going on family adventures, meeting new people and just hanging out. Her favorite activity was to curl up and snuggle next to us. When she was born and ready to go to a new family, she picked us and our hearts melted every day since for the next sixteen years. We are so grateful to have her in our lives, we will forever have a special place in our hearts for her. We miss you baby girl.
