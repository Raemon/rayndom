# In Memory of “Foxy”

“Foxy”

“Our Foxy girl, what a sweet, loving, and humble soul you were, a one of kind for sure. How we miss you dearly but are so grateful for the almost 16 years we had you for. You loved us all and showed us daily with your sweet snuggles we each long for now. We miss your barks demanding we pick you up to cuddle with us on the sofa, we miss your smile of satisfaction when you cuddled near us but most of all we miss your presence walking in the door, waking up each morning, and going to bed each night, there waiting on us to give us all the love and that you did. An unconditional love you were to your human brother and sister for their entire lives. You taught us so much, as a family we were blessed to have you in our lives to care for every single day. “If love could have saved you, you would have lived forever”. Until we meet again our soul pup, we will carry you in our hearts forever.”

We love you, our sweet Foxy girl. ~ 12/11/2024
