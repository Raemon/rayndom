# In Memory of Lucy

Lucy

Lucy was part of my little family and a very special cat. A sassy redhead, her first family returned her to the SPCA due to her feistiness. So lucky for me! Her spirit made me smile and her company gave me comfort. She was a true feline friend and I miss her very much.
