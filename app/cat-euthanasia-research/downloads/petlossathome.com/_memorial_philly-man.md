# In Memory of Philly Man

Philly Man

Thank You Pet Loss At Home for your Wonderful Services that you provide. It is not one that you want to have to call upon, but when you do it is a true blessing to have people like you all to make yourselves available. Our Family on Sunday Night had to say goodbye to our Loving Brother Philly. He was 13 years old and his spirit was strong, but his poor body could not keep up with him and we had to make the hardest decision of our lives. We take comfort in knowing that Philly Man is not in pain Anymore and that he is whole again with all 4 legs and running with his Brother Chief who we lost just 4 months ago. Dr Allegra Liu was professional,Helpful and a God Send for our family. We did not want to have to take our precious boy to the Vet as he has had some bad visits this past year. I wish you all nothing but the best and commend the work you do and the peace you bring to so many families. Long Live Philly Man and Chief.
