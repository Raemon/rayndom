# Resources

## Support Groups

  * [The Association for Pet Loss and Bereavement](https://www.aplb.org/chat-2/) – Chatrooms with free expert supportive guidance. One of the best online support groups.
  * [Grief Healing](http://www.griefhealing.com/discussion-group.htm) – Discussion group.
  * [Chances Spot](https://chancesspot.org/pet-loss-support/index.php) – Support forums, and helpful information about pet loss.
  * [Rainbow Bridge](https://www.rainbowsbridge.com/Grief_Support_Center/Grief_Support_Home.htm) – Support forum and chatroom.



## Workbook

  * [A Peaceful Path: A Supportive Guide through Pet Loss](https://www.amazon.com/Peaceful-Path-Supportive-Guide-Through/dp/0985855215/) – Self-paced workbook which allows you to validate your pain, explores why you feel this loss and helps you navigate through the grief in a healthy direction.

## Artwork

  * [Vet Artwork ](https://www.vetartwork.com/petmemorial)

To Create Beauty From Within (Remember your pet uniquely.)

For many years veterinarian offices have been decorating their walls with art. We aim to bring something different to the world of veterinary artwork. Our mission is to create cellular artwork to advance the field of animal science, and bring remembrance to animals and pets. Art is used for decoration, education, and healing. To the naked eye, our pieces look like colorful abstract art, but a deeper look reveals the cellular morphology and the “beauty within”. We invite you to design your walls something beautiful and meaningful.
