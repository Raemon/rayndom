# Pet Aftercare Services 

## Communal and Private Pet Cremation

We handle various arrangements for pet owners dealing with the loss of their beloved furry friends. From communal to private pet cremation, you can count on us to carefully and efficiently manage the remains of your pet after passing. To learn more about the various services we offer, please continue reading through this page. 

## Our Pet Aftercare Services

Please view our Pricing page for descriptions of the Communal and Private Cremation services we provide. **Please have a person at home that can assist with restraining and/or lifting your pet. Also, please provide a towel, sheet or blanket for your pet that you do not need returned**. Specialty Urns, Clay Pawprints and other keepsakes are also available and can be shown to you and discussed during our visit.| 

## Call or Email Our Office

We are here to guide you as you go through the entire process of making difficult decisions regarding the passing of your pet. Should you have further inquiries about the arrangements our licensed veterinarians handle, do not hesitate to contact us via phone or [email](mailto:info@peacefulpetpassing.org). We look forward to hearing from you!
