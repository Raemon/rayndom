## Emergency Care

Being a house call practice we are not equipped to handle situations that require emergency care. If you feel your pet needs emergency care, please contact one of the following centers:

  * [Animal Emergency Center of the Quad Cities:](https://qcanimaler.org/) 563- 344-9599
  * BluePearl Pet Hospital in Cedar Rapids IA: 319-841-5161
  * [Illinois CVM Teaching Hospital](https://vetmed.illinois.edu/hospital/veterinary-teaching-hospital/veterinary-specialties/emergency-24-7/): 217-333-5300
  * [Iowa State CVM Teaching Hospital](https://vetmed.iastate.edu/vmc/clients/emergency-services): 515-294-4900



### Your pet needs immediate care when:

  * Severe traumatic injury, such as vehicle accident, animal fight, notable blood loss, gaping wound/ laceration, etc
  * Ingestion of a chemical, poison, or recreational drug
  * Difficulty breathing, rapid shallow breaths
  * Unresponsiveness, seizure-like activity (trembling, shaking, loss of consciousness) that occurs in clusters or longer than 90 seconds.
  * Eye injury
  * Non-weight-bearing lameness and pain
  * Profuse bloody diarrhea AND not eating, lethargic, shaking, etc
