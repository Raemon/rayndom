## Privacy Policy

Our privacy policy at Hello Harmony Vet is to never sell your information to any party. It’s that simple! And we strictly adhere to this policy. Any information collected or provided via opt-in text messaging is kept private and only used for client communication, customer service and to better coordinate your pet’s medical needs. We will NEVER sell your personal information to any other party. Information collected is used only for customer service care, information about services, and to communicate about your pet’s health. Choosing to opt-in to receiving text messages or filling out our on-line form does not exclude you in any way from your information being protected by out company.
