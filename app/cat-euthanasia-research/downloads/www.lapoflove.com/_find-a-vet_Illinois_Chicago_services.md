#### Payment options

To make the experience as peaceful as possible, a credit card over the phone before the appointment is preferred. 

We provide payment plan options and accept [CareCredit](https://www.carecredit.com/?sitecode=1gfasemla55). The CareCredit credit card provides simple, budget-friendly financing options, giving pet owners flexible ways to pay for pet services over time.

Most pet insurance companies also cover Lap of Love’s services and we are happy to help with any paperwork required for reimbursement.
