Lap of Love's Support Center consists of over 200 empathetic and specially trained Veterinary Care Coordinators who are united by their commitment to provide unwavering support to pet families as they navigate the challenges of end-of-life pet care. Driven by their passion for this vital mission, our dedicated team members stand ready to assist from 7 a.m. to 11 p.m. ET, 365 days a year—including weekends and holidays.
