# Welcome to Our Berkeley Animal Hospital

Berkeley Dog & Cat Hospital is a full service animal hospital which offers routine veterinary care with **extended weekday hours** and **24/7 emergency services** to pets in Berkeley and beyond.

From preventative care and surgery, to diagnostic technology and [dental services](/site/veterinary-services-east-bay/dentistry "Dental Care, Berkeley Vets"), our wide variety of services can meet all of your beloved pet's needs.

We care for Berkeley’s companion animals and have developed a reputation over our century of operation for our expertise, compassion, and dedication to pet health.
