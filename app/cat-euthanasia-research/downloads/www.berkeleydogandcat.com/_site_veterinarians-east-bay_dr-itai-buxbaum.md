# Dr. Itai Buxbaum

Dr. Itai Buxbaum is a veterinarian in Berkeley with a special interest in surgery, dental care, and companion animal medicine.

[Book Online](/site/book-online) [ (510) 848-5041 ](tel:5108485041)

## Dr. Itai Buxbaum

Dr. Itai Buxbaum grew up in Portland, OR and earned his undergraduate degree in Animal Biology from the University of British Columbia in Vancouver, Canada. He completed his veterinary training at the Royal (Dick) School of Veterinary Studies at the University of Edinburgh in the UK.

Dr. Buxbaum has worked as a veterinarian in both California and Israel. His professional interests include surgery, dental care, and companion animal medicine.

When he’s not in the clinic, Dr. Buxbaum enjoys spending time with his wife, their two young children, and their Pomeranian, Chippy Cheddar. His hobbies include mountain biking and snowboarding.
