If your cat is feeling under the weather they will show subtle changes in behavior and energy level, but these changes can be difficult for pet parents to detect. That's why it's important to monitor your kitty's health and watch for these signs of common and serious cat illnesses.

## [Preventing Heartworm Disease in Cats & Dogs](https://www.berkeleydogandcat.com/site/blog/2020/08/17/preventing-heartworm-disease-pet-dogs)

Heartworm disease is a serious condition that can result in heart failure, severe lung disease, damage to other organs and even death for pets in Berkeley. The disease is typically found in cats and dogs. Here, our vets explain why prevention is key.

[Read more](https://www.berkeleydogandcat.com/site/blog/2020/08/17/preventing-heartworm-disease-pet-dogs)

## [Protecting Your Pet (And Your Family) From Ticks](https://www.berkeleydogandcat.com/site/blog/2020/08/17/protecting-your-pet-from-ticks)

Ticks can spread a number of serious diseases and are therefore dangerous to people and pets. Our Berkeley vets explain how these external parasites thrive, including which signs to beware of, and how to keep ticks away from your pets and your family. 

[Read more](https://www.berkeleydogandcat.com/site/blog/2020/08/17/protecting-your-pet-from-ticks)

## [How do I know if my pet has Lyme disease?](https://www.berkeleydogandcat.com/site/blog/2020/08/16/signs-symptoms-lyme-disease-cats-dogs)

Lyme disease is one of the most common tick-transmitted diseases in the world. In this post, our Berkeley vets share information about Lyme disease in pets: what it is, symptoms to watch for, and treatment options. 

[Read more](https://www.berkeleydogandcat.com/site/blog/2020/08/16/signs-symptoms-lyme-disease-cats-dogs)

## [Why should I bring my pet in for a fecal exam?](https://www.berkeleydogandcat.com/site/blog/2020/08/10/why-pet-fecal-exam)

Getting yearly fecal exams at your vet's office is the best way to keep your pet and your family safe from intestinal parasites. Annual fecals give your Berkeley vet a chance to check for intestinal parasites before more serious conditions arise. 

[Read more](https://www.berkeleydogandcat.com/site/blog/2020/08/10/why-pet-fecal-exam)

## [How can I tell if my cat or dog has fleas?](https://www.berkeleydogandcat.com/site/blog/2020/08/10/cat-dog-fleas)

Fleas are the most common external parasite, and they can make your pet miserable! If left untreated, they may even lead to infections and cause serious diseases. Our Berkeley vets explain early signs of fleas, and what to do if your pet does have fleas.
