# Why Trust Us As Your Pet's Veterinarian?

At Berkeley Dog & Cat Hospital, our team of veterinary professionals work together in our advanced facility to make sure that your beloved pet gets the care that they need and deserve.

[Book Online](/site/book-online) [ (510) 848-5041 ](tel:5108485041)

### For over a century, we have been offering a wide range of services under one roof. 

First opened in 1907, we have been providing veterinary care to Berkeley and the Berkeley ever since. From core care offerings like routine care and dental care to specialized surgeries, you can trust us to provide complete care and diagnoses.

[Our Services](/site/veterinary-services-east-bay)

### You're in good hands with our team of skilled and compassionate vets and specialists.

Our veterinary teams works closely together to provide you and your pet with care that is specially customized for your needs. We also have a number of specialists who work with us to provide specialized care for your unique pet. Our experience and compassion make all the difference when it comes to your pet's health and happiness.

[Our Vets](/site/veterinarians-east-bay)

### We put your pet's long term health first by focusing on preventative care.

At Berkeley Dog & Cat Hospital, we believe that preventative care is the best way to maintain your pet's good health throughout their whole life. Because of this, we encourage pet parents to keep their companions up to date on their vaccinations and routine care. 

[Routine Care](/site/veterinary-services-east-bay/cat-dog-routine-exams) [Vaccines & Parasite Prevention](/site/veterinary-services-east-bay/cat-dog-vaccinations-parasite-prevention)

### Our advanced in-house diagnostic lab offers quick results and treatment options. 

Our vets are dedicated to providing quality care to every single one of our patients. Our highly advanced in-house diagnostic lab allows us to conduct tests using radiographs, MRIS, ultrasound, EKG, CT scans and more. Using our technology, we can rapidly deliver diagnoses and treatment plans to target your pet's unique needs.

[Veterinary Laboratory](/site/veterinary-lab-east-bay)

### Our online pharmacy allows you to renew your pet's prescriptions quickly and easily.

Think of us as your one stop shop for your pet's prescriptions. Our vets know your pet, so why wouldn't you want them to fulfill your prescriptions as well? Our team will use our knowledge and familiarity with your pet's needs to provide you with high quality service.

[Shop Now](/site/pet-shop-pharmacy-east-bay)
