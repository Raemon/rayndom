# About Our Hospital

## Welcome to Our Berkeley Animal Hospital

At Berkeley Dog & Cat Hospital, we have been providing our clients and their beloved pets with advanced and compassionate veterinary care since 1907. We are dedicated to providing the best possible service to our patients.

[Book Online](/site/book-online) [ (510) 848-5041 ](tel:5108485041)

### Veterinary Care You Can Depend On

As a full service hospital, we are proud to offer many advanced and comprehensive care options to keep your companion pet happy and healthy. These range from [routine care](/site/veterinary-services-east-bay/cat-dog-routine-exams "Wellness Exams, Berkeley Vets") and [vaccinations](/site/veterinary-services-east-bay/cat-dog-vaccinations-parasite-prevention "Vaccinations & Prevention, Berkeley Vets") to [dental care](/site/veterinary-services-east-bay/dentistry "Dental Care, Berkeley Vets").

[](https://aaha.org "Berkeley Dog & Cat Hospital is an AAHA-accredited hospital in Berkeley | Opens in a new tab")Our AAHA-accredited hospital also offer surgical services including Tibial Tuberosity Advancement, dental surgery, spaying & neutering and splenectomies.

[Core Care Services](/site/veterinary-services-east-bay) [Surgical Care](/site/veterinary-surgery-east-bay)

### 117 Years & Going Strong

We're proud to celebrate our rich and long history of serving the Berkeley community since 1907. Read the article to learn more about the history of our hospital and veterinarians, and the comprehensive veterinary care we provide to our clients and their beloved pets.

[Read Article](/files/berkeley-dog-cat-hospital-1907-history.pdf)

### Our Pet Care Philosophy

As a full service veterinary hospital, Berkeley Dog & Cat Hospital is dedicated to providing a broad range of diagnostic procedures. We strive to offer compassionate advice alongside scientifically advanced veterinary care, allowing you and your family to enjoy your time with your pet companion for many years to come. 

Our job is to treat your pets when they aren’t feeling well and help you learn how to keep them happy and healthy every day. We are proud of the quality of services we offer today and of the rich history of our hospital. We have been serving Berkeley for more than a century. 

[What Sets Us Apart](/site/why-trust-animal-hospital-east-bay)

### Compassionate, Experienced Vets

Our vets and consulting specialists at Berkeley Dog & Cat Hospital are here to help you and your pet. We are dedicated to providing exceptional veterinary care for you and your companion pet while creating a friendly and comfortable environment for you both. We are proud to also offer daytime urgent and emergency care services. 

[Meet Our Veterinarians](/site/veterinarians-east-bay)

### Our Commitment

We aim to ensure that you are comfortable trusting your beloved pet to our staff's care. At Berkeley Dog & Cat Hospital, we treat your pets as if they were our own. 

Contact us to arrange a preliminary appointment today! We would love to have the opportunity to get to know you and your companion pet.
