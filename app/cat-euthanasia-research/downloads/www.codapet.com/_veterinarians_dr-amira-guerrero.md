About Me

## Why I provide at-home pet euthanasia

Since I can remember I have always wanted to work with animals and being a veterinarian allowed me to pursue that dream. In 2022 my hospice kitty was ready to cross the rainbow bridge and I wanted to have her pass at home. Being able to perform an in-home euthanasia for her, on the couch she always laid on, was the greatest gift for her and me. After experiencing it myself, I knew how precious an in-home euthasia was and I wanted to offer that to the community.
