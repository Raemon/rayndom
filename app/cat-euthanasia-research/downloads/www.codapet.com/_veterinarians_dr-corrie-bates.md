About Me

## Why I provide at-home pet euthanasia

I've always felt the passing of our beloved pets deserves more time than is possible in the clinic setting and also our beloved companions deserve to have their last moments in their safe space not the sometimes anxiety producing veterinary clinic for some pets. Additionally, I encourage pet owners going through this difficult time to share a few words or stories of their beloved companions and even choose song(s) to play. I am sensitive to any cultural, religious or spiritual ceremonial aspects that owners may want to include to help honor their pet on their final passage.
