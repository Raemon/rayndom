About Me

## Why I provide at-home pet euthanasia

I became a veterinarian because I loved the science. But even more importantly I wanted to help better connect animals with their pet parents. I think as a veterinarian my job is to apply my medical knowledge and experience to help people understand what their pet needs from them so that pet owners can make the best decisions about care for their furry family members. I want to do home euthanasia in order to honor the human animal bond and all that our pets bring to our lives. I truly believe that euthanasia is the last best gift we can offer suffering animals and I want to facilitate the most stress-free, compassionate and comfortable goodbye for both the animals and their families.
