About Me

## Why I provide at-home pet euthanasia

Throughout my career, I have witnessed firsthand the difference between clinic-based and in-home euthanasia experiences. Time and again, I've seen the profound relief and comfort that in-home euthanasia brings to both pets and their families during one of life's most difficult moments. This understanding inspired me to offer this service to my community, allowing beloved companions to pass peacefully in the comfort of their own homes, surrounded by the people and places they know and love. My wife Sarah and I are blessed with three boys, and together we share our home with two German Shorthaired Pointers, Ellie and Judge, along with a small flock of chickens. My experiences as both a father and pet parent have given me an even deeper appreciation for the irreplaceable role our animals play in our families. When I'm not working, you'll often find me on a family adventure, camping and hiking, tending to my garden, or participating in domestic and international mission trips. My approach to end-of-life care centers on providing a comfortable, peaceful experience for families and their beloved pets. Every cherished companion deserves to transition with dignity, surrounded by love, in the place they've always called home.
