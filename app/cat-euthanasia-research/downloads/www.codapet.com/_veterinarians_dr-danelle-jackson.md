About Me

## Why I provide at-home pet euthanasia

I became a vet because of how important veterinary care was to the success of my family's farm. I loved being able to watch our farm vets work on our cows. I've spent over 10 years working in small animal veterinary clinics and have seen how stressful the process of euthanizing an animal in a clinic can be. There are other clients, loud noises, and emergencies. In-home euthanasia provides a calmer environment that the pets and families are familiar with to reduce the stress for everyone involved. I always worried about clients having to drive home from a clinic while distraught, and in home euthanasia eliminates that worry.
