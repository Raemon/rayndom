About Me

## Why I provide at-home pet euthanasia

I always wanted to help the voiceless (animals) for as long as I can remembered. After working years in emergency medicine, I want to ensure pet owner's final moments with their precious pets. I see many patients coming to the ER just for euthanasia. We truly try to make the euthanasia experience at the hospital as peaceful as possible. But performing a euthanasia at the pet's home would ensure the best possible experience for them.
