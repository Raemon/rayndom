# North Star Animal Nutrition - Pet Nutrition Consulting - Nationwide

(19 reviews)

Verified Member since Aug 2023 

North Star Animal Nutrition is an independent animal nutrition company servicing equine, canine and feline clients. I believe that your pets nutrition should be as unique as your pet. With a masters in animal nutrition and over 10 years of experience helping owners do the best by their animals I understand the struggles of making sure your pets are as healthy as possible in a confusing market. Together we will discuss the nutritional challenges your pet faces and your goals for their health. We will then come up with a cost effective solution to make sure your pet is feeling their best. I will provide a balanced diet or feeding plan, which meets the standards of AAFCO or NRC, and provide you with a full nutritional analysis and recipe if a home-cooked diet is decided upon. I am excited to speak with you and learn more about your pets needs. Please contact and book me on Petworks. Thank you.
