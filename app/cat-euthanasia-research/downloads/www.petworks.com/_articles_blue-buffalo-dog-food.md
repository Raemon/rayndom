From its inception, Blue Buffalo was guided by the principle of providing pets with food made from the finest natural ingredients. One of Blue Buffalo‚Äôs standout innovations is its proprietary LifeSource Bits. These are nutrient-packed kibble pieces included in every bag of Blue Buffalo dry pet food.
