# Healing Hands Equine and Animal Chiropractic Care - Kingman, AZ

Dr. Lee Stewart, a graduate of Sherman College of Chiropractic in Spartanburg, South Carolina, brings over four decades of experience to the realm of animal chiropractic care. Having successfully run a Chiropractic practice in the greater Philadelphia area for 40 years, he has honed his skills in providing exceptional care not only to humans but also to their beloved pets. With a profound love for animals, Dr. Stewart is fully qualified and certified to offer chiropractic services to cats, dogs, horses, and rabbits, addressing their unique needs and promoting their overall well-being.

Dr. Stewart’s approach goes beyond traditional adjustments, utilizing his innate intuition and expertise to identify and correct structural imbalances through advanced techniques such as Muscle Balancing, Acupressure/Meridian Therapy, and Low Force techniques. His lifelong passion for horses, cultivated from his first ride as a teenager, has driven his commitment to deliver essential chiropractic care to these noble creatures. He extends his specialized services to dogs and small animals, ensuring every pet receives the tailored attention they deserve. His flexible cancellation policy is an added convenience, emphasizing his understanding of the unpredictable nature of pet care.

Clients seeking top-tier mobile chiropractic services for their pets will find Dr. Stewart’s dedication and skill unmatched. His ability to travel to clients underscores his commitment to accessibility and convenience, making quality pet care available right at your doorstep.
