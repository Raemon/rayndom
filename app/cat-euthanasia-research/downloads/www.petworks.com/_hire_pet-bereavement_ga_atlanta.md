#### more info

**AMA**

**How can a pet bereavement counselor help me with the loss of my pet?**

A pet bereavement counselor in Atlanta, GA offers emotional support and guidance to help you process the deep grief that often follows the loss of a beloved animal companion. These professionals provide a safe, compassionate space to talk about your feelings, validate your pain, and help you find ways to honor your pet’s memory. Sessions can help you navigate guilt, loneliness, and the unique challenges of pet loss, which are often misunderstood or minimized by others.

**What are some local towns and cities with pet bereavement counselors near me?**

Fulton County, Marietta, Sandy Springs, Roswell, Alpharetta, Smyrna, Dunwoody, East Point, Kennesaw, Lawrenceville, Duluth

**What should I expect during a pet loss counseling session?**

During a session, you can expect a warm, nonjudgmental conversation where you're encouraged to express your thoughts and emotions openly. The Atlanta, Georgia counselor may help you explore coping strategies, manage difficult anniversaries, or create rituals for closure and healing. Sessions are tailored to your pace and grief journey and may take place in person, by phone, or through secure video chat. Some counselors are licensed therapists, while others are trained grief coaches or certified in pet loss support.

**How much does Atlanta pet bereavement counseling typically cost?**

Prices vary based on the counselor’s qualifications, location, and session length, but general ranges include:

Licensed therapists or LCSWs: $80–$150 per session; Certified pet loss grief counselors or coaches: $50–$100 per session; Group sessions or virtual support circles: Often free to $40 per session.

Many pet parents find even one or two sessions provide meaningful comfort during the grieving process.
