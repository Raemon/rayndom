The benefits of feline chiropractic therapy include:

Pain relief: Eases discomfort caused by conditions like arthritis, joint issues, or injuries from physical activity or accidents.

Enhanced mobility: Helps restore proper spinal and joint alignment, improving flexibility and range of motion.

Preventative care: Routine chiropractic visits can catch early musculoskeletal problems, preventing more serious conditions that could require surgery or medication.
