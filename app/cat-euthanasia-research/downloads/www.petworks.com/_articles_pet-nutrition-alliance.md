# Pet Nutrition Alliance: Empowering Pet Health Through Science

The Pet Nutrition Alliance (PNA) is a nonprofit coalition dedicated to enhancing pet health by promoting evidence-based nutritional practices for dogs and cats. Backed by a group of respected veterinary organizations, the PNA equips veterinary professionals with the tools and resources they need to guide pet owners toward better, healthier dietary choices. In a world filled with overwhelming pet food options and marketing claims, the PNA brings clarity, credibility, and confidence to the conversation around pet nutrition.

### 1\. **Educational Tools for Veterinary Professionals**

  * Offers tools such as **calorie calculators** (available in English, Spanish, and French), **body and muscle condition scoring charts** , feeding guides, and printable client handouts aimed at improving nutrition consultations [AVMA+1vmae.org+1](https://www.avma.org/javma-news/2014-03-15/website-provides-tools-pet-nutrition?utm_source=chatgpt.com).

### 2\. **Nutritional Assessment Advocacy**
