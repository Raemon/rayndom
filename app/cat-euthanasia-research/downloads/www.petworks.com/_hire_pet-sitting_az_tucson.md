**How much does in-home pet sitting cost in 2026?**

Average rates in 2026 range from $20–$45 per hour for dogs, cats, and small animals. Full-day or overnight visits can range $75–$200 depending on your pet’s needs and the sitter’s experience. Many sitters also offer package deals for recurring visits or extended stays.

**What services can an in-home pet sitter provide?**

In-home pet sitters in Tucson, AZ handle feeding, walking, administering medications, playtime, and companionship. Pets that experience separation anxiety, require special care, or prefer familiar surroundings benefit most from in-home pet sitting. Older pets, kittens, puppies, or pets with medical needs may also require a dedicated sitter. A sitter in Tucson, AZ can tailor care to your pet’s lifestyle.
