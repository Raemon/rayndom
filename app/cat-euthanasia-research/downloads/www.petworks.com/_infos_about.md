# about petworks

For your furry, fluffy, feathery, scaly, soft, spiky pets – whether they purr, chirp, bark, neigh, squeak, or oink – we’re there. To give them a brighter smile, a healthier diet, a soothing massage, a day in the spotlight, a place to heal. Everything they may need.

For our loving pet parents, who spare no expense when it comes to the care of precious pets. To help you design the perfect healthy diet, to train your fun-loving (but sometimes disobedient) pup, to connect you with the best animal chiropractor in your area, to honor your pet’s life when it has to end, or to deal with the grief that accompanies this difficult loss.

  


The [**Petworks.com**](https://www.petworks.com) pet care booking platform is not all fluff. Since we launched in 2021, we've enabled over 80,000 of our registered Pet Parents to securely book our 5,000 verified Pet Care Professionals, who provide the very best personalized pet care services throughout North America. 

  


And PetWorks helps pet owners care for their animals with practical tips and expert insights. Our peer reviewed library of over 1,000 [**pet care articles**](https://www.petworks.com/articles/) spans the care of dogs, cats, horses, birds, and house pets. All written and curated by experienced professionals and industry leaders, to ensure accurate, trustworthy, and ethically produced pet care health and wellness guidance.
