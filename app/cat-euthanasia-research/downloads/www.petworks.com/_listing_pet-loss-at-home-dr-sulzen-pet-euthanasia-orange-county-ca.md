# Pet Loss at Home - Dr. Sulzen - Pet Euthanasia - Orange County, CA

(3 reviews)

Member since Jun 2019 

At our caring team in Santa Ana, CA, we understand that losing a pet is one of life's most challenging experiences. With over two years of dedicated service to the pet community of Orange County, we specialize in compassionate at-home euthanasia for cats, dogs, guinea pigs, hamsters, and rabbits. Our licensed veterinarians bring a wealth of expertise, holding certifications from esteemed organizations such as the American Animal Hospital Association (AAHA), Association for Pet Loss and Bereavement (APLB), and the Companion Animal Euthanasia Training Academy (CAETA). This extensive training ensures that we provide the most compassionate and respectful care during this difficult time.

We offer a comprehensive range of services designed to support you through every step of the process, from planning and coping to memorializing your beloved pet. Our visits include thoughtful touches like lighting candles, playing relaxing music, and arranging blankets or flower petals to create a soothing environment. Each home visit involves a simple sedation followed by euthanasia, with options for home burial or cremation, with or without ashes. While our home visits may be more costly due to the extensive time and care involved, we believe in providing the utmost comfort and dignity to your pet and your family.

Our team travels across large service areas, dedicating time to ensure that we can be there when you need us most. We typically spend 30 to 60 minutes at your home to allow for a peaceful farewell. We encourage you to surround yourself with family and friends during this time and give your other pets the opportunity to say goodbye. If you're facing an urgent situation, please don't hesitate to call us at 877-219-4811. We are here to help you every step of the way.
