# Pet Loss at Home - In Home Pet Euthanasia Service - San Diego, CA

(3 reviews)

Member since Jun 2019 

At our compassionate pet euthanasia service, we understand that losing a beloved pet is one of life's most challenging moments. Serving pet parents throughout San Diego County since April 2021, we are dedicated to providing a peaceful and respectful end-of-life experience for your cherished companions right in the comfort of your home. Our team, including Dr. Joe, a licensed veterinarian, offers a supportive presence during this difficult time for dogs, cats, and small animals like chinchillas, ferrets, guinea pigs, hamsters, hedgehogs, mice, rabbits, and rats. We bring our expertise and care, accredited by the American Animal Hospital Association (AAHA), the American Veterinary Medical Association (AVMA), the International Association for Animal Hospice and Palliative Care (IAAHPC), and the Association for Pet Loss and Bereavement (APLB), directly to your doorstep.

Our comprehensive service includes everything from planning and coping strategies to comforting rituals such as lighting candles, playing relaxing music, and creating a serene environment with blankets, pillows, or flower petals arranged in a heart shape. Guided by our Companion Animal Euthanasia Training Academy (CAETA) certification, we prioritize gentle sedation and compassionate euthanasia, ensuring your pet's comfort and dignity. We offer flexible options for aftercare, including home burial or cremation, allowing you to choose whether you would like ashes returned. Our empathetic approach ensures that you and your pet receive the utmost care and respect during this tender time.

We are committed to being there for you when you need us most, including urgent or emergency situations. Our mobile services offer flexibility and convenience, and our cancellation policy remains flexible to accommodate your needs. Your beloved pet deserves a dignified farewell, and we are here to help you every step of the way. Hang in there. We are here to help. If your situation is urgent or an emergency, please call us for help at 877-219-4811. Thank You.
