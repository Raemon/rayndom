During further visits, holistic healing sessions may include, veterinary orthopedic manipulation, cold laser acupuncture, Reiki healing, chiropractic, nutritional counseling, diet consulting, allergy testing, functional blood chemistry evaluation, massage, ozone treatment, cold laser, CBD, hospice, Reiki and more.

Dr. Gail Marasse has degrees and certifications in the following: Dietetics, Holistic Nutrition, Chiropractic, Reiki Healing, Animal Massage, Endocrinology, Functional Blood Chemistry, and Veterinary Orthopedic Manipulation.

She has also formulated her own line of natural pet remedies.

Dr. Gail has dedicated her life to continue learning how to help heal the animals with non-drugging compounds and actively blogs and writes in this capacity. She donates time and treatment to several rescues in her local area of Ventura County, including AFLAR (All For Love Animal Rescue), CARL (Canine Adoption and Rescue League), Rosies Dog Rescue and APARC (Santa Paula Animal Rescue Center). She is also a member of the AHVMA (American Holistic Veterinary Medicine Association).
