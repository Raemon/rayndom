# The Engagement Ring Cuts Everyone Will Be Talking About in 2026

  * [ __ The Mountain Terrace  ](https://www.themountainterrace.com/author/eba4a1c11763e757/)
  * [ __ December 14, 2025 ](https://www.themountainterrace.com/2025/12/14/)
  * __ [Engagement Rings](https://www.themountainterrace.com/category/engagement-rings/)



![The Engagement Ring Cuts Everyone Will Be Talking About in 2026-min](https://www.themountainterrace.com/wp-content/uploads/2025/12/The-Engagement-Ring-Cuts-Everyone-Will-Be-Talking-About-in-2026-min.jpg)

Choosing an engagement ring is one of the most meaningful decisions a couple makes. In 2026, future brides and grooms are moving toward styles that feel more personal, expressive, and deeply symbolic. Instead of sticking to the usual ring shapes or classic settings, people are exploring fresh designs, unique stones, and modern twists on traditional favorites.

If you are starting your search and feel overwhelmed by all the engagement ring cuts, this guide breaks down the shapes that will shine the brightest in 2026, so you can choose a ring that truly reflects your love story.

![Trending Engagement Ring Diamond Shapes](https://www.themountainterrace.com/wp-content/uploads/2025/12/Trending-Engagement-Ring-Diamond-Shapes-683x1024.jpg)

## 1\. Elongated Oval Cut

![Elongated Oval Cut](https://www.themountainterrace.com/wp-content/uploads/2025/12/Elongated-Oval-Cut-min.jpg)

PHOTO BY [BENZ & CO](https://www.benzdiamonds.com/)

The elongated oval continues to rise in popularity because it delivers maximum sparkle with a soft, refined look.

**Why it’s trending**

  * Appears larger per carat
  * Flatters every finger shape
  * Minimalist yet eye-catching



**Best for:** Anyone who wants brilliance with a modern, elegant silhouette.  
**Tips:** Choose a well-cut oval to avoid the “bow-tie” shadow.  
**Special note:** One of the most flattering cuts for slim and wide fingers.

## 2\. Antique Cushion Cut

![Antique Cushion Cut-min](https://www.themountainterrace.com/wp-content/uploads/2025/12/Antique-Cushion-Cut-min.jpg)

PHOTO BY [SOPHIE EPTON](https://sophieepton.com/)

Romantic, soft, and full of old-world charm, this shape is perfect for those who want vintage beauty with a modern touch.

**Why it’s trending**

  * Pairs beautifully with yellow gold
  * Heirloom-inspired designs feel meaningful
  * Works with both simple and decorative settings



**Best for:** Lovers of classic, timeless, and heirloom styles.

**Special note:** No two antique cushions look the same, making each ring truly unique.

## 3\. Pear Cut

![Pear Cut](https://www.themountainterrace.com/wp-content/uploads/2025/12/image-1-1.jpg)

PHOTO BY [IA MORE DESIGN](https://www.lamoredesign.com/)

Elegant and bold, the pear cut is making a major comeback in modern and artistic engagement styles.

**Why it’s trending**

  * Appears larger per carat
  * Stunning in solitaire or halo settings
  * Both slim and wide versions are popular



**How to style:** Perfect with slim bands, halos, or clean solitaire settings.  
**Special note:** Known for giving the look of a larger diamond without the extra cost.

## 4\. Hexagon Cut

![Hexagon Cut](https://www.themountainterrace.com/wp-content/uploads/2025/12/image-5.jpg)

PHOTO BY [SHINE OF DIAMOND](https://shineofdiamond.com/)

A bold, geometric favorite that stands out instantly. Ideal for those who want something different from classic shapes.

**Why it’s trending**

  * Works beautifully with lab-grown diamonds
  * Modern, architectural design
  * Perfect for non-traditional ring lovers



**Best for:** People who prefer edgy, artistic styles.  
**How to style:** Pair with a simple metal band to highlight the geometry.  
**Special note:** Looks especially striking in bezel settings.

## 5\. Asscher Cut

![Asscher Cut](https://www.themountainterrace.com/wp-content/uploads/2025/12/image.jpg)

PHOTO BY [SOPHIE EPTON](https://sophieepton.com/)

Clean, symmetrical, and glamorous, the Asscher cut gives a luxurious vintage feel.

**Why it’s trending**

  * Rise in vintage-inspired jewelry
  * Stunning in platinum and white gold
  * Structured and elegant



**Tips:** Choose higher clarity since step cuts show inclusions more easily.  
**How to style:** Best with minimal or Art Deco–inspired settings.  
**Special note:** Offers a “hall-of-mirrors” light effect that few cuts can match.

## 6\. Radiant Cut

![Radiant Cut](https://www.themountainterrace.com/wp-content/uploads/2025/12/image-2.jpg)

PHOTO BY [WALMART](https://www.walmart.com/), [BENZ & CO](https://www.benzdiamonds.com/)

Perfect for those who want intense sparkle with a modern shape.

**Why it’s trending**

  * Exceptional brilliance
  * Softer corners create a romantic look
  * Works with bold bands, thin bands, and hidden halos



**How to style:** Beautiful with side stones or hidden halos.  
**Special note:** Often appears brighter than emerald or Asscher cuts.

## 7\. Trillion Cut

![Trillion Cut](https://www.themountainterrace.com/wp-content/uploads/2025/12/image-2-1.jpg)

PHOTO BY [CAUSYOU](https://causyou.com/)

Sharp, modern, and futuristic, the trillion cut is a standout choice.

**Why it’s trending**

  * Dramatic, artistic angles
  * Ideal for two-stone and solitaire rings
  * Works well with bezel and open-side settings



**Best for:** Those who want a bold, unconventional design.

**Special note:** One of the most modern-looking shapes available.

## 8\. Emerald Cutz

![Emerald Cut](https://www.themountainterrace.com/wp-content/uploads/2025/12/Emerald-Cut.jpg)

PHOTO BY [LILY ARKWRIGHT](https://lilyarkwright.com/)

A sideways emerald instantly becomes chic, minimal, and refreshing.

**Why it’s trending**

  * Unique yet subtle
  * Perfect for sleek, clean designs
  * Works with both delicate and bold bands



**How to style:** Stunning with a thin gold band or all-metal setting.  
**Special note:** Highlights the diamond’s clarity beautifully.

## 9\. Marquise Cut

![Marquise Cut](https://www.themountainterrace.com/wp-content/uploads/2025/12/Marquise-Cut-1024x768.jpg)

Dramatic and glamorous, the marquise is back in a big way.

**Why it’s trending**

  * Looks larger per carat
  * Finger-flattering, elongated shape
  * Works in both modern and vintage settings



**Best for:** Those who love bold, standout designs.  
**Tips:** Choose V-tips on both ends for extra protection.  
**Special note:** Creates one of the most elegant optical illusions for longer-looking fingers.

## 10\. Heart-Shaped Diamond

![Heart-Shaped Diamond](https://www.themountainterrace.com/wp-content/uploads/2025/12/image-3.jpg)

PHOTO BY [KEN & DNA DESIGN](https://shop.kenanddanadesign.com/)

Romantic and expressive, the heart shape is trending again thanks to bold brides and innovative settings.

**Why it’s trending**

  * A symbol of love made literal
  * Looks gorgeous in solitaires
  * Works well with halos for more brilliance



**Best for:** People who want a deeply meaningful and elegant design.  
**Special note:** Requires excellent symmetry for the best look.

## 11\. Round Brilliant with Hidden Halo

![Round Brilliant with Hidden Halo](https://www.themountainterrace.com/wp-content/uploads/2025/12/image-4.jpg)

A modern twist on the iconic round cut, hidden halos add sparkle without changing the classic silhouette.

**Why it’s trending**

  * Extra shine without looking “too busy”
  * Timeless but upgraded
  * Works with thin, thick, or split-shank bands



**How to style:** Perfect with pavé bands or simple solitaires.  
**Special note:** Hidden halos make the center diamond look significantly larger.

## What’s Driving These 2026 Cut Trends?

### 1\. Personal Expression Matters

Couples want rings that feel unique, not generic. That’s why geometric and artistic cuts are rising fast.

### 2\. Vintage Meets Modern

People love history and nostalgia, but want designs that still feel fresh. This is boosting antique cushions, Asschers, and marquise diamonds.

### 3\. Lab-Grown Diamonds = More Options

Because lab-grown diamonds are more affordable, buyers are embracing uncommon shapes of diamond rings without limitations.

### 4\. Social Media Inspiration

Platforms like Instagram, TikTok, and Pinterest influence trends like elongated ovals, bold bands, and two-stone rings.

## How to Choose the Right Engagement Ring Cut

Choosing the right cut is easier when you consider:

  * **Your hand shape:** elongated cuts make fingers look slimmer
  * **Your lifestyle:** some shapes need more protection
  * **Sparkle preference:** radiant = brilliance, emerald = clarity
  * **Your personal style:** classic, modern, bold, or vintage?



Your ring should feel like a natural extension of your personality.

## Final Thoughts

The engagement ring trends of 2026 highlight individuality, creativity, and meaningful design. Whether you prefer timeless elegance or bold geometry, there’s a diamond shape that perfectly reflects your love and style. Explore, compare, and choose the one that speaks to you.
