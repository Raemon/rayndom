# Social Events

## Celebrate your next anniversary, birthday, or social event in rustic charm. 

Experience the warmth of our service and cozy country-style lodge for your next family gathering, company celebration or holiday party.

Nestled among the redwoods with breathtaking views of the San Francisco Bay and the East Bay hills, The Mountain Terrace is located just outside of Redwood City and Palo Alto. 

You and your guests will enjoy seasonal flavors, award-winning local wines and our refined, yet comfortable service as our team takes care of all the details of your special gathering.

We are the perfect location for your next birthday celebration, holiday party, bar/bat mitzvah, baby shower, bridal shower, or memorial service.

The Mountain Terrace offers an extensive range of services for up to 300 guests including event management, personalized menu planning with our in-house caterer and parking for cars or shuttle buses.
