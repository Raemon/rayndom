# Food & Drink

## Local flavors, expertly prepared to your taste by our in-house chefs.

Our distinctive, in-house catering services are known throughout the region as the perfect complement to our rustic setting and create the ultimate wedding, corporate or social event for you and your guests.

Our culinary team at The Mountain Terrace use farm fresh, locally-sourced, produce, meats and ingredients to create the personalized food and drink experience you envision.

You will enjoy your choice of seasonal flavors paired with award-winning local wines that will leave your guests raving about your wedding, corporate or social event.

The Mountain Terrace offers personalized menu planning with our in-house caterer, with a full suite of food and drink options to serve up to 300 guests.
