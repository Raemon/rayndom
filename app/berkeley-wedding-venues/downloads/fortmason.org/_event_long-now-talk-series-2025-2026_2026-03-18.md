## Long Now Talks

The [Long Now Foundation](https://longnow.org/talks) presents a talk series in the Cowell Theater, Pier 2 at Fort Mason Center For Arts & Culture.

_The Cowell Theater_

* * *

## [Indy Johar](https://longnow.org/ideas/02026-johar/)

Johar is co-founder of [Dark Matter Labs](https://darkmatterlabs.org/) and of the RIBA award winning architecture and urban practice [Architecture 00](https://www.architecture00.net/). He is also a founding director of [Open Systems Lab](https://www.opensystemslab.io/), seeded [WikiHouse](https://www.wikihouse.cc/) and [Open Desk](https://www.opendesk.cc/) . 

January 27, 7:00 p.m.

_Indy Johar_

_[Stefan Sagmeister](https://sagmeister.com)_

## [Finally, Something Good](https://longnow.org/ideas/finally-something-good) With Designer [Stefan Sagmeister](https://sagmeister.com)

A two-time Grammy winner and recipient of practically every important design award, Sagmesiter talks about how happiness and beauty connect to our design and their impact on our daily lives.

February 17, 7:00 p.m.

## [Melody Jue](https://longnow.org/talks/02026-jue/)

Jue is the author of [ _Wild Blue Media: Thinking Through Seawater_](https://www.dukeupress.edu/wild-blue-media), which won the Speculative Fictions and Cultures of Science Book Prize, and the co-editor of [ _Saturation: An Elemental Politics_](https://www.dukeupress.edu/saturation) with Rafico Ruiz. Her research and writings focus on ocean humanities, science fiction, media studies, science and technology studies, and the environmental humanities. 

March 18, 7:00 p.m.

_[Melody Jue](http://www.melodyjue.info/home)_

[Báyò Akómoláfé](https://www.bayoakomolafe.net/)

## [Báyò Akómoláfé](https://longnow.org/talks/02026-akomolafe/)

[Akómoláfé](https://www.bayoakomolafe.net/) is a philosopher, writer, activist, professor of psychology, and executive director of [The Emergence Network](https://www.emergencenetwork.org/). Rooted with the Yoruba people, Akómoláfé works as an essayist, poet, and is the author of  _[These Wilds Beyond Our Fences](https://www.penguinrandomhouse.com/books/555561/these-wilds-beyond-our-fences-by-bayo-akomolafe-foreword-by-charles-eisenstein): Letters To My Daughter On Humanity’s Search for Home_.

May 5, 7:00 p.m.
