## Farmers Market

The Fort Mason Center For Arts & Culture Farmers Market is open Sundays, from **9:30 a.m.** to **1:30 p.m.** , rain or shine. The FMCAC Farmers Market features more than 50 family farmers and gourmet food purveyors.

## MARKET FEATURES

Discover the bounty of the San Francisco Bay Area’s microclimates, the North Bay’s organic farms, and San Joaquin Valley’s finest harvests. This bustling market is the perfect place to find ingredients for any culinary adventure or weeknight meal.

Customers enjoy grocery shopping for items such as pastured meats and eggs, freshly baked breads and pastries, artisan cheeses, local honey, certified organic produce, Asian fruits and vegetables, unique heirloom varieties, and much more.

Visit [California Grown](https://californiagrown.org/blog/whats-in-season-in-california) and the [San Francisco Department Of The Environment](https://www.sfenvironment.org/vegetables-fruits-in-season-bay-area) for seasonal crops calendars to find out what fruits and vegetables are available each month.
