## _Reading Series Extravaganza_

Rainbow Zebra Productions LLC and Magic Theatre present the [ _Reading Series Extravaganza_](https://www.rainbowzebraproductions.org/calendar) 2026 at Fort Mason Center For Arts & Culture.

## PERFORMANCE DETAILS

The series features work by nine playwrights on Mondays, September 22, 2025, September 29, 2025, October 20, 2025, November 24, 2025, December 15, 2025, January 12, 2026, February 9, 2026, March 23, 2026, and April 13, 2026; 7:00 p.m. to 8:00 p.m., 8:30 p.m., or 9:00 p.m. [Andrea Gordon](https://www.3girlstheatre.org/associate-artists/andrea-gordon) directs all of the readings.

First up is Michael Lynch’s [ _Book Of Glass_](https://www.rainbowzebraproductions.org/calendar/bookofglass) on September 22, 2025. [ _My Lunch With Trish_](https://www.rainbowzebraproductions.org/calendar) by [Andrea Gordon](https://www.3girlstheatre.org/associate-artists/andrea-gordon) appears on September 29, 2025. [Susan Jackson](https://www.3girlstheatre.org/company/susan-jackson)‘s [ _Leaving Simone_](https://www.rainbowzebraproductions.org/calendar) takes the stage on October 20, 2025.

For Winter 2025, [ _Training Wheels_](https://www.rainbowzebraproductions.org/calendar) by [Robin Bradford](https://www.3girlstheatre.org/company/robin-bradford) happens on November 24, 2025. [ _A Pickwick Christmas_](https://www.rainbowzebraproductions.org/calendar) adapted by [Brian Lohmann](https://www.linkedin.com/in/brian-lohmann-7b825816) adds holiday cheer on December 15, 2025. [ _The Secret Museum_](https://www.rainbowzebraproductions.org/calendar) by [Lynne Kaufman](https://www.3girlstheatre.org/associate-artists/lynne-kaufman) follows on January 12, 2026.

[_Drunk At The Base Of The Bodhi Tree_](https://www.rainbowzebraproductions.org/calendar) by [Julie Hébert](https://juliehebert.com/) has a reading on February 9, 2026. [Lisa Ramirez’](https://www.3girlstheatre.org/full-3gt-personnel/lisa-ramirez)s [ _Remember Me_](https://www.rainbowzebraproductions.org/calendar) occurs on March 23, 2026. The final selection is [ _Painting Through The Dark_](https://www.rainbowzebraproductions.org/calendar) by Gemma Whelan on April 13, 2026. [Andrea Gordon](https://www.3girlstheatre.org/associate-artists/andrea-gordon) directs all of the readings.

Free Admission ([Reserve space in advance](https://www.classy.org/event/reading-series-extravaganza/e590229/register/new/select-tickets))
