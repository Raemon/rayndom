## Presented By BATS Improv

Explore the world of improv with Fort Mason Center For Arts & Culture resident organization BATS Improv. Saturday nights offer in-person shows at the Bayfront Theater In Building B.

## BATS IMPROV FEATURES

[_Guilty Pleasures: An Improvised Soap Opera_](https://app.gopassage.com/events/bats-improv-presents-guilty-pleasures-11-01-25-11-22-25) lights up Saturdays, November 1 to 22, 2025, 8:00 p.m. to 10:00 p.m.

[_An Improvised Dickens Musical_](https://app.gopassage.com/events/bats-improv-presents-dickens-2025) returns on Saturdays, November 29, 2025 to December 27, 2025, 8:00 p.m. to 10:00 p.m.

[_Plot Twist: New Year’s Eve_](https://app.gopassage.com/events/Plot-Twist-NYE-12-31-25) closes out 2025 on Wednesday, December 31, 2025, 8:00 p.m. to 10:00 p.m. $30 to $35 ($32.38 to $37.64 with fees)

For more improv, visit the BATS [online YouTube video playlist](https://www.youtube.com/playlist?list=FLnsiX_2XoTaF1ISWlltE22g) for free digital clips of BATS Improv’s greatest hits. Online shows from past performances, as well as other BATS Improv videos, are free to view by following [Facebook links](https://www.facebook.com/pg/batsimprov/videos).
