## California Lawyers For The Arts Virtual & In-Person Workshops 2025 FEATURES

Fort Mason Center For Arts & Culture resident organization California Lawyers For The Arts (CLA) offers [online Zoom seminars](https://www.calawyersforthearts.org/events) and phone consultations and has compiled an extensive [digital resource guide](https://www.calawyersforthearts.org/page-1863855) for the arts community. Recently, CLA added a comprehensive directory of trusted resources for 2025 Southern California wildfire relief, including ways to access assistance, support recovery efforts, and contribute to those in need (access the link on the [CLA home page](https://www.calawyersforthearts.org/)).

The “[Phone-In Entertainment Legal Clinic](https://www.calawyersforthearts.org/event-6343801)” is available on Thursday, November 5, 2025, 5:00 p.m. to 7:00 p.m. Register before 5:00 p.m. on Thursday, October 30, 2025 to receive a 20- to 30-minute phone call appointment with an attorney. ($20) ([Register in advance](https://www.calawyersforthearts.org/event-6343801/Registration))

“[Bridging Creative Conflicts – Mediation In Arts Disputes](https://www.calawyersforthearts.org/event-6300945)” takes place on Zoom, Wednesday, November 5, 2025, 6:00 p.m. to 7:30 p.m. Aaron Kemp and Alexandra Bloedorn, Program Coordinator for [Arts Arbitration and Mediation Services](https://www.calawyersforthearts.org/arts-arbitration-mediation-services.html) in the Los Angeles office of CLA, explain how mediation works and how it can help. ($20 to $40, [Register in advance](https://www.calawyersforthearts.org/event-6300945/Registration))

“[The Legal Lifecycle Of A Production](https://www.calawyersforthearts.org/event-6353140)” happens on Zoom, Wednesday, December 10, 2025, 12:00 p.m. to 1:15 p.m. [April M. Paredes](https://www.linkedin.com/in/aprilparedes) of [Ascent Legal](https://ascentlegal.io/attorneys/april-paredes) APLC and [Dani B. Darling](https://www.linkedin.com/in/dani-b-darling) of Netflix Business & Legal Affairs lead the webinar. ($20 to $40, [Register in advance](https://www.calawyersforthearts.org/event-6353140/Registration))

CLA also has an active [YouTube channel](https://www.youtube.com/channel/UChiJdPMGV0yknXN3Rd5YHKw) with [digital archives](https://www.youtube.com/channel/UChiJdPMGV0yknXN3Rd5YHKw) of some seminars that can be viewed for free 24 hours per day on a daily basis. Access a selection of seminars from the “Relax With Tax For Artists & Creatives” series, as well as educational resources about bookkeeping, merchandising, intellectual property law, music production, publishing, and many more topics.
