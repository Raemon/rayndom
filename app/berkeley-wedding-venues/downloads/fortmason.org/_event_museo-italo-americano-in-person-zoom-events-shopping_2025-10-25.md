## Museo Italo Americano: In-Person & Zoom Events & Shopping FEATURES

Fort Mason Center for Arts & Culture’s resident group Museo Italo Americano has online and in-person shopping and in-person and Zoom events during 2025.

Find wonderful gifts at the Museo’s [online shop](https://museoitaloamericano.org/shop). You can also visit the shop in-person, Tuesday through Saturday, 12:00 p.m. to 4:00 p.m. and Sunday, 10:00 a.m. to 2:00 p.m. Discover beautiful [Murano glass hearts;](https://museoitaloamericano.org/shop/product-categories/blown-glass) [fused- and blown-glass jewelry](https://museoitaloamericano.org/shop/product-categories/jewelry); brilliant [blown-glass and fused-glass birds](https://museoitaloamericano.org/shop/product-categories/blown-glass); books; and [Italia-branded men’s items](https://museoitaloamericano.org/shop/product-categories/italia).

“Cena & Telechat [Virtual Version]” conversations over meals or wine or prosecco continue on Mondays, [November 17, 2025](https://sfmuseo.org/event/cena-telechat-11-25) and [December 15, 2025](https://sfmuseo.org/event/cena-telechat-12-25); from 6:00 p.m. to 7:00 p.m. Conversations are in Italian and happen on Zoom. (FREE for members, $10 for non-members). Register [online](https://museoitaloamericano.org/events); email [info@SFMuseo.org](mailto:info@SFMuseo.org); or call (415) 673-2200.

“[Antipasti d’Autunno](https://sfmuseo.org/event/antipasti25)” invites all to savor the flavors of Autumn in Italy with an evening that celebrates the season’s bounty on Wednesday, November 5, 2025, 6:00 p.m. to 8:30 p.m. Enjoy exquisite regional dishes paired with fine Italian wines, thoughtfully curated to capture the essence of Fall. Renowned [Chef Alfredo Ancona](https://www.angelinorestaurant.com/our-family-1) of [Angelino Restaurant](https://www.angelinorestaurant.com/) in Sausalito, CA guides attendees on this culinary journey, showcasing the rich traditions and diversity of Italian cuisine. Jason Chietti, Vice President of [Siena Imports](https://sienaimports.com/), pairs special Italian wines to compliment the meal. he evening concludes with an Opportunity Drawing, offering the chance to win local restaurant gift cards, select wines, Italian lessons at the Museo, and other enticing prizes. ($150 for members, $200 for non-members)

Evening Program  
Hosted Aperitivo**:** 6:00 p.m. to 7:00 p.m.  
Dinner: 7:00 p.m. to 8:30 p.m.

“[Discover Calabria](https://sfmuseo.org/event/calabria)” features a talk with Franco Cirelli and celebrates the beauty of Calabria on Sunday, November 16, 2025, 4:30 p.m. to 6:30 p.m. This lecture is part of the Museo’s “Regional Sunday Series.” ($10 for members, $20 for non-members)
