## Leg[à](https://sfmuseo.org/exhibition/legami-bonds)mi/Bonds Presented By Museo Italo Americano

Museo Italo Americano presents “[Legàmi/Bonds](https://sfmuseo.org/exhibition/legami-bonds)” at Fort Mason Center For Arts & Culture. “[Legàmi/Bonds](https://sfmuseo.org/exhibition/legami-bonds)” connects art, artists, and donors through selected works by 44 artists from the Museo’s permanent collection.

Marietta Patricia Leis, “[Tond](https://sfmuseo.org/artwork/leis-marietta-patricia-tondo)[o](https://sfmuseo.org/artwork/leis-marietta-patricia-tondo)”

## EXHIBITION FEATURES

“[Legàmi/Bonds](https://sfmuseo.org/exhibition/legami-bonds)” connects art, artists, and donors through selected works by 44 artists from the Museo’s permanent collection. 

The exhibition features works from local artists Beniamino Bufano and Lawrence Ferlinghetti to international figures such as Giorgio De Chirico and Tina Modotti.

[](http://fortmason.org/wp-content/uploads/2025/10/museo_pomodoro_featured.jpg)Arnoldo Pomodoro, “[Tavola Della Memoria II](https://sfmuseo.org/artwork/pomodoro-arnaldo-tavola-della-memoria-ii)“ [](http://fortmason.org/wp-content/uploads/2021/05/museo_bufano_madonna_featured.jpg)Beniamino Bufano, “Madonna,” Great Meadow, Fort Mason [](http://fortmason.org/wp-content/uploads/2025/10/museo_leis_tondo_featured.jpg)Marietta Patricia Leis, “[Tond](https://sfmuseo.org/artwork/leis-marietta-patricia-tondo)[o](https://sfmuseo.org/artwork/leis-marietta-patricia-tondo)”
