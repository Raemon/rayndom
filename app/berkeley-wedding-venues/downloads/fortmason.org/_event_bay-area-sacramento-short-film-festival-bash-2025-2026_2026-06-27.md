## Bay Area & Sacramento Short Film Festival FEATURES

Discover filmmaking creativity at the Bay Area & Sacramento Short Film Festival (BASH) 2025 in Building D at Fort Mason Center For Arts & Culture. BASH is a semi-annual film festival showcasing a diverse sampling of San Francisco Bay Area- and Sacramento-made shorts and mini-features, award-winning directors, and amateurs breaking in the industry and blowing everyone away.

From epic comedies and horror to serious dramas and documentaries, BASH selections are eclectic and range all across the board. Audience choice winners win trophies and exposure on BASH networks, plus red carpet pictures.
