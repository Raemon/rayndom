## BATS SCHOOL OF IMPROV FEATURES

Fort Mason Center for Arts & Culture resident group BATS Improv’s acclaimed [School Of Improv](http://www.improv.org/school) offers in-person and virtual online classes for adults, children, and teens. Adult classes cover many aspects of improv: games, exercises, scene work, character development, spontaneity, and more.

Adult classes include a complete series of [Foundation](https://www.improv.org/school/#foundation) 1, 2, 3, 4, 5 and 6 classes, [Specialty](https://www.improv.org/school/#specialty) classes, [Performance Labs](https://www.improv.org/school/#labs), and [Improv Jams](https://www.improv.org/school/#jams). Youth Classes are also available.

For more information, visit the [BATS School Of Improv](http://www.improv.org/school) pages for class meetings dates, times, prices, and any pre-requisites.
