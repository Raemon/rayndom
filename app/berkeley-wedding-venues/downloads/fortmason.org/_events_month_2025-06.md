###  Museo Italo Americano – Echoes: Sharon D’Amico, Lar Landa & Nina Temple

[ ](https://fortmason.org/event/museo-italo-americano-echoes-sharon-damico-lar-landa-nina-temple/ "Museo Italo Americano – Echoes: Sharon D’Amico, Lar Landa & Nina Temple")

June 24, 2025 @ 12:00 pm \- October 12, 2025 @ 4:00 pm

###  [ Museo Italo Americano – Echoes: Sharon D’Amico, Lar Landa & Nina Temple ](https://fortmason.org/event/museo-italo-americano-echoes-sharon-damico-lar-landa-nina-temple/ "Museo Italo Americano – Echoes: Sharon D’Amico, Lar Landa & Nina Temple")

Museo Italo Americano presents “Echoes” at Fort Mason Center For Arts & Culture. This exhibit showcases photography by Sharon D’Amico, paintings by Lar Landa, and ink on paper works by Nina Temple.

$10
