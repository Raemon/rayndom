## Young Performers Theatre Academy Fall 2025 FEATURES

Fort Mason Center for Arts & Culture resident group Young Performers Theatre offers in-person [Fall 2025 classes](https://www.ypt.org/classes) for children who love the stage. The 10- to 11-week classes start Monday, September 15, 2025 and continue through Friday, December 12, 2025. (No classes on Monday, October 13, 2025; Monday through Friday, November 10 to 14, 2025; or November 24 to 28, 2025.) YPT’s classes are held in the Southside Theater, Building D, third floor.

The season includes “[Let’s Pretend”](https://www.eventbrite.com/e/lets-pretend-345-430-tickets-1344649776679) for ages three to five years old, meeting on Monday, September 15, 2025 through December 8, 2025, [3:45 p.m. to 4:30 p.m](https://www.eventbrite.com/e/lets-pretend-345-430-tickets-1344649776679). (No classes on Monday, October 13, 2025; November 10, 2025; or November 24, 2025.) $350 ($364.74 with fees)

“[Creative Drama](https://www.ypt.org/classes)” for ages five to eight years (grades one through three) meets on Tuesday, 4:00 p.m. to 5:00 p.m., [September 16, 2025 through December 9, 2025](https://www.eventbrite.com/e/creative-drama-tuesdays-tickets-1344659305179); or Thursday, [September 18, 2025 through December 11, 2025](https://www.eventbrite.com/e/creative-drama-thursdays-tickets-1344671842679); 4:00 p.m. to 5:00 p.m. (No classes on Tuesday, November 11, 2025 or November 25, 2025; or on Thursday; November 13, 2025 or November 27, 2025.) The final class features a performance for family and friends. Tuition is $400 ($428.67 with fees).

“[Presenting … The Show](https://www.eventbrite.com/e/presenting-the-show-tickets-1344673457509)!” for students in grades three through six (ages eight to 11 years old) meets on Wednesday, 4:00 p.m. to 5:30 p.m., from September 17, 2025 through December 10, 2025. (No classes on November 12, 2025 or November 26, 2025.) Students present a show on the final day of class. Tuition is $410 ($426.96 with fees).

“[Presenting … The Show, Act II](https://www.eventbrite.com/e/presenting-the-show-act-ii-tickets-1344681421329)!” offers students in grades four through seven (ages 10 through 14 years old) a workshop to create characters and narratives on Friday, September 19 2025 through December 12, 2025, 4:30 p.m. to 6:00 p.m. (No classes on Friday, November 14, 2025 or November 28, 2025.) The final class date results in a play staged for a live audience. Tuition is $410 ($426.96 with fees).
