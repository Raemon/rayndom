## Soulful Christmas 2025 FEATURES

Enjoy a spirited, soulful holiday gospel concert with Lorraine Hansberry Theatre (LHTSF)’s acclaimed gospel choir performing [ _Soulful Christmas_](https://www.lhtsf.org/soulful) at Magic Theatre in Building D, Third Floor. This incredibly popular event is sure to sell out and features fun, witty dialogue, and favorite music that brings everyone to their feet. While conjuring a winter wonderland filled with upbeat music, joyful lyrics, and high-energy performances, some of the San Francisco Bay Area’s favorite powerhouse singers blow the roof off the house.

Directed by [Margo Hall](https://www.margohall.com/) with Musical Director [Dr. Yvonne Cobbs](https://yvonneccobbs.com/),  _Soulful Christmas_ is the holiday celebration to experience.  _Soulful Christmas_ features an ensemble of the Bay Area’s most powerful voices delivering joyous melodies, soul-stirring vocals, and a performance overflowing with holiday cheer. The program includes beloved selections such as “O Come All Ye Faithful,” “Silent Night,” “White Christmas,” “Jingle Bell Rock,” “Carol of the Bells,” and more.
