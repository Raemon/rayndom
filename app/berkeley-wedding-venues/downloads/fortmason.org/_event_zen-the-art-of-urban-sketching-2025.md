## Presented By FLAX Art & Design Store

Join local artist [Zip Lehnus](https://www.ziplehnus.net/) at [FLAX Art & Design Store](https://flaxart.com/workshops-events) for in-person, outdoor workshops, “[Zen + The Art of Urban Sketching](https://flaxart.com/zen-the-art-of-urban-sketching),” at Fort Mason Center for Arts & Culture.

## Zen & The Art Of Urban Sketching FEATURES

Join local artist [Zip Lehnus](https://www.ziplehnus.net/) at [FLAX Art & Design Store](https://flaxart.com/workshops-events) for in-person, outdoor workshops, “[Zen + The Art of Urban Sketching](https://flaxart.com/zen-the-art-of-urban-sketching),” at Fort Mason Center for Arts & Culture. Lehnus is a science writer, naturalist, urban artist, and avid sketcher whose subjects reflect his science and nature interests. Lehnus has a gallery on [Instagram](https://www.instagram.com/BigStickArts).

The workshop begins at FLAX in Building D, where participants pick up their urban sketching kit (included in the $75 class fee), go over basic urban sketching techniques, and set off to discover beautiful outdoor settings perfect for urban sketching. 

The $75 workshop happens on Saturday, October 18, 2025; from 12:00 p.m. to 3:00 p.m., and includes everything needed to jump-start a passion for urban sketching (more than $57 worth of items).

[Materials included](https://flaxart.com/zen-the-art-of-urban-sketching#materials) are: the [Sakura Koi Watercolor Pocket Set](https://flaxart.com/koi-watercolor-sketch-boxes) (a $27.95 value), the [Strathmore Vision Mixed Media Pad](https://www.strathmoreartist.com/vision/vision-mixed-media) ($10.75 value), three [Sakura Pigma Micron Pens](https://flaxart.com/pigma-micron-pens) (0.45 mm tip) in black, orange, and purple (an $8.97 value), a [Uni-Ball Gel Impact Pen](https://flaxart.com/uni-ball-gel-impact-pen-white) in Coconut White (a $3.95 value), a [Palomino Blackwing Pencil](https://flaxart.com/pencils/blackwing) (matte, soft, $2.50), a [General Pencil Extra-Soft White Vinyl Eraser](https://flaxart.com/factis-extra-soft-vinyl-eraser) ($1.25), and a [Magnesium Wedge Sharpener](https://flaxart.com/kum-metal-single-hole-sharpener) ($1.75).
