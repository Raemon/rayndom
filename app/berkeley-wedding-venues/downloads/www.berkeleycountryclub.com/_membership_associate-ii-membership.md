# Associate II Membership

Associate II Members enjoy full access to the Club with limited tee time booking windows and no voting rights. Associate II Membership is for individuals aged 30 to 39 and is based on the oldest of the two which will be primary member of the account. This is a family membership that includes a spouse or significant other and any children under the age of 26. Associate II Members are not required to pay an additional initiation fee when reaching the age of 40. Dues will increase to the equity level at that time. 

Privileges
