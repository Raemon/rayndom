# Transitional Offer

This is perfect for avid golfers to experience the private golf club life and smoothly transition into a full golfing membership. Enjoy a one-year membership with no initiation fee!

Individual Membership featuring flexible tee time booking, a 4-hour pace of play, breathtaking views of the SF Bay Area from every hole, access to member tournaments and social activities, and more. Benefit from special rates for converting to full membership.

Offer available for Equity, Associate II and Associate I memberships.
