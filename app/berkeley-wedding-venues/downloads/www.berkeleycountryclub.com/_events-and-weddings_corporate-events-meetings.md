# Corporate Events & Meetings

## Host Your Corporate Event with Unforgettable Bay Views

Berkeley Country Club offers a sophisticated and versatile setting for corporate events, meetings, and celebrationswith panoramic views of the San Francisco Bay and skyline. From executive retreats to large-scale conferences, our event spaces are fully equipped to ensure a seamless experience.

[Request Event Information](https://www.berkeleycountryclub.com/events-and-weddings/forms/request-information)

### Bay Ballroom

**Elegant. Spacious. Iconic Views.**  
The Bay Ballroom is our largest indoor venue, featuring a stunning outlook of the Golden Gate Bridge and San Francisco skyline. With room for up to **220 guests** , it's perfect for conferences, awards ceremonies, company parties, or networking events. **Key Features:**

  * Full bar

  * Stage and dance floor

  * Built-in fireplace

  * Direct access to Vista Room




### Vista Room

**An Intimate Space with a View**  
Connected to the Bay Ballroom, the Vista Room is ideal for smaller gatherings or as a complementary space for pre- or post-event functions. With a capacity of 25-220 guest, depending on layout, It offers sweeping bay views in a cozy, upscale atmosphere. **Ideal For:**

  * Cocktail hours

  * Seated dinners and buffets

  * Breakout sessions or ceremonies




### Vista Deck

**Outdoor Elegance Overlooking the Bay**  
Host your event under the open sky on our stunning Vista Deck. With a capacity of up to **220 guests** , it's the ideal setting for outdoor ceremonies, happy hours, and celebrations. **Amenities:**

  * Elevator access

  * Electrical setup for DJs and vendors

  * Tray-passed drinks and appetizers

  * Customizable event setup




### Food & Beverage

**Seasonal Cuisine, Crafted Cocktails**  
Executive Chef **Dan Torresan** curates seasonal menus with locally sourced ingredients to suit every palate. Choose from **plated meals** , **buffet service** , or **passed hors d'oeuvres** tailored to your event style. Our **award-winning mixologists** elevate the bar experience with signature cocktails, premium spirits, and table-side wine service.

### Custom Room Layouts

We design flexible room configurations to meet your event goalsfrom round tables seating 10, to highboys and cocktail tables. Choose from a variety of linen colors, folds, and table setups. Bringing your own décor or furniture? We're happy to accommodate.

### Audio-Visual Support

Our event spaces come equipped with complete sound systems. We also offer:

  * Projector and screen rental

  * On-site AV support

  * Vendor-ready infrastructure for DJs and tech teams




### Dedicated Event Teams

Our experienced teamfrom sales to coordinationis committed to excellence and professionalism. Events are led by **on-site Event Coordinators** to ensure every detail is executed flawlessly and will guide you through planning a high-end, customized experience.

### Preferred Vendor Network

We'll connect you with trusted professionals who know our venue and deliver exceptional service. Take the stress out of planningour vendor partners include:

  * Florists

  * Photographers

  * Event designers

  * Bakers and specialty caterers




Let Berkeley Country Club be the backdrop to your next corporate event. **Contact us today** to schedule a tour or request a proposal.
