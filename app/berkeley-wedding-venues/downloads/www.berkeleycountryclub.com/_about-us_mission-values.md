# Mission & Values

**Vision**

To be a premier member-owned private golf club in the San Francisco Bay Area.

**Mission**

To offer members a championship golf course designed by Robert Hunter and William Watson in 1920 with incomparable views of the Bay Area. Berkeley Country Club (BCC) provides both casual and elegant dining with a range of fun social activities in a historic clubhouse designed by noted architect Walter Ratcliffe, Jr. We celebrate our long history of diversity in a friendly and welcoming environment, making BCC a very special place for our multi-generational membership.

**Core Values**

• Encourage inclusion, participation and a sense of belonging in a respectful atmosphere for members, guests and staff.

• Promote enjoyable activities and interactions among members and guests

• Grow the game of golf through outreach to and program activities for younger generations.

• Treat our employees as important contributors and empower them to act in the best interests of our club.

• Govern in a transparent manner, providing clear information to members and encouraging their input.

• Manage the club in a fiscally prudent and sustainable manner.

• Commit to the growth and retention of memberships.

• Encourage and foster a volunteer ethic within our membership.

• Share our appreciation for the game of golf, its traditions and life lessons.

• Serve as responsible and caring stewards of our club to continuously improve the course and facilities.

• Continue to respect our environment and be good citizens of our surrounding communities.
