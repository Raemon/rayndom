#  Your Private Golf Destination 

#### A home away from home

Founded in 1920, Berkeley Country Club has long been the hidden gem in the East Bay Hills. With unparalleled, panoramic views of the Golden Gate and Bay, BCC's 18-hole championship caliber course is unlike any you've seen. Its challenging design, immaculate fairways, and undulating greens make every round feel unique, and a pleasure for both the novice and serious golfer. 

Berkeley Country Club offers full-service amenities to help round out all facets of the game and meet personal health goals, including a newly designed, top-tier fitness center, driving range, and simulator room. Three separate dining halls, world-class cuisine, and a friendly, accommodating staff provides a perfect way to relax before or after a round. Come join us on our outdoor patio where you can savor a cocktail and witness a world-class, unrivaled sunset.
