#  About Us 

Berkeley Country Club is home to a welcoming, diverse membership that is truly passionate about the game of golf. From casual rounds to friendly games to competitive tournaments, golfers of all skill levels will feel right at home at BCC. We are proud of our commitment to the greater community, which champions inclusiveness and acceptance. 

The club regularly hosts outside functions and charitable events, and its sky deck makes it a coveted wedding destination for many newlyweds. Berkeley Country Club has long been among the most racially and gender diverse private clubs in the Bay Area. As an open-space landholder, we take our environmental stewardship seriously and have adopted agronomic practices that support conservation and protect the natural habitat. Come out for a round! Be advised, though: once you do, you might never want to play anywhere else.
