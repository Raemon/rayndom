# Equity Membership

Equity Members enjoy full access to golf, social, equity and voting privileges. This is a family membership that includes a spouse or significant other and any children under the age of 26. Host guests on course with preferred green fee rates and take advantage of complimentary weekday guest passes annually. Equity Members have access to Lakeridge Athletic Club amenities for no additional cost. 

Privileges 

  * Voting Rights 




  * 18-hole Golf Course 




  * 7 Day Advance Tee Time Booking 




  * Annual Complimentary Weekday Guest Passes 




  * Practice Facilities & Golf Simulator 




  * Fitness Center 




  * Lakeridge Athletic Club Amenities - Access to year-round heated Swimming Pool, Tennis, Bocce, and more. 




  * The Hunter Grill Restaurant 




  * Member Events & Activities - Supper Club, Bingo Night, Men’s & Women’s Invitational, Easter Brunch Buffet, Happy Hour & Live Entertainment, and Holiday Events
