## FAQ:

____ Q: What is included in my ticket?

Each ticket includes admission to the one-hour live performance by Spencer Grey in the Carriage House, followed by a self-guided Flashlight Tour of the Winchester Mystery House.

____ Q: What kind of performance is Mind & Magic?

Mind & Magic is a **close-up, personal, and interactive** experience. Guests will experience mind-reading, stunning magic, clean humor, and audience participation. 

____ Q: How long is the entire experience?

The magic show is approximately 60 minutes long. The self-guided Flashlight Tour typically takes about 45–60 minutes to complete, depending on your pace.

____ Q: Is there age restrictions?

The show is recommended for adults, suitable for ages 10 and up.

Participants must be 5 years or older to join the Flashlight Tours.

____ Q: Is the experience wheelchair accessible?

The Carriage House, where the magic show takes place, is fully accessible. However, please note that the Flashlight Tour portion of the evening involves many stairs and narrow hallways and is **not** wheelchair accessible. If you have mobility concerns but are able to navigate a limited number of steps, please reach out to us at **[[email protected]](/cdn-cgi/l/email-protection)** or call **408-247-2000** so we can discuss the tour’s physical requirements and how we might best accommodate you.

____ Q: Where can I find more information about the Flashlight Tour?

For specific details, safety guidelines, and additional FAQs regarding the tour portion of your evening, please visit our [Flashlight Tour Information Page.](https://winchestermysteryhouse.com/flashlight-tour/)

____ Q: Can I get a refund or reschedule my tickets?

No. Mind & Magic is a Special Event, and all tickets are non-refundable and non-transferable.

If you have additional questions or need further assistance, feel free to contact our support team at [[email protected]](/cdn-cgi/l/email-protection). We look forward to welcoming you at Winchester Mystery House!
