## FAQ:

____ Q: What is the Flashlight Tour?

A: The Flashlight Tour is a self-guided evening tour that allows brave guests to explore the dark halls of the mansion with a flashlight. It is a rare chance to see one of the world’s most alleged haunted mansions at night!

____ Q: Is this a guided tour?

A: This is a self guided tour, you’ll move in a linear one way path through the house. Winchester Mystery House staff will be available for questions and assistance along the tour route.

____ Q: Is there age restrictions for the Flashlight Tour?

A: Yes, participants must be 5 years or older to join the Flashlight Tours.

____ Q: Can I bring my own paranormal investigation equipment?

A: You’re welcome to bring your own paranormal investigation equipment provided that it is handheld and will not cause any damage to the house or disruption to tours.

____ Q: DO I NEED TO BRING MY OWN FLASHLIGHT?

A: No, a flashlight will be provided for you. You are welcome to bring your own if you would like.

____ Q: Who will I be on tour with?

A: This is a self guided tour. The group you come with will be the group you travel through the house with.

____ Q: Are Flashlight Tours wheelchair accessible?

A: Unfortunately, due to the historical nature of the Winchester Mystery House, the Flashlight Tours are not wheelchair accessible. 

____ Q: Are photos and videos allowed on flashlight tours?

A: Yes! Photos and videos are allowed during Flashlight Tours, but please remember to turn off your flash. We also ask that you’re mindful of others—don’t block walkways or stay in one area too long, as it may prevent other guests from enjoying that part of the tour. Our guides may kindly ask you to move along if needed.

____ Q: Can I cancel or reschedule my Flashlight Tour ticket?

A: Tickets are non-refundable, but we may consider rescheduling requests based on availability. Please reach out to our customer support for assistance.

If you have additional questions or need further assistance, feel free to contact our support team at [[email protected]](/cdn-cgi/l/email-protection). We look forward to welcoming you to an unforgettable exploration at Winchester Mystery House!
