### MANSION TOUR FAQS

Q: What Is The Mansion Tour About?

The daily Mansion Tour at the Winchester Mystery House guides you through 110 of the 160 rooms in the home. During this tour you will learn about the amazing woman at the heart of this home and see all the unique architectural designs and features it holds. Come delve into the mystery and find out why this home is so captivating!

Q: Is The Mansion Tour Child Friendly?

All that talk of paranormal sometimes worries parents that the tour will be too scary for children. Not to worry – all ages are welcome and the tour is very family friendly! Children four and under are free and ages 5-12 will need a ticket. 

Q: Is The House Really Haunted?

That’s just part of the mystery, right? Since 1923, many guests and employees have experienced strange phenomena while inside Sarah Winchester’s beautiful mansion. We have been listed on plenty of “world’s most haunted places” lists, but we encourage you to come and find out for yourself.

Q: Do You Have Any Discounts?

Please visit <https://winchestermysteryhouse.com/discounts-promotions/> for our current discount and promotions.

Q: How Long Does The Mansion Tour Last?

The duration of the Mansion Tour typically lasts approximately 65 minutes.

Q: Is Photography And Videography Allowed During The Mansion Tour?

Photography is permitted during the Mansion Tour, but flash photography and videography is not. Please respect any guidelines provided by our tour guides.

Q: How Can I Book Tickets For The Mansion Tour?

Tickets for the Mansion Tour can be booked online through our website at winchestermysteryhouse.com or purchased at the Winchester Mystery House ticket office. We recommend booking in advance to secure your spot as availability may be limited.

Q: Is The Mansion Tour Wheelchair Accessible?

Due to the historic nature of the Winchester Mystery House, the inside of the mansion is not wheelchair accessible. This tour involves multiple stairs and extensive walking throughout the home.

Please contact us in advance to discuss any accessibility requirements, and we will do our best to accommodate your needs. We have ADA Video Tours available onsite if needed. You can find accessibility information [here](https://winchestermysteryhouse.com/accessibilty/).

Q: Can I Cancel Or Reschedule My Mansion Tour Booking?

Cancellations and rescheduling are subject to our tour booking policies. Please refer to your booking confirmation or contact our customer service team for assistance with any changes to your reservation.

### BASEMENT ACCESS FAQS

Q: What Is The Basement Access Add On?

A: The Basement Access is an exclusive add on experience after your Mansion Tour. Guests will be able to enjoy exclusive _self-guided_ access to not just one, but _two_ basements of the mansion. Steps away after the conclusion of your mansion tour, descend into darkness and explore the deepest depths of the house most known for its reported paranormal activity. This experience allows guests to freely explore both basements at their own pace with guides available for stories and questions. 

Q: Is The Basement Access Child Friendly?

A: Due to the exposed areas of the basement, which require safety helmets, children under 5 are not permitted. It is recommended that children must be 5 and up and be able to walk unassisted. We do ask that any minor be accompanied with an adult at any time. 

Q: Is the Basement Access included in the regular admission ticket to the Winchester Mystery House?

A: No, Basement Access is a separate add on experience and is not included in the regular admission ticket. We do recommend purchasing the add on experience for after your Mansion Tour to have the ultimate Winchester Mystery House exploration during your visit. 

Q: How Long Does The Basement Access Last?

A: The duration of the Basement Access can range from 20 minutes to 35 minutes, depending on how long you wish to explore.

Q: Are photography and videography allowed during the Basement Access?

A: Photography is permitted during the Basement Access, but flash photography and videography is not. Please respect any guidelines provided by our tour guides.

Q: How can I book tickets for the Basement Access?

A: Tickets for the Basement Access can be booked online through our website at winchestermysteryhouse.com or purchased at the Winchester Mystery House ticket office. This is available as an add on to the Mansion Tour only and is not available as a stand alone experience. **To purchase the Basement add-on when booking online….** First Select and add your Mansion Tour to your cart. Then fill out your contact information. Finally, on the next page, you’ll see the option to add the basement access to your visit.

Q: Is the Basement Access wheelchair accessible?

A: Unfortunately, due to the historic nature of the Winchester Mystery House, the basements are not wheelchair accessible. Please contact us in advance to discuss any accessibility requirements, and we will do our best to accommodate your needs.

Q: Can I cancel Basement Access add on?

Cancellations and rescheduling are subject to our tour booking policies. Please refer to your booking confirmation or contact our customer service team for assistance with any changes to your reservation.
