PARKING

Winchester Mystery House offers complimentary parking to guests. Due to nearby construction around Santana Row, parking is limited. Parking is available in the front lot with an overflow lot behind our Estate, off of Olsen Drive. There is additional parking at the following:

  * Winchester Garage - 330 Winchester Blvd, San Jose, CA 95128
  * Parking Garage - 377 Santana Row San Jose, CA 95128



*Please be aware that Olsen Drive does not permit on-street parking and towing or ticketing may occur.

*Lockers are available onsite for guests to put valuables during their tour.

DRIVING DIRECTIONS

From Sacramento and The Central Valley

Take I-680 South to San Jose. Take Mission Blvd. exit in Fremont and connect to I-880 South. Take I-880 South to San Jose. Take Stevens Creek Blvd. exit. Proceed West on Stevens Creek Blvd. to Winchester Blvd. Turn left onto Winchester Blvd. and proceed two stoplights, and the Winchester Mystery House™ will be on your right.

From Santa Cruz

Take Highway 17 North to San Jose. Take Stevens Creek Blvd. exit. Proceed West on Stevens Creek Blvd. to Winchester Blvd. Turn left onto Winchester Blvd. and proceed two stoplights, and the Winchester Mystery House™ will be on your right.

From Monterey

Take Highway 1 North. Take Highway 156 East and connect onto Highway 101 North. Take Highway 101 North to San Jose. In San Jose, take I-880 South. Take Stevens Creek Blvd. exit. Proceed West on Stevens Creek Blvd. to Winchester Blvd. Turn left onto Winchester Blvd. and proceed two stoplights, and the Winchester Mystery House™ will be on your right.

From Highway 99 and I-5

Take Highway 152 West to Highway 101. Take Highway 101 North to San Jose. In San Jose, take I-880 South. Take Stevens Creek Blvd. exit. Proceed West on Stevens Creek Blvd. to Winchester Blvd. Turn left onto Winchester Blvd. and proceed two stoplights, and the Winchester Mystery House™ will be on your right.

From San Francisco And The North Bay

Take I-280 South to San Jose. Take Winchester Blvd. exit. At stoplight, turn left. At next stoplight (Winchester Blvd.), turn left. Proceed two stoplights, and the Winchester Mystery House™ will be on your left.

From Oakland And The East Bay

Take I-280 South to San Jose. Take Winchester Blvd. exit. At stoplight, turn left. At next stoplight (Winchester Blvd.), turn left. Proceed two stoplights, and the Winchester Mystery House™ will be on your left.

From San Francisco Via Caltrain

Take CalTrain (4th and King) to Santa Clara Station. Transfer to Santa Clara Valley Transportation Agency (VTA) Bus #60 Southbound. Bus stop is in front of Winchester Mystery House™. CalTrain: 1-800-660-4287, VTA: 1-408-321-2300
