## GROUP TOUR

A private group tour at the Winchester Mystery House is the ultimate group bonding experience. Let us host you and your group of friends, family, or coworkers on a tour of Sarah Winchester’s beautiful but bizarre home. Dive deeper into the mystery and unlock the secrets of the world-famous Winchester Mystery House. **Discounts are only available for groups of 20 or more**. Book your group tour today!

## SCHOOL & YOUTH TRIP

Looking for a fun and historical youth trip for your school, scout group, or summer camp? Give your youth group a once-in-a-lifetime historical experience at the Winchester Mystery House! The historical experience at the estate is a great way for kids to learn about Victorian architecture, Sarah Winchester and her influence on the Santa Clara Valley, and what life and work was like on the estate in the late 1800’s.

**Please note that we require a minimum of three weeks notice when booking to allow for proper preparation and staffing. Thank you for your understanding.**
