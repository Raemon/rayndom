Venture into Sarah’s secluded attic spaces with this exclusive, self-guided experience.

This journey allows you to witness rarely-seen architecture and construction, including original materials rescued after the 1906 earthquake. You will also explore the infamous Witches Cap room, known for its reported paranormal activity.

Explore these unique spaces at your own pace with expert guides nearby to share stories and answer questions along the way. Time estimate: 15–30 minutes. Ages 5+.

ADA TOUR

Due to the historic nature of the Winchester Estate, the Mansion Tour is not wheelchair accessible. An ADA Tour is available for guests on property with mobility issues. The ADA tour includes a video tour in one of the accessible garden buildings and the self-guided Sarah Winchester Garden Tour.
