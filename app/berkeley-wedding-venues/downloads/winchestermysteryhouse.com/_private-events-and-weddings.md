## winchester estate weddings

Experience the enchantment of saying your vows in the Front Gardens with the beautiful and bizarre mansion as your backdrop. Following the ceremony, continue the celebration in the delightful ambiance of Sarah’s bright & airy Greenhouse or the charmingly rustic Carriage House, where your reception will unfold in style.

The Winchester Estate, a magnificent Queen Anne style Victorian mansion is an ideal location for couples looking for something completely unique. **Discover availability and pricing by inquiring now.**

Winchester Mystery House® is open to the public daily and operating hours vary seasonally. We do close the gardens to the public during ceremonies as well as provide private event space for your wedding or event. Please note that events and weddings cannot be held on Halloween, Thanksgiving, and Christmas Day.
