## About this venue

The Santa Clara Marriot is located in the heart of Silicon Valley and in close proximity to the Santa Clara Convention Center, as well as local businesses like Cisco, HP EMC and Intel. The contemporary facilities of the Santa Clara Marriot make a great setting for your meeting, event or wedding. Treat your guests to top notch accommodations, relaxing surroundings and extraordinary service at this northern California hotel. The Santa Clara Marriot features 24 meeting rooms for a total of 23,500 square feet of meeting space. Accommodate up to 1,200 guests in the California Ballroom, 400 guests in the Hall of Cities Ballroom and 500 in the Grand Ballroom.
