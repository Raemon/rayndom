### How do I create a Gathering?

It's so easy! 1. Enter a name for the Gathering 2. If you purchased a Wedding Package, copy the Purchase Code from the Thank You email to the Purchase Code field. 3. Click 'Start Gathering' Don't forget to share the Gathering link with everyone!

### How do I change the Top Bar Image?

Option 1: Click the blue button on the Top Bar Image itself and select a file from your computer or phone. Option 2: Click the blue button on any image in the gallery.

### How do I control the Gathering settings?

If you are the owner of the Gathering and you have purchased a Wedding Package for this Gathering you can control some Gathering options. 1. Click 'Open Gathering Options'. 2. You can toggle 'Allow edit of Top Bar Image' to allow guests to change it. 3. You can toggle 'Allow visitors to delete images' to allow guests to delete images.
