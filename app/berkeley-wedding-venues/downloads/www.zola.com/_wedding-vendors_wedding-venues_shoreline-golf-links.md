## About this venue

With sweeping views of the iconic San Francisco Bay and the lush greens of Shoreline Golf Links, this serene setting in the heart of Silicon Valley is perfect for your wedding day. Whether you're planning an intimate celebration or a grand affair, Michael’s at Shoreline combines natural beauty with elegant hospitality. Our experienced event team is dedicated to making your day seamless and unforgettable — from the moment your guests arrive to your final toast.
