## About this venue

Surrounded by 50 acres of organically farmed vineyards, the grounds at BARRA of Mendocino offer an exquisite setting for your cherished celebration. Whether it be our 5,000 square foot tasting room with its 40-foot, wood beam ceilings or the sprawling event lawn and perennial gardens, this distinctive venue provides you with endless indoor and outdoor options to capture memories that will last a lifetime! When you are making your final venue decisions, rest assured that you are working with a trusted, respected partner in BARRA of Mendocino. Charlie Barra planted his first vineyard in Redwood Valley in 1955 and the Barra family has owned the BARRA of Mendocino Tasting Room & Event Center since 1996, hosting over 50 events a year at this popular Mendocino County location.

### Can we bring our own alcohol?

We require you to purchase at least 3 cases of our wine to serve at your event. Outside beer is allowed (cans or kegs preferred) as well as outside bubbly. No hard liquor is allowed.

Yes! Plenty of parking available.

### Do you have accessible facilities?

Yes, all our facilities are wheelchair accessible.

### How many hours are included in our booking?

Your reservation includes five hours from start to finish for your event. That does not include setting up inside the day before or outside the day of your event.

Yes, we require a 1 day 1 million dollar liability policy. Details in the contract due 30 days prior to your event.
