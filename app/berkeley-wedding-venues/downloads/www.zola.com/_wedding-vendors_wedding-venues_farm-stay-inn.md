## About this venue

Blackbird Farm is a rare jewel in rural Anderson Valley, sitting atop a mountain ridge, surrounded by redwoods and pine trees, containing open meadows, scenic lakes, and established fruit tree groves. Blackbird Farm operates as a Farm Stay Inn that hosts weddings, corporate retreats, vacations, and other events throughout the spring, summer and fall months. There are multiple indoor and outdoor ceremony and reception spaces available. A unique feature of Blackbird Farm Stay Inn is our guest cabins that can be rented for up to 20 overnight guests.
