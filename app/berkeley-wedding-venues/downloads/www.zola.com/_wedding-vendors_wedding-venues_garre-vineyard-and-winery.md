## About this venue

Bella Rosa is a gorgeous wedding venue located at Garré Vineyard & Winery in the heart of Livermore Valley Wine Country. Both the venue and winery are the owner's grandmother, "Nonna" Rosa Garré's, name's sake. The English Garden style ceremony space is set on a lawn with a stage for the bridal party. The reception space includes outdoor patios for cocktail hour, a large granite and wood embellished bar, and a large dining room with wood flooring and 12 chandeliers. Breathtaking location for your upcoming nuptials.

April, May, June, July, August, September, October, November, and December

January, February, and March

No.

Yes, we have a large parking area for self-parking.

Yes, all our facilities are wheelchair accessible. We also have ramps at the main and side entrances, hearing-impaired fire alarm lights, and trained staff.

3 hour time-frame prior to ceremony start time for set-up, 5 1/2 hour time-frame for ceremony and reception, your choice of start time, Plates, Glassware, Silverware, Tables, Cocktail Tables, Mahogany Chiavari Chairs, White Folding Ceremony Chairs, Floor-length Ivory Linen and Napkins, Private Bridal Suite, Private Groom's Room, Professional Bartenders and Waitstaff, On-Site Venue Director
