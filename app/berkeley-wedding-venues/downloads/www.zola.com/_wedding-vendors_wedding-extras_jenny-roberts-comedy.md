Jenny Roberts is a comedian based in Oakland, CA. She features, headlines, and hosts comedy shows all over the Bay Area, and also works as an event MC. Her style is edgy, playful, and opinionated, blending highbrow quantum physics and lowbrow female anatomy with no shame. She's unabashedly feminist and ready to delight your wedding guests.

As a performer, I'll get your guests amped up and ready to party, and as an event MC, I'll work with you and your wedding team to make sure everyone is announced the way they want and the wedding day runs smoothly. You can't go wrong with me!
