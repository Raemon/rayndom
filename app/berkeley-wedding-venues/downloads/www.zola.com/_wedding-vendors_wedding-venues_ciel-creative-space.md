## Frequently asked questions

Ask them a question

### Do you have accessible facilities?

Yes, all our facilities are wheelchair accessible. We also have ramps at the main and side entrances, hearing-impaired fire alarm lights, and trained staff.

### What is your peak season?

March, April and May

### What is your off-peak season?

January, February and December

### Can we bring our own alcohol?

Yes, we charge a flat corkage fee of $750, the fee is waived if you select one of our preferred vendors.

### Do you have on-site parking?

Yes, we have two available parking lots. A front and back lot, spots in the front lot are $18 for the day. Spots in the back lot are $25. Unmetered street parking is also available.

### Do we need to get insurance?

A single day COI is required for every event booking.

### What’s your cancellation policy?

A confirmed booking that is cancelled more than 91 days prior to the event date and time will be refunded in full, minus a 5% processing fee. Cancellations made from 61-90 days prior to the event will be charged 20% of the basic cost of the total space rental. The remaining 80% of the basic fee plus deposits will be refunded. Cancellations made from 31-60 days prior to the event will be charged 50% of the basic cost of the total space rental. The remaining 50% of the basic fee plus deposits will be refunded. Cancellations made less than 30 days prior to the event date will be charged 100% of the basic cost of the total space rental
