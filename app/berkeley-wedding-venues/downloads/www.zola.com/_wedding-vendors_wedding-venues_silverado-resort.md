## About this venue

Nestled within Napa Valley’s rolling vineyards and ancient oaks, Silverado Resort offers an effortless blend of relaxed luxury and timeless setting. With a collection of indoor and outdoor venues—from garden ceremonies to open-air decks to elegant ballrooms—you can move seamlessly from one moment of the day to the next without ever leaving the property. Guests can settle in and enjoy the full resort experience, with beautifully appointed rooms, a world-class spa, pools, golf, and endless ways to unwind. It’s a place where the entire wedding weekend feels connected, elevated, and grounded in the natural beauty of the Napa Valley.
