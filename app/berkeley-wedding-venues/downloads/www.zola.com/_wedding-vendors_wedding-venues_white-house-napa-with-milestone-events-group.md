## About this venue

White House Napa is a historic, yet modern private estate in the heart of Wine Country that’s perfect for smaller weddings. First constructed in 1886, this Victorian mansion offers time-honored design with contemporary updates and modern amenities in downtown Napa. The newly renovated property features 17 luxury guestrooms, including 2 Master Bridal Suites with a conjoined balcony, an outdoor pool and hot tub, and three beautiful outdoor event spaces to host your private ceremony, cocktail hour, and reception.
