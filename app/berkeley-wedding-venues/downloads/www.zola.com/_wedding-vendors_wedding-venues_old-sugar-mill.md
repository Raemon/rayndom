## About this venue

Originally built in 1934 as an operating beet sugar refinery, the Old Sugar Mill now hosts fifteen unique wineries offering varietals from all over Northern California. Located in the heart of Clarksburg, CA, just fifteen minutes south of downtown Sacramento, the Old Sugar Mill is the perfect place for your wedding, social or corporate event. Our Industrial Wine Country setting is where memories will be treasured for a lifetime. At Old Sugar Mill, we are passionate about creating unforgettable experiences. We believe your special event deserves to be orchestrated by a caring and accomplished team set in a historic and iconic winery venue. From our old-world brick Boiler Room to our charming and quaint French Quarter, the unique character of our property will provide a rich and diverse backdrop for your celebration. OSM can scale events from intimate to grand and can accommodate any size occasion.
