### What are event extras?

Wedding event extras are the additional elements and services that go beyond the standard offerings of a wedding venue or wedding package. They are designed to enhance the guest experience, personalize the celebration, and create memorable moments for you and your partner! you can explore our supply of vendors ranging from [firework displays](/wedding-vendors/search/wedding-extras--fireworks), [photo booths](/wedding-vendors/search/wedding-extras--photo-booths), [cigar rollers](/wedding-vendors/search/wedding-extras--cigar-station), [childcare buses](/wedding-vendors/search/wedding-extras--child-care) and even [animal visits](/wedding-vendors/search/wedding-extras--performers) for photo opportunities! Based on your overall budget and vision, there is something for everyone.
