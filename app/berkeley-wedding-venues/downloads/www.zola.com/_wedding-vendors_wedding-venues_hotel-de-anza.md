## Our event spaces

### Palm Court Terrace

Tiled fountains and warm terra-cotta walls lined with ferns and flowers give this exquisite courtyard and garden a Mediterranean flair. With a 30-foot-high canopy, the Palm Court Terrace is a lovely setting for weddings and events of up to 200 people, all year round. Located next to the Hedley Club, the Palm Court Terrace is just the place for your next celebration.
