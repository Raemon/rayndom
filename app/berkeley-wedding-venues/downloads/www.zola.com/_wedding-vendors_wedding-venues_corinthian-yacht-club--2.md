## About this venue

Located at the heart of the waterfront edge of Main Street in the little quaint picturesque town of Tiburon, the newly renovated and 100+ year old Corinthian Yacht Club is known for its stunning majestic panoramic views of the San Francisco cityscape, its classic romantic colonial structure, its unparalleled exquisite nautical atmosphere, and above all its extraordinary award winning menu and delightful service. These unwavering characteristics, which the Corinthian Yacht Club prides itself on, makes for a timeless, memorable, picture-perfect and spectacular event of any form. With years of experience, the Corinthian Yacht Club’s talented team of professionals is at its best when delivering an exquisite event.

## Our event spaces

### THE GRAND BALLROOM

So all aboard Mateys! The Corinthian Yacht Club is setting sail on an expedition sure to fill your event with wonderful memories to last a lifetime!

The Grand Ballroom is a spacious, wood-paneled luxurious room, encompassing stunning high ceilings, illuminating alabaster chandeliers, radiant draped tools, an impressive wood-burning stone fireplace, a generous stage equipped to hold a large live band, Disc Jockey, or any other form of entertainment of your choosing, and a gorgeous hardwood floor perfect for a spectacular seated dinner and endless dancing. The Grand Ballroom is directly connected to the Sun Porch, Outside Deck and Cocktail Bar and can accommodate 250 guests. The Grand Ballroom is just as it is described, Grand, making it perfect for celebrating all special occasions and milestones in one’s life.

### THE SUN PORCH

Sky’s the limit.

The Sun Porch is a glass-enclosed solarium with classic white wood paneled walls and wainscoting, pristine white Corinthian columns, and expansive floor-to-ceiling high windows overlooking the stunning backdrop of the San Francisco Bay. The Sun Porch is conveniently connected to the Grand Ballroom and Outside Deck. The beautiful panoramic views of the harbors natural beauty, the San Francisco Skyline, and the historical landmarks such as the Bay Bridge, Alcatraz and Angels Island, accompanied by the heavenly ambiance provided by this incredible solarium makes for a perfect wedding ceremony, cocktail reception, and so much more.
