## About this venue

El Macero Country Club, nestled in the heart of a breathtaking 18-hole championship golf course, just 15 minutes from Downtown Sacramento. Our friendly and professional staff will work with you to bring your dream wedding to life and ensure your vision in order to deliver the ultimate wedding experience for you and your guests.
