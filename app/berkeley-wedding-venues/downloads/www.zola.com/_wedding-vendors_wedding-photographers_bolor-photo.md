At Bolor Photo, we specialize in preserving the beauty and essence of a wide array of wedding traditions and ceremonies. With years of experience and a deep appreciation for cultural diversity, our team is adept at documenting American traditional weddings, Hindu weddings, Sikh weddings, Eastern Orthodox weddings, Catholic weddings, Jewish weddings, Chinese tea ceremonies, City Hall weddings, and elopements.

Whether you're a couple seeking timeless memories of your special day, a ceremony venue or reception venue wanting to showcase your space through our lens, or a florist, coordinator, or other wedding vendor looking to collaborate on creating stunning visual narratives, Bolor Photo is here to serve you.
