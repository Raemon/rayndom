## About this venue

Set against the rolling foothills of Bennett Peak and surrounded by towering oaks and redwoods, Iron & Vine at Bennett Valley Golf Course offers a breathtaking backdrop for your wedding celebration. Nestled within this scenic landscape, Iron & Vine Restaurant, Bar & Event Center is the heart of the venue — a beautifully appointed space designed with weddings in mind. With its romantic ambiance, modern-rustic interiors, and sweeping views of the course and surrounding woodlands, Iron & Vine is the perfect setting for ceremonies, receptions, and everything in between. Whether you're planning an elegant soirée or a relaxed, nature-inspired event, Iron & Vine combines natural charm with sophisticated service. Create timeless memories at Bennett Valley, and let Iron & Vine bring your wedding vision to life.
