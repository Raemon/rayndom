## Frequently asked questions

### What kind of weddings can officiants help with?

We’re proud to offer officiants that offer [secular weddings](/wedding-vendors/search/wedding-officiants--non-religious-ceremony) that focus on the couple’s love, commitment, and personal values; as well as officiants aligned with [specific faiths](/wedding-vendors/search/wedding-officiants--religious). These officiants can perform ceremonies according to the traditions, rituals, and doctrines of that particular faith.

### Can officiants help me with my vows?

We know that couples like private and personalized vows, and there are definitely some officiants who can help with this. We offer officiants on Zola that support non-secular ceremonies that allow you to tailor your vows and commitment in a memorable and meaningful way.
