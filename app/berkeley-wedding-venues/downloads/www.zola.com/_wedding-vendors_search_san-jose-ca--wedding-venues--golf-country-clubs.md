### 

San Jose offers you the perfect blend of Silicon Valley innovation and California charm for your big day. You'll discover venue diversity spanning from the Winchester Mystery House with its Victorian intrigue to the ultra-modern Tech Interactive featuring a three-level atrium and interactive exhibits. The region's Mediterranean climate gives you reliable weather for outdoor ceremonies from April through October.
