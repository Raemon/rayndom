## About this venue

Once a part of a larger working ranch, the Twisting T-Bar remains a working ranch that encompasses roughly 160+ acres of Nevada range land that borders the East Fork of the Carson River. The 1800's barn easily accommodates 80 people for a sit-down event with an adjacent field for tenting and a total capacity of 250 guests. A custom bridal suite, space for a bar and dancing allow for an open and free flowing event. Vintage farming and ranch equipment add to the ambiance and history.
