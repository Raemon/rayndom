## About this venue

Set in the heart of Sonoma Valley Wine Country, surrounded by endless vineyards and rolling green hills, St. Francis Winery & Vineyards offers a breathtaking setting for your special day. With nearly 50 years of winemaking heritage, this iconic estate provides an unforgettable backdrop for both intimate gatherings and grand celebrations of up to 200 guests. Guests are welcomed by the winery’s dramatic bell tower — a stunning first impression that captures the timeless beauty and charm of the property. From the sunlit Grand Lawn framed by the majestic Mayacamas Mountains to the elegant indoor spaces, every corner of St. Francis Winery & Vineyards is designed to inspire romance. Photos taken here become treasured memories, beautifully reflecting the essence of Sonoma Valley.
