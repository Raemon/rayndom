## Frequently asked questions

Ask them a question

### What is your peak season?

May-October

### What is your off-peak season?

November- April

### Can we bring our own alcohol?

You can bring in everything for your own bar! We provide the glasses, rolling bar and ice. You hire a bartender (we provide you with a list of companies to hire from).

### Do you have accessible facilities?

Yes

### Do you have on-site parking?

We do not have parking on site, we do have a large paid lot owned by the state parks directly next to the event center.

### What's included in your starting rental fee?

We provide the following items with all rentals: - Cross back wooden chairs - Full length linens - Napkins - Wedding Suite 2 Hours Before Ceremony - Rectangle Wood Arch - Reception White Folding Chairs (on pavers only) - 6 Cocktail Tables - Water Station - 5 ft Round Guest Tables - fits 8-10 guests - As many gift/ guest book/ dessert/buffet tables you need - Indoor Dance Floor - Bars and Ice - Glassware, Flatware, China and Bar Glassware

### Can we add on time to our rental?

You can add additional hours for a fee of $500 per hour.

### Is the beach area included?

The beach between our venue and Lake Tahoe is part of CA State Parks and is open to the public, and you have the option for a portion of your ceremony to take place on the sand. Most beach goers are respectful of weddings and we set up fencing and signage to let the public know there will be an event.

### Is there a back up plan for bad weather?

We always have an option to move your entire ceremony and reception inside.

### What time does our event need to end?

County ordinances is that outside music must be off at 10 pm (last song at 9:45pm). All speakers & lights on terrace are turned off, with doors closed at 10 pm. Inside music is a hard stop at 11 pm (last song at 10:45pm).

### Can we bring our dog?

We welcome your pets for your ceremony. Please have a plan for an offsite location after the ceremony. Only service animals can stay at the venue after the ceremony.
