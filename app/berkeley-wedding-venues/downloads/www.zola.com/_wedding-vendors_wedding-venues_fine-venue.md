## About this venue

Nestled in the rolling countryside of Oakdale, California, our charming 3 acre venue offers a one-of-a-kind setting for weddings, receptions, and special celebrations. The centerpiece of our property is a beautifully restored steam train, creating a timeless and romantic backdrop that captures the spirit of vintage California charm. Our spacious shop building features a fully equipped kitchen and can comfortably host up to 50 guests, ideal for intimate gatherings, rehearsal dinners, or private receptions. Surrounded by wide-open fields, rustic scenery, and the soothing sounds of the countryside, Oakdale Fine Venue blends rustic elegance with nostalgic wonder—perfect for couples looking for something truly unique. Whether you’re planning a country-chic ceremony, a cozy indoor celebration, or photo sessions with the historic train, our venue offers endless possibilities and unforgettable memories.
