## Frequently asked questions

7 Answers

Ask them a question

### What is your peak season?

May through October is the main season, but Barndiva can host events year-round.

### What is your off-peak season?

November though April are considered non-peak months, but indoor event space is available all year.

### Can we bring our own alcohol?

All alcohol is provided and served by Barndiva. We offer tiered bar packages and options to customize garden-to-glass cocktails.

### Do you have on-site parking?

Adjacent to Barndiva is a large parking lot along with ample safe street parking after dark. Should you wish to host valet parking for your guests, Barndiva can arrange for valet services at an extra charge. At certain times of the day during high season, Barndiva may request valet parking for your event should your numbers exceed 200.

### Do you have accessible facilities?

Yes, all our facilities are wheelchair accessible.

### What’s included in your starting rental fee?

Catering staff that will manage setup & breakdown of in-house rentals- 8' banquet tables, iron chairs for ceremony & dining, white china, silverware, glassware for tabletop and bars, and white linens

### How many hours are included in our booking?

The facility rental is for 6 hours of event time, not including setup and breakdown. With dancing being indoors, hosts have the option of continuing hour-by-hour for an additional fee, until 2am.
