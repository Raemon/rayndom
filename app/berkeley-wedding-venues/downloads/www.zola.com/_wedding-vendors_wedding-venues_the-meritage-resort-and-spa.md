## Frequently asked questions

Ask them a question

### What are your current parking options?

Both self-parking and valet parking options are available at The Meritage Resort and Spa and Grand Reserve at The Meritage. At The Meritage Resort and Spa, self-parking is $10 per night and valet parking is $20 per day. At Grand Reserve at The Meritage, self-parking and valet parking are included.

### Is the resort pet-friendly?

Yes. We do allow dogs. Pets are not to be left in rooms unattended.

### Do you have accessible facilities?

Yes we do have accessible facilities.

### What is the food and beverage minimum?

This is the minimum amount you are required to spend at The Meritage Resort and Spa on food and beverage, excluding service fee and taxes. Food & beverage minimums are customized to your guest count, event space, and day of the week.

### What is the per person price?

$250 per person, excluding the 25% taxable service fee and 7.75% applicable taxes.

### Do you hold dates?

No, we do not hold dates without an executed contract and deposit amount received.

### Do you require a wedding planner?

Yes, we require a professional wedding planner, preferably from our preferred vendor list, to ensure that everything runs efficiently.

### Are we required to use your preferred vendors?

No, however, we highly recommend that you do, as these vendors are familiar with our property and policies.

### Do you have on-site parking?

The Meritage Resort and Spa has onsite parking available for your guests. Fees apply for overnight parking: $10 per car per night for self-parking and $20 per car per night for valet parking.

### Do you include a menu tasting?

Yes, we include a complimentary menu tasting for the couple and their wedding planner. You may bring additional guests for a fee. Your event manager will be present.

### Do you have a getting ready room?

Yes, a complimentary one bedroom/studio suite is included in your package for any night during your schedule of events at The Meritage Resort and Spa.

### What time does the event need to end?

Any outdoor event must end by 10:00 pm. However, you can utilize indoor spaces until 1:00 am. Any additional hours past the six hours included in your package requires an hourly fee.

### What is the payment plan? What forms of payment do you accept? Is it refundable?

A 50% payment is due at signing and two payments of 25% are due leading up to the month of your event. We gladly accept debit/credit card, ACH (direct bank transfer) or check. All deposits are non-refundable.

### What is the cancellation policy?

If a cancellation occurs 0 to 30 days prior to the event date, 100% of the banquet food and beverage, guest room and event space rental costs will be due. If a cancellation occurs 31 to 90 days prior to the event date, 90% will be due. If a cancellation occurs 91 to 180 days prior to the event date, 80% will be due. If a cancellation occurs 181 to 340 days prior to the event date, 70% will be due. If a cancellation occurs between the time of the contract signing and 340 days prior to the event date, 50% will be due.

### Can I bring in a cake from an outside baker?

Yes, you can bring a cake from an outside vendor. There is a $5 per person cake cutting fee.

### Is there a corkage fee?

There is a $35 per bottle corkage fee, we allow 2 cases of 750ml bottles.

### After my wedding is booked, what comes next?

You will be introduced to your personal event manager who will guide you and your wedding planner through the process.
