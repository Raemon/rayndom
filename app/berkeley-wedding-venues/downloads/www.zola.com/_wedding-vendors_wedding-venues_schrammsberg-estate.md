## Frequently asked questions

9 Answers

Ask them a question

### Do you host events year round?

As an all-outdoor venue we only accommodate events from mid-May through mid-October.

### Do we need an appointment to tour the property?

Yes. We are happy to schedule you a tour, just give us a call!

### What is the maximum number of guests you can accommodate?

Our venue can comfortably accommodate up to 300 guests.

### Where does the wedding party get ready?

We offer the option to rent the house for $300 on the day of your wedding, from 8am-11pm. The house rental includes two guest rooms and a master suite with ensuite bathroom on the second floor, a full bathroom downstairs, a guest room, and use of the common areas on the first floor.

### Do you have an in-house DJ and catering?

No, we do not have an in-house DJ or catering. We can provide you with a list of our recommended DJs and caterers in the area.

### Can we bring our own alcohol?

Yes, however, hard-liquor in the form of shots and neat drinks is only permitted for one hour directly after your ceremony. After the "cocktail hour", the hard liquor must be served as mixed drinks.

### Do we need to get insurance?

Yes, we do ask that you provide your own event liability insurance. This can easily be done online with an events insurance agency, or as an event-rider on your current policy.

### Do you have on-site parking?

Yes, we have staffed on-site parking where guests will be directed on where they can park their car.

### What is your peak season?

We do not have a peak season, and prices are the same throughout our season, which spans mid-May through mid-October.
