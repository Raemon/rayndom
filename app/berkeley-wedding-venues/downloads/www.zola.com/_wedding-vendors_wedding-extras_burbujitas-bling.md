Burbujitas & Bling is a Latina-owned jewelry brand offering dainty, meaningful permanent jewelry designed to celebrate love, self-expression, and connection. Founded by Jocelyn, each piece is custom-fitted and seamlessly welded for a clasp-free finish that’s effortless and elegant. Perfect for weddings and special events, our permanent jewelry creates lasting memories—whether shared between partners, friends, or loved ones—marking meaningful moments with intention and style.

As a Latina-owned brand, my work is inspired by culture, confidence, and community. I bring warmth, intention, and personal connection to every permanent jewelry experience, creating meaningful, custom-fitted pieces that celebrate love, shared moments, and lasting connections beyond the day itself.

**In three words:** Confident, Fun, Honest
