## About this venue

Our timeless venue is a perfect fit for your event. The San Jose Woman's Club Landmark historic venue, number 32 on the Historic Register, is the ideal place for your special event. The 1929 California Mission Revival style structure was built for the SJWC and designed by the notable architectural firm of Wolfe & Higgins. It includes many tiled features created by famed ceramicist Albert Solon. The Historic Venue provides in-kind donation of lowered cost rental of the SJWC to local community and arts groups for fund-raising events, performances and meetings. Rent the entire Historic Landmark Venue for your event, you and up to 350 guests have the use of the entire facility including the Landmark ballroom, tea room, kitchen and fireside room.

### Can we bring our own alcohol?

You can bring your own alcohol - hidden charges won't increase your bill. Bartending station: bring in a licensed and trained bartender to serve your guests in style.

### Do you have on-site parking?

A small parking lot suitable for vendors, key participants and handicap parking is available onsite. 6-story parking structure located a block away on the corner of S. 10th and E. San Fernando Streets. Entrance is on E. San Fernando Street. The garage is open from 6:00 a.m. to 01:00 a.m. everyday. Permit Pay-Stations are available on floors 3 and above.

### Do you have accessible facilities?

Yes, and handicap parking is available onsite.

### Dressing Area:

Get ready away from your guests in the bride’s room with a private bath and appear on the stage at the magic moment or walk down the aisle.

### Do you allow DJs?

Want a DJ, band or musicians? Bring in your favorite. Until 10pm any night!

### Do you allow outside caterers?

We have a list of preferred caterers that know the venue well but you are welcome to bring your own licensed provider.

### How many hours are included in our booking?

Ballroom and full facility booking is for 8 consecutive hours, including: four (3) hours decorating time, five (4) hours of event time, one (1) hour of clean up time as well as tables and chairs. Any additional time will be billed at $300 per hour.

### Do we need to get insurance?

A certificate of insurance naming the San Jose Woman’s Club as an additional insured with a $1 million per occurrence limit is required of the Client and all vendors prior to date of use. Must include Host Liquor coverage when serving alcohol. All vendors must also show evidence of Worker’s Compensation. Caterers who will serve liquor must show host liquor liability coverage.
