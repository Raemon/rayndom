## About this venue

The exquisite Casa Bella Event Center is located near Niles Canyon between Fremont and Pleasanton. Nestled in the heart of quaint downtown Sunol, Casa Bella offers the rare experience of hosting events in a setting that encompasses old world glamour - inclusive of a vintage steam train station - amidst the gorgeous views of Pleasanton Ridge and Sunol’s rolling hills.
