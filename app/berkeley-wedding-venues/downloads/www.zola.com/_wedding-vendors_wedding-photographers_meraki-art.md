### How far in advance should we book you for our wedding?

I encourage you to reach out as soon as possible about my availability, even if you‚Äôre planning on a shorter time frame. Especially those that require travel. ### Do you work with assistants or other photographers?

Currently just me. But I can bring another photographer or assistant if you require. But the price will be different accordingly.

### Do you also offer video services?

No, since I'm on my own now. I have to focus on the photography first. I may expand the service later when I have a team.

### How long will it take to see our pictures?

The turnaround time is around 3-4 weeks.

### How many pictures will we get?

You can expect at least 80 to 100 images per hour of photographing. But keep in mind this estimate varies depending on your wedding package, number of guests, venue type, and other factors. And for each shooting hour, you can get 10 retouched photos and the rest with a filter on as results.

### What‚Äôs your back-up plan if you can‚Äôt photograph our wedding as planned?

I do whatever it takes to make sure I show up on time. Not only I know that this is your important moment, but also I value my business. I typically leave a day in advance and stay nearby to ensure that, no matter what happens, I'm close by.

### What's your style in directing poses?

I would ask what's your preference, if directing more would make you feel more reliable I can do that. Some people prefer to be themselves, I don't mind either. This is a major topic that I will discuss thoroughly before the wedding, to make sure I can serve the clients the way they prefer the best.

### What are raw files? Can I have them?

Raw files are the original files that contain more data to process in software. You can definitely have them, it's just the raw file can't be opened by regular media check apps. It can only be viewed inside a professional photo process app, such as Lightroom, Photoshop, etc.
