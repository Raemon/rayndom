## About this venue

Mandala Springs is a stunning hotel wedding venue based in Cobb, California. This luxurious hotel is nestled in Northern California’s Mayacamas Mountains, offering a stunning natural setting for your special celebration - only two hours from the bustling California cities of Sacramento and San Francisco. You and your loved ones will be situated on a 112-acre picturesque mountain-retreat sanctuary with a number of breathtaking indoor and outdoor ceremony locations to choose from. Couples and their loved ones are welcome to enjoy a unique wedding experience at Mandala Springs, with a number of special activities offered, including miles of walking trails with gorgeous Northern California mountain vistas, and beautiful bonfire locations.
