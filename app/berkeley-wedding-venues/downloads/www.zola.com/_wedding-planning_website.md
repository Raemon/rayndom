### FAQ

  * #### Do we need to create a wedding website?

It’ll save tons of time! Trust us: Guests will have a million questions about the schedule, where to stay, dress code… the list goes on. Instead of juggling 100 extra texts and emails, share all those logistics and let guests shop your gift registry and RSVP on one beautiful, free website. Guests can refer to it anytime leading up to (and even on) your wedding day. Plus, make updates to your custom wedding website anytime there’s a change in plans. Delivering a change the date announcement? Add it to your website. Need to share some time-sensitive destination wedding details? Update everyone in a simple and accessible place. Zola’s free wedding websites also offer password protection, custom domain names, convenient registry integration, and wedding planning features that help you collect online RSVPS, ask for meal preferences, and more. A website is one amazing hub that simplifies things for you and your guests.

  * #### What should be on our wedding website?

Consider all the wedding details guests may text or call you to ask about, like directions, accommodations, dress code, and more. Chances are you won't be able to fit everything on your save the date or wedding invitation. A custom wedding website lets you include everything your guest list may need to prepare for your wedding day. With Zola's free wedding website builder, you can customize your website with all this info in minutes, plus add fun photos, share your registry, and let guests RSVP online. Ultimately, what you include on your wedding website is up to you and how personalized you want it to be. It's best practice, however, to include the weekend's schedule of events (with the location, date, and time of each one), the suggested attire, and a list of things to do and places to eat around town for visiting guests. You can also include personal “love stories” about you as a couple (like how you met or got engaged), RSVP information (whether you're looking for an online RSVP form or mailed card), and fun photos of you two. Don't forget travel and transportation info (like shuttle schedules, directions to the wedding venue, reserved hotel blocks, and parking), FAQs, a wedding hashtag, and anything else your guests need to do before or on the wedding date. [For more, check out our Expert Advice post](/expert-advice/how-to-write-your-wedding-website).

  * #### Should our website match our wedding stationery?

Guests love the seamless experience of having gorgeous save the dates and invitations that match to your wedding website. Take it a step further and match your wedding theme and paper decorations (like menus and programs!) to your website, too. At Zola, we make it so easy to create a beautiful wedding website and matching invitation suite. We have hundreds of options for every wedding style, from classic, to bohemian, and everything in between. You can easily customize any of our designs with your engagement photo, wedding details, and more. Enjoy great paper perks, too, like free shipping, free guest addressing, and free envelopes to help make the wedding planning process easier on you and your wallet.

  * #### What if we don’t know how to create a wedding website?

We got you! Zola has hundreds of free, beautiful website templates that you can customize in minutes. We'll help you set it up in a few clicks, then you can use our simple drag-and-drop website builder to add your wedding details, photos, fun stories, and more.
