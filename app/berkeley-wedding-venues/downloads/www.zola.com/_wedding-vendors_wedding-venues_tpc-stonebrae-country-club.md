## Frequently asked questions

13 Answers

Ask them a question

### Do you have a site fee for wedding receptions at your venue?

Yes the site fee and other package is available by emailing hbamont@stonebrae.com

### Do you have a site fee for wedding ceremonies at your venue?

Yes $1,000 flat rate.

### Which of the following are included in starting site fee?

Chairs, China, Clean Up, Event Planning, Flatware, Glassware, Linens, Parking, Set Up, Tables.

### Which of the following are included in the cost of wedding catering?

please email hbamont@stonebrae.com to receive a full wedding package via email!

### Do you allow outside catering?

Yes, as long as the caterer is fully licensed and provides liability insurance. The caterer must be onsite with their staff to serve the food (i.e., it cannot be picked up and brought in by the guests).

### Do you have on-site parking?

Yes we have close to 150 parking spaces and parking is free.

### Since you're a private club do you allow non-members to book at your club?

We sure do! You do not have to be a member to book event space at our club.

### Can we bring our own alcohol?

We allow guests to bring in outside wine at a $25 per bottle corkage fee. All other alcohol must be purchased through the club. We are very flexible as to how you'd like to set up the bar service. Please email hbamont@stonebrae.com to discuss options.

### Do you have accessible facilities?

Yes, all our facilities are wheelchair accessible. We also have ramps at the main and side entrances, hearing-impaired fire alarm lights, and trained staff.

### How many hours are included in our booking?

Your booking includes 5 full hours (ceremony is separate). So your 5 hours would start once cocktail hour begins. We are very flexible on set-up time. The room is usually set the morning of the wedding and we do not charge additional for vendors to come in early to set up. Vendors may arrive as early as 8am to set up.

### How do we get to your venue from the nearest airport?

The closest airport is Oakland International which is appx. 35 minutes from our venue. The next closests is San Francisco International Airport.

### Do we need to get insurance?

No, our venue is fully insured.

### Do you allow pets?

We realize your fur babies are an integral part of your family and we welcome them our venue.
