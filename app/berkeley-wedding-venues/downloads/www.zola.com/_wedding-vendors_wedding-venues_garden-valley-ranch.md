## About this venue

Thank you for your interest in visiting Garden Valley Ranch! Garden Valley Ranch is a historic landmark in Petaluma that traces its origins back to the late 1800's. This working rose farm cultivates 8,000 rosebushes over five acres of beautiful fields and gardens. Our world renowned garden roses bloom from May through October. Landscaped plantings featuring roses on pergolas, columns, arches and carefully selected perennials, annuals and bulbs combine to create a truly magical setting. Garden Valley Ranch also enjoys a large koi pond, water fountains and numerous special events and arts & entertainment throughout the year. Plan a visit today!

## Frequently asked questions

9 Answers

Ask them a question

### Can we also host our rehearsal dinner?

Yes, absolutely! Rehearsal dinners are considered separate events but we can put together a customized package proposal.

### What is your site fee for a wedding or event?

We have a flat fee of $10,500 for up to 150 guests. Weddings can be held on a Friday, Saturday or Sunday.

### What is your maximum capacity?

Our comfortable capacity is 150. We can accommodate more with supplemental bathrooms and shuttles.

### What is the timing for events?

All music must be off at 9pm and guests and vendors must be off property by 10:00pm. Access to the venue for set-up is 10:00am on event day.

### Do you require a wedding planner?

Yes, we require a day-of wedding coordinator. If you need assistance selecting one, please inquire for a list of suggested planners.

### Do you have a required vendor list?

We have a list of approved caterers and you must choose one of the caterers from this list or obtain written approval from us before signing with a caterer not on our list. Additionally we highly recommend using local preferred vendors from our list, though they are not required.

### What is your alcohol policy?

You may provide alcoholic beverage for your event (including liquor) without any corkage fees. Properly licensed service staff, through your caterer, is required.

### Where is your closest lodging?

There are numerous options within a short distance from GVR including Hotel Petaluma, Best Western, Quality Inn, Sheraton, Hampton Inn, Graton Hotel and Casino and many more

### Is GVR licensed and insured?

GVR's zoning allows for a limited number of events per year that promote and support our agricultural activities. We are a fully insured business.
