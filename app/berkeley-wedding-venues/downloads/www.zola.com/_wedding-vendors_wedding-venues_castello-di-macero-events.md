## About this venue

Castello di Macero is a breathtaking, castle-inspired estate in the heart of Davis, California, blending Old World charm with modern amenities. The venue features versatile outdoor spaces, including sunlit courtyards, romantic gardens, and open-air patios—perfect for ceremonies, cocktail hours, and dining under the stars. Ideal for both intimate gatherings such as elopements and proposals and larger celebrations, our flexible capacity ensures your guest list feels right at home. Up to 20 of your closest family and friends can stay on-site, turning your celebration into a full wedding weekend. The atmosphere is sophisticated yet inviting, with picturesque views and timeless architecture creating a storybook setting. With our all-inclusive packages, you’ll enjoy exceptional catering, personalized service, and seamless planning support, allowing you to simply relax and savor every moment from “Yes” to “I do.”
