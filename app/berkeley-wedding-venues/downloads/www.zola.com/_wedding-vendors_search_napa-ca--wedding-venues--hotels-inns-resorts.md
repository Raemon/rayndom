## About wedding venues in Napa, CA

### 

Napa Valley brings together more than 400 wineries, Mediterranean-inspired architecture and Michelin-starred dining to create one of America's most sought-after wedding destinations. When you celebrate here, your guests don't just attend a wedding; they experience acclaimed wines and stunning vistas with venues set among rolling vineyards, historic estates and manicured gardens that showcase the region's natural beauty in every photo you'll treasure.

### How Much Does a Napa Wedding Venue Cost?

Wedding venues in Napa typically cost between $11,400 and $13,900 for site fees alone, which runs higher than the national average of $6,500-$12,000. This investment makes up about 18% of your total wedding budget. For a complete Napa wedding experience, you can expect these average totals based on your guest count:

Guest Count| Average Total Cost  
---|---  
50 guests| $30,958  
100 guests| $51,628  
150 guests| $71,052  
200 guests| $88,397  
250 guests| $105,742  
300 guests| $123,086  
  
### What Types of Wedding Venues Can You Find in Napa Valley?

Napa offers distinctive venue categories that each provide their own unique experience for your special day. Charles Krug Winery, founded back in 1861, gives you upscale food and drink options with state-of-the-art production capabilities that'll impress your wine-loving guests. For luxury resort options, Auberge du Soleil now requires full resort buyouts for weddings but rewards you with their Michelin-starred culinary program. Meadowood Napa Valley features one of only five Michelin three-star restaurants in the western US; talk about a memorable wedding meal! If you're looking for an estate venue, The Estate Yountville offers luxurious event spaces with diverse options for both your ceremony and reception. For garden settings, Milliken Creek Inn provides beautiful ceremonies on three verdant acres overlooking the Napa River.

### Which Napa Venues Offer Accessible Wedding Experiences?

Silverado Resort makes accessibility a priority with golf carts to transport guests across multiple venue spaces and can comfortably accommodate up to 280 of your friends and family. Stanly Ranch offers accessible luxury accommodations with versatile spaces designed with floor-to-ceiling windows and open floor plans that create a stunning backdrop for your celebration. You'll have room for up to 300 guests and enjoy a convenient location near downtown Napa that makes it easy for everyone to attend.

### How Can Napa Venues Create Engaging Remote Wedding Experiences?

Carneros Resort creates immersive experiences for guests who can't attend in person through interactive live streaming with chat integration so everyone feels included. The Estate Yountville goes beyond basic video by organizing virtual experiences and coordinating remote guest participation that keeps everyone engaged. Four Seasons Napa Valley takes connection to another level by facilitating real-time Q&A sessions between in-person and virtual attendees so no one misses out on sharing their congratulations with you.

### Where Can You Find Outstanding Culinary Experiences in Napa?

The Culinary Institute of America at Copia offers world-class regional wines and cuisine with dedicated event spaces that'll impress even your foodie friends. Your guests will love the expertly prepared dishes created by talented chefs in training. Farmstead at Long Meadow Ranch features elevated farm-to-table cuisine and live fire cooking options in downtown St. Helena that showcase the freshest local ingredients in a truly memorable dining experience.

Booking early gives you the best chance of securing your dream Napa venue.
