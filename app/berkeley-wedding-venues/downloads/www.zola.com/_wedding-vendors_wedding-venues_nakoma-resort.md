## About this venue

From breathtaking mountain backdrops to an intimate garden venue, a wedding at Nakoma Resort offers an uncrowded, tranquil, unpretentious setting that features our Frank Lloyd Wright-designed Clubhouse. Our friendly and knowledgeable staff will take care of every detail so you can focus on your special day. The new mountain-modern Lodge will comfortably accommodate your friends, family, and even your dog. Let Nakoma help ensure your Tahoe wedding is all that you’ve dreamed. For those couples who are looking for an affordable Tahoe wedding venue in the Lost Sierra, Nakoma Resort offers privacy, peace, and quiet just 60 miles from Lake Tahoe and 54 miles from Reno, Nevada. We pride ourselves on our superior ability to create a reasonable wedding package unique to your wishes.
