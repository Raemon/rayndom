## Frequently asked questions

10 Answers

Ask them a question

### What’s included in your starting rental fee?

The venue, parking, hospitality room, an on-site facility coordinator, a 1-hour ceremony rehearsal, two 1-hour walkthrough vendor planning meetings, security, and the taxes. Complimentary telescope viewings on Friday and Saturday nights, weather permitting.

### How many hours are included in our booking?

Your reservation in most venues includes seven hours from start to finish from 5 PM to 12 AM, including the time it takes for vendors to set up and clean up. Additional hours after midnight are available for a fee. The Courtyard is additionally available for daytime events from 9 AM to 4 PM.

### Do we need to get insurance?

Yes, you will need $1,000,000 in coverage.

### What are the catering options?

It is highly recommended that clients coordinate with one of Chabot's 11 preferred caterers who are all well-versed with the museum's policies, although a buyout option of $1,000 is available for outside caterers. The caterer provides all service personnel, equipment, and they handle rental orders for tables, linens, dishware, etc. as well as set up and clean up.

### What is your peak season?

April-October

### What is your off-peak season?

January, February, March

### Can we bring our own alcohol?

All alcohol must be served by certified bartenders. Only companies with a liquor license may serve liquor on-premises. You may provide your bar company with alcohol to serve.

### Do you have on-site parking?

There are over 400 free parking spaces.

### Do you have accessible facilities?

Yes, the entire facility is wheelchair accessible.

### What’s your cancellation policy?

If a booking is canceled in writing 150+ days prior to the event, 100% of the deposit minus a $600 processing fee will be returned; 90–150 days prior to the event 50% of the deposit will be returned. Cancellations received less than 90 days in advance do not qualify for a refund.
