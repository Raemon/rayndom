## About this venue

Located in Danville, in the picturesque old downtown area, Aracely Lounge offers a relaxed, welcoming space which includes a large patio + garden complete with lounge seating, string lights, and heat lamps. As you enter the cozy interior, there are options for bar seating, table or lounge area dining. With a focus on local, seasonal, healthy, and delicious options, our menu offers a selection of California cuisine with options for cocktail hour, buffet, family style or plated. Our bar offers sightglass coffee + espresso options, and a full bar with a selection of seasonal cocktails, wine, + beer. We host a variety of all-inclusive private events including weddings + corporate events, and we offer a private room for smaller parties of up to 30 guests upstairs.
