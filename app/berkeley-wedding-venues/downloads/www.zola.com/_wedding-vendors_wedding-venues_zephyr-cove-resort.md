### M.S. Dixie II

Explore Lake Tahoe aboard our iconic fleet!

The M.S. Dixie II is built in the traditional Southern style of the steamboats that once cruised the Mississippi River. Built upriver on the Mississippi and transported in sections to Zephyr Cove, she made her first launch on Lake Tahoe in 1994. She is 151' long and 33' wide and has three decks. The M.S.
