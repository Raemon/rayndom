## About this venue

Turn your wedding celebration into a relaxing getaway at the Napa Valley Marriott Hotel & Spa. Experience the best of Northern California with a laid-back, resort-style atmosphere, nestled among beautiful garden spaces and lush courtyards with glowing fire pits. Our hotel offers over 22,000 sq ft of elegant indoor and outdoor venues, making us the perfect place for everything from grand receptions to intimate rehearsal dinners under majestic Redwood trees. With on-site dining, a spa, and convenient access to the best wineries and downtown Napa, we provide an easy-going, memorable, and luxurious resort experience for you and your guests.
