# Sold Out: Members Only Bird Walk

## May 21, 2025 @ 9:00 am \- 10:30 am

$20

This walk is full. Please email gardenmembership@berkeley.edu to be added to our notification list to be informed of openings. Thank you for your interest!

Join Chris Carmichael and Garden volunteer Susan Greef in search of both resident and migrant birds in the Garden’s many bird-friendly micro habitats. Both beginning and experienced bird watchers are welcome.

Refunds will be offered for up to two weeks ahead of the event but due to capacity limits (only 15 spaces will be available). No refunds after Wednesday, April 30th.

Heavy rain will cancel this event but you will be notified by 10:00 am the day of the event. In case of a rain cancellation a full refund will be applied.

Not a member, and want to attend? Join today!

Cost: $20
