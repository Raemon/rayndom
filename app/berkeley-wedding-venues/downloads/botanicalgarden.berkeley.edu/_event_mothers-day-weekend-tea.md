# Sold out: Mother’s Day Weekend Tea

## May 10, 2025 @ 10:00 am \- 12:00 pm

$55 – $75

Treat the mothers in your life to a lovely morning tea party, held in our historic Julia Morgan Hall. The botanical theme is edible flowers and will include interactive elements including a crafting activity and take home item. (Specific craft to be announced.)

All program fees include same-day admission to enjoy the Garden (an $18 value per adult).

**$75 Adults/$60 Youth (5–17)**  
**$70 Member Adult/$55 Youth**

Email us to be added to a waitlist, gardenprograms@berkeley.edu
