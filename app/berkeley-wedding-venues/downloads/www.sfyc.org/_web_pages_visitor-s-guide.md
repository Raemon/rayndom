# Things to See and Do Around the Club

### Welcome to Belvedere/Tiburon, California! Please discover some of our favorite local Marin County and Northern California locations to make your stay here more fun and memorable.

**  
[Download the SFYC Digital Visitor’s Brochure](https://www.dropbox.com/scl/fi/fjj426u39y2tzwjbx4wr5/SFYC-Visitors-Guide_V5_QR.pdf?rlkey=m9s1ej9suug435n0sqmx1fg30&st=ividdtep&dl=0)  
  
[Tiburon Chamber of Commerce Directory & Events](https://business.tiburonchamber.org/list/)**

### Necessities

  * **Banking/ATM:** Chase, Wells Fargo, Citibank [in CVS] 
  * CVS Pharmacy 
  * Emergency / Urgent Care: Dignity HealthGo 
  * **Shipping** : Tiburon Mail Service, US Post Office 
  * Tiburon Dental 
  * Vogue Dry Cleaners 



### Dining

  * **Bungalow Kitchen:** Elevated scenic dining and bar, with upstairs lounge, a pool table and “late nights” with DJ. 
  * **Caffe Acri:** A café and coffee shop on the ferry landing; also serves lunch and dinner. SFYC Member-owned! 
  * **Lola’s Taqueria:** Casual or carry-out Mexican. 
  * **Luna Blu:** Waterfront Italian food. 
  * **Malibu Farm:** Scenic indoor/outdoor dining overlooking the Bay with a storefront that serves coffee, baked goods and ice cream. 
  * **Ming’s:** Casual or carry-out Chinese food. 
  * **Petite Left Bank:** French and California cuisine with indoor/outdoor dining off Main. 
  * **Rustic Bakery:** Café with fresh baked goods. 
  * **Sam’s Anchor Café:** A Tiburon landmark with docking and dining on the pier. 
  * **Salt + Pepper:** Excellent breakfast, lunch and dinner spot on Main Street. 
  * **Servino Ristorante + Enoteca:** Traditional Italian food and wine bar on upper Ark Row. SFYC Member-owned! 
  * **The Caprice:** Fine dining; the only restaurant with a view of the Golden Gate Bridge. SFYC Member-owned! 
  * **Waypoint Pizza:** Casual pizza spot. SFYC Member-owned! 



### Wine Tasting

  * The Caviar Co - SFYC Member-owned! 
  * In Good Taste 
  * Servino Enoteca - SFYC Member-owned! 
  * Squalo Vino 
  * Tiburon Wine 



### Grocery Stores

  * Woodlands (a three-minute walk from SFYC) 
  * Nugget Market (a five-minute drive up Tiburon Boulevard) 
  * Safeway (a ten-minute drive to Strawberry Village) 
  * Whole Foods (two locations in Mill Valley) 
  * Trader Joe’s (a twelve-minute drive to Corte Madera) 



### Wellness

  * Yoga: The Studio (Mill Valley) or Metta (Corte Madera) 
  * Massage, Facial or Acupuncture: Resonance Spa or Evo Spa 
  * Manicure/Pedicure: Tiburon Nails or Camino Nail Spa 



### Lodging

  * The Lodge at Tiburon 
  * The Waters Edge Hotel 



### Things to See and Do Around the Club

  * Angel Island 
  * Golden Gate Bridge 
  * Golden Gate Ferry (Angel Island - Tiburon Terminal) 
  * Hiking on Mount Tamalpais 
  * Marin Headlands 
  * Mountain Biking in Marin 
  * Muir Beach 
  * Muir Woods 
  * Napa and Sonoma Wine Country 



Here are some of our dining suggestions: 

  * Ad Hoc – best dinner 
  * Boonfly Café – known for their mini donuts and breakfast 
  * Brasswood – pulled mozzarella is a favorite 
  * Charter Oak – famous for their burger 
  * Farm – located in Carneros Resort & Spa 
  * Goose & Gander – locals’ favorite 
  * Gott’s Roadside – excellent fast-casual lunch 
  * Solage – poolside/resort dining at Solbar and Picoba 
  * Tomales Bay
