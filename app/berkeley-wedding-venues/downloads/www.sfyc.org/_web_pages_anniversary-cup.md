# Anniversary Cup (Club Series)

Saturday, April 27, 2019  
  
![](/documents/10184/20619/Anniversary+Cup.jpg)  
  
The first race on Knox on Saturday was the Anniversary Cup, started for the 125th anniversary of the Club. Puffy, shifty and chilly breeze provided some difficult racing for the 23 boats taking part. The race committee was led by PRO Don Wieneke, with Lynnie Spiller, Jack Mclaughlin, Karen Golden, Stephen Golden, Dave Krone, Frank Defesche, Moe Roddy, Tom Roberts, John Stuart, and Bill Kircher.
