# Catch Me If You Can - Women Skippers Edition

Saturday, September 19, 2020   
  
SFYC's 2020 pursuit-style racing continued on Saturday with a women skippers' version. One man was allowed on each boat, and a total of four crew maximum. The big boats sailed a 10-mile course out to Blackaller while the smaller boats raced 7.5 miles around a mark at Yellowbluff. Fourteen boats participated in beautiful moderate fall breeze.   
  
**Congratulations to our winners:**

  * Fleet 1 First Place: **Jenni Daily** with**Cassidy Lynch** , **Liisa** **Doyle** , and **Matt Frymier** on Parking Lot
  * Fleet 2 First Place: **Andrea Finn** with **Jock MacLean** and**Jenny Krone** on Kookaburra
