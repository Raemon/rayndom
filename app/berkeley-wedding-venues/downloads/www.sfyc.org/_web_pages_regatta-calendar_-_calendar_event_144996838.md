##  _29er Nationals_

Regatta Information: 

  * There is a welcome BBQ on Thursday evening. Competitors and Coaches are pre-paid through entry fees. Other supporters may purchase dinners on-site with Visa or Mastercard for $38 (tax and service included).
  * Parents may set up guest cards at the SFYC Front Desk in order to use SFYC dining and bar services.
  * Boats and trailers must be parked in the sound end of the SFYC parking lot in the designated space.
