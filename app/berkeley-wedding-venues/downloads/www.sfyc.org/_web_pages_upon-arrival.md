# Upon Arrival

All visiting sailors must register at the Clubhouse, and their boats must be registered by completing the guest vessel registration form. Upon registration, visitors will receive a guest card. Please note that The Club accepts credit card payment from visiting guests at the Galley, Bar and Ship's Store. There will be a 10% guest fee added to these purchases. Cash is no longer accepted. A valid personal check will be accepted for guest dock fees. 

##  Parking 

Parking in the SFYC lot is reserved for Members, their guests and SFYC event attendees only. All vehicles parked in the SFYC lot must display a current SFYC parking sticker or guest parking pass. 

If the lot is full, here are some alternatives: 

  * Community Road (down San Rafael Avenue, past the Police Station) is free and not time-restricted. It's a three-minute walk to The Club. 
  * Peninsula Road, past the two-hour parking signs, is free and not time-restricted. It's a five-minute walk to The Club. 
  * Bank of America lot at 1601 Tiburon Boulevard charges a daily rate. It's an eight-minute walk to The Club.
