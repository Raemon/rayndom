# Staff Commodores' Cup

Saturday, April 27, 2019 12:00 AM  
  
![](/documents/10184/20763/Staff+Commodores.jpg)  
  
The second race of the day was the Staff Commodores' Cup, which requires a Staff Commodore to be on board. This year drew nine boats in two divisions.
