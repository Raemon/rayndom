# Optimist Pacific Coast Championship

8/24/2019, 8/25/2019   
  
![](/documents/10184/22140/Optimist+Pacific+Coast+Championship.jpg)

## Flawless Conditions at the 2019 Opti PCCs 

The Opti PCCs were at SFYC again this year, and the conditions were perfect. The Berkeley Circle provided 14-20 knots, with only a few sailors having a bit of a tough time in the 20-knot breeze. This regatta is a qualifier for next spring's National Team Trials, and SFYC sailors took home the top three spots! Congratulations Tor Svendsen, Talia Hamlin and Leo Robillard for a great regatta.
