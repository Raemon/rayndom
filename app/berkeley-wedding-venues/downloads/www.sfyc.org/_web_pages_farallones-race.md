# Farallones Race (Full-Crewed)

Saturday, June 08, 2019   
  
![](/documents/10184/21089/Farallones+Race.jpg)  
  
Sunday's weather was perfect for the Farallones Race. A light northerly at the start turned into a moderate northwesterly for the trip out and back, which brought everyone home well before dark. Congratulations to SFYC boats on the podium: Blue (1st in PHRO1), Peregrine (2nd in PHRO1), and Bloom County (2nd in PHRO2).   
  
All race information and registration at [OYRA website](https://yra.org/yra-racing/oyra-series/).
