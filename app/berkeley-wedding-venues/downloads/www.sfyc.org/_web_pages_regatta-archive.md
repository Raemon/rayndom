# Regatta Archive

Since its founding in 1869, SFYC has hosted thousands of days of racing on one of the country's most celebrated bodies of water. Stored in the archives of our clubhouse are sketches of the Club's first race courses, as well as thrilling reports of our earliest regattas which were covered in detail in _The San Francisco Chronicle_. We are proud to continue this tradition. 

Here, we archive SFYC regatta documents, results, reports and some photos.
