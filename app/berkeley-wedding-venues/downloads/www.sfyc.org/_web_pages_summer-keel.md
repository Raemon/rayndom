# Summer Keel

8/10/2019, 8/11/2019  
  
![](/documents/10184/22001/Summer+Keel.jpg)

SFYC's Summer Keelboat Regatta Four fleets enjoyed slow-building breeze on the Berkeley Circle each day, with the first race each day in a very moderate 10-12 knots. In the 22-boat strong J/105 fleet, SFYC's Tim Russell on Ne*Ne ran away with the win over SFYC's Ryan Simmons on Blackhawk in second.  
  
The race committee was led by PRO Vicki Sodaro with S/C Suzie Moore, Lynnie Spiller, Bill Kircher, Gabrille Lajoie, Roxanne Fairbairn, Larry Stoehr, Tony Scott, Alex Rothenberg, Bryan Moore, Mary Grant, Susan Sanders, Jane Elkins, and Forrest Gay. The protest committee had work both days, and was Tom Roberts, Vickie Gilmour, and John Super.
