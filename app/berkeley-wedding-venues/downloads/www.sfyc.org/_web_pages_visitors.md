The San Francisco Yacht Club offers a full range of reciprocal privileges to club members from around the world at our year-round facilities, which include our docks and harbor, our clubhouse, a full-service restaurant and bar. 

##  Hours Of Operation 

The clubhouse and offices open at 0800 hours, Wednesday through Sunday. The Club's grounds consist of three main buildings – the Clubhouse, the Cove House and the Sailing Center. The Front Desk is located in the Clubhouse. The Harbormaster is located in a small building to the south of the Sailing Center.

##  Directions 

###  By Land 

The San Francisco Yacht Club is located at 98 Beach Road in Belvedere, California. To arrive by land, take US 101 to the Tiburon Blvd. exit. Head East on Tiburon Blvd. for approximately 4 miles. At the light, go right on Beach Road. Beach Road bends to the right and the Club is .3 miles on the left side. The parking lot is just past the Clubhouse on Beach Road, past the large hedge. 

###  By Sea 

SFYC is located at 37°52.33' N and 122°27.64' W, on Raccoon Strait across from Angel Island. Members of reciprocal Clubs are welcome. Please contact our Port Captain via e-mail at [[email protected]](/cdn-cgi/l/email-protection) prior to your arrival.
