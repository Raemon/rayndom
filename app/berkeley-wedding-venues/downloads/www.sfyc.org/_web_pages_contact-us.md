# Directions

##  By Land 

Take US 101 to the Tiburon Blvd. exit. Head East on Tiburon Blvd. for approximately 4 miles. At the light, go right on Beach Road. Beach Road bends to the right and the Club is .3 miles on the left side. The parking lot is just past the clubhouse on Beach Road, past the large hedge. 

##  By Sea 

SFYC is located on Raccoon Strait across from Angel Island. Members of reciprocal Clubs are welcome. Please contact our Port Captain via e-mail at [[email protected]](/cdn-cgi/l/email-protection#11417e63655270616570787f51627768723f7e6376 "Click to send an Email") prior to your arrival.
