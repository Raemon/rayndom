# Summer BAYS #3 (Youth)

8/3/2019, 8/4/2019   
  
![](/documents/10184/21807/Summer+BAYS.jpg)

## Race Report: BAYS Youth #3 

SFYC hosted the third stop of the BAYS Summer Series for Optis, C420s, Lasers, CFJs and RS Teras. The Opti Championship fleet, C420s and Lasers raced at Southampton in 16-23 knots for six races. The Opti green fleet, CFJs and RS Teras had great racing in the Cove and got in nine races.
