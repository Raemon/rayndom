##  First Login Instructions 

To login to the member's only side of the website, please enter your member number as your username. Your initial password will be your date of birth in the following format "**MMDDYYYY** ". Spouse's initial usernames are the member's number followed by a "dash one". Spouse's passwords are the same - the member's number, followed by a dash one as in: "9999-1".__

##  Forgot Your Password? 

Have your member number ready. Click the **Forgot Password** link and enter your member number. You will receive an email with your password. If you would like additional assistance, please contact the Club.
