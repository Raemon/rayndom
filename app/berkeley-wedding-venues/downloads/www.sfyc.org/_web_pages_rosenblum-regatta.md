# Rosenblum Regatta (Club Series)

Saturday, March 23, 2019  
  
![](/documents/10184/20184/Rosenblum+Regatta.jpg)

The Club Championship Series kicked off on March 23 with the Rosenblum Regatta. Twenty-eight boats competed in two races on Knox with the first race in about 10 knots and the second building to 18-21 knots. The next Club Series race is the Anniversary Cup on April 27, which will be immediately followed by the Staff Commodores' Cup race.  
  
The Rosenblum race committee was led by PRO**Julie Wiard** , with **Shawn Davies,** **Christopher Devairakkam** , **Jack Mclaughlin** , **Jay** **Hooker** , **Bill Kircher** , **Stephen Golden** , **Jeff Zarwell** , **David Wiard** and **Roxanne Fairbairn**.  
  
Special thanks to Dry Creek Vineyards and Fort Point Brewery for the sponsored wine and beer after the race!
