# Hart Nunes (Mercuries)

Saturday, July 06, 2019   
  
The annual Hart Nunes Mercury Regatta took place on Saturday, July 6, with eight boats competing. Hal McCormack was the PRO with Richard vonEhrenkrook assisting.   
  
SFYC's Scott Easom won handily.
