###  Compliments & Comments 

The San Francisco Yacht Club is proud to introduce _**Compliments and Comments**_ — a simple, direct way for Members to share their thoughts and experiences. Whether it’s a memorable evening in the dining room, a suggestion for future programs, a shout-out to a staff member who went above and beyond or helpful feedback on how we can improve, we want to hear from you.  
  
Members are encouraged to email [[email protected]](/cdn-cgi/l/email-protection#83e0eceef3efeaeee6edf7f0c3f0e5fae0adecf1e4bcf0f6e1e9e6e0f7bec0eceef3efeaeee6edf7f0a6b1b3a6b1b5a6b1b3c0eceeeee6edf7f0) with any feedback about club dining, events, amenities or overall experiences. Your insights help us celebrate what’s working so we can make the most of your SFYC experience.  
  
This is your Club — thank you for shaping its future!
