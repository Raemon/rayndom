# Summer Sweetness

Kohl Mansion was a lovely setting for this wedding. With hints of dusty purples and creams, it was absolutely stunning. Enchanting floral arrangements served as the focal point for all of the table settings. Elegant pops of gold and gilded edge details made a romantic night and aesthetic. 

A BIG thank you:  
Food: Knights Catering  
Lighting & music: Verducci Productions  
Baker: Rachel’s Cakes  
Flowers: Asiel Designs
