# Elegant Opulence

Back in January, Kohl Mansion hosted a breathtaking wedding. This intimate affair was filled with delicate and magical touches throughout the evening. Everything was perfect. From the floral designs, tablescapes to the bride and groom. It was romantically inviting in every way. It felt like a daydream, a winter wedding where every ounce of charm radiated in glory. This wonderful team of vendors captured all of the details flawlessly.
