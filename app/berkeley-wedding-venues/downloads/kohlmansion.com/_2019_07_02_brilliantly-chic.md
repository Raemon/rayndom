# Brilliantly Chic

Elegant shades of cream and blue surrounded Kohl for a lovely summer wedding recently. Beautifully designed long tables were thoughtfully put together throughout the Great Hall. On the lawn outside, the ceremony was sophisticated and had an amazing backdrop! What a perfect day it was!

A BIG thank you to:  
Blooming Wed Event Coordination  
Amanda Vidmar Florals  
Weir Catering  
Big Fun DJs  
Kristia- Harpist
