# Fanfare Recap- Winter Show 2019

WOW! Fanfare was a success! With an amazing turn out, vendors were able to really showcase their best work and interact with excited brides and grooms from all over the San Francisco Bay Area. Gathering wedding dress trends, tablescape ideas, listening to music and enjoying delightful food and beverages, and even witnessing a mini dancing session (some guests maybe even had to participate!) made this event so wonderful. We look forward to hosting this event again next year!
