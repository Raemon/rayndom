# A Sweet Surprise

On Friday, Kohl Mansion served as the perfect backdrop to this stunning surprise birthday party! A husband and kids surprised their mom/wife with an incredible night to remember!! A fancy formula of whimsical met sophisticated and adorned Kohl in such a tasteful way. One hundred of the family’s closest family and friends enjoyed an intimate candlelit dinner followed by a night filled with dancing!

A BIG thank you to:  
-Coordinator: Claire Curran Events  
-Catering: Toast Catering  
-Lighting: Verducci Productions  
-Band: All Star Jukebox
