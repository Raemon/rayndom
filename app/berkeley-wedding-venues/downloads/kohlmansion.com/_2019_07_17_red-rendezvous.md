# Red Rendezvous

A red and black color theme created a bold yet beautiful ambiance for this summer wedding over the weekend. Thousands of red roses and acrylic details gave Kohl a modern twist. These features elevated Kohl Mansion in a sophisticated manner and romantically complimented the historic details of the property in a marvelous and exciting way.
