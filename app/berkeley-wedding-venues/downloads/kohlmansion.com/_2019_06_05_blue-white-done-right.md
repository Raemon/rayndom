# Blue & White Done Right

Saturday’s wedding at Kohl was the perfect way to start off the summer season. Timeless hues of blue and white united tastefully with the elegant features of the mansion. The patio was filled with beautiful tablescapes of blue, white and a touch of silver. The night was fabulous and all guests enjoyed celebrating this wonderful couple!

A BIG thank you to:  
Cooridnator: Kaella Lynn  
Lighting: Enchanced Lighting  
Caterer: Betty Zlatchin  
Florist: Amanda Vidmar  
Specialty China on tables: Frances Lane
