# Holiday Decor

Every year, Kohl Mansion’s preferred vendors volunteer to elaborately decorate the Mansion. Kohl Mansion showcased an incredible array of holiday decor throughout the space. Each room was filled with tasteful elements of design that elevated the Mansion’s details. The collaboration of vendors to make Kohl Mansion radiate for the holiday season is so special. We hope to see you next holiday season, cheers to 2018!!
