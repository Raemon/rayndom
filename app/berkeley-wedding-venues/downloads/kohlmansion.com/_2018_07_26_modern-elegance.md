# Modern Elegance

Kohl Mansion was layered with style and modern touches last Saturday for an incredible summer wedding. With alfresco dining on the patio and the ceremony on the lawn, this was an incredibly intimate and lovely evening. A mix of dreamy and organic tones made this a night to remember! 

**ALL PHOTOS SHOWN WERE TAKEN AND PROVIDED BY ADVTR COLLECTIVE 

A BIG thank you to:  
-Photography: ADVTR Collective  
-Coordinator: Christine Garrison  
-Lighting & Music: Denon & Doyle  
-Food: Toast Catering  
-Cakes: Rachel’s Cakes
