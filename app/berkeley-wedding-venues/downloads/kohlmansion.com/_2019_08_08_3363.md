# Exciting & Eventful

Recently, a wedding at Kohl took place between two UC Berkeley Cal graduates. The evening was lively and filled with so much excitement. Especially when members of the famous Cal band made a special appearance to kick off the dancing portion of the night! The bride, groom and guests enjoyed an incredibly memorable day that was fully enjoyed and so much fun!

A BIG thank you to:  
University of California Berkeley Band  
Food: Toast Catering  
Music: Denon & Doyle  
Coordinator & flowers: Details by Design
