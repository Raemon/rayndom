# Elegant and Chic

Tones of mauve, cream and soft touches of green, illuminated Kohl Mansion for this unbelievably chic wedding. An intimate winter night was filled with love, joy and absolute beauty. Sophisticated yet feminine floral designs and table settings elevated Kohl Mansion’s elegance. The delicate color palette complimented the bride’s vision so romantically. This was a spectacular night that was tastefully designed! Congratulations to the newlyweds!

A BIG thank you to:  
-Coordinator: Blooming Wed  
-Catering: Le Papillon  
-Baker: Studio Cake  
-Harpist: Krista Strader  
-Music: Big Fun DJ
