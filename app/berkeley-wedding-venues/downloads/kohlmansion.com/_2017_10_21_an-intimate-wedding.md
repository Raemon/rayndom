# An Intimate Wedding

On October 21st, the Kohl Mansion hosted an intimate wedding filled with shades of red and grey. The ceremony was a lovely setting outside on the Kohl Mansion lawn, and the reception was brought indoors for a romantic evening. Tasteful fall hues filled the Great Hall for dining and throughout the Mansion that night. A warm ambiance was created using various candles and beautiful floral arrangements.

A BIG thank you to:  
-Coordinator: Amaze Events  
-Catering: Le Papillion  
-Desserts: Rocko’s Ice Cream Tacos  
-Lighting: Enhanced Lighting  
-Band: The Lucky Devils
