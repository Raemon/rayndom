# Pretty in Pink

Shades of pink created an enchanting ambiance for this lovely couple last weekend. A romantic flair and feminine vibe made the Kohl sparkle and shine. Lovely table-scapes caused guests to swoon with amazement! Everyone enjoyed an incredible night celebrating with this couple. 

A BIG thank you to:  
Food: Le Papillon  
Flowers: Vo Floral Design  
Music: Big Fun DJs  
Dessert: Rocko’s Tacos
