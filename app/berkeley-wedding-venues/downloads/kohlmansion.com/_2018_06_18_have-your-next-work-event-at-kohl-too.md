# Have your next work event at Kohl too!

Kohl Mansion served as a perfect meeting spot on Tuesday, June 12th for a corporate event that took place all day. With multiple keynote speakers, flexibility to have different rooms for interaction, and other accommodations, Kohl was a great fit for this large work function!
