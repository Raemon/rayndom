# Marvelously Magical

Whimsical, magical, enchanting, these are only a few words to describe this jaw-dropping “Harry Potter” inspired wedding that took place over the weekend at Kohl Mansion. Incredible details of all things wizardly, tastefully complimented Kohl in such a spectacular way. The homemade floral Platform 9 3/4 sign was the backdrop against the patio’s bougainvillea and kicked the night off with excitement. 

The Great Hall was delicately designed with various inspiration from the literary series for a lovely dining experience, while the Morning Room housed a flawless oversized sketch of “Hogwarts” and the wand movements of different spells. There wasn’t a detail overlooked! The Bride & Groom were gleaming with happiness while guests enjoyed a night of celebrating and soaking in the elegant fantasy decor.
