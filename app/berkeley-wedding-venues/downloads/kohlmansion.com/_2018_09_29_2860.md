# 

Simple elegance swooned Kohl Mansion last Saturday with a lovely wedding! The Great Hall was the focal point of the night with many traditional details. A delicate and delightful ambiance made the night a very memorable one for the bride, groom and guests!

A BIG thank you to:  
-Catering: Continental Catering  
-Lighting & Music: Sound in Motion  
-Beverages: Smith Beverages  
-Coordinator: Camden Mobley
