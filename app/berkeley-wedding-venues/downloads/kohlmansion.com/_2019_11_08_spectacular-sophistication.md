# Spectacular Sophistication

Creamy whites and solid neutrals created an incredible setting against the mansion’s bougainvillea on the patio. The contrast of magenta tastefully complimented the color palette for this wedding. Sophisticated details and a loving friend and family made this a night to remember!!
