# Simply Beautiful

Kohl Mansion hosted a lovely wedding this past Sunday with an intimate group and simple elegance. Dining and dancing took place in the Great Hall and cocktail hour was celebrated on the patio. Guests enjoyed the night fully and loved sharing this special day with the bride and groom!

A BIG thank you to: 

Food & Drink: Continental Catering

Flowers: Karen Baba

Baker: Satura Cakes 

Music: Big Fun DJs
