# Escape to Tuscany- a tablescape collaboration

As you know, Kohl Mansion’s beauty is breathtaking, with multiple pockets of opportunity to create amazing settings and a spectacular ambiance. It truly makes it a destination on the San Francisco Peninsula. Take a peek at a styled photoshoot that took place recently! 

Chairs, table, linens, glassware, silverware and dishes provided by Hensley Event Resources  
Florals & Styling and other miscellaneous items designed by The Glam Design Studio and Baldocchi & Daughters
