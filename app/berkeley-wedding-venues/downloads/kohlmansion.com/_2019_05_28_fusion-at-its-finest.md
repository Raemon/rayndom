# Fusion at it’s finest

During Sunday’s wedding, guests enjoyed a few multicultural surprises throughout the night! At cocktail hour, lions emerged and danced, grabbing the attention of the guests. As the night continued, beautiful dancers in traditional Mexican clothing debuted on the dance floor. Both cultural elements were fused together to create a memorable night for the bride and groom! 

A BIG thank you to:  
Food & Drinks: Toast Catering  
Florals: Pico Soriano Florals  
Event Planner: Alex Barranco  
Music: Big Fun DJs  
Entertainment: Ballet Folklorica Mexicano & We Fly High- Lion Dance
