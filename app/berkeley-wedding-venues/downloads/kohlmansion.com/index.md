### **KOHL MANSION**  
2750 Adeline Drive • Burlingame, CA 94010

**From Highway 280 North:**  
Take Trousdale Avenue exit, immediate right onto skyline (south), left on Hillside Drive, left on Alvarado and through the gates of the Estate.

**From Highway 280 South:**  
Take Trousdale/Burlingame exit, at stop sign turn left and go under freeway overpass, immediate right onto skyline going south, left on Hillside Drive, left on Alvarado and through the gates of the Estate.

**From Highway 101:**  
Take the Broadway exit heading west, right on El Camino Real, left on Hillside Drive, right on Alvarado and through the gates of the Estate.
