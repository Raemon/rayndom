# Intimate and romantic

Yesterday’s wedding at Kohl Mansion was simply elegant. With the ceremony on the lawn, and the mansion as the backdrop, it was stunning! Inside, pink, lavender and cream romantically dressed the Great Hall for dining and dancing along with the Morning Room for desserts. The Green Room was the perfect spot to have your photos taken throughout the night!
