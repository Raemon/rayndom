# Morning Glory

Kohl Mansion is breathtaking no matter what time of the day! A few weeks ago we had an intimate morning ceremony on the patio. It was a beautiful day where the bride and groom said “I Do” underneath the incredible bougainvillea while the guests enjoyed the morning sun.

A BIG thank you to:  
-Coordinator: Miley Whitefield  
-Florist: Karen Baba
