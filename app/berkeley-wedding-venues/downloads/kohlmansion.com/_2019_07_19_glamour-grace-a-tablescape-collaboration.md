# Glamour & Grace- a tablescape collaboration

Feminine and floral details delicately dazzled Kohl Mansion recently for a collaboration with Hensley Event Resources. Soft shades of lilac, pink and cream tastefully compliments the incredibly full bloomed, bougainvillea. A mix of modern and chic elements created a delightful aesthetic!

Chairs, table, linens, glassware, silverware and dishes provided by Hensley Event Resources  
Florals & Styling and other miscellaneous items designed by The Glam Design Studio and Baldocchi & Daughters
