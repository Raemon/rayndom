# Around the World

Kohl Mansion was the host for a fun and lively event for those in the beverage industry. Beam Suntory, a beverage company, celebrated their annual event with incredible custom bars throughout the Great Hall, Morning room, Patio and Library with a global vibe. Each bar had a unique display and theme to reflect the type of drink. Catering did a great job incorporating the menu with the different tastes! It was a very interactive event that sparked vendors with ideas for future events to follow. 

A BIG thank you to:  
Coordination: Synergy Marketing Partners  
Food: Knights Catering  
Tent: Hensley
