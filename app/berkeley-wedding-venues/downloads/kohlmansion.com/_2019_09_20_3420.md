# 

Kohl Mansion hosted yet another beautiful wedding this past Saturday. Lovely lighting accents on the dance floor and pretty floral arrangements made a lasting impression. The bride, groom & their guests had a wonderful night celebrating!

A BIG thank you to:  
Coordinator: Celebrations by Heart- Julie  
Caterers: Continental Catering  
Lighting & Music: Verducci Productions  
Florist: Lisa Berlin Solberg
