# Rustic chic & pink

Kohl Mansion served as the perfect backdrop a few weekends ago for an amazing August wedding. The lawn area was the focal point for the ceremony, while the patio was adorned in rustic and chic elements for a stunning dining scene. It was perfect! Outdoor bistro lights, dancing in the Great Hall and dessert in the Black and White room (Morning room) made this wedding absolutely radiant!
