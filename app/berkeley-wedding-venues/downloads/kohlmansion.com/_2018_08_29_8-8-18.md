# 8.18.18

The most popular wedding date of 2018 paired up nicely with an amazing wedding at Kohl Mansion. With shades of pinks and purples, it was a night filled with so much joy and love. The bride and groom were radiating with happiness while all the guests enjoyed a summer dinner night in the Great Hall.
