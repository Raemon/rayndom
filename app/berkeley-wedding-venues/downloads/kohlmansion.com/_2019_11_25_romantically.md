# Romantic & Ravishing

A lovely wedding with beautiful details created a beautiful atmosphere for a sweet couple. With the ceremony in the Dining Room, dinner in the Great Hall & cocktail hour outside on the patio, was the perfect mix for this intimate night. The bride and groom were beaming with happiness and enjoyed a beautiful day!

A BIG thank you to: 

Coordinator & Florals: Blooming Wed Events-Lisa 

Catering: Weir Catering 

Music: Big Fun DJs
