On October 7, 2017, Kohl Mansion’s Director of Events, Terri Baldocchi, hosted a wedding for her daughter, Gianna, who married her high school sweetheart, Eric. After the church ceremony at their local parish, Our Lady of Angels, the bride and groom, along with all their guests headed up to the Mansion for an unforgettable evening!

The color palette was composed of dusty blues, muted mauves, a touch of navy and of course, rose gold. The Mansion was dazzling with enchantment and filled with so much love! Sophisticated details, tasteful design elements, incredible vendors, the most energetic bridal party and guest list, made this night absolute perfection. Kohl Mansion served as the most desirable backdrop that day, everyone was smiling ear to ear. Everything from the table tops, to the mouth watering menu, to the room designs all contributed to a magnificent wedding that will be forever remembered. Congratulations to the new Mr. & Mrs Anderson!!! Gianna La Mariana Eric Hijo de Ander
