# A Sensational Soiree

This incredibly elegant and stunning wedding sure made Kohl Mansion dazzle a few weeks ago. A candle lit ambiance mixed with muted tones lead to a lovely, intimate night with the couple’s closest family and friends. All of the wedding details delicately complimented Kohl Mansion in such a beautiful way!!

A BIG thank you to:  
Food: Toast Catering  
Music & Lighting: Big Fun DJ’s  
Makeup: Miabel  
Beverages: Smith Beverages
