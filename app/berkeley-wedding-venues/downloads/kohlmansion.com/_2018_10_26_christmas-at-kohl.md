# Christmas at Kohl

Wednesday, November 28, 2018 • 5:00-9:00pm

Join us for the annual Christmas at Kohl Holiday Boutique! Don’t miss the opportunity to visit Kohl Mansion, festively decorated to get you in the holiday spirit! 

More than 60 vendors will display their unique items and food stuffs, ready to help you cross items off of your holiday shopping list. There will also be a wonderful Docent Presentation about the history of Kohl Mansion. Light Refreshments will be sold for your enjoyment.

$10.00 Donation for Entrance; Free for Children under 12

Extra parking will be available at Our Lady of Angels Church, 1721 Hillside Drive, Burlingame with a shuttle to the event leaving every 15 minutes, starting at 5:30 p.m.
