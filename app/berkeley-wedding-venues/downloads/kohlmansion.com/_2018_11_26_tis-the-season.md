# ‘Tis the season

Kohl Mansion is in the midst of transforming into a holiday spectacular! Our florists and volunteers are working really hard to get Kohl dressed for the most wonderful time of year! More photos to come, stay tuned!

Florists shown here are:  
-Pico Soriano Designs decorating the upper foyer  
-Absolute Elegance decorating the Great Hall
