# A Summer like wedding in November!

Yesterday at Kohl Mansion a lovely and intimate wedding was held. With Kohl’s bright fuchsia bougainvillea  
as the couple’s backdrop and incredible warm weather made the perfect setting for this sweet bride and groom’s big day. Guests enjoyed a wonderful night outside mixing and mingling and celebrating the new Mr. & Mrs.!

A BIG thank you to:  
-Coordinator: Marian Ashworth  
-Catering: Joshua Charles Catering
