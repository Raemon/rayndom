# Christmas at Kohl 2019 event

**Christmas at Kohl**

**December 4 • 5-9pm**

The annual Christmas at Kohl Holiday Boutique is December 4th! More than 60 vendors will display their unique items and food stuffs, ready to help you cross items off of your Christmas Shopping list. Don’t miss the opportunity to visit Kohl Mansion, festively decorated to get you in the holiday spirit!

There will also be a wonderful Docent Presentation about Kohl Mansion’s history. Light Refreshments will be available for purchase.  
  
$10.00 donation for entrance, collected at the door.  
Children under 12 and Mercy students are free  
  
Extra parking will be available at Our Lady of Angels Church, 1721 Hillside Drive, Burlingame with a shuttle to Mercy High School every 15 minutes starting at 5:30pm.
