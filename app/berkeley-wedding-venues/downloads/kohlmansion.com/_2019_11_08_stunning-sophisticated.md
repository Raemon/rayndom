# Stunning & Sophisticated

Rich elements of black, white and greenery sparked excitement for this amazing Kohl wedding. It was gorgeous! The couple was beyond happy to share this special day with everyone near and dear to them! The details were filled with beauty and unique features. It was an incredible night for this lovely couple!
