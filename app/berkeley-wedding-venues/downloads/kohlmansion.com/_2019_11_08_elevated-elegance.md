# Elevated Elegance

Kohl Mansion was elegantly dressed in marvelous tones a few weekends ago for a Mercy alumna’s wedding. Beautiful neutrals and textures made this wedding sparkle. The bride and groom enjoyed an incredible night with their loved ones and amazing music and decor! Congratulations to newly weds, Lauren & Andrew!

A BIG thank you to: 

Coordinator: Walter Hough 

Lighting: Magical Lighting 

Florist: Sweetness & Light

Caterer: La Boca Fina

Baker: Katrina Rozelle 

Beverages: Greg Smith 

Music: Big Fun DJs
