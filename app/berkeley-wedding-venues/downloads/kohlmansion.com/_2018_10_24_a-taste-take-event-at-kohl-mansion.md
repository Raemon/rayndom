# A Taste & Take Event at Kohl Mansion

WHEN: Friday, November 16, 2018 • 7:00-10:00pm

WHAT: Join us for a fun and festive evening at Kohl Mansion, showcasing local wine, spirits, and food! Enjoy live music, mingle with friends, and experience the Mansion in its holiday best.

WHO: Open to the public 21 & OVER!  
Ticket information: $25 in Advance, $30 at the Door

JOIN: Click below for tickets, event information, vendor and sponsorship opportunities.  
https://mercyhsb.ejoinme.org/MyEvents/TasteTake2018/tabid/1004502/Default.aspx

All Event proceeds and 50% of all wine sales benefit Mercy High School Burlingame.
