# Summer Spectacular

Last Saturday, Kohl Mansion had a beautiful wedding! The ceremony on the lawn majestically captured the romantic setting while the reception took place all inside. Dinner and dancing were enjoyed by all guests and the bride and groom. Lovely tones of burgundy and lilac complimented the Great Hall. Everyone had a wonderful time celebrating these newly weds!
