# Sophisticated & Spectacular

A few weeks ago, Kohl Mansion hosted a beautiful wedding. With the ceremony on the lawn and reception inside the Great Hall, it was a lovely night. Filled with incredible floral arrangements, well designed place settings and fantastic music, it was spectacular!
