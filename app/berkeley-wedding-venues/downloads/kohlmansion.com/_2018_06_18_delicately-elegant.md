# Delicately elegant

Saturday’s wedding at Kohl Mansion was filled with traditional and elegant elements. With tones of silver, pink, and sage green tastefully dressed Kohl in a sophisticated way. It was a lovely night that was shared with the bride and groom’s closest family and friends.
