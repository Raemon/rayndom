# Join us for: “A Taste & Take Event at Kohl Mansion”

**A Taste & Take Event at Kohl Mansion**

**November 22 • 7-10pm**

Join us for a fun and festive evening at Kohl Mansion, showcasing local wine, spirits, and food! Enjoy live music, mingle with friends, and experience the Mansion decorated in its holiday best.  
  
Buy tickets at <https://tinyurl.com/KohlMansionWineNight>  
$25 in Advance-$30 at the Door  
  
All Event proceeds and 50% of all wine sales benefit Mercy High School Burlingame!
