# Elegantly Effortless

Summer is in full swing at Kohl Mansion! This romantically charming wedding shined with excitement and beauty this past weekend. Decorated with all neutral tones and textures, this wedding was classic and chic in every way. The bride and groom were radiating in their glory and guests enjoyed an incredible night on the patio under the stars.
