![](https://i0.wp.com/kohlmansion.com/wp-content/uploads/2018/04/Kohl_logo_map.png?resize=34%2C34&ssl=1)**Beyond its grandeur** , Kohl Mansion offers corporate and social gatherings something other venues can’t: flexibility. When you host an event at the mansion, you have access to the entire mansion. The wood-paneled Great Hall, the elegant furnishings, the English rose gardens, the terrace and more. This allows you to create a flow and a feeling like nowhere else.

Ideal for holiday parties, fundraisers, team building and retreats. Kohl Mansion also gives you access to the finest vendors in the area. So whether you want to applaud outstanding achievements or inspire even greater ones, this is the place to make it happen, with an event that’s uniquely yours.
