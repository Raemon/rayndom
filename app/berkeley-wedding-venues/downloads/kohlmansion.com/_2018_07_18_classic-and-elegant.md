# Classic and Elegant

This past weekend, Kohl Mansion’s Great Hall served a special purpose; a wedding ceremony! The mahogany details in the original wood paneling along with the mahogany chiavari chairs classically gave this white and burgundy wedding a sensational vibe. All guests enjoyed an incredible meal outside on the patio dressed in crisp white linens, and show-stopping white floral arrangements. 

A BIG thank you to:

Food: Toast Catering  
Lighting: Enhanced  
Music: Sound in Motion
