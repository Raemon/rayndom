# A sweet ending to 2018- 2019 Holiday events are booking fast!

Kohl Mansion wrapped up another fantastic year hosting a series of Holiday parties and occasions throughout the month of December. We can’t wait to see what is in store for 2019! A HUGE thank you to all of our wonderful vendors and clients that continue to make Kohl Mansion such a cherished and highly valued piece of history on the San Francisco peninsula. 

Kohl Mansion is filling up fast for 2019 Holiday events! Book your next holiday event with Terri or Dianne today!!
