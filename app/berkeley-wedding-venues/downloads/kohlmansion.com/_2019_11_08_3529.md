# Fabulous & Fun

What an incredible wedding! Warm, welcoming, beautiful and charming are just a few words to describe this special day! Guests enjoyed dining on Kohl’s patio and dancing in the Great Hall. Everyone was radiating with happiness for this adorable couple! Lovely details showcasing the season’s best color palette, tastefully complimented Kohl Mansion.
