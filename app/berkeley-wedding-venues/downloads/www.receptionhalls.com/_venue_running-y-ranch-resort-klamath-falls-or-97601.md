# Running Y Ranch Resort Klamath Falls, Oregon

Exchange vows under towering pine trees against the backdrop of the Cascade Mountain Range at Running Y Ranch Resort, one of the most stunning Southern Oregon wedding venues.  
  
Ideal for both intimate receptions amongst close friends and grand gatherings, the ranch is more than a wedding venue. The property is one of Klamath Falls' top family reunion destinations, with available space for anniversary parties, birthday bashes and more. Whatever the style or size of your celebration, you can count on Running Y Ranch to help make your day unforgettable.
