# Merrimon Estate at PDL Beach Properties Beaufort, North Carolina

Magnificent 15 Acre Estate on the Intracoastal Waterway in Beaufort NC!  
  
This incredible 15 Acre Estate on the water can be yours by the weekend, week, or month. Your privacy is assured in this beautiful gated property. Sleeping up to 17 with 6 bedrooms, 5 full baths and 1/2 bath bring your family, hold a corporate retreat or have a wedding inside the 40 seat pavilion which rests on the grounds. Enjoy the vacation of your dreams in this opulent home. This property comes with 3 deep water boat slips. Stroll the grounds and enjoy the private beach or relax on the included sleeping porch. Bring your boat, enjoy the fishing dock or play at the beach, all can be yours. This is a great place to bring your kayak. Enjoy the full game room with Billiards, a poker table, and luxurious full bath. There are fireplaces, fire pits, and a horseshoe pit. A golf cart is included in your stay to move around the property.  
  
If interested in renting this property to host an event our Rental fees for Peak season are $1000 and Off-Season $500.00.
