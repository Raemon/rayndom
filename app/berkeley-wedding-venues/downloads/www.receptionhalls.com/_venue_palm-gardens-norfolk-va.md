# Palm Gardens Norfolk, Virginia

Palm Gardens is located in Norfolk conveniently located near Shopping Malls, and Entertainment, and minutes from the Light Rail System. The Palms 2400 square feet of space is available for all your for all your meeting, special event, and catering needs.  
  
Our “At Your Service’ philosophy” is shared by every associate. This is the defining difference that will make your event a success. As our guest, your group is a part of the Palms family and we are “At Your Service!”  
  
Our building is equipped with the most up to date technology equipment for your business needs. Our complimentary Wireless High Speed Internet access is available throughout the building. Palm Gardens is equipped with the most up to date light and laser experience that you have ever seen in a facility of this kind. Come enjoy a new ambiance at the Palm.  
  
Our friendly staff can assist you in putting together a dream event. Whether it’s a Wedding Reception, Holiday Ball, VIP Birthday party Family Reunion or your own creative affair, we can make it a day to remember. We have facilities to hold 200+ people for your events let us serve you!  
  
Palm Gardens is available to cater your Breakfast, Lunch, and Dinner events on and off sit as well. We provide full featured catering and banquet services as well as “Bagged Lunches” for your corporate meeting staff. We are happy to help you arrange your event in our facilities from colors to decorations to make your event that one of a kind experience.  
  
Palm Gardens, a cool, clean classy venue located in Norfolk. We’re waiting to serve you!
