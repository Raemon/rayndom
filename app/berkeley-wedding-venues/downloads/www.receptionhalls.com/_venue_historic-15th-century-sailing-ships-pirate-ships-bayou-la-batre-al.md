# Historic 15th century Sailing Ships (Pirate Ships) Bayou La Batre, Alabama

Step on board historic 15th century sailing ships and get swept back in time. No other ships like us traveling the US waterways.. Book your unique wedding or event today. Make your wedding one for the ages.  
  
All activities are dock side and include access to three decks, kitchen and music. Crew members are provided as servers and dishwashers.
