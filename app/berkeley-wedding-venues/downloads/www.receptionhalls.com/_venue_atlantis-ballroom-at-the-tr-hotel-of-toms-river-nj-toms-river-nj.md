# Atlantis Ballroom at the TR Hotel of Toms River NJ Toms River, New Jersey

At the Atlantis Ballroom of TR Hotel in Toms River, you will experience only the best for your next event! Our Ocean County NJ venue hall has everything you need all under one roof. We are proud to be a top NJ banquet facility for weddings, bar/bat mitzvahs, meetings and corporate events, and more. With over 7,00 square feet of meeting space, an experienced, knowledgeable staff, and numerous catering menus to fit your needs and budget, we will always make sure your event makes an impact. Our reception facility features indoor and outdoor ceremony areas for weddings, a huge dance floor for up to 225 guests, beautiful accommodations at our hotel, and so much more.
