# Portuguese Athletic Club (PAC) San Jose, California

The Portuguese Athletic Club (PAC) is a sports, social and cultural organization promoting sports, culture, art and recreation.  
  
When we are not hosting events we are renting out the hall for special events such as Weddings, Quinceaneras, birthday parties, etc.  
  
Visit us on facebook under SJ PAC  
  
Established in 1962  
Formed in 1962 by Portuguese immigrants for a place in the community to gather socially and for sporting events.
