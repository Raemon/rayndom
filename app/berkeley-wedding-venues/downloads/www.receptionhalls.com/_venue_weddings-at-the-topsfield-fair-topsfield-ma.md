# Weddings at the Topsfield Fair Topsfield, Massachusetts

Here at Coolidge Hall on the Topsfield Fairgrounds, we pride ourselves on providing one of the best wedding venues on the North Shore. Whether you are having your ceremony, reception, or both events on the Fairgrounds, our number one goal is to provide you with a location for the wedding of your dreams. To provide the ultimate experience for our Brides and Grooms, we offer extensive customization and flexibility, as each wedding we host is unique to the couple getting married.
