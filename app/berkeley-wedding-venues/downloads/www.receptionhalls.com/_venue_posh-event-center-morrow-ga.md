# Posh Event Center Morrow, Georgia

We understand that every event is going to be different, and you’ll need accommodations to ensure your event goes off without a hitch. When you rent an event space from us, you’ll get access to the following as well:  
*Prep kitchen with an accessible servicing bar for beverages and a double door refrigerator   
*A separate area to be utilized for food service, Photo op stations (a 360 photobooth or backdrops), or a Breakout session space for meetings  
*Mutli-private bathrooms for men and women  
Also available for rental:  
*Charger plates  
*Premium table linen  
*Linen napkins  
*Centerpiece options  
*Cherry blossom trees  
*Lounge seating  
The Venue Rental starts at $1200 and is for up to 100 people: Includes 6 hour event space rental with exposed brick wall, white chiavari chairs w/ padding, round and rectangular tables, tablecloths (a variety of color options); event space layout, kitchen prep area, separate room for food tables; multi-stalled restrooms; & free parking.  
  
You can schedule an on-site visit to see the venue in-person @ https://go.thryv.com/site/posheventcenter/online-scheduling?service=jsfs6523030x67dk
