# Dukessa Houston Houston, Texas

An oasis in the heart of Houston, Dukessa - with its timeless elegance and classic architecture – is the perfect choice for many Special Events such as Weddings, Holiday parties, and Corporate events. Dukessa hosts extraordinary events with an in-house award-winning culinary team as well as professional and friendly staff that strive to make our client’s events smooth and stress-free. Located in the prime Galleria area, Dukessa can host events of all sizes ranging from 50-300 guests. Our Clients love our beautifully-lit courtyard to enjoy cocktails and our classic wine room, with iron cellar doors and hand-carved stone columns, to Say I-do! Floor to ceiling windows in our main ballroom bring the “outside in” as clients dance and party the night away. A 5-star customer experience is priority at Dukessa and we strive for to make it happen for all our clients, every day, in every way!
