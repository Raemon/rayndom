# Buck Ridge Barn Harrison, Arkansas

Buck Ridge Barn, tucked away in the heart of the Ozark Mountains, is Harrison Arkansas' newest event venue. It is located on 265 acres of private land and is the perfect place for celebrating life's most important events. We can accommodate weddings, receptions, parties, banquets, reunions, proms, corporate and church retreats, and more.  
Buck Ridge Barn is a 100 year old authentic barn infused with elegant elements such as dramatic fabric draping and chandeliers. The space transforms into an amazing rustic chic setting.  
The picturesque views at Buck Ridge Barn afford great opportunity for a truly unique event and sensational backdrops to create dream photos of a vintage wedding.
