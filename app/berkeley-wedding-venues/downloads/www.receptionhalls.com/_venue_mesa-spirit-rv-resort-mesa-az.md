# Mesa Spirit RV Resort Mesa, Arizona

Looking for a venue for your next meeting, conference, party or reunion? Mesa Spirit has facilities on property that will hold up to 500 people, stage, sound system, A/V equipment, tables &amp; chairs and full service catering for all your needs. Beautiful property with a ballroom, 3 swimming pools, a billiards room, picnic areas with gas BBQ's and fully furnished rental properties available for your guests to stay during your event.
