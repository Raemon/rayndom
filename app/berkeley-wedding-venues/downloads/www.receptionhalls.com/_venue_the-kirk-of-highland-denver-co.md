# The Kirk of Highland Denver, Colorado

The Kirk of Highland is a wedding and event venue situated in the heart of the historic Highland neighborhood in Denver, Colorado. The historic venue was constructed in 1890 and remains one of the city’s most recognizable sights.   
  
Facilities and Capacity The Kirk of Highland offers three unique event spaces that can accommodate up to 225 wedding guests. The 2,800-square-foot private patio garden provides a gorgeous, intimate outdoor space for you and your guests to enjoy with our market lights and working fountain. The gallery provides an open, spacious area for an indoor reception- the perfect blank canvas for your event! The 4,000-square-foot space is ideal for dining and dancing the night away among our stained glass windows. Our historic and unique Sanctuary is the perfect place for a wedding ceremony, church, cnerts, speaking events, and more with the 288 original seats.   
  
Additional amenities include:   
Getting ready spaces  
Tables, chairs, and linens included  
WiFi throughout  
Free parking for 25 cars + free street parking
