# Luxx Lab Event Suite Greystone Park, New Jersey

The Luxx Lab Event Suite is a boutique photography event studio perfect for hosting any event. Whether it is a bridal shower, baby shower, charity brunch, business meeting, dinner social, micro-wedding, pop-up shop, wedding reception, or a photoshoot, we add luxury to every special occasion. This spacious, 1,600 square-foot space, is a modern suite located in Morris Plains, NJ and proudly serving Morris County and the surrounding areas. Our suite features a “blank canvas” that is perfect for you to customize for a unique & memorable experience
