# Starlight Chateau Omaha, Nebraska

Starlight Chateau is a spacious 110 year-old church. Inside ceremonies in the Chapel Ballroom. Outside ceremonies in the courtyard. Have both Wedding and Reception for no extra charge. Wedding arch &amp; many decorations included. ~ OPEN CATERING ~ Customers may arrange their own food and beverage, including alcohol ! $250 reserves the event date, monthly payments for the balance. Easy highway access, free off street fenced parking. Open Dates ~ Available On Short Notice. More photos @ StarlightChateau.com Call Call Call (402) 731 - 1876 for rental rates and discounts.

### Venue Details

Capacity (Seated / Cocktail): **210 / 300**

The Chateau has 4 different areas to gather with family and friends. The elegant Chapel Ballroom seats up to 210 guests, with elevated stage and full wood floor. The relaxing Skylight Room seats 190 guests, for casual receptions, birthday parties, dances and family gatherings. A romantic Southern-style courtyard with unique stone grotto and veranda welcomes up to 150 guests for outdoor weddings, cocktail parties and summer evening events. The conference room is perfect for small gatherings of 15 to 35 guests, for small wedding receptions, birthday parties, showers and meetings.

Max Number of Simultaneous Events: **127**
