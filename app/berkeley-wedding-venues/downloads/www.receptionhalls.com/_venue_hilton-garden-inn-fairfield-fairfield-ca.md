# Hilton Garden Inn Fairfield Fairfield, California

Hilton Garden Inn Fairfield is a premier wedding and event venue located in Fairfield, CA just off I-80 freeway with all of the amenities you and your guests could imagine. Our 4,225 square foot ballroom can accommodate parties up to 350 people. In house catering by Chef Peter Garcia is sure to impress. Planning for your wedding couldn't be any easier. Contact our planner, and we'll take care of the details so you can focus on enjoying - not worrying over - your big day. Everything you need...from banquet facilities to menu selections...can be found within our bright, inviting hotel. We also specialize spoiling everyone on your list with spacious rooms, beautifully landscaped grounds and stress-relieving swimming and fitness facilities.
