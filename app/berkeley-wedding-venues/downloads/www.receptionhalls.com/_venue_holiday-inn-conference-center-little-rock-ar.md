# Holiday Inn Conference Center Little Rock, Arkansas

We welcome you to Little Rock's beautiful, all new hotel - Holiday Inn Conference Center. This new Little Rock hotel is located 1 mile from the airport with free shuttle, 5 minutes from downtown Little Rock, President Clinton Library and River Market.  
  
Wireless &amp; wired free high-speed Internet is available. Enjoy our executive and business level rooms, outdoor pool, fitness center, business center and atrium. Our staff is trained in ADA facilities with Opening Doors certification. Grab a bite at Boston's Restaurant &amp; Sports Bar or the AV8 Cafe, Parking is free. We are close to Verizon Arena, IMAX and Toltec Mounds.   
  
Our friendly employees take a special interest in our guests' comfort. We offer a large amount of meeting space with the meetings promise of Holiday Inns. Our certified sales experts have been in the hotel business for years, insuring that your meeting, wedding, reunion and/or reception will be a true success, without surprises.  
  
We are a popular meeting favorite among the Little Rock hotels.
