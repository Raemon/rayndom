# Best Western Plus Waterville Waterville, Maine

BEST WESTERN PLUS Waterville Grand Hotel is the only place business, leisure, and Best Western Rewards members choose to host their events while in Central Maine! The Best Western Waterville is conveniently located right off Interstate 95.  
  
With over 5,000 square feet of newly remodeled meeting and event space, we can accommodate anything from your corporate meeting to social functions. Our different banquet room options make it easy to plan your wedding reception, funeral gathering, class reunion, baby or wedding shower, corporate training session, board meeting and many more.  
  
We boast a seating capacity of 140, offering wedding packages, courtyard ceremonies and even have a Notary Public on staff. With onsite catering, our professional staff is waiting to create the ideal package to ensure that your event is successful. Our array of menu selections is certain to meet all of your budgetary needs.  
  
Our well-appointed and newly- remodeled guest rooms will offer all the comforts and conveniences you have come to expect for your out of town guests. New LCD flat screen TV’s will allow them to watch their favorite shows in HD. Fiber optic wireless connectivity will let keep then connected to work and home while they are away. Your guests will experience a great night sleep with our new mattresses and pillows. They will wake in the morning ready to attend your event.  
  
On site at the Best Western Plus is O’Brien’s Irish Restaurant and Pub, a great way to end the day with dinner and a drink or start the day with breakfast. Our indoor heated salt water swimming pool, hot tub and fitness center will allow your guests to start the day with a workout and end the day with a relaxing dip in the pool or hot tub.  
Whether you’re planning a social or corporate function we are you’re perfect match.   
  
Memories are waiting to be made here at central Maine’s finest full service hotel, the Best Western Plus Waterville.
