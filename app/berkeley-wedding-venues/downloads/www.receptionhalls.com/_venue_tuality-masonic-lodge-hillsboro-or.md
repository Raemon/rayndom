# Tuality Masonic Lodge Hillsboro, Oregon

Perfect venue for receptions, birthdays, quinces, class reunions, family reunions and other events. This classic mission style building offers a spacious banquet hall in downtown Hillsboro. Tables and chairs are provided. Private parking for up to 12 cars. Street parking available.   
  
Beer and wine service available for a fee. Kitchen for service or food prep is available.
