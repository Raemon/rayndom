# Marysville Historical Society & Museum Marysville, Washington

![Photo of Marysville Historical Society & Museum](/media/WA/12577/marysville-historical-society-museum-marysville-wa-98270.jpg)

The Marysville Historical Society and Museum is one of Snohomish County's best kept secret! Located next to Jennings Park, we are an affordable &amp; wonderful location to host an event. Whether you are planning a class reunion, club or private event, baby shower, anniversary party, wedding, or memorial we can help accommodate your celebration! Event coordination available at a discounted rate through an independent event coordination company. Please contact Donna DeAngelo-Hurst, our event coordinator, at 360-659-3090.
