# Americana Kitchen & Bar Hightstown, New Jersey

THE PERFECT VENUE FOR YOUR NEXT EVENT  
We offer beautiful spaces for your private party or corporate function, conveniently located on Route 130 in East Windsor, NJ. Our professional catering staff will assist you with all aspects of your event.  
  
EVERY SPECIAL OCCASION DESERVES TO BE CELEBRATED IN A SPECIAL PLACE.  
Whether your celebrating an anniversary, a communion, bridal shower or any other special moment Americana Kitchen and Bar is committed to impeccable service and delicious food.  
  
A WONDERFUL SETTING FOR CORPORATE DINNERS, MEETINGS, AND SEMINARS.  
We are well equipped to meet your audio/visual equipment needs. We have the culinary muscle and wine list to please the most discerning palette and our professional conference planers will assist you at every turn.  
  
WE LOVE TO BAKE  
At the heart of Americana is our bakery. Our Pastry Chef Ernie Rich brings his 35 years of baking passion and experience to everything he makes, from birthday cakes to wedding cakes. And for the last ten years, Caser Lopez manages our artisan bread and hand-made croissant production
