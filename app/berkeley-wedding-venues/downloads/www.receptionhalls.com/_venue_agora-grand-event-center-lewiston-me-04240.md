# Agora Grand Event Center Lewiston, Maine

The Agora Grand Event Center is Maine's newest and most elegant venue, perfect for the destination wedding of your dreams. If you're planning to say "I do," treat yourself to a mesmerizing Reception Hall, a gorgeous chapel with original stained glass, and a luxurious balcony lounge to pamper you and your wedding party. As Maine's most unique all-inclusive wedding venue built for small and large weddings alike, the Agora Grand includes New England's only honeymoon suite created in a former church tower.
