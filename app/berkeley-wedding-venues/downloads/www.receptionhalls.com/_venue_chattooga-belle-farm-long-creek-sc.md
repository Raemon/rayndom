# Chattooga Belle Farm Long Creek, South Carolina

Chattooga Belle Farm is a 138-acre farm and vineyard in South Carolina in the shadow of the Blue Ridge Mountains. The large event barn features indoor heated space or a large covered patio area with views of the mountains and fields. It also features a commercial kitchen, restrooms and lawn space for tents.
