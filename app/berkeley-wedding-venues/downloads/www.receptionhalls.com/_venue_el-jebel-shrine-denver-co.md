# El Jebel Shrine Denver, Colorado

Our Beautiful center offers fully catered food and beverage service in our great Imperial Ballroom, Grand Ballroom, or smaller dining and meeting rooms. The imperial ballroom provides a sense of unparalleled ambiance and elegance with its full theatrical stage, chandelier lighting and mezzanine level seated balcony. Available for wedding receptions, rehearsal dinners, awards banquets, special events or business meetings. We can accommodate 20-1,250 guests with free ample parking. The historically charming building is surrounded by Willis Case golf course with a prestigious view of the Rocky Mountain Front Range.
