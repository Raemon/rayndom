# Uptown Hall at Parkland F.C. Langhorne, Pennsylvania

Uptown Hall at the Parkland F.C. is a ground level rental hall located in Langhorne PA just minutes from far northeast Philadelphia. The hall was renovated in Oct 2015 including new lighting, floors, tables, chairs, etc. The hall is owned by the Parkland Fire Co. and leased full time to another 501(c) charitable organization for use as their meeting place and for event rentals. Kitchen can be used for serving, warming, ice machine, counter opening that is perfect for serving beverages. You can bring your own food/ caterer, your own beverages, your own DJ or band. Parking lots right across the street.
