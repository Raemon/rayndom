# Haight Home Bed & Breakfast Oakley, Idaho

![Photo of Haight Home Bed & Breakfast](/media/ID/11104/haight-home-bed-breakfast-oakley-id.jpg)

Thank you for considering the historic Haight Home as a location for your event!  
  
We choose to share the beauty of our gardens with those who love nature and the outdoors and who will respect our property as if it were their own. If you choose to use our property, you will be expected to comply with rules and guidelines to ensure that we can continue to allow its use. The rules will be considered part of the contract, so please read and understand them before deciding to commit to our contract. Failure to follow our rules and guidelines, will result in the forfeiture of a portion of or all of your damage deposit. Please request an email with these guidelines if you would like to see them!  
  
Our list of rules and guidelines may seem extensive, but this is a historic home, not public property. We spend many hours working on it to make it a pleasurable place of beauty for your event. We welcome all who can respect the guidelines. If you have any questions, please do not hesitate to contact us at 208-862-STAY(7829).

### Pricing

Budget Tier:  **$**

Minimim price for 100 guests: **$ 1,000**

Site/Rental Fee: **$900**

Without alcohol - $900 + refundable $250.00 damage and cleaning deposit. Weddings with alcohol- $1100 + $250.00 refundable cleaning and damage deposit. **A valid credit card must be kept on file at the time of the event in the case of any damages or extra charges incurred that exceed the $250 deposit. These rates include use of Historic Beauty and Historic Charmer rooms in the house to change for the bridal party, use of the Garage for bathroom access during the event, and use of the kitchen for food prep. A $200 non-refundable deposit is due at the time of reserving your date (this amount goes towards your balance). The remaining balance must be paid in full two (2) weeks prior to date of event.
