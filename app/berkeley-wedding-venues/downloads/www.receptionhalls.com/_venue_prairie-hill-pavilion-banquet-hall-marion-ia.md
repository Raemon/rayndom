# Prairie Hill Pavilion Banquet Hall Marion, Iowa

Welcome to Prairie Hill Pavilion! We are a one-stop shop for your special wedding reception experience. Prairie Hill Pavilion provides the hall room, catering services, a full-service bar, and optional DJ Disc Jockey entertainment. We can also offer catered out service, either by special delivery, or carry-out in bulk. Another specialty service is our convention and corporate services for training, banquets, and special events such as holiday parties. Need a venue for larger groups such as funeral dinners? We can do that also - and at reduced special prices. Fund raising events for charities and special needs - we do those too! Coming Soon: need to cater your own meal? Check with us about "cater your own food" option. Prairie Hill Pavilion offers many items for decorating the hall, including: pipe &amp; drape backdrop, table runners, mirror tiles, candle holders, and up-lighting.  
  
We provide services the following areas of Iowa: Linn County, Cedar Rapids, Iowa City, Johnson County, Marion, Hiawatha, Robins, Fairfax, Anamosa, Central City, Center Point, Lisbon, Mount Vernon, Ely, Shueyville, North Liberty, Springville, Jones County, Coggin, Alburnett, Manchester, Monticello, Amana.
