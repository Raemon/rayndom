# D'Ignazio's Towne House Media, Pennsylvania

![Photo of D'Ignazio's Towne House](/media/PA/264/dignazios-towne-house-media-pa.jpg)

D'Ignazio's Towne House has been a Delaware Valley tradition for over 55 years. With 10 unique and elegant rooms, we welcome events up to 250. Award-winning food and impeccable service have made the Towne House a premier destination in the Tri-State Area. Certified Wedding Consultant on-site.
