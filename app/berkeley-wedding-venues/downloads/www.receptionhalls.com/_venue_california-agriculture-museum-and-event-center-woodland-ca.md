# California Agriculture Museum and Event Center Woodland, California

We have several venues on site to create the perfect ambiance you seek. Our banquet hall and Conference Room seats 325- 700 and has an attached commercial kitchen. The courtyard makes a stunning backdrop for wedding ceremony or summer party. For a truly unique experience, reserve a wing of the museum and treat your guests to an evening surrounded by early Californian history!   
Please visit our website for more photos, or call for a more personalized discussion as to how the California   
Agriculture Museum can be transformed into the spot you're searching for.
