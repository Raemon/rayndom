# Bread & Chocolate Goshen, Indiana

![Photo of Bread & Chocolate](/media/IN/11097/bread-chocolate-goshen-in.jpg)

Bread &amp; Chocolate is an event venue and catering facility located in the historic Old Bag Factory in Goshen, IN.  
  
We have been creating memorable events for more than 20 years. Our contemporary urban loft style venues create the perfect ambiance for weddings, meetings or whatever your event requires.  
  
The Bread &amp; Chocolate kitchen creates delicious cuisine using fresh ingredients and elegant presentation to WOW your guests. Gracious service and attention to detail are all part of the care that we provide our clients.  
  
Bread &amp; Chocolate catering is also available at your favorite location as a delivery or with our excellent service. We have catered tented weddings, company picnics, and private in-home parties.
