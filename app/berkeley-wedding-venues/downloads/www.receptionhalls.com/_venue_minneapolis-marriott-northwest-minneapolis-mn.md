# Minneapolis Marriott Northwest Minneapolis, Minnesota

The new all-suite Minneapolis Marriott Northwest is the Metro's leading wedding reception venue offering 6 unique ballrooms capable of accommodating up to 600. Lavishly adorned ballrooms feature cozy fireplaces and dazzling chandeliers. Enjoy award-winning food and a wedding as unique as you.
