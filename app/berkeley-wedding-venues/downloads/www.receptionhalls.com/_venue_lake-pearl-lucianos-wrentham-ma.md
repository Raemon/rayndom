# Lake Pearl Luciano's Wrentham, Massachusetts

Lake Pearl Luciano's with its spectacular waterfront location, elegant interiors and romantic grounds is a favorite Massachusetts wedding reception site. Offering a variety of distinctive settings, Lake Pearl Luciano's can accommodate on-site wedding ceremonies, receptions and rehearsal dinners from 50 to 550 guests. The Grand Ballroom is ideal for large-scale wedding receptions, while the Tree Top Room with its dramatic walls of glass and breathtaking views is perfect for weddings and rehearsal dinners for up to 110 guests. In season, the Victorian Tent offers a unique setting for beachfront ceremonies and outdoor receptions.  
  
Brides will enjoy the convenience of separate entryways, personal bridal suites, private cocktail lounges, dedicated bridal attendants and access to the romantic grounds - idea for photography. Delectable cuisine, expertly served, ensures success. Choose from one of our unique wedding packages to accommodate your individual needs. Special Promotions for Friday evening and Saturday daytime receptions offer savings.
