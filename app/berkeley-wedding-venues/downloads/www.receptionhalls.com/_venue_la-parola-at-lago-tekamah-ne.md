# La Parola at Lago Tekamah, Nebraska

La Parola al Lago is a waterfront venue located at Summit Lake, a natural spring fed lake.. 

### Contact Info

2800 County Road G • Tekamah, NE • 68061 

[Call us](tel:402-541-5275)

Capacity (Seated / Cocktail): **400 / 150**

Entire restaurant, deck, bar and banquet areas. Ample parking.

Max Number of Simultaneous Events: **3**
