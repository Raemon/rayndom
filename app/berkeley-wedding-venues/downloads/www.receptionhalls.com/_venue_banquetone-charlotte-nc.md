# BanquetOne Charlotte, North Carolina

BanquetOne is a modern Venue with luxurious look and feel.   
  
Hardwood floor throughout, chandelier adorned ceiling and prep kitchen.  
  
Located in a professional business park, with plenty of free parking, the venue is 3300 SQFT of comfortable space.   
  
Tables, chairs, setup and cleanup are provided. Everything else can be brought in to fully customize your event.  
  
Easy access from I77 and I485. Location cannot be better. Near Northlake mall and Huntersville on Statesville Ave.
