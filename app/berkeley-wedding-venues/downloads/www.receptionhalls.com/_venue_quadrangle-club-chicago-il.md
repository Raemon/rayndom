# Quadrangle Club Chicago, Illinois

Located in the heart of the beautiful Hyde Park neighborhood, the Quadrangle Club is filled with unique 1920s-era architectural details. Our culinary offerings are unsurpassed in quality and presentation. The Quadrangle Club offers five unique event spaces to accommodate groups of all sizes. The rooms can be used in combination to create dozens of different floor plans and set up styles. With a reputation for quality food and personal service, the Quadrangle Club is the ideal venue to host your wedding. Whether you are planning the rehearsal dinner, reception, or morning after brunch, our staff will make sure your day is truly memorable.
