# Salbeh Lounge Fall River, Massachusetts

Salbeh Lounge is a private member club. It is a place where members experience the Old Country without ever stepping foot on a plane. Whether you are ethnic Lebanese, Portuguese, Irish, French, Italian, Spanish or other, you can come enjoy private events with your family or friends and feel like you are back home. The Banquet Hall is a beautiful space that is available for rent for any even such as weddings, anniversaries, birthdays, baptisms, holiday parties and many more. It has a 160 people capacity which is large enough for most events.  
  
The Bar area has a 38 people capacity. It‘s a place where you can enjoy a drink with friends, watch sports, play keno or have a light snack. In addition to that, there are two rooms for playing a variety of card games for adults as well as a play room that is designs with everyone in mind. The play room area has a pool/ping pong table, video games, an air hockey table and a foosball table.  
  
Bar: The Bar area is a place where you can enjoy a drink with friends, watch sports, play card and keno or have a light snack. Have a blast while enjoying tasty and refreshing drinks, along with great music.  
  
Banquet Hall: The Banquet Hall is available for rent for any even such as weddings, anniversaries, birthdays, Baptisms, holiday parties and many more. It has a capacity of 160 people and is available for booking to celebrate your special occasions in the best way possible.  
  
Playroom: The play room area has a pool/ping pong table, video games, an air hockey table, card games and a Foosball table. You, your kids and your friends can enjoy playing in the arcade and spending quality time together.  
  
Event Planning: Whether it’s your birthday, wedding, conference or a simple gathering, Salbeh Lounge’s event planning team is here to make sure they take care of everything so you can have an unforgettable time.
