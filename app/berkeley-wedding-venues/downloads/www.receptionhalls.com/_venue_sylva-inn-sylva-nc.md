# Sylva Inn Sylva, North Carolina

The Sylva Inn is nestled in the heart of the Smokies and is a wonderful place to plan a wedding reception, family reunion, company retreat, seminar, group gathering, or any other event that you might be planning. The meeting one is over 3,600 square feet and can be divided into 1, 2 or 3 rooms.
