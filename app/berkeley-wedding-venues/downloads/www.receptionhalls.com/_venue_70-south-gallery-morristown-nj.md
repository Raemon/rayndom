# 70 South Gallery Morristown, New Jersey

Shown by appointment only, this downtown Morristown, NJ, Gallery is an ideal location for your special occasion. Whether you are looking for a candlelit dinner or prefer standing room only for your crowd of 100, this bright, contemporary space can accommodate a wide array of clients hoping to feel an air of sophistication with a simultaneous nod to Main Street. With its rotating exhibits of world-class art, 70 South is the perfect choice for smaller weddings, rehearsal dinners, bridal and baby showers, milestone celebrations, product launches, fundraisers, corporate dinners, and more. Rental fee $1,000.
