# Acropolis On Freret New Orleans, Louisiana

The Mediterranean decor, menu and wine selection at Acropolis On Freret is an AUTHENTIC setting and experience for your special event. Whether you are planning an intimate dinner, reception, shower, corporate or networking event, our mission is to provide an atmosphere that feels like a little piece of the Mediterranean right here in New Orleans!  
Our passion is great food, exquisite wine and extreme customer service! We are family here and we are honored to host you and your gatherings; large, small or catered.  
  
Book your special day and join us for a Taste of Mediterranean, only at Acropolis On Freret.
