# Glorious Banquet Hall Davie Fort Lauderdale, Florida

When memories should last a lifetime, trust the one stop venue for all your party needs. Glorious Banquet Hall is a full services banquet hall located in Sunrise, Florida. We provide an Inclusive experience at an affordable price. Each event is assigned their own personal event planner that will make your perfect day come true.  
  
Glorious provides seating for over 240 guest and ample parking. We cater to events from as little as 50 people on up to as 240 people. We also offer packages that include custom centerpieces, decoration, balloon décor and more.
