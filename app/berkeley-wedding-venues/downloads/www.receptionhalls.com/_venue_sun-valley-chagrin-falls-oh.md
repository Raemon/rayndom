# Sun Valley Chagrin Falls, Ohio

Sun Valley, a lovely oasis in the greater Cleveland area. This locally owned and managed property is an elegant backdrop for formal gatherings or casual celebrations. The airy, modern, boutique venue is designed for an easy flow of seated dinners and standing social events in a cozy private hideaway.  
  
Sitting on 25 acres, we are the perfect location for your dream wedding. Enjoy your ceremony surrounded by lush greenery and stunning views. Celebrate your reception outdoors under bistro lights or indoors in our open and inviting dining room, which boasts a neutral palette and handcrafted details
