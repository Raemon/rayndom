# The Loft at Babe Matteri's Reno, Nevada

2900 Sq. Ft. of Temperature controlled Special Occasion Space, The Loft is Featured upper level, Nestled in the heart of Somersett. From The Loft, Guests can enjoy Scenic views from a panoramic window view, as well as two balconies. Just beneath, Guests can enjoy two restaurants, as well as an on property gaming lounge.   
Private Catering on site through Babe Matteri's. Free Menu Consultations available upon request.
