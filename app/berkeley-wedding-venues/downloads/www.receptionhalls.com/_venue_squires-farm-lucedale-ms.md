# Squires' Farm Lucedale, Mississippi

Squires' Farm Weddings and Events is proud to offer you our venue Squires' Farm. We are a functioning farm with three amazing lakes in a private setting on the Alabama/Mississippi Border. We welcome animals, outside vendors, and have plenty of open land for you to design the event of your dreams. We also offer wedding planning to help you plan and design the wedding of your dreams. Please visit our website to see more of our venue and what we have to offer!
