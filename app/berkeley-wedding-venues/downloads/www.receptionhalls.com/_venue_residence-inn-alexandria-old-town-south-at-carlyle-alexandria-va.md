# Residence Inn Alexandria Old Town South at Carlyle Alexandria, Virginia

![Photo of Residence Inn Alexandria Old Town South at Carlyle](/media/VA/10772/residence-inn-alexandria-old-town-south-at-carlyle-alexandria-va.jpg)

Located near Old Town Alexandria, our hotel provides the perfect accommodations and event space for wedding parties and banquets. Along with our well-appointed suites, guests enjoy complimentary daily hot breakfast, fitness center access, and evening social hours. Our hotel is conveniently located near two Metro stations, offering easy access to local wedding sites and downtown Washington, DC.
