# The Coronado Ballroom Saint Louis, Missouri

A Lavishly Appointed Ballroom, State of the Art Meeting Rooms, Spectacular Menus, Decadent Desserts and Pampering Staff Await You &amp; Your Guests  
Description  
Banquet facility within a historic building, The Coronado, in St Louis Missouri.  
General Information  
Combine exquisite, elegant surroundings with exceptional service and cuisine and you get: an event at St Louis’ historic Coronado Ballroom and Meeting Facility. The ideal venue for a variety of events including weddings receptions, ceremonies and rehearsal dinners, corporate meetings and events and a host of other social gatherings and galas.  
  
At the Coronado, we are not just another venue, but we are event specialists. With over 50 years of experience, our team of event planners and world-class chefs are dedicated to making your next event nothing short of perfect. We offer full planning assistance to insure your event is flawless and personalized for you.
