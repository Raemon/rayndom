# The Founders Golf Club Sarasota, Florida

![Photo of The Founders Golf Club](/media/FL/12550/the-founders-golf-club-sarasota-fl-34240.jpg)

The Founders Club is a prestigious golfing community nestled among fragrant magnolia trees and century-old, majestic, moss-covered oaks. It epitomizes the charming old Florida plantation style feel with a 21st century class and elegance. Let this unforgettable landscape provide a breathtakingly unique backdrop for your wedding or special event!
