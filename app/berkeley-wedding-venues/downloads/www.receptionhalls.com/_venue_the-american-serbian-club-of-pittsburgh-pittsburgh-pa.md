# The American Serbian Club of Pittsburgh Pittsburgh, Pennsylvania

Excellent venue for wedding receptions, banquets, Christmas parties, showers, meetings, and services. Best value for a hall rental in the Southside and Pittsburgh area. 1 block from Carson Street.

\- Two kitchens  
\- Two TVs (upstairs)  
\- Stage (downstairs)  
\- Reception area
