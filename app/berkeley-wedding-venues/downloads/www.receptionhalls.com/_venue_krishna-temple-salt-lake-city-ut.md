# Krishna Temple Salt Lake City, Utah

Situated near downtown Salt Lake City, Krishna Temple Auditorium is quite suited for small and medium gatherings and events such as birthday parties, school functions, weddings, music concerts and religious gatherings and functions. We can provide vegetarian catering service for up to 250 guests though you could bring your own vegetiarian food also.A smaller (Garuda) room for smaller events for seating up to 50 guests is also available.   
Krishna Temple is non-smoking and non-drinking, vegetarian facility.
