# Medicine Park Aquarium Medicine Park, Oklahoma

The Medicine Park Aquarium & Natural Sciences Center is the ideal place to begin a lifetime of love and happiness. Our unique venue provides a one-of-a-kind experience that your guests will talk about for years to come. Breathtaking views of the Wichita Mountains coupled with our assortment of beautiful native Oklahoma wildlife create an unforgettable ambiance. Inside the aquarium you can electrify your love by the Electric Eels and outside you can beatify your blessings in the Botanical Gardens. Explore our affordable packages and have the chance to work hand-in-hand with our Event Coordinator. Go to www.mpmns.org/private-events for more information and packages.
