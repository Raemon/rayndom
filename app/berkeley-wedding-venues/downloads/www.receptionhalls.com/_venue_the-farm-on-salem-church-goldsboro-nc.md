# The Farm on Salem Church Goldsboro, North Carolina

Private residence with spacious Great Room and stunning historic parlour plus numerous other spaces, with gardens and lawn for outside venues. Our facility is on 15 private acres surrounded by mature trees and fields for overflow parking. Decorated in European styles, the interior spaces are luxurious but can accommodate either sit-down dining or stand-up receptions. Wonderfully spacious Bedrooms are available for overnight via airbnb.
