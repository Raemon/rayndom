# San Manuel Village Event Center Highland, California

Thank you for reaching out to the Event Center at San Manuel Village. We are thrilled to be a part of your event!  
We welcome you to Highland’s first all-inclusive Event Center, the perfect location for any occasion. The contemporary design, stunning architecture and beautiful landscaping compliment the venue, with extraordinary views of the San Bernardino Mountains. Easily accessible, it is located at the corner of Highland and Boulder Avenues, just west of Highway 330.  
Our mission is to provide excellent and dependable service during your planning process, leaving you time to focus on this special time in your life, to create those keepsake moments.  
  
Not only do we offer several catering options to work with any budget and full bar services, we also offer special rates for a block of rooms for your guests. Associated with the Hampton Inn &amp; Suites- Highland, your guests can have the convenience of staying nearby.  
For further information, or to book an event,  
contact Tasha Howe at (909) 907-3006 or via e-mail tasha.howe@hilton.com
