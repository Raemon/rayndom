# Cross Pointe Venue and Chapel Mendenhall, Mississippi

Cross Pointe Venue and Chapel is a of a kind Wedding Venue. It is the perfect back drop if you are looking for a picturesque and natural backdrop for your wedding. Not a Barn and can easily be transformed to your Vision. We have both indoor and outdoor options.   
  
Though we love weddings, we welcome Class Reunions, Bridal showers, Holiday Parties, corporate retreats and other events.
