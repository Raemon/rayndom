# Channel Club Monmouth Beach, New Jersey

Your wedding celebration will be a day you and your guests will remember for many years to come. The Channel Club, located in Monmouth Beach, NJ provides a breathtaking backdrop for this special day.  
  
With panoramic water views and our exclusive waterfront setting, this is the perfect site for your once in a lifetime celebration. Whether you want to utilize the outside deck for your ceremony by the water to go along with a poolside cocktail hour or take advantage of our beautiful bi-level facility, we can accommodate you.  
  
Our reception room, with huge windows on three sides overlooking the water and evening sunsets... can accommodate up to 250 people and our attentive staff and exquisite cuisine will impress you and your guests.  
  
Make the Channel Club your choice for your dream wedding by the waterfront!
