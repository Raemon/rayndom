# Wedgewood Ventura Ventura, California

![Photo of Wedgewood Ventura](/media/CA/9776/wedgewood-ventura-ventura-ca.jpg)

Our original site is still the standard for Ventura County wedding locations. Wedgewood Wedding and Banquet Center at Buenaventura Golf Course is a top outdoor wedding venue in Southern California, punctuated by our Value, Service, and Convenience that is unmatched.  
  
Some of the spectacular, unique features of our Ventura wedding location:  
  
* Awarded “Ventura County’s Best Banquet Facility” since 2001  
* Picturesque wedding site nestled in a rose garden surrounded by majestic palm trees  
* Arched wooden pergola at ceremony site  
* Up to 350 seated guests  
* Recently renovated banquet room featuring a fireplace, fabulous bar, and beautiful golf course views
