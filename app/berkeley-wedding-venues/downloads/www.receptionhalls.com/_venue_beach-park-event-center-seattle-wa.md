# Beach Park Event Center Seattle, Washington

The City of Des Moines offers an ideal location for events of any caliber. Our venues can comfortably hold anywhere from 40 to 400 guests with plenty of room for decorations. Have your choice of scenic locations in both rustic and contemporary buildings designed for versatility. The Des Moines Beach Park Event Center is the home to the Auditorium boasting over 3,700 square feet of flexible event space. This facility is perfect for any occasion. Large family gatherings such as Reunions and Wedding Receptions shine in this building. The Auditorium has a 450 square foot stage that is perfect for entertainment or your head table. It can seat up 360 people or accommodate 525 for standing capacity. The Des Moines Beach Park Event Center is also home to the Founders Lodge. This building once housed the Camp Staff when the Covenant Church owned and operated the Church Camp prior to 1984. This facility is perfect for any event anticipating an attendance of 80 or fewer people. It serves as a wonderful Ready Room for the bride and groom waiting to walk the aisle in the Beach Park. The Beach Park is surrounded by the grassy fields of the Meadow and Promontory. These outdoor spaces offer prime, up-close views of the Puget Sound and islands beyond. Since the Dining Hall was renovated in 2015, it has been one of our most popular Facilities! It has a capacity is 175 with tables and chairs to accommodate 150. The Dining Hall boasts a full commercial Kitchen available for renters to add to their event at $150.00. It is perfect for midsize events of any type and has beautiful wood floors, high ceilings, and a large open deck. The small lawn out front can be used for games, ceremonies, or simple mingling during the warmer months.  
These facilities not limited to just weddings and reunions. Consider using this facility as the main stage for your next Company Event, Auction, or Meeting!
