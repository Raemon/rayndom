# The Bold Host Event Venue Powhatan, Virginia

Here at The Bold Host Event Venue, we blend excellence with affordability! We are a premier event and décor venue located in Powhatan, just minutes away from Westchester Commons and 288 off Midlothian Turnpike.  
  
We host a wide range of events and can host various occasions, themes and sizes. Choose from a variety of props, backdrops, linens, lighting, centerpieces, 360 Photo Booth, Throne Chairs, a GLAM room and more!  
  
We offer 3,000sqft of versatile space that can accommodate up to 110 guests (82 seated), ensuring your event is both spacious and spectacular! A blank canvas is awaiting your vision for you to be, The Bold Host of your next event!  
  
All events include our Lounge with Kitchenette, 2 Restrooms, Tables, Clear Chiavari Chairs, Table Linens, Ice Chest, Bluetooth Surround Sound Speaker w/ Mic, Wifi and Cleaning Service.  
  
All vendors welcome!
