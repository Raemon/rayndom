# The Domain Restaurant and Lounge Houston, Texas

![Photo of The Domain Restaurant and Lounge](/media/TX/13036/the-domain-restaurant-and-lounge-houston-tx.jpg)

The Domain is a five thousand square footage exquisite lounge and restaurant,  
With a Chic Porcelain tiles, Fuax paint wall designs, Ostrich leather chairs, Rounded granite bar, with a water-fall granite design in the middle. When sitting on the bar, upon looking at the sky, one will experience the Rolls Royce starlight engulfing the size of the bar directly. Our hand picked and well orchestrated chandelier will be the gaze of our trending clients. The Domain walls which feature a Gold arch designs with a structural panel in the middle, were handcrafted one at a time. A top notch sound system that can enhance the way you experience your music, has become one of the ways, the creators of the Domain intend to woo our clients.
