# Chateau Adare Murrieta, California

Chateau Adare is a family owned and operated private estate venue on ten acres of land. We offer several options for both ceremony and reception areas. Situated between the oak trees decorated with twinkle lights and grassy areas under market lights this outdoor venue has it all. Some amenities include:  
\- outdoor wood burning fireplace  
\- outdoor pizza oven  
\- flagstone dance floor  
\- pool, horses, tennis court  
\- newly renovated bridal suite  
\- parking on site  
\- one event per day  
\- tea garden with fountain  
\- great photo ops
