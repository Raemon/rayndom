# Dowds' Country Inn & Event Center Lyme, New Hampshire

Quaint, New Hampshire Destination in the valleys of the White Mountains. Our 1780's home hosts 20 individually appointed guest-rooms, a 200 seat banquet hall, 6 acres of lush, garden laden property, and provides full catering services. We've been providing courteous customer service for lodging guests and events of all kinds, including conferences, birthdays, banquets, and more. The Upper Valley's Premier Wedding Venue for over 26 years . Voted Best of the Best Bed &amp; Breakfast for 8 Consecutive Years!
