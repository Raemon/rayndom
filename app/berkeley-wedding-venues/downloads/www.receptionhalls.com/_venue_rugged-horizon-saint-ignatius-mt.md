# Rugged Horizon Saint Ignatius, Montana

Craftsman barn with amenities set at the base of the Mission Mountains  
Celebrate your big day in the country with the Mission Mountains as your backdrop. Our barn was built with weddings in mind, and the bridal suite is sure to take your breath away if the views don't! Enjoy a rustic barn wedding any time of year, the heated floors will keep you toasty-warm inside. Close to Missoula and Polson, so guests have no problem finding accommodations. Enjoy a stunning ceremony location in the hay meadow or front lawn, then walk right over to the barn for a good ol' fashioned reception!
