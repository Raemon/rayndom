# Hewing Hotel Minneapolis, Minnesota

Welcome to the Hewing Hotel!  
  
The century-old brick and timber warehouse in Minneapolis’ historic North Loop neighborhood is currently being reshaped into a truly unique, independent, 124-room hotel. Hewing Hotel will provide neighbors and travelers an authentic Minneapolis experience and a gathering place with unrivaled service. A cornerstone of the North Loop, the Hewing Hotel will be close to it all, but far from ordinary.  
  
Host an event with us in our stunning Andrew's Ballroom with seating for up to 176 and 250 guests for a cocktail style reception!  
  
Please inquire with us regarding a tour of our property!
