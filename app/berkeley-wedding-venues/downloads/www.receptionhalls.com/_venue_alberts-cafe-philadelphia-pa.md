# Albert's Cafe Philadelphia, Pennsylvania

![Photo of Albert's Cafe](/media/PA/496/alberts-cafe-philadelphia-pa.jpg)

Be it a corporate event or a personal party, Northeast Catering offers one of the finest banquet reception halls in Philadelphia! Even those who live in Bucks County and Bensalem, PA, choose to host their event in our reception hall! With five rooms available and space for up to 400 guests, we can accommodate any party or celebration. Popular events hosted with us include business meetings and parties; beef n’ beer events and fundraisers; funeral luncheons; baby and bridal showers; rehearsal dinners, wedding receptions, and anniversary parties; and, of course, birthday bashes!
