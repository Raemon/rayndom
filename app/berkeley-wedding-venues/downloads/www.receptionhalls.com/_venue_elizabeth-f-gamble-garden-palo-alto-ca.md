# Elizabeth F. Gamble Garden Palo Alto, California

Elizabeth F. Gamble Garden is a fabulous venue located in Palo Alto, CA, that would be ideal for a variety of wedding celebrations. Rehearsal dinners, bridal showers, and various parties can all be enjoyed at this peaceful and serene setting. Cocooned by nature and beautifully landscaped grounds, there are ample eye-catching features, colors, and arrangements to behold. Undoubtedly, it would be easy to imagine enjoying a wedding brunch here as everyone regales each other with tales of the previous day’s reception revelries.  
  
These outstanding premises are named after Elizabeth Frances Gamble whose grandfather was one half of the famous Procter & Gamble entrepreneurship. The entire property started to take shape in the early 20th century when the main building was constructed before the gardens were landscaped. Ultimately, it became a public estate in 1985 and was lovingly tended to and invested in over the years. Today, it combines historic features with up-to-date additions that lend it great appeal for those who are planning a variety of wedding-related events. Owing to the ample outdoor gathering spots, it is sure to please those planning al fresco occasions or open-air events. Indoors, too, there is a great deal of sophisticated decor and design that surely does justice to all manner of high-end get-togethers. These would include wedding showers, private celebrations, or pre-ceremony soirees.  
  
A variety of function areas are available to enjoy at Elizabeth F. Gamble Garden. The Carriage House comes with a kitchen space where culinary delights can easily be prepared in style. Out on the accompanying patio, hors d’oeuvres and cocktails can be savored by the couples and their invitees as everyone sinks into the enjoyment of the day. In addition, the Tea House and adjoining patio are also part of the property along with the two Wisteria and Rose Gardens.
