# The Asheville Masonic Temple Asheville, North Carolina

An exceptional event venue, The Asheville Masonic Temple (AMT) is located in the heart of downtown Asheville. The historic building is within walking distance of all of the downtown hotels as well as Asheville’s amazing collection of shops, restaurants, breweries and attractions. Providing a collection of exceptional venue spaces on several levels the AMT is a truly spectacular venue. You and your guests will have an extraordinary experience in this historic building designed by the noted architect, Richard Shape Smith more than a century ago. We can also provide all-inclusive packages so you do not have to worry about a thing! With so many options for entertaining and staging you can create an experience of a lifetime!
