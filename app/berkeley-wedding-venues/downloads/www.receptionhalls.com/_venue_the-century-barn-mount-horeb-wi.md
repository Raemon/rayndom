# The Century Barn Mount Horeb, Wisconsin

The Century Barn is an event venue located just outside Mt Horeb, Wisconsin! The barn sits atop a rolling hill, overlooking a picturesque landscape of open fields, a fragrant hops garden, with the hazy Blue Mounds on the horizon and gorgeous sunsets. This setting is an impressive and versatile environment for any occasion!  
  
Our venue is a D-I-Y rentable space, so you are able to create the celebration you desire! Set-up where you decide, bring your favorite food &amp; drinks for your guests, add your unique ideas, the possibilities are endless! We always have open vending, so any caterer can be used! Even food carts frequent our venue! We also have hi-speed Wi-Fi, and many rental items including a Smart TV! If you have any questions, let us know!
