# Alpine Lakes, Enchantments, Lyman Glacier Lake Stevens, Washington

[ Please Call ](tel:4252584141)

Thank you for your interest in our banquet rooms. We have 3 spacious meeting areas available for rent. The first is a classroom style room called Lyman Glacier with a seating capacity of 40. We then have our Alpine Lakes room and our Enchantments room, both 841 square feet with a maximum capacity of 75. These two rooms combine to create our ballroom which is 1700 square feet with a maximum capacity of 160.   
Please contact us with any further inquiries, we are excited to be able to serve you.
