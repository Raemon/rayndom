# Tago Center Atlanta, Georgia

Tago Center is a new modern style venue, we cater to the generation that is not looking for your typical ballroom scene. Tago Center is located in heart of Atlanta, close to Top Golf in Midtown off of Howell mill rd. Our space is uniquely designed and can be recreated with the right decor. If you want to give your guests and yourself a unique and new experience that will be remembered for every, come and visit us at the Tago Center. We cater all types of occasions, and insure a memorable event.
