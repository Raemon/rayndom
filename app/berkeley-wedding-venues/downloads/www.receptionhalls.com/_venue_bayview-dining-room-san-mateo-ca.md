# Bayview Dining Room San Mateo, California

Sitting at the top of Hillsdale Boulevard in the College Center on CSM's gorgeous campus, the Bayview Dining Room features floor to ceiling windows, opening up the  
space to copious natural light; in addition to striking views of the San Francisco Bay Area and its surrounding mountain ranges.  
  
Our unique location creates a beautiful setting for all types of weekend functions. This versatile event space also includes option breakout rooms upstairs for conference events, as well as in house catering, and AV resources for added convenience.
