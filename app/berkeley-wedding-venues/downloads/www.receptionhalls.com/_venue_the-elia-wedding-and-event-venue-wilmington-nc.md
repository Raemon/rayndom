# The Elia - Wedding and Event Venue Wilmington, North Carolina

The Elia is a Wilmington NC wedding venue and events space located in the heart of Downtown Wilmington. We are the only venue in the city that offers an “All-Inclusive Rental Package” which saves clients time, money and streamlines the planning process. With over 7,000 square feet of an open floor plan layout, The Elia can host up to 175 guests, making it one of Downtown Wilmington's largest event venues.
