# The Fillmore San Francisco, California

Experience the color and energy of the Fillmore’s extraordinary past. Walls and tables are covered with a dazzling display of posters, tickets, handbills, newspaper articles and gorgeous  
original artwork from hundreds of past events that have taken place within this nostalgic location. You won’t find an exhibition like this anywhere else in the world, which makes this gallery a truly one-of-a-kind place to hold an outstanding event whether it’s for 100 or 1100. After nearly a century in the entertainment business, The Fillmore still has the power to wow your audience!
