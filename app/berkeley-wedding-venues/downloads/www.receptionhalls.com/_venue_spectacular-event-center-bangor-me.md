# Spectacular Event Center Bangor, Maine

The Spectacular Event Center &amp; Catering Company is a special event facility accommodating 25-300 guests. We offer three ballrooms to host your wedding reception as well as a beautiful outdoor garden gazebo for your ceremony.  
  
Spectacular Event Center &amp; Catering provides all of your food services for any type of event. Looking for a caterer to go off-site? We can do that for you; please contact us for more information. We own all of the necessary equipment to deliver a perfect meal to your guests.  
  
Spectacular Event Center has been in business for over 12 years, serving high quality food at affordable prices and providing nothing but the best customer service.
