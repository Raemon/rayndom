# Brianza Gardens and Winery Crittenden, Kentucky

Brianza Gardens and Winery is the Premier Intimate Wedding and Event location. Brianza is a little piece of Italy nestled in the hills of northern Kentucky offering outdoor and indoor receptions sites. We can accommodate parties from 8 to 70. Private tastings for up to 25. Brianza is easy to find, less than 3 miles off of Interstate 75. With your event includes use of the grounds and gardens for your guests and scenic vineyard views. Open to the public for wine tastings Saturdays 11 to 8
