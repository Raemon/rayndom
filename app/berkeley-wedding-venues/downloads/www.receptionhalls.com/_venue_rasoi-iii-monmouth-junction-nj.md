# Rasoi III Monmouth Junction, New Jersey

![Photo of Rasoi III](/media/NJ/226/rasoi-iii-monmouth-junction-nj.jpg)

Welcome to Rasoi and thank you for your interest in our catering facilities. Our banquet facilities offer contemporary elegance at its best, with four acres of beautiful outdoor botanical gardens (gazebo and waterfalls, and indoor and outdoor ceremony sites). With impeccable service and exquisite cuisine, we strive to make your special occasion an unforgettable event.  
  
Visit our glass enclosed Garden Room, overlooking 4 acres of beautiful landscaped gardens. It features an extra large dance floor and ceiling to floor windows and can accommodate up to 500 people. Or be enchanted by the winding Mahogany staircase, large dance floor, and skylights that create a spectacular setting for our elegant Rasoi Room. It can accommodate up to 210 people.
