# Sheraton Seattle Hotel Seattle, Washington

Your ideal wedding awaits at Sheraton Seattle Hotel. Whether you’re planning an intimate gathering or a grand celebration, our wedding specialists will see to every detail with expertise and care – leaving you free to revel in your big day. Select from a range of stunning venues: the inviting 9,440 square-foot Metropolitan Ballroom; the spacious 18,300 square-foot Grand Ballroom; the magnificent 3,400 square-foot Cirrus Ballroom, a 35th-floor gem with floor-to-ceiling windows that offer stunning views of Elliot Bay, the Olympic Mountains and the downtown cityscape.
