# Sandstone Vineyards Kuna, Idaho

Welcome to Sandstone Vineyards, a Beautiful vineyard and garden venue for a small or large group with tent available for year round availability. Indoor rooms and restrooms, walking paths, pools, Koirala ponds, orchard and beautiful views of the mountainsame. Close to freeway, airport and hotels. Outside caterers allowed and alcohol friendly.
