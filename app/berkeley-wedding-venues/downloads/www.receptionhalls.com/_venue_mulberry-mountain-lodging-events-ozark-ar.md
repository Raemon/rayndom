# Mulberry Mountain Lodging & Events Ozark, Arkansas

![Photo of Mulberry Mountain Lodging & Events](/media/AR/10587/mulberry-mountain-lodging-events-ozark-ar.jpg)

Mulberry Mountain offers 6,000 sq. ft. of meeting and reception space in our newly constructed mountain top convention hall.   
  
This beautiful Ozark Mountain setting provides the perfect atmosphere for all most any occasion. Whether it's a family reunion, company retreat, or that special wedding day, Mulberry Mountain can help you create an unforgettable experience.   
  
Large cedar beams and an oversized native stone fireplace create a cozy lodge setting in this spacious hall. Call or email us today to start planning your next or gathering at Mulberry Mountain (866) 667-1919.
