# Edmonds Yacht Club Edmonds, Washington

The perfect setting to celebrate &amp; connect! Our impressive waterfront facility features a large banquet hall and outdoor patio with spectacular views of Puget Sound. Inside you'll find a fully equipped kitchen, bar, and audio visual equipment. Tables, chairs, dinnerware, silverware, drinkware and complimentary coffee/tea supplies are included with your rental fee. You may select your own caterer and there is plenty of free parking. Edmonds Yacht Club is amazing, affordable, and perfect for your event. Please call us to schedule your personal tour.
