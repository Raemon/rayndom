# The Newark Club Newark, New Jersey

[ Please Call ](tel:973-242-0658)

![Photo of The Newark Club](/media/NJ/234/the-newark-club-newark-nj.jpg)

Located on the 22nd floor of One Newark Center and adjacent to Newark's Celebrated Performing Arts Complex, the Newark Club is New Jersey's newest and most spectacular ballroom setting, creating a mood of elegance and sophistication for any occasion.  
  
Affairs at the Newark Club are hosted one party at a time and are made more memorable by a cocktail hour featuring award-winning cuisine, a famous silver service champagne and wine bar, and hors d'oeuvres stations embellished with magnificent antiques, estate pieces and candelabras. Adding to the experience is an expansive, artistically designed inlaid dance floor, comfortable arm chairs for all guests and a dramatic wall of windows to capture the panoramic views of the New York skyline.  
  
Convenient and secure indoor valet parking assures that the Newark Club offers your guests comfort and convenience form start to finish.  
  
Whether your affair is for 100 or 350 guests, the Newark Club promises to take your breath away with New Jersey's most extraordinary catering experience.
