# CaliPaso Winery Paso Robles, California

CaliPaso Winery (Villa Toscana) is located on California's beautiful central coast in Paso Robles. The elegant architecture, soothing colors, and attention to detail appeal to even the most discriminating tastes. Celebrity guests compare the setting of Villa Toscana to Tuscany, with good reason. Italian architecture conjures images of umber tones, sweeping classic lines, arches, wrought iron gates and stone floors. This would be the perfect and most romantic place to have your dream wedding!
