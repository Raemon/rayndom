# The Atlantic Tullahoma, Tennessee

The Atlantic is a glamourous Art Deco inspired wedding venue and corporate event center located in Tullahoma, Tennessee. The historic building constructed in 1940 underwent full restoration in 2021. The ballroom can accomidate 288 guests and features polished black concrete floors, exposed brick walls, and art deco lighting, making it the perfect location for a elegant wedding or corporate event.
