# Michigan Flywheelers Museum South Haven, Michigan

Rustic or elegant - the Michigan Flywheelers Museum can be the perfect place to celebrate weddings, company get-togethers, milestone birthdays and other important life events.  
STEPHENSON BARN  
Our restored 1900s post and beam barn is the perfect place for a rustic or elegant event. An addition to the barn includes bathrooms and a warming kitchen. Rental includes chairs, tables and trash disposal. $2,500 per event.   
  
PEACEFUL KNOLL CHURCH  
Our 1920s country-style church, which is located in Old Towne. It seats approximately 45 people. $200 per event.  
  
Other features include a log cabin, Over The Hill Forge, Old Tyme Jail and plenty of beautiful places to hold your ceremony or take pictures.
