# Happily Ever After Farms Flag Pond, Tennessee

![Photo of Happily Ever After Farms](/media/TN/10665/happily-ever-after-farms-flag-pond-tn.jpg)

Are you planning a destination wedding or looking for elopement package?   
  
Look no further!  
  
Happily Ever After Farms provides a gorgeous Great Smoky Mountain Wedding backdrop for couples seeking a small destination wedding or elopement package. Located in Flag Pond, Tennessee (population 300), just 35 minutes from Asheville and Johnson City, Happily Ever After Farms is the perfect backdrop for your event, offering everything from elopements, petite weddings,rehearsal dinners and weddings up to 100 guests. Providing over 80 acres of privacy to our couples and their special event, our elopement and wedding packages offer the Bride and Groom the opportunity to enjoy their special day worry free.   
  
Let us help take the stress out of planning, contact our Event Coordinator to book your destination wedding or elopement package today!  
  
Or if your are just wanting to get away from the hustle and bustle of life, our Bed and Breakfast, "Tree House", is available for nightly and weekly rentals.
