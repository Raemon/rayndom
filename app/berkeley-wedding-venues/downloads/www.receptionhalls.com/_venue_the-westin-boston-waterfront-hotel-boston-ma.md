# The Westin Boston Waterfront Hotel Boston, Massachusetts

![Photo of The Westin Boston Waterfront Hotel](/media/MA/10969/the-westin-boston-waterfront-hotel-boston-ma.jpg)

Our Grand Ballroom is an elegant Boston wedding venue and can accommodate up to 1,080 guests. Perfectly appointed, the Grand Ballroom provides the ultimate ambiance for your wedding. May it be a ceremony on the garden terrace or cocktails in the Grand Foyer, our decadent cuisine and full service staff are dedicated to making your day a dream come true.
