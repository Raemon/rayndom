# River Chase Harpers Ferry, West Virginia

River Chase is a family-owned and operated historic wedding and event venue in Harpers Ferry, WV. River Chase provides the perfect backdrop for weddings, celebrations, and photography sessions with breathtaking views of the Blue Ridge Mountains and direct access to the Potomac River.  
  
River Chase is situated on 9-acres and includes…  
  
1870s historic barn  
  
Sweeping views of the Potomac River and the Blue Ridge Mountains  
  
Infinity Lawn  
  
Rathskeller (bar under the barn)  
  
Conservatory  
  
Bridal suite  
  
Groomsmen room  
  
Large deck and terrace
