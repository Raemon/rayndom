# Columbus Hall & Banquet Center Dexter, Michigan

Our motto, “The perfect place for your event,” Let the Columbus Hall and Banquet Center help you make your special occasion a memorable one. The Columbus HBC provides a venue that gives you an opportunity to fashion a space to compliment your event, where your creative ideas can come to life. Wedding receptions are the majority of rentals, but the space can also be used for wedding ceremonies, birthday parties, graduation parties, fundraisers, off-site business luncheons and/or meetings, craft show, etc. – the opportunities are endless. We offer a private, worry-free, comfortable establishment. There is convenient, lighted parking right outside the front door and the building is accessible to all.
