# Events at Sundance Tamassee, South Carolina

Events at Sundance is a beautiful country venue set in the foothills of upstate South Carolina. We have gardens, birdbaths, archways, firepit, 25x50 terrace, bridal room(decorated in your colors), 1/2 acre open field for games, beautiful backdrops, antique couch for pictures, choice of archways, accents such as decorated mason jars, old barn as backdrop, mountan veiws, old picture frames, antique dresser, and more.  
We also have horse and buggy rides for the bride and groom and pony rides for the kids parties. we have catering, cake baker, photographer, sound system, all available upon booking.
