# Tavern+Bowl Chula Vista, California

![Photo of Tavern+Bowl](/media/CA/11090/tavernbowl-chula-vista-ca.jpg)

Located in community of Eastlake, Tavern+Bowl features a gourmet tavern menu that's still affordable, great happy hours, daily specials, tons of event space and the best bowling around! Whether catching your favorite team, grabbing a quick bite for lunch, taking a load off at happy hour, or hosting an unforgettable party, Tavern+Bowl is where you let the good times roll!Tavern+Bowl locations are perfect for hosting events whether a birthday party, school, or corporate event, and we have an events team ready to make planning easy.
