# Davis Event Space Harrisonville, Missouri

Davis Event Space offers you multiple ways to celebrate your happily ever after! Our beautiful, historic wedding venue is one of the most affordable venues available in the Kansas City Metro area. We can accommodate both larger weddings without being overly crowded or smaller weddings wanting an intimate feel. Our wedding packages include full use of our entire venue (Davis Ballroom and Harlan Loft) and we help you plan and create the atmosphere you are wanting for your wedding day.  
  
Davis Event Space is not only perfect as a wedding venue, but for celebrations and parties of any type, and is versatile enough to be a great setting for corporate meetings, office parties, proms, reunions, holiday events, and many more. With multiple features such as exposed brick, bistro lighting, custom-designed lighting fixtures, a grand staircase, a custom-built bar, and much more, you will want to see this stunning venue for yourself.
