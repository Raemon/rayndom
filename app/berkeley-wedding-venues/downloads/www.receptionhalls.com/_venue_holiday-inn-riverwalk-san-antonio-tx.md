# Holiday Inn Riverwalk San Antonio, Texas

We are known as The Flagship Holiday Inn for IHG as we are the only Holiday Inn owned and managed by IHG!   
  
Located in the heart of downtown and directly on the banks of the famous Riverwalk, Holiday Inn Riverwalk is a contemporary hotel that offers the ultimate travel experience. Our contemporary hotel features a warm atmosphere with modern design features.  
  
Surrounded by world-class dining, entertainment, professional sports arenas and family- friendly attractions including: The Alamo, SeaWorld, Six Flags, Alamodome, and in the theatre district.  
  
We will be sure to keep drawing you in by offering spectacular views, delectable culinary options, and exceptional hospitality! Whether you are taking in the scenery of the Riverwalk, visiting the historical landmarks of the city, partaking in cultural activities in La Villita, or lounging on a patio, San Antonio is a great destination to have fun and explore!   
We’re a hotel who specializes in groups and meetings to ensure your guests have a one of a kind experience in Downtown San Antonio, and a successful event.   
If you have a minute or two to learn more about us, you can visit us here at www.hiriverwalk.com.
