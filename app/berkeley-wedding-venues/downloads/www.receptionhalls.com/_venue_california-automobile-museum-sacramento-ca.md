# California Automobile Museum Sacramento, California

Tired of the same old boring venues? Have your event at the California Automobile Museum and give your guests something extra! We have over 130 cars on display that are available for viewing during your event.  
  
Our main hall boasts high ceilings and ambient lighting perfect for any occasion. Host your next Holiday Party, Gala, Sweet 16, Wedding Reception, Quinceanera, Corporate Meeting or Prom--we'll bring the classics! Seating up to 350 persons.  
  
Our Education center is a private, climate-controlled room perfect for educational classes, sales meetings, private receptions--you name it. Holds 72 guests with tables, 90 "Classroom Style" and 100 "Cocktail Style".  
  
Price dependent on room rental, number of people, day of the week.
