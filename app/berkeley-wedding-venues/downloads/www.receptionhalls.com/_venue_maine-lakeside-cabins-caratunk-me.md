# Maine Lakeside Cabins Caratunk, Maine

Maine Lakeside Cabins &amp; Event Center is nestled directly on the shore of Wyman Lake. The complex includes six modern lakeside cabins, 4 luxurious guest houses, and the main Lodge. The Lodge can accommodate up 125 people and its private lakeside setting specifically caters to events that prefer exclusive use of the entire complex. Have your event outside along the banks of Wyman Lake. There is a field located right along the lake providing breath taking views and space for a rental tent should there be a need for parties of over 125 guests. There is also another indoor location, the Pavilion that can accommodate up to 80 guests.
