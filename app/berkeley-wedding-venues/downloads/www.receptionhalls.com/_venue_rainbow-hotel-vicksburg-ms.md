# Rainbow Hotel Vicksburg, Mississippi

![Photo of Rainbow Hotel](/media/MS/10317/rainbow-hotel-vicksburg-ms.jpg)

Rainbow Hotel Casino 1350 Warrenton Road Phone: 601-638-7111 Fax: 601-630-9046 Capacity: 20-500 Rainbow features two event areas for varying party sizes. Our Rainbow Arena is very reasonably priced and great for receptions or ceremony and reception combinations from 150-500 people. Located outside the main casino building, the Arena features easy access and generous parking. The River Room is our smaller meeting/event room. It is located inside the casino building adjacent to the buffet and is great for showers, rehearsal dinners or small receptions up to approximately 100 people. The hotel itself has king, queen, double and suite availability. It also has complimentary breakfast, in-room coffee makers, in-room safes, iron/ironing board, hairdryers, in-room microwave, in-room fridge, AM/FM clock radios, 37" remote control TV, data port phones, whirlpools in our suites, free wireless high speed internet. In addition to those items the hotel also has pet friendly rooms, indoor heated pool &amp; jacuzzi, exercise facilities, non-smoking rooms are available.
