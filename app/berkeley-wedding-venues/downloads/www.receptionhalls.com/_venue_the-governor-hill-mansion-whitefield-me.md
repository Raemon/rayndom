# The Governor Hill Mansion Whitefield, Maine

Enjoy your special day in one of Maine's most beautiful, stately homes. The Governor Hill Mansion provides a unique setting for all kinds of occasions. We offer quality and affordable catering and a flexible or custom menu. Free on-site parking, no hidden fees. The grounds of the mansion make for beautiful and elegant photographs.
