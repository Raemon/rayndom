# Minnesota National Golf Course Mcgregor, Minnesota

![Photo of Minnesota National Golf Course](/media/MN/9696/minnesota-national-golf-course-mcgregor-mn.jpg)

Our experienced staff will help you plan and host your special events in any of three indoor and outdoor venues. Choose from three Event Centers for your reception in our new log framed Club House while enjoying the view of our 27 hole championship golf course. The Gatehouse restaurant and Irish Pub are open daily.
