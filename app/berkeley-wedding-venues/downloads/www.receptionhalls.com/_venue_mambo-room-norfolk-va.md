# Mambo Room Norfolk, Virginia

The Crystal Ballroom is a unique hidden treasure located inside the Mambo Room Cultural Event Center in the Norfolk Ghent area within the Coastal Virginia region offering an exquisite setting for any special day.  
  
What sets us apart from others, aside from the beauty, is that we love what we do!  
  
Our venue is elegant but still maintains that distinct Mambo Room charm, making you feel right at home.  
  
It's the perfect place for you to hold your next special private event or party.  
  
Our beautiful space can be tailored to your needs and we offer reasonable pricing to meet your budget.
