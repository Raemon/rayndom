# Artmore Hotel Atlanta, Georgia

The Artmore has a variety of meeting, event and function space to cater to your every need. Outdoors, the Courtyard or the Cocktail Garden can accommodate weddings, receptions, rehearsal dinners, you name it and we’ll make it happen. At night your event becomes quite dramatic as the fire pit and water feature add an element your guests won’t soon forget.
