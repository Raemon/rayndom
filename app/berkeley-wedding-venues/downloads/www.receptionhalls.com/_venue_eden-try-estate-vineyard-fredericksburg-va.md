# Eden Try Estate & Vineyard Fredericksburg, Virginia

12 magnificent acres of naturally beautiful estate grounds, formal gardens, vineyard barn reception hall, manicured lawns, cascading fountains and vineyard/winery create a uniquely romantic backdrop for your event that will impress your guests.   
  
The Estate is yours for the day because we hold only one catered event per day.   
  
Great location for the convenience of your guests! Less than four miles from hotels, restaurants, and major shopping. Only six miles from Interstate I-95 and Fredericksburg, Virginia’s, historic district.
