# Buckley's Caterers Brooklyn, New York

![Photo of Buckley's Caterers](/media/NY/35/buckleys-caterers-brooklyn-ny.jpg)

Combining the very best of food and service with a total dedication toward customer satisfaction is a tradition at Buckley's. Come in, visit with them, taste their food and see why you will be treated as a special guest by this family-owned and operated establishment.  
  
Buckley's holds only one reception at a time in order to give you their full attention and dedication. The Buckley Family knows how important your special event is to you. In addition to a trained and attentive professional waitstaff , a personal maitre d' assigned to the bride and groom will satisfy your every need and desire.
