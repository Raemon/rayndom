# Giorgios at Fox Hill Calverton, New York

Our wedding and banquet facilities are available 7 days weekly for private and corporate functions. Our newly decorated banquet room accommodates up to 400 guests. Small or large bookings welcome.  
  
We boast * Panoramic Long Island Views * A choice of location for cocktail hour - either our outdoor terrace with magnificent views or our separate large indoor cocktail room * Valet Parking * White glove French service * An outdoor garden with gazebo facing the golf course for photos & ceremonies * A Spacious & Beautifully appointed Bridal Suite with terrace * Special Rates for non-profit organizations  
  
We handle your special occasions in a warm and professional manner (one party at a time) offering you the finest service available on the East End.
