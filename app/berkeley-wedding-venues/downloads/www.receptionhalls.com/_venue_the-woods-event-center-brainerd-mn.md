# THE WOODS EVENT CENTER Brainerd, Minnesota

![Photo of THE WOODS EVENT CENTER](/media/MN/12191/the-woods-event-center-brainerd-mn.jpg)

Nestled in THE WOODS is the premier wedding/conference/all celebration events venue in the Brainerd Lakes Area featuring the most popular tend to date. Vintage rustic, nature inspired decor with limitless whimsical themes.   
We bring the rustic inside our event center featuring a full kitchen, bar and ballroom suited for celebrating.
