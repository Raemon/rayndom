# Scott's Jack London Square Oakland, California

Scott's offers an exceptional dining experience for on-site events in any of our six private spaces overlooking the Oakland Estuary and San Francisco skyline. From grand ballrooms to elegant dining rooms, our venue encompasses the perfect backdrop for your special event. We specialize in curating a wide range of elegant events and occasions, including corporate meetings & events, wedding celebrations, non-profit fundraisers & galas, birthday celebrations, graduation celebrations, Bar & Bat Mitzvahs, celebrations of life, and more.
