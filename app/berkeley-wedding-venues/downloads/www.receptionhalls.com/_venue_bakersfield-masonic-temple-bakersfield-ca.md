# Bakersfield Masonic Temple Bakersfield, California

[ Please Call ](tel:661-322-3848)

![Photo of Bakersfield Masonic Temple](/media/CA/10992/bakersfield-masonic-temple-bakersfield-ca.jpg)

Our venue allows for your Wedding and Reception all under one roof. The best kept secret in Bakersfield is our beautiful Vintage Theater on the 3rd floor. Afterwards its a quick trip down the elevator to the Reception Hall where the celebration begins!   
  
Rentals are available for weddings, receptions, birthday parties, anniversaries, quinceaneras, memorials, and funerals. For information or appointments please call 322-3848.
