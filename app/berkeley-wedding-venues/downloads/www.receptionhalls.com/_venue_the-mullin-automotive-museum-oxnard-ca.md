# The Mullin Automotive Museum Oxnard, California

![Photo of The Mullin Automotive Museum](/media/CA/12390/the-mullin-automotive-museum-oxnard-ca.jpg)

The Mullin Automotive Museum is a world class automotive and art deco museum featuring some of the most beautiful cars in the world. Not just for "car people," the museum features art deco sculpture and art, including artistic works by the Bugatti family, providing eye candy at every turn. Host an elegant affair in a space that will impress you and your guests.   
  
The museum building features a main showroom, theater and mezzanine, all of which can be prepared in style for your special day. Give your guests a first class experience in a world class atmosphere.
