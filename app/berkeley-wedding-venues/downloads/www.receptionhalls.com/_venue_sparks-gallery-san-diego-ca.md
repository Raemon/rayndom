# Sparks Gallery San Diego, California

Sparks Gallery showcases collaborative exhibitions curated exhibitions by the gallery owner, and solo exhibitions. With a focus on surreal and impressionistic artwork, the gallery curates towards emotive works in themes ranging from portraiture and figurative to landscape and conceptual.
