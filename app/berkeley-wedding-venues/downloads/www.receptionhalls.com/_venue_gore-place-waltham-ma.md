# Gore Place Waltham, Massachusetts

Gore Place is one of the finest country estates in New England that remain from the Federal era.It is available for social and corporate events including outdoor wedding ceremonies and receptions, private parties, bar &amp; bat mitzvahs, holiday parties and company outings.  
  
The Estate's 45 acres of gardens, lawns and farmland provide a beautiful setting for your tented reception from the smallest to the largest of gatherings. Gore Place is conveniently located nine miles outside Boston and a short drive to the local Waltham and Newton hotels. This historic country landscape provides a pastoral peaceful setting for any occasion.  
  
Surrounded by large evergreens and magnificent hardwoods, the landscaped historic Mansion park area makes a unique setting for all types of gatherings. Gore Place maintains an elegant three season tent on the grounds available from May through October and a newly renovated historic 1793 Carriage House- equipped with A/V, AC, and free WiFi.
