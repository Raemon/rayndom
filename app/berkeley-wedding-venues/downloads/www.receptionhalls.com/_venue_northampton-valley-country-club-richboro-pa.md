# Northampton Valley Country Club Richboro, Pennsylvania

Northampton Valley Country Club, located in Bucks County, Pennsylvania is one of the ideal wedding locations for your unforgettable wedding reception and outdoor wedding ceremony. Surrounded by 127 acres in the rolling countryside of Bucks County, Pennsylvania, Northampton Valley Country Club provides a one of a kind wedding site to host both your indoor or outdoor wedding ceremony and wedding reception. We are one of the most well known wedding reception sites in Bucks County and the surrounding Philadelphia area. As you approach the country club, driving down our famous tree-lined drive, you will begin to view our beautifully manicured grounds and see why we have the ideal wedding location for an outdoor wedding ceremony. The breathtaking views, sophisticated elegance and inviting warmth will provide you with a truly elegant setting and a wedding day to cherish and remember forever.  
  
Northampton Valley Country Club is unique from other wedding locations in the Philadelphia area and Bucks County because we provide two diverse, outdoor wedding ceremony areas and two well dressed reception venues. The Crystal Ballroom has an elegant, traditional feel, while the Northampton Ballroom has a chic, sophisticated, urban vibe. Northampton Valley Country Club, in Bucks County, provides you with the option of a smaller, intimate wedding ceremony and wedding reception of 50 guests to a grand wedding ceremony and wedding reception of 280 guests. Both of our wedding Ballrooms feature beautiful panoramic views of the golf course.
