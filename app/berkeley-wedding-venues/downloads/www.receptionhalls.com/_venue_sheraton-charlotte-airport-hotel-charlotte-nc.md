Sheraton Charlotte Airport Hotel wants to help you plan the most important event in your life, your wedding day. Ideal for wedding venues in Charlotte NC, our upscale boutique hotel boasts intimate surroundings, classic hospitality and elegant function space, which will cater to the most discriminating taste. Our service is friendly and attentive, yet nonintrusive. This most extraordinary day will be made even more non-stressful through our expert consulting We would be delighted to place our facility at your disposal, providing the perfect setting for an intimate Ceremony and Reception, while working within the limits of your budget.
