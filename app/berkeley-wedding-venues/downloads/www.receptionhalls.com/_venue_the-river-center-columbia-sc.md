# The River Center Columbia, South Carolina

The River Center offers all the amenities necessary for corporate meetings, receptions, weddings, reunions and private parties. With over 5,000 square feet of flexible meeting space, the River Center can be subdivided into as many as five rooms allowing you to select the exact size and configuration that best suits your group. Glass doors in each meeting room open onto patios overlooking dense woods for a picturesque view. Each meeting room in the spacious venue is equipped with a drop down screens, projector, sound system, and Internet access. Tables and chairs are included with your rental as well as the parking fee.  
  
Nestled into Saluda Shoals Park, the River Center provides a feeling of seclusion but it is easily accessed by Bush River Road, St. Andrews Road, Lake Murray Boulevard, Harbison Boulevard or Piney Grove Road. If you are in search of a meeting facility that offers a unique atmosphere and an escape from the hustle and bustle of everyday, consider The River Center.
