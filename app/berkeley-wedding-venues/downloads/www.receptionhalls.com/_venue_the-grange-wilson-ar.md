# The Grange Wilson, Arkansas

Surrounded by luscious gardens, the Grange at Wilson Gardens is the perfect choice for your special day. The Grange offers a truly unique space, complimented by beautiful landscaping and gardens. The versatility and endless setup options allow for both wedding and reception to take place all in one space. Our staff is eager to help you create an unparalleled wedding experience, filled with cherished memories of your big day.
