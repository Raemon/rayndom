# Harbor View Loft San Diego, California

Harbor View Loft is located on San Diego's breathtaking Harbor Island, with expansive views of the Harbor, Marina, Coronado Bridge, and twinkling city lights. Our property is all about the views; it features a wraparound balcony, rooftop ceremony/cocktail hour area, and panoramic windows. As the sun sets, you can take in the beauty of America's Finest City. We offer All-Inclusive Menu and Bar Packages to make your planning easy! Host your wedding, private event, or corporate function in our amazing event space, with all-inclusive options, and weekday and weekend dates available.
