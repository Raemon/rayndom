# The University Club Shreveport, Louisiana

For over 30 years, the University Club has been exceeding our guests’ needs with first class food and priceless service. The University Club offers the most exquisite panoramic views of Shreveport–Bossier. The Club has meeting space ideal for any size function. Whether you are looking to host your holiday affair, book your business luncheon needs, or searching for that breath taking place to have your wedding reception, the University Club is ready to accommodate you!   
If you are looking to impress your most important clients or treat your sweetheart to a romantic dinner above the city, the University Club is the perfect place!
