# Incubate Gallery Birmingham, Alabama

Looking for a venue space to hold a special event? Then look no further, Incubate Gallery has the spot you need. We provide a smaller, more intimate venue with the ambiance of local art as your backdrop. Whether it’s a rehearsal dinner (we can seat up to 80 guests) or a meeting/seminar (up to 120 guests – chairs only) or just an old fashioned walk-about meet and greet (up to 200 guests), we are here for you. Come join us and see what all the excitement and buzz is about in Avondale! Located on 41st Street South by Avondale Brewing Company, 41st Street Pub &amp; Aircraft sales, Freshfully and Saw’s Soul Kitchen. Don’t delay, have your event planner call us today! Incubate Gallery is a nonalcoholic facility.
