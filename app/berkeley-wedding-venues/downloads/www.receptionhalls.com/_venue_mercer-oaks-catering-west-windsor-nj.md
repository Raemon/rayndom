# Mercer Oaks Catering West Windsor, New Jersey

Mercer Oaks Catering is an owner operated full service catering company that prides itself on a commitment to excellence, attention to detail and the ability to create unique and memorable dining experiences.  
  
Known for our outstanding food style and unsurpassed event planning, we deliver on our unmatched ability to surpass your expectations for any occasion. We attribute our success to our consistent high quality and professionalism, both in cuisine and service.  
  
With exclusive catering rights at Mercer Oaks Golf Course, our management team has assembled an impressive culinary and service staff poised to address your every need. Our relationships with vendors such as florists, DJ’S, bakeries, photographers and hotels enable you to address all your event needs in a smooth and effortless fashion. We are also experts at assisting our clients with venue recommendations, menu design, equipment rentals, and contract services…everything necessary to produce an unforgettable event where no detail is ever overlooked.  
  
Whether you are planning a wedding, picnic, corporate event, golf outing, holiday party or a Bar/Bat Mitzvah, Mercer Oaks provides:  
  
Customizable our standard menus to tantalize any food taste  
Event planners who leave no details to chance  
Courteous and friendly servers  
The ability to accommodate large or small events  
Let us make your next event a most memorable one!
