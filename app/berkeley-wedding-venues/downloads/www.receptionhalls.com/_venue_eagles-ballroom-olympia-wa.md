# Eagles Ballroom Olympia, Washington

![Photo of Eagles Ballroom](/media/WA/10559/eagles-ballroom-olympia-wa.jpg)

Olympia's largest ballroom with a 4000 sq. ft. wooden dance floor. Elegant setting for weddings, reunions conferences and events. Ballroom accommodates 200. We have free on-site parking.
