# Hyatt Regency Mission Bay Spa and Marina San Diego, California

Celebrate the love you cherish at Hyatt Regency Mission Bay Spa and Marina – San Diego. A unique wedding site, we proudly offer one of the most scenic locations in Southern California. A Mission Bay wedding will have the bride beaming and the guests staying to enjoy the surroundings until the sun sets over the Pacific Ocean.  
  
So after you say "yes," consider our proposal to help create one of the most memorable days of your life. Enjoy the benefits of our landmark waterfront hotel, which captures the spirit of the bay and marina with a contemporary maritime theme. Our catering professionals will assist you with customized ceremony and reception packages that will reflect your personal style and preferences. Everything you need for an unforgettable celebration is all here for you.
