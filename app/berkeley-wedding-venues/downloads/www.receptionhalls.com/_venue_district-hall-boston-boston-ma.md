# District Hall Boston Boston, Massachusetts

Hello! Thank you for visiting District Hall Boston! District Hall is the world’s first free-standing public innovation center. We are the living room of Boston’s innovation community: a place where people can work, exchange knowledge, build meaningful relationships, and make their ideas happen.   
  
District Hall provides companies and their employees a welcoming, safe and inclusive environment to network, brainstorm, and connect. Our variety of Pod meeting rooms are allocated for small and large groups, or if you are just looking to work individually. We also have large rooms for social events such as cocktail hours, conferences and weddings!
