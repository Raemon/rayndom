# The Olde Mill Inn & Grain House Restaurant Basking Ridge, New Jersey

The Olde Mill Inn and Grain House Restaurant are the perfect settings to host your next social event. Whether the occasion is intimate or grande, our Inn Representatives are experienced in all phases of party planning: Children, Adults, Traditional, Themed, whatever the occasion it will be truly remarkable. From the distinctively decorated appointed room right down to the well dressed tables. You and your guests will celebrate Inn Style with mouth watering culinary creations, and a detail oriented and attentive staff will open the doors for your most memorable affair to remember. All handled as if your event was our own, paying attention to even the littlest details.  
  
Each appointed room is unique in décor and leads directly to a terrace, patio or deck overlooking English Gardens and manicured lawns. See how easy and relaxing entertaining can be when planning your event Inn Style from the biggest celebrations to the most private of affairs, our Inn Representatives will diligently be by your side assisting every step of the way. The Inn can also accommodate to special dietary and Kosher catering needs.
