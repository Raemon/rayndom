# Gilford Community Church Gilford, New Hampshire

![Photo of Gilford Community Church](/media/NH/12129/gilford-community-church-gilford-nh.jpg)

Romantic, Tranquil, Picturesque  
For a truly romantic, tranquil, picturesque New England wedding, the Gilford Community Church is the perfect venue. Committed to celebrating and honoring love regardless of any religious tradition or background, we want to provide you with a service that is unique to your ideals.   
  
Located in the heart of Gilford Village, New Hampshire, couples will find the fully renovated Gilford Community Church a lovely and tranquil place to celebrate a special day they will always remember.   
Brides will enjoy our tasteful, air conditioned bridal suite with bright views of the courtyard. Guests will be comfortable in our large but still intimate sanctuary that can accommodate up to 400 people. Walk down our elegant stairway to the Event Room, the perfect space to party the night away. Food service will be a breeze with our attached professional kitchen.   
The Gilford Community Church is the perfect destination for weddings and receptions.
