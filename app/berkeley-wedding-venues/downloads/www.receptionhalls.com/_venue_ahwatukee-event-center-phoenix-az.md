# Ahwatukee Event Center Phoenix, Arizona

The Ahwatukee Event Center is in the scenic and tranquil village of Ahwatukee. It features incredible panoramic views of South Mountain Regional Park, one of the largest municipal parks in the world. With our open floor plans, family friendly location, and affordable prices, we are the perfect place to host special events for any occasion.
