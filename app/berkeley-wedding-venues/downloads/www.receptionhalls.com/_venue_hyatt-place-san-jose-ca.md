# Hyatt Place San Jose, California

![Photo of Hyatt Place](/media/CA/12528/hyatt-place-san-jose-ca.jpg)

You don’t have to plan your wedding by yourself. When you choose the Hyatt Place San Jose/Downtown hotel, you get the help you need to make sure that even the smallest detail is not overlooked. From the music to the flowers and everything in between, our experienced staff will dedicate themselves to giving you the wedding experience you’ve been dreaming about.  
  
Plus, your guests will love all of the hotel and room amenities we offer. After a fun-filled night of dancing and socializing, everyone will appreciate the comfort of our plush, oversized beds. Plus, there will be freshly brewed coffee and tea to help everyone wake up and celebrate the day after at our Coffee to Cocktails bar, featuring Seattle’s Best® coffees and premium wine, beer and cocktails.  
  
We also save you money with our group blocks, which means that you and your guests will get the best possible rates for your special day. You’ll get a customized menu, help planning every detail, all of our first-rate hotel and room amenities and unbeatable rates when you make us your wedding destination.
