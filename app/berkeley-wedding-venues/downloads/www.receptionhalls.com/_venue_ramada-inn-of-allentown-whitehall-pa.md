# Ramada Inn of Allentown Whitehall, Pennsylvania

Ramada Allentown/ Whitehall has Three different Banquet/ Function Spaces to choose from. They each have their own unique atmosphere.   
  
We are the ONLY Lehigh Valley Hotel surrounded by over 40 Restaurants including local favorites and fantastic chain restaurants all within 2 miles of the Hotel!!  
  
We offer discounted group rates for your out of town guest.  
  
We have Shooter's Bar and Grill on site as well as a bar in our Picasso Ballroom.   
  
We offer on site Catering or we allow that you may bring your own food.   
  
Come in today to see any one of our rooms!!  
  
We look forward to hosting your event!
