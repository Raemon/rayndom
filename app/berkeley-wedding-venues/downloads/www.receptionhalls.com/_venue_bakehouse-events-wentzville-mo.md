# From Scratch Bakehouse & Events Wentzville, Missouri

Our charming 110 year old house is located in historic, downtown Wentzville and offers unique possibilities both during the week and on weekends. We have capacity for 50 people inside our house and there is room for more on our large patio out back.   
You can use your own outside caterer for both food and drink, our only requirement is that you order any desserts from our onsite dessert caterer From Scratch Bakehouse! Please see our full dessert menu at www.fromscratchbakehouse.com  
Please visit our website for more info and pictures of our beautiful, little yellow house! www.bakehouseevents.com
