# Sona's Indian Cuisine Greensboro, North Carolina

Sona's Indian Cuisine is one of a kind beautiful restaurant built in 2015. It is the only fine dining Indian restaurant in the Triad. It is very big and is capable of seating large parties. We have hosted several parties (birthday, wedding, etc) and have been very successful. Exceptional Indian food is served at the parties. The space is available as a party venue for reservation. Please call for further information.
