# deck655 San Diego, California

Located just on the edge of downtown San Diego, deck655 is a versatile venue that can be customized for weddings, social functions, and corporate events. It features a spacious indoor game room, outdoor amphitheater-style seating, and sweeping views from Point Loma to Coronado. This stunning venue features additional patios with lounge furniture, built-in heating, and lighting, surround sound, and more - all to create a perfect environment for entertaining up to 350 guests.
