# JD Lewis Multipurpose Center Raleigh, North Carolina

The J.D Lewis Multipurpose Center was established in 2004 in efforts to bring the community together through enriching programs, activities, and life experiences.  
  
Our state-of-the-art center is nicely equipped with multiple Flat Screen TVs, Multimedia Projector, SMART Board, PA System with wireless microphone and Full Kitchen.  
  
Our CBC Room is a carpeted, furnished room perfect for smaller events such as weddings (ceremony and reception), business meeting and trainings, birthday celebrations, baby, and wedding showers as well as other events. The CBC Room is a 1,800 square foot space.  
  
We do work with vendors that we can refer you to but we don't restrict you to vendors you can use. We are open to your ideas to make your vision a reality.  
  
Contact us and come take a tour of our facility, You will be glad you did!
