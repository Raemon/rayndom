# The Enchanted Meadows Weston, Oregon

Nestled in the Blue Mountains just fifteen minutes above Weston, Oregon, our 40 acre site makes a beautiful setting for your outdoor wedding. This family operated venue has a 30x40 wedding building, a wedding path that leads you to a unique alter. A 15x20 outdoor dance floor, where you can dance under the twinkling stars. Please visit our website for more information. www.theenchantedmeadows.com
