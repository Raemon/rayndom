# Cháteau at Medicine Lake Minneapolis, Minnesota

The Château at Medicine Lake is where relaxed retreat meets convenience. Just minutes from downtown Minneapolis, the Château offers clients a calm and private setting with warm, inviting interiors, spacious landscaped grounds, and all the conveniences to make your next meeting, event or celebration a success!  
  
Mintáhoe Catering &amp; Events is proud to own and operate the Château at Medicine Lake and we look forward to helping make your wedding reception, picnic or gathering of any kind simply unforgettable.
