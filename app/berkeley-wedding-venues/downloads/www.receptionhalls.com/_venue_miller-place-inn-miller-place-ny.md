# Miller Place Inn Miller Place, New York

Nestled in the historic hamlet of Miller Place is one of the great "finds" of Long Island -- The Miller Place Inn. Built in the 18th Century in a quiet setting of beautiful lawns and gazebos. Miller Place Inn is an elegant reminder of a more refined, genteel life style once enjoyed by the elite of Long Island. A feeling of gracious hospitality awaits you in a grand French Country manner.  
  
For over two decades the Miller Place Inn has been meticulously hosting one affair at a time. Your Maitre D' will greet you at the front door with champagne and strawberries and escort you to your luxurious bridal suite. Your arriving guests are ushered into the Cocktail Reception Room with its twenty foot vaulted ceiling, balcony and concert grand piano.  
  
After cocktails the party continues in the Victorian style Banquet Room. One immediately notes the spaciousness of the oversized dance floor. A fabulous dinner awaits your guests. Miller Place's award-winning chef will custom design a menu to please even the most demanding palate. Every detail of your affair is carefully orchestrated by a professional and talented Banquet Staff.  
  
Allow the Miller Place Inn to provide you, your family and friends with a day filled with lifelong memories....
