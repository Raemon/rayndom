# St Louis CITY Penthouse Saint Louis, Missouri

"St Louis CITY Penthouse" is over 8,000 square feet, three stories & known as the Sexiest Penthouse in Saint Louis. This rare penthouse has been recently updated with all-new furnishings that are designed for today's minimalist casual modern comforts with a splash of glamorous luxury.   
  
The Penthouse is not available for the wedding reception; however, it is available for all the other festivities that go along with that important week, including the intimate wedding reception after party, rehearsal dinner, bridal shower or bachelorette party, and so on.   
  
Located directly on Lucas Park, which fronts the Stunning Historic St Louis Main Library. There are amazing views on all sides. You can't miss the views of the next-door Saint Louis Museum Ferris Wheel, along with loving the colorful custom lighting effects & the trick stereo surround system playing your favorite artists throughout the Penthouse!
