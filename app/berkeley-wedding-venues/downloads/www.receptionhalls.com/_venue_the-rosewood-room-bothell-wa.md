# The Rosewood Room Bothell, Washington

The Rosewood Room is an elegant venue where charm meets contemporary. It is a venue for all of life’s events including weddings, corporate meetings, and other social gatherings. Alexa’s Catering provides all the essentials, at no extra charge, including tables, chairs, flatware, china, setup and cleanup. The Rosewood Room by Alexa’s Catering is a full-service venue. Our comprehensive services include planning, coordination, unique seasonal menus, an in-house bar, and design.
