# The Highland Room Highland Park, Michigan

Step into a warm and welcoming event space ideal for weddings, birthday parties, baby showers, corporate gatherings, and more. This roomy, industrial-inspired venue blends rustic charm with modern comfort, offering a versatile blank canvas for your next celebration.  
  
The open-concept layout includes a spacious main hall with high ceilings, large windows for natural light, and polished concrete floors. The layout is fully customizable—whether you're planning a seated dinner, cocktail-style reception, or dance party, the space adapts beautifully to your needs.  
  
Unique features include a stunning accent wall, dimmable lighting, built-in bar area, prep kitchen access, and stylish modern restrooms. Tables, chairs, and basic linens are available for use, making setup stress-free.  
  
Conveniently located in a lively downtown area with ample parking, this space is perfect for hosts looking to create a memorable experience in a stylish, easy-to-use venue.
