# Concord University Point Athens, West Virginia

Are you looking for a wedding, reunion, conference or special events venue?  
  
Concord University's newest facility, University Point, features a vaulted-ceiling chapel with a wall of windows and an abundance of natural light. The Wilkes Family Chapel is a one-of-a-kind room with a serene mountainside view. For small meetings or preparation space we offer the Seminar Room. The Pais Reception Hall offers an attractive banquet space opening onto a large outdoor terrace.  
  
Help us create a memorable wedding or event for you on "The Campus Beautiful". Please contact our office for prices and availability.
