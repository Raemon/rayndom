# VFW Post 2051 Hall Harbor Springs, Michigan

Our VFW Post and Auxiliary are excited to announce that we are renting out our wonderful hall space to the public! Contact us for your special event needs!   
Our space can hold up to 289 people and includes tables and chairs and a large kitchen (with serving area) for your event!   
Call ‪(231) 753-8588 and and leave a message, please allow 48hrs for us to get back with you.   
Perfect for birthdays, wedding receptions, baby showers, corporate events and more!   
Pricing for weekends start at $800/day plus deposit.  
Weekdays start at $500/day plus deposit.   
VFW members get discounted pricing and VFW Life members associated with the post get 1 free rental per year!
