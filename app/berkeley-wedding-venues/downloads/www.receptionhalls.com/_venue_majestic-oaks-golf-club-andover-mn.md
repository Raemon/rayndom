# Majestic Oaks Golf Club Andover, Minnesota

Majestic Oaks Golf Club is one of the Twin Cities premier golf clubs and special event venues. We are proud to offer two amazing pond-side ceremony sites, options for indoor ceremonies, and three uniquely designed ballrooms   
all among a beautifully landscaped golf course. With seating available for up to 450 guests, each ballroom overlooks the lush golf course and large pond.  
  
Here at Majestic Oaks, you will find the best traditions of service, culinary excellence, and hospitality. We offer a full spectrum of services and menu customization. No matter what time of the year, Majestic Oaks offers an  
ideal setting for any special occasion. Bring us your ideas; we are ready to help make your event both unique and memorable for you and your guests.
