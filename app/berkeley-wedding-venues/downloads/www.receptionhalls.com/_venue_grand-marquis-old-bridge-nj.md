# Grand Marquis Old Bridge, New Jersey

The Grand Marquis is proud to announce that its facility is undergoing a multi million dollar renovation. During renovations we are open and available for tours please call us at 732 679 5700.  
  
The Grand Marquis is elegant crystal, stunning linens and imperial silver packaged in one of the area’s most beautiful buildings, featuring a huge 8,400 square foot ballroom and the truly unique Dome Room. Our facility is easily accessible from anywhere in New Jersey, NYC and Staten Island via the Garden State Parkway, Routes 9 and 18 and the NJ Turnpike and can accommodate upwards of 600 guests.
