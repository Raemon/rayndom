# The Woman's Club of Chevy Chase Chevy Chase, Maryland

![Photo of The Woman's Club of Chevy Chase](/media/MD/13078/the-womans-club-of-chevy-chase-chevy-chase-md.jpg)

The Woman’s Club of Chevy Chase provides a unique special event venue for weddings, bar and bat mitzvahs, special occasions, garden parties, and meetings.   
  
The club can accommodate 250 seated guests. The venue offers tables, chairs, ample parking, large auditorium, smaller side room with large windows, and a beautiful garden setting. Its convenient location near Washington, DC is the perfect venue for indoor and outdoor events.  
  
The Woman’s Club of Chevy Chase was established in 1913 as a charitable organization for local women to help children and families in need in Montgomery County and the State of Maryland.
