### Venue Details

Capacity (Seated / Cocktail): **300 /**

Our most requested space, the Fellowship Hall (seats 250) can be arranged for wedding receptions, staged performances, vendor fairs, or private parties. Our sanctuary (seats 300) is surrounded by beautiful stained glass and is ideal for concerts, speakers, and wedding ceremonies. Our Community Room (seats 50) is perfect for conversations and best suited for showers, classes, board retreats, and committee meetings.

Max Number of Simultaneous Events: **1**

Outside Catering Permitted (BYO): **Yes**

Venue Type/Style: **Ballroom, Historical Building**
