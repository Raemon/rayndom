# Park Avenue Events Freehold, New Jersey

For those planning a wedding, bar/bat mitzvah, christening, shower, birthday or anniversary party, PARK AVENUE EVENTS is the perfect environment to celebrate your special occasion for 30 to 200 of your family and friends. We offer two private rooms with features that include a private bar, media player and extra-large viewing screens, elevator access, private restrooms, hardwood floors, and room for live entertainment and a dance floor.  
  
For those looking for a meeting facility for professional events, we offer the ideal gathering place for pharmaceutical parties, medical groups, financial institutions, networking agencies, and more. Our private rooms include beautiful surroundings and the complimentary use of projectors and projection screens, all a midst the pairing of excellent food, beverage, and service.
