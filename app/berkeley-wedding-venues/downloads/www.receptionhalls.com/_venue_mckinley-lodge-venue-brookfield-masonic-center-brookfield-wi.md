# McKinley Lodge Venue- Brookfield Masonic Center Brookfield, Wisconsin

We have a large building in Brookfield that we offer usage of the entire building for your private event. Weddings, Birthdays, family reunions, Quinceanera - sweet 16, celebration of life, retirement party, vow renewals, baby showers and etc. All are welcomed, no need to ask or wonder. It is only $125 per hour. You set up the tables and decorate how you like. These photos are examples of how others imagination has saved them thousands. You can bring in your own food, buffet, potluck style etc and drink/refreshments as well just another way to save money. We do not require any certain vendor's so you can save, and we allow you to have DJs or a band for your event too. Heck you can even hold the ceremony on one side and the reception on the other. Contact us for a tour. Building Can accommodate up to 360 people. Contact us for a number or email and we will be happy to send it to you for questions.
