# Hetherington Hills Bentleyville, Pennsylvania

![Photo of Hetherington Hills](/media/PA/11743/hetherington-hills-bentleyville-pa.jpg)

Hetherington Hills is a naturally beautiful farm perfect for your wedding day. Let us help you to create the wedding day of your dreams.  
  
If you are looking for a beautiful outdoor setting for your ceremony look no further. Imagine your guests looking on while you walk across the bridge out to the stunning gazebo along the waters edge.  
  
The pond, wildflowers, and trees will be a beautiful backdrop as you and your loved one exchange vows of love to one another.   
  
Our newly renovated barn is a perfect setting for your country ceremony. You and your guests will enjoy an intimate setting seated inside the over 100 year old barn.
