# Eagle Point Lodge Clinton, Iowa

Eagle Point Lodge is located in Eagle Point Park in Clinton, Iowa overlooking the widest part of the Mississippi River just north of the dam. This Lodge can look rustic or it can be dressed up for an elegant affair. The Lodge has a capacity of 299 people. This wi-fi facility is open and offers great views throughout the year.
