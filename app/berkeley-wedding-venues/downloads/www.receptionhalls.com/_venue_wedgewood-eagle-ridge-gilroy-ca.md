# Wedgewood Eagle Ridge Gilroy, California

Just 20 minutes south of San Jose is one of the most pristine Bay Area wedding locations. Wedgewood Wedding and Banquet Center at Eagle Ridge Golf Club gives any guest the country club atmosphere without the country club price tag.  
  
Some of the spectacular, unique features of our Gilroy wedding location:  
  
* Perched overlooking hills of Gilroy, giving you a 360 degree view of the surrounding countryside  
* Up to 275 seated guests  
* Beautiful new clubhouse, the perfect complement to a golf course wedding  
* Gazebo overlooking the course, making Eagle Ridge a top outdoor wedding venue in Northern California  
* Fireplace and 30-foot ceilings in the banquet hall  
* Terrace on second floor available for less formal events
