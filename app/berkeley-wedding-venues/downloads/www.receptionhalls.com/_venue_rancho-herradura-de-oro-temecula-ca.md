# Rancho Herradura De Oro Temecula, California

Rancho Herradura De Oro is a 20 Acre ranch style venue located in the beautiful city of Temecula, California. It is available for any type of event: Weddings, Ceremonies, Corporate Gatherings, Baby Showers, Birthday Parties. Beautifully built barn, covered patios to pick from for your picture perfect ceremony, “Cantina” bar, 2 large round pens and arenas that can easily accommodate 200+ people comfortably. 20 acres of beautiful greenery and spring flowers.
