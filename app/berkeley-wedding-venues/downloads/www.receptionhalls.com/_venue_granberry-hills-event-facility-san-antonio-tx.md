# Granberry Hills Event Facility San Antonio, Texas

Granberry Hills is the ideal location to host your wedding, quinceañera, or corporate event. Granberry Hills offers the aesthetic beauty of outdoor events with the conveniences of superior indoor facilities. Set on 15 impeccably landscaped acres with easy access to downtown hotels for out-of-town guests, our venues can accommodate events large or small.  
  
Whether you choose to have your event on the Gardenside Courtyard &amp; Ballroom or on the Hillside Covered Pavilion &amp; Ballroom, you will be assisted by an on-site Event Manager to ensure every detail is attended to with professionalism and excellence. Our staff will handle all the setup and cleanup to relieve you of any additional stress.  
  
Call (210) 590-8067 and speak to one of our on-site Event Managers to schedule a time for a tour!
