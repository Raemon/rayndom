# Motte Historical Museum Inc. Sun City, California

This 9000,00 sq feet Rustic Barn is perfect for a country/shabby chic wedding and reception. The barn features Antique/classic cars from 1910 - 1969 as well as a loft with community history.   
  
Plan your wedding in our garden back yard decorated with rustic farm equipment. Guests will have a fantastic time inside for the reception many classic cars can remain inside for your special day.   
  
Included in the packaging: Tables and Chairs for up to 200. A dance floor in the designated area, a rustic wedding arbor for the garden or indoors, and an onside coordinator the day of the event.
