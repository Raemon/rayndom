# Inn at Pleasant Lake New London, New Hampshire

There is nothing more romantic than an intimate garden wedding overlooking a shimmering lake with a mountain backdrop. The Inn at Pleasant Lake is a stunning lakefront New Hampshire wedding venue with a caring staff that will help make your special day a treasured memory. Our award-winning New Hampshire inn and restaurant are perfectly suited to host intimate wedding receptions up to 200 guests, rehearsal dinners, or elegant bridal showers with a personal touch. The variety of packages and services offered ensures that your day will be as unique as you are.  
  
\+ We are owner run, and care for our guests personally  
  
\+ We are full service, and customize to meet your needs (we are not a formula venue you rent for a couple of hours)  
  
\+ Our property becomes your “home away from home” for the weekend of your event. We want you to feel at home, and are here to help you get the most out of your experience  
  
\+ We enjoy a breathtaking setting – Pleasant Lake, beach access, resort amenities and activities, Mountain Views, Historic Barn and Inn with a 150 year legacy of hospitality  
  
\+ Our dining experience is often the most memorable part of our events – your guests will be raving about the meal for a long time to come
