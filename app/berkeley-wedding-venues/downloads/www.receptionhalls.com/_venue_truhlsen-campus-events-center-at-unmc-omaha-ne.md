# Truhlsen Campus Events Center at UNMC Omaha, Nebraska

The Dr. Stanley M. and Dorothy Truhlsen Campus Events Center is the newest venue in the heart of midtown Omaha. Housed in the Michael F. Sorrell Center for Health Science Education on the University of Nebraska Medical Center campus, the Events Center features modern architecture with neutral colors throughout, a beautiful atrium with large glass windows, a spacious ballroom that can accommodate up to 325 guests for a dinner and 500 for a cocktail reception, and an experienced and attentive events staff to assist in your planning process. This is the ideal location to host your unique event!
