# Grandwood Park Park District Community Center Gurnee, Illinois

Grandwood Park Park District is a great affordable choice for your indoor or outdoor rental facility needs. Our accommodations are perfect for parties, receptions, banquets, meetings, family gatherings, reunions, showers, graduations, etc. Your rental fees include use of WiFi, kitchen area, round tables, rectangle tables, chairs, cleaning supplies, garbage cans and garbage bags, and limited refrigeration space. Renters are responsible for set up and clean up of their event.
