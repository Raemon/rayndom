# The Villa Westminster, California

Welcome to The Villa at Lifetime Weddings &amp; Events,  
  
The Villa, is a warm Mediterranean and romantic Spanish inspired venue. The Villa offers one of the most incredible and unique settings with its lush gardens and fountains, natural tone enriched décor, floor to ceiling windows treated with beautiful chiffon curtains and hand brushed crystal chandeliers. The Villa provides an indoor and outdoor setting for your ceremony. The Villa can accommodate any type of event, from casual to formal, dining to wedding receptions, and meetings to corporate social events, up to 300 guests. The wonderful staff of The Villa will ensure that you will get the attention and service that will lead to a successful event. Being easily accessible to both the 405 and 22 freeways, your guests will enjoy the convenience and intimacy of what we call, The Villa.  
  
Lifetime Weddings &amp; Events is a decor and floral company at The Villa. We make the wedding of your dreams vivid in color with the expertise of our consultants as we guide you through the steps in making your wedding day like no other. Our exemplary services in planning, décor and floral will not only surpass those of our competitors, but will also surpass your every expectation. Not only does Lifetime provide exceptional services, but we are also home to all of your floral and decor needs. From personal flowers to ceremony and reception arrangements, our creative designers will help you create a stunning wedding that will making a lasting first and forever impression. Choose over 50+ style/color linens and napkins, a variety of classic chair covers with 20+ sash colors, a collection of endless choices in lightings, beautiful draping and more! Our goal is to provide you with the wedding you have been dreaming about.
