# Mayfair Farms West Orange, New Jersey

If you are looking for country elegance, pristine gardens, over 50 years of experience, excellent food and attention to every detail, then look no further. Mayfair Farms has earned its reputation by consistently providing high quality affairs to customers seeking excellence in fine catering.  
  
A Mayfair wedding is a special affair planned by you, for you. Come visit Mayfair Farms and meet with one of the Horn Family, who have owned and operated The Mayfair since 1943, or one of their Banquet Planners. They will escort you through their beautiful ballrooms and answer any questions about your special day. Attention to detail is critical to your wedding's success.  
  
Situated on 14 magnificent acres of rolling lawns and manicured gardens, The Mayfair specializes in outdoor ceremonies and cocktail hours in an elegant country setting. There is no better location in Essex County to host your wedding than Mayfair Farms. Less than 20 minutes from Manhattan, Staten Island, Bergen, Passaic or Morris County, Mayfair Farms is the jewel of Essex County.
