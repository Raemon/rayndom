# Events at the Mansion San Martin, California

EVENTS AT THE MANSION  
The Perfect Place to Make Beautiful Memories Together  
Weddings and Other Private Events  
  
BEAUTIFUL, PRIVATE, INTIMATE, OUTDOOR VENUE  
This venue offers a charming water fountain, evergreen plant shrubs, meticulously manicured lawn, big and beautiful green arch and an enchanting backdrop view. Perfect for a small intimate wedding ceremony.   
Sunny, Big Backyard Sports Court ideal for pop up events, outdoor group workout / exercise, small private parties, outdoor meetings, photography with good lighting and backyard landscape, and any other special occasion. You are free to add your own personal touch if needed.
