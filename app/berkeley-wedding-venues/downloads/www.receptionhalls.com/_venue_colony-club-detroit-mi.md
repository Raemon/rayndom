# Colony Club Detroit, Michigan

![Photo of Colony Club](/media/MI/10067/colony-club-detroit-mi.jpg)

Plan your wedding ceremony and reception at Detroit’s historic Colony Club! The Louis XIV Style Grand Ballroom at Colony Club accommodates receptions of 125-400 guests for dinner and dancing. We offer convenient, all-inclusive wedding packages that include plated entrée service and a premium bar.
