# Rock Bottom Restaurant & Brwry Warrenville, Illinois

Finding just the right spot for your next social or business event takes special planning. Rock Bottom Restaurant &amp; Brewery can provide your group a exciting experience in a comfortable, entertaining atmosphere. Rock Bottom has a loft that offers semi-private and private event accommodations, all centered around a functional brewery. Our loft is equipped with a full bar and TV's either for a casual event with the game on or a magical slideshow of the soon to be bride and groom. This space can be transformed from a casual party space to an elegant wedding reception.
