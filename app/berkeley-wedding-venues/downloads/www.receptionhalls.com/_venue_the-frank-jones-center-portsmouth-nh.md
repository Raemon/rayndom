# The Frank Jones Center Portsmouth, New Hampshire

Welcome to The Frank Jones Center  
  
  
A COMPLETE FACILITY FOR BOTH CORPORATE AND SPECIAL EVENTS!  
  
  
The Frank Jones Center offers elegant and versatile function rooms capable of accommodating smaller meetings or larger groups to a maximum of 900 guests. We are an all- inclusive facility taking care of your travel arrangements, fine catering, to music, photography, seminar equipment and more. Our facility is a beautifully-appointed full service center for your next banquet, wedding, prom, reunion, meeting, trade show, auction and fundraiser!   
  
  
Make your next special event a memorable successful experience.
