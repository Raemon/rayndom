# Courtyard by Marriott Baldwin Park Baldwin Park, California

Ideally located in the heart of San Gabriel Valley, Courtyard by Marriott Baldwin Park offers a beautiful ballroom that can host up to 350 people. In addition, the hotel offers 6 banquet and meeting rooms to choose from with all catering done in house. Various lunch and dinner menus to choose from whether your hosting an imitate gathering or large wedding.
