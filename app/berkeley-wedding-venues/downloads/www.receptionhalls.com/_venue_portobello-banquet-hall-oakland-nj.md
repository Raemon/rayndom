# Portobello Banquet Hall Oakland, New Jersey

Beautiful catering hall located in Oakland, New Jersey. We can accommodate parties of 20 - 240. Plated menus and buffet packages available. Hold your Sweet 16, School Function, Bar/Bat Mitzvah, Wedding, Bridal/Baby Shower, Prom or Birthday Party at our Venue. Easy to get to from Route 208.
