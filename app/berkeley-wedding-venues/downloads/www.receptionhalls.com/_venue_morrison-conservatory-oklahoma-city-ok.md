# Morrison Conservatory Oklahoma City, Oklahoma

Morrison Conservatory is the perfect venue for an intimate wedding and reception. We have been in business now for 65 years and still family owned and operated. Our venue is an indoor Garden Conservatory that features tropical and blooming plants in serpentine wall planters and our main focal point is our stunning waterfall. We also have an upper level that has a beautiful vintage fountain. Our rental fee's can not be beat in the Oklahoma City Area.. Come check our venue out...
