# Tipsy Putt - Sacramento Sacramento, California

Play and party to your heart's content at Tipsy Putt, an indoor mini-golf bar and restaurant venue with locations across Northern California. Whether it be wedding welcome parties, rehearsal dinners, bachelor/ette parties, or after parties - we are no strangers to celebrating new chapters.   
  
From mini-golf to cornhole, Tipsy Putt has an variety of activities for your most competitive attendees. With high-end facilities to match, your 10-300 guests are bound to have a blast as they celebrate you. We have a stylish sports bar with tables, chairs, and flat-screen TVs, as well as a vast selection of local craft beers and draft cocktails. Tipsy Putt also offers delicious, Mexi-Cali inspired, pub fare with individual ordering or catering options available.  
  
Hosting your wedding event at Tipsy Putt gives you plenty of support throughout the planning phase. From rentals to the sound, lighting, and equipment for games, our team will provide everything you need for your special occasion. We look forward to hosting your party and encourage you to Drink Local, Putt Tipsy!
