### Venue Details

Capacity (Seated / Cocktail): **300 / 500**

For a Wedding Ceremony the capacity is 160. Seated Dinner is 300. Cocktail parties are 500 max. There is also a dance area.

Max Number of Simultaneous Events: **1**

Outside Catering Permitted (BYO): **Yes**

Venue Type/Style: **Banquet Hall**

On-Site Cuisine Options: **Catering on site**

### Pricing
