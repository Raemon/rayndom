# Lake Shore Plaza Lees Summit, Missouri

CELEBRATIONS TO REMEMBER BEGIN AT LAKE SHORE PLAZA!  
  
Lake Shore Plaza is surrounded by trees and is located across the street from the Lake Lotawana Memorial Rose Garden. The Rose Garden landscape boasts seasonal flowers, brick patio, gazebo and arbor. It is a picture perfect setting for your ceremony or photographs. Lake Shore Plaza has a unique, yet rustic, feel. You are welcomed by a glass framed, two-story foyer with a stone-lined staircase and water feature. Exposed stone walls, neutral, stained concrete floors, and fireplace with rustic mantle and lounge area create the perfect backdrop for your event. Our extremely versatile space provides endless floor plan and design options for intimate gatherings or medium sized events. It is the perfect place for your next event!  
  
FEATURES  
-Affordable pricing allows you to increase spending on other products and services  
Spacious, yet still intimate, makes Lake Shore Plaza the perfect space for plated dinners or buffets up to 176 guests. We can easily accommodate a cocktail receptions for up to 225 guests  
-Open catering and alcohol policy allows flexibility in your planning and budget  
-Over-sized catering kitchen is fully equipped with ice maker, double oven, range, two refrigerator/freezers, large granite island with abundant counter space for easy prep and plating  
-Handicapped accessible design and elevator makes it convenient for all guests  
-Convenient private parking within gated lot  
-Across the street is the Lake Lotawana Memorial Rose Garden, a perfect backdrop for photos or a wedding ceremony. The garden is accented with trees, flowers, arbor and Gazebo.*
