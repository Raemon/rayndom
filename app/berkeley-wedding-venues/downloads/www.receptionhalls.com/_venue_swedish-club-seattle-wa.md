# Swedish Club Seattle, Washington

Seattle meeting and event venue located a few minutes north of downtown. The Swedish Cultural Center was originally founded in 1892, and has grown from modest beginnings to become a prominent meeting space in the Northwest Community. The club has 9 different rooms, including a kitchen, that are all available for rental from the public. This space is great for business meetings, weddings and receptions, parties and various other celebrations. The Swedish Cultural Center is a non-profit organization located just moments from the popular Fremont neighborhood and downtown Seattle.
