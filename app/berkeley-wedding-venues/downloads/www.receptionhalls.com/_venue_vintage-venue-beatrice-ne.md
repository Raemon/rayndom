# Vintage Venue Beatrice, Nebraska

Vintage Venue is a spacious 7,000 Square Foot, modern-industrial space large enough to host up to 400 dinner guests, or a standing room cocktail party event for up to 600 guests. Each event is treated separately and accommodated to make your ideas a reality. From weddings to corporate meetings, to fundraising events, the Vintage Venue gives a charming setting for the event of your dreams.

### Venue Details

Capacity (Seated / Cocktail): **600 / 600**

Bar setup is available: The bar is optional for your event. We have a variety of drink packages to choose from. Whether its a cash bar, open bar or offering drink tickets to your guests we have you covered. Parking: There are a number of Public Parking Spaces within a 2 block radius of Vintage Venue. Catering and Food: Colleen’s Catering is the preferred vendor for any group that has their function at Vintage Venue. The kitchen will not be open for any group to use. Alcohol Policy: Since Vintage Venue carries the Nebraska Alcohol Permit, we cannot allow any outside drinks or alcohol into Vintage Venue. Linens: Vintage Venue staff sets up all tables and chairs and places linens on all tables. Black, White and Ivory are included in the prices, other colors are an additional fee. Guaranteed Pricing: When you sign the Vintage Venue Contract for your event, even if it’s 6-12 months away the pricing will not change.
