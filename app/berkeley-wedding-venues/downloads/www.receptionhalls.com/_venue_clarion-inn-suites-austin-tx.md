# Aiden by Best Western - Austin City Hotel Austin, Texas

Aiden is boutique-coolness. Authentic. Unscripted. Engaging. A true Austin experience, located in the trendy and hip SoCo District. We have approximately 5,600 square feet of affordable and well equipped conference facilities for banquets, conferences, meetings, office parties, weddings and receptions. The elegantly decorated ballroom is 3,900 square feet and features an oak dance floor, a state-of-the art sound system and high speed Internet access, with a maximum number of guests is 230. The ballroom can be split into 4 banquet rooms with a maximum of 48 guest per banquet room. The "Attic" is 1,700 square feet with a loft and bar, perfect for parties and small events, with a maximum number of guest 48. Contact the sales department today!!
