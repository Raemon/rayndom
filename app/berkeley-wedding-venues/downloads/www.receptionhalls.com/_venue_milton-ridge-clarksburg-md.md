# Milton Ridge Clarksburg, Maryland

Milton Ridge offers unique All-Inclusive packages for beautifully personalized events at a tremendous value. The warm ambiance, air of romance, and outstanding service of Milton Ridge make it one of the leading venues for weddings and banquets in the Washington DC, Metropolitan area. It would be a privilege for Milton Ridge to serve as the venue for your wedding or event. Tours of the property and the reception site, as well as consultations with our staff, can be arranged by calling 240 257 2560 or through our website at www.miltonridge.com
