# Liberty House Restaurant Jersey City, New Jersey

Liberty House Restaurant was designed to exemplify our "view with a room" concept and focus on the magnificent Manhattan skyline views while tantalizing your senses with our award-winning cuisine, handcrafted decor details, live music, inviting seating areas, and lavish grounds. Our main dining room offers spectacular first floor views and opens up to our patios and gardens while our Grand Ballroom offers second floor views with a wrap-around terrace. We offer unlimited possibilities for your dining, wedding, social, or corporate events.
