# West Hartford Meeting & Conference Center West Hartford, Connecticut

Beautifully situated in a neighborhood setting steeped in New England tradition. The impressive brick building, recognized for its elegant, turn-of-the century architecture, features a graceful, staired entryway accented by stately columns. The interior has an expansive hall with polished hardwood floors, and a brass-railed balcony. The Conference Center is a fully modern, climate-controlled facility featuring a full range of amenities to accommodate individual requests and custom set-ups. The Conference Center has a service kitchen, coat room, stage, separate meeting room and Wi-Fi.
