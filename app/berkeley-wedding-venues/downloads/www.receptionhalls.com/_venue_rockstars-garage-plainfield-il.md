# Rockstars Garage Plainfield, Illinois

Rockstars Garage is a small, unique event venue located in the Illinois suburb of Plainfield—about 30 miles southwest of Chicago. Complete with a stage, state-of-the-art music equipment, green room, technical booth and two-level lounge with comfortable seating, this one-of-a-kind, intimate space is perfect for small wedding receptions, cocktail receptions, special events, live music and entertainment, corporate outings, birthday/holiday parties and more.
