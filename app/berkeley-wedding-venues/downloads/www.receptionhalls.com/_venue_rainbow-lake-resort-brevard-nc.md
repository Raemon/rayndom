# Rainbow Lake Resort Brevard, North Carolina

We have had the honor of hosting many local and destination weddings here at Rainbow Lake Resort. Our wedding packages include an island with a gazebo for your ceremony, with an arched bridge, a wedding swan float for the brides’ entrance, a covered pavilion for receptions that can accommodate up to 175 attendees which can be enclosed in inclement weather and cottages on our property to accommodate the bride and groom on their special day.
