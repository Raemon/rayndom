# American Legion Le Center, Minnesota

Our newly remodeled banquet hall with seating up to 320, second and third seatings available for parties up to 600. Offering great food and hospitality, fabulous service. We strive to meet your budget needs and are able to provide estimates for your special event. "Private in house" catering and serving facilities to guarantee your food is served fresh and hot, buffet and ala carte menu options. Lower level party room for private occasions. We offer full service/staffed bar. We make your wedding reception or special event a truly remarkable affair.
