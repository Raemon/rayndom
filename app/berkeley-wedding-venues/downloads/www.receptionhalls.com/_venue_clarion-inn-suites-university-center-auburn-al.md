# Clarion Inn & Suites University Center Auburn, Alabama

AUBURN EVENT VENUE, located at the Clarion Inn &amp; Suites University Center, offers the largest ballroom in town and combined meeting space totaling over 9,000 square feet that is easily dividable to accommodate functions of all sizes. All this and more makes it the ideal choice for your conference, seminar, social event or wedding. We look forward to welcoming you and helping you plan your next event.
