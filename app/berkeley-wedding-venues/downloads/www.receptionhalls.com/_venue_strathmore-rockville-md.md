# The Mansion at Strathmore Rockville, Maryland

Strathmore is a wedding venue featuring a Georgian mansion and stunning outdoor gardens located in Rockville, Maryland. With two main event spaces available, you and your partner will have the option to say “I do” in an elegant mansion setting or in the modern Music Center building. No matter which space you choose, your wedding day is sure to be unique and magical when you book with Strathmore.   
  
Facilities and Capacity  
With capacity for up to 125 guests, Strathmore’s event spaces are perfect for intimate wedding gatherings. The Mansion at Strathmore is set atop a hill and dressed with a sweeping iron staircase, boasting elegance and class upon first glance. Around the mansion you’ll find delightful walking paths, manicured gardens, an outdoor gazebo and a stunning sculpture garden. For more modern style weddings, you’ll want to utilize The Music Center at Strathmore with its six-story glass walls and windows throughout. The Music Center features exceptional natural light along with natural wood accents for a warm and inviting atmosphere. The two outdoor terraces located adjacent to this space feature breathtaking views of the 11 acres of hills that surround it.
