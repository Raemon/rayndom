# Rolling Hills Country Club Arlington, Texas

Rolling Hills Country Club is a beautiful facility with a breathtaking view of our golf course. The banquet room will accommodate up to 230 guests and has cathedral ceilings with glass and rock accents. We provide a large dance floor, all the tables, chairs, china, glassware and silver. Our outdoor, tree shaded ceremony site has a view of the pond on the 16th hole with a Texas sunset to the west. We have a gourmet chef and a professional wedding coordinator on staff.  
  
Plenty of free parking and valet upon request.
