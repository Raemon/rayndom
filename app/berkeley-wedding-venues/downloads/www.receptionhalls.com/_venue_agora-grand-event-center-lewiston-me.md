# Agora Grand Event Center Lewiston, Maine

The Agora Grand Event Center will open in May, 2016, as one of Maine's premier event centers. If you're planning to say "I do", treat yourself to a picturesque setting, an on-site chapel, and a luxury balcony lounge overlooking the magnificent reception hall. If you're planning a meeting or conference, treat your guests to 6,000 square feet of meeting space and plenty of free parking. If you're planning a celebration, enjoy a gourmet meal, excellent bar service, and a state-of-the-art sound system that will keep your guests dancing late into the night.
