# PURE LOUNGE PRIVATE EVENT VENUE Sunnyvale, California

![Photo of PURE LOUNGE PRIVATE EVENT VENUE](/media/CA/11908/pure-lounge-private-event-venue-sunnyvale-ca.jpg)

Pure Lounge is a unique and upscale private event venue perfect for corporate functions and wedding receptions. Classical architecture and beautiful modern decor provide the perfect ambiance for any event style. Built-in LED cocktail tables create dramatic effects and drop-down ceiling stylish appointments are the best complement to the Intelligent Light effects. Pure Lounge is designed for an exquisite, elegant and memorable celebration. We offer full bar service, professional catering, theme props, the most hi-tech Audio/Visual capabilities, a professional DJ deck, interactive elements and the best hospitality to our Clients. At Pure Lounge our team of event planners create extraordinary experiences.
