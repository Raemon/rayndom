# Anderson Island Weddings Anderson Island, Washington

Located on Anderson Island, a 20 minute ferry ride from Steilacoom, WA this rustic island location offers a year round variety of uses with spectacular views in a peaceful setting. The panoramic views across the sound provide many photo opportunities. Anderson Island Weddings &amp; Events will offer you and your guests a beautiful landscaped park like setting. South Puget Sound's newest outdoor wedding and special event venue. Set above the water's edge the Venue awaits you and your guests.
