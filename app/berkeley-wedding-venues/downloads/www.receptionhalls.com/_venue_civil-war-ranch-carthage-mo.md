### Venue Details

Capacity (Seated / Cocktail): **150 / 400**

2nd-floor Banquet Hall is L-shaped: East section (north to south) is 40 x 40 feet; West section (east to west) is 20 x 20 feet. First-floor Banquet Room (40 x 40 ft.) is in the center of the building. There are three original horse stalls to the east and an original tack closet on the north end. On the west side of the first-floor Banquet Room are the men's restroom (9 x 12 feet), women's restroom (12 x 12 feet), Bridal Dressing Room (7 x 16 feet), and food preparation room (7 x 16 feet). Pole Barn is ideal for shaded outdoor meetings, luncheons, picnics, dinners, seminars, exhibits, birthday parties, reunions, wedding ceremonies and reception space. Pole Barn opens to the 120 x 70 ft. Barn Courtyard. The two are always rented together. Seating for 120 by the side of dock and ranch pond.

Max Number of Simultaneous Events: **1**

Outside Catering Permitted (BYO): **Yes**

Venue Type/Style: **Banquet Hall, Waterfront, Ballroom, Loft, Garden, Historical Building, Barn**

On-Site Cuisine Options: **Use Your Own Caterer**
