# Music Box Private Event Space San Diego San Diego, California

![Photo of Music Box Private Event Space San Diego](/media/CA/13881/music-box-private-event-space-san-diego-san-diego-ca.jpg)

Music Box Private Event Space San Diego is located in the heart of downtown San Diego and Little Italy. The venue contains 3 stories of event space, totaling over 13,000 square feet (with accommodations for up to 700 people), and all 3 floors have views of the open stage/theater inside. Our private venue space is designed to impress and is perfect for live music shows, private parties and events, private concerts, magic shows, corporate events, and parties. We want to help you make your dream event a reality. Our team is passionate about making every event unique and special for our clients. Call us and tell us more about your dream event - we’re here to bring it to life!
