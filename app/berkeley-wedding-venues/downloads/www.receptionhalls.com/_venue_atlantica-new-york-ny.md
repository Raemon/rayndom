# Atlantica New York, New York

The 145' mega motor yacht, Atlantica is a 10 million dollar tri-level yacht, USCG certified for 400 guests. The dining salon will comfortably seat 225 guests and features a beautiful parquet dance floor. This Yacht charter with its spacious multi deck can accommodate largest of parties comfortably.
