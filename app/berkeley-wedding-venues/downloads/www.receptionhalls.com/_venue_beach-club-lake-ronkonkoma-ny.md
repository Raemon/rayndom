# Beach Club Ronkonkoma, New York

Located in historic Lake Ronkonkoma, Beach Club Estate has a history of it's own. Once known as the Lighthouse Hotel, and "situated amid wholesome and healful surroundings" the Lighthouse Hotel was favored in Long Island. Almost 60 ago, the now know Beach Club Estate was described as "the most appealing establishments of its kind in that section." Now Beach Club Estate strives to make your wedding day one you will never forget.  
  
Tucked away on Rose Drive on Long Island's beautiful Lake Ronkonkoma, you and your guests can stroll the fabulous grounds, wander along the Lakeside outdoor Chapel, make wishes on the beautiful angel fountain and enjoy breathtaking views of our magnificent sunsets. It's the perfect lakeside setting for every wedding reception, especially one as important as yours.  
Whether you plan your wedding beside our Floridian palm trees on a bright summer afternoon, or on a brisk winter night, you are sure to fall in love with Beach Club Esate.  
  
Spectacular views, superb cuisine, and exceptional service create an unforgettable wedding, a family celebration or special catered events for you. Our professional banquet consultants and our wedding planners will give your reception a special style to create memories you'll cherish forever.
