# The Anchorage at the Lake Tilton, New Hampshire

Picturesque Lakeview Wedding Location. Choose the beautiful lakefront grounds of the Anchorage at the Lake for your upcoming wedding ceremony. Our large and beautiful deck overlooking Lake Winnisquam is the perfect location to exchange your vows. Wedding rental space at The Anchorage is free of charge when renting 4 or more cabins on the property. Otherwise, a $500 rental fee will be applied.
