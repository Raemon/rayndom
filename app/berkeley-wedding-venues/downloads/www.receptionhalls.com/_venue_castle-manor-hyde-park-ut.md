# Castle Manor Hyde Park, Utah

Castle Manor is one of the premier Utah reception centers. We offer an exceptional value for both indoor and outdoor events as you create memories that last a lifetime. We have several areas available to accommodate groups of all sizes. Areas can seat dining from 50 to 700 people. We accommodate all events ranging from corporate events, school dances, birthday and anniversary parties, to all wedding events. All packages include tables, chairs, standard linens, audio/video, and setup/takedown. A Bride's room is also included on any wedding package. Our facility offers a warm classy touch to any event.
