# Historic St. Martin's Church Showell, Maryland

We are delighted you are considering Historic St. Martin’s Church Foundation for your special day! Situated on over 30 wooded acres surrounded by century old trees, the property boasts a restored 18th century Flemish bond brick church and surrounding grounds which are available for tented receptions. From the moment, your guests enter the church, they will find a private and comfortable setting perfect for your ceremony. An architectural gem in Berlin, and less than 10 minutes from the beach St. Martin’s has the distinction of being listed on the National Register of Historic Places and plays a vital role in the local community and beyond as a place for year-round recreation, weddings, as well as host to the classics in music and preservation education.
