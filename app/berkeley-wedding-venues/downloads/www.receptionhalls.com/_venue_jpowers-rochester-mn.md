# J.Powers Rochester, Minnesota

J. Powers at the Hilton is a unique, sophisticated wedding venue in Rochester, MN. This luxurious setting makes every moment feel special. Large ballrooms set the scene for memorable, upscale occasions. To top it all off, an experienced events team is on hand to fine-comb the details.   
  
This deluxe property overlooks the trendy downtown district of Rochester. This thriving area offers an inspiring backdrop for the big day. There is plenty for couples and their guests to explore during their stay. J. Powers at the Hilton was founded by Rochester native Joe Powers. This stunning event venue has been carefully designed to accommodate lavish celebrations. Inside, glowing chandeliers and stunning floor length linens create an atmosphere of upscale romance. Wooden dance floors feel timelessly elegant for a memorable first dance. Ceilings reach 19 feet overhead, creating a grand ambiance for large celebrations. Floor-to-ceiling windows let natural light fill the space during the day. As the sun sets, the glow of the city will light up the night. Modern fixtures and furnishings give each ballroom a sleek finish. The team at the venue will help add the finishing touches with decor and floral recommendations. Couples can choose layouts that work well with their vision. The venue offers a choice of flexible ballroom spaces. The largest of these is the Dr. John Noseworthy Hall, with room for up to 500 guests. Featuring stunning city views, this ballroom can be modified to suit a range of styles. The Mother Alfred Moes outdoor terrace is ideal for unique events and intimate celebrations. This bright, open-air space provides an elegant garden-style setting. With a capacity for up to 400 guests, engagement parties, ceremonies, and sophisticated cocktail receptions are all possible. The newlyweds can steal a quiet moment watching the sunset. Guests will be able to soak in the sights of the city as they enjoy a tasteful event.
