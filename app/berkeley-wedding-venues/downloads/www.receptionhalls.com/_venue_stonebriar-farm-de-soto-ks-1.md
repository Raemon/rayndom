# StoneBriar Farm De Soto, Kansas

StoneBriar Farm is a family owned and operated strawberry and cut flower farm with a beautiful barn available for weddings and other special events. We accommodate up to 275 guests in a fully climate controlled space surrounded by beautiful Kansas scenery. Just 30 minutes west of downtown Kansas City with great highway access, you can feel secluded without sacrificing convenience. Bring your own vendors that fit your style and budget - no expensive bar packages here!
