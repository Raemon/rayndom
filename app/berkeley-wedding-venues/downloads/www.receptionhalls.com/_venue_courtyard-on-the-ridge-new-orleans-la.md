# Courtyard on the Ridge New Orleans, Louisiana

Courtyard on the Ridge brings you French Quarter elegance in an affordable setting, making your special day unforgettable. Our facility offers a charming, Vieux Carre themed courtyard, more than 11,000 square feet of ceremony and reception space and can accommodate up to 600 guests. Entrust the catering of your wedding to our vast experience of more than 27 years. Our cuisine is expertly prepared from scratch, never frozen and served by our professional and dedicated staff to ensure you have a memorable day.  
  
Why be one in a million, when you can be one of a kind? We look forward to being a part of this momentous occasion and thank you for your interest in Courtyard on the Ridge.
