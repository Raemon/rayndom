# The Crystal Room Staten Island, New York

Discover the gracious food, personal attention and impeccable service which awaits you at the Crystal Room. Come and visit our newly decorated facilities which sits in a three acre country club setting. Perfect for weddings, banquets, graduations, confirmations, engagements, rehearsal dinners, retirement parties, bar/bat mitzvahs... all the special events of your life. Enjoy our outdoor Gazebo and beautiful Chinese bridge, a perfect setting for those memorable pictures. At the Crystal Room, the price of elegance is not out of reach.
