# The Club At Garden Ridge San Antonio, Texas

![Photo of The Club At Garden Ridge](/media/TX/13902/the-club-at-garden-ridge-san-antonio-tx.jpg)

The Club at Garden Ridge is an elegant wedding event venue located six miles northeast of San Antonio in Garden Ridge, Texas. Couples will be surrounded by beautiful grounds featuring two lakes, hundreds of oak trees and a creek bed with a stone bridge. This venue boasts over 7,000 square feet of event space, including a grand ballroom and a ceremony pavilion. The Club at Garden Ridge can accommodate both indoor and outdoor weddings in their park-like setting.  
  
Facilities and Capacity  
Couples can accommodate up to 250 guests at this waterfront venue. Their new ballroom features wood floors, open tongue and groove ceilings, stone columns and a wrap-around porch. It also features an 18-foot, built-in stone and granite bar, along with additional seating on their porch and grounds. Their outdoor Ceremony Pavilion overlooks their gorgeous lakes and water fountain.  
  
Services Offered  
This Hill Country venue allows you exclusive use of their property the day of your wedding. They’ll provide an on-site venue coordinator to oversee your day, and plenty of complimentary parking for your guests. Additional services include:  
  
Bridal suite  
Groom’s man cave  
Family lounge  
Setup  
Cleanup  
Outside vendors  
Chairs  
Tables  
Shuttle  
Wheelchair accessible  
  
Other Services  
In addition to hosting both your ceremony and reception, The Club at Garden Ridge is also happy to host your rehearsal dinner. Additional events include:  
  
Engagement party  
Bridal shower  
Farewell brunch
