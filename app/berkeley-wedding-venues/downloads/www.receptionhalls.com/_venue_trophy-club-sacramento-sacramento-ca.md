# Trophy Club - Sacramento Sacramento, California

Trophy Club is Northern California’s Premiere Cocktail Lounge in Sacramento and Emeryville. We are a fine drinking establishment, perfect for an intimate gathering. We can accommodate groups with up 50 guests, catering options available.  
  
Hidden in plain sight, the Trophy Club offers creative craft cocktails and an exclusive and discreet environment with a secret entrance. Experience our handcrafted cocktails curated from our extensive liquor selection, house dehydrated fruit garnishes, barrel aged liquors, aromatics, and more!
