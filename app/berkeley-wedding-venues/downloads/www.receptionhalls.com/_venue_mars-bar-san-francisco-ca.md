# Mars Bar San Francisco, California

![Photo of Mars Bar](/media/CA/11528/mars-bar-san-francisco-ca.jpg)

Mars Bar and Restaurant has been a San Francisco staple and neighborhood favorite for over a decade. Located in SF's SOMA district @ 7th and Brannan, Mars Bar is the perfect place to begin an evening, end your work day or grab a mid-day bite or drink with delicious eats, great cocktails and a warn friendly staff. Guest can dine indoors or outside on our infamous heated patio.
