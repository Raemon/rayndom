# The Mansfield Barn Jericho, Vermont

Located in idyllic Jericho, Vermont, The Mansfield Barn is a meticulously renovated post and beam Vermont Wedding Barn, originally built in the 1840's. The property has been lovingly restored and converted to a unique and charming Vermont wedding venue for weddings and special events. Located at the foot of Mount Mansfield, The Mansfield Barn is a pristine Northern Vermont Wedding Barn venue.
