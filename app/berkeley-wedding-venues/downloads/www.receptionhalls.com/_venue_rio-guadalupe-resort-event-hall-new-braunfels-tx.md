# Rio Guadalupe Resort Event Hall New Braunfels, Texas

Rio Guadalupe Resort’s event hall overlooks the Guadalupe River and is the perfect setting for intimate wedding receptions, family reunions, church events, an RV Club Rally – you name it!  
  
Deck with Spectacular Guadalupe River Views  
Wi-fi Connectivity  
Handicap Accessible  
Tables and Chairs for up to 64 Guests  
Air-conditioned & Heated  
Two Refrigerators  
Microwave  
Oven & Range Top  
Beautiful 14′ Pecan Serving Counter  
Overnight guests of the Resort may receive a discount when renting the event hall. Discounts are also available for river rafting and tubing trips. Available for reservations year-round. Recommendations for local catering services available upon request.
