# Bell Tower Bistro & Patisserie Saratoga, California

"On the corner of the beautiful and quaint downtown Saratoga, the Bell Tower Bistro is a local favorite spot for breakfast, brunch, lunch and coffee &amp; dessert. Our menu includes a wide variety of breakfast options, crepes, desserts, hot and cold beverages, French pastries, European sandwiches, salads, and more. Vegetarian and vegan options are also available.   
  
Our produce is always local and organic and our sauces, buns, fries, chips, and pastries are made in-house. You can’t beat that.  
  
The Bell Tower Bistro is family-friendly, dog-friendly, great for groups, and perfect for anyone looking for a delicious sit-down meal or a quick bite!"
