# Wind Walker Homestead Spring City, Utah

Wind Walker Homestead Wedding Venue has a 2400 square foot dining/banquet hall that has a large commercial stainless kitchen with 2 double ovens, fridges and freezers. Bathroom and shower room & a new brides room fall 2020. Large fireplace makes this a cozy space in the cooler times of the year. Also an 8000 foot manicured lawn area and fire-pit adjacent to the building. 50 mbs wifi. Great space for family reunions and weddings. Call for availability and pricing for weddings.  
Also has an outdoor patio with bistro chairs and tables that overlook the lawn area. The building is rustic timber posts and newly painted interior walls in 2020. There is also central heating and a large A/C unit that keeps the space comfortable. Call or email with requests for more details and additional pics.  
Wind Walker Homestead also has onsite Ranchettes with 2 bed/3 bath, 1000 foot units as well as studios that sleep 3, 5, 6 ,8, 10 or 12 guests in Kings, queens, full and twin beds. We are in central Utah where the skys are clear, the stars bright and the hospitality real. We look forward to welcoming you to help with your very special wedding day.
