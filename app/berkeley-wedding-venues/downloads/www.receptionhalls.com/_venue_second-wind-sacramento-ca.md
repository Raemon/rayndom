# Second Wind Sacramento, California

Looking for a charmingly rustic wedding location that feels like you're far from town, but is conveniently located in the town of Freeport? Check out Second Wind! Six acres located along the Delta in Freeport, CA. Featuring a rustic barn, mature oak trees, grassy area and outdoor fire pit. Twinkle lights, music, laughter and love- this is the perfect setting!
