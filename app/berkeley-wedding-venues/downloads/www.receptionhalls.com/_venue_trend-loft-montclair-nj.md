# Trend Loft Montclair, New Jersey

Trend Loft is your blank slate for your next intimate gathering, art show or office/meeting space. It is convenient to trains and buses, and a municipal parking lot is located behind the building. Situated across from the Wellmont Theater and down the street from the Montclair Film Festival and the Montclair Art Museum, an area known for visual and performing arts. The space offers a built in bar and features two rooms, perfect for Pre and Post Theater Parties, Art Shows, Pop-up Events, Rehearsal Dinners, Bridal and Baby Showers, Birthday Parties, Meetings, Office Space and more.
