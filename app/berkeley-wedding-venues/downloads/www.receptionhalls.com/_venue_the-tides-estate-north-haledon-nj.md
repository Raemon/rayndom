# The Tides Estate Haledon, New Jersey

Your special day has finally arrived and The Tides is ready to greet you with an elegant choice of settings and service that is fast, friendly and caring. Our employees are eager to please you, try to anticipate your needs and respond personally to questions and problems.  
  
Catering options are inventive, beautifully presented and as far from the ordinary as your imagination will allow.  
  
We cater banquets of up to 375 guests, complete with floral arrangements, ice carvings, impeccable table settings and service to match. Or, we can put together smaller receptions with hors d'oeuvres, bar service and special niceties. Red and white wines are served with dinner, the open bar is always top shelf.  
  
Professional chefs, long associated with The Tides, prepare feasts of the finest ingredients. There's even 7 acres of magnificent gardens for photographs. The ideal setting for outdoor marriage ceremonies!
