# The Ocean Club Salter Path, North Carolina

The Ocean Club Wedding Venue is the premier coastal destination for a picture perfect wedding celebration. Ceremonies on the white sand beach, in our palm tree gardens overlooking the pool or on the sound overlooking the inter~coastal waterway. The choice is yours! Dine either inside or outside on our covered veranda and dance under the stars. The Ocean Club will create exquisite memories of your wedding day that will last a lifetime. Because you are the bride~
