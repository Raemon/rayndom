# East Village Tavern + Bowl San Diego, California

Opened in 2007 in East Village, the heart of Downtown San Diego. We are 2 blocks from Petco Park and 6 blocks from San Diego Convention Center. East Village Tavern+Bowl (EVT) is the ideal venue to host your reception, welcome party, or bachelor/bachelorette party. Whether it's an action-packed bowling tournament or your annual sales meeting, we'll do all the work.   
  
The Events Menu is filled with comfort foods from appetizers like sliders, wings & flatbreads, extending to full buffet-style meal featuring short ribs, salmon, & more. Our full-service bar has 30 tap handles, another 15 bottled and canned beers, PLUS a wine and spirits program!  
  
With 12 lanes of furious bowling action, the largest LED screen downtown + 30 more glowing flat-screen TVs, pool tables & board/garden-style games, there’s tons of action to keep the party rolling.
