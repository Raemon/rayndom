# Westwood Country Club Vienna, Virginia

Westwood Country Club is a club located in Northern VA, right outside of Tysons. Our large ballroom overlooks our beautiful 18 hole golf course, making it the perfect venue for any ceremony or reception needs. The golf course and gazebo are a great backdrop for any wedding photos or portrait engagement photos.
