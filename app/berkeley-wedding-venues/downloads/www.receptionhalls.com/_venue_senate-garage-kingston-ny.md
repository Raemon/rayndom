# Senate Garage Kingston, New York

Senate Garage is a dramatic industrial event venue in the Hudson Valley, within the beautiful grounds of the Senate House in the heart of Uptown Kingston's Stockade District. This converted 1921 garage is available for weddings, corporate functions and celebrations of all kinds.
