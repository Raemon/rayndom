# Clarion Inn & Summit Center Tupelo, Mississippi

The Summit Center is part of the Clarion Inn and Rodeway Inn @ Suites. It is a 14,000 sq. foot banquet facility built for varies types of events. It consists of 3 removable walls capable of creating up to 4 separate rooms, all with audio and video.
