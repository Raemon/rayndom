# The Great Hall & Conference Center Germantown, Tennessee

The Great Hall is more than 8,600 square feet of class, perfect for parties and receptions or luncheons and banquets.  
  
Add the high-tech Conference Center Theatre, and the entire facility becomes a contemporary conference center, perfect for meetings, presentations, training sessions and conferences, as well as trade shows.
