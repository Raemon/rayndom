# SLPS Event Center Irving, Texas

SLPS Event Center is the perfect site for any occasion that demands a touch of sophistication and elegance. Our luxurious ballroom can host up to 1100 guests reception style and 2000 guest theater style seating. It comes fully equipped with built-in screens, projectors and state-of-the-art audio/visual equipment. To help create the perfect ambiance for your occasion, turn to the attentive and professional coordinators of the SLPS Event Center. Whether you envision an intimate commemoration with just you and your beloved, or a festive gathering of family and friends, rest assured every detail will be meticulously handled to perfection.  
  
Experienced SLPS Event Center staff members can help you pick from many packages and customizable floor plans to rival any weddings, all pre wedding events, quinceañeras, birthday parties, anniversaries and corporate events. Our attentive experts will be with you every step of the way, taking the stress out of event planning and proper execution. If you should have any questions or concerns, please do not hesitate to call or email us. We look forward to assisting you with all of your needs.
