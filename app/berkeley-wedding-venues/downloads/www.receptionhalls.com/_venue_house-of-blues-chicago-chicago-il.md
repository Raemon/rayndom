# House of Blues Chicago Chicago, Illinois

Presenting a new era in special event venues, House of Blues is the place to have your reception. An exciting menu featuring traditional American cuisine with a Southern flare will amaze your guests. Our state-of-the-art sound, lighting and staging offers guests of 20 or 1,800 a richly unique experience.  
  
With access to the world’s greatest musical entertainment, state-of-the-art lighting and sound, custom menus and mixology, this phenomenal 55,000 square-foot entertainment arena is part Mississippi Delta juke joint and part 1920s opera house. The four-level space is comprised of three distinctive environments including a VIP members-only club, a rocking Restaurant & Bar featuring contemporary and traditional Southern art and cuisine and a spectacular multi-story Music Hall.
