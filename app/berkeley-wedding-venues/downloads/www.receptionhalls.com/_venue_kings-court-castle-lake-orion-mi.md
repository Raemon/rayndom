# Kings Court Castle Lake Orion, Michigan

Plan your event in a luxurious setting like no other, a masterpiece of architecture and expert craftsmanship built in the historic setting of Canterbury Village in beautiful Lake Orion, Michigan. What bride-to-be could resist her Fairy Tale wedding in an elegant, romantic castle ballroom?   
  
It is also perfect for a formal gathering or business setting. Whether it is a dinner buffet, family style dinner or an individually plated meal you are looking for, we have something to accommodate everyone. So visit King's Court Castle and experience its charm and grandeur for a lifetime of memories.
