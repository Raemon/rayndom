# Rococo Theatre Lincoln, Nebraska

The most elegant venue in town is available for your private event. Your guests are sure to be captivated and both ceremony's and receptions performed at the Rococo Theatre have always been enchanting. Included with your rental is:  
  
\- Use of the Theatre (stage and all tables/chairs) from noon til midnight  
\- In house bar  
\- 20'x40' cinema style screen  
\- Name on the marquee  
\- State of the Art Lighting and Sound  
-Within walking distance of hotels and parking  
  
The Rococo's gracious and knowledgeable staff will delight you with their ability to anticipate your every need. No detail, however small, will be overlooked so that you can relax and enjoy your once-in-a-lifetime celebration with your family and friends.
