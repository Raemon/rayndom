# On a Roll Madison, New Jersey

Located in Madison, NJ, On a Roll is a gourmet deli and caterer offering the finest selection of gourmet entrees, soups, salads and gourmet sandwiches available. We will work with your to meet your needs for any occasion.  
  
While we do not offer our space as a venue, we can deliver your order to wherever your event is taking place. We also provide set-up, servers and bartenders for your convenience.
