# The Norwalk Art Space Norwalk, Connecticut

The Norwalk Art Space at ADK House is debuting a contemporary, airy, and artistic new event space, conveniently located off I-95 in Norwalk. Featuring a 2,200sf gallery, versatile loft space, separate 450sf dining area, and outdoor patio, ADK House offers many flexible options for your next special event. Please contact events@thenorwalkartspace.org with any questions.
