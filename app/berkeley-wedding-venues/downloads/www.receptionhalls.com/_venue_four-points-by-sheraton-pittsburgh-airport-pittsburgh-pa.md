# Four Points by Sheraton Pittsburgh Pittsburgh, Pennsylvania

Nestled on a quiet hilltop just six miles from the airport and 18 miles from downtown Pittsburgh, the Four Points by Sheraton, Pittsburgh Airport is an ideal destination for both business and leisure travelers. Located in the RIDC Park West complex, many local corporations including US Airways, Dick's Sporting Goods and Computer Associates are easily accessible.
