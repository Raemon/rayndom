# THE HIDEAWAY BANQUET AND EVENTS HALL Las Vegas, Nevada

The Hideaway is a Banquet and Events Hall located in Las Vegas, Nevada, within minutes south of the famous Las Vegas Strip and the Allegiant Stadium.  
  
Recently renovated now accommodating up to 100 guests and is elegant, intimate and affordable. Our services include weddings, receptions, bridal showers, baby showers, birthday parties, holiday parties, retirement parties, celebrations of life, graduation parties, quinceaneras, custom events and more!  
  
This 3,000 square foot space amenities includes Tables and Chairs, Photo Area, Bar Area, Sound Booth (WI-FI, Bluetooth, Audio Speakers, Cordless Mics) Bridal/Changing Room, Serving Area, Kitchen and Balcony area!  
  
The Hideaway is a family-owned and operated company that offers event planning services that cater to your every need. Our venue features Laurens Lavish Creations and Rentals.  
  
If you're looking for the perfect space to host a wedding and reception for up to 50 guests check us out! What you don't see will surprise you!  
  
Additional services available from this accommodating venue include:  
  
Catering  
Setup services  
Cleanup services  
Event Planning  
Event Rentals
