# Loray Mill Event Hall Gastonia, North Carolina

Loray Mill Event Hall is part of a renovated textile mill located in Gastonia, NC.This unique venue has exposed original brick, high bay ceilings, and antique windows. The possibilities are endless in this 3,164 square feet hall that is perfect for your next special occasion. Come make your event history at Loray Mill Event Hall.
