# StoneBriar Farm De Soto, Kansas

Want to plan your next event surrounded by a beautiful Kansas backdrop, but want to stay local? StoneBriar Farms is for you. Conveniently located off 10 highway in DeSoto, KS, StoneBriar Farms is the perfect location for your event. Our spacious indoor event space is handicap accessible and comfortably seats 275 people. Amenities include changing facilities for weddings, a prep area designed in consultation with caterers, and a beautiful bar area with access to both the indoor hall and covered outdoor patio. Need more space? Our large adjacent green space is ideal for tents, food trucks or outdoor activities.
