# Nassau Inn Princeton, New Jersey

[ Please Call ](tel:609-921-7500)

Since 1756, the Nassau Inn has been the meeting place of distinction in Princeton, well known for its gracious hospitality and elegant facilities. From its convenient location in the heart of downtown - directly across from Princeton University - to its rustic ambiance and sophisticated charm, the historic Nassau Inn is one of New Jersey's premier hotels.  
  
Specializing in business functions, the Nassau Inn features distinctive corporate menus, private meeting rooms, conference suites and a grand ballroom to meet every business need.  
  
Our Sales Managers are available for every aspect of your event planning. A special day deserves both the catering and banquet expertise that has become our signature. Our exceptional attention to detail will make your wedding or social affair an elegant and extraordinary occasion to remember.
