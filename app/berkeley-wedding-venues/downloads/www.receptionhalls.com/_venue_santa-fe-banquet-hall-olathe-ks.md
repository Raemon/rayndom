# Santa Fe Banquet Hall Olathe, Kansas

Santa Fe Banquet Hall is a multipurpose venue with plenty of parking located in historic downtown Olathe, KS. Our large, spacious hall is ideal for a number of different types of events. With the ability to accommodate up to 200 persons. We are a large hall, but we maintain reasonable prices with our clients’ best interest in mind. Planning an event of any size can be a time consuming process. With so many details to consider, take comfort in knowing that our experience will help.
