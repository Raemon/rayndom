# Rex Manor Catering Hall Brooklyn, New York

The Rex Manor has been a New York landmark for over 75 years. One of the few remaining catering halls located in Brooklyn, the Rex Manor offers excellence in service and cuisine. Our outstanding and experienced staff always strives to make every affair a special one.  
  
On June 8, 2009, the Rex Manor celebrated its 25th anniversary under its most recent ownership. John Kafkalas, Chris Markakis, and Steve Valadakis welcome you to share their love of good food and good friends in an atmosphere of warmth and hospitality. They promise to do their best to make your special occasion a memorable one. Many local schools, hospitals, churches, groups, clubs, and organizations have been part of the Rex Manor family. The Rex Manor has long been a part of the Brooklyn community and has served generations of families throughout the years. Although many of our customers have relocated, they still return to the Rex Manor to cater their cherished events.  
  
From corporate dinners to elegant weddings, the Rex Manor ensures that every affair is handled elegantly and tastefully. Events numbering 25 to 400 will be celebrated with the same courteous service and attention to detail. We offer the finest and freshest dishes and take pride in the quality of our menus. We would be honored to offer our services to you.  
  
Our banquet managers welcome the opportunity to assist you with party planning. With expertise and professionalism, our staff will guide you throughout your party experience. We look forward to making your day one you will never forget.
