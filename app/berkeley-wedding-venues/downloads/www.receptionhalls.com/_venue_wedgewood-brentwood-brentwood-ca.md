# Wedgewood Brentwood Brentwood, California

![Photo of Wedgewood Brentwood](/media/CA/9768/wedgewood-brentwood-brentwood-ca.jpg)

Located off the new 4 Freeway bypass in Brentwood is one of the most pristine event locations in Northern California. Wedgewood Wedding and Banquet Center at Brentwood Golf Club gives any guest the country club atmosphere without the country club price tag.  
  
Some of the spectacular, unique features of Brentwood:  
  
* Fabulous setting just to the east of Mount Diablo  
* Beautifully manicured grounds with a keenly-placed banquet room  
* Banquet room with a wall of windows overlooking the golf course, lake, and waterfalls  
* Dedicated wedding site with fountain, arbor, and designer stone  
* Event seating capacity of up to 350 people, with the ability to intimately seat groups as low as 50.
