# New Beginnings Historic Farm Rutherfordton, North Carolina

Step back into the 1900's with historic home and farm with surrounding rolling hills. Western North Carolina and New Beginnings Historic Farm is the perfect place for your special event. The 1910 farm house beautifully decorated in antique furniture is a perfect backdrop for your dream wedding. Bridal couples can choose from wide range of customizable wedding packages to suite almost every budget. We offer outdoor garden parties intimate indoor gatherings in our historic home or combination to fit your particular needs.
