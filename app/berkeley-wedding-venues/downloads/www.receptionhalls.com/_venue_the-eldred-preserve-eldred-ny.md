# The Eldred Preserve Eldred, New York

Surrounded by the natural beauty of the Catskill Mountains, The Eldred Preserve is a luxury wedding venue featuring a variety of formal event spaces, both indoors and outdoors. This exquisite property couples modern luxury with the rustic charm of the Sullivan Catskills, making it the perfectly unique venue for any small- to medium-sized wedding celebration (up to 100 people).  
  
Our delicious catering menu boasts farm-fresh ingredients, and you're sure to find a package that suits your needs. Our food and beverage team is always happy to create a custom menu, or suggest alternative selections to meet the dietary needs of your guests.  
  
Our dedicated events team is committed to making your special day even more memorable at The Eldred Preserve, and will work with you every step of the way to ensure your wedding features the refined luxury that guests will immediately recognize as second-to-none.
