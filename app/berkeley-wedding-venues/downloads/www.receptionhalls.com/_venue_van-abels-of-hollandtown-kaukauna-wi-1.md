# Van Abel's of Hollandtown Kaukauna, Wisconsin

Van Abel's of Hollandtown is a historic wedding venue that has been hosting the greater Fox Valley and Green Bay's Milestone events for over 70 years. Our event coordinators can assist your planning needs to help you create a memorable event of a lifetime. Whether it is an elaborate wedding or a gathering of close family and friends, Van Abel’s is here to take your event from imagination to celebration.
