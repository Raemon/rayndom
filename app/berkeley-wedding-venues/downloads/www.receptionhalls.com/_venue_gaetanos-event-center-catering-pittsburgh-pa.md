# Gaetano's Event Center & Catering Pittsburgh, Pennsylvania

Let us add our personal touch to your special day. Gaetanos' offers 3 private rooms accommodating up to 300 guests for your wedding reception, ceremony, business meeting or special event. Each room has a bar and dance floor. You will find our complete packages to have delicious menu options with affordable pricing. Located one mile south of downtown Pittsburgh, Gaetano's is the perfect location for your next event.  
  
Off-site Catering is our specialty. We will bring our quality food and service to your office, party room, outside space or home. You may choose from one of our prearranged packages or we can customize a menu to suit your needs. We can also provide linens, glassware, decorations and servers.
