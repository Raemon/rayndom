# Colorado National Golf Club Erie, Colorado

Congratulations on your engagement!   
  
We would be honored to have the opportunity to be a part of your special day. At Colorado National Golf Club, The views of Long’s Peak and the Rocky Mountain Range, along with our eighteen hole golf course, create a perfect backdrop for any special occasion.  
  
The Ceremony Site is ideally suited for groups up to 200 people with an expansive view of the Front Range. Our impressive Master’s Restaurant, President’s Room and Patio can host receptions for as many as 200 guests.   
  
Please know that we are happy to work with you to design a custom menu and create an event that is unique to your specific needs. As always, feel free to give us a call with any questions or requests you have with regards to your special day. Our professional onsite coordinator is ready to handle all of your needs.
