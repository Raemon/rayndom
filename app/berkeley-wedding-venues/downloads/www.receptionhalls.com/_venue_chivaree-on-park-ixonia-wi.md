# Chivaree on Park Ixonia, Wisconsin

Constructed in 1949, Chivaree on Park is a newly renovated wedding and events venue located in Ixonia, Wisconsin. Combining nostalgic elegance with industrial glam design elements, the versatile interior spaces are well suited for intimate dinners to large wedding reception and galas.
