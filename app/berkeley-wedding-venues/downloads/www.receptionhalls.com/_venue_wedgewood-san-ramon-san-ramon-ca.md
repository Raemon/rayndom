# Wedgewood San Ramon San Ramon, California

![Photo of Wedgewood San Ramon](/media/CA/9773/wedgewood-san-ramon-san-ramon-ca.jpg)

Located in the heart of the San Ramon Valley is one of the top Contra Costa County wedding locations. Wedgewood Wedding and Banquet Center at San Ramon Golf Club combines a beautiful outdoor Northern California wedding venue with the Value, Service, and Convenience that is Wedgewood’s standard.  
  
Some of the spectacular, unique features of our San Ramon wedding location:  
  
* Winner of the “Best of the Knot 2008-2009 Wedding Venues”  
* Wedding site features a gazebo set under a giant willow tree in front of the golf course’s lake  
* 400 seat banquet room overlooking the golf course, featuring a fireplace, sunken bar, and entire wall of windows overlooking the golf course  
* Largest bridal dressing room in Contra Costa County, featuring floor-to-ceiling mirrors and handsome antique armoire
