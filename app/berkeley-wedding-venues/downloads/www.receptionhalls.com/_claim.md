##  Venue Owners/Managers - Claim Your Listing 

## How do I claim my listing to make changes?

If you no longer have access to the email account associated with your listing, please [create a new account](https://www.receptionhalls.com/register) and follow the links to [claim your listing](https://www.receptionhalls.com/owners/claim/request). If you have questions, please [contact us](https://www.receptionhalls.com/contact) or email help@receptionhalls.com directly. 

## How did my venue get listed on ReceptionHalls?

ReceptionHalls has been online for **over 20 years** \--Wow! If you're not sure how or when your venue was listed, it was likely posted by a previous employee. There's no need to worry. Just [create a new account](https://www.receptionhalls.com/register) and we'll help you recover access to your venue's listing. If you have questions, [contact us](https://www.receptionhalls.com/contact) at any time. 

## I already have an account, but forgot my password.

No problem. You can [reset your password](https://www.receptionhalls.com/password/reset).
