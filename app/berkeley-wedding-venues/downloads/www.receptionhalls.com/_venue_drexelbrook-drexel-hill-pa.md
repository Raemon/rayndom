# Drexelbrook Drexel Hill, Pennsylvania

At Drexelbrook, we are committed to creating you own personal wedding experience one detail at time. We have been creating wedding memories for over 50 years. Generations of families choose Drexelbrook for our elegant atmosphere, superior service and exceptional cuisine.  
  
We have designed your wedding experience with you and your guests in mind; from the planning, to the day of, and after, we will be there every step of the way. We encourage you to visit us to experience the beauty and elegance of the Mansion and the Grandeur of our new Grand Ballrooms.  
  
Our Afternoon Receptions begin at $80 per guest, Evening receptions begins at $115 per guest. Special consideration in pricing is available upon request for Saturday afternoon, Sunday and some Friday receptions.
