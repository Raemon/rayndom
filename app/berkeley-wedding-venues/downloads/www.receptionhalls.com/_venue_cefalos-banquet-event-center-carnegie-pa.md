# Cefalo's Banquet & Event Center Carnegie, Pennsylvania

Cefalo’s can offer a truly unique wedding reception or special event for parties of up to 200 guests. The owners take pride in their attention to detail and hands-on approach. We will act as your event coordinator during your planning and on the day of your event, so you can relax and enjoy your occasion. Whether it be your wedding, baby shower, birthday, corporate event or class reunion, your party is a memorable event, and Cefalo’s is the perfect venue for you and your guests.
