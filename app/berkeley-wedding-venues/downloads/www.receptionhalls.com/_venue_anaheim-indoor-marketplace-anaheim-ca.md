# Anaheim Indoor Marketplace Anaheim, California

The Studio is a versatile, multi-purpose facility for weddings, quinceañeras, meetings, receptions, and conferences. The Studio includes 23 round tables, 3 square tables, 200 chairs, a stage &amp; dance floor, clean restrooms, a service room, cleaning service, security and plenty of free parking. The spacious room can accommodate up to 299. Other services available are: decoration, a projector, two television screens, a DJ, catering service, and photos &amp; video service. The Marketplace is within close proximity to Disneyland and easy access to the 5 Freeway. Host your next event at this charming location.   
  
The Placita is an outdoor banquet hall and includes a covered patio. The outdoor facility site is ideal for any occasion and can accommodate up to 200 guests. Spaces are available for small gatherings or for a special event. The facility has an onsite restaurant for catering services and a snack bar option offering a vast array of fruits. The Placita also offers a sport facility rental option to host any type of sporting event.
