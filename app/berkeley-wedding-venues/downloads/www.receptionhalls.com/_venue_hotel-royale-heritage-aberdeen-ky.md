# Hotel Royale Heritage Aberdeen, Kentucky

Hotel Royale Heritage is a 60-room hotel property spread over lush green expanse of 1.25 acres in the heart of Nasik city. It is located at the confluence of the traditional city with temples and the ghat's as well as the modern city with restaurants, cafe's, parks, malls and industries.  
  
Rooms - We provide 1, 2,3,4 and 5 Bedded family rooms, couples rooms with private terrace or balcony with cross ventilation on the upper floors of the hotel having 60 AC/NON-AC 1 to 5 bedded rooms and dormitory also available. We have ground floor opening in the centre square. We also have a common sit out, resort like atmosphere, low rise structure, laid back atmosphere, green open spaces.
