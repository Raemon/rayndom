# The Yellow Barn at Shenandoah Caverns Quicksburg, Virginia

![Photo of The Yellow Barn at Shenandoah Caverns](/media/VA/12043/the-yellow-barn-at-shenandoah-caverns-quicksburg-va.jpg)

Make your event unforgettable at the Yellow Barn! Host your wedding at a unique barn venue, only 2 minutes from I-81 in the heart of the beautiful Shenandoah Valley. We're less than 2 hours from D.C.   
  
The Yellow Barn offers outdoor ceremonies with indoor receptions for up to 150 guests, or indoor reception seating for up to 300.   
  
The Barn features over 4,500 sq ft of event space surrounded by antique carriages and farm equipment. The building is heated and air conditioned with full restrooms. It is also equipped with a catering kitchen. We don't require that you book specific vendors; you can use whoever you prefer. We have a list of local vendors available if needed.  
  
Make your wedding a memorable occasion! Contact us for more information.
