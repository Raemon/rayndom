# Buttonwood Manor Matawan, New Jersey

[ Please Call ](tel:732-566-6220)

Overlooking picturesque Lake Lefferts, the Buttonwood Manor provides the perfect setting for your special day. There are two main banquet rooms available, the Greenhouse on the main level, looking out onto a garden, will accommodate up to 125 guests. The Caribee Room on the lower level, accommodating up to 275, features a panoramic view of the lake as well as an outdoor deck which can also be used for ceremonies and cocktail hour. Smaller rooms for more intimate affairs are also available. All rooms are wheelchair accessible.  
  
The lovely outdoor gardens provide a marvelous backdrop for wedding pictures.  
  
A fully trained staff will work with you to plan and coordinate your affair from choosing your linens and flower arrangements to selecting your entree, all of which are included in the package price. Also included, a cocktail hour with 150 premium liquors and hot and cold hors d'oeuvres served butler style, the wedding cake and, with our compliments, a champagne toast to the bride and groom. Valet parking is also provided.  
  
The Buttonwood Manor has been creating beautiful weddings for over 50 years, and we hope you will allow us to <i>"Make your Special Day a Dream Come True"</i>.
