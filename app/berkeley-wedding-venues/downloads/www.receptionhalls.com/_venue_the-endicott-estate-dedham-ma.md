# The Endicott Estate Dedham, Massachusetts

A gracious and elegant turn of the century mansion located on over 18 acres of lush green lawn punctuated by stately elm, spruce and weeping willow trees which offers the perfect combination of sophistication and warmth.  
The mansion is decorated in period furnishings, works of art, italian marble fireplaces, rich paneling, and classic oriental carpeting. The open floor plan was especially designed for entertaining which opens to a beautiful veranda overlooking the pristine grounds.
