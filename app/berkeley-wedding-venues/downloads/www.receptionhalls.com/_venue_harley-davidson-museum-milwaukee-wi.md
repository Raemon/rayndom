# 1903 Events at the Harley-Davidson Museum Milwaukee, Wisconsin

Rooted in timeless quality, authenticity and hospitality, 1903 Events at the Harley-Davidson Museum showcases customizable menus centered around the craft of food and beverage. Bold and original, we will bring your event to life.  
  
With an awe-inspiring design, the Harley-Davidson Museum is set among 20 acres in downtown Milwaukee and is surrounded on three sides by the Menomonee River. The various indoor and outdoor spaces can accommodate intimate meetings for 10 or campus-wide bashes for up to 10,000.
