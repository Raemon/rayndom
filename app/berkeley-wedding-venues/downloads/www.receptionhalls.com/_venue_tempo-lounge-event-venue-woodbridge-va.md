# Tempo Lounge & Event Venue Woodbridge, Virginia

Come experience Tempo! Tempo Lounge & Events Venue features 7,400 square feet of open space with 14-foot ceilings and is designed to host event experiences of all kinds. Our clients absolutely love the mezzanine level that overlooks the main hall. Whether a group of 20 or 300, Tempo offers an upscale industrial look and is sure to meet your venue needs. You will not find anything similar offered within miles of our location. It’s the perfect space for private or corporate events. Host your milestone event, corporate party, launch party, music showcase, fashion showcase, or live entertainment here. It is a great space for a podcast, a photo shoot, or even a music video.   
  
Our lounge clients come for the Vibe, but our private event clients go with us because of the sound system, LED uplights, 12’ projector screen and projector, and the music themed interior. The space also includes tables, chairs, and the availability of a wealth of event décor services to include linens, backdrops, tablescapes, and florals.   
  
Our neighborhood lounge and event venue, situated just steps from Route 1 in Dumfries VA, brings you all the things you love – great food, excellent drinks, awesome music, top-notch customer service and most important – the Vibe. To meet your culinary needs, Tempo Lounge & Events Venue and Creation Kitchen have fused their expertise to offer you an extraordinary food experience. Creation Kitchen, a full-service catering company, founded by Executive Chef, Reggie Anderson Jr, brings unique and unforgettable flavors to make your experience memorable. Reggie’s passion for service and creating unique culinary experiences has been cultivated over the last 20+ years. Whether you imagine interactive food stations, passed hors d'oeuvres, a plated meal or served buffet, our team can execute flawlessly. Come celebrate food, relationships, milestones, and accomplishments at Tempo.
