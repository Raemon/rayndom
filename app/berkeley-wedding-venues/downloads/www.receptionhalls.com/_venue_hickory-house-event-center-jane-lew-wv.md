# Hickory House Event Center Jane Lew, West Virginia

Our event venue is available for any special occasion needs. We offer seating up to 250 guests, along with event planning, and catering services. Our talented caterers prepare all food homemade for you the day of your event. We also have a beautiful landscape for excellent photo opportunities, or even an exclusive outdoor wedding ceremony. Our event coordinator will help you out every step of the way, in order for your day to be exactly what you envision.
