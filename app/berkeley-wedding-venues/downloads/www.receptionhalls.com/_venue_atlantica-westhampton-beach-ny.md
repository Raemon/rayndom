# Atlantica Westhampton Beach, New York

![Photo of Atlantica](/media/NY/77/atlantica-westhampton-beach-ny.jpg)

What do you get when you combine the "best chef in the hamptons"  
  
with the most stunning beach on Long Island?  
  
You get an unforgettable place to hold your once-in-a-lifetime event.  
  
With 3 walls of glass providing panoramic views of the beach, this location is one of a kind. The food is outrageous and is prepared by the staff of Tierra Mar.  
  
Please call us at 631.288.6577 and make an appointment to speak with Ginny. Together, you will plan your menu and get ready for the most spectacular event of your life.  
  
We are open all year for weddings and private parties.   
  
3 walls of glass provide panoramic ocean views with seating for up to 200 people and cocktail receptions for up to 300 people.
