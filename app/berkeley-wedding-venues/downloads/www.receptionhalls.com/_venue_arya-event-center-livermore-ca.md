# Arya Event Center Livermore, California

The Arya Event Center in Livermore is the newest event venue located in the San Francisco Bay Area. Book this private and luxurious venue located next to the lush rolling Livermore hills. We host weddings, social events, receptions, and corporate meetings.  
  
Nestled within the Livermore, California hills, the Arya Event Center is a new 18,500 square foot venue. We would love to host your wedding, reception, birthday, anniversary, or corporate events. Contact us today to learn about availability, pricing, menus, and other policies.  
  
Epic Ballroom  
  
Our exquisite ballroom was professionally designed with porcelain floors, custom woodworking on every wall, and modern lighting systems for your event to be unforgettable. The grand ballroom can be configured to your taste with multiple full bars and room for over 500 guests.  
  
Entrance  
  
High ceilings, custom lighting, and chandeliers set the Arya Event Center entrance apart. Our entranceway was designed to make a long-lasting impression. Multiple spaces can be combined or sectioned off to your desire.  
  
Bridal Suite  
  
The Arya Event Center has multiple suites that can be used as your Bridal Suite. Tastefully designed to cater to the bride and her closest family and friends, the suite features private dressing rooms, full-length mirrors, and makeup areas.  
  
Commercial Kitchen  
  
Our full commercial kitchen is accessible from multiple entrances. Our stainless steel laced, the fully loaded kitchen is a great place for prepping and cooking meals for your events. Equipped with ovens, stoves, a full-size commercial hood, and is perfect for your caterer's needs.
