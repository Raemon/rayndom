# Deer Valley Lodge Barneveld, Wisconsin

Deer Valley Lodge is the perfect venue to host your Rehearsal Dinner, Ceremony, Wedding Reception, Gift Opening, and even your overnight Guests. Our 5,000 sq ft Banquet Hall, overlooking our 27 Hole Golf Course, can accommodate 400 people. The Facility consists of a Full Service Bar, on-site Chef, Large Outdoor Patio & Wedding Gazebo with Golf Course View, in-house Event Coordinators, 62 Hotel Rooms, 12,000 square foot Water Park, and a Built-In Dance Floor. We will assist you in planning for your event, take care of set-up and clean-up, as well as have an Event Coordinator on site the day of your event to make sure your day is perfect. Deer Valley Lodge will make the perfect backdrop for your Special Day! We would love to send you a Wedding Packet complete with pricing, pictures, and more information. Email TheLodge@DeerValleyLodgeWI.com or call 608-924-1600 and ask for one of our Event Coordinators.
