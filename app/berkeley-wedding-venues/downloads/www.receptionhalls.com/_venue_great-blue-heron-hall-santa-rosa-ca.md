# Great Blue Heron Hall Santa Rosa, California

The Laguna Environmental Center is home to the Great Blue Heron Hall, a 1,540 square foot event space with 1,200 square foot patio and magnificent views of the Laguna and Mt. St. Helena. The barn style building is designed to fit naturally into the working farm site with its 140 year agricultural history. Heron Hall can comfortably host up to 150 guests and provides convenient rest rooms with green features, multiple microphones and built-in speakers, and a large 14’ft screen and projector. Other highlights of the hall include a caterer-friendly kitchen equipped with all new kitchen appliances.
