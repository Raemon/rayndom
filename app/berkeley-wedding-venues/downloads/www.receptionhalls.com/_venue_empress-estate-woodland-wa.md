# Empress Estate Woodland, Washington

Unforgettable moments await at The Empress Estate. Situated on breathtaking gardens and woodlands with views of the valley and Lewis River, this estate is a rare find that offers character and privacy that will be exclusively yours for your special occasion. We have mastered the art of events, along with the details and service that go into making that event a success. We pride ourselves on meeting high standards and creating a truly distinctive occasion, from the décor and service to the culinary delights. Often called a romantic fairytale setting, The Empress Estate is truly one-of-a-kind. Whether searching for the best outdoor or indoor wedding venue, traditional weddings, cultural weddings, or the most modern of celebrations, your day will be truly unique when enjoyed at our estate. We would be pleased to welcome you to The Empress Estate ‘family’ of delighted guests and happy couples.
