# Consortium Venue Shreveport, Louisiana

Consortium Venue is an event facility located at 1925 North Market in Shreveport, Louisiana. Our venue is a multipurpose facility that can host your meetings, luncheons, seminars, weddings, receptions, bridal and baby showers, reunions, any of your holiday gatherings and all of your special occasion events.
