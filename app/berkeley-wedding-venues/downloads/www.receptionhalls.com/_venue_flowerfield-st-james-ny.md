# Flowerfield Saint James, New York

The grounds of this private 56 acre estate include a splendid array of fruit orchards, manicured gardens and wine vineyards, as well as beautiful reflection ponds and fountains. A quaint bridge crosses over the stream onto a small secluded island where in the center you will find a charming gazebo. Flowerfield is the perfect location for elegant affairs, corporate barbecues and outings, as well as smaller events.  
  
Only one event is held at a time in order to ensure your privacy and the undivided attention of our staff during your event. This elegant North Shore space prides itself on flawless presentation and service. Our professional staff of party planners will assist you with every detail to create the event of your dreams.
