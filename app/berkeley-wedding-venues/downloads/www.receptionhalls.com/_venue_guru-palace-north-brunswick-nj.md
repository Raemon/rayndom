# guru palace North Brunswick, New Jersey

A true oasis for fans of Indian cuisine, The Guru Palace serves authentic Indian cuisine in an up market, fine dining setting. Enter the glass doors of the restaurants, and prepare to embark on a gastronomical journey of epic proportions through India.  
  
Dining at The Guru Palace is truly a quest for the senses, a majestic feast for the palate as well as the eyes.  
  
Award wining chef Jasbir Singh is a true artist, painstakingly and lovingly perfecting every single one of his creations right down to the minute details, creating a spectacular culinary experience that resonates in the mind long after the meal is over. Leave all preconceived notions about Indian cuisine at the door, because the meal promises to be a one-of-a-kind experience-traditional Indian cuisine that is given a modern twist, redefining boundaries and stereotypes. The ultimate dining experience, expect first class treatment, all the way to the top-notch, superb selection of sweets to finish off the meal.  
  
Gone are the elaborate, gold-gilted, garish over-the-top decorations that are characteristic of most Indian restaurants. Instead, color changing LED Glass walls. Luxury pervades the interiors with the stylish Water fountains. Hanging Jhula type chairs for couple, cool furniture, warmth of Glass chandelier design as Indian glass bangles which steals all attention. Diners can enjoy savory Indian cuisine against the fine backdrop of color and sheer elegance.
