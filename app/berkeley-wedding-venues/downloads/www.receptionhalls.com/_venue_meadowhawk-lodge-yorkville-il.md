# Meadowhawk Lodge Yorkville, Illinois

Meadowhawk Lodge is nestled in the beautiful Hoover Forest Preserve! Host up to 100 people in this rustic, open floor plan. Put your own stamp on your event with access the entire day before to decorate the venue, bring in decor, food, and beverages. Our staff will work with you to create a floorplan that meets your needs and we will be on hand during your event to ensure the comfort and safety of your guests as well as work with your contracted vendors to ensure a smooth event! Plus, you have the freedom to bring in your own food, if you choose not to have the event catered by one of our Preferred Catering Services. Alcohol is allowed as long as there is a licensed bartender on site, who is a part of our Preferred Bartender list. We provide accommodations onsite in one of three bunkhouses, each featuring a full kitchen and bathroom facilities. Visit https://kendallforest.com/special-events-yorkville.php
