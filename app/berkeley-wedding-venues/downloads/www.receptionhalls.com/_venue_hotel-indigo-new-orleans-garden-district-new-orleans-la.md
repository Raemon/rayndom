# Hotel Indigo New Orleans Garden District New Orleans, Louisiana

![Photo of Hotel Indigo New Orleans Garden District](/media/LA/11043/hotel-indigo-new-orleans-garden-district-new-orleans-la.jpg)

The Hotel Indigo New Orleans Garden District is one of the premier upscale hotels in New Orleans. This St. Charles Avenue hotel is on the Mardi Gras parade route. Step outside our front door to take the St. Charles Avenue Streetcar from our hotel to the French Quarter.
