# H.L. Palmer Masonic Center Milwaukee, Wisconsin

H.L. Palmer Masonic Center offers a large dining hall and kitchen for your event Wedding receptions, birthday parties, family reunions, Quinceanera's, baby showers and other events can be held in the space. The space can be rented from 8am until midnight. We provide use of tables and chairs. You can bring in your own food and drinks or bring in your own caterer of choice. We do not require you to use any certain vendors. Just another way to save money with the do-it-yourself choice. Even hire your own DJ or a band for your event. What if you do not need the whole day special- you can rent it out for only $125 per hour as well!
