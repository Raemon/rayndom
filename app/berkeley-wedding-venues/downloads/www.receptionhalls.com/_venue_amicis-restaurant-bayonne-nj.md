# Amici's Restaurant Bayonne, New Jersey

From Italy with Love describes the excellent cuisine and comfortable ambiance of Amici's (meaning friends) Fine Dining and Martini Lounge. Owner Francis Szklarski has renovated the dining rooms and introduced Bayonne's only martini lounge. The restaurant is also available for weddings, rehearsal dinners, bridal showers, corporate lunches, birthday celebrations, and your special event. Please contact us for more information.
