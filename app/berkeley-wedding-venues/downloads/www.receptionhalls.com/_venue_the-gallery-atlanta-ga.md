# The Gallery Atlanta, Georgia

Our Gallery is perfect for events and productions of all sizes.   
Our space has accommodated everything from music videos and films to large after hour parties.  
The space is separated into a North and South gallery. The galleries can be partitioned off further, making it easy for guests to use a smaller area if desired. Dressing rooms and additional break our rooms are available as add-ons in our main building connected to the warehouse.  
  
Host your next TV show or movie shoot at our facilities!   
We are able to provide a wide variety of spaces for multiple different shots as well as room for your full crew.  
  
Equipped with:  
Bay doors (ground level and raised)  
Handicap restroom
