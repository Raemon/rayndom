# Father Val Hall Powhatan, Virginia

Father Val Hall is an Elegant, Wedding &amp; Event Venue specializing in Wedding Ceremonies &amp; Receptions, Quinceanera's and Special events.  
Father Val Hall is a spacious, attractively decorated venue conveniently located in Powhatan County, 40 minutes from Richmond and 1 hour from Charlottesville.  
  
The Reception Hall is an attractively decorated venue with a cathedral ceiling, elegant chandeliers and built in 20 x 20 dance floor. The room is perfectly designed for 120 to 150 seated dining and 200 for standing reception.   
  
Also included in the rental is a 20 x 30 common area, a fully equipped warming kitchen &amp; large, lighted parking lot.   
  
The venue is part of the St. John Neumann Church complex and has its own entrance, reception hall, kitchen and meeting rooms. One does not have to belong to the church to rent the facility.  
  
The outdoor ceremony site is conveniently situated to the banquet/reception hall &amp; makes it easy for guests to enter the venue after the ceremony. The well landscaped terraced gardens, attractive stone pathway and gazebo create a peaceful, secluded setting to exchange your vows .
