# Razberry's Frenchtown, New Jersey

Razberry’s Banquet center continues to be a highly sought-after location for all types of special occasions. We are located in Frenchtown, Central New Jersey.  
  
Our dedicated professionals with extensive event and wedding planning experience will ensure that your special occasion turns out exactly the way you have planned. We believe that affordable should never mean cheap when you are searching for the best Banquet Center and wedding venues in Hunterdon county. Our Ball Room can seat 300+ guests and our Reflection Room can seat up to 130+ guests making Razberry’s a perfect venue for any size event.
