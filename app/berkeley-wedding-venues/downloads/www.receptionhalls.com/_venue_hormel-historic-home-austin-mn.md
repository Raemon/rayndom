# Hormel Historic Home Austin, Minnesota

History. Beauty. Charm.  
A unique experience you and your guests will cherish and remember.  
  
Our 1871 home and Peace Garden offer a Memorable setting.  
Responsive Service staff will make the event easy to plan and to enjoy.  
Flexible options make southern Minnesota’s most Elegant event site Affordable for everyone.  
  
Whether your event is outdoors or inside, the Hormel Historic Home will make your day one you won't forget. From intimate groups of 30-40 to full gatherings up to 250, your every need will be seen to by our Professional Wedding Coordinator and our staff.   
  
With flexible room layouts, multiple catering and bar options, and extensive wedding planning experience, the Hormel Historic Home is your premiere wedding venue.
