# Hilton Garden Inn Martinsburg, West Virginia

The Hilton Garden Inn Martinsburg is home to the newest and largest Ballroom in WV’s Eastern Panhandle. Celebrate your day surrounded by friends &amp; family in this beautiful 5,000 square foot space featuring expansive windows, no obstructive columns/pillars, and a built-in dance floor. Guests indulge in the delicious cuisine created by Executive Chef Todd Ault and his crew of culinary professionals. Full bar service is available to host or as a cash option for your guests. Hotel Sleeping Accommodations include a refrigerator and microwave in every room and free Wi-Fi. Additional amenities include onsite fitness center, heated indoor pool, business center, full service restaurant and bar open for breakfast, happy hour and dinner. Evening room service is available.
