# TwoFortyThirty New York, New York

TwoFortyThirty includes first and third floors with open environments and a SoHo House feel. This beautiful historic firehouse is perfect for social and corporate events including meetings, television, and much more. The venue has two conference rooms, a kitchen, use of other floors, and state-of-the-art technology capabilities.
