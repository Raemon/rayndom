# WSI Hall Lincoln, Nebraska

Celebrate your special event at WSI Hall!  
  
WSI hall is available for rent for special events. Weddings, receptions, birthday parties, anniversaries and other celebrations.   
  
The two story building holds approximately 150 people on each floor. The upper floor features a stage and bar, and the lower floor has a kitchen and bar area. There is ample parking available behind the building.  
  
For your convenience, you can bring your own food for your special event or hire your own caterer! You can also provide your own beverages (alcoholic and non-alcoholic) to stock the bar.  
  
Rent one floor or both floor. Call us to schedule a tour!

### Pricing

Minimim price for 100 guests: **$ 1,000**

Building built in the 1920's retains original woodwork and flooring (upper floor). Lower level remodeled within the past 2 years. Each floor holds 150 per floor. Upper floor has bar area and stage. Lower level has serving area plus a full kitchen. Customer supplies their own liquor and food.
