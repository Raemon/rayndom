# Wu Chow Austin, Texas

Wu Chow brings authentic, fresh Chinese food to a modern, sophisticated space in the heart of downtown Austin.Techniques are traditional, and many of the dishes are classic with regional influences sourced from local purveyors and farmers. Chef Ji Peng Chen and Chef Ling Qi Wu were born, raised, and trained in China and incorporate their experience into the menu.
