# The Fez Aliquippa, Pennsylvania

The Fez is one of Pittsburgh's premier venues for weddings, banquets, and extraordinary events. With over 50 years of experience, we have hosted over 5,000 wedding receptions, corporate gatherings, fundraising galas, school banquets, and more. Our spacious event venue features the Terrace Room, Balconade Room, and Grand Ballroom for your special occasion celebrations.
