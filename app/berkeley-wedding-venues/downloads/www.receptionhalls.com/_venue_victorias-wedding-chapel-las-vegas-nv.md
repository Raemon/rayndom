The Victorias Family has been in the business of hosting weddings and receptions for over 20 years!   
  
In today's economy many couples find themselves making sacrifices. We believe you shouldn't have to make sacrifices when it comes to your wedding day. We offer a complete formal wedding and reception for an affordable price. Weddings are all we do. Our business continues to thrive today because we do our job very well.  
  
In a city that is renowned for it's hospitality, our traditionally trained and formally attired staff stands head and shoulders above the rest. Courteous, attentive and always enthusiastic, it is the quality of service that distinguishes a Victoria's Family event.  
  
Our professional Event Coordinators orchestrate truly unforgettable occasions. Your own Maitre 'd is always on hand to personally ensure that your every wish is fulfilled and that every member of your party is cared for and is enjoying the celebrations.
