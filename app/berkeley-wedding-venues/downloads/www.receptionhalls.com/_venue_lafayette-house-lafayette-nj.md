# Lafayette House Lafayette, New Jersey

The gracious elegance of this room is enhanced by its simplicity. The decor done in shades of cream and accented with its candelabra chandeliers & fireplace is the ideal setting for the most formal of Weddings, yet is still suitable for your business meetings. The menu can be customized to fill your needs.  
  
Smaller Private dining rooms are also available to accommodate your more intimate affairs.
