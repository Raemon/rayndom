# Sage Event Center Houston, Texas

Nestled against the iconic skyscrapers of Downtown Houston, Sage Event Center reflects the luxury and vitality of the surrounding area. Sage Event Center is a full service venue featuring two beautiful ballrooms and a spacious foyer. This sophisticated reception hall is ideal for rehearsal dinners, wedding receptions, anniversary parties, prom, graduation, and more! The Grand Ballroom is offset by a majestic stained glass entryway and features a high ceiling with crystal chandeliers, accommodating up to 500 guests. The Sage Room creates a more intimate setting, allowing up to 240 guests and featuring a private street entrance facing the historic Market Square Park.
