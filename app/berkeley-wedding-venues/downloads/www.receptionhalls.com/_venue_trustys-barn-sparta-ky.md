# Trusty's Barn Sparta, Kentucky

Our goal is for each event to be truly personal and special, and we support everyone's right to be married to the person they love. We enjoy an unhurried pace, and for that reason we only book one event per weekend. There is plenty of time to set up, rehearse, add personal touches, and settle in to fully enjoy your celebration in this unique and beautiful space. Where elegance meets down home.
