# VFW Post 4659 - Hall Utica, Michigan

VFW Post 4659 Banquet and Hall Center is your premier banquet venue for all your celebrations and events.   
We are located in Shelby Township off of Van Dyke Just north of 24 Mile.   
You can have your event catered or you can do it yourself in our large fully equipped kitchen.  
We are devoted to you and it is our pleasure to take part in sharing your most treasured memories!  
Visit VFWPost4659.org for more details and booking your event.
