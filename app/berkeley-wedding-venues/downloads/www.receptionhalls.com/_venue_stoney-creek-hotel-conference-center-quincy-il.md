# Stoney Creek Hotel & Conference Center Quincy, Illinois

Our Northwoods Conference Center showcases our largest conference facility and its Northwoods Room boasts 2,046 sq.ft. and can accommodate up to 200 guests.  
  
Our antler chandeliers and Northwoods ambiance will enhance your reunion, wedding reception, or corporate meeting. Events large and small can be accommodated with a full range of services. Food catering, beverage service, and audio/visual equipment is available for all occasions.  
  
The Pines Room is located on the second floor of our hotel, overlooking the pool, it can accommodate approximately 30 guests.  
  
The Hearth Room offers an informal, unique atmosphere with comfortable seating and with fireplace creating a relaxing, yet refreshing environment. This room is a popular choice for smaller group meetings, cocktail and appetizer mixers. This room can accommodate up to 40 guests for a social event and up to 12 guests for a conference style meeting.
