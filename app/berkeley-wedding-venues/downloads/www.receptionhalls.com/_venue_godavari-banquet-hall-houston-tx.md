# Godavari Banquet hall Houston, Texas

Conferences, Birthdays, Meetings, Holiday Parties, graduations.... We welcome events of all types and sizes. Our expansive, newly remodeled banquet hall is easily accessible from the Highway. Our Main Room accommodates up to 100 guests. We provide tables, chairs and set up, at no extra cost. We also have bartenders, servers, Wi-fi. We have onsite kitchen and we also outside caterers too. Call to to schedule your Birthday party, Graduation, reception, convention, trade show, luncheon, party and much more. 713-489 3789. Shiva Naidu will be happy to set an appointment with you.
