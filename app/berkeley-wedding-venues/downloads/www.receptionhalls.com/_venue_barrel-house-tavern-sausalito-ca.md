# Barrel House Tavern Sausalito, California

Our restaurant features an open floor plan offering gorgeous views of the San Francisco Bay from every seat in the house. Let us wine and dine you while you and your guests enjoy sweeping views of the Bay Bridge, Angel Island, Alcatraz, and the San Francisco Skyline. Your friends and family, corporate party, or wedding guests are guaranteed to fall in love with the space while enjoying a house-crafted cocktail and farm-to-table fare.
