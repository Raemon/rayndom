# The Merion Riverton, New Jersey

The Merion has been South Jersey's premiere catering and special events facility for over 50 years. During its most recent renovations, The Merion has incorporated exquisite and classical European designs. Its marbleized columns, grand stair case, and cathedral hallways will leave you breathless. The Merion has continually been acclaimed for its delectable and exceptional cuisine at every event. Accompanied by professional white-gloved service, every detail is tastefully considered to make your special occasion a memorable one. Your place or Ours!
