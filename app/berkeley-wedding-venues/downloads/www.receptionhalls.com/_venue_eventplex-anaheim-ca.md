# EventPlex Anaheim, California

EventPlex is Orange County's premier event venue in Anaheim, California. Our event venues are able to accommodate meetings, conferences, conventions, trade shows, celebrations, private and special events. We pride ourselves on providing a memorable experience with excellent flexibility, services, and cost savings. We also provide on-site parking and Wi-Fi. Our event planning team has years of experience and is passionate about what they do. We are here to ensure your event is stress free and everything you envisioned so you have a great experience!  
  
We're located in the heart of Orange County with easy access to major freeways and we’re a few minutes away from Disneyland, the Angel Stadium, the Honda Center and a wide selection of hotels.  
  
We offer multiple rooms and open spaces of all sizes at lower prices for business, social, private, and sporting events.  
  
Call us at 714.978.9000 to book your next event!
