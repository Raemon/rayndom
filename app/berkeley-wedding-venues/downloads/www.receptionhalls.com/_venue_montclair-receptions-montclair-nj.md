BANQUET HALL IN MONTCLAIR, NJ  
  
Banquet Hall available in Montclair, NJ   
  
$499 for 5 hours  
  
We can accommodate up to 75 people.  
  
We can arrange catering if you need it or you can bring your own.  
  
Suitable for all occasions ? weddings, birthdays, sweet sixteen, meetings, seminars, religious functions etc.  
  
There is ample parking in a municipal parking lot behind the hall on Glenridge Ave (intersection of Glenridge Ave and Forest St). Nights and weekends parking is available in permit spaces.   
  
  
Call Rose for more information or to make a reservation. Please call at least one day in advance of your event.
