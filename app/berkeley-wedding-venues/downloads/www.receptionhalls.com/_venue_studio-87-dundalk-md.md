# Studio 87 Dundalk, Maryland

Studio 87 is minimalistic and modular, used as a creative space, art gallery, and intimate private rental space. We have free on-site parking, so you can exit your vehicle and walk right in.   
  
This space is perfect for calm and reserved events, dinners, bridal showers, baby showers, and small and intimate celebrations. We can also comfortably serve meetings, workshops, pop-up shops, classes, and other social-style events.   
  
Unfortunately, we do not host wild events with an uncontrolled amount of guests, uncontrolled alcohol consumption, or rowdy or lit-style events, kindly contact another event space.   
  
The owner of Studio 87 is also a long-time event and wedding photographer.
