# Mt View Golf Course Ewing, New Jersey

The Mountainview Clubhouse is the ideal setting for any occasion. This award winning clubhouse offers a banquet hall for up to 121 people which can be expanded to include the Grille Room which has seating for 61. There is also a stunning outdoor patio with picturesque views of the 18th green with seating up to 50 people. Your event will be catered by Karl H Schott Catering with over 30 years experience. You supply the occasion and Karl H Schott Catering will supply you and your guests with a variety of dishes that are all delectable gourmet culinary masterpieces.
