# Bentley meeting room Branford, Connecticut

![Photo of Bentley meeting room](/media/CT/10323/bentley-meeting-room-branford-ct.jpg)

Let us host your next corporate meeting, boardroom business, family reunion, or even a birthday party! Our versatile 450 sq. ft. Bentley Room is equipped with wired high speed internet, flip chart, white board and screen. We can also facilitate Audio/Visual equipment rental. It can accommodate 40 theater-style, and 24 classroom-style. The Bentley Room is located on the first floor, providing easy access for everyone.  
  
Bentley meeting room can accommodate up to 40 people theater style. Easy on/off I-95 exit 56. Projector screen available upon request . Host your meetings , events or workshop to experience hometown hospitality.
