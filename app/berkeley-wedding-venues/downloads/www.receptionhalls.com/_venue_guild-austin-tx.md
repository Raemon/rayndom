# Guild Austin, Texas

Guild provides a modern, luminous space with nautical touches for executive dinners, intimate celebrations, corporate gatherings, and more. A full buy-out can accommodate up to 160 seated guests or 250 standing guests. Our private dining room equipped with AV setup and display accommodates 40 seated guests or 60 standing guests. There are also multiple patios surrounding the restaurant for semi-private gatherings large or small.
