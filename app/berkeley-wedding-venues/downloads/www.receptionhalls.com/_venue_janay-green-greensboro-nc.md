# Janay Green Greensboro, North Carolina

Located in downtown Greensboro, GreenHill’s artful venue makes any occasion a standout party, business function, or wedding celebration. With options to reserve our gallery, art studios, or both—we can provide the setting for an intimate workshop or a lively gathering.  
  
Our rental hours begin after we close to the public at 5pm.

### Venue Details

Capacity (Seated / Cocktail): **75 / 100**

We are a gallery space, and the number of seated or standing capacity depends on the current exhibition. During this unpredictable time, we can accommodate 100 or fewer persons in our gallery space, to allow for some distancing.

Max Number of Simultaneous Events: **1**

Outside Catering Permitted (BYO): **Yes**

Venue Type/Style: **Museum**

On-Site Cuisine Options: **Use Your Own Caterer**

### Pricing

Site/Rental Fee: **$600**

Gallery rentals start at $600. Pricing is based on hours used, not per person, and day of the week. Please call or email for this information.
