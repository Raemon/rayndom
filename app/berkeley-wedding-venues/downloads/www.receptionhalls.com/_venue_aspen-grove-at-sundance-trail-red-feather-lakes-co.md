# Aspen Grove at Sundance Trail Red Feather Lakes, Colorado

Crisp mountain air, beautiful scenery, the sweet smell of pine and the low rustle of horses in the distance are just some of the wonderful things that make Aspen Grove at Sundance Trail Guest Ranch a hidden gem.  
Our all-inclusive wedding packages &amp; special event venues are situated just 45 minutes north of Fort Collins, Colorado.  
The pet friendly dude ranch location is nestled on 140 acres bordering Roosevelt National Forest and offers breathtaking surroundings
