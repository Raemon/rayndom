# The Gallery by Left Lane Productions Thomasville, North Carolina

The Gallery by Left Lane Productions is an exquisite space for hosting anything from an intimate wedding to a business seminar. With a professional and dedicated staff, we have the ability to coordinate and direct your event from the planning stages throughout completion. The Gallery was constructed in the early 1900's and is located in beautiful downtown Thomasville, NC. The atmosphere is one of a kind and with it's original hard wood floors, vintage tile ceilings and stained glass windows it is assured that your event will an event remember!
