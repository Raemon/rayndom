# 28on27 New York, New York

28on27 nestles inside a 2,500 sq foot space in the flat iron area of Manhattan. Hardwood floors, and high ceilings provide a loft feel while iron castings, pillars and soft furnishings maintain a cool edgy elegance.  
  
Fully equipped with Audio Capabilities, Production Services, and Freight and passenger elevators 28on27 is a special destination.
