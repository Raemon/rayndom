# Beck Event Space Harrisonville, Missouri

Beck Event Space is a wedding venue near Kansas City and Lee's Summit in Harrisonville, MO. It has a capacity of up to 300. The space has an industrial-chic vibe with exposed brick and plenty of natural lighting. The ballroom is 5700 square feet with a grand staircase for your grand entrance.  
  
There is a spacious bridal suite for you and your bride's maids to get ready.  
  
There is also a groom's room for the guys.  
  
You have access to the space 12 hours before the event.   
  
You have a blank canvas to create your Instagram-Worthy wedding and reception. We can provide catering or you can hire your own. We will provide you with a list of reputable vendors that have worked with us in the past.  
  
Local accommodations include multiple Bed and Breakfast Inns and hotels. There is ample parking directly across the street from the venue.
