# Rogue Valley Country Club Medford, Oregon

Rogue Valley Country Club would like to help make your next event spectacular. Whether you are looking to have an elegant ballroom affair or a casual and sophisticated outdoor experience on our terrace- your wish is our command. We take pride in helping you put your best foot forward by offering an extensive selection of services that can be tailored to your day. No detail is too small for our trained and professional banquet staff. With ample parking and a multitude of venue options that can accommodate  
up to 350 guests, RVCC is an exceptional location in East Medford. It is our sincere desire to help make your next event an affair to remember!
