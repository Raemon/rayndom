# Love Walk Social Cafe Detroit, Michigan

![Photo of Love Walk Social Cafe](/media/MI/11850/love-walk-social-cafe-detroit-mi.jpg)

If you are looking for a small venue with a comfortable and great atmosphere tee Love Walk Social Cafe is it. We offer table and chairs that seat 50 guest which is perfect for baby or bridal showers, book signings, listening parties &amp; more.
