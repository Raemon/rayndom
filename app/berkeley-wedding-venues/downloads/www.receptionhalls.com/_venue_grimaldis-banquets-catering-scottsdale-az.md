# Grimaldis Banquets & Catering Scottsdale, Arizona

Sure, we have specialized in NY style coal-fired brick oven pizza since the 1930's, but we also boast a full Italian menu for banquets and private events as well.  
  
At Grimaldi's, we are dedicated to making your event both effortless and unforgettable. Our chefs and professional event coordinators work with you to create a custom menu specific for you.  
  
Whether your event is an intimate affair or large gathering, we can accomodate your group at our   
Old Town Scottsdale or Chandler location.  
  
Extraordinary food, exceptional service and atmosphere make us the best choice for your memorable event!
