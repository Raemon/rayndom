# The Westin Kansas City at Crown Center Kansas City, Missouri

Encompassed by Hallmark's Crown Center, which offers over 80 acres of entertainment and shopping, the Westin Kansas City at Crown Center is ideally located at the heart of the City of Fountains. We feature indoor and outdoor event spaces to suit your ceremony and reception needs. Celebrate under the elegant chandeliers of our Century Ballroom, or take your guests outside to our games deck or garden terrace. We look forward to perfecting the details of your dream wedding, including developing a menu and offering deluxe accommodations for your traveling guests. All you have left is to say "I do."
