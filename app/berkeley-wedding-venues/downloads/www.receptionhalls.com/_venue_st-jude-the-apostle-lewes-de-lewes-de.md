# St. Jude The Apostle, Lewes De Lewes, Delaware

St. Jude The Apostle Parish Life Center specializes in offering an affordable venue for all your entertainment needs. Our passion is to create a wonderful experience for our clients.   
  
Our Parish Life Center holds up to 300 people with a large seating area and dance floor and 450 auditorium style. A commercial kitchen is also available for the approved caterers. Ample parking is available with our convenient location right off of Route One in Lewes, De. Our hall provides ideal facilities for: Wedding Ceremonies, Wedding Receptions, Plated &amp; Buffet Dinner Receptions, Wedding Rehearsal Dinners, Award Banquets, Business Meetings, Church Retreats, Holiday Parties, Charity Fundraising Events, Birthday Parties, Holiday Parties, Retirement Parties, and Special Luncheon Events.  
  
Choose St. Jude The Apostle Banquet Facilities to create truly memorable moments. We are committed to ensuring that all the details of your Banquet come together as you envision them.  
  
Our expansive banquet space make St. Jude’s an ideal choice for hosting your banquet or business meeting.   
  
To make reservations or request a quote for an banquet, wedding, or other special event, please contact our Facilities Rental Coordinator, Jim Stewart at (302) 645-8059.
