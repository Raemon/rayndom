# The Gardens of Cranesburyview New Braunfels, Texas

An Italian Sculptured Garden Event Venue  
The Gardens at Cranesbury View. A Romantic San Antonio and Austin Wedding Location  
The Gardens of Cranesbury View offers your Wedding dreams to become an enchanting story book reality. Filled with European ambiance and charm. The Gardens of Cranesbury View is captivating, yet inviting, creating a hospitable environment for you and your guest. The beauty of God’s creation is truly evident among the gardens and surrounding hill sides. Nestled on 32 acres with gorgeous Italian villa like views, this venue features beautiful Italian sculptured gardens with scrolled boxwoods, large urns with color, and water features where your guest can causally stroll.  
  
The venue will include a ballroom that replicates an old English chapel. This ballroom can be used for a church like setting for your ceremony, or your reception. The outdoor lawn area is able to accommodate guests beautifully under the starry nights of Texas. Our event venue is distinctively different and perfect for Weddings, Parties &amp; Corporate Events. Our location is perfectly located off of 46 in New Braunfels, in between 281 and I-35.
