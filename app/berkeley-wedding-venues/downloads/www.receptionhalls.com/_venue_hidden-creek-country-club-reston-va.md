# Hidden Creek Country Club Reston, Virginia

![Photo of Hidden Creek Country Club](/media/VA/10945/hidden-creek-country-club-reston-va.jpg)

Nestled in the heart of Reston, 1 mile from Reston Town Center, come and see why Hidden Creek is known as the Hidden Jewel of Reston; surrounded by an oasis of rolling landscapesand golf course views.  
  
Designed to take full advantage of mother nature’s splendor, Hidden Creek features an outdoor ceremony site, a year-round covered deck and picturesque views all around! We have adequate space to accommodate up to 200 of your guests along with plenty of dancing and entertaining space! We do all of our own catering, overseeing every last detail.  
  
Plus, here at Hidden Creek, we can arrange a bachelor's golf day, your bachelorette party and - when the time comes - your anniversary party! The details for these are seamless for you! While you don't have to be a Member to enjoy our Club, we can certainly offer the pampering our Members receive by walking you through all the planning!  
  
Please call for a personalized tour and see it for yourself! We look forward to chatting with you!
