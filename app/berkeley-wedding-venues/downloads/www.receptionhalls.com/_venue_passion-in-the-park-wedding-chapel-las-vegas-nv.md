# Passion in the Park Wedding Chapel Las Vegas, Nevada

With lush tropical grounds, a romantic gazebo and four elegant ballrooms, Alexis Park Resort is a top venue for Las Vegas weddings.  
  
Say your vows under our wedding gazebo, surrounded by beautiful landscaping and glittering lights, then enjoy panoramic views of the Las Vegas Strip as you dance the night away on our rooftop. For a more traditional atmosphere, book one of the resort’s ballrooms for a candlelit banquet under twinkling chandeliers. We also have a stylish lounge bar and poolside restaurant available for your rehearsal dinner and pre-wedding celebrations.  
  
At Alexis Park we believe every wedding should be unique, and we’re committed to customizing a celebration that reflects who you are as a couple. From flowers to gourmet catering, our professional wedding coordinators will be on-call to guarantee your day flows seamlessly. Just tell us your vision and leave the details to us.  
  
Eager to begin planning your event? Contact our Sales Department to start planning your dream wedding today.
