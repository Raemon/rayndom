# East Wind Caterers Wading River, New York

As your drive east on the north shore brings you closer to the eastern end of Long Island, the easy country lifestyle starts to take over and your mind wanders to a place where all your worries fade.... East Wind  
  
East Wind Caterers, with three beautiful locations; The Cottage, The Estate and The Grand Ballroom are set at the gateway to Long Islands wine country.  
  
A family owned business on the eastern end of the island can only mean one thing, a commitment to personalized service. With only one wedding at a time, each of your guests is like a guest of honor.  
  
With all the charms of our beautiful Cottage and the stately elegance of our Estate building, along with the Grandness of The Inn, it's no easy decision on which location you will choose. So just remember, which ever one it is, you will still have the same professional, friendly service and all the experience of our culinary trained chefs. So come into the East Wind and relax in our country lifestyle.
