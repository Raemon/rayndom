# The Inn at Woodstock Hill Woodstock, Connecticut

Built in 1816 this historic building is located in Connecticut’s northeast “Quiet Corner” off of scenic Route 169. With warmth, courtesy and attentiveness, our skilled chef and professional staff will be able to assist you in creating the wedding you have always dreamed of.  
We have hosted weddings, rehearsal dinner, and receptions since the 1980s, and our professional staff are ready to meet your every need with genuine care and concern. Our award-winning facility consists of twenty-one beautifully decorated private rooms to accommodate your wedding party and guests, and our exceptional dining and beverage facilities can be designed to accommodate your budget and menu preferences.
