# Dossin Great Lakes Museum Detroit, Michigan

The Dossin Great Lakes Museum is located in belle Isle state Park in the Detroit River. Ceremonies for up to 75 people are held in the Gothic Room with beautiful woodwork and a stained glass window. Receptions for up to 125 people are held in DeRoy Hall, which directly over looks the Detroit River. The entire museum can accommodate strolling events for up to 400 guests.
