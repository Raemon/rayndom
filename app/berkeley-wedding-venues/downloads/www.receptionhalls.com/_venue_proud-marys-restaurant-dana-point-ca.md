# Proud Mary's Restaurant Dana Point, California

Proud Mary's Restaurant, Dana Point — If you are looking for an unique waterfront venue in Dana Point Harbor, this is the spot. Year round patio that can accommodate 110 plus indoor beautiful bar/lounge that seats 25. Prices start at $26.00 per person for full Italian buffet dinner or choose a more traditional sit down dinner. Dance area and band area available. Restaurant is also for rent for the evening starting at $1500.00 for three hours or only bar for $150.00.
