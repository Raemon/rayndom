# Saint Irene's Tualatin, Oregon

Saint Irene’s is a versatile event venue with two space options to accommodate a myriad of gatherings. Weddings, receptions, family dinners, conferences, corporate events, and meetings.  
Our unique space is divided into two rooms, the Venue Room and the High Dive tasting room. The Venue Room is separated from the High Dive with contractible garage doors, which can be opened for larger gatherings. The Venue Room can comfortably seat up to 80 guests, and may accommodate up to 100 standing people for less formal occasions. The Venue Room and High Dive may be rented together to accommodate up to 100 seated guests, or 125 standing.   
We offer professional event planning services, custom menus, fresh flower arrangements, distinctive décor, multiple floorplan layouts, and custom linen colors. We have a projector and screen available for guests to use.
