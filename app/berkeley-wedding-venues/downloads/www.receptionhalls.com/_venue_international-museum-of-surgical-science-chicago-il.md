# International Museum of Surgical Science Chicago, Illinois

Thank you for your interest in the International Museum of Surgical Science. The Museum is housed in a historic mansion built in 1917, with beautiful views of Lake Michigan. It also boasts some impressive architectural points such as original Italian marble floors and fireplaces, an exquisite library filled with over 4,000 rare medical texts, and beautiful gilded staircases- all original to the home. Guests from past events have also enjoyed the opportunity to peruse Museum exhibits. Some of the Museum's most popular exhibits include: an old style apothecary, ophthalmology exhibit, x-ray exhibit, medical illustrations, and much more. Museum staff enjoy working with event hosts to create the perfect event and make it truly come alive in the space. Thank you again for your interest and we look forward to hearing from you!
