# Hawk Ranch Murrieta, California

![Photo of Hawk Ranch](/media/CA/11448/hawk-ranch-murrieta-ca.jpg)

Hawk Ranch is a gated forty acre working ranch with a picturesque pond surrounded by manicured landscaping. 300 trees, parking on-site, bridal room, permanent restrooms and a Gallery that seats up to 100 inside with views overlooking the pond area makes for a beautiful setting. The outdoor dining and reception area can accommodate up to 250 guests. All-inclusive packages available. One event is hosted at a time giving our guests exclusivity. Breathtaking photo sites. Optional on-site accommodations for out of town family and friends available.
