# Europa Village Temecula, California

Europa Village combines the charm and style of Old-World Europe with the beauty and service Temecula Wine Country has come to be know for. Europa Village is the perfect venue for hosting meetings, weddings, retreats & any other special events.  
  
At Europa Village, the possibilities are endless! Choose from any number of extraordinary locations inspired by the landscapes and architecture of Spain, Italy and France. Allow us to set the scene for a wedding as spectacular as your love story.
