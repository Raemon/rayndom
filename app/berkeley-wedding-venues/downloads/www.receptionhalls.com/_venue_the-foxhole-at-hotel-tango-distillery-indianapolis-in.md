# The Foxhole at Hotel Tango Distillery Indianapolis, Indiana

With a breath-taking custom brick fireplace, built by our founders’ father, the Foxhole can comfortably accommodate groups of all sizes up to 110 people. You’ll notice nods to our military roots throughout the space, with camouflage accents and custom artwork; as well as nods to our distillery with barrels and bottle wall.   
Enjoy a unique venue while sipping on Hotel Tango craft cocktails in this completely private space. The Foxhole at Hotel Tango is perfect for rehearsal dinners, intimate weddings, social celebrations, corporate events, and more!
