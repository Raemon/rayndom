# Riverview Ballroom Cliffside Park, New Jersey

If you are looking a stylish clifftop wedding venue, overlooking the NYC Skyline and the Hudson River you have come to the right place. Riverview Ballroom provides space and service for all kinds of different events from Weddings, Kosher Events, Birthdays, Social and Corporate. Here, at Riverview Ballroom, we do not shy away from extravagance, but we also know the value of tastefulness and elegance. Due to our spacious Ballrooms we are able to accommodate up to 700 seated guests for the Reception.  
  
Choose Riverview Ballroom, for our beautifully crafted spaces and top-notch service with its talented and experienced staff.
