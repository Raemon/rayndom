# The 107 Chattanooga Chattanooga, Tennessee

Chattanooga's newest wedding and event venue The 107 Chattanooga is an industrial style venue; designed for receptions, weddings, fundraisers, corporate events, video shoots, and more.  
  
Centrally located just minutes from Downtown, Cleveland, Ooltewah and North Georgia with a variety of hotels within walking distance.
