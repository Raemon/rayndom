# e.j. michael's fine catering Staten Island, New York

![Photo of e.j. michael's fine catering](/media/NY/10056/ej-michaels-fine-catering-staten-island-ny.jpg)

Located on the South Shore of Staten Island, e.j. michaels fine catering is the perfect setting for any occasion, large or small, with elegant banquet rooms and fine catering designed to meet your every need. e.j. michael's offers exceptional cuisine and impeccable service in a gracious setting.  
  
Our newly renovated and beautifully decorated banquet rooms will leave you with memories to last a lifetime when you celebrate life's special occasions with us. Our goal is to provide you with quality food and service in an atmosphere that is unsurpassed. Our professional wait staff will attend to your every need.  
  
Our Catering Hall is designed to accommodate your every affair:  
* Weddings  
* Sweet Sixteen’s  
* Engagement Parties  
* Birthday Parties  
* Rehearsal Dinners  
* Fundraisers  
* Bridal Showers  
* Baby Showers  
* Anniversary Parties  
* Corporate Events  
* Holiday Parties  
* School Banquets  
* Graduation Parties  
* Communion Parties  
* Retirement Parties   
* Christenings  
* Confirmation Parties  
  
e.j. michael's also offers fine catering for off premise locations, as well as corporate or school events.
