# Whispering Pines Milford, New Jersey

Whispering Pines is a commercial banquet hall suitable for everything from birthdays and sweet sixteen parties to weddings. The hall seats 140 banquet style comfortably and up to 370 auditorium style or cocktail reception. You may choose your own caterer and BYOB or we can recommend a full service caterer for your affair.  
  
Whispering Pines is operated by the Holland Township Volunteer Fire Company and all proceeds support the company.
