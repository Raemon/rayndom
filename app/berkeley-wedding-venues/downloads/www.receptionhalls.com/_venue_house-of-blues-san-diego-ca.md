# House of Blues San Diego, California

House of Blues San Diego offers four distinct areas that are the perfect location to host your next corporate event, private concert, social gathering, wedding, reception or private dinner. Located in the heart of downtown San Diego, House of Blues San Diego accommodates 25 to 1500 guests and is 1.1 miles from the San Diego Convention Center. Our talented staff handles every detail, so you don’t have to!
