# Caret Cellars Caret, Virginia

Caret Cellars is a boutique winery that opened in 2014. We offer affordable and beautiful elopement and micro wedding packages for up to 40 people.   
  
You have your choice of DIY and inclusive packages with a greatly discounted rate for weddings held Monday through Thursday. All packages include our wines. We work with amazing vendors, including catering, but you are free to choose any licensed and insured vendor.   
  
Located in a historic region of Virginia, we are with 20 minutes of restaurants and hotels, and guests will have lots to see and do if they opt to arrive before your wedding day or stay a few days after.

### Venue Details

Capacity (Seated / Cocktail): **40 /**

We are a winery that offers inside and outside accommodations. Our inside option is 40 guests. If you want an outside wedding with a larger guest count, we encourage you to rent a tent and a portable restroom. Virginia weather can be iffy!

Max Number of Simultaneous Events: **1**

Outside Catering Permitted (BYO): **Yes**

On-Site Cuisine Options: **American, Vegetarian, Use Your Own Caterer, Custom**
