# Remedy Park Chambersburg, Pennsylvania

Remedy Park is a 2,600 sq ft modern venue with accompanying backyard oasis in the middle of downtown Chambersburg's industrial backdrop.   
  
Our onsite park is equipped with two outdoor hardscaped patio areas with wheelchair accessible walkways, an outdoor catering kitchen, two lavatories and plenty of room for tents, games, and privatized seating areas.   
  
Remedy Park is perfect for personal and corporate events alike!
