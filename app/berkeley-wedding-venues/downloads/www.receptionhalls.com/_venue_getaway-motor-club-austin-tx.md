# Getaway Motor Club Austin, Texas

Getaway Motor Club is a unique event venue located just East of Downtown Austin! Highlights of our venue include the built in stage with custom downtown skyline artwork and lighting, projection screen, pool table/arcade room, custom upstairs bar/lounge and seating capacity on the main floor of 300 guests in reception style seating and up to 500 guests in theater style seating. For smaller parties the bar/lounge area can host 50 people seated and 80 people standing comfortably! Call us today for a tour of our uniquely Austin venue!**Special: Book any wedding date to take place before September 30, 2013 and receive the venue rental for $3,500.00!
