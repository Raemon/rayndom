# Peking Palace Falmouth, Massachusetts

Featuring Chinese Cuisine and Japanese Sushi for 27 years, our modern Chinese and Japanese dishes bring you the authentic and sizzling taste of China and Japan through perfectly prepared and exotically balanced flavors that will surely please the eye and excite your taste buds.  
  
Clean, airy and spacious, today’s modern Peking Palace offers a full Sushi Bar, spacious dining room, lounge, function room, flat-screen TVs and also offers catering for all your special occasions.
