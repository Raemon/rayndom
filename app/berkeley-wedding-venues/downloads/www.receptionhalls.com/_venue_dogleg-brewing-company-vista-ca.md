# Dogleg Brewing Company Vista, California

Nestled on the border of Vista & Carlsbad overlooking the old Carlsbad Raceway, Dogleg Brewing Company is the perfect spot to host an event -- from small corporate lunches and birthdays to full buy-out weddings! Our spacious tasting room has a great view and ample parking, plus we're family & dog friendly!  
  
AMENITIES INCLUDE:  
20 beers on tap & 2 hard seltzers (we can offer wine as option for private events)  
State-of-the-art Foresight Sports GC Hawk golf simulator  
Golf clubs for men, women & lefties  
Indoor putting green  
Lighted patio with a view  
Spacious tasting room with capacity up to 100 people  
Projector screen, Sounds system & TVs for presentations  
Free Wifi  
Easy, ample parking
