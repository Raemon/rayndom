# Output Brooklyn, New York

Situated on Wythe Avenue along Brooklyn's waterfront, Output lies in the heart of Williamsburg, the industrial district turned art and music hub that is now the city's hottest neighborhood. One stop from Manhattan and just off the BQE, Output is the ideal location for your next social or corporate event.
