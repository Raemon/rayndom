### Pricing

Budget Tier:  **$**

Minimim price for 100 guests: **$ 350,000**

We have three distinct areas that have different pricing if used independantly. The VIP Lounge which holds 100 guests, has a private restroom access, private bar, several HD TV's on the wall, include soft seating and hightops. This area ranges between $3,500 - $4,500. The Lobby area can accommodate 200 guests, includes private access to restrooms, a bar, TV's, 1 wireless microphone, and more for a price range of $4,000 - $8,000.
