# DoubleTree by Hilton Raleigh Brownstone-University Raleigh, North Carolina

![Photo of DoubleTree by Hilton Raleigh Brownstone-University](/media/NC/11750/doubletree-by-hilton-raleigh-brownstone-university-raleigh-nc.jpg)

Welcome to the Doubletree Brownstone Hotel! We are here to make your wedding everything you've been dreaming of. We can accommodate ceremonies and receptions for up to 500 people. Our sales and catering staff have over 20 years of experience planning weddings and special events, so you can expect the knowledge and expertise of a wedding planner without paying for one!  
  
With every wedding package we also include many items for which you will pay extra at other venues. Things like: unlimited selection of table linens, centerpieces, uplighting and even the dance floor. For your out of town guests, we offer discounted sleeping room rates and a complimentary suite for the bride and groom with breakfast inclusive on the big night. Come see all that Raleigh and the Doubletree Brownstone Hotel have to offer for your special occasion!
