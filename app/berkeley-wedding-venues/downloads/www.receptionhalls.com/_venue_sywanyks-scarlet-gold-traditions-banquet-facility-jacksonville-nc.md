# Sywanyks Scarlet & Gold Traditions Banquet Facility Jacksonville, North Carolina

Sywanyks Night Club has been in business for over 20 years, providing a one of a kind venue that is a unique and professional establishment. Sywanyks has been hosting functions for many years, from Mess Nights to Sweet 16. We have met many great people and had the most enjoyment helping out our customers with their special event. We provide everything you will need to host your event, and no hidden charges or fees. Sywanyks supports our Military men and women and also our Law Enforcement, EMT and Firefighters. For our appreciation we offer all of them the use of our facility FREE of charge
