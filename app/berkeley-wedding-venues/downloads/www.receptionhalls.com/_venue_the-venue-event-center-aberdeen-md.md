# The Venue Event Center Aberdeen, Maryland

The Venue is available for the following events and parties:  
\- Wedding and Receptions - Family Reunions  
\- Graduation Parties - Special Occasions  
\- Engagement Parties - Business or Family Parties  
\- Bridal and Baby Showers - Award Banquets  
\- Holiday Parties - Social Club Meetings  
\- Dinner Parties - Classes or Workshops  
\- Class Reunions -Memorial services/ repass - - AND MORE  
Rental of The Venue provides features such as:  
\- approximately 2200 square feet of event space  
\- Capacity up to 125 people (Covid Capacity is 60)  
\- Small warming kitchen for food preparation and storage during your event  
\- Tables, and chairs are included in rental  
\- Linen tablecloths, chair covers, table Centerpieces and additional décor are available for rent at additional cost  
\- Portable Stage is available for rent  
\- 24-hour alarm and surveillance cameras  
\- Plenty of Parking  
\- Exclusive list of preferred vendors including (but not limited to) Event Planners, Disk Jockeys, Caterers, Bakers, Balloon Arches, custom treats table, custom backdrop’s, Party buses, ordained ministers, Photographers, photo booth, Entertainment such as musician’s, comedians, motivational speakers, casino dealers… and more.
