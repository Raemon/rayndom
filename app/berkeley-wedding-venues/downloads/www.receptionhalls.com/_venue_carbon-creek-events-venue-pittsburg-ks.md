# Carbon Creek Event Venue Pittsburg, Kansas

Carbon Creek Event Venue provides couples with nearly 6,000 square feet of indoor event space and has a maximum guest capacity of 400 people. Carbon Creek Events & Venue has an array of premium features, including a large dance floor in the center of the space with mirrored ceilings. It also has a built-in sound and lighting system, bringing your guests together to create precious memories during your event. Carbon Creek Event Venue is also focused on ease of access for guests, providing ample parking space as well as wheelchair accessibility.
