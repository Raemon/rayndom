# Applewood Farm Pell City, Alabama

Celebrate your next event at Applewood Farm – A Private Estate situated on over 80 magnificent acres, with rolling hills and majestic ponds. Your ceremony or reception can take place in our, 4500 sq. ft. Crystal Chandelier Barn (Central AC &amp; Heat), or outside on our Pergola Stage right next to one of our beautiful ponds. We can accommodate intimate ceremonies and receptions from 10 to 200= guests.  
  
You can also spend your romantic honeymoon or anniversary get-a-way at either our 1841 Log Cabin or Country Cottage. www.applewoodfarm.com
