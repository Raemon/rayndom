# Rooftop Patio Event Space New York, New York

The Rooftop Patio Event Space provides a regular meeting space for civic groups that include Community Planning Board 9, police, parks and business leaders, as well as a venue for other community based agencies’ conferences, retreats, bridal showers, birthday parties, baby showers, workshops and all types of events. Located on the penthouse level of Broadway Housing Communities’ buildings known as The Rio and Dorothy Day Apartments, rooftop space is surrounded by lovely landscaped terraces; an unobstructed view of the Hudson River and George Washington Bridge are additional attractions at the rooftop patio event space.
