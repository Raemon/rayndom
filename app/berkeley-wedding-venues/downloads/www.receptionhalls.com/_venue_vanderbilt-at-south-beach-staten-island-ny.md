# Vanderbilt at South Beach Staten Island, New York

From it’s inception and planning with an award  
winning architectural team, The Vanderbilt at  
South Beach has been designed to offer the latest  
in technological and comfort amenities enveloped  
in impeccable taste and elegance.  
Within it’s stunning surroundings, our facility  
offers a refined elegance unmatched in New York.  
We offer full serviced events developed with  
creative event planners, who work with you to  
make your vision a reality for this very special day.  
With dramatic ocean views, relaxing natural  
acoustics, and a truly magnificent vista of the  
Verrazano Narrows Bridge, our facility is the  
perfect setting for your special event.
