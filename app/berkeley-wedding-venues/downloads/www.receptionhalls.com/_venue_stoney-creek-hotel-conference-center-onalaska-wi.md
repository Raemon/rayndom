# Stoney Creek Hotel & Conference Center Onalaska, Wisconsin

![Photo of Stoney Creek Hotel & Conference Center](/media/WI/9988/stoney-creek-hotel-conference-center-onalaska-wi.jpg)

Nestled here in Onalaska, WI we are just ten minutes from the University of Wisconsin La Crosse and in the heart of the beautiful Coulee Region. Our unique atmosphere offers a cozy setting for your special events, and our exemplary staff is here to ensure your event runs smoothly, is executed to plan and minimizes your stress.   
  
Our hotel has approximately 12,000 square feet of meeting space, and our Northwoods Conference Center will accommodate a reception of up to 510 guests. This room features three rustic chandeliers, spacious stage, in-house sound system, video projection capabilities, 14-foot ceilings, and a large projection screen. The entire conference room may also be divided into three separate rooms – Salons A, B, and C. In addition, we have several break-out rooms to accommodate 5 – 100 people. All of our meeting space will bring a touch of Northwoods ambiance to your event.   
  
Adjacent to the Northwoods Conference Center is our Watering Hole, the perfect location for cocktail receptions. Our carefully selected caterers offer a variety of menus served plated or buffet-style, and full bar service is also available.  
  
Here at the Stoney Creek Inn in La Crosse/Onalaska, WI, we are large enough to serve your every need, yet quaint enough to make you feel relaxed and “at home.”  
  
Come to Stoney Creek and experience the great outdoors – indoors!
