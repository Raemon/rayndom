# Willow Brook Golf Course Albertville, Alabama

Willow Brook is a beautiful 18 hole golf course located in Albertville, AL. Willow Brook has a new deck overlooking the course, which is a beautiful spot for an outdoor wedding. The golf course also has numerous other spots for a wedding should the couple choose a different area.
