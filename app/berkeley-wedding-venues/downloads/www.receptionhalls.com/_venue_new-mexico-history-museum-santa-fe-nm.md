# New Mexico History Museum Santa Fe, New Mexico

Set in the heart of 400-year-old Santa Fe, New Mexico, the celebrated New Mexico History Museum and Palace of the Governors – built in 1610 and designated as both a National Historic Landmark and National Treasure – offers a truly remarkable venue for couples seeking an unforgettable wedding experience. We invite you to join the many gratified couples who have chosen to step into the past while creating memories for the future.  
Reach out to schedule your personalized tour. The Museum looks forward to welcoming you.
