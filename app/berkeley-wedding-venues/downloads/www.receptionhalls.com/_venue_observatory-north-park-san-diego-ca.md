# Observatory North Park San Diego, California

Observatory North Park is the perfect blend of rock meets elegance for your next award ceremony, corporate event, private concert, fundraiser or performing arts competition. Featuring 20,000 square feet of flexible design space, the architecturally stunning Observatory North Park has an atmosphere that celebrates extraordinary hospitality and full-service event production.Located in the heart of San Diego, this spacious venue features state-of-the-art lighting and sound, a massive theatrical stage, removable auditorium seating, and an elite bar. With access to the world’s greatest musical entertainment, full-service event production, custom menus and mixology, Observatory North Park will exceed expectations of even the most discerning guest.
