# State Theatre Event Center Columbus, Kansas

Experience timeless charm and modern elegance at the State Theatre Event Center in downtown Columbus, Kansas. This fully restored 7,000 sq. ft. historic movie theatre offers the perfect event space for weddings, receptions, corporate events, baby showers, and more.  
  
With its unique blend of vintage architecture and contemporary amenities—plus a beautiful outdoor courtyard ideal for ceremonies or cocktail hours—our venue delivers unforgettable moments in a truly iconic setting.
