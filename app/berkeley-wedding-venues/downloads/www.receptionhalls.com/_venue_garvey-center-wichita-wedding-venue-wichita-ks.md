# Garvey Center - Wichita Wedding Venue Wichita, Kansas

The Garvey Center is one of the few Wichita wedding venues that allows outside catering. We believe the food you choose for your wedding reception should reflect your personal budget and style. At the Garvey Center Banquet Facility, you have the freedom to cater your own meal or work with the local caterer of your choice. Alcohol – In addition to food, we allow parties to bring in their own alcohol and/or bartender.* *Please contact us for more information regarding our alcohol policy When you book an event in the elegant Forum Room or Mediterranean Room of the Garvey Center, you can relax knowing you will have ample time to prepare the space and enjoy the wedding reception of your dreams. When you book a room in this elegant Wichita wedding venue, you will have use of it from 9 a.m. to 2 a.m. For an extra $300, you may have the room on Friday night to set up for the next day.
