# Glory House Events Irving, Texas

Glory House has a romantic, intimate feel. We make it easy for you by including reception itinerary, ceremony coordination, table linen and centerpieces, lots of candle light, delicious appetizers and entrees, and drinks. You may bring you own alcohol, we provide the bar setup and service. We are located in Heritage Crossing in downtown Irving, Texas.
