# Wedgewood Metro Oakland, California

![Photo of Wedgewood Metro](/media/CA/9770/wedgewood-metro-oakland-ca.jpg)

Overlooking the 880 Freeway near the Oakland Airport is one of the best outdoor wedding locations in the Northern California. Wedgewood Wedding and Banquet Center at Metropolitan Golf Links is the country club setting for a Bay Area wedding venue without the country club price tag that has eluded you until now.  
  
Some of the spectacular, unique features of our Oakland wedding location:  
  
* Golf course wedding site with sweeping views of San Francisco skyline in the background  
* State-of-the-art 3,000 square foot event tent with a built-in bar, sound system, dance floor, carpeting, and dropped fans  
* Tent can hold up to 225 people, and combined with the banquet hall Metro can serve 350  
* Great East Bay location for corporate and non-profit events  
* Awarded sanctuary status by Audubon International
