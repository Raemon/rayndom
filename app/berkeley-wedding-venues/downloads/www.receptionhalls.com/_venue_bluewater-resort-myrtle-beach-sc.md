# Bluewater Resort Myrtle Beach, South Carolina

We are your perfect destination or local location for your next dream wedding, special occasion or meeting. Bluewater Oceanfront Resort is at the southern end of the Grand Strand. We are 10 minutes from the Myrtle Beach International Airport, and conveniently located to Market Common, Broadway at the Beach, as well as other area attractions. Our large 3rd floor Atrium Room can accommodate up to 120 guests with access to our atrium for cocktail hour, food or beverage display’s. Located on the 15th floor, the Blue Eyes Lounge can accommodate up to 55 guests and offers spectacular views of the Atlantic Ocean and Myrtle Beach. Room rentals include tables and chairs. Your ceremony can be on the oceanfront patio or from our private beach area. We can make your planning process easier with our additional services and list of vendors. Outside caterers are permitted. Our meeting rooms have secured WiFi, bluetooth conference tools, and large screen TV’s for presentations. We have an onsite restaurant, seasonal tiki bar, and complimentary secured garage parking. We can arrange overnight accommodations, elevating your experience for your destination dream wedding.
