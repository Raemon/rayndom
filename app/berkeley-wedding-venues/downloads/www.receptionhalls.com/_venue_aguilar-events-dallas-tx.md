# AGUILAR EVENTS Dallas, Texas

Aguilar Events is a family owned business run by the Aguilar Family since 2011. We pride ourselves with having the lowest pricing in the business. We offer the lowest price we can to our customer's in the following areas: hall, linens, decorations, and catering. While our goal is to beat the competition, we do not sacrifice attention to detail and customer service. Our objective is that every customer has the best experience with us which in turn will refer a friend to us. Referrals are our business. Call to arrange a no obligation visit to see what we can offer you for your next event. We are a full service venue were you can get just what you need.
