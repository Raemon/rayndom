# The Melon Ballroom Davis, California

Attached to Davis' beloved local music store, Watermelon Music, The Melon Ballroom is used by teachers and performers of all types for recitals, concerts, meetings, and receptions. This small live performance venue is perfect for hosting live music, small stage plays, fancy dinners, parties and more! The humble space features a 12'x16' stage, a small kitchen, green room, 100 banquet-style chairs, a baby grand piano and in-house sound system. Contact Watermelon Music for more info!
