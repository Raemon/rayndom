# Stover Hall Luray, Virginia

Nestled between the Blue Ridge and the Massanutten Mountain Ranges in the beautiful Shenandoah Valley, Stover Hall Wedding Venue is an example of Virginia's history, architecture and nature at its best. Situated on five acres and surrounded by magnificent views, Stover Hall's elegant 1830's main house, period outbuildings and rustic, updated barn with modern conveniences present the perfect venue for your special day.   
At Stover Hall our couples enjoy the convenience of holding their rehearsal, rehearsal dinner, wedding and reception all in one fabulous location.
