# The Maplewood "Little" Club Maplewood, New Jersey

Nestled in a historic residential neighborhood, The Maplewood Club offers a beautiful setting for private events. Family and friends or professional colleagues will be immediately at ease in this secluded, treed setting in the heart of Maplewood. Established as a Tennis Club in 1916, the charming Clubhouse has a newly updated dining room, and is designed to host groups of up to 100 guests. A lovely garden and terrace provide a beautiful setting for cocktail hour. We have parking for 50 cars, plus Maplewood is only a 35 minute train ride from Penn Station NY, and only 15 minutes from Newark International Airport.
