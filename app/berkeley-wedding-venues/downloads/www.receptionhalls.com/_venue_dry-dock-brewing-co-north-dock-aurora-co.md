# Dry Dock Brewing Co - North Dock Aurora, Colorado

Get the real brewery experience and host your event or wedding amidst the fermentation tanks. Throw in some of our fun selections of games like Cornhole, Giant Jenga, or our favorite, Can Smash, and you are bound to have a great event. If our amazing beer is not enough, you also have the option of bringing in wine and spirits.
