# Days Inn Towson, Maryland

![Photo of Days Inn](/media/MD/12589/days-inn-towson-md.jpg)

Host a memorable event with help from the dedicated staff of Days Inn Towson. Our hotel is home to a versatile banquet facility in Towson near downtown Baltimore. With 2,800 square feet of space, and comfortable accommodations for your guests, we take the stress out of planning your next event. Whether you are planning a sales conference for medical professionals, awards banquet for a social group or a family wedding party, our venue makes it so you don't have to sacrifice the comfort or convenience of your guests for affordability. Contact the event staff of Days Inn Towson to learn more about our hotel's great location near the Baltimore Convention Center.
