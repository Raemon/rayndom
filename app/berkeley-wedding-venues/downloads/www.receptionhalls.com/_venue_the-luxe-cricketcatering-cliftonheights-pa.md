# The Luxe-Venue By Cricket Catering Lansdowne, Pennsylvania

The Luxe-Venue by Cricket Catering is a beautiful reception venue in Delaware County designed to cater to all of life's milestones, including Showers, Rehearsal Dinners, Anniversaries, Celebrations of Life, Holiday Celebrations and Corporate Meetings. Enjoy elegance and professional courteous service along with exquisitely customized and perfectly executed and presented menus. Your event will be complete with custom colored and textured linens, gold chivari chairs and fine table setting details.  
  
Handicap accessible.  
  
Parking is available in our lot along with additional street parking and municipal lot parking.
