# Bridgetown Mill House Langhorne, Pennsylvania

We are a Fine dining restaurant and Inn. We are located on 8 acres of beautiful forest and a creek. The building was built in 1777 and was used as a residence of Mill House owners. The Old Mill ruins still stand to this day on the property.Our Main Dining room can fit comfortably 55 people. On cold days, we have a wood burning fireplace going. Our Great Hall dining room has a electric fireplace and that can fit up to 35 people. Wood dining room fits comfortably 20 people it also has an electric fireplace.  
We have a beautiful patio outside which can be used in spring and summer time for dancing, ceremony or outside eating for 100+ guests. Tent events are a favorite of our 100+ guests. We would like the honor to cater to your event. Our Inn has five romantic bedrooms, which come with free Wi-Fi and HBO. Breakfast included as well.
