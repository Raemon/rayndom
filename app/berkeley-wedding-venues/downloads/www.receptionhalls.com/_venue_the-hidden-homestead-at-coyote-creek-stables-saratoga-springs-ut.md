# The Hidden Homestead at Coyote Creek Stables Saratoga Springs, Utah

![Photo of The Hidden Homestead at Coyote Creek  Stables](/media/UT/11999/the-hidden-homestead-at-coyote-creek-stables-saratoga-springs-ut.jpg)

We are a full service wedding and event venue specializing in all things rustic-formal. Our space of over 4,000 sq. feet can accommodate seating for 160 guests and standing room for over 300 guests. We charge per day, and not per person, making it very affordable. Our pricing starts at $1799 Tues-Thurs and $2199 Fri-Sat.
