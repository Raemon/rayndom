# Izaak Walton Event Center Sioux Falls, South Dakota

The Sioux Falls Izaak Walton League chapter was chartered in 1925. It is the oldest and most respected conservation club in South Dakota. The club is nestled near the northeast edge of Sioux Falls in a secluded, private country setting surrounded by nature.   
  
Throughout the year, Sioux Falls Izaak Walton hosts a variety of functions from weddings and reunions to company picnics. The private country setting allows for a variety of outdoor activities, including trap shooting & conservation for all ages to enjoy.   
  
The events held during the year allow Sioux Falls Izaak Walton to honor its mission statement by providing endowments towards the preservation of nature and the conservation of its land and resources for future generations.  
  
The grounds are the perfect place to host a wedding or other special event with a gazebo, outdoor grill, picnic tables, playground and more. The inside provides a unique setting complete with tables, chairs, kitchen, and bar.
