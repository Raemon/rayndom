# Dream Makers Moncks Corner, South Carolina

Dream Makers is an event hall like no other. It's located in Moncks Corner, SC off of North Highway 52, near Heritage Cleaners and not too far from various hotels. The event hall is affordable and it has a venue occupancy of about 200 people. Here at Dream Makers, we work with our customers, consulting and coordinating their events from start to finish. We make sure every event is your dream come true. Our staff of professional wedding coordinators, graphic designers, fashion designers, audio/video specialist, cosmetologist, nail techs and more can give you the tools you need to make your event a success. We also provide many event supplies and equipment that are not normally used in events. Our prices are competitive. We offer hourly rates and block rates. If you are a promoter we make sure that your event is profitable, providing you with graphic services, advertisement, catering, audio/visual equipment. For more information on our services, please contact us.
