# McNutt House - Main Floor Vicksburg, Mississippi

The McNutt House (circa 1826) offers 3 great venues certain to fullfil your event needs from business meeting to party or banquet. If you are preparing for a smaller event in a charming setting, look no further. The first floor of the main house includes the parlor and joining dining room area adaptable to host a small wedding, social, rehearsal dinner or party. The adjacent sunroom overlooks the gardens and contains a wet bar and buffet well suited as a caterer's staging area or bartender station.
