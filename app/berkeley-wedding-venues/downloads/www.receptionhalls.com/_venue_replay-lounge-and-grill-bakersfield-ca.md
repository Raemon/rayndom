# Replay Lounge and Grill Bakersfield, California

Replay Lounge and Grill offers a banquet room, nightclub, and restaurant. We have three projection screens and 18 HD TV's to have presentations or pictures/videos to be displayed. We have a large kitchen to prepare our food or self catering is allowed. Fully staffed with chefs, bartenders, servers, and Dj's. The Quality Inn is located next door for any out of town guests that would like to stay the night. Group rate discounts for the Hotel are available.
