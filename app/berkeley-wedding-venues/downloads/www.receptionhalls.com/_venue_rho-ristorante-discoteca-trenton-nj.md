# Rho Ristorante Discoteca Trenton, New Jersey

![Photo of Rho Ristorante Discoteca](/media/NJ/10344/rho-ristorante-discoteca-trenton-nj.jpg)

Rho provides an upscale, casual dining &amp; nightclub experience. For private parties and corporate events from 25 to 1500 guests, Rho is your party planning solution. We are located along the Delaware River, in Historic Trenton.  
  
Rho offers impressive food, beverage and service for the following events:  
  
  
Anniversaries and Birthdays  
Bar/Bat Mitzvahs  
Business Meetings  
Company Picnics  
Fund Raising Events  
Graduations  
Custom Events  
Holiday Parties  
Reunions  
School Dances &amp; Proms  
Showers &amp; Rehearsal Dinners  
Sweet 16s  
Wedding Receptions
