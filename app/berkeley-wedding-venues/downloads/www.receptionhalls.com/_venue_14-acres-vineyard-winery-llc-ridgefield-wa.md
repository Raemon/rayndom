# 14 Acres Vineyard & Winery, LLC Ridgefield, Washington

Spacious, tranquil winery and vineyard located at St. Ridgefield, WA offering services for holding small and large-scale weddings. Exchanging marital views in a natural and open setting, while enjoying the beautiful hills as the backdrop is an option. A classic barrel house for closed door wedding ceremonies, adorned with vaulted ceilings is on offer, too. Facilities offered at the barrel house include a dance floor and Wireless Internet. Accommodation capacity available for 800 delegates.
