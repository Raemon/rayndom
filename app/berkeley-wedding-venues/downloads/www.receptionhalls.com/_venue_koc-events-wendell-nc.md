# Wake Services & Management Wendell, North Carolina

Wake service Rentals is committed to providing a venue that is reasonably priced while providing the perfect atmosphere for your special event! Our event center is located on approximately 30 acres, located near Highway 64 and New Hope Road. We offer a banquet hall, prep kitchen, a picnic pavilion, beautiful pond, lighted volleyball courts, picnic area, softball field, a lounge serving beer and wine, and plenty of parking for your event. Our event center is perfect for weddings, birthdays, off-site corporate meetings and family reunions. Volleyball, softball and baseball leagues are welcome!
