# Eagle Brook Country Club Geneva, Illinois

Thank you for checking out Eagle Brook Country Club. Our club is located in Geneva, IL. We specialize in weddings, quinceaneras, showers, corporate events/luncheons, holiday parties and many other events. Our venue consists of a grand ballroom that fits up to 300 people, with a beautiful attached terrace that overlooks the golf course. Our other room is the "Eagle's Nest" which holds up to 40 people and has a beautiful fireplace and large windows overlooking the golf course. Please feel free to stop by our venue, or give us a call with any questions you may have!
