# Firehouse 1918 Gainesville, Texas

This was the Fire station for over 100 years has been reimagined for your unique gathering. We are located in the heart of downtown Gainesville with plenty of parking. FireHouse 1918 is perfect for receptions, weddings, birthdays, corporate events, rehearsal dinners, markets, showers, etc, It is the perfect venue for up to 96 people, a unique and intimate event, along with a bar and serving areas, Bridal Suite and upscale restrooms.  
  
We also have two Historic Airbnb Lofts available upstairs! Historic Calaboose Loft and Historic Bell Tower Loft!
