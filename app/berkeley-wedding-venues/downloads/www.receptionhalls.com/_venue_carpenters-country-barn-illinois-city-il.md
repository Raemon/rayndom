# Carpenter's Country Barn Illinois City, Illinois

![Photo of Carpenter's Country Barn](/media/IL/303/carpenters-country-barn-illinois-city-il.jpg)

Built in the early 1900's this renovated barn offers the friendship and country setting that only an old barn can provide. Situated in the heart of farm country and conveniently located just 8 miles from Muscatine, IA and 25 miles from Moline, IL on Rt. 92. in Illinois City, IL. The Barn can seat up to 200 guests and features a bar, bathroom, kitchen and heat.
