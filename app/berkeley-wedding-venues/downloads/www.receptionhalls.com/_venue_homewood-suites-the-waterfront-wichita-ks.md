# Homewood Suites @The Waterfront Wichita, Kansas

![Photo of Homewood Suites @The Waterfront](/media/KS/11906/homewood-suites-the-waterfront-wichita-ks.jpg)

Plan your meeting, corporate event, or wedding at Homewood Suites @ The Waterfront Wichita to take advantage of our lake setting, beautiful grounds, and multiple meeting room venue offerings. Our meeting spaces available for rent include a boardroom, The Walker Library, our Lighthouse traditional banquet-sized meeting space, plus outdoor seating as well. While you’re here, take advantage of our dedicated staff, A/V equipment, catering options, WiFi internet service and much more. During downtime or for team building, guests can enjoy the many offerings our lakeside location offers including, a putting green, jogging trail, a full basketball court and of course great views of the lake.
