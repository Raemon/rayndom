# La Estancia Bella Wimberley, Texas

La Estancia Bella is a wedding venue uniquely situated in the heart of the Texas Hill Country on a stunning 100 acre family longhorn ranch. A distinctive experience with a brand new 6500sf grand reception hall, stage, dance floor, catering kitchen, courtyards, gardens, barn, plentiful parking, bride/groom dressing quarters, indoor/outdoor bars, lodging and gazebo with terraced seating. Experience winding trails under spectacular oak trees through a breathtakingly memorable experience. Hill Country’s best at your fingertips; wineries, shopping, live music, restaurants…
