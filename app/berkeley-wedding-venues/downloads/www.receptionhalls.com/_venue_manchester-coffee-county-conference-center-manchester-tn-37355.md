# Manchester-Coffee County Conference Center Manchester, Tennessee

Complete with a sprawling 13,000 square feet of flexible meeting &amp; event space, The Manchester-Coffee County Conference Center is Coffee County's premier facility for on-point professional gatherings, stunning receptions, and efficient small meetings.   
  
In addition, The Manchester-Coffee County Conference Center, situated along I-24, is the only conference space within a 30 mile radius to offer its guests convenient connected lodging, via Holiday Inn Express Manchester, along with multiple surrounding hotel properties.  
  
Our primary objective as a full-service local conference center is to foster new relationships among the business community and beyond. Whether you are looking for meeting space, a fabulous wedding reception, or a slick corporate gathering, we invite you to join the Manchester-Coffee County Conference Center to make connections happen!
