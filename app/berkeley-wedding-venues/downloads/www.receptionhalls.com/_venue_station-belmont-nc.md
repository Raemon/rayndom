# Station Belmont, North Carolina

Now taking bookings for private parties, our unique and historical building is the perfect location for your next event. Right here beside the Belmont train tracks, our bar and charming patio is ideal for gatherings, celebrations, reunions and networking events. We will work with you to tailor the space to your needs.
