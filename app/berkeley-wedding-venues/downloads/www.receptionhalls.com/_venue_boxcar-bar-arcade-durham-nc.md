# Boxcar Bar + Arcade Durham, North Carolina

Boxcar Bar + Arcade is downtown Durham's ultimate arcade bar, featuring dozens of modern and classic arcade games, a full bar with 24 rotating craft drafts, pizza kitchen, year-round patio with covered and open-air seating (plus ping pong and other patio games). Order up fresh, house-made, preservative-free pizzas, soft pretzels and salads, catch live music on the patio, compete in free tournaments & score daily drink deals. We are 21+, dog-friendly and open 7 days/week!
