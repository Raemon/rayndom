# The Taverne of Richfield Richfield, Ohio

The Taverne of Richfield is a wonderful, and rustic building from 1886 that can accommodate parties of 110 guests. Our chef inspired banquet menu offers many choices for you and your guests. The Taverne offers different packages that are affordable for any budget. We are more then happy to entertain for weddings, rehearsal dinners, bridal showers, baby showers, or corporate events. ### Venue Details

Capacity (Seated / Cocktail): **110 / 70**

We offer 3 different rooms for your needs. We have a Rose Room for family get together or corporate events that hold 25. We have a comfortable low key Martini Lounge that holds 60. And our beautiful, and rustic Grand Ball Room holds 110.

Max Number of Simultaneous Events: **3**

Outside Catering Permitted (BYO): **No**

### Pricing

Budget Tier:  **$**

Minimim price for 100 guests: **$ 2,300**

Site/Rental Fee: **$200**

We are an affordable banquet facility, that can work with different budgets to make your special event a memorable occasion. The Taverne's pricing starts at $23 (for 3 courses) to $50 per plate. Deposits are used as a holding deposit, they are refunded at the end of your banquet. There are no hidden fees when holding you event with us; room fee and linen fee are incorporated into your pricing.
