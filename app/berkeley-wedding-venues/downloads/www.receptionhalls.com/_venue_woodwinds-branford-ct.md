# WoodWinds Branford, Connecticut

Nestled away from civilization on seven wooded acres of pristine gardens, waterscapes, and waterfalls. Further enhancing this setting is the wedding bridge over a natural pond and flower bedecked gazebo.  
  
A spacious deck overlooking the Branford river, the floral gazebo surrounded by lush acreage, or the gardens with the sloping waterfall are just a few of the splendid sights for your outdoor ceremony. WoodWinds can also accommodate full outdoor catered receptions in it's tented lawn area set against a dramatic wooded landscape.  
  
WoodWinds' award winning cuisine is a sumptuous feast with an International flair, with special attention paid to detail and artful presentation. Your guests will be delighted with the splendor and dedication to detail and beauty.  
  
WoodWinds' professional staff shares over thirty years experience in creating your perfect day and delight in helping with every aspect from guiding you with music and photographic choices to in-house invitation shopping, cake and confection selections and unique decorating ideas.
