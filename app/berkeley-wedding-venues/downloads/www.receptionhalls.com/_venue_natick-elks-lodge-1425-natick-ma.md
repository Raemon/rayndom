# Natick Elks Lodge 1425 Natick, Massachusetts

We have one of the largest function halls in Natick, MA. Our function hall has been recently renovated with new paint and carpeting. We can accommodate up to 250 people and we have a full kitchen and bar. One of the largest dance floors in the area and a stage for music, etc. We have full sound and have outdoor amenities as well. Plenty of parking is available.  
  
Our function hall is available for parties, business meetings, training seminars and many other types of functions. Rental fees for “party type” functions are based on a 5 hour rental. You are welcome to come in ahead of time on the day of the function to decorate at no additional charge.
