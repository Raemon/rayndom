# The Saturday Club Wayne, Pennsylvania

If you are looking for a place to have a special party or event, then The Saturday Club is the place for you! Whether it's a wedding reception for 100 or a birthday party for 25, The Saturday Club's ballroom can accommodate your next function.  
  
With our soaring wood beamed ceiling and beautiful maple hardwood floor, our clubhouse is also suitable for rehearsal dinners, corporate meetings, fundraising events, bar and bat mitzvahs, family and school reunions, and all types of parties. The room also has a stage which is perfect for a band, disc jockey, and other performances.   
  
Pricing is as follows:
