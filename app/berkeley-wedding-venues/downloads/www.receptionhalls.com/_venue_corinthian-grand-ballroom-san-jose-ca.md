# Corinthian Grand Ballroom San Jose, California

The Corinthian Grand Ballroom is Silicon Valley’s most elegant and historic private venue.  
  
Located in San Jose, we are within minutes of Cupertino, Campbell, Los Gatos, Mountain View, Saratoga, Morgan Hill, and Milpitas.  
  
The Corinthian possesses all of the space, decor, and resources to provide an exceptional experience for a wide variety of events. Our team has delighted thousands of event hosts and guests – you are in excellent hands.
