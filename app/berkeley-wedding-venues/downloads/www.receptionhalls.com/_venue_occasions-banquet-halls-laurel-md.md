# Occasions Banquet Halls Laurel, Maryland

Occasions Banquet Hall is an elegant and affordable full service banquet hall specializing in weddings, Quinceañeras, Bar and Bat Mitzvahs, Sangeets, Henna parties, and Maharanis.  
  
Choosing Occasions Banquet Hall is a statement of its own. Holding an event at our reception venue says you choose elegance and top service at an affordable price.  
  
Please call us on (240) 801-2171 for an introduction to our event facilities and services, to schedule a tour of our event halls, or to reserve a date for your next event.  
  
Exceptional events happen at Occasions. Create your dream celebration with us.
