# Pawtucket Armory Arts Center Pawtucket, Rhode Island

The Drill Hall at the Pawtucket Armory Arts Center is by far the most unique event rental space in Rhode Island. It is perfect for CHARITY EVENTS, WEDDINGS, GALAS, PROMS, CRAFT SHOWS, EXPOS, INDOOR MARKETS and FAIRS. Our size and uniqueness allows us to cater to any stretch of the imagination. With the capacity for up to 600 people this 11,000 square foot event hall is the most cost-efficient venue in New England.
