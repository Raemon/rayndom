# Red Horse Barn Huntington Beach, California

Red Horse Barn – Huntington Beach's Hidden Garden Gem  
Nestled within a peaceful equestrian center just minutes from the sandy beaches of coastal Orange County, Red Horse Barn is a one-of-a-kind wedding and event venue that beautifully combines rustic charm with an elegant garden atmosphere. With its lush green lawns, mature trees wrapped in romantic twinkle lights, and a serene ceremony lawn framed by natural foliage and white fences, the venue offers a timeless setting that feels both intimate and expansive.  
  
Whether you envision a grand celebration under the stars or a cozy gathering with your closest loved ones, Red Horse Barn offers flexible layouts that can accommodate up to 250 guests while still maintaining a warm, inviting feel for smaller weddings and events. The venue’s natural beauty speaks for itself, requiring minimal décor to create a magical and memorable experience.  
  
Our thoughtfully curated all-inclusive packages are designed to simplify your planning process while elevating your event. Starting at $23,000 for up to 100 guests (site fee included), each package includes a comprehensive selection of top-tier wedding services: catering, coordination, floral design, DJ/entertainment, rentals, staffing, and more. Additional guests over the initial 100 can be added at a per-person rate, and customizations are available to reflect your unique vision.  
  
From the moment you book, you’ll be guided by our team of experienced wedding professionals who are dedicated to making your day seamless and stress-free. We pride ourselves on personalized service, exceptional attention to detail, and a commitment to bringing your dream wedding to life.  
  
Red Horse Barn is more than just a venue—it’s a place where unforgettable moments are made. Come experience the magic for yourself.
