# Oak Point Events Sacramento, California

Our main hall boasts over 1700 square feet, ample parking and room to host up to 150 guests.  
  
Oak Point Events facility is conveniently and centrally located 15 minutes from Downtown Sacramento and just over 20 minutes from the Sacramento International Airport.   
  
Finding the perfect venue also includes finding the right price for your budget. Oak Point Events offers an exceptional space without breaking the bank.  
  
We offer a kitchen space that is equipped with a large refrigerator and food heater for keeping food cold or hot and ready to serve.  
  
All of our packages include an on-site hostess for the duration of your event and a comprehensive cleaning. Because we host private parties, alcohol is allowed at your own risk. Liability insurance is optional, but recommended, and the renting party will be required to sign a liability release form.
