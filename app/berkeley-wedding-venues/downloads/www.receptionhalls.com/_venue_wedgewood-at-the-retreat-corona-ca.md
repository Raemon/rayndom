# The Retreat by Wedgewood Weddings Corona, California

Nestled atop a hill in Corona, The Retreat by Wedgewood Weddings offers endless miles of incredible valley views. A true escape, this premier wedding venue is beaming with the glamor of a Tuscan-style villa. Say your vows on the manicured lawn complete with neatly trimmed hedges, lush trees, and beautiful white rose bushes. Then, celebrate at your choice of the grand ballroom or under the stars of the outdoor terrace with glimmering city lights in the distance. This romantic escape perfectly balances lavish taste along with affordability to give you a truly unforgettable experience.  
  
\- Private Tuscan Villa with Gorgeous Finishes  
\- Fountain Courtyards and Outdoor Fireplaces  
\- Expansive Outdoor Terrace with Valley Views  
\- High-End Gated Community  
\- Exclusivity - One Event at a Time  
  
The Retreat by Wedgewood Weddings feels secluded in a gated community yet easily reachable from Los Angeles and Orange Counties. The highly-rated team at this picturesque event venue executes a flawless wedding for couples - all while ensuring a stress-free planning experience leading up to it. It attracts couples seeking a destination wedding as well as local couples from Fullerton, Riverside, Moreno Valley, Temecula, Irvine, Ontario, Rancho Cucamonga, San Bernardino, Chino, Santa Ana, Fontana, Redlands, Covina, Yorba Linda, Lake Elsinore, Pomona, and Murrieta.  
  
***  
Come and explore The Retreat by Wedgewood Weddings and learn what makes our approach to special occasions so remarkable. We combine a spectacular wedding venue, with a huge amount of wedding expertise, a generous helping of artisan vendors, and a delicious dollop of beautiful SoCal sunshine!
