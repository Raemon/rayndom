# Mehfil Banquet and Conference Center Germantown, Maryland

For a very special occasion, Mehfil banquet and conference center offers a beautiful and cozy venue, with all the comforts and elegant decoration that will make any host and their guests shine.  
  
With a finely decorated lobby and hall, an 18' x 10' stage, a comfortable VIP lounge, a fully equipped service room and an 78' x 54' main hall area for social events with up to 200 people (max. seating with limited dance area) or a conference event with up to 240 people (max. seating, No dance area), without a doubt, Mehfil will make any type of event a dream come true.  
  
Rental prices includes the following amenities:  
  
\- 78' x 54' main hall with round tables and cushioned chairs for dining and rectangular tables for buffet style food service.   
\- 18' x 10' Stage, hall with Bluetooth audio, video projector with large screen and wireless microphones system.  
\- Full equipped Kitchenette for food service: Refrigerator, freezer, food warmers, electric range, microwave and ice maker.  
\- VIP Lounge.  
\- WiFi Internet Access.  
\- Free self-parking.   
\- Up to 4 complimentary reserved parking spaces near the entrance for the event celebrant upon request.  
\- Wheelchair accessible.  
\- Kids Highchairs.  
  
Other amenities included with Mehfil Catering Staff services:  
\- Chafing Dishes and food serving ware.  
\- Cold and hot drinks dispensers.  
\- Chinaware (dinnerware, flatware and glassware) and charger plates (Available only in Gold color).  
  
Contact us for additional services prices and availability.
