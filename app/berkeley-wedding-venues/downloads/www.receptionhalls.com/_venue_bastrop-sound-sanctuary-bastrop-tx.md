# Bastrop Sound Sanctuary Bastrop, Texas

Bastrop Sound Sanctuary is a beautiful historic church building that is now available for hosting many types of events and gatherings. We include chairs, tables, and other common supplies with your booking so you can save time and money when planning and setting up your event. You can rent a room or the whole property for an hour or even for the whole weekend. Take a look around www.BastropSoundSanctuary.com and if you have questions please give us a call or send us a message!
