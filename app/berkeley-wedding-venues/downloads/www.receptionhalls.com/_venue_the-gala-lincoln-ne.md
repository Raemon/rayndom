# The Gala Lincoln, Nebraska

The Gala - Lincoln Reception Hall &amp; Event Center  
  
The Gala - Lincoln is designed with successful events and parties in mind. We have over 30 years experience in event planning and consider each event special and work to design every event with the client's interest in mind  
  
7500 total sq. ft  
375 person capacity  
Large patio &amp; beer garden  
Built in professional dj booth &amp; nightclub sound and lighting system  
Up lighting and ambient lighting available  
Chairs &amp; tables and all clean up included in every package  
Stage, backdrops and decorations available  
Full service and professionally staffed bar  
Large walk in cooler and kitchen prep area  
New and very clean restrooms  
Linens available  
Event coordinator / planner available for all events  
  
To make catering easy and more affordable The Gala - Lincoln allows our clients to provide their own catering
