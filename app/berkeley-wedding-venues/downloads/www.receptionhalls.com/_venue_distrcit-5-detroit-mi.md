# District 5 Detroit, Michigan

Located in the heart of Detroit’s Eastern Market, District 5 is a great space for intimate weddings, brunches, rehearsal dinners, corporate, non-profit, and so much more. Completely renovated with high-end finishes, District 5 is a unique industrial, yet refined, space. With 4,000 square feet of event space, District 5 comes standard with a state-of-the-art sound system and DJ hookup, multiple TVs, surround sound speakers, venue furniture, two bars, a rooftop lounge, WIFI, and more.
