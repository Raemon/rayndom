# Fair Hills Resort Detroit Lakes, Minnesota

Located on the north shore of beautiful Pelican Lake, Fair Hills Resort is the perfect destination location for the most memorable day of your life. Nestled on 27 acres, with 27 holes of golf available, two swimming pools, a Jacuzzi, six tennis courts and with 1000 feet of lake shore, there are countless activities to entertain you and your guests.  
  
Our full service staff will work with you to make all the arrangements for a picture perfect wedding including rehearsal dinner locations and catering, ceremony locations, cocktail hour, dinner, food, beverage and cake, dance, honeymoon cabin and lodging for guests.  
  
You and your guests will feel relaxed and invigorated by the ambiance of the northern Minnesota lakes and woods. Let us help you build your dream wedding at Fair Hills Resort!
