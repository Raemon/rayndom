# Ravella Lake Las Vegas Resort & Spa Henderson, Nevada

![Photo of Ravella Lake Las Vegas Resort & Spa](/media/NV/10106/ravella-lake-las-vegas-resort-spa-henderson-nv.jpg)

Only 17 miles from the Las Vegas Strip but worlds away from an ordinary experience, Ravella at Lake Las Vegas attracts celebrities, thrill seekers, or those seeking a peaceful retreat with easy access to the bright lights of the city. From boating under the deep blue desert sky to sipping cocktails next to a lagoon, Ravella at Lake Las Vegas lets guests revel in a series of magical moments.  
  
The Mediterranean-inspired architecture of the adjacent village and its picturesque marina invokes the feel of the Old World, with upscale shops, fine dining, and championship golf all just steps away. Our day spa with its full-service salon is staffed by a team of professionals waiting to soothe your tired muscles or give you a new cut and color so you can arrive at dinner fully refreshed. Enjoy a casual meal on the terrace as you look out over landscaped gardens or let us deliver a snack or a beverage straight to your poolside lounge chair.  
  
Your celebration should be as individual as you are, and at Ravella at Lake Las Vegas, we're committed to bringing your unique vision to life. View Ravella's La Capella di Amore wedding site at www.ravellaweddings.com  
  
Whether you'd like your guests to put on their dancing shoes for an elaborate gala or kick off their shoes entirely for an intimate wedding on the beach, our staff will work with you to make sure every detail is in place. Take advantage of our beautiful surroundings by hosting an anniversary party in the Florentine Garden, enjoying cocktails on the lawn of the Pontevecchio Park, or walking down the aisle of the charming La Capella di Amore, located directly over water on our Pontevecchio Bridge. Elegantly decorated indoor venues, such as our Estate Boardroom and Monte Lago Ballroom, are visually impressive, while our award-winning banquet menus delight your taste buds.  
  
Our wedding and event packages allow you to simplify the planning process while preserving your celebration's unique character. Contact our experts to find out how Ravella at Lake Las Vegas can help you turn your dream into reality at (702) 567-4734.  
  
Comfortable productivity -- those are the bywords that inspired the design of our event venues, which cover 39,000 total square feet meeting space out of which is 7,400 square feet of pre-function space including our Pontevecchio Pavilion with its stunning lakeside views and 5,600 square feet of outdoor function space. For a more unconventional approach to a professional gathering, take advantage of our extensive selection of outdoor venues and host an al fresco meeting. Our skilled culinary staff will enhance your event with an expansive banquet featuring wholesome, energizing selections.  
  
Whatever the scope may be, we're committed to providing you with an experience that reflects your vision and leaves a lasting impression on each of your guests.
