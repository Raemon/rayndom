# Wake County Shrine Club Raleigh, North Carolina

The Wake County Shrine Club is located near Crabtree Mall and is located on beautiful landscape property on Lead Mine Rd. We allow clients to hire their own caterers for their event. We do not offer an in-house caterer. A stage and dance floor compliment our facility along with a split level floor plan. Call us for a tour. We have a very competitive pricing schedule.
