# The White Dove Barn Beechgrove, Tennessee

![Photo of The White Dove Barn](/media/TN/13102/the-white-dove-barn-beechgrove-tn-1.jpg)

The White Dove Barn is a rustic yet sophisticated wedding venue located in Beechgrove, Tennessee. Couples and guests will be conveniently located just 15 minutes from Murfreesboro. With this barn setting complete with rolling hills and wide open spaces, couples will be able to experience an abundant amount of charm on their wedding day.  
  
Facilities and Capacity  
Couples can invite up to 250 friends and family to their wedding ceremony or reception at The White Dove Barn. The barn, gleaming with its whitewashed interior and exterior backdrop provides a beautiful setting for weddings and receptions. The barn features elegant touches such as chandeliers and soaring windows to complement your wedding event and provide a clear view of the property’s scenic surroundings with mountains in the distance.  
  
Services Offered  
When couples say their vows at The White Dove Barn, they can rest assured that, regardless of the season, they will have the perfect charming and chic venue. The barn features air-conditioning for warmer season weddings as well as heat for cooler season weddings. Regardless of the style of your wedding, the planning team can help create the wedding of your dreams. The White Dove Barn now offers all-inclusive options with selections ranging from fine china, flatware, glassware, decor and more! All services purchased at The White Dove Barn include setup and teardown. Getting ready spaces are available for the respective wedding parties to make preparations for their special day!
