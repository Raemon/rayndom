# Hillendale Country Club Phoenix, Maryland

Nestled in the rolling hills of Baltimore County horse country, Hillendale Country Club is just minutes away from Hunt Valley, Towson and Bel Air and just a short drive from downtown Baltimore. Hillendale is a private, member-owned Club, but is available for non-members to host events as well.  
From Weddings, Corporate Events, Birthday Parties, Anniversary Celebrations, Sweet 16 or Bar/Bat Mitzvahs, Private Dinner Parties, Fundraising Events and Golf Outings of all sizes. Our experienced culinary and service staff are here to ensure you have the best experience, and a worry-free, memorable and enjoyable day. We help with the layout of the room, menu planning and any other questions or concerns you may have.
