# Distinction Catering & Special Events Hall Hayes, Virginia

![Photo of Distinction Catering & Special Events Hall](/media/VA/10789/distinction-catering-special-events-hall-hayes-va.jpg)

At Distinction, we take care to provide to our customers high quality services personalized for their unique needs.  
  
Distinction Catering and Special Events Hall is located in the White Marsh Shopping Center at 4888 George Washington Memorial Highway in Hayes, VA.  
  
Distinction is the area's newest full service catering hall for weddings, birthdays, anniversaries, banquets, school functions, showers, reunions, dinner theaters, recitals, fundraisers, corporate meetings, and trade shows. The elegant and spacious Enchanted Ballroom accommodates up to 300 guests. The Serendipity Room can accommodate up to 75 guests for theater seating or up to 54 table seated guests. Room rentals without catering are also available.  
  
Let us do all the work while you enjoy yourself!
