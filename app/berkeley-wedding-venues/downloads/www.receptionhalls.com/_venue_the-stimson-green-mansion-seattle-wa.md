# The Stimson-Green Mansion Seattle, Washington

The newly renovated Stimson-Green Mansion maintains its historic glamour and charm as one of Seattle’s original event venues. The splendor of the Stimson-Green Mansion derives from an assortment of vintage artifacts, fixtures, fireplaces and leaded windows. This beautiful, eclectic setting is the site of fine dining, creative cuisine and attentive services. Also known as Seattle's ultimate party house, we invite you to host your next event amongst the festive and warm ambiance of this amazing historic home.
