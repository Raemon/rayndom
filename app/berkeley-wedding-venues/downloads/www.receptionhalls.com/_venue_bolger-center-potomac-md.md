# Bolger Center Potomac, Maryland

![Photo of Bolger Center](/media/MD/10105/bolger-center-potomac-md.jpg)

The Bolger Center is an IACC approved venue offering a distraction free setting for meetings, trainings and team building programs. With 70 spacious meeting rooms for groups up to 450, classic meeting rooms, boardrooms and computer labs. Designed with comfort in mind, each of our guest rooms has been freshly renovated to provide a combination of relaxation and productivity. Within your guest room you will be surrounded by classic touches and modern amenities.   
  
From joyous celebrations to fairytale weddings, the Bolger Hotel &amp; Conference Center is the perfect host for your special day. Located just minutes from Washington D.C. and Bethesda, in the heart of Potomac, we provide an excellent venue for your event! Choose from our historic Stained Glass Hall, once the original chapel, lined with beautiful stained glass windows, or the new and elegant Ben Franklin Hall. Eighty three acres of lawns and gardens are a breathtaking backdrop for indoor and outdoor weddings, receptions and special events.   
  
At the Bolger Center, our experienced event planners will guide you every step of the way and ensure that we exceed your expectations on that special day.
