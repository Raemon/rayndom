# 1830 Chophouse Lebanon, Indiana

1830 Chophouse Event Center is located in a beautifully restored historic building in downtown Lebanon Indiana. The event center is perfect for business meetings, family gatherings, rehearsal dinners, and small weddings. The rooms offer full bars and a wide variety of food and beverage options to select from. We have a wide selection of premium whiskey and a trained chef on staff to design and prepare the menu. We offer convenient access to local hotels and Interstate access.
