# 1768 Hubbell Homestead Bennington, Vermont

Vermont is a distinctive wedding destination for couples who want to bring their loved ones together and have a truly memorable celebration. At the 1768 Hubbell Homestead your guests will be treated to an authentic Vermont experience. From cocktails around the post and beam barn, outfitted with comfortable rocking chairs, to relax and take in the Vermont scenery; to gourmet culinary creations served by local caterers who bring the taste of Vermont to life; to finally the bonfire, overlooking the Green Mountains. Our onsite event supervision service will attend to every detail, making the 1768 Hubbell Homestead the ultimate wedding day experience.
