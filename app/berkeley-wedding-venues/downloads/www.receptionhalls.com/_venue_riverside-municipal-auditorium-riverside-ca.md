# Riverside Municipal Auditorium Riverside, California

With access to the world’s greatest musical entertainment, full-service event production, state-of-the-art lighting and sound, custom menus and mixology, this 20,637 square-foot venue will exceed expectations of even the most discerning guest. Featuring three unique spaces, from the intimate Garden Rooms to the spacious Music Hall and versatile Courtyard, Riverside Municipal Auditorium has an atmosphere that celebrates extraordinary hospitality and service.
