# The Olson Mansion Maple Valley, Washington

The Olson Mansion property is a 42 acre venue in Maple Valley, WA. It was previously known as The Links at Olson Mansion, a 9-hole golf course. It is now owned by New Community Church.  
  
This historic landmark is the perfect place for your indoor and/or outdoor event! It is a one of a kind venue that possesses charm and elegance!
