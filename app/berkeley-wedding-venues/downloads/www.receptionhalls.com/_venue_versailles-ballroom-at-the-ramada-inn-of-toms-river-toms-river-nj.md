# Versailles Ballroom at the Ramada Inn of Toms River Toms River, New Jersey

The Versailles Ballroom at the Ramada Toms River is an Ocean County favorite for weddings, bar/bat mitzvahs, sweet 16's, meetings, and more! With over 7,500 square feet of meeting/event space, an exceptional experienced staff, numerous catering menus to fit your needs and budget, and plenty of accessories to personalize, we know you next event will be a memorable one. The beautiful Versailles Ballroom can hold up to 300 guests with dance floor (340 without) while our lush outdoor space is perfect for photos. We look forward to hosting your next event. Call us today at 732-719-1206 for details regarding all our package options.
