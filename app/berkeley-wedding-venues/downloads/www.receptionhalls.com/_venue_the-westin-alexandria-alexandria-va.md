# The Westin Alexandria Alexandria, Virginia

Event planners and guests repeatedly tell us that our hotel is beautiful in an "un" commercial style, “like a boutique hotel, just larger...". Our elegant hotel is an integral component of Alexandria’s newest community, The Carlyle at King Street Metro.  
  
Our large well-appointed ballroom and event spaces are perfect for Alexandria weddings, galas and other types of entertaining. We can host dinners up to 550 guests and cocktail receptions for over 1,000. With nearly 8,000 square feet of flexible Ballroom space and nearly that amount in addition for cocktail reception area, the possibilities for unique entertaining is endless. Additionally, there are seven beautiful smaller perimeter event spaces which can be used for a variety of more intimate functions or as vignettes attached to larger events.  
  
Our culinary team provides a world class dining experience with elegant menus expertly prepared and served at the peak of temperature and quality. Whether hosting multi day meetings with various dining functions attached or an evening dinner dance, guests always comment on how delicious the food is at the Westin Alexandria.  
  
Our hotel features 319 beautifully appointed guest rooms, including 10 Suites and one Presidential Suite with sweeping views of the Masonic Temple and Old Town Alexandria. All rooms feature High Speed Internet Access and flat screen LCD televisions. The signature Westin Heavenly Bed provides guests with the ultimate comfort in every guest room. Sumptuous, stylish, and plush, the all-white bed consists of a custom designed pillow top mattress, down blanket, three crisp sheets, luxurious duvet, and five dreamy pillows. To complement the bed, guest rooms also feature the highly acclaimed Westin Heavenly Bath. This includes a custom-designed rainfall showerhead featuring an additional hand held spray, as well as a curved shower rod that adds an extra eight inches of elbow room.
