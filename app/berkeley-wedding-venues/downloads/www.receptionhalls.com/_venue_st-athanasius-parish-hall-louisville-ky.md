# St Athanasius Parish Hall Louisville, Kentucky

[ Please Call ](tel:502-396-5614)

![Photo of St Athanasius Parish Hall](/media/KY/10817/st-athanasius-parish-hall-louisville-ky.jpg)

With a capacity of up to 600 people, the St. Athanasius Parish Hall provides an affordable venue for weddings, receptions, parties, meetings, recitals and community events.  
  
Some of the Hall's attributes are:  
  
A 100' x 80' main floor (8000 square feet) area  
A large stage area   
Rest rooms are accessible directly from the main floor  
Kitchen area with a walk-in refrigerator   
Beer and soft drink service  
Optional alcoholic beverage service  
Free parking
