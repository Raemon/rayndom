# Belle Grove Plantation King George, Virginia

Pledge your love in a beautiful wedding ceremony on Belle Grove’s front lawn, overlooking the Rappahannock River. Built in 1791, this venue offers a modern step back in time with historic elegance and southern charm. This stately, historic mansion has four Master Suites with private baths and views of the river and plantation   
  
Belle Grove Plantation, the birthplace of President James Madison, is located in King George County, Virginia. This hidden jewel on the Northern Neck is just 90 minutes from Washington DC or Baltimore, 50 minutes from Richmond and less than a half hour from Fredericksburg.
