# The Gem Theatre Detroit, Michigan

![Photo of The Gem Theatre](/media/MI/10066/the-gem-theatre-detroit-mi.jpg)

Detroit’s historic Gem Theatre provides dining spaces appropriate for intimate parties of 85 or large celebrations of up to 300 guests. We offer convenient, affordable, all-inclusive wedding packages that include plated entrée service and a premium bar surrounded by amazing 1920’s architecture.
