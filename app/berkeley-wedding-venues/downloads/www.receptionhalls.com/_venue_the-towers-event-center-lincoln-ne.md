# The Towers Event Center Lincoln, Nebraska

![Photo of The Towers Event Center](/media/NE/10790/the-towers-event-center-lincoln-ne.jpg)

The Towers Event Center is a elegant venue located in the Clocktower Shopping Center located @ 70th &amp; A Streets in Lincoln, Nebraska.  
We Cater and Host events with a capacity of 200+ guests. We maintain a full service catering and full service bar operation. We can accomodate most any event that you may be are hosting. We maintain an inviting, welcoming and gracious atmosphere. We look forward to the opportunity to assist you hosting your next special event.
