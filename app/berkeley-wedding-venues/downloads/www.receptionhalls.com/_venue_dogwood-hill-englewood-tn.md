# Dogwood Hill Englewood, Tennessee

Offering more covered space than other outdoor venues in the area, our country garden venue is the place where garden meets barn. Our authentic 100 year old reception barn is customized to include a greenhouse foyer. The attached atrium ceremony space with pergola provides a large amount of covered seating space in addition to a covered ceremony area. Air conditioned bridal suite, separate groom's suite, on site wedding planner and more.
