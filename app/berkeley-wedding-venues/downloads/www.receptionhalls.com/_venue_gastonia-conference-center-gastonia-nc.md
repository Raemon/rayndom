# Gastonia Conference Center Gastonia, North Carolina

Gastonia Conference Center is a symbol of the hard-working people of Gastonia: on the move toward civic, economic, and environmental sustainability. Designed to pay homage to the surrounding architecture and heritage of the area, the Center is also a state-of-the-art facility featuring the latest in communications and presentation technology and nine conveniently located electric vehicle (EV) charging stations. The iconic two-story facility accommodates over 450 attendees and 350 seated banquet function and features 10, separate, purpose-built and distraction-free meeting rooms, event space, and 5,100 square-foot ballroom.
