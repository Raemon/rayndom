# Maryland City Volunteer Fire Dept Laurel, Maryland

![Photo of Maryland City Volunteer Fire Dept](/media/MD/12036/maryland-city-volunteer-fire-dept-laurel-md.jpg)

General Information:  
Hall capacity: 125 seated guests  
Seating: Oblong tables (seat 8-10 people)  
Round tables (seat 8 people)  
Security Deposit: $400 due at contract signing  
Rental Fee: rates are per person based on party size and type  
  
We do NOT offer a cash bar option, however, if having alcohol at your event you must obtain a One-day Liquor License from the Anne Arundel County Board of Liquor Licensing  
  
Rental fee includes:  
4 full hours for event, plus 2 additional for set up immediately preceding your event time, plus 1 hour after event to cleanup.  
Floor set up and use of tables and chairs.  
Use of kitchen facility (you must provide all cookware, utensils, plates, napkins and flatware)  
  
Additional fees/services:  
Holiday bookings – please send email to: Hall@MCVFD.ORG  
  
Additional set up or event hours:  
$50 per additional set up hour for setup time.  
$150 per additional event hours for non alcoholic events only.  
$250 per additional hours to extend alcoholic events.
