# The Pavilion on the Hudson Poughkeepsie, New York

The Pavilion on the Hudson is perfect for when you want to host your private event or gathering outdoors. This unique special event space offers all of the benefits of a covered outdoor venue, with the convenience of indoor restroom facilities and onsite parking. It is the ideal venue for weddings, fundraising events and galas, birthday and graduation parties, family reunions, corporate events …and more!  
  
Nowhere will you find a private venue closer to the breathtaking Hudson River than at the Pavilion on the Hudson.  
  
We look forward to hosting a truly memorable experience for you and your guests!

### Venue Details

Capacity (Seated / Cocktail): **200 / 250**

Our open air pavilion is footsteps away from the Hudson River on the historic Poughkeepsie waterfront. Available May - September.

Max Number of Simultaneous Events: **1**

Outside Catering Permitted (BYO): **Yes**

Venue Type/Style: **Waterfront, Garden, Raw Space**
