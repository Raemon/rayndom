# Grand Crystal Ballroom At The Crest Lithonia, Georgia

From the moment you step foot into our beautiful event venue, you will be swept away by the modern glamor and style. Our high ceiling, beautiful chandeliers and large dance floor are only a few of the things that make our banquet hall so special.  
  
Once you get past the beauty of our venue, you will appreciate the excellent service from our professional staff and delight in the dining experience.  
  
Grand Crystal Ballroom At the Crest Banquet Hall offers 3 different venue spaces. Ballroom I: 6972 & 6976 Main Street can accomdate between 20 to 250 guest. Our Ballroom II: 7027 Swift Street can comfortably accommodate up to 500 guests inside and up to 1000 guests outdoor. At Grand Crystal Ballroom At the Crest Banquet Hall, we will do everything in our power to make sure your special occasion is exactly that—special!  
  
Our Team can handle all of your catering needs as well as event planning services.
