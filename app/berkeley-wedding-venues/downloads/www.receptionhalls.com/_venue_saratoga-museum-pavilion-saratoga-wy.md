# Saratoga Museum Pavilion Saratoga, Wyoming

This pavilion is on the grounds of the Saratoga Museum. Built in the 1990's, this venue is a wonderful place to celebrate a wedding, a birthday, a family reunion or even a baby shower! Concerts are welcome to. Electricity is available and the pavilion has beautiful lighting for evening events.

### Venue Details

Capacity (Seated / Cocktail): **80 / 400**

we have 75 metal folding chairs but the beautifully landscaped grounds allow for BYOC or blankets.

Max Number of Simultaneous Events: **1**

Outside Catering Permitted (BYO): **Yes**

On-Site Cuisine Options: **Use Your Own Caterer**

### Pricing

Site/Rental Fee: **$150**

The price is for entire use of the pavilion. Includes the use of banquet tables and metal folding chairs. Public restrooms within walking distance.
