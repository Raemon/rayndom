# Twelve West Main Thomasville, North Carolina

Twelve West Main is a charming venue for wedding ceremonies &amp; receptions located in a building built circa 1916 which still boasts original hardwood flooring and rich architectural detail. All of our bridal rentals include all day access to the facility, tables, chairs, table linens, staff set-up &amp; tear-down of your customized floorplan, and is the best value in the Triad. Call today to schedule your tour of Twelve West Main today!

Pricing varies by season and day of the week but is always considered a great value. We consider the months of April, May, June, September, and October to be 'peak' season.
