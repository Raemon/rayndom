# The DelRay Ballroom Lincoln, Nebraska

The atmosphere at The DelRay Ballroom is reminiscent of the 30’s and 40’s art deco period with a beautiful wooden dance floor along with rustic brick walls. The ballroom is located in the Historic Haymarket District and can seat up to 350 people or 700 standing.   
Food service is available through your choice of 3 high quality caterers. Annette’s Catering (402-267-7445) Premier Catering (402-323-8420) and The Green Gateau Restaurant &amp; Catering (402-477-3444). A full service bar is provided directly through The DelRay. Feel free to visit our website for more information. Hope to here from you!!
