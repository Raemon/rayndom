# The Capitol Room Washington, DC

![Photo of The Capitol Room](/media/DC/12327/the-capitol-room-washington-dc.jpg)

Located in historic Union Station, the Capitol Room overlooks the capitol, and is centrally located with easy access to Metro. Please ask about our flexible seating options for up to 200 guests. We have meal packages available for every event. From your plated meal, to your tailgate buffet.
