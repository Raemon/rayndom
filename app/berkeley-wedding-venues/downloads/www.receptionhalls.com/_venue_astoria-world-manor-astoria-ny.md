# Astoria World Manor Astoria, New York

Conveniently located at the foot of the Triboro Bridge in New York City. Luxurious banquet rooms designed in elegant, traditional and contemporary settings. We offer exquisitely decorated function rooms to suit every need from corporate to social events. Permanent wedding chapel, and two professional photography studios also on premises.
