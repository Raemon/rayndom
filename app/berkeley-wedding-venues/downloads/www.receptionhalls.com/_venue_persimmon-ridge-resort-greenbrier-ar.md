# Persimmon Ridge Resort Greenbrier, Arkansas

![Photo of Persimmon Ridge Resort](/media/AR/12539/persimmon-ridge-resort-greenbrier-ar.jpg)

Located on a sprawling 90 acres of lush green lawns, sparkling waters and serene woods in Greenbrier, AR is our little corner of the world that we just can’t wait to share with you! Persimmon Ridge Resort offers fully customizable events. From the most intimate gatherings to crowds of several hundred, we’ve got you covered! Our team is dedicated to your vision, and we will not rest until we have made your dream wedding into reality. Our unique offerings include scenic views, wildlife areas, fishing, lodging, hiking, biking, disc golf, picnic sites and so much more. Your event rental comes with exclusive use of the resort for the full day, set up and tear down, seating and tables up to 100 guests (additional seating available upon request), ceremony site and decorative arch of your choice (inside the event center or one of several outdoor locations about the property), reception site of your choice, event planning and coordination, security, walk through rehearsal opportunity, and one photography sitting before the big day (great for engagement photos or bridal portraits!). Give us a call or shoot us a message to schedule your private tour. We would love to see you in person, and we know you are going to #lovetheridge just as much as we do!
