# Salishan Spa & Golf Resort Gleneden Beach, Oregon

![Photo of Salishan Spa & Golf Resort](/media/OR/11680/salishan-spa-golf-resort-gleneden-beach-or.jpg)

Nestled between the beach and the bluffs, Salishan is the perfect location to enjoy your spectacular wedding celebration. Allow our professional and courteous catering staff to guide you through every glorious detail of this incomparable journey.
