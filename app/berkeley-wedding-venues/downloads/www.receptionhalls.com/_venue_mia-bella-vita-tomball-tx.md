# Mia Bella Vita Tomball, Texas

![Photo of Mia Bella Vita](/media/TX/12555/mia-bella-vita-tomball-tx.jpg)

Mia Bella Vita is an intimate, all-inclusive venue situated on 10 acres in Tomball, TX. We feature an outdoor ceremony area with a large indoor reception ballroom. We can accommodate 50-400 guests.  
Great for group staff meetings or team building retreats!
