# The Event Center @ Whitney Ranch Las Vegas, Nevada

The Event Center at Whitney Ranch is our diverse offerings for an unforgettable event in the heart of Las Vegas. This is a premier wedding venue located in Henderson, Nevada, conveniently situated right off the 95 and Russell Rd. It offers easy access from the freeway. It features a beautiful staircase grand entry and a large reception hall that can comfortably seat up to 230 guests.  
  
With the most unique and flexible package system in Las Vegas, we guarantee a package perfect for you, as you get to create the celebration of your dreams.
