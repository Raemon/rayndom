# The Foundry at Rae's Creek Augusta, Georgia

A special day requires a special setting.  
  
The Foundry at Rae’s Creek provides a beautiful backdrop, customized amenities and a serene setting for your most important occasions. The Foundry is an extraordinary choice for your Augusta event. Whether hosting a corporate function, birthday party, family reunion, charity gala, or wedding, you will find The Foundry to be a scenic and accommodating venue.  
  
Planning your event is made simple by a team of experienced professionals that will consult with you on every detail and expertly execute your vision for the day. From traditional Southern-style fare to contemporary cuisine, our catering director will work with you to build a menu that suites your style and taste. Guarantee that your special day will be memorable for everyone in attendance. Contact us today about hosting your event at The Foundry at Rae’s Creek.
