# The Old Town Chapel Bakersfield, California

The Old Town Chapel is a Historical Land Mark in Bakersfield CA. We offer a chapel, Ballroom, Courtyard and a variety of supplemental rooms including 4 bridal rooms and a grooms changing room. We boast a bell tower where guests can ring in the news of their nuptials. We host a variety of personal and corporate events including bridal and baby showers, birthday parties and conferences. If you are looking for something unique that will inspire your guests with the feelings of history and beauty this is the place.
