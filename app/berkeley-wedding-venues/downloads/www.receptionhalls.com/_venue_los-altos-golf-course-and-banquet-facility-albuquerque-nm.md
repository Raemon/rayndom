# Los Altos Golf Course and Banquet Facility Albuquerque, New Mexico

At Los Altos Golf Course &amp; Banquet Facility you are assured beautiful views of the golf course, mature trees and surrounding area—whether for a meeting, luncheon, reception, dinner, wedding or other occasion. Our banquet room adjoins a large outdoor patio perfect for those summer evenings.  
  
Our team at Los Altos is passionate about great food with great service! Because we create menus designed with you in mind, your day will be one you and your guests will always remember.   
  
You'll have room to accommodate your wedding and reception, meeting or social event for up to 175 guests and best of all...be able to celebrate and share your special day with family and friends  
  
Los Altos Golf Course and Banquet Facility is nestled in the foothills of the Sandia Mountains directly off the I-40 freeway and Eubank...Easily accessible to the airport and many local hotels.
