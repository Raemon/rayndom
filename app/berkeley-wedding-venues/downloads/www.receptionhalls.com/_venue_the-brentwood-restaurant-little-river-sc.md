# The Brentwood Restaurant Little River, South Carolina

Low Country Weddings with a French Flair  
The Brentwood Restaurant &amp; Wine Bistro is the perfect venue for your next special event. This historic Victorian house will transform your special day into a one-of-a- kind celebration that is both modern and timeless. From bridal showers and rehearsal dinners, anniversaries, to wedding ceremonies and receptions, you will experience our award winning cuisine and attentive staff in an elegant and truly unique setting. We will work intimately with you to help you realize all of your wedding day dreams and desires from start to finish.  
  
Our venue features five private indoor dining areas as well as a newly built outdoor covered pavilion with koi pond and waterfall, three tier fountain, outdoor bar, event stage, outdoor kitchen and Chef’s private garden. Any size, any budget. Customized for you. We attend to every detail as if this was the most important celebration ever held... because it is yours!
