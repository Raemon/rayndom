# Casas de Suenos Old Town Historic Inn and Reception Hall Albuquerque, New Mexico

The Casas de Suenos Old Town Historic Inn and Reception hall offers one of Albuquerque finest weddings, receptions and event venues. Our outdoor courtyard and gazebo and our new 4800 sq. ft. indoor reception hall can accommodate up to 160 guests seated. Visit our venue to see why we are known as "Albuquerque's Best Kept Wedding Secret"!
