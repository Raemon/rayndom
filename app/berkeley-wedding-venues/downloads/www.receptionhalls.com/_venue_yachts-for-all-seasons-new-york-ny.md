# Yachts For All Seasons New York, New York

Imagine your reception or private event aboard a beautiful yacht with stunning, unobstructed, panoramic views of the New York City skyline. Now imagine choosing from our selection of over 30 grand yachts in ports throughout New York City. Yachts For All Seasons can make your dream, nautical, event come true.  
  
From small, intimate gatherings to large weddings and banquets, we will help you plan the most luxurious and memorable event possible. We have been planning Weddings, Bar/Bat Mitzvahs, Sweet 16, Birthdays, Anniversaries and Corporate Events for over 25 years. As a full service Yacht Charter Company, we can provide you with everything from DJ’s to tour guides, florists to chocolate fountains, and bands to Limos.  
  
Contact us to learn more.
