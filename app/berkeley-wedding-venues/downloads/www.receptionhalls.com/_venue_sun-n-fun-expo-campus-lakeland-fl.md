# SUN 'n FUN Expo Campus Lakeland, Florida

The SUN ‘n FUN Expo Campus at SUN ‘n FUN FLY-IN, INC. is one of the most unique venues in Central Florida. Our wide-open space on over 200 acres of natural Florida setting has access to the Lakeland Linder International Airport (LAL) and hosts the annual SUN ‘n FUN Aerospace Expo in March or April. Located along the 1-4 High-Tech Corridor, only 5 miles off 1-4 (Exit 25), the SUN ‘n FUN Expo Campus is easily accessible to the Tampa and Orlando metro areas. In addition to our acreage, the Expo Campus has a myriad of special event spaces - from boardrooms to clubhouses, to aircraft hangars, there is space to accommodate most needs. Onsite Camping (R/V’s and tents) is available for your group. Major festivals, galas, trade shows, weddings, sporting events, and 5k’s have all found their perfect home at the SUN ‘n FUN Expo Campus.  
  
It is our policy to deliver friendly service, can-do attitudes and outstanding results for our clients. Our professional, experienced, and flexible event staff will assist you in all stages of the event process - from initial planning and event management, to a seamless breakdown.  
  
For 45 years, the leadership, staff, and volunteers of SUN ‘N FUN FLY-IN, INC., a 501(c)(3) not-for-profit organization, have produced the second-largest airshow and fly-in in North America. Each Spring, the SUN ‘n FUN Expo Campus hosts over 225,000 visitors from around the world for a week of aviation-related activities.  
  
Proceeds from events held on the SUN ‘n FUN Expo Campus fund over $2 million annually in aviation scholarships and education programs at the Aerospace Center for Excellence (ACE) at SUN ‘n FUN FLY-IN, INC.
