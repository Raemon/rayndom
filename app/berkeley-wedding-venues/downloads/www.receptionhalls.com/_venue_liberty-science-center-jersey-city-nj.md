# Liberty Science Center Jersey City, New Jersey

Liberty Science Center's unique atmosphere offers a number of meeting venues, theaters and exhibition spaces that can easily accommodate any size group. Combined with awe-inspiring views of Manhattan and the Statue of Liberty, and on-site catering options, our expert event planning staff can help make your event one to remember. Whether you're planning an elegant sit-down dinner or fun-filled evening of dancing and activities, magnificent special event experiences await you at Liberty Science Center.
