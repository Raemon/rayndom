# Fallbrook Estate by Wedgewood Weddings Fallbrook, California

Stunning Country Estate Set Deep in the North County Hills, Rustic Meets Chic  
If you’re looking to blend exceptional scenery with exquisite architecture, Fallbrook Estate by Wedgewood Weddings is calling you. Explore the dramatic courtyard featuring oversize beams, a dramatic stone fountain, live palms, and remarkable views. Head down to the newly created ceremony area with a grand pergola, and then mingle with your friends and family in the outdoor cocktail area or lounging bar, before heading to the beautiful grand ballroom, featuring spectacular views all augmented by decorative fixtures and fittings. Tucked away in the hills of northern San Diego county, this location means easy access to ocean views, city lights, and wine country.  
  
\- Grand Ballroom with Huge Windows, Vaulted Ceilings, and Stylish Chandeliers  
\- Grand Ballroom with Huge Windows, High Ceiling & Stylish Chandeliers  
\- Exceptional Photo Opportunities Indoors & Out  
\- Choice of Two Ceremony Sites  
\- Striking Curb Appeal with Year-Round Foliage & Mature Trees  
  
Fallbrook by Wedgewood Weddings is beautifully secluded yet easily reachable from Riverside and Orange Counties. This superb location attracts couples from Temecula, Murrieta, Menifee, Oceanside, Carlsbad, Vista, Bonsall, Pala, San Marcos, Escondido, Wildomar, San Clemente, and Rainbow – as well as many couples who decide on a destination wedding.  
  
As with all Wedgewood Wedding properties, when you book Fallbrook Estate, you access our simple-to-understand and customize wedding packages, access to our preferred vendor network and as much time as you need with our team of expert - and enthusiastic - wedding planners!
