# Holiday Inn City Centre Sioux Falls, South Dakota

The Holiday Inn City Centre extends our congratulations to you on your engagement and would like to say thank you for considering us to host your reception.  
  
Set in the heart of vibrant Downtown Sioux Falls, the Holiday Inn City Centre, offers an indescribable reception ambiance. Our hotel has served as a landmark site for weddings and   
other social gatherings for years, enchanting couples and guests with inspired and breathtaking surroundings. We are extremely proud of our premier reception space, including the Starlite Ballroom, our professional staff, and our talented culinary team.  
  
Here at the Holiday Inn City Centre, you will find that both you and your experienced wedding specialist have something in common: a passion for making your day memorable. Whether your special occasion is an intimate or grand affair you deserve the finest in accommodations and service. Your wedding specialist will assist you every step of the way to ensure that the planning and every detail of your reception is flawless. Our vow is to make your reception the most   
stunning and memorable celebration of your life.  
  
Let us worry about the details so you can enjoy celebrating and treasuring the memories of your special day. All you have to say is “I do”…to having your reception at the   
Holiday Inn City Centre.
