# Park Avenue Club Florham Park, New Jersey

..A Historic Venue With a Modern Twist..  
  
Park Avenue Club is a modern restored dairy barn serving as a private dining club in northern New Jersey; priding itself on being the premier destination for fine dining &amp; stylish weddings.  
  
RE-INTRODUCING NEW JERSEY'S CLASSIC DESTINATION IN THE FALL OF 2018! Come Fall 2018, Park Avenue Club will host weddings in their brand new ballroom and cocktail space, hosting up to 300 guests. Boasting vaulted ceilings, natural light and private outdoor patio space amongst our gardens. Relax in the luxurious, stately ambiance of Park Avenue Club while an attentive staff offers the utmost hospitality to ensure your wedding goes off without a hitch.  
Hosting intimate weddings for up to 75 guests from now until September 2018 in the site of the 1800's original barn hayloft. A dramatic three-story space with cream-colored walls featuring massive knotty pine beams, tall Palladian windows draped in beige silk with views of our paved patio gardens and four multi-tiered stately chandeliers for intimate, flattering light. The room also has two working brick fireplaces.  
The most special aspect of enjoying your big day with us, is sharing with us in "The Spirit of Giving". The Park Avenue Club is a unique model for philanthropy by providing support to charitable and cultural organizations through our relationship with the Park Avenue Foundation, which supports 10 non-profits and cultural organizations. *Affordable membership required
