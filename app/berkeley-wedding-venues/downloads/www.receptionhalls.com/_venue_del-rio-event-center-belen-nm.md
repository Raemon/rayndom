# Del Rio Event Center Belen, New Mexico

The venue will come with a hall of close to 15000 sq feet with separate Men and Womens bathrooms. It also comes with outside concrete pads for doing outdoor events.   
  
It also has 2 changing rooms in the hall for Bridal make up etc.   
  
Only event space is included along with the electricty (utilities). Rest of the item can be procured by the organizing party. Pricing is negotiable based on the number of people with a minimum.

Del Rio Plaza is proud to serve Belen, NM, and the local surroundings to host all kinds of large or small events with a capacity of up to 1,500 people. The hall is almost 15000 sq feet with ample parking and a large size concrete pad (0.5 + 1.5 acres) for the extended venue.
