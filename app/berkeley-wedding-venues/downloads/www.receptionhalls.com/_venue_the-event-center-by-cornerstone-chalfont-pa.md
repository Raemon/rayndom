# The Event Center by Cornerstone New Hope, Pennsylvania

The Event Center by Cornerstone- A fully customizable venue that allows our clients to create their ideal ambiance leaving guests amazed with unforgettable memories. The venue accommodates up to 300 guests seated, and 500 guests for standing room only event. The space includes, LED dimmable lighting, a large theater stage, big enough for 10+ person band, and large dance floor. With a full commercial kitchen, and bar; clients can choose their own licensed and insured caterer. No matter the occasion our highly experienced event staff is here to assist you from booking to execution ensuring every event runs smoothly, and is a great success!
