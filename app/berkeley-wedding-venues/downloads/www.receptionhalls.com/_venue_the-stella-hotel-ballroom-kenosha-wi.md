# The Stella Hotel & Ballroom Kenosha, Wisconsin

The Stella Hotel & Ballroom offers a luxurious setting for a gorgeous, one-of-a-kind Wedding Event. Among Kenosha wedding venues, The Stella Hotel & Ballroom is known for its historic elegance, contemporary service, and ideal Downtown Kenosha location, just minutes from the shores of Lake Michigan. Expect nothing short of grandeur for your beautiful wedding in Downtown Kenosha.
