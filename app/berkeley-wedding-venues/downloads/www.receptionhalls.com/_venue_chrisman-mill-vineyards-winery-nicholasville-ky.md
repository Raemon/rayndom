# Chrisman Mill Vineyards & Winery Nicholasville, Kentucky

Chrisman Mill Vineyards and Winery is located in Jessamine County, Kentucky.   
Our three outdoor ceremony sites and reception site are all set amidste the rolling hills of our vineyards and gardens, providing guests a most relaxing atmosphere.  
Our Tuscan Room is available for smaller indoor parties.  
Gourmet catering is done on-site by Chef Denise Nelson and staff. While the most popular choice for is buffet dining, but we also offer plated choices and a prix fixe menu.  
Our event coordinator will personally meet with you to discuss what you envision for your event and work with you to achieve it. We will never double book an event - your day is reserved for you!  
Additionally we offer flower arrangements, wedding cakes, make-up artist services and event photography.  
Our tasting room is open Tuesday thru Saturday, 10am - 5pm. Please stop by - we'd love to see you. If you would like to meet with us to discuss options for your event please call in advance. Thank you for considering Chrisman Mill Vineyards for your event. *Please note: we are open on Sundays for weddings and events only.
