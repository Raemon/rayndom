# Maple Leaf Events Stevenson, Washington

Whether your hosting a formal or informal event, our facility offers everything you could need for your celebration. We provide both indoor/outdoor wedding ceremony space, beautiful landscaping, indoor restrooms, indoor reception hall, an on-site preparation kitchen, free use of tables and chairs with your booking, and an amazing view of the Columbia River Gorge. We can accommodate up to 150 guests and have parking for approximately 60 cars. We also feature a bridal suite and groomsman lounges. They are designed to provide ample room for all wedding party members to get primped and pampered on the big day. Give us a call today or visit our website!
