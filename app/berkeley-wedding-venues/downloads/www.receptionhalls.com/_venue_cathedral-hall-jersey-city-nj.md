# Cathedral Hall Jersey City, New Jersey

Cathedral Hall is Jersey City's newest, largest, and most affordable wedding venue. A maintained decommissioned church, Cathedral hall boasts 53 foot ceilings, stained glass windows, original wood flooring and over 20,000 square feet starting at $4000 for a 24 hour rental.
