# Four Seasons Banquet House, Inc. Gibsonia, Pennsylvania

Known as the Hidden Jewel of Gibsonia …  
  
Only one minute from North Park, the Four Seasons Banquet House is located on 13 beautiful privately owned acres, which allows us to offer a unique blend of Elegance and Nature.  
  
When planning a Wedding, Anniversary party, Holiday party or Picnic the Four Seasons is a premiere banquet hall that you and your guests deserve.  
All our menus are prepared onsite with top quality products and the freshest produce the area has to offer.  
  
The Four Seasons can accommodate 200 guests inside. Our outside grounds and picnic area are welcoming up to 400 guests.  
  
The Four Seasons offers onsite Wedding ceremonies with recommended non-denomination ministers.   
Four Seasons offers a level packing lot that makes grounds handicap accessible.  
**************   
Are you looking for a casual event, consider our picnic packages.Referral list includes venues for pony rides, petting zoos, bouncing inflatable, puppet shows to a trackless train!   
  
**************  
Our packages are designed to give you options.   
We invite you to experience the elegance of our facility, complimented with a serene atmosphere, engaged with a professional staff, delicious foods and seasoned with experienced event planners.
