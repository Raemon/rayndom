# Stony Point Hall Baldwin City, Kansas

Come visit this peaceful, special place for your wedding, reception, party or corporate function. We provide an elegant country space to meet all your needs and exceed all your expectations. Our purpose at Stony Point Hall is to provide an idyllic, unique facility in a loving, caring atmosphere for special life celebrations.  
  
Our hall includes all the modern amenities of a city facility in a historic, rural setting. Situated on a Civil War era farm in the picturesque Vinland Valley, you will love the views, atmosphere and seclusion.  
  
We do all that we can to make your special day...special! Our entire focus is on fulfilling your needs for those once in a lifetime memories. Come visit Stony Point Hall and see what is possible!
