# Sheraton Charlotte Hotel Charlotte, North Carolina

![Photo of Sheraton Charlotte Hotel](/media/NC/10927/sheraton-charlotte-hotel-charlotte-nc.jpg)

The Sheraton Charlotte Hotel is ideally located in Uptown Charlotte and nestled amidst the most popular downtown Charlotte attractions. Find yourself moments away from the NASCAR Hall of Fame, Bank of America Stadium - Home of the Carolina Panthers, Time Warner Cable Arena, and the Epicentre - Uptown Charlotte's premier dining and entertainment complex. Whether you’re traveling for business or pleasure, you’ll find the perfect combination of comfort and convenience during your stay.
