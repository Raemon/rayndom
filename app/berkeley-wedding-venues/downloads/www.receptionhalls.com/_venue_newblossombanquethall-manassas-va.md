# NewBlossomBanquetHall Manassas, Virginia

Our banquet hall can seat anywhere from a small private gathering up to 300 guests, making it a perfect location for any sized event.   
Perfect for:   
-Weddings  
-Birthday Parties  
-Quinceaneras  
-Baby Showers  
...Or any event needing that special touch!  
  
We can offer you our catering service or please feel free to bring your own. A portable bar is available for alcoholic drinks.  
  
During the business week we can cater to Corporate Events, Clubs and Associations, Community and Non-Profit Groups, Church Events and Much More.
