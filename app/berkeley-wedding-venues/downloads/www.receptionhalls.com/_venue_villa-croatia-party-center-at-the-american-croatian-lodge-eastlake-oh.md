# Villa Croatia Party Center at The American Croatian Lodge Eastlake, Ohio

The American Croatian Lodge is located in the heart of Eastlake, Ohio. The Lodge has two spacious Banquet Halls on our Campus with the ability to accommodate a variety of different events. The Main Hall just had a extensive renovation completed as of January 2016. The hall has the capacity to seat 814 guests and has a variety of upgrades from up lighting built into the walls, one of the biggest dance floors in the area, a newly renovated three station bar and many more from floor to ceiling. The West End Hall has ability to accommodate 321 guests. Both of the Halls have their own stage, dance floor, bathrooms and entrances.
