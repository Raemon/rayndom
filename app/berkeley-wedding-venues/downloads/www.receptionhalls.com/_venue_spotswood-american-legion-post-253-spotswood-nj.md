# Spotswood American Legion Post 253 Spotswood, New Jersey

Overlooking picturesque Devoe Lake and waterfall, the Spotswood American Legion Post 253 provides the perfect setting for your special day. There are two rental areas available, the indoor main hall (120 max. capacity) and our outdoor pavilion area (250 max. capacity). The outdoor area looks out onto the lake and waterfalls that provides a marvelous backdrop for photographs. Weddings, dances, dinners, birthday parties, baby/bridal showers and business meetings of all kinds have been held at this lovely venue. A fully trained staff will work with you to plan and coordinate any affair. We are devoted to you and it is our pleasure to take part in sharing your most treasured memories!   
  
* All areas of the facility are wheelchair accessible. *
