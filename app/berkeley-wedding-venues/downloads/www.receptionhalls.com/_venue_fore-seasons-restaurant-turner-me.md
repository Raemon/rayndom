# Fore Seasons Restaurant Turner, Maine

![Photo of Fore Seasons Restaurant](/media/ME/11965/fore-seasons-restaurant-turner-me.jpg)

Fore Seasons located at Turner Highlands Country Club offers unique and flexible indoor/outdoor event space. Our elegant banquet room overlooking the golf course with a panoramic mountain view will comfortably seat up to 125 guests. Need more space not a problem! Let us work together to create an event that is unique and memorable and, most importantly, reflects your style and our expertise.
