# Stoney Creek Hotel & Conference Center East Peoria, Illinois

We are a unique, custom-designed lodge hotel located in East Peoria, IL that is large enough to serve your every need, yet quaint enough to make you feel relaxed and “at home.”   
  
With over 5,300 total square feet of meeting space in five distinct rooms our Conference Center facilities can certainly accommodate your meeting room needs. We use a variety of excellent catering services and have state of the art A/V equipment available  
  
Our 164 guest rooms offer a variety of accommodations including 15 suites, each with a stone fireplace and a unique décor theme. All overnight guests enjoy the many amenities Stoney Creek Inn has to offer such as complimentary breakfast and our “Wilderness Park” Recreation Area, which includes an indoor/outdoor swim-through pool, dry sauna, whirlpool spa, exercise facility, and an outdoor patio with a spectacular river view. We are also home to the Loose Moose Saloon, a great place to grab a pizza and a drink!   
  
Our newest addition features a private access entry, business center, upgraded amenities, and 2 luxurious one-bedroom suites, each complete with a whirlpool, full kitchen, stone fireplace, an all-season porch with a river view, and three televisions!   
  
Our property is conveniently located just off I-74 at the beautiful East Peoria Marina and within no more than ten miles of the many attractions and services that Greater Peoria area has to offer, including parks, wildlife prairie, a water park, shopping and golf to name just a few. The Peoria area also has many fine eating establishments within a short walk or a short drive of our facility. Three excellent restaurants neighbor the hotel, including Basta Mangiare, Jonah’s Seafood House &amp; Oyster Bar, and The Eastport Galley. The Par-A-Dice Riverboat Casino is located just a ½ mile from our hotel!  
  
We look forward to making your event a success and one to remember!
