# The Emporium Patchogue, New York

![Photo of The Emporium](/media/NY/11507/the-emporium-patchogue-ny.jpg)

The Emporium – located at 9 Railroad Avenue in Patchogue, redefines the entertainment experience with a combination of live music, dining and dancing. Centered around a large stage, a beautiful outdoor beer garden, 3 bars serving top shelf liquor, bottled beer and 48 draft lines and a 1100-capacity performance venue with live music. The Emporium offers an array of culinary delights including Seared Tuna, Dragon Chicken Tempura, Vodka Flamed Shrimp, Lobster Ravioli, as well as Kobe Beef Burgers and Pizza. The Emporium is conveniently located just few blocks away from the Patchogue train station.  
  
The Emporium offers catering for private events such as Sweet Sixteens, Bar and Bat Mitzvahs, Graduation Parties, Prom Events, Birthday Parties, Bachelorette Parties, and any event you want to be memorable!
