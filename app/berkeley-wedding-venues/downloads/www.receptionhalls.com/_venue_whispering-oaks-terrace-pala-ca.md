# Whispering Oaks Terrace Pala, California

Create your own special wedding at the Whispering Oaks Terrace. Whispering Oaks is a great place for "do-it-yourself" and/ or "I'm on a budget" wedding. Whether you're dreaming of an outdoor ceremony, an intimate winter time ceremony or a unique reception or rehearsal dinner, Whispering Oaks Terrace is for you!   
  
Whispering Oaks Terrace is conveniently located between Temecula and Pala in Southern California. The closest hotels are Pechanga Resort and Casino and Pala Casino.  
  
At Whispering Oaks Terrace, you can have a beautiful wedding and still stay within their budget. The rate is $3900.00 to $4200.00 for the rental of the venue. The price also includes a bridal/engagement photo session time with your photographer, if desired. Beer, wine and champagne are allowed with proper insurance. Bring your own caterer, DJ, florist, or photographer.
