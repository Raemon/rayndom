# Valley Falls Center Grafton, West Virginia

Located in Wild, Wonderful West Virginia and next door to the waterfalls at Valley Falls State Park, Valley Falls Center is the premier location for weddings and special events including bridal showers, baby showers, family reunions, birthday parties, and much more.  
Conveniently located 7 miles from I-79 Exit 137. We are just past the entrance to the Valley Falls State Park and just over the Taylor County line. We are far enough from Fairmont and Grafton to provide a private location for your event, but close enough to be easily accessible for your guests.
