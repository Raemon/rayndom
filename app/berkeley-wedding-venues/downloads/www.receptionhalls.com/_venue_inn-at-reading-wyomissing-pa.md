# Inn at Reading Reading, Pennsylvania

![Photo of Inn at Reading](/media/PA/256/inn-at-reading-wyomissing-pa.jpg)

With 13 meeting rooms, the Inn At Reading provides over 13,000 square feet of flexible space in our conference center. From our Boardrooms to our Ballroom, we can host from 10 to 1200 guests. Whether you are planning the wedding reception you've always dreamed of, a social event your guests will never forget, or a business meeting that is destined for success, our professional banquet staff will cater to all your needs.
