# Camp Pollock Sacramento, California

Camp Pollock is located in the heart of the Lower American River Parkway, just north of downtown Sacramento. The Sacramento Valley Conservancy has been managing and maintaining the 11-acre riverfront site since 2013. The beautiful property is open to the general public every day from sunrise to sunset, with free parking. Camp Pollock features a 3,000 square foot lodge built in the 1920s with a wraparound porch and magnificent stone fireplace, a covered deck, native plant garden courtyard, river access, and large field surrounded by mature trees.
