# Fair Haven community center Alexandria, Virginia

3500+ square foot venue, private hall, parking lot provided, metro accessible, bathrooms on site, stage, folding chairs and tables provided, ticketing booth at front entrance available. The Hall is located 10 minutes from Old Town Alexandria, 10 minutes from National Harbor, accessible by Rt. 1 (Richmond Highway) or N. Kings Highway, and approximately a 5 minute walk from the Huntington Metro Station. The Hall is tucked away in a quiet neighborhood. When it is not being rented, it serves as a gathering place for the community and a polling location for voters in the area. Perfect for small performances (dance, music, theater), parties (birthday parties, retirement parties, graduation parties, BBQs), or meetings (church groups, civic associations, special interest groups). Regular monthly or weekly rentals also available. Are you a self-employed yoga, fitness or dance instructor, looking for a spot to hold your classes? Are you looking for a venue for your group to meet? Are you a tutor who is looking for a space to hold classes? This building is the place for you! Established business owners receive special rates and priority scheduling for regular classes. Contact us today for Rental Rates!
