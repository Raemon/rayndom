# Grove City Wedding Chapel Grove City, Ohio

The Grove City Wedding Chapel is located in the historic Town Center of Grove City. Recently renovated the Chapel is handicap accessible, seats up to 100 guests and is steps away from ample public parking. Rentals for the Chapel are managed by Venue 62, a full service reception center about one block away.
