# Secret Window Weddings & Events Monument, Colorado

We're not your typical venue. If you want an outdoor wedding and a venue with a fun, laid back feel, then this is it! Our indoor banquet room has glass doors that open up to a large landscaped patio garden with mountain views. Exchange your vows in our private outdoor garden and dance the night away under the stars. Want your furry friend to be part of your wedding? No problem! We're dog friendly, food truck friendly, and 4/20 friendly... because it's Colorado!  
  
Call us today to schedule a tour and let us help you make your wedding dreams come true. We look forward to hearing from you!
