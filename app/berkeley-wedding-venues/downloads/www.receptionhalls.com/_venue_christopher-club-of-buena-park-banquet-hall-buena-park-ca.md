# Christopher Club of Buena Park - Banquet Hall Buena Park, California

The Christopher Club Banquet Hall is Buena Parks’s most easiest and convenient venues to hold of your special day. Whether you are planning a Wedding, Birthday, Quinceanera or Banquet, the Christopher Club Hall offers a variety of accessible and convenient package options to make planning your celebration memorable, relaxing and enjoyable. Located in the beautiful city of Buena Park, California - The Center of the Southland. Our hall is conveniently situated where Interstate Highway 5 (I-5) and the California State Route 91 intersect. Take Beach exit off the 5 freeway and Magnolia exits.
