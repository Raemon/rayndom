# Sheraton Raleigh Hotel Raleigh, North Carolina

![Photo of Sheraton Raleigh Hotel](/media/NC/10925/sheraton-raleigh-hotel-raleigh-nc.jpg)

Weddings at the Sheraton Raleigh Hotel offer space for up to 300 people in either our Oak Forest or Hannover ballrooms to toast, to cheer and to share your day! Our Raleigh wedding planner, Lauren DeAlto brings her talents and passion to each moment of your wedding - from the planning process to the rehearsal dinner, ceremony and reception. Our executive Chef and attentive catering team will prepare creations that will flavor the table with a hearty supply of "oohs &amp; aahs" to share with the most important people in your world. Our central downtown location close to many things to do in Raleigh provides numerous activities for you and your guests to enjoy.
