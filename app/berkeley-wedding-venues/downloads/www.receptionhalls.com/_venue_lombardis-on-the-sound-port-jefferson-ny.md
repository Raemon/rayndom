# Lombardi's on the Sound Port Jefferson, New York

Enjoy the privacy of being the only bride in the estate settings. Situated on Long Islands picturesque north shore with panoramic views of the Long Island Sound and Connecticut. You'll enjoy superb service every moment on your wedding day...From the elegant cocktail hour to customized menus deliciously created by master chefs. Our courteous and professional staff will thrill you and your quests as every detail is handled with perfection. We also offer outdoor ceremony's and cocktail hours with views that can't be matched.
