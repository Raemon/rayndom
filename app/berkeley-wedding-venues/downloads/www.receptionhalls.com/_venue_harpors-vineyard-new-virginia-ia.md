# Harpors Vineyard New Virginia, Iowa

Harpor’s Vineyard is a gorgeous estate featuring a barn called “Red” which hosts your bar and dance floor. For your main event and seating area there are two 1/2 acres of vineyards surrounding you and as many guest as you need. Finally, we have a large pond which makes ceremonies and pictures a gorgeous addition to the property. Our estate is completely open to you and your guest for any part of the service.
