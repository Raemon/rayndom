# Church Street Catering Montclair, New Jersey

Church Street Catering offers the very best in complete off-premise catering. Our emphasis is on the very finest quality foods, guest satisfaction and excellent standards of service. From a Simple Delivery to a Full Service Catered Event, we prepare delicious food for tens to hundreds.   
  
Various off premises locations where we cater include, but are not limited to, mansions, museums, artist lofts and other unique settings. Please contact us for a free, no obligation appointment.
