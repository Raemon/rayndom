# Vanity Fare Lakewood, New Jersey

Vanity Fare Caterers offering on site catering for your special event. Imagine your wedding venue, bar mitzvah, corporate function, sweet 16, or anniversary party...  
  
Begin by having your wedding ceremony outdoors in our beautiful poolside gazebo, or in our elegantly appointed ballroom. Your family and friends will marvel at the sumptuous cocktail hour in our Regency Suite. Your fantasies will come true as you dine and dance the night away in our Ballroom surrounded by Italian crystal chandeliers, gold leaf molding and silk centerpieces.  
  
As your guests happily retire to their rooms in the adjacent hotel, the two of you can relax in the Jacuzzi in your private complementary bridal suite as you relive the most special day in your lives ... all at Vanity Fare Caterers.  
  
And don't forget to make an appointment with Vanity Fare Caterers and see how our years of expertise can take the stress out of many of your decisions and make your wedding the "one of a kind" day you have dreamed about.
