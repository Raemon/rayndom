Bottles &amp; Wood is the San Diego's best event destination! If you're looking for a venue space, glassware, wood ware to rent for your next corporate meeting, special event, or birthday party, then you've come to the right place! ### Venue Details

Capacity (Seated / Cocktail): **150 / 200**

Bottles &amp; Wood is conveniently located just east off the 805 freeway. Our space is 4,000 sq. ft. in total, which includes a 1,000 sq. ft. showroom, a 3,000 sq. ft. warehouse, a 3-compartment sink, refrigerator/freezer, ample parking, two lavish bathrooms and intimate lighting. Our capacity is approximately 200 in total.

Max Number of Simultaneous Events: **127**

Outside Catering Permitted (BYO): **Yes**

On-Site Cuisine Options: **Use Your Own Caterer**

### Pricing

Budget Tier:  **$**

Site/Rental Fee: **$499**

Bottles &amp; Wood offers its contemporary showroom and warehouse for a set-up/clean-up fee of $499. The venue is the perfect setting to serve artisanal beer, food, wine and liquor, while entertaining guests. For events hosted at Bottles &amp; Wood, the company will provide its one-of-a-kind glassware and serving ware for use. Bottles &amp; Wood rents its handcrafted tableware, lighting, and furniture for events held off-site as well. We offer a 20% discount for all non-profits!
