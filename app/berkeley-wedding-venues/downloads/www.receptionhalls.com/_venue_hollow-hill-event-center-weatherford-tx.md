# Hollow Hill Event Center Weatherford, Texas

The lovely countryside and extensive amenities at Hollow Hill are the perfect setting for a wedding, reception, reunion, or any other event. The award-winning Fort Worth wedding venue might look like it could only exists in a storybook, but it’s actually located just outside the city. Vast but intimate, the property has many hidden gardens and sweeping landscapes that result in breathtaking photos. As a family-run establishment, the staff understand that each couple and their loved ones want to focus on what matters so they attend to even the smallest details.  
From the wedding to the reception, the bride’s room to the guest rooms, morning till evening, you’ll have everything you desire for the perfect country wedding.
