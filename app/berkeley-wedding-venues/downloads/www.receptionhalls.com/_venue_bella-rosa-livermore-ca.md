# Bella Rosa Livermore, California

![Photo of Bella Rosa](/media/CA/12037/bella-rosa-livermore-ca.jpg)

Located in the historic Livermore Valley Wine Country, Bella Rosa, at Garré Winery offers a picturesque setting alongside the vineyards. With its sweeping views of the surrounding rolling hills and vineyards to temperate weather throughout the year, Garré offers the perfect location for your special day.
