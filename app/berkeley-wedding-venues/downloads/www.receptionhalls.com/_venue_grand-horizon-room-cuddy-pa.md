# Grand Horizon Room Cuddy, Pennsylvania

Our facility provides a very nice venue at an extremely affordable price. You will have a coordinator throughout the entire planning process to assist you with all of your needs.   
  
The Grand Horizon Banquet Room includes linens, tablecloths and napkins, china and silverware, set up and clean up of every event. Arrangements can also be made to use the outside facilities.
