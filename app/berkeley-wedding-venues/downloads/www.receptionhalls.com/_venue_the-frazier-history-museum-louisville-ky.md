# The Frazier History Museum Louisville, Kentucky

The Frazier History Museum offers several rental spaces to meet your event’s specific needs. We are a unique venue for wedding ceremonies and receptions, dinners, cocktail receptions, holiday parties, bar/bat mitzvahs, proms, daytime business meetings and many other types of events. You can host your event outdoors on our beautiful Rooftop Garden overlooking The Ohio or inside one of the Museum’s large, modern event spaces.
