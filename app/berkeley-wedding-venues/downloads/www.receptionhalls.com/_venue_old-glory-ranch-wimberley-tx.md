# Old Glory Ranch Wimberley, Texas

First class celebrations in simple country elegance. Located in the historic town of Wimberley, Texas ideally positioned between Austin and San Antonio is Old Glory Ranch and Chapel Hall, a special events and premiere destination venue. Situated on 2,200 acres of Hill Country range along 1.5 miles of the emerald green Blanco River, Old Glory Ranch is the perfect backdrop for your next wedding, rehearsal dinner, corporate or musical event and more! Old Glory Ranch is the perfect wedding destination location and provides the sophistication and service you would expect from a 'big city' event in a more picturesque setting.  
  
Old Glory Ranch has redefined hospitality and entertainment to ‘glorify’ your special event. Chapel Hall is complete with architectural antiques, turn of the century stained glass windows, and hand forged iron details making it an ideal choice for your wedding. Are you looking for outdoor options? Our riverside locations or beautiful Oak grove with flanking fields of flowing tall grass are perfect for photographs. We have held memorable Texas Hill Country weddings and events for our wonderful clients, and we would love to host your wedding and event as well.  
  
Old Glory Ranch has welcomed couples from around the United States and the world! Our attention to customer service and details are unmatched and have earned the honors from WeddingWire Bride’s Choice and The Knot Best of Weddings. Plenty of reviews and photos are available for you to peruse. We would love to have you set up an appointment, come by Old Glory Ranch and see why you will fall in love with the space.  
  
We pride ourselves on service and don't feel you'll get any better, anywhere. See what other brides have to say by visiting our website kudos link, or stop by our Facebook to view recent weddings and begin to imagine how beautiful we can make your event.
