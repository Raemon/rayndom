# McNutt House - Maggie's Hall Vicksburg, Mississippi

The McNutt House (circa 1826) offers 3 great venues certain to fullfil your event needs from business meeting to party or banquet. Maggie's hall offers 900 sq' of open floor space and includes a kitchen ideal for you caterer's staging area, buffet line or bar tender station. Two double door entries with the side leading into a lovely courtyard with terraced gardens.
