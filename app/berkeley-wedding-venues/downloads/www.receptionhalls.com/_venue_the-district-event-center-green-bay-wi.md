# The District Event Center Green Bay, Wisconsin

![Photo of The District Event Center](/media/WI/10765/the-district-event-center-green-bay-wi.jpg)

Take your upcoming event to the next level and hold your special day in the unique surroundings of The District Event Center. Enjoy the most exciting part of the Green Bay area, the Lambeau Field District, and then unwind in one of the 145 beautiful rooms after your event.  
  
The District will accommodate 20 to 500 people with privacy and sophistication in any of our five event areas. They are the perfect place for elegant wedding receptions, showers, rehearsal dinners, anniversaries, birthdays, funerals, retirements, nonprofit benefits, conventions, corporate meetings, private parties, and more. We'll find the perfect space with the ideal atmosphere for your event - whether it's for community, family, business or just plain fun.
