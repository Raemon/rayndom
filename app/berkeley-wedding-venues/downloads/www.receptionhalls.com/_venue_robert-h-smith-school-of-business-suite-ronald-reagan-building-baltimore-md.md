# Robert H. Smith School of Business Suite - Ronald Reagan Building Baltimore, Maryland

Smith Venues provides meeting and conference space in two convenient locations: downtown Washington, D.C. and near Baltimore, Maryland's Inner Harbor. Located on historic Pennsylvania Avenue, the Ronald Reagan building is in the heart of Washington D.C., making our suite ideal for off-site meetings, staff retreats, workshops, small conferences and other events.
