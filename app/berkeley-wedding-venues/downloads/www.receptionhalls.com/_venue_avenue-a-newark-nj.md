# Avenue A Newark, New Jersey

Shown by appointment only, Avenue A is as versatile as it is fun with gleaming boarded floors, a fleet of vintage cars to delight your guests, and multiple mezzanines for relaxing or celebrating.   
This unique and flexible event space accommodates all types of events from formal weddings and receptions, to boisterous bar and bat mitzvah parties, to both intimate and large corporate and social events as well as fundraisers. Only two blocks off Route 78 in the “Ironbound” section of Newark, New Jersey, the venue’s state-of-the-art audio/visual capabilities are an added bonus for those looking for digital entertainment, sophisticated presentation options, or other unexpected multimedia elements to their events. Rental fees start at $10,000.
