# Lewes Room at Irish Eyes Lewes, Delaware

The Lewes room sits on the 2nd floor of Irish Eyes Pub and Restaurant, overlooking the Lewes Harbor and located just half a mile from the beach! We have plenty of menu package options, a private bar, a built-in dance floor, a year-round accessible 11 sq.ft. deck, rest rooms, and elevator access.
