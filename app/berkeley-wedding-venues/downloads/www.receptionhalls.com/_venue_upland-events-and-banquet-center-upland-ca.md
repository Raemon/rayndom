# Upland Events and Banquet Center Upland, California

Upland Events and Banquet is a distinctive venue located on a lovely tree lined street in the heart of Upland.   
  
Conveniently close to Los Angeles and Orange Counties, Upland Events and Banquet is an ideal location for weddings, corporate events, quinceñeras, church events, and all of your large group needs.   
  
The venue includes a spacious banquet room, large foyer with a built in bar, a separate band staging area, kitchen, and large conference rooms. No matter how large or small your event, Upland Events and Banquet is committed to providing you an exceptional location customized for your event.

Our venue has a private parking lot, bridal suites, a newly renovated bar, and a train to take first look photos! We are a full package venue that offers two different packages a basic package and deluxe package. We can also do a customized package to meet your needs! Our basic package includes meals, beverages, servers, basic linens, cloth napkins, glassware, tableware, tables, upgraded chairs, a 9' x 9' dance floor, security and cleanup. Our deluxe package includes all of the above plus Hors D'oeuvres, cake, bartender setup, a basic photo and video package, ceiling drapes, limo service, upgraded lines, a larger dance floor (22' x 16'), and a DJ.
