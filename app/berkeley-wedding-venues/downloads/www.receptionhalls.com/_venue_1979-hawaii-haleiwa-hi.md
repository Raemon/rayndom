# 1979 Hawaii Haleiwa, Hawaii

1979Hawaii is a full-service production company, producing island style, elegant events. Utilizing 15 years of experience in the event industry, offering full service catering, bar service, rentals & event planning/coordinating packages. Specializing in tasteful island cuisine, tropical bar services with selection of bar front, and built in-house Hardwood rentals. 1979Hawaii services events from Fundraisers, Picnics, Corporate Events to Weddings, Holiday Parties and Private Events.
