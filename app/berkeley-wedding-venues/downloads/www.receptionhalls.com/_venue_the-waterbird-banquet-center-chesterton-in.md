# The WaterBird Banquet Center Chesterton, Indiana

Welcome to The WaterBird Banquet Center. We have been providing services to Northwest Indiana for more than 25 years. We specialize in any and every event type big or small. We can facilitate up to 350 people. Whether it be corporate, religious, fraternal, fundraising or a wedding we can cater to your needs whatever they may be. It would be our pleasure to make your event everything you have hoped for and more!
