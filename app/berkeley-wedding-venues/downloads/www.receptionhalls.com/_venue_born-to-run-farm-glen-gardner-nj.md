# Born to Run Farm Glen Gardner, New Jersey

Born to Run Farm is a family-owned property located on the top of Schooley’s Mountain in Glen Gardner, NJ. The farm’s 130 acres of land overlooks the Appalachian Mountains and includes two ponds, an orchard, and multiple fields with various types of livestock. Let us help you enjoy a wedding, birthday, or celebration of a lifetime. Gorgeous clear skies and sunsets during the spring and summer as well as crisp colorful days of fall are all perfect days for an outdoor event at Born to Run Farm.
