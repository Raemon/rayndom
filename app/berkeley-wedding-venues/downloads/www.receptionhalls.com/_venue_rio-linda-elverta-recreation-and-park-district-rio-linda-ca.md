# Rio Linda Elverta Recreation and Park District Rio Linda, California

Spacious Community Center Building nestled in a beautifully landscaped park. High ceilings and gorgeous natural lighting during the day from architecturally designed windows. Full industrial kitchen with ice machine inside building. Attached private patio with direct access to the park. Tables, chairs, and indoor restrooms included in rental. Perfect for weddings, birthdays, company meetings, or any other kind of event. Also available as classroom or workshop space, simply inquire!
