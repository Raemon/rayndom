# The Ballroom at Quail Hollow Charlotte, North Carolina

![Photo of The Ballroom at Quail Hollow](/media/NC/12549/the-ballroom-at-quail-hollow-charlotte-nc-28210.jpg)

Imagine dancing with your sweetheart on your wedding day in the most beautiful, romantic ballroom in Charlotte, NC. The Ballroom at Quail Hollow is here to make your fairytale dreams come to life.   
The venue boasts a dazzling antique French crystal chandelier, hardwood floors, two fireplaces, and timeless European décor. Additionally, The Ballroom features a state-of-the-art sound system already loaded with a wide selection of music from your favorite classics to today's hits. The Ballroom offers amenities such as access to a kitchen, parking, wifi, and included tables, chairs, and luxury furniture.  
The Ballroom is ready to make your wedding dreams come true! Call today to schedule your free tour and consultation!
