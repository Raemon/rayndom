# Ramada Limited Huntington, West Virginia

![Photo of Ramada Limited](/media/WV/11637/ramada-limited-huntington-wv.jpg)

We have a beautiful space that we rent for weddings and receptions. The space can hold up to 175 seats for dinner with extra space for a dance area. We provide tables and chairs, as well the set up and tear down of those. There is a full kitchen for use in the space. We are a limited hotel we do not provide food or beverages, you would need to either prepare food yourself or bring in a caterer. Pricing depends on the space needed. Our Facebook page has tons of pictures if you would like to check that out, just search for Huntington Ramada Limited Hotel and Conference center. If you have any furthers questions feel free to contact Shannon at the Ramada Limited.
