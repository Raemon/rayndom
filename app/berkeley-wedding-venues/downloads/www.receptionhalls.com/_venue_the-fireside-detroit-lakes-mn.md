# The Fireside Detroit Lakes, Minnesota

![Photo of The Fireside](/media/MN/11643/the-fireside-detroit-lakes-mn.jpg)

Host your rehearsal dinner and/or wedding reception at The Fireside overlooking beautiful Lake Detroit. The Fireside is located less than half a mile from The Lodge on Lake Detroit. We can customize the event details to your needs and budget. You can reserve the porch, dining room, or both spaces depending on the size of your event. Total seating capacity is 250. Work with our executive chef and beverage manger to craft a menu that is as unique as you are. We offer options for seated meals, buffets, and appetizer service as well as linens that are available for your event.
