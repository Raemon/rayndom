# The Regency Weddings and Conference Center Pompton Plains, New Jersey

The Regency Wedding & Conference Center offers a magnificent setting for weddings, corporate functions and social events of all kind. Our catering department is inside the Baymont Hotel, therefore the bride and groom will get a complimentary honeymoon suite the night of the wedding. We also offer a private complimentary breakfast room for you and your guest the next morning. We also offer onsite ceremony inside or outside. Contact us today!
