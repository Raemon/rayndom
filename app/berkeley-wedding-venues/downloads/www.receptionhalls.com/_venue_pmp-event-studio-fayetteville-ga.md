# PMP Event Studio Fayetteville, Georgia

PMP Event Studio is the perfect space to host your intimate event! This space offers over 1900 square feet of open floor plan to let your imagination run wild. The studio can hold up to 90 people. The studio is ideal for small weddings, birthday parties, bridal/baby showers, pop-up shops, photo shoots, branding installations, classes or meetings. We are located off of GA HWY 85 and Promenade Parkway. Across from the Fayetteville Pavilion in the new AutoZone shopping center.
