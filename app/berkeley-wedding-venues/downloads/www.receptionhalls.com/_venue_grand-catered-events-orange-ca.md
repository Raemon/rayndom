# Grand Catered Events at the Orange Conference Center Orange, California

Positioned east of the Santa Ana River on South Flower Street, the Orange Banquet Center presents blissful couples a luxurious and full-service space to host an unforgettable wedding and reception. Located in the affluent community of Orange, this venue rests within proximity to major highways for easy access. An ideal choice for brides and grooms looking for in-house service, the Center provides everything you need for your special day. If you are planning an indoor ceremony, this venue offers several stunning spaces to share your vows. Equipped with a spacious banquet hall, this space is easily dividing into sections for intimate gatherings or provides plenty of room for a grand celebration. If you are envisioning an elegant and memorable space to tie the knot, allow the Orange Banquet Center to make your wedding day dreams a reality.
