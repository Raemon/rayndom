# Old St Mary's Reception Halls Orono, Maine

There is not often that a venue provides a sense of magnificence and glory, and perfect photo backdrop, in every spot!  
  
This historic venue with stone exterior of a European Castle, splendid original stain glass windows, original woodwork, custom lighting, and the Grand Wedding Bell, provides the elements of a farirytale wedding or any Royal Event, right here in Maine!
