# Hands On Children's Museum Olympia, Washington

The most unique Olympia wedding venue!  
  
Hands On is perfect for brides and grooms seeking a fun, whimsical venue.  
The museum is truly family friendly! Have your ceremony in the open air and for your reception, dance to a live band and play in the museum. Perfect for youngsters and the young at heart!  
Looking for a unique location? No problem. Spacious galleries filled with over 150 fun, hands-on exhibits will excite your youngest to oldest guests.  
Exceptional, personalized planning with a dedicated event coordinator.  
Waterfront views of the East Bay and close proximity to Olympia’s downtown hotels, shopping, and other attractions.  
Beautiful, eco-friendly, and LEED- and Green Globes-certified building with state-of-the-art sustainable features.
