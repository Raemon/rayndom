# New Harmony Inn Resort and Conference Center New Harmony, Indiana

What is the New Harmony Inn??  
  
A Unique Destination: A Romantic Experience?  
Schedule your Beautiful Wedding In Enchanting New Harmony Indiana.  
  
From the rehearsal dinner to the reception, each wedding event in New Harmony is a pleasant walk or lovely carriage ride in one enchanting place. The town’s charming historic dwellings, quiet tree lined streets, and serene atmosphere offer you the perfect setting for such an important time in your life.  
  
The centrally located New Harmony Inn Resort and Conference Center has made this historic resort a captivating wedding destination. New Harmony Inn is one of more that 115 worldwide distinctive and independent properties ranging from sophisticated city-center hotels to peaceful countryside retreat.  
  
The Rehearsal Dinner  
The “Home of the Red Geranium Restaurant”, the New Harmony Inn Resort offers a variety of dining options for the rehearsal dinner. The Red Geranium Chef can create an elegant plated dinner served with carefully selected wine or can arrange a casual menu incorporating the theme of the evening.   
  
The Ceremony  
Of all the events on your wedding day, the ceremony is the most precious. With beautiful lakeside settings, lush gardens, unique architecture and historical charm, New Harmony provides ideal surroundings for indoor and outdoor weddings.  
  
The Food  
Deciding what type of meal to be served at the reception begins with planning the type of service that will accompany the fare. The catering staff at the New Harmony Inn can arrange a simple brunch, a casual buffet, or a formal plated luncheon or dinner.  
The Conference Center, with its spacious grand ballroom-like atmosphere, can accommodate up to 300 guests for dining and dancing. Catering services, provided by the New Harmony Inn, are available at the Conference Center, as well as off-site venues.  
  
The Lodging  
The convenience of lodging your out of town guests at the New Harmony Inn eases transportation and safety issues.
