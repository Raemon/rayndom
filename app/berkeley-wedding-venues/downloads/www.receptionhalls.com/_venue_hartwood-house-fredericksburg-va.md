# Hartwood House Fredericksburg, Virginia

Hartwood House is a historic Federal-style home located just five miles from Fredericksburg, Virginia.  
  
The 26-acre property provides scenic and elegant indoor/outdoor dining and bar services.  
  
Known as a sought-after Virginia wedding and special events venue, Hartwood House will provide a memorable experience for you and your guests.  
  
For more information on event hosting and catering services provided by Hartwood House, please contact us at 703-501-2290
