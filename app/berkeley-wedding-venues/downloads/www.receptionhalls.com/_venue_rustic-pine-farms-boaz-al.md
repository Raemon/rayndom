# Rustic Pine Farms Boaz, Alabama

Rustic Pine Farms offers a 40' x 80' barn nestled alongside a beautiful backdrop of pine trees and rolling fields. Our unique landscape provides several outdoor options for your dream wedding, while the barn includes restrooms, a prep kitchen and more. Let Rustic Pine Farms help you create the wedding you've always dreamed of!
