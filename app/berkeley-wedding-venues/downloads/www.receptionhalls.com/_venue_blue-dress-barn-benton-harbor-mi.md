# Blue Dress Barn Benton Harbor, Michigan

As seen in Chicago Magazine..A unique Reception Hall located inside an historic White-Washed Barn..wrought Iron chandeliers..timber ceiling..a one of a kind space. Martha Stewart would love this..seats 280.  
  
The Blue Dress Barn seats 280 in a 4,600 square foot area under a vast open timber roof with a dance floor and stage lit by elegant wrought iron chandeliers. Two second story decks add 600 square feet overlooking gardens and fields.  
  
In the gardens are tables beneath shade umbrellas, chairs and benches around fires.
