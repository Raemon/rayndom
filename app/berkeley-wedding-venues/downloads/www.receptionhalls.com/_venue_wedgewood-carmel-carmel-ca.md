# Wedgewood Carmel Carmel, California

![Photo of Wedgewood Carmel](/media/CA/10112/wedgewood-carmel-carmel-ca.jpg)

Wedgewood Carmel offers a gorgeous, affordable wedding location on the Monterey Peninsula. Famous for destination weddings, this location usually comes at a very high price. At Wedgewood's Carmel Valley location, you'll get the wedding of your dreams at a price you can afford. You'll also experience our signature Value, Service, and Convenience.  
  
Wedgewood Carmel is situated in Carmel Valley just off Highway 1. The Spanish-style clubhouse sits on the beautiful Rancho Canada Golf Course. With two banquet rooms to choose from, you're sure to find the perfect fit. The spacious ballroom seats 400 guests, while the Fiesta Room seats 175 guests and provides sweeping views of the golf course and ceremony site. If your heart is set on getting married on the beach, we can take care of you. Talk to us about our beach ceremony packages, then enjoy your reception back at the golf club.  
  
We look forward to planning your dream wedding in breathtaking Carmel.
