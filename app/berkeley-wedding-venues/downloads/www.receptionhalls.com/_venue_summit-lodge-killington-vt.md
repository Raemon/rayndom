# Summit Lodge Killington, Vermont

Vermont weddings… surrounded by your friends and family are extra special here at the Summit. Whether it’s a summer, spring, fall or winter wedding, we can make your wedding day dreams come true. We offer a wealth of experience hosting weddings and banquets here at the Summit Lodge in Killington, Vermont.  
  
If you are looking for a Justice of the Peace, a band or a DJ, florist, cake baker, or a photographer we can recommend the best in our area to assist you with your needs.  
  
We will help you plan the perfect Vermont country wedding.  
  
Our outdoor gazebo with panoramic views of Killington Mountain and the Green Mountains of Vermont, is the perfect setting for your wedding ceremony.  
  
If the New England weather does not cooperate, our spacious Living Room can quickly be converted to accommodate your matrimonial ceremony.  
  
Have your cocktail hour in our terrace and gazebo bar overlooking the pool or in the Living Room with the comfort of the field-stone fireplace, next to the O’Dwyer’s Pub. Then move into the Green Mountain room for an elegant reception.  
  
Choose from a variety of menus for your reception. Starting with an extensive Hors D’oeurvres selection for your cocktail hour and then moving onto the main meal.  
  
We offer a sit-down meal, buffet, food stations, and even a poolside BBQ. We will work with you to customize for your specific needs.  
  
If you are considering a winter wedding, contact us about our special winter wedding rates and packages. Ask about early Spring and late Fall Vermont Destination Weddings for additional value.
