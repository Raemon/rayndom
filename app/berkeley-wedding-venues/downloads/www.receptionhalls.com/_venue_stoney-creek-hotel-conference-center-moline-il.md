# Stoney Creek Hotel & Conference Center Moline, Illinois

Discover the Stoney Creek Hotel and Conference Center. We are located along the scenic banks of the Mississippi River in the heart of Downtown Moline, Illinois, perfectly located for all of your guests from near and far.  
  
For groups from four to four hundred, the Stoney Creek Hotel &amp; Conference Center is the perfect location for all of your milestones, from birthdays and retirement parties, graduations and anniversaries to the biggest day of your life. Let our experienced staff do all of the work – we will finalize all of the details and make your event special and one of a kind. Enjoy the comforts of home without the stress.
