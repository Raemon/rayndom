# The Carey Gardens Wedding Venue Snohomish, Washington

SERENE AND BEAUTIFUL SURROUNDINGS  
The Carey Gardens is situated on five private acres minutes from Woodinville. With mountain views, a reflection pond and fountain, colorful foliage, inviting gathering spots and many natural photo opportunities, we have meticulously created a venue that will enchant you and your guests.   
  
Our venue services include tables, chairs and linens, fire pits, wedding arch, deck for the ceremony at the water's edge, dance floor, two bars, large reception tent, gazebo, flower beds, outdoor games and more.
