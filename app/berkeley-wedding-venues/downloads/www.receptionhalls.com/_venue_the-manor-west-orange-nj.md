# The Manor West Orange, New Jersey

Successful catered events are the result of careful planning and coordination of even the smallest details. The Manor's professional staff guides every step of your function - be it an intimate party, major corporate function, wedding, bar mitzvah, or anniversary - so that you can relax and share your guests' enjoyment.  
  
The Private Dining Staff can offer many wonderful options, from cocktail receptions to full-course dinners unrivaled anywhere. Your functions will be enhanced by antique serving pieces and Lillien porcelain place settings designed in Europe expressly for The Manor. Theme party or Chef's Table Dinner, white glove service is the standard - and The Manor is the restaurant that sets the standards.
