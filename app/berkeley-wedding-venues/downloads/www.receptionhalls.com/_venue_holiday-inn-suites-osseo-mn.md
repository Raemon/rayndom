# Holiday Inn & Suites Osseo, Minnesota

![Photo of Holiday Inn & Suites](/media/MN/11075/holiday-inn-suites-osseo-mn.jpg)

Happily ever after starts here!  
  
Ideally situated in the heart of the Arbor Lakes Shopping and Entertainment District with easy access to I-94, I-494 and just off I-694, the Holiday Inn Hotel &amp; Suites at Arbor Lakes in Maple Grove, MN is a beautiful Venetian-themed hotel that features grand accommodations that put you close to NW Minneapolis' most exciting attractions and businesses.  
  
Thank you for considering the Holiday Inn &amp; Suites for your wedding ceremony and/or reception! Our Naples Room has a beautiful backdrop for your special day and can accommodate up to 120 guests with a head table, dance floor and display tables. Included is a wonderful private pre-function area adjacent to the space with a convenient built-in bar. We have a extensive catering kitchen and full beverage and liquor service to meet your every desire.   
  
Please keep in mind that this is just the beginning! Call today for more information and availability.
