# Dover Rent-All Tents & Events Dover, Delaware

Dover Rent-All Tents &amp; Events is the most diverse and experienced tent and party rental company on the Delmarva Peninsula. We have been providing party and wedding rentals to Delaware and Maryland since our founding, in 1966.  
  
From our centrally located facility in Dover, Delaware, we offer over 80 diverse tent sizes from just 4’ wide tent to a spectacular 80’ x 180’ Tent!!! We offer tables in 15 styles and chairs in 12 different styles and colors! Dover Rent-All Tents &amp; Events rents china, glassware and flatware in many different shapes, styles and sizes to compliment your special event. We offer linen in dozens of shapes and sizes to match any table with over 50 solid colors and over 100 prints and patterns to choose from!  
  
Our experienced sales team is ready to help you plan your event from start to finish! Whether you are planning a wedding, a casual party, or a large corporate event, we have the experience and the know how to make it unforgettable.
