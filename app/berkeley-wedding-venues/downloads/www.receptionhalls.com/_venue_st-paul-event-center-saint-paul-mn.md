# St. Paul Event Center Saint Paul, Minnesota

Brand New in 2020 - St. Paul Event Center is a contemporary, stylish space overlooking the vibrant downtown St. Paul skyline. The open-concept venue reflects the historic location (previously the Dayton building) accented by modern finishings and high-end details.   
  
The space can hold up to 450 guests (COVID-19 restrictions allow us to seat up to 175 guests), with custom floor plan options to create an intimate experience for groups of any size.   
  
Features & Amenities:  
  
• Beautiful downtown St. Paul location  
• Floor to ceiling windows with scenic views  
• Open floor plan concept  
• Convenient parking options  
• Wedding suite with stylist station & lounge seating  
• Customizable ceremony, cocktail & reception space including lounge space  
• Access to Minnesota Wild practice facility for amazing photo ops (ask for details!)  
• Customizable mood lighting & A/V capabilities  
• In-House catering by CRAVE Catering. Ability to use outside catering for other menu styles.   
• Bar services by St. Paul Event Center  
• Virtual guests + live streaming ability  
• Professional event staff   
• Coat check  
• Event tables, chairs + lounge areas  
  
We can't wait to work with you to create a memorable event!
