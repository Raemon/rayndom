# Cloud 9 Marine Enterprises Inc. New York, New York

At your every beck and call are the men and women of Cloud 9 Yachts. Dressed in formal attire, the Cloud 9 Yacht crew provides exceptional white glove service. With over 23 years of experience, the expertise and professionalism of the trained staff will make your event the best experience ever.  
  
With two wonderfully sized Yachts, we can accomodate all your party and event needs! We feature:  
  
* Yacht built specifically for NY cruising  
* Personal attention given to each party  
* Owners are aboard for the cruise  
* 22 years of established service to the industry  
* Fine chinaware  
* Crystal stemware for drinks  
* Full commercial galley to prepare the freshest of meals  
* Private chef on board  
* Custom menus  
* Professional uniformed crew  
* Departures from the marina of your choice (tri state area of NY, NJ, and CT)
