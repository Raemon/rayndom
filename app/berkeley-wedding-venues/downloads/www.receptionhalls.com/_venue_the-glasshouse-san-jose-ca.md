# The Glasshouse San Jose, California

As one of the largest venue spaces located in the heart of downtown San Jose, The GlassHouse is known for its glamorous and stylish interiors with many contemporary twists. Complemented by its stunning open-air patio, handicap access, onsite recommendations, bird suite, insurance, and wireless internet. It’s perfect for a wedding and reception of up to 400 guests!
