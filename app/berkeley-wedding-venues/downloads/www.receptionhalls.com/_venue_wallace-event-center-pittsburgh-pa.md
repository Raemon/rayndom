# Wallace Event Center Pittsburgh, Pennsylvania

![Photo of Wallace Event Center](/media/PA/11425/wallace-event-center-pittsburgh-pa.jpg)

The 14-acre Sherwood Event Center is open from mid-May through the end of September. It's tucked away just east of Pittsburgh, nestled on the edge of Wilkinsburg adjacent to the Forest Hills border.  
  
  
Amenities  
Whether you are planning a corporate picnic, a luxurious wedding, or simply want to have a family BBQ, the Sherwood Event Center has everything you need for a fantastic party. Here is a list of the amenities you can enjoy:  
  
2,800 square foot outdoor, covered pavilion suitable for music, dancing, live bands, etc.  
30+ tables and 200+ chairs for your use  
Large, in-ground pool  
Certified lifeguards  
Lounge chairs and tables surround the pool for your relaxation  
Tennis and basketball courts on-site are included  
More than enough parking space for everyone  
There is also an abundance of green space on our 14-acre property so you will feel relaxed and comfortable.  
  
\- See more at: http://www.hosannahouse.org/sherwood.asp#sthash.7DI4lGlJ.dpuf  
  
Wallace Event Center  
  
Amenities  
6,000 square foot banquet hall  
Chairs to seat up to 450 guests  
60 inch round tables for dining or conference seating  
6 foot rectangular tables for your display or catering needs

Sound system with wired, handheld microphones  
Input connections for just about any type of external device; iPod, CD player, MP3, etc.  
Input to connect an external sound system for greater flexibility for your DJ  
Hospitality suite for you and/or your honorees  
Theater stage large enough for your presentation or production
