# Aspen Meadows Resort Aspen, Colorado

Nestled on 40 sweeping acres in the historic West End of Aspen, Colorado, and home to the Aspen Institute, this inspiring resort is breathtaking to say the least. Situated along the Roaring Fork River, this unique Aspen hotel is surrounded by stunning views of the Colorado Rockies. A renowned getaway, the Aspen Meadows boasts an array of unique offerings from unmatched dining to onsite art galleries to praised meeting spaces to a variety of outdoor adventures. There’s no wonder why the Aspen Meadows Resort is the perfect escape to relax, rejuvenate and recharge. No matter the season, Aspen sparkles with a host of activities, events and culture for all to enjoy. The picturesque Roaring Fork Valley is perfect for any getaway. As Colorado is home to some of the best adventures and fabulous weather, there's no wonder why Aspen has been claimed as an international outdoor activity hot-spot. From renowned cultural events, to mountain adventures, there’s a reason why visitors fall head over heels for this destination gem.
