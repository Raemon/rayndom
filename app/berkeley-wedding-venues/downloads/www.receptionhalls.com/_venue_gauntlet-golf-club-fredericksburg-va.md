# Gauntlet Golf Club Fredericksburg, Virginia

Our clubhouse features panoramic views of the beautiful Gauntlet Golf Course, located within the picturesque setting of Curtis Memorial Park in Stafford County, Virginia. You'll love our outside terrace, which provides the perfect setting to begin your special affair. The Twilight Room will comfortably accommodate banquets and parties of up to 150 people.   
The Gauntlet Golf Club in Fredericksburg takes pride in offering complete, personalized catering services. Our catering menu offers a mouthwatering collection of food and beverage choices that is always custom-tailored to meet your individual requirements.   
Whether you are planning a business luncheon or the wedding reception of your dreams, you will find a friendly staff ready to offer you the warm, personal service you deserve, to create a magical moment to remember.
