# Magic Moments Private Event Estate Oceanside, California

Magic Moments is a Unique venue in the Eastern part of San Diego County. It has a Greek architectural theme with the beautiful mountains as a backdrop. We work with affordable linen providers to enhance the ambiance of your wedding. All tables/resin chairs included. We also carry belly bars. Dance area, serving area, ceremony area, you have to come out to appreciate it! Call me for a showing!
