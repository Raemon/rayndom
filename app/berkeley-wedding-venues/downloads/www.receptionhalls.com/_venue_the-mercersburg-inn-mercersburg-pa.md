# The Mercersburg Inn Mercersburg, Pennsylvania

Mercersburg Inn is a fairy tale Pennsylvania wedding venue for your special day! Your guests will be raving about our stunning Georgian mansion set on over 5 acres of terraced lawns and gardens. We are the perfect setting for a magical celebration for up to 150 guests, including indoor and outdoor tented reception sites.  
  
The inn offers 17 beautiful guest rooms, so your closest family and friends can stay on-site to enjoy the celebration! Luxurious amenities include cozy fireplaces, whirlpool tubs, plush robes, free Wi-Fi and private en-suite baths in every room. In addition, a gourmet breakfast is included every morning. Our highly acclaimed chef at our fine dining restaurant will help you plan a wedding feast to remember!  
  
Extend your stay and enjoy a romantic honeymoon with us as well. Nearby activities include skiing, hiking, golf, swimming, visiting historic sites, shopping, and winery or brewery tours. We also offer all-inclusive elopement and vow renewal packages for more intimate celebrations.  
  
Visit mercersburginn.com for photos and more details on our events. We look forward to helping you plan the wedding of your dreams!
