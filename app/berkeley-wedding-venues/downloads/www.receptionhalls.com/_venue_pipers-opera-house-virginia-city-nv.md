# Piper's Opera House Virginia City, Nevada

Piper's Opera House is located in the Historic town of Virginia City which is located just 30 minutes from Reno and Carson City and about 45 minutes from Lake Tahoe. Our theater can accommodate up to 300 people. We invite you to have your wedding, reception, meeting or convention at this one of a kind venue.
