# Residence Inn Birmingham Inverness Birmingham, Alabama

![Photo of Residence Inn Birmingham Inverness](/media/AL/11538/residence-inn-birmingham-inverness-birmingham-al.jpg)

Tucked away in a quiet corner of the city, Residence Inn Birmingham Inverness is located on 280 near 459, just 2 miles from great shopping and dining at The Summit and 15 minutes from the Riverchase Galleria. Our Inverness hotel is close to the new Grandview Medical Center and a short drive to the Mercedes plant. Enjoy complimentary Wi-Fi in all rooms as well as a free breakfast buffet each morning in the lobby. Whether it's time to work or rest, retreat to spacious suites with separate spaces for living, working, dining and dreaming. One of the most convenient extended-stay hotels in Inverness, AL, we also offer a free grocery-shopping service and fully equipped kitchens in each suite. Break a sweat in the fitness center, take a dip in the outdoor pool or head to the on-site Sport Court. On select evenings, make sure to stop by The Residence Inn Mix, an evening social hour featuring light fare and free beverages. Whatever brings you to Birmingham, make our hotel the home base of your next trip!
