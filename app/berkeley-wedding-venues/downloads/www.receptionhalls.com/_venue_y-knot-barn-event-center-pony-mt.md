# Y Knot Barn & Event Center Pony, Montana

Step back in time at the Y Knot Barn nestled under the shadow of Hollowtop Mountain in the historical town of Pony, Montana. Leave the hurriedness of the city behind and enter this small quaint village community for your perfect wedding day. This 180 acre family ranch which hosts the Y Knot Barn provides breathtaking views, endless photo opportunities and a relaxing atmosphere.
