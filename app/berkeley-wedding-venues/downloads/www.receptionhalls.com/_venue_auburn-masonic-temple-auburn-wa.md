# Auburn Masonic Temple Auburn, Washington

The Auburn Masonic Temple is a unique venue conveniently located in Downtown Auburn at the corner of Main Street East and Auburn Way South.   
  
Able to accommodate 100 seated, with a comfortable capacity of around 200 guests, the Dining Room features sprawling hardwood floors and open space that allows you to configure the exact layout you’ll need for your event. Light color walls and earthly oak tones create a tranquil atmosphere.   
  
Your rental includes the use of all chairs, tables (12 round, 60" and 10 rectangle, 104"), full kitchen with no catering requirements and dressing rooms for ladies and men.  
  
Do you need a space to hold your next event? Holiday parties, birthday parties, corporate events, public or private events, weddings, banquets, reception, memorials, birthdays, quinceanera, bar mitzvah, bat mitzvah, Sweet 16, Seijin-no-Hi, Ji Li and Guan Li, our facility can handle it all!   
  
Come and tour our facility to see if it might work for you.
