# Kingsmill Resort Williamsburg, Virginia

Located in historic Williamsburg, Virginia, Kingsmill Resort offers four difference golf courses to play(The River Course, The Plantation Course, The Woods Course, and Bray Links), spa services, plenty of fun activities for kids including a complete tennis center, boating, kayaking, hiking, biking, walking trails, and fishing. With 16,000 square feet of 16 IACC-certified meeting rooms, Kingsmill provides plenty of business facilities. This resort is an ideal destination for family vacations, romantic getaways, and wedding ceremonies and receptions. It offers 425 comfortable guest rooms.
