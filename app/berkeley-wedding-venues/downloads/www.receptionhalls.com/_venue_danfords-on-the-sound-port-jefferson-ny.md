# Danfords On The Sound Port Jefferson, New York

[ Please Call ](tel:631-928-5200)

![Photo of Danfords On The Sound](/media/NY/83/danfords-on-the-sound-port-jefferson-ny.jpg)

Nestled in the heart of historic Port Jefferson, a mere 50 miles from New York City, on the heritage North Shore of Long Island, Danfords on the Sound provides everything you could possibly ask for- every season of the year.  
  
Events and special occasions are more memorable at Danfords because our experienced Catering and Sales staff help you create just the right menu- and the right mood.  
  
Our banquet services feature theme buffets with cuisine from around the world, gourmet wine tasting dinners and much more. There's something for everyone's taste and diet, with no compromise on quality!  
  
With its breathtaking views of The Sound and charming touches of antiquity, Danfords provides a most romantic setting for a wedding.  
  
Yacht Receptions Available.  
Enjoy a Complimentary Honeymoon Suite.
