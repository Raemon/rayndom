# Sky Ridge Pavilion Eureka Springs, Arkansas

The new Sky Ridge Pavilion has indoor dining capacity for up to 150 people with a covered terrace providing sweeping vistas of Ozark valleys surrounding Cedar Creek. Amenities include a wood burning fireplace built from native stone, a window wall running the length of the public space, full catering kitchen and outdoor fire circle. The covered terrace provides additional seating and event space with views of the resort's two ponds and the waterfalls that create Cedar Creek . From formal receptions to family reunions, the Sky Ridge Pavilion makes your special event easy to plan, affordable and best of all...memorable.
