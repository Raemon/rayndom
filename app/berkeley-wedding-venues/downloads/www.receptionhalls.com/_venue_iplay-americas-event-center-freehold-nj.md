# iPlay America’s Event Center Freehold, New Jersey

![Photo of iPlay America’s Event Center](/media/NJ/10633/iplay-americas-event-center-freehold-nj.jpg)

Conveniently located at the intersection of Route 9 and Route 33 in Freehold, NJ, our special event venue in New Jersey offers a non-traditional approach to professional events and special occasions. Encore offers 20,000-square-feet of combined space including luxury VIP suites, three large banquet rooms that can be combined, and an 12,000-square-foot multipurpose event center. Work with one of our event planners to coordinate a flooring configuration, choose furniture and décor, and choose a tailored menu for your special day. Encore’s highly experienced event planners bring an unparalleled level of creativity and service to every occasion.
