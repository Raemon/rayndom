# Dirty Martini Washington, DC

Dirty Martini, a unique, upscale, sophisticated and casual place to enjoy great food and drinks, do business and more importantly have a good time! We are proud to say that we have brilliantly integrated both a classic dining club and a modern restaurant / lounge into one fantastic culinary phenomenon. And - to top it off we have packed Dirty Martini with a dedicated, passionate, and prodigious staff!  
  
We have a beautiful Mezzanine level perfect for banquets, receptions and corporate events!! Contact us for more information!
