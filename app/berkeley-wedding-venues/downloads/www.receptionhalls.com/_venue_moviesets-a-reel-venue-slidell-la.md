# Moviesets: A Reel Venue Slidell, Louisiana

Moviesets: A Reel Venue  
  
A party's dream come true; be "in" the movie! host your event midst props and set dressings from actual movies! Moviesets is Slidell's most unique venue as well as a professional and functioning movie prop studio and film location to Louisiana's exploding film industry.  
  
-Over 10,000 sq. feet of space  
-Dancehall and performance stage  
-VIP room  
-2 bars or more  
-Photo booths  
-Private rooms of all sizes  
-Tables, chairs, and buffet tables  
-Kitchenette  
-Ample parking  
-Outdoor area for cooking  
-Sets from films such as: Dawn of the Planet of the Apes, 12 Years a Slave, Maze Runner, GI JOE: Retaliation, Django Unchained, Abraham Lincoln: Vampire Hunter, 22 Jumpstreet!  
  
Make your event as unique and unforgettable as you are!
