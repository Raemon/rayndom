# Port Washington Masonic Center Port Washington, Wisconsin

We have a large building in Port Washington that we offer usage of the building and kitchen for your private event. Weddings, Birthdays, family reunions, Quinceanera - sweet 16, celebration of life, repass, retirement party, vow renewals, baby showers and etc. All are welcomed, no need to ask or wonder. You set up the tables and decorate how you like. You can bring in your own food, buffet, potluck style etc. and drink/refreshments as well. Just another way to save money. We do not require any certain vendor's so you can save, and we allow you to have DJs or a band for your event too. You can even hold your wedding ceremony and the reception here. Contact us for a tour. Building Can accommodate up to 125 people. Contact us for a number or email and we will be happy to send it to you for questions.
