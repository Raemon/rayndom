# Ellettsville Masonic Lodge #245 Ellettsville, Indiana

The Ellettsville Masonic Lodge is located conveniently in the downtown area of Ellettsville, and home to the Ellettsville Masons of #245. They are opening up their space to share in the community for hosting your next event, meeting or small party.   
  
Dining space, that comes with a kitchen and serving area, along with tables and chairs that is the perfect setting for reoccurring meetings, reunions, or host your next birthday, retirement or engagement party.   
  
Please contact to set up a viewing of the space and set up your next event.
