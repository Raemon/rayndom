# Oasis Reception Facility Paris, Kentucky

Announcing the grand opening of the Oasis Reception Facility. Just 15 minutes from Lexington, Georgetown, Winchester and Cynthiana in the heart of beautiful downtown Paris. This historic landmark has been beautifully restored and includes hardwood floors, exposed brick walls and a new bridal suite. Oasis also has 4 additional reception venues, both indoors and out.
