# Grafton Lodge Lake Lure, North Carolina

The Grafton Lodge property is 1/2 a mile from our beautiful Lake Lure in Western North Carolina. We are also just 2 miles from Chimney Rock Village. We are nestled on the hillside in the woods. We are a quiet lodge for the Bride and Groom and up to 12 additional family or friends to stay at, each of the 6 rooms and 1 Cottage have private entrance/bathrooms on the property. There is a covered deck with a real wood fireplace for evening enjoyment. A small reception or dinner can take place on the covered deck or there is the Riverside Event Center that can hold up to 200 guests for a larger reception. There are other properties with in a 2-mile radius for additional guest to stay.
