# Club Auto Sport San Jose, California

Rev up your next event by choosing Club Auto Sport, Silicon Valley's chic club and premier events center.  
  
With its modern design, sleek lines, 20-foot window walls, and the latest technology, the 10,000 square foot clubhouse and lounge is ideal for any function from 15 to 1,500 guests. Your company party. Executive training workshop. New product unveiling. Charity fundraiser. Prom or other social event. Even a wedding or bar mitzvah. Plus, the elegant, private TMFC patio at Club Auto Sport is just right for the most intimate gatherings.  
  
We handle everything - from catering to videography and entertainment. Let Club Auto Sport drive your event to exhilarating new heights.
