# Agassiz Village Poland, Maine

Agassiz Village is the quintessential setting for your wedding celebration. Our 40 building facility is nestled in 330 acres of sprawling fields in Poland, ME. The venue offers the ideal combination of nature and rustic charm, and its picturesque Lake Thompson shoreline provides the perfect backdrop for exquisite wedding photos. Hold your ceremony in our open air amphitheater, underneath our storybook gazebo, or on one of two islands. Your guests will enjoy our dining facility, cozy summer cabins, smores by the fire, kayaking, canoeing, and so much more! Our unique space is available in May, June, late August, September and October. Capacity: up to 250 guests Initial Rental Cost $4,500. Activities and overnight accommodations are an additional cost.
