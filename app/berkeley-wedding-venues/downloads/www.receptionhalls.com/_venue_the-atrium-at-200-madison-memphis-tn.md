# The Atrium at 200 Madison Memphis, Tennessee

Set in the heart of downtown Memphis, the Visible Community Atrium and Concert Hall at Visible Music College is the perfect choice for your next event!  
  
On the trolley line, our venue is just across the street from AutoZone Park and a short walk or trolley ride to The Peabody and Main Street.  
  
An iconic landmark, the venue offers a soaring Atrium of approximately 4,500 square feet. In addition, the adjacent 2,400 square foot Concert Hall is complete with a catering kitchen and two restrooms.  
  
Additional event space available to rent includes the Vining Concert Hall at 2,300 square feet that comes with a stage and built in audio and video capabilities and the Wilson Center at 3,900 square feet that includes a gorgeous balcony with great views of Autozone Park and downtown.  
  
Meeting space and classroom space is also available at an hourly rate.

### Venue Details

Capacity (Seated / Cocktail): **200 / 250**

Our Atrium can accommodate up to 200 guests, with our Concert Hall accommodating up to 150 guests. We have both banquet-style and buffet-style tables to be used for your event.

Max Number of Simultaneous Events: **1**

Outside Catering Permitted (BYO): **Yes**

Venue Type/Style: **Atrium**

On-Site Cuisine Options: **Use Your Own Caterer**

### Pricing

Minimim price for 100 guests: **$ 1,800**

Rental rates for the Atrium and the Concert Hall are fixed daily rates which vary depending on whether the event is day time (less expensive) or night time (more expensive) and the day of the week with Saturday night being the most expensive. We also offer deep discounts to non-profits.
