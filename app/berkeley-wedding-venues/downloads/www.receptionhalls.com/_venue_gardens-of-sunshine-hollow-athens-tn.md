# Gardens of Sunshine Hollow Athens, Tennessee

Indoor or Garden weddings with Receptions in our 5000 square foot event center. Home cooked food and wedding cakes with free guest tables, chairs and linens. Largest private gardens in Tennessee with twenty acres of landscaping for beautiful wedding pictures. Reception venue has twenty foot high ceilings with exposed wood beams and rustic chandeliers and wall sconces. Air conditioned and heated with four bathrooms. Active Military discounts. Off season discounts.
