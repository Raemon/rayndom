# The Legacy Barn Kokomo, Indiana

Beautiful Rustic Barn Wedding Venue! Let us make your dream wedding come true! We offer the whole weekend Friday, Saturday & Sunday! We have options for an Indoor &/or Outdoor Wedding Ceremony with your Reception inside our gorgeous rustic barn with all modern amenities and suites for you and your bridal party to enjoy all weekend!   
Secluded on 12 acres of beautiful property, located just 6 miles west of downtown Kokomo, Indiana. We have on site catering for your every food & beverage need, as well as Day of Directing and Wedding Coordinating Services. We take care of every single thing you need from the chair and tables, to the linens you choose and a room FULL of beautiful decorations for our Legacy guests to use for free!   
Contact us today to reserve your Dream wedding!
