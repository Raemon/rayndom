# Glynallyn, The Castle Morristown, New Jersey

MAKE GLYNALLYN, THE CASTLE THE STORYBOOK SETTING OF YOUR OWN CELEBRATION...  
  
Glynallyn, The Castle is available for exclusive private events, including weddings, corporate events, birthdays, holiday parties, photoshoots and intimate gatherings, with a limited number of events hosted each season.  
  
The Castle’s many rooms allow for specialized entertaining and décor from the stunning two-story Great Hall, Library, Billiards Room, Cigar Room and old-fashioned bar, to the intimate dungeon and opulent second floor bridal suite offering comfort and privacy for the bride and wedding party. The rear of the castle opens up to a beautiful back terrace overlooking the vibrant gardens and a serene pool where a moat once stood.
