# Norfolk Botanical Garden Norfolk, Virginia

Surrounded by water and beautiful blooms, Norfolk Botanical Garden is the ideal setting for dream weddings and elegant celebrations. With thirteen serene outdoor locations to choose from and wedding packages for every budget, the Garden can accommodate an extravagant affair for up to 400 guests or an intimate party of 20, and everything in between! Each of our premium wedding packages are paired with indoor and outdoor reception locations. We also offer engagement and elopement packages as well as ceremony only or reception only options.
