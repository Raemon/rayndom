# Holiday Inn & Suites Bolingbrook Bolingbrook, Illinois

Intimate Weddings - perfect for up to 180 guests. Winter & Spring dates available! Mention this site to enjoy discounted rates for all December-May dates.  
You will be the only wedding couple onsite! The ballroom features neutral decor and 3,773 sq ft with dedicated pre-function space. In addition, the Holiday Inn & Suites features 145 guestrooms and suites. All guestrooms feature high-speed internet, Keurig Coffeemakers, fridge and microwave. Guests will enjoy Hanover & Hardy's Restaurant and Lounge or unwinding in large indoor pool, spa and fitness center. Complimentary Presidential Suite and discounted room block with wedding packages. Take the Elevator Home!  
  
When choosing Holiday Inn & Suites Bolingbrook for your event, your Catering Sales Manager will work with you to create customized menus from our onsite catering and help to make your wedding day memorable. Our packages include tables, chairs, floor length linen, China, flatware, glassware, dancefloor as well as audio visual services available to add to package. Complimentary Parking. Wheelchair accessible. Outside Ethnic Catering Service Packages available.
