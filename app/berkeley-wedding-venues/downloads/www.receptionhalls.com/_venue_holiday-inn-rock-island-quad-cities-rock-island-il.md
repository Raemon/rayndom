# Holiday Inn Rock Island - Quad Cities Rock Island, Illinois

The Holiday Inn Hotel & Conference Center Rock Island is a full-service hotel with two restaurants, B’s Kitchen & the Rock Island Ale House, serving breakfast, lunch, dinner and room service; 172 guest rooms & 3 suites; and over 10,000 sq. ft. of flexible meeting and banquet space.  
  
The Holiday Inn Rock Island is located just two blocks from the banks of the Mississippi River in vibrant Downtown Rock Island with art galleries, restaurants, brew pubs, nightclubs, comedy club, professional dinner theatre, antique stores, and unique shops.  
  
Our newly renovated modern ballroom and meeting space can seat from 5 to 500 guests with complimentary high-speed internet access. Whether planning a corporate event, an intimate cocktail party, a celebratory banquet or wedding reception, you will have our undivided attention.  
  
Each of our renovated 172 guest rooms and 3 suites offer voicemail, phone & data-port, complimentary WiFi, Keurig coffee maker, iron & ironing board, hair dryer, flat screen TV, microwave and refrigerator.  
  
Free parking, WiFi, and airport shuttle are just a few of the complimentary services we provide. During your stay, visit our snack & beverage shop located next to the front desk, enjoy a morning workout with a few laps in our indoor pool, or relax in the sauna or complete a quick workout in our Ultimate Fitness Center facility located on the second floor.  
  
We have you covered at the Holiday Inn Rock Island.
