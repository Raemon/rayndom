# Studio 1060 Kissimmee, Florida

Hablamos español!  
  
Studio 1060 is a fully updated event venue with fresh, modern look. Dimmable LED lights, neutral vinyl floors, and large windows serve as the perfect canvas for your decoration, though the space needs very few decorations as it is already stunning!   
  
Here is what comes included with your rental-  
  
-Full day rental (10 am to 12 am)  
-1,300 square feet  
-Fully finished floors (no carpet!)  
-Six 60' round tables   
-54 chairs  
-Six chafing dish racks  
-Two 6' x 3' tables for food or additional seating  
-LED Dimmable lights throughout  
-Fully handicapped accessible entryway and handicapped accessible restroom  
-May bring your own caterer or self-cater  
-May bring your own alcohol  
  
This space would best suit parties of 72 or fewer. Call, text, or visit our website to schedule a walkthrough.
