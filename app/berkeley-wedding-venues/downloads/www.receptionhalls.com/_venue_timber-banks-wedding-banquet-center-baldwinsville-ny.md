# Timber Banks Wedding & Banquet Center Baldwinsville, New York

Timber Banks Golf Club &amp; Banquet Facility offers a picturesque setting to have your wedding ceremony and reception. Located on one of the most beautiful golf courses in Central New York, this is an ideal place to ensure gorgeous wedding photos. The tented facility can seat up to 300 guests. Perfect for the indoor/outdoor wedding you've always imagined. Enjoy our enclosed facility with a fabric draped ceiling with crystal chandeliers, indoor restrooms, blacktop floor. Side panels with windows overlooking the 18th hole and double glass doors for the entryway. The ceremony site's backdrop is the Seneca River with beautiful scenery surrounding the sight. We have a private location for the bridal party to prepare and an amazing golf course for the guys to enjoy. With an experienced event coordinator to assist in planning your big day, it will be the wedding you’ve always dreamed of.
