# Lofty Hitchins Lawrenceburg, Tennessee

Whether it is vintage, rustic, or a little outdoor elegance you are looking for, our venue will fit your style. Lofty Hitchins' is a beautiful barn set in the countryside of Lawrenceburg in Southern Middle Tennessee. With vintage pews in the upstairs ceremony area and vintage style tables and chairs downstairs for the reception, our venue makes the perfect selection for you without having to go through the hassle of renting from multiple companies. It also features private bride's and groom's suits for the wedding party to get ready in. Our Venue is available for Weddings, Receptions, Reunions, Corporate Events, Birthday Parties, and much more.
