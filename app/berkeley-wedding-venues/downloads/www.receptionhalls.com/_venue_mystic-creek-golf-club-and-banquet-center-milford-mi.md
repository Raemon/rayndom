# Mystic Creek Golf Club and Banquet Center Milford, Michigan

Your perfect wedding is assured at Mystic Creek Golf Club &amp; Banquet Center. It offers the ambiance, gourmet excellence and professional, courteous service for that most important event in your life. Mystic Creek specially creates each event, from black tie formal to upscale elegance, for up to 300 guests.  
  
Mystic Creek offers the perfect settings for your special event. Your wedding ceremony at the Gazebo nestled in the pines. The panorama of the Oakland County countryside and breathtakingly beautiful landscape can be shared from the patio, the deck or the exquisitely decorated glass walled banquet center.  
  
Only Mystic Creek creates special programs for all social occasions. Engagements, Bridal showers, Rehearsal dinners, bachelor dinners, Wedding ceremonies, Receptions.
