# Pickerill Estate House Yorkville, Illinois

Pickerill Estate House is nestled in the beautiful Pickerill-Pigott Forest Preserve! Host up to 130 people in this historical home, featuring a huge outdoor covered patio. Put your own stamp on your event with access the entire day before to decorate the venue, bring in décor, food, and beverages. Our staff will work with you to create a floorplan that meets your needs and we will be on hand during your event to ensure the comfort and safety of your guests as well as work with your contracted vendors to ensure a smooth event! Plus, you have the freedom to bring in your own food, if you choose not to have the event catered by one of our Preferred Catering Services. Alcohol is allowed as long as there is a licensed bartender on site, who is a part of our Preferred Bartender list. Visit https://kendallforest.com/special-events-yorkville.php
