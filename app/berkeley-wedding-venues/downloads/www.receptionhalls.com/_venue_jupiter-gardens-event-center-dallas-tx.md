# Jupiter Gardens Event Center Dallas, Texas

Are you looking to rent a glamorous and affordable event center in Dallas? The Jupiter Gardens Event Center provides you with the glamour of ‘The Big D’ in the newly renovated conference and wedding reception venue in Dallas. Gorgeously appointed with modern and Tuscany decor, the hall dazzles with neutral colors and adjustable chandelier lighting conducive for any occasion. Located right on the LBJ Freway, the rental event center which opened in January 2013 is easily accessible from the north, south, east and west. Come and experience our hospitality in our fabulous banquet hall. You will want to bring your guests here once you experience our hospitality. Visit our website at www.jupitergardenseventcenter.com for more information.
