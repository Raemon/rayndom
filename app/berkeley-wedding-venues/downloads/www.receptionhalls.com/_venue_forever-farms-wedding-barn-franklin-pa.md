# Forever Farm's Wedding Barn Franklin, Pennsylvania

Forever Farm Wedding Barn, minutes from hotel & other amenities but yet sits on 150+ acres. Approx an hour north of Pittsburgh. Breath taking views, ceremony site overlooks pond and deck provided. Large deck overlooking ceremony and Stain Glass as the back drop of your elevated head table.
