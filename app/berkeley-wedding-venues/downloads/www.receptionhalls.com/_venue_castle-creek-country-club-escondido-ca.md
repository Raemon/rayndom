# Castle Creek Country Club Escondido, California

Nestled in the foothills of North San Diego is Castle Creek Country Club. We are everything you have been looking for in a wedding, reception or meeting venue. Beautiful surroundings, an elegant banquet facility and fine cuisine expertly executed by Trupiano's Catering and Banquet Services. We offer a unique wedding experience, that can't be found anywhere else. We boast an 18 hole, PGA approved, Championship Golf Course, making us the perfect location for a corporate retreat, sales meeting or destination event. Enjoy the Temecula Valley Wine Country, local casinos and all that San Diego has to offer. Visit our website at www.fallbrookweddingsandevents.com
