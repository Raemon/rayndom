# Luxe Event Venue Charlotte, North Carolina

New and Modern building with 22 FT high ceiling, chandeliers and marble floor. Blank canvas design so you can create your own event. Huge, attached outdoor covered patio. Bring your own Food, Alcohol, DJ, Decorations and attendants. Located on a wooded, private street with wide open space. Very easy 2 minute access from 2 interstate highways.
