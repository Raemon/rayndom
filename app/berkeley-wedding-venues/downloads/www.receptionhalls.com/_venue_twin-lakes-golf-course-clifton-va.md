# Twin Lakes Golf Course Clifton, Virginia

Plan the perfect, memorable day sure to impress guests while staying within your means. The Twin Lakes Clubhouse provides an elegant atmosphere complete with a spacious layout and picturesque views of the 18 hole. Our friendly and helpful staff are dedicated to ensuring an unforgettable and relaxing event based on your special requests. Our pristine greens, lush landscaping and serene lakes, make for the perfect ceremony location and backdrop to create timeless photo memories. Book a tour today to experience sophisticated simplicity firsthand. Conveniently located within minutes of airports, lodging and our nation’s capital. Also available for rehearsal dinners, parties and showers.
