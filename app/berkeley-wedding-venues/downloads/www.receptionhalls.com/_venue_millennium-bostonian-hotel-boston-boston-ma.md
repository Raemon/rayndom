# Millennium Bostonian Hotel Boston Boston, Massachusetts

![Photo of Millennium Bostonian Hotel Boston](/media/MA/10446/millennium-bostonian-hotel-boston-boston-ma.jpg)

Located across from Faneuil Hall, the Millennium Bostonian Hotel offers a historical and beautiful Boston wedding venue. The Seasons Ballroom is regarded as one of the best wedding venues in the region, ensuring unforgettable receptions, rehearsals and bridal showers. Couples can welcome up to 150 guests here, and complement their special day with spectacular views of the city skyline. Catering from the hotel and outside can be accommodated.
