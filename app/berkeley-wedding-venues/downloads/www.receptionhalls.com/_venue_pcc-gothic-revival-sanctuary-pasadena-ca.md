# First Congregational Church Pasadena, California

The Gothic Revival Sanctuary located in Providence Christian College is a beautiful venue for weddings! The banquet hall is a perfect venue for wedding receptions, dances, award ceremonies, company events, etc.   
  
For more information about availability and pricing, please call (626) 696-4050 or email elizabeth.siri@providencecc.edu. We are happy to give you a tour of the sanctuary and banquet hall and to answer any questions you might have.

### Pricing

Minimim price for 100 guests: **$ 2,500**

The standard fee for the use of the sanctuary for weddings is $2,500. There is a $500 deposit to hold the venue for your date. This deposit is required at the time of booking and is non-refundable. The $2,500 fee includes: 60-minute rehearsal 3-hour wedding time block Dedicated Coordinator for rehearsal & ceremony Brides Room & Grooms Room Custodial services Minster Looking to Livestream Your Ceremony? We have live stream capability to stream and record your ceremony to include friends and family who are unable to attend. The live stream fee is $150/hour. This fee does not include editing. We also have a wedding reception/event banquet hall which can be rented for $2,500. There is a $500 deposit to hold the venue for your date. This deposit is required at the time of booking and is non-refundable. The banquet hall is a perfect venue for wedding receptions, dances, award ceremonies, company events, etc. Our parlors can accommodate up to 25 to 80 guests, while our large reception hall can hold up to 500 guests.
