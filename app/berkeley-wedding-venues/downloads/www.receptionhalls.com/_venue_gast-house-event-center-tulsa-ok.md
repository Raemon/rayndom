# GAST House Event Center Tulsa, Oklahoma

The GAST Event Center, built in 1929, with its beautiful,original Gothic-style architecture has been the perfect venue for countless wedding ceremonies and receptions since 1992. Hardwood floors, vaulted ceilings and gorgeous lighting make it the ideal setting for any event.   
  
We have one main hall for combined ceremony/reception events as well as private parties and corporate events.   
  
We have no preset vendor list so clients can use whomever they choose!
