# River's Edge Venue Chattanooga, Tennessee

We are a riverfront venue offering picturesque views and gorgeous landscapes. We offer 3 distinct areas to suit all types of events:  
1\. Outdoor deck overlooking river  
2\. Screened in porch (2000 sq. ft.) for weather backup  
3\. Banquet hall equipped with full bar and giant staging kitchen.  
One great price gets this all.
