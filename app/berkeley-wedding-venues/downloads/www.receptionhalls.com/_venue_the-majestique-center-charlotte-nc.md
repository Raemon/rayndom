# The Majestique Center Charlotte, North Carolina

![Photo of The Majestique Center](/media/NC/10836/the-majestique-center-charlotte-nc.jpg)

The Majestique Center is a newly renovated space and is conveniently located in Charlotte, NC. Our facility has a capacity of up to 190 banquet style. The Majestique offers great package deals with Certified Event Planners.The Majestique Center is a great solution for your weddings, corporate events, meetings, fundraisers, private parties, bridal and baby showers,birthdays,quinceanera/sweet 16 party, graduations, Christmas/New Year’s eve parties, family reunions, church gatherings and many more - Let us do the work!
