# Smokey Hollow Selbyville, Delaware

Smokey Hollow Estate Barn is the Eastern Shore’s newest premier wedding and event venue. This Private Estate offers event space for ceremonies, receptions, fundraisers, non-profits and corporate events alike. The barn boast 5000 sq. ft. allowing for events up to 288 guests. Crystal chandeliers accent the room giving a sense of elegance, the walls are finished with brick accents offering a timeworn patina. Authentic barn doors open to the adjacent space designated for cocktail parties or space to expand with the addition of a tent. As the owners, Mark and Karen collaborate they are always coming up with something new as they are completing the interior. We will keep you posted as the interior becomes rustic but elegant.  
  
  
  
Nestled in Selbyville, Delaware, Smokey Hollow Estate is just a 10-minute drive from the Maryland and Delaware beaches. With sweeping picturesque views, a beautifully manicured 100-acre property, Smokey Hollow is the ideal “blank canvas” to create your signature event. The property boasts exquisite gardens, a fresh water pond, greenhouse, guest house.

If you prefer a tented event? Take cover in style! Choose from a Pole, Frame or the gorgeous Sail Cloth Tent which have a translucent fabric creating an airy atmosphere that is soft and warm during the day and vibrant and exciting at night. All tents can be draped so they enclose the space just enough to feel intimate but also allow guests to see the gorgeous setting outside. Adorn the tent with a variety of lighting; Paper Lanterns, Café and Vineyard Lighting or Crystal Chandeliers- all are sure to impress. The options are endless!
