# Golden Gates Restaurant Philadelphia, Pennsylvania

Golden Gates Restaurant is the perfect venue for any special occasion in Northeast Philadelphia. Our banquet hall can accommodate up to 420 guests, making it suitable for events of any size.  
We offer four different menu options at different price points. If needed, we can customize the menu to accommodate any dietary preferences or restrictions. Our professional chefs will prepare delicious food that will satisfy every palate.  
  
We guarantee an unforgettable celebration. Our entertainment team will create a festive atmosphere and provide entertainment that will leave a lasting impression on you and your guests.  
  
We also offer catering services. If you need catering for an off-site event, our professionals are ready to fulfill all your wishes and requirements.
