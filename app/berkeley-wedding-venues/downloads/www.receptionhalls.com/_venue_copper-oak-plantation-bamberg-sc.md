# Copper Oak Plantation Bamberg, South Carolina

Copper Oak Plantation is on 20 acres in bamberg sc. It is lined with huge beautiful oak trees and a pond with a dock. The 4360 sq ft barn has a huge main room, full kitchen, huge bar, 2 restrooms with 2 stalls in each, and a dressing room.
