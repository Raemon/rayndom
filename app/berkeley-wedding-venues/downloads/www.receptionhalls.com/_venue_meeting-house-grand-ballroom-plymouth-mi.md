# Meeting House Grand Ballroom Plymouth, Michigan

![Photo of Meeting House Grand Ballroom](/media/MI/265/meeting-house-grand-ballroom-plymouth-mi.jpg)

The Meeting House is a Victorian inspired single event wedding reception banquet facility. The ballroom features 18 foot high ceilings, orndate crown moldings, fireplace, grand piano and 2nd floor balcony perfect for the bouquet toss. Our culinary graduate chef is well knon for generous portions, expertly prepared and formally presented by waiters in white glove.  
  
The owners have personally overseen every wedding since 1994 (over 1,000 wedding receptions). Randy and Mary have extensive refferences that they would be pleased to share with you when you schedule your tour of the Meeting House Grand Ballroom.
