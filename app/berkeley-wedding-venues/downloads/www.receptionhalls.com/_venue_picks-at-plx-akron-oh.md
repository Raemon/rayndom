# Pick's at PLX Akron, Ohio

Host your next event at Pick's in Portage Lakes! Our beautiful waterfront property offers two spacious indoor banquet halls that can accommodate parties ranging from 20-100 guests. In addition, we have an outdoor space available seasonally.   
  
Choose from a variety of meal and beverage packages tailored to your needs such as a Mimosa Bar, Bloody Mary Bar, Taco Bar, or Cheesecake Bar.   
  
Our trained, professional staff are prepared to make your big day memorable for you and your guests! To start planning your event, call 330-794-5157 or email us at info@picksatplx.com

### Pricing

Minimim price for 100 guests: **$ 2,000**

The Lakeview Room: For parties with 35-75 guests and requires a room fee of $100 Monday-Thursday and $200 Friday-Sunday. The room fee holds the date and pays for the space and must be in the form of cash or check. Monday-Thursday the food/drink minimum is $400, but if the event is Friday-Sunday the food/drink minimum is $800. All food orders must be placed two weeks in advance. The Clocktower Room: Seats up to 35 guests and requires a room fee of $50 Monday-Thursday and $100 Friday-Sunday. This room fee holds the date and pays for the space and must be in the form of cash or a check. Monday-Thursday there is no food/drink minimum, but if the event is Friday-Sunday the food/drink minimum is $500. All food must be purchased from our catering menu.
