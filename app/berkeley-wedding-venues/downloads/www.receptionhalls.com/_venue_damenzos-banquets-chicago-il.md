# Damenzo's Banquets Chicago, Illinois

Damenzo’s Banquets is a family owned and operated business for over 29 years, with superb food and experienced and friendly staff, we can ensure your event will be long remembered. Our banquet facility offers a warm inviting atmosphere with spacious ceilings and beautiful wood throughout.  
  
Our multi-purpose facility can accommodate up to 150 guests and meet the needs of individuals, groups and organizations. With specially priced packages for every style and budget, full catering and bar services, and professional staff, Damenzo’s can make your special event an unforgettable one.
