# DoubleTree by Hilton Whittier Los Angeles Whittier, California

Welcome to the DoubleTree by Hilton Whittier Los Angeles hotel located in the historical Uptown Whittier district. We are within driving distance of Disneyland, Knott’s Berry Farm, Orange County, Downtown Los Angeles, Hollywood and shopping in Beverly Hills! Nestled in the lush area of Whittier we are close to the I-605, I-60 and I-5 freeways. Our hotel is the perfect destination for business travelers, family vacations, meetings and special events. Our humble destination includes 200 comfortable and spacious hotel rooms, heated outdoor pool and Jacuzzi, on-site restaurant, on-site fitness center, business center and over 12,000 sq ft of private meeting and event space.
