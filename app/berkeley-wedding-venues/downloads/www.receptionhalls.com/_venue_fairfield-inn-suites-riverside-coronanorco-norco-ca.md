# Fairfield Inn & Suites Riverside Corona/Norco Norco, California

Welcome to Fairfield Inn & Suites Riverside Corona/Norco, the ideal location for your next event. Our hotel offers a variety of versatile event spaces that can accommodate a range of meetings, conferences, and special events. Whether you are hosting an intimate gathering or a large-scale event, our experienced event staff is here to help you plan and execute a successful event.  
  
We understand that catering is a key component of any successful event, which is why our hotel offers a range of catering options to suit your needs. From coffee breaks and continental breakfasts to full-service buffets and plated dinners, our team of culinary experts will work with you to create a customized menu that suits your preferences and budget.  
  
Our hotel is conveniently located just off the 15 freeway, making it easily accessible from anywhere in the Inland Empire and beyond. We also offer free on-site parking for your convenience.
