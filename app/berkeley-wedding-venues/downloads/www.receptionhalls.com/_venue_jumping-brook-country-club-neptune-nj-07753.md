# Jumping Brook Country Club Neptune, New Jersey

Jumping Brook Country Club is one of the top Country Clubs in Monmouth County NJ and offers exceptional cuisine and gracious service that is set on a spectacular 225 acre Championship Golf Course. Choose one of our three lavish Neptune New Jersey banquet halls in our 35,000 square foot clubhouse for your special event. Each room offers panoramic views of the lush surrounding grounds. Weddings, Bar/Bat Mitzvahs, Anniversaries, Golf Outings, Proms, Christenings and Glatt Kosher affairs are just a few events that our Catering Sales Representatives will help you plan. Our goal here at our golf club is dedication to the highest standards of quality, our personal touch and attention to detail will assure you that your special occasion will be a complete and memorable success.
