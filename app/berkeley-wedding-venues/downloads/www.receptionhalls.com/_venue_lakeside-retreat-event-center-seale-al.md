# Lakeside Retreat & Event Center Seale, Alabama

Conference or wedding venue overlooking a beautiful lake and well-kept grounds minutes from Columbus, Georgia. Over 3,500 square feet of available lake view meeting space, with audio-visual and dining capability and catering for up to 200 people. Facility also has 48 hotel rooms, including king size bedrooms, double queen suites, and bunk style rooms for your youth retreats. Foyer areas available for displays or networking receptions.
