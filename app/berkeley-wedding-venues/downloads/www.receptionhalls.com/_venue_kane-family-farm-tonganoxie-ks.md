# Kane Family Farm Tonganoxie, Kansas

Capacity and Facilities   
Kane Family Farm is a beautiful setting that utilizes restored stone barns and stunning outdoor areas with all of the resort conveniences. The venue can comfortably accommodate up to 300 guests with more options being added by 2020. The historic farmhouse has been completely renovated and is available for nightly rentals, and a bridal suite. Take one look at this venue and you will be ready to start planning the perfect event!   
  
Services Offered  
The Kane Family Farm is a beautiful venue and in addition to the stunning property, the staff also offers a variety of packages and options that are so important for events of all shapes and sizes. The team has full-time planners to help you design and customize your event, as well as handle all of the little details that can be so difficult while planning an event. We have DJ services, catering, bar services, and full event rentals, to cover all the bases for your perfect event. On the day of your event, the staff offers a turn-key operation of setup and cleanup and management so your only responsibility is to sit back and enjoy your event. Onsite parking, valet and wheelchair access are available to all guests in attendance. Book your complimentary consultation today and watch your event come to life.   
  
Cuisine  
Catering and drink options are available to make your event. You and a select few guests are invited to attend a tasting before nailing down your menu to ensure your food is nothing short of perfect. Servers and bartenders are included in every package. Services include:   
  
* Buffets, Cocktail reception, Family-style  
* Hors d'oeuvres  
* Plated  
* Stations  
* Dessert  
* Open bar  
* Specialty drinks
