# The Water's Edge Resort & Spa Westbrook, Connecticut

![Photo of The Water's Edge Resort & Spa](/media/CT/54/the-waters-edge-resort-spa-westbrook-ct.jpg)

Elegant surroundings, innovative cuisine and unparalled service all create the perfect setting for your special occasion.  
  
Water's Edge Resort &amp; Spa is a luxury, year round resort, situated on twenty acres overlooking Long Island Sound. Your wedding guests can stay overnight in one of the 167 hotel rooms, villas and suites, indulge in exotic treatments at the new Spa at Water's Edge, swim in the indoor and outdoor pools, play a game of tennis, bask in the sun on the private beach or relax to live entertainment in the Sea View Lounge.  
  
Their distinctive wedding facilities include outdoor garden terraces and lawns offering a magnificent setting for your wedding ceremony or receiving hour. The reception continues with dinner and dancing in either the Grand Ballroom or Royal Ballroom, featuring panoramic ocean views.   
  
Allow the Water's Edge Resort &amp; Spa's impeccable standards of hospitality to create the wedding celebration of your dreams. Their master chefs create an unparalleled culinary experience while the professional service staff attends to every detail as planned by you and your personal wedding coordinator.  
  
Just two hours from New York or Boston, the only facility in Connecticut with direct beachfront on Long Island Sound, makes Water's Edge Resort &amp; Spa your once in a lifetime wedding destination.
