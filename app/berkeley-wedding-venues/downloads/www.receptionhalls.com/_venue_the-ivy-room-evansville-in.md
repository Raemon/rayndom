# The Ivy Room Evansville, Indiana

Do you need a wedding or banquet venue that can hold your expanding guest list?  
  
The Ivy Room is one of Evansville’s largest wedding venues with 9,000 square feet that can hold over 500 guests.   
Centrally located and minutes from Downtown off the Lloyd Expressway  
  
Opened in 1974, the Ivy Room is a wonderful place to celebrate all your special occasions.  
  
With a spacious, unobstructed layout, the Ivy Room can accommodate up to 500 people with banquet style seating or up to 700 with theater style seating. The possibilities are endless at the Ivy Room.
