# Eco-Institute at Pickards Mountain Chapel Hill, North Carolina

The Eco-Institute’s Organic Farm is a rustic natural sanctuary for your Sacred Ceremony and Celebration. We welcome you to celebrate your Union in our outdoor sanctuary held by beauty, wonder, and compassion for all of Life. We welcome all loving Unions. Funds raised by the Eco-Institute at Pickards Mountain through hosting weddings and events support our Educational Programs.
