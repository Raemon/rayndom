# Park Savoy Florham Park, New Jersey

Welcome to The Park Savoy. Since 1982, we have pursued a single goal: to create a setting for weddings warmed by timeless elegance and matched by seamless service. From superb cuisine to lovely natural settings and unparalleled staff, The Park Savoy is wonderfully unique. And in keeping with our commitment to service, we cater to only one wedding at a time so that we can fully meet your personal needs.  
  
But come, click on a link below to browse through the rooms of our landmark mansion and let your imagination provide the guests, the music and the magic of your wedding day. It is a day we would be honored to share with you.
