# The American Legion Post 177 Fairfax, Virginia

Fairfax American Legion Post 177 has spacious and affordable banquet halls for your wedding, special event, or meeting. Please contact our Banquet Manager calling (703) 273-2250 ext. 3  
  
With two banquet halls we can accommodate Weddings receptions, Birthdays, Anniversaries, Office parties, Luncheons, Christmas parties, Seminars, Conferences, Business Functions, Services, Amenities and many options.
