# The HiFi Dallas Dallas, Texas

Featuring 25,000 square-feet of flexible design space, The HiFi is the perfect blend of rock and elegance, both classic and cutting edge. From the intimate Echo Lounge to the upscale and multi-tiered Music Hall, The HiFi has an atmosphere that celebrates extraordinary hospitality and service. Situated in the bustling Design District, near the American Airlines Center and less than 3 miles from the Kay Bailey Hutchinson Convention Center, The HiFi accommodates 25 to 700 guests for private events.   
  
This unique setting is the perfect location to host your corporate event, private concert, fundraiser, bar/ bat mitzvah, wedding or reception. With access to the world’s greatest musical entertainment, full-service event production, experiential marketing capabilities, custom menus and mixology - our unique venue will exceed your guests’ expectations. Our talented staff handles every detail, so you don’t have to!
