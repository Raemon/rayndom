# Knock Music House Atlanta, Georgia

Knock Music House is an Atlanta private event planning and coordination company. We provide VIP rock star experiences for kids, adults, and businesses.  
  
We have Atlanta event venues in Virginia-Highland at 1052 St Charles Ave NE and in Lenox Park at 1789 Cheshire Bridge Rd. Our Lenox Park location is best suited for large events from 150 - 750 people and our VAHI location is perfect for small events with a max capacity of 125 people. We also provide full-service event planning, DJs and entertainment, and event coordination.  
  
​  
  
No matter what size event, we create VIP Rock Star experiences for kids, adults, and businesses. We provide full-service event planning complete with party entertainment, event photography, videography, photo booth, food and beverage, and more.   
  
​  
  
Knock Music House is the perfect event venue for your wedding, holiday party, birthday party, sweet sixteen, private event, graduation party, team building event, corporate party, and more.
