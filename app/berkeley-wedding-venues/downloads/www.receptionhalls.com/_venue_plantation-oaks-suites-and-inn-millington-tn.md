# Plantation Oaks Suites and Inn Millington, Tennessee

![Photo of Plantation Oaks Suites and Inn](/media/TN/9977/plantation-oaks-suites-and-inn-millington-tn.jpg)

The Plantation Oaks Suites and Inn is a perfect venue for the budget conscious meeting planner. Not only do we have meeting space adjacent to the hotel that can accomodate up to 100 people in rounds but outside areas to accomodate picnics etc. Be sure to ask about our group rates when book your next meeting! The hotel features offers standard rooms and three types of suites.
