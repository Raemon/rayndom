# Aruba Steve's Island Grill Warren, Rhode Island

Aruba Steve’s Island Grill is the newest addition to Main Street in Warren, RI! Our large upstairs comfortably accommodates more than 40 guests around the tropical bar, cocktail tables, and window seating that overlooks our large outdoor entertainment courtyard for live music and drinks.   
  
Our 1,000 sq. ft. function room is the ideal location for all private events including graduation, birthday, and anniversary parties, wedding and baby showers, rehearsal dinners, cocktail events, retirement parties and much more! The private room comfortably seats a total of 85 guests, but can be expanded and opened to the bar area to accommodate 130 guests for larger events. The space also features a 120-inch projector screen that is available for a variety of uses adding to the amenities of any event and is available for reservations anytime upon request.
