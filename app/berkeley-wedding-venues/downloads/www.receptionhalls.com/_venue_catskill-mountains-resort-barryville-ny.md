# Catskill Mountains Resort Barryville, New York

We are THE Wedding Venue in Sullivan County NY to make your special day spectacular. Let us do the work for you!   
  
Brand New Banquet Hall can host and cater up to 250 guest Weddings, Banquets, Bar Mitzvah, Sweet Sixteens, Quinceañeras, Parties, Birthdays, Repasts and a whole lot more.   
  
We feature a Beautiful Country setting, Fountains and Gazebo for Pictures, Luxury Lodging, Fine Dining, Brick oven Pizza, 3 Bars, Lounge, Live Entertainment, oversize swimming pool in season, numerous hiking and biking trails adjacent to the resort and minutes from all area attractions including Bethel Woods Concerts at the site of the original Woodstock festival, seasonal eagle watching, Hunting on private acreage, fishing, canoeing, Kayaking and rafting on the Delaware.  
  
We are an easy drive from New York City, New Jersey and Pennsylvania and have ample parking for families and groups.
