# Wedgewood Crystal Springs Burlingame, California

![Photo of Wedgewood Crystal Springs](/media/CA/9774/wedgewood-crystal-springs-burlingame-ca.jpg)

Located off the 280 Freeway in Burlingame is the best outdoor wedding location in San Francisco. Wedgewood Wedding and Banquet Center at Crystal Springs Golf Course gives you the beautiful Bay Area wedding venue you have been looking for along with the Value, Service, and Convenience that is our trademark.  
  
Some of the spectacular, unique features of our San Francisco wedding site:  
  
* Located on a certified wildlife preserve overlooking the Crystal Springs Reservoir  
* Gazebo wedding site situated on a cliff overlooking glistening waters and tree-covered ridges below  
* Banquet hall with views of course and scenery, and seating for 300  
* Centralized banquet facility for San Francisco – great for hosting corporate and charity events
