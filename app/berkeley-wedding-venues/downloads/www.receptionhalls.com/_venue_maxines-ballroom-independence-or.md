# Maxine's Ballroom Independence, Oregon

Built originally as an 1880s Masonic hall and used as an Odd Fellows lodge, Maxine’s has been restored to turn-of-the-century splendor and is a perfect venue for your special event.  
  
Maxine's is an ideal intimate venue for dances, weddings, birthdays and more. A caterer's staging area is available as well as banquet tables and chairs for up to 49 people.
