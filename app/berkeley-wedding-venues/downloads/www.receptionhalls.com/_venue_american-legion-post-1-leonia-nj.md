# American Legion Post #1 Leonia, New Jersey

The American Legion Post #1 is a beautiful and wonderfully renovated hall. The two room space has wood floors and lovely wood wall covering. The Post dates back to 1919 and is a perfect historic site for your event. Weddings, dances, dinners, birthday parties and BUSINESS MEETINGS all kinds have been held in this lovely venue.
