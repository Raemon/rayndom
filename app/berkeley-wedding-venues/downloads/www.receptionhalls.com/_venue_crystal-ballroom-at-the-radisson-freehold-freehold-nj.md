# Crystal Ballroom at Radisson Freehold Freehold, New Jersey

The Crystal Ballroom at the Radisson Freehold, is a beautiful Monmouth County banquet hall that is the perfect location for your wedding, bar/bat mitzvah, sweet 16, meeting/conference, birthday party, and more! Our exceptional, experienced staff knows what it takes to make sure your next event is a memorable one. We work hard to make sure everything goes seamlessly. The Crystal Ballroom can accommodate up to 300 guests with a dance floor, and the Emerald Ballroom is available for more intimate affairs. With over 8,600 square feet of meeting space, an experienced, knowledgeable staff, and numerous catering menus to fit your needs and budget, we will always make sure your event makes an impact. Our reception facility features indoor and outdoor ceremony areas for weddings, a huge dance floor for up to 300 guests, picturesque outdoor patio, beautiful accommodations at the Radisson, and so much more. Contact us today at 732-780-3405.
