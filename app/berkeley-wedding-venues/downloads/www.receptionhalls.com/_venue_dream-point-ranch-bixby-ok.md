# Dream Point Ranch Bixby, Oklahoma

180 acres of wooded mountaintop provide beauty and views that only nature can provide.   
  
The Venue at Dream Point Ranch is a 5,400 square foot white barn with dark wood accents that is perfect for large or small weddings and receptions.   
  
Brides can also choose to get married outdoors and even choose a tent reception if not wanting to utilize the Venue.   
  
Beautiful views of the Arkansas River as few have ever seen it and large ponds are available for guests to enjoy, providing a unique location with exclusivity at it's finest.
