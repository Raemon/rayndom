# Hanford Ranch Winery and Events Galt, California

![Photo of Hanford Ranch Winery and Events](/media/CA/11159/hanford-ranch-winery-and-events-galt-ca.jpg)

Congratulations and thank you for considering Hanford Ranch Winery and Events for your upcoming wedding. We look forward to being your host and helping to make your special day magical and memorable.   
  
Hanford Ranch Winery and Events is nestled on a 40-acre hilltop located 30 minutes north of Lodi and overlooks spectacular 360 views of the Sierra Mountains breathtaking whitecaps, endless vineyards, agricultural land and beautiful emerald green meadows.   
  
The wall of glass windows on one side of the rustic Tasting Room opens out onto the deck to accommodate your DJ or small band and extends to an out-door dance floor that overlooks the rustic-country venue.   
  
The Ranch is the perfect location for you and your fiancé to exchange vows of love for one another and begin a lifetime together as ‘Mr. and Mrs’  
  
All of the following services are included in our Facility Fee:  
  
Site access for Engagement Photos (date and time to be scheduled with events coordinator)  
Rehearsal prior to your wedding (date and time to be scheduled with events coordinator)   
Private and exclusive use of the facility and grounds the day before your wedding for set-up   
Private and exclusive use of the facility and grounds the day of your wedding beginning at 5:30pm – 12:00am for your wedding ceremony   
Vintage Bus Bride’s Quarters  
Early arrival the day of your wedding to allow you and your bridal party to get ready, decorate and for vendor set-up  
Ceremony site   
Ceremony chairs  
5,000 square foot out-door reception area   
Tables and chairs for your reception  
Tasting Room and bar area for your social gathering  
Out-door lighted dance floor area  
Lighting and electrical hook-ups for vendors  
On-site events coordinator throughout duration of your wedding  
Set-up/Break-down and clean-up  
Complimentary parking  
Golf Carts to meander around the ranch for intimate photo opportunities  
*Sunday evening weddings are limited to 1:00pm – 8pm.
