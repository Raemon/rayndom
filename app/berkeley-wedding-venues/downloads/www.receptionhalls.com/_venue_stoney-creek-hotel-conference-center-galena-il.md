# Stoney Creek Hotel & Conference Center Galena, Illinois

Our Northwoods conference room is perfectly-sized to host our corporate guests' sales meetings, retreats, or strategy sessions. While our newlyweds appreciate the intimate setting we provide for rehearsal dinners.  
  
The rustic atmosphere provides an environment that is both unique and comfortable. We offer complete food and beverage service and audio/visual equipment. We work with a list of preferred caterers to offer you a wide variety of delicacies at a price that works with every budget.   
  
We've planned numerous activities to enhance our guests' events such as trail rides, golf, skiing, or fishing trips to enhance your event! We look forward to assisting you plan a productive and memorable event!  
  
We are large enough to serve your every need, yet quaint enough to make you feel relaxed and at home.
