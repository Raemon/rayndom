# Millennium Center Winston Salem, North Carolina

The Millennium Center is a privately owned, historic, multi leveled Winston Salem wedding venue and event space in the heart of downtown. We employ a diverse and experienced event staff who will diligently build, produce and breakdown your event, eliminating the hassle of coordinating multiple vendors. This includes our skilled, award winning culinary team, servers, bartenders, event management, in house coordination, custodians and security, access to our AV techs and even our in-house floral department if you so choose. Tours of the building are by appointment only. Email or call for yours today.
