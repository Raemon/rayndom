# Holiday Inn Club Vacations at Ascutney Mountain Resort Brownsville, Vermont

![Photo of Holiday Inn Club Vacations at Ascutney Mountain Resort](/media/VT/11758/holiday-inn-club-vacations-at-ascutney-mountain-resort-brownsville-vt.jpg)

Holiday Inn Club Vacations® at Ascutney Mountain Resort is the perfect location to make your wedding memorable, unique and fun. The beautiful mountain surroundings will ensure you have a breathtaking wedding everyone will remember!
