# Forest Farms Fredericksburg, Virginia

Family owned farm on sprawling Fredericksburg fields. Historic farm home lovingly upkept by two brothers and on-site staff. Host your next intimate event in one of the rustic buildings on the grounds or have a celebration in picturesque tree lined meadows.   
  
This event and retreat space boasts the original 18th century timbers and brick floored kitchen, modern renovated full bath, and basic AV capabilities. Guests can enjoy 51 peaceful acres of nature trails, guided tours, and opportunities for glamping and serene camping experiences.   
  
More than an event venue, this sustainable living village aims to serve Stafford County and surrounding areas through farm fresh foods, educational workshops and community connection.
