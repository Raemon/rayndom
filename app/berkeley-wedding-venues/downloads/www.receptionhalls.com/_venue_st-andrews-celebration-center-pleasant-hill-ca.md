# St. Andrew's Celebration Center Pleasant Hill, California

Welcome to the Celebration Center at St. Andrew's Presbyterian Church in Pleasant Hill, CA!  
  
Have you been looking for that perfect place for your wedding or celebration? One that incorporates modern features and beautiful natural surroundings? Well look no further...we have all this and more.  
  
Centrally located in picturesque Pleasant Hill, the Celebration Center is the perfect combination of ‘something old’ and ‘something new’!   
  
St. Andrew’s welcomes individuals, community groups, and corporations to experience the peace, comfort, and beauty of our facility.
