# Noble Lake House Leander, Texas

Come create treasured memories at the Noble Lake House, as you savor time with family and friends. Situated on nearly 2 acres, overlooking Lake Travis, this resort-like property provides a luxury main house and charming cottage for you and your guests, comfortably sleeping 16 people, or up to 21 overnight, with a maximum of 80 gathering during the day.  
  
Offering a beautiful view from the terrace that looks out upon the refreshing private pool and spa, this is all about lakeside elegance.  
  
Whether you are wanting a special place to celebrate a family reunion, birthday party, small to medium wedding, wedding shower, baby shower, church event, corporate off-site, graduation celebration, seeking a self-care trip, or just need a wonderful get-a-way, our Noble Lake House is a unique setting for such an occasion!
