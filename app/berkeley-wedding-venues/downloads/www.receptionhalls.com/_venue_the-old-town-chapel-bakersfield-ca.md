# The Old Town Chapel Bakersfield, California

Turn your dreams into a reality!  
Say 'I Do' in this vintage and historic building in Old Town Kern.  
Complete with original wooden floors, folding pews, and stained glass windows inside the chapel.  
Included in your booking is access to our beautiful brides room, grooms man cave, and bell tower.
