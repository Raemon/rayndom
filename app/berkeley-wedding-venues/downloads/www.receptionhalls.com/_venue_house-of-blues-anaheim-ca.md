# House of Blues Anaheim, California

The House of Blues Anaheim is a unique venue in the Orange County, California area. This state-of-the-art Music venue features 5 spaces all uniquely designed for any special event. The venue is located just six minutes from the Downtown Disney District.  
Facilities and Capacity  
Clients can invite a total of 3,300 guests to their event at the House of Blues Anaheim. The Restaurant & Bar accommodates 315 guests reception style. The colorful and eclectic art covers the walls surrounding a built-in stage. A polished cement floor adds to the industrial vibe of the venue.   
Our Luxury nightclub, the Foundation Room, accommodates up to 175 guests among the rich ornate lounge furniture and ornately carved wooden accents.   
The Parish features a stage, a built-in bar of exposed brick and saturated stained glass windows high on the walls that stretch up toward the tall ceilings.   
The Music Hall is the largest individual room with up to 1,700 guests for a reception. The room features a wrap-around second-story balcony with additional seating, vibrant artwork hung along the walls and a grand stage.   
All of our spaces would be able to operate separately or connected together for a unique and fun event.
