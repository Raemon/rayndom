# Rockingham Ballroom Newmarket, New Hampshire

![Photo of Rockingham Ballroom](/media/NH/7730/rockingham-ballroom-newmarket-nh.jpg)

The Rockingham Ballroom is New Hampshire's premier venue for ballroom dancing and dance lessons, and also offers a broad selection of musical entertainment evenings, including Disco, Swing, and Country.
