# W Chicago City Center Chicago, Illinois

![Photo of W Chicago City Center](/media/IL/11137/w-chicago-city-center-chicago-il.jpg)

Perfect for work and play, W Chicago City Center offers everything you need to make your business beautiful, meeting memorable, and special event spectacular. Creative in approach and innovative in design, W Chicago City Center is a breath of fresh air for your attendees.   
  
Open your mind and let the ideas flow. With 12 function rooms totaling 14,000 square feet of cutting edge conference facilities, we have all the space and technology you need to meet, greet, and connect. Our large, airy 4,158 square foot Great Room with its vaulted ceiling can accommodate the grandest of dreams. Keep things intimate. Choose the cozy, loft-like room with a fireplace.
