# Memory Lanes and Flashback Cafe Minneapolis, Minnesota

Memory Lanes and Flashback Cafe is the perfect non-traditional venue for weddings and receptions. A state-of-the-art entertainment center, Memory Lanes is equipped with 30 bowling lanes, a high-tech sound and light system, and audio and visual equipment.   
  
Flashback Cafe offers intimate seating for 100, a full bar, and full-service kitchen serving scratch gourmet food. Offering everything from light appetizer buffets to full dinners, your event is sure to please.   
  
We offer many options for groups ranging from 20-500 in all price ranges. Reserve some lanes or rent the facility. With our customizable packages, you are sure to find something that meets your needs.
