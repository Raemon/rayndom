# Brick Iron Studios Newark, New Jersey

Brick Iron Studios is the social collaboration headquarters just steps from the Prudential Center Arena. We welcome corporate events, meetings, media production, private parties, and celebrations of all types.  
  
Our location is a discreet penthouse loft that’s in the Original Thomas Edison factory building. The Brick Iron Studios has a rustic yet chic look that includes an elevator for easy access to our distinguished and open flexible floor plan. The space has a modernized industrial feel, featuring exposed brick, weathered wood aesthetics, canyon black steel beams, elaborate ductwork, and brushed aluminum finishes. Over a dozen skylights and oversized industrial windows allow for wide rays of natural light to beam through.  
  
Brick Iron Studios is a premier venue for social and corporate events in Newark. We pride ourselves on our understated elegance and impeccable service. Our main focus is making sure that you and your guests have a flawless and enjoyable experience when hosting an event with us.
