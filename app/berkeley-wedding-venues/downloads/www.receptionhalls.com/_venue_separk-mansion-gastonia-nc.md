# Separk Mansion Gastonia, North Carolina

Get Married in a Mansion  
  
Imagine having your wedding in one of the most romantic settings in Charlotte North Carolina. This romantic Italian renascence revival mansion built in 1920 is reminiscent of the Great Gatsby and the roaring 20s. The mansion features a formal garden for wedding ceremonies, as well as a large ballroom, bridal and groom suites. The beautiful architectural details of this historic mansion will provide endless opportunities for that perfect wedding portrait.  
  
Whether an intimate family wedding or a grand event with hundreds of guests, Separk Mansion is the ideal setting for your wedding day.
