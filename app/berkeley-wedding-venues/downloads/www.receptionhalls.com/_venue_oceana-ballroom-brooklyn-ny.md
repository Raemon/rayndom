# Oceana Ballroom Brooklyn, New York

Situated in the center of Brighton Beach, just steps from the ocean, The Oceana Ballroom is the most updated Landmark Hospitality venue in Brooklyn. When planning life’s most memorable events, we invite you to share your special day with your guests at Brooklyn’s best-kept secret.  
  
Upon arrival your guests are swept up our red carpet and guided through our enchanting lobby. Followed by our glass entryway, The Oceana Ballroom’s grand ballroom boasts 30’ hand-painted ceilings, expandable interiors, gilded gold columns and crystal chandeliers, offering your guests a feeling of grandeur and opulence.  
  
With numerous menu choices expertly crafted by our Executive Chef, we have endless options to cater to every budget.  
  
Our professional Event Specialists understand exactly what is needed to flawlessly execute your event, and are fully equipped to provide a full range of event planning services. From choosing the décor, planning the menu, detailing logistics, equipment rental and vendor coordination, our results will be beyond your expectations.
