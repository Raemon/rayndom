# Hilton Springfield Springfield, Virginia

![Photo of Hilton Springfield](/media/VA/11821/hilton-springfield-springfield-va.jpg)

Host a board meeting, corporate event, educational workshop, wedding reception or special social occasion at the Hilton Springfield hotel. Meeting planners and organizers can take advantage of 7,000 sq. ft. of flexible event space capable of accommodating eight to 800 Guests. Impress colleagues with an insightful presentation during brainstorming sessions, or stage a keynote address and black-tie dinner to welcome industry insiders to a week-long conference. Brides and grooms to be seeking the perfect wedding venue can set the stage for an event to remember in the grand Potomac Ballroom, which boasts 6,272 sq. ft. of obstruction-free event space. Take advantage of full-scale catering and banquet services featuring fresh, creative menus with regionally-sourced ingredients at our hotel in Springfield, VA. Treat Guests to impeccable service provided by a tenured team for flawless results.
