# White Mountain Weddings Freedom, New Hampshire

White Mountain Weddings, a rustic waterfront wedding venue in Freedom, New Hampshire, is known for its beautiful and fun wedding weekends! White Mountain Weddings venue believes your wedding shouldn't just be a day, but rather an entire weekend of celebration with your family and friends. The venue offers a weekend packed full of water skiing, sailing, campfires, and enjoying the lakeside beach. This 100-acre private resort is a beautiful wedding destination for everyone to enjoy.  
  
Facilities and Capacity  
Couples may host up to 500 wedding guests at White Mountain Weddings venue. There are five different event spaces that range from indoor to outdoor weddings. This wedding venue is all about being unique. You may choose to arrive at the altar by boat, instead of walking down the aisle or enjoy a canoe ride with your loved one from the wedding ceremony to the reception. The creativity at Camp Cody never fades, and they are ready for you to enjoy all they have to offer.
