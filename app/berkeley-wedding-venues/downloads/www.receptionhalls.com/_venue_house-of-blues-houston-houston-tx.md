# House of Blues Houston Houston, Texas

![Photo of House of Blues Houston](/media/TX/9960/house-of-blues-houston-houston-tx.jpg)

From the plush Foundation Room lounge to the colorful soaring backdrop of the Grand Music Hall, House of Blues offers a variety of creative spaces for wedding parties and receptions. Located in the heart of downtown Houston, this beautiful venue offers turnkey options and gracious service. Whether you're looking for a fun &amp; funky rockin' ceremony and reception, or a lavish formal affair, we'll assist in making your big day a big hit! Make yourself at home in our House!
