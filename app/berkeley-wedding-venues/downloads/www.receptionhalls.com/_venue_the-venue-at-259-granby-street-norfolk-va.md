# The Venue at 259 Granby Street Norfolk, Virginia

On Historic Granby Street! A modern Industrial feel, this is the perfect intimate space for up to 70 guests! Weddings, receptions, birthdays, retirement party, baby shower, even a private Murder Mystery night! This wonderful space has 1600 sq ft for your event to includes an upper level (400 sq ft). There is a beautiful bar that can be set for beverage service, hors d'oeuvres, buffet, or however you want to use it! At the back of the space is the private kitchen with a full freezer and refrigerator, sinks and room for a caterer to stage from.
