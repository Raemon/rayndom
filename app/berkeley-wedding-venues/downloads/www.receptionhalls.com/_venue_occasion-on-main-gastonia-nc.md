# Occasion on Main Gastonia, North Carolina

Occasion on Main is a newly remodeled, multi-level wedding and event venue located in the heart of Gastonia, North Carolina, proudly serving Charlotte, Mount Holly, Shelby, Lincolnton, and surrounding areas across the Carolinas.  
  
Thoughtfully restored to preserve the historic charm of its original structure, the venue features exposed brick, hardwood floors, open-beam ceilings, and modern lighting. This blend of rustic elegance and contemporary style creates a warm, inviting backdrop for weddings and special events of all kinds.  
  
We are passionate about delivering exceptional service and are committed to making every event at Occasion on Main truly unforgettable. With all ceremonies and receptions hosted indoors, you never have to worry about weather disruptions. We offer comprehensive in-house décor, catering, and bar services, while also welcoming outside vendors to ensure flexibility and personalization for your special day.
