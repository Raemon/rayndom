# The Southern Belle Venue Delmar, Maryland

At Southern Belle Barn we strive to provide the most elegant and personable wedding experiences on the Eastern Shore. We believe in stress-free, client-tailored weddings and events.  
  
The property is home to a sweet cottage, beautiful outdoor ceremony site, private bridal suite, grooms lounge, and of course, a breathtaking, reception-ready barn. This humbly polished barn features: 6 foot crystal chandeliers, vaulted ceilings, creative lighting, handsome wood beams, custom farm tables, and ambience to set the mood. As beautiful as it is bash-worthy, Southern Belle Barn is full of wedding day splendor.
