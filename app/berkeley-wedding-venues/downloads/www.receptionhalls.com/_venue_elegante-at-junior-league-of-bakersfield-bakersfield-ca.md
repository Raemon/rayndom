# Elegante at Junior League of Bakersfield Bakersfield, California

Elegante is a unique and truly eye catching wedding venue that you won’t find anywhere else in the world. Its beauty is one that is comparable to nature itself, and it will be the perfect area to host your special event with your family and friends. You will find that as soon as you enter the venue you will be immersed in its magnificence and the beauty it has to offer.  
  
With an intimate feel and loads of personality, Elegante might be the perfect place for your wedding or next special event. Standing regally at the corner of 19th &amp; E Streets, this historic jewel is located in the up and coming downtown Bakersfield. The venue is conveniently located within blocks of the arts district, a four star hotel, quality restaurants and the Padre Hotel.
