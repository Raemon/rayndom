# Hayes Mansion Hotel San Jose, California

Once a private estate, the historically grand Hayes Mansion now welcomes guests to experience comfortable elegance amid lush landscapes and dramatic views of surrounding mountains. The cultural thrills of San Francisco, the vineyards of Napa Valley, and the sparkling beaches of Santa Cruz all lie within easy reach of the estate's prime location, 15 miles from San Jose International Airport and 45 minutes from San Francisco and Monterey.
