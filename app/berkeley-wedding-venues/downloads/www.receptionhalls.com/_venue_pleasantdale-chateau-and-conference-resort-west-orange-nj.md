# Pleasantdale Château and Conference Resort West Orange, New Jersey

Pleasantdale Chateau and Conference Resort is a special retreat where visitors can immerse themselves in tranquillity and privacy. Brides will find few sites in the United States which can match the photo opportunities.  
  
Built during the period of 1920-1933, this beautiful estate, situated on 40 acres of formal gardens, rolling lawns and shaded ponds, offers peace and tranquility.
