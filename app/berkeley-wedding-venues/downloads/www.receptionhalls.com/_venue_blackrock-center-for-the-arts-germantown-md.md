# BlackRock Center for the Arts Germantown, Maryland

![Photo of BlackRock Center for the Arts](/media/MD/14046/blackrock-center-for-the-arts-germantown-md.jpg)

BlackRock Center for the Arts is an inclusive space that houses many different programs, events, camps, and classes. An auditorium, a theater, two art galleries, various art studies, various classrooms, an outdoor stage, and many more provide different experiences for different groups.
