# The Rectory on Princess Street Alexandria, Virginia

The Rectory on Princess Street is an elegant wedding & event venue in Old Town Alexandria, VA. Dating from 1785 and the current home of Classical Movements’ since 2014, the building formerly served as the rectory of the nearby historic Christ Church, once the church of George Washington.   
  
There are up to 9 well appointed spaces to book for your next event including our picturesque garden, our elegant ballroom and rustic-style atrium.   
  
The Rectory on Princess Street is available for micro weddings, elopements, engagement parties, photo shoots, dinner parties, meetings, concerts, & more.
