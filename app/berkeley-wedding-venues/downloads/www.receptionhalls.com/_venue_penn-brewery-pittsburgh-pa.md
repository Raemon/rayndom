# Penn Brewery Pittsburgh, Pennsylvania

A memorable event begins with a one-of-a-kind venue. Wow your guests at Penn Brewery’s 19th-centuryfacility, replete with a traditional German brewhouse and bierhalle, cobblestone biergarten, and rare original lagering caves. Listed on the National Register of Historic Places, Penn offers a variety of indoor and outdoor spaces for your private party, along with a full banquet menu including craft beers brewed on site. Personalize your event with a brewery tour, custom-labeled beer bottle favors, and more. Penn hosts weddings, rehearsal dinners, showers, corporate events, birthday parties, and special occasions of all kinds.
