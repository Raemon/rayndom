# Angels Event Center Baltimore, Maryland

Welcome to Angels Event Center. We are the perfect location for the person who wants to party on a budget. We offer two rooms for your party and we tailor all of the details to your needs. Each room comes equipped with a bar and full array of tables and chairs which are available to you at no additional cost. With us, you can bring your own food or have us cater it. You can do your own decorations or sit back, relax and let us bring your dreams to life with our creativity. We offer venue-only and all-inclusive packages.  
We host all groups and all party types--from baby showers, wedding receptions to retirement parties. We do it all. We are the best kept secret in Baltimore with prices that can't be beat. Stop by today and book your next event.
