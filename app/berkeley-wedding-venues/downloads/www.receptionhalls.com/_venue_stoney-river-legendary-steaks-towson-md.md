# Stoney River Legendary Steaks Towson, Maryland

![Photo of Stoney River Legendary Steaks](/media/MD/10963/stoney-river-legendary-steaks-towson-md.jpg)

Stoney River is conveniently located off 695 in Towson. We offer legendary steaks and fresh seafood. Our space seats up to 40 guests with 2 smaller rooms available for smaller parties. No room fees and complimentary valet parking make us a perfect spot for rehearsal dinners or small reception dinners!
