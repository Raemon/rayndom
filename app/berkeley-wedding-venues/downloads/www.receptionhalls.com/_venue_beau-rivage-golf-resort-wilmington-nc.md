# Beau Rivage Golf & Resort Wilmington, North Carolina

Just five miles from the cobblestone streets of historic downtown Wilmington, Beau Rivage Golf &amp; Resort provides an unforgettable coastal setting for wedding receptions, elegant banquets, corporate meetings, intimate affairs and other special gatherings. The comfort and convenience of on-site lodging accommodations, versatile banquet facilities and fantastic guest amenities just steps away create nearly limitless ways to customize events at a quiet, secluded retreat between the Cape Fear River and the Atlantic Ocean.  
  
Beau Rivage Golf &amp; Resort features spacious clubhouse suites and over 6,000 square feet of sophisticated function space. After meetings, an award-winning championship golf course, professional tennis courts, outdoor swimming, casual bar, lounge and an extensive selection of guest programs provide a host of relaxing activities throughout your stay.
