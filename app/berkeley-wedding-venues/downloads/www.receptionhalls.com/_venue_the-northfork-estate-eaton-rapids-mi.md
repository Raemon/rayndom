# The Northfork Estate Eaton Rapids, Michigan

The Northfork Estate is the Premier Setting for the Wedding of your Dreams. Located just 30 minutes south of Lansing. Join us on our tranquil setting where we can host an intimate gathering or an event of up to 300 people.  
With our private ponds, majestic landscaping, winding road and natural meadows, The Northfork Estate awaits your wedding, reception, ceremony and bridal shower.  
Then Northfork Estate is seated on 50 acres in lovely Eaton Rapids, Michigan.  
Call us today to make your next event the most Elegant and Distinctive!
