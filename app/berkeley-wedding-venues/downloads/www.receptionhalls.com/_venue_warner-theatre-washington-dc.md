# Warner Theatre Washington, DC

Wow your guests at this historic Washington DC space. This incredible venue is the perfect location to host your corporate event, private concert, wedding, reception or business meeting. A large, brightly lit marquee — which you can light in any color — showcases your brand, while main entrance poster boxes can be used to elevate your occasion. Located less than a mile from Walter E. Washington Convention Center, the Warner Theatre can accommodate from 25 to 1,960 guests. Our talented staff handles every detail, so you don’t have to!
