# Sona Thirteen Morristown, New Jersey

Shown by appointment only, Sona Thirteen with its exposed brick and a friendly, laid-back vibe is as welcoming as it is fun. With two unique event spaces, Sona Thirteen boasts a private space downstairs in addition to the full bar/restaurant that can be rented out for your celebrations, rehearsal dinners, after-parties, and more. Rental fees start at $1,000.
