# The Venue at Lenoir City Lenoir City, Tennessee

![Photo of The Venue at Lenoir City](/media/TN/13337/the-venue-at-lenoir-city-lenoir-city-tn.jpg)

A Knoxville area premier venue for conferences, conventions, weddings, reunions, and other milestone events.​  
-19,000 square feet of premium event space.  
-Multiple meeting spaces and open break-out areas.  
-Large ballroom to accommodate up to 600-700 guests for your seated banquet, 1,000 guests reception (seating/standing) style, and 1,200 theater style.  
-Covered outdoor patio and lovely garden area.  
-State of the art audiovisual systems, including Bose sound and videoconferencing ability.  
-Complimentary WiFi (21 hot-spots throughout facility).  
-Large and generously equipped catering kitchen.  
-Eco-friendly features, including intelligent windows.  
-Conveniently located at I-75 and I-40 (just 3 miles west of Farragut) with ample free parking.
