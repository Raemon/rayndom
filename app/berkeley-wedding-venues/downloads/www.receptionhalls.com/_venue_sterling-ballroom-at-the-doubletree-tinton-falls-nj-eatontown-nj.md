# Sterling Ballroom at the DoubleTree Tinton Falls NJ Eatontown, New Jersey

At the Sterling Ballroom of the DoubleTree Tinton Falls, NJ, we want to make your next occasion a flawless event. We are proud to be a top Monmouth County facility for weddings, bar mitzvahs/bat mitzvahs, sweet 16's, meetings, and more! We have everything you need at Sterling Ballroom. We can host up to 225 guests with an open dance floor. Hold your outdoor ceremony under our picturesque white pergola flanked by greenery or have an indoor ceremony beneath the warm glow of our sparkling chandeliers. Your guests will appreciate the convenience of staying on-premises for your ceremony, cocktail hour, reception, and overnight accommodations (plus any extras like an after party or brunch with the newlyweds) at the DoubleTree by Hilton of Tinton Falls, NJ. With over 8,4000 square feet of meeting space, an experienced, knowledgeable staff, and numerous catering options to fit your needs and budget, we will always make sure your meeting/events make an impact! For more information on all our catering options, visit our website or call 732-544-9305 today!
