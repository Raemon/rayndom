# The Brunswick Hotel and Tavern Brunswick, Maine

![Photo of The Brunswick Hotel and Tavern](/media/ME/12466/the-brunswick-hotel-and-tavern-brunswick-me.jpg)

The Brunswick Hotel and Tavern is able accommodate weddings up to 120 people in our Robinson Room or outdoor patio. With spectacular service and comfortable accomodations, we aim to provide the most memorable location for your special day in Maine.  
  
Our mission is to create your perfect day through our chef's culinary delights and our staff's gracious service amidst the natural splendor of Midcoast, Maine.  
  
If a special presentation is a part of your celebration, the Inn is ready to accommodate your technology needs with dropdown screens, LCDs, a fully integrated sound system, and complimentary high-speed Internet, both wired and wireless.
