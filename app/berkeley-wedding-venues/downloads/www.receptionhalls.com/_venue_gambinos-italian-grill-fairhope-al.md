# Gambino's Italian Grill Fairhope, Alabama

The Bayview Room at Gambino's Italian Grill over looks beautiful Mobile Bay and seats around 80. The Bayview Room has been a popular choice for brides for the Rehearsal Dinners and Receptions. It is available Tue-Sat for parties over 40 (unless other arrangements have been made). These parties will have a custom designed set menu for your guests.   
  
Contact our Events Coordinator, Nicole Gambino for more information. nicolegambinoevents@gmail.com or 251-367-6634.
