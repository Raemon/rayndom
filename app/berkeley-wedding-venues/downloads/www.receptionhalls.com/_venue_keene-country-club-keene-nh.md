# Keene Country Club Keene, New Hampshire

The Keene Country Club invites you to spend your special day with us. Our award winning banquet facility has much to offer, including a spacious ballroom with a large fireplace that will serve as a charming backdrop for your wedding, but you also get the Card Room, which provides a great place to hold your guest book, place cards or any photos you might wish to share. You could even use it as candy station! The Card Room leads into the Inside Porch, which is perfect for cocktail hour and both rooms have fireplaces to add warmth and charm during the cold months. During the warmer months, you have use of our fully roofed screened in porch, which can accommodate weddings for up to 140 guests with a view of the golf course. You can enjoy the feeling of the outdoors without worrying about the weather. When you book a wedding with us, it is our pleasure to offer the bride and groom a free foursome of golf to be enjoyed the weekend of the wedding! Whatever you decide you want on your wedding day, our friendly professional staff will do what they can to help ensure your wedding day can be the perfect day of which you’ve dreamed.
