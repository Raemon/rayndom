# The Brennan House Louisville, Kentucky

![Photo of The Brennan House](/media/KY/11352/the-brennan-house-louisville-ky.jpg)

Louisville Kentucky’s dream wedding venue is perfect for your celebration, party and more. The Brennan House museum is one amongst old Louisville’s most cherished and famous historical monuments. The Brennan House was planned by tobacco wholesaler Francis Slaughter Jones Ronald and purchased in 1884 by Thomas Brennan.
