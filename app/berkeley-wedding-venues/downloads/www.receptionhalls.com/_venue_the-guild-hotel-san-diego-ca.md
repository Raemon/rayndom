# The Guild Hotel San Diego, California

The Guild Hotel is a Marriott Tribute Portfolio property located in San Diego, California, and offers a unique and stylish wedding venue space. The hotel itself is housed in a historic building that was originally constructed in 1924, and has since been transformed into a modern and chic destination that seamlessly blends old-world charm with contemporary design.  
  
For weddings, the Guild Hotel offers a variety of event spaces that can accommodate both intimate gatherings and larger celebrations. The most popular venue is the Grace Ballroom, which is an elegant and spacious room with beautiful crystal chandeliers and high ceilings. This ballroom can accommodate up to 200 guests and features large windows that let in plenty of natural light, adding to the ambiance of the space.  
  
In addition to the Grace Ballroom, the Guild Hotel also offers a variety of other event spaces that can be used for weddings, including the beautiful outdoor Poppy. The hotel's on-site catering team can provide a range of delicious food and beverage options to suit your taste and budget, and there are also many nearby attractions and activities for your guests to enjoy during their stay in San Diego.
