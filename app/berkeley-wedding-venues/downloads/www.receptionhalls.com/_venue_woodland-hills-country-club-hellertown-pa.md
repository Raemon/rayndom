# Woodland Hills Country Club Hellertown, Pennsylvania

[ Please Call ](tel:610-838-7192)

Picture your event held in our gracious brick country club, take in the views from our atrium, overlooking the championship golf course here in the stunning Pennsylvania countryside. Savor our fine cuisine as you delight in our impeccable service.  
  
Woodland Hills Country Club offers an exquisite location for your Wedding, Banquet, or other special event. Our expert planners can help you choose from one of our complete Wedding Packages or work with you to create the unique dinning experience you always dreamed of.  
  
Woodland Hills Country Club also has an outdoor pavilion and and NEW Gazebo for your special day. The Gazebo is available for pictures and wedding ceremonies.
