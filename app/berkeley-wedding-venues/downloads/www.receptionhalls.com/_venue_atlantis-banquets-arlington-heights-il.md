# Atlantis Banquets Arlington Heights, Illinois

**PLEASE SEE OUR WEBSITE FOR THE MOST ACCURATE MAP AND DIRECTIONS**  
  
Established in 1976, we provide courteous, efficient service and fine food for any occasion. Our goal is to make your event as wonderful as you imagine it!  
  
We welcome you into Atlantis Banquets to see everything our venue has to offer. In addition to a beautiful and very clean facility, expert set up, pretty china, and fantastic food, our expertise and experience cannot be beat. We will work with you from the very beginning to the very end to add that professional flare to your event that's sure to impress, and allow you to sit back, relax, and enjoy, whether you're hosting an event for 30 or 600!  
  
Our facility is spacious inside and out, with ample seating for up to 600 guests, and plenty of parking (1000 parking spaces available!). Our banquet hall features a lobby, coat check, bride's and groom's rooms, two bars, two stages, 14 foot ceilings, chandeliers, and a huge marble dance floor!  
  
Additionally we offer a wide variety of menu options, and room and seating arrangements to help customize your event. We hope that you will share your next event with us!  
  
To view our menus and pictures, please visit our website: www.atlantisbanquets.com.  
  
Call us to set up your personal consultation, or just drop in during our office hours to see our facilities. We are happy to offer our expertise to make your event everything you'd like it to be and invite you to see our beautiful banquet hall!  
  
Ask us about where you can get a deal nearby for guests that need a hotel for the night, including free shuttle service!  
  
Thank you!
