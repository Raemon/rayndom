# Golden Village Palms Hemet, California

Our spacious ballroom can seat 650 persons comfortably. Our grand clubhouse is the life of any occasion featuring a fully equipped activity rooms, and more. We have hosted holiday gatherings, tribute concerts, and everything in between.  
  
Enjoy your private party around the lounge fire pit while sipping on select wines from the bar. The open-air yet sophisticated garden space is ideal for your next exclusive event.  
  
Add a little depth to your festivities. 3 free-form pools are perfect for birthdays, bachelor parties, and more. Live performances, drinks, and cabana pads will make this an evening to remember.
