# Berkeley City Club Berkeley, California

![Photo of Berkeley City Club](/media/CA/11158/berkeley-city-club-berkeley-ca-94704.jpg)

HOST YOUR MEETING OR EVENT at the Berkeley City Club offering finely-attuned service, impeccable cuisine, and a stunning array of unique rooms and gardens for intimate gatherings or large conferences. Celebrate your event in the stately Member’s Lounge, our elegant Ballroom, or the lively and colorful Terrace. Our event rooms flow together nicely, if multiple areas are chosen for an event. Let our events team assist you in creating a memorable occasion that is sure to leave you and your guests impressed. Julia Morgan carefully designed each space to offer ease of service in a beautiful and unique atmosphere.
