# 75th Street Events Kansas City, Missouri

75th Street Events, nestled in the heart of Waldo is a stunning place to create special occasions for those beginning their journey together.   
  
The main space of the venue is a blank canvas to bring your dreamy ideas to life. The couple and their guests can enjoy Novella's Drawing Room, an outside deck with furniture, and we offer a prep area for the caterers. The Suite fits right into the neighborhood. You and your party will have plenty of gorgeous space to get ready with 400 sq. ft with a private entrance and deck.   
  
75th Street Events also offers 3 small wedding packages; an elopement, wedding up to 30 guests or small wedding up to 50 guests.
