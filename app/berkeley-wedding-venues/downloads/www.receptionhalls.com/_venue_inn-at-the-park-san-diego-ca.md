# Inn at the Park San Diego, California

Create an unforgettable beginning to your life together amid the history, romance, and splendor of The Inn at the Park Hotel – with a entire floor that is exquisitely yours on your special day.  
  
Experience the exclusivity and elegance that truly distinguishes San Diego weddings, commitment ceremonies and special celebration events. Since 1926, The Inn at the Park Hotels has enchanted visitors with soaring 1920?s architecture, gracious hospitality, and our fashionable, yet trendy urban setting alongside lush Balboa Park. Today, this inspiring San Diego wedding reception site is one of the city’s premier spots for celebration. Stirring settings, such as the stunning Top of the Park Penthouse, are awash in the playful sophistication of the Southern California coast. A private rooftop terrace offers breathtaking views of the San Diego skyline. Blending inspiring surroundings with refined service, the Inn at the Park Hotel strikes the perfect tone for your special day.
