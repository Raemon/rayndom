# Bakersfield Museum of Art Bakersfield, California

The Museum provides a unique setting for any occasion. Located in Mill Creek/Central Park in downtown Bakersfield, the Bakersfield Museum of Art has ample parking available in the parking lot and on side streets. Please call Adam Schwartz at (661) 323-7219 to make an appointment to visit the Museum.
