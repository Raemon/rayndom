# Orpheum Theatre Sioux City, Iowa

The historic Orpheum Theatre is the perfect spot for your big day! Known as one of Sioux City's gems, the theatre was originally built in 1927 and was brought back to it's original glory in 2001. With ceremony and reception options in the theatre or the lobby, our team of experienced professionals will make sure that your wedding is the fairy tale that you've always dreamed of! Call or email Valerie in the business office at 712-258-9164 ext. 3 or valerie@orpheumlive.com for more information.
