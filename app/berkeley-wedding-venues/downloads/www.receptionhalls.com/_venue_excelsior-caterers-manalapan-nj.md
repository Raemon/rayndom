# Excelsior Caterers Manalapan, New Jersey

Memories to cherish for a lifetime. All of your dreams for that special day come true at the Excelsior.  
  
Your guests arrive beneath a canopy where our valets greet and direct them toward a majestic Grand Foyer of shimmering lights and polished brass. As they ascend our breathtaking circular staircase, the magic awaits! Upstairs, a spectacular glass arched atrium provides the perfect ambiance for cocktails and hors d'oeuvres. Two elegant ballrooms complete the fantasy with opulent chandeliers, giant dance floors, and stunning table settings that promise you and your guests a truly memorable affair.  
  
From the canopy that leads you to our majestic grand foyer...to our breathtaking circular staircase...to our spectacular glass arched atrium and two elegant ballrooms...to our gourmet menus and attention to every detail; the Excelsior exceeds your expectations and affirms your good taste.
