# Michael's Eighth Avenue Glen Burnie, Maryland

![Photo of Michael's Eighth Avenue](/media/MD/11443/michaels-eighth-avenue-glen-burnie-md.jpg)

Michael's Eighth Avenue is a beautiful venue located conveniently located between Baltimore and Annapolis. We offer a variety of food options, as well as many all inclusive packages. We also have a breathtaking outdoor garden for ceremonies and cocktail hour, as well as a space for small, intimate receptions. Call our office, 410.768.7901 for more information and to schedule a tour today!
