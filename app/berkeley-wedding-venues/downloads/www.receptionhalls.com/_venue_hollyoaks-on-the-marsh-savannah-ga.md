# HollyOaks on the Marsh Savannah, Georgia

HollyOaks on the Marsh is a private estate and historic wedding venue in Savannah GA that hosts up to eight Luxury Weddings + Corporate Events for custom, elevated experiences. Just 15-minutes from downtown Savannah, HollyOaks offers an enchanted, idyllic private estate venue that tourists would never get to experience. HollyOaks on the Marsh hosts experiences that you will want to remember forever, with one timeless goal . . . to bring people together in celebration.
