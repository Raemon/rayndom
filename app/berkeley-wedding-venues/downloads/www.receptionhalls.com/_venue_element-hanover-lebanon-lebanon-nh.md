# Element Hanover - Lebanon Lebanon, New Hampshire

![Photo of Element Hanover - Lebanon](/media/NH/11384/element-hanover-lebanon-lebanon-nh.jpg)

Planning a special event? Whether it's a family reunion or an office retreat, Element Hanover-Lebanon invites you to plan your occasion in our Hanover, NH event spaces. We provide everything you'll need to ensure a productive and energizing environment in our 300 sq. ft. meeting space for your Hanover events and meetings. Our spaces are open and bright, with clean, urban-inspired design and oversized windows, creating a place you can thrive and awaken your senses.   
  
Enjoy modular furnishings, a flexible layout, and well-designed seating that you can customize for both business meetings and social events. With features like state-of-the-art A/V technology, a 50" flat-panel HDTV, all-in-one connectivity station and easy-access power outlets, every presentation is polished and impressive. Plus, keep everyone connected with our white board wall and complimentary wired and wireless high-speed Internet access.
