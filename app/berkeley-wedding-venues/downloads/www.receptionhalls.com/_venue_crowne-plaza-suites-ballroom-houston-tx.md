# Crowne Plaza Suites Ballroom Houston, Texas

Start planning your next event or group travel outing with us! Let the Crowne Plaza Houston Suites help you coordinate your group travel, including meeting planning and room blocks for your guests.  
  
Our Crowne Plaza Suites event planners can help you arrange everything you need from meetings to reunions to weddings, all of which can be hosted onsite in our Beautiful Ballroom.  
The Crowne Plaza Suites Sugar Land is pleased to offer special group rates to both party members as well as out-of-town guests.   
  
Allow your guests to enjoy an overnight stay in one of our plush suites and take advantage of our Atrium Restaurant and Lounge, heated indoor pool and whirpool.
