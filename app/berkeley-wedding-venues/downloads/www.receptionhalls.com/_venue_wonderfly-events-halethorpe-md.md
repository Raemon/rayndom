# Wonderfly Events Halethorpe, Maryland

We Specialize in Unforgettable Events  
  
We're committed to offering all our guests a one-of-a-kind experience every single time! We pride ourselves in offering an assortment of high-quality amenities designed to make your Event easy to plan, and a dream to experience.  
  
What's Included:  
  
. Space: Up to 11,000 square feet of bookable event space!  
. Free Parking: Our parking lot can accommodate up to 75 cars  
. Tables and Chairs for up to 100 guests (additional can be rented from a vendor)  
. Music and PA System  
. Kitchen Space Included: Two refrigerators/freezers, a microwave, and a sink  
. Dedicated Event Managers/Staff: To help make sure everything runs smoothly!  
  
Located right off 695 in Baltimore County and just minutes from downtown Baltimore and Howard County, Wonderfly Events is easily accessible from any direction. Submit an online request or call us to begin your Wonderfly experience today!
