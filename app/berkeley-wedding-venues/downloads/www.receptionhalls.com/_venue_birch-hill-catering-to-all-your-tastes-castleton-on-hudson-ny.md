# Birch Hill "Catering to all your tastes" Castleton On Hudson, New York

Down a country lane, just fifteen minutes from Albany, is a destination where dreams come true. Just off I-90, in the rolling, historic Dutch pastures and woodlands of Rensselaer County,a destination where elegance and casual fun co-mingle.  
  
Birch Hill exudes the casual elegance you seek for your most treasured times. From our spacious ballrooms to the outdoor pavilions, lawns, glades, and terraces, Our 110+ acres of Birch Hill invite you to celebrate.  
  
The Columbia Ballroom has a soaring cathedral ceiling of redwood, arched beams, A Cloudlike Batiste Cream Ceiling Liner, Golden Chiavari Chairs and a new L.E.D. Color wash to help support your wedding theme, bringing it to a whole new level of elegance, floor-to-ceiling windows and a cozy fireplace. Our Conservatory Ballroom has a beautiful glass rooftop, arched windows, and mirrors and, is perfect for an unforgettable background!   
  
Envision cocktails and Hors D’oeuvres on our garden terrace, or a casual reception at our Sunset Pavilion. We have the perfect locations for your tented wedding Nestled in the Pines, or set by the Water’s Edge. The Historic Stable Gate Barn and Vineyards, The Meadows Adirondack Ballroom,The Berkshire Ballroom at Comfort Inn and Suites and the Ballrooms at Sixty State Street Place are just a sampling of the offerings for you to choose from with Birch Hill as your Caterer.  
  
Whether your wedding ceremony is in front of our candlelit fireplace, Under the Conservatory Or outdoors amidst the giant pine trees, the possibilities at Birch Hill are endless.  
  
However you envision your wedding, our Bridal Consultants will help you create a memorable and magical occasion. Our highly trained staff will be there for you each and every step of the way, from ceremony and introduction details to florals, specialty linens, theme creation, or tent rentals... whatever it takes to make your event simply spectacular!  
  
Birch Hill provides an unsurpassed benefit...our culinary staff!   
With trained Chefs from the Culinary Institute of America, Johnson &amp; Wales and the New England Culinary Institute, our passion, style, standards, and creativity are endless. Our menus are flexible and range in price to fit your budget.  
  
At Birch Hill we have over 81 years and Four generations of experience in making people happy. Call us today, tour our beautiful grounds and meet with one of our Experienced and caring Bridal Consultants.  
  
We look forward to hearing from you soon!
