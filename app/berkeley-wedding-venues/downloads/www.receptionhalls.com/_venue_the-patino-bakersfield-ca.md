# The Patino Bakersfield, California

The Patino offers a banquet room that seats up to 200 guest in banquet room, and with a total 300 capacity if additional barside room seating is needed.   
We allow onsite front lawn wedding ceremony for up to 100 in our gated grounds.   
  
Our doors have been open since 1998 and hope that you will choose The Patino as your wedding and event venue.
