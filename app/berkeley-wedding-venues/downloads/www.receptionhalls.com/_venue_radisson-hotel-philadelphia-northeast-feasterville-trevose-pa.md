# Radisson Hotel Philadelphia Northeast Feasterville Trevose, Pennsylvania

The day you've been dreaming of is approaching, so let the Radisson Hotel Philadelphia Northeast make all of your wedding dreams reality. Capture the beauty and romance of the perfect setting for your most precious day. Enjoy the splendor of our magnificent ballrooms, the personal service of our staff and our marvelous menu creations.   
  
Host your rehearsal dinner in one of our hotel's elegant banquet rooms. You may choose from one of our dinner menus or ask our Catering Team to tailor something specific for your group.  
  
The Radisson Hotel Philadelphia Northeast features a range of package options, from plated meals to buffet dinners. All packages include a five-hour reception, premium open bar, floral centerpieces for your tables, and a deluxe bridal suite for the couple. Each package may be custom tailored to the couple’s unique needs as well.   
  
Farewell brunches at the Radisson Hotel Philadelphia Northeast are affordable and simple to organize, and they are an excellent chance to get your group together and thank them for attending your wedding celebration. Our extensive selection of breakfast buffets is sure to suit any morning palette, and your guests will have ample time to flow in and out as they please to grab a bite to eat before checking out.
