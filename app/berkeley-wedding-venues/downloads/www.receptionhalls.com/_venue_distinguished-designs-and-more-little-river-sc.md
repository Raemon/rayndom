# Distinguished Designs and More Little River, South Carolina

Distinguished Designs and More is a wedding and event venue in Little River, South Carolina. The venue contains a draped ceiling and shear curtain walls. Located in close proximity to North Myrtle Beach. The site can accommodate up to 100 guest and can be decorated to reflect your vision.  
The passionate staff strives to produce a fully customized wedding for each client. This dedicated team can assist with setup, cleanup, coordination, and more to make the process as simple as possible. Additionally, the venue does flowers, decor, and more that best fit their theme. The staff at the venue can supply various rental items to help the day run as smoothly as possible, including a wedding arch, audiovisual equipment, tables and chairs. Furthermore, we provide catering and bar services to keep guests feeling satisfied throughout the day.  
The skilled culinary team at the venue offers a number of versatile menu options for couples to choose from. The staff can provide a variety of services during the celebrations, from lavish breakfast buffets and brunch stations to delicious plated banquets.
