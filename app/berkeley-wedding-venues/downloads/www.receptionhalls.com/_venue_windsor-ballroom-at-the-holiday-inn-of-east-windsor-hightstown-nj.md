# Windsor Ballroom at the Holiday Inn of East Windsor Hightstown, New Jersey

At the Windsor Ballroom at the Holiday Inn East Windsor, we believe in bringing extravagant beauty to each and every event without an extravagant price. Our menus offer an array of choices from the classic to the daringly distinctive. White gloved service and artful execution will add polish to your platinum occasion. We feature a five-hour premium open bar, artfully executed cuisine, chic centerpieces, and indoor and outdoor ceremony space. Add a personal flair to your celebration with Hollywood Lighting, decadent dessert options, an ice sculpture, and more! We are also pleased to offer a complimentary bridal suite for your special day and premier guest rooms at discounted rates for your guests. Call us today at 609-443-8000 x600 to learn all about our packages.
