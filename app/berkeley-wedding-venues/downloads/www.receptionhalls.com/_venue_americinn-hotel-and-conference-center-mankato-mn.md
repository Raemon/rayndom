# AmericInn Hotel & Event Center Mankato, Minnesota

The Perfect Place for Weddings in Mankato, Minnesota  
  
  
Our conference center is the perfect venue for your wedding events with a capacity of 250 people. We can do it all from bridal showers, bachelor parties, grooms dinners, ceremonies, receptions and gift openings.   
  
The AmericInn Hotel &amp; Conference Center serves as a one-stop shop for vendors, offering assistance with décor, entertainment providers, florists, and more.  
  
Special wedding room rates are available for all of your out of town guests or in town family and friends that will be attending your wedding. Signature honeymoon suites are also available for your special night. Discounted rates may apply for the wedding blocks including a free whirlpool suite for the bride and groom with a block of 25 rooms held and paid. Whirlpool and fireplace suites are also available.
