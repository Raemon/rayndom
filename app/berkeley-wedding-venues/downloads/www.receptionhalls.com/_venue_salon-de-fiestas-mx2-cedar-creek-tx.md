# Salón de Fiestas MX2 Cedar Creek, Texas

Salón De Fiestas MX-2 is an Event Center, that is affordable & popular venue to hold a Wedding, Quinceanera, Baby Shower, Engagement Party or even a business meeting. Located near Bastrop, Tx off of Highway 71. The Salon De Fiestas MX-2 is a charming venue features an extremely flexible design able to accommodate 230 attendees at a fraction of the price of many other facilities. Available Monday thru Sunday from 8am to 1am. You will have access to the venue an ENTIRE EXTRA day to decorate and make your vision come to life! We are affordable & flexible for any event.
