# Stoney Creek Hotel & Conference Center Saint Joseph, Missouri

For the past decade, the Stoney Creek Hotel &amp; Conference Center has taken pride in executing graceful and elegant weddings, receptions, family reunions and many other events for 15-350 people.   
  
Our staff prides itself on taking the time and care to set up our Frontier Ballroom to your exact specifications, leaving you to enjoy your memorable day.   
  
We offer four select caterers offering everything from home-cooking to fine-dining.   
  
Connected to our 10,000 square foot conference center is our 129 room hotel offering suites, bunk-bed rooms, and other guest rooms.   
  
Just off I-29, we're located and driven to be the perfect place to make new memories or commemorate your special occasion.
