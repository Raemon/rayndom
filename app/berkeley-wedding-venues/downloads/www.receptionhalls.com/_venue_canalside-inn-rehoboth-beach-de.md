# Canalside Inn Rehoboth Beach, Delaware

There’s no better place than the Nation’s Summer Capital to host your next event, family reunion, wedding, bachelorette party, fundraiser, or corporate celebration. Canalside Inn is happy to help you and your family, group or team organize the perfect party, conference or special event or corporate retreat. Canalside offers 600 square feet of indoor event space, and over 3000 square feet of outdoor space, more than enough to host an executive corporate group, an intimate well-being wedding, family reunion, or outdoor dinner party!

Our Venue: Canalside Inn boasts over 3,000 total square feet of outdoor space to host your event. Perfect for an intimate wedding of up to 100 guests right along the picturesque Rehoboth-Lewes Canal. Host your wedding reception, rehearsal dinner, or bridal brunch on site and provide lodging accommodations for your bridal party. 13 rooms sleep 30-36 guests. Location: We're located 3/4 of a mile from the beach at the start of Rehoboth Avenue. Perfect walking distance to all of Rehoboth Beach's downtown restaurants, shops, and bars. Food and Beverage: All food and beverages services must be contracted through Greater Good Events of DE unless otherwise agreed upon. Clients work directly with Greater Good Events to determine menu and beverage service. Transportation: Ask about our beach shuttle seats up to 16 guests at a time.
