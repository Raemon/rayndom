# Bensalem Country Club Bensalem, Pennsylvania

The Rose Room is the Jewel of Bensalem Country Club. Our elegantly appointed room accommodates groups up to 200 and overlooks our lush and beautifully manicured Golf Course.  
  
Adjoining The Rose Room is a lovely canopied veranda where your guests might enjoy a cocktail or just take in the beautiful view.   
Our picturesque grounds are the perfect setting for your Wedding Ceremony or Wedding Photography. Every detail is meticulously attended to by our experienced Wedding and Party Planning Staff.  
  
Your Wedding or Special Event is very important to us.
