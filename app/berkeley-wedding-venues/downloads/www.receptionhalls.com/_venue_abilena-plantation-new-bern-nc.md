# Abilena Plantation New Bern, North Carolina

All Abilena Plantation weddings include....  
  
You get your own private mansion for the weekend!  
?  
Historic three story riverfront southern mansion built in 1927. Over 5300 square foot home and all 5 acres are yours for the weekend!  
250' wide waterfront property with amazing views of the mile wide Neuse River!!!  
Private waterfront ceremony site that is perfectly suited for weddings with its own private beach.  
The original Carriage House with a 1600 square foot Pavilion for weddings, receptions, and rehearsal dinners. The Pavilion can be enclosed with white sides for weddings all year long!!  
  
Rental of the entire home, unlike a Bed &amp; Breakfast, you will not be sharing the home with other people during your stay  
26x40 in ground private pool!   
8 person in ground Jacuzzi tub with a river view!  
Bath towels and linens provided.  
Full kitchen with coffee maker, refrigerator, stove, dishes and silverware  
Formal Dining room that seats 8, but can be used for any purpose needed and the table can be moved if needed.  
7 Queen beds, sleeps up to 14 people  
3 full baths on the second floor and a half bath on the first and third floor.   
Bath house outside with two bathrooms for wedding guests and for pool parties.  
Cable TV/VCR/DVD  
Wireless High Speed Internet Service  
off street parking for up to 200 people!  
Stainless Steel gas grill for the guests  
Private room just for the Wedding Dress!!  
Salon with two chairs for hair and make-up!!  
  
  
(15) 60 inch round tables seat 120, (4) 6 foot rectangle tables (4) pub tables  
120 White chairs for the reception  
White benches for the ceremony that seat 120 people  
Guys get ready in the third floor game room with poker table, pool table, flat screen TV/ DVD. Wii in the 3rd floor game room, bring your games!!  
  
Weddings larger than 120 are an option with the addition of a rental tent.
