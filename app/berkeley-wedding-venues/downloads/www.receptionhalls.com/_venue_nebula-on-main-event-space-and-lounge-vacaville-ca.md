# Nebula on Main | Event Space and Lounge Vacaville, California

Nebula on Main in Vacaville offers a unique colored venue with thousands of LED lights that can be programmed any color to match your color scheme. Large LED video screens throughout venue to capture your best videos and pictures along with a concert quality sound system. BEAUTIFUL gold decor and modern bathrooms. Large patio that can accommodate a outdoor reception and gathering.
