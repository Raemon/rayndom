# Inn on the Square Greenwood, South Carolina

![Photo of Inn on the Square](/media/SC/11843/inn-on-the-square-greenwood-sc-29646.jpg)

Remaining faithful to the original architecture and the historical integrity, the Inn on the Square has been carefully renovated to preserve its classic style and offer guests the ultimate in comfort.  
  
Conveniently located in beautiful Uptown Greenwood, South Carolina, the Inn on the Square offers exceptional hospitality in an inviting and welcoming atmosphere. Our modern amenities provide our guests and visitors with everything they need for an enjoyable stay: full-service restaurant, casual bar/lounge, comfortable and beautifully decorated guest rooms with en suite bathrooms, complimentary breakfast, attentive staff and impressive event venues.  
  
The Inn on the Square in Uptown Greenwood offers you gracious, southern hospitality and luxury amenities combined with your choice of our large ballroom setting or uniquely decorated outdoor courtyard or both. Our guest rooms present the welcoming hub for your out-of-town guests and wedding party. Catering is provided by our highly trained culinary team. From weddings to corporate events, make the Inn on the Square your go-to catering place. We cater off site as well.  
  
By choosing the Inn on the Square’s Catering team for your celebration, you can breathe easy in the knowledge that our attentive staff will provide personalized service to make your special day an unforgettable experience.
