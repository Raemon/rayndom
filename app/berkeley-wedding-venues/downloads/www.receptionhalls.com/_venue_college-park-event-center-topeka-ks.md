# College Park Event Center Topeka, Kansas

Let College Park Event Center be your next location to host an event in Topeka. We offer a great place to have your weekly classes or seminars in a central location just 4 blocks west of Washburn University.  
The event center is ideal for parties of 12-48 guests. We are equipped with a kitchen to accommodate your party, reception, bridal/baby shower, reception, get together as well as any event needed.  
Most packages come with full computer, video projector, white board, podium w/mic and speaker use. We also offer paper products and utensils for most casual events at no extra charge.  
We offer competitive rates on hourly, half day and full day rentals as well as discounts if needing monthly or weekly functions.  
There are also 2 breakout conference rooms available at an additional fee if needed.  
Please contact us for a tour today.
