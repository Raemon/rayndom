# Belcourt Castle Newport, Rhode Island

[ Please Call ](tel:401-846-0669)

![Photo of Belcourt Castle](/media/RI/190/belcourt-castle-newport-ri.jpg)

Belcourt Castle comes alive for your special occasion! Our full-service event planning is surprisingly affordable and well worth exploring...  
  
Treat friends and family to an extravagant evening at your wedding, or by hosting a business banquet, birthday, reunion, awards celebration, Christmas Party, or other private or corporate event.  
  
A wedding ceremony held in the French Gothic Ballroom is a fairy tale come true.  
  
Visualize tantalizing displays of creative foods on a priceless antique tables or sideboards. Expect elegant glassware and silver, fine china and linens with superb catered service and the finest cuisine to create the kind of ambiance found in European castles or nineteenth century millionaire's residences. Even with a modest budget for catering, the elegance remains.  
The maitre d' announces dinner with an antique Chinese bell. The Italian Banquet Hall has been set most elegantly - according to your special requirements. Champagne or wines are carefully poured for each guest as the courses from appetizer to dessert flow smoothly to the meal's conclusion. The experience is made so much more perfect when there is fabulous entertainment.  
After dinner dancing to a fine band or professional Disc Jockey in the Ballroom or Banquet Hall, cordial service in the English Library, your awards ceremony, the Birthday cake, a short recital or choral concert, guest speaker of your choosing can be added to extend the evening.
