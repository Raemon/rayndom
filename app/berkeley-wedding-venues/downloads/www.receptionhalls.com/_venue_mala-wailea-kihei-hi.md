# Mala Wailea Kihei, Hawaii

Located right on the water in the beautiful Marriott Resort and Spa in Wailea, Maui, our restaurant can accommodate groups from a small intimate setting up to a gathering of 120 guests. With breathtaking sunset views, the spirit of aloha, and delicious food created by Chef Mark Ellman and prepared by Top Chef Finalist Chef Sheldon Simeon, Mala Wailea is the perfect place for your destination wedding reception!
