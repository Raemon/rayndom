# Wilder Center Wilder, Vermont

The former Wilder Congregational Church underwent a full restoration in 2010 and has been transformed into Wilder Center, the Upper Valley's premier event space for weddings, concerts, gala dinners, and private parties. A stunning combination of 19th century architecture and 21st century technology, Wilder Center is the perfect location for your next event.
