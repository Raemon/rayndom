# Wedgewood Sterling Hills Camarillo, California

Nestled in hills overlooking Camarillo is one of the best outdoor wedding venues in Ventura County. Wedgewood Wedding and Banquet Center at Sterling Hills Golf Club is your choice for a stunning Camarillo wedding site that comes at a fraction of the price you would anticipate.  
  
Some of the spectacular, unique features of our Camarillo wedding location:  
  
-Picturesque wedding site with the Topa Topa Mountains in the background  
-Gazebo overlooking the golf course  
-Up to 225 seated guests  
-Panoramic banquet room views of the first and ninth fairways  
-Spanish style clubhouse with dramatic landscaping
