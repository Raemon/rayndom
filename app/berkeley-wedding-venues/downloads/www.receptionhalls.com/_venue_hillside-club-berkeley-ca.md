# Hillside Club Berkeley, California

The Hillside Club is ideal for private party rentals, celebrations, conferences, meetings, weddings, exhibitions, memorials, lectures, concerts, ceremonies, formal and informal events.   
  
The approximately 2000 square-foot hall can accommodate up to 200 people and 125 people for seated dining. It is available year-round.  
  
The Hillside Club is a 1920's Historic Maybeck building.
