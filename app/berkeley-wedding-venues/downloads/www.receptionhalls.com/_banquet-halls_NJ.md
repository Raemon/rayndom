New Jersey is a great place for your wedding reception. Just across the Hudson river, you'll find breathtaking views of the New York City skyline, many beautiful outdoor venues, country clubs and inns. You'll also find some beautiful and budget-friendly banquet halls throughout New Jersey. If you are looking for glitz and glamor, be sure to check out Atlantic City.
