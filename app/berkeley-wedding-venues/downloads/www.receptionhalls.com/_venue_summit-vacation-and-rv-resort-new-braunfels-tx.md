# Summit Vacation and RV Resort New Braunfels, Texas

The Summit Vacation and RV Resort located in New Braunfels, Texas off scenic River Road is the perfect place to say, “I Do”. Being situated close to Canyon Lake and along the breathtaking banks of the Guadalupe River, we offer the ideal setting for a wedding ceremony. The Summit offers a River-side chapel for the nuptials and a brand new 2000 sq. ft. slate river front patio for an intimate reception/gathering/ceremony.   
Additionally, The Summit offers our newly renovated clubhouse and outdoor covered pavilion which holds up to 150 guests leaving ample room for a DJ of your choice and a dance floor. The wedding party may use the Summit’s full-size kitchen or bring in your own catering both at no additional cost. We also work with area caterers if needed.  
Need a place for your guest to stay? We offer 30 cabins, 13 Tree Top Villas facing the Texas Hill Country and tree covered RV sites for family and friends to stay in. If you and your guest decide to “make it” a weekend at the Summit, we offer endless amenities to enjoy which include, 3 swimming pools, mini putt-putt, sand volley ball, horseshoes, tube service for floating the Guadalupe river, tennis, shuffleboard and the list goes on. The Summit offers several different wedding packages suitable for every budget.
