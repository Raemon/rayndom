# Kah-Nee-Ta Hot Springs Resort Warm Springs, Oregon

Welcome to Kah-Nee-Ta Hot Springs Resort – A Prized Resort of Sacred Land, Healing Waters, Unforgettable Gatherings.  
  
At Kah-Nee-Ta, we believe in more than just hospitality – WE CARE. Located on the sacred land of the Warm Springs Reservation, our resort is a place of healing, connection, and renewal, where our hot springs pools have restored body and spirit.  
  
Kah-Nee-Ta offers a variety of group-friendly lodging options, from cozy hotel rooms and a spacious river house to full hook-up RV sites and our signature teepees – all designed to provide comfort and a sense of place in the high desert landscape.  
  
Guests can relax in our healing mineral pools and enjoy a wide range of on-site recreation, including sports, games, seasonal river floats, and horseback riding – perfect for downtime or team bonding.  
  
Depending on the season, we can accommodate everything from intimate retreats to large-scale events, all surrounded by natural beauty and guided by our commitment to care, culture, and community.  
  
Let us help you create a group experience that’s rooted in tradition and rich with unforgettable moments.
