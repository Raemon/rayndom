# Café Postale Omaha, Nebraska

Café Postale is located in the Rail & Commerce Building nestled in Omaha’s Little Italy neighborhood. Located just steps from the Old Market historic district, the space blends modern concepts with reimagined historic elements that nod to the building’s past as a postal railway transport hub.   
  
The careful preservation of historic features like the vintage vault door impart a sophisticated ambience to the venue. Featuring a flexible café space and brick-lined terrace, Café Postale offers the opportunity to host a variety of events including corporate retreats, intimate weddings and milestone celebrations.   
  
Let us help you weave the intersecting stories of our space with your special event!
