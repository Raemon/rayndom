# The White House Beach Club Ventura, California

The White House Beach Club was just remodeled and tastefully decorated to accommodate all your event needs. A cozy and reconfigurable indoor space with a full size bar, lounge areas, attached bathrooms and seating for up to 100. Outside is perfect for dinner, ceremony or just to mingle with an outdoor fireplace, chandeliers and a stage with seating for many more. We will accommodate any size event from hour long meetings to all day parties. Onsite are two fully furnished apartments loaded with amenities that are available for temporary stays. We also have many catering options from one of our amazing restaurants or you can even provide your own. Dedicated and talented staff will see to your needs and exceed your expectations.
