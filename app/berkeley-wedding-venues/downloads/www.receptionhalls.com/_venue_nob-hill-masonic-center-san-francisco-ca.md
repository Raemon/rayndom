# Nob Hill Masonic Center San Francisco, California

In the City by the Bay, America’s favorite destination for meetings and conventions, there is a special neighborhood that presents an awe-inspiring picture of historic San Francisco – illustrious Nob Hill. Surrounded by world class hotels that have a blend of elegance and charm, the Nob Hill Masonic Center has been a part of San Francisco’s culture for over 50 years. Conveniently located just blocks away from downtown San Francisco, the magnificent Nob Hill Masonic Center offers the ultimate in convenience, with flexible meetings and exhibition space  
along with excellent support services. Whatever the event, large or small, business or social, our staff will work with you to provide the personal attention necessary to ensure its success whether it is 50 to 3,000 people.
