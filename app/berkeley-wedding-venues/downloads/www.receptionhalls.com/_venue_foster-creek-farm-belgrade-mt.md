# Foster Creek Farm Belgrade, Montana

With views of four mountain ranges, Foster Creek Farm offers a tranquil, private setting for your special event. A 1923 barn is the focus of our venue. Newly remodeled, the barn offers a fireplace, bathrooms, full kitchen and deck. Along with the barn, Foster Creek Farm includes a Bridal Suite and Groom’s Cabin… Glamping tents… And the Huffine House, the original farmhouse complete with three bedrooms and two baths. At Foster Creek Farm we strive to create a serene and peaceful setting for your event.
