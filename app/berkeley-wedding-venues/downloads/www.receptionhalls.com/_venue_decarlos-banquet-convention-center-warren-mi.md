# DeCarlo's Banquet & Convention Center Warren, Michigan

![Photo of DeCarlo's Banquet & Convention Center](/media/MI/9783/decarlos-banquet-convention-center-warren-mi.jpg)

DeCarlo's is one of the finest full catering banquet and convention centers in Southeast Michigan. This is a family owned and operated business that was established in 1969. DeCarlo's houses seven beautifully renovated rooms on two levels (with elevator accessibility)  
that can accommodate anywhere from 35 - 1425 people.
