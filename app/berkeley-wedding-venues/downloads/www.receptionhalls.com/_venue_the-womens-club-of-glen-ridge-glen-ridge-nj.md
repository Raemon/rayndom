# The Women's Club of Glen Ridge Glen Ridge, New Jersey

The Women's Club of Glen Ridge offers a unique and charming venue for your wedding or private party. Located in historic, tree-lined Glen Ridge, New Jersey, the Club is convenient to all major highways and public transportation.  
  
Built in 1925, our beautiful Georgian Colonial Clubhouse is a unique alternative to conventional wedding sites and large impersonal catering halls. It provides the perfect combination of intimate space and ballroom style seating to accommodate all of your guests.  
  
Affairs at the Women's Club are hosted one event at a time to provide clients with the ultimate in privacy. Clients select their own caterers and florists, so arrangements are always exactly to your liking.
