# Hollywood Sports Banquet Hall Bellflower, California

Got any upcoming Company events? Weddings? School Functions? Or just anywhere you need a wonderful space for your celebration?  
  
Here at Hollywood Sports Park we not only offer fun in an extreme sense but we also offer a BEAUTIFUL Banquet hall perfect for any form of event or celebration!  
  
Have a specific budget? NO PROBLEM! When working with our amazing sales team we know you'll be ecstatic with the help and budgeting we can offer you to make your event the BEST!  
  
Some of our Benefits include:  
-Exquisite décor showered in burgundy's and gold's with filigree accents.  
  
-Downstairs capacity: sit-down is 200 and upstairs capacity is 50. (Except with functions or events that need a dancing area, downstairs capacity is 150.)  
  
-Rooms are available for viewing 7 days a week. Appointments can be made with our event coordinator.  
  
-Rental room service includes: set-up, clean-up, tables and chairs and table cloths.  
  
-Deposits are non-refundable and are applied to your event. A deposit will reserve the date and space. Entire balance is due one week prior to event. We accept cash, cashier's check or credit card (VISA and Mastercard only).
