# Majors Estate Smoky Mountain Lodging and Events Venue Clyde, North Carolina

Envision your wedding here at Majors Estate, an idyllic private mountain ranch where country elegance meets rustic charm. In the heart of the Smoky Mountains, 200+ acres are nestled in the scenic woodland trails. Our exquisite riverfront surroundings offer the perfect setting for couples looking to treat their guests to a memorable wedding that showcases the natural beauty of North Carolina. Whether you are looking for an intimate ceremony in our traditional white chapel or an over-the-top tented or barn celebration, our flexible access to indoor and outdoor locations provides endless options to bring your dream wedding to life!
