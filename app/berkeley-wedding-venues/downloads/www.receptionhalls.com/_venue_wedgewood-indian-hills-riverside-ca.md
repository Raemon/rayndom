# Wedgewood Indian Hills Riverside, California

Centrally located between Riverside, Ontario, and San Bernardino is one of the top outdoor wedding locations in the Inland Empire. Wedgewood Wedding and Banquet Center at Indian Hills Golf Club gives any guest the elegant Riverside wedding you have been searching for, while still giving you the Value, Service and Convenience that is Wedgewood’s promise.  
  
Some of the spectacular, unique features of our Riverside wedding location:  
  
* Brand new garden wedding site with gazebo overlooking the golf course  
* Sunset banquet room with picturesque views of the course and seating for 400  
* Scenic room with seating for 150 for more intimate events, overlooking ceremony site  
* Bride's room comes with creamy white leather couches, mirrors and direct access to a nicely outfitted bathroom with showers and a steamer
