# The King's Courtyard Phoenix, Arizona

The King's Courtyard is a Renaissance/Celtic/Medieval wedding, reception and special event site. We offer reasonably priced all inclusive packages or partial packages for both theme and traditional events. Our site offers an outdoor chapel with indoor and outdoor dining as well as being fully decorated in a Renaissance/Celitc and Medieval them.
