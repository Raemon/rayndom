# LeGrand Center Shelby, North Carolina

Nestled in the rolling piedmont of the southwest region of North Carolina, Cleveland County is a gateway between Asheville and Charlotte, and is at the center of two of the largest metropolitan areas of the Carolinas- Charlotte and Greenville/Spartanburg. LeGrand Center offers a variety of unique ceremony and reception spaces that are sure to delight any bride. Our 20,000 square foot expandable ballroom can easily accommodate your wedding or reception with ease and sophistication.
