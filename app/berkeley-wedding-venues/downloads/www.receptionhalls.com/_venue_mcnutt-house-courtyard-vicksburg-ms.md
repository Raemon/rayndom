# McNutt House - Courtyard Vicksburg, Mississippi

The McNutt House (circa 1826) offers 3 great venues certain to fullfil your event needs from business meeting to party or banquet. The Courtyard offers ample open space and includes a kitchen ideal for you caterer's staging area. The Courtyard features multiple decks, patios, gazebos, water features, walkways and lovely terraced gardens. A large grassed area is well suited for a seated wedding or set up for a banquet or as a picnic area.
