Whether you need a private event venue for an award banquet, family reunion, anniversary party or dream wedding, choosing Tin Roof Events will ensure your event is unforgettable. Located near Dayton in Sidney, Ohio, Tin Roof Events offers you a one of a kind setting for any occasion.
