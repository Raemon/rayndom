# Bing on Broad Memphis, Tennessee

We are a unique venue with a chic vibe in the heart of Memphis, TN. We are the perfect backdrop for your next celebration. We have tables, white chivari chairs, a catering prep area, and a built in bar. From cocktail parties, formal seated dinners, corporate meetings, or celebrations for any occasion; Bing on Broad is the perfect unique venue for up to 99 guests. Our space can be configured to your vision with a separate buffet room, a catering prep area, and a built-in bar.

### Venue Details

Capacity (Seated / Cocktail): **100 /**

We offer an elegant setting with all of the charm and splendor you would want. We offer a distinctive and tasteful setting for receptions, rehearsal dinners, showers, and celebrations you might be planning. Create your own intriguing style, at Bing on Broad Events! Our versatile venue provides an opportunity to celebrate any occasion!

Max Number of Simultaneous Events: **100**

Outside Catering Permitted (BYO): **Yes**

Venue Type/Style: **Banquet Hall, Historical Building**

On-Site Cuisine Options: **No onsite catering available**

### Pricing

Minimim price for 100 guests: **$ 2,000**

Site/Rental Fee: **$2,000**

Parking: We have street parking in front of our building and to the west and east of our building. Catering: Catering services can only be provided by the approved caterers on our list. Caterers and their servers/bussers need to stay onsite during the event. Hours: Events will have 8 hours of access to our facility. Additional hours can be arranged for a fee. All events must end at midnight and personal belongings must be removed at the end of the evening. Decorations: Banners, signs, decorations, etc., are not to be taped, glued or otherwise affixed to walls, floors and/or hung from light fixtures or ceilings. No glitter or confetti are allowed in our facility. All candles must be in enclosed containers. Anything other than tabletop decorations must be approved prior to the event. Sound System: We do not have speakers or any type of sound system at our venue. Deposit: Our deposit is half of the facility fee—it is non-refundable, but is applied towards the balance. The balance is due two weeks prior to your event.
