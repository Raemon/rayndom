## Overview

The North Basin Strip is the portion of the McLaughlin Eastshore State Park located south of the Gilman Sports Fields in Berkeley. It is approximately 20 acres and includes 2,800 linear feet of shoreline. The Park District is embarking on a planning project to develop a preliminary design to improve this portion of the park that will include several opportunities for public engagement.

### Project Goals

**Habitat and Public Access.** Improve habitat and develop public access facilities, consistent with the Eastshore State Park General Plan. 

**Climate Resiliency.** Design for climate resiliency.

**Green Infrastructure.** Use nature-based design solutions where appropriate.

**Actionable.** Ensure improvements are within operational and budgetary constraints of the Park District.
