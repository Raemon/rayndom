Park Rule Violations

**To report an Emergency or Crime/Suspicious Activity in Progress,**  
**call 911 or[(510) 881-1121](tel:1-510-881-1121) from a cell, 24 hours a day.**

To report Non-Emergency Park Rule Violations such as reckless bike/skate/scooter riding, failure to properly leash or control pets, and improper trail usage such as eBikes or mountain bikes on restricted trails, please call our Non-Emergency Public Safety Dispatch at [(510) 881-1833](tel:1-510-881-1833), 24 hours a day, or submit a Park Watch Report online at the link below.

To report non-emergency safety hazards, or damaged trails and facilities, talk to a park ranger or call Public Safety Dispatch at [(510) 881-1833](tel:1-510- 881-1833). Be sure to mention the nature and exact location of the problem including the trail or area name.
