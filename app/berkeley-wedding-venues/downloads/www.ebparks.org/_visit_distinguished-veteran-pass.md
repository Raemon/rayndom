At time of entry to the parks, persons presenting the “** _Distinguished Veteran Pass_** ” issued by California State Parks will receive the following benefits:

  * A free day-use parking pass (at all parks that charge for parking)
  * Active, retired, and former military personnel receive FREE day-use parking, and free entry to Ardenwood Historic Farm admission on Veterans Day, November 11.
  * Free entry to Ardenwood Historic Farm (except during special events).
  * The Regional Parks Foundation offers a [Special Access Membership](https://www.regionalparksfoundation.org/join/benefits) for seniors 62 years or older, disabled persons, veterans, or full-time students. Proof of eligibility required.
  * **NOTE** : This pass is ** _not valid_** for special events.



Honorably discharged war veterans who are residents of California with an overall/combined rating at 50% or greater service-connected disability, or were held as prisoners of war by forces hostile to the United States, or recipients of the Congressional Medal of Honor are eligible to receive the [Distinguished Veteran Pass](https://www.parks.ca.gov/?page_id=29610), formerly the  _Disabled War Veteran/Prisoner of War Pass._

CA State Parks Pass Sales Office  
1416 9thStreet, Room #144  
Sacramento, CA 95814  
Phone: (800) 777-0369 ext. 3  
Internet: [www.parks.ca.gov](https://www.parks.ca.gov/?page_id=29610)

Please note that this lifetime pass was formerly known as the “Disabled Veteran/Prisoner of War Pass,” and that pass is valid for the benefits described in this section. Veterans seeking eligibility must contact California State Parks to obtain the pass.
