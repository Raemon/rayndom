## 

South Park Drive Annual Closure for Newt Migration

**South Park Drive** in Tilden Park will be closed to vehicles from **November 1, 2025** through **March 31, 2026** for the protection of California newts as they utilize the road to migrate during the rainy season. Please adjust your travel plans by using other access routes such as **Shasta Road** or **Golf Course Drive**. **South Park Drive** will remain open to pedestrians and bicyclists. [More Info](/newts).

![ACSP Golf logo for web](/sites/default/files/styles/original_800w_/public/ACSP-golf-certseal-web.png?itok=c1MIKxAF)

The Tilden Park Golf Course [turned 85 in 2022](/about-us/whats-new/news/tilden-golf-course-turns-85). This historic public golf course is located within [Tilden Regional Park](/parks/tilden) on Golf Course Drive. Enter the park from Golf Course Road or Shasta Road off Grizzly Peak Boulevard in Berkeley. Amenities include an 18-hole course with a driving range, practice green, cart rental, pro shop, with the new restaurant and bar, The Overlook. 

This 6,294-yard championship course was designed by William Park Bell Jr. and constructed during the Great Depression by the East Bay Regional Park District, Works Progress Administration (WPA), and Civilian Conservation Corps (CCC) in 1936-37.

In 2010, it was awarded Best Golf Course for Oakland and the East Bay by the SF Guardian’s Best of the Bay. The rolling tree-lined fairways and contoured greens make this golf course exceptional and memorable. The [Tilden Park Golf Course](https://www.tildenparkgc.com/) is the 67th golf course in California and the 1,009th in the world to be designated as a Certified Audubon Cooperative Sanctuary. 

  * Holes: 18
  * Yardage: 6294/5823, 5823/5399
  * Par: 70, 71
  * Accuracy is needed on this mature course (opened in 1937). [More info](https://www.golfcoursetournaments.com/tournament-venues/california/san-francisco-bay-area/tilden-park-golf-course).



View the Calendar for [upcoming events](https://tildenparkgc.com/events-calendar/).

## Features

  * ### Learn to Play Golf Lessons

A variety of lesson programs are available for different skill levels. [View lesson programs](https://tildenparkgc.com/instruction/).  

  * ### The Tilden Tiers

The covered and heated three-tiered driving range makes practicing possible 365 days a year–rain or shine.
  * ### The Overlook

Open for lunch daily. [View menu](https://overlookattilden.com/menu/). 
  * ### Plan a Golf Outing

Tilden Golf Course is an exceptional setting for a round of unforgettable golf, or an outstanding tournament or special event. [Plan a Golf Event or Tournament](https://www.golfcoursetournaments.com/tournament-venues/california/san-francisco-bay-area/tilden-park-golf-course)

  * ### Host Special Events

Ideal for small business dinners, bridal/baby showers, corporate meetings, holiday parties, graduation parties, fundraising events, and other special occasions. [Plan a Private Event](https://www.golfcoursetournaments.com/tournament-venues/california/san-francisco-bay-area/tilden-park-golf-course)
