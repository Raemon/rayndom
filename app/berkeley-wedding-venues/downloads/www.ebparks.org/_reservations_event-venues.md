Reservations are currently booking through December of 2026. Reservations for 2027 will open on Wednesday, January 14, 2026 at 9 a.m. On opening day, reservations may be made by phone only, walk-ins will not be accepted

For your next event, consider one of our event venues or outdoor spaces set in a natural park environment. Your guests will enjoy the unique historic, artistic, and cultural spaces for all group sizes inside and out. 

Our four event venues are ideal for weddings, celebrations, workshops, and retreats in tranquil parkland and waterfront settings at affordable rates. Our approved caterer and preferred vendor partners complete the package by offering their expertise and experience producing events to help guide you through your event planning journey. Contact our Rental Facilities Office at [[email protected]](/cdn-cgi/l/email-protection#650717041f0c092500071504170e164b0a1702) or (510) 544-3164.

We also have many outdoor picnic sites throughout our parks that are perfect for weddings of all sizes. To make reservations for an outdoor location, call 1-888-EBPARKS ([1-888-327-2757](tel:1-888-327-2757)), press option 2.

If you prefer a completely full service option, check out our event spaces operated by our approved and experienced park partners.
