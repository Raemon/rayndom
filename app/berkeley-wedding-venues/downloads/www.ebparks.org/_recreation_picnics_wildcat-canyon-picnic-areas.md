Hours

The park is open from 5:00am – 10:00pm. Curfew hours are 10:00pm – 5:00am.

**Parking Lot Hours**  
The open hours for the main parking lot gate at Alvarado Staging Area are:  
November - February: 7:30am – 5:00pm  
March - May: 7:30am – 6:00pm  
June - August: 7:30am – 7:00pm  
September - October: 7:30am – 6:00pm

## Venue Activities

Image

![Picnicing](/sites/default/files/2021-10/icon_activity_picnic.svg)

Picnicking

There are FOUR (4) reservable sites at [Wildcat Canyon Regional Park](/parks/wildcat). Beer and wine are allowed without a permit. 

[![Map of Wildcat Canyon Regional Park](/sites/default/files/Wildcat-Canyon-Picnic-Area-Map.png)](https://www.ebparks.org/sites/default/files/wildcatcanyon_map.pdf)

### Acacia Picnic Area (35 person capacity)

Maximum site capacity is 35  
Flush toilets at the site.  
**Parking is very limited.** Please carpool as much as possible.  
This site requires a short walk up a paved hill. The site overlooks the playground.

[Site Info](https://apm.activecommunities.com/ebparks/Facility_Search?facility_id=119) | [Map](/sites/default/files/wildcatcanyon_map.pdf) | [FAQs](https://www.ebparks.org/recreation/picnics) | [Availability](https://apm.activecommunities.com/ebparks/Facility_Search?IsCalendar=true&facility_id=119&year=2023&month=3&startyear=2023&endyear=2023) | [Contact Reservations](https://www.ebparks.org/recreation/picnics/wildcat-canyon-picnic-areas#contact)

### Alvarado Picnic Area (150 person capacity)

Maximum site capacity is 150  
Flush toilets. **Parking is very limited.** Please carpool as much as possible. Shady or Partially Shaded.

**ADA Accessibility:** ADA accessible picnic table(s). The toilets are also ADA accessible.

### Bay Picnic Area (50 person capacity)

Maximum site capacity is 50  
Flush toilets (550ft away). There is a slight incline to get to the site. **Parking is very limited.** Please carpool as much as possible. Full Shade.  
This very shady site is adjacent to a meadow. It is an under-used area just outside the central part of the park and is ideal for weddings. 

[Site Info](https://apm.activecommunities.com/ebparks/Facility_Search?facility_id=122) | [Map](/sites/default/files/wildcatcanyon_map.pdf) | [FAQs](https://www.ebparks.org/recreation/picnics) | [Availability](https://apm.activecommunities.com/ebparks/Facility_Search?IsCalendar=true&facility_id=122&year=2023&month=3&startyear=2023&endyear=2023) | [Contact Reservations](https://www.ebparks.org/recreation/picnics/wildcat-canyon-picnic-areas#contact)

### Elderberry Picnic Area (50 person capacity)

Maximum site capacity is 50  
Flush toilets at the site. Shady or Partially Shaded

The site is next to the Gazebo and lawn in the middle of the park. An ideal place for a small wedding reception. 

**ADA Accessibility:** ADA accessible picnic table(s). The toilets are also ADA accessible.

[Site Info](https://apm.activecommunities.com/ebparks/Facility_Search?facility_id=125) | [Map](/sites/default/files/wildcatcanyon_map.pdf) | [FAQs](https://www.ebparks.org/recreation/picnics) | [Availability](https://apm.activecommunities.com/ebparks/Facility_Search?IsCalendar=true&facility_id=125&year=2023&month=3&startyear=2023&endyear=2023) | [Contact Reservations](https://www.ebparks.org/recreation/picnics/wildcat-canyon-picnic-areas#contact)
