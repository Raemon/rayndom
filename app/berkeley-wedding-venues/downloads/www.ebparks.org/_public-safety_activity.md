# Police & Fire Activity

__Back to top

The East Bay Regional Park District has launched a new system to make disclosable public safety information more accessible to the public.

  * [Access Police and Fire Activity information](https://ebpd.crimegraphics.com/2013/default.aspx) including location, date and time, description and outcome and view details on a near-real-time interactive map
  * [Sign up](https://ebpd.crimegraphics.com/) for daily or weekly email updates
  * View crime information for the East Bay Regional Park District



The system is provided by Sun Ridge Systems, Inc. - Citizen RIMS and housed on [Crimegraphics.com](https://crimegraphics.com/), which integrates with the Park District’s existing Public Safety Computer Aided Dispatch (CAD) and Records Management System (RMS). 

View [Press Release](/about-us/whats-new/news/park-district-launches-new-system-make-information-about-police-and-fire)

[ View Police & Fire Activity (Opens in new window) ](https://ebpd.crimegraphics.com)

#### User Notice

Access to disclosable public safety activity information is provided as a public service from the East Bay Regional Park District and subject to applicable terms of use. To protect privacy, the Crimegraphics.com website does not provide information regarding juvenile offenders, or specific information regarding calls for service that are protected by confidentiality laws or sensitive in nature. Crimegraphics.com is a third-party resource, and by using this website you will be subject to its license or use terms, and its privacy policy and security practices. The East Bay Regional Park District does not guarantee the accuracy, completeness, or timeliness of the disclosable information contained on this website regarding specific incidents, crimes, or people with respect to the omission of information that may have not yet been filed or is pending filing with a court(s) of jurisdiction relating to criminal offenses.

### 

Do I need to sign up to view activity information?

No, you can view Police and Fire Activity information by visiting [**https://ebpd.crimegraphics.com**](https://ebpd.crimegraphics.com). If you would like to receive a daily or weekly email update with specific information about public safety activities in the parks, you can click "Sign up for Alerts" at [**https://ebpd.crimegraphics.com**](https://ebpd.crimegraphics.com).

### 

How will my email address be used?

When you sign up for daily or weekly updates your email address is entered into the third-party [https://ebpd.crimegraphics.com](https://ebpd.crimegraphics.com/) website hosted by Sun Ridge Systems, Inc. You will only receive the emails you subscribe to during the sign-up process.

### 

What information can I access?

You can see basic information about public safety activity (police and fire incidents) and crimes that occur within the East Bay Regional Park District. You can also see the Daily Bulletin which provides additional information about each activity.

**User Notice:** Access to disclosable public safety activity information is provided as a public service from the East Bay Regional Park District and subject to applicable terms of use. To protect privacy, the Crimegraphics.com website does not provide information regarding juvenile offenders, or specific information regarding calls for service that are protected by confidentiality laws or sensitive in nature. The East Bay Regional Park District does not guarantee the accuracy, completeness, or timeliness of the disclosable information contained on this website regarding specific incidents, crimes, or people with respect to the omission of information that may have not yet been filed or is pending filing with a court(s) of jurisdiction relating to criminal offenses.

### 

Can I access information in real-time?

Most activity information (police and fire incidents) will be available on <https://ebpd.crimegraphics.com> four hours after they have been completed. Crimes will only show after the police report has been approved _._

### 

Can I see incidents that happen around my neighborhood?

The East Bay Regional Park District’s <https://ebpd.crimegraphics.com> webpage will only show activities that occur within the East Bay Regional Park District’s jurisdiction. Check [Crimegraphics.com](https://crimegraphics.com/) to see if the city where you live participates in Citizen RIMS. 

### 

Can I report incidents through Crimegraphics.com?

No, please contact our Dispatch center at **(510) 881 1833** or, in case of an emergency, call **911** or **(510) 881 1121**. If you would like to report an issue in an East Bay Regional Park District park that is not currently taking place and not an emergency (only informational), please use [Park Watch Report Form](https://www.ebparks.org/public-safety/incident/park-watch-report).
