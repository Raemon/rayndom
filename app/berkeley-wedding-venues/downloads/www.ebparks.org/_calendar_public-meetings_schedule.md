## Board Study Sessions 2026

Study Sessions take place as Special Meetings of the Board of Directors in alignment with the Park District’s annual budget process on focus topics for forwarding-looking discussions to inform future policy and strategic issue framing and planning. Study Sessions are open to the public. For more information to view upcoming meetings, go to [our Public Meetings page](/calendar/public-meetings).

## Board Committee Meetings 2026

Standing Board Committees serve in an advisory capacity to the full Board of Directors in the decision-making process. These standing committees deal with broad sets of policy issues in stewardship of public lands under the jurisdiction of the East Bay Regional Park District.

## Special Meetings: Site Visits/Tours

Field site visits (aka Board Tours) take place as Special Meetings of the Board of Directors to focus on park operations, natural and cultural resources, capital and natural restoration projects, and programs taking place on public lands within the Park District’s jurisdiction. These field site visits are open to the public. For more information and to R.S.V.P. send email to [[email protected]](/cdn-cgi/l/email-protection#04466b657660576d7061506b7176774461667465766f772a6b7663) or call (510) 544-2550.

## Park Advisory Committee (PAC) Meetings 2026

Appointed by the Board of Directors, the East Bay Regional Park Advisory Committee serves in an advisory capacity representing community perspectives to inform policy action by the Board.
