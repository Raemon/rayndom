## The Fire Department’s Mission

The Fire Department supports the mission, vision, and values of the East Bay Regional Park District. We will help create a landscape that is safe, healthy, resilient, and more resistant to the negative impacts of fire for our parklands and the communities we serve. We will provide professional and effective fire prevention, fuels management, education, suppression, water safety, rescue, and emergency medical services.

**For more information on how we accomplish our mission, please explore our department’s sections below...**
