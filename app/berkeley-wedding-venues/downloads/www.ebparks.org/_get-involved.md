# Get Involved

  * [Volunteer](/volunteer)
  * [Memberships & Donations](/get-involved/regional-parks-foundation)



__Back to top

All hands on deck! Get involved in myriad ways. Volunteer in trail maintenance, shoreline cleanup, habitat restoration or volunteer as Docents, Trail Safety members, Ambassadors. Serve your community with your time and talents, and/or with your membership, donations and bequest. Make a big difference towards a better world and a healthier planet.
