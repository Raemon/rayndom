__Back to top

## Celebrating Hispanic Heritage Month

Join us in celebrating heritage rooted in all Latin American countries during Hispanic Heritage Month from September 15 to October 15 as we share stories and contributions from both Hispanic and Latino Americans. The Park District is committed to enhancing diversity, equity, and inclusion, and making our agency welcoming and reflective of the communities we serve.

### Los Charros Online Exhibit
