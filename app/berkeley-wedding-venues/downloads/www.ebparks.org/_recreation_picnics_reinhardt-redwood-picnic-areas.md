Fees

Parking: $5 per vehicle; $5 per trailered vehicle. Buses: $25 per bus. Fees are collected on weekends and major holidays April through October and only at the Redwood Gate entrance.

Hours

Open between 5am and 10pm unless otherwise posted or permitted

## Venue Activities

Image

![Picnicing](/sites/default/files/2021-10/icon_activity_picnic.svg)

Picnicking

There are THREE (3) reservable sites at [Reinhardt Redwood Regional Park](/parks/reinhardt-redwood). Beer and wine are allowed without an additional permit.

#### Old Church (50 person capacity)

This is a popular wedding site. It is a ¼ mile paved hike to the site from the parking lot. The amphitheater seats 40 people. There are chemical toilets near the site.

  * [Site information](https://apm.activecommunities.com/ebparks/Facility_Search?facility_id=47)
  * Site Photos



#### Owl (50 person capacity)

Parking is approximately 50 yards away. Flush toilets are nearby. This is an ADA site.

  * [Site information](https://apm.activecommunities.com/ebparks/Facility_Search?facility_id=52)
  * Site Photos



#### Quail (75 person capacity)

Parking is approximately 50 yards away. Bathrooms are not close. This is an ADA site.

  * [Site information](https://apm.activecommunities.com/ebparks/Facility_Search?facility_id=57)
  * Site photos



Visit the Picnic’s [Frequently Asked Questions](/recreation/picnics) for information on fees, rules, making a reservation, and other important information when making a picnic reservation.

  * Redwood Regional Park: [Specific information](/parks/reinhardt-redwood)
  * Redwood Regional Park: [Park map](/sites/default/files/maps/R%20Redwood_2020-9-15-1.pdf)
