# Trail User Working Group

__Back to top

## History

In October 2019, the East Bay Regional Park District (Park District) Board of Directors held a public workshop on Trails. At that workshop, the Board heard testimony from a number of different park and trail users. The Board directed staff to convene a working group made up of stakeholders who are active trail users. On August 21, 2020, a Trail User Working Group was convened by Park District staff at the first meeting. Meetings were held throughout 2020 and 2021, and the final meeting was held on February 7, 2022. The meetings were recorded and are available on the [Trail User Working Group - Videos](/publications-media/videos/tuwg) page. A final report, [Goals, Issues & Solutions: A Summary of Concepts for New Trail Development (PDF)](/sites/default/files/Summary-of-Goals-Issues-Solutions-final-to-print-rfs.pdf) was published in March 2022.

## Group Description (Participants)

In addition to Park District staff, the Trail User Working Group is made up of two people from each of the following categories of trail users.

  * [List of Working Group Member's Names and Organizations (PDF)](/sites/default/files/trail_user_working_group_members_public_list_nov_2020.pdf)
  * Bicyclists
  * Conservation
  * Dog Owners
  * Equestrians
  * Hiking
  * Land Trusts
  * Regional Perspectives
  * Youth
  * Additional Park Stakeholders
  * EBRPD Park Advisory Committee
  * EBRPD Multicultural Advisory Committee
  * Regional Parks Foundation
