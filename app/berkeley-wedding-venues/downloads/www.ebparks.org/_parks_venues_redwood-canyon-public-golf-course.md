Redwood Canyon Public Golf Course (formerly Willow Park) is operated by Touchstone Golf Inc. The course is celebrated as one of the Bay Area’s most enjoyable golf destinations and includes a pro shop as well as food and beverage services. Redwood Canyon Public Golf Course is located in Castro Valley at 17007 Redwood Road. The golf course can be found in the top right corner of the Anthony Chabot Regional Park map. Call [(510) 537-8001](tel:1-510-537-8001) for information.

  * Holes: 18
  * Yardage: 6201/5783, 5783/5227
  * Par: 71, 72/71
  * Rating: 69.7/67.4, 72.4/69.2
  * Slope: 115/110, 124/117
  * Flat, tight fairways with a creek and wildlife
  * Hours: dawn to dusk
