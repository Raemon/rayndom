## Camping Reservations

East Bay Regional Park District offers a wide variety of camping options including family camping, group camping, backpacking, and equestrian camping across Alameda and Contra Costa Counties. The [District Map (PDF)](/sites/default/files/general-broc_eng-2025-06.pdf) shows the locations of camping sites.

#### Important Updates: 

  * **Group** and **backpack** campsites are released in**2 Six-month block release windows**. On “opening dates”, reservations are taken by phone only. **Maximum 2 reservations per call.**

    * Group and Backpacking reservations with **arrival date from January through June 2026** are now open.
    * For Group and Backpacking campsites with **arrival dates from July through December 2026** , sites will be open Wed., April 22, 2026 at 9:00 a.m.
  * Family campsites, including tent sites, full-hookup sites, and cabins, are released on a rolling 12-week window. Reservations can be made here: [www.reserveamerica.com](http://www.reserveamerica.com/) or by calling the EBRPD Reservations Department.

  * Please review the [Group Campsite Minimums, Maximums, and # of Vehicles](/sites/default/files/ebprd-group-camping-minimum-2026.pdf?v=20251210) allowed.

  * Please review the [Seasonal and Site Closure List (PDF) ](/sites/default/files/seasonal-site-closures-10152025.pdf)before calling to inquire. 

  * Reservations Department:**1-888-327-2757, option 2 (9:00 am – 4:00 pm, Pacific Time; Closed weekends and holidays). Canadian residents call 510-544-2707.**
