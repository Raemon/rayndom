## 

Shadow Cliffs Lake Water Quality Conditions

  * There is a [Caution Advisory](https://www.ebparks.org/sites/default/files/HAB-Caution-2021.pdf) posted for blue-green algae at Shadow Cliffs. See the [Blue-Green Algae Information](https://www.ebparks.org/natural-resources/water-quality/blue-green-algae) page for additional details.

## Venue Activities

Image

![Picnicing](/sites/default/files/2021-10/icon_activity_picnic.svg)

Picnicking

There are six (6) reservable sites at [Shadow Cliffs Regional Recreation Area](/parks/shadow-cliffs). Alcohol is prohibited in the entire park.**** There is a parking fee.

#### Fiesta (100 person capacity)

  * [Site information](https://apm.activecommunities.com/ebparks/Facility_Search?facility_id=99)
  * Site Photos



#### Lago Del Sol (100 person capacity)

This site is next to the lake and near the concession stand. ADA accessible picnic table(s).

  * [Site information](https://apm.activecommunities.com/ebparks/Facility_Search?facility_id=107)
  * Site Photos



#### Lakeside (200 person capacity)

This site is wheelchair accessible. ADA accessible picnic table(s).

  * [Site information](https://apm.activecommunities.com/ebparks/Facility_Search?facility_id=102)
  * Site Photos



#### Marina View (75 person capacity)

There is no shade at this site. The fishing dock, fish cleaning station, and flush toilets are near the site. ADA accessible picnic table(s).

  * [Site information](https://apm.activecommunities.com/ebparks/Facility_Search?facility_id=109)
  * Site Photos



#### North Beach (50 person capacity)

There is no shade site at this site. This site is closest to the beach and near the parking lot. ADA accessible picnic table(s). Flush toilets are 100 yards away.

  * [Site information](https://apm.activecommunities.com/ebparks/Facility_Search?facility_id=112)
  * Site Photos



#### Seven Limbs (100 person capacity)

  * [Site information](https://apm.activecommunities.com/ebparks/Facility_Search?facility_id=113)
  * Site Photos



Visit the Picnic’s [Frequently Asked Questions](/recreation/picnics) for information on fees, rules, making a reservation, and other important information when making a picnic reservation.

  * Shadow Cliffs Regional Recreation Area:****[Specific information](/parks/shadow-cliffs)
