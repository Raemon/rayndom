Fees

Parking: No Fee

January through March, and the Wed. before Thanksgiving through December:  
Tues. through Sun.: $4/adults (18+ years), $3/seniors (62+ year), $2/children (4-17 years), under 4 years - free.

April through the Tues. before Thanksgiving:  
Tues., Wed. & Sat.: $4/adults (18+ years), $3/seniors (62+ year), $2/children (4-17 years), under 4 years - free.  
Thurs., Fri. and Sun.: $6/adults (18+ years), $5/seniors (62+ year), $4/children (4-17 years), under 4 years - free. 

Special Event Entry Fees  
$8-$30/adults* (18+ years), $6-$30/seniors* (62+ year), $5-$25/children* (4-17 years)  
*Special event entry fees may vary. Please call (510) 544-2797 or email [[email protected]](/cdn-cgi/l/email-protection#fd9c8a8b948e9489bd989f8d9c8f968ed3928f9a) for updated information.

## Venue Activities

Image

![Picnicing](/sites/default/files/2021-10/icon_activity_picnic.svg)

Picnicking

There is one (1) reservable site at [Ardenwood Historic Farm](/parks/ardenwood). This picnic site does not allow any modern recreation equipment (i.e., soccer balls, footballs, bats, bicycles, scooters, inflatables, streamers, balloons, piñatas, frisbees, etc.…). Caterers, BBQs, and other heat sources are not allowed. Beer and wine are allowed without a permit. Dogs are not allowed.

#### E-I-O (50 person capacity)

  * [Site information](https://apm.activecommunities.com/ebparks/Facility_Search?facility_id=9)
  * Site photos
