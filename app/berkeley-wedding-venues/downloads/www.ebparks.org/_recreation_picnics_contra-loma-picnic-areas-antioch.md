## 

Contra Loma Reservoir Water Quality Conditions

  * A [Caution Advisory](https://www.ebparks.org/sites/default/files/HAB-Caution-2021.pdf "Caution Advisory") for blue-green algae is posted. See [Blue-Green Algae Information](https://www.ebparks.org/natural-resources/water-quality/blue-green-algae) for additional details.



There are six (6) reservable sites at [Contra Loma Regional Park](/parks/contra-loma) There is no alcohol allowed at any time in the park. Entrance to the swimming area is first come, first serve. A picnic reservation does not guarantee entrance to the swim area.

**For Picnic Reservations,****please call our Reservations Department.****Phone:** 1-888-EBPARKS ([888-327-2757](tel:1-888-327-2757)), option 2.

## Picnic Sites

### Cattail Cove (150 person capacity)

Maximum site capacity is 150  
This site is far from the parking lot. Located next to a large lawn. Shady or Partially Shaded. Closest to swim lagoon. Close to flush toilets.

**ADA Accessibility:** Close to ADA accessible restroom. Accessible Drinking Fountain

### Locust Grove (200 person capacity)

Maximum site capacity is 200  
Parking Nearby. Near lawn area and park entrance. Partially Shaded. Boat Launch Nearby. There are three chemical toilets near the boat ramp and two chemical toilets at East Lake Road.

### Loma Island (150 person capacity)

Maximum site capacity is 150

It is a HIKE-IN site located 75 feet over a bridge adjacent to a lawn area with parking 200 yards away. There is one chemical toilet 100 yards away. Shady or Partially Shaded. Popular site for weddings. 

**ADA Accessibility:** ADA accessible picnic table(s).
