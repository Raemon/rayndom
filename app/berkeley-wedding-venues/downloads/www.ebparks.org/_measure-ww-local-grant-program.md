# Measure WW Local Grant Program

__Back to top

### Measure WW Local Grant Program Projects

[Measure WW](https://ebparks.org/measure-ww) has awarded a total of $117 million in funding for projects that benefit 46 cities, communities, local park and recreation districts, county service areas, and the Oakland Zoo. The $117 million in grants was leveraged at 277% for $324 million in project development.

Learn more: [Measure WW Grant Report 2009-2019](/sites/default/files/Measure%20WW%20Grant%20Report%202019.pdf) [pdf]
