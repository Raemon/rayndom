## Venue Activities

Image

![Picnicing](/sites/default/files/2021-10/icon_activity_picnic.svg)

Picnicking

There are nine (9) reservable sites at [Miller Knox](/parks/miller-knox). There is no alcohol allowed at any time in the park.

#### Big Meadow (100 person capacity)

There is no shade at this site.

  * [Site information](https://apm.activecommunities.com/ebparks/Facility_Search?facility_id=100)
  * Site photos



#### Canvasback (50 person capacity)

This side is close to the lagoon.

  * [Site information](https://apm.activecommunities.com/ebparks/Facility_Search?facility_id=101)
  * Site photos



#### Hills View (75 person capacity)

  * [Site information](https://apm.activecommunities.com/ebparks/Facility_Search?facility_id=103)
  * Site photos



#### Killdeer (150 person capacity)

This site is next to the “tot lot” and children’s playground.

  * [Site information](https://apm.activecommunities.com/ebparks/Facility_Search?facility_id=104)
  * Site photos



#### Lagoon View (50 person capacity)

This site has a view of the lagoon.

  * [Site information](https://apm.activecommunities.com/ebparks/Facility_Search?facility_id=105)
  * Site photos



#### Marsh Hawk (35 person capacity)

  * [Site information](https://apm.activecommunities.com/ebparks/Facility_Search?facility_id=106)
  * Site photos



#### Pickleweed (50 person capacity)

This site is fairly secluded.

  * [Site information](https://apm.activecommunities.com/ebparks/Facility_Search?facility_id=108)
  * Site photos



#### Pintail (200 person capacity)

This site is approximately 100 yards from the parking lot.

  * [Site information](https://apm.activecommunities.com/ebparks/Facility_Search?facility_id=110)
  * Site photos



#### Sea Gull (75 person capacity)

  * [Site information](https://apm.activecommunities.com/ebparks/Facility_Search?facility_id=111)
  * Site photos



Visit the Picnic’s [Frequently Asked Questions](https://ebparks.org/recreation/picnics) for information on fees, rules, making a reservation, and other important information when making a picnic reservation.

  * Point Pinole Regional Shoreline: [Specific information](https://ebparks.org/parks/miller-knox)
  * Point Pinole Regional Shoreline: [Park map](/sites/default/files/miller_knox_map.pdf)
