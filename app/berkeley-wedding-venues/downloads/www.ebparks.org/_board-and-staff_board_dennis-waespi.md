# Ward 3 - Dennis Waespi

__Back to top

Image

![Dennis Waespi, Ward 3 Board Member](/sites/default/files/styles/1x1_400w/public/Dennis-Waespi.png?h=1e66e246&itok=zg8K9SJE)

WARD 3 BOARD MEMBER  
**Dennis Waespi  
Treasurer**  
Resides in Castro Valley  
Term expires end of 2026  
[[email protected]](https://mail.google.com/mail/?view=cm&fs=1&tf=1&to=dwaespi@ebparks.org)

Dennis Waespi was elected to the Board of Directors in November 2014. He worked at the East Bay Regional Park District in a variety of positions for 38 years, beginning in 1974. Director Waespi served on the Castro Valley Sanitary District Board of Directors from 1999 to 2011 and on the Hayward Area Recreation and Park District Board of Directors from 2008 to 2014. He also served on the Board of Directors of the Alameda County Waste Management Authority, the Alameda County Source Reduction and Recycling Board, and the Alameda County Parks, Recreation, and Historical Commission.  
  
Director Waespi is currently a member of the Alameda County Fire Advisory Commission and the Castro Valley Sanitary District Community Advisory Committee. He is married, resides in Castro Valley and hikes daily in Lake Chabot Park.

[Current Ethics Certificate](/sites/default/files/Waespi%20ethics%20cert_0.pdf)  _(170 KB) [PDF]_

Regional Parks in Ward 3 include: Anthony Chabot Regional Park, Ardenwood Historic Farm, Cull Canyon Regional Recreation Area, Don Castro Regional Recreation Area, Dry Creek Pioneer Regional Park, Dublin Hills Regional Park, Five Canyons Open Space Regional Preserve, Garin Regional Park, Hayward Regional Shoreline, Lake Chabot Regional Park, Little Hills Regional Recreation Area, Pleasanton Ridge Regional Park, Quarry Lakes Regional Recreation Area, Vargas Plateau Regional Park

Cities in Ward 3 include: Castro Valley, Fairview, a portion of Fremont, Hayward, San Lorenzo, Union City
