## Venue Activities

Image

![Picnicing](/sites/default/files/2021-10/icon_activity_picnic.svg)

Picnicking

There are three (3) reservable sites at [Point Pinole Regional Shoreline](/parks/point-pinole). Beer and wine are allowed without an additional permit

#### Giant Cluster (50 person capacity)

This site is near the volleyball nets and horseshoe pits. ADA chemical toilet near the site.

  * [Site information](https://apm.activecommunities.com/ebparks/Facility_Search?facility_id=114)
  * Site photos



#### The Palms (35 person capacity)

This site is near the volleyball nets, horseshoe pits, and the tot lot. ADA chemical toilet 50 yards from the site.

  * [Site information](https://apm.activecommunities.com/ebparks/Facility_Search?facility_id=116)
  * Site photos



#### Shoreline (50 person capacity)

  * [Site information](https://apm.activecommunities.com/ebparks/Facility_Search?facility_id=115)
  * Site photos



Visit the Picnic’s [Frequently Asked Questions](/recreation/picnics) for information on fees, rules, making a reservation, and other important information when making a picnic reservation.

  * Point Pinole Regional Shoreline: [Specific information](/parks/point-pinole)
  * Point Pinole Regional Shoreline: [Park map](/sites/default/files/maps/Point%20Pinole%2020180726.pdf)
