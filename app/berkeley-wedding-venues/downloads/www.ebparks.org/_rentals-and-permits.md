The Reservations Department often experiences very high call volumes with extended wait times. If the call center has reached the maximum number of calls or you encounter a busy signal, please try your call again at a later time. We work through calls as quickly as possible, and we appreciate your patience and look forward to helping you.  
---  
  
The Reservations Department oversees reservations for 131 reservable picnic sites across 24 parks; a range of camping opportunities including backpacking, family, group, and equestrian camping at 15 parks and reservations for four event venues. In addition, the Department processes activity registration, and facilitates the issuance and management of a variety of permits. Reservations for picnic areas are currently being accepted for the entire 2026 calendar year. Please review **Important Dates** below for the availability of other reservation types. 

The Reservations Department can be reached by phone at (888) 327-2757, option 2, Monday through Friday, 9am– 4pm (closed weekends and holidays); in person via walk-in service at District Headquarters (2950 Peralta Oaks Ct., Oakland); or by email at [[email protected]](/cdn-cgi/l/email-protection#6e1c0b1d0b1c180f1a0701001d2e0b0c1e0f1c051d40011c09) for general inquires only (no reservations accepted by email).

To view the availability (picnic sites or event venues, visit our [View Facility Availability](https://apm.activecommunities.com/ebparks/Facility_Search) page.

To view campsite availability, or to make family campsite reservations, go to [www.reserveamerica.com](https://www.reserveamerica.com) and search for the desired location.
