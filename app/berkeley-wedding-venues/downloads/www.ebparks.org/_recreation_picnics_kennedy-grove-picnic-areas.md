##  Overview

Fees

Parking: $5 seasonal--weekends and holidays. $5 per trailered vehicle. Buses: $25/per bus. Fees are charged from April 1 to October 31

Hours

Park Hours  
Nov.-Feb.: 8am-4:30pm  
Mar.: 8am-6pm  
Apr.: 8am-7pm  
May-Aug.: 8am-8pm  
Sep.: 8am-7pm  
Oct.: 8am-6pm

Address

6531 San Pablo Dam Road  
El Sobrante, CA, US 94803

Phone

Reservations Department  
1-888-327-2757 option 2  
Kennedy Grove Regional Recreation Area  
1-888-327-2757, option 3, extension 4534

Picnicking

There are seven (7) reservable sites at [Kennedy Grove Regional Recreation Area](/parks/kennedy). Beer and wine are allowed at without an additional permit.

#### Abbie Lane (50 person capacity)

  * [Site information](https://apm.activecommunities.com/ebparks/Facility_Search?facility_id=44)
  * Site photos

  * Site photos

  * [Site information](https://apm.activecommunities.com/ebparks/Facility_Search?facility_id=48)
  * Site photos



#### Hitching Post (50 person capacity)

  * [Site information](https://apm.activecommunities.com/ebparks/Facility_Search?facility_id=53)
  * Site photos
