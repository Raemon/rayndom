Fees

Parking: $5 per vehicle (when kiosk is attended); $5 per trailered vehicle. Buses: $25 per bus. Fees are subject to change.

Hours

Park Curfew  
10 p.m. - 5 a.m.

Park/Gate Hours  
Nov. 1 - Mar. 5: 8 a.m. - 6 p.m.  
Mar. 6 - Apr. 16: 8 a.m. - 7 p.m.  
Apr. 17 - May 21: 8 a.m. - 8 p.m.  
May 22 - Aug. 27: 8 a.m. - 9 p.m.  
Aug. 28 - Sep. 24: 8 a.m. - 8 p.m.  
Sep. 25 - Oct. 29: 8 a.m. - 7 p.m.

Visitor Center  
Open Saturdays through Labor Day 11:30 a.m. - 5 p.m. 

Dry Creek Garden Hours  
Open Thursdays - Saturdays, 10 a.m. - 4 p.m.

## Venue Activities

Image

![Picnicing](/sites/default/files/2021-10/icon_activity_picnic.svg)

Picnicking

There are four (4) reservable sites at [Garin/Dry Creek Pioneer Regional Parks](/parks/garin). Beer and wine are allowed without an additional permit.

#### Buttonwood (50 person capacity)

Parking and flush toilets approximately 100 yards away

  * [Site information](https://apm.activecommunities.com/ebparks/Facility_Search?facility_id=40)
  * Site photos



#### Cattlemen’s (50 person capacity)

Parking and flush toilets approximately 100 yards away

  * [Site information](https://apm.activecommunities.com/ebparks/Facility_Search?facility_id=41)
  * Site photos



#### Pioneer (50 person capacity)

Parking and flush toilets approximately 100 yards away

  * [Site information](https://apm.activecommunities.com/ebparks/Facility_Search?facility_id=42)
  * Site photos



#### Ranchside (50 person capacity)

Parking and flush toilets approximately 150 yards away

  * [Site information](https://apm.activecommunities.com/ebparks/Facility_Search?facility_id=43)
  * Site photos



Visit the Picnic’s [Frequently Asked Questions](/recreation/picnics) for information on fees, rules, making a reservation, and other important information when making a picnic reservation.
