# Del Valle Picnic Areas - Livermore

__Back to top

## 

Del Valle Boat Launch Water Quality Conditions

  * Swimming is not permitted due to a [Danger Advisory](https://www.ebparks.org/sites/default/files/HAB-Danger-2021-incl-boats.pdf "Danger Advisory") for blue-green algae. See [Blue-Green Algae Information](https://www.ebparks.org/natural-resources/water-quality/blue-green-algae) for additional details 
  * [Monitoring Program](https://www.ebparks.org/recreation/swimming/del-valle#monitor)

Fees

Parking: $5 per vehicle. $5 per trailered vehicle. $25 per bus.   
No parking fee at Arroyo staging. No drop-offs of any kind.

Flat Entry Fee $10 on weekends and holidays, April through Labor Day

Fees are subject to change.

## Venue Activities

Image

![Picnicing](/sites/default/files/2021-10/icon_activity_picnic.svg)

Picnicking

There are twelve (12) reservable sites at [Del Valle Regional Park](/parks/del-valle). Beer and wine are allowed without an additional permit.

  * **East Side sites**
  * Agua Vista
  * Arroyo Mocho
  * Beach View
  * Coyote Corner
  * Fiesta Grande
  * Oak Point


  * **West Side sites**
  * Gray Pine
  * Lichen Bark
  * Shady Oak
  * School House Flats
  * Valley Oak
  * Vista Del Lago



### East Side picnic sites

#### Agua Vista (150 person capacity)

Near parking lot. Restrooms approximately 150 yards away

  * [Site information](https://apm.activecommunities.com/ebparks/Facility_Search?facility_id=78)
  * Site photos



#### Arroyo Mocho (150 person capacity)

Near parking. Site is ADA accessible

  * [Site information](https://apm.activecommunities.com/ebparks/Facility_Search?facility_id=83)
  * Site photos



#### Beach View (50 person capacity)

Close to concession, restrooms, and swim area. Site is wheelchair accessible

  * [Site information](https://apm.activecommunities.com/ebparks/Facility_Search?facility_id=84)
  * Site photos




#### Coyote Corner (50 person capacity)

Close to swim area and bathrooms. Far from parking lot

  * [Site information](https://apm.activecommunities.com/ebparks/Facility_Search?facility_id=86)
  * Site photos



#### Fiesta Grande (500 person capacity)

Near parking lot. Restrooms approximately 100 yards away

  * [Site information](https://apm.activecommunities.com/ebparks/Facility_Search?facility_id=87)
  * Site Photo



#### Oak Point (150 person capacity)

Close to lake. Flush toilets nearby. Moderate distance from parking lot

  * [Site information](https://apm.activecommunities.com/ebparks/Facility_Search?facility_id=93)
  * Site photos



### West Side picnic sites

#### Gray Pine (50 person capacity)

Close to the lake. Flush toilets approximately 50 yards away

  * [Site Information](https://apm.activecommunities.com/ebparks/Facility_Search?facility_id=90)
  * Site photos



#### Lichen Bark (300 person capacity)

Flush toilets nearby. Close to parking. This is a popular site for weddings.

  * [Site Information](https://apm.activecommunities.com/ebparks/Facility_Search?facility_id=91)
  * Site photos



#### Shady Oak (75 person capacity)

View of the lake

  * [Site Information](https://apm.activecommunities.com/ebparks/Facility_Search?facility_id=97)
  * Site photos



#### School House Flats (150 person capacity)

View of lake. Close to parking.

  * [Site Information](https://apm.activecommunities.com/ebparks/Facility_Search?facility_id=95)
  * Site photos



#### Valley Oak (75 person capacity)

View of lake. Next to the Rocky Ridge Visitor Center and bathrooms

  * [Site Information](https://apm.activecommunities.com/ebparks/Facility_Search?facility_id=98)
  * Site photos



#### Vista Del Lago (35 person capacity)

Close to lake. Adjacent to the Rocky Ridge Visitor Center. Sloped paved access trail to the site. ADA picnic tables available.

  * [Site Information](https://apm.activecommunities.com/ebparks/Facility_Search?facility_id=350)
  * Site photos



Visit the Picnic’s [Frequently Asked Questions](/recreation/picnics) for information on fees, rules, making a reservation, and other important information when making a picnic reservation.

  * Del Valle [specific information](/parks/del-valle)
