# Volunteer Resources

__Back to top

Volunteering at the East Bay Regional Parks is a fun and exciting way to make an impact at the East Bay Parks! Please use the following resources to better understand our volunteer policies and practices. 

## **Waivers and Service Agreements**

Most of these forms are stored with your EBRPD Volunteer account and can be accessed through your account dashboard. 

Form| Description  
---|---  
[Individual EBRPD Service Agreement](https://www.ebparks.org/sites/default/files/EBRPD%20COVID%20Waiver%20Individual.pdf)| For reference. This form is filed from your account dashboard.  
[EBRPD Group Waiver](/sites/default/files/EBRPD-Group-Waiver.pdf)| This will be provided at your event, but copies can also be accessed here.   
[EBRPD Volunteer Event Waiver](/sites/default/files/EBRPD-Event-Waiver.pdf)| This will be provided at your event by EBRPD Staff. It is designed for walkup volunteers.  
[EBRPD Volunteer Permission for Minors](/sites/default/files/Parent-Permission-to-Volunteer.pdf)| Please fill this form out for Youth under the age of 18. It must be signed by a parent or legal guardian. _Youth under the age of 13 must be accompanied by an adult._  
[EBRPD Field Trip Waiver](/sites/default/files/EBRPD-Vol-Field-Trip-Waiver-Form.pdf)| Please bring this form if you are a teacher or organizer bringing a group of youth to a volunteer event.  
  
## Guides for Our Volunteer Database

Our new Volunteer Database is a one stop shop for all our volunteer programs! Please use these guides to help you get started. 

Form| Description  
---|---  
[Create an Account and Program Search](/sites/default/files/Volunteer-login-program-search-instructions-Samaritan-DS.pdf)| How to create an account on the EBRPD Volunteer Database and search for existing programs.  
[Reset your account](/sites/default/files/SAM-Volunteer-Password-Reset-Instructions.pdf)| How to reset your password on the EBRPD Volunteer Database.  
[How to log hours](https://www.ebparks.org/sites/default/files/GENERAL%20How%20to%20log%20hours.pdf)| Some programs require participants to log their own hours. Learn how to do so here!   
  
## **Submitting a Service Proposal**

Do you have an idea for a volunteer project? Want to apply for a group volunteer event, show your interest in becoming a docent, or propose an Eagle Scout Project?

Form| Description  
---|---  
**[Apply Here!](https://apply.ebparks.org/custom/501/volunteer_home)**| 

  * **Group Volunteer Events**
  * **Individual Volunteer Events**
  * **Trail Maintenance Programs**
  * **Docent Interest Forms**
  * **Eagle and Gold Award Scout Projects**

  
[Guide: How to submit a Service Proposal](/sites/default/files/How-to-Propose-a-Service-Project.pdf)| This guide will walk you through the steps of applying for a Service Proposal  
  
Thank you for your interest in Volunteering at the East Bay Parks. Still have questions or need more information? Please feel free to contact us at [[email protected]](/cdn-cgi/l/email-protection#acdac3c0d9c2d8c9c9dedfecc9cedccddec7df82c3decb) or by calling (510)544-2229.
