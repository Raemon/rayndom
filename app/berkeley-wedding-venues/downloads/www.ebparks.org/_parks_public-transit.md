Visit [Transit 511 website](https://511.org/ "511.org") for comprehensive Transit Information for the San Francisco Bay Area as well as being able to create a complete public transit trip plan to most District parks and facilities.

  * Try the [Schedules and Route Finder](http://www.511.org/ "Go to Transit 511's Schedules and Route Finder page") to locate a route map or route schedule
  * Try the [511 Take Transit Trip Planner](http://511.org/ "Go to 511's Take Transit Trip Planner page"). It will help plan a trip to the parks using public transit.



For**accessible transportation** to the parks, visit the 511 website at [511.org](https://511.org/transit/accessibility) or call 511 for the most current transit information. (TDD (hearing impaired line)/TTY: 800-448-9790; ParaTransit: 510-287-5000)

## Public Transit Access to Parks

Where available, our [park maps](/maps) include transit information.

The following parks are a selection of those accessible by AC Transit:

  * [MLK Jr Regional Shoreline](/parks/martin-luther-king), Oakland [AC Transit #98](https://www.actransit.org/bus-lines-schedules/98), [AC Transit #73](https://www.actransit.org/bus-lines-schedules/73)
  * [Point Pinole Regional Shoreline](/parks/point-pinole), Richmond [AC Transit #71](https://www.actransit.org/bus-lines-schedules/71)
  * [Temescal Regional Recreation Area](/parks/temescal), Oakland: [AC Transit Transbay Lines V](https://www.actransit.org/bus-lines-schedules/V)
  * [Don Castro Regional Recreation Area](/parks/don-castro), Hayward: [AC Transit #95](https://www.actransit.org/bus-lines-schedules/95)
  * [Shadow Cliffs Regional Recreation Area](/parks/shadow-cliffs), Pleasanton: [WHEELS #10R bus](https://www.wheelsbus.com/routes/route-10r/) from Dublin/Pleasanton BART
  * [Ted and Kathy Radke Martinez Regional Shoreline](/parks/radke-martinez), Martinez:[ WESTCAT #30Z](https://www.westcat.org/home/ScheduleView?routeId=76), [County Connection #98X](https://countyconnection.com/routes/98X/), [#19](https://countyconnection.com/routes/19/), [#16](https://countyconnection.com/routes/16/), [#18](https://countyconnection.com/routes/18/), [#28](https://countyconnection.com/routes/28/)/627, and weekend [#316](https://countyconnection.com/routes/316/)



## Parks Express

[**Parks Express**](/programs/community-outreach/parks-express) is a program of the East Bay Regional Park District, providing low-cost transportation for low-income schools and groups serving children from low-income families, seniors or people with disabilities in Alameda and Contra Costa counties to come to the East Bay Regional Parks. Information may be obtained by calling Parks Express at 510-544-2205.
