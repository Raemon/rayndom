## **Parks to People: Bringing our parks into virtual classrooms, homes, and communities**

East Bay Regional Park District is committed to lifelong learning and providing opportunities for people to make their own discoveries about our parks. These digital resources are designed to be used in educators’ virtual classrooms, at home, or as part of any online learning. 

**VIEW:**Naturalist Videos | Virtual Reality Park Experiences | Nature Activities and Games | Facebook

### Naturalist Videos

Discover your East Bay Regional Parks through these short videos highlighting the wonders and opportunities across the parks.  
View **[Naturalist Videos](/programs/digital-learning/naturalist-videos) **

### Virtual and Augmented Reality Park Experiences

East Bay Regional Park District and Timelooper, Inc. have developed virtual tours of selected Regional Parks. People can now virtually visit the parks to discover more about these lands and their unique stories.  
Visit **[Virtual Parks](/programs/digital-learning/virtual-parks)**.
