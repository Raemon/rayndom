South Park Drive Annual Closure for Newt Migration

**South Park Drive** in Tilden Park will be closed to vehicles from **November 1, 2025** through **March 31, 2026** for the protection of California newts as they utilize the road to migrate during the rainy season. Please adjust your travel plans by using other access routes such as **Shasta Road** or **Golf Course Drive**. **South Park Drive** will remain open to pedestrians and bicyclists. [More Info](/newts).

There are thirteen (13) reservable sites at [Tilden Regional Park](/parks/tilden). Beer and wine are allowed without a permit.

**For Picnic Reservations,****please call our Reservations Department.****Phone:** 1-888-EBPARKS ([888-327-2757](tel:1-888-327-2757)), option 2.
