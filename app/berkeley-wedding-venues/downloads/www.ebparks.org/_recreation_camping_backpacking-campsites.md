# Backpacking Campsites

__Back to top

Backpacking sites offer the opportunity for groups of 1-30 to hike and camp in various parks across the District. Site capacity and distances vary. Sites are primitive, but most offer water, a chemical toilet, and table. Campfires are not permitted at any of the backpacking sites. Dogs are allowed at select sites.

  * Reservations are required and must be made at least 2 business days prior to your arrival date. (Example: The last day to reserve for a Friday arrival date is Wednesday.)
  * Booking Window: Sites are released in 2 Six-month block release windows. Maximum 2 reservations per call.
    * Group and Backpacking reservations with **arrival date from January through June 2026** are now open.
    * For Group and Backpacking campsites with **arrival dates from July through December 2026** , sites will be open Wed., April 22, 2026 at 9:00 a.m.


  * [Backpack Camping FAQs](https://www.ebparks.org/backpack-camping-frequently-asked-questions) and important reservation information
  * [Fees](https://www.ebparks.org/camping-fees)
