## Venue Activities

Image

![Picnicing](/sites/default/files/2021-10/icon_activity_picnic.svg)

Picnicking

There are five (5) reservable sites at [ Dumbarton Quarry Campground on the Bay](https://www.ebparks.org/parks/dumbarton-quarry). Beer and wine are allowed without a permit. Picnic areas surround the campground and are close to parking and restrooms. There is also a playground located near the entry kiosk and at the north end of the park there are beautiful views of Coyote Hills Regional Park.   
**For Picnic Reservations,****please call our Reservations Department.****Phone:** 1-888-EBPARKS ([888-327-2757](tel:1-888-327-2757)), option 2. 

  * DeSilva Picnic Area (75 person capacity)
  * Jarvis Picnic Area (50 person capacity)
  * Mowry Picnic Area (50 person capacity)
  * Patterson Picnic Area (50 person capacity)
  * Agua Caliente Picnic Area (50 person capacity)

## Picnic Sites

### DeSilva Picnic Area (75 person capacity)

Maximum site capacity is 75  
Great for kids' parties and is located adjacent to the playground, a large field, and restrooms.  
Water fountains are located outside the restrooms.

**ADA Accessibility:** ADA accessible picnic table(s). Accessible Path. Accessible Restroom Nearby
