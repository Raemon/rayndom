## 

Temporary parking closures due to capacity

Due to increased visitation, primarily on Saturday and Sunday, vehicle access to Coyote Hills may close temporarily. Vehicle access to the park typically reopens when capacity is available, but expect very extended wait times to enter. Additional Parking is available at [Dumbarton Quarry Campground](/parks/dumbarton-quarry).

Please submit any questions or comments using our [Contact Us Form](/contact/form).

### 

Coyote Hills Naturalist Programs

Join us for our monthly programs!

[ View Programs (PDF) (Opens in new window) ](/sites/default/files/np-vc-coyote-hills-month-flyer-programs.pdf?v=3&linkLoc=highlight)

Coyote Hills Visitor Center contains educational displays and exhibits as well as the Possum's Pocket Nature Store. The exhibits portray the Ohlone way of life and include a tule reed boat constructed by park staff and volunteers using Native American methods. Other exhibits cover the park's natural history and wildlife.

Naturalist-led programs, including tours of the marsh and surrounding area, highlight the park’s cultural and natural history. An annual Gathering of Ohlone Peoples highlights the ways today’s Ohlone's are bringing their cultures into the future. To learn more about the park’s history and natural wonders, or naturalist-led walks and other programs, visit the Visitor Center Wednesdays through Sundays, 10am - 4pm.
