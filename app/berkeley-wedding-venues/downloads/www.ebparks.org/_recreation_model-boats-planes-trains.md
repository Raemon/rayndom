The use of model boats and model airplanes in the East Bay Regional Park District is restricted to certain locations for reasons of public safety.

  * Model Boats
  * Model Airplanes (Gliders)
  * Model Trains



### 

### Model Boats

Contact Model Mariners Club at [(510) 797-5422](tel:1-510-797-5422). Facility is located adjacent to [Alameda Creek Trail](/parks/trails/alameda_creek/default.htm) in Fremont.

### 

### Model Airplanes (Gliders, not Drones)

  * Glider Hill in [Coyote Hills Regional Park](/parks/coyote_hills/default.htm) in Fremont (see [park map](/sites/default/files/coyotehills_map.pdf "Coyote Hills map")). 
  * [Del Valle Regional Park](/parks/del_valle/default.htm), south of Livermore, on Del Valle Road near entry to the park. 
  * [Mission Peak Regional Preserve](/parks/mission/default.htm), Fremont. Contact [South Bay Soaring Society](http://www.sbss.org/) at (408) 287-0375.



### 

### Model Trains

  * [Golden State Model Railroad Museum](http://www.gsmrm.org/): Facility is located at [Miller/Knox Regional Shoreline](/parks/miller_knox/default.htm), 900 Dornan Drive, Point Richmond, CA 94801.
  * [Redwood Valley Railway:](https://www.facebook.com/RedwoodValleyRailway/) Miniature steam train located off Grizzly Peak Blvd. in [Tilden Regional Park](/parks/tilden/default.htm). 
  * [Golden Gate Live Steamers](http://www.ggls.org/): Train rides and model trains. Facility located off Grizzly Peak Blvd. in [Tilden Regional Park](/parks/tilden/default.htm).
