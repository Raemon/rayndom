[Fire Danger Rating Areas (FDRA)](https://www.ebparks.org/public-safety/fire/fire-danger-and-weather-information) | [Remote Automated Weather Stations (RAWS)](https://www.ebparks.org/public-safety/fire/weather-station-raws)

The Regional Parks are divided into Four **Fire Danger Rating Areas** (**FDRA**)**:** Bay Zone, Northwest Zone, Northeast Zone, and Central Zone. The weather, fire fuels, and other conditions that determine fire danger within each FDRA are generally the same. 

## Map of Fire Danger Rating Areas

  * **BAY FDRA** = **BAY ZONE** Fire Danger Rating Area
  * **NORTHWEST FDRA** = **NORTHWEST ZONE** Fire Danger Rating Area
  * **NORTHEAST FDRA** = **NORTHEAST ZONE** Fire Danger Rating Area
  * **CENTRAL FDRA** = **CENTRAL** Fire Danger Rating Area

## RAWS

View [East Bay Regional Park District - RAWS Stations](https://www.google.com/maps/d/viewer?mid=1R4TAWp461Q5VYogxo5897-9l88-vXp-G&ll=37.78611817522028%2C-122.07607272460939&z=11 "RAWS Map on Google Maps") indicated on the map by yellow pins [Google Map].  
Weather data gathered from these areas by [Remote Automated Weather Stations (RAWS)](https://www.ebparks.org/public-safety/fire/weather-station-raws) are used to determine the likelihood of a damaging and dangerous fire.
