## Picnic Sites

### Crow Picnic Area (50 person capacity)

Maximum site capacity is 50  
Parking lot is 100 yards from site - a wagon is recommended to transport heavy items.

**ADA Accessibility:** ADA accessible site. Accessible Restroom 100 feet from site

### Newt Hollow Picnic Area (150 person capacity)

Maximum site capacity is 150  
A popular site for weddings, it is well shaded by large oak trees and has a platform often used for small wedding ceremonies.  
It is located near the Bear Creek entrance to the park. Two chemical toilets are 200 yards from site.

### Oak Grove Picnic Area (50 person capacity)

Maximum site capacity is 50  
The site is located at the Bear Creek entrance to park at the edge of a large meadow next to a steep creek bank. Shady or Partially Shaded.

[Site Info](https://apm.activecommunities.com/ebparks/Facility_Search?facility_id=16) | [Map](/sites/default/files/maps/Briones_20200625.pdf) | [FAQs](https://www.ebparks.org/recreation/picnics) | [Availability](https://apm.activecommunities.com/ebparks/Facility_Search?IsCalendar=true&facility_id=16&year=2023&month=3&startyear=2023&endyear=2023) | [Contact Reservations](/recreation/picnics/briones-picnic-areas#contact)
