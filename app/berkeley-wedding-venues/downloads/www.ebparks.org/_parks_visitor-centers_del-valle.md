## 

Del Valle Boat Launch Water Quality Conditions

  * Swimming is not permitted due to a [Danger Advisory](https://www.ebparks.org/sites/default/files/HAB-Danger-2021-incl-boats.pdf "Danger Advisory") for blue-green algae. See [Blue-Green Algae Information](https://www.ebparks.org/natural-resources/water-quality/blue-green-algae) for additional details 
  * [Monitoring Program](https://www.ebparks.org/recreation/swimming/del-valle#monitor)



## 

Del Valle Regional Park is open with limited amenities

  * The Marina Store is open between 7 a.m. and 5 p.m. daily to purchase bait, tackle, fishing licenses, snacks, and drinks.
  * The Camp Store is open 4 p.m. to 8 p.m. on Fridays; 8 a.m. to 8 p.m. on Saturdays; and 8 a.m. to 12 p.m. on Sundays.
  * Boat rentals (motorboats, patio boats) are on a first-come, first-served basis, weather permitting. 
    * Daily Hours: Sunrise to 1:45 p.m.; boats must be returned by 4 p.m. 
  * Use caution on storm-damaged trails. Signs are posted.
  * Construction equipment/noise may be present during normal working hours.
  * The Visitor Center is open. See hours below.



##### The following amenities remain closed:

  * The Swim Beach Food Concession remains closed until further notice.
  * Entire Campground is opened with the exception of a few sites located in **Lower Phase 2**. These sites are under repair. **Please contact Reservations if you have questions about cancellations.**  
Call [1-888-327-2757](tel:1-888-327-2757), option 2. *For Canadian residents, please use [5](tel:1-510-544-2707)[10-544-2707](tel:1-510-544-2707).

### 

Del Valle Naturalist Programs

Join us for our monthly programs!

[ View Programs (PDF) (Opens in new window) ](/sites/default/files/jan-2026-del-valle-naturalists-programs.pdf?linkLoc=highlight)

The visitor center is on the lake's west side and contains exhibits and information about the natural history of Del Valle and surrounding wilderness. Campers and park visitors are encouraged to visit and talk with naturalists and student aides about the park and the lake and to take part in the exciting programs happening year round.
