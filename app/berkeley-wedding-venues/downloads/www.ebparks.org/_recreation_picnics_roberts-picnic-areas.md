Fees

Parking: $5 per vehicle; $5 per trailered vehicle. Buses: $25/per bus.

Hours

Gate Hours  
Nov.-Feb.: 8am-5pm  
March: 8am-6pm  
April: 8am-7pm  
May-Aug.: 8am-8pm  
September: 8am-7pm  
October: 8am-6pm

Opens 8am year-round  
Closes based on daylight hours. Early closures based on lack of park users or foul weather.

## Venue Activities

Image

![Picnicing](/sites/default/files/2021-10/icon_activity_picnic.svg)

Picnicking

There are ten (10) reservable sites at [Roberts Regional Recreation Area](/parks/roberts).**** Beer and wine are allowed without a permit. There is a parking fee.

Roberts Regional Recreation Area has been a popular destination for weddings in the Redwoods. Please see the [Commonly Asked Questions and Guidelines for Weddings at Robert (PDF)](/sites/default/files/Roberts-Wedding-FAQs.pdf) to learn more about rules and regulations specific to this park.

### Anna Costa Picnic Area (50 person capacity)

Maximum site capacity is 50  
Partially Shaded. Anna Costa is not located in the main area of the park, but further north on Skyline Blvd with a separate parking lot at the Redwood Bowl staging area. This site requires a short walk uphill. Parking is 400 yards away. 

**This site is not suitable for guests with mobility concerns.**

### Bay Vista Picnic Area (100 person capacity)

Maximum site capacity is 100  
This is a popular site for weddings. Flush toilets are 75 yards away. It is a short, uphill walk from the parking lot. For weddings, the site must be combined with the Madrone site. 

*Must reserve both Bay Vista and Madrone for a wedding

### Diablo Vista Picnic Area (75 person capacity)

Maximum site capacity is 75.  
Diablo Vista has 11 ten-foot tables, a ten-foot serving table, a double BBQ, and a drinking fountain with a spigot. There is a shared lawn area approximately 100 yards away. The site is uphill from the parking lot, 150 yards away. Far from the restrooms.

### Huckleberry Picnic Area (50 person capacity)

Maximum site capacity is 50  
This site is very shady as is it under the redwood grove. The parking lot is 50 yards away. This is a popular wedding site. For weddings, the site must be combined with the Roberts site. The playground and pool are across the parking lot. Flush toilets are 100 yards away.

*Must reserve both Huckleberry and Roberts Grove for a wedding

Maximum site capacity is 100  
Manzanita has 20 ten-foot tables, 2 ten-foot serving tables, a single BBQ, 2 double BBQs, and a drinking fountain with spigot. It is located approximately 100 yards from the ball diamond and field area. The site is very sunny. Flush toilets near parking lot.

### Oak Picnic Area (35 person capacity)

Maximum site capacity is 35  
Flush toilets nearby. It is a shady site located on the far side of the upper lawn area adjacent to the children's play area. It is a short walk uphill. The swimming pool is a short walk away. The parking lot is 75 yards away.

### Redwood Bowl Picnic Area (50 person capacity)

Maximum site capacity is 50  
Redwood Bowl is not located in the main park area of Roberts Park, but further north on Skyline Blvd with a separate parking lot at the Redwood Bowl staging area. Parking 300 yards away from site. This site requires a short walk uphill. Shady or Partially Shaded. It is located adjacent to a large lawn area and restrooms. 

**This site is not suitable for guests with mobility concerns.**
