Introduction of a New Invasive Species: Golden Mussels

**NOTICE:** Upcoming changes to watercraft inspections starting May 7, 2025 due the invasive Golden Mussel, which has recently been identified in North America. KNOW BEFORE YOU GO, visit the [Invasive Mussels Page](/recreation/boating/invasive-mussels) more information on current inspection procedures.

The East Bay Regional Park District has numerous opportunities for watercraft launch and rental. All watercraft need to have wearable Coast Guard approved Personal Flotation Device (PFD) available for each passenger.

## Boating Field Trips (Kayak and Canoe)

The Outdoor Recreation Department provides affordable boating field trips for schools and community organizations.   
[More information and Apply for a Boating Field Trip](/programs/outdoor-recreation#field-trip).

## Canoeing and Kayaking Programs

[Boating Activity Calendar](https://apm.activecommunities.com/ebparks/Activity_Search?IsAdvanced=False&ddlSortBy=Simple+Date&ActivityCategoryID=18&DaysOfWeek=&SearchLevelID=2&NumberOfItemsPerPage=100&IsSearch=true "Boating") Discover Boating Activities, Classes, and Programs!

Join us for one of our regularly scheduled public canoeing or kayaking trips in a group setting, where our engaging Outdoor Recreation staff will guide you on a fun paddle exploring our Regional Parks’ waterways and lakes. We offer a variety of programs; all include paddle and safety instruction, and gear. Some programs also feature a naturalist.  
[Register for one of our public Canoeing or Kayaking programs](https://anc.apm.activecommunities.com/ebparks/activity/search?onlineSiteId=0&activity_select_param=2&activity_keyword=kayak&viewMode=list).
