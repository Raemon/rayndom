## About the Facility

Once a park residence and then a senior center, Fern Cottage is nestled amongst ferns and groves of redwood trees within the Kennedy Grove Regional Recreation Area. With two outdoor decks, a private backyard with a landscaped lawn and garden, and a cozy stove pipe fireplace, Fern Cottage feels like a little home away from home.

Fern Cottage is perfect for micro weddings, celebrations, bridal and baby showers, small workshops, and retreats. Our approved caterers and preferred vendors can help you flawlessly execute your event from start to finish. We also offer a DIY non-catered option for reservations that end by 7pm.

### 

To Reach Fern Cottage

**Download:** [Fern Cottage Directions Map](https://ebparks.org/sites/default/files/FernCottage-MapDirections-2021.pdf) | [Fern Cottage Parking Map](https://ebparks.org/sites/default/files/FernCottage-MapParking-2021.JPG)

The Fern Cottage (Building) is located in [Kennedy Grove Regional Recreation Area](https://www.ebparks.org/parks/kennedy/default.htm), on [6531 San Pablo Dam Road](https://www.google.com/maps/place/6531+San+Pablo+Dam+Rd,+El+Sobrante,+CA+94803/@37.9475779,-122.2667253,17z/data=!3m1!4b1!4m2!3m1!1s0x8085798a1fea443f:0xb80b24cb90315f69 "Fern Cottage GMap"). 

  * **From Highway 80:**
    * Take San Pablo Dam Road exit
    * Turn south on San Pablo Dam Road
    * Proceed about one-quarter mile south of Castro Ranch Road, through El Sobrante to the Kennedy Grove entrance (on the left)
  * **From Highway 24:**
    * Take the Orinda exit
    * Turn north on Camino Pablo, which becomes San Pablo Dam Road
    * Drive past the San Pablo Reservoir Recreation Area entrance
    * Drive about one mile to the Kennedy Grove park entrance (on the right).



#### Parking

There is a charge of $5.00 per car on weekends and holidays, April through October (fees subject to change).
