# Outdoor Fitness Permit

__Back to top

## Outdoor Fitness Permit

An Outdoor Fitness Permit is required for any individual who wishes to use park district land to hold group classes with a focus on physical fitness.

If requesting outdoor fitness classes in multiple parks, a separate application and permit fee is required for **each** park.

Questions? Email [[email protected]](/cdn-cgi/l/email-protection#671502141702150a0e132702051706150c1449081500)

**Minimum Requirements**

  1. Complete Outdoor Fitness application with signed Terms and Conditions
  2. Schedule mandatory pre-program meeting with Park Supervisor
  3. Provide proof of current insurance (District insurance requirements can be found [here](/sites/default/files/EBRPD-Insurance-Requirements.pdf))
  4. Purchase $250.00 annual Outdoor Fitness permit once approved



## Outdoor Fitness Permit Process

These steps should be followed in the order in which they are listed.

### Step 1 - Submit Application Packet

Complete all fields on the [application form (PDF)](/sites/default/files/Outdoor-Fitness-Permit-Application.pdf), including required attachments, and return via email, fax, or mail to the address listed on the application. Attach a map indicating the proposed location of the outdoor fitness.

  * Attach proof of insurance



### Step 2 - Application Review

Each application will be reviewed for approval once received. Please include alternative park locations in case your first choice is not available. If the proposed location(s) are not approved, the application will be denied. 

### Step 3 - Meet with Park Supervisor

You will be provided with the Park Supervisor's contact information when your application is approved. You must contact the Park Supervisor to discuss your application, proposed location, etc. to be approved.

### Step 4 – Submit Insurance

Submit General Liability insurance that meets District requirements. Please review the [insurance requirements (PDF)](/sites/default/files/EBRPD-Insurance-Requirements.pdf).

### Step 5 - Purchase Permit

After the completion of all steps listed above, purchase the Outdoor Fitness Permit. The permit is $250.00 per calendar year/location.

**Please allow at least 60 days for the review and processing of applications.**
