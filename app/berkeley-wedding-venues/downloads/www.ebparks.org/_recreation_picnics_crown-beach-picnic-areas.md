## Venue Activities

Image

![Picnicing](/sites/default/files/2021-10/icon_activity_picnic.svg)

Picnicking

There are six (6) reservable sites at [Crown Memorial State Beach](/parks/crown-beach). Beer and wine is only allowed with a picnic reservation and has an additional fee.

#### City View (50 person capacity)

View of San Francisco. Closest site to Boardsports, Kiteboard, Windsurfing, and kayaks. ADA picnic tables available. Flush toilets 25 yard away.

  * [Site information](https://apm.activecommunities.com/ebparks/Facility_Search?facility_id=45)
  * Site photos



#### Crolls Garden (100 person capacity)

Site is wheelchair accessible. ADA picnic tables available. Flush toilets 25 yards away.

  * [Site information](https://apm.activecommunities.com/ebparks/Facility_Search?facility_id=49)
  * Site photos



#### Mariners Lagoon (75 person capacity)

ADA picnic tables available

  * [Site information](https://apm.activecommunities.com/ebparks/Facility_Search?facility_id=50)
  * Site photos



#### Neptune (150 person capacity)

Flush toilets 25 yard away. ADA picnic tables available. View of the water. Site is wheelchair accessible.

  * [Site information](https://apm.activecommunities.com/ebparks/Facility_Search?facility_id=54)
  * Site photos



#### Sand Castle (50 person capacity)

Closest site to Bath House

  * [Site information](https://apm.activecommunities.com/ebparks/Facility_Search?facility_id=55)
  * Site photos



#### Seawind (100 person capacity)

ADA picnic tables available

  * [Site information](https://apm.activecommunities.com/ebparks/Facility_Search?facility_id=51)
  * Site photos



Visit the Picnic’s [Frequently Asked Questions](/recreation/picnics) for information on fees, rules, making a reservation, and other important information when making a picnic reservation.

  * Crown Beach [specific information](/parks/crown-beach)
