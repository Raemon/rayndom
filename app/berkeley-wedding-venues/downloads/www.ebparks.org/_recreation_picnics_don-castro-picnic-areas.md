## Venue Activities

Image

![Picnicing](/sites/default/files/2021-10/icon_activity_picnic.svg)

Picnicking

There are two (2) reservable sites at [Don Castro Regional Recreation Area](/parks/don-castro). Beer and wine are allowed with an additional permit. Alcohol is not allowed inside the swim area. Entrance to the swimming area is first come, first serve. A picnic reservation does not guarantee entrance to the swim area.





#### Siesta (100 person capacity)

Parking lot is approximately 300 yards away. Site is near fishing dock and lawn. ADA chemical toilet.

  * [Site information](https://apm.activecommunities.com/ebparks/Facility_Search?facility_id=13)
