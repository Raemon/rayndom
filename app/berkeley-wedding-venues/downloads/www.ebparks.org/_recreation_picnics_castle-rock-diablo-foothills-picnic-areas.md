Picnicking

Image

![Castle Rock Regional Recreation Area Map](/sites/default/files/styles/original_800w_/public/Castle_Rock_1850w_32c.gif?itok=hS7B5q7h)

There are eight (8) reservable sites at [Castle Rock Regional Recreation Area](/parks/castle-rock) & [Diablo Foothills Regional Park](/parks/diablo-foothills). Beer and wine are allowed without a permit. Parking is a quarter mile away from picnic sites.

**ADA Accessibility:** The pathways within the park are wheelchair accessible. Other wheelchair accessible facilities in the park include restrooms, changing rooms and drinking fountains. All picnic sites have ADA picnic tables available. If you require transportation assistance, please contact the park staff. 

**For Picnic Reservations,****please call our Reservations Department.****Phone:** 1-888-EBPARKS ([888-327-2757](tel:1-888-327-2757)), option 2.
