\- Proctor staging area, Redwood Road, Oakland

\- Bear Creek staging area, Bear Creek Road east of Orinda Village.  
\- Alhambra Creek staging area, off Reliez Valley Road, Martinez.

\- Laughlin Ranch staging area, 3837 Laughlin Road, Livermore 94551 (north end of Laughlin Road in Livermore).

\- Nejedley Staging Area, Carquinez Scenic Drive, Martinez  
\- Eckley Pier staging area, downhill from the Bull Valley staging area, Carquinez Scenic Drive, Crockett.

\- End of Frederickson Lane, Antioch. Follow directions in park for best staging
  * [Coyote Hills Regional Park](/parks/coyote-hills)  
\- Quarry staging area, Patterson Ranch Road, Fremont
  * [Crockett Hills Regional Park](/parks/crockett-hills)  
\- Crockett Ranch staging area, Crockett Blvd, Crockett
  * [Diablo Foothills Regional Park](/parks/diablo-foothills)  
\- Orchard staging area, end Castle Rock Road, Walnut Creek  
\- Livorna staging Area, Livorna Road, Alamo.
  * [Del Valle Regional Park](/parks/del-valle)  
\- Arroyo Road staging area, south end of Arroyo Road, Livermore  
\- Numerous staging areas in the park, end of Del Valle Road, Livermore.
  * [Don Castro Regional Recreation Area](/parks/don-castro)  
\- North end of Woodroe Ave., Castro Valley.
  * [Dublin Hills Regional Park](/parks/dublin-hills)  
\- Donlon Point staging area, east of the intersection of Dublin Boulevard and Marshall Canyon Drive, Dublin.
  * [Dry Creek Pioneer Regional Park](/parks/garin)  
\- Staging area at end of May Road, Union City. Due to limited space, please use the overflow lots.  
Short trailers are best due to narrow access road and turning radius.  
Weekdays are best as space fills up quickly on weekends April through October.
  * [Garin Regional Park](/parks/garin)  
\- East end of Garin Ave., Hayward.  
Due to limited space, please use the overflow lots.  
Weekdays are best as space fills up quickly on weekends April through October.
  * [Las Trampas Regional Wilderness](/parks/las-trampas)  
\- Just past the park entry gate on Bollinger Canyon Road (San Ramon) on the east side of the road.
  * [Mission Peak Regional Preserve](/parks/mission)

  * [Morgan Territory Regional Preserve](/parks/morgan)  
\- Morgan Territory Road staging area  
\- Morgan Territory Road, Livermore
  * [Pleasanton Ridge Regional Park](/parks/pleasanton)  
\- Foothill staging area, Foothill Road, south of Castlewood Drive, Pleasanton

  * [Reinhardt Redwood Regional Park](/parks/reinhardt-redwood)

\- MacDonald Gate staging area, Redwood Road, Oakland, about 1/2 mile SW of the Canyon Meadow staging area  
\- Big Bear staging area, Redwood Road, Oakland, about 1/4 mile SW of the MacDonald Gate Staging Area  
\- Trudeau Center, 11500 Skyline Boulevard, Oakland
  * [Roberts Regional Recreation Area](/parks/roberts/)  
\- Staging area at 10570 Skyline Boulevard, Oakland  
\- Redwood Bowl staging area, Skyline Boulevard, Oakland, just south of the Chabot Space and Science Center.
  * [Round Valley Regional Preserve](/parks/round-valley)

  * [Sibley Volcanic Regional Preserve](/parks/sibley)

  * [Tilden Regional Park](/parks/tilden)
