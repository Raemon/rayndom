## Administrative Staff

The East Bay Regional Parks District Fire Department and Police Department are both managed by the Assistant General Manager for the Public Safety Division. The Fire Chief oversees the Fire Department with the help of the Assistant Fire Chief and Assistant Chief of Lifeguard Service. The administrative team also includes a Secretary and an Administrative Analyst.
