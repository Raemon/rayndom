Public Feedback - We want to hear from you!

For _**Emergencies**_ or _**Public Safety**_ concerns, contact our Public Safety Dispatch as follows:  
**Emergency/Crime in Progress: Call 911 or[(510) 881-1121](tel:1-510-881-1121)** from a cell, 24 hours a day.  
**Non-Emergency Public Safety Dispatch Line:** [(510) 881-1833](tel:1-510-881-1833), 24 hours a day,  
or submit a **[Park Watch Report](/public-safety/incident/park-watch-report)** online.

**Non-Emergency Trail Safety Hazards or Damaged Trails/Facilities:** Locate a park ranger or call Public Safety Dispatch at [(510) 881-1833](tel:1-510- 881-1833). Be sure to mention the nature and exact location of the problem including the trail or area name.

**General Questions:** 1-888-EBPARKS / [1-888-327-2757](tel:1-888-327-2757) (Mon-Fri, 8:30am to 5pm)  
**Reservations:** [1-888-327-2757](tel:1-888-327-2757), select option 2 (Mon-Fri, 9am-4pm)

For all other inquiries, please send a message using the link below.
