# Retiree Benefit Administration

__Back to top

## **Welcome Notice**

Welcome to the Park District’s Retiree Benefit Administration webpage. This is your central hub for information regarding the benefits provided by the Park District. Your retiree benefits are a well-deserved reward for your years of service. Here, you can learn how to make the most of your benefits and make any necessary changes.

Stay engaged with the Park District by visiting this portal regularly to stay informed about the latest updates. This site will be updated throughout the calendar year to ensure you have access to the most current information.

## **Quick Announcements**

Annual Retiree Substantiation Required:

  * [2026 Annual AARP Retiree Notice (PDF)](/sites/default/files/20251026-final-notice-to-retiree-2026-aarp-annual-reimbursement.pdf)
  * [2026 Annual Substantiation Retiree Notice (PDF)](/sites/default/files/20251124-Notice-to-Retiree-2026-Annual-Substantiation.pdf)
  * [2026 Medicare Part B Reimbursement Retiree Notice (PDF)](/sites/default/files/20251124-Notice-to-Retiree-Medicare-Part-B-Reimbursement.pdf)



For more information about your retiree reimbursements or billing, please visit the [Retirement Reimbursements and Billings](/retirement-reimbursements-and-billing) Link  
  
For more information about the 401a or 457 Deferred Compensation Plan, please visit the [Deferred Compensation Plan](/deferred-compensation-plan) Link  
  
To learn more about the discussion and recommendations provided by the Deferred Compensation Advisory Committee, please review past meeting agendas and notes at the  [Deferred Comp Advisory Committee](/deferred-compensation-advisory-committee) Link.

## **Contact US**

For general questions related to your retiree benefits, call the Human Resources Department at (510) 544-2154 or send an email to [[email protected]](/cdn-cgi/l/email-protection#c48c9686a1aaa1a2adb0b784a1a6b4a5b6afb7eaabb6a3).
