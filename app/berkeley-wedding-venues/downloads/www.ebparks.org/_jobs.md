## Human Resources Division

The Human Resources Division is dedicated to:

  * providing an innovative workplace that fosters equality and professional growth to support the District's vision and mission.
  * serving as a strategic business partner committed to pursuing excellence in recruiting, engaging, developing, and retaining a diverse and inclusive workforce that reflects the communities we serve.



_**The East Bay Regional Park District is an Equal Opportunity Employer.**_
