# Lake Chabot Picnic Areas - Castro Valley

__Back to top

## 

Lake Chabot Water Quality Conditions

  * A [Danger Advisory](https://www.ebparks.org/sites/default/files/HAB-Danger-2021-incl-boats.pdf "Danger Advisory") for blue-green algae is posted. See [Blue-Green Algae Information](https://www.ebparks.org/natural-resources/water-quality/blue-green-algae) for additional details.
  * **Swimming is never permitted at Lake Chabot.**

Fees

Parking: $5 per vehicle, $5 per trailered vehicle. Buses: $25/per bus.  
Daily fishing access: $5 per day.  
Boating: Boating, kayaking & sailing fees  
Lake tours: $10 per hour per adult, $6 per hour per 12 yr & under.  
Guided kayak tour: $45 per 2.5 hours per adult, $25 per 2.5 hours per 12 yr & under.  
Charter boat: $120 per hour.

## Venue Activities

Image

![Picnicing](/sites/default/files/2021-10/icon_activity_picnic.svg)

Picnicking

There are six (6) reservable sites at [Lake Chabot Regional Park](/parks/lake-chabot). Beer and wine are allowed without an additional permit.

#### Cove (200 person capacity)

Nearest site to concession and marina. Flush toilets approximately 50 yards away. Parking approximately 200 yards away.

  * [Site information](https://apm.activecommunities.com/ebparks/Facility_Search?facility_id=64)
  * Site photos



#### Elderberry (150 person capacity)

ADA accessible picnic table(s). Marina and flush toilets approximately 100 yards away. Parking next to site. Paved path to the site.

  * [Site information](https://apm.activecommunities.com/ebparks/Facility_Search?facility_id=69)
  * Site photos



#### Heron (75 person capacity)

Site is on top of lawn knoll; there is a moderate uphill/incline walk to the site. Farthest site from parking lot

  * [Site information](https://apm.activecommunities.com/ebparks/Facility_Search?facility_id=71)
  * Site photos



#### Mallard (50 person capacity)

Site is uphill. Parking and flush toilets approximately 200 yards away.

  * [Site information](https://apm.activecommunities.com/ebparks/Facility_Search?facility_id=73)
  * Site photos



#### Turtle (100 person capacity)

ADA accessible picnic table(s). Flush toilets approximately 200 yards away. Parking approximately 50 yards away.

  * [Site information](https://apm.activecommunities.com/ebparks/Facility_Search?facility_id=82)
  * Site photos



#### Willow (150 person capacity)

ADA accessible picnic table(s). Flush toilets approximately 50 yards away. Parking approximately 200 yards away.

  * [Site information](https://apm.activecommunities.com/ebparks/Facility_Search?facility_id=88)
  * Site photos
