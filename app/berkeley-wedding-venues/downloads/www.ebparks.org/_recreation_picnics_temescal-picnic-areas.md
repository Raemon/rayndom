# Temescal Picnic Areas - Oakland

__Back to top

## 

Lake Temescal Water Quality Conditions

  * Lake Temescal is CLOSED to swimming for the season.
  * A [Caution Advisory](https://www.ebparks.org/sites/default/files/HAB-Caution-2021.pdf) is posted for blue-green algae. For more information see [Blue-Green Algae](https://www.ebparks.org/natural-resources/water-quality/blue-green-algae).
  * [Monitoring Program](https://www.ebparks.org/recreation/swimming/lake-temescal#monitor)

## Venue Activities

Image

![Picnicing](/sites/default/files/2021-10/icon_activity_picnic.svg)

Picnicking

There are five (5) reservable sites at [Temescal Regional Recreation Area](/parks/temescal). Beer and wine are allowed without a permit.

#### Big Rock (50 person capacity)

There is no shade at this site. This site is near the south parking lot, but still a 200 yard walk away. Flush toilets are 100 yards away. This site has a view of the lake.

  * [Site information](https://apm.activecommunities.com/ebparks/Facility_Search?facility_id=117)
  * Site Photos



#### Landvale (75 person capacity)

There is no shade at this site. This site is near the north parking lot, but still a 50 yard walk away. Vault toilets are 100 yards away. This site is a wheelchair accessible site. ADA accessible picnic table(s). The freeway can be seen and heard at this site.

  * [Site information](https://apm.activecommunities.com/ebparks/Facility_Search?facility_id=118)
  * Site Photos



#### North Temescal (150 person capacity)

This site is near the north parking lot. Vault toilets nearby and are ADA accessible. This site is a wheelchair accessible site. ADA accessible picnic table(s). There is a view of the lake. The site is close to the “tot lot” and the fishing dock.

  * [Site information](https://apm.activecommunities.com/ebparks/Facility_Search?facility_id=120)
  * Site Photos



#### Parkview (75 person capacity)

This site is accessed by a short walk uphill. This site is near the south parking lot, but still a 150 yard walk away. Flush toilets are 75 yards away.

  * [Site information](https://apm.activecommunities.com/ebparks/Facility_Search?facility_id=123)
  * Site Photos



#### Streamside (75 person capacity)

This site is near the south parking lot, but still a 200 yard walk away. Flush toilets are 100 yards away. There is a lawn and stream adjacent to the site. This site has shade.

  * [Site information](https://apm.activecommunities.com/ebparks/Facility_Search?facility_id=126)
  * Site Photos



Visit the Picnic’s [Frequently Asked Questions](/recreation/picnics) for information on fees, rules, making a reservation, and other important information when making a picnic reservation.
