# District Fishing Permit

__Back to top

A [California Fishing License](https://wildlife.ca.gov/Licensing/Fishing) is required for anglers, (16+ years of age), to fish at all eligible inland waters (lakes, ponds and reservoirs) of the East Bay Regional Park District to offset the cost of fish plants at those locations. To learn more please visit our [Fishing Webpage](/recreation/fishing).

### Where are Park District Fishing Permits Required?

| Required| Not Required  
---|---|---  
Alameda Creek Trail|  | X  
Contra Loma| X|   
Cull Canyon| X|   
Del Valle| X|   
Don Castro|  | X  
Garin Jordan Pond|  | X  
Lake Chabot| X|   
Quarry Lakes (including Horseshoe and Rainbow Lakes)| X|   
Shadow Cliffs| X|   
Temescal| X|   
Tilden Lake Anza|  | X  
Unnamed Park District Pons|  | X  
  
### **District Fishing Permit Fees**

  * **Daily**  
A daily District Fishing Permit, ($5/day), can be purchased at most of the park entry kiosks or from dispensers found at many of the fishing lakes. Purchase a daily District Fishing Permit if you plan to fish at Contra Loma, Del Valle, Lake Chabot, Shadow Cliffs, Quarry Lakes, or Temescal. View [Fishing Information](https://www.ebparks.org/activities/fishing/default.htm).   
  
[Daily Fishing Permit (Online)](https://anc.apm.activecommunities.com/ebparks/reservation/landing/search/detail/557)
  * **Annual**  
[Regional Parks Foundation Membership](https://www.regionalparksfoundation.org/join/benefits) categories **Angler** ($300) and **Special Access Angler** ($180), include all the benefits of Basic Membership plus boat launch (excludes Invasive Mussel Inspection fees) and an annual District Fishing Permit for one named individual. 
  * A [California State Fishing License](https://wildlife.ca.gov/Licensing/Fishing) is also required for individuals 16+ years of age.
