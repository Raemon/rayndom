### Get Ready for the Golden Sneaker Contest: March 2–13!

During this much-loved event, Alameda County students, classrooms, and schools encourage one another to walk, roll, carpool, and/or use public transit by working as a class to increase participation. One winning classroom per school will receive a coveted Golden Sneaker trophy made with donated Golden State Warrior shoes. [Learn more!](https://alamedacountysr2s.org/our-services/plan-an-event/golden-sneaker-contest/)

### Healthy Kids, Safer Streets, Strong Communities

The Alameda County Safe Routes to Schools (SR2S) Program organizes and supports fun, educational activities that encourage families to walk, bike, carpool, and take transit to school. Whether you’re just getting started or want to get more involved, follow the links below to find what you need!
