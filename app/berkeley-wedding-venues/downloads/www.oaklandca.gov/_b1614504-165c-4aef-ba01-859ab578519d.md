  * [Mechanical, Electrical & Plumbing (MEP) Permits](https://www.oaklandca.gov/My-Household/Building-and-Remodeling/Homeowner-Projects-Permits/Mechanical-Electrical-Plumbing-MEP-Permits "Mechanical, Electrical & Plumbing \(MEP\) Permits") All mechanical, electrical & plumbing work requires permits.
  * [Ticket Distribution Training](https://www.oaklandca.gov/Government/Boards-Commissions/Public-Ethics-Commission/Government-Ethics/Ticket-Distribution-Training "Ticket Distribution Training") This training is intended for all City of Oakland Public Servants who distribute or receive City-issued event tickets.
  * [SB-9: Two-Unit Homes and Parcel Subdivisions](https://www.oaklandca.gov/My-Household/Building-and-Remodeling/Homeowner-Projects-Permits/SB-9-Two-Unit-Homes-and-Parcel-Subdivisions "SB-9: Two-Unit Homes and Parcel Subdivisions") SB-9 allow more homes to be built on single-family lots or to split the lot.
  * [Single Family Home](https://www.oaklandca.gov/My-Household/Building-and-Remodeling/Homeowner-Projects-Permits/Single-Family-Home "Single Family Home") New homes require Design Review and Building Permits.
  * [Swimming Pools & Hot Tubs](https://www.oaklandca.gov/My-Household/Building-and-Remodeling/Homeowner-Projects-Permits/Swimming-Pools-Hot-Tubs "Swimming Pools & Hot Tubs") Building new or altering or repairing an existing pool requires a Planning Review and Building Permits.
  * [House Stairways and Railings](https://www.oaklandca.gov/My-Household/Building-and-Remodeling/Homeowner-Projects-Permits/House-Stairways-and-Railings "House Stairways and Railings") To build, repair or replace your house's stairways or railings, you will need to obtain Planning approval and a Building Permit.
  * [Major Multi-Family Residential, Mixed-Use & Commercial Projects](https://www.oaklandca.gov/Planning-Building/Your-Commercial-Projects-Permits/Major-Multi-Family-Residential-Mixed-Use-Commercial-Projects "Major Multi-Family Residential, Mixed-Use & Commercial Projects") Major multi-family residential, mixed-use or large-scale commercial projects require Planning entitlements and Building Permits.
  * [Multi-Unit Residence (3-4 Units)](https://www.oaklandca.gov/Planning-Building/Your-Commercial-Projects-Permits/Multi-Unit-Residence-3-4-Units "Multi-Unit Residence \(3-4 Units\)") Multi-unit residences of 3-4 units or more require Design Review and Building Permits.
  * [Legalize Your Unpermitted Accessory Dwelling Unit (ADU) or Junior ADU](https://www.oaklandca.gov/My-Household/Building-and-Remodeling/Homeowner-Projects-Permits/Legalize-Your-Unpermitted-Accessory-Dwelling-Unit-ADU-or-Junior-ADU-JADU "Legalize Your Unpermitted Accessory Dwelling Unit \(ADU\) or Junior ADU") Learn how to legalize your unpermitted Accessory Dwelling Unit (ADU).
  * [ADU with Single Family Home](https://www.oaklandca.gov/My-Household/Building-and-Remodeling/Homeowner-Projects-Permits/Accessory-Dwelling-Units-ADUs/ADU-with-Single-Family-Home "ADU with Single Family Home") ADUs require Zoning Review and Building Permits.

You can report blight, housing or zoning violations online or with the OAK311 app.
  * [Check Status of Your Property Complaint](https://www.oaklandca.gov/Planning-Building/Code-Enforcement-Services/Check-Status-of-Your-Property-Complaint "Check Status of Your Property Complaint") The City of Oakland's goal is to keep our neighborhoods and community clean, safe and free of blight. You can report blight, housing or zoning violations online or with the OAK311 app.
  * [Apply to the Emergency Nuisance Abatement Program (Contractors)](https://www.oaklandca.gov/Planning-Building/Code-Enforcement-Services/Apply-to-the-Emergency-Nuisance-Abatement-Program-Contractors "Apply to the Emergency Nuisance Abatement Program \(Contractors\)") Apply to become a City contractor to help keep our neighborhoods and community clean, safe and free of blight.
  * [Replace Windows](https://www.oaklandca.gov/My-Household/Building-and-Remodeling/Homeowner-Projects-Permits/Replace-Windows "Replace Windows") Building Permits are required for any window replacement.
  * [Schedule Your Building Inspection](https://www.oaklandca.gov/Planning-Building/Building-Construction-Permits-Inspections/Building-Inspections/Schedule-Your-Building-Inspection "Schedule Your Building Inspection") Schedule your Building Inspection by downloading our scheduling app.
  * [Creek Protection Permits](https://www.oaklandca.gov/Community/Community-Development/Sustainability-Environment/Creeks-Watershed-and-Stormwater/Creek-Protection-Permits "Creek Protection Permits") Creek Protection Permits may be obtained through Zoning Review.
  * [Apply for a Temporary Certificate of Occupancy](https://www.oaklandca.gov/Planning-Building/Building-Construction-Permits-Inspections/Building-Permits/Apply-for-a-Temporary-Certificate-of-Occupancy-TCO "Apply for a Temporary Certificate of Occupancy") Only projects that are new (from ground up), new units, or where a change in occupancy is taking place are issued a TCO. Additions, remodels, alterations, and tenant improvements are not issued a TCO.
  * [Request Mediation of your Public Records Request](https://www.oaklandca.gov/Government/Boards-Commissions/Public-Ethics-Commission/Request-Mediation-of-your-Public-Records-Request "Request Mediation of your Public Records Request") The Public Ethics Commission conducts mediation of public records requests made by members of the public to City departments for records within the department’s control.
  * [Join the Public Ethics Commission](https://www.oaklandca.gov/Government/Boards-Commissions/Public-Ethics-Commission/Join-the-Public-Ethics-Commission "Join the Public Ethics Commission") The Public Ethics Commission seeks community members with a passion for public service to help influence policy toward greater fairness, openness, honesty, and integrity.

  * [Submit Revisions to Your Permitted Project](https://www.oaklandca.gov/Planning-Building/Permit-Process-Overview/Submit-Revisions-to-Your-Permitted-Project "Submit Revisions to Your Permitted Project") During construction, projects may encounter a need to revise their approved project plans. Below are the different types of revisions and processes to submit revised plans for review and approval.
  * [One-Stop Permit Center: In-Person & Online Services](https://www.oaklandca.gov/Planning-Building/Permit-Process-Overview/One-Stop-Permit-Center-In-Person-Online-Services "One-Stop Permit Center: In-Person & Online Services") Learn about our in-person and online Permit Center services.
  * [Transfer a Permit](https://www.oaklandca.gov/Planning-Building/Permit-Process-Overview/Transfer-a-Permit "Transfer a Permit") A Permit Transfer can be requested when a property is being sold and the new owner wants to continue work under an existing permit, a contractor changes during a construction project, or a developer or project manager is replaced mid-project.
  * [Stop Work Order Procedures](https://www.oaklandca.gov/Planning-Building/Building-Construction-Permits-Inspections/Building-Inspections/Stop-Work-Order-Procedures "Stop Work Order Procedures") If you received a Stop Work from a City of Oakland Building Inspector, you must stop all construction activities. You must either revise your permits, correct the violations, and/or apply for permits depending on the type of project, as per the three types are described on this page.
  * [Apply to Early Childhood and Family Services Options](https://www.oaklandca.gov/Community/Child-and-Youth-Services/Early-Childhood-and-Family-Services/Apply-to-Early-Childhood-and-Family-Services-Options "Apply to Early Childhood and Family Services Options") Find out if you are eligible and how to apply for the City of Oakland Early Childhood and Family Services Options.
  * [Housing Projects Eligible for Faster, Streamlined Review](https://www.oaklandca.gov/Planning-Building/Your-Commercial-Projects-Permits/Housing-Projects-Eligible-for-Faster-Streamlined-Review "Housing Projects Eligible for Faster, Streamlined Review") Most housing projects are now eligible for faster, streamlined review, from single family homes to multifamily buildings, along with affordable or mixed-income housing projects.
  * [Pay a Parking Ticket](https://www.oaklandca.gov/Public-Safety-Streets/Parking-Vehicles/Parking-Tickets/Pay-a-Parking-Ticket "Pay a Parking Ticket") Pay your Oakland parking ticket easily online, by phone, mail, or in person! Avoid late fees by paying on time.
  * [Special Event Permit Application](https://www.oaklandca.gov/Community/Events-Culture/Special-Events-Permits/Special-Event-Permit-Application "Special Event Permit Application") Ready to host an unforgettable event in Oakland? Our streamlined special event permitting process makes it easy! Whether you’re planning a festival, concert, or gathering, we’re here to help.
  * [Additions & Conversions to Habitable Space](https://www.oaklandca.gov/My-Household/Building-and-Remodeling/Homeowner-Projects-Permits/Additions-Conversions-to-Habitable-Space "Additions & Conversions to Habitable Space") Additions or conversions require Design Review and Building Permits.
  * [Deck Permits](https://www.oaklandca.gov/My-Household/Building-and-Remodeling/Homeowner-Projects-Permits/Deck-Permits "Deck Permits") Decks require Building Permits, and Zoning Review may be required.
  * [Sign Permits](https://www.oaklandca.gov/Planning-Building/Your-Commercial-Projects-Permits/Sign-Permits "Sign Permits") Business signs require a Zoning Permit.
  * [Register as a Lobbyist](https://www.oaklandca.gov/Government/Boards-Commissions/Public-Ethics-Commission/Lobbyist-Rules/Register-as-a-Lobbyist "Register as a Lobbyist") Register before lobbying activity begins
  * [Ask for a Lobbyist Registration Fee Waiver](https://www.oaklandca.gov/Government/Boards-Commissions/Public-Ethics-Commission/Lobbyist-Rules/Ask-for-a-Lobbyist-Registration-Fee-Waiver "Ask for a Lobbyist Registration Fee Waiver") Lobbyists representing non-profit organizations and small businesses that meet certain tax status and/or income requirements may apply for reduced or waived registration fees.
  * [Fence Permits](https://www.oaklandca.gov/My-Household/Building-and-Remodeling/Homeowner-Projects-Permits/Fence-Permits "Fence Permits") Fences 3.5' to 6' in front yards require a Zoning Permit, and may require a Building Permit.
  * [Gates](https://www.oaklandca.gov/Planning-Building/Your-Commercial-Projects-Permits/Gates "Gates") Gates 3.5' or taller in front of a property require Planning approval, and if over 7' high, a Building Permit.
