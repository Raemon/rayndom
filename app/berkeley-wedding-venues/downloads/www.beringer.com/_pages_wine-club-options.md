##  How to take advantage of the perks just for members 

![](//www.beringer.com/cdn/shop/files/beringer-favicon.png?v=1752076587&width=320)

COMPLIMENTARY TASTINGS 

Beringer club members receive complimentary tastings for up to 4 adults (6 adults for Collectors' Circle members), or discounted tastings for the upgraded experiences. Taste of Beringer and Reserve wine club members receive 4 complimentary tastings per calendar year. Collector's Circle wine club members receive 6 complimentary tastings per calendar year. Reservations are made on our website's [tastings page](/pages/visit "Visit"), which is connected to our reservation system, Tock. Tock requires a separate account sign-up to use their system, but once you have the account it will be easy to book all subsequent tastings. Or you can give us a call at [(707) 257-5771 ](tel:707-257-5771 "call us")Monday-Saturday 9am-6pm and Sunday 9am-5pm and we’d be happy to make the reservation for you over the phone.

![](//www.beringer.com/cdn/shop/files/beringer-favicon.png?v=1752076587&width=320)

DISCOUNTS 

To apply your member discount - 20% off wine, 10% off merchandise - you can shop online or at our tasting room. To shop online, [log into your account](/account/login?return_url=%2Faccount "/account/login?return_url=%2Faccount"). As you shop under "WINES", you will see your member pricing already applied. This pricing should flow through to checkout. If you have any issues, please contact Customer Service.

To shop at our tasting room, where there is a much larger selection of merchandise and some winery-only wines, please notify your host that you are a member and they will apply your member discount.

![](//www.beringer.com/cdn/shop/files/beringer-favicon.png?v=1752076587&width=320)

MEMBER-ONLY EVENTS 

Beringer hosts members at a number of intimate events like our annual Valentine's Day Dinner and Collectors' Circle appreciation to larger festive events like our Wine Club on the Road where we host pop-ups across the country. Visit our [Events](https://beringer-vineyards.myshopify.com/pages/winery-events "Winery Events") page to reserve your spot at our upcoming events.

![](//www.beringer.com/cdn/shop/files/beringer-favicon.png?v=1752076587&width=320)

EARLY ACCESS 

We will notify you via email when there is a new wine coming out that has limited production. Typically, we will also make this wine available to you first via your club shipments.

![](//www.beringer.com/cdn/shop/files/beringer-favicon.png?v=1752076587&width=320)

DEDICATED OFFERS 

Enjoy specially curated member exclusive summer and holiday optional wine club shipments.

Special [Gold Status](https://www.thewineshop.com/en/goldstatus.html?utm_medium=email&utm_source=sfmc&utm_campaign=Gold+Status+-+HEW+2+years&utm_term=&utm_content=da81e073-3cdc-ea11-a813-000d3a3037f8&registered=yes&suppress=true) offers for members with 2 & 5 year tenure including access to The Wine Shop and complimentary tastings and member exclusive events at our sister wineries.

Hotel discounts on stays in Napa Valley. Some blackout dates may apply.

  * [Alila Napa Valley](https://www.hyatt.com/en-US/hotel/california/alila-napa-valley/apcal?offercode=NVMBR): Exclusive room rates & 15% off treatments at Spa Alila



![](//www.beringer.com/cdn/shop/files/beringer-favicon.png?v=1752076587&width=320)

CLUB PACK ADD-ONS 

With $25 flat rate shipping on club orders, it's a great time to stock up and add more of your favorite Beringer wines to your club pack. Ten days before your club shipment, you will receive an email with all the details including a link to customize your club order.

![](//www.beringer.com/cdn/shop/files/beringer-favicon.png?v=1752076587&width=320)

HOST YOUR OWN BERINGER PRIVATE EXPERIENCE 

Members receive discounts on private events at the winery for 15 or more guests. Please [contact](/pages/groups-inquiry "Groups Inquiry") our events team for details and special pricing.
