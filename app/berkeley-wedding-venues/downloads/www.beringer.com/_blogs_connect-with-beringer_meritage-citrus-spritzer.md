# Meritage Citrus Spritzer

Jun 17, 2025

![Citrus Spritzer](//www.beringer.com/cdn/shop/articles/1000X650_BER_Spritz.png?v=1750058194&width=320)

Discover the perfect blend of elegance and refreshment with our Citrus Spritzer, a zesty lemon-infused cocktail showcasing our premium 2021 Winery Exclusive Meritage White Wine. This vibrant and crisp creation captures the essence of our exclusive collection while offering a sophisticated taste experience. Ideal for spring celebrations or casual Sunday brunches, our Meritage White Wine adds vibrant citrus notes, gentle floral hints, and subtle mineral undertones to this elegant drink. Let each refreshing sip transport you to the sunny landscapes of our vineyard estates.

### Ingredients

  * 4 oz Beringer Winery 2021 Winery Exclusive Meritage White Wine
  * 1 oz fresh lemon juice
  * 1 oz Simple syrup Club soda, to top off
  * Ice
  * Lemon or lime wheel and fresh mint, for garnish



### Instructions

  1. Fill a wine glass or a stemless glass with ice.
  2. Add the Beringer Winery 2021 Winery Exclusive Meritage Wine Wine, fresh lemon juice, and simple syrup to the glass.
  3. Stir gently to combine the ingredients.
  4. Top off with a splash of club soda for a refreshing fizz.
  5. Garnish with a lemon wheel and a sprig of fresh mint.
  6. Savor your Meritage Citrus Spritzer!



![2021 Winery Exclusive Knights Valley Meritage White Wine](https://cdn.shopify.com/s/files/1/0680/7266/9353/files/BER_21-SC-Meritage-White-Wine-WE.png?v=1750058167)

## 2021 Winery Exclusive Knights Valley Meritage White Wine

A latest white blend, the Winery Exclusive wines are particularly special. In this blend, we combined Semillon and Sauvignon Blanc that displays floral aromatics, like chamomile, that are balanced by crisp minerality.
