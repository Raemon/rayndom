# St. Helena's Holiday Wine Barrel Tree

Jun 17, 2025

![St. Helena's Holiday Wine Barrel Tree](//www.beringer.com/cdn/shop/articles/1000X650_BER_Barrel_Tree.png?v=1768605978&width=320)

_Made up of 109 wine barrels donated by Beringer Vineyards_

## Holidays in St.Helena

The city of [St. Helena](https://www.sthelena.com/ "City of St. Helena") and the [St. Helena Chamber of Commerce](https://www.sthelenachamber.com/ "St. Helena Chamber of Commerce") are making spirits bright with holiday décor and a series of family-friendly activities scheduled for St. Helena residents and visitors throughout December. Beringer Vineyards has donated 109 wine barrels to help create St. Helena's one-of-a-kind holiday wine barrel tree which is located at Hunt Ave & Main Street until January 2nd. What a joy and privilege it is for Beringer to be part of so many memories being made this holiday season!

![St. Helena Holiday Wine Barrel Tree](https://cdn.shopify.com/s/files/1/0680/7266/9353/files/840X1100_Barrel_Tree.png?v=1750052407)

## St. Helena Holiday Wine Barrel Tree Fun Facts

### Provided by the St. Helena Chamber of Commerce

  1. 109 Total Wine Barrels
  2. All Barrels Donated by Beringer
  3. Approximately 10,500 Pounds
  4. Nearly 32 Feet Tall
  5. Earthquake Proof
  6. 232 Feet of Garland Used
  7. Nearly 1,800 Ornaments



![Beringer Vineyards Rhine House](https://cdn.shopify.com/s/files/1/0680/7266/9353/files/840X550_BER_Lights.png?v=1749775492)

## Spend the Holidays at Beringer

The Holidays at Beringer are a special time, if you haven't visited, we highly recommend it. Every inch of the property is decorated for the holiday season, from the historic Rhine House to our wine caves. We hope to see you for an exquisite tasting experience.

![Bancroft Ranch](https://cdn.shopify.com/s/files/1/0680/7266/9353/files/Bancroft-Ranch-Merlot-3866-September-2019-photo-credit-Mark-Beringer_840x650_e4957480-97f9-4914-99a1-1c6f4395f150.jpg?v=1749775524)

## Upcoming Events in St. Helena

Explore St. Helena before 2023 is over. The wineries, restaurants, and local shops are like no other. The city has events for everyone in the family to enjoy this holiday season.
