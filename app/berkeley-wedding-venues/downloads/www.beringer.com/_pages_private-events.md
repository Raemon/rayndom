##  rehearsal dinners 

###  AT THE HUDSON HOUSE 

Beringer Vineyards embodies the history of Napa Valley, the craftsmanship of fine wines, and the culinary delights that showcase our wines to their fullest. Gather your clients, teammates, friends or family for an elegant dinner to celebrate a special occasion, customer appreciation or a momentous milestone. 

  * This luxury reception and three course, wine paired meal is an all-inclusive experience starting at $385++ per person, for up to 90 guests.
  * Luxury tier wines can be added for an additional $25++ per person.
  * For those interested in a bit of history, start the evening off with a tour of our iconic property and a wine reception in the historic caves, for an additional fee.
  * We have space for up to 150 guests in our beautiful Citrus Grove. For less than 60 guests, the Redwood Grove creates a magical intimate dinner center in the circle of these giant trees. 
  * For events with more than 90 guests, rentals and decor will be an additional cost.
  * With a food and beverage minimum of $3,750++, we are able to accommodate intimate groups.
