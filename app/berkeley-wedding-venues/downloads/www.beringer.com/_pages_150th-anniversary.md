##  Celebrating a Century and a Half of Legendary Winemaking 

In 2026, Beringer Vineyards proudly marks 150 years as Napa Valley’s longest continuously operating winery—a legacy built on vision, craftsmanship and innovation since 1876. This milestone is more than an anniversary; it’s a tribute to generations of winemakers, the beauty of Napa Valley, and the enduring spirit that shaped California wine.

Throughout the year, we invite you to join us for an extraordinary calendar of events designed to honor our past and toast to the future. From historic tours and immersive tastings to culinary experiences and live music weekends, every moment celebrates the artistry and resilience that define Beringer.

[ Discover Our Story ](https://www.beringer.com/pages/pioneer-in-napa)
