# Beringer 2023 Gift Guide

Jun 17, 2025

![Beringer 2023 Gift Guide](//www.beringer.com/cdn/shop/articles/1000X650_BER_Gift_Guide_Image.png?v=1768607466&width=320)

#### Gifting Big and Small

Since the first vintage in 1876, Beringer wines have been on dining room tables for celebrations both big and small. At this table is where the memories are made, and stories are told. Our collection of iconic wines is the gift of experience for family, friends, and clients alike. We offer gifts both large and small to take care of every special person on your list.

![Private Reserve Collection](https://cdn.shopify.com/s/files/1/0680/7266/9353/files/840X650_Holiday_Private_Reserve.png?v=1750052658)

## Private Reserve For All Occasions

Our Private Reserve Collection is for the times and occasions worth celebrating. These wines are not only great for the connoisseurs and collectors in your life, but there’s a history and full sensory experience in every bottle which makes them a memorable gift for anyone on your list.

![Knights Valley collection](https://cdn.shopify.com/s/files/1/0680/7266/9353/files/840X650_BER_KnightsValley.png?v=1750052690)

## Knights Valley Wines For Every Party

Gift a wine from our Knights Valley collection, a range of styles and flavors we achieve in the remarkable Knights Valley region.

![Beringer Magnum Bottles](https://cdn.shopify.com/s/files/1/0680/7266/9353/files/840X650_BER_PR_CAB.png?v=1750052710)

## Magnum Bottles For Every Table

Our large format options are a stand-out gift, offering a cherished treasure and a great display piece. They also add luster to any holiday party and dinner table. We’d be honored if you gifted them to friends, colleagues, family, and loved ones or shared them with guests during your next memory-making celebration.

![Beringer Stocking](https://cdn.shopify.com/s/files/1/0680/7266/9353/files/420X550_BER_KV_Stocking.png?v=1750052734)

## 375ML Bottles For Every Stocking

Our small format options are a perfect size to add to any gift or in any stocking. Smaller in size, but still feature the same award-winning wines that will impress anyone receiving them.

![Beringer Gifting](https://cdn.shopify.com/s/files/1/0680/7266/9353/files/840X650_Holiday_Knights_Valley_Corp_Gifting.png?v=1750052759)

## Say Thank You With Beringer

Our dedicated wine concierges take the work out of corporate and employee gifting, building a custom plan to fit every client and budget.

![Beringer Wine Club](https://cdn.shopify.com/s/files/1/0680/7266/9353/files/840X650_BER_WINECLUB.png?v=1750052795)

## Gift Yourself a Wine Club Membership

  * 20% off wine, 10% off merchandise
  * 20% off select tours for up to six people
  * Complimentary tasting +3 guests
  * Invitations to exclusive events
  * Dedicated wine ambassador when you visit
