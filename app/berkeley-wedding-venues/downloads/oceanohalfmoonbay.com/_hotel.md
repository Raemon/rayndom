# HOTEL 

The Oceano Hotel and Spa, Half Moon Bay’s premier luxury hotel, featuring magnificent views of the coast, incredible harbor sunsets, and lavish accommodations. Our interior features a refined décor that merges seamlessly with our seaport style architecture and the natural surroundings. Each of our 95-suites is adorned with a fireplace and private veranda overlooking the spectacular coastal scenery. And next to the Oceano Hotel in Half Moon Bay is our quaint 11-room Oceano Inn. After a day of exploring numerous restaurants and seaside activities, visit the Oceano Coastal Spa for a tranquil and soothing spa treatment before watching the sunset from the decks at the Oceano Bar & Grill. Discover all the Oceano Hotel and Half Moon Bay has to offer.

### LOCALLY SOURCED FOOD 

## CHEF PREPARED 

The Oceano Bar & Grill, located in the Lobby of The Oceano Hotel, offers scenic harbor views and outdoor seating in a casual setting. Our menu features fresh seafood and locally-sourced ingredients from nearby farms, bringing you the unique flavors of the Pacific Coastline. Alongside great food is a full service bar featuring local wines, craft beers, and specialty cocktails. Stop off at The Oceano Bar & Grill for a taste of Half Moon Bay and a nightcap while enjoying a beautiful harbor sunset. If you are staying at The Oceano Hotel, enjoy all of the Bar & Grill menu offerings right from your suite with in-room dining.

  * Local ingredients
  * Fresh seafood
  * Local wine & beer
  * Seasonal menu
  * Full bar & bartender
  * Catering for events & weddings
