# Oceano Grand Villa

## PREMIER 2-BEDROOM VILLA WITH MOUNTAIN VIEWS

## Summary

  * 2 separate bedrooms



  * 1460 ft2



  * 2 separate bathrooms



The Oceano Grand Villa provides the ultimate in accommodations for families or extended-stay travelers. The two-bedroom and two-bath suite, offers a master bedroom with king size bed and a master bath with deep soaking tub and walk-in granite shower. The additional bedroom offers two double beds. The suite’s second bathroom is easily accessible for all guests and has a shower and tub combination. The spacious full kitchen and living room with fireplace, create the prefect home away from home experience. Enjoy a large balcony off the living room with peaceful views of the Santa Cruz Mountain range.
