### Half Moon Bay

# HOTEL & INN 

### Suites & Villas

Oceano Hotel is a harbor front place of refuge. Our rooms are spacious, divided from a full bathroom, a bedroom, and a living area. The hotel rooms are on all 3 floors of the main hotel. If you would like more space or are staying for an extended period of travel, we also have Villas. The Villas have a private hallway attached to the main hotel, giving you an extra layer of privacy.

### Oceano Inn

Oceano Inn is a quaint cottage style Inn, and a separate building from the main hotel. The Oceano Inn is a great option for large weddings groups, or people searching for a more affordable rate on the same incredible hotel property. With the Inn sitting closer to the harbor, you get to experience true coastal comfort.

To book the entire Inn for your group call 650-726-5400.
