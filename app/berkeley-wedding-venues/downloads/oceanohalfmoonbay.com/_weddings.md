#  Romantic Half Moon Bay Wedding Venues 

Celebrate your love story at one of the most enchanting Half Moon Bay wedding venues—Oceano Hotel & Spa. With sweeping ocean views, elegant indoor and outdoor settings, and personalized planning, we turn dream weddings into stunning realities. Whether you’re envisioning an intimate gathering or a grand celebration, our coastal location offers the perfect backdrop for memories that last a lifetime.

From the moment you arrive, you’ll be surrounded by the natural beauty of the California coast, paired with luxury accommodations and exceptional service. Let your Half Moon Bay wedding be as timeless as your love.

## Top Wedding Venues

![destination weddings Half Moon Bay](data:image/svg+xml,%3Csvg%20xmlns='http://www.w3.org/2000/svg'%20viewBox='0%200%20327%20234'%3E%3C/svg%3E)![destination weddings Half Moon Bay](https://oceanohalfmoonbay.com/wp-content/uploads/2022/06/weddings-grandballroom_huf1b4c09426728259192ba928ea6a59c7_36306_327x0_resize_q80_lanczos.webp)

### GRAND BALLROOM / ATRIUM

The Oceano Grand Ballroom & Atrium is an impressive 4,400 square feet. The Grand Ballroom is elegantly lit with golden chandeliers, while the Atrium offers an ethereal glass ceiling for your first dance under the stars. Your wedding coordinator can also recommend lighting vendors who can create a unique setting for your Half Moon Bay wedding reception.

![destination weddings Half Moon Bay](data:image/svg+xml,%3Csvg%20xmlns='http://www.w3.org/2000/svg'%20viewBox='0%200%20327%20218'%3E%3C/svg%3E)![destination weddings Half Moon Bay](https://oceanohalfmoonbay.com/wp-content/uploads/2022/06/weddings-gatehouse_hu62278bd5e812d1e3dfff18f16dabc56d_224943_327x0_resize_q80_lanczos.webp)

### THE GATE HOUSE

Nestled in the garden is our newest event space, The Gate House. This rustic event space is reminiscent of a coastal barn with dark wooden beams, wrought-iron chandeliers, and hardwood floor. Glass French doors open onto a scenic white patio, and a stone fireplace is the perfect accent for a reception in the garden, making it one of the most charming wedding venues Half Moon Bay CA has to offer.

![destination weddings Half Moon Bay](data:image/svg+xml,%3Csvg%20xmlns='http://www.w3.org/2000/svg'%20viewBox='0%200%20327%20234'%3E%3C/svg%3E)![destination weddings Half Moon Bay](https://oceanohalfmoonbay.com/wp-content/uploads/2022/06/weddings-garden_huf1b4c09426728259192ba928ea6a59c7_32988_327x0_resize_q80_lanczos-1.webp)

### WEDDING GARDEN

The seaside garden venue offers a romantic outdoor setting for your ceremony. The surrounding white fence and ornamental bushes provide privacy and complement the coastal atmosphere. Wedding guests await the bride’s entry while enjoying scenic harbor views. Italian Cypress Trees and the white arbor provide the perfect canvas for floral arrangements or classic draping decor, making it one of the most picturesque waterfront wedding venues in California.
