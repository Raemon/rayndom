### Half Moon Bay

# EXPERIENCES 

Half Moon Bay, California, is a unique destination on the Golden State’s Pacific Coastline. With a rich history in exploration and agriculture dating back to the 1800s, there is so much to see along the Coastline. For a pace-altering experience, visit the grand California Redwood preserves or tour one of the many specialty farms. Stroll historic downtown Main Street for dozens of incredible boutique restaurants and shops. For those seeking a peaceful refuge, the calming sound of waves along the shoreline greet you at Pillar Point Harbor. Picturesque walking trails, especially the Coastal Trail and Mavericks Trail, provide the ultimate photo opportunities with spectacular views of the Pacific Ocean. To plan your next getaway to charming Half Moon Bay, you won’t need to look any further than The Oceano Hotel & Spa.
