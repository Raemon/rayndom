# Your Perfect Half Moon Bay Event Space

Host your next gathering where the ocean meets inspiration. The Oceano Hotel & Spa offers a premier Half Moon Bay event space designed to elevate every occasion—from team-building retreats to elegant galas. With over 12,000 sq. ft. of flexible venues including a grand ballroom, waterfront meeting rooms, and a charming outdoor courtyard, we provide a setting that is as breathtaking as it is functional.

### [GRAND BALLROOM / ATRIUM ](https://my.matterport.com/show/?m=9LDe6i4KDxe)

The Oceano Grand Ballroom & Atrium is an impressive 4,400 square feet. The Grand Ballroom is the ultimate space for large conference groups, while the adjacent Atrium is the perfect setting for dining or breakout sessions. The Atrium features a glass skylight with natural lighting and lounge-style furniture. In the evening, the Atrium can be converted to a cocktail reception or dinner space, making it a versatile Half Moon Bay event space for any occasion.

[![meetings and offsite events](data:image/svg+xml,%3Csvg%20xmlns='http://www.w3.org/2000/svg'%20viewBox='0%200%20792%20495'%3E%3C/svg%3E)![meetings and offsite events](https://oceanohalfmoonbay.com/wp-content/uploads/2022/06/Montara-Room-Photo.webp)](https://my.matterport.com/show/?m=ZiTdLCFbdWE)

### [MONTARA ROOM](https://my.matterport.com/show/?m=ZiTdLCFbdWE)

The Montara Room is the ideal space for business meetings. Sliding glass doors open onto a balcony overlooking coastal views and provide natural lighting to the space. Features of the Montara Room include three decorative columns separating the general session from the dining space, as well as a built-in screen for projection, making it a perfect Half Moon Bay meeting space for productive and inspiring gatherings. 

[![meetings and offsite events](data:image/svg+xml,%3Csvg%20xmlns='http://www.w3.org/2000/svg'%20viewBox='0%200%20715%20506'%3E%3C/svg%3E)![meetings and offsite events](https://oceanohalfmoonbay.com/wp-content/uploads/2022/06/mirada.webp)](https://my.matterport.com/show/?m=cfQbw8QVsna)

### [MIRADA ROOM ](https://my.matterport.com/show/?m=cfQbw8QVsna)

The Mirada Room is the perfect location for smaller-sized meetings. This event space offers natural lighting and sliding glass doors that lead out to decks overlooking a landscaped garden and harbor views. Features of the Mirada Room include a built-in screen for projection, making it an ideal choice for corporate offsites Bay Area professionals will appreciate.

### [THE GATE HOUSE ](https://my.matterport.com/show/?m=271r1VBghDG)
