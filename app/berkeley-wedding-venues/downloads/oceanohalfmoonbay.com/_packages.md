## FAQs

###  ____ What types of vacation packages are available at Oceano Hotel & Spa?

The Oceano Hotel & Spa offers a variety of packages, including romantic getaways, weekend retreats, and family-friendly escapes. Each package is curated to highlight luxury, comfort, and coastal experiences.

###  ____ Does the hotel offer spa treatments?

Yes, the on-site Oceano Coastal Spa provides exclusive [spa](https://oceanohalfmoonbay.com/spa/) treatments in Bay Area, CA to enhance your stay with relaxation and wellness options.

###  ____ Can I customize a romantic getaway package?

Yes, couples can enjoy personalized touches, including upgraded services such as bottle of sparkling wine, rose petal service, and box of chocolates. Add on a spa treatment for an extra layer of indulgence. Contact our team via the Contact Page for more details.

###  ____ Are the packages suitable for family trips?

Absolutely. Our packages accommodate families and friends, with easy access to beaches, hiking trails, and coastal attractions ideal for all ages, plus relaxing spa options for adults.
