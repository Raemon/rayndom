## About this vendor

![Domenica Goode](https://cdn0.weddingwire.com/vendor/617170/original/960/jpg/uo_2071716_29468.jpeg?r=1726475064)

#####  Domenica Goode 

Owner 

__Woman-owned

__No team details yet

Cartalia is a wedding stationery service operating out of Newport Beach, California, specializing in personalized, one-of-a-kind designs. This luxury business was founded in 2012 by London College of Fashion graduate Domenica Francesca Goode who instilled a fine art sensibility into its creations. The company continues to provide timeless handmade stationery containing the most intricate of details effortlessly produced on premium paper.

**Services Offered**

The Cartalia team specializes in elegant laser cutting and foiling with a lavish artistic edge. From conception to the final print, couples can enjoy an attentive service beginning with a consultation with an in-house graphic designer. Once a bespoke design has been finalized, the team gets to work handcrafting the unique creation with an array of chosen embellishments, including letterpress, color ink, and ribbons. Couples can opt for:

  * Pocket fold invitaitons
  * Passport invites
  * RSVP cards
  * Save the dates
  * Thank you cards
  * Postponement cards
