## About

Tiburon Tavern Restaurant presents a cozy aesthetic with a rustic-chic and timeless elegance. With a culinary philosophy ruled by the seasons, the Tavern’s menus are created with ingredients selected fresh daily from Marin County’s Certified Organic Farming community and locally and regionally-sourced ingredients from farms and purveyors. Located in the heart of downtown Tiburon, California and attached to the The Lodge at Tiburon, minutes away from local shops and vendors with great waterside photo oppotunities. Tiburon Tavern is owned and ran by the same well known and local owners of Luna Blu, Renzo and Crystal Azzarello.

**Facilities and Capacity**

If you'd like to enjoy fresh air and picturesque views under the sun or under the evening moonlight and stars, you may opt to rent the Skydeck rooftop space providing over 1,800 square feet of space for your event. The Skydeck can comfortably host up to 75 guests and able to accomodate a full ceremony if desired. In addition, you can choose the front or back patio with outdoor fireplaces for a cozy ambiance, the front patio with street side views with over 1,700 square feet giving room to enjoy a more of the lively rustic outdoor scenery. The back patio which space is private and can extend into the courtyard providing enough space for up to 110 people, close to two fire places with coverage of up to 60 guests and built in heaters to have even the chilliest night become cozy! If you prefer to hold your reception indoors, the restaurant's interior features rustic-chic decor, and is easily accessible, with close to 2,000 square feet including a fully stocked bar area great for cocktail hour and mingling. All areas with flexible setup of formal or casual settings, dance floor available as well upon request.

**Services Offered**

Tiburon Tavern offers a very flexible and inviting menu. Various packages and menu food options to meet almost any budget, customizable alcohol and drink packages to get the party going and special non alcoholic options available. Kid friendly menus, sit down and plated, family style and buffet options all available. Tables and chairs and Linens all available onsite, dance floor ready and DJ and Florist available upon request.
