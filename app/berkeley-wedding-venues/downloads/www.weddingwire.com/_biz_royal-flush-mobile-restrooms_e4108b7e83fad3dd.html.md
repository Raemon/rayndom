## About

Based in Zanesville, Ohio and serving the central Ohio areas including: Columbus, Newark, Lancaster, & Cambridge. Royal Flush Mobile Restrooms adds comfort and convenience to any wedding or special event. Wherever you're hosting your occasion, your guests will be able to freshen up and get back to the dance floor with ease. Their team aims to do everything they can to ensure your day goes smoothly. 

**Services Offered**

Royal Flush Mobile Restrooms can provide a 3-stall trailer that can accommodate around 200 people a day. The space features flushing toilets and running water and is heated to handle all seasons. They can also provide a generator if no electricity is available on-site. In addition to 24-hour or weekend packages, they also offer rentals short-term for a 4-week rental or long-term for 3 months or more. For more information, contact Royal Flush Mobile Restrooms.
