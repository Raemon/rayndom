Happy Days: Events is a wedding coordination company based in Des Moines, Iowa. This talented team has more than a decade of combined experience. They are passionate about creating a stress-free planning process for couples so that every happy couple can focus on enjoying the excitement of their upcoming nuptials.

**Services Offered**

Happy Days: Events offers a variety of coordination packages, including day-of coordination. From cultural and religious weddings to military and civil unions, these planners will be delighted to plan your wedding your way. The team of event experts can help with timeline creation, budget analysis, vendor referrals, to-do lists, and finalizing every precious detail. They can also assist with an array of decor rentals and procurement, as well as setup and teardown services.
