Jefferson Street Mansion by Wedgewood Weddings is a beautiful vintage venue located in the historic area of Benicia, California. Overlooking the stunning Carquinez Strait, this neoclassical-style mansion is nestled in the heart of the Bay Area. Replete with nostalgic decor and authentic period pieces, the mansion boasts a timeless charm. Couples who choose to wed here are sure to be impressed by its myriad of romantic settings and inviting ambiance. Their seasoned staff is committed to maintaining the venue‚Äôs tradition of hospitality and will strive to bring your vision to life in this elegant getaway.

Jefferson Street Mansion stands proud as part of Historic Benicia's Arsenal as it was once the Executive Officer's Quarters. Built in 1861, the East Coast architects found inspiration in French military heroes such as Napolean and American Revolution General Lafayette and incorporated many elements of the Empire style of design. Once the US Military of the Arsenal was decommissioned in 1960, the mansion moved into private ownership. In 1998, it was purchased by its current owner and restored to its former glory, specifically to be a beautiful setting for special events.

**Facilities and Capacity**

Jefferson Street Mansion by Wedgewood Weddings can accommodate up to 200 guests for an outdoor ceremony. Enjoy water views of the Bay, manicured landscaping, and immaculate vintage styling throughout the venue. You can also make use of the tented pavilion with twinkle lit trees to host a reception.

**Services Offered**

Enjoy full-service event planning with four different package options for every price point: Build-your-own, Classic, Premier & Elite. The dedicated Wedgewood Weddings team is happy to work with all couples to customize any package.
