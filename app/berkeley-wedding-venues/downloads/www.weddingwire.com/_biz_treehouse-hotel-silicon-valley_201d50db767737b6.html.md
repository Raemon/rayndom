Treehouse Hotel Silicon Valley is an enchanting wedding venue located in Sunnyvale, California.

**About Treehouse Hotel Silicon Valley**

At Treehouse Hotel Silicon Valley, we create weddings that feel like a warm, joyful escape. Inspired by the charm of childhood treehouses, our venue combines cozy, handcrafted spaces with a touch of nostalgia: think soft lighting, local flavors, and inviting corners that spark connection. Every detail is designed to reflect your love story, making your day feel personal, heartfelt, and truly yours. Whether you’re dreaming of a romantic ceremony under the stars or a lively reception in one of our unique indoor spaces, our team is here to bring your vision to life. We focus on creating moments that matter, with thoughtful touches that make your guests feel right at home. At Treehouse, your wedding becomes a celebration wrapped in warmth and charm, where every moment feels magical.

**Facilities and Capacity**

Treehouse Hotel Silicon Valley offers flexible spaces perfect for weddings of any size. Our Event Barn brings a rustic yet modern vibe, while 7,750 square feet of indoor and outdoor areas blend natural beauty with thoughtful design. Tree-lined paths, lush greenery, and a cobblestone patio create a romantic backdrop for weddings. Event spaces available include:

  * Event Lawn
  * Beer Garden
  * The Barn—4,600 square feet
  * The Vale—1,800 square feet
  * Poppy Boardroom—675 square feet
  * Marigold Boardroom—675 square feet



**Services Offered**

From intimate gatherings to grand celebrations, Treehouse Hotel Silicon Valley is your go-to venue for a wedding that feels uniquely you. Our dedicated wedding specialist will take care of every detail so you can focus on enjoying your day. Event services offered include the following:

  * Guest accommodations
  * Seamless Planning services
  * Setup and cleanup
  * Bridal suite
  * Event rentals
  * Lighting and sound
  * Complimentary bike rentals



**Cuisine**

At Treehouse Hotel, our wedding catering services create unforgettable culinary experiences tailored to your special day. Led by our Executive Chef, our team designs seasonal banquet menus featuring fresh, local, farm-to-table ingredients crafted into vibrant dishes that are both delicious and wholesome. These dishes ensure a dining experience that elevates your celebration.

**Accommodation**

Our 111 rooms mix cozy natural textures with playful details, like artisan pillows, for a stay that feels both nostalgic and fresh. Wedding packages come with extras like food and beverage credits, room upgrades, and even in-room camping vibes for a fun twist. Accessible rooms ensure inclusivity for all guests, while the treehouse-inspired design offers a peaceful retreat for you and your guests.

**Why It’s Perfect for Couples**

Treehouse Hotel Silicon Valley is where playful meets polished, making it ideal for couples who want a wedding that’s memorable and meaningful. With unique and vibrant spaces like our Event Barn, Beer Garden, and Event Lawn, every moment will shine. Surrounded by lush greenery and a cozy atmosphere, it’s the perfect place to celebrate your love story.
