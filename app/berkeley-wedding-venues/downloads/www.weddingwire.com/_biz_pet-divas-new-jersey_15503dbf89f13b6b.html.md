## About this vendor

![TinaRose Yuli](https://cdn0.weddingwire.com/vendor/214022/original/960/jpg/uo_2220412_057dae40-0409-4097-b602-75627e57e57c.jpeg?r=1765870606)

#####  TinaRose Yuli 

Owner Pet Divas New Jersey 

__Woman-owned

__5 years in business

__We speak English

__Small team (2-10 members)

Located in Colts Neck, New Jersey, Pet Divas New Jersey offers professional and compassionate pet care services designed to ensure that your furry friends can be present on your wedding day. Founded by a retired school principal and lifelong pet enthusiast, the company provides customized care for dogs, cats, birds, and small animals. Each member of the Pet Divas team is certified, insured, bonded, and CPR-trained, ensuring the highest level of professionalism and safety.

**Services Offered**

Pet Divas New Jersey specializes in a one-of-a-kind Wedding Day Pet Attendant Service, perfect for couples who want their pets to share in their celebration. From coordinating adorable photo moments, preparing your pet to walk down the aisle as "Flower Girl" or "Dog of Honor", transporting pets between venues and keeping them calm during the festivities, the team ensures each pet’s comfort and safety throughout the day. Couples can focus on their special moments, knowing their beloved companions are in the best hands. Reach out to the Pet Divas New Jersey team today!
