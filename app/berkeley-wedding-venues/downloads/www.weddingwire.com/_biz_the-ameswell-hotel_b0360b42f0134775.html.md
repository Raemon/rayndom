__5 years in business

The Ameswell Hotel is a modern wedding venue located in Mountain View, California. Available from Summer 2020, this hotel offers a one-of-a-kind backdrop for celebrations with its distinct architecture and design inspired by California’s wilderness. With luxurious amenities and a variety of event spaces, this location can accommodate both intimate and grand gatherings.

**Facilities and Capacity**

Beginning in 2020, this hotel offers over 15,000-square-feet of space for engagement parties, wedding showers, elopements, rehearsal dinners, ceremonies, and receptions. The Ballroom is an adaptable area providing a contemporary setting with large windows, plenty of natural light, audio and visual equipment, and carefully designed furnishings. With the ability to be divided into smaller rooms, the Ballroom can accommodate large and intimate gatherings. For an outdoor celebration, the Event Lawn offers a spacious area for seating and a dance floor under a tent. The property’s indoor and outdoor bar can host up to 200 seated guests and contains a smaller dining room for a private dinner. The Hangar One Lounge located adjacent to the hotel’s main lobby serves as an art gallery where couples and their loved ones can enjoy work by local artists. For intimate gatherings, the Library and two board rooms are comfortable places to enjoy the company of your loved ones.

**Services Offered**

The Ameswell Hotel aims to provide wedding services starting in Summer 2020. Their catering service offers buffet, family-style, plated, and station-style dining arrangements. Friendly bartenders can serve a variety of beverages including house beer, liquor, and wine, premium liquor, specialty beer and wine, signature drinks, and champagne toasts. The venue offers a range of event items such as tables, chairs, china, barware, flatware, glassware, and linens.

**Accommodations**

The accommodations at The Ameswell Hotel have been thoughtfully designed to foster a restful and peaceful environment. Its 255 spacious guest rooms feature large windows to allow for plenty of natural light. Happy couples and their loved ones can enjoy high-quality linens, pillows, and mattresses in rooms with modern designs and amenities. This hotel offers a resort-like ambiance with a swimming pool, fitness center, walking paths, fire pits, and a beer garden.

**Location**

This venue is located just 12 miles northwest of San Jose. For out-of-town guests, San Jose International Airport is nearly 9 miles northwest and San Francisco International Airport is 24 miles southeast of the hotel. To-be-newlyweds and their loved ones can enjoy a variety of nearby attractions such as the NASA Ames Visitor Center and the Computer History Museum. Be sure to check out the gallery for an exclusive 360° Virtual Tour!
