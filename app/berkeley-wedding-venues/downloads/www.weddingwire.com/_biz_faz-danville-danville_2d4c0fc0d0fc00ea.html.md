## About

Faz Restaurants & Catering specializes in Mediterranean Cuisine with an emphasis on fresh Mesquite Grilled Kabobs, House-made Pastas, Fresh Pizza from the Wood Burning Oven and Spit Fire Rotisserie Meats. We have developed an unsurpassed reputation in the Bay Area with 5 locations that offer a multitude of entertaining options. Faz Restaurants is THE place to host your next celebration.

Our Catering Department offers you a wide selection of services and menus, ranging from simple to elegant. Choose from custom catering packages; executive boardroom drop off box lunches; hors d' oeuvres, buffets, sit down or served dinners & theme events. Fabulous food is what we do.
