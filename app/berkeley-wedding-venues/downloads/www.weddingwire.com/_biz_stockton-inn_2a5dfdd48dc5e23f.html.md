Stockton Inn is a wedding rehearsal dinner venue located in Stockton, New Jersey. A destination for milestone celebrations and private dinners, this venue is steeped in over 300 years of history, having been originally built in built in 1710. It was once frequented by F. Scott Fitzgerald and Jackie Kennedy, and now welcomes guests looking for a charming getaway near Lambertville and New Hope.

**Facilities and Capacity**

Stockton Inn provides welcoming spaces for intimate gatherings of up to 16 guests. The timeless spaces are the perfect setting for unforgettable experiences with loved ones. With beautifully renovated spaces with their historic character carefully preserved, it is a place you can transform to match your vision for your dream event. Both dining concepts at Stockton Inn are led by Executive Chef Bob Truitt, who brings experience from top NYC restaurants and Spain. The variety allows you to choose between intimate pasta dinners at Finch or celebratory tavern fare at Dog & Deer, all within the historic Stockton Inn setting.

**Services Offered**

Stockton Inn has two different restaurant concepts, so you can choose one to suit your personal taste and vision for your event. Menus highlight fresh seasonal ingredients. Dog and Deer Tavern offers elevated tavern classics like Steak Frites and Spicy Tuna Tartare in a relaxed, upscale setting. Enjoy craft cocktails from world-renowned bartender Brian Miller or local beers. At Finch, homemade pasta is the star of the show, with an ever-changing line-up of expertly crafted dishes. From celery root ravioli to lobster cappelletti, every pasta is made fresh in-house with seasonal ingredients and house-made sauces.
