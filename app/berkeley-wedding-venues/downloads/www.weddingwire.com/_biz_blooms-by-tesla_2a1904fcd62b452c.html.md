## About this vendor

![Tesla Welch](https://cdn0.weddingwire.com/vendor/840522/original/960/jpg/uo_2225048_5174f43e-74b1-4c33-956f-c116df925d6a.jpeg?r=1769137365)

#####  Tesla Welch 

Owner, Lead Florist 

__Woman-owned

__6 years in business

__We speak English

__Small team (2-10 members)

Blooms by Tesla is a premier wedding floral service based in Lehi, Utah. We specialize in thoughtfully designed florals that feel effortless, elevated, and unique to each wedding. We believe flowers should do more than look beautiful - they should tell your story, set the tone, and make your wedding day feel unmistakably you.

Our style blends timeless elegance with a fresh, modern sensibility. Think lush textures, intentional color palettes, and artful details that feel refined without ever feeling stiff. From romantic bouquets and statement ceremony installations to curated reception florals, every piece is designed with care, purpose, and an eye for the small details that make a big impact.

At Blooms By Tesla, we're known for being both creative and highly organized. We pride ourselves on clear communication, seamless planning, and a calm, professional presence on your wedding day - so you can relax and enjoy the moment. We take time to understand your vision, your venue, and your priorities, ensuring your florals feel cohesive and meaningful from start to finish.

Whether your wedding style is classic, garden-inspired, modern, or somewhere beautifully in between, our goal is to create florals that feel intentional, memorable, and perfectly tailored to you.

Your vision matters here - and we can't wait to bring it to life.
