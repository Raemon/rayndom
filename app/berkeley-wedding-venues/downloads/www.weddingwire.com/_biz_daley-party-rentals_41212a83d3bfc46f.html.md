## About

Daley Party Rentals is a tent and wedding rentals business based in Quogue, New York. For years, our company has brought joy to couples on their big day by helping ease their worries and bring their vision to life. We are professionals and passionate about our community and love to see our friends and families of Long Island celebrate together.

**Services Offered**

Our team at Daley Party Rentals will collaborate with you at each step to ensure every last detail comes together perfectly. We offer couples inclusive packages for high-peak and pole tents alongside tables and chairs. Our collection also includes high-quality linens, bars, catering equipment, dance floors, and elegant outdoor lighting. If you're unsure what you need, we invite couples to reach out with a few details, and they are more than happy to help you fine-tune your design.
