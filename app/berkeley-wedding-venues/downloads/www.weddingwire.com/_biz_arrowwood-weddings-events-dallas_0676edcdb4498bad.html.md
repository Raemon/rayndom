## About this vendor

![Taylor + Byron](https://cdn0.weddingwire.com/vendor/262310/original/960/jpg/uo_1013262_e3a50273-0e8c-46e6-9e80-6e2df7eac0f0.jpeg?r=1732653009)

#####  Taylor + Byron 

Owner 

__Woman-owned

__8 years in business

__We speak English and Spanish

__Small team (2-10 members)

We are Arrowwood Weddings + Events, an affordable wedding venue located just 30 minutes south of Dallas. Our 10-acre, secluded, whimsical, wooded retreat offers a non-traditional venue with lush greenery, hundreds of mature trees, and tons of string lights, providing a modern rustic feel. Our space is pre-decorated with multiple ceremony backdrops, a hanging white floral dance floor, white drapes, and a photo wall (all included)! Perfect for those seeking a venue that is boho, dark romantic, or a forest setting for an intimate wedding. We even have resident wedding llamas and a beer burro to keep things fun and light-hearted! End the night with our convenient on-site lodging for your wedding party or guests!

At Arrowwood Weddings + Events, we offer everything to make your big day stress-free, from a decor package where we provide the decor and do the setup/breakdown, day-of-coordination, overnight accommodations, a preferred vendor list, and much more! We also believe weddings should be an unforgettable day for all, that is why we bring the fun with our a la carte add-ons, such as our fireworks, llama experiences, beer burro, s'mores bar, yard games, champagne wall, and more! You won't find another accommodating venue able to make your special day more turnkey!
