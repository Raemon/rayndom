Deep in the heart of Houlka, Mississippi, 40 Oaks is a stunning wedding venue backdropped by the serenity of the lake, and the magnificence of the tall trees that give this space its name. Featuring a bridal suite, dressing rooms, and overnight amenities, it has everything needed for a magical wedding day. Our venue accommodates up to 300 guests, providing the ideal balance of comfort and grandeur. Let us help you start planning an unforgettable experience.
