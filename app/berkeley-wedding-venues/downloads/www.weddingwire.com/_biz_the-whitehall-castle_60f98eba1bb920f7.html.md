## About this vendor

![Roark &amp; Liz](https://cdn0.weddingwire.com/vendor/622212/original/960/jpg/uo_2212226_9b6938ca-5123-49c3-88fa-5e3ccd5e071e.jpeg?r=1750092492)

Whitehall Castle is a gorgeous, historic wedding venue in the charming town of Whitehall, New York. Formerly a National Guard armory, it is a brick and stone structure designed by famed architect Isaac Perry in 1899. The venue has been carefully restored to preserve its rugged beauty and modernize its spaces for lively celebrations.

**Facilities and Capacity**

The castle accommodates up to 300 guests in its grand ballroom and indoor/outdoor ceremony spaces. Couples are welcome to exchange vows on the carefully manicured lawns or within the breathtaking interiors of Whitehall Castle. The ballroom includes a selection of tables, chairs, and decor pieces, allowing you to tailor the layout to suit your unique needs. Luxurious dressing rooms are available onsite, allowing you and your entourage to prepare in style. Ample parking options are provided within the grounds, guaranteeing ease of access for your guests. Offering a blend of history, grandeur, and heartfelt design, Whitehall Castle is an unmatched setting to host your most cherished moments.

**Services Offered**

The venue's friendly team will be delighted to help coordinate your dream celebration. Couples are welcome to personalize the space to their tastes and hire external suppliers to bring their special day to life. A fully equipped catering kitchen is available on-site, offering a seamless experience for your chosen caterer to set up and execute their service. Customizable bar options are also provided, guaranteeing a healthy flow of beverages throughout your event. Reach out to Whitehall Castle and allow the team to transform your special day into a royal affair.
