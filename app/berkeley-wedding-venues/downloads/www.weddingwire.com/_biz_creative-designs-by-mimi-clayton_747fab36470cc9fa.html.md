## About

Art and design has always been a passion for me. With a degree in Graphic Design and more than eight years as a designer, I have had the privilege of creating and providing invitations for friends and family as well as a wide variety of clients. I have a passion for typography, elegant designs and beautiful papers and have worked hard to provide quality service that encourages return clients.

  
I am happy to assist and guide you as your special and individual theme, color, shape, and texture is carried throughout. I specialize in simple, elegant and one of a kind invitations.  
  
I take pride in creating your invitations and make it one of the most pleasant aspects of planning your event. I understand that your time is valuable. Each client is given the individual attention and unconstrained time they deserve; therefore, I offer one-hour consultations, by appointment only, for your convenience.  
  
I invite you to browse my website and visualize every occasion and/or social event you could possibly host!  
  
I also welcome your calls and emails anytime!  
  
Cheers and Happy Wedding Planning!
