Part of the [wedding planning process](https://www.weddingwire.com/wedding-ideas/wedding-planning-timeline) is buying wedding gifts for those who have helped and supported you along the way. This includes a maid of honor gift (or [matron of honor](https://www.weddingwire.com/wedding-ideas/maid-of-honor-vs-matron-of-honor) gift), which is one of the most important tokens of gratitude you'll shop for ahead of your special day. Not only did your MOH help you navigate [the highs and lows of planning your wedding](https://www.weddingwire.com/wedding-ideas/the-ultimate-maid-of-honor-duties-checklist), she also coordinated your [bachelorette party](https://www.weddingwire.com/wedding-ideas/how-to-plan-a-destination-bachelorette-party), bridal shower, and maybe even your proposal. Most importantly, she's been supportive of your relationship since the beginning. To show your appreciation, we've rounded up some of the best maid of honor gifts that you can easily shop now. From jewelry and glassware to [gift box subscriptions](https://www.weddingwire.com/wedding-ideas/best-gift-subscription-box) and cozy accessories, everything on this list will convey how lucky you are to have your MOH on board.
