## About this vendor

![Margaret Colhoun](https://cdn0.weddingwire.com/vendor/219712/original/960/jpg/uo_2217912_e5b3be1e-48a9-4f07-a4ab-908915580623.jpeg?r=1757658087)

Everinscription is a wedding stationery atelier operating out of Brooklyn, New York, that crafts elegant designs tailored to your vision. If you're looking for a highly personalized invitation suite, these professionals can deliver.

**Services Offered**

As you get ready to tie the knot, meet with the skilled team at Everinscription and describe your event. These artists approach each commission as an _objet d'art,_ aiming to craft a unique keepsake using rare and unexpected materials. In addition to refined paper invitations, this company can create truly one-of-a-kind invites on objects such as pearls and even iridescent nautilus shells. As well as invitations, Everinscription offers a range of table numbers and other essential wedding stationery. Invite your guests to the big day with stunning keepsake stationery, courtesy of this brilliant atelier.
