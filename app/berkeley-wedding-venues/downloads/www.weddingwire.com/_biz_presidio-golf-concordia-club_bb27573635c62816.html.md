## About this vendor

![Tracey Lyons](https://cdn0.weddingwire.com/vendor/895022/original/960/jpg/uo_2220598_377b1c41-84f7-40bc-944a-1f11a22dd528.jpeg?r=1761695075)

Director of Sales & Events (Wedding Specialist) 

__131 years in business

__We speak English

__Large team (21+ members)

Presidio Golf & Concordia Club is a spectacular country club and wedding venue in San Francisco, California. This iconic setting is located on the stunning backdrop of the Presidio National Park, offering a breathtaking backdrop on which to seal your love.

**Facilities and Capacity**

A range of newly renovated spaces is available within the venue, allowing you to select the setting that best suits the style and size of your celebration. The Main Dining Room boasts a capacity of up to 120 seated guests (or 80 with a dance floor), overlooking the golf course and offering sweeping city views. This exceptional space provides flexible setup options, with a dance floor, a large drop-down screen, and a projector ideal for entertainment. The venue's Main Bar is the perfect setting for guests to enjoy a cocktail hour before moving into the dining room for the main portion of the evening. Smaller celebrations take place in the Lawson Little Room. With majestic views of the 10th tee and the 18th green, it is an intimate space where couples can come together with their nearest and dearest to celebrate their big day. For truly extravagant occasions, Presidio Golf & Concordia Club is available for full buyouts, offering you and your guests exclusive access to the entire venue.

**Services Offered**

The dedicated events team at Presidio Golf & Concordia Club will be delighted to assist with bringing your dream celebration to life. From design and setup to day-of coordination, they will ensure each detail is perfectly in place. Catering and bar services are available on-site, with delicious menus that will truly impress your guests. What's more, the venue boasts a range of exciting activities on-site, including the expansive golf course as well as spa treatments and leisure facilities. Couples and guests alike can enjoy an unforgettable wedding weekend with fabulous entertainment throughout.
