## About this vendor

![Julia Thayne](https://cdn0.weddingwire.com/vendor/449422/original/960/jpg/uo_2224944_b0085d83-2227-40ef-b8a0-fa7c4190b58a.jpeg?r=1768023416)

Designer 

__Woman-owned

__2 years in business

__We speak English

__Small team (2-10 members)

Dixie’s Flower Shop in Kaysville, Utah, specializes in **custom wedding flowers** designed to make your special day unforgettable. We create beautiful **bridal bouquets, wedding centerpieces, ceremony flowers, and reception floral designs** tailored to your style and vision. From romantic and classic weddings to modern and elegant celebrations, our expert florists use fresh, high-quality blooms to bring your dream wedding to life.

As a trusted **local wedding florist** , Dixie’s Flower Shop offers personalized consultations, thoughtful design, and reliable service from start to finish. Whether you’re planning an intimate ceremony or a large celebration, we’re proud to provide stunning **wedding floral arrangements** that add beauty and meaning to every moment.
