## About this vendor

![Kelly Raymond](https://cdn0.weddingwire.com/vendor/003422/original/960/jpg/uo_2224300_7268bd80-41ad-4158-837d-03231ec07bc4.jpeg?r=1767921874)

Spruceberry Farm is a barn-style wedding venue located in Cumberland, Virginia. We believe your wedding shouldn't just be an event—it should be a private escape where "happily ever after" feels right at home. Nestled among sweeping fields and scenic orchards, our family-owned farm offers a warm, inviting haven designed for couples who value relaxed elegance and authentic connection.

**What We Are Known For**

We are celebrated for our breathtaking **"Golden Hour"** ceremonies and our iconic Barn, a beautiful barn-style venue that blends rustic architecture with modern comfort. Whether it's the glow of string lights over an outdoor celebration or the handcrafted wooden beams of our indoor spaces, Spruceberry Farm is known for its stunning, versatile backdrops. Beyond the views, we are a destination for small-batch craft beer, wine, and freshly produced food, ensuring your guests enjoy a true taste of Virginia hospitality.

**How We Make Your Wedding Special**

  * **Total Versatility:** From intimate gatherings to grand celebrations, our flexible spaces accommodate 350+ guests indoors and up to 1,000+ outdoors, giving you a "clean slate" to design your dream day.
  * **Effortless Planning:** We take the stress out of your "I Do" with full wedding planning services and in-house catering, beer, and wine.
  * **A "Home Away From Home" Experience:** Unlike traditional venues, we focus on an interactive, family-friendly atmosphere where your guests can relax and make memories that last long after the final dance.
  * **Exclusive Amenities:** Our property features on-site changing rooms, a large outdoor stage for weddings, live music, and endless photo opportunities among our Christmas tree farm and lush greenery.



Now booking for **2026**! We invite you to schedule a tour and see why Spruceberry Farm is the heart of Virginia's most romantic love stories.
