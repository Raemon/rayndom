## About

The Museum of Fine Arts provides the quintessential St. Petersburg experience with sophistication and elegance. Invite guests to see centuries of art, breathtaking paintings and sculptures while celebrating your occasion. Stunning event spaces at the Museum include a classic ballroom with gorgeous crystal chandeliers, beautiful gardens with lush foliage, and a sleek, contemporary Conservatory overlooking Tampa Bay. The Museum becomes a backdrop for your wedding, and there is no other place like it in the Tampa Bay area. We have been awarded the Wedding Wire Couple Choice Award for several consecutive years and love being a part of so many love stories.

We have both indoor and outdoor spaces for your events, both classic and contemporary, depending on your party's size and vibe. The MFA is a non-profit, so your special day will help support the museum collection, exhibitions, and programs.
