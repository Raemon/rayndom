## About this vendor

![Todd and Stephanie Bluffestone](https://cdn0.weddingwire.com/vendor/873522/original/960/jpg/uo_2225378_4da75063-0a8a-42bd-ad98-e8e3153dc2ff.jpeg?r=1768868381)

#####  Todd and Stephanie Bluffestone 

Owners 

__1 year in business

__We speak English

__Small team (2-10 members)

Signature Chuppah is a wedding chuppah rental company based in Cleveland, Ohio. Offering their beautiful handcrafted chuppah to elevate your occasion, we would love to make your special day even more exceptional. Owners Todd and Stephanie Bluffestone founded this company after their son was planning his wedding, and they couldn't find the right chuppah to match their vision and the ones they liked were too expensive. Father and son then decided to build their own. Building the chuppah became a family project and in the end, two were made. The idea to pffer to rent out the chuppahs came when requests to borrow and rent the chuppahs continued to pour in.

**Services Offered**

The simple elegance of the classic wood, pergola design makes this Chuppah ideal for many wedding styles. Its pergola-styled elegance has become a hit with many couples, both Jewish and non Jewish. We hope it will be the ideal fit for your celebration too! Reach out today to learn more about rentals and services.
