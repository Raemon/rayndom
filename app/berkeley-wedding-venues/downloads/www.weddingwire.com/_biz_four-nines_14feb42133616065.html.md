Located in Scottsdale, Arizona, Four Nines is a luxury wedding jeweler dedicated to finding the perfect piece for every couple's big day. Couples are invited into a world of true luxury, where each piece is carefully selected with intention and backed by a deep knowledge of fine jewelry and collectible treasures.

**Services Offered**

Four Nines offers an expansive range of wedding jewelry, including wedding bands, eternity rings, diamond bands, and gold bands. In addition to its bridal selections, Four Nines presents fine jewelry, vintage pieces, certified pre-owned watches, luxury handbags, and rare finds from iconic designers such as Cartier, Tiffany & Co., Chanel, and Louis Vuitton. Four Nines helps couples discover pieces that are worthy of their special day. Reach out to our specialists today and start your journey to finding your dream piece.
