# 24 Honeymoon Dresses for Your First Newlywed Vacay

Start planning your post-wedding looks with these picks.

Packing for your honeymoon is probably something that will fall off your radar while you're in the midst of wedding planning, but after you hang up your wedding dress, you want your vacation looks to be just as cute. A few honeymoon dresses should be at the top of your packing list (along with [honeymoon lingerie](https://www.weddingwire.com/wedding-ideas/honeymoon-lingerie) and [pajamas](https://www.weddingwire.com/wedding-ideas/bridal-pajamas)), and we're here to help you curate your wardrobe. From party dresses that are perfect for a newlywed dinner date, to comfortable T-shirt inspired styles you can wear while lounging around, these easy-to-pack looks will have you heading for the virtual checkout line ASAP. See our picks below for every kind of honeymooner, whether you're [jetting off to Europe](https://www.weddingwire.com/wedding-ideas/european-honeymoon-on-a-budget) for world-class cuisine and wine, or you're headed to the Caribbean to soak up the sun.
