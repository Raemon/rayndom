Emily's Event Venue, nestled in the heart of Mohnton, Pennsylvania, offers a beautiful venue space for weddings and other special occasions. It is located just about halfway between Reading and Morganstown for easy access to local amenities such as overnight accommodations and vendors that can help make any event unforgettable. It is also just an hour's drive from Philadelphia and its international airport, for convenience if loved ones are coming from out of state or abroad. With a central location and an exceptional team of staff, it is the perfect place for couples to tie the knot.

**Facilities and Capacity**

As it was originally built in the 1800s, Emily's Event Venue holds a rich legacy of serving the local community. What was once an inn, post office, and restaurant is now a premium event venue. When soon-to-be-weds and their guests first enter the venue, they will be greeted with a historic yet elegant charm. Inside, there is a versatile and charming space complete with timeless architecture, scenic grounds, and modern amenities for a one-of-a-kind wedding day.

**Services Offered**

Whether it's a wedding, birthday, corporate event, or other celebration, their dedicated team works hard to ensure every occasion is an unforgettable experience. They also host community events with a different theme each week. Couples and some of their guests can have a pre-wedding get-together at a themed event with delicious food, vibrant cocktails, and great music that will have them on the dance floor all night long. Reach out to Emily's Event Venue today to find out more about the wedding packages they offer.
