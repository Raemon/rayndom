San Ramon by Wedgewood Weddings is a romantic country club wedding venue in San Ramon, California. This venue offers wonderful views, scenic ceremony spaces, an elegant ballroom, and much more to create your amazing wedding event. This venue is ideal for couples seeking a sophisticated venue steeped in nature and natural light for a grand wedding celebration.

**Facilities and Capacity**

San Ramon by Wedgewood Weddings can accommodate up to 250 guests for a beautiful indoor reception. Enjoy a spacious ballroom with wall-to-wall, floor-to-ceiling windows looking out onto the serene ceremony site with the sun setting behind.

**Venue Features At-A-Glance**

Capacity: 250 Guests  
Reception: Indoor Reception Room  
Views: Golf Course, Willow Trees, Pond  
Building: Classic Clubhouse  
Dressing Suite: Available for Changing and Final Touches  
Photo Opportunities: Bountiful Indoor and Outdoor Options  
Services Offered  
Enjoy full-service event planning with four package options for every price point: Build-your-own, Classic, Premier & Elite. The dedicated Wedgewood Weddings team is happy to work with all couples to customize any package.
