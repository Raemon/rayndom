Ceremonies By Kristen is a wedding officiant service based in Saint Charles, Missouri, that happily works with couples throughout the greater St. Louis area. Owner Kristen became a wedding celebrant in 2021 to officiate her sister's wedding and has been helping couples organize their ceremonies ever since. She's now a certified minister with American Marriage Ministries and is happy to perform religious, non-religious, and interfaith ceremonies.

**Services Offered**

Ceremonies By Kristen provides a range of ceremony types, from low-key elopements to lavish celebrations, to ensure clients get exactly what they want. Kristen will schedule a one-on-one consultation with you to get a better idea of your day-of vision and help you select a package that fulfills your needs. With years of speaking and writing experience, she will be ready to collaborate with you and create a personalized, one-of-a-kind experience at the altar.
