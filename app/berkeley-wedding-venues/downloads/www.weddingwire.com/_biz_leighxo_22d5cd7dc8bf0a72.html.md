## About

Based in Cleveland, Ohio, LeighXO is a live wedding illustrator with a love for hand-drawn gifts and favors. Owner Leighann adores enhancing celebrations with her beautiful artwork. These milestones are so full of joy, love, and laughter, which is why being a part of them means so much to her.

**Services Offered**

LeighXO can add unique and creative flair to your big day, designing custom drawings of your nearest and dearest. Leighann will take photos of your guests and transform them into sketches—all made with custom stationery and vibrant ink for a truly striking memento. Thoughtfully wrapped and gifted to your loved ones at the end of the occasion, everyone will be blown away by the beauty and artistry. These services are available to couples saying "I do" across the Northeast Ohio region and beyond.
