When the everyday bridesmaid party dress simply won't do, a trendy bridesmaid jumpsuit is our favorite alternative. When it comes to wedding party attire, jumpsuits and pantsuits have skyrocketed in popularity over the last few years, thanks to their stylish yet comfortable vibe, and we don't see this trend going away anytime soon. Bridesmaid jumpsuits are also extremely east to [re-wear long after the wedding day](https://www.weddingwire.com/wedding-ideas/dresses-bridesmaids-will-wear-again), which means you'll score major points with your crew (instead of asking them to buy a dress they may never use again). Whether you're engaged and deciding [what your bridesmaids should wear](https://www.weddingwire.com/wedding-ideas/which-bridesmaid-dresses-should-your-bridesmaids-wear), or you're a bridesmaid who's been tasked with finding the perfect jumpsuit for your friend's wedding, the great news is that there are plenty of options on the market. Ready to get started? Below, we've hand-picked a variety of bridesmaid jumpsuits in different fabrics, colors, styles, and levels of formality that you can shop right now.
