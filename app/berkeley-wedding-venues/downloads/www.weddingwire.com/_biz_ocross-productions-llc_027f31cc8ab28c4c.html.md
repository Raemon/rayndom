Ocross Productions LLC (DBA Ocross Productions) is a full-service event planning and entertainment company in the Bronx, New York. The business provides a variety of blended and diverse disc jockey and master of ceremonies services, along with up-the-minute event planning and embellishment services, for various events such as weddings, receptions, private birthday parties, virtual events, corporate events, and much more. Our services will transform the dreamy concept into reality by coordinating with clients for every fine detail of occasions to produce exceptional and memorable ceremonies artistically.

Owner rOmezie "DJ Ocross" Omeokwe brings over 10 years of disk jockey experience to the industry. This first-generation Nigerian-American DJ specializes in effortless blends of Hip-Hop, Afrobeats, R&B, Soca, and Pop music. With his extensive musical knowledge and professional expertise, DJ Ocross promises to get every guest making moves on the dance floor.

**Services Offered**

Ocross Productions, LLC provides to-be-weds with a range of services including master of ceremonies and a DJ set. Having hosted his own radio show, DJ Ocross delivers an engaging and enjoyable performance that will set the tone for the wedding celebrations. Couples can design a wedding package that fits their specific needs, safe in the hands of a professional with experience in venues across New York City. DJ Ocross invites couples to explore his SoundCloud site to taste his musical style.
