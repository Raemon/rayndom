Meriden Party Rentals, LLC is a wedding company based in Meriden, CT. Since, 2008 this business has been Connecticut’s trusted source for premium event rentals. We proudly serve Meriden and surrounding areas with an extensive inventory of tables, chairs, dance floors, luxury restrooms, linens, throne chairs, and a wide selection of high-quality tents, which are ideal for weddings, graduations, birthdays, and corporate events across the state.

  
As well as that, we offer top-rated inflatable rentals in Connecticut, such as bounce houses, obstacle courses, water slides, and interactive games.

Boasting reliable delivery, fantastic customer service with over 200+ reviews on Google, and some of the best rental selections in Connecticut, Meriden Party Rentals is your go-to partner for exceptional events.
