French Country Manor Estate in New Providence, Pennsylvania, is a stunning wedding venue. The property is steeped in history and retains much of its authentic building materials, including the barn, gardens, paths, woodlands, and the old mill stream with a waterfall.

**Facilities and Capacity**

At this family-run venue, you and up to 200 of your loved ones will have access to private decks, lovely outdoor porches, and a pond, as well as peaceful natural spaces such as gardens in bloom, trails, and a stream. French Country Manor Estate offers fantastic opportunities for taking gorgeous wedding photographs you will want to share and display in your home. You will find both indoor and outdoor spaces available for both your ceremony and reception. Dressing rooms are available for you and your wedding parties to get ready and relax before the celebrations begin. For anyone wishing to stay at this marvelous location for a day or two to unwind before or after the festivities, French Country Manor Estate offers luxurious lodging accommodations for up to 75 guests. The French Country Manor Estate Bed & Breakfast includes the Secret Garden Suite, the Windows on the Stream Suite, the Suite on the Pond, and the Scarlet Pimpernel Suite. Each one of these beautiful rooms is decorated with period pieces and fresh flowers from the garden. Every suite comes with a private bath, fireplace, cable television, streaming services, and sound systems. All guests have access to the library, living rooms, breakfast and dinning rooms, outdoor decks, patios, and grounds. Guests can enjoy a delicious five-course gourmet breakfast throughout their stay. The French Country Manor Woodland Retreat, Farm House, Historic Strasburg Home, and Cottage offer large kitchens, living rooms, recreational entertainment spaces, and numerous bedrooms. Each location has a fire pit, grill, outdoor lighting, outdoor games and activities, gazebos, outdoor furniture for eating or entertaining, and trails for hiking and biking. 

**Services Offered**

The friendly professionals at French Country Manor Estate look forward to welcoming you and helping you plan the wedding day of your dreams. They will strive to ensure every detail is handled with care and exceeds your expectations, whether you're planning a grand affair, an intimate gathering, or wish to elope with your love. Reach out to the French Country Manor Estate crew to start planning an event you and your guests will never forget.
