Poppy Ridge Golf Course is a simple and elegant 27-hole golf course and wedding venue located in Livermore, California. Neatly tucked away in the East Bay, the property sits in the heart of Livermore wine country. This location is a short drive away from San Francisco and popular Bay Area attractions. Nestled in the rolling foothills of Livermore wine country and surrounded by vineyards, this venue is renowned for its beautiful views and superior service. The property boasts acres of open land and well-manicured grounds of a 27-hole golf course. Experience the magic of Poppy Ridge Golf Course and the Livermore Valley wine country in a spectacular and picturesque setting.
