5 out of 5 rating 

5.0 

Some might say that the inside of this little church might be dated, and maybe they're right. I remember thinking that should they choose to replace the carpet with wood floors, it would be nicer.   
  
However, I do love, love, love this church! Working with Jeannie was a dream. She responded quickly to emails and phone calls, and when faced with a rowdy bunch (sorry Jeannie!) at the rehearsal, she was more than just a little patient.  
  
The wedding ceremony was not perfect, but it was very us, and I would not hesitate to say that this lovely church had a lot to do with it. Everyone loved the homey and simple look to it, and I gotta say that ringing the bell after getting married was really fun, too!
