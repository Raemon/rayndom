## About

A banquet hall-style wedding venue in San Francisco, California, San Francisco Italian Athletic Club offers couples and their guests a taste of Italy in North Beach America. Versatile in nature, the venue caters to a variety of celebration sizes, from intimate ceremonies to energetic receptions. With roots tracing back to 1917, San Francisco Italian Athletic Club is the perfect choice for a one-of-a-kind, knot-tying celebration!

**Facilities and Capacity**

Nestled in the chic and pedestrian-friendly neighborhood of Little Italy, San Francisco Italian Athletic Club comfortably accommodates a maximum of 350 guests. Boasting 2,800 square feet of space, the venue’s spacious Grand Ballroom is ideal for beautiful ceremonies and receptions.

**Services Offered**

Couples who book San Francisco Italian Athletic Club for their special day benefit from a variety of wedding-related services and amenities. Said options include, but are not limited to, the following:

  * Setup
  * Cleanup
  * Event planning
  * Event rentals
  * Lighting/sound
  * Wireless Internet



**Cuisine**

San Francisco Italian Athletic Club’s talented executive chef works with each couple to craft a delectable wedding dining experience. From menu planning to beverage options, couples benefit from the following in-house catering services:

  * Tastings
  * Server(s)
  * Hors d'oeuvres
  * Buffet-style dinner
  * Family-style dinner
  * Plated-style dinner



**Other Facilities**

San Francisco Italian Athletic Club offers a number of versatile spaces for couples to utilize during their wedding celebration. Featuring a balcony overlooking Washington Square Park, the venue’s Park View Room is outfitted with a numerous amenities, such as a fireplace, projection system and full kitchen. Accommodating a maximum of 40 seated guests and 75 standing, the space is ideal for rehearsal dinners and engagement parties.

**Other Services**

Interested San Francisco Italian Athletic Club couples are more than welcome to include bar services with their celebration. Said services include, but are not limited to, the following:

  * Liquor license
  * Bartender(s)
  * Open bar
  * Cash bar
  * Limited bar
  * Premium liquor
  * House liquor
  * Specialty wine
  * House wine
  * Signature drink
