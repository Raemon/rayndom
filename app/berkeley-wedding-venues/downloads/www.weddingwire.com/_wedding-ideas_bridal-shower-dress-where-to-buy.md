When it comes to white bridal shower dresses, you might think your options are limited to demure, lacy numbers that don't reveal too much skin and aren't too form-fitting. But we're here to remind you that these days, there are no hard and fast rules when it comes to wedding dresses and bridal fashion, and that goes for all of [your pre-wedding attire too](https://www.weddingwire.com/wedding-ideas/all-the-pre-wedding-parties). In this shoppable roundup of bridal shower dresses, we're sharing something for every type of bride. From leg-baring mini dresses and curve-hugging frocks to casual dresses and formal options, these looks will definitely get your guests' attention—and some could even double as bachelorette party or [rehearsal dinner dresses](https://www.weddingwire.com/wedding-ideas/rehearsal-dinner-dresses-and-outfits). If you're looking for a bridal shower outfit that matches your personal style  _and_ will win you fashion points with party guests young and old, keep reading. Happy shopping!
