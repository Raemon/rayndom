## About

Based in the San Francisco Bay Area, Design des Troy is a boutique design studio that creates bespoke and custom wedding invitations. We fell in love with designing our own wedding invitations, and we've continued to design custom invitations for almost 20 years. We specialize in classicism with a contemporary twist, but are happy to accommodate your unique style and taste.

**Products Offered**  
Design des Troy can produce a wide range of customized products that will tie your celebration together. They will happily listen to your ideas and suggestions. Products include:

  * Custom invitations  

  * Imprinted napkins  

  * Menus  

  * Place cards  

  * Programs  

  * RSVP cards  

  * Save the dates
  * Thank-yous



  
**Other Services**  
This Bay Area design company can also provide a variety of invitation services. Couples may choose from:

  * Addressing  

  * Assembly  

  * Calligraphy  

  * Consultations  

  * Design  

  * Eco-friendly  

  * Engraving  

  * Foil-stamped  

  * Laser cut  

  * Letterpress  

  * Printing
