## About

Amadeus Music Group is a classical music group based in Naples, Florida, that can tell your love story through melody. This team of world-class musicians has been orchestrating breathtaking performances for many years, sharing their talents with audiences throughout South Florida. They would be honored to provide the musical backdrop for your happily ever after,

**Services Offered**

Whether you envision strolling down the aisle to the soothing sound of violins or being serenaded by a string quartet during your cocktail hour, Amadeus Music Group can elevate your event with music. The group's versatility and intuitive approach ensure that every piece played on your special day will reflect you, your beloved, and the atmosphere of your celebration. You will meet with the group's music consultant before your big day to explore the many different instruments, genres, and performance styles they offer. If you're a fan of contemporary musical selections, prepare to be amazed, as Amadeus Music Group can bring an orchestral twist to the latest tunes! For wedding music that echoes the love and joy that brought you to your special day, Amadeus Music Group is the perfect choice.
