## About this vendor

![Duane Zobrist](https://cdn0.weddingwire.com/vendor/004312/original/960/jpg/uo_2213400_b9e2657f-2f51-4e4c-9e7d-26f774c3c269.jpeg?r=1759429727)

Peak To Prairie Western is a company that creates custom hats for unforgettable weddings and events in Colorado Springs, Colorado. Its skilled and professional team believes that every celebration deserves a signature touch, and they specialize in creating entertaining, immersive, and interactive hat-shaping experiences for couples and their guests. The business is owned by Duane Zobrist, an entrepreneur and storyteller who shows a deep appreciation for heritage and craftsmanship in his work.

**Services Offered**

Family and friends can step up to the steaming stations and select from premium wool felt or beaver blend before watching the expert shapers create art in front of their eyes. Peak To Prairie Western can do it all, from cowboy hats to fedoras; their creations match every style and wedding aesthetic. Nearlyweds can choose from a range of packages, including the Heritage Package, which offers a luxury guest experience and flexible options to fit each vision and budget.
