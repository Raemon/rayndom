## About this vendor

__

No photo yet

No owner details yet.

__1 year in business

__We speak English

__Small team (2-10 members)

Personalized Event Rentals, based in Englewood, Ohio, provides fantastic rental solutions for weddings and other milestone events. Their talented team will work alongside soon-to-be-weds every step of the way so they can enjoy every precious moment. From birthdays and corporate events to the special day itself, they go above and beyond to exceed any expectations.

**Services Offered**

Their tailored services ensure that couples get everything they are looking for and more to bring their special event to life. They offer a range of tables and chairs for a seamless and unforgettable experience on the special day. Their selection ranges from round and rectangular options to plastic and padded items to suit the occasion. They travel to events throughout Central and Southwest Ohio. Reach out to Personalized Event Rentals today to find out more about the packages they offer.
