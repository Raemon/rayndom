## About

Set on 25 waterfront acres, Craneway Pavilion delivers an awe-inspiring panorama of the Bay, San Francisco skyline and surrounding environs—matched only by the dazzling architecture and high-end appointments available inside.

Our spacious, open design make this the ideal site for world-class corporate meetings, product launches, speaking engagements, concerts, film or photo shoots and celebrations for any occasion.

Our team will help you to create a unique and exciting, top-level experience that guests will remember for years to come. With full production capability and an extensive network of stellar vendor partners, our staff will encourage your daring, energize event programming and support all involved to think creatively, dexterously and with a wholistic vision of success. At Craneway Pavilion, our metric of success is fully integrated with the success of our clients. We invite you to let your imagination lead the way.
