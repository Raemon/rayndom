JvEnt is a premier wedding entertainment company operating out of Germantown, Maryland. This business is led by the talented DJ Vargvs, who brings cultures together and creates unforgettable experiences at weddings and special events.

**Services Offered**

As you get ready to celebrate, meet with DJ Vargvs and describe your goals for the big day. He can create a custom package that meets all of your entertainment needs. In addition to crafting a playlist that matches your tastes, DJ Vargvs offers state-of-the-art sound equipment, MC services, and timeline coordination to keep the events of the evening running smoothly throughout the evening. With JvEnt, you and your guests are in for an incredible evening of fun as you tie the knot. Just reach out, and get a complimentary quote for your special day.
