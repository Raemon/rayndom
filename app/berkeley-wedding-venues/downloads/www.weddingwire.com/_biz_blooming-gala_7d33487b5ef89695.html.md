Blooming Gala is a wedding florist service based in Fountain Valley, California. They also serve all of Orange County, Los Angeles County, Riverside County, and parts of San Diego County. This business is owned and operated by Debbie Hill. She has a wealth of experience creating spectacular arrangements for special events. She is proud to use fresh, locally-sourced florals for each design!

**Services Offered**

Blooming Gala can meet with you to design a range of different floral accessories to match your wedding vision. You can commission this team to create bouquets, boutonnières, arches, centerpieces, and more. The team will manage all delivery and setup of your pieces so you can enjoy your wedding spectacle to the fullest.
