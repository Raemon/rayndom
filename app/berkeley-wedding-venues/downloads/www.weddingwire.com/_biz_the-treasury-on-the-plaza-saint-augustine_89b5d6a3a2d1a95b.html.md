Discover the perfect unique, historic venue for your wedding in downtown St. Augustine, Florida. The Treasury on the Plaza was once a grand bank dating back to the 1920s. The space has been transformed to perfectly fit modern weddings and events, but many of the incredible art-deco-era architectural details remain. Wow your guests with one of The Treasury's most talked about features: The Vault Bar! The bank vault from the 1920s has been transformed into a speakeasy-style bar, right in the middle of your reception space!

VENUE SPACES:

You'll receive exclusive access to ALL of The Treasury's jaw-dropping venue spaces on your wedding day.

The Grande Ballroom, with its towering ceilings and ornate columns, easily accommodates up to 175 guests for wedding ceremonies or seated banquets.

The Vault Bar, located inside The Grand Ballroom, was once a working bank vault! Not only does The Vault serve up incredible hand-crafted cocktails, it also serves as the perfect place to make a grand reception entrance that will keep your guests talking about your wedding for years to come!

The Treasury also has a versatile space for cocktail hour called The Exchange Gallery located next to The Grand Ballroom. The Grande Foyer is the perfect space to welcome guests, and take first looks in front of our famous bay window overlooking downtown St. Augustine's Central Park. Get ready in one of our two private suites, The Plaza Lounge and Bankers Lounge. We offer in-person or virtual tours 7 days a week! Our team would love to show you around and help you discover what makes The Treasury so special!

PACKAGES:

Do you want to customize every element of your wedding day, or do you prefer a wedding package with some of your vendors included? The Treasury has three unique wedding package options to choose from! Check out the "Pricing" section on WeddingWire for more package details. Our team is here to help you determine which package is best suited for your needs. Reach out today to get started on your planning journey!
