## About this vendor

![Alan Lai](https://cdn0.weddingwire.com/vendor/053522/original/960/jpg/uo_2225350_eb52954f-8fb4-47f6-adc0-341652f3a825.jpeg?r=1768353631)

#####  Alan Lai 

COO 

__Asian-owned

__1 year in business

__We speak Chinese, English and Hindi

__Small team (2-10 members)

Regale, based in sunny San Francisco, California, is a one-of-a-kind production company, offering unequaled services, creating unique, original, and touching wedding favors and gifts. The team aims to capture the human soul as guests share blessings and heartfelt stories that are brought together to craft a touching narrative of the big day’s magic. 

**Services Offered**

Regale is an interactive story engine that stitches together perspectives, memories, and well-wishes shared by the wedding’s cherished invitees. Using AI-driven prompts, the attendees’ words are synthesized, harvesting fresh and existing material to create and tell a singular, meaningful story. From cinematic eBooks and physical heirloom books to customized theme songs and audiobooks featuring loved ones’ actual voices, Regale’s services make for timeless keepsakes. Nearlyweds can contact the devoted and friendly Regale staff today to find out more about their offerings and packages.
