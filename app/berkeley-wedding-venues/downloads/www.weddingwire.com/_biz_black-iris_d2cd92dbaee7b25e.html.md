## About this vendor

![Jeff Palumbo](https://cdn0.weddingwire.com/vendor/421312/original/960/jpg/uo_2213124_83c3ef0a-8bea-4478-8540-7375cfaf4bc2.jpeg?r=1754609085)

#####  Jeff Palumbo 

Partner 

__11 years in business

__We speak English

__Small team (2-10 members)

Black Iris is a unique wedding venue in Richmond, Virginia, that welcomes you to host an unforgettable celebration with family and friends. This one-of-a-kind social club is a place where visions come to life for artists, thinkers, friends, and enthusiasts. That's why the team has built 5,000+ square feet of club space that acts as a blank canvas for your most precious day.

**Facilities and Capacity**

At Black Iris, visitors can enjoy all kinds of activities and events, from performances and exhibitions to games and some good conversation. More importantly, it provides a picture-perfect setting for all your wedding festivities. This venue accommodates 100 to 150 guests, making it an excellent choice if you plan to celebrate with your nearest and dearest. It features a gorgeous dressing room, allowing you to relax and prepare for this once-in-a-lifetime occasion. When it's time to say, 'I do,' you can tie the knot at the ceremony site and celebrate in the venue's reception area afterward.

**Services Offered**

In addition to providing spectacular event spaces for all your celebrations, Black Iris employs a team of professionals who can craft a seamless wedding experience. They offer top-notch event planning assistance to help you transform dreams into reality. What's more, the property boasts a full-service bar for you and your guests, ensuring everyone has a drink in hand all night long. ✨ “Black Iris Social Club offers an elegant private setting for our members to host weddings, receptions, and special occasions.  
Event-host memberships are available for couples who wish to celebrate with us.”
