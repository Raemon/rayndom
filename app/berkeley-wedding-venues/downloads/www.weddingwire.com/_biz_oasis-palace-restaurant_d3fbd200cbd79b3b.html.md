Based in Newark, California, Oasis Palace Restaurant is a wedding venue that contains various banquet halls. While the on-site restaurant fuses several cultures in its cuisine, its event halls blend classic luxury with contemporary features. It can be adapted to suit whatever the theme of your wedding may be. The hard-working team at this restaurant wants to help ease the pressures of planning a wedding in any way they can. They believe that the couple should be able to focus on enjoying their day and spending time with their loved ones.

**Facilities and Capacity**

Including the restaurant and various event halls, Oasis Palace covers 34,000 square feet. It boasts three stylish wedding spaces alongside three even more sumptuous halls, ideal for the couple who want an opulent experience. From the marble floors to the numerous chandeliers, this venue is for the couple who want to experience luxury. The Crystal Hall oozes glamour, while the Grand Ballroom will leave the happy couple feeling like royalty. Smooth white walls, shiny floors, and showstopping chandeliers in the Venetian Hall give couples an elegant blank canvas to work with. For a blend of tradition and glamour, Dynasty Hall is perfect for receptions and dancing. Spaces can be further decorated to truly fit your dream vision of your day. The restaurant’s multiple spaces can accommodate anything from an intimate gathering to a huge party, as it can hold up to 1,000 guests. This property is wheelchair accessible, as loved ones shouldn’t have to worry about difficulties attending your wedding.

**Services Offered**

Oasis Palace can provide couples with an event planner to aid them in fully personalizing their chosen hall to fit their specific wedding style. Get-ready rooms cut down on time spent moving between locations so you can worry less about keeping your guests waiting. Instead, you can savor the day and enjoy being surrounded by your nearest and dearest. Couples can opt for a casual buffet-style meal, allowing their loved ones to mill around and socialize, or they can choose plated service. This venue can also accommodate cocktail receptions and hors d’oeuvres. The happy couple can rest easy knowing that servers and bartenders can be provided allowing them to focus on making memories with their new partner to last a lifetime. This venue’s multiple halls can be used for either your ceremony or reception, your engagement party or rehearsal dinner. The restaurant’s team can take charge of setup and cleanup, leaving couples to say their goodbyes to their loved ones.
