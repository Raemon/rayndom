Say Yes! is a wedding officiant in the San Francisco East Bay area of California that has been performing ceremonies since September 2007. Janette Shutts is an ordained officiant who has had the pleasure of working with over**470** couples, helping them express their love and commitment in a customized ceremony that reflects them both. She loves helping create intimate and personalized ceremonies. Janette has received WeddingWire‚Äôs Bride‚Äôs Choice Award and Couple‚Äôs Choice Award from 2010 to 2025.

**Services Offered**

Say Yes! can officiate civil unions, interfaith ceremonies, non-religious, and single religion ceremonies for couples belonging to no denomination or who are Agnostic, Baptist, Episcopalian, Methodist, and Presbyterian. Her ceremony package includes:

  * Availability any day of the week
  * Customizable ceremony materials
  * Free consultation to learn about your vision
  * Professional ceremony performance



**Areas Covered**

Although Say Yes! is based in the East Bay Area (Tri-Valley, Pleasanton, Livermore, Dublin) Janette can also officiate in San Jose, Gilroy, Morgan Hill, Los Gatos, Saratoga, Campbell, Sacramento, San Francisco, Oakland, Mill Valley and the Monterey Bay areas as well as anywhere in California. If wanting out of state, she just has to check with the local county to see what their requirements are, but since Janette is ordained through the Universal Life Church she can perform ceremonies in almost every state!
