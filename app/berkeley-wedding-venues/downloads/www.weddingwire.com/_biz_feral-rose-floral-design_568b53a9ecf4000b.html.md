## About this vendor

![Sarah Poyourow](https://cdn0.weddingwire.com/vendor/253422/original/960/jpg/uo_2224352_ff57f810-89c5-46aa-b94e-d9110f10ab32.jpeg?r=1768299114)

#####  Sarah Poyourow 

Owner/ Creative Director 

__Woman-owned

__3 years in business

__We speak English

__Just me

Feral Rose Floral Design is a floral design studio in San Diego, California, serving weddings in the San Diego area and beyond. Led by talented florist Sarah Poyourow, these experts ensure that on your big day, you're surrounded by vibrant blooms that are an extension of your love story.

**Services Offered**

Sarah's unique design style combines a love for wild beauty with a dedication to refined elegance. She believes your wedding arrangements should feel expressive and intentional, enhancing the romantic atmosphere of your celebration. Before you tie the knot, meet with Sarah and describe your vision, theme, and favorite blooms. She'll craft a package that handles all of your needs, from corsages and boutonnieres to centerpieces, arches, and your stunning bouquet. Feral Rose Floral Design handles delivery and setup, so you can simply relax and enjoy the celebration with your nearest and dearest.
