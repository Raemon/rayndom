#####  Byron Bishop II 

Officiant 

__Black-owned

__3 years in business

__We speak English and Spanish

__Just me

Hitched Heart Ceremonies is a professional wedding officiant service in Madison, Wisconsin. Byron Bishop II, will be your skilled officiant on the big day, guiding you through the ceremony creation process step by step. 

**Services Offered**

As you prepare to tie the knot, meet with Byron, and he'll get to know you and learn about your love story. This experienced officiant with a warm personality is happy to work with couples of all backgrounds and identities. Together, you'll craft a personalized ceremony that reflects your individual personalities, shared values, and hopes for the future. Officiant services with Hitched Heart Ceremonies will include ceremony rehearsals, the personal delivery of your wedding license to the clerk, and follow-up calls leading up to the wedding day. With Byron, there won't be any issue leading to when you say, "I do."
