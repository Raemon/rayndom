__We speak English

Il Fornaio is a beautiful restaurant space ideal for a variety of pre-wedding celebrations, such as rehearsal dinners, wedding showers, and other events. Located in San Jose, California, Il Fornaio serves up Italian dishes in a setting that makes couples feel like they've wandered straight into a restaurant in the middle of Rome.

**Services Offered**

The Il Fornaio team works with each couple to create a comprehensive menu of dishes featuring starters such as calamaretti fritti and bruschetta al pomodoro, as well as classic mains such as tagliatelle bolognese and linguine mare chiaro. Couples can indulge their guests in desserts, such as gelato and tiramisu, which provide them with a taste of Italy. Each event at Il Fornaio is handled by their in-house event coordinator meaning the couple can relax and enjoy their celebration without any pressure.
