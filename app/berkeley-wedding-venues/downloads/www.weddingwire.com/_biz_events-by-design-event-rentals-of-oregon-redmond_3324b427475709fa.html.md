## About

Events by Design, Event Rentals of Oregon, is an experienced wedding event rental business located in Redmond, Oregon. With over 25 years of combined experience in the wedding industry, their staff specialize in event planning, day-of event coordination, theme decorating, and wedding rentals. The staff is committed to providing a stress-free, unique experience for each couple.  
  
**Services Offered**  
The services offered by Events by Design, Event Rentals of Oregon extend to delivery, set up, and clean up for ordered event pieces. Wedding planning services can also be discussed with their professional party planners, who can assist couples with every aspect from beginning to end, or just provide day-of coordination. The business holds an inventory of the following items:

  * Aisle runners
  * Altar/arch
  * Audio equipment
  * Barware
  * Centerpieces
  * Chairs and chair covers
  * China
  * Chuppah
  * Dance floors
  * Decor
  * Dinnerware
  * Drapery
  * Flatware
  * Glassware
  * Lights
  * Linens
  * Signage
  * Tables
  * Tents and tent accessories
  * Trinkets
  * Vases
