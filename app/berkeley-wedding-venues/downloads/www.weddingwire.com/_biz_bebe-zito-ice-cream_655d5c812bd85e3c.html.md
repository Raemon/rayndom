## About this vendor

![Ben and Gabriella](https://cdn0.weddingwire.com/vendor/150351/original/960/jpg/uo_2153051_cb18acd0-d253-49c9-a96b-a8e3ee40494c.jpeg?r=1759340932)

#####  Ben and Gabriella 

Owners 

__Hispanic or Latinx-owned, Woman-owned

__6 years in business

__We speak English, Portuguese and Spanish

__Large team (21+ members)

Bebe Zito Ice Cream is a wedding catering business in Minneapolis, Minnesota, that offers delicious ice cream at weddings and special events. The company is owned by Ben and Gabriella, whose passion lies in providing you and your loved ones with tasty flavors made using high-quality ingredients.

**Services Offered**

With Bebe Zito Ice Cream, you will have a delightful ice cream station set up at your venue, ready to serve your guests. Gluten-free, dairy-free, and vegan options are available for anyone who may have those specific dietary requirements, so everyone can enjoy the wonderful offerings this company brings. The ice cream at Bebe Zito Ice Cream is served in single-serving cups, cones, or as ice cream sandwiches. You can even rent out a dip cabinet with scoop artists!

**Cuisine**

This is a small-batch ice cream business that was born out of a true love for this frozen treat. All ice cream is made with creativity and with quality in mind. Some of the more unique flavors include Animal Frosting, Blackberry Butter Birthday Cake, Blueberry London Fog, and Brazilian Rocky Road, to name a few.
