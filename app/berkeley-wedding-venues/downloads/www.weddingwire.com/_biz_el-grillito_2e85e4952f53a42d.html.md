## About this vendor

![Juan Carlos](https://cdn0.weddingwire.com/vendor/668212/original/960/jpg/uo_2212866_854ebaa9-b999-47f0-8e5a-e40e6a4ea240.jpeg?r=1750948037)

#####  Juan Carlos 

Owner 

__Hispanic or Latinx-owned, Woman-owned

__4 years in business

__We speak English and Spanish

__Small team (2-10 members)

El Grillito is a wedding catering company in Cooper Square, New York, offering a delicious slice of Mexican cuisine on the big day. The name of the business is a reference to the owner, Juan Carlos' grandmother, who would take him to the local fields to play games as a child. She was a massive inspiration in his life and his culinary habits, telling him to cook from the heart and that it would always taste great. Juan Carlos has more than 20 years of experience in the kitchen and knows how to make the wedding day truly mouthwatering.

**Services Offered**

El Grillito offers catering services for weddings of all sizes, from an intimate gathering of close family and friends to a lavish affair welcoming hundreds of guests. Nearlyweds can arrange a meeting with Juan Carlos to discuss the menu in detail and to customize it to match their wedding day desires. From burritos and quesadillas to tacos and tortas, El Grillito cooks everything fresh to ensure the wedding day tastes incredible and remains unforgettable.
