Electra Yacht Charters is a boat wedding venue located in Richmond, California. You can say I do while embarking on a trip across the iconic and beautiful San Francisco Bay. Originally designed and built in 1930, it offers a classic and timeless feel for your magical day.

**Facilities and Capacity**

Electra Yacht Charters offers a selection of event spaces for your ceremony and reception. The yacht features large windows that pool natural light and warm sunrays throughout the boat. With a backdrop of glistening waters and the stunning Golden Gate Bridge, you can trust your wedding day will be unforgettable. The vessel boasts a vintage and luxurious feel, with exposed wooden beams and warm lighting. It has a sense of old-world charm and is ideal for couples who are hoping to host a unique event on the open waters. After you say I do, you and your guests can enjoy mingling by the bar area, which offers stunning views and a number of seating options.

**Services Offered**

Whether you are planning a small, intimate gathering or a large-scale celebration, the team can help bring your vision to life. Their wedding experts can work closely with you to ensure your wedding dreams come true. They ensure every detail is considered, from music to decorations and your wedding feast menu. You can choose from a range of customizable packages that include catering and entertainment and can be tailored to suit your budget. The team would be honored to be a part of your special day.
