Tree House Heaven is a stunning outdoor venue for weddings and other special events nestled just outside of Battle Lake, Minnesota. With the town just a 10-minute drive away, plus an array of eye-catching lakes and natural surroundings, it is truly a one-of-a-kind setting for any celebration. Newlyweds can wow their guests with a truly unique countryside escape for an unforgettable experience unlike any other.

**Facilities and Capacity**

This multi-acre treehouse estate is encompassed by three massive oak trees, which blend rustic charm and modern elegance. Couples can start their day off with an enchanting ceremony on the soaring decks of the tree house with up to 200 guests. Or choose the perfectly manicured lawns with panoramic landscape views set at its backdrop, with space for up to 400 people. Then, newlyweds can host a whimsical garden-style reception where they can dine, sip, and dance the night away under a glistening sky full of stars.

**Services Offered**

Their talented events team specializes in award-winning planning and luxury amenities that transform any occasion into a once-in-a-lifetime celebration. They go above and beyond to ensure every detail of the wedding day truly reflects the wonderful love story behind it. A choice of packages with one or two-day options is available that include exclusive access during the stay, table and chair rentals, linens, a day-of coordinator, and plenty more. Soon-to-be-weds can reach out to Tree House Heaven today to find out more about the services they offer and get started on planning the outdoor wedding they have always dreamed of.
