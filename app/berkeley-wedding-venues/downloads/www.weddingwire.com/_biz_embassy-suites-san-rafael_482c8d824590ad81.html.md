## About this vendor

![Michelle Ranger](https://cdn0.weddingwire.com/vendor/769091/original/960/jpg/uo_1190967_6ae980a3-08b3-45c2-860d-70836d0c6891.jpeg?r=1746477561)

Director of Sales 

__41 years in business

__We speak English and Spanish

__Small team (2-10 members)

Embassy Suites San Rafael is a hotel wedding venue located in San Rafael, California. Situated north of the famous San Francisco Golden Gate Bridge, this property is ideal for couples who want to be near this iconic bridge as well as Sonoma and Napa Valley. With over 19,000 square feet of event space in combination with friendly service and top-notch amenities, this venue is sure to take great care of you and your loved ones on your special day.

**Facilities and Capacity**

It doesn't matter if your wedding guest list is big or small, Embassy Suites San Rafael offers 19 meeting areas that can fit a number of gathering sizes. To begin your big day on a good note, the venue will provide you with a get-ready suite where you and your close wedding party can prepare for your celebrations. Both ceremonies and receptions can take place in the same event hall if desired. Their largest room is the San Rafael Ballroom, which has 6,048 square feet of space and can accommodate up to 500 people. It boasts a contemporary yet minimalistic design that can easily suit a range of themes and decor layouts. The rooms also come with WiFi and AV equipment for projections.

**Services Offered**

When planning your big day at Embassy Suites San Rafael, the team will go above and beyond your expectations to ensure a memorable day. The head chef and his talented crew can craft your evening meal. With your taste buds in mind, they will create delicious cuisine that everyone can enjoy. After your celebrations, guests can decide if they would like to stay overnight in their comfortable accommodation. What's more, the hotel offers a fitness center, an indoor pool, and pet-friendly rooms.
