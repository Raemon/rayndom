## About this vendor

![Jacqueline Thain](https://cdn0.weddingwire.com/vendor/810322/original/960/jpg/uo_2223018_5320d0e8-3edb-4283-8958-e7403e6ab204.jpeg?r=1764868766)

#####  Jacqueline Thain 

Owner 

__Woman-owned

__5 years in business

__We speak English and Spanish

__Small team (2-10 members)

At Rue & Pear Flower Co. in Loveland, CO, we design wedding florals with a focus on clarity, collaboration, and exceptional execution. As a farmer-florist studio, we grow a significant portion of our flowers, which means our clients receive fresher, more unique, and truly seasonal blooms.  
  
Our process is built around you. We take the time to understand your style, priorities, and budget, and then create designs that support the experience you want your guests to have. We communicate clearly, provide guidance where it’s needed, and handle the floral details so you can move through planning with confidence.  
  
  
Whether you’re choosing an elopement bundle or a full design plan, you can expect professionalism, reliability, and thoughtful design grounded in both artistry and horticultural knowledge. Our goal is simple: to deliver flowers that elevate your wedding day and make the planning process easier, not harder.  
  
  
From intimate elopements to full-scale weddings, each piece is crafted with creativity, intention, and respect for the season. Our goal is for you to feel supported, understood, and celebrated throughout the entire design process.

  
If you’re looking for florals that feel authentic, design-driven, and rooted in sustainable growing practices, we’re here to create something meaningful for you.
