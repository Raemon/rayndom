## About

Pretty Tea Parties and Events is an intimate venue in Walnut Creek, California where you can welcome **up to 40** of your nearest and dearest for an unforgettable small-scale gathering. If you envisage an elegant setting for your function, this unique destination is just that and more. Attentive staff will await your arrival and provide you with all the assistance you need to make your experience a resounding success.

We are a wonderful private event site for a Bridal Shower, Baby Shower, Engagement Celebration, After Rehearsal, Reception or any other celebration for **up to 40** of your dearest guests.

**Facilities and Capacity**

Immerse yourself in old-world inspired charm as you enter the main event area at Pretty Tea Parties and Events. Decadent gold scrollwork and large oil paintings line the walls while magnificent chandeliers hang overhead. Vintage French furniture adds a sense of European sophistication to the room's atmosphere, while the cozy fireplace displays a stunning vintage clock and matching candelabras for another welcoming feature. You will also find a section for your ceremony in front of a gorgeous flower wall. The venue has private get-ready facilities available for your pre-event preparations.

**Services Offered**

Pretty Tea Parties and Events strives to make your entire planning process feel stress-free by offering a selection of mindfully curated service packages. What’s more, your rental of the property also includes the use of the venue’s various event furniture collections. On the day of your function, the staff here provide set-up services to ensure everything is ready to go when you arrive. Caterers will find a well lit kitchen for plating and serving. The property has sufficient parking spaces for your gathering, while the entire venue is entirely wheelchair accessible for you and all of your guests' peace of mind.
