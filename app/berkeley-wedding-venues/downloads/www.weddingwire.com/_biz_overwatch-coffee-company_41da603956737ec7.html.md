## About this vendor

![Taylor Barnes](https://cdn0.weddingwire.com/vendor/829322/original/960/jpg/uo_2223928_945bc2b9-ef4c-47ad-aa63-9e97788a7603.jpeg?r=1765993672)

#####  Taylor Barnes 

Founder & CEO 

__Veteran-owned

__1 year in business

__We speak English

__Small team (2-10 members)

Overwatch Coffee Company is a veteran-owned, active-duty-operated mobile coffee and specialty beverage catering service based in Beaufort, South Carolina. Founded by active-duty military officers, we pair disciplined execution with thoughtful hospitality—delivering an elevated coffee experience for weddings, milestone events, and Marine Corps boot camp graduations at MCRD Parris Island. We are intentionally built to serve high-volume crowds without sacrificing quality. Our systems, equipment, and team are designed for speed, consistency, and professionalism—allowing us to serve hundreds of guests efficiently while maintaining the craftsmanship and care expected of a premium coffee experience. For families celebrating their our customers, that means shorter lines, smooth service, and exceptional drinks served with warmth and professionalism. We believe the best moments are built on connection and care. Our customer-first approach ensures every interaction feels personal, even at scale. We understand the significance of these moments, our approach is intentionally customer-first, designed to feel effortless for couples and unforgettable for guests. From streamlined setup and smooth service flow to personalized menus and signature drinks, every detail is handled with quiet efficiency—so you can stay present and enjoy your day. Rooted in service and built on efficiency, our wedding offerings are designed with a romantic, contemporary aesthetic that complements your vision rather than competes with it. Clean design, thoughtful presentation, and genuine hospitality allow our coffee bar to blend seamlessly into your event while still standing out as a memorable experience. Whether it’s a signature latte inspired by your love story, a stylish espresso bar for cocktail hour, or cozy drinks to keep guests lingering a little longer, Overwatch Coffee Company delivers intentional service, creative expression, and heartfelt hospitality—crafted to make your celebration feel as meaningful as it is beautiful.
