__Small team (2-10 members)

What the Pluck?! is a classical-style live wedding band in North Babylon, New York. Expertly trained, these talented musicians have entertained wedding crowds together since 2019. Their style incorporates classical renditions of popular modern-day songs and various other musical styles to complement any occasion. 

**Services Offered**

Happily switching between classical, pop, jazz, folk, and movie or show themes, What the Pluck?! offers exceptional musical accompaniment for your wedding day. Their packages include ceremony-only music or delightful background tunes for your ceremony and reception. The musicians would be delighted to discuss your preferences with you, helping you select the perfect tunes to enhance the ambiance of your special day. Reach out to What the Pluck?! and allow these talented artists to bring a touch of elegance and class to your celebration.
