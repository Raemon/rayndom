## About

Operating out of Modesto, California, Bogdan Condor Photography is a wedding photography company that can help capture the most precious moments of your life. Owner and operator Bogdan Condor is a passionate storyteller with the expertise to document your day in stunning detail. He likes to craft timeless and elegant imagery you'll get to cherish for years to come. 

**Services Offered**

At Bogdan Condor Photography, Bogdan will go above and beyond to capture the essence of your one-of-a-kind love story. He'll ensure you feel comfortable in front of the camera so all your genuine emotions shine through. In addition to Bogdan's top-notch photography skills, he offers various comprehensive services. You can opt for wedding portraits, an engagement shoot, and 'trash the dress' pictures. When the big day comes around, Bogdan will provide a second shooter, as well as drone footage. Afterward, you'll receive countless stunning digital files of your special day and a spectacular wedding album to treasure forever!
