Situated in Dallas, Texas, WalliCo Diamonds offers beautiful jewelry, including wedding bands and engagement rings. This team of professionals with work with you for a truly personalized and positive experience. From natural and lab-grown diamonds to rare colored gemstones, at WalliCo Diamonds, you are sure to find something that will speak to you.

**Services Offered**

At WalliCo Diamonds, the crew specializes in custom rings and fine jewelry that represent the love shared between you and your future spouse. From the first consultation to the day your lovely piece is delivered, they will strive to ensure the smallest detail is to your taste. You may choose between a virtual appointment or meeting the team in person, depending on your preference and availability. You may even opt to browse the online store and contact the company with any questions you may have. WalliCo Diamonds offers nationwide delivery and will hand-deliver items in Dallas.
