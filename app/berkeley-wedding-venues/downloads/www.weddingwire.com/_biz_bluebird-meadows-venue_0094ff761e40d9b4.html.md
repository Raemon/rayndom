## About this vendor

![Sara Little](https://cdn0.weddingwire.com/vendor/277422/original/960/jpg/uo_2224772_3b3c920e-e5f3-4a93-bfc6-a17dcfbe962c.jpeg?r=1767675951)

Owner 

__No business details yet

__1 year in business

__We speak English

__Small team (2-10 members)

Located in the mountains of Chewelah, Washington, Bluebird Meadows Venue is a place where wedding dreams come true and everlasting memories are made. This charming mountain farm, measuring over 300 acres, is located just 40 minutes outside of Spokane, WA, and an hour from CDA, ID.

**Facilities and Capacity**

Bluebird Meadows Venue features a 1920 era barn with a beautiful chandelier and milled solid timber floors designed to hold an elegant, whimsical wedding that you and up to 150 of your family and friends will love. There is also another barn, built circa 1900, located on the property that serves as a lovely, picturesque backdrop. With expansive views in all directions, it truly is a magical place meant for special occasions. Bluebird Meadows offers two houses on-site, meaning you and your loved ones can make the wedding a weekend event. Please do not hesitate to call with any questions. We would be happy to host your dream wedding.
