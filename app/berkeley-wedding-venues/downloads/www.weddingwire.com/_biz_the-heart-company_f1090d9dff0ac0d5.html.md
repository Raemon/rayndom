THE HEART COMPANY, based in New York City, is a woman-founded vegan beauty brand that strives to spread love and positivity through carefully crafted wedding fragrances for the perfect gift or favor. All of their products reflect their ethical and sustainable practices and are registered with The Vegan Society, Birmingham, UK. Their specialty fragrances make for great wedding gifts or favors, adding a touch of luxury and romance to every celebration.

The team at THE HEART COMPANY believes that every love story deserves a memorable scent. That is where Wedding Love, their perfume specifically designed for weddings, comes in. With an elegant blend of florals, romantic accents, and a touch of warmth, this fragrance will enchant the senses. Beyond serving as the signature perfume for your big day, Wedding Love will be able to bring you back to your precious memories every time you wear it. This perfume and the rest of THE HEART COMPANY's collection also make thoughtful gifts for wedding parties and guests.
