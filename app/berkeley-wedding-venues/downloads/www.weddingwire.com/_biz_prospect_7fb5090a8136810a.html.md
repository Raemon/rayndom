Prospect is an urban, farm-to-table restaurant and wedding venue located in the financial district of San Francisco, California. This contemporary locale has been welcoming patrons for unforgettable experiences since 2010.

**Facilities and Capacity**

Situated on the ground floor of San Francisco’s Infinity Towers, just a block from the Embarcadero and not far from the San Francisco International Airport, Prospect offers a convenient location for you and your guests. Inside, the warm interior has been thoughtfully designed to create an atmosphere that is refined yet approachable. You and up to 65 guests can celebrate in The Candace Room, a private space with floor-to-ceiling windows for taking in the stunning cityscape. Alternatively, enjoy the loft-like semi-private dining area, which boasts similar views from its large windows for up to 40 guests to enjoy. Finally, the entire restaurant, replete with a large bar area, can be privately booked for celebrations of up to 200 individuals. No matter how you choose to celebrate, you’ll be treated to an exceptional meal of California cuisine made using seasonal ingredients sourced locally from farms and markets.

**Services Offered**

Prospect is proud of its hospitality-driven approach. There, you and your loved ones will feel welcomed by friendly and attentive service from the moment you arrive. They’ll happily work with you to craft a custom menu, allowing you to tailor your special day to your exact vision. From its convenient location to its inviting atmosphere and impeccable food and service, Prospect may be the perfect setting for your journey into forever.
