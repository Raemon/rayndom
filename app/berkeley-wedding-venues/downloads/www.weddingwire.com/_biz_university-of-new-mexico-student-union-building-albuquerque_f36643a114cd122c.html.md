## About this vendor

![River Moore](https://cdn0.weddingwire.com/vendor/682811/original/960/jpg/uo_118286_15523ce3-c2d9-4efe-bcff-b59704f4b094.jpeg?r=1757376937)

Based in Albuquerque, New Mexico, the University of New Mexico Student Union Building is a professional venue available for weddings and special events. Boasting a variety of spaces, it offers a flexible backdrop to host life's most cherished occasions. Whether you dream of an intimate gathering or a grand, gala-like reception, this exceptional venue offers the ideal setting for your special day.

**Facilities and Capacity**

The Student Union Building boasts 21 event spaces, with the ability to accommodate up to 2,000 people. The Grand Ballroom is the main function room, with 11,000 square feet and balcony access. This space features high ceilings and a twinkling chandelier that adds to the ambiance of the occasion. The Grand Ballroom welcomes up to 700 guests for banquet-style celebrations. The Student Union Building includes three additional ballrooms, with a capacity of 180-240 for dining and dancing. The venue also includes a selection of large and small meeting rooms, as well as additional spaces like the bright and airy atriums and the cozy theatre room. The University of New Mexico Student Union is able to provide a selection of event items to help set up your celebration. These include tables, chairs, and audiovisual equipment to be used by your chosen entertainers.

**Services Offered**

Couples who host their wedding celebration in the Student Union Building can avail themselves of a range of services. A dedicated team of event planners will be on hand throughout the entire process, helping coordinate and set up your dream celebration. On-site catering services are available, providing delicious dishes to complement the theme and style of your special day. Reach out to The University of New Mexico Student Union Building and begin planning your wedding within this fully equipped, professional event venue.
