#####  Parker Orms 

Owner 

__4 years in business

__We speak English and Spanish

__Small team (2-10 members)

Hats by Parker Thomas is a company in Wheat Ridge, Colorado, that offers delightful wedding favors in the form of personalized hats. Parker Thomas is a third-generation hat maker who brings a wealth of expertise and legacy to their services and products. The team of creatives will go all out to offer the most personalized, high-quality service, ensuring your gifts match your unique vision. Previous clients include well-known companies like Virgin Galactic, Safari Club International, and Lucchese.

**Services Offered**

Whether you're planning an intimate occasion or a large-scale gathering, Hats by Parker Thomas can accommodate any number of guests. Spoil your friends and family with custom hats they can cherish as a memento of your big day. They would be delighted to bring the niche art of hat-making to your next special event. Reach out to the team today for more information about their packages and services.
