## About

One of the thirty venues to be recognized on Washingtonian Magazine's list of "Very Best Wedding Vendors: Reception Spaces & Venues" in the DC Metro Area.

Located in Alexandria, VA, the Torpedo Factory Art Center is a museum venue that hosts weddings, corporate events, bar/bat mitzvahs, and much more. This historic property is set on the waterfront, offering spectacular views of the Potomac River. Washington D.C is nearby, allowing couples to enjoy an intimate and private affair while being close to the lively ambiance of a city. An outdoor patio, which can be tented, is also available for rent for cocktail hour or ceremonies.

Couples who choose to marry at the Art Center can expect a truly spectacular setting for their celebration and a staff that is eager to help you plan and execute your dream day. The Torpedo Factory Art Center was once a working torpedo factory before opening to the public in 1974. It now acts as a studio for artists who create one-of-a-kind pieces. These pieces are showcased throughout the venue, offering a multitude of opportunities for distinctive wedding photos. The property has retained its authentic industrial charm, with exposed piping and high ceilings. Combined with artistic embellishments, this creates a modern and stylish environment for you and your guests to enjoy.

The Torpedo Factory Art Center can host up to 220 guests for banquet-style occasions and up to 700 people for a standing cocktail reception. Along with ceremonies and receptions, it offers an elegant backdrop for pre-nuptial celebrations, including engagement parties and rehearsal dinners. We also offer the Overlook Room on the third floor for cocktail hour and small ceremonies. It boasts 50-foot panoramic views of the Potomac River and has been wowing guests since its opening in 2023.

Dedicated staff will set up draping and adjust the lighting, including up-lights and bistro lights, at no additional cost to you. This museum also welcomes outside vendors, allowing you to add your personal touch to your day. Vendor parking is reserved in front of the venue, provided free of charge by the City of Alexandria. For your convenience, we can recommend a list of catering companies that we have worked with for years and trust implicitly. Dancing and music are encouraged on our spacious dance floor, which eliminates the need for additional flooring rental. Parking is available nearby for guests who drive.

What You'll Love About Torpedo Factory Art Center:

Couples found the staff professional, creative, dedicated, and always ready to help clients save costs. They appreciated that the venue is flexible and will accommodate any aesthetic. Its beautiful views and industrial vibe have suited various wedding themes. Notably, you are allowed to bring your purchased alcohol, including red wine.
