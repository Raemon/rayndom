#####  Kelsea Fischer 

Owner 

__Woman-owned

__9 years in business

__We speak English

__Just me

Based in Castle Rock, Colorado, Fischer Fine Art offers a one-of-a-kind classical style of live wedding painting. This style of training takes a long time to achieve, and the outcome is a realistic style of painting. The painting style will capture the essence of your wedding day and make the perfect addition to your home after the big day. Fischer Fine Art works with oil on panels and comes in multiple sizes. Premium supplies are also used.
