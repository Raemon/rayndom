## About

Kiara Event Planning is a wedding coordination business located out of Hayward, California. Owners Aokash and Sharon Chand treat all of their clients as close friends and work hard to bring their wedding visions to life. With over ten years of experience, this couple decided to launch their own business and they chose to name it after their daughter. By utilizing their innovative ideas and professional mindsets, this duo hopes to create personalized and unforgettable occasions for their clients. Any special event is about creating lasting memories and this team is dedicated to helping plan that incredible occasion.

**Services Offered**

Kiara Event Planning is on hand to guarantee that your event runs as smoothly as possible. Couples can choose a day-of coordination package or they can opt for full planning assistance, there is a service to suit every circumstance. From budget analysis and vendor referrals to preparing guest lists and timelines, this dynamic duo will work hard to make sure that your day is as stress-free as possible.
