## About

Hannah Mason Art is a watercolor artist available to live paint at wedding celebrations and special events, or paint your portrait or bouquet, based in Lafayette, Louisiana but able to travel anywhere. Hannah can beautifully capture a moment from your special day to create a timeless masterpiece that can be enjoyed for years to come.

**Services Offered**

Hannah Mason Art is available to live paint at your wedding, and can also create a painting as a wedding gift, both before and after your special day. Hannahâ€™s live painting services provide a unique addition to wedding celebrations and allow you and your guests to marvel at your painting being created in real-time. She works to interpret the essence of your day and the key details to create a stunning visual depiction that encapsulates your wedding occasion. Bespoke wedding commissions can serve as delightful, one-of-a-kind gifts or mementos of your special day.
