Nestled by blossoming blooms and an enchanting atmosphere, Ruth Bancroft Garden is a one-of-a-kind wedding venue located in Walnut Creek, California. Ideal for couples who adore the outdoors, this botanical venue boasts natural beauty around every corner. This destination can grant you every wish with picturesque spaces, streamlined services, and more.

**Facilities and Capacity**

The Ruth Bancroft Garden offers indoor and outdoor venues for your engagement party, wedding shower, rehearsal dinner, elopement, ceremony, or reception. The garden is colorful, with various floral features, from waving trees to round cacti. This garden also includes a small pond with multiple rocks and a smooth stone edge. You and your partner may also say "I do" in front of the charming gazebo or dance with all your loved ones in the modern Great Room. This stylish room has polished floors, white walls, and circular lighting. Furthermore, the venue will provide you, your partner, and your party members with a contemporary chic dressing room.

**Services Offered**

The management team at Ruth Bancroft Garden hopes to make your event as memorable as possible. Thus, they provide several services, such as setup, cleanup, and event rentals, so your day looks flawless. These items may vary between dinner tables and a state-of-the-art projecto. These wedding professionals will also be honored to work with your outside vendors to ensure every inch of your day reflects your personality and love story.

**What You'll Love About The Ruth Bancroft Garden**

The Ruth Bancroft Garden offers a beautiful, picture-perfect setting for the most important day of your life. In addition to its dedicated staff, breathtaking scenery, and flexible event spaces, the property also invites you to book a private tour. This way, you can check out the venue for yourself and see how it can help you transform sparkling dreams into reality!
