Tokio Sushi Truck is a catering company in Philadelphia, Pennsylvania, offering a fresh, modern, on‑site sushi experience on the wedding day.

**About Us**

•Tokio Sushi Truck provides premium sushi catering directly at your event.

•• Modern and professional experience.

•• Fresh sushi prepared on-site.

•• Service available for small and large events, private parties, and all types of gatherings.

**Why Choose Tokyo Sushi for Your Event?**

Premium Quality Ingredients

We use only fresh, high■quality fish and ingredients to ensure every bite is memorable.

Professional On■Site Service

Our team prepares and serves sushi on■site, creating a live culinary experience your guests will love.

Customizable Menus

Choose from a variety of specialty rolls, classic rolls, nigiri, and appetizers.

We tailor every menu to match the style and needs of your event.

Perfect for Weddings & Special Occasions

We provide a refined presentation, elegant setup, and smooth service that enhance the atmosphere of

any wedding or celebration.

Pricing Information

Final pricing depends on:

• The menu items you select

• The number of guests

• The duration of service

• The distance/travel to your venue

Once we receive these details, we can prepare a personalized quote for your event.
