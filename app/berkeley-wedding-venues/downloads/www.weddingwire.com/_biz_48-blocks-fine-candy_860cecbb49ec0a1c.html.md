## About

48 Blocks Fine Candy48 Blocks Fine Candy is a wedding favors and gifts company in Falls Church, VA. The business crafts traditional confections in non-traditional flavors and turns traditions on its head. Using family recipes for marshmallows and more, we're creating unique sweets for discerning customers in DC, MD, and VA.

All our marshmallows, brittles, and fruit patés are handmade in small batches in our northern Virginia facility. We use locally sourced specialty ingredients from the mid-Atlantic when possible, including local honey, for our Bee Kind marshmallow collection. With more than 100 traditional and non-traditional flavors, we offer bespoke wedding favors and guest bag treats.

Check out the gourmet s'mores boxes - our best-selling wedding favor. It's available in a variety of flavors and can be customized in multiple ways! Curate your selection of marshmallow flavors for wedding guest bags, or maybe just keep it simple and select Brown Butter Bourbon Krispy Treats for the after-party. Whatever you choose, the 48 Blocks team is here to ensure it's the perfect sweet addition to your celebration.

48 Blocks Fine Candy offers an extensive selection of gluten-free confections and will work with you to ensure all your guests can celebrate sweetly!

We invite you to forget everything you think about marshmallows and more until you try the unique sweets from 48 Blocks Fine Candy.
