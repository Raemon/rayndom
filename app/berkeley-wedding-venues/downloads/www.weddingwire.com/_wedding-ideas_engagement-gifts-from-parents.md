# 23 Engagement Gifts From Parents to the Happy Couple

If your son or daughter just got engaged, these thoughtful engagement gifts will kickstart the celebrations to come.

We have included third party products to help you navigate and enjoy life’s biggest moments. Purchases made through links on this page may earn us a commission.

One minute you were cradling them in your arms, and now they've found love in the arms of another. Your little girl or boy is getting married and you want to get them the perfect [engagement gift](https://www.weddingwire.com/wedding-ideas/engagement-party-gift-etiquette) before the wedding planning gets into full swing. Cue these engagement gifts from parents. If you're ready to start shopping, we've hand-picked some of the best gifts for the newly engaged couple, whether you're the parents of the bride-to-be, parents of the groom, a future parent-in-law, or another guardian family member. Below, you'll find [unique engagement gift ideas](https://www.weddingwire.com/wedding-ideas/engagement-gift-ideas) to let the happy couple know just how excited you are for them and their big day to come, including sentimental keepsakes, practical engagement gifts, and everything in between.

## Preserved Flower Bouquet
