## About this vendor

![Ben Kaye-Skinner](https://cdn0.weddingwire.com/vendor/460302/original/960/jpg/uo_2203064_06879794-c81d-424c-965a-de4bfd3e3429.jpeg?r=1746555479)

#####  Ben Kaye-Skinner 

Musician 

__15 years in business

__We speak English and Spanish

__Just me

Ben Kaye-Skinner is a music composer and musician from Geneva, Nebraska, who performs at weddings and special events. Ben brings more than 15 years of experience, having performed at several weddings and even composed music for his own nuptials. He understands what audiences are looking for and what is practical in various situations.

**Services Offered**

Whether you love classical music like Mozart, enjoy the talent of Nat King Cole, prefer dancing to Beyonce, or headbanging to Slipknot, Ben can bring your musical dreams to life. With Ben, you will have someone to assist you in planning your music from the ceremony to the final dance. He will make sure to listen to you and understand your personal tastes to provide you with a truly bespoke performance that is representative of your love story.
