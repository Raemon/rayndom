From its hilltop location, Boulder Ridge by Wedgewood Weddings offers breathtaking panoramic views of the rolling hills and Almaden Valley in virtually all directions. Located at the prestigious Bay Club Boulder Ridge, this lavish wedding venue is a natural choice for happy couples in and around Santa Clara County who seek a stunning and convenient location for their special day.

**Unmatched Grandeur and Endless Possibilities Await**

With a spacious capacity of up to 200 guests, Boulder Ridge's functional grand hall design easily accommodates intimate celebrations and larger gatherings. The hilltop ceremony site, adorned with an intricate wrought-iron arbor, provides a picturesque backdrop for exchanging vows, with panoramic views of the valley creating a memorable setting. The upscale grand hall, complete with a cozy fireplace and expansive windows, sets the stage for a sophisticated and elegant reception. At the same time, the contemporary chandeliers and high ceilings add a touch of luxury to the indoor space.
