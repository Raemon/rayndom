Camellia Co. Events is a premier wedding planning company in Geneva, Illinois, serving couples across the Chicagoland area. Led by Kara, an experienced wedding and event planner, they'll help bring your beautiful vision to life as you get ready to say, "I do."

**Services Offered**

Before the big day, meet with Kara, and she'll get to know you and learn about your hopes for the celebration. If you're looking for an expert guide to bring everything together for you, Camellia Co. Events offers full-service planning packages, covering everything from design to vendor coordination. Alternatively, you can choose the Collaborative Planning package if you've started planning but need assistance with aspects like vendor selection. For couples that have a clear plan in mind and want help with execution, month-of coordination is ideal, as Kara will ensure every detail falls into place. With Camellia Co. Events, you'll be in the best hands on your wedding planning journey.
