The Marin Art & Garden Center is a stunning wedding venue in Ross, California. This independent, non-profit organization sits on 11 acres of beautiful, expertly maintained gardens. Our award-winning indoor and outdoor locations offer a peaceful garden setting to perfectly accommodate your event, whether it is large or small. We are fully equipped with tables and chairs, a kitchen with an ice machine, and more.

We offer the following spaces...

**Livermore Pavilion**

The Livermore Pavilion is spacious and airy, with a wall of sliding glass doors that open out onto the Livermore Deck and Stratford Gardens. Two gas-fired rustic stone fireplaces heighten the ambiance - one inside and the other on the deck. Just off the deck are the Stratford Gardens, which include an ample green lawn, flowering gardens, and an outdoor dining area. It can accommodate up to 180 guests seated indoors in case of rain and up to 300 in the outdoor dining area, but it still feels cozy with only 100. The outdoor area boasts a combination of twinkle lights and bistro lighting.

**Gazebo Lawn**

The Gazebo Lawn is our most popular wedding ceremony spot with a gorgeous white gazebo surrounded by Japanese maples, redwoods, and an expansive lawn. The Gazebo Lawn can hold up to 300 for a ceremony, or can be intimate with 100 guests.

**Fountain Pond**

The Fountain Pond is an intimate space for a wedding ceremony of up to 120 guests. If you desire greenery and seclusion, this is the right spot. It can also be used for cocktail hour for up to 200 guests.

**Facilities and Capacity**

With 11 acres of beautiful, expertly maintained gardens, Marin Art & Garden Center can accommodate up to 300 of your closest friends and family. Our venue offers an endless number of settings to choose from for your big day, including indoor, covered outdoor, and uncovered outdoor locations.

**Cuisine**

Although Marin Art & Garden Center does not have an on-site chef or culinary team, our venue provides a list of approved caterers with whom we have worked closely throughout the years. The caterers on our list are certified Green businesses that practice sustainability.

**Going Green**

Marin Art and Garden Center was founded in 1945 by the head of the Bay Area Conservation League. Since 1945, our mission has been to preserve the natural environment.

**What You'll Love About Marin Art & Garden Cente**

At this venue, you will enjoy stunning backdrops, perfect for capturing picturesque moments of your wedding. Their supportive venue manager will provide guidance and support throughout the planning process.
