Your bridesmaids invest [a lot of their time and effort](https://www.weddingwire.com/wedding-ideas/bridesmaid-duties-checklist) into making your big day a memorable one, so taking time to ensure that they're happy with their dress style is just a simple way to return the favor. If one or several of your 'maids are pregnant, it's even more important. When selecting a maternity bridesmaid dress, consider how far along in their pregnancy your BFF(s) will be on your wedding day. The most comfortable and elegant designs for [pregnant bridal party members](https://www.weddingwire.com/wedding-ideas/pregnant-bridesmaid-etiquette) have an empire waist bodice and an A-line skirt to best accommodate a growing baby bump. Other bestsellers include wrap dresses and frocks cut from stretchy fabrics that won't feel restrictive or require special undergarments. Need some real-life examples? Here are 23 incredibly stylish maternity dresses for your pregnant bridesmaids to shop so they'll feel amazing on your special day.
