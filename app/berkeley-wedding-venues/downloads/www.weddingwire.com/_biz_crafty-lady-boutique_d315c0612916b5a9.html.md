## About this vendor

![Alexia Haubert](https://cdn0.weddingwire.com/vendor/307705/original/960/jpg/uo_1507703_8641.jpeg?r=1746476777)

#####  Alexia Haubert 

Owner/Artisan 

__Woman-owned

__Just me

Crafty Lady Boutique is a wedding favors business based in Toledo, Ohio, specializing in creative wedding party proposal boxes and personalized gifts. Founded in 2018 by Alexia Haubert, the company arose from her passion for crafts and a personal need for customized favors. Meeting the demand for high-quality, affordable gifts, Alexia keeps a close eye on the latest trends, consistently striving to grow and develop her crafting repertoire. Every item is customizable and packaged with care, creating an extra special element to wedding party proposals.

**Services Offered**

Crafty Lady Boutique creates a wide variety of customizable gifts and ready-made proposal packages for to-be-weds planning their wedding. Available in multiple colors and themes, items range from personalized makeup bags and getting-ready robes to drink sleeves and embossed Champagne flutes. Additional services include nationwide shipping and gift boxes.
