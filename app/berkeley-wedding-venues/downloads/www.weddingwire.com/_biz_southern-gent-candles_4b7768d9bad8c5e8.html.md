## About this vendor

![Caitlin Campbell](https://cdn0.weddingwire.com/vendor/453012/original/960/jpg/uo_2210354_3ad207dd-da6e-4213-996d-acc72f2bcaf5.jpeg?r=1747424563)

#####  Caitlin Campbell 

Owner 

__Woman-owned

__5 years in business

__We speak English and Spanish

__Small team (2-10 members)

Based out of Rocheport, Missouri, Southern Gent Candles is a wedding favor business offering stunning hand-poured candles for your big day. The team operates from a 100-year-old brick building, creating one-of-a-kind gifts to elevate every special event. They cater to all kinds of occasions and budgets, from intimate soirees to large-scale gatherings.

**Services Offered**

Southern Gent Candles offers a range of comprehensive services for weddings and milestone celebrations. Their stunning candle collection features various scents, including mahogany, chai tea, agarwood, chocolate, and espresso. What's more, the company can provide a one-of-a-kind Candle Bar for your upcoming event. With this service, your guests browse and pick out their own candle containers and scents to take home with them. It's a great way to add a unique and personalized touch to your long-awaited festivities.
