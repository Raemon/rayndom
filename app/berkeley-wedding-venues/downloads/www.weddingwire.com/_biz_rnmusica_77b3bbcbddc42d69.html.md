## About this vendor

No owner details yet.

__41 years in business

__We speak English

__Small team (2-10 members)

DJ Rob has been rocking weddings, parties, and events for over 40 years, originally in South Florida, and now here in the Deland area for the last 12 years. He loves to collaborate with each couple to make their wedding everything they've dreamed of. He and his team have the ability to tailor the music to suit your taste and entertain your guests. An accomplished MC, he can keep the festivities flowing and high-energy or laid-back and low-key to suit your particular style.

DJ Todd is a seasoned radio DJ from upstate NY. He possesses a broad range of musical knowledge and a smooth vocal delivery. He'll rock your reception with a sophisticated vibe and keep it coming.

Our support team will light up your space and help your DJ keep the sound just right.

RnMusica is an entertainment company based in Deland, Florida, that provides wedding DJ services.
