Looking for something to wear to your granddaughter's wedding? These outfits are elegant and appropriate for the occasion.

As a grandmother, you've always had a special role in your granddaughter's life, and the same applies when it comes to her big day. As part of the bride's family and extended wedding party, you deserve to shine in a dazzling grandmother-of-the-bride dress. Before you shop, be sure to consider any special requests that the couple may have, such as following [the dress code](https://www.weddingwire.com/wedding-ideas/the-ultimate-guide-to-wedding-dress-codes), dressing for a specific theme (for example, a  _Great Gatsby_ wedding), or [wearing a specific color](https://www.weddingwire.com/wedding-ideas/colors-you-cant-wear-to-a-wedding). And most importantly, choose a gown that is just as comfortable as it is beautiful—feeling like the best version of yourself on their wedding day will help you celebrate the happy couple without distractions or worries about your wardrobe. If you're ready to choose your outfit, consider these gorgeous grandmother-of-the-bride dresses you can shop now.
