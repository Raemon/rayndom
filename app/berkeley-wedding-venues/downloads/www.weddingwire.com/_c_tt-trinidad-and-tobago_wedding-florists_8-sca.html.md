5.0 out of 5 rating 

5.0 

I cannot say enough wonderful things about Kaneesha and the incredible work she did for our wedding! From the very first phone call, she was attentive, creative, and genuinely excited to bring our vision to life. I enjoyed working with her so much, that I happened to convince her to step out of her comfort zone and create something which she hadn’t done before- floral malas for a Hindu ceremony. She was willing to learn, and she successfully made dreams come true. I had a one inspiration photo for my bouquet, which just so happened to be her previous work and some general ideas, but she took the vision and turned it into something even more unique and beautiful than I could have imagined. Each bouquet and boutonniere was stunning. The flowers were vibrant, natural yet elegant which matched our style and decor perfectly. Beyond their talent, Kaneesha was also a dream to work with- professional, kind, responsive, and clearly passionate about what they do. I felt completely at ease knowing they had everything under control. If you're looking for a florist who will exceed your expectations and help make your wedding day unforgettable, I highly recommend Kaneesha Primus. She is a true artist and a joy to work with!
