## About this vendor

![Joda Burgess](https://cdn0.weddingwire.com/vendor/497022/original/960/jpg/uo_2220794_8ae9b60d-cac9-4e21-be3f-9c66a9a1b89c.jpeg?r=1761727843)

#####  Joda Burgess 

Owner 

__1 year in business

__We speak English

__Small team (2-10 members)

Welcome to Legacy Estates. Eastern Kentucky's premier upscale barn venue, located in the heart of Kentucky's countryside on 250 acres. Designed with sophistication and style, our venue is a truly elevated wedding experience offering the perfect place to celebrate life's most meaningful moments. Our inclusive wedding packages and personalized planning ensure your wedding day will be memorable and catered specifically to you.

Our Legacy Garden is the perfect backdrop for you to say "I Do", featuring a paved aisle that leads to covered archways and sweeping landscaping.

Our Legacy Barn is equipped to hold 300+ guests. Inside, you will find natural lighting, a caterer's warming kitchen complete with ice machines and a built-in bar. Leading outside is a charming patio with cozy gas logs. The perfect spot to unwind.

Our Legacy Pavilion is an open-air, roofed structure perfect for a cocktail hour. Inside, you will find a built-in bar and cocktail tables for mingling.

Outside the space, you will find the Legacy Courtyard with landscaped gardens, walking paths, and benches for seating.

Our property also features two picturesque ponds, one of which includes a beautifully decorated rowboat - a charming setting for romantic couple photos.

Each of our packages is designed to be all-inclusive to make your day not only designed around you but also stress-free. Each package includes:

  * Wedding Planner/Day of Coordinator
  * Elegant Chiavari Chairs
  * Rectangle and Round Tables
  * Cocktail Tables
  * Table Linens
  * Personalized Decor



We would be honored to host your celebration and to bring your vision to life! From our romantic courtyard to the elegant indoor barn, all the little details are designed to create lasting memories for a lifetime. We can't wait to welcome you and to celebrate your special day with us!

Legacy Estates is a barn wedding venue located in Ashland, Kentucky.
