## About this vendor

![Sophie](https://cdn0.weddingwire.com/vendor/205812/original/960/jpg/uo_2218502_89ba6ae9-f4eb-407a-86c6-e2a3c0e60f99.jpeg?r=1758940003)

#####  Sophie 

Creative Director 

__Hispanic or Latinx-owned, Woman-owned

__3 years in business

__We speak English and Spanish

__Small team (2-10 members)

Mementro is a unique wedding favors and gifts company based in Los Angeles, California. The team prides itself on translating the moments that make up each couple's love story into tangible keepsakes that can be shared with their guests. Each item is custom-designed to reflect the beauty and significance of your milestone occasion. 

**Services Offered**

From matchbooks and playing cards to shot glasses and t-shirts, each piece is personalized, turning your story into something you and your guests can hold onto. Each Mementro product is delivered packed in a silver box perfect for displaying on your special day. Under the lid, you will find a printed legend that tells the story behind every detail. Operating on a wait list basis, couples are invited to get in touch, share the journey that has led to their engagement, and have their love come to life with stunning gifts and favors for their guests.
