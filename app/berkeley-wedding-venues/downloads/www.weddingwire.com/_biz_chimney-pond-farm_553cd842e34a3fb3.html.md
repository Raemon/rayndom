Chimney Pond Farm is a picturesque wedding venue located in Glenville, North Carolina. With a beautiful view, on-site lodging, and a newly renovated reception space, what more could you ask for in your mountain wedding venue?

How do we do it, and how do we make this happen:

  * **Package includes Venue Coordination Team.**
  * We can **customize** your package with the upgrades we have available.
  * You can choose all your own vendors, but we also have lists of pre-approved caterers and other vendors for you to use as well. This allows for flexibility and makes _**your unique vision come to life!**_
  * _**Package also includes:**_
    * Getting ready spaces for both sides of the wedding party.
    * Ceremony site with white padded chairs and decorations of your choice.
    * Reception space with tables and chairs and decorations of your choice.
    * Staffed golf carts to transport guests before and after the ceremony.
  * **Working with destination couples** is something we consider ourselves experts in! We offer: 
    * Initial consult/mini-planning meeting for all couples.
    * Our team is available via email and phone to answer questions throughout the planning process.
    * We enjoy connecting you with local and trusted vendors and ensuring you have the tools and resources you need to plan your dream wedding! Tell us what you are looking for, and we can refer you to someone who can offer that service.
    * We try to make it easier for you and _**include some basic things at no additional charge**_ so you do not have to bring them, make them, or rent them (easels, table number choices, variety of signs, baskets, and more.
    * Each couple has a final planning meeting (8-10 weeks before the wedding day) with a team member.
    * We have on-site lodging for up to 22 guests. This is great for wedding parties and families!
    * **Rain plans are available at no additional cost**.



Did we mention that we are pet-friendly? If you want your fur baby to be a part of your day, let us know, and we can make it happen!

Call or message us today to see if Chimney Pond Farm is the perfect fit for you!
