Fantasy Sound Event Services is a Livermore, California-based company that offers a variety of wedding DJ services to enhance your event.

**Services Offered**

  * Disc Jockey
  * MC services
  * Lighting and Decor
  * Photo Booth
  * Audio/Visual
  * Coordination



With over 35 years in the industry, Fantasy Sound Event Services works with you from start to finish. This ensures that your wedding is everything you've hoped for and more. They provide personal consultations to create a lighting design based on your vision, learn your music taste, and discuss other services offered. On the day, they can arrive with a high-quality sound system, microphones, and thoughtful add-ons like a disco ball, uplighting, projectors, and more. Playing anything from country, hip-hop, and rock to R&B, top 40, and oldies, these professionals will tailor their music to meet your individual preferences.
