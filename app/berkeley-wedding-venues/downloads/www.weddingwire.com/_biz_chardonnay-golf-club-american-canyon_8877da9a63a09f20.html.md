The Chardonnay Golf Club and wedding venue offers serene elegance and intimate charm in the idyllic setting of Napa Valley, California. Nestled amongst thousands of beautiful grapevines and meadows, this property is the perfect site for a true Wine Country wedding! Whether you envision exchanging vows where vineyards blossom or hosting your reception in a magnificent ballroom, your vision can become reality at The Chardonnay Golf Club. 

**Facilities and Capacity**

Accommodating up to 400 guests, the event spaces at The Chardonnay Golf Club can be transformed to fit any event theme or color scheme. Before your special day begins, you and your nearest and dearest will spend time in the private dressing suites, getting ready and making memories together. A short stroll outdoors will lead you to the picturesque ceremony site, where you and your beloved will exchange vows surrounded by rolling hills and vineyards. Your reception can take place outdoors in an event tent on the lawn or within the stunning ballroom. The venue’s events team will set up your chosen space with tables, chairs, and other décor items for your convenience. 

**Services Offered**

The professional banquet staff at The Chardonnay Golf Club hosts many unforgettable weddings, banquets, and meetings throughout the year. They will go above and beyond to bring an extraordinary touch to your celebration. From detailed event coordination to luxury catering, you will enjoy the five-star services that Chardonnay provides.
