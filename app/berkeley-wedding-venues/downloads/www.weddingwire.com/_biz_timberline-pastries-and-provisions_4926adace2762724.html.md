## About this vendor

![Jada S. Norris](https://cdn0.weddingwire.com/vendor/883712/original/960/jpg/uo_2217388_03704f2e-6299-4d09-b8e0-f713e042d1e2.jpeg?r=1757083344)

#####  Jada S. Norris 

Owner 

__Black-owned, Woman-owned

__1 year in business

__We speak English

__Just me

Timberline Pastries and Provisions is a wedding caterer based in Idaho Springs, Colorado. It is a mountain-themed coffee, smoothie, and pastry trailer, perfect on the morning of the big day or after a mouthwatering reception dinner. Owner Jada S. Norris has more than seven years of experience working as a barista and has also been the manager of a renowned local bakery. She brings a deep love and passion for hospitality while adding another layer of flavor to proceedings.

**Services Offered**

On the wedding day, Timberline Pastries and Provisions provides everything from fresh pastries to chilled smoothies, as they create an experience as refreshing as the air high up in the Rocky Mountains. Couples and their wedding party can get fueled up for the day, first thing in the morning, indulging in coffee and various breakfast treats. Additionally, they can sip an early-evening coffee by the bar as the party gets going and the dance floor starts to fill. Timberline Pastries and Provisions will add a wonderful touch to every event, day or night.
