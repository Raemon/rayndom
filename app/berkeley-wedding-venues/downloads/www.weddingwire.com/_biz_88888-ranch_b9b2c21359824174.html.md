__1 year in business

Escape to 88888 Ranch, a breathtaking private venue in Wills Point that blends rustic Texas charm with modern comfort. Our expansive indoor–outdoor spaces, on-site lodging, and unique entertainment amenities create the perfect setting for multi-day weddings and unforgettable celebrations. Whether you're planning an intimate gathering or a large, lively event, Five 8 Ranch offers everything you need to bring your vision to life.

**Amenities & Property Features**

  * **150 ft x 150 ft Event Courtyard** (converted pickleball courts) with flexible ceremony and reception layouts

  * **Main House Rental** for bridal prep, gatherings, photography sessions, and overnight stays

  * **On-Site Airbnb** ideal for out-of-town guests or a private wedding suite

  * **In-Laws Home** offering additional overnight lodging for family or VIPs

  * **Swimming Pool** for welcome parties, rehearsal events, or post-wedding brunches

  * **Stocked Pond** providing a scenic backdrop for ceremonies, portraits, and intimate gatherings

  * **Golf Simulator** for pre-event entertainment, groomsmen activities, or weekend guest fun

  * **Poker Table Lounge** for relaxed group hangouts and late-night gatherings

  * **Indoor Loft Seating** with additional guest space and elevated viewpoints

  * **Built-In Bar & Mini Kitchen** for beverage service and catering support

  * **Ample Outdoor Space** for lawn games, cocktail hour, or open-air receptions

  * **Preferred Vendor List** including catering, DJs, florals, rentals, photography, and more

Our Dream Wedding at Five 8 Ranch!

We celebrated our wedding in September 2025 at Five 8 Ranch, and it was truly perfect. The owners of the property were incredibly accommodating and worked so hard to make the ranch a beautiful place for our special day. Our wedding planner and the Five 8 Ranch team worked seamlessly together, which made the entire planning process stress-free.  
  
The ranch is absolutely stunning. The trees, ponds, wide-open landscape, and farm animals created the most picturesque backdrop. Our photos turned out beautifully, and the venue offered so many different options for unique and charming photo locations.  
  
Transportation throughout the venue was such a special part of the day. Guests enjoyed a tractor ride with hay bales, a fun and memorable way to travel across the ranch. Along the way, they were able to see the animals on the property, adding a charming and personal touch to the experience.  
  
The Airbnb on the property was another huge plus. It provided a comfortable, convenient space for part of the bridal party to stay in and get ready on the day of the wedding.  
  
One of the standout features of Five 8 Ranch is its versatility. The pickleball court was transformed into an incredible dance floor and reception area, creating a fun and unique setup that our guests absolutely loved.  
  
Overall, Five 8 Ranch is a gorgeous and welcoming venue for couples who want a relaxed outdoor setting with plenty of natural charm and flexibility to make the day uniquely theirs. We’re so grateful for the care, effort, and heart that went into making our wedding day truly unforgettable.
