## About

Simply Torn Art and Design is a stationery company located in Shrewsbury, Missouri, that serves the St. Louis area. Owner Christa offers beautiful wedding invitations and products sure to please couples and their receivers. All creations are designed with care and are custom made with the couple in mind.  
  
**Services Offered**  
Christa promises that Simply Torn Art and Design will help couples with all their stationery needs and concerns. She has many services and products that will help couples with their wedding day while also incorporating their personality. She makes custom invitations, menus, place cards, programs, RSVP cards, Save the Dates, and Thank You cards. Simply Torn Art and Design also offers the following invitation services:  


  * Addressing  

  * Assembly  

  * Consultations  

  * Design  

  * Embossed  

  * Foil-stamped  

  * Letterpress  

  * Printing  

  * Shipping  

  * Thermography
