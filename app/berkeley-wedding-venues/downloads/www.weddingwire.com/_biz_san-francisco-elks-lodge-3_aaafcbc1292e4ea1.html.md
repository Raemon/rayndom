## About

San Francisco Elks Lodge #3 is a stylish wedding venue in San Francisco, California, established in 1924 as a meeting place for members of the Elks Club. Luxe, lavish, and laced with history, this one-of-a-kind locale has you and your loved ones set for the celebration of a lifetime.

**Facilities and Capacity**

Couples who host their happily ever after at San Francisco Elks Lodge #3 have access to two tastefully furnished, Spanish gothic-style spaces. The Grand Ballroom, complete with painted ceiling panels and wrought-iron chandeliers, is ideal for sweet, intimate ceremonies and dazzling dinner parties. Boasting a stage for teary-eyed toasts and top-notch entertainment, as well as a hardwood dance floor for all your fabulous numbers, this banquet hall is built for nothing less than nights to remember. Beyond the foyer of the Grand Ballroom is a lush lounge area illuminated by French-style floor-to-ceiling windows and glamorous chandeliers with gold finishes. This pre-function area also features a built-in mahogany bar where guests can mix and mingle over bubbly and other beverages.

**Services Offered**

San Francisco Elks Lodge #3 comes with all the equipment you need for your nuptial event, from furniture to lighting and flatware. In-house catering is also on the cards for a taste of San Fran fine dining. If you'd like to know more about this stunning location, feel free to contact the team today.
