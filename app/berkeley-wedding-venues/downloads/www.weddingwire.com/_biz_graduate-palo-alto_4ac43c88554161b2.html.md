## About

Graduate Palo Alto is a historic hotel venue in Palo Alto, California. Brimming with vintage accents and old-school charm, this lovingly restored inn is the perfect place to start your married life. It offers a comfortable and tastefully decorated space to celebrate, with local attractions in the downtown area right at its doorstep.

**Facilities and Capacity**

Filled with natural light and antique furniture, Graduate Palo Alto is a truly stunning place to host your intimate wedding reception or pre-wedding events. It sits just minutes away from numerous shops and bars and the majestic campus of Stanford University. The hotel was originally built in 1929 before undergoing a complete renovation to maintain and highlight its Spanish-colonial roots. With antique paintings and hand-painted wallpaper at every turn, this stylish event space is filled with unique treasures and timeless gems. The in-house cafe makes a cozy and relaxing spot for coffee with loved ones, while the rooftop bar offers unmissable views. Featuring delicate string lighting and classic bar furniture, this space is made for mingling and socializing.

**Services Offered**

Graduate Palo Alto offers a variety of services to help you organize your dream wedding. The attentive staff can provide assistance with booking overnight accommodation and valet parking for you and your guests. Numerous rooms are available, all furnished with luxury bedding, modern amenities, and wireless internet. If you are environmentally conscious, the hotel can offer EV charging ports and complimentary bike rentals. The venue is also pet-friendly, allowing you to bring your cherished furry friend along for the festivities.
