## About this vendor

![Erica Banda](https://cdn0.weddingwire.com/vendor/565590/original/960/jpg/uo_2095565_edd7242b-6997-453e-8725-53ebe8af185b.jpeg?r=1762847916)

#####  Erica Banda 

Lead Planner 

__Hispanic or Latinx-owned, Woman-owned

__3 years in business

__We speak English and Spanish

__Small team (2-10 members)

CasaBanda Events is a wedding photo booth business based in San Antonio, Texas. Owner Erica aims to streamline your wedding planning with her playful photo services and affordable packages. A great alternative to traditional photography services, these booths allow guests to be themselves without fear of judgment.

**Services Offered**

CasaBanda Events offers customizable wedding packages to suit any celebration, whether you're planning an intimate or grand event. They include on-site printing, a selection of props, and support with social media sharing. During a consultation, Erica will work with you to ensure your booth matches your style and that you have everything you need to capture each memorable moment. She is happy to travel throughout San Antonio, Austin, and the surrounding areas.
