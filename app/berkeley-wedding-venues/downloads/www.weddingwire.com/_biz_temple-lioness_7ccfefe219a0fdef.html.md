**Welcome to Temple Lioness, a wedding officiant in Key West, FL,** where your love becomes a sacred flame and your ceremony a living threshold.

****

Whether you’re exchanging vows for the first time or rekindling your devotion,  
**Each offering is designed to honor your story, your love and it's legacy.**

****

Couples often come looking for a ceremony that feels **authentic, well-paced, and truly reflective of who they are.** The intention is to **create a grounded, heartfelt experience that honors your relationship** without anything feeling forced or overly formal.

Each ceremony is custom-designed to reflect your story, values, and connection. **Personalized vows, gentle rituals, and meaningful blessings are woven together to create a calm, connected atmosphere** —helping you feel present, supported, and confident as you step into marriage.

With over 16 years of experience in ceremony facilitation, embodiment practices, and relational wellness, **Lalita brings a steady, professional presence that helps couples feel relaxed and cared for throughout the process.** Offerings range from simple ceremonies to more immersive couples experiences, all fully customized to match your vision, timeline, and style.

**All packages include the option to have Lalita officiate or not.**
