## About

Catering to California’s San Francisco Bay Area, The Sartorial Sketcher is a live illustration wedding favor business that will help elevate your long-awaited celebration. The company offers a one-of-a-kind experience where you and your guests can enjoy a collection of commissioned watercolor works and haute couture illustrations. Owner and operator Victoria caters to various special occasions, from fashion shows to weddings and pre-wedding festivities.

**Services Offered**

The Sartorial Sketcher offers a range of unique wedding favors for your special day. Victoria can perform live event sketches of your pre-wedding party and of your guests. Offering custom-commissioned mixed media and watercolor illustrations, The Sartorial Sketcher also allows you to transform creative dreams into reality!
