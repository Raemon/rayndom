## About

Yoshi's SF openly accepts all wedding inquiries for ceremonies and receptions. Commonly known as a world-class music venue and Top 100 Restaurant, Yoshi's has been fortunate to host some of the most exclusive, unique weddings in the Bay Area. Aside from in-house catering provided by our prestigious chef Shotaro Kamio, Yoshi's will happily assist in renting linens, florals, lighting, a/v, and more!
