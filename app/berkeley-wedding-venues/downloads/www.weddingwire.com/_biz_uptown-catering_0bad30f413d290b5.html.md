## About this vendor

![Paul &amp; Brigette Barbas](https://cdn0.weddingwire.com/vendor/915171/original/960/jpg/uo_1171519_2e755d95-958e-43f2-aa52-12bd9c6d2c57.jpeg?r=1769037408)

#####  Paul & Brigette Barbas 

Owners/Operators 

__15 years in business

__We speak English

__Large team (21+ members)

Uptown Catering, a business operating out of Traverse City, Michigan, was born out of a love for entertaining others on special occasions such as weddings and private gatherings. Its skilled team has developed a reputation for top-quality service wherever they go, be it in the Grand Rapids area or further afield. Both catering and bar services are available, making sure each wedding day flows seamlessly, remembered forever by those lucky enough to attend.

**Services Offered**

With Uptown Catering, delivery and setup are included. Newlyweds can relax safely in the knowledge that every detail, however major or minor, is under control, allowing them to focus their energy on celebrating a magical day with their loved ones. The staff at Uptown Catering will work alongside their couples from the very beginning, crafting their food and drink services to match the needs of each event. Additionally, they maintain connections and work with several outside businesses that offer event spaces, cakes, planners, florals, and more to perfect the wedding ceremony and reception.
