Love & Letterpress is a wedding invitation design studio operating out of Delaware, Ohio. Their approach emphasizes reliability and creativity, ensuring couples receive stunning and unique invitations to mark the big day. 

**Services Offered**

Love & Letterpress provides a comprehensive range of wedding stationery services, including custom invitations. With in-house printing capabilities and access to specialized partner resources, the team can accommodate a wide variety of templates, styles, and custom requests. Competitively priced and with a wealth of industry experience, they are the premier option for bespoke wedding stationery. From classic to modern, Love & Letterpress delivers thoughtful, tailored results that make every detail feel personal and intentional. Reach out today for some examples of their past work and then start designing your bespoke wedding invitations.
