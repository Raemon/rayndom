## About

Our outdoor pool/terrace is perfect for a casual ceremony and reception or dinner. Completely remodeled with a fire pit, Bar-B-Q, hot tub and bar. Abundant teak tables and chairs with umbrellas.

This 177 room hotel in the heart of the Silicon Valley, provides easy access and complimentary Full Buffet Breakfast every morning. Reasonable rates for wedding groups and a complimentary room for each 20 rooms occupied. Parking and internet are complimentary.
