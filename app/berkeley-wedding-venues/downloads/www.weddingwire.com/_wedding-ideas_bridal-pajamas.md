Whether you're looking for bridal pajamas you can wear while getting ready on the big day, or you're building up your honeymoon wardrobe, it's important to choose [loungewear](https://www.weddingwire.com/wedding-ideas/bridal-loungewear) that makes you feel comfortable  _and_ confident. From [silky robes](https://www.weddingwire.com/wedding-ideas/bridal-robes) and patterned PJ sets to "fancy" sweatpants and sultry satin sleepwear, you've got plenty to choose from—and [lingerie](https://www.weddingwire.com/wedding-ideas/boudoir-outfits) isn't your only option. First things first: Before shopping, figure out if comfort is your main priority, or if you'd rather opt for something more stylish. Luckily, we've found options that give you both, and if you're in the market for matching [bridesmaid pajama sets](https://www.weddingwire.com/wedding-ideas/bridesmaid-pajama-sets), we've got you covered there too. Ready to shop? Sit back, relax, and pick out your comfy wedding day uniform below.
