Riverside Gables Bed & Breakfast is a chic Victorian-era B&B and wedding venue in Berea, Ohio. This chic and historic setting offers everything you need for an unforgettable wedding celebration.

**Facilities and Capacity**

This venue is in downtown Berea, conveniently located just a short drive from Cleveland Hopkins Airport for your out-of-town guests. You and your loved ones are welcome to stay on-site in the tasteful accommodations for your wedding weekend. Before the big day, explore all that the Cleveland area has to offer, from the Rock and Roll Hall of Fame to the natural beauty of Cleveland Metroparks. On your wedding day, you'll be able to welcome up to 50 guests indoors, or 115 outdoors, to Riverside Gables. For outdoor events, tenting options are available, so you can dine and dance the night away under the stars. A red barn is available as well, giving you more space for a refreshments setup. 

**Services Offered**

The experienced events team at Riverside Gables Bed & Breakfast loves to work with nearlyweds to help create their dream wedding day. In addition to the four comfortable guest rooms on-site, the venue is known for its delicious and wholesome breakfast options. For the duration of your stay, including your big day, you can start your morning with fresh and tasty breakfast dishes. Twenty-five parking spaces are available on-site, and this lovely B&B is ADA-compliant. Just reach out to the team, describe your vision, and they'll work to meet all of your wedding day needs.
