## About this vendor

![Dileesa Archer](https://cdn0.weddingwire.com/vendor/073422/original/960/jpg/uo_2224370_16487468-ef16-497f-b4ac-264108c5bbc6.jpeg?r=1766667620)

#####  Dileesa Archer 

Owner 

__Black-owned, Woman-owned

__17 years in business

__We speak English and Spanish

__Just me

American Singing Studio is a music service in Boynton Beach, Florida.  
Dileesa has 25 years of experience as a classical, gospel, R&B/Soul, Jazz, pop, and musical theater singer and teacher. With a Master's degree in Vocal performance, she is able to deliver elegant, powerhouse, smooth, and sultry vocals on demand to create unforgettable moments on your special day. Imagine your guests being seated while ‘Ave Maria’ is being sung, or having your first dance to the Pop song that describes you best as a couple.  
Dileesa holds a Master of Music in Vocal Performance and is currently a Doctoral candidate in Music Education. Singing is a lifelong passion, and that birthed a vocal coaching business, complete with products and services, as well as 25 + years of professional singing, ranging from operas to studio sessions to voice-overs.  
Dileesa is excited to bring a classy, elegant, and emotional musical experience to your special day.

With Dileesa, your vision not only comes to life, but it will transcend your expectations. Booking her will guarantee a beautiful memory etched in time.

Dileesa is available as a soloist or full band.
