Lafayette Park Hotel is a gorgeous wedding ceremony and reception venue located in Lafayette, California. Known as the "Crown Jewel" of San Francisco East Bay hotels, it is also home to award-winning restaurants and a luxurious spa.

**Facilities and Capacity**

Lafayette Park Hotel's maximum capacity is 220 guests. The hotel's plush accommodations and five different event spaces provide unlimited options for couples looking to have a classic, elegant, and luxurious wedding day. Lafayette Park Hotel offers both indoor and uncovered outdoor settings, including ballrooms and banquet halls. Lafayette Park Hotel wants to ensure that all its guests feel safe and comfortable at all times, which is why it provides several different transportation and access points. The wheelchair-accessible venue offers parking, shuttles, and valet services for you and your guests.

**Services Offered**

Lafayette Park Hotel is available for bridal showers, elopements, receptions, ceremonies, engagement parties, and rehearsal dinners. The venue offers what seems like an endless amount of accommodations to suit all of their client's needs. Some of those services include:

  * Bridal suite
  * Event planning
  * In-house bar
  * Set up
  * All-inclusive packages
  * Clean up
  * Rentals
  * In-house catering
  * Wireless internet



**Cuisine**

On top of being an all-around fantastic venue, Lafayette Park Hotel also provides its guests with extraordinary food that will leave their pallets satisfied and their stomachs full. The food and beverage items they provide include barware, flatware, linens, china, and glassware. Their catering services include offering different options and serving styles of the cuisine that the happy couple picks. Those services range from buffet, dessert, and stations to tastings, cocktail receptions, and hors d'oeuvres.
