**Best of Boston Weddings 2026 | Boston Magazine: "If “rustic” makes you think of mason jars, think again. The Barn at Gibbet Hill redefines the genre with its soaring post-and-beam architecture, polished wood interiors, and panoramic views of rolling pastureland. Set on a working farm in Groton, the venue blends country character with refined hospitality: think firefly evenings, farm-to-table menus, and sunsets that wash the hills in gold."**

The Barn at Gibbet Hill is a wedding venue nestled amidst picturesque rolling hills of conservation land in Groton, Massachusetts. This beautifully renovated and restored 100-year-old Barn exudes rustic elegance.

**Facilities and Capacity**

The Barn at Gibbet Hill offers unparalleled views and a range of options to make your ceremony and reception truly remarkable. It provides couples with an exclusive and breathtaking ceremony location at the top of Gibbet Hill. Imagine saying "I do" amidst the enchanting ivy-covered ruins of Bancroft's Castle. With a generous capacity of up to 224 guests, including space for a dance floor, the Barn ensures ample room for everyone to celebrate your special day. Enhancing the experience further, an outside deck with a permanent roof and heating facilities serves as an ideal setting for cocktail receptions. Guests can mingle and relax as they enjoy the surrounding beauty.

**Services Offered**

The Barn at Gibbet Hill goes above and beyond with its amenities. It is equipped with a full-service kitchen and bar. Their dedicated staff is committed to attending to every detail, ensuring your event runs seamlessly and effortlessly. Highlighting their commitment to quality, the Barn at Gibbet Hill takes pride in their farm-to-table cuisine, which showcases the freshest ingredients sourced from local farms and producers.
