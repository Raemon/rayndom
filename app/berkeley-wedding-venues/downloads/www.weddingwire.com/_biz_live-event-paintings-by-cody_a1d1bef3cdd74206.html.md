## About

Having a live event painter is the perfect way to capture the memories of your special day while adding an air of elegance to your wedding. Cody draws from decades of experience with realism oil painting to create a one-of-a-kind, custom wedding painting that reflects the emotion and atmosphere of your event. Guests are welcome to watch and engage with the artist throughout the day while she works.

Cody also offers quick on-site watercolor portraits of guests that can be used as a unique and classy favor for smaller events, and also studio commissions.  
  
This artist provides live painting sessions for events throughout the greater New York City metropolitan region and beyond: Westchester, Warwick, Cornwall, Poconos, Catskills, Bergen County, and Rockland County and willing to travel further. This is an all-inclusive and LGBTQ+ allied business that believes that all love is equal.
