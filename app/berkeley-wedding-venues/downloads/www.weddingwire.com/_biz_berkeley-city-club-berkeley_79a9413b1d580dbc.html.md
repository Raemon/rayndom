Berkeley City Club is a wedding and event venue, historic hotel, restaurant & bar, and private social club located just steps from the UC Berkeley campus. Guests will enjoy the unique architecture of this historic landmark building designed by reowned architect, Julia Morgan. We offer multiple indoor and outdoor event spaces for your celebration, as well as in-house catering provided by Julia's Restaurant.

  
**FACILITIES AND CAPACITY**

**Berkeley City Club offers multiple unique event spaces for intimate and grand events:**

  * **1st Floor Event Spaces** : 
    * **Drawing Room** \- _Ceremony up to 100 guests OR Reception up to 60 guests without a space for dancing OR 40 guests with space for dancing. This indoor space includes cathedral ceilings, a private powder room & private access to the West Courtyard. _
    * **West Courtyard** \- _attached to Drawing Room; available as cocktail hour location when Drawing Room has been reserved._
    * **East Courtyard** _-_Ceremony up to 40 seated guests or up to 50 standing guests OR an intimate breakfast or lunch with up to 24 guests. This lush outdoor space does not include overhead cover, heat, or overhead lighting.__
    * **Loggia Room** __\- intimate location that can accommodate up to 16 seated guests at one long table  
  
__
  * **2nd Floor Event Spaces:**
    * **Member's Lounge** \- _Ceremony up to 90 seated guests OR Cocktail Hour up to 120 guests (standing) OR Reception with up to 68 seated guests (no space for dancing) or 48 seated guests (with space to dance). This indoor space includes an oversized fireplace, baby grand piano, and antique lounge furniture that cannot be removed from the room._
  *     * **Venetian Ballroom** \- _Reception up to 200 with space for dancing or 240 without dancing_. _Our Ballroom boasts beaufitul vaulted ceilings, original chandeliers, and a built-in stage perfect for a band of DJ._
    * **Covered Terrace** \- _Ceremony up to 180 guests OR Cocktail Hour up to 200 (standing)._ _This covered outdoor space is attached to the Member's Lounge and Ballroom and cannot be rented separately._



**SERVICES OFFERED**

**This beautiful full-service venue has been serving the Bay Area since 1930. Services included are as follows:**

  * In-House Food & Beverages (_no outside catering allowed_)
  * Tables, Chairs, Ivory Linens, Plates, Utensils and Glassware
  * Wi-Fi Access
  * Group Block Reservations (38 guest rooms on-site, including 4 suites)

Wonderful wedding in historic setting

We just celebrated our son's wedding at the Berkeley City Club on September 30, 2023. It is a fantastic venue and we highly recommend it. The building is beautiful, and for weddings, you have the use of several rooms (virtually the entire second floor), including the ballroom, members lounge (library) and foyer. Most guests had not seen the venue and just raved about how fantastic it is. BCC did a really good job in setting up and implementing the wedding, also. When we first booked the venue, there was turnover in their lead staff, but that ended well before the wedding and we were well taken care of prior to the wedding with all the last minute planning, details, etc. that need to be worked out. BCC has a really good restaurant on site, which was helpful as the food was really good. The room looked gorgeous and service of both the food and the bar was fantastic. The grooms brought their own cake and this was nicely served as well. Other perks of getting married there include a free suite for the wedding couple the wedding night and half off the night before, reserved parking for the wedding couple and a photo shoot by the drop-dead gorgeous indoor pool (this is a Julia Morgan designed facility and it is outstanding architecturally). It is also very nice that the BCC is also a hotel. All of the grooms' families and most of the wedding party stayed there, which was not only convenient but fun as we kept running into people the day before, the day of and the day after the wedding. There is also a really good venue (Beta Lounge) two blocks away where there was a really fun after-party. And the guests (mostly from out-of-state) really enjoyed the Berkeley vibe, one block from the UC Berkeley campus and a nice student atmosphere. We have only good things to say about the venue. If you are looking for an historic and gorgeous venue in an urban area (also convenient for transportation purposes) with a great staff and service, we highly recommend the Berkeley City Club!
