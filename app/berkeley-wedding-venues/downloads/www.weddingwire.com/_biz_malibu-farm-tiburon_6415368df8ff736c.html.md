Located in the heart of Belvedere Tiburon, California, Malibu Farm Tiburon is an unforgettable waterfront setting for weddings and other special events. Couples can work closely with the expert staff to make their vision for their special day a reality. You and your guests can make memories that last for years to come in this incredible venue.

**Facilities and Capacity**

Malibu Farm Tiburon offers both indoor and outdoor spaces that feature picturesque coastal vistas. From the first and second-floor patios, you can enjoy the California sun and fresh sea breezes as you mingle with your family and friends. The indoor dining areas are more climate-controlled but still offer the same stunning views of the water. For more intimate occasions, you can host up to 12 people in the private dining. The restaurant is filled with contemporary furniture and decor inspired by the state's effortlessly cool style.

**Services Offered**

The expert chefs at Malibu Farm Tiburon can craft a mouthwatering menu for the occasion. With multiple food packages to choose from, you can customize their services to fit your needs. Popular dishes include kale caesar salad, filet mignon, pan-seared halibut, spicy chorizo pizza, lobster mac and cheese, deviled eggs, and more. They also offer stations, like grazing boards, a charcuterie table, or an oyster bar. During the celebration, friendly bartenders can serve drinks to keep your guests hydrated and help them toast to your new chapter. The team at Malibu Farm Tiburon takes great pride in the opportunity to be a part of your big day.
