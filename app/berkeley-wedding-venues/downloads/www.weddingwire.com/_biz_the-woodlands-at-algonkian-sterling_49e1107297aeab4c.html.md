Located on the banks of the Potomac River in Sterling, Virginia, is The Woodlands at Algonkian, a premiere event and wedding venue. Situated on 850 acres of rolling hills and glorious landscape, this site offers outstanding scenic views of the surrounding regional park. Couples who book this facility can avail of a naturally exquisite backdrop for their event, offering a plethora of opportunities for wedding photos.

**Facilities and Capacity**

The Woodlands can accommodate up to 200 guests for a formal banquet-style reception, and features both indoor and outdoor spaces. Couples who wish to make the most of the surrounding scenery can wed under a structured wooden pergola with view of the forest. The Loudoun ballroom offers a second stunning space for an indoor reception, with floor-to-ceiling windows that cover an entire wall of the two-story building. Lofty ceilings and a stone wall fireplace complete this elegant and refined space.

**Services Offered**

The Woodlands at Algonkian can cater to all event types. We host one event per day, clients will have use of the property for up to eight hours. The on-site venue coordinators ensure the event runs smoothly, and are there to assist with the details and support the couple with anything they may need. Items such as Chiavari reception chairs, folding ceremony chairs, tables, and audio equipment are available, along with necessities like glassware and flatware. Bar services are available, including house and premium liquors, house beers and wines, and signature drinks. This venue’s peak season occurs from April through to October. Please note that linens are not included in the venue rental.

**Great Blue Heron Catering**

We have a fantastic catering department, Great Blue Heron, which offers a full range of food options. Their menu is attached and everyone who plans their event at The Woodlands can attend one of their tastings. All purchase and service of alcoholic beverages at The Woodlands is handled by Great Blue Heron.

The Venue of our Dreams!

The Woodlands at Algonkian was the perfect wedding venue for us and we cannot recommend it highly enough. It was the ideal balance of aesthetics and practicality. It truly is a beautiful space, but welcoming rather than pretentious. The outdoor ceremony space is phenomenal (they just re-did their pergola) and the indoor reception space likewise looked fantastic (the high ceilings/tan stone was gorgeous even before we added the linens/flowers/etc. and my jaw dropped when we saw the final finished space, it was so lovely). The onsite bridal suite was spacious and well-designed (and while there isn’t an officially designated groomsmen room, there was a convenient area onsite where the gentlemen could finish getting ready). One thing to note is that with the Woodlands you are required to use their in-house catering service Great Blue Heron, but this was just fine with us, as their food/bar selections were of good quality (particularly the bar package) and having both the venue and catering under one roof made things so much easier to navigate. They also do allow pass-through services (we did late night Vietnamese banh mi and it was a hit!).   
  
However, the dealmaker for us for the Woodlands as wedding venue was the ability to rent the Riverfront Cottages (located on site as part of the larger Algonkian Regional Park, which also houses the Woodlands at Algonkian venue itself). We used the cottages to put up most of the wedding party (including both older parents and families with young children) and having all the major players physically on site the evening before/day of the wedding was a logistical godsend for us. The cabins themselves are nice/of good quality located right on the river (a 1-2 minute drive or pleasant moderate walk from the venue itself) and they absolutely met our needs. We even did our “rehearsal dinner” as a picnic at the cottages because we wanted something more relaxed and this worked out beautifully. Additionally, for the families with kids, if you rent from the cottages, you also get free access to the waterpark and minigolf areas that are part of the larger Algonkian Regional Park, which was great. And there are also tons of hotels only a short way away from the park/reception venue (most with shuttles to the nearby airport), so the Woodlands is also a great choice for accommodating out-of-town wedding guests as well.   
  
In summary, our wedding at the Woodlands at Algonkian was the wedding of our dreams and the high quality of the Woodlands as a venue was the foundation upon which everything was built. It was a beautiful venue space, the staff was great to work with, the catering met our needs perfectly, and the logistical convenience of being able to put up the wedding party on site at the Riverfront Cottages could not be beat. We wholeheartedly recommend the Woodlands at Algonkian to anyone for their wedding (or anything else)!
