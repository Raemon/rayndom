## About

Oakland Arena and Ring Central Coliseum is a versatile event venue in Oakland, California, that hosts weddings, parties, and other events. With an impressive variety of function spaces available, you will be spoiled for choice when you come here. Whether you dream of saying your vows on a theater balcony or raising a toast in a luxury lounge, it can all happen at this stunning venue.

**Facilities and Capacity**

Oakland Arena and Ring Central Coliseum can accommodate intimate weddings, large-scale gatherings, and everything in between. If you're keeping things simple with a micro wedding, you can celebrate in one of the Theater Boxes. Situated on a private balcony, these spaces can accommodate between four and eight guests. They are fitted with comfortable seating and include a full-service concierge. Elsewhere in the venue, you will find the Legends Lounge, a contemporary reception space that can hold 136 seated guests or 200 standing. This room is furnished with high and low tables and is equipped with a large bar at the center. For events on a grand scale, the Shibe Park Tavern can facilitate up to 1,000 guests. Spacious and contemporary, it offers clear views of the stadium playing field and boasts one of the longest sports bars in California. This space is ideal for entertaining guests, as it features a shuffleboard, a pool table, and several flatscreen TVs.

**Services Offered**

Whatever you have in mind for your wedding celebration, the staff at Oakland Arena and Ring Central Coliseum will be happy to help. Catering is available onsite at the Levy Restaurants, which provide a variety of delicious menu options. Pre-paid parking spaces are also open to those traveling by car. If you'd like more information on services, contact the venue today with your queries.
