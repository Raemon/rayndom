Based in Asheville, North Carolina, the Plum Print team creates one-of-a-kind wedding invitations to help you reminisce on this once-in-a-lifetime occasion forever. With their unique services, you'll get to craft a stunning book containing all your special keepsakes. With exceptional customer service, professionally and custom-made products, and various printing options, Plum Print could be exactly what you're looking for.

**Services Offered**

These skilled artists transform treasured wedding cards and invitations into long-lasting keepsakes. Each wedding book is completely custom and bound to last a lifetime. What's more, the entire process is truly seamless from start to finish. Once you receive a box from Plum Print, you'll fill it with the greeting cards received at your engagement, wedding shower, and on your wedding day. Then, the team will professionally photograph each card and design your one-of-a-kind book, which is available in hardcover, layflat, or linen.
