Operating out of Campbell, California, Telefèric Barcelona brings the flavors of Spain to weddings in the Golden State. Founded by the Padrosa family, the company is known for creating immersive, unforgettable dining experiences. Their passionate culinary team instills a sense of the Mediterranean spirit into every celebration they cook for, with food that lives long in memory. 

**Cuisine**

For couples who love Spanish cuisine and culture, Telefèric Barcelona is he premier choice. From authentic tapas to show-stopping live paella cooking, they have the big day features the aromas and flavors of Barcelona. Their menus highlight bold, traditional flavors paired with modern flair, from classic Iberian ingredients to beautifully presented small plates and desserts. They also cater to a wide range of dietary requirements, ensuring that no one is left out of the wedding feast. Reach out today to start designing a wedding menu like no other.
