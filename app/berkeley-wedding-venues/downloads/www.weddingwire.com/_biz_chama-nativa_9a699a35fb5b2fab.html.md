**Chama Nativa is a full-service wedding venue and Brazilian steakhouse in Gilroy, California, designed for couples who want an effortless, elegant celebration.** Our all-inclusive wedding packages take the stress out of planning — everything from the table settings and floral decor to the gourmet dinner and desserts is included.

Our chandelier-lit Grand Ballroom holds up to 100 guests and is fully dressed with tall floral centerpieces, customizable ambient lighting, and elegant place settings — all provided in-house. Couples can also host smaller gatherings in the Signature Salon or Intimate Dining Room, with the same attention to detail and full-service support.

Chama Nativa’s signature dining experience features over 15 flame-grilled meats served tableside (including filet mignon, picanha, and leg of lamb), a gourmet salad bar, and family-style desserts like crème brûlée and molten cake. Non-alcoholic drinks are unlimited, and bar packages are available to fit your preferences.

Ceremony packages are available for an additional fee and include aisle floral decor, guest seating, and a seamless room transition into your reception.

**Included in Every Package:**  
• Wedding reception space with full decor  
• Tables, chairs, lighting, and service staff  
• Unlimited rodízio dining experience + salad bar  
• Family-style desserts + non-alcoholic beverages  
• Optional bar and ceremony upgrades
