Fairview Metropolitan is a country club wedding venue located in Oakland, California. This modern golf club offers to-be-weds both indoor and outdoor settings where they can celebrate their exciting day. Situated in the East Bay, this venue’s position allows couples to have an intimate gathering with their loved ones near the energy of city life while maintaining a private atmosphere. The club also has beautiful views of the San Francisco skyline. As well as wedding ceremonies and receptions, this venue also hosts engagement parties and showers. With both the gorgeous skyline and rolling hills, couples will enjoy the best of both worlds. The Oakland International Airport is minutes away, as well as plenty of museums, restaurants, bars, and shops.

**Facilities and Capacity**

Couples who choose Fairview Metropolitan may celebrate with up to 250 wedding guests. The vibrant green golf courses and sprawling countryside create a beautiful backdrop for a lovely outdoor wedding ceremony. An enclosed pavilion allows for a charming reception with a built-in dance floor, carpeting for comfort, and floor-to-ceiling windows to observe the countryside and skyline. Couples may also choose to host events in the beautiful banquet hall if they prefer an indoor experience. This venue comes with parking and wheelchair access.

**Services Offered**

The event experts at Fairview Metropolitan are ready to help to-be-weds with any questions they might have about coordinating their big day. The club's wedding professionals are happy to offer a free wedding planning consultation to make sure couples have the day of their dreams. This venue also allows couples to build their own all-inclusive package that fits their unique needs. Pre-designed packages are also available for added ease. Some of the features of the Elite package include DJ and MC services, a champagne toast, centerpieces, guest book, hosted bar, and ceremony flowers. Couples may utilize the venue’s various event items, including a photo booth, audio equipment, and a chocolate fountain among others. Catering is also offered as a possible feature, for further convenience.
