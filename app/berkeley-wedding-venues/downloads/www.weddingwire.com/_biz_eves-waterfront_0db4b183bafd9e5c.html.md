Eve's Waterfront is a gorgeous restaurant wedding venue in Oakland, California. Boasting stunning waterfront views, with an expansive deck that allows your guests to breathe in the fresh ocean air, it is the ideal backdrop for laid-back celebrations in The Golden State. Steeped in character and tasteful nautical style, Eve's Waterfront provides exceptional services that bring your dream wedding to life.

**Facilities and Capacity**

The restaurant offers a range of fabulous banquet rooms designed to wow your guests with unmatched views and exceptional design. Couples are welcome to host their ceremony on the waterfront deck, which accommodates 60 seated guests. Exchange vows soundtracked by the gentle lapping of ocean waves and enjoy panoramic vistas that stretch as far as the eye can see. Indoors, various spaces accommodate your every need. The Waterfront dining room welcomes up to 120 seated guests, with elegant layout options for your reception. Fitted with large windows, high ceilings, and warm wood paneling, it is a bright and spacious venue. Multiple other banquet rooms are available, accommodating intimate guest lists of 10 to 60 people. Among others, the Ocean Bar offers a steady flow of beverages throughout your special occasion, while the Starboard Hall is the perfect reception area for cocktail hours and dining with 50 of your nearest and dearest. Eve's Waterfront is available for full buyouts, welcoming 400 seated to 800 standing guests.

**Services Offered**

The dedicated team at Eve's Waterfront will be delighted to work with you to bring your wedding day to life. They offer comprehensive planning services, ensuring everything is perfectly in place ahead of your celebration. A range of dining options is available, with delicious dishes perfectly cooked and elegantly presented to complement the occasion. Couples can opt for formal plated service or buffet dining, accompanied by hosted bar options that keep the drinks flowing all evening long. Reach out to Eve's Waterfront, and enjoy the venue's elegant interiors and breathtaking ocean views as you raise a toast to your love story.
