Willow Creek Farm is a charming countryside wedding venue nestled in Urbana, Illinois. The all-inclusive experience at this enchanting farm will set the stage for an unforgettable celebration of love you'll remember forever. With a rustic chic ambiance, stunning spaces, and unwavering customer service, you are sure to enjoy everything you need to throw a one-of-a-kind event. 

**Facilities and Capacity**

Both indoor and outdoor areas are available to host your wedding, ensuring a big day that's tailored to your needs. Imagine an intimate ceremony under the open sky, where you say 'I do' surrounded by gorgeous views and the natural beauty of the Illinois country. The 2,500-square-foot event space features a warm and romantic atmosphere for you to mingle, dine, and dance with up to 200 guests. Inside, a rustic-inspired style is prevalent, with wooden accents, elegant lighting fixtures, and a charming silo bar. Guests can enjoy a drink and raise a toast outside on the paved patio area during a cocktail hour. You'll also have plenty of opportunities to snap some photos with bucolic backdrops everywhere you turn. A well-appointed commercial kitchen is also available for your preferred caterer to take advantage of. 

**Services Offered**

Whatever your wedding vision, the team at Willow Creek Farm would be delighted to help you bring it to life with warm hospitality and a commitment to excellence. This venue is fully customizable, ensuring you can curate a big day that feels authentically you. With modern amenities and impeccable service, your celebration should run seamlessly from start to finish. Reach out today and learn more about their services and facilities!
