Uncommon Vows L.L.C. is a wedding officiant in Floresville, Texas. I am a licensed and insured wedding professional and will travel (travel fees apply). Your wedding ceremony is the foundation of your big day, and it is an incredible opportunity to set the tone for the rest of your celebration. Many couples feel that the real fun begins at the reception, leaving the ceremony to be a more serious and traditional affair. I believe you can have an unforgettable and enjoyable ceremony, too!

**Services Offered**

At Uncommon Vows L.L.C.,, I can help create a unique and memorable ceremony filled with laughter, joy, and fun moments that will be remembered by you and your guests for years to come.

From incorporating personal anecdotes and meaningful traditions to adding playful elements, there are countless ways to make your ceremony an enjoyable and lighthearted experience. So, let's break from tradition and make your wedding ceremony just as much fun as the reception. Imagine your guests laughing and cheering as you exchange your vows or your wedding party performing a choreographed dance down the aisle. With my help, we can create a uniquely YOU ceremony filled with fun and joy.

Contact me today to start planning your one-of-a-kind Uncommon Vows L.L.C. ceremony.
