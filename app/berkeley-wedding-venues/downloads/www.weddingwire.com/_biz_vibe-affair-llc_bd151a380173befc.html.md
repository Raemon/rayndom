Vibe Affair LLC is a professional wedding planning company based in Harrisburg, Pennsylvania. At the heart of our service are the values of love, the creation of cherished memories, unwavering kindness, and, most importantly, providing our clients with complete peace of mind. We believe in treating our clients as an extension of our own family. We are not just service providers; we are the empathetic listeners who absorb every detail, the compassionate consolers who understand the pressures, the enthusiastic cheerleaders who celebrate every milestone, and the diligent workers who bring every vision to life. Our clients depend on us as their steadfast support system.

**Services Offered**

Susan grew up in New York City, and now brings her talents to nearlyweds in Central Pennsylvania and beyond. She began her career in Human Resources, where she picked up critical problem-solving and people skills. If you want to work with Susan throughout your entire planning journey, you can avail yourself of full-service planning, covering all the little details leading up to the big day. For couples who just need assistance with execution, Vibe Affair LLC also offers day-of coordination, which begins 12 weeks before the celebration. With this knowledgeable planner, you'll be in the best hands as you say, "I do."
