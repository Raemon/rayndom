Martoca Beach Garden is a beachfront outdoor wedding venue in Puerto Vallarta, Mexico. It is designed for private and intimate beach weddings. Only minutes from Puerto Vallarta and Punta Mita.

**Facilities and Capacity**

A lush tropical garden surrounds the beach garden, making this venue ideal for unique, custom weddings with an authentic Mexican flair. Your wedding will feature incredible views of the bay from every angle, and clients can enjoy creating the event of their dreams using the beach garden as a beautiful canvas.

**Services Offered**

Martoca Wedding is a family-run and family-owned wedding business with a staff passionate about helping clients discover the beauty and create lasting memories at this beautiful beach garden. They maintain a flexible and relaxed approach to helping you plan the custom, one-of-a-kind ceremony of your dreams while also executing your wedding plans and decor according to your exact specifications. They love to create custom weddings with a Mexican flair and provide local cuisine and table settings in order to celebrate the culture of the surrounding areas. They utilize decor elements such as driftwood, bougainvillaea, palm leaves, Talavera, shell curtains, handcrafted centrepieces, blown glass, and amazing lighting to create a one-of-a-kind ceremony that illustrates the richness of Mexican culture.

Along with fantastic decor, they also offer the following event services:

  * Bar services
  * Cake
  * Catering services
  * Clean up
  * Event planner Included
  * Get ready rooms
  * Lighting/sound
  * Pet friendly
  * Preferred vendors only
  * Set up



**Accommodations**

This wedding venue is located in Bucerias, in a secluded nook between Punta Mita and Nuevo Vallarta. It is surrounded by a variety of all-inclusive resorts and villas, as well as condos directly adjacent to the venue. The venue is within a 30-minute drive from various tourist destinations, including Sayulita, Punta Mita, and Puerto Vallarta, and it offers transportation services for couples and wedding guests.
