## About this vendor

__Small team (2-10 members)

Situated in Oakland, California, Kissel Uptown Oakland is a modern venue for engagements, weddings, anniversaries, and private functions. This location can dazzle your guests with its breathtaking architecture, bold design, and friendly service staff. Between your walk down the aisle and your moment on the dance floor, you and your partner can enjoy juicy appetizers, a comfortable atmosphere, and luxury services.

**Facilities and Capacity**

You can start your wedding in Kissel Uptown Oakland's large rooftop area. With over 9,000 square feet of event space and a dreamy view, you or your partner can welcome everyone to your joyous celebration. From there, you can float down the aisle under the stars or walk in their indoor venue. You can have up to 160 of your closest family and friends by your side. All areas are wheelchair-friendly and are available for all ceremony types, including civil unions, elopements, religious ceremonies, second weddings, and more.

**Services Offered**

Kissel Uptown Oakland offers a variety of professional services for your event. This can include taking care of setting up your space before the celebration, leaving you with more time to spend getting ready with your nearest and dearest. They can also help with cleaning up after the festivities. The venue has a selection of event rentals available for you to use throughout your day, including tables, chairs, linens, china, and audio equipment. Their team of skilled staff provides fast wireless internet connectivity for you and your beloved friends to enjoy. They also have liability insurance and offer on-site accommodation to all your traveled guests.

**Cuisine**

The chefs at Kissel Uptown Oakland want you and your significant other to have an incredible culinary experience on your happiest day. Therefore, they provide delicious dishes and tasty treats from the hotel's private restaurant. This hotel also offers custom catering services and proficient bar services throughout your wedding day.
