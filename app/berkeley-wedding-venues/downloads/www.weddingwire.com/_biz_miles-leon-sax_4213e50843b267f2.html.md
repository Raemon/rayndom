__Just me

Miles Leon Sax is a Providence, Rhode Island–based wedding musician known for blending live saxophone performance with DJ and MC services. An internationally touring artist and cruise ship headliner, Miles brings a unique mix of jazz foundation, modern pop, R&B, and soul to weddings across New England. His high-energy performances and interactive style elevate every wedding into an unforgettable and immersive musical experience.

**Services Offered**

Wedding services include live saxophone music for ceremonies, cocktail hours, dinners, and receptions. From smooth background music for the cocktail hour to curated setlists for the ceremony, Miles creates the perfect vibe for every moment of the big day. For receptions, he combines live sax solos with expertly mixed DJ tracks while also providing MC services. Miles can perform solo or alongside a DJ, offering a dynamic, guest-focused experience that keeps the dance floor jumping all night long.
