## About

Forest Hill Park is a picturesque wedding venue located in Perry, Georgia. This venue offers beautiful scenery indoors and out. Forest Hill Park provides the flexibility to bring in vendors so your wedding can be just how you invisioned it. With positive reviews and numerous weddings served, Forest Hill Park is an excellent setting for any wedding.  
  
**Facilities and Capacity**  
Forest Hill Park can accommodate up to 100 guests. This location houses a white chapel, covered bridge, a steeple that is a perfect backdrop for a ceremony or pictures, naturally made holly tree canopy, and a red train car for a dressing area. The outdoor park includes extensive grounds and a patio. The chapel consists of a kitchenette and a dressing room.

**Packages**

-Venue only packages for the full day or for 2 days

-Small hourly venue only packages for small ceremonies/ elopements

-All inclusive elopement pacakges fro up to 10 guests

-All inclusive micro wedding packages fro up to 30 guests
