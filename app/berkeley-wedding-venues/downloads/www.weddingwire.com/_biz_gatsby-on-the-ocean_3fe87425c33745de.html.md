__7 years in business

One of Long Island’s most notable seaside wedding venues, Gatsby on the Ocean is found at Jones Beach’s well-known West Bath House, New York. Happily ever after begins at Gatsby on the Ocean and there are several fantastic wedding packages available to suit all tastes and budgets. This venue really is the perfect choice for couples who wish to celebrate their upcoming nuptials in elegance and style.

**Facilities and Capacity**

The venue can cater to events varying in size and comfortably accommodates parties of 30 to 260 guests. Couples can choose to exchange vows by the ocean, or in a tented ceremony and reception area on their lush and spacious lawn. Their landscaped gardens provide the perfect location for guests to take a pre-dinner stroll, or to enjoy a cocktail or two. The property boasts a large and elegant ballroom, complete with high beamed ceilings, a hardwood dance floor, and large windows streaming natural light throughout the room. A spacious changing suite is also provided, to allow the couple to prepare for the upcoming event in comfort. At Gatsby on the Ocean, the team here works hard to ensure newlyweds' wedding experience is one they can treasure for years to come.

**Services Offered**

The team at Gatsby on the Ocean strives to ensure that couples' wedding plans are coordinated with precise attention to detail. From popular packages such as the Gold, Platinum, and Great Gatsby, the expert coordinators here are happy to assist couples in selecting one that suits their needs. Excellent catering and bar services are provided on-site, with delectable hors d'oeuvres and cocktails available for guests to enjoy. The creative chefs offer several delicious wedding menu options, and dietary requirements are also accommodated. The team at Gatsby on the Ocean can also help with decor, with linens and customized menu cards also provided. The informed staff here are happy to advise on local accommodations, both hotels and holiday rentals.
