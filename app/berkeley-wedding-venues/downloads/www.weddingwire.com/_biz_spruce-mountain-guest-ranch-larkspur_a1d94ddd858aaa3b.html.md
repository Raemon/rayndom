## About this vendor

![Tara Ames](https://cdn0.weddingwire.com/vendor/576383/original/960/jpg/uo_383675_19619114-5c8c-4d17-856d-13bb06006421.jpeg?r=1726469642)

Spruce Mountain Ranch is a wedding venue on a historic ranch situated in Larkspur, Colorado. Sophisticated and rustic, the event spaces offer both convenient amenities and stylish interiors well suited to weddings and other events. It is located just 30 minutes from both Denver and Colorado Springs, making it easily accessible to guests. The staff at this family-owned ranch are enthusiastic about welcoming couples to their mountainous retreat.

**Facilities and Capacity**

Located on 450 acres, Spruce Mountain Ranch is divided into two event areas, each with outdoor and indoor options for events. This allows couples the ability to host their wedding in any season. Elements like stone and wood architectural details, fireplaces, lush landscaping, and more help to create a tranquil and refined vibe. On the Lower Ranch, ceremonies can begin in Grace’s Chapel, a garden site with a whimsical willow tree. From here, Spruce Mountain can be seen in the distance. Vow exchanges can also take place at Mountainside, which features a pond and vistas of the sprawling ranch. Both of these ceremony areas can accommodate up to 250 guests. Outdoor receptions can be set up on The Courtyard, which can also serve as a setting for “I dos.” It is in between The Colorado Room and Albert’s Lodge. Both of these indoor locations are suited for ceremonies and receptions. Their lodge-inspired layouts create an inviting ambience for festivities of between 80 and 250, depending on the chosen banquet hall.

The Upper Ranch similarly features indoor and outdoor options. Vows can be heard by loved ones in the wide-open Trey’s Vista, a spot that immerses guests in the landscape. Galas with 1,200 guests can be held in The Ponderosa Room. Extending from this space is The Spruce Room, which can be used alone or with The Ponderosa. Outside, The Courtyard works as an area for cocktails, or for ceremonies and receptions. Couples may also express their love and commitment at the waterfront Lakeside Terrace which additionally works for rehearsal brunches.

**Services Offered**

At Spruce Mountain Ranch, a property manager will be present to facilitate a couple’s use of the space. Many rental packages include access to both indoor and outdoor spaces. The staff provides setup and cleanup services in addition to dressing rooms for the couple’s convenience. Parking is also available. Tables and chairs for the ceremony and reception can be provided as well. The venue is outfitted with contemporary amenities that are integrated into spaces featuring hand-crafted touches, blending the best of both worlds.

**What You'll Love About Spruce Mountain Ranch**

Couples love the incredible all-round facilities this venue offers. They also praise the breathtaking natural beauty of the location and how it offers the staff go above and beyond. To see more, check out the gallery.
