## About this vendor

![Christine Wilson](https://cdn0.weddingwire.com/vendor/861947/original/960/jpg/uo_749168_f695e83d-6b2b-4fc0-a0e6-40806db383f9.jpeg?r=1765948429)

#####  Christine Wilson 

Venue Manager 

__11 years in business

__We speak English

__Just me

The Barn at Oak Manor is a scenic and romantic wedding venue located in Newnan, GA. Just 20 miles from the Atlanta airport - and minutes from Interstate 85 - this charming retreat offers both convenience and seclusion for local and out-of-town guests. The tranquil grounds feature rolling green lawns, serene wooded backdrops, and a stately colonial-style manor perfect for day-of preparations. In addition to weddings and receptions, the estate welcomes elopements, engagement parties, showers, and rehearsal dinners.

Your wedding day at The Barn at Oak Manor begins in The Manor, a charming two-story home where you and the wedding party to relax and get ready in two private, beautifully appointed suites. These lounges offer ample space, elegant furnishings, and full mirrors for a calm and comfortable start to the celebration. The manor also includes a full kitchen, multiple TVs, and a two-story wrap-around verandah with a cozy daybed. A private racquetball court adds a fun, unexpected way to ease any pre-wedding nerves.

Ceremonies take place beneath The Oak, a breathtaking, centuries-old tree that stands at the heart of the property. At its base sits a dedicated ceremony stage where you can exchange vows in the shade of its towering branches. From there, guests transition to the barn for a warm and memorable reception. The spacious barn features a tin ceiling draped with twinkling lights, three oversized doors that open to the grounds and fill the room with natural light, and space for up to 250–300 guests. The adjoining patio includes a bar and offers an inviting setting for dancing under the stars.

Our team understands that planning a wedding can feel overwhelming, and we are here to make the process seamless and enjoyable. We offer thoughtfully curated, all-inclusive packages featuring tables, chairs, décor, drapery, and bar setups to simplify your planning experience. Venue Manager Christine will partner with you throughout the journey to bring your vision to life. Couples are welcome to choose any vendors they love, and we are happy to provide a suggested vendor list for inspiration. The barn also includes a fully equipped prep kitchen, giving your caterer everything they need to ensure a smooth and flawless event.
