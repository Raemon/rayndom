Whether it’s for a shower or the big day, every guest wants to [score the perfect wedding gift in 2020](https://www.weddingwire.com/wedding-ideas/when-to-send-a-wedding-gift). Luckily, the days of registries filled exclusively with fine china are over, and finding unique wedding gifts is easier than ever before. While standbys like home goods, tools, and electronics are always a good idea, don’t forget to consider out-of-the-box ideas that truly represent the couple.
