## About this vendor

![Courtney Morgan](https://cdn0.weddingwire.com/vendor/646160/original/960/jpg/uo_61646_31adcc60-4b59-4336-9c48-d2404ee01664.jpeg?r=1738599043)

Director of Events 

__22 years in business

__We speak English and Italian

__Large team (21+ members)

Based in Miramar Beach, Florida, SunQuest Cruises offers the chance to enjoy the wonders of the ocean while you celebrate your wedding. The team here understands that your wedding is no ordinary day and deserves a venue that goes above and beyond expectations. It is their aim to help you host a wedding that leaves a lasting impression.

**Facilities and Capacity**

SunQuest Cruises offers the SOLARIS Yacht for weddings hosted off the Emerald Coast. It is 125 feet in length and lets couples and their loved ones celebrate on the waters of Destin, Florida. Ceremonies can take place on the Sky Deck or even at a beach. From there, the newlyweds and their guests can enjoy the boat's many offerings. There are three decks, and a total of 149 guests can be accommodated on board.

**Services Offered**

To help make your wedding on SunQuest Cruises' SOLARIS Yacht go smoothly, a wedding planner guides you throughout the process. They can assist you in customizing your day while the day-of coordination team works to bring your vision to life. There is also an executive chef who prepares the fine dining experience. Couples can choose from different packages depending on their needs, ideas, and the size of their wedding. Other services include access to a florist to craft bespoke floral arrangements, centerpieces, decor, and installations. This venue also provides white linens, glassware, china, cutlery, barware, and standard tables and chairs. During your occasion, the venue's service staff work the event, providing high standards of customer care.
