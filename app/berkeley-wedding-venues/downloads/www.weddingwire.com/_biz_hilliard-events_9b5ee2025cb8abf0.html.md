## About this vendor

No photo yet

No owner details yet.

__2 years in business

__We speak English

__Small team (2-10 members)

At Hilliard Events, we strive to make your wedding dreams come true. Both our intimate SteamBoiler Room, perfect for microweddings, and our gorgeous Building 4 Ballroom provide a beautiful backdrop to your special day. Lovely in their own right, your wedding will be stunning whether you opt for simple floral decor or go big with uplighting, draping, greenery, and statement pieces. Likewise, we want your first meal as a married couple to be exactly how you envision it. If you want a streamlined process and enjoy classic American and Italian fare, choose from our 2 catering partners. If you are hoping to replicate the food you enjoyed on your first date, are looking forward to including your special cultural cuisine, or just want to share your favorite food truck with your loved ones, you can do that at Hilliard Events. We also know that your pups make the cutest ring-bearers, so we are pet-friendly. If there is something you are dreaming about for your wedding, we will work tirelessly to find a way to make it come true. Hilliard Events encourages you to explore the possibilities and make your wedding truly unique.

Hilliard Events is a wedding venue located in Manchester, Connecticut.
