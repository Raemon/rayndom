# 18 Pocket Squares to Upgrade Your Wedding Suit or Tux

Whether you go for trendy, classic or bold, any of these pocket squares will level up your wedding look.

Accessorizing with a wedding pocket square on your special day is tradition if you're going for a classic ensemble, but it also allows you to tie your look together and express your personal style—plus, it just looks cool. By tucking this piece of fabric into the breast pocket of [your suit or tuxedo](https://www.weddingwire.com/wedding-ideas/what-s-the-difference-between-a-tuxedo-and-a-suit), you can incorporate a pop of color, an eye-catching pattern, or a luxe fabric (like silk). From point folds to puff folds and square folds, we guarantee that a pocket square elevates any look.

Whether you’re tying the knot at your local courthouse or hosting the ultimate [black-tie event](https://www.weddingwire.com/wedding-ideas/black-tie-wedding-attire), there’s a pocket square on the market bound to fit your vision. Ready to take your wedding attire to the next level? Keep reading to see our favorite wedding pocket squares for formal occasions that you can shop right now.
