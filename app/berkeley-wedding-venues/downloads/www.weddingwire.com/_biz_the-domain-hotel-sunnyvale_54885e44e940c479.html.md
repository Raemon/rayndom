## About

  
Events to remember happen every week at The Domain Hotel. Beyond the Grand Lobby; Weddings, Receptions, Anniversaries, and other Life Cycle Celebrations have been creating memories for decades.

Families gathering together, to strengthen old bonds, and make new ones, create memories that last a lifetime.

Our dedicated team will partner with you, ensuring that you are comfortable and secure with the details of your special event.

Please contact us to schedule a tour and see where you might be creating your generation's next lifetime memories!

  * Unique Lobby Setting and Staircase for Pictures
  * 9,065 sq ft indoor/outdoor event space for up to 400 guests
  * Wedding packages can be customized to delight your palate and personalize your event
  * Group rates for guest accommodations 
  * Complimentary High-Speed Wi-Fi
  * Poolside Cabanas
  * Bytes Restaurant & Bar
  * Room Service
  * Located in the heart of Silicon Valley
