## About

Special Events by Los Cuates Restaurant is an upscale event destination in Gilroy, California. Made for making memories, they welcome celebrations of all kind, including birthdays, weddings, and private parties.

**Facilities and Capacity**

Their outdoor patio is a lovely ceremonial site and an equally fantastic backdrop for those all-important photographs. After saying "I do," you and your loved ones can gather inside Special Events by Los Cuates Restaurant for a magical reception. Spacious as it is, this venue can accommodate up to 300 people. With a bar on site and sufficient space for a dance floor, you can liven up the occasion as you'd like. Smaller weddings of anywhere from 50-100 guests will also find themselves at home since you can configure the space as you see fit.

**Services Offered**

You can choose from among their all-inclusive packages to suit your needs and budget the best. The staff at Special Events by Los Cuates Restaurant is a dedicated bunch who will go above and beyond to bring to life the wedding of your dreams. Feel free to drop in to tour the grounds during the weekdays or make an appointment if the weekends are more convenient.

**Catering**

With aguas frescas and non-alcoholic cider options, the bartenders at Special Events by Los Cuates Restaurant will be on-hand to top up your glasses. The culinary team and the serving staff will also work hand-in-hand to ensure a seamless service. As for the menu, you can choose up to three entrees and two sides alongside a dessert. From fajitas to pasta to steamed vegetables to churro bites with chocolate, the food is sure to be a crowd-pleaser.
