## About this vendor

![Chef Jared Rogers](https://cdn0.weddingwire.com/vendor/482222/original/960/jpg/uo_2222284_ad1ac13a-dcfc-4617-9efa-838124922b03.jpeg?r=1767915788)

#####  Chef Jared Rogers 

Co-Founder & Executive Chef 

__Hispanic or Latinx-owned, Woman-owned

__2 years in business

__We speak English

__Small team (2-10 members)

Our mobile charcuterie experience brings a sophisticated yet relaxed culinary delight directly to your celebration. We offer a customizable, interactive charcuterie experience, featuring a chef-curated selection of premium cured meats, artisanal cheeses, fresh fruits, gourmet crackers, and delectable accompaniments served out of our adorable mobile eatery. It's the perfect way to create an unforgettable event that speaks to your unique style and love for delicious, high-quality food. Let us add a touch of edible artistry to your special day, creating a memorable moment that your guests will be raving about long after the last bite.

Our own journey from high school sweethearts to the shared adventure of creating Rocky Mountain Charcuterie has instilled in us a deep appreciation for lasting love. This understanding fuels our dedication to making your wedding day the most amazing day possible. We pour that same care and intention into every board we create, every interaction we have, and every detail we manage. Let us be a part of your beautiful beginning. Allow Rocky Mountain Charcuterie to add a touch of gourmet delight and heartfelt service to your wedding day, creating memories that are as rich and satisfying as our carefully crafted charcuterie. - Jared & Margaret
