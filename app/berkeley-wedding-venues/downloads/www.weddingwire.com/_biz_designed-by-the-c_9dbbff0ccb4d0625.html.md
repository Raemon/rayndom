## About

Designed by the C is a fine art design studio in Long Beach, New York specializing in creating one-of-a-kind, handcrafted stationery for weddings and other special occasions. Carolyn, the owner and creative director, will collaborate with you to bring your unique vision to life for the most special day of your life. Carolyn is a designer and illustrator with a love for all creative things. She has a strong passion for design and has had a paintbrush in her hand since birth. She would love to add her creative magic to your wedding invitations.

**Services Offered**

Wedding services with Designed by the C include custom invitations, place cards, save-the-dates, imprinted napkins, programs, thank-yous, menus, and RSVP cards. Carolyn is happy to design, print, foil-stamp, letterpress, assemble, and address your special stationery. You can anticipate wedding pieces that truly represent your personal love story.
