The Ebell of Los Angeles is a historical wedding venue located in Los Angeles, California. The property is situated in the heart of the city and was built in 1927 as an architectural masterpiece. The Clubhouse is also home to Los Angeles' longest-standing women's club. You and your guests will enjoy the enchanting details of this beautiful space. Make it your own, as this venue is versatile enough to transform into your desired aesthetic or stand alone in its own beauty. The hotel's staff is happy to help you through the entire planning process, ensuring that your day runs as smoothly as possible. On the day of your event, you and your loved ones can enjoy a delicious unforgettable meal created by their dedicated culinary team.

**Facilities and Capacity**

The Ebell of Los Angeles offers both indoor and uncovered outdoor event spaces. The clubhouse includes two ballrooms, an art salon, a courtyard garden, and an elegant 1,200-seat, Broadway-style theatre. Before your event, you and your wedding party can utilize the get-ready rooms to relax and prepare for the big day. Complimentary parking and WiFi are available to you and your guests. The Ebell of Los Angeles is wheelchair accessible.

**Services Offered**

The Ebell of Los Angeles invites you to host your ceremony, reception, rehearsal dinner, and engagement party at their facility. All-inclusive packages are available. The staff is happy to assist you with setting up and cleaning up the wedding to make sure that everything is exactly as you imagined. There are several event items and rentals for your use, including:

  * Tables and chairs
  * Glassware and barware
  * Table linens
  * Vintage furniture
  * A dance floor, lighting, and more



**Cuisine**

At The Ebell of Los Angeles, an outstanding culinary experience is just as important as the ambience and architecture. Chef Jason Sperber and his staff serve up innovative versions of classic favorites with a uniquely California twist. In addition, their bar menu includes premium cocktails that can be perfectly paired with your menu.
