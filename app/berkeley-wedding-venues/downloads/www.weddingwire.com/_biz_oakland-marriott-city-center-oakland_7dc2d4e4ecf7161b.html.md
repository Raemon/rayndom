## About

Stay in style at Oakland Marriott City Center, your preferred hotel and wedding venue in downtown Oakland, CA.

Home to a vibrant arts scene and world-class restaurants, Oakland offers an endless array of things to do. Book your wedding in Oakland in one of our event venues, featuring more than 100,000 square feet of meeting space. Maintain your health routine in our hotel's well-equipped fitness center or indulge in cuisine at Iron & Oak, our signature restaurant and bar. Recharge in our hotel rooms and suites that reflect Oakland's chic downtown vibe, featuring eclectic art, soundproof windows, plush bedding, ample workstations, and spa-like bathrooms with rainfall showerheads. Upgrade to an M Club room or suite and enjoy exclusive perks such as complimentary breakfasts and desserts.

To-be-weds are invited to host an unforgettable ceremony or reception in our Skyline Room, Grand Ballroom, and more. Our Skyline Room has incredible views of San Francisco Bay and the surrounding city. Couples who want to celebrate outdoors can host a charming cocktail reception on the Terrace, which overlooks the city's historic downtown area. The comfortable on-site accommodation offers couples and their guests somewhere cozy they can rest after a busy day of celebration.

Catering would be taken care of by the in-house culinary team. Our chefs are happy to tailor a menu to your specific tastes and offer options to suit all taste buds.

Visit us at 1001 Broadway, Oakland CA 94607. Contact us now with your details for a personalized quote!
