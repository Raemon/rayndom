The Oliver Building, nestled in Kansas City's vibrant and historic West Bottoms district, offers a one-of-a-kind venue experience for your most memorable occasion. This beautifully restored 1880s industrial gem spans over 30,000 square feet across three unique floors, blending timeless charm with modern amenities. Whether you’re hosting an intimate gathering or a grand celebration, the Oliver Building provides an unparalleled setting filled with character and style.

As a family-owned and operated venue, we’re committed to making your event as personal and seamless as possible. From the moment you arrive, you'll experience exceptional service and attention to detail. Our space includes two private suites with luxurious amenities for getting ready and a third suite dedicated to family use, ensuring comfort and convenience throughout your event.

When you choose The Oliver Building, you enjoy:

  * Exclusive whole weekend rentals for complete flexibility
  * Ample secure parking and gift storage for peace of mind
  * Open vendor policies, allowing you to bring your vision to life
