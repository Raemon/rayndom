## About this vendor

![Adam Copeland](https://cdn0.weddingwire.com/vendor/899422/original/960/jpg/uo_2224998_8adca6a1-36ab-49bf-aa81-4104fa3ae12d.jpeg?r=1767944667)

#####  Adam Copeland 

Co-Owner 

__2 years in business

__We speak English

__Small team (2-10 members)

Faith Haven Cabins and Event Center is a romantic retreat and wedding venue in Malta, Ohio. Nestled on 50 acres of serene woodland and rolling hills, this Southeastern Ohio gem offers a beautiful, intimate setting for wedding celebrations.

**Facilities and Capacity**

At Faith Haven Cabins and Event Center, you and your guests can stay on-site and enjoy a rejuvenating getaway in the heart of nature. Book a wedding weekend, and relax in the luxurious cabins equipped with modern amenities. The surrounding area offers plenty to explore, including charming downtown McConnelsville and Burr Oak State Park. On your big day, say, "I do," under a canopy of trees, or in an open meadow with lush greenery as your backdrop. Then, you can throw an unforgettable reception in the event center, a charming space that offers both a warm and elegant feel. Altogether, you're able to welcome up to 200 loved ones to the property for your one-of-a-kind wedding day. 

**Services Offered**

The two families that own and operate Faith Haven Cabins and Event Center will work with you from the beginning of your wedding planning journey to ensure your day is seamless. In addition to accommodations, your wedding package can include rentals like tables, chairs, and decor, as well as clean-up services. The venue also has an open vendor policy, so you're welcome to bring in your preferred vendors to cater on the day. Whether it's an intimate elopement or a big weekend bash, the talented team at Faith Haven Cabins and Event Center is ready to help bring your wedding dreams to life.
