__We speak English

__Small team (2-10 members)

Ice Cream Pedaler is a woman-run, Arizona-based ice cream cart specializing in handcrafted ice cream sandwiches and refreshing sorbet push pops. Served from our vintage-inspired tricycle and push cart, we provide a unique ice cream catering experience for weddings, private parties, and special events across the valley and beyond. Our nostalgic setup and elevated frozen treats create memorable, interactive dessert moments your guests will love.
