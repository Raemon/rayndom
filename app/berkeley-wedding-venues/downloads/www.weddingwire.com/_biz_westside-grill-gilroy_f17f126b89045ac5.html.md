## About

The Westside Grill is a family owned, neighborhood restaurant with a menu of favorites, an ever-changning list of daily specials, a full bar, and friendly staff. It's just righ whether you're looking for a delicious dinner or accomodations for a rehersal dinner or wedding reception. Our banquet faciliites are available to host parties, receptions, and business meetings. 

  * **Additional Details:** Banquet/Private Rooms, Bar Dining, Bar/Lounge, Beer, Dancing, Entertainment, Full Bar, Happy Hour, Late Night, Non-Smoking Restaurant, Patio/Outdoor Dining, Personal wines welcome (corkage fee applies), Private Room, Smoking Area (patio only), Takeout, Weekend Brunch, Wheelchair Access, Wine, Wine Bar
  * **Catering:** Catering available for corporate and private events.
  * **Private Party Facilities:** Great for any special occasion, Wedding’s, Birthday's, Anniversary's or any community event.
