Located in the heart of Texas Hill Country in Kyle, Texas, Texas Old Town is ideal for local destination weddings and special events with a rustic elegant flair. Just minutes from Austin and a short drive from San Antonio, this company offers four different wedding venues, offering beautiful Southern charm and affordable packages. From their turn-key planning services to the picturesque ceremony grounds, Texas Old Town promises to make your event a day to remember. Their event staff has years of experience with weddings of all kinds. Couples looking for a rustic retreat are welcome, as well as those planning an intimate and elegant elopement with just a few close loved ones. Visit Texas Old Town to see how they can make your wedding dream a reality.

**Facilities and Capacity**

Texas Old Town is nestled on 55 acres of stunning Texas pastures and rolling country hills. Each of their four reception venues offers a private ceremony and cocktail hour space overlooking the meadows and surrounding scenery. Outdoor areas feature rustic patio bars, wooden gazebos for your ceremony, fire pits, and much more. Each venue also provides get-ready rooms for you and your wedding party to use throughout the day. Tejas, the largest indoor space, resembles a vintage country dance hall, with rustic lumber floors and charming handcrafted benches and dining tables. Up to 350 guests can enjoy dinner and dancing in this space, or step outside to the wrap-around porch to get some fresh air and enjoy cocktails under the stars. Redbud is an elegant rustic hall with handcrafted cedar pillars, a red oak dance floor, exposed beams, and romantic drape lighting. The Sage Hall is a larger hall featuring a dance floor and stage for your evening entertainment, boasting high ceilings, red oak floors, and a beautiful staircase. From the covered porch and portico, guests can gaze at the signature water feature, bridge, and seasonal blooms that surround this incredible property. The newest venue at Texas Old Town is the Stone Hall, which can accommodate 320 guests. It features limestone walls, iron doors, crystal chandeliers, and a beautiful staircase to make your entrance to the reception. The Stone Hall offers a private entrance and parking lots for your guests’ convenience.
