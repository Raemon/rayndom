Located in the scenic area of Blue Bell, PA, Normandy Farm Hotel is a historic wedding venue dating back to the early 1700s. Every aspect of the property been meticulously restored to blend the historic with the modern.  
  
With convenient “**one-stop-shop** ” wedding planning, Normandy Farm Hotel features two rustic ballrooms - each with their own ceremony garden, cocktail hour space, outside terrace, ballroom, hospitality suite, bar and staff:  
_\- The Grand Ballroom accommodates up to 350 guests_  
 _\- The Silos Ballroom accommodates up to 160 guests_  
  
Normandy Farm Hotel is a wedding venue that ensures easy planning, where you can have all the elements of a wedding weekend experience on one property!  
  
**WEDDING CATERING & OPEN BAR**  
Catering services are provided in-house by the talented culinary staff led by executive chef Mtele Abubakar. Our wedding packages include cocktail hour, dinner, dessert station, and wedding cake (made in house by our pastry team), and a Premium Open Bar.  
  
**FARMER'S DAUGHTER RESTAURANT & BAR**  
This Farm-to-Table restaurant uses only fresh and local ingredients. Couples can host their Rehearsal Dinner and Post Wedding Brunch on site. The Farmer’s Daughter Bar is another great space where our guests can gather to continue celebrating at a Wedding After Party. The Farmer’s Daughter is also available to host intimate restaurant-style weddings up to 70 guests, as well as showers and social events.  
  
**HOTEL**  
Adding to the wedding weekend experience, bridal party and guests can stay at the on-site hotel which has 153 guest rooms and suites. Overnight stays feature hotel breakfast, luxurious furnishings, and the latest modern amenities. Our spacious hotel suites make for the perfect getting ready space on wedding day!  
  
**WILLOW and THISTLE FLORAL & DÉCOR**  
Nestled inside the White Wall at Normandy Farm, couples can make planning even easier by taking advantage of our convenient full-service floral and event design company.  
  
**HITCHED PRODUCTIONS and TYLER BOYE PHOTOGRAPHY**  
Our on-site photography studio & creative team helps make your day perfect by offering Photography, Video, Photobooth and DJ Services.  
  
**FARM TO PAPER CO.**  
Your all-in-one destination for custom event stationery and signage. From elegant invitations to full wedding suites and day-of signage, our team crafts personalized designs made just for you.  
  
_Also check out…_  
**The BELLE BALLROOM at The Blue Bell Country Club**  
We offer additional ballroom space at our sister property, located less than one mile from Normandy Farm. The Belle Ballroom at The Blue Bell Country Club features a classic timeless ballroom that holds up to 240 guests. Included with our wedding package is a complimentary shuttle to take your guests from the Normandy Farm Hotel to the Belle, so your guests can experience all the same amenities that Normandy Farm has to offer.  
  
**_Please contact us today for a more information and to set up a tour. We would be honored to host your special day!_**
