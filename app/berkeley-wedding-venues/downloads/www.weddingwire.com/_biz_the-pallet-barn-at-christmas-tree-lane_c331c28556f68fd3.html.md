Nestled in the majestic Hudson Valley countryside lies The Pallet Barn at Christmas Tree Lane in Wallkill, New York, a stunning and welcoming wedding venue. Couples can celebrate their love in a captivating rustic setting, surrounded by spectacular spruces, fascinating firs, and their cherished invitees. The pristine property is also easily accessible to traveling guests, only a short drive from New York Stewart International Airport and both I-87 and I-84. Set on a working Christmas tree farm, The Pallet Barn offers an authentic and unique wedding day experience, helping dream visions come to life.

**Facilities and Capacity**

Nearlyweds can avail of the farm’s customizable outdoor and indoor event spaces, providing versatility in addition to breathtaking natural beauty. The Pallet Barn at Christmas Tree Lane has a capacity of 100 attendees, offering a cozy atmosphere to tie the knot. The barn’s interior features all the necessary amenities to ensure an unforgettable celebration, including a kitchen prep area, bar, buffet area, and a dedicated DJ space. A wood-burning fire pit is also available, allowing you and your loved ones to enjoy the magnificent Empire State night sky with comfort and warmth.

**Services Offered**

A once-in-a-lifetime moment deserves the very best customer service, and The Pallet Barn at Christmas Tree Lane’s on-site staff goes above and beyond to help make each event memorable. Moreover, the team can provide lighting and sound equipment, rustic décor items, tables, chairs, and heating and air-conditioning are available should the weather intervene. When couples choose The Pallet Barn at Christmas Tree Lane, they’re getting a unique, cozy, and genuine slice of Hudson Valley beauty for their big day.
