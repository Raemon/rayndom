Minister 4 All is a wedding officiant company based in Maryland. This minister would be delighted to be a part of your milestone occasion and help you and your loved ones make meaningful memories. Deciding to become ordained as a way to give back to others, they can go all out to ensure a heartfelt celebration.

**Services Offered**

Minister 4 All works with couples from all walks of life and believes that everyone deserves to be respected and heard, no matter their background. They offer services for all of life's most important moments, including weddings, home blessings, child blessings, naming ceremonies, and more. Whatever your vision or celebration type, they've got you covered with top-class customer care and options to suit everyone. Contact Minister 4 All for more information and chat about your dream ceremony today.
