![Hotel MOHI](https://cdn0.weddingwire.com/vendor/859612/original/960/jpg/uo_2216958_33da5b2b-350d-486f-8f61-eef4e84ec10a.jpeg?r=1757461552)

#####  Hotel MOHI 

Sales & Events 

__No business details yet

__1 year in business

__We speak English

__Small team (2-10 members)

Hotel MOHI by Appellation is a stylish and modern hotel and wedding venue in Morgan Hill, California. Over the last century, Morgan Hill has become a culinary and cultural hub with a walkable downtown that combines cosmopolitan offerings with the warmth and charm of a small town. The hotel provides a unique space for couples to come together with friends and family and honor the beauty of their love story. 

**Facilities and Capacity**

Welcoming up to 600 guests, MOHI by Appellation provides a spacious and flexible backdrop for your ceremony, cocktail hour, and reception. Choose from four gorgeous spaces, and allow yourself to become immersed in the modern elegance of this fabulous hotel. Tables, chairs, and linens are available from the venue, and you and your beloved are welcome to set up the space however you wish. Overnight accommodations are available within the hotel's fresh, modern guest rooms, offering the perfect opportunity for a multi-day celebration. What's more, Hotel MOHI is conveniently located within 15 minutes of more than a dozen wineries, bustling bars, and vibrant nightlife, making wine strolls and beer crawls the go-to activities for you and your cherished guests. 

**Services Offered**

Hotel MOHI by Appellation offers world-class catering services designed to complement your special occasion. Each dish is crafted with a farm-to-table approach, honoring the abundance of the land. Newlyweds can expect their wedding meal to be complemented by delicious, locally crafted wines. The friendly events team will be delighted to assist with planning and coordinating your dream celebration, ensuring everything is perfectly in place as you enjoy festivities on the gorgeous backdrop of Morgan Hill.
