#####  Oksana Fomichenko 

Owner & Lead Designer 

__3 years in business

__We speak English and Russian

__Small team (2-10 members)

Based in North Port, Florida, Sacred Floral Design provides wedding floral services for the Sarasota, Bradenton, and Tampa Bay areas. This creative team specializes in creating custom floral arrangements, each bloom thoughtfully picked to reflect your style.

**Services Offered**

From bouquets to centerpieces and full-scale event decor, the Sacred Floral Design team brings heart and artistry to every wedding they work on. Their designs will add a touch of beauty and elegance to your big day, making it an unforgettable experience. During a consultation, they will work to understand your vision and plan how to bring it to life. On the big day, they will provide delivery and setup to ensure every element is perfectly arranged. Whether you envision a classic look or something more whimsical, Sacred Floral Design will make your dreams a reality.
