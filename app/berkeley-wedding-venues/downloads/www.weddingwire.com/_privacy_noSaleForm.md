# Your Privacy Choices

When you interact with us or our services, we may disclose personal information to our third-party partners as described in our [Privacy Policy](https://www.theknot.com/privacy-policy), including for advertising-related purposes. Some of this activity may be considered “selling,” “sharing,” or processing of personal information for targeted advertising purposes under applicable law. Residents of California, Colorado, Connecticut, Delaware, Iowa, Minnesota, Montana, Nebraska, New Hampshire, New Jersey, Oregon, Rhode Island, Texas, Utah and Virginia or states with similar comprehensive privacy laws can opt out of the “sale” or “sharing” of their personal information and processing of their personal information for targeted advertising purposes as set forth below. 

  * [Click here](https://www.theknot.com/dashboard#:~:text=Your%20Privacy%20Choices) to opt out of our use of cookies and similar tracking technologies for purposes that constitute sales, sharing, and/or processing for targeted advertising. 
  * To opt out of the disclosure of your name and contact information for sales, sharing, and/or processing for targeted advertising, please fill out the form below. 



Please note that opting out **does not** prevent us from delivering contextual advertising, conducting cross-device tracking for us to serve you first-party ads directly, or using information for non-targeted advertising purposes, such as attribution and analytics. We may also still disclose personal information with our partners to help us perform functions such as measuring ad effectiveness, controlling how many times you see an ad, determining ad performance, providing and securing ad, providing aggregate statistics and analytics, and/or reducing ad fraud. In such instances, these partners will act as our service providers. The above choices will not opt you out of the use of previously collected and disclosed information or all interest-based advertising (such as that conducted on other sites). 

# Submit a request

  * Your account and all the information and content saved in that account will be removed. Please note that certain of your information won’t be deleted in accordance with applicable law or to satisfy applicable legal/regulatory obligations and exercise/defense legal rights.
  * Your registry manager and registry account (if you have one) will be removed. Retail registries will remain active on retailer sites.
  * Please be informed that if you have an account in TheKnot.com and in TheBump.com, they are connected. Therefore, if you choose to delete one, your content and all information saved in both accounts will be removed.
  * Your comment forum account and posts won't be removed.
