## About this vendor

![Crispina ffrench](https://cdn0.weddingwire.com/vendor/050612/original/960/jpg/uo_2216050_09d42c18-eb70-4ee1-97fb-f82a2bcf203c.jpeg?r=1758141310)

#####  Crispina ffrench 

Sister/Designer/Partner 

__Woman-owned

__54 years in business

__We speak English

__Small team (2-10 members)

The Dolphin Studio is a creative small business based in Stockbridge, Massachusetts, that hand-silkscreen prints wedding invitations (among other things). Founded in 1970 by local art teachers Primm and John Ffrench, this third-generation family-run company continues to craft beautiful custom designs for wedding couples. Choose an image from our 50+ year archive to launch your custom-designed wedding packet. Your packet can include Engagement Party Invitations, Save-The-Date announcements, Invitations, RSVP cards, Rehearsal Invitations, Placecards, Commemorative Wedding Prints, Napkins, Tablecloths, and event signage. Our unique process and aesthetic promise to enhance a creative couple's special celebrations with professional, handmade elements.
