Located in beautiful Mountain View, California, Hyatt Centric Mountain View is a hotel wedding venue. This hotel is designed with modern amenities and a contemporary style, offering each guest a luxury experience. This venue offers breathtaking views over the Santa Cruz Mountains and is an ideal location for those traveling for the big day. With a plethora of dining, shopping, and entertainment options nearby, couples and their guests can truly make the most of their stay here.

**Facilities and Capacity**

Spread over almost 10,000 square feet of space, Hyatt Centric Mountain View boasts several indoor and outdoor spaces that can be customized to the couple's needs and tastes. Say "I do" in style before an evening of celebration with loved ones. Take in the views from the second-story terrace or cozy up by the firepit after a decadent meal and reception in one of the many indoor event areas. The hotel has 167 rooms for overnight guests, some with patio and balcony space for a truly opulent experience. This full-service venue also has a gym and swimming pool for guests to enjoy throughout their stay.

**Services Offered**

In-house event planners are available to take care of the details of the day. Couples can contact the venue to begin their wedding planning journey and see how the day can be tailored to them.

**Cuisine**

Fairchilds Public House, the hotel's on-site eatery, will provide bar and catering services. The menu is inspired by California favorites, with dishes crafted using locally sourced ingredients and produce. The restaurant can accommodate up to 48 individuals in its intimate lounge setting. Cabana-style beverages and food are also available with a view on the second floor.
