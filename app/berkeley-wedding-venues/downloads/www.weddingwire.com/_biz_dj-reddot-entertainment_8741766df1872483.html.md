DJ RedDot Entertainment is run by a talented wedding DJ in Atlanta, Georgia. Carson Patel, also known as DJ RedDot, is a seasoned professional when it comes to performing for weddings and special events. He has years of experience playing for milestone celebrations, corporate parties, and nightclub events. With Carson's extensive list of songs and top-of-the-range equipment, you can relax knowing your wedding will be a spectacular and memorable occasion.

**Services Offered**

DJ RedDot will show up early, be prepared, and keep your wedding event flowing smoothly from the first kiss to the last dance. What's more, Carson offers a list of comprehensive services, including custom playlists, professional speakers, exquisite lighting, and several microphones. You also have the option of choosing DJ RedDot Entertainment for exceptional MC services.
