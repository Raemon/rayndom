## About this vendor

![Kimberly DeFranco](https://cdn0.weddingwire.com/vendor/627812/original/960/jpg/uo_2218726_227f7c9e-9ca2-4a25-b5db-c1f07e056179.jpeg?r=1762301999)

#####  Kimberly DeFranco 

Owner 

__Woman-owned

__15 years in business

__We speak English

__Small team (2-10 members)

Amelia Daisy Designs is a business in Amityville, New York, offering a style of timeless floral artistry that illuminates each wedding venue. Samantha Martinez and the team at Amelia specialize in creating exquisite installations that transform the wedding scene into a breathtaking work of art, from floral arches to grand staircase designs, and everything in between.

**Services Offered**

The staff at Amelia Daisy Designs combines artistic vision with expert craftsmanship to guarantee each couple celebrates the wedding of their dreams. They make sure each detail matters, no matter how significant, from every petal to every placement, elevating the overall aesthetic and leaving a lasting impression on guests. Listed among the many signature offerings from Amelia Daisy Designs are floral arches and backdrops, hanging installations and chandeliers, table florals, centerpieces, and so much more. Nearlyweds can arrange a private consultation with Samantha to discuss their blossom needs while telling her all about their love story and wedding day journey.
