Historic Venue 1902 is a historic wedding venue in Sanford, Florida, that believes in traditional Southern hospitality. Located in the lovely town of Sandford in a quaint and serene park, this venue is one of Central Florida's oldest surviving schoolhouses and historical landmarks. Historic Venue 1902 is a turn-of-the-century grammar school that has been beautifully restored to feature the perfect balance between old-world charm and city sophistication. Privately owned and operated, Historic Venue 1902's passionate team will treat you and your guests to an unforgettable experience.

**Facilities and Capacity**

Historic Venue 1902 offers three main indoor and outdoor spaces and can accommodate up to 150 wedding guests. The Outdoor Ceremony Area comprises a large garden with an indoor rain plan available. The Reception Ballroom offers a 3,000-square-foot upstairs auditorium with chandeliers and a stage outfitted with A/V equipment. The Social Hall is an ample downstairs space with a custom wood bar and gorgeous fixtures. The Historic Classroom is an intimate downstairs space for micro weddings and personal get-togethers. Picturesque chandeliers, abundant natural light, beautiful hardwood floors, exposed brick, and a neutral palette await you inside this unique piece of Florida history.

**Services Offered**

Historic Venue 1902 is a full-service wedding venue that offers event coordination and planning for weddings and other social events. Staff will be provided to ensure seamless and perfect event execution. Additionally, the team offers top-notch bartending services for milestone events. You have the option of having a 'Bubbly Bar Set-Up' in the wedding suite for pre-ceremony toasts and gatherings.

**Location**

Historic Venue 1902 is located just down the street from the bustling city center of Sanford, Florida. This venue is near the region's newest high-end eateries, craft breweries, eclectic boutiques, scenic walking trails, and burgeoning theater scene.
