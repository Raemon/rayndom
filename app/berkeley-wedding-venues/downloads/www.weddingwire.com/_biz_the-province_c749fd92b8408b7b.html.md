The Province restaurant is a relaxed fine dining restaurant and wedding venue in San Jose, California, that transforms into a fairytale backdrop for the most spectacular events.

We have exciting news to announce! Prime Catering with over 30 years in executive catering with some of the finest hotels and restaurants around the country has brought their magic to the Province Restaurant! Gone are the days of static buffet lines. Prime‚Äôs interactive food stations allow guests to personalize their meals and engage with the culinary process. From DIY taco bars and sushi rolling stations to build-your-own salad bars and pasta stations, interactive setups make the dining experience more memorable and fun. The Prime Catering team is dedicated and committed to providing our guests with dining services that they will fondly recall.

**Facilities and Capacity**

The Province Restaurant can accommodate up to 350 guests for a complete buyout for your reception. We have multiple event spaces, including a spectacular patio with fireplaces, heaters, and string lights.

We are proud to practice sustainability with our program, as we believe in helping to improve recycling efforts in hospitality.

The Province Restaurant celebrates Diversity! Our owner is a Veteran of the US Navy, and our VP of Sales is a Native American and female!

  * Large Private Dining Room- hosts up to 80 guests
  * Small Dining Room- hosts up to 60 guests
  * Bar Area- hosts up to 150 guests for cocktail hour
  * Patio- hosts up to 150 guests
  * Entire Restaurant- hosts up to 350 guests

We are committed to delivering a new level of elevated customer service to each event. Not only do we have an elegant venue, but we will assign your very own concierge to assist you in the planning of your event! The Province and its exceptional team of professionals handle the execution and details of your event so you can focus on getting married! The Province Restaurant has trusting relationships with many local vendors, providing all Entertainment, Audiovisual, Decorations, Rentals, and Photography requests.
