Fairview Crystal Springs is a modern wedding venue in Burlingame, California, just 20 minutes away from San Francisco. This country club is a wonderful choice for couples who would prefer a celebration in the quiet countryside while still being close to the city. The establishment offers all-inclusive packages to make planning as smooth as possible and to create special occasions that are unique to the needs of their clients.

**Facilities and Capacity  
****  
**Fairview Crystal Springs allows you to host up to 250 guests for your big day. This country club sits amid scenic views of sprawling golf courses and the magnificent countryside, including the picturesque Lower Crystal Springs Reservoir. If you choose to have your ceremony outside, this setting can be the backdrop as you and your partner exchange vows. Inside, the renovated banquet room suits both large and intimate events. Bright white walls, pale brick columns, and beautiful chandeliers give the space a refined, modern charm. Here, you can host your reception and cocktail hour, allowing you and your guests to admire the sun shining over the surrounding hills. To facilitate everyone celebrating, the venue offers parking as well as wheelchair access. This country club is also happy to accommodate pre-nuptial festivities such as engagement parties and showers.

**Services Offered  
****  
**The wedding professionals at Fairview Crystal Springs are trained, experienced and well-equipped to help you with your planning needs. Along with customizable, all-inclusive packages, this venue offers complimentary consultations so that you can meet with experts and begin transforming your vision into a reality. The country club can provide various event items, including a photo booth, audio equipment, and a dance floor for lively receptions. To streamline the planning process, the venue has created a list of preferred vendors including a bakery, florist, and invitation creator. The staff will be there to clean up and set up your event on the day to ensure that your celebration is as seamless as possible.
