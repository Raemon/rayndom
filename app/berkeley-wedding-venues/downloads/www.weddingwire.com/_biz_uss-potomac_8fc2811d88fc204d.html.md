## About this vendor

![Jennifer Pettley](https://cdn0.weddingwire.com/vendor/110238/original/960/jpg/uo_1832011_2fd41336-a0bc-4c30-a413-e63e696932b8.jpeg?r=1767757829)

#####  Jennifer Pettley 

Executive Director 

__31 years in business

__We speak English

__Medium team (11-20 members)

Experience your wedding on the San Francisco Bay, aboard President Franklin D, Roosevelt's "Floating White House," the fully restored and historic _USS Potomac_. Start your history together on this amazing vessel, which played host to European kings and queens, including Britain's King George VI and Queen Elizabeth, as well as senior government and diplomtic officials, and luminaries.

The ship is moored at Oakland's Jack London Square. Potomac offers three-hour cruises on the San Francisco Bay, as well as dockside weddings. 120 guest capacity.

Private charter includes use of the yacht for 5.5 hours, including set-up and take-down time, a five-person Coast Guard licensed crew, and three history docents. List of preferred caterers available.

USS Potomac is a boat wedding venue sailing out of Oakland, California. Pick-ups and drop-offs are also available in San Francisco and Sausalito.
