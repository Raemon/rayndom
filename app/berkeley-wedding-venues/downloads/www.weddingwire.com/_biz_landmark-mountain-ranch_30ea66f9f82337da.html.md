Landmark Mountain Ranch, found just outside of Kamiah, Idaho, is a beautiful barn venue perfect for weddings and other special events. Nestled in the heart of the Gem State, this venue is surrounded by rolling fields and enchanting woodland that's home to some wildlife. With exceptional services and a passionate team, every celebration will be truly unforgettable.

**Facilities and Capacity**

Couples can start their day off in the upstairs of the Barn, where a bedroom apartment awaits for the morning preparations, while the converted silo is ready to use as a lounge. Then, they can tie the knot in their choice of outdoor ceremony sites, each with a breathtaking backdrop to set the scene. Then, for the reception, everyone can head into the spacious Barn that blends rustic charm and modern elegance. The venue is complete with a large main floor, a draped ceiling with glistening wagon wheel chandeliers, and a full-service bar with taps ready for use. Newlyweds and their guests will have a vibrant evening of dining, mingling, and dancing the night away with loved ones.

**Services Offered**

Their talented team of planners, stylists, and support staff goes above and beyond to ensure a seamless experience from start to finish. They will work alongside soon-to-be-weds to ensure their wedding truly reflects their wonderful love story. From the art, decor, and flowers to the furniture and the smallest of details, every detail is personalized to match unique styles and tastes. Different packages are available with options such as rentals, flowers, parking, and full weekend private access. Reach out to Landmark Mountain Ranch today to get started.
