## About

Vina Enoteca is an Italian restaurant and historic wedding venue in Palo Alto, California. The restaurant was established in the historic Stanford Barn building, which dates back to 1888. The estate was used as a winery for many years before passing ownership. It is now one of Palo Alto’s most coveted Italian restaurants and intimate wedding venues for all styles and budgets. Vina Enoteca brings the charm of the old Italian country to the heart of Silicon Valley. You’ll enjoy warm, inspired Italian dishes and exceptional service throughout the evening. No other restaurant wedding venue compares to what Vina Enoteca can provide.

**Facilities and Capacity**

Vina Enoteca provides private indoor event spaces for weddings of up to 200 seating guests and up to 300 standing. The private event rooms feature original brickwork and rustic wooden beams to set the ambiance for your reception. These spacious dining rooms provide plenty of space or a small dance floor and seating for your guests. All private event spaces can be custom-decorated to suit your wedding theme.

**Services Offered**

Vina Enoteca helps couples plan and prepare their dream wedding experience with as little hassle as possible. You’ll be able to work with the professional event planning, culinary, and wait staff to determine a seamless flow of events and a custom menu that suits your needs. Vina Enoteca coordinates with your other vendors to prepare accordingly and ensure that your decor, entertainment, and other elements of the celebration are well-executed within the venue space. Vina Enoteca also provides catering at other venues.

**Cuisine**

Vina Enoteca is known for its fine wines and delicious Italian cuisine throughout Palo Alto. They will help you design a custom menu of wines, cocktails, and a multi-course meal or buffet. Catering can be provided onsite or at another venue if requested. Their most popular dishes include the campanelle, osso bucco, and authentic Italian pizzas. They will help you pair wines with each course for a fulfilling dining experience.
