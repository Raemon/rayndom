Hidden Paths Travel, LLC is a wedding travel agency based in Shelbyville, Kentucky. This boutique, concierge-level travel planning company specializes in wedding and celebration travel. They are dedicated to discovering unique travel experiences and ensuring peace of mind for couples and their guests.

**Services Offered**  
Hidden Paths Travel, LLC is built on the belief that wedding experiences should feel effortless, personal, and seamless. They handle destination wedding travel planning and coordination, cruise wedding experiences, and bachelorette and bachelor celebration planning. Their travel experts plan every detail of your wedding travel experience. This includes resort and cruise contracts to room block coordination and payment tracking. They also assist individual guests with any of their travel questions. Their comprehensive planning services protect your time, budget, and peace of mind.
