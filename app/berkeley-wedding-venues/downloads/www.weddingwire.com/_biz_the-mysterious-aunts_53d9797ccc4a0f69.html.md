Located in Cincinnati, Ohio, The Mysterious Aunts offer a range of bespoke wedding favors that are sure to suit any couple or party. What started as two roommates in their kitchen creating candles has blossomed into a beautiful artisan business that delights in creating a plethora of items that are sure to suit any big occasion. You can find everything from soaps and scents to candles, and so much more.
