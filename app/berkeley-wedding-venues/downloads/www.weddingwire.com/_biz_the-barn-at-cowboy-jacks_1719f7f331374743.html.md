## About this vendor

![Melissa Michael](https://cdn0.weddingwire.com/vendor/614122/original/960/jpg/uo_2221416_bcf4bd58-6cc4-4559-a042-1b112e81b3d6.jpeg?r=1768833655)

The Barn at Cowboy Jack's is a beautiful wedding and events venue nestled just outside of Saint Paul, Minnesota. Since 2008, the founders of the company have expanded their roots to eleven different locations throughout the upper Midwest. Each establishment stays true to its commitment to quality and building a loving community. With top-notch hospitality, a delicious menu, and exceptional services unlike any other, they can transform each couple's special day into a once-in-a-lifetime celebration.

**Facilities and Capacity**

The Barn at Cowboy Jack's is a beloved destination for locals and visitors alike. When newlyweds step inside, they will be met with a charming rustic chic atmosphere filled with modern comforts and amenities such as fully equipped air conditioning. With a versatile space for up to 250 guests, soon-to-be-weds can host a small, intimate get-together or a grand-scale, lively event that is sure to wow any crowd. The barn truly is a blank canvas for couples to craft a celebration that truly reflects their styles and tastes, whether they envision casual country charm or romantic sophistication.

**Services Offered**

Their dedicated team will be there every step of the way to ensure everything goes off without a hitch. They will help craft a tasty menu filled with a diverse selection to suit every taste. Pre-/post wedding celebrations, such as showers, rehearsal dinners, and day-after farewells, are also welcomed for a seamless and unforgettable experience from start to finish. Couples can reach out to the staff at The Barn at Cowboy Jack's today to find out more about the packages and services they offer.
