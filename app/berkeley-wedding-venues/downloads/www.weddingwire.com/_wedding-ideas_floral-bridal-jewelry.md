Floral bridal earrings have a way of looking just right alongside any type of wedding attire, whether you've chosen a formal ball gown, a flowy boho silhouette, or a flirty garden party frock. Choosing them as your bridal jewelry will play up the romantic mood of the event—but more than that, floral earrings are a lovely gift idea for your [bridesmaids](https://www.weddingwire.com/wedding-ideas/bridesmaid-jewelry) or [mother of the bride](https://www.weddingwire.com/wedding-ideas/mother-of-the-bride-gifts) if you're also looking for bridal party accessories. Whether you want to splurge on a sparkly set of baubles to commemorate this special occasion or you're looking for something simple and quick, there are plenty of floral bridal earrings to choose from. Shop some of the trending bestsellers and our favorites below.
