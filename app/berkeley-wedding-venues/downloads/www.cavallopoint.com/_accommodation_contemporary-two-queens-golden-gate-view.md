# Contemporary Two Queens Golden Gate View

  * Sleeps 4
  * 2 Queen Beds
  * 450 sq. ft.



Nestled on the hillside, the Contemporary Two Queens Golden Gate View rooms at Cavallo Point blend modern design, luxurious amenities, and spectacular vistas.

Housed in two-story buildings constructed to the highest standards of green architecture, the rooms feature two queen beds, high thread-count organic linens, bamboo furnishings, and inviting fireplaces. Each of these San Francisco Bay Area hotel accommodations showcases breathtaking views of the Golden Gate Bridge. Accommodating up to four persons in 450 square feet, this room is ideal for families with children or friends traveling together.

  * ## Local Cheese Display
