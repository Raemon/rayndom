# Suggested Itineraries for 5 Perfect Days

To help you plan your visit and envision everything our local area has to offer, we’ve assembled a series of suggested family-friendly itineraries. Tickets and information for activities are available online, and our concierge, Marianne Wong, can also help you plan.

## Day 1: Adventure Close to Home

  * [Morning coffee & pastries](https://www.cavallopoint.com/dine/) – relax in our rocking chairs, offering picturesque views of the Golden Gate Bridg  

  * Visit the [Bay Area Discovery Museum](https://bayareadiscoverymuseum.org/) – just a 5-minute walk from Cavallo Point, the Discovery Museum offers outdoor and indoor exhibition spaces and daily programs for children 6 months of age to 8 years. Be sure to take advantage of our special 3-Day Family Pass, exclusive to guests of Cavallo Point! Contact our Concierge for details.  

  * Enjoy a picnic on the parade ground lawn across from our reception building  

  * Hike up the panoramic [Chapel Steps Trail](https://www.cavallopoint.com/discover/outdoor-adventure/hiking/) above Cavallo Point for incredible views of San Francisco, the Bay and the Golden Gate Bridge  

  * Enjoy a complimentary glass of wine in our reception area  

  * Dinner at [Farley](https://www.cavallopoint.com/dine/farley/)



## Day 2: Marin Beach Day

  * Morning stroll along nearby Travis Marina
  * Breakfast at [Farley](https://www.cavallopoint.com/dine/farley/)
  * Take our complimentary shuttle to [Rodeo Beach](https://www.parksconservancy.org/parks/rodeo-beach)
  * Picnic lunch at Rodeo Beach
  * Visit [The Marine Mammal Center](https://www.marinemammalcenter.org/), the world’s largest marine mammal hospital. As part of our exclusive partnership, Cavallo Point guests enjoy complimentary behind-the-scenes tours, seven days a week with advanced reservations!
  * Afternoon kite flying on the parade ground
  * End the day with a historic walking tour of [Fort Baker](https://www.nps.gov/articles/fort-baker.htm)
  * Sip on a complimentary glass of wine in a rocking chair on our front porch
  * Enjoy dinner in your room via [in-room dining](https://www.cavallopoint.com/dine/in-room-dining/) and a selection of family board games



## Day 3: A Day in the City by the Bay

  * [Morning yoga](https://www.cavallopoint.com/events/morning-yoga/) (for adults to get ready for the day!)
  * Enjoy breakfast in your room
  * [Drive to San Francisco in a Lexus vehicle](https://www.cavallopoint.com/events/lexus-test-drives/)
  * Explore the new [Presidio Tunnel Tops Park](https://presidio.gov/explore/attractions/presidio-tunnel-tops)
  * Visit the parklands of [Golden Gate Park](http://www.sftravel.com/article/visitors-guide-to-golden-gate-park) and experience:
    * [The California Academy of Sciences](https://www.calacademy.org/) – offering an aquarium, planetarium, rainforest, and natural history museum with exhibits and experiences for explorers of all ages
    * [Japanese Tea Garden](https://www.japaneseteagardensf.com/)


  * Lunch in San Francisco
  * Relax with early evening drinks on the terrace at [Farley](https://www.cavallopoint.com/dine/farley/)
  * Dinner at [Farley](https://www.cavallopoint.com/dine/farley/)



## Day 4: Explore the Wonder of Marin

  * Breakfast at [Farley](https://www.cavallopoint.com/dine/farley/)
  * Visit the ancient forest of [Muir Woods](https://www.nps.gov/muwo/index.htm) – Walk among old growth coast redwoods, cooling their roots in the fresh water of Redwood Creek and lifting their crowns to reach the sun and fog. Federally protected as a National Monument since 1908, this primeval forest is both refuge and laboratory, revealing our relationship with the living landscape. 
  * Take a scenic drive along the Marin Headlands [in a Lexus vehicle](https://www.cavallopoint.com/events/lexus-test-drives/) and enjoy lunch in Sausalito.
  * Enjoy a [watercolor class](https://www.cavallopoint.com/events/watercolor) led by our Cavallo Point watercolorist
  * Sip a complimentary glass of wine by the firepit
  * [Dinner in Sausalito](https://www.sausalito.org/lets-eat)



## Day 5: San Francisco by Sea

  * Complimentary morning coffee & pastries
  * Take our complimentary shuttle to the Sausalito ferry terminal
  * Enjoy a [ferry ride from Sausalito](https://www.goldengate.org/ferry/route-schedule/sausalito-san-francisco/) to San Francisco
  * Explore the historic Ferry Building
  * Visit the nearby [Exploratorium](https://www.exploratorium.edu/) – offering more than 600 interactive exhibits in six spacious indoor and outdoor galleries
  * Lunch in [Chinatown](http://www.sftravel.com/neighborhoods/chinatown) or along the [Embarcadero](http://www.sftravel.com/neighborhoods/embarcadero-financial-district)
  * Purchase dinner provisions in the [Ferry Building Marketplace](https://www.ferrybuildingmarketplace.com/)
  * Settle in for the ferry back to Sausalito
  * Enjoy a quiet evening in your room with a picnic dinner purchased from the Ferry Building Marketplace
