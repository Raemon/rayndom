# Lunch in Paris

****Special Event****

**Location** : The Cooking School (upstairs from Farley), Building 602

**Time** : 11:00 am – 2:00 pm

For me, food always tastes even better on vacation. One of my favorite meals is a delicious lunch at a Parisian cafe. Join us in the kitchen for a romantic lunch and learn how to recreate these beautiful dishes in between your travel adventures!

* * *

**Menu may include:**

Croque Monsieur Bites – Béchamel – Gruyere Cheese – French Jambon

Braised Leeks with Champagne Vinaigrette

Smoked Trout Salad with Cucumbers, Heirloom Potatoes and Dijon Dressing

Moules Marinieres – Mussels, Fennel, White Wine and Grilled Country Bread

Classic Steak Frite with Maitre d’Hotel butter

Tarte Tatin – Caramelized Apple Tart and Vanilla Bean Ice Cream

* * *

Should you need to cancel your reservation, you may do so up to 72 hours prior to the event. Cancellations made less than 72 hours in advance will not be refunded. Minimum age for public classes is 15 years old, unless otherwise noted.
