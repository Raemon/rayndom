# ADA Accessibility

Here at Cavallo Point, we’re committed to serving the needs of all of our guests and exceeding expectations for a memorable stay. Our goal is to provide every guest with an accessible, barrier-free experience—and we take pride in the many ADA amenities and features we have implemented throughout the lodge and surrounding property. We hope to welcome you soon!

Overall Accessibility Features

  * Compliant ramps, handrails & wheelchair accessibility in main lobby entrance
  * Accessible check-in desk to welcome guests
  * Seven accessible guest rooms located among our[ Historic](https://www.cavallopoint.com/stay/historic-lodging/) &[ Contemporary](https://www.cavallopoint.com/stay/contemporary-lodging/) accommodations, featuring different room types, views & amenities
  * Maps noting accessible entries, routes & accessible parking on property
  * Walkways & curbs with compliant ramps at intersections or in major traffic locations, as well as detectable warnings
  * ADA-accessible on-site venues, including [Farley Bar](https://www.cavallopoint.com/dine/farley/), [Healing Arts Center & Spa](https://www.cavallopoint.com/spa/), [the Mercantile](https://www.cavallopoint.com/discover/shopping-art/), [Cooking School](https://www.cavallopoint.com/discover/cooking-school/), [meeting rooms](https://www.cavallopoint.com/gatherings/meetings-events/venues/) & nature path
  * Service animals welcome at no extra charge or deposit (please note guests are responsible for damage caused by their service animals)
  * Accessible public restrooms
  * Communication kit available 
  * Each ADA-accessible guest room reserved for a specific guest when a booking is made



Parking & Transportation

  * 17 accessible parking spaces
  * Accessible parking at each accessible guest room
  * Valet and van-accessible parking
  * Local shuttle service available to Sausalito & Rodeo Beach (Wednesday to Sunday)



Healing Arts Center & Spa

  * Accessible spa main entrance with button activation
  * Accessible spa facilities, including locker rooms, treatment rooms, public restrooms & roll-in showers with folding seats, etc.
  * Accessible meditation pool entrances & areas, plus lift access available
  * Accessible fitness area, entrances & paths between equipment
  * Accessible parking



ADA-Accessible Guest Room Features

  * Reserved accessible parking at each accessible guest room
  * Guest rooms for the deaf & hard of hearing, with closed-captioned TVs
  * Braille & tactile signage where required
  * Telephones with volume control & TTY capability
  * Closets with lower clothes bars, irons & ironing boards
  * Compliant bathroom facilities, including roll-in showers and grab bars
  * In-room visual alarms in bedrooms & bathrooms
  * Compliant clear space throughout guest rooms
  * Accessible in-room safes
  * In-room dining
  * Lower beds



_Please note: Due to the historic nature of these buildings, all doorways could not be widened to accommodate larger wheelchairs and not all furnishings are accessible._

For guests who require aids or services, please contact the front desk or our welcoming staff at [1.888.651.2003](tel:18886512003).
