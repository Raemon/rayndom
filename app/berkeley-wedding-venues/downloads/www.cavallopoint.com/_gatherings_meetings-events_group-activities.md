# Group Activities

Play with Purpose at Cavallo Point

Cavallo Point is where culture, culinary discovery, and adventure meet. Here, both professional and social groups experience a unique base camp for “playing with purpose”.

Our Cavallo Point group activities and Marin County team-building venues can make your group’s retreat more productive, memorable, and fun. Let our expert team of experience designers work with you to customize a program for the group’s specific needs and culture. Take a look at the options and consider the possibilities.
