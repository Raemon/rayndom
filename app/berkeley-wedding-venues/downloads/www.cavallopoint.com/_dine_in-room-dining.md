# In-Room Dining 

From Farm-to-Fork to Your Room

Enjoy elevated American classics and hand-crafted cocktails, prepared with an artful twist on farm-to-fork, from the comfort of your guest room. Offering a wide selection of local and traditional favorites made with the freshest ingredients, our in-room dining menu offers plenty of delicious options to cure your Cavallo Point cravings.
