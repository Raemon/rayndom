# Date Night

Embark on an enchanting escape against the backdrop of the Golden Gate Bridge with our Date Night package at Cavallo Point. Begin your stay with a selection from our In-Room Custom Cocktail menu followed by dinner in the comfort of your guestroom, accompanied by a wine selection. Wake the next morning to a delightful in-room breakfast, served with your choice of refreshing mimosas or invigorating Bloody Mary’s. The package includes:

  * Choice of accommodations in select Historic or Contemporary rooms
  * Dinner for two with wine in your guestroom*
  * In-room cocktail*
  * Breakfast in your guestroom with a choice of Mimosas or Bloody Marys*



* * *

Plus, you’ll also enjoy our wide array of luxurious amenities and services*:

  * Valet & self-parking
  * WiFi access in your guest room and public spaces
  * Welcome minibar amenity
  * Daily coffee, tea & pastries (7:00 am – 10:00 am)
  * Welcome beverages at check-in
  * 24-hour fitness center
  * Vintage electric bikes
  * Daily yoga classes
  * Weekend guided hikes & history tours
  * Bellman & concierge services
  * Lexus vehicles available for guest use
  * Shuttle service to Rodeo Beach & Sausalito Ferry Terminal, Wednesday to Sunday



* * *

_*Offer Details: Dinner and Breakfast are inclusive of fees, gratuity, and taxes. In-room cocktail is ordered and delivered via Room Service after arrival. Package must be booked at least three days prior to arrival. Offer subject to availability; blackout dates may apply. Outdoor meditation pool is for adults 18 and older. Additional services and amenities are included in a resort fee of $65 per night/per guestroom._
