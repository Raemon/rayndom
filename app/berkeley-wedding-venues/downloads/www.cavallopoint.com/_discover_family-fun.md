# Family Fun

Great Days Together at Cavallo Point

Cavallo Point is an ideal destination for families. Our parade ground lawn is perfect for running around or having a family picnic. Next door, sharing our secluded cove, is the Bay Area Discovery Museum. Our backyard offers 80,000 acres of the Golden Gate National Recreation Area — with built-in fun for kids like hiking and beachcombing — and San Francisco and her attractions are just across the bridge.

Book your stay, then plan your play!

## 5 Perfect Days for Families

A day at the beach, exploring Marin’s redwood forest, and sailing along the bay are just some of the fun you and your family can enjoy together at Cavallo Point.

## Our Valued Local Partners

We are extremely fortunate to have cultivated exclusive partnerships with two remarkable organizations nearby in the Golden Gate National Recreation Area: [The Marine Mammal Center](https://www.marinemammalcenter.org/) and the [Bay Area Discovery Museum](https://bayareadiscoverymuseum.org/). Cavallo Point guests receive privileged access and unique offers graciously provided by each partner!

Learn more about these partnerships on our [Local Attractions](https://www.cavallopoint.com/discover/local-attractions/) page.
