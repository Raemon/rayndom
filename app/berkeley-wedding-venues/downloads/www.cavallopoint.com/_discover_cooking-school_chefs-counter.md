# Chef’s Counter

An Exclusive Chef-Driven Experience

Offering memorable, curated events for small groups of guests, the Chef’s Counter is an evening of exceptional food and wine unlike anything else. With a maximum capacity of eight people, this exclusive chef-driven dining series blends the best elements of an evening at an acclaimed restaurant with a private chef experience in your home.

Seated around the Cooking School’s airy yet intimate space, you’ll interact and learn from the chef, as each dish is deftly prepared before you. Enjoy an exquisite meal of five courses, along with pairings from our award-winning wine list.
