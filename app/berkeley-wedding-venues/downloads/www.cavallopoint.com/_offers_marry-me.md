# Will You Marry Me Experience

Few of life’s questions are as important as “Will you marry me?” Cavallo Point’s “Will You Marry Me” Experience sets the stage for an extraordinary proposal, offering a setting as memorable as the moment. Say “I do” with 30% off a stay in one of our luxurious Historic or Contemporary suites. A photographer will be on hand to capture the moment in the setting of your choice, followed by a celebratory bottle of chilled Champagne and a romantic dinner for two at Sula. All set against the breathtaking backdrop of the Golden Gate Bridge – a once-in-a-lifetime moment in a one-of-a-kind setting.

##### The Will You Marry Me Experience includes:

  * 30% Off a Stay in Your Choice of a Historic or Contemporary Suite
  * Professional Photographer Session – _The photographer will work with you to select the perfect onsite location_
  * Dinner for Two at Sula _($150 food and beverage credit)_
  * Bottle of Champagne 



* * *

Plus, you’ll also enjoy our wide array of luxurious amenities and services*:

  * Valet & self-parking
  * WiFi access in your guest room and public spaces
  * Welcome minibar amenity
  * Daily coffee, tea & pastries (7:00 am – 10:00 am)
  * Welcome beverages at check-in
  * 24-hour fitness center
  * Vintage electric bikes
  * Daily yoga classes
  * Weekend guided hikes & history tours
  * Bellman & concierge services
  * Lexus vehicles available for guest use
  * Shuttle service to Rodeo Beach & Sausalito Ferry Terminal, Wednesday to Sunday



* * *

_*Offer Details: Offer is subject to availability, blackout dates may apply, and is not valid with any other offers, packages or promotions. Must be booked 5 days in advance and any cancellations must be made 7 days prior to the date of arrival for a full refund. Cancellations made inside of 7 days will not be refunded. Includes a $150 food and beverage credit to cover dinner at Sula. Not valid for third-party bookings or negotiated rates. Additional amenities and services are included in a resort fee of $65 per night/per guestroom._
