# Luxurious Amenities

Enjoy a luxurious hotel experience in a spectacular national park setting at Cavallo Point. In addition to our award-winning San Francisco Bay Area accommodations and world-class programs for cultural enrichment and experiential learning, we offer a wide array of exceptional amenities and exclusive services—all of which reflect the lodge’s green philosophy of environmental and social responsibility.

## Lodge Amenities & Services

  * Valet & self-parking

  * WiFi & hard-wired internet access in your guest room

  * Welcome minibar amenity

  * Daily coffee, tea & pastries (7:00 – 10:00 am)



  * Welcome beverages at check-in

  * 24-hour fitness center

  * Vintage electric bikes

  * Weekday Yoga classes



  * Weekend guided hikes and history tours

  * Bellman & concierge services

  * Lexus vehicles available for guest use (valid U.S. driver’s license required)

  * Shuttle service to Rodeo Beach & Sausalito Ferry Terminal, Wednesday – Sunday




_* These are included in your rate as part of the resort fee._

##### Eco Luxe Pups

At Cavallo Point, every member of your family is important to us, including your furry friends. 

The Eco Luxe Pups program offers all the comforts of home—and that’s why we provide you with environmentally friendly, organic, and sustainable dog products made by local businesses.

Your pup is welcomed with a bag of treats and in-room dog products made right here in Marin County and the San Francisco Bay Area, including:

  * Luxurious bedding
  * Custom food & water bowls by Sausalito Pottery
  * Organic cookies & treats
  * Pet Swag Bag, which includes a gift and local treats



When booking, please indicate if you are bringing one or two dogs. A one-time, non-refundable fee of $150 applies.
