# Cavallo Point Careers

Join our exceptional hospitality team at Cavallo Point, the luxury resort by the Golden Gate Bridge near San Francisco, California. Cavallo Point is an award-winning property that promotes environmental and social responsibility, supports sustainable development, and offers a unique and soulful guest experience.

Cavallo Point’s goal is to set a new standard for quality and integrity. We seek staff members who share these values and deliver a gracious guest experience with passion and commitment. Because we value teamwork and individuality, the contributions of our employees matter. We welcome staff members who enjoy what they do, treat colleagues with respect and guests with esteem, and have fun.

When you work for Cavallo Point, the motto is simple: Greet everyone you meet as if he or she were an old friend.

Please note: If we don’t currently have an open position in your area, you may submit a [resume](https://theapplicantmanager.com/jobs?pos=CP0000) or an [Application for Employment](https://theapplicantmanager.com/application?co=cp&new). If you already have an application in progress, you can retrieve and edit it by clicking [here](https://theapplicantmanager.com/application?co=cp&retrieve).
