# Historic Lodging

### Turn-of-the-Century Character, Luxury-Focused Amenities

With scenic city vistas and expansive green spaces, our Historic Lodging at Cavallo Point blends Colonial Revival character with luxury-focused amenities. These landmark San Francisco Bay Area hotel accommodations are impeccably restored, featuring authentic turn-of-the-century details, spacious front porches, and panoramic windows that open to San Francisco Bay breezes.

## Historic City View Junior Suite

  * Sleeps 4
  * 1 King Bed & 1 Sleeper Sofa
  * Total 430 sq. ft.



Showcasing beautiful bay and city skyline views, the spacious Historic City View Junior Suites features a king bed with organic linens, a separate sitting area with a sleeper sofa and vintage architectural details. Additionally, some of these rooms offer a cozy fireplace.

## Historic King Suite

  * Sleeps 4
  * 1 King Bed + Sleeper Sofa
  * Total 540 sq. ft.



Enjoy a myriad of stunning views and a tranquil retreat in our expansive 540-square-foot Historic King Suites at Cavallo Point. Some of these rooms offer a cozy fireplace, enhancing the separate living room, where a queen sleeper sofa can accommodate two additional people.
