# Tea Bar

A place in our spa to relax, sip and savor

A soothing space to unwind, the Tea Bar in the Healing Arts Center & Spa offers a variety of healthy tonics and teas made with the finest ingredients, along with an array of fresh dishes . Allow our staff to nurture you with nature’s revitalizing flavors, and enjoy your refreshments indoors at the Tea Bar or, if you have a scheduled spa treatment, in the outdoor garden.
