# Paella Party

**Location** : The Cooking School (upstairs from Farley), Building 602

**Time** : 5:00 pm – 8:00 pm

Come try one of Spain’s most well-known and favorite dishes. The dish takes its name from the wide, shallow traditional pan used to cook it over an open fire, paella being the word for a frying pan in Valencian/Catalan dialects.

* * *

**Menu may include:**

Spanish Tapas Board – Iberico Ham and Veggies

Butter Lettuce Salad with Lemon Shallot Vinaigrette

Vegetable Paella

Paella Valencia

Warm Churros – Chocolate & Caramel Sauces
