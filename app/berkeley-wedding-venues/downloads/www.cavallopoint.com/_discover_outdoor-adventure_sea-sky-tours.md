# Sea + Sky Tours

## See the Bay Area from a New Perspective

See the Bay Area from every angle with our Sea + Sky Tours. Sail under the Golden Gate Bridge or take to the air for a bird’s-eye view of the city’s most famous landmarks. From romance to a rush of adrenaline, these unique tours are a wonderful addition to your Cavallo Point experience.

Bay Cruises

Renowned as the most comfortable and stylish sailboat in the Bay Area, Freda B is a beautiful, historic 80-foor classic coastal schooner. Bay Cruises captures the sights of San Francisco Bay with nostalgic charm and romance.

  * Sunset Sails are available most Fridays and Saturdays; $140/person
  * Marine Wildlife + Ecology Sails are available most Sundays; $140/person
  * Private Sails are also available; please inquire for pricing




Private Yacht Excursions

Experience San Francisco Bay on a private yacht! Cimba is a classic 32’ motor yacht for private groups of up to 8 people. Design your own excursion, or we can create one for you and your group!




Seaplane Tours Over the Bay

Our tailored group tour flight takes you over San Francisco Bay, Crissy Field, downtown San Francisco, Oracle Park, and circles back over the Bay and Alcatraz. Finally, your flight passes over Angel Island, the historic Immigrant Gateway to the West, and lands back in Richardson Bay.

  * Alcatraz and City Sites Tour – 20 minutes, $255
  * Golden Gate Bridge Tour – 30 minutes, $310
  * Sunset Champagne Tour – 40 minutes, $405
  * Greater Bay Area Tour – 45 minutes, $360
  * Coastal Tour – 60 minutes, $415




Golden Gate Bridge & San Francisco Helicopter Tours

Experience an unforgettable perspective of San Francisco from above, including Alcatraz, the Golden Gate Bridge, the city skyline, and more.

  * Alcatraz City Sites Helicopter Adventure – 15-20 minutes, $340/person
  * Golden Gate Helicopter Adventure – 25-30 minutes, $405/person
  * Private charter available; please inquire for pricing
