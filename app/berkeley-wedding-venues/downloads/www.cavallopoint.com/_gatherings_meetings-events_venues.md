# Meeting & Event Spaces

### Bay Area Meeting Venues That Feel Worlds Away

Close to everything, but away from it all, Cavallo Point offers an inspired backdrop for business meetings, corporate retreats, and special events for groups of 10 to 300. Indoors, our Bay Area corporate meeting venues provide 14,000 square feet of adaptable meeting space, while outdoors, we offer 12,500 square feet of spectacular space framed by stately trees and unparalleled vistas of the bridge, bay, and city skyline.

Whatever the location or occasion, our expert staff ensures your function goes smoothly from beginning to end.
