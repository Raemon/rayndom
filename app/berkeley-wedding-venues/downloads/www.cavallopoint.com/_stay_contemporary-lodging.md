# Contemporary Lodging

### Environmental Sensitivity, Modern Style

Showcasing breathtaking views of the Golden Gate Bridge and San Francisco Bay, the Contemporary lodging at Cavallo Point seamlessly blends environmental sensitivity with modern style. From their hillside perch above historic Fort Baker, these contemporary rooms and suites feature private entrances, floor-to-ceiling windows, radiant heat floors, and cozy fireplaces. All are constructed to the highest standards of green architecture.
