# Stay at Cavallo Point

Nestled at the foot of the Golden Gate Bridge, our lodge offers two distinctive styles of accommodations. Both feature a luxurious hotel experience with breathtaking views, a green ethos, and exceptional amenities.

### [Contemporary LodgingShowcasing spectacular views of the Golden Gate Bridge, our hillside Contemporary rooms and suites seamlessly blend environmental sensitivity with modern style. Each room and suite offers a private entrance, floor-to-ceiling windows, a cozy fireplace, and is constructed to the highest standard of green architecture.

  * View Contemporary Lodging

](https://www.cavallopoint.com/stay/contemporary-lodging/ "View Contemporary Lodging")

### [Historic AccommodationsWith scenic city vistas and expansive green spaces, these impeccably restored landmark accommodations blend Colonial Revival character with luxury-focused amenities. Each space features authentic turn-of-the-century details, spacious front porches, and panoramic windows that open to San Francisco Bay breezes.

## Accommodation FAQ’s

[View All FAQ's](https://www.cavallopoint.com/policies-faqs/?cat=accommodations)

How do I manage my existing reservation?

You can view and manage your existing reservation by visiting our reservation portal. Click the link below and sign in with your booking details:  
[View Existing Reservation](https://be.synxis.com/signin?&chain=10237&hotel=26855&level=hotel&locale=en-US)

What are your check-in and check-out times?

The check-in time is 4:00 pm, and the check-out time is 12:00 noon.

Do you have facilities for guests with disabilities?

Cavallo Point welcomes guests with disabilities. ADA lodging is available. For more information, please visit our [Accessibility page](https://www.cavallopoint.com/accessibility/).

Do you offer shuttle service?

We are pleased to offer a Cavallo Point complimentary shuttle service, you can find more information [here](https://www.cavallopoint.com/contact-directions/).

What is the weather like?

For current Sausalito, California, weather information, please visit [www.weather.com](https://weather.com/en-CA/weather/today/l/44a331d14cebcabded59eb2441914ebbc61a461a9a86da49311adc52e8c4ad4f).

What are your dog policies?

Cavallo Point is pleased to welcome you and your dog. In an effort to ensure the peace and tranquility of all guests, we ask for your assistance with the following:

  * When booking, please select the [Eco Luxe Pups](https://www.cavallopoint.com/roomaddons/eco-luxe-pups/) package add-on ($150 per room per stay), and indicate in the “Additional Details” section if you are bringing one or two dogs. We can accept a maximum of two friendly dogs per room. The $150 fee is required and non-refundable. If there is any damage to the room due to your pet, an additional charge will be applied for the cost of repairs.
  * Dogs must be leashed at all times while on property, including the Parade Ground lawn area in front of the lodge (in accordance with National Park Service rules). Please refrain from tying your pet to trees and other objects on property.
  * Please do not leave pets unattended in guest rooms out of respect for other guests around you. For housekeeping to enter your room, owners must be present or the pet removed from the room.
  * Dogs are restricted from entering the Healing Arts Center & Spa as well as Farley and Sula, with the exception of the terrace in front of the restaurant and bar.
  * The owner hereby represents to Cavallo Point that their pet is not inclined to bite, and that in the event that their dog bites causing injury while on Lodge premises, the owner hereby agrees to fully hold harmless and indemnify Cavallo Point from any liability, which Cavallo Point may incur, including, but not limited to, reasonable attorney fees, relating to such dog bite.
