# Wedding Venues

### Inspired Spaces for Wonderful Celebrations

Cavallo Point offers over 25,000 square feet of unique and distinctive indoor and outdoor wedding space. Our Bay Area wedding venues provide unparalleled views of the Golden Gate Bridge and San Francisco Bay, plus one-of-a-kind spaces for bridesmaid lunches, rehearsal dinners, and Champagne receptions.

## Mission Blue Lawn and Kober Lawn

  * Total 6000 sq. ft.
  * Up to 250



Whether you prefer a venue secluded among the trees or one with Golden Gate views, our diverse outdoor areas add exceptional ambience to special events. Mission Blue Lawn can be tented for receptions (at an additional cost) to accommodate weddings of up to 250 people with a dance floor. Its adjacent concrete patio is perfect for an after-ceremony Champagne toast.

  * [View Capacity Chart](https://www.cavallopoint.com/content/uploads/2023/09/CAV_weddings_missionblue_rgb_single.pdf)

## Tidewater Room and Foyer, Surfbird Room and Foyer

  * Total 2050 sq. ft.
  * Up to 190



These light-filled function rooms provide a perfect backdrop for weddings or events.

  * [View Capacity Chart](https://www.cavallopoint.com/content/uploads/2023/09/CAV_weddings_surftidewater_rgb_single.pdf)

## Mission Blue

  * Total 2034 sq. ft.
  * Up to 200



Formerly Fort Baker’s historic chapel, Mission Blue is an inviting Bay Area wedding venue, and can accommodate up to 200 people for ceremonies or up to 90 people for receptions with a dance area. Mission Blue also features a stone patio outdoors where guests can mix and mingle.

  * [View Capacity Chart](https://www.cavallopoint.com/content/uploads/2023/09/CAV_weddings_missionblue_rgb_single.pdf)
  * [Take Virtual Tour](https://visitingmedia.com/tt8/?ttid=cavallo-point-icms/#/360?group=1&tour=3)

## Verbena A and Verbena B

  * Total 2500 sq. ft.
  * Up to 190



This elegant space with two working fireplaces is best suited for smaller weddings. The room has access to a large indoor foyer and heated porches with Golden Gate and bay views. 

  * [View Capacity Chart](https://www.cavallopoint.com/content/uploads/2023/09/CAV_weddings_verbena_rgb_single.pdf)
  * [Take Virtual Tour](https://visitingmedia.com/tt8/?ttid=cavallo-point-icms/#/3d-model/0/1)

## Callippe Ballroom, Foyer and Firepit, and Silverspot Room

  * Total 7000 sq. ft.
  * Up to 530



The Callippe Ballroom, located in Fort Baker’s historic gymnasium, features 33' vaulted ceilings with classic exposed beams. The elegant space holds 140 for a full plated service with dance floor, or 160 with dancing in the foyer. A private foyer is perfect for cocktail receptions. Outside, the large terrace with firepit and Golden Gate views provides additional space for guests to mingle.

  * [View Capacity Chart](https://www.cavallopoint.com/content/uploads/2023/09/CAV_weddings_calippe_rgb_single.pdf)
  * [Take Virtual Tour](https://visitingmedia.com/tt8/?ttid=cavallo-point-icms/#/360?group=1&tour=0)



  *   * 


## La Mariposa Borracha

  * Total 798 sq. ft.
  * Up to 20



This handsome and distinctive room showcases highlights from the Cavallo Point’s exquisite wine collection. The space is highlighted by a custom-crafted, reclaimed walnut table as its centerpiece. This room, which accommodates up to 20 guests for a seated dinner, is ideal for an elegant small wedding, rehearsal dinner or reception.

  * [View Capacity Chart](https://www.cavallopoint.com/content/uploads/2023/09/CAV_weddings_mariposa_rgb_single.pdf)
  * [Take Virtual Tour](https://visitingmedia.com/tt8/?ttid=cavallo-point-icms/#/3d-model)
