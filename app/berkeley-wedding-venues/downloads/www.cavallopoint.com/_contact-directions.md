###### Transportation Options

From our Cavallo Point shuttle service and on-property Lexus vehicles to San Francisco’s premier car service, we are pleased to offer a number of transportation options during your stay with us. Should you have any additional transportation requirements, please contact our Concierge or Front Desk.

###### Cavallo Point Shuttle Service

Cavallo Point offers daily complimentary shuttle service for our hotel guests to and from the Sausalito Ferry Landing and Rodeo Beach. The shuttle operates Wednesday to Sunday and arrangements must be made 12 hours in advance. Please ask our Concierge or Front Desk for details.

**Drop Off To Rodeo Beach:** 12:00 PM  
**Pick Up From Rodeo Beach:** 4:00 PM

###### Lexus

As a benefit to our guests, several Lexus vehicles are available for guests to use for test drives and to explore the surrounding area. Cars are available on a first come, first serve basis for a two-hour period. Please call [(888) 651-2003](tel:888.651.2003) or visit the Front Desk for details.
