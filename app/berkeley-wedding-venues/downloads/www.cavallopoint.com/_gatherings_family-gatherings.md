# Small Family Gatherings

### Create Lasting Family Memories

Gather your loved ones for a memorable retreat at Cavallo Point. A natural haven for small family gatherings, Cavallo Point’s location at the foot of the majestic Golden Gate offers the ideal setting for graduations, reunions, multigenerational travel, and all other types of family events. Bring everyone closer together as your family solely occupies one of our charming Historic buildings. Each family group occupies its own spacious guestroom, allowing your entire family to share precious moments on the porch area, creating cherished memories amidst the stunning backdrop of the Golden Gate National Recreation Area.

Our exclusive Family Gatherings package offers a significant discount off our Best Flexible Rate along with a generous Food and beverage credit that adjusts accordingly depending on the size of your group. For more information, please contact our Reservations department at [(888) 883-0812](tel:8888830812) or [[email protected]](/cdn-cgi/l/email-protection#f280978197808493869b9d9c81b2919384939e9e9d829d9b9c86dc919d9f). We invite you to embrace the spirit of family at Cavallo Point, where every moment is an opportunity for connection and celebration
