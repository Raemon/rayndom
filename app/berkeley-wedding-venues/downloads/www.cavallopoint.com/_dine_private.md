# Private Dining

Beautiful Backdrops & Fantastic Food

Cavallo Point brings your private event or special occasion to life with inspired menu options and remarkable views of the Golden Gate Bridge and San Francisco Bay.  
  
The unique, elegant venue in [Sula](https://www.cavallopoint.com/dine/sula/) and an extraordinary team of event professionals come together to transform your vision into an unforgettable experience, whether it be an intimate anniversary, wedding celebration, birthday or corporate event.
