# Sula

Elevated Dining in a Stylish Setting

Offering modern American cuisine with cultural influences from around the world, utilizing fresh, local, and seasonal ingredients, guests of Sula experience elevated, yet comfortable dining in a stylish setting that blends the property’s heritage with an inspired, modern feel. Complement your selections with carefully crafted cocktails that highlight small batch distillers and classic wines from all regions of the world. Then, satisfy your sweet tooth with a delectable selection of desserts.

Open daily for breakfast, weekend brunch, lunch and dinner, dining can be enjoyed indoors in the dining room or outdoors on the terrace with sweeping views of the city skyline, San Francisco Bay, and the Golden Gate Bridge. An exquisite culinary experience set amidst a beautiful backdrop, sip into a sea of flavors and immerse yourself in the sights of the city when dining at Sula.

NEW YEAR’S EVE DINNER!
