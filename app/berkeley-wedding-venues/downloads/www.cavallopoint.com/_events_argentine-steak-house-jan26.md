# Argentine Steak House

**Location** : The Cooking School (upstairs from Farley), Building 602

**Time** : 6:00 pm – 9:00 pm

As one of five countries with more cattle than people, Argentina is renowned for eating more beef than any other country in the world. We have combined some signature beef dishes with other traditional favorites to round out a beautiful, authentic menu.

* * *

**Menu may include:**

Grilled Vegetables with Burrata

Argentinean Beef Empanadas

Cast Iron Provoleta with Grilled Bread

Humita – Steamed Corn Tamale

Grilled New York Steak with Chimichurri

Caramelized Banana Tart with Crème Fraîche
