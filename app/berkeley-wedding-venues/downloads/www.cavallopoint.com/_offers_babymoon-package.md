# Mama Goddess Babymoon Package

The journey into motherhood is one of life’s most transformative experiences. Before sleepless nights and endless diaper changes, there is this precious window of time to slow down, breathe deeply, and honor the incredible transformation happening within you. Our Mama Goddess Babymoon Package offers the perfect sanctuary to connect with your inner self while surrounded by the breathtaking natural beauty of the Golden Gate Bridge and Marin Headlands. Take time to nurture yourself before preparing for the beautiful adventure ahead.

##### Your package includes:

  * Overnight accommodation in the room of your choice at 15% off
  * Choice of one 60-minute spa treatment at the Healing Arts Center & Spa:
    * Mama Goddess Massage (pregnancy-safe massage to ease tension and honor your body’s incredible work)
    * Mama Glow Facial (gentle, radiance-enhancing facial that celebrates your natural pregnancy glow)
  * Special babymoon gift



* * *

Plus, you’ll also enjoy our wide array of luxurious amenities and services*:

  * Valet & self-parking
  * WiFi access in your guest room and public spaces
  * Welcome minibar amenity
  * Daily coffee, tea & pastries (7:00 am – 10:00 am)
  * Welcome beverages at check-in
  * 24-hour fitness center
  * Vintage electric bikes
  * Daily yoga classes
  * Weekend guided hikes & history tours
  * Bellman & concierge services
  * Lexus vehicles available for guest use
  * Shuttle service to Rodeo Beach & Sausalito Ferry Terminal, Wednesday to Sunday



* * *

_*Offer Details: Package must be booked at least 3 weeks prior to arrival. Inclusions are offered per stay and must be redeemed during time of stay; inclusions are non-transferable and non-refundable. Package may not be combined with other offers. Offer subject to availability and blackout dates may apply. Outdoor meditation pool is for adults 18 and older. Additional services and amenities are included in a resort fee of $65 per night/per guestroom._
