# Suite Spot Promotion

### Experience Our Refreshed Historic Suites

Discover the _Suite Spot_ at Cavallo Point and be among the first to experience our newly renovated Historic guestrooms and suites. Thoughtfully reimagined with an updated color palette, rich textures, and refined details, these refreshed spaces offer a perfect blend of elegance and warmth.

To celebrate, we’re offering our Suite Spot promotion, available exclusively in all Historic Suites for stays through March 31. Enjoy a $200 resort credit to spend however you like—savoring delicious meals at Sula or Farley, unwinding at the spa, or finding something special at the Mercantile. Plus, elevate your stay with a [**Custom Cocktail Package**](https://www.cavallopoint.com/amenities/#room-amenities) and enjoy your favorite cocktail in the comfort of your suite. Raise a glass to history reimagined and make your next getaway a toast-worthy occasion at Cavallo Point.

##### The Suite Spot Promotion includes:

  * Your Choice of Historic Suite Accommodations
  * $200 Resort Credit _(one credit per stay)_
  * In-Room Custom Cocktail Package



* * *

Plus, you’ll also enjoy our wide array of luxurious amenities and services:

  * Valet & self-parking
  * WiFi access in your guest room and public spaces
  * Welcome minibar amenity
  * Daily coffee, tea & pastries (7:00 am – 10:00 am)
  * Welcome beverages at check-in
  * 24-hour fitness center
  * Vintage electric bikes
  * Daily yoga classes
  * Weekend guided hikes & history tours
  * Bellman & concierge services
  * Lexus vehicles available for guest use
  * Shuttle service to Rodeo Beach & Sausalito Ferry Terminal, Wednesday to Sunday



* * *

*Offer Details: Valid for stays in Historic Suites through March 31, 2026. Includes one $200 resort credit per room, per stay and one Custom Cocktail Package. Offer is subject to availability, blackout dates may apply and may not be combined with any other offers or promotions. Not valid for Group reservations (5 or more reservations). Additional services and amenities are included in a resort fee of $65 per night/per guestroom.
