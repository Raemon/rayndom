# Mediterranean Feast

**Location** : The Cooking School (upstairs from Farley), Building 602

**Time** : 5:00 pm – 8:00 pm

The Mediterranean table is about gathering, community, family, hospitality, and of course, fresh, amazing and delicious food. Join us for a special gathering around our amazing cooking school table and enjoy!

* * *

**Menu may include:**

Mezze: Hummus – Baba Ghanoush – Muhammara – Tzatziki

Arugula & Potato Salad with Lemon Basil Dressing

Mediterranean Roasted Chicken with Prunes and Olives

Baked Halibut with Lemon, Garlic & Herbs

Handmade Pita Bread

Pistachio Kataifi Trifle

* * *

Should you need to cancel your reservation, you may do so up to 72 hours prior to the event. Cancellations made less than 72 hours in advance will not be refunded. Minimum age for public classes is 15 years old, unless otherwise noted.
