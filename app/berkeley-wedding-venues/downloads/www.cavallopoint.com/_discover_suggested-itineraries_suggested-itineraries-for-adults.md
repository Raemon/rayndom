# 7 Perfect Days for Adults

Adult-Focused Suggested Itineraries

With so many things to do, sometimes it’s hard to know where to start. To assist in planning your visit, we’ve put together some itineraries for adults. Wine Country excursions, private meditation instruction, and a pampering day at the spa are just a few of the suggested itineraries we have waiting for you.

## Day 1: A Day at Cavallo Point

  * [Morning yoga](https://www.cavallopoint.com/events/morning-yoga-2/) – center yourself and begin the day with a complimentary yoga class
  * Morning coffee & pastries – relax in our rocking chairs, offering picturesque views of the Golden Gate Bridge
  * Ride a complimentary vintage electric bike across the Golden Gate Bridge
  * Lunch at [Farley](https://www.cavallopoint.com/dine/farley/)
  * Hike up the [Chapel Steps Trail](https://www.cavallopoint.com/discover/outdoor-adventure/hiking/) above Cavallo Point
  * Decompress with a spa treatment at the [Healing Arts Center & Spa](https://www.cavallopoint.com/spa/), then soak in the meditation pool or detoxify in the eucalyptus-infused steam room
  * Sip a complimentary glass of wine by the firepit  

  * Dinner at [Sula](https://www.cavallopoint.com/dine/sula/)



## Day 2: Water Sports & Reflection

  * Enjoy breakfast in your room  

  * Experience a day on the bay with [your choice of water sports](https://www.cavallopoint.com/discover/outdoor-adventure/water-sports/)  

    * Kayaking class in Sausalito  

    * Stand-up paddle boarding class   

    * Surfing class at a beach in Marin  

  * Venture into [Sausalito for lunch](https://www.sausalito.org/lets-eat)
  * [Private meditation instruction](https://www.cavallopoint.com/spa/healing-arts-services/) at the Healing Arts Center & Spa – find your center, release stress, and bring harmony to your life
  * Cocktails at [Sula Lounge](https://www.cavallopoint.com/dine/sula/)
  * Enjoy a fun, engaging class at the [Cooking School](https://www.cavallopoint.com/discover/cooking-school/)



## Day 3: Wine Country Excursion

These are just a few suggested ideas for your choice of a Sonoma or Napa experience. Our Les Clefs d’Or Chef Concierge is available to work with you to create an exceptional itinerary customized to your tastes and preferences.  


### Sonoma  


  * [Domaine Carneros](https://www.domainecarneros.com/) – Sit down for a lovely Sparkling Champagne tasting and Tsar Nicoulai Caviar Flight
  * [Ramsgate Winery](https://ramsgatewinery.com/) – Enjoy the views along with a seasonal food & wine experience
  * [Viansa Winery](https://www.viansa.com/) – Private outlook tasting
  * Dinner at The Plaza in [downtown Sonoma](https://www.sonomavalley.com/food-wine/)



### Napa  


  * [Domaine Carneros](https://www.domainecarneros.com/) – Sparkling Champagne tasting and Tsar Nicoulai Caviar Flight
  * Lunch at [Farmstead at Long Meadow Ranch](https://www.longmeadowranch.com/eat-drink/restaurant/)
  * [Stag’s Leap Wine Cellars](https://www.stagsleap.com/) – Settle in for an estate collection tasting flight
  * [Dinner in Yountville](https://yountville.com/dining/)



## Day 4: A Day in the City

  * Complimentary morning coffee & pastries
  * Take our complimentary shuttle to the [Sausalito ferry](https://www.goldengate.org/ferry/schedules-maps/) terminal
  * Enjoy a ferry ride from Sausalito to San Francisco
  * Explore the historic [Ferry Building Marketplace](https://www.ferrybuildingmarketplace.com/)
  * Journey to the lush parklands of [Golden Gate Park](http://www.sftravel.com/article/visitors-guide-to-golden-gate-park)
    * Discover the exhibits at the [deYoung Museum](https://www.famsf.org/visit/de-young): The fine art museum is home to American art from the 17th century through today, textile arts and costumes, African art, Oceanic art, arts of the Americas, and international contemporary art.
    * Visit the [Japanese Tea Garden](https://www.japaneseteagardensf.com/): It features classic elements such as an arched drum bridge, pagodas, stone lanterns, stepping stone paths, native Japanese plants, serene koi ponds, and a zen garden.
  * Lunch in [Chinatown](http://www.sftravel.com/neighborhoods/chinatown) or along the [Embarcadero](http://www.sftravel.com/neighborhoods/embarcadero-financial-district)
  * Purchase dinner provisions in the [Ferry Building Marketplace](https://www.ferrybuildingmarketplace.com/)
  * Settle in for the ferry ride back to Sausalito
  * Enjoy a quiet evening in your room with a picnic dinner purchased from Ferry Building Marketplace



## Day 5: Sea & Sky Adventures

  * [Morning yoga](https://www.cavallopoint.com/events/morning-yoga-2/)
  * Morning coffee & pastries around the firepit
  * Express yourself with a [watercolor class](https://www.cavallopoint.com/events/watercolor) with our Cavallo Point watercolorist. 
  * Enjoy [lunch in Sausalito](https://www.sausalito.org/lets-eat)
  * Choose between a [Sea or Sky Tour](https://www.cavallopoint.com/discover/outdoor-adventure/sea-sky-tours/):
    * Sail under the Golden Gate Bridge with an afternoon sailing excursion from Sausalito Harbor
    * Take to the air in a seaplane or helicopter for a bird’s-eye view of the city’s most famous landmarks
  * Relax with early evening drinks on the terrace at [Farley](https://www.cavallopoint.com/dine/farley/)
  * Dinner at [Farley](https://www.cavallopoint.com/dine/farley/)



## Day 6: Rodeo Beach Is Calling

  * A morning stroll along nearby Travis Marina
  * Breakfast at [Farley](https://www.cavallopoint.com/dine/farley/)
  * Take our complimentary shuttle to [Rodeo Beach](https://www.parksconservancy.org/parks/rodeo-beach)
  * Picnic lunch at Rodeo Beach
  * Visit the [Marine Mammal Center](https://www.marinemammalcenter.org/) – Go to the world’s largest marine mammal hospital to see the life-saving care provided to patients so they can be released back into the wild. Watch as meals are prepared in the Fish Kitchen, peek into labs to learn more about scientific research, and explore interactive exhibits for a behind-the-scenes look at what it takes to rescue hundreds of marine mammals every year.
  * [Fort Baker](https://www.nps.gov/articles/fort-baker.htm) History Tour – our resident historian leads you on an enlightening complimentary history walk around the Cavall Point property
  * Drinks and dinner at [Sula Lounge](https://www.cavallopoint.com/dine/sula/)



## Day 7: Explore the Natural Wonders of Marin

  * Breakfast at [Farley](https://www.cavallopoint.com/dine/farley/)
  * Visit the ancient forest of [Muir Woods](https://www.nps.gov/muwo/index.htm) – Walk among old growth coast redwoods, cooling their roots in the freshwater of Redwood Creek and lifting their crowns to reach the sun and fog. Federally protected as a National Monument since 1908, this primeval forest is both refuge and laboratory, revealing our relationship with the living landscape.
  * Take a scenic drive and eat lunch along the Marin Coast [in a Lexus vehicle](https://www.cavallopoint.com/events/lexus-test-drives/)
  * Drive to the lovely town of Tiburon for a picturesque walk along the Bay to [Paradise Beach Park](https://www.parks.marincounty.org/parkspreserves/parks/paradise-beach)
  * Sip a complimentary glass of wine by the firepit
  * [Dinner in Sausalito](https://www.sausalito.org/lets-eat)
