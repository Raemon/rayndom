# Summer Begins at Cavallo Point

## Your Basecamp for Bay Area Adventure

This summer, make Cavallo Point your destination from which to enjoy everything the Bay Area has to offer. With over 75,000+ acres of national parkland surrounding us, enjoy ample opportunities to explore, discover, relax and play within minutes of your guestroom. Whether you choose to get outside and explore the nearby Napa Valley Wine Country or sights of San Francisco, or prefer to stay closer to home and take advantage of the 350+ acres of trails for running, hiking, and biking, there is truly something for everyone. We invite you to visit us at Cavallo Point and create your summer story by the Bay!
