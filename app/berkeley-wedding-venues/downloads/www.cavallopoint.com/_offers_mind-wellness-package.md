# Mind & Body Wellness Package

Embark on a transformative journey of relaxation and rejuvenation with our Mind & Body Wellness Package. Your experience begins with a stay in our luxurious accommodations featuring a state-of-the-art Bryte Balance™ smart bed, ensuring a restful night’s sleep. Immerse yourself in a world of tranquility at the renowned Healing Arts Center & Spa with a day pass allowing you to explore our extensive facilities and serene surroundings before or after your choice of a spiritual healing arts treatment along with a kombucha tasting at our Tea Bar. The following morning, engage your body and mind with a yoga class and a healthy beverage from the Tea Bar to kickstart your day, all set against the breathtaking backdrop of the Golden Gate Bridge and the bay. 

##### Your package includes:

  * Accommodations in your choice of a Historic or Contemporary King room fitted with a Bryte Balance™ smart bed
  * Choice of Healing Arts Treatment for one person 
    * Shamanic Journey (90 min)

    * Chakra Energy Balancing (60 min)

    * ____Reiki (90 min)

  * Kombucha Tasting at The Tea Bar
  * Day Pass at the Healing Arts Center & Spa
  * Morning Yoga
  * Healthy Beverage from The Tea Bar



_Package rate is $888 per night._
