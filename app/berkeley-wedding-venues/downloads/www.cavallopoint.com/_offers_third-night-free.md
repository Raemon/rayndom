# Third Night Free

### Stay, Play & Save at Cavallo Point

**Stay three nights for the price of two** — an exceptional offer available on every Cavallo Point room type!

* * *

With this Third Night Free package in the San Francisco Bay Area, you’ll also enjoy a wide array of luxurious amenities and services.*

  * Valet & self-parking
  * WiFi access in your guest room and public spaces
  * Welcome minibar amenity
  * Daily coffee, tea & pastries (7:00 am – 10:00 am)
  * Welcome beverages at check in
  * 24-hour fitness center
  * Vintage electric bikes
  * Daily yoga classes
  * Weekend guided hikes & history tours
  * Bellman & concierge services
  * Lexus vehicles available for guest use  

  * Shuttle service to Rodeo Beach & Sausalito Ferry Terminal, Wednesday to Sunday



* * *

_*Offer Details: The total price reflects the complimentary night discount already applied. Offer is subject to availability and blackout dates may apply. Outdoor meditation pool is for adults 18 and older. Additional services and amenities are included in a resort fee of $65 per night/per guestroom._
