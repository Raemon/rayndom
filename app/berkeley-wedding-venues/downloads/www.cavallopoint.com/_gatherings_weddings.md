# Weddings at Cavallo Point

### Unforgettable Celebrations in an Extraordinary Place

One of Northern California’s most romantic locations, the award-winning Cavallo Point marries breathtaking beauty in a historic setting with understated luxury, inviting accommodations, acclaimed food and wine, and exceptional activities, all onsite.

Combined with flexible indoor and outdoor event space — including the historic Mission Blue Chapel and Callippe Ballroom — Cavallo Point is the perfect location for your unforgettable San Francisco Bay Area wedding.

## Cavallo Point Wedding Highlights

  * Stunning natural setting with views of Golden Gate Bridge, San Francisco and bay
  * Set within national parklands, just minutes from San Francisco
  * One-of-a-kind wedding venues indoors and out accommodate 10 to 250 guests
  * Historic and Contemporary guest rooms and suites, luxurious and green
  * Special customizable wedding menus, enhanced by an exceptional wine cellar
  * One single source for all event needs
  * Professional catering staff to create a flawless event from start to finish
  * Outstanding amenities and activities onsite and nearby to enhance the festivities

Do you offer group room blocks for special events?

Yes, we are happy to offer discounted group room rates for contracted blocks. Please call and ask to speak with a member of our team for additional details and to check availability.

How do I get to Cavallo Point?

The resort is located at 601 Murray Circle, Fort Baker, Sausalito, CA 94965. For additional details, please visit our [Directions](https://www.cavallopoint.com/contact-directions/) page. Most wedding guests use a combination of transportation methods, depending on budget and time constraints.
