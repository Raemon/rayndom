# Historic King Suite with City View

  * Sleeps 4
  * 1 King Bed + Sleeper Sofa
  * 540 sq. ft.



Located in impeccably restored buildings around the parade ground of Fort Baker, the Historic King Suite with City View blends distinctive character with exceptional amenities — and each suite features breathtaking views of San Francisco Bay and the city skyline.

Averaging 540 square feet in living space, these expansive San Francisco Bay Area hotel accommodations offer a king bed with organic linens, elegant furnishings, vintage architectural details, ceiling fan, and windows that open to bay breezes. An inviting gas fireplace enhances the separate living room, where a queen sleeper sofa can accommodate two additional guests. Outdoors on the first floor, enjoy shared front porches with chairs that invite relaxation.

Shaken or Stirred? Enjoy custom cocktails in the comfort of your room!

  * ## “The Bavarian”

Treat yourself to three (3) ice cold local ales alongside baked pretzels, grain mustard and a bag of salt-roasted pistachios.

  * ## Local Cheese Display

Enjoy a Chef's selection of three (3) local cheeses displayed under glass and served alongside grapes, fig spread, nuts and artisanal crackers. 

  * ## One Dozen Roses

Make your romantic getaway in the San Francisco Bay Area extra special with one dozen roses waiting for you in your room upon arrival.

  * ## Housemade Cookies & Milk

Nothing says home like milk and cookies. Two jugs of chilled milk and a jug of assorted housemade cookies, freshly baked by Cavallo Point’s very own pastry chef, will be delivered to your room upon arrival or at a time of your choosing.

  * ## The Wine Country Experience

Raise a glass to your stay at Cavallo Point! Enjoy a half bottle of red wine and a half bottle of white wine from our award-winning wine cellar, along with an assortment of snacks including local cheese and salami, olives, grapes, berries, dried fruit, fig jam, chocolate and nuts.

  * ## Sparkling Wine & Truffles

A special touch with high impact, sparkling wine and truffles make for a lovely way to start and end a special day.

  * ## Eco Luxe Pups

At Cavallo Point, every member of your family is important to us, including your furry friends. 

  * ## Mini Cupcakes

A small bite of heaven. Delight in a chef's selection of seven (7) mini cupcakes. Cupcakes can be customized for the holidays.
