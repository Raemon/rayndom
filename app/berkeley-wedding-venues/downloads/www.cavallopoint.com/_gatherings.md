## Gather at Cavallo Point

The Perfect Location for Your Unforgettable Event

Set at the foot of the majestic Golden Gate Bridge in historic Fort Baker, Cavallo Point is a natural gathering place for groups large and small.

Our San Francisco Bay Area event venues provide over 25,000 square feet of adaptable meeting and event space at which to celebrate, work, and play. To complement the experience, we offer a fresh and eco-friendly approach to team-building, group activities, and so much more — with endless adventures and famous attractions right at your doorstep.

## Facilities & Features

  * Breathtaking views of San Francisco, the bay & Golden Gate Bridge

  * Diverse venues for breakouts, food & beverage functions & pre-event space

  * An incomparable location: historic Fort Baker, surrounded by Golden Gate National Park lands

  * State-of-the-art technology with high-speed internet, drop-down screens & built-in sound

  * 7 miles from San Francisco; 21 miles from SFO & 27 miles from OAK airports

  * Banquet facilities, customized menus & private dining rooms



  * Launching point for day trips to Napa & Sonoma wine country, plus Monterey & Carmel

  * Understated luxury in 142 diverse Historic & Contemporary guest rooms & suites

  * Exceptional team-building & group activities

  * A stellar wine list, one of California’s largest and most diverse

  * 14,000 sq. ft. indoors; events & sit-down dinners from 10 to 200

  * 12,500+ sq. ft. outdoors; receptions for up to 300
