## FAQ"S

###### COMMON QUESTIONS ANSWERED

**When is the Delta Estate available for me to rent?**  
The Delta Estate is guaranteed to be available for the night of booked your event at Belle Vie Vineyard.

**What if we want more than one night?**  
Additional nights are based on event bookings at Belle Vie Vineyard. If we do not have another event scheduled for the day before or the day after your event, we can offer you those nights at 1/2 price!  
The event calendar will be reviewed 45 days before your event and extra nights may be added then.

**How many guests can we invite?**  
The maximum occupancy for the Delta Estate is 24 guests.

**How many rooms can we/should we reserve?**  
The Delta Estate requires a minimum reservation of 4 rooms. The standard rental will always include the entire “West Wing” and sleeps a maximum of 8 guests.  
Additional rooms in the “East Wing” may be added on to the standard rental. Each additional room can sleep up to 4 guests. NOTE: All guests must have an assigned room. No last-minute stayovers or sleeping in common areas.

**How long is our stay?**  
Check-in time is 12 noon on the day of your event.  
Check-out time is 10am the following day.  
Early check-in or late check out is based on availability and must be approved. Approval may be determined no more than 45 days before your event.

**Will there be a security deposit?**  
Yes. The security deposit will be held (not charged) on your credit card at check-in. Your card will only be charged in the unfortunate event of damage to the Delta Estate. NOTE: The contracted renter of the Delta Estate will be responsible for all damages made by any/all guests.

**IMPORTANT ADDITIONAL INFO & POLICIES**

  * Pets are not permitted in the Delta Estate at this time
  * Children are welcome with adult supervision, but please note: THIS HOUSE IS NOT CHILDPROOF
  * BYO food and beverage – You are welcome to bring your own meals, snacks and beverages.NOTE: All food and beverage brought into the Delta Estate must remain in the Delta Estate. Food and beverage may NOT be transferred to or consumed at Belle Vie Vineyard during your event. NO EXCEPTIONS
