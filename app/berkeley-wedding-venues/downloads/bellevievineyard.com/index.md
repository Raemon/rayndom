## A Private and Secluded Estate The Perfect Place to Host Your Special Event

Situated on the banks of the San Joaquin River, Belle Vie Vineyard is a perfect backdrop for your special event. Our private 37-acre estate located on Sherman Island in California’s historic Sacramento River Delta. We offer complete privacy in both indoor and beautiful outdoor spaces for your gathering. No matter where your imagination takes you, charming and rustic to old-world elegance, we can create your dream at Belle Vie. Wedding, family reunion, company picnic, meetings or charity fundraiser – come celebrate your ‘Beautiful Life’ with us!
