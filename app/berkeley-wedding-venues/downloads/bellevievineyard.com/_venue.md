### The Villa

ACCOMMODATES 50-200

Endless indoor options under one roof with a clean neutral palette to showcase your special vision. Our villa is comprised of 3 distinct spaces, the foyer, the party room and the grand hall. We offer the ability to separate for multiple uses or leave open for one big event space to host your indoor event.

### The Cypress Courtyard & Lawn

ACCOMMODATES 50-250

The courtyard is a unique event space enclosed by towering columns and Cypress trees and surrounded by shady lawns. Options for this space include ceremony, cocktail hour, music and dancing, seated dinners or almost anything you imagine. Specialty lighting can transform this space into something right out of a dream.

### The Pavilion Lawn

ACCOMMODATES 50-300

The largest of our outdoor spaces offers an expansive perfectly manicured lawn, a patio center stage and features a 500 sq ft trellis covered in fragrant jasmine. This space allows large groups to gather under a tent or the open skies for ceremonies, seated dinners, live music and dancing and don’t forget the lawn games. The adjacent vineyard makes the perfect backdrop to an already beautiful outdoor space

### The Pier

ACCOMMODATES 2-50

Take a short walk up the hill to the crown jewel of Belle Vie. The river views from our private 90 ft pier offer breathtaking sunsets over the Antioch Bridge and the stunning backdrop of Mount Diablo. It’s available for small ceremonies, quiet moments together and the must have sunset pictures.

#### The Party Room

ACCOMMODATES 2-60

A mixed use room to host indoor ceremonies, cocktail hours, small seated dinners, buffet service or food stations, all night dance parties and space for the extra fun stuff like a photo booth and lounge

#### The Grand Hall

ACCOMMODATES 2-200

This beautiful dining room boasts marble floors and a clean neutral palette that allows your unique details and personality to shine through. It is large, open, well-lit and can host up to 200 guests for a seated dinner. The design options are limitless and specialty lighting could really make a statement.
