# 4th Quarter Wine Club Release Party – Club Members Only

November 18, 2022  @  5:00 pm  –  8:00 pm 

It’s our pleasure to invite our wine club members back to our first live wine club release party since the start of the pandemic!

This is exclusive for Retzlaff Wine Club Members only – Maximum 2 persons per membership.

Live music, wine, food – FREE to club members

This party will be held outdoors on our beautiful lawn, rain or shine.

## **Please RSVP via email by Tuesday, November 15:**

  * **Include your name, # in your party, and choice of vegetarian/non-vegetarian food preference:**[wineclubs@retzlaffwinery.com](mailto:wineclubs@retzlaffwinery.com)
