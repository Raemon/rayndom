# 3rd Quarter Wine Club Party

September 16, 2023  @  5:00 pm  –  8:00 pm 

Retzlaff wine club members are invited to join us for our harvest-themed club pick-up party!

Live music, wine and harvest foods to put you in the mood for the new season. 

RSVP by Sept 12 with email to: [wineclubs@retzlaffwinery.com](mailto:wineclubs@retzlaffwinery.com). Limit 2 per membership.
