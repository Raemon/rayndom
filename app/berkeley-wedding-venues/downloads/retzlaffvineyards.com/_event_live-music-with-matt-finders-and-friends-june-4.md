# Live Music with Matt Finders and Friends

June 4, 2023  @  12:00 pm  –  4:30 pm 

Join us for a great day with wine tasting and picnics, with live music from Matt Finders and Friends on the lovely Retzlaff Vineyards outdoor grounds. Admission is only with a Wine Tasting or Picnic Table reservation. See our [Visit Us details](https://retzlaffvineyards.com/visit-us/). 

## Matt Finders and Friends

This six-piece band covers a wide range of musical styles, including jazz, blues, funk, and Latin. With Matt Finders on bass, the group includes John Whitney on guitar, Kelly Fasman on drums, John R Burr on keyboards, Noel Catura on sax, and Karen Marguth on vocals. Collectively, this group of musicians has performed with the Tonight Show Band, Etta James, Branford Marsalis, Tower of Power, Benny Goodman, Allison Brown, Maria Muldaur, Natalie Cole, Kenny Loggins, Sting, and many more! Whether it’s a swinging standard, a Bossa Nova, or a funky dance tune, this group has it covered. [Learn More](https://www.jazzlabb.com/matt-finders-and-friends)

The Matt Finders & Friends band

## Reservations Recommended

Please [make your reservation](https://retzlaffvineyards.com/wine-tasting-and-picnicing/) for wine tasting and/or wine picnic tables in advance to guarantee a space for you and your guests. Join the Retzlaff Wine Club and enjoy complimentary picnic space and quarterly tastings! [Learn More](https://retzlaffvineyards.com/wine-club/)

## On-site Food Options

Tasteful Catering and Events is on-site with freshly prepared meal items for purchase.  
A selection of snack items and non-alcoholic beverages are also available for purchase in the Retzlaff Wine Shop, along with our award-winning wines. You may also bring in your own picnic food. _Please, no food during a Cellar Masters wine-tasting session._

No outside alcohol of any kind is allowed.
