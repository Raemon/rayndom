# Quarterly Wine Club Pick-up Party

May 20, 2023  @  5:00 pm  –  8:00 pm 

We look forward to welcoming our Quarterly Wine Club members to our quarterly wine club pick-up party, **May 20th, from 5 – 8 pm.**

Enjoy a springtime tasting of fine Retzlaff wines along with a food paring created by Vicky and Abraham of Tasteful Catering and Events

This release will be held outdoors on our beautiful lawn, rain or shine.

Club Members Only. Maximum 2 persons per membership. Not a member? It’s free to join! [](https://retzlaffvineyards.com/wine-club/)[Click here](https://retzlaffvineyards.com/wine-club/) to learn how it works: <https://retzlaffvineyards.com/wine-club/>

**Please RSVP by May 15, 2023, to[wineclubs@retzlaffwinery.com](mailto:wineclubs@retzlaffwinery.com) Please include your name and number in your party.**
