# Live Music with Matt Finders Jazz Trio

### 

November 11, 2023  @  12:00 pm  –  4:30 pm 

Enjoy live music from the [Matt Finders Jazz Trio](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Matt_Finders) on the lovely Retzlaff Vineyards outdoor picnic grounds. Admission is only with a [Wine Tasting or Picnic Table reservation](https://retzlaffvineyards.com/wine-tasting-and-picnicing/). See our [Visit Us](https://retzlaffvineyards.com/visit-us/) page to learn more.

Note: Live music will be canceled if rain is forecasted.

## The Matt Finders Jazz Trio 

A group of seasoned local musicians skillfully perform the American Songbook as well as more contemporary Latin and blues tunes. The performers change from time to time, but they always provide a great soundtrack to your wine day.   


## Reservations Recommended

Please [make your reservatio](https://retzlaffvineyards.com/wine-tasting-and-picnicing/)[n](https://retzlaffvineyards.com/wine-tasting-and-picnicing/) in advance for wine tasting and/or picnic table space to enjoy wine by the bottle. 

Join the Retzlaff Wine Club and enjoy complimentary picnic space and quarterly tastings! [Learn More](https://retzlaffvineyards.com/wine-club/)

## Food Options

Tasteful Catering and Events is on-site with freshly prepared meal items for purchase.  
A selection of snack items and non-alcoholic beverages are also available for purchase in the Retzlaff Wine Shop, along with our award-winning wines. You may also bring in your own picnic food. _Please, no food during a Cellar Masters wine-tasting session._

No outside alcohol of any kind is allowed.

Wine and live music with the Matt Finders Jazz Trio at Retzlaff Vineyards – a great way to spend a Saturday!
