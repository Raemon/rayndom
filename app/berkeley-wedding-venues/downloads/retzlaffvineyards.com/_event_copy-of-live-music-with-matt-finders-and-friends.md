# Live Music: Marcel Smith with Matt Finders and Friends

### Plus winery food sales with Tasteful Catering & Events

September 4, 2022  @  12:00 pm  –  4:30 pm 

The amazing vocalist, Marcel Smith, is performing live in Livermore for the first time, at Retzlaff Vineyards. Joined by the Matt Finders and Friends band, come out and enjoy one of the most extraordinary singers you’ll ever hear. See video below. 

Enjoy the tunes while wine tasting in the shaded tent or in a wine picnic under a shady tree on the Retzlaff lawn. On-site food options are available from Tasteful Catering and Events. Sorry, no outside food is allowed on days with a caterer on-site. 

Please [make your reservation](https://retzlaffvineyards.com/wine-tasting-and-picnicing/) for wine tasting and/or wine picnic tables in advance.   
Join the Retzlaff Wine Club and enjoy complimentary picnic space and quarterly tastings! [Learn More](https://retzlaffvineyards.com/wine-club/)

Marcel Smith  
Blues, Soul, Gospel

* * *

Bring an appetite and purchase amazing winery food choices from Tasteful Catering and Events. You can even pre-order and pick up tamales via email to Vicky at [Order.tastefulcatering@gmail.com](mailto:Order.tastefulcatering@gmail.com).
