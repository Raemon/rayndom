# Sunday December 19: Steve Fread and the Lovely Lanette, plus Tasteful Catering & Events

December 19, 2021  @  12:00 pm  –  4:30 pm 

Live music all day on the Retzlaff grounds! The smooth tunes today are provided by a Retzlaff favorite, Steve Fread and his lovely wife Lanette. 

Delicious food options are also available for purchase from Tasteful Catering and Events. Tamale Lovers: Pre-order tamales to take home! Email Vicky at [Order.tastefulcatering@gmail.com](mailto:Order.tastefulcatering@gmail.com). Best tamales in the valley, vegetarian and vegan options available.

Please make your [wine picnic table and/or tasting reservations online.](https://retzlaffvineyards.com/wine-tasting-and-picnicing/)
