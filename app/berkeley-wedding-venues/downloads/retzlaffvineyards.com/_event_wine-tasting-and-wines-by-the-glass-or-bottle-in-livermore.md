# Open for Wine Tasting and Wine by the Glass or Bottle

### 

December 10, 2022  @  12:00 pm  –  4:30 pm 

Join us for wine tasting in our covered tasting area, or enjoy a glass or bottle of wine on the grounds. Due to the rainy forecast, we will NOT have live music or an on-site food vendor, but you may bring your own picnic food. 

## Reservations Recommended

Please [make your reservation](https://retzlaffvineyards.com/wine-tasting-and-picnicing/) for wine tasting and/or seatung at picnic tables in advance to guarantee a space for you and your guests. Join the Retzlaff Wine Club and enjoy complimentary picnic space and quarterly tastings! [Learn More](https://retzlaffvineyards.com/wine-club/)
