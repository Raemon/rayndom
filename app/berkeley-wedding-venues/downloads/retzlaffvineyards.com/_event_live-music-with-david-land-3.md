# Live Music with David Land

### 

April 2, 2023  @  12:00 pm  –  4:30 pm 

Join us for wine tasting, or a wine picnic outdoors on the lovely Retzlaff picnic grounds, including live music with David “Ouzo” Land.   


  
Please [make your reservation](https://retzlaffvineyards.com/wine-tasting-and-picnicing/) for wine tasting and/or wine picnic tables in advance. Join the Retzlaff Wine Club and enjoy complimentary picnic space and quarterly tastings! [Learn More](https://retzlaffvineyards.com/wine-club/)

## Food Options

The Retzlaff shop has a selection of cheese and other snack items available for purchase, You May also bring in food for your wine picnic.
