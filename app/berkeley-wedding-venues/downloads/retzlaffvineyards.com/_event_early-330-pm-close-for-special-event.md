# Early 3:30 pm Close for Special Event

July 17, 2022  @  3:30 pm 

This is a rare “Bring your own picnic” day. Retzlaff closes at 3:30 PM on this day for Opera In the Vineyard.   
There will be no live music or caterer on-site this day. Feel free to bring your own picnic food or purchase light snacks from the Retzlaff shop.
