# Yoga in the Vineyard – Live Music, Food, Wine & Yoga

August 24, 2023  @  6:00 pm  –  8:00 pm 

### 

Bring your mat and join us on the grass–There’s sun AND shade– at the beautiful family-owned Retzlaff Vineyards Winery. Instructor Carl Baylor of Dragonfly Yoga teaches an All-Level, outdoor vinyasa flow class on the vineyard lawn.  
Delicious outdoor yoga from 6- 7 PM, followed by wine tasting, food and Live Music with songwriter Jon Williams from Crawdad Republic.

**Cost:** $35 (includes yoga, winetasting of several beautiful wines, and food)  
_Note: all workshops are non-transferable and non-refundable_
