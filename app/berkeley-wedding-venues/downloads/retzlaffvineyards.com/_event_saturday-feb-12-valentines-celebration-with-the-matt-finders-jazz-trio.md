# Valentine’s weekend celebration with The Matt Finders Jazz Trio

### Plus winery food sales with Tasteful Catering & Events

February 13, 2022  @  12:00 pm  –  4:30 pm 

Valentine’s weekend fun continues at Retzlaff. Enjoy wine, music, and shopping from the many Makers Movement artisans on-site. Plus, each reservation will be entered to win a prize from Retzlaff Vineyards. Please make your wine tasting and/or wine picnic table [reservations in advance](https://retzlaffvineyards.com/wine-tasting-and-picnicing/).

Sunday, the Matt Finders Jazz Trio performs favorite Jazz tunes from **noon – 2:30.**

Bring an appetite and purchase amazing winery food choices from Tasteful Catering and Events. You can even pre-order and pick up tamales via email to Vicky at [Order.tastefulcatering@gmail.com](mailto:Order.tastefulcatering@gmail.com). 

## The Matt Finders Jazz Trio

Matt Finders Jazz Trio

The Matt Finders Trio is a group of seasoned local musicians skillfully performing the American Songbook as well as more contemporary Latin and blues tunes. The group consists of Matt Finders on bass, John R Burr on keyboards, and Kelly Fasman on drums. The group has been performing together for nearly ten years. As a trombonist, Matt was a member of The Tonight Show With Jay Leno Band for 17 years. John R Burr toured for many years with Maria Muldaur and bluegrass legend, Allison Brown. Kelly Fasman worked with Kenny Loggins and Peter Cetera. Come out and enjoy! 

Food for purchase onsite from Tasteful Catering & Events

Tasteful Catering and Events will be serving a great selection of wine-friendly food, including vegan options. You can also pre-order and pick up their famous tamales via email to Vicky at [Order.tastefulcatering@gmail.com](mailto:Order.tastefulcatering@gmail.com).
