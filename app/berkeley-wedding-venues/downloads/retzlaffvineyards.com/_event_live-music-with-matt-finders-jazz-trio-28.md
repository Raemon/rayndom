# Live Music with Matt Finders Jazz Trio

### 

November 27, 2022  @  12:00 pm  –  4:30 pm 

Come out for wine and live music with the [Matt Finders Jazz Trio](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Matt_Finders). Enjoy wine tasting, or a wine picnic outdoors on the lovely Retzlaff Vineyards picnic grounds, with live music from Matt and a variety of musicians setting the mood. Plus enjoy winery food options from Tasteful Catering and Events. 

## The Matt Finders Jazz Trio 

is a rotating group of seasoned local musicians skillfully performing the American Songbook as well as more contemporary Latin and blues tunes. The performers change from time to time, but always provide a great backdrop to your wine day.   


## Reservations Recommended

Please [make your reservatio](https://retzlaffvineyards.com/wine-tasting-and-picnicing/)[n](https://retzlaffvineyards.com/wine-tasting-and-picnicing/) for wine tasting and/or wine picnic tables in advance to guarantee a space for you and your guests. Join the Retzlaff Wine Club and enjoy complimentary picnic space and quarterly tastings! [Learn More](https://retzlaffvineyards.com/wine-club/)

## On-site Food Sales

Bring an appetite and purchase excellent winery food choices from [Tasteful Catering and Events](https://tastefulcateringandevents.com/). Sorry, but no outside food is permitted when we have a caterer on-site. No outside alcohol of any kind is allowed.

Wine and live music with the Matt Finders Jazz Trio at Retzlaff Vineyards – a great way to spend a Sunday!
