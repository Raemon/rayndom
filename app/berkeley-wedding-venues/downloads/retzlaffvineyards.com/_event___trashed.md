# Barrel Tasting Weekend Day 1: Wine, Music & Food

### 

March 18, 2023  @  12:00 pm  –  4:30 pm 

It’s Barrel Tasting Weekend at Retzlaff Vineyards! A festive weekend of wine, live music, great food, and a fun mix of Makers Movement vendors both Saturday and Sunday on the friendly Retzlaff lawn.

## Wine Tasting, Barrel Tasting &/or Picnic with bottle purchase

### Enjoy your visit 3 ways:

  * **Barrel Tasting:** sample young wines from the barrel and then from the bottle to see how our wines progress during aging. $25 per person — no reservation required.
  * **Traditional & Premium wine-tasting flight**: sample a flight of popular Retzlaff wines. Starting at $30 per person, [reservations are recommended](https://retzlaffvineyards.com/wine-tasting-and-picnicing/) as space is limited.
  * **Bottle Picnic:** Enjoy wines by the bottle at a reserved table on the lawn: enjoy the music and shop between sips. [Reservations are recommended](https://retzlaffvineyards.com/wine-tasting-and-picnicing/) as space is limited.



We will also have amazing live music and fresh food options for purchase from Tasteful Catering and Events.

## Saturday: The Matt Finders Jazz Trio 

This rotating group of seasoned local musicians skillfully perform the American Songbook as well as more contemporary Latin and blues tunes. The performers change from time to time, but they always provide a great soundtrack to your wine day. 

## Makers Movement Vendors Saturday and Sunday

One-of-a-kind accessories, clothing, and crafts

## Onsite Caterer this Weekend

Enjoy delicious lunch and snack options from Tasteful Catering and events.

## Wine Tasting & Picnic Reservations Recommended

Please [make your reservatio](https://retzlaffvineyards.com/wine-tasting-and-picnicing/)[n](https://retzlaffvineyards.com/wine-tasting-and-picnicing/) in advance for wine tasting and/or picnic table space to enjoy wine by the bottle. Join the Retzlaff Wine Club and enjoy complimentary picnic space and quarterly tastings! [Learn More](https://retzlaffvineyards.com/wine-club/)
