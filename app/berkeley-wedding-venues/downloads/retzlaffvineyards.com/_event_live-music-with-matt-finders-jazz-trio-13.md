# Live Music with Matt Finders Jazz Trio / Early Close @ 2:30 for Special Event

### 

July 23, 2022  @  12:00 pm  –  2:30 pm 

Retzlaff is open half-day for outdoor wine tasting and wine picnics, with live music from the Matt Finders Jazz Trio. Plus enjoy winery food options from Tasteful Catering and Events. 

Please [make your reservation](https://retzlaffvineyards.com/wine-tasting-and-picnicing/) for wine tasting and/or wine picnic tables in advance. Join the Retzlaff Wine Club and enjoy complimentary picnic space and quarterly tastings! [Learn More](https://retzlaffvineyards.com/wine-club/)

The Matt Finders Trio is a group of seasoned local musicians skillfully performing the American Songbook as well as more contemporary Latin and blues tunes. The group (Matt Finders, John R Burr, and Kelly Fasman) has been performing together for nearly ten years. Individually they have performed and toured with The Tonight Show With Jay Leno Band, Maria Muldaur, Allison Brown, Kenny Loggins and Peter Cetera. 

Bring an appetite and purchase amazing winery food choices from Tasteful Catering and Events. You can even pre-order tamales for pickup: email Vicky at [Order.tastefulcatering@gmail.com](mailto:Order.tastefulcatering@gmail.com). Sorry, but no outside food is permitted when we have a caterer on-site.
