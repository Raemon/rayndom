# Live Music with Matt Finders and Friends

March 27, 2022  @  12:00 pm  –  4:30 pm 

Join us for wine tasting, or a wine picnic, and kick up your heels to the live music from Matt Finders and Friends. Plus enjoy winery food options from Tasteful Catering and Events. 

Please [make your reservation](https://retzlaffvineyards.com/wine-tasting-and-picnicing/) for wine tasting and/or wine picnic tables in advance.   
Join the Retzlaff Wine Club and enjoy complimentary picnic space and quarterly tastings! [Learn More](https://retzlaffvineyards.com/wine-club/)

Matt Finders and Friends band

Matt Finders and Friends is a six-piece band that covers a wide array of musical styles, including jazz, blues, funk, and Latin. With Matt Finders on bass, the group includes John Whitney on guitar, Kelly Fasman on drums, John R Burr on keyboards, Noel Catura on sax, and Karen Marguth, vocals. Collectively, this group of local musicians has performed with the Tonight Show Band, Etta James, Branford Marsalis, Tower of Power, Benny Goodman, Allison Brown, Maria Muldaur, Natalie Cole, Kenny Loggins, Sting, and many more! Whether it’s a swinging standard, a Bossa Nova, or a funky dance tune, this group has it covered. [Learn More](https://www.jazzlabb.com/matt-finders-and-friends)
