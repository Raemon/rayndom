# Live Music with Steve Fread

### Plus winery food sales with Tasteful Catering & Events

February 20, 2022  @  12:00 pm  –  4:30 pm 

Please make your wine tasting and/or wine picnic table [reservations in advance](https://retzlaffvineyards.com/wine-tasting-and-picnicing/) for this date.

Live music is provided by a Retzlaff favorite, Steve Fread, featuring upbeat popular hits from all decades and genres. Delicious food options are also available for purchase from Tasteful Catering and Events. 

Bring an appetite and purchase amazing winery food choices from Tasteful Catering and Events. You can even pre-order tamales for pickup: email Vicky at [Order.tastefulcatering@gmail.com](mailto:Order.tastefulcatering@gmail.com). 

Steve Fread

Food for purchase from Tasteful Catering & Events
