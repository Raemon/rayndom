# Easter Day Picnic

April 17, 2022  @  12:00 pm  –  4:00 pm 

SOLD OUT! We hope you enjoy your holiday!

The annual Easter Day Picnic at Retzlaff returns Sunday, April 17th! Enjoy an Easter Sunday with family and friends (and wine) listening to the incomparable Jackson Rohm and his percussion-playing friend Biff.

Admission is ONLY with a[ pre-reserved picnic table](https://book.peek.com/s/b48b4333-f3c4-4a1b-9416-b602fb4277a8/b0ZmA) with seating for up to six adults and kids.  
Kids welcome!  
Grounds open at 11:30 and close at 4:00 pm.   
_Bring your own picnic food._ There will **not** be an on-site caterer this day.  
Minimum wine purchase of 3 bottles per table.

**Pricing:**   
_Regular admission_ : $75 per table with 6 seats for adults and kids.   
_Retzlaff Wine Club members_ : $50 per table with 6 seats for adults and kids. [Learn more about the Wine Club](https://retzlaffvineyards.com/wine-club/) before making your reservation. 

[Reserve your table](https://book.peek.com/s/b48b4333-f3c4-4a1b-9416-b602fb4277a8/b0ZmA) soon, as this event always sells out fast. [Book Now](https://book.peek.com/s/b48b4333-)

Jackson Rohm
