# Live Music with The Breedloves

### 

April 30, 2023  @  12:00 pm  –  4:30 pm 

Enjoy live music from the [Breedloves](https://the-breedloves.com/) on the lovely Retzlaff Vineyards outdoor grounds. Admission is only with a Wine Tasting or Picnic Table reservation (included perks for our wine club members). See our [Visit Us details](https://retzlaffvineyards.com/visit-us/). 

## The Breedloves

[Guitar artists, Jay Kirkland and Barbara Gorin](https://the-breedloves.com/) are the Breedloves Acoustic Dynamic Duo, whose romantic, original acoustic music and innovative covers shine with their musical chemistry and passion for all things guitar. 

The Breedloves: Jay Kirkland and Barbara Gorin

  


## Reservations Recommended

Please [make your reservatio](https://retzlaffvineyards.com/wine-tasting-and-picnicing/)[n](https://retzlaffvineyards.com/wine-tasting-and-picnicing/) in advance for wine tasting and/or picnic table space to enjoy wine by the bottle. Join the Retzlaff Wine Club and enjoy complimentary picnic space and quarterly tastings! [Learn More](https://retzlaffvineyards.com/wine-club/)

## Food Options

A selection of snack items and non-alcoholic beverages are available for purchase in the Retzlaff Wine Shop, along with our award-winning wines. No outside alcohol of any kind is allowed.

Wine and live music with Lisa Long at Retzlaff Vineyards – a great way to spend a Sunday afternoon!
