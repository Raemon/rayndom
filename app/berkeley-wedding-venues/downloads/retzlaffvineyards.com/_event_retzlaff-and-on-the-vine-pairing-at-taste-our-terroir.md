# Retzlaff and On The Vine Pairing at “Taste Our Terroir”

October 19, 2023  @  4:30 pm  –  9:00 pm 

Retzlaff Vineyards and On The Vine are paired up to compete with several other teams at _2023 Taste our Terroir_ , Livermore Valley’s premier food and wine affair on Thursday, October 19th. 

Held at the Bella Rosa Event Center at Garré Vineyard and Winery, this annual Food and Wine pairing competition features 12 wineries and local eateries presenting food and wine pairings to delight the senses, with a dessert bar featuring additional wineries. Part of the 4-day _Taste: The Livermore Valley Wine Experience._

##### 

Tickets and details are at the [Livermore Valley Wine Community](https://www.lvwine.org/event/9823/taste-the-livermore-valley-wine-experience) website.
