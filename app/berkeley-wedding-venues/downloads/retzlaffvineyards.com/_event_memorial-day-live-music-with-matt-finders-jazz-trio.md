# Memorial Day: Live Music with Matt Finders And Friends!

May 30, 2022  @  12:00 pm  –  4:30 pm 

### No caterer this day, but the Retzlaff Shop will have snack items for sale, and guests may bring their own food.

Join us for wine tasting, or a wine picnic outdoors on the lovely Retzlaff picnic grounds, with live music from the fabulous Matt Finders & Friends band. 

Please [make your reservation](https://retzlaffvineyards.com/wine-tasting-and-picnicing/) for wine tasting and/or wine picnic tables in advance. Join the Retzlaff Wine Club and enjoy complimentary picnic space and quarterly tastings! [Learn More](https://retzlaffvineyards.com/wine-club/)

The Retzlaff shop has snack items available for sale. Guests may also bring outside food and needed utensils. Please dispose of all trash when finished. 

Matt Finders Jazz Trio

Matt Finders and Friends is a six-piece band that covers jazz, blues, funk, and Latin. With Matt Finders on bass, the group includes John Whitney on guitar, Kelly Fasman on drums, John R Burr on keyboards, Noel Catura on sax, and Karen Marguth on vocals. Collectively, this group of local musicians has performed with the Tonight Show Band, Etta James, Branford Marsalis, Tower of Power, Benny Goodman, Allison Brown, Maria Muldaur, Natalie Cole, Kenny Loggins, Sting, and many more! Whether it’s a swinging standard, a Bossa Nova, or a funky dance tune, this group has it covered.
