# Live Music with Patrick Noel & The Lucky Sevens

### Plus winery food sales with Tasteful Catering & Events

July 10, 2022  @  12:00 pm  –  4:30 pm 

Join us for wine tasting, or a wine picnic outdoors on the lovely Retzlaff picnic grounds, with live music from the Patrick Noel & Lucky Sevens band. Plus enjoy winery food options from Tasteful Catering and Events. 

Straight from the Livermore Valley, the Patrick Noel & The Lucky Sevens band performs a variety of Americana and original tunes. Patrick Noel Russ writes original songs to honor the Livermore valley, and the band plays a variety of Americana tunes from artists such as Wilco, Dwight Yokam, Los Lobos, NRBQ, Antsy McClain, Johnny Cash, John Hiatt, The Mavericks, Willie Nelson, Emmylou Harris, John Prine, The Cure, and last but not least, Elvis. Find more info at [www.theluckysevens.com](http://www.theluckysevens.com/), or view and listen to Patrick’s original music [here](https://patricknoel.bandcamp.com/album/my-hometown).

Please [make your reservation](https://retzlaffvineyards.com/wine-tasting-and-picnicing/) for wine tasting and/or wine picnic tables in advance. Join the Retzlaff Wine Club and enjoy complimentary picnic space and quarterly tastings! [Learn More](https://retzlaffvineyards.com/wine-club/)

Bring an appetite and purchase amazing winery food choices from Tasteful Catering and Events. You can even pre-order tamales for pickup: email Vicky at [Order.tastefulcatering@gmail.com](mailto:Order.tastefulcatering@gmail.com). 

Sorry, but no outside food is permitted when we have a caterer on-site.
