# Live Music with The 256 Band

### 

March 4, 2023  @  12:00 pm  –  4:30 pm 

Come out for wine and live music with the 256 Band. Enjoy wine tasting, or a wine by the bottle at a picnic table outdoors on the lovely Retzlaff Vineyards picnic grounds. 

## Reservations Recommended

Please [make your reservatio](https://retzlaffvineyards.com/wine-tasting-and-picnicing/)[n](https://retzlaffvineyards.com/wine-tasting-and-picnicing/) in advance for wine tasting and/or picnic table space to enjoy wine by the bottle. Join the Retzlaff Wine Club and enjoy complimentary picnic space and quarterly tastings! [Learn More](https://retzlaffvineyards.com/wine-club/)

## Food Options

A selection of snack items and non-alcoholic beverages are available for purchase in the Retzlaff Wine Shop, along with our award winning wines. No outside alcohol of any kind is allowed.

Wine and live music at Retzlaff Vineyards – a great way to spend a Saturday!

## The 256 Band

The 256 band is a fun local band, playing a great mix of Rock, Country, Blues, Reggae, and Funk favorites with a song list that will get you dancing and grooving.
