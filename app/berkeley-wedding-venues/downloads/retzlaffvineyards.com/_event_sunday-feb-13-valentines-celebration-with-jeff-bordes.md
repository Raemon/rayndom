# Valentine’s weekend celebration with Jeff Bordes & The Burnt Ends band

### Plus winery food sales with Tasteful Catering & Events

February 12, 2022  @  12:00 pm  –  4:30 pm 

Celebrate love this Valentine’s weekend at Retzlaff. Enjoy Wine, music, and shop the many independent Makers Movement artisans who will be on-site. Plus, each reservation is entered to win a prize from Retzlaff Vineyards. Please make your wine tasting and/or wine picnic table [reservations in advance](https://retzlaffvineyards.com/wine-tasting-and-picnicing/).

Saturday, The Sexy Trumpet returns! Jeff Bordes & the Burnt Ends band are back to perform popular jazz favorites throughout the day. 

Bring an appetite and purchase amazing winery food choices from Tasteful Catering and Events. You can even pre-order and pick up tamales via email to Vicky at [Order.tastefulcatering@gmail.com](mailto:Order.tastefulcatering@gmail.com). 

## Jeff Bordes and the Burnt Ends

Jeff Bordes

Jeff Bordes is known the world over and has performed and recorded with esteemed artists such as Jon Faddis, Branford Marsalis, Alex Acuna, Giovanni Hidalgo, Horacio “El Negro” Hernandez, Richie Cole, David Garibaldi, Tom Petty, Steve Earle, Stevie Nicks, Sheryl Crow, Karl Denson, Sugarhill Gang, Dana Carvey and most recently, John Mellencamp. [jeffbordes.com](http://www.jeffbordes.com/)

Food for purchase onsite from Tasteful Catering & Events

Tasteful Catering and Events will be serving a great selection of wine-friendly food, including vegan options. You can also pre-order and pick up their famous tamales via email to Vicky at [Order.tastefulcatering@gmail.com](mailto:Order.tastefulcatering@gmail.com).
