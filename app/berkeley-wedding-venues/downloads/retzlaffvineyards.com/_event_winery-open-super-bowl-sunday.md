# Winery Open Super Bowl Sunday

February 12, 2023  @  12:00 pm  –  4:30 pm 

Join us at the winery for your Super Bowl pre-game. [Reserve your spot](https://retzlaffvineyards.com/wine-tasting-and-picnicing/) to enjoy a bottle on the lawn, or enjoy a wine-tasting session.

No live music. No caterer this day, but feel free to bring your own snacks or lunch. The Retzlaff shop also has a selection of packaged snack items available for sale.
