# Yoga in the Vineyard

August 18, 2022  @  6:00 pm  –  8:00 pm 

**Last one of the season!** Bring your mat and join us on the grass–There’s sun AND shade– at the beautiful family-owned Retzlaff Vineyards Winery. Yoga instructor Suzanna Spring teaches an All-Level, outdoor vinyasa flow class on the vineyard lawn, with live music by Phil Didlake.  
  
Yoga is from 6:00 – 7PM, followed by wine tasting and food.  
Savor the organic vineyards, the trees, the hills, and an endless blue sky! It’s summer at the beautiful family-owned Retzlaff Vineyards Winery.  
  
**Cost:** $35 (includes yoga, wine tasting of several beautiful wines, and food).  
**Monthly/Yearly Members receive 10% off.**__

_Note: all workshops are non-transferable and non-refundable_
