# Father’s Day Picnic & Live Music with LunaFish

June 18, 2023  @  11:30 am  –  4:00 pm 

A day for the Dads full of family, friends, wine, and the [LunaFish](https://www.lunafishband.com/) band! 

Admission is ONLY with a[ ](https://book.peek.com/s/b48b4333-f3c4-4a1b-9416-b602fb4277a8/b0ZmA)pre-reserved picnic table for seating up to six adults and kids.   
Grounds open at 11:30 am and close at 4:00 pm. 

**Pricing:**   
_Regular admission_ : **$75 per table** with 6 seats for adults and kids.   
**_Retzlaff Wine Club members_ : $60 per table** with 6 seats for adults and kids. Only 1 discounted table per club membership. [Learn more about the Wine Club](https://retzlaffvineyards.com/wine-club/) before making your reservation.  
[Book your table here](https://book.peek.com/s/b48b4333-f3c4-4a1b-9416-b602fb4277a8/lwW62) or at <https://book.peek.com/s/b48b4333-f3c4-4a1b-9416-b602fb4277a8/lwW62>

This event always sells out fast – Sorry, tickets are non-refundable.

_**Bring your own picnic food.**_ There will **not** be an on-site caterer this day.   
**Minimum wine purchase of 3 bottles per table.** Outside alcohol of any kind is not permitted.

[ Purchase Tables in Advance ](https://book.peek.com/s/b48b4333-f3c4-4a1b-9416-b602fb4277a8/lwW62)
