# Winery Open New Year’s Eve

December 31, 2022  @  12:00 pm  –  3:00 pm 

Join us at the winery for some New Year’s cheer! [Reserve your spot](https://retzlaffvineyards.com/wine-tasting-and-picnicing/) to enjoy a bottle on the lawn, or enjoy a wine-tasting session.

Special holiday hours are 12-3 pm.

No caterer this day, so feel free to bring your own snacks or lunch. The Retzlaff shop also has a selection of packaged snacks and non-alcoholic beverages available for purchase.
