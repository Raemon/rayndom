# Wednesday – Livermore Valley Winery of the Week

July 12, 2023  @  5:00 pm  –  8:00 pm 

Retzlaff is the Livermore Valley Winery of the week! Join us Wednesday, July 12th from 5-8 pm for a mid-week wine down. Enjoy wines by the bottle or glass, live music with the Matt Finders Jazz Trio, and watch the setting sun on the lovely Retzlaff grounds. Plus, Tasteful Catering and Events will be on-site with yummy food for purchase. 

Reservations NOT required, come on by!
