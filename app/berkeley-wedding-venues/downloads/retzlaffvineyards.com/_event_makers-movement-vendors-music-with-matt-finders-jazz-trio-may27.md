# Makers Movement Market and Live Music

### 

May 27, 2023  @  12:00 pm  –  4:30 pm 

Sip and shop our Makers Movement Market vendors, while enjoying wine and live music with the [Matt Finders Jazz Trio](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Matt_Finders). Reserve a wine tasting, or book a picnic table to enjoy wines by the bottle on the lovely Retzlaff Vineyards picnic grounds. 

## The Matt Finders Jazz Trio 

This group of seasoned local musicians skillfully perform the American Songbook as well as more contemporary Latin and blues tunes. A great soundtrack to your wine day.   


## Reservations Recommended

Please [make your reservatio](https://retzlaffvineyards.com/wine-tasting-and-picnicing/)[n](https://retzlaffvineyards.com/wine-tasting-and-picnicing/) in advance for wine tasting and/or picnic table space to enjoy wine by the bottle. Join the Retzlaff Wine Club and enjoy complimentary picnic space and quarterly tastings! [Learn More](https://retzlaffvineyards.com/wine-club/)

## Food Options

Tasteful Catering and Events will be onsite offering lunch and charcuterie items for sale. A selection of snack items and non-alcoholic beverages are available for purchase in the Retzlaff Wine Shop, along with our award-winning wines. No outside alcohol of any kind is allowed.

Shopping, wine and live music with the Matt Finders Jazz Trio at Retzlaff Vineyards – a great way to spend your Saturday!
