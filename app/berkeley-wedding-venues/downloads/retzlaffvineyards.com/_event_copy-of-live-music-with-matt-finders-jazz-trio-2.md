# Live Music with Matt Finders Jazz Trio

### Plus winery food sales with Tasteful Catering & Events

October 22, 2022  @  12:00 pm  –  4:30 pm 

Join us for wine tasting, or a wine picnic outdoors on the lovely Retzlaff Vineyards picnic grounds, with live music from the Matt Finders Jazz Trio. Plus enjoy winery food options from Tasteful Catering and Events. 

## The Matt Finders Jazz Trio 

is a group of seasoned local musicians skillfully performing the American Songbook as well as more contemporary Latin and blues tunes.   


## Reservations Recommended

Please [make your reservation](https://retzlaffvineyards.com/wine-tasting-and-picnicing/) for wine tasting and/or wine picnic tables in advance to guarantee a space for you and your guests. Join the Retzlaff Wine Club and enjoy complimentary picnic space and quarterly tastings! [Learn More](https://retzlaffvineyards.com/wine-club/)

## On-site Food Sales

Bring an appetite and purchase excellent winery food choices from Tasteful Catering and Events. Sorry, but no outside food is permitted when we have a caterer on-site. No outside alcohol of any kind is allowed.

Wine Tasting and picnics with live jazz music at Retzlaff Vineyards – a great way to spend a Saturday!

Matt Finders Jazz Trio

Food for purchase from Tasteful Catering & Events
