# Annual Futures Club Release Celebration – Day 3

February 11, 2023  @  12:00 pm  –  4:30 pm 

It’s that time of year! At this year’s annual celebration members of the Retzlaff Vineyard’s Wine Futures Club can choose a date (February 4th, 5th, or 11th) to sample our 2022 wines from the barrel and our 2021 bottled wines. Enjoy a delightful food pairing, live music, and Salome’s Chocolate Chili Espresso Spice Cake with our famous Port. 

This exclusive event is limited to Futures Club members and 1 guest, with a maximum of 25 attendees per scheduled session.   
[Reservations Required.](https://retzlaffvineyards.com/wine-tasting-and-picnicing/) See your club email for detailed reservation instructions.
