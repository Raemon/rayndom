# Live Music with Steve Fread & the Lovely Lanette

### 

May 28, 2023  @  12:00 pm  –  4:30 pm 

Enjoy live music from Steve & Lanette Fread on the lovely Retzlaff Vineyards outdoor picnic grounds. Admission is only with a Wine Tasting or Picnic Table reservation (included perks for our wine club members). See our [Visit Us details](https://retzlaffvineyards.com/visit-us/). 

## Steve Fread

A popular Retzlaff performer, Steve Fread plays classic rock and popular hits from all decades and genres, a great soundtrack to a relaxing day in the vineyard. 

## Reservations Recommended

Book a wine-tasting flight or book a picnic to enjoy wines by-the-bottle on the lovely Retzlaff grounds. Please make your tasting and/or wine picnic table [reservations in advance](https://retzlaffvineyards.com/wine-tasting-and-picnicing/) for this date. Join the Retzlaff Wine Club and enjoy complimentary picnic space and quarterly tastings! [Learn More](https://retzlaffvineyards.com/wine-club/)

## Food Options

No caterer this day, but you may bring in your own picnic and supplies. Also, the Retzlaff shop has a small selection of packaged snacks, cheeses, and non-alcoholic beverages for sale, along with our wonderful wines. No outside alcohol of any kind is allowed.
