# SOLD OUT: Mothers Day Picnic

May 8, 2022  @  12:00 pm  –  4:00 pm 

The annual Mothers Day Picnic at Retzlaff is sold out for Sunday, May 8th!   
Those who have a reservation will enjoy an afternoon with Mom, family, and friends (and wine) listening to the[ John Brothers Piano Co](https://www.johnbrothers.net/), featuring Tyler Miller from the original Tumbledown House! Kids welcome!  
Admission is ONLY with a[ ](https://book.peek.com/s/b48b4333-f3c4-4a1b-9416-b602fb4277a8/b0ZmA)pre-reserved picnic table for seating up to six adults and kids.   
Grounds open at 11:30 am and close at 4:00 pm.   


_**Bring your own picnic food.**_ There will **not** be an on-site caterer this day.  
Minimum wine purchase of 3 bottles per table. Outside alcohol of any kind is **_not_** permitted.
