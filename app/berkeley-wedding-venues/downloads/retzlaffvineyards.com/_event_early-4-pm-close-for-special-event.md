# Early 4 pm Close for Special Event

July 9, 2022  @  4:00 pm 

This is a rare “Bring your own picnic” day.   
Retzlaff closes at 4 PM on this day for a private evening event. There will be no live music or caterer on-site this day. Feel free to bring your own picnic food or purchase light snacks in the Retzlaff shop.
