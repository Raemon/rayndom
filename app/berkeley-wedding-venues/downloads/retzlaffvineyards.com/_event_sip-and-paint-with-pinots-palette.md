# Sip and Paint with Pinot’s Palette

July 14, 2022  @  6:00 pm  –  8:00 pm 

Join us on the lawn area at Retzlaff for a fun evening of sipping and painting!  
A Pinot’s Palette local artist will walk you through the step-by-step painting of Sweet Succulents on a 16×20 canvas. Feel free to bring a picnic to enjoy with award-winning Retzlaff wines made with organic grapes. Evenings in the summer don’t get any sweeter than this! Wine is not included and is available for purchase. Doors open at 5:30. Attendees must be 21 and over for this event. **The painting class will begin promptly at 6:00 PM.**

The more the merrier, invite your friends!  


**Cost:** $49 in advance.   
**Tickets and more info[at Pinot’s Palette](https://www.pinotspalette.com/livermore/event/594043) website: <https://www.pinotspalette.com/livermore/event/594043>**

A sweet arrangement of succulents can enhance and compliment  
any rustic home decor setting. Best of all you don’t have to water these!
