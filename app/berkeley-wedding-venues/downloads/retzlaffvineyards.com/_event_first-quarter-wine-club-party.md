# Valentine’s Sweetheart Quarterly Wine Club Pick-up Party

February 18, 2023  @  5:00 pm  –  8:00 pm 

We look forward to welcoming our Quarterly Wine Club members to the first quarter wine club pick-up on February 18, from 5 – 8 pm.

Enjoy a sweetheart tasting of fine Retzlaff wines paired with delicious sweets exquisitely created by Passion Pastry.

Rain or shine.

This release will be held outdoors on our beautiful lawn.

Please RSVP by February 14, 2023

Club Members Only

Maximum 2 persons per membership

[RSVP / Your name and # in your party.](mailto:wineclubs@retzlaffwinery.com)

Not a member? It’s free to join! [ ](https://retzlaffvineyards.com/wine-club/)[Click here](https://retzlaffvineyards.com/wine-club/) to learn how it works: <https://retzlaffvineyards.com/wine-club/>
