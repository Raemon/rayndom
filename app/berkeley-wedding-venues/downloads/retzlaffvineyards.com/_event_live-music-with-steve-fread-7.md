# Live Music with Steve Fread

### 

December 18, 2022  @  12:00 pm  –  4:30 pm 

Join us for wine tasting or a picnic on the lovely Retzlaff grounds with great live music from Steve Fread. A Retzlaff favorite, Steve Fread plays upbeat popular hits from all decades and genres, a great soundtrack to a relaxing day in the vineyard. 

## Reservations Recommended

Please make your wine-tasting and/or wine picnic table [reservations in advance](https://retzlaffvineyards.com/wine-tasting-and-picnicing/) for this date. Join the Retzlaff Wine Club and enjoy complimentary picnic space and quarterly tastings! [Learn More](https://retzlaffvineyards.com/wine-club/)

## On-site Food Sales

Bring an appetite and purchase amazing winery food choices from Tasteful Catering and Events. You can even pre-order tamales for pickup: email Vicky at [Order.tastefulcatering@gmail.com](mailto:Order.tastefulcatering@gmail.com). Sorry, no outside food is allowed when we have a caterer on-site. 

No outside alcohol of any kind is allowed.

Steve Fread
