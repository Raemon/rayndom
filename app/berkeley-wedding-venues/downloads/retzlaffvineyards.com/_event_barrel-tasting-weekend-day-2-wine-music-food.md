# Barrel Tasting Weekend Day 2: Wine & Vendors

### 

March 19, 2023  @  12:00 pm  –  4:30 pm 

It’s day 2 of Barrel Tasting Weekend at Retzlaff Vineyards. Explore wines in the barrel or sample our bottled wines. Shop among a mix of Makers Movement vendors on the friendly Retzlaff lawn.

## Wine Tasting, Barrel Tasting &/or Picnic with bottle purchase

### Enjoy your visit 3 ways:

  * **Barrel Tasting:** sample young wines from the barrel and then from the bottle to see how our wines progress during aging. $25 per person — no reservation required.
  * **Traditional & Premium wine-tasting flight**: sample a flight of popular Retzlaff wines. Starting at $30 per person, [reservations are recommended](https://retzlaffvineyards.com/wine-tasting-and-picnicing/) as space is limited.
  * **Bottle Picnic:** Enjoy wines by the bottle at a reserved table on the lawn: enjoy the music and shop between sips. [Reservations are recommended](https://retzlaffvineyards.com/wine-tasting-and-picnicing/) as space is limited.



We will also have amazing live music and fresh food options for purchase from Tasteful Catering and Events.
