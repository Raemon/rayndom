# Live Music with Steve Fread

### 

November 5, 2023  @  12:00 pm  –  4:30 pm 

Enjoy live music from Steve & Lanette Fread on the lovely Retzlaff Vineyards outdoor picnic grounds. Admission is only with a [Wine Tasting or Picnic Table reservation](https://retzlaffvineyards.com/wine-tasting-and-picnicing/). See our [Visit Us](https://retzlaffvineyards.com/visit-us/) page to learn more.

## Steve Fread

A popular Retzlaff performer, Steve Fread plays classic rock and popular hits from all decades and genres, accompanied by his lovely wife Lanette. Together, they provide a great soundtrack to a relaxing day in the vineyard. 

## Reservations Recommended in Advance

Book a wine-tasting flight or book a picnic to enjoy wines by the bottle on the lovely Retzlaff grounds. Please make your tasting and/or wine picnic table [reservations in advance](https://retzlaffvineyards.com/wine-tasting-and-picnicing/). 

Join the Retzlaff Wine Club and enjoy complimentary picnic space and quarterly tastings! [Learn more](https://retzlaffvineyards.com/wine-club/). 

## Food Options

A selection of snack items and non-alcoholic beverages are available for purchase in the Retzlaff Wine Shop, along with our award-winning wines. You may also bring in your own picnic food. _Please, no food during a Cellar Masters wine-tasting session._

Outside alcohol of any kind is not permitted.
