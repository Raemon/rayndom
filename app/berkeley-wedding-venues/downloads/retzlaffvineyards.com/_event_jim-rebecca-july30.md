# Live Music with The Jim & Rebecca Acoustic Duet

### 

July 30, 2023  @  12:00 pm  –  4:30 pm 

Enjoy live music from Jim and Rebecca King on the lovely Retzlaff Vineyards outdoor picnic grounds. Admission is only with a [Wine Tasting or Picnic Table reservation](https://retzlaffvineyards.com/wine-tasting-and-picnicing/). See our [Visit Us](https://retzlaffvineyards.com/visit-us/) page to learn more.

## The Jim and Rebecca Acoustic Duet

These singer-songwriters perform seven decades of chart-topping popular songs and original music, featuring lush acoustic guitar and vocal harmonies.

## Reservations Recommended in Advance

Book a wine-tasting flight or book a picnic to enjoy wines by-the-bottle on the lovely Retzlaff grounds. Please make your tasting and/or wine picnic table [reservations in advance](https://retzlaffvineyards.com/wine-tasting-and-picnicing/). 

Join the Retzlaff Wine Club and enjoy complimentary picnic space and quarterly tastings! [Learn more](https://retzlaffvineyards.com/wine-club/). 

## Food Options

Tasteful Catering and Events is on-site with freshly prepared meal items for purchase.  
A selection of snack items and non-alcoholic beverages are also available for purchase in the Retzlaff Wine Shop, along with our award-winning wines. You may also bring in your own picnic food. _Please, no food during a Cellar Masters wine-tasting session._

No outside alcohol of any kind is allowed.
