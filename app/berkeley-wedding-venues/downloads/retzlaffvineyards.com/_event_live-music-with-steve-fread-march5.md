# Live Music with Steve Fread

### 

March 5, 2023  @  12:00 pm  –  4:30 pm 

Join us for Live Music with Steve & Lanette Fread at Retzlaff Vineyards. Enjoy a tasting flight or purchase a bottle to enjoy at a table on the lovely Retzlaff grounds. Steve & Lanette play classic rock and popular hits from all decades and genres. A great soundtrack to a relaxing day in the vineyard.

## Reservations Recommended

Please make your wine-tasting and/or wine picnic table [reservations in advance](https://retzlaffvineyards.com/wine-tasting-and-picnicing/) for this date. Join the Retzlaff Wine Club and enjoy complimentary picnic space and quarterly tastings! [Learn More](https://retzlaffvineyards.com/wine-club/)

## Food Options

No caterer this day, but you may bring in your own picnic and supplies. Also, the Retzlaff shop has a small selection of packaged snacks, cheeses, and non-alcoholic beverages for sale, along with our wonderful wines. No outside alcohol of any kind is allowed.

No outside alcohol of any kind is allowed.

Steve Fread
