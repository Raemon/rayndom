# Live Music with The Breedloves

### 

July 9, 2023  @  12:00 pm  –  4:30 pm 

Enjoy live music from the [Breedloves](https://the-breedloves.com/) on the lovely Retzlaff Vineyards outdoor grounds. Admission is only with a Wine Tasting or Picnic Table reservation. See our [Visit Us](https://retzlaffvineyards.com/visit-us/) page for details. 

## The Breedloves

[Guitar artists, Jay Kirkland and Barbara Gorin](https://the-breedloves.com/) are the Breedloves Acoustic Dynamic Duo, whose romantic, original acoustic music and innovative covers shine with their musical chemistry and passion for all things guitar. 

The Breedloves: Jay Kirkland and Barbara Gorin

  


## Reservations Recommended in Advance

Please [make your reservatio](https://retzlaffvineyards.com/wine-tasting-and-picnicing/)[n](https://retzlaffvineyards.com/wine-tasting-and-picnicing/) in advance for wine tasting and/or picnic table space to enjoy wine by the bottle. We know things happen spontaneously, so we will try to accommodate small groups if space is available. 

Join the Retzlaff Wine Club and enjoy complimentary picnic space and quarterly tastings! [Learn More](https://retzlaffvineyards.com/wine-club/)

## Food Options

Tasteful Catering and Events is on-site with freshly prepared meal items for purchase.  
A selection of snack items and non-alcoholic beverages are also available for purchase in the Retzlaff Wine Shop, along with our award-winning wines. You may also bring in your own picnic food. _Please, no food during a Cellar Masters wine-tasting session._ No outside alcohol of any kind is allowed.

Food for purchase from Tasteful Catering & Events

Wine and live music with The Breedloves at Retzlaff Vineyards – a great way to spend a Sunday afternoon!
