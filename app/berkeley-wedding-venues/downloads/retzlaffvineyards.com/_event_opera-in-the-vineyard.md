# Opera in the Vineyard

July 17, 2022  @  5:00 pm  –  8:00 pm 

Opera In the Vineyard returns to Retzlaff Vineyards. Presented by Livermore Valley Opera, this is a wonderful evening of live music outdoors, surrounded by vineyards at our historic Livermore homestead. Limited Seating. You may buy complete tables or individual tickets. No outside wine allowed.

Hear wonderful music performed by:

  * Alex Boyer – tenor
  * Liisa Dávila – soprano
  * Kathleen Moss – mezzo soprano
  * Kirk Eichelberger – bass
  * Varvara Milinder – pianist



They will perform arias and ensembles from Opera and Broadway. You may also sign up to purchase an “Aria a la Carte” to be sung at your table.

**Important notice:** To sit with friends, make advance arrangements to purchase seats together at a particular available table. Carpooling recommended.

$50 of each ticket is a tax-deductible donation.

Details and tickets available at the [Livermore Valley Opera website](https://www.livermorevalleyopera.com/event/opera-in-the-vineyard-july-2022/)
