# Holidays in the Vineyards Vendor Fair, Plus Live Music with The Jim and Rebecca Acoustic Duet

### 

December 4, 2022  @  12:00 pm  –  4:30 pm 

Day 2 of this weekend’s event!

Retzlaff celebrates Holidays in the Vineyards with a vendor fair of over 15 vendors especially curated for the holidays by the Makers Movement. Plus, enjoy live music, wine tasting or wine-by-the-bottle sales, and food for purchase while you shop and get ready for the holidays. Join us for a Livermore Valley Wine Country holiday tradition!

## Live Music with Jim and Rebecca Acoustic Duet

These singer-songwriters perform seven decades of chart-topping popular songs and original music, featuring lush acoustic guitar and vocal harmonies.

## Reservations Recommended

Please [make your reservation](https://retzlaffvineyards.com/wine-tasting-and-picnicing/) for wine tasting and/or wine picnic tables in advance to ensure space for you and your group. Join the Retzlaff Wine Club and enjoy complimentary picnic space and quarterly tastings! [Learn More](https://retzlaffvineyards.com/wine-club/)

## On-site Food Sales

Bring an appetite and purchase excellent winery food choices from Tasteful Catering and Events. You can even pre-order tamales for pickup: email Vicky at [Order.tastefulcatering@gmail.com](mailto:Order.tastefulcatering@gmail.com). 

Sorry, but no outside food is permitted when we have a caterer on-site. Outside alcohol is never allowed.
