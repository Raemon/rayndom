# Live Music with The Jim and Rebecca Acoustic Duet

### 

August 14, 2022  @  12:00 pm  –  4:30 pm 

Join us for wine tasting, or a wine picnic outdoors on the lovely Retzlaff picnic grounds, with live music from The Jim and Rebecca Acoustic Duet. Plus enjoy winery food options from Tasteful Catering and Events. 

These singer song/writers perform seven decades of chart-topping popular songs and original music, featuring lush acoustic guitar and vocal harmonies.

Please [make your reservation](https://retzlaffvineyards.com/wine-tasting-and-picnicing/) for wine tasting and/or wine picnic tables in advance. Join the Retzlaff Wine Club and enjoy complimentary picnic space and quarterly tastings! [Learn More](https://retzlaffvineyards.com/wine-club/)

Bring an appetite and purchase excellent winery food choices from Tasteful Catering and Events. You can even pre-order tamales for pickup: email Vicky at [Order.tastefulcatering@gmail.com](mailto:Order.tastefulcatering@gmail.com). 

Sorry, but no outside food is permitted when we have a caterer on-site.

Jim and Rebecca King

Food for purchase from Tasteful Catering & Events
