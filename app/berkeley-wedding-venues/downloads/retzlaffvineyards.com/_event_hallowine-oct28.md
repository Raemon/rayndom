# Hallowine @ Retzlaff: Music, Shopping, and Me & My Pup Costume Contest

### 

October 28, 2023  @  12:00 pm  –  4:30 pm 

Bring your dogs and friends to celebrate Halloween at Retzlaff with wine, music, shopping and a “Me and My Pup” costume contest. All on the lovely Retzlaff Vineyards outdoor picnic grounds. Admission is only with a [Wine Tasting or Picnic Table reservation](https://retzlaffvineyards.com/wine-tasting-and-picnicing/). See our [Visit Us](https://retzlaffvineyards.com/visit-us/) page to learn more.

## Me and My Pup Costume Contest

Retzlaff is always a dog-friendly place. Come dressed with your 4 legged best friend in a matching costume, and you might win a fun prize. The contest will be awarded at 2 p.m.

## Fall Makers Market

Sip and shop our amazing vendors of exquisite hand-crafted items.

## The Matt Finders Jazz Trio 

This group of seasoned local musicians brings the best in contemporary Jazz, Latin, and Blues. Always a great soundtrack to your wine day.   


## Reservations Recommended

Please [make your reservatio](https://retzlaffvineyards.com/wine-tasting-and-picnicing/)[n](https://retzlaffvineyards.com/wine-tasting-and-picnicing/) in advance for wine tasting and/or picnic table space to enjoy wine by the bottle. 

Join the Retzlaff Wine Club and enjoy complimentary picnic space and quarterly tastings! [Learn More](https://retzlaffvineyards.com/wine-club/)

## Food Options

Tasteful Catering and Events is on-site with freshly prepared meal items for purchase.  
A selection of snack items and non-alcoholic beverages are also available for purchase in the Retzlaff Wine Shop, along with our award-winning wines. You may also bring in your own picnic food. _Please, no food during a Cellar Masters wine-tasting session._

No outside alcohol of any kind is allowed.
