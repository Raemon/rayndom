# Barrel Tasting Weekend & Live Music with Matt Finders Jazz Trio

### Plus wine tasting, picnics, unique Makers Market vendors and food sales all weekend. 

March 19, 2022  @  12:00 pm  –  4:30 pm 

It’s a festive Barrel Tasting weekend with live music, Makers Market vendors, great food, and fabulous wine at Retzlaff Vineyards! 

The hand-curated market for our Retzlaff Barrel Tasting weekend 3/19 + 3/20 features a vibrant mix of local artisans offering small-batch wellness + self-care products, Eco-conscious candles, unique jewelry, home decor textiles, woodwork, pet-centric offerings, quilting, fashion, and macrame.

**Three ways to visit to join the fun:**

  1. Taste samples of Retzlaff wines that are still in the barrel as part of Barrel Tasting weekend, sponsored by the Livermore Valley Wine Growers Association. [Get Barrel Tasting Tickets here](https://www.lvwine.org/event/8288/barrel-tasting-weekend) at the [lvwine.org website](https://www.lvwine.org/event/8288/barrel-tasting-weekend). 
  2. Reserve a full flight Retzlaff wine tasting: [reservations limited – book in advance](https://retzlaffvineyards.com/wine-tasting-and-picnicing/)
  3. Reserve a Retzlaff wine picnic spot to relax on the lawn and enjoy the festivities: [reservations limited – book in advance](https://retzlaffvineyards.com/wine-tasting-and-picnicing/)



This will be a fun and busy weekend. Make your tasting &/or picnic table [reservations in advance](https://retzlaffvineyards.com/wine-tasting-and-picnicing/).   
Join the Retzlaff Wine Club and enjoy complimentary picnic space and quarterly tastings! [Learn More](https://retzlaffvineyards.com/wine-club/)

**Saturday: The Matt Finders Trio** is a group of seasoned local musicians skillfully performing the American Songbook as well as more contemporary Latin and blues tunes. The group consists of Matt Finders on bass, John R Burr on keyboards, and Kelly Fasman on drums. The group has been performing together for nearly ten years. As a trombonist, Matt was a member of The Tonight Show With Jay Leno Band for 17 years. John R Burr toured for many years with Maria Muldaur and bluegrass legend, Allison Brown. Kelly Fasman worked with Kenny Loggins and Peter Cetera. Come out and enjoy!

Bring an appetite and purchase amazing winery food choices from Tasteful Catering and Events. You can even pre-order tamales for pickup: email Vicky at [Order.tastefulcatering@gmail.com](mailto:Order.tastefulcatering@gmail.com).
