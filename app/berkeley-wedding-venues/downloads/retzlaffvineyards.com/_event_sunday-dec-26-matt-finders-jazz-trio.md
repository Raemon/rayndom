# Sunday, Dec 26: Wine Tasting indoors; no live music or food vendor due to rain.

December 26, 2021  @  12:00 pm  –  4:30 pm 

Come out and enjoy the day after Christmas with wine tasting indoors in the tank room. Please make your wine tasting and/or wine picnic table [reservations in advance.](https://retzlaffvineyards.com/wine-tasting-and-picnicing/)

Sorry, due to rain, there will not be live music nor an on-site food vendor this day. Light snacks are available for purchase in the Retzlaff shop.
