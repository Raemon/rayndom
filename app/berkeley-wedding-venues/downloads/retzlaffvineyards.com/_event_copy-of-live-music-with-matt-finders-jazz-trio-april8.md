# Live Music with Matt Finders Jazz Trio

### 

April 8, 2023  @  12:00 pm  –  4:30 pm 

Come out for wine and live music with the [Matt Finders Jazz Trio](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Matt_Finders). Enjoy wine tasting, or a wines by the bottle at a picnic table outdoors on the lovely Retzlaff Vineyards picnic grounds. 

## The Matt Finders Jazz Trio 

is a rotating group of seasoned local musicians skillfully performing the American Songbook as well as more contemporary Latin and blues tunes. The performers change from time to time, but they always provide a great soundtrack to your wine day.   


## Reservations Recommended

Please [make your reservatio](https://retzlaffvineyards.com/wine-tasting-and-picnicing/)[n](https://retzlaffvineyards.com/wine-tasting-and-picnicing/) in advance for wine tasting and/or picnic table space to enjoy wine by the bottle. Join the Retzlaff Wine Club and enjoy complimentary picnic space and quarterly tastings! [Learn More](https://retzlaffvineyards.com/wine-club/)

## Food Options

A selection of snack items and non-alcoholic beverages are available for purchase in the Retzlaff Wine Shop, along with our award-winning wines. No outside alcohol of any kind is allowed.

Wine and live music with the Matt Finders Jazz Trio at Retzlaff Vineyards – a great way to spend a Saturday!
