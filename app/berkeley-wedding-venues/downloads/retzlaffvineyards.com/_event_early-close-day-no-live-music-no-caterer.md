# EARLY CLOSE Day: No live music, no caterer

### 

June 4, 2022  @  12:00 pm  –  2:30 pm 

Retzlaff is closing early this day for a private wedding. We will be open for Wine Tasting and Wine Picnics until 2:30.   
Please [make your reservation](https://retzlaffvineyards.com/wine-tasting-and-picnicing/) in advance. Join the Retzlaff Wine Club and enjoy complimentary picnic space and quarterly tastings! [Learn More](https://retzlaffvineyards.com/wine-club/)

The Retzlaff shop will have snack items available for sale. Guests may also bring outside food and needed utensils. Please dispose of all trash when finished.
