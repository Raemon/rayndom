# Live Music with The LK Project

### 

May 21, 2023  @  12:00 pm  –  4:30 pm 

Come out for wine and live music with [The LK Project](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Matt_Finders) on the lovely Retzlaff Vineyards outdoor picnic grounds. Admission is only with a Wine Tasting or Picnic Table reservation (included perks for our wine club members). See our [Visit Us details](https://retzlaffvineyards.com/visit-us/). 

## The LK Project

[The LK Project](https://sites.google.com/view/thelkproject/) is a singer/songwriter trio featuring guitarists Mike Lickiss and Chris Carter, and bassist Ken Cuozzo. They play an acoustic-based musical blend with strong vocal harmonies and heartfelt lyrics. In addition to their own original compositions, their performances feature a wide variety of popular cover tunes from the past five decades.  


The LK Project

## Reservations Recommended

Please [make your reservatio](https://retzlaffvineyards.com/wine-tasting-and-picnicing/)[n](https://retzlaffvineyards.com/wine-tasting-and-picnicing/) in advance for wine tasting and/or picnic table space to enjoy wine by the bottle. Join the Retzlaff Wine Club and enjoy complimentary picnic space and quarterly tastings! [Learn More](https://retzlaffvineyards.com/wine-club/)

## Food Options

A selection of snack items and non-alcoholic beverages are available for purchase in the Retzlaff Wine Shop, along with our award-winning wines. No outside alcohol of any kind is allowed.

Wine and live music with The LK Project at Retzlaff Vineyards – a great way to spend a Saturday!
