# 5th Annual Wine & Wags: WINE, FOOD, Music, VENDORS

### Food sales with Tasteful Catering & Events

April 30, 2022  @  12:00 pm  –  4:30 pm 

**Bring your dogs and enjoy Wine & Wags at Retzlaff Vineyards. This fun day includes: **

  * Parent & Pooch matching costume competition, such as: Sonny & Cher, Thing 1 & Thing 2, you get the idea!
  * The Makers Movement with met-related products
  * Live music with the Matt Finders Jazz Trio
  * Food for purchase from Tasteful Events and Catering



Plus, The [German Shepherd Rescue of Northern California](https://www.gsrnc.org/) (GSRNR) will be on-site. Who knows, you may find your next bundle of love!

**Three ways to visit us this day:**

  1. Book a full flight 1-hour wine tasting at Retzlaff: [reservations limited – book in advance](https://retzlaffvineyards.com/wine-tasting-and-picnicing/)
  2. Reserve a 2-hour wine picnic spot to relax on the Retzlaff lawn while you enjoy the festivities: [reservations limited – book in advance](https://retzlaffvineyards.com/wine-tasting-and-picnicing/)
  3. OR purchase a [Multi-Winery Wine & Wags](https://www.lvwine.org/event/8363/wine-and-wags-2022) ticket for entrance to any of the 25+ participating wineries, including Retzlaff. Get [Multi-Winery Tickets here](https://www.lvwine.org/event/8363/wine-and-wags-2022) at the [lvwine.org](http://lvwine.org) website.



Please [make your reservation](https://retzlaffvineyards.com/wine-tasting-and-picnicing/) for wine tasting and/or wine picnic tables in advance. Join the Retzlaff Wine Club and enjoy complimentary picnic space and quarterly tastings! [Learn More](https://retzlaffvineyards.com/wine-club/)

## Live Music with the Matt Finders Trio

The Matt Finders Trio is a group of seasoned local musicians skillfully performing the American Songbook as well as more contemporary Latin and blues tunes. The group consists of Matt Finders on bass, John R Burr on keyboards, and Kelly Fasman on drums. The group has been performing together for nearly ten years. As a trombonist, Matt was a member of The Tonight Show With Jay Leno Band for 17 years. John R Burr toured for many years with Maria Muldaur and bluegrass legend, Allison Brown. Kelly Fasman worked with Kenny Loggins and Peter Cetera. Come out and enjoy!
