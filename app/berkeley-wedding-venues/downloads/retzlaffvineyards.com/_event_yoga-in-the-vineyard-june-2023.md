# Yoga in the Vineyard – Live Music, Food, Wine & Yoga

June 22, 2023  @  6:00 pm  –  8:00 pm 

### Summer Solstice fun! 

Bring your mat and join us on the grass for yoga, music, food, and wine at Retzlaff Vineyards. There’s sun AND shade at the beautiful family-owned Retzlaff Vineyards Winery.   
Yoga instructor Carl Baylor teaches an All-Level, outdoor vinyasa flow class on the vineyard lawn.  
Outdoor yoga from 6 – 7 pm, followed by wine tasting and food 7 -8 pm.

Savor the vineyards, the hills, and an endless blue sky! It’s summer.

**Cost:** $35 (includes yoga, wine tasting of several beautiful wines, and food).  
_Note: all workshops are non-transferable and non-refundable_
