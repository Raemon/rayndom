# Live Music with Matt Finders and Friends

### Plus winery food sales with Tasteful Catering & Events

October 16, 2022  @  12:00 pm  –  4:30 pm 

Join us for a great day of wine tasting or picnicking, and enjoy free live music from Matt Finders and Friends.

## Matt Finders and Friends

This six-piece band covers a wide array of musical styles, including jazz, blues, funk, and Latin. The group includes Matt Finders on bass, John Whitney on guitar, Kelly Fasman on drums, John R Burr on keyboards, Noel Catura on sax, and Karen Marguth on vocals. Collectively, this group of musicians has performed with the Tonight Show Band, Etta James, Branford Marsalis, Tower of Power, Benny Goodman, Allison Brown, Maria Muldaur, Natalie Cole, Kenny Loggins, Sting, and many more! Whether it’s a swinging standard, a Bossa Nova, or a funky dance tune, this group has it covered. [Learn More](https://www.jazzlabb.com/matt-finders-and-friends)
