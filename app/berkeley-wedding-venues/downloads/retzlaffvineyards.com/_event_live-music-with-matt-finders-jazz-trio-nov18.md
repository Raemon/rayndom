# Live Music with Matt Finders Jazz Trio

### 

November 18, 2023  @  12:00 pm  –  4:30 pm 

Enjoy wine tasting and live music from the [Matt Finders Jazz Trio](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Matt_Finders) inside the Retzlaff Vineyards Tank Room. Or, enjoy a wine picnic under the big tent. Admission is only with a [Wine Tasting or Picnic Table reservation](https://retzlaffvineyards.com/wine-tasting-and-picnicing/). See our [Visit Us](https://retzlaffvineyards.com/visit-us/) page to learn more.

No food vendor today – Bring your own picnic or purchase snacks from our shop.

## Toys for Tots

Retzlaff Vineyards is a proud participant in this year’s Toys for Tots drive. Bring a smile to a child while you enjoy some holiday cheer at the winery! December 3 is the last day to drop off unwrapped toys and teen gifts in our Toys for Tots bin. 

## The Matt Finders Jazz Trio 

A group of seasoned local musicians skillfully perform the American Songbook as well as more contemporary Latin and blues tunes. The performers change from time to time, but they always provide a great soundtrack to your wine day. _Note: Music will be canceled if rain is forecasted._  


## Reservations Recommended

Please [make your reservatio](https://retzlaffvineyards.com/wine-tasting-and-picnicing/)[n](https://retzlaffvineyards.com/wine-tasting-and-picnicing/) in advance for wine tasting and/or picnic table space to enjoy wine by the bottle. 

Join the Retzlaff Wine Club and enjoy complimentary picnic space and quarterly tastings! [Learn More](https://retzlaffvineyards.com/wine-club/)

## Food Options

A selection of snack items and non-alcoholic beverages are available for purchase in the Retzlaff Wine Shop, along with our award-winning wines. You may also bring in your own picnic food. _Please, no food during a Cellar Masters wine-tasting session._

No outside alcohol of any kind is allowed.

Wine and live music with the Matt Finders Jazz Trio at Retzlaff Vineyards – a great way to spend a Saturday!
