# Weddings

![Weddings at the Exploratorium](/sites/default/files/styles/responsive_200w/public/2023-03/visit_rentals_weddings_marquee.jpg.webp?itok=FGZx1Rvn)

Weddings

Celebrate your wedding in a unique venue with exciting exhibits and stunning views.

## Wedding Packages

We're pleased to offer a wedding package that features our best galleries for wedding ceremonies and receptions.

Our [East](/rentals/east-gallery "Moore East Gallery") and [Fisher Bay Observatory Gallery & Terrace](/rentals/bay-observatory-gallery-terrace "Fisher Bay Observatory Gallery & Terrace") bundle is the perfect setting for an unforgettable wedding. The [Fisher Bay Observatory Gallery & Terrace](/rentals/bay-observatory-gallery-terrace "Fisher Bay Observatory Gallery & Terrace") features sweeping views of the city and Bay, and an attached outdoor terrace provides a fantastic backdrop for ceremonies or receptions. The [East Gallery](/rentals/east-gallery "Moore East Gallery") offers its own stunning views and houses our exciting life-sciences exhibit collection, which highlights the beauty of living things.

## Facility Rental Options

At Pier 15, the museum is divided into galleries filled with exhibits ranging from mechanical contraptions to artworks highlighting the beauty of the physical phenomena that surround us. Galleries are available to rent either individually or in combination.
