# Studio for Public Spaces

![Studio for Public Spaces](/sites/default/files/styles/responsive_200w/public/2023-03/Screen%20Shot%202023-03-28%20at%204.40.34%20PM.png.webp?itok=EEergA3h)

Studio for Public Spaces

The Studio for Public Spaces works in the public realm to create exhibits and environments that encourage play, exploration, creativity, and social connection.
