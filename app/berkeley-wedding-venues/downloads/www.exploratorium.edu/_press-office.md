# Press Office

![Press Office](/sites/default/files/styles/responsive_200w/public/2023-03/press-office_marquee.jpg.webp?itok=jXEM6GwN)

Press Office

The Exploratorium celebrates the role a free press plays in cultivating an informed, curious, and confident society. Our press office exists to help working journalists find stories worth telling, access materials and experts, and to support you in sharing our work with the world. 

For any queries or requests, contact [media@exploratorium.edu](mailto:media@exploratorium.edu).

## About the Exploratorium

* * *

Since 1969, the Exploratorium has connected the public with scientists, artists, teachers, policy-makers, doers, thinkers, and makers to create extraordinary learning experiences that upend perceptions and cultivate curiosity. We’re committed to **inspiring visitors, empowering educators** , and **fueling the global movement** of discovery and science-awareness.
