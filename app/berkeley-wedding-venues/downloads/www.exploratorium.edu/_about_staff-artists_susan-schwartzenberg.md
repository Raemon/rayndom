# Susan Schwartzenberg: Staff Artists

![A surreal and abstract image showing multiple reflections of a person within distorted, spherical shapes.](/sites/default/files/styles/scale_image_500x600/public/susan_schwartzenberg960x1272_0_0.jpg.webp?itok=EWL_EIO3)

Susan Schwartzenberg

Senior Artist and Curator

![A surreal and abstract image showing multiple reflections of a person within distorted, spherical shapes.](/sites/default/files/styles/scale_image_500x600/public/susan_schwartzenberg960x1272_0_0.jpg.webp?itok=EWL_EIO3)

Susan Schwartzenberg is a senior artist at the Exploratorium, where she leads the development of the [Fisher Bay Observatory Gallery](/visit/gallery-6). She has been a curator, photographer, designer, and artist, and served as director of media for the museum. She has participated in many exhibit development and Web-based projects. Susan was a Loeb Fellow at the Harvard Graduate School of Design, and has taught at the San Francisco Art Institute, the California College of Art, and Stanford University. As a photographer and visual artist, she has received numerous awards, and has taken part in residencies and exhibitions worldwide. She is known for her public art, including recent works at Stanford University and San Francisco’s McLaren Park.
