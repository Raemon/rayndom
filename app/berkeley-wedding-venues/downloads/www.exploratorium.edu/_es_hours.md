##### Horario exclusivo para miembros

###### Every Sunday, 10:00 am–Noon 

All year long we set aside Sunday mornings for Daytime members, donors, and Corporate members—10:00 a.m. to noon is just for you. Join us for a leisurely visit, enjoy lunch at the Tinkering Table Restaurant, and then head out for your next adventure.
