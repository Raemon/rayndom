## Your Gift Has a Positive Impact

[ ![Jim Simons stands in front of a blackboard filled with mathematical equations and diagrams, gesturing as he teaches.](/sites/default/files/styles/responsive_3_2_350w/public/2024-06/Jim-Simmons_spotlight.jpg.webp?h=9e499333&itok=k509j_oL) ](https://www.exploratorium.edu/press-office/press-releases/remembering-jim-simons)

#### Exploratorium Remembers Jim Simons

Philanthropist’s legacy will continue to inspire and support scientists for generations.

[Learn More](/press-office/press-releases/remembering-jim-simons "Remembering Jim Simons")

[ ![Professional Development Programs](/sites/default/files/styles/responsive_3_2_350w/public/2023-08/professional-dev.jpg.webp?h=a6967b5f&itok=0ukG1WNJ) ](/education/impact)

#### We're Changing the Way Science Is Taught 

The Exploratorium is a professional home and hub for PreK–12 teachers and a local laboratory for global science learning. We’ve trained thousands of teachers and leaders to make science come alive for millions of students. 

[Teaching & Learning Impact](/education/impact "Teachers Matter")

[ ![High School Explainer dissecting a cows eye in front of a group of fascinated kids](/sites/default/files/styles/responsive_3_2_350w/public/2023-01/Explainer_CowEye_DSC_4993_AS-2000x1000.jpg.webp?h=c9a3a702&itok=aIC0sj2f) ](/about/explainers)

#### Investing in Bay Area Teens

Since 1969, the High School Explainer program has welcomed more than 3,500 students to explore STEM fields while gaining career and life skills. During our closure, we're reimagining the program for future generations of Explainers.

[Explainer Programs](/about/explainers "Explainer Programs")

[ ![Global Collaborations: Why We Do It](/sites/default/files/styles/responsive_3_2_350w/public/2023-11/global-impact.jpg.webp?h=9e499333&itok=lw4u37D6) ](/global)

#### Building the Global Community

From San Francisco to Ramallah, and from NASA to the Boys & Girls Club, we collaborate with people and organizations to create joyful learning and build a strong global community of learners and educators.

[Global Collaborations](/global "Global Collaborations")

[ ![Field trips banner](/sites/default/files/styles/responsive_3_2_350w/public/2023-01/Fieldtrips_DSC_4956.jpg.webp?h=8554f2f5&itok=jKGfmmjh) ](/visit/field-trips)

#### Welcoming All Young Scientists to Pier 15

Every kid deserves an “aha!” moment here. About 62,000 students, chaperones, and teachers from Title I schools receive free field trip admission each year.

[School Field Trips](/visit/field-trips "School Field Trips")

[ ![A group of people walking outside a large, colorful commercial building.](/sites/default/files/styles/responsive_3_2_350w/public/press/960x380-MiddleGround2.jpg.webp?h=c515b0a0&itok=rDD5pBd-) ](/middleground)

#### Shedding Light on Human Behavior

Pull up a chair and dive into Middle Ground, our free offsite exhibition that’s bringing strangers together in San Francisco’s Civic Center. Interactive experiences will get you thinking about bias and stereotyping, humor and generosity, how we work together, and how we pull apart into tribes.

[Discover Middle Ground](/middleground "Middle Ground")

![Bubble](/sites/default/files/styles/kdcl_hero_mobile_1x/public/hero/2023-08/bb83e6ee-80c8-4a58-8242-532114b606a5.jpeg.webp?h=a3bf7d6d&itok=DuYZsT6Q)
