# Musical Bench

Exhibit

![people sitting on a bench](/sites/default/files/2023-03/musical-bench-pod.jpeg)

Musical Bench

Location

[Gallery 5](/galleries/gallery-5)

Musical Bench

Musical Bench plays intriguing tones when users complete an electrical circuit by holding hands. Created in collaboration with our Tinkering Studio.

* * *

This exhibit is part of the collection:

[Living Innovation Zone: Pause on Market Street](/publicspaces/projects/liz "Pause: a Living Innovation Zone \(LIZ\) on Market Street")
