##### Public Transit

###### By Muni

  * F Market streetcars stop in front of the museum
  * Nearby Muni lines: J, K, L, M, N, T + buses 1, 2, 12, 14, 38



###### By BART

  * Get off at Embarcadero Station
  * Walk 10‚Äì15 mins along the waterfront to Pier 15



###### By Ferry

  * From Oakland, Alameda, Vallejo, Larkspur, Sausalito, Tiburon, or South SF
  * Arrives at Ferry Building ‚Äî 10 min walk



###### From Transbay Terminal

  * Served by AC Transit, SamTrans, and Golden Gate Transit
  * Walk approx. 20 mins or take Muni or rideshare to Pier 15



###### From Caltrain

  * Exit at Fourth & King
  * Take Muni Metro N or T to Embarcadero Station
  * Walk 10‚Äì15 mins to the museum



###### From Sacramento Area

Take Capitol Corridor to: 

  * Richmond ‚Üí BART to Embarcadero
  * Emeryville ‚Üí Bus to Financial District (10‚Äì15 min walk)
  * Jack London Square ‚Üí Ferry to Ferry Building



##### By Bike

Biking to the museum is easy! The Embarcadero features a protected bike path that runs right past the Exploratorium.

We offer ample public bike racks on site, plus two Public Bike Work Stations for quick repairs:

  * South apron of Pier 15
  * In front of Pier 17



Bring your bike and enjoy a safe, scenic ride along the waterfront!
