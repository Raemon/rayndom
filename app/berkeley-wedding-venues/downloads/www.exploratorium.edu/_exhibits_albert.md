Albert

A mechanical object may seem alive if it responds to you.

_Albert_ was part of _Cybernetic Serendipity,_ a groundbreaking and much acclaimed exhibition. It was created in 1967 and the exhibition opened at the Exploratorium in 1969, the first year the museum was open.
