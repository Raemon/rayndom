Matching Gifts

You can greatly **enhance your contribution** in support of the Exploratorium's mission by leveraging your company's matching gift program.

## Maximize Your Impact with a Matching Gift!

Many companies encourage their employees' charitable activities by matching—and sometimes exceeding—their donations to chosen charities and nonprofits. This simple and effective approach can **significantly amplify your contribution** to institutions you care about, such as the Exploratorium!

#### Your generosity enables us to: 

  * **Offer free field trips** for the Bay Area’s under-resourced Title I schools and vouchers for every Title I student to bring their families back for a free return visit
  * **Empower our High School Explainers** —a diverse group of passionate young learners aged 15–21—who inspire visitors, lead demonstrations, and manage museum operations while developing valuable skills and fostering a love for discovery
  * **Provide K–12 educators with professional development** opportunities through hands-on, inquiry-based learning, collaborative support networks, and strategies to create inclusive and equitable science classrooms
  * And so much more! Explore our [2024 Impact Report](/impact-report-24 "Impact Report FY24").
