# Gabriela Linares Sosa: Osher Fellows

![Gabriela Linares Sosa](/sites/default/files/styles/scale_image_500x600/public/2023-06/sosa.jpg.webp?itok=bmUefY-p)

Gabriela Linares Sosa

Osher Fellow • May 2023

![Gabriela Linares Sosa](/sites/default/files/styles/scale_image_500x600/public/2023-06/sosa.jpg.webp?itok=bmUefY-p)

Gabriela is Zapotec from Oaxaca, a biologist by profession, with a master’s degree in Agroecosystem Productivity. Since 2007, she has worked for a non-profit organization, focusing on Indigenous rights, including issues of self-determination and land rights.
