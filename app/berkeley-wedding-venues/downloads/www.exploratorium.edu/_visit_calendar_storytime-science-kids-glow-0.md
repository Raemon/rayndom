Storytime Science for Kids: Glow

###### Limited capacity and recommended for families with kids ages 4 to 8.

Join us for Storytime Science and enjoy an engaging storybook read-aloud followed by a related activity geared toward young children (and their grown-ups). Come hear a story, get hands-on, and learn something new with the Exploratorium’s own Vivian Altmann!
