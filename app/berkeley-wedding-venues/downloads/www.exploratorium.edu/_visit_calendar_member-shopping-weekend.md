# Member Shopping Weekend

![a wrapped gift in a spectrum of colored light](/sites/default/files/styles/responsive_200w/public/2023-12/rainbow-present_daytime-membership_marquee.jpg.webp?itok=mKjg6ZFc)

Date

Past

  * Thu, Dec 4 \- Sun, Dec 7 2025 • All day




Member Shopping Weekend

Have fun with the whole family and get all of your holiday shopping done at the Exploratorium! Enjoy double discounts at our stores for one weekend only, with 20% off all products*. We’ll take care of all your shopping needs, from helping you pick out the perfect gift for even the hardest people to shop for, to providing free gift bags.

_*Discount does not apply to sale merchandise._

### Don’t miss _Glow_!

Recharge your winter wonder with this beloved annual expansion of our exhibits on light. Then get your holiday shopping done (with free gift bags and shopping-bag check) while supporting science education.

[ ![Silhouetted figure with raised arms against a vibrant, colorful backdrop.](/sites/default/files/styles/responsive_16_9_350w/public/2025-08/FY25_GLOW_web-750x422-v2.jpg.webp?h=a6967b5f&itok=_7l2hXFE)GlowThu, Nov 20 2025 \- Sun, Jan 25 2026 • All day Play in the light. Discover radiant artworks that invite you to play and dream in their glow.](/visit/calendar/glow)

### Enjoy these holiday benefits.

Free Gift Bags for Exploratorium Purchases

Enjoy free gift bags and tissue paper with purchase while supplies last.

Free Shopping-Bag Check

Leave your purchases in our Main Store for free! Get all your shopping done and then enjoy the Exploratorium without carrying any extra bags.

Toys for Tots Donation Station

Drop off a new toy for someone in need in our Toys for Tots barrel.

Free Reusable Shopping Tote

This weekend only, get a free reusable tote with any onsite purchase of $25 or more. This take-anywhere bag folds into its own pouch.

### Double your discount online too.

Can’t make it to Pier 15? Be sure to check your email inbox for an exclusive promo code to get your 20% discount when purchasing retail products online. 

Valid December 4–7, 2025.
