# Press Video

Press Video

The Exploratorium is home to an immense media archive. 

**To request video, please follow these steps.**

  1. Email: [media@exploratorium.edu](mailto:media@exploratorium.edu).
  2. Include the name of your broadcast station and preferred format.
  3. If you would like video to be sent to you overnight, include your overnight service account number as well as your address and phone.
  4. We will reply by email and provide video promptly if your request meets our definition of working media.



**Note:** We make video available ONLY to members of the media working on stories about the Exploratorium. To discuss use rights for any other purpose, please contact [permissions@exploratorium.edu](mailto:permissions@exploratorium.edu).
