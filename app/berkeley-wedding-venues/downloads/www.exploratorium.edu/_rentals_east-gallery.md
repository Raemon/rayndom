# Moore East Gallery

Moore East Gallery

## Explore life from DNA and cells to organisms and ecosystems.

With large windows overlooking the Bay, the East Gallery brings the outside world in, offering experiences and exhibits that let guests explore life at different scales. It’s perfect for intimate seated dinners, dancing, and receptions.

**Square Footage**| **Seated Dining Capacity***|  **Standing Reception Capacity***  
---|---|---  
7,000| 90–200| 450  
  
 _*Capacities subject to change_
