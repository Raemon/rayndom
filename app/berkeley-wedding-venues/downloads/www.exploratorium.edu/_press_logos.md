# Logos

Logos

The Exploratorium logos are provided for use online and in print.

The Exploratorium authorizes use of its logo to noncommercial or educational facilities only. All other uses, including, but not limited to, use in any print publication, are strictly prohibited without prior written authorization. Please direct requests to [permissions@exploratorium.edu](mailto:permissions@exploratorium.edu).

Be sure to read our [Use Policy](/about/use-policy "Use Policy") for guidelines in linking to our site and content usage.
