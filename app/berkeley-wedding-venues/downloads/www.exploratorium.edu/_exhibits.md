# Explore Our Exhibits

![Exhibits](/sites/default/files/styles/responsive_200w/public/2023-03/exhibits_3954.jpg.webp?itok=SwY1dQPI)

Explore Our Exhibits

Here at the Exploratorium, you don’t look at exhibits—you  _play_ with them. Dance with your own shadow, levitate, touch a tornado, mix colors and break light apart, stop time, start a conversation, capture a wave, explode your mind; it all can (and does) happen here.

[ ![School Field Trips](/sites/default/files/styles/responsive_16_9_350w/public/2023-03/20_RedBlueButton_DSC_4159_GL%20%281%29.jpg.webp?h=d1cb525d&itok=9qjJ9v2d)School Field Trips Register now and bring your class on a field trip.](/visit/field-trips)

[The Exploratorium](/), established in 1969, is an internationally renowned museum of art, science, and human perception located in San Francisco, California. Its hundreds of hands-on exhibits are designed to promote science discovery.

[Global Collaborations](/partnerships/global-collaborations) The Exploratorium's mission is to create inquiry-based experiences that transform learning worldwide. Through Global Collaborations, we share our deep knowledge of exhibit making, program creation, and professional development with partner organizations around the world.
