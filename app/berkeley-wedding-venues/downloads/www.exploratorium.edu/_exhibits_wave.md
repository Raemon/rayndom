# Wave

![A display of colorful, luminous orbs flowing in a wave-like motion.](/sites/default/files/styles/responsive_200w/public/2025-10/Wave-2_Marquee.jpg.webp?itok=fpxFdOvg)

Exhibit

Wave

Additional Information

__

Creator

Squidsoup, 2019

Wave

Wave consists of nearly 500 suspended orbs in the form of a breaking ocean wave, brought to life with light and sound. Each autonomous orb in this immersive artwork contains LED lights, a speaker, and a microcomputer. Collectively the orbs create a hypnotic illuminated and resonant experience.

[squidsoup.org](https://www.squidsoup.org/)

About the Artist

Pioneers in the use of light, sound, and technology, Squidsoup’s artworks combine physical and dynamic digital spaces with novel and intuitive forms of interaction to produce responsive and spellbinding environments. The art collective’s installations have been shown worldwide.
