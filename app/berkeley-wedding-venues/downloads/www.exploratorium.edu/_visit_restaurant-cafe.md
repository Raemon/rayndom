## The Tinkering Table Restaurant

At The Tinkering Table, dining becomes a hands-on adventure. Our menu is a modular playground of **build-your-own bowls** and **made-to-order melts** , wraps, and sweets designed for kids, grown-ups, and food lovers alike. Think hearty, **family-friendly options** served in a **fast** , **casual** style that fits seamlessly into your visit to the Exploratorium. Part café, part test kitchen, it’s the place to experiment with flavors, invent a plate that’s completely your own, and refuel before diving back into discovery.

## Catalyst Coffee Lab

At Catalyst Coffee Lab, we blend bold Addictive Coffee Roasters **espresso and drip brews** with fizzy beaker drinks and **brain-boosting bites** to stimulate conversation and creativity. It’s a place where science and flavor collide, offering a great start to your day or the perfect bookend to your visit. Whether you’re looking for a **speedy snack** , a **quick meal** , or your daily **caffeine fix** , Catalyst fuels both body and mind.
