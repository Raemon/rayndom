# Submit Your Pledge

Thank you for your interest in the Exploratorium’s Oppenheimer Circle.

Submission is optional and this form is not legally binding for you or your estate. We also understand that plans often change, so whenever possible, please send us an update so that we may maintain an accurate record of your bequest intentions.

Providing copies of your documents for the Exploratorium’s records help ensure accurate accounting of your future gift, and as with all personal data, will remain confidential.

If you have any questions, please contact [development@exploratorium.edu](mailto:development@exploratorium.edu) or call [415.528.4866](tel:4155284866). Please complete and submit this Letter of Intent so we can recognize your planned gift intentions.
