# Membership

Take your curiosity out to play all year–day or night.* Become a Become a Daytime or After Dark member!

Membership pays for itself in less than three visits! Walk in free to one of the Bay Area’s most beloved museums, enjoy great discounts at Pier 15 and beyond, and support our nonprofit mission! With exciting seasonal exhibitions and 700+ interactive exhibits to explore, you’ll never have the same visit twice.

***Yes** , all memberships now include admission to After Dark (18+)!

### Membership Benefits

  * Unlimited free Daytime and After Dark admission to Pier 15
  * Members-only VIP hours, every Sunday 10:00 a.m.–noon. Get the place to yourself and street parking is free!
  * 10% discount at the Exploratorium Stores, The Tinkering Table Restaurant, and Catalyst Coffee Lab (except alcohol)
  * $3 off tickets to our world-famous Tactile Dome ($5 off for Supporter and Patron levels)
  * Exclusive invitations to members-only events and exhibition previews
  * $10 off daytime tickets for guests
  * Free admission to 350+ ASTC science centers and museums worldwide (starting 90+ miles away)
  * Double your impact with a [matching gift from your employer](/support/matching-gifts "Matching Gifts")
  * [Member account](https://visit.exploratorium.edu/ "Membership Login") where you can check your expiration, download your digital membership card, resend your tax letter, and more
