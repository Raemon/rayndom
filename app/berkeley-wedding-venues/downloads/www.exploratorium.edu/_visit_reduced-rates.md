# Free and Reduced Admission

![A person standing in front of a vibrant, abstract digital display with colorful, geometric patterns.](/sites/default/files/styles/responsive_200w/public/2025-11/GLOW_Opening-13-marquee.jpg.webp?itok=lK3fyoZH)

Free and Reduced Admission

The Exploratorium is dedicated to expanding access to all by providing free and low-cost admission opportunities throughout the year. 

### Exploratorium for All 

We’re committed to making the Exploratorium accessible to everyone. Visitors with an EBT/SNAP/CalFresh, Medi-Cal, WIC card, or Lifeline Pass can purchase discounted tickets or memberships on site (not available online). 

  * $5 admission to Daytime or After Dark (ages 18+) for up to 4 tickets
  * $39 Daytime Family Explorers Membership
  * $19 Dual After Dark Membership (ages 18+) 



Bring your qualifying card and matching photo ID to the Ticketing Desk. (Tickets and membership purchases are only sold on site, not online. P-EBT cards and EBT Sun Bucks cards do not qualify for these discounts.) 

We proudly participate in the Museums for All and San Francisco Museums for All programs, helping make museums accessible nationwide. 

Visit [Museums4All.org](https://museums4all.org/) or [sfhsa.org/san-francisco-museums-all](https://www.sfhsa.org/san-francisco-museums-all) to explore other participating museums.

[ ![Museums for All](/sites/default/files/styles/webp/public/2023-02/Museums-for-All-Logo_CMYK_copyright.png.webp?itok=2SLE-LSM) ](https://museums4all.org/)

### Discover & Go Library Program 

Get free admission to the Exploratorium and 35+ Bay Area cultural attractions through your local library’s Discover & Go program. Check with your library or visit its website to see if passes are available.

### Community Family Pass Program 

The Exploratorium provides free admission to families, youth, and small groups working with select Bay Area community-based organizations (CBOs). CBOs manage a check-out system for passes, which allows their constituents to develop lasting relationships with the Exploratorium. 

* * *

### Accessibility for Visitors with Disabilities 

[For visitors with disabilities](/visit/accessibility "Accessibility at the Exploratorium") we offer complimentary admission for accompanying ADA aides. Please call 415.528.4407. 

Learn more about [Accessibility at the Exploratorium →](/visit/accessibility "Accessibility at the Exploratorium")

* * *

### Free Admission for California Public School Teachers 

California public school PreK–12 teachers receive free admission to the Exploratorium. Teachers who qualify will be given one free, nontransferable, general admission voucher. Please note that these vouchers are not applicable toward admissions associated with Field Trip groups. 

Teachers are invited to apply for the program by [completing this form](/visit/reduced-rates/teacher-application "Free Admission for CA Public School Teachers"). Please bring your teacher ID. 

* * *

### Discounted Admission for Non-California Public School Teachers

Public school PreK–12 teachers not from California receive a $10 discount on admission to the Exploratorium. Teachers who qualify will be given one nontransferable general admission voucher.

Please bring your teacher ID.

* * *

### Free Field Trips for Title I Schools 

Title I K–12 schools receive free admission to the Exploratorium, subject to availability, throughout the school year. 

Learn more about [School Field Trips to the Exploratorium →](/visit/field-trips "School Field Trips")

* * *

### ASTC Passport Program Members

The Exploratorium offers **free admission** for members of**ASTC Passport Program** institutions. The benefit **covers 2 people** regardless of your membership level at your home institution.

To obtain tickets, bring your institution’s membership card and a photo ID or other proof of residence to our on-site Ticketing Desk on the day of your visit. One visiting adult must be named on the membership card used for entry. Home institutions and the member’s home address must be at least 90 miles away from the Exploratorium.

* * *

### AAA Reduced Admission 

AAA Members may purchase reduced admission tickets on site. Present your AAA card at Ticketing to purchase your tickets. 

* * *

### Military Reduced Admission 

Active, retired, and reservist military personnel may purchase reduced admission tickets on site. Present your military ID or proof of service (VA card or DD-214) at Ticketing to purchase your tickets. 

* * *

To learn more about any of these programs, please email [visit@exploratorium.edu](mailto:visit@exploratorium.edu).
