## Welcome to the Exploratorium Explainer Alumni Network!

Whether you were an Explainer when founder Frank Oppenheimer first opened our museum doors in 1969, or are one of the most recent graduates of the program, you join more than 3,500 former High School Explainers in sharing a unique Bay Area experience. 

The Explainer Alumni Network will offer opportunities to: 

  * Connect with other former Explainers
  * Join us for special events and free visits
  * Mentor recent Explainer alums—or receive mentorship!
  * Give your feedback to the Explainer programs
  * Receive updates and news about the Explainer program 



Stay tuned!
