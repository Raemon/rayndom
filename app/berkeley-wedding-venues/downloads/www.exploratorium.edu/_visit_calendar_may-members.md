May Is for Members

We wouldn't be here without our members, and May is when we make sure you know it!

### Enjoy Extra Member Benefits in May

  * A special 15% discount at the Exploratorium Stores
  * **Daytime members** get free admission to After Dark Thursday Nights in May (ages 18+ only)
  * **After Dark members** get free admission during Daytime hours in May
  * Members receive a gift with purchase at the main Exploratorium Store (while supplies last)
  * *NEW* From May 3 –May 31, receive **discounted admission at the following museums**. Show your valid membership card and a matching photo ID at their Ticketing desk to receive 50% off on up to 6 tickets.
    * [The Tech Interactive](https://www.thetech.org/)
    * [CuriOdyssey](https://curiodyssey.org/) (Entrance and parking fee for Coyote Point Recreation Area is $6 per vehicle. CuriOdyssey is open Memorial Day, however the park tends to be very busy that day, so parking may be difficult.)
    * [Chabot Space & Science Center](https://chabotspace.org/)
    * [Filoli](https://filoli.org/) (weekdays in May only; email [tickets@filoli.org](mailto:tickets@filoli.org) prior to your visit or purchase on site when you arrive)
  * *NEW* [**Tactile Dome tickets**](/visit/tactile-dome "Tactile Dome")**are 50% off** for members in May! Ask at the Ticketing or Information desks when you arrive, or call 415.528.4444 (option 5) to purchase your tickets.



#### Not a member yet? 

Please consider [joining us today](/membership "Membership")! Members are the backbone of our organization and help ensure the continuity of our mission to create inquiry-based experiences that transform learning worldwide. We can’t wait to see you at the museum.
