# Donor & Corporate Member FAQs

![Donor FAQ](/sites/default/files/styles/responsive_200w/public/2023-03/support_donor_faq_marquee.jpg.webp?itok=iI6PkgSO)

Donor & Corporate Member FAQs

Planning your visit? Learn about visiting the museum as a corporate member or a member of our donor community.

### Donor FAQs

##### Do I need a ticket to visit the Exploratorium?

No, all you need is your Exploratorium donor card and ID. Donors are welcome to visit anytime without a prior reservation.

##### Where should I enter when I arrive for my visit?

Donors can skip the line at the ticketing counter and proceed through the member entrance line, or check in at the Front Desk in the bulkhead area. (Or [see this handy map.](https://www.exploratorium.edu/visit#map))

##### How can my guests visit without me?

You can share your donor card with your guests through your Apple or Android Wallet, or by forwarding your digital card email to them (resend this email to yourself by logging in to your account [here](https://visit.exploratorium.edu/?j=2524195&sfmc_sub=153402272&l=59_HTML&u=93282531&mid=7227180&jb=1&utm_source=Exacttarget&utm_medium=email&utm_campaign=Ticketure%20Announcement%E2%80%94Donors)). If you have any issues, please email us at [development@exploratorium.edu](mailto:development@exploratorium.edu) or call Institutional Advancement at 415.528.4866.

### Corporate Member FAQs

##### How can my company become an Exploratorium Corporate Member?

We'd love to partner with your company! Corporate Membership offers valuable benefits including employee admission, guest passes, and opportunities to engage with our innovative programs.

Learn more about [Corporate Membership levels, benefits, and how to join.](/support/corporate-engagement "Engage Your Business")

You can also contact our Corporate Engagement team directly at [corporate.engagement@exploratorium.edu](mailto:corporate.engagement@exploratorium.edu) or 415.528.4843.

Our team will be happy to discuss membership options and answer any questions about how Corporate Membership can benefit your organization.

##### My company is a corporate member. How do I get my ticket to visit the Exploratorium?

**If your company has "Show &Go" pass: **You can obtain your admission ticket from our Ticketing Desk on the day of your visit by showing your work badge.

**If you have an e-ticket code:** If you received an e-ticket code via email from your company, you can redeem it for complimentary admission either online or in person.

  * **Online:** Enter the code in the “Apply a Promo Code” section at [checkout](/tickets "Buy Tickets"). You may redeem multiple codes within one checkout process.
  * **On site:** Show the original email with your unique code at our Ticketing Desk.



**Need more information?** Corporate Member ticket allocations vary by company membership level. For questions about your company's specific benefits, please contact:

  * Your HR administrator, or
  * Our ticketing office at [reservations@exploratorium.edu](mailto:reservations@exploratorium.edu) or 415.528.4407.
