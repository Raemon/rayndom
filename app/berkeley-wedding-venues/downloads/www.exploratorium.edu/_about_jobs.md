# Work at the Exploratorium

![Staff Photo 2023](/sites/default/files/styles/responsive_200w/public/2024-03/jobs-marquee.png.webp?itok=TBzUB8rV)

Work at the Exploratorium

Dig deeper and we think you'll discover that this is a wonderful place to work.

## Join Our Team and Make a Difference

At the Exploratorium, we’re passionate about curiosity-driven learning, science, and art. We create extraordinary exhibits, projects, programs, and events that ignite curiosity and cultivate the confidence that sparks enthusiastic exploration, original thinking, and brave leaps forward. It’s important, life-changing work and we’re proud to be doing it.

We're also inspired by our colleagues, who are innovative, quirky, talented, interested and interesting people.

Dive in and discover what it's like to be part of our team. We hope to see you soon at one of our staff gatherings.

## What We Do

![Bernoulli AND Lens](/sites/default/files/styles/responsive_3_2_350w/public/2024-03/BernoulliANDLens_DSC_7282_GL.jpg.webp?h=8d93c087&itok=o2p8Zoo5)

##### Inspiring Visitors 

Creating astonishing exhibits, events, and programs that ignite curiosity, upend perspectives, spark enthusiastic exploration and original thinking, and cultivate the confidence to take brave leaps forward. 

Learn more about [exhibit making](/exhibits/exhibit-making "Exhibit Making").  
Check out our [events and programs](/visit/calendar "Upcoming Events Calendar").

![teacher-institute-main](/sites/default/files/styles/responsive_3_2_350w/public/2024-03/teacher-institute-main-page_0.jpeg.webp?h=c515b0a0&itok=wZOJVjYr)

##### Empowering Educators 

Designing teaching resources and inquiry-based experiences that equip educators with the tools they need to ignite student curiosity, encourage active exploration, and foster enthusiasm for learning. 

Learn more about [Educator Engagement](/education "Education") at the Exploratorium.

![Global Collaborations: Why We Do It](/sites/default/files/styles/responsive_3_2_350w/public/2023-11/global-impact.jpg.webp?h=9e499333&itok=lw4u37D6)

##### Fueling a Global Movement 

Crossing borders and boundaries, partnering with science centers and organizations worldwide, sharing our experience and expertise, and empowering like-minded collaborators to profoundly transform how people learn. 

Learn how we are collaborating with partners worldwide through [Global Collaborations](/global "Global Collaborations").

![Supporting behind the scenes](/sites/default/files/styles/responsive_3_2_350w/public/2024-03/JoshuaLight_DSC_7985_GL.jpg.webp?h=451ae50a&itok=ShWcOmQU)

##### Supporting Behind the Scenes 

Contributing to teams—from Institutional Advancement to Operations to Graphics—that support our operations and mission.

### Employee Benefits

Working here comes with lots of benefits—in addition to working at a spectacular location.

###### Health and Well-Being

  * Health, dental, and vision plans
  * A Health Savings Account (HSA)
  * An Employee Assistance Plan



###### Financial

  * A Flexible Savings Account (FSA) 
  * A 529 college savings plan 
  * Employer contributions to a 403(b) plan 
  * Life insurance



###### Perks

  * Free admission to our amazing events 
  * Tasty talks or films at lunchtime 
  * Well-behaved dogs are welcome 
  * Discounts at the onsite Stores, restaurant, and café



###### Vacation and Sick Time

  * Generous vacation time 
  * Paid holidays and sick leave

### Notice of Nondiscrimination

The Exploratorium is committed to ensuring equal employment opportunity. All employment decisions, policies, and practices are in accordance with applicable federal, state, and local anti-discrimination laws.

The Exploratorium will not engage in or tolerate unlawful discrimination (including any form of unlawful harassment or retaliation) with regard to any employment decision, policy, or practice based on a person’s race, color, religion, national origin, sex, pregnancy, age, physical or mental disability, medical condition, sexual orientation, gender identity, veteran status, military service, or other characteristic protected by federal, state, or local law.

This policy applies to all terms and conditions of employment, including, but not limited to, recruiting, hiring, compensation, benefits, training, assignments, evaluations, coaching, promotion, discipline, termination, and layoff. It is important to make explicit that this policy also applies to the Exploratorium’s efforts to promote equity and inclusion. In any efforts of this kind, there will be no preferences, limitations, classifications, or separations based on race, sex or any aforementioned protected characteristic.

By way of this policy statement, all equity and inclusion efforts are modified as may be necessary to comply with this policy statement and will be administered in accordance with it.
