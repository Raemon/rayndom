# Osher West Gallery

Osher West Gallery

## Experiment with thoughts, feelings, and social behavior.

The West Gallery, which integrates exhibits on psychology and art, houses the museum’s iconic Tactile Dome and Black Box—a space for immersive installations. Closest to the Embarcadero and adjacent to the Forum, the West Gallery is great for seated dinners and evening receptions, including product launches, alumni gatherings, and more.

**Square Footage**| **Seated Dining Capacity***|  **Standing Reception Capacity***  
---|---|---  
16,000| 70–200| 500  
  
 _*Capacities subject to change_
