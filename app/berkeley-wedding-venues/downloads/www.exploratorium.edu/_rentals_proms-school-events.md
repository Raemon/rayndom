# Proms and School Events

![Students dancing at a prom night party in the Exploratorium](/sites/default/files/styles/responsive_200w/public/2023-10/prom-rentals_marquee.jpg.webp?itok=Hey62ut6)

Proms and School Events

Celebrate your prom alongside intriguing exhibits and create an experience you and your class will never forget. 

## Prom at the Exploratorium

We’re pleased to offer our galleries individually or in bundles for student proms, formals, homecomings, grad nights, and more! We have a variety of gallery options for both small and large events, including built-in entertainment and plenty of great photo opportunities for all your guests. All of our galleries feature interactive exhibits to explore and play with—consider the [Bechtel Central Gallery](/visit/rentals/central-gallery) or the [Osher West Gallery](/visit/rentals/west-gallery) for the largest dance floors, or the [Fisher Bay Observatory & Terrace](/visit/rentals/bay-observatory-gallery-terrace) or the [East Gallery](/visit/rentals/east-gallery) for views of the Bay.

**Our Exploratorium Prom package includes:**

  * A 15% non-profit (501c3) discount on base rental fees 
  * Interactive exhibits in the gallery or galleries rented
  * Our Explainer staff to help facilitate interactive exhibits with guests 
  * An on-site facility coordinator
