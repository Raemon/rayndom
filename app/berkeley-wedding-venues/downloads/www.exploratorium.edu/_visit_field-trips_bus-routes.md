# Bus Routes for Field Trips and Other Groups

Bus Routes for Field Trips and Other Groups

#### Arrival and Departure Times for Buses

If you're coming to the Exploratorium by school or charter bus, please make your bus reservations _after_ your Field Trip reservation, so you can be sure that your preferred time slot is available. We carefully coordinate bus arrival and departure times so they don't interrupt the flow of traffic on the Embarcadero.

###### School Bus / Charter Bus

| Bus Arrival| Bus Departure  
---|---  
10:00 am| 12:30 pm  
10:30 am| 1:00 pm  
11:00 am| 1:30 pm  
11:30 am| 2:00 pm  
Noon| 2:30 pm  
| 

###### Public Transit / Car

| Bus Arrival| Bus Departure  
---|---  
10:00 am| Any time up to 3:00 pm  
10:30 am| Any time up to 3:00 pm  
11:00 am| Any time up to 3:00 pm  
11:30 am| Any time up to 3:00 pm  
Noon| Any time up to 3:00 pm  
  
###### Drop-Off and Pick-Up Directions

Bus drop-off occurs directly in front of the Exploratorium at Pier 17 on San Francisco’s Embarcadero. Visit our [Getting Here](/node/3681 "Getting Here & Parking ") page for more information. Bus drivers will receive parking information upon arrival and directed to one of our [Short-Term Bus Parking Locations](/sites/default/files/2025-10/Exploratorium-Bus-Parking.pdf).
