Daytime Members-Only Hours

### Every Sunday 

All year long we set aside Sunday mornings for Daytime members—10:00 a.m. to noon is just for you. Join us for a leisurely visit, enjoy lunch at The Tinkering Table Restaurant, and then head out for your next adventure. 

#### Not a member yet?****

Please consider [joining us today](/membership "Membership")! Members are the backbone of our organization and help ensure the continuity of our mission to create inquiry-based experiences that transform learning worldwide. We can’t wait to see you at the museum.
