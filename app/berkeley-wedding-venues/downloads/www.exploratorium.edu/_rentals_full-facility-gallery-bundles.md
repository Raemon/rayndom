Full Facility & Gallery Bundles

## [Book your event today!](/rentals/form "Rental Inquiry Form")

Perfect for holiday party planning. Combine galleries for more exhibit experiences.

You can have it all: Rent the entire Exploratorium facility and get spectacular waterfront views of San Francisco and the Bay, a lively landscape of hundreds of exhibits, flexible event spaces, superb catering, and skilled staff.

With our gallery bundle options, you can craft the perfect museum selection for your event. Whether you’re looking for a progressive celebration with distinct spaces for your cocktail reception, dinner, and dessert or planning an entertaining reception for 500 guests (or more!), we are happy to help you find the perfect fit.
