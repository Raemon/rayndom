# Holiday Parties

![A giant moon hovers above a red-hued gallery decorated for a party](/sites/default/files/styles/responsive_200w/public/2023-09/Callan-Exploratorium-042722_001_marquee_3.jpg.webp?itok=fEdZnvpF)

Holiday Parties

Book your holiday party at the Exploratorium and let your guests experience lights, visual arts, and glowing pieces throughout the museum.   


> The Exploratorium offers five gallery spaces that can be bundled together to create unforgettable experiences for your guests.
> 
> Rent the [full facility](https://www.exploratorium.edu/visit/rentals/full-facility-gallery-bundles) and get exclusive use of the museum for up to 3,500 guests.

**Our galleries become extra magical in December with the arrival of _Glow_ , a seasonal exhibition featuring luminous artworks big and small.** Book your **holiday party** with us and invite your guests to experience and play with light, color, and shadow throughout the museum.

Enhance your event with our in-house add-on options that include LED lighting packages, customized monitor artwork for branding, outdoor electric heat lamps, and many more options you can secure directly from us.
