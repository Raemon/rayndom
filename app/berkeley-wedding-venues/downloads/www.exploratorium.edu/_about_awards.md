# Awards and Other Recognition

Awards and Other Recognition

The Exploratorium has received numerous awards and nominations from educational organizations, science institutions, and Internet pioneers. We are proud that we have been recognized for our work in creating a culture of learning through innovative environments, programs, and tools to help people nurture their curiosity about the world around them.

[Innovator Green Business](https://search.greenbiztracker.org/business/the-exploratorium-san-francisco)

In 2023 the Exploratorium became the largest net zero energy museum in the U.S., if not the world. [Learn more here](/press-office/press-releases/exploratorium-achieves-historic-goal-net-zero-energy "Exploratorium achieves historic goal of Net Zero Energy operations ") about this historic accomplishment.
