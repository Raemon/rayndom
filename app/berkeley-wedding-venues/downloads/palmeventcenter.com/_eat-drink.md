# Eat

![](/images/site/eat-drink/eat-02.jpg)

Fine-dining at its best, the Palm features delicious cuisine inspired by wine country. Our culinary team creates menus that are custom tailored to weddings, with perfectly balanced flavors, expertly timed courses, and impeccable service. Decadent and delightful, meals feature selections every guest is sure to enjoy. Our menus feature a variety of passed hors d’oeuvres, salads, soups and entrées for their wedding menu. Menu enhancements are also available to customize menus further with cocktail hour bites and drinks, late night treats, and upgraded entrées.

# DRINK

![](/images/site/eat-drink/drink-02.jpg)

Artful, delicious drinks set the mood for a fun wine country wedding. The Palm’s impressive modern bar serves up custom cocktails, top-tier liquors, beers on tap, and delightful Rubino Estates wines. All wedding packages come with 4.5 hours of full, open bar service, plus champagne and wine pours during meal service. Delicious non-alcoholic and mocktail options may be choosen for non-drinkers. Additional inventive drink stations, such as a boba tea bar, agua fresca, and dalgona coffee are also available.
