![Get Married](/images/site/get-married/title-get-married.png)

We love weddings.

We tear up seeing the bride or groom walk down the aisle. We swoon over a couple’s first dance. We love hearing a speech describing how the couple first met each other.

We believe weddings should be romantic, unique, and fun – all the while treating guests to an unforgettable evening.

![](/images/site/get-married/get-married-2.jpg)

In addition to a 5-hour reception, we offer wedding ceremony packages for couples to marry on-site. Couples may choose between our outdoor vineyard-view ceremony site, or our indoor Estate Room, lined with wine barrels and gleaming candlelight. 

![](/images/site/get-married/get-married-3.jpg)

Our coordinators are experts in planning weddings. Included in our services, our day-of coordinators handle all the logistical details the day of the wedding, the rehearsal with friends and family, and the execution of meal service. 

![](/images/site/get-married/get-married-4.jpg)

We want couples to have a relaxed wedding day full of memories, not stress. Getting married at the Palm means a stunning wedding with beautiful wine country views, delicious food, a full open bar, and attentive service.
