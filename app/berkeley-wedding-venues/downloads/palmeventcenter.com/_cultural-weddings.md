![Cultural Weddings](/images/site/cultural/title-cultural.png)

Every couple has a unique love story.

![](/images/site/cultural/cultural-hero-2.jpg)

A love story that weaves together two families with different customs, cultures and traditions.

![](/images/site/cultural/cultural-hero-3.jpg)

Our coordinators are experts at creating seamless, elegant weddings that incorporate these special moments. Whether it is an 8-course Chinese/Vietnamese banquet; a tea ceremony; a baraat; a coin, veil and cord ceremony; or a sofreh; we can guide you with interlacing these traditions into a wedding that flows smoothly, has happy family members, and is stress-free.
