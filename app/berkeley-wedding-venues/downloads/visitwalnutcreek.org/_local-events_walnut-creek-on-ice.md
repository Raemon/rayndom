# Walnut Creek on Ice

Mar 30

Written By [Calvin Jung](/local-events?author=5925f5892e69cf3e95962347)

[ ![](https://images.squarespace-cdn.com/content/v1/604e4c7978231847fc102150/421b538d-2101-4bf3-a707-718cceef1069/Events-Walnut-Creek-On-Ice.jpg) ](https://walnutcreekonice.com)

An anticipated 50,000 skaters and a thousand more spectators have the opportunity to ice skate in downtown Walnut Creek. This 13+ week extravaganza by the Walnut Creek Chamber of Commerce and the Downtown Business Association, offers unrivaled partnering opportunities and an opportunity to put your brand name in front of a highly lucrative market, at the time of year when exposure is most easily turned into sales and a genuine interest. Be seen at the Coolest Place in the East Bay: By being a sponsor of Walnut Creek on Ice your logo will be on television, radio, print media, rink dasher boards and in your customers’ hearts and minds for over 13 weeks.

Walnut Creek On Ice offers group lessons and open skating times and is also available for private parties and events. Bring your family or friends and celebrate the holidays on ice!

[ Learn more ](https://walnutcreekonice.com)
