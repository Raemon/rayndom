The Gardens offer both indoor and outdoor spaces.

## Ruth Bancroft Garden

Host your wedding ceremony at the beautiful and unique Ruth Bancroft Garden. The 3½-acre garden provides a sculptural backdrop for your gathering. Indoor and outdoor event facilities are available with bookings open from February through October. Maximum capacity for indoor events is 100 guests seated or standing; outdoor gatherings can accommodate up to 150 seated or standing.

[ Go Here ](https://www.ruthbancroftgarden.org/private-events/)

## Prime Steakhouse & Martini Garden

Nestled in the heart of Walnut Creek, Prime Rib at The Garden perfectly marries the whimsical with the functional. It features a variety of customizable indoor and outdoor space along with a new speakeasy room. Their experienced event staff is dedicated to ensuring that your event is executed flawlessly, providing personalized attention and exceptional service every step of the way. Whether you're planning a wedding, corporate event, or social event, the unique spaces provide the perfect backdrop for creating unforgettable memories.. 

[ Go Here ](https://www.primeribwc.com/private-events)

## The Gardens at Heather Farm

The Gardens at Heather Farm has over six landscaped acres full of colorful flowerbeds, rock gardens, and water features. It offers a variety of distinctive indoor and outdoor event spaces. Not surprisingly, it's one of the East Bay's most sought-after venues for weddings, parties, meetings, seminars, and other special occasions.

[ Go Here ](https://gardenshf.org/private-events/)

#### MORE VENUE RECOMMENDATIONS

* * *

#### Shadelands Ranch

The beautifully maintained landscaping that surrounds the Shadelands Ranch house and the picturesque rose-bush-surrounded gazebo provide the perfect setting for a summer wedding. When you hold a wedding at Shadelands Ranch, you and your guests will be reminded of the gracious hospitality extended to Ranch guests in days gone by. The convenient location and cost-effective, uncomplicated pricing make Shadelands Ranch an ideal venue. [Go Here](https://wchistory.org/museum/rentals/)

#### Boundary Oak Golf Course

Host your wedding ceremony and reception at the scenic Clubhouse at Boundary Oak. Choose from two outdoor ceremony site options at either the Lake View or on the Patio. Then move the celebrations indoors to the gorgeous Mira Room. The Mira Room has two levels with breathtaking sunset views and adjoins the Patio. The Vista Room is a great space for dinner or for larger parties. Start with cocktails in the Atrium and follow with dinner in the Celebration Ballroom. The Celebration Ballroom is the largest event space, seating 275. The Clubhouse at Boundary Oak offers amazing views, photo opportunities on the course and private dressing rooms. [Go Here](https://www.playboundaryoak.com/hosting-an-event)

#### Civic Park Community Center

Imagine your special day at beautiful Civic Park Community Center. The spacious wedding gazebo and park is located near downtown Walnut Creek. The large assembly hall seats up to 250 guests, and the commercial kitchen is available for catering staff of your selection. Other features on property include bridal room, banquet tables and chairs, sound system and small patio space. [Go Here](https://www.walnutcreekartsrec.org/parks-facilities/facility-rentals/civic-park-community-center-assembly-hall)

#### Creekside Hall

The Creekside Hall is located just inside the Walnut Creek Elks Lodge just south of Walnut Creek’s downtown shopping district. The hall has four rooms available to rent for your special day. The Great Room seats 150 guests with a dance floor. Bring your own caterer to use the full-size kitchen. There are two full service bars also available, and plenty of free parking for your guests. [Go Here](https://creeksidehall.com/meeting-and-banquet-rooms/)

#### Shadelands Art Center

Host the perfect wedding at one of the East Bay’s largest and most affordable venue, the Shadelands Art Center. The high vaulted ceiling and hardwood floored auditorium seats 300 guests comfortably. The center provides state-of-the-art sound and lighting systems, a full kitchen for caterer of your choice, a bridal room and access to large enclosed patio. [Go Here](http://walnutcreekartsrec.org/parks-facilities/facility-rentals/center-for-community-arts-auditorium)
