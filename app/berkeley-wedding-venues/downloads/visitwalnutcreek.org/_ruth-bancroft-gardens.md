## Bring your dream wedding to life!

Unify your love in a world-renowned garden! The Ruth Bancroft Garden features 3.5 acres of succulents and drought-tolerant plants that make for stunning photos and a one-of-a-kind setting for your special day. Wedding receptions can be setup either outdoors or indoors in our new Visitor and Education Center.

Voted “** _Best Wedding Venue in the East Bay_** ” by Diablo Magazine editors and readers, June 2020
