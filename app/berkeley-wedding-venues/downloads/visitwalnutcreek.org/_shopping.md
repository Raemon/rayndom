## World-Class   
Shopping

Voted the best shopping in San Francisco’s East Bay, Walnut Creek features more than 200 boutiques and classic retail stores. Stroll the twinkling tree-lined walkways of historic downtown or hop on the free trolley to Broadway Plaza to quickly see why Walnut Creek is the ultimate shopping destination!

Here’s a sampling of the shops in Walnut Creek, or check out the latest listings at:   
[**Broadway Plaza**](https://www.broadwayplaza.com/) and [**Downtown Walnut Creek**.](https://www.walnutcreekdowntown.com/shopping/)
