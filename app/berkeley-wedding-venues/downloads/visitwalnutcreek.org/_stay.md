The hotels of Walnut Creek, within a 40-minute drive of San Francisco and Oakland International Airport, offer a location of convenience, beauty and comfort. Central to the city’s downtown shopping and restaurants and surrounded by scenic views of Mt. Diablo, our collection of hotels gives you easy access to a variety of experiences and activities, meeting rooms and lodging options. Choices range from well-appointed hotels to affordable inns. Many hotels have been recently renovated and updated.

If you need assistance making a decision for your convention, sales meeting, wedding or leisure stay, please [contact us](/contact) at the Walnut Creek Convention and Visitors Bureau.
