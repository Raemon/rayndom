# Eclectic, Electric.

Walnut Creek is a city of complementary contrasts. It brings together historic and modern, cityscape and landscape, and urban and hometown to create diversity of experience. Our multicultural mecca of food, art, music, and health and wellness at the base of Mt. Diablo offers up one-of-a-kind events that span the senses.

**Plan your visit around our fun events for every season under the sun.**
