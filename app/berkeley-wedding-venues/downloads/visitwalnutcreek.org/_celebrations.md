## Shadelands Ranch

The beautifully maintained landscaping that surrounds the Shadelands Ranch house and the picturesque rose-bush-surrounded gazebo provide a classic environment for your special gathering. When you hold an event at Shadelands Ranch, you and your guests will be reminded of the gracious hospitality extended to Ranch guests in days gone by. The convenient location and cost-effective, uncomplicated pricing make Shadelands Ranch an ideal venue.

[ Go Here ](https://wchistory.org/museum/rentals/)

## Ruth Bancroft Garden

Host your event at the beautiful and unique Ruth Bancroft Garden. The 3½-acre garden provides a sculptural backdrop for your gathering. Indoor and outdoor event facilities are available with bookings open from February through October. Maximum capacity for indoor events is 100 guests seated or standing; outdoor gatherings can accommodate up to 150 seated or standing.

[ Go Here ](https://www.ruthbancroftgarden.org/rentals/)

## La Fontaine

Discover modern elegance at La Fontaine. The Italian and French restaurant offers an upscale space for corporate and social events. It has a versatile space that can accommodate up to 150 people and features a fully private room for smaller meetings or gatherings.

## The Gardens at Heather Farm

The Gardens at Heather Farm has over six landscaped acres full of colorful flowerbeds, rock gardens, and water features. It offers a variety of distinctive indoor and outdoor event spaces. Not surprisingly, it's one of the East Bay's most sought-after venues for weddings, parties, meetings, seminars, and other special occasions.

[ Go Here ](https://gardenshf.org/private-events/)

MORE VENUE RECOMMENDATIONS

* * *

#### Boundary Oak Golf Course

Host your event at the scenic Clubhouse at Boundary Oak. Celebrate inside The Mira Room with two levels of breathtaking sunset views and adjoining the Patio. The Vista Room is a great space for dinner or for larger parties. Start with cocktails in the Atrium and follow with dinner in the Celebration Ballroom. The Celebration Ballroom is the largest event space, seating 275. The Clubhouse at Boundary Oak offers amazing views. [Go Here](https://www.playboundaryoak.com/hosting-an-event)

#### Civic Park Community Center

Imagine your special gathering at beautiful Civic Park Community Center. The spacious gazebo and park is located near downtown Walnut Creek. The large assembly hall seats up to 250 guests, and the commercial kitchen is available for catering staff of your selection. Other features on property include banquet tables and chairs, sound system and small patio space. [Go Here](https://www.walnutcreekartsrec.org/parks-facilities/facility-rentals/civic-park-community-center-assembly-hall)

#### Creekside Hall

The Creekside Hall is located just inside the Walnut Creek Elks Lodge just south of Walnut Creek’s downtown shopping district. The hall has four rooms available to rent for your special event. The Great Room seats 150 guests with a dance floor. Bring your own caterer to use the full-size kitchen. There are two full service bars also available, and plenty of free parking for your guests. [Go Here](https://creeksidehall.com/meeting-and-banquet-rooms/)

#### Center for Community Arts Auditorium

Host the perfect party at one of the East Bay’s largest and most affordable venue, the Shadelands Art Center. The high vaulted ceiling and hardwood floored auditorium seats 300 guests comfortably. The center provides state-of-the-art sound and lighting systems, a full kitchen for caterer of your choice and access to a large enclosed patio. [Go Here](http://walnutcreekartsrec.org/parks-facilities/facility-rentals/center-for-community-arts-auditorium)

#### Shadowbrook Winery

Nestled at the base of Mt. Diablo and surrounded by rolling vineyards and olive trees reminiscent of a Tuscan landscape, Shadowbrook Winery offers a warm familiarity for your next private event. Whether it’s a corporate meeting or team building or an intimate gathering of family and friends, events at Shadowbrook are as handcrafted as our wines. For additional details, please inquire to our exclusive event partner Meetings at Meritage LLC. [Go Here](https://shadowbrookwinery.com/visit-us/meetings-at-shadowbrook/#anchor2)
