# **Accessible, Incredible**

Cities up and down the corridor converge on our cultural hub, with all highways pointing to a quick trip, whether for the day or overnight. Walnut Creek serves as an accessible oasis from San Francisco, a sunny award-winning shopping spot only 35 minutes by BART. And once you get here, you can explore our city by foot. Our bustling downtown spans a cube of walkable blocks so everything is central. You also can board our local trolley car for a city tour with a side of Bay Area history.

**Walk, hike, bike, ride, skate, swim. Whatever way you motor, we transport you to new places.**
