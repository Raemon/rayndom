# Art & Wine Festival

Jun 1

Written By [Calvin Jung](/local-events?author=5925f5892e69cf3e95962347)

[ ![](https://images.squarespace-cdn.com/content/v1/604e4c7978231847fc102150/2253df97-de39-4e9a-9d3b-3f7ca8314ce7/Events-Walnut-Creek-Art-Wine-Festival-2025.jpg) ](https://walnut-creek.com/art-wine-festival-2022/)

Fun for the entire family, the festival offers two days of exposure to 60,000 + attendees from all over the Bay Area as they fill the park to enjoy art and commercial vendor booths, a wide range of wines, a craft beer garden, continuous music, food, children’s activities, and special attractions.

[ Learn more ](https://www.walnut-creek.com/art-wine-festival-2023/)
