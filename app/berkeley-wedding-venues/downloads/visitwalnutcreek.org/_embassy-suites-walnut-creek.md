When you stay at Embassy Suites Walnut Creek in Walnut Creek, you’ll be in the business district with convenient access to the Gardens at Heather Farm and Lindsay Wildlife Museum. This family-friendly hotel is within close proximity of Ruth Bancroft Garden as well as world-class shopping at Broadway Plaza.

Our all-suite Walnut Creek hotel is just steps from Bay Area Rapid Transit (BART), providing easy access to the cities of San Francisco and Oakland, as well as the Oakland (OAK) and San Francisco International Airports (SFO). We’re also convenient to corporations such as Kaiser Permanente and John Muir Hospital.
