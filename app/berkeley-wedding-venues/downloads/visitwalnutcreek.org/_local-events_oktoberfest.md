# Oktoberfest

Oct 1

Written By [Calvin Jung](/local-events?author=5925f5892e69cf3e95962347)

[ ![](https://images.squarespace-cdn.com/content/v1/604e4c7978231847fc102150/cd988aed-fa4c-44ea-8095-2ebffc7e5c02/Events-Walnut-Creek-Oktoberfest.jpg) ](https://walnutcreekdowntown.com/marquee-events/octoberfest/)

It’s time to clink glasses and lift your spirits with  _Ein Prosit der Gemütlichkeit —_ a toast to cheers and good times — in honor of the upcoming fall season and popular Oktoberfest celebration.

Join us in the biergarten or on the dance floor for another epic Oktoberfest in Walnut Creek. It’s a great place to show off your dirndl dress or don the lederhosen (not mandatory!). This **ADMISSION FREE** , day-long, family-friendly event recreates the beloved German fall celebration.

Oktoberfest will include live entertainment, music, dancing, arts & crafts, biergarten with dozens of craft beers, wine, international music, Kinder Platz Kids Zone, gourmet food booths and over 30 vendors.

[ Learn more ](https://walnutcreekdowntown.com/marquee-events/oktoberfest/)
