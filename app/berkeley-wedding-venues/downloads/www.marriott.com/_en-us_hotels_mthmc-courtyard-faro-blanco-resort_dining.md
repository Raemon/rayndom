# Florida Keys Dining in Marathon

## Dining

Start your morning with a healthy breakfast and then enjoy your lunch and dinner poolside as you choose from our delectable locally-inspired menus. Indulge in fresh, local seafood dishes or just enjoy a tropical cocktail at our outdoor bar. When dining at Blue Waters you will find yourself in a fun, casual atmosphere with waterfront views in the heart of the Florida Keys. Blue Waters is open to resort guests and locals and offers live entertainment throughout the week. With views of the ocean, it is the perfect spot for a sunset dining experience. 

Dine With Us – Open Daily to Guests & Locals

Our restaurants are open daily for breakfast, lunch & dinner- welcoming both guests and locals! Locals can park onsite and enter through the pool gates for easy access. Join us for great food in a relaxed setting!

Experience Florida Keys Dining

###  Frequently Asked Questions

What restaurants does Courtyard by Marriott Faro Blanco Resort have on property? 

The on-property restaurants at Courtyard by Marriott Faro Blanco Resort are:

Blue Waters Bar & Grill The Bistro – Eat. Drink. Connect.® Blue Waters Room Service

Does Courtyard by Marriott Faro Blanco Resort offer room service? 

Yes, room service is available at Courtyard by Marriott Faro Blanco Resort.

What cuisine is offered at Courtyard by Marriott Faro Blanco Resort? 

The cuisines offered at Courtyard by Marriott Faro Blanco Resort are:

American at Blue Waters Bar & Grill, The Bistro – Eat. Drink. Connect.®, Blue Waters Room Service

Does Courtyard by Marriott Faro Blanco Resort offer complimentary breakfast? 

Complimentary breakfast is not currently served at Courtyard by Marriott Faro Blanco Resort. View on-site and nearby dining options on our [dining page](https://www.marriott.com/en-us/hotels/mthmc-courtyard-faro-blanco-resort/dining/#on-site-dining).
