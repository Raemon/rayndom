# Designer Suites

## Designer Suites

Experience the essence of Manhattan elegance in the luxury suites at The St. Regis New York. Each suite is a timeless blend of classic sophistication and modern comfort, featuring residential-style layouts, bespoke furnishings, and signature St. Regis Butler Service. From sweeping city views to grand marble bathrooms and curated art collections, every detail is designed to indulge. Whether hosting, relaxing, or celebrating, these suites offer an unmatched setting in the heart of New York City. 

### Presidential Suite

Spanning 3,000 square feet, the Presidential Suite is a curated haven of quiet elegance. With sweeping views of Central Park, antique accents, and plush textures, each space—from the dramatic entrance to the exquisite dining room and richly appointed office—is designed to delight. Detailed dressing rooms and magnificent baths complete this bespoke retreat above Fifth Avenue.

## Iconic Luxury in the Heart of Manhattan

Located on the 16th floor, the Presidential Suite at The St. Regis New York is the epitome of timeless grandeur.
