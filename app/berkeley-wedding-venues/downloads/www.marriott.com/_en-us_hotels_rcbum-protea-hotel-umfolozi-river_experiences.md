###  Frequently Asked Questions

Does Protea Hotel Umfolozi River have a fitness center on-site? 

No, Protea Hotel Umfolozi River does not have a fitness center on-site.

Does Protea Hotel Umfolozi River have a pool on-site? 

Yes, Protea Hotel Umfolozi River has one Outdoor pool on-site for hotel guests.

What are some local attractions and things to do near Protea Hotel Umfolozi River? 

Richards Bay Game Reserve, The Port, Isiphaphalazi Butterfly Dome, Umfolozi Golf Club, Zulu Croc and more are nearby Protea Hotel Umfolozi River.
