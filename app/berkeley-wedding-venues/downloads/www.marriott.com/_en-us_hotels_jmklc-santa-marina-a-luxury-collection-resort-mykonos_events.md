## Events

Incentive events become more inspiring and rewarding at Santa Marina, Mykonos. To host meetings and formal gatherings, Elais Restaurant provides a stunning wedding and event venue with breathtaking views of the Aegean Sea. The indoor area is 120 m² (1,291 ft²), this versatile space can accommodate up to 80 people. For outdoor entertaining, Santa Marina, Mykonos offers many spectacular possibilities, including the outdoor terrace of Elais Restaurant, the Oasis Pool & Lounge, Buddha-Bar Beach, and the Santa Marina Beach Bar. Our property also boasts vast and beautiful landscapes overlooking the Aegean, capable of hosting up to 300 guests. These flexible spaces are perfect for the most impressive and imaginative event setups.

### A Sparkling Setting for the Perfect Day

Let the glamour and sophistication of our Aegean hideaway create a magical destination for your wedding. Whether for an elopement or a magnificent celebration with up to 300 guests, Santa Marina provides the most elegantly romantic setting in all of Mykonos. Imagine your ceremony in our traditional whitewashed stone chapel, an intimate setting at the end of the peninsula. Plan your ceremony outdoors on a brilliantly green lawn, set against a breathtaking view of the Aegean. Or share your vows on our curve of golden sand, the only private beach on Mykonos. Afterwards, host your reception dinner and party in one of our beautiful restaurants or pool bars, or entertain right on the beach. Our dedicated Santa Marina wedding planners will suggest bespoke menus and musicians. Enhance your experience with spa treatments, salon appointments and plenty of time for relaxing and exploring the island – creating an unforgettable Mykonos honeymoon.
