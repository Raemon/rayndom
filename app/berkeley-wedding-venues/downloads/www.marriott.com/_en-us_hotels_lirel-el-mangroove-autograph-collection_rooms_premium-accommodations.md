# Suites and Villas 

## Premium Accommodations 

El Mangroove’s premium accommodations blend Costa Rican design, laid-back luxury, and immersive natural surroundings. Discover beachfront suites and spacious villas designed to inspire relaxation and connection. 

Indulge in the Luxury 

## Beachfront Lodging 

At El Mangroove, Autograph Collection, each room and suite is a sophisticated retreat on the Guanacaste beachfront, where contemporary luxury meets locally inspired décor. Spacious layouts are complemented by plush beds, flat-screen TVs with premium channels, and spa-like bathrooms that include luxurious amenities such as a lighted makeup mirror, robe, and walk-in shower.
