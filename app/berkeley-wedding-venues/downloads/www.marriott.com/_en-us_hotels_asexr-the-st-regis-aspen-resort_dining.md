# Savor authentic Colorado cuisine in our fine dining restaurants

## Dining

The dining experiences at The St. Regis Aspen Resort are a celebration of Colorado’s vibrant flavors and a culinary reflection of our iconic mountain destination in Aspen. Our restaurant, Velvet Buck, offers modern mountain cuisine with a contemporary twist; Mountain Social Bar & Lounge serves bespoke cocktails alongside an elevated menu; and The Snow Lodge combines fine dining with curated musical experiences. 

At This Hotel

###  Frequently Asked Questions

What restaurants does The St. Regis Aspen Resort have on property? 

The on-property restaurants at The St. Regis Aspen Resort are:

Velvet Buck Mountain Social Bar & Lounge Pool Bar & Patio The Snow Lodge

Does The St. Regis Aspen Resort offer room service? 

Yes, room service is available at The St. Regis Aspen Resort.

What cuisine is offered at The St. Regis Aspen Resort? 

The cuisines offered at The St. Regis Aspen Resort are:

Italian at The Snow Lodge American at Mountain Social Bar & Lounge, Pool Bar & Patio Multiple cuisines at Velvet Buck

How do I book a table at one of the restaurants at The St. Regis Aspen Resort? 

To book a table at Velvet Buck [click here](http://www.opentable.com/restaurant/profile/26836/reserve?restref=26836) To book a table at Mountain Social Bar & Lounge [click here](http://www.opentable.com/restaurant/profile/1083577/reserve?restref=1083577) To book a table at Pool Bar & Patio [click here](http://www.opentable.com/restaurant/profile/1083574/reserve?restref=1083574) To book a table at The Snow Lodge [click here](https://www.thesnowlodge.com/reservations) You can also call +1 970-920-3300 to place a reservation.

Does The St. Regis Aspen Resort offer complimentary breakfast? 

Complimentary breakfast is not currently served at The St. Regis Aspen Resort. View on-site and nearby dining options on our [dining page](https://www.marriott.com/en-us/hotels/asexr-the-st-regis-aspen-resort/dining/#on-site-dining).
