### Meetings and Events

The Reykjavik EDITION brings together events, cuisine, luxury service, and style in Iceland.

Each Reykjavik meeting venue is supported by cutting-edge audiovisual technology.

Events are designed by a team of experts and feature menus designed by our Executive Chef at TIDES.

Our ballroom is light-filled with floor-to-ceiling glass doors, an ideal wedding venue in Reykjavik.

Each of our three flexible Studio spaces can be combined to create one large space.

The Corner Suites and Penthouse offer unique spaces for meetings with harbor and mountain views.

For the Darker Hours 

### Sunset

Sunset is a private events space, providing a distinct venue for exclusive gatherings. It offers various amenities and features that make it suitable for a range of events. The space is equipped with an elevation platform, a projector, and facilities to accommodate DJ sets, bands, and other forms of entertainment. In addition to these features, Sunset also provides a designated area for clothing, a photo booth, and 80’s arcades.

### Signature Event and Meeting Space

The Reykjavik EDITION offers modern meeting and event spaces, including flexible studios, a boardroom with natural light, bleach oat-wide plank floorings, and a grand ballroom with pre-function space. The flexible ballroom with floor-to-ceiling glass windows can be divided into two separate spaces, while large glass doors are wide enough to accommodate a car. Within the ballroom is a hanging alabaster chandelier paired with natural felt overwraps draped around the ballroom.
