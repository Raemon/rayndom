# Dining With All Five Senses

## Dining

Thanks to its storied history and bountiful natural setting, Kyoto has a rich food culture. At HOTEL THE MITSUI KYOTO, the culinary team selects the finest ingredients from around the world and across Japan, starting with famed local Kyoto produce, to deliver the ultimate gastronomic experiences to your table. Our property consists of four dining experiences, including TOKI, our Kyoto Michelin restaurant, FORNI, THE GARDEN BAR, and SHIKI-NO-MA, all of which surround our beautifully curated garden so diners can enjoy viewing the rich tapestry of the seasons.

### Innovative Kyoto-French TOKI

Kyoto, the "Water Capital" with a thousand-year history, is named for its rivers like the Kamogawa and Katsuragawa, and is fed by surrounding mountains and abundant underground sources. This soft water is ideal for making flavorful dashi stock and growing vegetables with a rich taste and aroma, unique to Japanese culture. Chef Asano, a Parisian-trained French chef, incorporates Kyoto's pristine water into his signature French fond, a soup stock made from vegetables and meat. At our Kyoto Michelin restaurant, guests and visitors of HOTEL THE MITSUI KYOTO can experience these flavors of the Water Capital while enjoying views of the enchanting courtyard garden.

Honoring Kyoto Through a Culinary Experience

###  Kira - Destination Cocktail 

From THE GARDEN BAR, guests can relax, unwind and enjoy unparalleled views of the garden in the dusk. Our martini style cocktail "KIRA" is a unique blend of Kyoto-distilled dry gin bursting with Japanese botanicals and aged sake. This delicious concoction is presented in large glass that resembles a sakazuki sake cup and is served with a garnish of seasonal herbs. Price: JPY3,200.

###  Kinobu × HOTEL THE MITSUI KYOTO 

HOTEL THE MITSUI KYOTO collaborates with Takuji Takahashi, the third-generation owner-chef at Kinobu and a renowned expert in Kyoto cuisine. Enjoy traditional Kyoto cuisine prepared exclusively for Hotel The Mitsui Kyoto by Kinobu in an elegant and beautiful setting. Price: Lunch JPY12,000～ (Bento) JPY28,000～ (Kaiseki)Dinner JPY40,000～ (Kaiseki).

###  Grilled Bone-in Mangen Premium Pork Loin 

Mangen-ton, a celebrated brand of pork from Shizuoka Prefecture, takes center stage in our grilled dishes. Unlike most farms in Japan, these pigs are raised for around 200 days, receiving the highest quality feed to ensure exceptional flavor and tenderness.
