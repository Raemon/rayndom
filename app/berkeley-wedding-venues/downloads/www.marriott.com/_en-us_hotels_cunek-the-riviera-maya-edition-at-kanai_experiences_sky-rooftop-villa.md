# SKY ROOFTOP VILLA: North America’s Largest Hotel Penthouse

The 2,500-square-meter Sky Rooftop Villa, one of the world's largest luxury rooftop villas in Playa del Carmen, rises above the mangroves with sweeping views of the Caribbean Sea and the nature reserve below. The five-bedroom retreat boasts ample living space, terraces, and a semi-Olympic infinity pool, creating an exclusive haven.

##  Architectural Innovation Within a Nature Reserve 

The Sky Rooftop Villa, the pinnacle of luxury at The Riviera Maya EDITION at Kanai, sits on a pristine beachfront with views over a lush mangrove. Merging architectural innovation with natural beauty, this villa offers an immersive escape into a 620-acre nature reserve. Enjoy unmatched exclusivity surrounded by tropical beaches, ancient Mayan cities, and the world’s second-largest coral reef.

[ Learn More Open in New Tab ](https://www.marriott.com/en-us/hotels/cunek-the-riviera-maya-mexico-edition/rooms/)

##  Where Design Becomes Art 

This creative hotel concept embraces Ian Schrager's vision for understated elegance while showcasing the personal, intimate, and elevated experience. Under his guidance this exclusive villa was designed by global award-winning New York-based architecture practice Edmonds International, with interiors designed by the equally renowned New York-based Rockwell Group.
