# WELCOME TO W OSAKA

## Bold Osaka Hotel Suites

Embark on your personal journey in one of our 337 exquisitely crafted hotel rooms and suites in Osaka, each adorned with the vibrant and daring designs that define W. Experience the perfect blend of style and comfort as you step into spacious bathrooms featuring custom-designed rain showers, while floor-to-ceiling windows frame captivating views of Osaka’s dynamic cityscape. Elevate your stay with state-of-the-art in-room technologies, including 55-inch LED TV screens and Bluetooth speakers, providing endless entertainment options with our MixBar at your disposal, you can craft your favorite cocktails right in the comfort of your room. Unwind and rejuvenate on signature W beds in our Osaka hotel suites, ensuring a restful night's sleep amidst the bustling energy of Osaka. 

Please note: all room sizes are approximate.

### Extreme Wow Penthouse Suite

Prepare to indulge in the ultimate luxury in our expansive 200 Penthouse Suite. Perched atop the highest floor of the hotel, this lavish retreat boasts soaring 4.5-meter-high ceilings, offering a sense of grandeur like no other. Wake up to sweeping panoramic views of Osaka from five interconnected rooms, including a spacious dining room, fully stocked bar, and open kitchen ideal for hosting private soirées and exclusive events. Unwind in the inviting living/social room, adorned with plush sofas and a state-of-the-art DJ booth, creating the perfect ambiance for entertaining.

## WOW Suite

Kick back in supreme comfort and have the time of your life in superb accommodation in Osaka, Japan, boasting an expansive living area, open kitchen, and six-seater dining room. Take up residency on the top two floors overlooking the glittering Osaka skyline, so you’ll be gifted with unparalleled views of Osaka’s happening downtown and with epic panoramas of the city’s skyline through inviting wall-to-wall windows.
