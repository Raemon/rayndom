# Fantastical B&F Labyrinth

## Dining

Extraordinary flavors blend tradition with experimentation, offering an unrivaled journey to suit any mood, day or night. A myriad of venues to be discovered, each with a different experience at every turn. À la carte breakfasts, multi-course lunches, sharing plates for dinner, or handcrafted cocktails at our cocktail bar in Prague. Our authentic flavors and unique concepts have earned these gastronomic hotspots a place on the city's best-of lists. 

###  The New Social Hub 

W Prague is the city’s newest social hotspot—whether you're vibing to top DJs spinning the best beats or seeking a more intimate setting, we’ve got something for everyone. Expect weekly DJ sets setting the tone for the weekend, whether at the vibrant W Lounge, the seasonal Above rooftop, or special one-off events at Minus One. Follow us on social media to stay in the loop and never miss a moment.

###  A New Cocktail Experience Awaits 

The menu invites you into a world of immersive storytelling, where each cocktail takes you on a sensory journey—blending bold flavors with the enchanting narratives of our Fantastic Garden. Whether you opt for a reimagined classic or an experimental creation, our talented team of mixologists will draw you into their story with every sip.

###  A Destination Within a Destination 

W Prague caters to every taste and desire—whether you're in the mood for a relaxing night in with a soothing treatment at Away Spa and room service, or ready to dress up and enjoy an evening out, we’ve got you covered. Indulge in a dining experience at Le Petit Beefbar au Grand Café, soak up the upbeat tunes curated by our Music Curator at W Lounge, and cap off your night with a signature cocktail at Minus One.
