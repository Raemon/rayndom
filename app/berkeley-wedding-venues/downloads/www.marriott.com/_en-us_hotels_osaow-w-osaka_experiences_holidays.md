# Holiday Events at W Osaka

## Celebrate This Festive Season in Osaka

W Hotels made the list. Step into the magic of this festive season at W Osaka, where Japanese culture meets contemporary designs, alongside the enchanting illuminations of Midosuji Boulevard. Indulge in festive themed menus from any of our dining outlets that are curated to impress. From sweet and savory afternoon tea sets to Christmas inspired course dinner and electric beats at DJ sessions, capture the true spirit of the season here with us. Then, conclude by unwinding with special body treatments especially designed for the winter season at the AWAY Spa, or simply in one of our comfortably designed guest rooms. 

### Festive Dining Experiences 

###  W Osaka Festive Cakes 

Complete your festive experience with W Osaka's exclusive festive cakes. Elevate your table with desserts that embody sophistication and seasonal elegance.

[ Learn More ](https://mixup.wosaka.com/en/festive)

###  MIXup 

Discover a haven of appetizing indulgence at MIXup, where every afternoon tea is a curated experience of tranquility and timeless charm.

[ Learn More ](https://mixup.wosaka.com/en/)

###  LIVING ROOM 

Celebrate the art of the season in style at LIVING ROOM, where holiday cheer meets vibrant luxurious energy. For a limited time only, festive afternoon tea sets, holiday-inspired parfaits and cocktails will be serve to capture the festive season magic.

[ Learn More ](https://livingroom.wosaka.com/en/)

###  Oh.Lala... 

This holiday season, Oh.lala... invites you to a limited edition festive menu crafted to warm hearts and delight your senses. From luxuriously plated mains to seasonal sides and decadent desserts, each dish is inspired by the flavors and spirit of the season.

[ Learn More ](https://ohlala.wosaka.com/)

###  Teppanyaki MYDO 

This season, elevate your festive celebration with a teppanyaki dining experience with fresh, seasonal ingredients.

[ Learn More ](https://mydo.wosaka.com/en/)

###  Sushi UIKIYO 

Experience culinary mastery up close where each dish is crafted with precision. Discover an intimate and elevated taste of Japanese tradition in this underwater world.

[ Learn More ](https://ukiyo.wosaka.com/en/)

### Celebrate This Festive Season

Experience the perfect blend of luxury lifestyle. Our winter gallery combines cool aesthetics with warm sophistication.
