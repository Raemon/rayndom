# Discover Our Luxury Spa, Pool and Dubai Desert Experiences

## Experiences

Experience authentic Arabian charm at Al Maha, a Luxury Collection Desert Resort & Spa. From tranquil moments to desert adventures, every stay at our Dubai luxury resort is intimate, timeless, and inspired by the spirit of the desert.

### Curated Desert Journeys

Your stay at Al Maha includes two desert experiences each night, thoughtfully curated to immerse you in the beauty and heritage of Arabia. Choose to wander the dunes on a sunset camel trek, embrace the exhilaration of a dune drive, or uncover the wonders of the Dubai Desert Conservation Reserve. For those seeking more, select activities can be further elevated to create an even more unforgettable journey.

Experiences

###  Wildlife Drive

Embark on a wildlife drive through the desert landscape and discover the diverse range of indigenous fauna and flora that call this place home. From the majestic Arabian Oryx to the graceful gazelles, the desert is teeming with life. Our experienced guides will share fascinating insights about the desert ecosystem and its inhabitants and guests will learn about the conservation efforts that are underway to protect them.

## Qalb al-Ṣaḥrā: Heart of the Desert

Gather by the glow of the campfire for an evening steeped in Bedouin tradition. Guided by our Field Experts, discover the spirit of desert life through tales of caravan journeys, timeless folklore, and the rituals that shaped Arabia’s nomadic heritage. Discover how Bedouins were once guided by the constellations and stories that unfold in the night sky.

Savor Arabic coffee with dates and spiced delicacies as the scent of bokhoor drifts through the night, while stories and traditions unfold beneath a canopy of stars.
