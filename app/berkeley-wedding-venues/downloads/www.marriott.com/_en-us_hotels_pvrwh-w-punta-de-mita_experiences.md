## Experiences

Nestled within the Pacific coast in the Mexican state of Nayarit, Punta de Mita has established itself as one of the country’s premier vacation destinations. And it’s no wonder, given the area’s stunning natural beauty and countless activities and attractions, from seaside marketplaces brimming with Huichol art and crafts, to world renowned dining and nightlife and terrific outdoor adventures.

## Secluded Beach

W Punta de Mita is set along Playa Veneros, one of Riviera Nayarit’s most iconic and swimmable beaches. Just steps from your room, you’ll find the region’s top surf break; ideal for both beginners and seasoned riders. Our certified surf vendor offers private and group lessons, available by advance booking. Whether you’re chasing waves or soaking up the sun, this secluded stretch of coastline delivers the perfect mix of thrill and chill.

###  Wet Deck & Pool 

There’s no better place to soak up the Punta de Mita sun than at WET, our shimmering outdoor pool. In fact, you might just find yourself camped out here on a regular basis during your hotel stay. Dip your toes in the water as you sip a fresh-pressed juice or a signature Mojito from our Huichol-inspired WET Deck.

###  WET TOO 

The newly opened WET TOO features an infinity pool steps away from the most pristine sandy white beaches, offering the most Instagrammable shots to glam out your getaway. Covet the sea breeze of the Pacific with refreshments or turn into a local “Pata Salada” by enjoying a catch of the day at Salero. 

Puerto Vallarta 

Stroll the colonial streets of this fascinating town and browse the shops for a memento of your time in Mexico. Puerto Vallarta is only 50 minutes from Punta Mita, making it perfect for day trips.

San Sebastian 

Journey into the Sierra Madre Mountains, and discover an unspoiled world, rich in history and culture. Once a mining town, San Sebastián is home to the Huichol people, one of the world’s last remaining indigenous tribes.

The Marietas Islands 

Nature lovers will be enchanted by these majestic, uninhabited islands, which were designated a national park in order to maintain their diverse marine and bird population. It’s a wonderful spot for whale watching, snorkeling, and scuba diving.

Sayulita 

This bohemian town is home to amazing surfing, one-of-a-kind local art, and funky boutiques. Stop by one of the many delicious eateries that dot the coast after a day of catching the waves. Or challenge yourself to a hike up Monkey Mountain.

Jungle Excursions 

Looking for an adrenaline rush, or simply a great workout in the Great Outdoors? Hike, zip line, or mountain bike along lush jungle trails.

Hotel Activity Highlights

Let’s Go Surfing 

Amplify your getaway with the best waves in the Mexican Pacific. For intermediate, beginner and pros, Punta de Mita has options for those who want to venture into surfing with several places to dominate the waves on short or longboards and on stand-up Paddleboards. 

Walk down from your room to our fast-breaking beach waves, with left and right rides, or head to the mellow reef break nearby at La Lancha, located 10 minutes by car from the resort. 

Plan a session of fast rides guided by professional and friendly instructors from Punta Mita Adventures, with equipment and rentals available on property.

Find Zen in the Jungle 

Reset body and soul with the outdoor sessions offered by AWAY spa. Maintain your workout routine at any of the relaxing classes: yoga nidra, singing bowl meditation, indoor cycling, available for guests through the spa.
