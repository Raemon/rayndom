## Wellness Retreats

Amidst breathtaking landscapes, you will explore a wellness journey. The tranquil energy of the mangrove, harmonizing with the serene sounds of local fauna, forms the perfect sanctuary for meditation. Enhance your retreat with our daily yoga sessions, tailored to all levels, offering gentle movements and dynamic practices for a profound connection. Participants can also partake in nature bike rides, kayaking, and paddleboarding, enriching their experience with engaging physical activities.

###  Jet Ski Tour 

Sea Doo wave runners are the perfect toy to cruise the Papagayo Peninsula with comfort and style. Get to discover the Great Pacific Ocean like never before in this experience full of adrenaline and fun. Our crew will take you to secluded beaches and mangroves where you can appreciate nature with a different perspective.

###  Half Day Ocean Experience 

In this 4-hour tour you will be able to enjoy the great Papagayo Peninsula with the best accommodations on board. Immerse yourself in the warm Waters of the great Pacific Ocean and take advantage of all the activities we have to offer. Our boats and crew are ready to take you and your loved ones on a life changing experience where you will feel connected to nature and its beauty.

###  Full Day Ocean Experience 

During this 8-hour tour you will be able to experience a more exclusive and unrepeatable connection with the ocean. During this getaway you can expect unique ocean encounters with whales, dolphins, turtles, devil rays among other incredible sea creatures ending the day sipping a glass of wine while watch the sun set over the Pacific Ocean.

###  VIP Fishing Experience 

During this tour you will experience a unique fishing tour in the biggest boat of our fleet. Get ready to get some mahi mahi, tuna, sailfish, snappers, rooster fish, amberjack, or wahoo among other species. This tour Includes beverages, snacks, fruits and lunch (full day).

###  Bioluminescence & 3-Hour Tour Stargazing 

This night adventure we will be heading far out from civilization where there is no light or other disturbance that might affect our sight. Once we find the perfect spot, we will start identifying the different constellations around and hopefully watch some shooting stars. Afterwards we will be heading to some unique locations where we will get to see bioluminescence and enjoy of a perfect night on board.
