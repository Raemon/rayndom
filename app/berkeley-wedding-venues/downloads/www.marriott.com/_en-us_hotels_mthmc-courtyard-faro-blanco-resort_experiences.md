Activities & Experiences in Marathon, Florida

###  Resort Experiences 

Treat yourself to classic s’mores by the fire, catch a movie in our indoor-outdoor cinema room, or retreat to our indoor game lounge for some friendly competition. There’s fun for everyone, no matter your mood!

###  Local Experiences 

Venture beyond the resort and discover some of the Florida Keys' most iconic spots. Visit the Turtle Hospital to learn about conservation efforts and meet the rescued sea turtles. Head to Bahia Honda State Park for breathtaking views, sandy beaches, and incredible snorkeling. Spend a relaxing day at Sombrero Beach, known for its crystal-clear waters, or take a scenic walk along the historic Old 7 Mile Bridge.

###  Blue Waters Marina 

Planning to arrive by water? Secure your spot and book a slip at Blue Waters Marina, conveniently located just steps from the resort. Whether you're here for a short stay or planning to explore the Keys by boat, our marina offers easy access to the open waters and a perfect place to dock while you enjoy all the amenities we have to offer.

[ Learn More ](https://dockwa.com/explore/destination/55cpldp-courtyard-faro-blanco-resort-marina?form=transient)

###  Family Fun 

Enjoy some outdoor fun on our game lawn, where you can challenge friends to a variety of lawn games. Take advantage of our complimentary bike rentals to explore the area at your own pace, or simply unwind and play in the sand on our sunning beach, soaking up the sun and the beautiful surroundings. There’s no shortage of ways to relax and enjoy your time here!

Explore Near the Resort

###  Turtle Hospital 

The Turtle Hospital is a working hospital facility focused on the rescue, rehabilitation, and release of sea turtles. Just a short walk from the resort, it offers informative tours where guests can learn about sea turtle conservation efforts and see the animals up close. Perfect for nature lovers and families.

[ Learn More ](https://www.turtlehospital.org/)

###  Dolphin Research Center 

The Dolphin Research Center is dedicated to the study and conservation of dolphins and other marine mammals. Visitors are invited to participate in interactive experiences as they learn about dolphin behavior, training, and conservation efforts.

[ Learn More ](https://dolphins.org/)

###  Old Seven Mile Bridge and Pigeon Key 

Offering stunning views of the surrounding waters, the Old Seven Mile Bridge is a must-see for history and nature enthusiasts. A short walk or bike ride away, you'll find Pigeon Key, a small island rich in history, once home to workers during the construction of the Florida Keys Over-Sea Railroad. Explore the island’s historic buildings, learn about its fascinating past, and enjoy beautiful views.

[ Learn About Pigeon Key ](https://pigeonkey.net/)

###  Bahia Honda State Park 

Bahia Honda State Park is a tropical paradise known for its pristine beaches, crystal-clear waters, and stunning nature trails. Perfect for swimming, snorkeling, kayaking, or simply relaxing, the park offers a variety of outdoor activities set against a backdrop of lush landscapes and scenic views.

[ Explore Bahia ](https://www.floridastateparks.org/bahiahonda/)

###  Sombrero Beach 

Sombrero Beach, located just a short drive or bike ride away from the resort, is one of the Florida Keys' most beautiful and serene beaches. With its soft white sand, clear turquoise waters, and calm atmosphere, it's perfect for swimming, sunbathing, picnicking, and enjoying a peaceful day by the sea. The beach also features picnic tables, restrooms, and a playground, making it an ideal spot for families.

[ Learn More ](https://www.ci.marathon.fl.us/parksrec/page/sombrero-beach)

###  Day Trips to Key West 

Visiting Key West is an unforgettable adventure with a vibrant mix of history, culture, & natural beauty. Home to famous landmarks like Ernest Hemingway's House and the Southernmost Point Buoy, guests can explore the lively streets filled with unique shops, cafes, and art galleries. Whether you're indulging in fresh seafood, enjoying watersports, or soaking in a beautiful sunset, there is something for everyone.

[ Visit Key West ](https://keywest.com/)
