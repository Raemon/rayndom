# A World of Discovery Awaits

Inspire a lifelong sense of wonder at these hotels and resorts where exploration lasts from morning to night. 

![Resort Exterior Plaza  ](https://cache.marriott.com/is/image/marriotts7prod/rz-tfsrz-resort-exterior-plz-14947-96725)

The Ritz-Carlton, Abama

A bastion of beauty in Tenerife, The Ritz-Carlton, Abama beckons well-traveled families with its immersive kids programming and expansive family suites. Whale-watching, zip-lining and walking tours are only a few of the activities on offer — each one an opportunity for young travelers to explore, grow and see the world through a broader lens, all within the comfort of a five-star escape. 

[Explore Abama](https://www.ritzcarlton.com/en/hotels/tfsrz-the-ritz-carlton-abama/overview/)

![suite swimming pool](https://cache.marriott.com/is/image/marriotts7prod/lc-chqlc-tropical-family-gran21098-48133)

Domes Zeen Chania, a Luxury Collection Resort, Crete

Tucked in a serene cove along Crete’s sun-drenched coast, Domes Zeen Chania has mastered the art of catering to families without sacrificing an ounce of style. Its ultra-chic, Montessori-inspired kids club goes beyond playtime, offering curated experiences that encourage curiosity, creativity and a deeper connection to the world around them. 

[Explore Domes Zeen Chania](https://www.marriott.com/en-us/hotels/chqlc-domes-zeen-chania-a-luxury-collection-resort-crete/overview/)

![Aerial view of hotel](https://cache.marriott.com/is/image/marriotts7prod/rz-sbarz-ritz-carlton-bacara-47-14648)

The Ritz-Carlton Bacara, Santa Barbara 

Set on 78 acres above the Pacific, this California resort invites families to explore the Gaviota Coast’s natural wonders with a mindful approach to sustainability. Spacious villas, Ritz Kids programming and a private beach combine for a family getaway that connects young travelers with nature — and the importance of preserving it. 

[Explore Bacara](https://www.ritzcarlton.com/en/hotels/sbarz-the-ritz-carlton-bacara-santa-barbara/overview/)

![longboat key beach](https://cache.marriott.com/is/image/marriotts7prod/xr-srqls-key-beach-28410)

The St. Regis Longboat Key

Famed for its pristine white-sand beaches and calm, warm waters, Longboat Key is a true paradise for families. Dive into the vibrant underwater world with the resort’s exclusive Under the Sea Lagoon Experiences, guided by expert marine aquarists. Interact with gentle rays, discover tropical marine life and enjoy a serene, family-friendly environment perfect for explorers of all skill levels. 

[Explore Longboat Key](https://www.marriott.com/en-us/hotels/srqls-the-st-regis-longboat-key-resort/overview/)

![Family Dining at Sear + Sea](https://cache.marriott.com/is/image/marriotts7prod/jw-mcojb-family-dining-38692)

JW Marriott Orlando Bonnet Creek 

Located within the gates of Walt Disney World, this Orlando resort offers seamless access to the city's iconic theme parks. With fine dining, a serene spa and poolside cabanas, it’s the perfect retreat for parents and kids alike. The rooftop terrace adds an extra touch of magic, offering nightly views of the spectacular Disney fireworks. 

[Explore Bonnet Creek](https://www.marriott.com/en-us/hotels/mcojb-jw-marriott-orlando-bonnet-creek-resort-and-spa/overview/)

![Aerial view of The Ritz-Carlton Amelia Island pool](https://cache.marriott.com/is/image/marriotts7prod/rz-jaxam-the-ritz-carlton-amelia-1-12312)

The Ritz-Carlton, Amelia Island

With the Atlantic Ocean as your playground, the opportunities for family fun are endless. From surfing and kayaking to beachcombing and bike riding, there’s something for everyone at this oceanfront resort. Part exploration, part discovery and personally designed for every age, the resort’s ecology field trips allow kids to get up close and personal with the island’s unique ecosystems. 

[Explore Amelia Island](https://www.ritzcarlton.com/en/hotels/jaxam-the-ritz-carlton-amelia-island/overview/)

![Lifestyle](https://cache.marriott.com/is/image/marriotts7prod/lc-sjdlc-solaz-lifestyle-121-69247)

Solaz, a Luxury Collection Resort, Los Cabos

From the kid-centric cooking classes and dedicated teen club to the four Olympic-sized pools and that incomparable Baja coastline, boredom is quickly banished upon arrival at this luxe Los Cabos beach resort. At the Cabo Nautas Kid’s Club, children are free to play and experience a space all their own under the watchful attention of expert childcare providers. 

[Explore Solaz](https://www.marriott.com/luxury/explore-luxury-suites/solaz-los-cabos)

![WET Deck Pool Day View](https://cache.marriott.com/is/image/marriotts7prod/wh-sinwh-wet-deck-day-view-32217)

W Singapore - Sentosa Cove 

Magic abounds from the moment you step inside this whimsical hotel. Design details and modern art combine with lush greenery for a sensorial experience sure to delight travelers of all ages. Cool off day or night at the sprawling outdoor pool with views of the sparkling South China Sea; buzz for service and have sips and snacks delivered to your complimentary private cabana.

[Explore Sentosa Cove](https://www.marriott.com/en-us/hotels/sinwh-w-singapore-sentosa-cove/overview/)

![FAMILY BY JW](https://cache.marriott.com/is/image/marriotts7prod/jw-cjuju-family-garden-12450)

JW Marriott Jeju Resort

Perched atop a cliff on South Korea’s stunning Jeju Island, JW Marriott Jeju Resort offers a tranquil retreat immersed in the island’s rich cultural heritage. Families can enjoy a mix of serene moments and thrilling adventures, while kids get to engage in enriching activities at the Kids Camp, including cooking classes that highlight local cuisine. 

[Explore Jeju Resort](https://www.marriott.com/en-us/hotels/cjuju-jw-marriott-jeju-resort-and-spa/overview/)
