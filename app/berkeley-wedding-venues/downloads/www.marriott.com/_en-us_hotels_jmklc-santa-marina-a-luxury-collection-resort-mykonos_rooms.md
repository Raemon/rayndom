# Immerse Into Divine Luxury

## Accommodations

Santa Marina offers 105 5-star guest rooms and suites, along with 12 luxury villas featuring two to seven bedrooms. Fully renovated in 2017, the accommodations feature an organic, modern style by celebrated Greek interior designer Silvina Macipe Krontiras. Interiors feature light tones with natural island accents of wood and raffia, enhanced by brilliant blue views of the Aegean Sea.

Villa experience & half day cruise offer

Immerse Yourself in Villa Luxury and Set Sail on a Half Day Cruise. Discover the true essence of luxury by staying at one of the Santa Marina Villas, offering an unparalleled experience of refined living in Mykonos.

###  Resort Room 

These charming guest rooms are ideal for exploring the resort and Mykonos Island. Design interiors offer comfort & luxury with a strong sense of personal service and amenities. All feature front or side sea view from the balcony or terrace, furnished with outdoor dining for two and where space allows, includes sunlounges. Sleeps two, in one king or queen bed or two twins. En-suite bathroom with walk-in shower. 

###  Superior Room 

These elegant guest rooms are ideal for exploring the resort and Mykonos Island. Design interiors offer comfort and luxury with a strong sense of personal service and amenities. All feature seamless sea views from the balcony or terrace, furnished with outdoor dining for two and where space allows, includes sunlounges. Sleeps two, in one king or queen bed or two twins. En-suite bathroom with walk-in shower.

###  Deluxe Room 

Timeless, opulent and beautiful, our flagship guest rooms provide the ideal combination of space, majestic sea views & location within the resort. Design interiors offer comfort and luxury with a strong sense of personal service and amenities. All feature seamless sea views from the balcony or terrace, furnished with outdoor dining and where space allows, includes sunlounges. Sleeps 2, in one king or two twins. 

###  Resort Suite 

With sparkling vistas of the Aegean, these sumptuous, designed suites are open concept in layout, combining the living and sleeping areas. All feature seamless sea views from the balcony or terrace, furnished with outdoor dining for two and sunlounges. Sleeps two, in one king or two twins. The sofa can be turned into an extra bed. En-suite marble bathroom with walk-in shower, some with whirl tub. 

###  Luxury Suite 

These luscious sea view suites are perfect for those who wish to have the living room separated from the bedroom for privacy or additional space. All feature seamless sea views from the balcony or terrace, furnished with outdoor dining for two and sunlounges. Sleeps two, in one king or two twins. The sofa can be turned into an extra bed. En-suite marble bathroom with walk-in shower and whirl tub. 

###  Family Retreat 

Families particularly enjoy this charming & elegant two-level suite combining space with privacy. Beautiful panorama sea views characterize its advantageous location. The ground level features a sofa for seating, the master bedroom with en-suite bathroom. The 2nd bedroom features 2 twin beds, its own en-suite bathroom & a private balcony. Sleeps up to 5, in 1 king bed & 2 twin beds, plus sofa bed.
