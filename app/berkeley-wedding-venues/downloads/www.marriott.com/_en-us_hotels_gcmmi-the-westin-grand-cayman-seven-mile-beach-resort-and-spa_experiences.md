## Experiences

Tucked away in the western Caribbean Sea, just 35 minutes from Miami. The Westin Grand Cayman sits on Grand Cayman— the largest of the three picturesque Cayman Islands. Our central location, just minutes from the airport, makes travel seamless. Nearby, discover can’t-miss attractions like Camana Bay and local shopping and dining. On-site, Red Sail Sports offers water-based adventures steps from your door. Whether diving into turquoise waters or exploring vibrant marine life, Grand Cayman is a paradise for active explorers and those seeking rejuvenation.

### Adult and Childrens Activities

From sunrise beach yoga and paddleboard clinics to evening s’mores by the fire, there’s something for everyone. Enjoy morning meditation, family game nights, water fitness classes, and exciting kids’ scavenger hunts. Some activities may require a 24-hour sign-up or carry an additional fee —check the schedule during your stay to plan your perfect day.

![Weddings](data:image/png;base64,iVBORw0KGgoAAAANSUhEUgAAAA4AAAAKCAYAAACE2W/HAAAAFklEQVR42mN8/fpLPQMZgHFU48jWCAD/vyDle0zFdgAAAABJRU5ErkJggg==)

Azure Coastal Retreat 

Seven Mile Beach is famed for its crystal-clear aquamarine waters and endless opportunities for water sports. Towering Australian pines line the shoreline, offering shade and a picturesque backdrop. With water temperatures averaging a warm 80°F (27°C) in winter, it’s perfect for year-round swimming, adventure, or simply soaking up the sun.

![Seven Mile Beach](data:image/png;base64,iVBORw0KGgoAAAANSUhEUgAAAA4AAAAKCAYAAACE2W/HAAAAFklEQVR42mN8/fpLPQMZgHFU48jWCAD/vyDle0zFdgAAAABJRU5ErkJggg==)

Endless Mind-blowing Fun 

With so many activities to choose from, a day on Seven Mile Beach can be as exhilarating or relaxing as you want it to be.

![Luxury Cabanas](data:image/png;base64,iVBORw0KGgoAAAANSUhEUgAAAA4AAAAKCAYAAACE2W/HAAAAFklEQVR42mN8/fpLPQMZgHFU48jWCAD/vyDle0zFdgAAAABJRU5ErkJggg==)

Beach Adventure Awaits 

Whether you enjoy just relaxing and watching the waves roll in or taking your vacation up a notch and parasailing over the Caribbean Sea, Seven Mile Beach has what you're looking for.

Exciting & Thrilling Activities

Adventure Seekers 

#### Kittiwake Wreck & Amazing Wall Scuba Diving

Cayman Islands Kittiwake shipwreck is designated a national Cayman Islands park and attraction managed by the Cayman Islands Tourism Association.

#### Atlantis Submarines

The Atlantis Submarine is a 48 passenger fully submerged excursion. The excursion takes place in the magnificent Grand Cayman underwater world.

#### Jet Ski Fishing

Jetski Safari and Fishing Tours offers action combined with 3 of Grand Cayman’s most popular water sports activities. Stingray Sandbar, Snorkeling, Beach stops with a bonus Rum Point or Kaibo visit.

#### Sea trek

An underwater experience that is ideal for swimmers and non-swimmers alike. Special equipment allows you to walk and breathe underwater while taking in the beautiful surrounding aquatic life.

#### Nautilus Semi-Submersible

Nautilus is an intrinsic part of Grand Cayman’s unique attractions; offering unforgettable underwater tours in the Cayman Islands. Nautilus offers adventures of a lifetime in their state of the art vessels.

Stand-Up Paddle Board Clinic 

The fast-growing sport of stand-up paddleboarding (SUP) is a fun and easy way to play on the water. Paddleboarding offers an amazing full body workout and since you’re standing at your full height you will enjoy an amazing view of Seven Mile Beach, reefs, marine life and whatever is passing our beautiful horizon. It’s the closest thing to walking on the water!

George Town 

The capital of the Cayman Islands, in the British West Indies. The city has a population of approximately 35,600 and is the largest city on Grand Cayman.

Couples Escapes 

Enjoy a romantic getaway with your partner.

#### Sunset Dinner Cruise

Dine under a thousand Caribbean stars on this memorable and romantic evening organized by Red Sail Sports.

#### Pampered Ponies

Walk, trot and canter along the stunning beaches and beach trails of Grand Cayman.

#### Tortuga Rum Factory

Founded in 1984, Tortuga Rum Company has grown to become the largest duty-free liquor business in the Cayman Islands. Visit the factory to sample their products, including renowned rum cake.

Red Sail Sports 

Red Sail Sports is Grand Cayman’s premier dive operator with 28 years serving divers in the Cayman Islands. We welcome you to dive some of the most pristine reefs in the Caribbean Sea. Grand Cayman’s aquatic playground is thrilling above water too, why not cruise around in a kayak or get up to speed on a waverunner? Our fully stocked watersports centers offer activities to suit the whole family.

Local Attractions

Fun for the Whole Family 

Explore the island with family-friendly activities such as dolphin encounters, Cayman Karting, and bicycle tours.

Camana Bay 

Discover Camana Bay—a lively waterfront town where you can satisfy your foodie cravings, shop for all things local and explore our weekly Farmers and Artisans Market. Swing lazily on a hammock, climb the 75-foot Observation Tower or be delighted by the sound of kids splashing in the fountains. It all happens only a short walk from Seven Mile Beach.

Mastic Reserve and Trail 

The Mastic Reserve on Grand Cayman protects part of the largest contiguous area of untouched, old growth dry forest remaining on the island. This area and other similar expanses of forest in Cayman are of international significance representing some of the last remaining examples of the Caribbean’s dry, subtropical, semi deciduous dry forest, which have been the target of particularly intense deforestation throughout the West Indies.

Boatswain's Beach 

Boatswain’s Beach is the new home of over 16,000 turtles of the Cayman Turtle Farm and promises to take your breath away with its fabulous arrays of turtles. A journey of discovery awaits you in this beautiful marine park with all the other exciting marine adventures. Enjoy everything from nature trails to a snorkeling lagoon, as well as a bird aviary, iguanas and other predator tanks, shopping arcades, and much more!

Stingray City 

Stingray City is 15 feet deep, where you can swim along and hand feed an array of awe-inspiring stingrays. Enjoy the Stingray Sandbar – only waist-deep – you can use a mask and snorkeling gear to watch the rays in more shallow water.

Queen Elizabeth II Botanical Park 

The Botanical Park is a must-see with its natural beauty of the Cayman Islands. Stroll through its 65 acres of land inhabited by iguanas, native migratory birds and other indigenous creatures. Also experience the large floral garden and a variety of exotic plant life.
