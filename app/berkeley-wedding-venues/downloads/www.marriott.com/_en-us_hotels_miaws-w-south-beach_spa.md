# Relax and Rejuvenate at Our Spa in South Beach, Miami

## AWAY Spa

Located within W South Beach, AWAY Spa is a 9,540-square-foot sanctuary for beauty, wellness, and renewal. This spa hotel in Miami Beach offers a curated menu of advanced treatments led by expert practitioners—from custom massages to results-driven skincare and holistic body therapies. 

## You’re Invited

Located on the fourth floor of W South Beach, AWAY Spa is a calming escape with multiple treatment rooms and a lush outdoor basking terrace. Unwind in the spa lounge or enjoy sensory touches like eucalyptus-infused steam showers. Each treatment room features Bluetooth connectivity, allowing guests to personalize their sound experience. Designed for restoration and renewal, our spa hotel in Miami Beach offers a fully immersive wellness experience.

## Therapeutic Treatments

At AWAY Spa South Beach, treatments are tailored with intention—each one crafted to meet individual needs through personalized rituals that go beyond the expected. From detox massages that support natural cleansing, to HydraFacials designed for deep renewal and refined skin, the approach is elevated, not overdone. Enhancements like CBD-infused Muscle Rescue and Himalayan Hot Stones offer targeted relief and metabolic support. Select services include access to a next-generation massage chair—ideal for a focused reset in under an hour.

## The Ultimate Bespoke Couples Massage

This couple’s massage in South Beach, Miami offers a serene side-by-side experience inside a private suite, featuring an 80-minute massage and 80-minute facial. Treatments are followed by time in a eucalyptus-infused steam shower with mineral-rich scrubs and muds. Afterward, couples can unwind with lunch and champagne on the spa terrace and leave with a curated AWAY Spa gift.

## Ahana Yoga

Set on the sands of Miami Beach, the Ahana Yoga pop-up studio brings mindful movement to the shoreline. Created by renowned yogi Dawn Feinberg, the program offers a balanced mix of breathwork, postures, and guided relaxation—designed for all levels. W’s Signature Power & Restore Flow is offered Monday–Wednesday at 7:30 AM and Thursday–Sunday at 9:00 AM. To book, call 305.938.3123.
