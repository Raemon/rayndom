## Dining

Delight your palate and nourish your body with a wealth of dining choices at The Westin Reserva Conchal, an All-Inclusive Golf Resort & Spa in Costa Rica. Indulge in the flavors of the world at our resort's contemporary restaurants. From Latin fare to Asian fusion, Continental to northern Italian specialties, our diverse range of world-class dining options is sure to stimulate your imagination and fulfill your cravings, while our lounges and bars beckon you with refreshing drinks and snacks. We are also pleased to offer 24-hour room service. Our all-inclusive package includes our free à la carte breakfasts, as well as lunch, dinner, snacks and beverages, both alcoholic and non-alcoholic, from any of our bars, pool bars and restaurants. Please note that dinner reservations are required at all of our Costa Rica resort's à la carte restaurants.

What to Expect

As part of Marriott International, we have implemented a multi-pronged approach to meet the health and safety requirements of our "Commitment to Clean" standards. This may affect some of our restaurant's hours of operation. Review the most updated information on what to expect during your stay.

[Learn More](https://whattoexpect.marriott.com/lirwi)
