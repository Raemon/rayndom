## Accommodations

Take in breathtaking panoramic sea views from all of the 145 contemporary rooms and suites at our resort in Los Cabos. Each room is exquisitely designed with local Mexican huanacaxtle wood and marble textures, outfitted with intelligent technology to provide access to everything at a finger touch. Select rooms and suites also showcase private plunge pools.

### Entertainment Haven

Distinguished art decor and exceptional amenities make our Villa Solaz a space for memorable moments. Immerse yourself in a world of delicate opulence celebrating the modern Mexican luxury with enchanting views and the extraordinary Artisan service.

![Guestroom ](data:image/png;base64,iVBORw0KGgoAAAANSUhEUgAAAA4AAAAKCAYAAACE2W/HAAAAFklEQVR42mN8/fpLPQMZgHFU48jWCAD/vyDle0zFdgAAAABJRU5ErkJggg==)

###  Three Bedroom Residence 

Experience the pinnacle of coastal luxury in our Three Bedroom Ocean View Residence. This expansive retreat offers breathtaking ocean views, and an array of exceptional features designed for ultimate comfort and elegance, with a touch of hyperpersonalization to cater to your every need. Enjoy spacious living areas, including a separate living and dining room, ideal for both relaxation and entertainment.

[ Virtual Tour ](https://my.matterport.com/show/?m=RaQPCizKouT)

###  Villa Solaz Four Bedrooms 

Villa Solaz redefines spacious luxury, offering an expansive 10,645 sq. ft. of refined living space and four private bedrooms, each meticulously designed to create an exquisite atmosphere for elevated gatherings. This extraordinary villa embodies the art of hyperpersonalization, where every detail is meticulously curated to enhance your stay.

[ Virtual Tour ](https://my.matterport.com/show/?m=Q9GKrLPB7jp)

###  Wellness Suite 

Immerse yourself in a sanctuary of relaxation and holistic well-being in our Wellness Suite. Designed with your total rejuvenation in mind, this suite offers two distinct experiences. The Wellness Suite features a bed massage table and a private steam room, creating a serene environment for deep relaxation and tranquility.

[ Virtual Tour ](https://my.matterport.com/show/?m=s29pFiGAyVK)

###  One Bedroom Plunge Pool Suite 

Enter to a serene private patio that invites immediate relaxation. Inside, you'll find an elegant dining area and a spacious living room, blending comfort with style. Step onto your private terrace to discover a plunge pool with stunning Sea of Cortez views. The bathroom features a unique outdoor shower option, enhancing your connection with nature. Every detail is crafted for a tranquil, opulent escape. 

[ Virtual Tour ](https://tours.senserievirtualmedia.com/tour/solaz-a-luxury-collection-resort-los-cabos-suites)

###  One Bedroom Ocean View Suite 

With breathtaking views of the Sea of Cortez, this suite is a masterpiece of sophisticated Mexican design adorned with opulent furnishings that invite you to relax and unwind. Step onto your private balcony with a hammock for relaxation and an outdoor hot tub. The suite includes a spacious living room, a refined dining area, and a stylish bar, blending elegance with modern comfort.

[ Virtual Tour ](https://tours.senserievirtualmedia.com/tour/solaz-a-luxury-collection-resort-los-cabos-suites1)

###  Oasis Room King Bed 

Unwind in the tranquil comfort of our Oasis Room. Featuring a private balcony with ocean views, this room invites you to relax in a hammock while enjoying the beautiful landscaping surroundings. The Oasis Room provides a serene retreat, where you can soak in the peaceful atmosphere and the natural beauty that surrounds you.

[ Virtual Tour ](https://my.matterport.com/show/?m=FwGpBLXqGbp)
