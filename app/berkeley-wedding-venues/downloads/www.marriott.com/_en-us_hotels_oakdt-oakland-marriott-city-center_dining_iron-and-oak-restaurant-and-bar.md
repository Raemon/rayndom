## Discover Culinary Delights at the Iron & Oak Restaurant and Bar

With an eye on fresh, flavorful fare, our spirited restaurant menu delights the senses. Locally sourced, organic ingredients are handcrafted with a Bay Area culinary perspective.

Evoking Oakland's unique sensibilities through a crafted menu, Iron & Oak is our stylish on-site restaurant serving breakfast, lunch and dinner daily. American and global-inspired dishes, regionally sourced products and local, organic ingredients unite to provide guests and Oakland residents a destination for exceptional cuisine in the city center. 

We are proud of our relationship with local purveyors to create and serve fresh, tasty dishes:

  * Drakes Brewing Company
  * Holy Gelato
  * Mary's Chicken
  * Brandt Beef
  * Cowgirl Creamery
  * The Posh Bakery



Enjoy our bar offerings of handcrafted cocktails and wine selections. Sample the special offerings of local beer flights, featuring Bear Republic Brewing Company, Anchor Brewing Company and other local breweries. Watch live sports or catch up with friends while sipping on some local spirits, featuring Hangar 1 Distillery and St. George Spirits.
