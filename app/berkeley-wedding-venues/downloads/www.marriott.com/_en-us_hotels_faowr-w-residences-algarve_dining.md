# Wine and Dine in Our Albufeira Restaurants

## Dining

W Algarve serves up a bold and diverse dining journey, where every venue offers a distinct flavor and vibe. At Market Kitchen, southern Portuguese cuisine is reimagined with playful modern twists, from Algarvian seafood cataplana to chef-selected sushi. Poolside at WET Deck, SIP @ WET offers nutritious bites and refreshing cocktails in the sun. For refined indulgence, Vino delights wine lovers with a curated cellar, artisanal cheeses, Iberian charcuterie, and golden-hour charm. Perched on a rooftop with sweeping ocean views, Papermoon Terrace is our award-winning Albufeira, Italian restaurant that delivers Italian cuisine in an elegant Mediterranean setting. AIR, the boho-chic cliffside lounge, blends Mediterranean bites, craft cocktails, and live music with breathtaking views. Whether you’re after petiscos and cocktails at W Lounge or a sophisticated pairing at Vino, dining at W Algarve is a sensory experience designed to surprise and satisfy. 

Contemporary Classics

## Market Kitchen Algarve

Created with a neighborhood ambience in mind, Market Kitchen invites guests to indulge in southern Portuguese cuisine with a modern flair. Discover relaxed, playful interpretations of Portuguese cuisine and international favorites that are both farm and ocean fresh. Highlights include the traditional Algarvian Sea Food Cataplana, Grilled Local Octopus, Whole Roasted Cauliflower, and Suckling Pig. Besides this unique selection of main dishes is also possible enjoying amazing grilled meats and a chef’s selection of Sushi for an unforgettable, interactive dining experience.

## Vino

Vino introduces a distinctive gastronomic experience to W Algarve, built around an exclusive wine list curated by our Master Sommelier, featuring standout regional, Portuguese, and international labels.

Designed for discerning wine lovers and curious epicureans, VINO offers over 200 international wines – 40 available by the glass – alongside a curated collection of artisan cheeses, Iberian charcuterie, upscale tinned fish, and fresh oysters, all thoughtfully chosen to complement the perfect pour. With its elegant setting and golden-hour glow, Vino is also the ideal spot to unwind and indulge as the sun sets over the Algarve coast.

### A New Destination for Taste Makers

W Algarve brings a bold new flavor to the southern coast, blending global inspiration with local flair. At Market Kitchen, a relaxed atmosphere meets playful creativity as southern Portuguese cuisine is reimagined with fresh ingredients, modern touches and Algarve wines. Overlooking the resort, Paper Moon brings the rhythm with sweeping Atlantic views. Sister to Milan’s Paper Moon Giardino, it serves award-winning Italian classics in a refined yet relaxed setting, while Paper Moon Rooftop Bar becomes the ideal spot for sunset drinks. For wine lovers, Vino offers an intimate escape with over 200 labels - 40 by the glass - paired with cheeses, Iberian charcuterie and premium tinned fish. As the sun sets, AIR invites you to embrace clifftop views, Mediterranean flavors and creative spritzes on our open-air deck. By day, SIP@WET keeps the energy flowing with cocktails and ocean-inspired plates. When night falls, W Lounge becomes the place to meet, mingle and make your own scene.
