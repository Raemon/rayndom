# Hotels in Bali, Indonesia

Bali is a magical island that combines the beauty of nature with a rich cultural heritage, where ancient traditions and warm hospitality are as alluring as the pristine beaches and verdant rice terraces. Whether you’re looking for a peaceful yoga retreat, the excitement of surfing in the morning, or the calm of a quiet beach, Bali offers a wide range of experiences. Marriott Bonvoy’s collection of hotels in Bali are conveniently located to provide easy access to the island’s top attractions, from the iconic Uluwatu Temple to the bustling markets of Ubud, ensuring that every moment of your stay is both comfortable and unforgettable.

  


Savor the city’s renowned cuisine, from fresh seafood by the coast to traditional Balinese dishes. Many of our hotels offer complimentary breakfast, so you can start your day with a delicious meal before you head out to explore. Whether you’re planning a romantic getaway, a family vacation, or a business trip, the amenities and services at Marriott Bonvoy’s Bali hotels, including spacious conference facilities and state-of-the-art fitness centers, cater to every need, making your stay truly unforgettable.

Bali is a tropical paradise that calls to nature enthusiasts with its verdant landscapes and vibrant ecosystems. You can explore the awe-inspiring volcanic summits of Mount Agung and Mount Batur, with their hiking trails that lead to breathtaking vistas and the chance to witness the most stunning sunrises. The city's lush rainforests, like those found in the West Bali National Park, are home to a rich variety of wildlife, including rare species such as the Bali Starling. For a more serene escape, the Tirta Gangga Water Palace awaits, with its beautifully landscaped gardens and tranquil water features creating a haven of peace.

If you're drawn to the water, Bali's beaches and crystal-clear waters are ideal for a variety of activities, from swimming and surfing to snorkeling. Discover the secluded coves of Padang Padang or the laid-back shores of Canggu for a day of pure relaxation. The city's coastal areas also offer more adventurous pursuits, like scuba diving and paddleboarding. For a touch of culture, the Uluwatu Temple, dramatically perched on a cliff, is the perfect spot to take in the sunset and learn about Bali's rich heritage.

The culture in Bali is a vibrant tapestry woven from centuries of tradition and spiritual practice, making it a unique and enchanting destination for travelers seeking a deeper connection with the local way of life. Marriott Bonvoy’s accommodations in Bali offer a gateway to this rich heritage, with many hotels featuring authentic Balinese architecture and design elements that reflect the island's artistic and spiritual essence. Guests can easily immerse themselves in the local culture by attending a traditional dance performance, exploring ancient temples like Tanah Lot, or participating in a Balinese cooking class to learn the secrets of the island’s flavorful cuisine. After a day of cultural exploration, unwind in the serene surroundings of your hotel, where the tranquil atmosphere and luxurious amenities provide the perfect respite.

Bali’s spiritual heart is felt in every corner of the island, from the daily offerings placed at the thresholds of homes and businesses to the intricate ceremonies that take place in its numerous temples. The city’s devotion to the arts is equally pronounced, with vibrant markets like the Ubud Art Market offering a wide array of handcrafted goods, from intricate wood carvings to colorful textiles. For a more personalized experience, consider a visit to a local village to witness the artisans at work, or join a yoga and meditation retreat to find balance and tranquility in this spiritually charged environment.

Bali's tropical monsoon climate ensures warm and humid conditions year-round, with distinct wet and dry seasons. The dry season, from April to October, is the perfect time to explore the city's temples, beaches, and cultural sites, with sunny days and temperatures typically in the mid-70s to low 80s Fahrenheit. Marriott Bonvoy’s properties in the city offer a range of amenities, from refreshing pools to air-conditioned rooms, ensuring a comfortable stay during the warmest months.

The wet season, from November to March, is characterized by more frequent rain showers, particularly in the afternoons. However, the rain also brings lush greenery and vibrant flowers to the landscape. Temperatures remain warm, usually ranging from the high 70s to the mid-80s Fahrenheit. Despite the occasional downpour, the wet season is a great time to experience the city’s laid-back atmosphere and traditional festivals. Marriott Bonvoy hotels offer a range of activities and services to keep you entertained, whether you choose to venture out or relax indoors.

Some Marriott Bonvoy hotels in the city offer complimentary shuttle services to and from the airport, ensuring a hassle-free start to your trip. If you’re arriving by sea, the city’s ports are well-equipped to welcome cruise ships and private boats, and are close to many of the major attractions and hotels.

Once you are in the city, you can easily explore its vibrant neighborhoods and natural wonders by using a combination of public and private transportation. The city’s modern buses and traditional Bemos (small vans) are inexpensive and offer a local flavor, though they may not be as direct or frequent as other options. Renting a scooter or motorcycle is a popular and exhilarating way to get around, allowing you to weave through traffic and discover hidden gems. For a more relaxed experience, car rental services are available at various locations, including the airport, and can be an ideal choice for longer trips or group travel.
