## Experiences

Nestled in the Arabian Gulf region of the United Arab Emirates (UAE), Dubai is a rare fusion of East and West. Its flourishing financial, oil and real estate industries distinguish Dubai as a strategic business hub while its world-class golf courses, unlimited shopping, stunning beaches and incomparable architectural feats—including the snowy slopes of Ski Dubai and tropical Palm Jumeirah Islands—make it unlike any other place on Earth. Impeccably located, Grosvenor House, a Luxury Collection Hotel, Dubai is near an array of cultural destinations. JBR (Jumeirah Beach Residence), The Walk at JBR, DIMC (Dubai international Marine Club), Media City, and Internet City are just minutes from the hotel and Jebel Ali Free Zone is a mere 15-minute drive away. Combining the best in Eastern hospitality with the height of Western modernity, Dubai is a city that thrills, inspires, and surprises. ### Local Area & Activities

Nearby Tennis 

Hard turf tennis courts are available around the corner at Le Royal Méridien Beach Resort & Spa.  
  
Arriving at Le Royal Méridien Beach Resort & Spa via a complimentary shuttle (which runs every 15 to 20 minutes), guests have access to four floodlit, hard turf tennis courts—although advance reservations are required. Racket rentals are available onsite for an additional fee of 15 AED per hour, per racket. Balls are available for purchase for 25 AED per three-ball can. Additionally, we are pleased to offer both private and group lessons through the Wilson Tennis Academy Dubai.  
  
Please note that guests under the age of 16 must be accompanied by an adult; contact the Concierge for more information or to make reservations.

Dine and Play 

Guests of Grosvenor House enjoy unlimited access to our sister resort, Le Royal Méridien Beach Resort & Spa, as well as complimentary shuttle service between the hotel and resort.  
  
At Le Royal Méridien Beach Resort & Spa, guests revel in multiple pools (including Hammam pools), a private beach, over 10 additional restaurants and bars, tennis courts, and an award-winning spa.
