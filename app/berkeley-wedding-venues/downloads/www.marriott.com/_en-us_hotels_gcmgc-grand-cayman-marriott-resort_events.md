# Events at Grand Cayman Marriott Resort

## Events

Whether you are planning your Cayman Islands meeting or a social gathering, the Grand Cayman Marriott offers flexible indoor and outdoor spaces on Seven Mile Beach. Our dedicated meeting planners are here to help you bring your Cayman Islands conference to life, ensuring every detail is meticulously handled. Impress your attendees with a farm-to-table menu crafted by our talented catering team, making your event in Grand Cayman truly unforgettable.
