# Wan Chai, Excitement in the heart of Hong Kong Island

## Experiences

Enjoy exceptional access to the very best of the city during your next stay at The St. Regis Hong Kong. Best explored on foot, Wan Chai district offers a wealth of heritage hideaways and modern hot spots. It is one of most chic areas in Hong Kong with a heady mix of old and new, East meets West, and where hip bars and restaurants mix with old tenement buildings and hidden temples among towering skyscrapers. Positioned at the heart of Hong Kong Island, our luxury hotel is within walking distance of the Hong Kong Convention & Exhibition Center and Victoria Harbour. And a close distance to Admiralty, Central and Causeway Bay where high-end luxury shopping centers are located. Allow our concierge and butler to recommend and curate a personalised Hong Kong trip, from dining, exploring, shopping, enjoying the famous Hong Kong nightlife and skyline, our butler team can assist in creating your Hong Kong memories.

###  Restore and Revitalise 

Ignite Your Inner Sparkle. Bring back your relaxed self by indulging in exclusive and high-performance spa treatments, each beginning with thorough one-on-one consultations to ensure your needs are addressed. Leave the rest to our therapists who will then tailor and make adjustments to each experience to meet your wellness aspirations.

###  Stretch and Flex 

Build Up Your Strength. Take your mind off your daily routines and realise your workout plans with a variety of unparalleled cardio and weight training equipment. Talk with our fitness trainers for professional advice, and sign up for one-on-one training sessions or regular weekend programmes to accomplish your health goals.

###  Dip and Splash 

Freshen Up Your Mind. Rain or shine, look up to the city jungle and take a swim at our heated outdoor swimming pool, or simply soak up the sun or catch the raindrops by relaxing in our sun loungers. Order a drink or some snacks from our Verandah Pool Bar to spice up your experience.

Enjoy bespoke cocktails created à la minute by our St. Regis Bar team, including signature cocktails such as Hung Sing Ye and WEPA!, as well as classic cocktails like Manhattan and Old Fashioned. We have also partnered with Distillerie de Paris to create bespoke spirits exclusively for our hotel.
