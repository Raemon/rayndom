# Plan Your Dream Wedding at Our Luxury Wedding Venue in the Desert

## Events

The Al Majlis Boardroom at Al Maha, a Luxury Collection Desert Resort & Spa, Dubai is a versatile Dubai event venue for meetings, conferences and special events such as wedding celebrations. The Boardroom is set within attractive grounds, protected from the sun by palm trees and shrubbery in which Arabian oryx and gazelle are frequently spotted grazing. Beautifully decorated with traditional Arabian antiques and artifacts, this meeting room was developed to completely meet the needs of corporate teams holding events. With the latest multimedia presentation and on-line video conferencing equipment, Al Majlis Boardroom is capable of being linked to other such facilities anywhere in the world. It offers various room and seating layouts and caters to groups ranging from 40 guests boardroom-style, 60 classroom-style or 90 theater-style, making it ideal for bespoke corporate or personal events.

## Life Enriching Adventures

Al Maha is a hidden within 225 square kilometers of the regions’ last unspoiled desert. Al Maha evokes an ancient Bedouin encampment and the dramatic Hajar Mountains on the horizon provide an exquisite backdrop to the 42 Villas, each nestled among a golden dune sculpted by warm desert breezes. The first conservation-led luxury resort in the region, Al Maha offers life-enriching interactions with the indigenous wildlife, culture and traditions of the region, made possible by our expert Field Guides. Explore the serene silence as you trek on a camel towards sunset. Dine under the rich blue velvet of the fabled Arabian Night skies.

## Timeless Traditions Renewed

In keeping with Arabic and other cultures, it is traditional for every bride to prepare for her wedding day with beauty rituals and treatments. Al Maha Spa lies sheltered under date palms within verdant gardens, surrounded by water features that mimic the gentle trickling of a “falaj”, an ancient Arabian mountain spring. The spa offers unique wellbeing rituals and beauty treatments for men and women, using the natural plants, herbs and essential oils of bespoke product ranges. Al Maha Spa incorporates single and double treatment rooms, a sauna, steam room, Jacuzzi and a gymnasium offering yoga classes in the mornings. Close your eyes, and relax mind and body in the cool sanctuary of Al Maha Spa.

Romance, Seclusion and Timelessness 

The intimacy and romantic ambiance of Al Maha come from the natural beauty and seclusion of the desert landscape that surrounds the resort. The Dubai Desert Conservation Reserve is a private sanctuary where guests of Al Maha enjoy privileged access to a diverse population of indigenous Arabian flora and fauna, over 100 species of resident and migratory birds, and mammal and reptile species including the Arabian Oryx, after which Al Maha is named. Explore the natural wonders and mystique of the desert and the blissful feeling of timelessness and solitude

Inspired By Arabian Heritage 

Inspired by the rich history of the Arabian Desert, the architecture and interior design at Al Maha reflect the traditional Bedouin way of life. Richly decorated with rare Arabian antiques and artifacts, hand-crafted rugs and furniture, each of the four configurations of Suite is nestled into the crest of a dune, enjoying absolute privacy. Exceptional levels of comfort provide a perfect location for guest and honeymoon accommodation, each Suite with a private temperature controlled pool from which to enjoy the panoramic vista across a golden desert landscape.

Finishing Touches 

All the finishing touches that make your celebration unique can be sourced and arranged by Al Maha Desert Resort and Spa, so you can relax and look forward to your stay.   


Floral decorations and linens, color schemes and themed décor, hair stylists and photographers can all be arranged to make your perfect wedding in the desert. We can find musicians, singers, entertainers and other performers for your celebration event.  
  
If you need assistance with transportation, we can source vintage or luxury cars, limousines and taxis and coaches to and from Dubai. Al Maha Desert Resort and Spa even has its own flock herd? of camels and a stable of thoroughbred horses, for a memorably authentic Arabian experience.
