# W Prague’s Signature Suites

## Suites

Each suite is designed to maximize comfort, featuring an open-plan bathroom and state-of-the-art amenities. From Marvelous Suites, to the WOW Bohemia Duplex Suite overlooking Wenceslas Square, and the expansive EWOW Suite with unique details and a spacious living area — it’s certain that suite dreams will come true. 

### Prague’s Prestige

The EWOW, WOW, Marvelous and Fantastic suites are gasp-worthy luxury spaces beyond your wishes.

## Extreme WOW Suite

With panoramic views that turn heads, this suite brings the outside in. Flooded with natural light, it offers stunning sights of Prague Castle and the National Museum. Enjoy a fireplace, a walk-in closet, and a sleek bar—all spanning 92 m². Unwind on a statement-piece sofa with a glass of the finest, ready to do it all again tomorrow.

## Your Ultimate Rest

While chic European aesthetics blend with local influences, thoughtfully curated amenities ensure comfort and delightful surprises—from smart lighting to Bluetooth speakers disguised as vintage radios, and of course, the often-quoted W Beds for ultimate rest.

### WOW Suite

Curved walls and expansive windows invite a sense of openness and effortless flow, while marble finishes and warm tones evoke timeless sophistication. Take in sweeping city views from the comfort of your bath, unwind beside the fireplace, or bask in the soft glow of a bespoke Preciosa chandelier. Spanning 87 sqm, the suite offers high-end amenities designed for ultimate comfort—kick back, relax, and indulge in the moment.

Next Level Presidential 

## A Suite Stay

Step into the WOW and EWOW Suites—where elevated design meets breathtaking views. Averaging 89 m² of thoughtfully curated space, each suite features curved walls and expansive windows that bring the outside in, offering sweeping panoramas of Prague Castle and the National Museum. Soak in the city from your bath, unwind by the fireplace, or enjoy a crafted cocktail at your sleek private bar. With marble finishes, warm tones, a walk-in closet, and a bespoke Preciosa chandelier, these suites embody timeless elegance with a bold modern edge. Whether WOW or EWOW, your stay is set to impress.

Boho Elegance, Redefined.

###  WOW Bohemia Duplex Suite

Step into 96 m² of boho-style elegance, where bold colors, plush furnishings, and exposed wooden beams create a space that’s both rustic and refined. Nestled within a heritage building, this one-of-a-kind suite effortlessly blends restored architectural charm with sleek contemporary design. Take in sweeping views over Wenceslas Square, unwind with indulgent Comfort Zone amenities, and immerse yourself in rich sound from Marshall speakers — your boho suite, reimagined.

### Fantastic Bohemia Suite

Overlooking the vibrant Wenceslas Square, this suite offers a front-row seat to the city's dynamic energy. Most feature elegant French balconies—ideal for soaking in the views—while inside, contemporary design blends seamlessly with plush amenities, creating a space crafted for maximum comfort and style.

## Old World Glamour Meets Bold New Concepts

Where two worlds collide—19th-century stuccos meet striking modern design. Choose between the Marvelous Suite, EWOW or WOW Suite, where contemporary style takes center stage, or the Marvelous or WOW Bohemia Duplex Suite, where historic charm is beautifully preserved. The suite features distinctive design elements and thoughtful amenities, offering a stay that’s both stylish and unforgettable.

Where Contemporary Style Meets Historic Charm

###  Marvelous Bohemia Suite

These bright and airy suites feature a spacious king-sized bed, vibrant modern design, and thoughtful touches like a fully stocked Mix Bar, indulgent amenities from Comfort Zone, and premium sound from Marshall Speakers. One of the suites proudly showcases an original 1905 fireplace, a stunning historic centerpiece that adds timeless character to the modern surroundings.

### Marvelous Suite

This stunning suite effortlessly blends bold design with refined comfort, offering a luxurious stay that’s both stylish and inviting. A spacious king-sized bed anchors the room, complemented by sleek mirrored accents that enhance its modern aesthetic. Enjoy thoughtful touches throughout—from a fully stocked Mix Bar and indulgent Comfort Zone amenities to a SteamOne steamer and rich sound from a Marshall speaker. Every detail is designed to elevate your experience.
