## Experiences

Grande Lakes Orlando has over 500 acres to explore with endless activities. Whether you want a fun-filled adventure or a relaxing getaway, our resort has it all. Enjoy luxury spa services, championship golf, and mountain biking, or try unique experiences such as falconry and eco-tours.

## Golf

The Central Florida landscape provided inspiration to legendary golf course designer Greg Norman, who worked with nature to create challenging greens, wide fairways, and hazards. The 18-hole championship course is a Certified Audubon Cooperative Sanctuary and tests the abilities of every skill level. Our Orlando golf resort also features a clubhouse, restaurant, and lessons for both children and adults.

### Nature-Inspired Spa

Welcome to our nature-inspired Spa retreat in Orlando, a sanctuary spanning 40,000 sq. ft. Disconnect from the world as you immerse in the tranquil elements of nature and emerge refreshed, relaxed, and revitalized. This wellness retreat boasts 40 treatment rooms and a full salon offering a wide array of services. Between or post-treatment, relax in our serene adult-only lap pool or refuel with nutritious fare at Vitale, Spa Café. Signature therapies, including massages and body treatments, incorporate herbs from the resort's organic garden, honey from its resident bees, and citrus from local orchards for a unique wellness experience.
