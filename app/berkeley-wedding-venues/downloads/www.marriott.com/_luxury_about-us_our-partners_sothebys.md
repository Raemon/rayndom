Iconic Pieces. Extraordinary Experiences.

Sotheby's

Together with Sotheby’s, we’re charting new territories and offering our guests once-in-a-lifetime auction experiences.

Luxury Group has partnered with Sotheby’s to offer the kinds of Iconic Pieces and Extraordinary Experiences that could only be made possible by such esteemed brands. This collaboration merges the world-class hospitality of Luxury Group’s renowned brands, with Sotheby’s exceptional auctions of jewelry, fashion, watches, and more. Together, they provide bespoke travel experiences and exclusive access, offering unparalleled opportunities for luxury and learning.

This partnership aims to redefine luxury by blending tangible and intangible experiences, providing unique and immersive moments for collectors and travelers alike. In 2024, The Luxury Group and Sotheby's auctioned a variety of captivating experiences designed to pair world-class hospitality with exceptional treasures from Sotheby's auctions offering guests the chance to explore their passions while discovering unforgettable destinations.

## Geneva Grandmasters

Watch enthusiasts had the opportunity to bid on an intimate horology immersion at The Ritz-Carlton Hotel de la Paix, Geneva with Girard-Perregaux.

![19.20 Spring Menu Dish](https://cache.marriott.com/is/image/marriotts7prod/lc-parlc-prince-de-galles-06080-18432)

## A Taste of France

Guests checked into Prince de Galles, a Luxury Collection Hotel, Paris before exploring the country’s finest wines and Champagnes and enjoying lunch with Laila Gohar.

## Alexander McQueen Discovery

Haute couture devotees enjoyed two nights at The London EDITION, a bespoke city tour and a personalized photography session by McQueen’s longtime collaborator, Ann Ray.

## Whiskey Reverie

Whiskey enthusiast Fawn Weaver joined guests for dinner at The Ritz-Carlton New York, NoMad before journeying on to the heart of America’s whiskey's scene: Tennessee.
