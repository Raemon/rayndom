# Luxury Suites & Signature St. Regis Butler Service

## Suites

Pursue your passion to relax, unwind, and get away in an exquisite suite at the best address in Chicago. Only with suite accommodations do you unlock the Signature St. Regis Butler Services, allowing you to savor the rarest luxury of all: time. 

### The Two-Bedroom Presidential Suite

Indulge in the epitome of luxury and sophistication with the 4,600 square foot Two-Bedroom Presidential Suite at The St. Regis Chicago, offering an unparalleled level of elegance and opulence, meticulously designed to exceed the expectations of the most discerning guests. Step into a light-filled jewel box surrounded by stunning views of downtown Chicago and Lake Michigan.

![Luxury Hotel in Downtown Chicago](data:image/png;base64,iVBORw0KGgoAAAANSUhEUgAAAA4AAAAKCAYAAACE2W/HAAAAFklEQVR42mN8/fpLPQMZgHFU48jWCAD/vyDle0zFdgAAAABJRU5ErkJggg==)

## The Jewel Box of Chicago's Best Address

Immerse yourself in an ambiance of timeless elegance & refinement. Partake in ultimate relaxation in the marble-clad principal bathroom overlooking Navy Pier, featuring an oversized, effervescent infinity soaking tub, rainfall shower, and premium bath amenities. Maintain your fitness routine in privacy in your personal fitness studio or unwind in the expansive living area, complete with a dining table for entertaining guests or enjoying intimate meals prepared in your private kitchen. Your personal St. Regis Butler will be on hand to assist with tasks big and small.

Unlock the Signature St. Regis Butler Services With Your Suite Accommodations

###  Packing Service 

Allow a St. Regis Butler to take care of the unpacking and storage of your luggage, or contact the Butler Service Desk to dispatch a butler for assistance with gathering and folding your garments before you depart.

###  Garment Pressing 

Enjoy complimentary pressings of up to two items daily. Contact your St. Regis Butler for the prompt pickup and return of any garments that require pressing.

###  Beverage Service 

Upon arrival, allow a St. Regis Butler to arrange coffee or tea service in your room. A St. Regis Butler can also arrive after your morning wake-up call to draw the curtains and deliver your coffee or tea as well as the day's news, through the Pressreader app.
