### Magnificent Weddings

The St. Regis New York invites you to begin your journey as a married couple with a wedding in the heart of Midtown Manhattan. Our fabled hotel is home to some of the most magnificent event spaces in New York, including a rooftop ballroom venue with sumptuous design and gilt chandeliers. Your personal St. Regis Wedding Specialist is by your side throughout the process, ensuring that every aspect of your New York wedding celebration fits your vision.

### The Iconic Address

Overlooking world-famous Fifth Avenue and ideally located in the heart of midtown Manhattan, this original 1904 classic Beaux Art landmark is Manhattan’s foremost address. As the first St. Regis hotel, The St. Regis New York premiered the legendary Astor family’s timeless legacy. Today, it features signature 24-hour St. Regis Butler service, an on-site florist, and the historic King Cole Bar.
