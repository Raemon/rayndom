### Event Venues

Stretching over 800 square meters, the Conference Centre at Grosvenor House comprises an array of timeless venues, including the expansive 480-square-meter Windsor Ballroom. Accommodating up to 400 guests, it is an ideal choice for large conferences, gala dinners, dine and dance parties, exhibitions, cocktail receptions, weddings, and more. Meanwhile, our refined boardroom sets the scene for a successful meeting while syndicate rooms offer additional, more intimate options. Although the décor in all venues is a similar mix of sophisticated cream and chocolate hues, elegant furnishings, and warm lighting, accented by engaging modern art, two venues—Kew and Eton—also offer windows, with Eton boasting a terrace featuring Dubai Marina views. 

### Windsor Ballroom
