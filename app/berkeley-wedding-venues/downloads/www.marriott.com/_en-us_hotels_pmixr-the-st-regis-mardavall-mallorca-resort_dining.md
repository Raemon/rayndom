## Dining

The St. Regis Mardavall Mallorca Resort offers an indulgent epicurean experience that brings to life the flair and color of Mediterranean and international flavors, inspired by the magnificent Spanish and Mallorcan culture. To dine at our hotel is to sip a glass of the finest vintage wine on a terrace overlooking the splendor of the sea, as world-class cuisine with impeccable grace is served in a truly unique ambience exclusive to this luxurious retreat. From exquisite Michelin Star gastronomy and gourmet Mediterranean offerings to delicate tapas and international selections, our menus find their muse in global themes and influences under the masterful hands of our talented chefs. We are pleased to arrange your unique Dine – Around with our sister properties, the Castillo Hotel Son Vida and the Sheraton Mallorca Arabella Golf Hotel. Enjoy the sensational choice of three luxury hotels, and six unique restaurants in Palma de Mallorca. 

Mar Sea Club – Special Opening Hours Notice

Please note that Mar Sea Club will be closed on 26th June due to a private event.

###  Frequently Asked Questions

What restaurants does The St. Regis Mardavall Mallorca Resort have on property? 

The on-property restaurants at The St. Regis Mardavall Mallorca Resort are:

Es Fum (Temporarily Closed) Mar Sea Club (Temporarily Closed) Terra The St. Regis Bar

Does The St. Regis Mardavall Mallorca Resort offer room service? 

Yes, room service is available at The St. Regis Mardavall Mallorca Resort.

What cuisine is offered at The St. Regis Mardavall Mallorca Resort? 

The cuisines offered at The St. Regis Mardavall Mallorca Resort are:

Multiple cuisines at Mar Sea Club (Temporarily Closed) Tapas at The St. Regis Bar Spanish at Terra International at Es Fum (Temporarily Closed)
