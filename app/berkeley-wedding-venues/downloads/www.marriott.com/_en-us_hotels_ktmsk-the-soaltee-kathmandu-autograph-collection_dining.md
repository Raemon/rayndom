International

Everyday

6:30 AM-10:30 PM

Indian 

Bring your senses to life with the vibrant flavors of Awadhi Indian gastronomy in Kathmandu. Our Indian-inspired restaurant offers a plethora of tantalizing dishes infused with exotic spices, accompanied by our selection of exceptional wines.

Everyday

6:30 PM-10:30 PM 

[+977 1-4273999 6520](tel:+977%201-4273999%206520)

Chinese 

Indulge your palate with an array of exquisite Chinese delicacies at Bao Xuan, specializing in irresistibly flavorsome dishes from regions across China. Our elegant Chinese restaurant in Kathmandu also serves expertly crafted cocktails and fine wines.

Everyday

6:30 PM-10:30 PM 

[+977 1-4273999 6540](tel:+977%201-4273999%206540)

###  Frequently Asked Questions

What restaurants does The Soaltee Kathmandu, Autograph Collection have on property? 

The on-property restaurants at The Soaltee Kathmandu, Autograph Collection are:

Garden Terrace Kakori Bao Xuan Patio

Does The Soaltee Kathmandu, Autograph Collection offer room service? 

Yes, room service is available at The Soaltee Kathmandu, Autograph Collection.

What cuisine is offered at The Soaltee Kathmandu, Autograph Collection? 

The cuisines offered at The Soaltee Kathmandu, Autograph Collection are:

American at Patio Chinese at Bao Xuan Indian at Kakori International at Garden Terrace

Does The Soaltee Kathmandu, Autograph Collection offer complimentary breakfast? 

Complimentary breakfast is not currently served at The Soaltee Kathmandu, Autograph Collection. View on-site and nearby dining options on our [dining page](https://www.marriott.com/en-us/hotels/ktmsk-the-soaltee-kathmandu-autograph-collection/dining/#on-site-dining).
