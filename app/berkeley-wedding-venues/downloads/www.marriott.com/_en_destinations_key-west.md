# Hotels in Key West, FL

Key West, the southernmost city in the United States, is a tropical paradise that beckons with a unique blend of laid-back charm and vibrant energy. Known for its crystal-clear waters and breathtaking sunsets, this island city offers a myriad of activities to suit every type of traveler. From the historic Old Town's quaint streets and Victorian architecture to the bustling nightlife along Duval Street, there's always something new to discover. Marriott Bonvoy’s collection of hotels in Key West serve as the perfect launchpad to explore this idyllic destination, providing comfortable accommodations and easy access to the city's top attractions.

  


Immerse yourself in the island’s rich maritime history at the Key West Shipwreck Museum, or wander through the peaceful gardens of the Ernest Hemingway Home and Museum. For the more adventurous, the city’s many water sports and boat tours offer an exciting way to explore the surrounding waters. With amenities such as pools, spas, and on-site dining, many Marriott Bonvoy hotels in Key West are the perfect place to relax and recharge. Whether you’re planning a romantic escape or a family vacation, Key West is sure to enchant, and our hotels are here to make your stay even more memorable.

Key West is a haven for nature enthusiasts, offering tons of opportunity for both sea and land adventures. The city's renowned beaches, from Smathers to Higgs, beckon for sun-soaked days, swimming, and underwater explorations. Nearby, the Florida Keys National Marine Sanctuary and abundance of marine life and the only coral barrier reef in the continental US. For a more tranquil experience, the Key West Butterfly and Nature Conservatory invites you to stroll amidst butterflies and exotic flora in a serene, indoor setting. 

History buffs can explore the historic Truman Annex, home to beautiful gardens and the Truman Little White House, where President Harry S. Truman spent 175 days of his presidency. The Key West Botanical Garden is a peaceful place to enjoy native flora and fauna, have a picnic, or take a leisurely walk. Whether you’re a beach bum, a botanist, or a birdwatcher, Key West’s natural beauty provides plenty of opportunities to enjoy the great outdoors.

The culture in Key West is a vibrant tapestry woven from the city’s storied past and its present-day laid-back charm. Marriott Bonvoy’s hotels in Key West offer a front-row seat to the island’s unique blend of artistic expression, historical landmarks, and tropical ambiance. Stroll through the streets to discover the legacy of renowned author Ernest Hemingway, visit the Key West Lighthouse to learn about the city’s maritime history, or explore the Truman Little White House for a glimpse into presidential life. After a day of cultural immersion, unwind in the comfort of your hotel with amenities like spa services, beach access, and rooftop bars that capture the essence of island living.

Key West’s artistic spirit is everywhere, from the galleries and studios showcasing local and international talent to the city’s annual events, including Fantasy Fest and the Key West Literary Seminar. For a more in-depth look at the city’s cultural offerings, take in a show at the historic Capitol Theatre or hop on a sunset cruise to experience Key West’s vibrant music and art scene in a truly magical setting.

Imagine a place where the sun shines bright and the weather is perfect every day. Welcome to Key West, where a tropical marine climate ensures warm and delightful conditions throughout the year. Summers, from June to August, are toasty and humid, with temperatures typically in the mid-80s to low 90s. Afternoon showers and thunderstorms are common, offering a welcome respite from the heat. Our Marriott Bonvoy hotels are ready to welcome you with well-appointed rooms, air conditioning, and refreshing pools, providing the ideal escape during the warmest months. 

The winter months, from December to February, are mild and dry, with temperatures ranging from the mid-70s to the low 80s Fahrenheit. This is the most popular time to visit, as the weather is ideal for exploring the city’s numerous outdoor activities and attractions. Spring and fall, from March to May and September to November, offer comfortable temperatures in the 70s to 80s, making these seasons perfect for enjoying the beaches and scenic walks. With its consistent warmth and sunshine, Key West is a delightful destination no matter when you choose to visit.

For those traveling by air, Key West International Airport is just a short distance from the city center and offers a range of flights to and from most major cities. Upon arrival, you can easily hail a taxi, book a ride with Uber or Lyft, or rent a car to take you to your choice of Marriott Bonvoy hotels. If you're driving, the Overseas Highway is a beautiful and convenient way to reach the island, connecting Key West to the mainland and other keys along the route. And for a truly unique experience, consider arriving by ferry, which allows you to bring your car or simply sit back and enjoy the scenic journey. 

Once in Key West, you'll find a compact and charming city that's a joy to explore on foot, especially in the historic Old Town area where many top attractions and hotels are located. For longer distances or to explore further afield, bicycles are a popular and eco-friendly option, with many bike rental shops available. Taxis and ride-sharing services are also readily accessible. For a unique experience, consider hopping on a golf cart or a moped, both of which are common and fun ways to navigate the city's laid-back streets.

Key West is a family paradise where you can explore the magic of the tropics. Kids will love the hands-on fun at the Key West Aquarium, where they can touch a ray and learn about the diverse marine life that calls the surrounding waters home. For a memorable experience, take a ride on the Conch Train Tour, which provides a narrated journey through the city’s historic neighborhoods, sharing fun facts and offbeat stories that will keep young minds entertained. The train’s open-air cars offer an unobstructed view of the lively streets and famous sites, making it an adventure they’ll never forget. 

When the sun is high and the energy is flowing, head to Higgs Beach for some beachside fun. The soft sands and shallow waters are ideal for building sandcastles and splashing around, while the playground and splash pad offer plenty of entertainment for all ages. For a touch of adventure, take the family to the Key West Shipwreck Museum, where they can discover the thrilling history of shipwrecks. Don’t miss the chance to pan for treasure and take home a piece of the city’s maritime past. With so much to see and do, Key West is the perfect destination for a family vacation.
