# Experience the slopes from our hotel near Snowmass Village

## Experiences

An alpine escape at the peak of luxury, W Aspen is a year-round destination. Our hotel near Aspen Highlands features premier access to Snowmass Village and Aspen Mountain - as well as Buttermilk Mountain, the home of X Games Aspen. From hiking endless trails or biking in warmer months to paragliding over our surrounding valleys, skiing and snowboarding in the winter, a stay here is anything but boring. 

## Activities & Events in Aspen

Your escape to Aspen just got a little easier. We have curated top activities and events, helping you plan your days and nights at our Alpine Resort within walking distance from the town main attractions.

###  Happenings at W Aspen 

W Community Event Managers have dialed in the fun all week long. From Live music, complimentary surprise and delights to fun activities and special offerings, there will be something for any guest arriving to the resort. 

###  Explore with our Partner 

Explore the stunning natural landscapes of Aspen and its surrounding areas with our partner, the Aspen Center for Environmental Studies (ACES). Whether it's winter or summer, you can join guided hikes led by a naturalist, providing an ideal opportunity to experience the beauty of the Roaring Fork Valley on foot. 

###  360 Views 

Experience breathtaking views of Aspen Mountain and the historic town year-round from the Rooftop at W Aspen. This unique outdoor venue offers a vibrant atmosphere, creative cocktails, and light bites, making it the perfect spot for a fun afternoon soaking up the sun.

### Your Gateway to the Aspen Community

Located in the heart of the town and just steps away from Aspen Mountain, W Aspen offers a luxurious Alpine experience to the surrounding community. Downtown Aspen is easily accessible, providing direct access to a vibrant restaurant scene, shopping area, and art galleries.  
  
You can easily get around town on foot or take advantage of the car drop-off service from W Aspen Wheel. During the winter months, enjoy the complimentary shuttle service to the main ski areas (Buttermilk and Highland). Aspen Mountain provides ski-in/ski-out access from the resort. In the summer, guests can explore the area with one of the resort's complimentary bikes. Additionally, guests can use one of the complimentary BMW cars available once per stay to discover the surrounding areas at their own pace.  
  
The Aspen public transit system offers easy and safe complimentary bus services to the main mountains around town. It also provides comprehensive routes (fees apply) through the Roaring Fork Valley to further explore the outstanding landscape.

## Winter Activites

Aspen is a world-renowned ski destination, nestled perfectly within the peaks of the Elk Mountains. In addition, this mountain town’s beautiful scenery and open space lends itself to many other types of winter recreation. And there are plenty of local events throughout the winter months to keep a visitor inspired.

### Skiing + Snowboarding

One lift ticket can be used at Aspen, Aspen Highlands, Buttermilk and Snowmass Mountains. Group/private lessons and excursions are offered through Aspen Skiing Company by certified professionals and are open to all experience levels. Our Wheels Team is delighted to offer complimentary mountain shuttles to Buttermilk and Aspen Highlands.

### Cross Country Skiing

Enjoy groomed track skiing through beautiful meadows and valleys. Instruction and rentals are located at the Aspen Cross Country Center or at the Ashcroft Ski Touring area.

### Heliskiing

The ultimate alpine adventure, Heliskiing offers the exhilarating opportunity to reach remote areas of pristine, untouched mountain terrain only accessible by helicopter. Carve out fresh runs while taking in the awe-inspiring views of a lifetime.

### Ice Skating

Make off-mountain memories at one of Aspen’s three ice skating rinks. Families can skate indoors at the Aspen Ice Garden and Lewis Ice Arena or choose to stargaze and skate, outside at the Silver Circle rink, just steps from the hotel.

### Snowshoeing

Float on trails of snow and explore Aspen’s winter wonderland on a snowshoe tour with an ACES (Aspen Center for Environmental Studies) naturalist guide. Family snowshoeing adventures are available daily.

### Snowcat

Discover untouched areas and leave fresh tracks on some of the most remote runs in Colorado ski country. Take a luxury snowcat through exclusive alpine landscapes and enjoy a gourmet lunch for an unforgettable experience.

### Snowmobiling

Experience an exhilarating snowmobile adventure to Maroon Bells, Independence Pass, Klondike Cabin, or the Lenado Area.

### Sleigh Rides

Enjoy a horse-drawn sleigh ride to a log cabin for lunch or dinner.

## Summer Activities

There are plenty of ways to explore our lovely mountain town in the warmer months. Temperatures hover at that golden range of high 70s, low 80s, and the Rocky Mountains show their full majestic glory free from the heavy snow. Here’s your guide to the best things to do in Aspen over the summer.

### Clay Shooting

Learn how to shoot clay discs with an experienced instructor for a fun and memorable activity during your time in Aspen. Shoot skeet and compete with your crew or practice your aim with trap shooting. All experience levels are welcome.

### Paragliding

Tours begin with a beautiful drive up Aspen mountain, where at the top guests will be briefed by a certified paragliding pilot. Flights are done Tandem style as the pilot steers your glider for a smooth majestic ride. Morning flights are available 7 days a week.

### Hot Air Ballooning

The best way to see all of Aspen without ever breaking a sweat. Soar above the beautiful mountain peaks at dawn in a hot air balloon. You can choose to ride solo, with a group, or reserve a private experience for an unforgettable trip with your significant other.

### Tennis

Clay and hard courts available at multiple locations in Aspen.

### Kayaking

Test your paddling skills on one of our inflatable kayak or “ducky” trips down the Roaring Fork River. Our inflatable kayak trips are very popular in August as the natural river flows drop.

### Horseback Riding

Explore Aspen Pioneer-style and horseback ride through through endless mountain trails. Great for families and groups of all experience levels. Tours are flexible and can range from one hour to a full day.

### White Water Rafting

Choose from options that range from a relaxing trip on class II rapids, to an adrenaline filled ride down Slaughterhouse Falls. Half days and full days are available for a variety of excursions. All of our outfitters are whitewater rafting certified.

### Biking

Experience the charm of Aspen on a complimentary W Aspen cruiser-style bicycle. Our Wheels Team is happy to assist with a reservation. It’s easy to get lost in the scenery - ask our Concierge team about guided tours as well as E-Bike options.

### Flyfishing

Our outfitter offers trips down the Roaring Fork and Colorado River all year long. Both wading tours and in-boat floating tours are available. Both half and full-day tours include a provided lunch. It’s the best time of the time of year to get out on the River.

### Stand Up Paddleboarding

Stand Up Paddle boards, or SUPs, are the hottest new sport on water and an exciting way to spend an active day on the river. SUPs are recommended for any adventure seeker looking for an exciting workout or just a thrilling way to spend the day.

### Hiking

Discover the beauty of Aspen on foot. Hike up to Alpine Lake for a swim, stroll through the iconic wildflower fields, or scale a summit for a picturesque picnic. Our Concierge is delighted to provide trail recommendations or arrange a guided hike.

### Carriage Rides

Carriage Rides are available on the corner of Galena and Hopkins from 1:30PM to 10PM daily. No reservations are required.

### Endless Playground

A never-ending playground of activities for all seasons, Aspen is a charming and historical town for outdoor enthusiasts. With 300 days of sunshine a year, our little town is a continual invitation to come out and explore. Embrace the vibrant food and art scene, discover local shops, enjoy the four mountain skiing areas in the winter, or explore the endless hiking and biking trails the rest of the year. Aspen is a one-of-a-kind town in the Rockies, a place like no other.

### Rooftop with Heated Pool

Join us with friends, family, or colleagues at our Rooftop, which offers 360-degree views of Aspen Mountain and the historic downtown. Our vibrant outdoor space is open year-round and features a bar where you can enjoy our mixologists' playful creations and fresh light bites. We also have a heated pool for relaxation with a DJ playing ambient beats. The Rooftop offers a unique open-air setup for enjoying the Aspen sun and stargazing at night. Rooftop open Monday to Sunday from 9 am to 6:00 pm (8:00 pm during summer months). Must be 21 years old to enter. Children must be accompanied by a legal guardian.
