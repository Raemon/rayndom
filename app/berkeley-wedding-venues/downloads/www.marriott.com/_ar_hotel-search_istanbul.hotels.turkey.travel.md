# Hotels in Istanbul, Turkey

Istanbul, the only city in the world that straddles two continents, is a fascinating blend of East and West. Here, the echoes of its Byzantine and Ottoman past resonate through its bustling streets and historic landmarks. The city is a treasure trove of culture, with its iconic sites such as the Hagia Sophia, Topkapi Palace, and the Grand Bazaar drawing visitors from around the world. Marriott Bonvoy’s hotels in Istanbul are strategically located to provide easy access to these must-see attractions, ensuring that your stay is both comfortable and convenient. Whether you're exploring the historic peninsula, enjoying the vibrant nightlife in Taksim, or savoring the flavors of traditional Turkish cuisine, our hotels offer the perfect base for your adventures.

  


From the serene beauty of the Bosphorus to the lively atmosphere of the Spice Market, Istanbul is a city of contrasts and endless discovery. Marriott Bonvoy’s hotels in Istanbul not only offer modern amenities and exceptional service but also provide unique experiences that reflect the city’s rich heritage. Many of our properties feature wellness centers, rooftop bars with panoramic views, and dining options that serve both local and international cuisines. With locations in key areas like the business district of Levent and the charming neighborhood of Besiktas, you can seamlessly transition from business meetings to leisurely strolls along the waterfront. No matter your reason for visiting, our hotels are designed to make your stay in Istanbul truly unforgettable.

Istanbul is a city that offers the best of both worlds, with its unique combination of urban and natural beauty. For those who enjoy spending time outdoors, there are plenty of green spaces and scenic spots to explore. Belgrade Forest, the largest park in the city, is a great place to escape from the hustle and bustle of everyday life. Here you can take a walk through the woodlands, relax by the streams, or enjoy a picnic with friends and family. If you prefer a more relaxed pace, Emirgan Park is the perfect place to unwind. The park is home to beautiful gardens, peaceful ponds, and stunning floral displays, especially during the annual Tulip Festival.

Along the Bosphorus, the city’s famous strait, you can take a leisurely stroll, cycle, or boat tour, and enjoy the stunning views of the historical palaces and modern skyscrapers that line the shore. The Bosphorus is also home to many parks and gardens, such as Yıldız and Göztepe, which offer a peaceful place to go for a morning run or an afternoon picnic. Whether you’re exploring the city’s forests, islands, or waterfront parks, Istanbul’s natural landscapes provide the perfect backdrop for both relaxation and adventure.

The culture of Istanbul is a mesmerizing blend of East and West, where ancient traditions and modern influences converge to create a unique and vibrant atmosphere. Staying at one of our hotels in the city places you at the crossroads of history and contemporary life. Explore the historic Grand Bazaar, a labyrinth of shops where you can haggle for exquisite handicrafts and local treasures, or visit the Hagia Sophia, a marvel that has stood as a church, mosque, and now a museum, reflecting the city’s rich religious heritage. After a day of cultural immersion, unwind in the comfort of your Marriott Bonvoy accommodations, which offer a range of amenities to ensure a relaxing stay.

Istanbul’s allure doesn’t end with its historical sites. The city is a vibrant arts center, with a multitude of galleries and theaters that celebrate both classic and cutting-edge works. The Istanbul Modern, a top-tier art museum, is home to a diverse collection of contemporary art, while the nearby Tophane-i Amire Cultural Center regularly hosts a variety of cultural events and exhibitions. To truly experience the city’s creative energy, take a leisurely walk through the Beyoğlu district, where the streets are adorned with colorful street art, and where you can explore a myriad of trendy boutiques and bustling cafes.

Istanbul's climate is a unique blend of the Mediterranean and continental, offering a diverse range of weather conditions throughout the year. The summer months, from June to August, are warm and dry, with temperatures averaging between 70 and 80 degrees Fahrenheit. This is the ideal time to explore the city's rich history, vibrant bazaars, and stunning waterfronts. The long, sunny days lead to pleasant, cooler evenings, perfect for leisurely strolls and al fresco dining. Our Marriott Bonvoy hotels in Istanbul are equipped with air-conditioned rooms and inviting pools, ensuring a comfortable and refreshing stay during the warmer months.

Winter in Istanbul, from December to February, is cool and wet, with temperatures ranging from 2 to 12 degrees Celsius, or 35 to 54 degrees Fahrenheit. Snow is possible, but it's not common. When it does snow, the city becomes a magical place, with the Hagia Sophia and Blue Mosque looking even more beautiful. Spring and fall, from March to May and September to November, are mild and pleasant, with temperatures in the 15 to 25 degrees Celsius, or 60 to 70 degrees Fahrenheit, range. These are the best times to visit if you want to enjoy outdoor activities, as the city is full of blooming flowers in spring and colorful foliage in fall. Whether you visit in the summer or winter, Marriott Bonvoy hotels in the city will provide the perfect base for your Istanbul experience.

Istanbul, with its two major airports, Istanbul Airport and Sabiha Gökçen International Airport, is easily accessible for visitors. Both airports offer a variety of transportation options to get you to your hotel. Taxis are readily available at both airports, and many Marriott Bonvoy hotels provide shuttle services for added convenience. For those arriving by land, the city’s extensive network of intercity buses and trains ensures smooth travel, with major stations like Sirkeci and Haydarpaşa serving as key transit hubs. Additionally, the city’s ferry services connect the European and Asian sides, offering a scenic and efficient way to explore both parts of this vibrant metropolis.

Once you're in Istanbul, you'll find getting around is a breeze. The city's public transportation system is comprehensive and includes the Istanbul Metro, trams, and buses, which can take you to popular attractions like the Grand Bazaar and Hagia Sophia. Taxis and ride-sharing services like BiTaksi and Uber are also widely available and can be hailed on the street or booked through an app. For a more local experience, consider taking a ferry ride along the Bosphorus. Not only is it a practical way to get around, but it also offers stunning views of the city's iconic skyline. Whether you're exploring on foot, by metro, or by ferry, you'll find that Istanbul's diverse transportation options make it easy to see and do everything on your list.

Istanbul is a city that has a lot to offer children. Miniaturk Park is a great place to start, where kids can explore miniature versions of some of the city's most famous landmarks and historical sites. The Istanbul Toy Museum is another popular spot, with a huge collection of toys and dolls that are sure to spark their imagination. If you're staying at one of our hotels in the city, you'll find that these attractions are easily accessible, making it simple to keep the kids entertained and ensuring that your family has a memorable stay.

For outdoor fun, the Istanbul Aquarium is a great choice, with interactive zones where children can touch and feel the marine life. The city’s parks, such as Yıldız Park and Emirgan Park, offer plenty of space for little ones to run around, with beautiful gardens, playgrounds, and even pony rides. After a day of exploring, enjoy a family meal at a local restaurant or café, with many offering kid-friendly options.
