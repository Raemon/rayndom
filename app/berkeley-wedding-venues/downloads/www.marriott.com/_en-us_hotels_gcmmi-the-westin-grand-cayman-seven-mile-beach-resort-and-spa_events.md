## Impressive Wedding Venues

The Governor’s Ballroom is positioned on the north side of the property and boasts 5600 square feet! Its west exposure has floor-to-ceiling windows overlooking the Caribbean Sea. The Governor’s Lawn offers a spectacular setting for wedding ceremonies, cocktail receptions and alfresco dinners. Complementing this ballroom and on the south end of the resort is our Galleon Ballroom with an additional 2600 sq. ft ideal for cocktail receptions and welcome events.
