###  Frequently Asked Questions

What restaurants does Kathmandu Marriott Hotel have on property? 

The on-property restaurants at Kathmandu Marriott Hotel are:

Thamel Kitchen Tahina Terrace M Club Lounge Raksi Music Bar Kathmandu Baking Company M Bistro by KBC Edamame

Does Kathmandu Marriott Hotel offer room service? 

Yes, room service is available at Kathmandu Marriott Hotel.

What cuisine is offered at Kathmandu Marriott Hotel? 

The cuisines offered at Kathmandu Marriott Hotel are:

Bar-B-Q at Raksi Music Bar Eclectic at M Club Lounge Mediterranean at Tahina Terrace Asian-Fusion at Edamame Coffee House at Kathmandu Baking Company, M Bistro by KBC International at Thamel Kitchen

How do I book a table at one of the restaurants at Kathmandu Marriott Hotel? 

To book a table at Thamel Kitchen [click here](http://www.sevenrooms.com/reservations/thamelkitchenktmmc?venues=thamelkitchenktmmc,edamamektmmc) To book a table at Edamame [click here](http://www.sevenrooms.com/reservations/edamamektmmc?venues=edamamektmmc,thamelkitchenktmmc) You can also call +977 1-5970300 to place a reservation.

Does Kathmandu Marriott Hotel offer complimentary breakfast? 

Complimentary breakfast is not currently served at Kathmandu Marriott Hotel. View on-site and nearby dining options on our [dining page](https://www.marriott.com/en-us/hotels/ktmmc-kathmandu-marriott-hotel/dining/#on-site-dining).
