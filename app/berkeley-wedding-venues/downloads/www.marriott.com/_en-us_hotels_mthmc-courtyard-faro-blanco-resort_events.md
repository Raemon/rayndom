## Easy Access, Endless Possibilities

Courtyard Faro Blanco Resort is perfectly positioned in Marathon, Florida—just 47 miles from Key West International Airport and 113 miles from Miami International Airport. Whether your group is arriving by boat, bus, or bike, getting here is easy. On-site shuttle and bus parking can be arranged, making logistics simple for bike tours, fishing groups, and incentive trips.

With our exclusive marina, guests can dock just steps from their rooms and head out for world-class fishing or enjoy scenic biking along the famous Seven Mile Bridge, located just one mile from the resort.

### Beachfront and Beyond

Courtyard Faro Blanco Resort offers more than just stunning views, it’s a destination designed for connection, relaxation, and adventure. Groups can unwind across four sparkling pools, lounge in private cabanas, or gather around beach bonfires under the stars. Our on-site catering, room service, and easy access to two marinas make planning effortless, whether you're here for a retreat, reunion, or incentive trip. From snorkeling excursions and fishing charters to lawn games and spa treatments, every amenity is crafted to enhance your group’s experience in the heart of the Florida Keys.

E-Shape, U-Shape and T-Shape 

Appropriate for groups of fewer than 40 people. These are best for interaction with a leader seated at the head of the setup. Audiovisual is usually best set up at the open end of the seating.

The most desirable setup for medium to large-size lectures. Requires a relatively large room. Tables provide attendees with space for spreading out materials and taking notes.
