## Accommodations

Find Your Balance in spacious guest rooms offering invigorating island views of Grand Cayman or revitalizing views of the sea. Our inspirations for design began with an abstract interpretation of the ocean, influenced by the colors and organic fluidity, our concepts are integrated with the surrounding environment. The rooms exude a Caribbean elegance by combining a fresh palette with a sophisticated touch of timeless coastal design and a continuity of local Caymanian flair.

### Island View Rooms

Offers a view of the interior of the island. The upper level rooms offer a view of Safe Haven golf course and the northeastern end of the island. Ground floor rooms offer patios.
