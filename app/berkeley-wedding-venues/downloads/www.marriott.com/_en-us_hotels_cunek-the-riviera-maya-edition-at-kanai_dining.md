# Modern Mexican Dining Experiences

## Dining

Within this magical ecosystem of nature and sustainable luxury, EDITION presents dining experiences unlike any other as envisioned by Chef Partners Tomas Bermudez and Paco Ruano. From the signature restaurants KI’IS, SO’OL Beach Club and Kitchen at EDITION to The Pool and Lobby Bars, every flavor delivers on the magic of the Maya and the category-defining vision of two of Mexico's culinary geniuses.

Restaurant Special Openings this September

Kitchen will open on Friday, Sept 27, offering its signature menu inspired by local flavors and textures. KI’IS by Paco Ruano will open on Thursday, Sept 12 for a six-hands dinner and Thursday, Sept 26 with regular service. We’re excited to keep on delighting your palate on these exceptional dates.

###  Frequently Asked Questions

What restaurants does The Riviera Maya EDITION at Kanai have on property? 

The on-property restaurants at The Riviera Maya EDITION at Kanai are:

KI'IS Restaurant, by Paco Ruano SO'OL Beach Club, by Tomás Bermúdez Kitchen at EDITION Pool Bar Lobby Bar

Does The Riviera Maya EDITION at Kanai offer room service? 

Yes, room service is available at The Riviera Maya EDITION at Kanai.

What cuisine is offered at The Riviera Maya EDITION at Kanai? 

The cuisines offered at The Riviera Maya EDITION at Kanai are:

Mediterranean at Pool Bar Mexican at Kitchen at EDITION Multiple cuisines at Lobby Bar Seafood at SO'OL Beach Club, by Tomás Bermúdez Other at KI'IS Restaurant, by Paco Ruano

How do I book a table at one of the restaurants at The Riviera Maya EDITION at Kanai?

Does The Riviera Maya EDITION at Kanai offer complimentary breakfast?
