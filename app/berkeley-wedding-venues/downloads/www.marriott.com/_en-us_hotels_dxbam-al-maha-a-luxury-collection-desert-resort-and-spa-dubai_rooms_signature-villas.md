# The Essence of Luxury Desert Villas

## Signature Villas

Set against the awe-inspiring backdrop of golden dunes and pristine wilderness, our luxurious villas with private pool blend timeless elegance with sophisticated design. Each villa features traditional furnishings and handcrafted décor offering a perfect balance of authenticity and indulgence.

### Presidential Villa

Secluded amidst the vast and breathtaking desert landscape, the Presidential Villa is Al Maha’s most expansive and premium villa. Spanning over 5,700 sq. ft. It features a private pool, 3 elegantly appointed bedrooms, 3 bathrooms, an inviting majilis, a grand living room, and dining area designed for exclusivity and refined gatherings.

## Refined Private Pool Deck

The private pool deck offers an elevated outdoor living experience where seclusion, comfort, and breathtaking natural beauty come together seamlessly. Overlooking the sweeping sand dunes, this expansive sanctuary features a temperature-controlled pool, ideal for a refreshing dip at any hour. The space is thoughtfully appointed with sunbeds, lounge chairs, and a table, creating the perfect setting to relax or dine in comfort.

Signature Villa Features

## Exclusive Intimate Gatherings

Elevate your gatherings in the Presidential Villa, where luxury and flexibility converge. Whether you choose the elegantly appointed indoor dining area, the tranquil courtyard, or your private terrace, each space is designed to offer the perfect setting for intimate gatherings. With room for up to 10 guests, the villa provides expansive, versatile locations to create lasting memories. Whichever setting you select, enjoy unparalleled service and attention to detail, ensuring that every occasion is nothing short of extraordinary.

## The Bedouin-Inspired Majlis

Step into an inviting sanctuary where tradition meets elegance. Designed to reflect the rich cultural heritage of the region, this expansive living area is adorned with handcrafted Arabian furnishings, intricate detailing, and authentic antiques that evoke a deep sense of place. Warm tones, soft textiles, and curated artifacts offer a glimpse into Bedouin artistry, while the gentle sound of flowing water enhances the serene atmosphere. Bathed in natural light and overlooking the sweeping desert landscape. The Majlis offers guests a peaceful retreat, a place to relax, reflect, and reconnect amid the timeless beauty of the dunes.

### Opulent Kitchenette

Designed for ease and elegance, it offers a refined and seamless addition to your private villa. Featuring handcrafted finishes and a warm, inviting aesthetic, it creates a serene setting for moments of quiet comfort. From tranquil morning rituals to soothing evening retreats, this well-appointed space adds a layer of thoughtful luxury to your stay.

### Emirates Villa

Set against the awe-inspiring backdrop of sweeping golden dunes, the Emirates Villa offers an expansive sanctuary spanning 4,035 sq. ft. Thoughtfully designed to celebrate the rich heritage of the region, the villa is adorned with authentic Arabian furnishings and intricate handcrafted details that immerse you in the essence of traditional Emirati culture from the moment you arrive.

Indulge in the serenity of your own private temperature-controlled pool, perfectly positioned to overlook the vast desert landscape. The villa also features a tranquil courtyard, an elegant sitting area, and a separate living room

## Crafted for Serenity

Nestled amid endless golden sands, the Emirates Villa promises ultimate privacy and an authentic sense of place. A shaded terrace leads to a serene plunge pool, while interiors are adorned with rich Arabian artistry. The soft rustle of the wind and birdsong blend with glimpses of oryx and gazelles wandering freely, offering an immersive connection to the natural world.

Redefining Luxury

###  Unwind in Comfort and Elegance

The villa unveils a world of desert sophistication. The living room is adorned with sumptuous sofas, perfect for unwinding in effortless style. Two exquisitely designed bedrooms—one with a king bed and the other with twin beds- seamlessly blend comfort with authentic Arabian charm. For a truly elevated retreat, experience a bespoke in-villa breakfast or lunch, thoughtfully crafted by our expert chefs. As night falls, indulge in an intimate dinner on your private pool deck, surrounded by the serene beauty of the desert under a canopy of stars.
