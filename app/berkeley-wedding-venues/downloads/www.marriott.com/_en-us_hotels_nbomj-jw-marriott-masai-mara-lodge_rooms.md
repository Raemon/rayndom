# Tented Suites in the Masai Mara

## Tented Suites

Discover unparalleled luxury in the heart of the wilderness with JW Marriott Masai Mara's tented suites. Each Masai Mara Game Reserve lodge invites guests to experience an authentic safari with breathtaking views of the savannah, vibrant sunrises, and enchanting sunsets. Designed for both comfort and immersion, these suites feature modern amenities while capturing the spirit of a classic safari camp. Enjoy a king-sized bed, en-suite bathrooms with indoor and outdoor showers, and a private deck with a temperature-controlled jacuzzi. Inspired by nature, the suite’s soft tones, natural textures, and local colors blend seamlessly with the landscape. Awake to the sounds of birds and distant lion roars before setting out on an exhilarating morning game drive. A stay at JW Marriott Masai Mara Lodge promises an unforgettable blend of luxury and adventure, making your safari experience truly exceptional.

## Deluxe Suite

Each canvas topped tent is woven into nature and set on the banks of the Talek River overlooking the picturesque Masai Mara National Reserve. The spacious and expansive luxury en-suite tents, each a 120 square meters, feature a King-sized bed or two Queen beds, a lounge area and decking featuring an outdoor whirlpool bath and shower. Additional features include a complimentary mini bar, safe, vanity with double basins and luxury amenities, writing desk with charging points, complimentary WiFi, and yoga mats.
