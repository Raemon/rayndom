The Art of Frescoes in Florence 




## Discover The Art of Frescoes in Florence 

Explore breathtaking frescoes and Renaissance art by legendary artists like Botticelli, Ghirlandaio, Masaccio, Paolo Uccello, and Filippino Lippi.

##  Chiesa di San Salvatore in Ognissanti 

Located on the same elegant piazza as The St. Regis Florence, its graceful façade hides a remarkable number of Renaissance frescoes painted by the most celebrated names in art history; Ghirlandaio and Botticelli, both born and bred in the city and true Florentine masters. 

##  Basilica di Santa Maria Novella 

Here the walls are a real ‘who’s who’ of the Renaissance art world. From one of the vastest cycles of frescoes in the whole city to unique, ground-breaking contributions to the fresco art form itself, by masters like Masaccio, Ghirlandaio, Paolo Uccello and Filippino Lippi.

##  Basilica di Santa Trinita 

Inside this basilica’s Sassetti Chapel are a collection of frescoes by Ghirlandaio, celebrated not only for their intricate detail and vivid colours, but also because they serve as a window into daily life in 15th century Florence. Ghirlandaio’s ‘Adoration of the Shepherds’ is considered his masterwork. 

##  Chiesa Di Santa Maria Del Carmine 

Renowned for hosting one of the masterpieces of early Renaissance art: the Brancacci Chapel, this church is home to the work of Masaccio and Masolino. Their frescoes showcase a revolutionary approach to depicting space and human form in an almost sculptural way.
