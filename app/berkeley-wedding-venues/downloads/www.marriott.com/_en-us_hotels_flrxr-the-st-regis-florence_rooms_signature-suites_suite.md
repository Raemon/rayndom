## Contemporary Luxury Suite

###### A unique design in a unique setting

A large furnished balcony allows seductive views of the timeless Arno River for guests of our Contemporary Luxury Suite. With its modern ambiance, the suite is a unique and expansive environment created for those who appreciate quiet luxury, unparalleled service and a refined sensibility. The palette of the suite is serene and sophisticated, featuring muted neutrals, wood flooring enhanced with wool carpets, and furnishing details that create a calm, cool effect. 

##  Living Area 

The sitting area features a fireplace and a two-or three-person sofa, while the work desk is ideal for connecting via wireless High Speed Internet Access and a direct-dial telephone. State-of-the-art entertainment includes a 60-inch Bang & Olufsen LCD flan screen TV with on-demand movies and DVD player, as well as a Bose sound system with an iPod / iPhone docking station. An alluring dining area seats eight for gourmet meals and casual gatherings.

##  Master Bedroom 

The separate bedroom houses one king-size bed, dressed in exclusive cotton sheets, as well as a 42-inch LCD flat screen TV. The marble master bathroom comprises a deep-soaking tub, a separate shower and double vanities. Additional nuances include a TV screen integrated into the mirror, an auxiliary telephone, premium Acqua di Parma amenities, plush bathrobes and slippers. Available in suites, elevated St. Regis Butler Service can provide for any request.

##  Floor Plan 

The unique Suite is composed of a living room with two or three person sofa, a dining area, a separate bedroom and a bathroom with a separate shower and double vanities.
