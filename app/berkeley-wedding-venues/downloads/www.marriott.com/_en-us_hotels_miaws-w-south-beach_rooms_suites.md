# Discover Our Unmatched Luxury Suites

## Suites

At W South Beach, experience elevated oceanfront living in our luxury suites. Floor-to-ceiling windows, private balconies, and sweeping ocean views are complemented by curated design details throughout. Spacious interiors, plush bedding, and spa-inspired bathrooms offer comfort with intention—enhanced by personalized service and exclusive amenities in our hotel suites in South Beach, Miami, Florida. 

### Penthouse 8

Penthouse 8 at W South Beach redefines luxury, offering expansive ocean views, a private rooftop plunge pool, and stylish, modern interiors. With multiple bedrooms, spacious living areas, and floor-to-ceiling windows, the penthouse provides an elevated retreat. Experience a modern design imagined by Urban Robot, one of the world's foremost architectural design firms, the suite acts as a fully functioning residence.

## The Penthouse Experience Inclusions

Indulge in a stay where the skyline is your backdrop and the ocean your soundtrack. The Penthouse Collective Experience at W South Beach is more than a room—it’s a curated journey of art, wellness, and insider access for our most discerning guests. Enjoy a hand-selected fruit welcome, two bespoke spa treatments, $200 in complimentary dry cleaning, and exclusive reduced valet rates. Your dedicated W Insider concierge unlocks the city’s hidden treasures, while daily fitness classes and a private tour of The Bass Museum inspire balance and culture. Relax with a one-day oceanfront sand bed, refresh with a complimentary non-alcoholic minibar, and depart with a personalized farewell gift. Elevate your expectations. Redefine your stay.
