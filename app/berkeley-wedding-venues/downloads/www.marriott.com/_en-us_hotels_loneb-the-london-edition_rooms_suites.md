# Accommodation & Design

## Suites

Our suites offer a spacious layout combining stylish simplicity, with decadent details. These suites feature oak floors and walls paneled in light or dark walnut wood. The living areas boast a tufted George Smith chair with gold-framed photography by Hendrik Kerstens, adding an artful touch. Bathrooms provide custom-made Le Labo amenities featuring the EDITION signature scent.

### Fitzrovia Suite

The Fitzrovia Suite offers a separate living room and is located on a high floor, providing stunning views. The bathrooms feature an enclosed rainforest shower for a luxurious experience.

Enjoy a spacious living room, dining area for 10, office, library, and additional guest bathroom. Guests of the 195-square-metre Penthouse can access their suite privately, and have the ability to interconnect with two additional guestrooms to create a spacious 3-bedroom suite.
