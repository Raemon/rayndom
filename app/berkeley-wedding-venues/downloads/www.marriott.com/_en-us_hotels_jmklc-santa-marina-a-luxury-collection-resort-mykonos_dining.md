# Discover the Tastes of Summer

## Dining

With four tempting options for dining and drinks – including a cocktail bar and the world’s first ever Buddha-Bar Beach – Santa Marina is one of Mykonos’ premier destinations for entertaining. The experience is enhanced by stunning views of the wide-open Aegean and the intimate Ornos Bay.

At This Hotel

###  Frequently Asked Questions

What restaurants does Santa Marina, a Luxury Collection Resort, Mykonos have on property? 

The on-property restaurants at Santa Marina, a Luxury Collection Resort, Mykonos are:

Buddha-Bar Beach Mykonos Oasis Pool & Lounge Santa Marina Beach Bar Elais

Does Santa Marina, a Luxury Collection Resort, Mykonos offer room service? 

Yes, room service is available at Santa Marina, a Luxury Collection Resort, Mykonos.

What cuisine is offered at Santa Marina, a Luxury Collection Resort, Mykonos? 

The cuisines offered at Santa Marina, a Luxury Collection Resort, Mykonos are:

Mediterranean at Elais Asian-Fusion at Buddha-Bar Beach Mykonos Other at Oasis Pool & Lounge, Santa Marina Beach Bar

How do I book a table at one of the restaurants at Santa Marina, a Luxury Collection Resort, Mykonos? 

To book a table at Buddha-Bar Beach Mykonos [click here](https://www.sevenrooms.com/reservations/buddhabarbeachmykonos) To book a table at Elais [click here](https://www.sevenrooms.com/reservations/elaisrestaurant) You can also call +30 228-9023220 to place a reservation.

Does Santa Marina, a Luxury Collection Resort, Mykonos offer complimentary breakfast? 

Free Buffet Breakfast is available at Santa Marina, a Luxury Collection Resort, Mykonos during the following hours: Monday-Sunday 8:00 - 11:00 AM. Free Continental Breakfast is available at Santa Marina, a Luxury Collection Resort, Mykonos during the following hours: Monday-Sunday 8:00 - 11:00 AM.
