# Ocean View Suites With Balconies

## Suites

Your stay, your way starts with front-row views of the Florida Keys. Our Ocean View Suites offer everything you need to for a tropical getaway or family vacation, from spacious living areas to private balconies overlooking the water. It’s the perfect space to relax, recharge, and make the island experience your own. 

### Ocean View Suite

Our Ocean View Studio Suites at Courtyard Faro Blanco Resort offer a peaceful Florida Keys escape with uninterrupted waterfront views of sunrise or sunset over the sea. Each suite includes a private, furnished balcony, your front-row seat to the beauty of the Keys, swaying palms and the marina.

Whether you're planning a romantic getaway, celebrating something special, or simply need space to get away, these suites offer a thoughtful blend of style, comfort and tropical surroundings. If you’re searching for a waterfront room with a balcony in the Florida Keys, your ideal stay begins here.

Step outside, breathe in the salt air, and let the view set the tone for your time in paradise.
