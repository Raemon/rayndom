Meetings Set-Up 

Energize your meetings or special events at W Hotels. Whatever the occasion, we'll create an atmosphere that'll engage your attendees and amplify your celebrations. We'll develop a meeting space that'll inspire and get ideas flowing.

Venues 

Magnify any occasion at W Punta de Mita, where our original indoor venues, beachside outdoor venues, and custom decorations set a vibrant scene for everything from conferences, product launches, and board meetings to celebrations, fashion shows, and incentive groups. Gather for a conference or cocktail hour in one of three state-of-the-art studios located near the resort’s Welcome Area. Featuring a wall-to-wall glass entrance with partial ocean views, these adaptable spaces range in size from 492 to 1,354 square feet and host up to 90 guests cocktail-style or up to 72 guests classroomstyle. An open-concept foyer also allows for easy connection between the three studios. 

Celebrate a successful day or a seminal anniversary with an epic banquet of up to 250 guests at our outdoor Garden Plaza. Situated at the heart of the resort, next to the lake, this stunning venue remixes palm trees and local flora with views of the WET Deck, Huichol Sunway, and ocean beyond. 

For smaller celebrations, like a cocktail banquet of up to 60 guests, the Spice Market terrace, on the roof of our Spice Market restaurant, also boasts ocean views from its wooden pergolas. 

Hosts with their hearts set on celebrating beachside can select from our Venazu Beach, Beachfront Area, and Chevycheria lounge, each with limitless Pacific Ocean vistas. 

Grand fiestas of up to 400 guests cocktail-style or up to 200 banquet-style electrify Venazu Beach, nestled between the Venazu restaurant and Chevycheria. Barbecues of up to 100 guests or cocktail banquets of up to 60 guests find a photo-ready home on the Beachfront Area’s natural palm and grass islands, while the Chevycheria Lounge is a memorable setting for up to 50 guests.

Wireless High-Speed Internet Access in Meeting Facilities 

Wireless High-Speed Internet access is available to all guests attending events at the resort. Please contact our meeting specialists for more information if hosting an event with specific internet requirements.

Banquet Kits 

Keep the party (or meeting) going with our Banquet Kits, available for breakfast, brunch, lunch, dinner, coffee breaks, and hors d'oeuvres. Each custom kit features original menus designed to illuminate the host’s vision and guests’ desires. 

Design your meal to match your meeting or event. Our signature Banquet Kits feature customizable menus for breakfast, lunch, dinner, coffee breaks, and hors d’oeuvres. You can also opt for one of our popular pre-set banquet menus, which feature everything from tempting Mexican dishes and ceviche to fresh barbecue and tacos. 

Even the service can be tailored to the occasion. Meals can be plated, offered buffet or family style, with the possibility for live cooking stations and box lunches.

Recess 

From stretching and yoga to brain-teasers and ice-breakers, W Hotels' meeting specialists will help you create unique, inspiring, team building and downright fun experiences that will transform your meeting and still complement the business at hand.

Audio-Visual Services 

Spark inspiration with the assistance of PSAV. Our in-house audiovisual partner places the latest technology at your fingertips as well as supplies an on-site group coordinator so you can take it easy and enjoy the show.

LCD screens, Wireless High-Speed Internet Access, and state-of-the-art audiovisual equipment put you within a millisecond of the world you're influencing. Amplify the moment with live music or a DJ. We'll connect you to local artists.  
  
Own the moment with the help of PSAV. Along with supplying an on-site group coordinator to tend to the details, our in-house audiovisual partner arranges everything from LCD projection screens and equipment to lighting options, microphones, speakers, and more.  
  
Please note that meeting and event venues do not feature built-in technology and all audiovisual equipment is provided upon request. Fees apply.
