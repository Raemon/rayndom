###  Frequently Asked Questions

What restaurants does Courtyard by Marriott Key West Waterfront have on property? 

The on-property restaurants at Courtyard by Marriott Key West Waterfront are:

The Bistro – Eat. Drink. Connect.® Tiki Bar

Does Courtyard by Marriott Key West Waterfront offer room service? 

No, room service is not offered at Courtyard by Marriott Key West Waterfront. You can view other dining options on our [dining page](https://www.marriott.com/en-us/hotels/eywcy-courtyard-key-west-waterfront/dining/).

What cuisine is offered at Courtyard by Marriott Key West Waterfront? 

The cuisines offered at Courtyard by Marriott Key West Waterfront are:

American at The Bistro – Eat. Drink. Connect.®, Tiki Bar

Does Courtyard by Marriott Key West Waterfront offer complimentary breakfast? 

Complimentary breakfast is not currently served at Courtyard by Marriott Key West Waterfront. View on-site and nearby dining options on our [dining page](https://www.marriott.com/en-us/hotels/eywcy-courtyard-key-west-waterfront/dining/#on-site-dining).
