# Hotels in Mexico

Mexico, a country of rich culture and breathtaking scenery, beckons you to discover its ancient ruins, colonial cities, and pristine beaches. From the vibrant streets of Mexico City to the tranquil shores of the Yucatán Peninsula, each region offers a unique tapestry of history, art, and natural splendor. Marriott Bonvoy’s collection of hotels in Mexico is strategically located to serve as your perfect home base, whether you're exploring the Teotihuacan pyramids, the charming streets of Guanajuato, or the idyllic beaches of the Riviera Maya. Be it a family holiday, a romantic escape, or a business trip, our hotels promise a stay that is both comfortable and memorable.

  


Savor the flavors of Mexico at our hotels, where traditional cuisine meets modern culinary techniques, offering a taste of the country’s rich gastronomic heritage. Many of our properties feature on-site restaurants, spas, and fitness centers to enhance your stay, while others are conveniently located near popular shopping districts and cultural sites. With a range of options from luxury resorts to boutique hotels, Marriott Bonvoy is committed to providing exceptional service and amenities that make your visit to Mexico both relaxing and exhilarating.
