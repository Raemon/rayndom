## Unwind at our spa hotel in Osaka

Welcome to AWAY Spa in Osaka, a massage spa and a refreshing sanctuary nestled within the urban cityscape. Whether you're seeking solo rejuvenation or a shared experience, our spa offers the perfect escape. With five treatment rooms, a relaxation zone, showers, sauna, steam room, and beauty bar, we provide a personalized pampering experience just for you. Step into our sensory oasis, where soothing colors, playful scents, and sustainable products come together to create the ultimate journey to relaxation. Your revitalization begins here.

## Our Global Product: Comfort Zone

[ comfort zone ] is a science-based, conscious skincare line born from the Davines Group, which began in Italy in 1983 as a small research lab and has since grown into a global leader and certified B Corp, committed to the highest sustainability standards. Their luxurious, clinically-tested skincare and body treatments are made in Italy with a deep dedication to both people and the planet. "Conscious" is their approach to sustainability, reflecting their responsibility to deliver exceptional results while honoring the skin and the environment. They prioritize high-quality ingredients, renewable resources, and recyclable packaging, with full CO2 compensation through the EthioTrees reforestation initiative in Ethiopia.

## Official Wellness Champion

Experience holistic wellness at AWAY Spa, where we’re proud to have received the Wellness Champion badge from the World Wellness Weekend Organization. We have held events such as personalized Nutrition Coaching Trial Sessions, where our expert nutritionist offers tailored advice on balanced eating and mindful nutrition, as well as revitalizing Half Day Wellness Programs featuring activities like Plogging, Pilates, and Meditation, open to both guests and the public. Additionally, our FIT gym hosts 10-minute Exercise sessions led by personal trainers who guide guests through efficient workouts, including kickboxing and circuit training. Stay tuned for future exciting events and opportunities to rejuvenate and connect in a supportive environment at our spa hotel in Osaka!
