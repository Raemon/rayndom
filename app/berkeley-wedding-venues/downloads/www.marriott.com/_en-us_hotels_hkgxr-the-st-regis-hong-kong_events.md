### Weddings & Occasions

Envision the elegant wedding of your dreams in our pillarless Astor Ballroom for up to 450 guests

Two Vincent Astor wedding venues are available for intimate bridal showers, rehearsals or ceremonies

Many of our Hong Kong party venues, such as Caroline Astor Room, are ideal for pre-bridal gatherings

Access your wedding venue from a private elevator near our hotel entrance and basement carpark

Event organizers have the option to privatize the entire floor for grandiose ceremonies and banquets

Celebrate the special day with The St. Regis Champagne Sabrage, a one-of-a-kind wedding experience

###  Plan Your Special Day 

The sixth floor evokes the audacious glamour and luxury of our first St. Regis hotel in New York with magnificent facilities including Astor Ballroom, Vincent Astor I & II, Caroline Astor Room and a gracious pre-function space, equipped with state-of-the-art audiovisual and production services.

###  Curated Menus 

From a traditional Chinese set dinner to a modern luncheon in Western style, late wedding suppers or intimate standing cocktail receptions, our chefs are dedicated to work out a creative and tailored menu for you based on your vision and preferences.

###  Iconic Spaces 

Our suite of captivating wedding venues measures over 10,000 square feet, each with outstanding characteristics that suits your specific needs. Plan your grand weddings at our pillarless Astor Ballroom, and invite guests to mingle at Vincent Astor which can easily be transformed for recreational purposes.

###  Bespoke Arrangement 

From planning to execution, St. Regis couples can anticipate meticulous care and attention from our experienced and professional team of wedding specialists. We have strong connection with industry professionals - from extraordinary floral arrangements to innovative staging ideas, our team is always here to make sure your wedding is planned to perfection.

Wedding Packages

###  Exquisite Wedding Suite Package 

Celebrate your wedding in style with exceptional suite benefits at The St. Regis Hong Kong. Allow your butler to arrange romantic room set-up, Chinese tea pots and cushion, as well as bespoke amenities for you, indulge in our St. Regis rituals and more.

###  Wedding Ceremony Package 

Host your wedding ceremony at the House of Astor and enjoy special privileges with our compliments, such as the arrangement of wedding ceremony arch with floral decoration, a six-tier model wedding cake, sabrage with champagne toasting for cake cutting ceremony, and more.

###  St. Regis Wedding Banquet Package 

Plan your big day with us and leave all details to our team of wedding specialists to tailor for you. Pamper yourself and your significant other an exceptional accommodation in our luxurious Honeymoon Suite.
