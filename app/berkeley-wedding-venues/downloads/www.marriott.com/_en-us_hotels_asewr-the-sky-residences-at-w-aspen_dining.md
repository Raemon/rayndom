# Elevated Dining in Aspen

## Dining

Fuel your Alpine stay at The Sky Residences at W Aspen with a collection of elevated cuisine in one of our three dining venues. Our options include dynamic flavors at the Living Room, sumptuous bites and a scene-worthy après at our outdoor WET Deck, flavors from the Far East at 39° and a private chef dining experience.

###  Frequently Asked Questions

What restaurants does The Sky Residences at W Aspen have on property? 

The on-property restaurants at The Sky Residences at W Aspen are:

Rooftop IN-ROOM DINING Private Chef Dining Hidalgo - Agave Cocktail Lounge 39 DEGREES LIVING ROOM

Does The Sky Residences at W Aspen offer room service? 

No, room service is not offered at The Sky Residences at W Aspen. You can view other dining options on our [dining page](https://www.marriott.com/en-us/hotels/asewr-the-sky-residences-at-w-aspen/dining/).

What cuisine is offered at The Sky Residences at W Aspen? 

The cuisines offered at The Sky Residences at W Aspen are:

Eclectic at Hidalgo - Agave Cocktail Lounge American at LIVING ROOM Multiple cuisines at Rooftop, IN-ROOM DINING, Private Chef Dining Other at 39 DEGREES

Does The Sky Residences at W Aspen offer complimentary breakfast? 

Complimentary breakfast is not currently served at The Sky Residences at W Aspen. View on-site and nearby dining options on our [dining page](https://www.marriott.com/en-us/hotels/asewr-the-sky-residences-at-w-aspen/dining/#on-site-dining).
