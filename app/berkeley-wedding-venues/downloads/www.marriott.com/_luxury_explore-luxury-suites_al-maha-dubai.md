Al Maha, a Luxury Collection Desert Resort and Spa, Dubai is a private, guest-only oasis nestled among the lush palm groves, emerald canopies and iconic sand dunes of the Dubai Desert Conservation Reserve. The Presidential Suite at Al Maha is the epitome of luxury and sophistication, offering guests an unforgettable desert experience. The suite offers three bedrooms that feature custom-made super king-size beds, handcrafted furnishings, and a private temperature-controlled swimming pool, all designed to provide guests with the utmost comfort and relaxation. The inviting majlis creates a calming ambience, enhanced by the beautiful Arabian antiques and artifacts on display. The gentle sound of running water adds to the sense of tranquility, creating a space where guests can truly unwind.

The Royal Suites combine some of the richest living experiences with an authentic ambience inspired by the many Arabian artifacts and antiques. A spacious lounge and two luxurious bedrooms, each with a custom-made super king-size bed and handcrafted furnishings, offer a relaxing experience. The wide terrace with sun loungers, private deck dining area, and a private pool present expansive views onto the open desert. Indigenous wildlife, such as gazelle or Arabian oryx, will frequently be seen grazing near the suites. Together with exemplary facilities, attentive service, and delightfully quiet surroundings, the Royal Suites offer an exclusive experience and a place of restful solace.
