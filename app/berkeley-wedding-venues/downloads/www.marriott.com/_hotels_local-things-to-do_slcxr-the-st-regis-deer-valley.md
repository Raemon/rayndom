# Hotel near Jordanelle Reservoir for an unforgettable stay

## Experiences

Nestled in the lower Deer Valley of Park City our upscale hotel with outdoor pools is primely positioned for year-round adventure. When fresh powder graces the Wasatch Mountains, The St. Regis Deer Valley offers guests convenient ski-in, and ski-out access to the slopes, as well as a private ski beach. Winter in Park City also boasts the intriguing Sun Dance Film Festival and an array of cold-weather activities including ice skating, snowshoeing, and horse-drawn sleigh rides. In the warmer months, marvel at the seasonless beauty of Park City as the snow is replaced with colorful mountain wildflowers. Come spring and summer, enjoy the temperate weather by hiking, biking, fly-fishing, golfing, and more. The St. Regis Deer Valley is located just minutes from the city's charming downtown area and 25 minutes from the famous Utah Olympic Park.

###  Hiking/Biking 

(Weather Permitting). Ranging from mellow, scenic hikes to adrenaline-raising rides, Park City offers some of the best mountain biking, road cycling and hiking available. The town's ski resorts provide lift-served, single- and double-track mountain biking and hiking on 325+ miles of trails.

###  Horseback Riding 

When you come to Park City in the summer, it's time to leave your cares-and your car-behind and experience the mountains like the miners who founded the city: on horseback. Ride up the mountains and down into the valleys, through the aspen trees and lush underbrush. Can you think of a better way to explore the Rockies?

###  Historic Main Street 

Park City's Main Street is lined with a variety of boutiques, galleries, restaurants, and pubs. The national award-winning High West Distillery (a fine whiskey and vodka distillery, general store, saloon and restaurant) and the Park City Museum are unique destinations worth a visit.

###  Hot Air Ballooning 

If you really want to experience summer in Park City, try it from a few thousand feet up. Rise to the elevation of the beautiful Wasatch Mountains, and ride gently above it all. A hot air balloon ride will take your breath away.

###  Water Sports 

There is no shortage of water recreation in and around the Park City Area. From sailing to kayaking to river rafting, soak in a water adventure that is sure to make your trip a memorable one.

###  Fishing 

In Park City, you're never far from a crystal-clear river or stream. Grab some waders and a pole and spend an afternoon-or a week-tapping your line across the cool mountain waters. Go out on your own, or with one of our experienced guides.

## Scenic Drives

Embark on an unforgettable journey through Deer Valley's breathtaking landscapes. Traverse the stunning Mirror Lake Highway, a 30-mile stretch from Kamas to Mirror Lake. Discover the wonders of the Alpine Loop, a captivating 20-mile loop starting from Sundance Resort. Indulge in the scenic majesty of Guardsman Pass, a captivating route connecting Park City to Big Cottonwood Canyon. Stay at our Park City hotel with outdoor pools near Park City Mountain Resort to immerse yourself in the beauty of nature and create lasting memories along these remarkable scenic byways.

### Utah Olympic Legacy Foundation

Our partnership with the Utah Olympic Legacy Foundation offers hotel guests elite-level bobsledding and biathlon experiences, with additional signature St. Regis enhancements. 

Learn More 

##### Bobsled With An Olympian

Experience the thrill of the 2002 Winter Games on the Bobsled track alongside an Olympic Silver Medalist, Valerie Fleming or Shauna Rohbock.For the first 45 minutes of the session, guests will hear Valerie or Shauna’s inspirational story and receive an exclusive Olympian-led tour of Utah Olympic Park’s bobsled track. Guests will also have an opportunity to visit and take photos at curve 12 of the track. After the safety demonstrations, visitors will take the ride of a lifetime! In less than mile, you will rip through 10 curves, pulling 3Gs and hitting speeds up to 70 mph. Your day on the track would not be complete without a medals ceremony of your own, an autographed photo from Shauna and Val, and a special souvenir from your time here at Utah Olympic Park.

##### Olympic Biathlon Experience

Discover why Biathlon is the most popular winter sport in Europe! As an “aspiring Olympian,” participants are coached by our professional staff as they learn to shoot with precision rifles that are comparable to equipment used during the Olympic Games. After a safety briefing, instructional range orientation, and time for practice, participants engage in a mini fun competition set up for the group. During the winter, you will either ski or snowshoe and in the summer, choose jogging or hiking. The biathlon course will raise heart rates and give participants a sense of authentic biathlon competition. Participants are challenged to hit all five targets.
