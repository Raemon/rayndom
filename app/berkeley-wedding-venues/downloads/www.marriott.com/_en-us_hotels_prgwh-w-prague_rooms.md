# Luxury Stays in the Heart of Prague

## Accommodations

Inspired by nature’s unruly ways, the guest rooms and hotel suites in Prague continue to pay tribute to the Art Nouveau style. Rhythmic, curvy lines flow through interiors themed in green. A final touch of Bohemia, reinterpreted through a bold design lens, completes the feast for your eyes. 

Design

### A Masterpiece of Design

A design that marries historical elegance with modern flair. The design, crafted by renowned firms Benoy, AvroKO, and Chapman Taylor, draws inspiration from Prague's rich cultural heritage and the whimsical art of Alphonse Mucha. The hotel seamlessly blends the historic Art Nouveau architecture with contemporary design elements, creating a unique and luxurious atmosphere. Inspired by magical gardens, the design incorporates floral chandeliers, embroidered corridors, and garden murals, adding a touch of nature and fantasy to the space. The 161 guestrooms and suites, including the WOW Bohemia Duplex, offer a mix of modern and Art Nouveau styles, with top-notch amenities like the AWAY Spa and Above Rooftop. W Prague is not just a place to stay; it's an immersive experience that celebrates the city's artistic and cultural legacy while offering modern luxury and comfort.

### Wow & E-Wow Suites

These flagships of luxury bring old-world glamour alive again with contemporary design. Panels with a glossy finish, elevated bars, and open bathrooms stretch out over a never-ending space. While E WOW comes with a hanging fireplace and floor-to-ceiling windows, both suites allow for panoramic views of the cityscape unfolding in front of you. This is the start to the most high-end hotel experience you’d wish for.
