# Culinary Experience in Reykjavik

## Dining

Our Reykjavik culinary hot spots include the expansive lobby bar, signature restaurant TIDES and TIDES Café, and our panoramic ROOF TOP Bar. Something unique to the hotel that has not been seen before in Iceland is that The Reykjavik EDITION is home to multiple occasions, all under one roof. TIDES, The Reykjavik EDITION´s signature restaurant, celebrates seafood from the pristine waters of Iceland alongside locally sourced meats in a warm and inviting environment. Featuring characteristically Nordic cooking techniques that value simplicity, the quality of the ingredients shines in every dish.

Michelin Guide Recommended TIDES

Explore the exquisite tastes of Iceland´s culinary legacy at TIDES. Our menu features the best seafood from the pristine waters of Iceland along with locally sourced meats and ingredients. Tuck into the night and enjoy the relaxed atmosphere with your favortie people.

### Delight Your Senses

Indulge your sense in some of the finest cuisine and custom crafted cocktails in any of our venues from breakfast to late night comfort food with a view. Whatever strikes your the mood we have the perfect setting to ensure a memorable experience.

###  Lobby Bar 

The Lobby Bar is a dynamic social space featuring an open-flame fireplace creating a warm central focal point for people to gather around. Serving locally produced craft beers, specialty cocktails with an Icelandic twist and wine by the glass, alongside a carefully selected snack menu; it is the ideal meeting place for guests and visitors.

###  TIDES Café 

For those on the go, Tides bakery and café - adjacent to the restaurant and accessible from the main street - serves locally roasted coffee, brewed in-house. Alongside this is a selection of quick bites and fresh sandwiches, as well as freshly baked pastries. The café also serves delicious sourdough or rye bread sandwiches and grab-and go meals that you can eat in to take away.

###  Frequently Asked Questions

What restaurants does The Reykjavik EDITION have on property?

Tides Restaurant Tides Cafe Lobby Bar The ROOF Breakfast at Tides Restaurant

Does The Reykjavik EDITION offer room service? 

Yes, room service is available at The Reykjavik EDITION.

What cuisine is offered at The Reykjavik EDITION? 

The cuisines offered at The Reykjavik EDITION are:

Coffee House at Tides Cafe International at Lobby Bar Other at Tides Restaurant, The ROOF, Breakfast at Tides Restaurant

How do I book a table at one of the restaurants at The Reykjavik EDITION? 

To book a table at Tides Restaurant [click here](https://www.dineout.is/editionreykjavik?lng=en) To book a table at The ROOF [click here](https://www.dineout.is/theroof?g=2&dt=2024-09-19T16:45&type=bookings) To book a table at Breakfast at Tides Restaurant [click here](https://www.dineout.is/editionreykjavik?lng=en) You can also call +354 582-0000 to place a reservation.
