# Hotels in United Arab Emirates

The United Arab Emirates is a place of captivating contrasts, where the allure of ancient traditions meets the sophistication of modern life. From the dazzling cityscape of Dubai to the tranquil deserts of Abu Dhabi, this country invites you to experience a luxury adventure like no other. Marriott Bonvoy’s collection of hotels in the United Arab Emirates provides the perfect launchpad for both your business and leisure pursuits, offering an array of amenities and services crafted to ensure a stay that is both comfortable and unforgettable. Whether you're exploring vibrant shopping districts, savoring world-class dining, or unwinding on pristine beaches, our hotels are strategically situated to provide you with easy access to the very best the United Arab Emirates has to offer.

  


Your journey to the United Arab Emirates is incomplete without a taste of its rich cultural tapestry. Explore the historic Al Ain Oasis, be awed by the grandeur of the Sheikh Zayed Grand Mosque, or immerse yourself in the vibrant art and culture at the Louvre Abu Dhabi. Marriott Bonvoy’s hotels in the United Arab Emirates are thoughtfully designed, offering complimentary Wi-Fi, top-notch fitness centers, and family-friendly amenities, catering to every traveler. With a range of properties across the country, from the bustling cityscape of Dubai to the serene coastal city of Ras Al Khaimah, you can easily customize your stay to match your preferences and schedule.
