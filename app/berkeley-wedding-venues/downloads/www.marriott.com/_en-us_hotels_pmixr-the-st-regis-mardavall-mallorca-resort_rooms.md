## Accommodations

With over a century of perfecting the art of hospitality, The St. Regis Mardavall Mallorca Resort invites you to discover 125 meticulously designed rooms and suites near Palma, each offering views of the Mediterranean Sea or the resort’s lush gardens. Every accommodation features a private terrace or balcony—an intimate setting for al fresco dining, toasting with friends, or simply unwinding with a glass of wine at sunset. Inside these spacious and exquisitely appointed luxury rooms and suites, no detail is left to chance, and no request is ever too small. Within these capacious and lavishly decorated rooms and luxury suites, no detail is overlooked and no request too insignificant. Fully wired with state-of-the-art entertainment and telecommunications systems, every stay is enhanced by the signature St. Regis Butler Service, anticipating each desire and attending to every detail to create an experience as personalized as it is unforgettable. 

Experience the Ultimate Mediterranean Escape

Our exclusive Mediterranean Escape offer promises an unforgettable stay in the heart of the Mediterranean. Enjoy a complimentary upgrade to a Junior Suite, where breathtaking sea views and personalized amenities await you.

### Explore Our Distinctive Suites

Experience the essence of refined comfort in our exclusive suites, where expansive interiors open onto generous terraces with captivating views of the Mediterranean Sea. Surrounded by the hotel’s lush gardens and the gentle coastal breeze, each suite offers an intimate connection to the natural beauty of Mallorca. A place where every moment is framed by the serenity of the sea and the timeless elegance of St. Regis.
