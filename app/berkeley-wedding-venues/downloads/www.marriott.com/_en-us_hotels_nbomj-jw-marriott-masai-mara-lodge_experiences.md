# Experiences at JW Marriott Masai Mara Lodge

## Experiences

Nestled in the heart of the African wilderness, the JW Marriott Masai Mara Lodge offers a retreat unlike any other, blending luxury with the raw beauty of nature. Guests are invited to immerse themselves in a world where the wild roams free and ancient traditions thrive. Then, after an adventurous day of sustainable tourism and safari game drives, take the opportunity to unwind in the Masai Mara spa with a sauna and gym.

Wildlife Encounters

## Unmatched Proximity to Nature

Overlooking the Main Masai Mara National Reserve and the Talek River, the Safari Lodge offers guests unparalleled close-up views of animals at the watering hole: The Hippo Pool. Its prime location ensures that game drives provide quick access to breathtaking wildlife encounters, making the Safari experience more intimate than ever.

## Cultural Immersion Experience

Experience the essence of the region with a visit to a nearby village, where you’ll engage with the local community and delve into their rich traditions and way of life. This immersive journey reveals the vibrant culture that embodies the spirit of the area. To complete the experience, guests can enjoy a mesmerizing Masai cultural dance right at the Lodge. 

![Guest and Maasai Bridge](https://cache.marriott.com/is/image/marriotts7prod/jw-nbomj-guest-and-maasai-26496-36087:Classic-Hor?wid=1300&fit=constrain)

## Canon Photography Studio

Canon and JW Marriott Masai Mara Lodge have partnered to elevate the safari experience through the art of photography. This brand collaboration offers guests, Canon mirrorless cameras and lenses. Our professional resident photographers offer guests their expertise to perfect their experiential photography skills to document their journey. This partnership creates an immersive safari, blending luxury with lasting, personal memories of the Mara's breathtaking landscapes and wildlife.

## Boma Dinners

Experience an unforgettable Boma Dinner at JW Marriott Masai Mara Lodge, where the warmth of a crackling fire meets the rich flavors of African cuisine. Indulge in expertly grilled meats (Nyama Choma), traditional dishes such as Ugali, and vibrant Masai dance performances under the starlit Mara sky—a true taste of culture and luxury.
