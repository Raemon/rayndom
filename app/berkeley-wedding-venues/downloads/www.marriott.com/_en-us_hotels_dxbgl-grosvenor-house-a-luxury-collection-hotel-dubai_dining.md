# Explore our enticing restaurants in Dubai

## Dining

Located steps away from The Walk at JBR, Grosvenor House, a Luxury Collection Hotel features a variety of elegant restaurants and bars overlooking Dubai Marina. The world-famous Buddha-Bar fuses elegant décor, vibrant cuisine and enchanting entertainment to create an inspiring ambiance. Our restaurant's Michelin Star-winner Chef Patron Vineet Bhatia showcases modern Indian fare at Indego by Vineet, while Toro Toro's menu combines pioneering tastes from Buenos Aires, São Paulo and more. For European bites, check out City Social by Michelin Star-winner Jason Atherton or Rhodes W1 which serves exceptional cuisine for brunch or dinner. When the sun sets, Siddhartha Lounge by Buddha-Bar, 7 Tales and other unique options beckon. Alongside this exceptional collection of Jumeirah Beach Residence restaurants, we offer a 24-hour In-Room Dining menu to satisfy guests' appetites any hour of the day.
