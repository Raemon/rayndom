# 

Discover Oakland, CA

A California Playground for the Venturesome 




###  Choose your California Adventure 

Explore the diverse culture and attractions of Oakland, including the Oakland Museum, Fox Theater, and Oaklandish for shopping, just to name a few. Outdoor enthusiasts can head to the East Bay Regional Park for hiking and biking trails, while animal lovers can enjoy the Oakland Zoo. San Francisco is just a short BART ride away, where you can ride the iconic trolley car, marvel at Lombard Street, and experience the downtown scene. Whether you're exploring the urban landscape or immersing yourself in nature, Oakland offers something for every interest.

###  Belong at the Zoo? 

Animal enthusiasts can delight in a diverse collection of exhibits at the Oakland Zoo, including the African savannah, Great Apes, California Trail, and Tropical Rainforest. The zoo also offers family-friendly entertainment such as Adventure Landing and educational programs for all ages. Conveniently located just a few miles from the Oakland Marriott City Center, the zoo is open daily for visitors to explore and enjoy. Our hotel offers easy access to Oakland Zoo tickets that can be conveniently purchased on our website and will be waiting for you upon your arrival.

######  Local Attractions 

###  Paramount Theater 

Oakland's Paramount Theatre is one of the finest remaining examples of Art Deco design in the United States. Restored to its original splendor, meticulously maintained, and fully upgraded to modern technical standards, the Paramount now serves all the arts. Today, the Paramount Theatre is the home of the Oakland Symphony and Oakland Ballet Company. As one of the San Francisco Bay Area's premiere performing arts facilities, the Paramount hosts a year-round schedule of popular music concerts, variety shows, comedy, dance and theatre.

![](data:image/png;base64,iVBORw0KGgoAAAANSUhEUgAAAAEAAAABCAQAAAC1HAwCAAAAC0lEQVR4nGNiOAMAANUAz5n+TlUAAAAASUVORK5CYII=)

######  Local Attractions 

##  Lake Merritt 

Lake Merritt is a focal point, it stands as the jewel of Oakland, even crowned with lights. A unique fresh and salt-water lake, the largest such lake located within an urban area consisting of 3.4 miles around the circumference of the lake, covering 155 acres of land.

![](data:image/png;base64,iVBORw0KGgoAAAANSUhEUgAAAAEAAAABCAQAAAC1HAwCAAAAC0lEQVR4nGNiOAMAANUAz5n+TlUAAAAASUVORK5CYII=)

######  Local Attractions 

##  Chabot Space Center 

Chabot Space & Science Center is a non-profit institution and community resource located on 13 trail-laced acres in Redwood Regional Park in Oakland amid the largest stand of coastal redwoods in the East Bay. Chabot features a 241-seat full dome planetarium, interactive and hands-on exhibits, space artifacts, a giant screen theater, a Challenger Learning Center, and the only research-level telescopes regularly available to the public for weekly live viewing in the Western United States. The [NASA Experience](https://chabotspace.org/visit/exhibits/the-nasa-experience/) is a hands-on exhibition that brings to life the thrilling, challenging and inspiring process of scientific discovery by showcasing the real stories and people at NASA’s Ames Research Center. Visitors step into the role of a NASA scientist through embarking on hands-on challenges, exploring more than 30+ objects that showcase Ames’ past and future, and getting to know real NASA scientists.

######  Nearby Areas 

##  Old Oakland 

Steeped in history, Old Oakland is a charming historic district featuring well-preserved Victorian architecture and cobblestone streets. This area offers a glimpse into Oakland's past with boutique shops, art galleries, and a vibrant farmer's market. It's a picturesque destination for those seeking a nostalgic experience.

![](data:image/png;base64,iVBORw0KGgoAAAANSUhEUgAAAAEAAAABCAQAAAC1HAwCAAAAC0lEQVR4nGNiOAMAANUAz5n+TlUAAAAASUVORK5CYII=)

######  Nearby Areas 

##  Jack London Square 

Nestled along the waterfront, Jack London Square is a bustling entertainment district that pays homage to the famous American author who once called Oakland home. This vibrant area boasts an array of waterfront dining options, shops, and cultural events, making it a hub for art, dining, and leisurely strolls by the bay.

![](data:image/png;base64,iVBORw0KGgoAAAANSUhEUgAAAAEAAAABCAQAAAC1HAwCAAAAC0lEQVR4nGNiOAMAANUAz5n+TlUAAAAASUVORK5CYII=)

######  Nearby Areas 

##  Chinatown 

Oakland's Chinatown is a vibrant cultural enclave that offers a taste of Asian traditions and flavors. It's a bustling neighborhood known for its diverse Asian cuisine, markets, and cultural festivals. Visitors can explore colorful streets adorned with lanterns, indulge in dim sum, and discover a rich tapestry of Chinese, Vietnamese, and other Asian cultures thriving in the heart of Oakland.

![](data:image/png;base64,iVBORw0KGgoAAAANSUhEUgAAAAEAAAABCAQAAAC1HAwCAAAAC0lEQVR4nGNiOAMAANUAz5n+TlUAAAAASUVORK5CYII=)

######  Nearby Areas 

##  Chinatown 

Oakland is the anchor of the East Bay region, encompassing cities like Berkeley, Emeryville, and Alameda. The East Bay is celebrated for its diverse communities, intellectual pursuits, and cultural attractions. Visitors can explore the iconic University of California, Berkeley campus, enjoy the vibrant arts scene, and take in breathtaking views of the San Francisco Bay while appreciating the East Bay's unique character.

###  Two Cities For The Price of One 

Discover the vibrant city of Oakland, CA. Marvel at the stunning architecture and rich history of the Pardee Home Museum or immerse yourself in the grandeur of the Paramount Theatre. Take a short trip across the bay to the bustling city of San Francisco and take in a show, upscale shopping, eclectic dining and views of the iconic Golden Gate Bridge. With a plethora of cultural attractions, Oakland is an ideal destination for the venturesome.
