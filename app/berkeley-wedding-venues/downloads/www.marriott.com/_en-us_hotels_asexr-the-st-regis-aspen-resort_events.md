### The Premier Venue for Weddings in Aspen

Breathtaking indoor and outdoor wedding venues in Aspen, anticipatory service, and exclusive wedding packages combine for a memorable celebration at The St. Regis Aspen Resort. Home to Aspen's largest ballroom, as well as an array of magnificent indoor and outdoor spaces steeped in the beauty of the Colorado Rocky Mountains, our Aspen wedding venue dazzles at every turn. Allow our St. Regis wedding specialists to assist in creating a tailored event package, one that vividly reflects your unique love.

## The Fountain Courtyard

Our outdoor Fountain Courtyard, located in the middle of our resort, is a remarkable terrace available for weddings in all seasons and a popular venue for Aspen wedding ceremonies. The courtyard gives you a captivating view of the Aspen Mountain and intimate privacy for your grand day. This Aspen wedding venue is large enough to be covered by a tent, or it can remain open to enjoy the crisp mountain air.

## The Grand Astor Ballroom

Located on the lower level of our resort, the St. Regis Ballroom can host up to 600 guests. Saturated in refined champagne hues, this wedding venue in Aspen, Colorado, sets the stage for a romantic wedding reception. Sconces light the fabric-paneled walls while six chandeliers create an enchanted setting. For more intimate weddings, the room divides into two salons, creating the perfect atmosphere for your guests.
