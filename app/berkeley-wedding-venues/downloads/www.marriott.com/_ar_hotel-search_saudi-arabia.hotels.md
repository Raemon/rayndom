# Hotels in Saudi Arabia

Saudi Arabia, a land of ancient history and modern marvels, offers a rich tapestry of cultural and natural wonders. From the bustling streets of Riyadh, the capital, to the historic sites of Jeddah, the country’s vibrant cities are a blend of traditional heritage and cutting-edge development. Marriott Bonvoy’s hotels in Saudi Arabia provide a luxurious base for your explorations, with convenient locations near iconic landmarks like the King Fahd’s Fountain and the Masmak Fortress. Whether you’re visiting for business, leisure, or to explore the country’s profound history, our hotels ensure a comfortable and stress-free stay with world-class amenities and exceptional service.

  


Venture into the vast deserts of Saudi Arabia and experience the breathtaking beauty of the Empty Quarter and the UNESCO World Heritage site of Al-Hijr Archaeological Site (Madain Saleh). Relax on the pristine beaches of the Red Sea, or immerse yourself in the local culture and cuisine of the cities of Al-Ula and Jeddah. Many of our hotels offer spa services, fitness centers, and family-friendly amenities, ensuring you can tailor your stay to your preferences. With Marriott Bonvoy, your stay in Saudi Arabia is sure to be both memorable and enriching.
