# Explore Our Distinctive Suites

## Premium Rooms

From our newly reimagined Three-Bedroom Royal Penthouse and Astor Suites — conceived as private coastal residences — to timeless accommodations imbued with Mediterranean spirit, every suite at The St. Regis Mardavall Mallorca Resort offers a distinctive expression of refined living. Designed to frame the sea and flooded with natural light, these serene spaces blend contemporary elegance with a strong sense of place. Whether seeking expansive family comfort or secluded sophistication, discover a collection curated to elevate every stay — always enhanced by the anticipatory care of the St. Regis Butler. 

### The Newly Renovated Royal Penthouse & Astor Suites

Elevate your stay at The St. Regis Mardavall Mallorca Resort with our most distinguished accommodations, the Royal Penthouse Suite and the Astor Suite – located on the fifth and fourth floors respectively–. Thoughtfully redesigned to capture the spirit of Mallorca, these expansive residences feature panoramic sea-view terraces, serene interiors enriched with artisanal touches, and carefully curated materials that reflect the island’s natural elegance. Immerse yourself in handcrafted refinement, enhanced by the discreet and intuitive service of the St. Regis Butler. From serene moments of retreat to unforgettable gatherings, these suites are crafted for those who appreciate life’s most exquisite details.

![Astor Suite living room and sea view terrace](data:image/png;base64,iVBORw0KGgoAAAANSUhEUgAAAA4AAAAKCAYAAACE2W/HAAAAFklEQVR42mN8/fpLPQMZgHFU48jWCAD/vyDle0zFdgAAAABJRU5ErkJggg==)

## Mediterranean Elegance, Redefined

Thoughtfully reimagined, these spacious signature suites—offering up to 287 m² and three bedrooms—combine the comfort of a private seaside residence with refined, contemporary design. Soft sand tones, natural wood, and flowing sheer curtains create a calm, luminous atmosphere inspired by Mallorca’s landscape. Every detail reflects artisanal heritage and the timeless spirit of St. Regis. From mornings on the terrace to the discreet presence of the St. Regis Butler, each moment is a seamless expression of comfort, privacy, and intuitive service.

Royal Penthouse & Astor Suites

## Space To Suit Every Stay: Up to 3 Bedrooms

With layouts offering up to three bedrooms and reaching 287 m², these signature suites evoke the ease and privacy of a seaside residence. Interiors have been reconfigured to frame the Mediterranean from the very first step inside, creating a natural sequence of spaces that flows gracefully toward the terrace, the expansive gardens facing the sea— and the light beyond.

## Recently Redesigned With Contemporary Mediterranean Grace

Freshly reimagined, these suites reflect a harmonious blend of coastal serenity and modern sophistication, capturing the natural beauty of Mallorca in every detail.

Inspired by the Mediterranean essence, the building’s distinctive character and the island’s landscapes, the design embraces timeless elegance over fleeting trends. Soft sand-hued walls, natural wood sliding doors, and sheer curtains that welcome the Mediterranean light all contribute to an atmosphere of calm sophistication.
