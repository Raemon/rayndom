# Discover our Doha, Qatar, hotel near the airport

## Experiences

Embark on a journey to discovery from Al Messila, a Luxury Collection Resort & Spa, Doha. For the global explorer, Qatar’s rich heritage and timeless culture comprise an infinite succession of surprises. From modern museums to grandiose architecture, the country’s most famous landmarks go beyond mere tourism and leave any visitor with a sense of belonging and meaning. Rooted in the indigenous Qatari experiences, this luxury hotel near Souq Waqif is the perfect starting point of your visit to Doha. Located 15 minutes from West Bay, the city center of the capital, Al Messila Resort & Spa is also only 10km from the Museum of Islamic Art and the National Museum of Qatar. Step out. The city is yours.

Frequently Asked Questions 

**Is Al Messila Spa a ladies-only spa?**

No.

Al Messila Spa welcomes ladies and gentlemen for a wellness experience in Doha. However, we do have a **dedicated ladies-only** area called Al Messila Ladies Wellness Retreat with ladies-only spa treatments, gyms, fitness studios and therapy pools.

**Do you offer day passes?**

Yes.

Guests may visit Al Messila Spa for the day, with our day pass offers. It will give you access to our indoor pool with natural light, saunas, steam rooms and more. For details on day passes, please call +974 4445 1916 or +974 4445 1917.

**Do you offer massages for gentlemen?**

Yes.

Al Messila Health Center includes a dedicated area with treatment rooms for gentlemen. You may view our gentlemen spa menu on the official Al Messila Spa website.

**Is there a minimum age requirement to access Al Messila Spa?**

For security reasons, the gym and spa facilities at Al Messila Spa can only be accessed by adults, above 18 years old.

**Can pregnant women receive treatments at Al Messila Spa?**

Please be informed that pregnant women are welcome to enjoy facial treatments only at Al Messila Spa.****

###  The Essence of Qatar 

Discover the essence of Qatar's rich heritage and culture at The National Museum of Qatar (NMoQ). Step into a vibrant and immersive space where diverse communities gather to explore Qatar's past, present, and future. NMoQ invites visitors to delve into captivating exhibitions that narrate the nation's journey, offering an enriching experience that resonates with the spirit of Qatar.

###  Museum of Islamic Art 

Explore the Museum of Islamic Art (MIA), home to one of the world's most extensive collections of Islamic art. Marvel at masterpieces from across the globe, showcasing the rich diversity and heritage of Islamic culture. This iconic museum offers a profound journey through centuries of artistic achievement, inviting visitors to immerse themselves in the beauty and significance of Islamic art.

###  Doha's Port Neighborhood 

Embark on a cruise to Doha’s dynamic skyline and discover the bustling Doha Port neighborhood. Anchored by the luxurious Grand Cruise Terminal, this area encompasses the vibrant Mina District and iconic attractions such as Box Park and The Museum of Islamic Art.
