Set above azure waters or nestled along beach coves, the villas at The Ritz-Carlton Maldives, Fari Islands, blend island living with modern design. Located on a private beach cove, The Ritz-Carlton Estate presents three pavilions with up to four bedrooms, a living and dining room, a kitchen, and an expansive sundeck with outdoor dining and an infinity pool overlooking a breathtaking Maldivian sunset. Every detail of the stay is curated by a dedicated Aris Meeha, island butler, and a personal chef.
