# Journeys of a Lifetime 

From exploring hidden gems off the beaten path to immersing yourself in vibrant cultures and breathtaking landscapes, these destinations transform the way we see the world.

The Ritz-Carlton Masai Mara Safari Camp

Bordering both the Masai Mara and the Serengeti, this luxe safari camp specializes in once-in-a-lifetime moments. Seamlessly integrated into the natural wilderness, suites feature private decks, plunge pools and butler service, but the real draw here is what lies just beyond. Awaken with a profound sense of awe as you witness the mesmerizing sights and sounds of the Great Migration. 

[Experience Masai Mara](https://www.ritzcarlton.com/en/hotels/nbomr-the-ritz-carlton-masai-mara-safari-camp/overview/)

![Suite Exterior - View from terrace](https://cache.marriott.com/is/image/marriotts7prod/jw-chqmj-suite-exterior-16586)

JW Marriott Crete Resort & Spa

Crete’s storied history comes to life at this resort on the sun-drenched Marathi coast, where guests can participate in olive-harvesting, cheese-making and beekeeping tours, gaining insight into traditional Cretan practices. The open-fire cuisine at Anóee provides a taste of local flavors and cooking techniques, and nearby Old Town Chania is a mythical maze of alleyways waiting to be explored.

[Escape to Crete](https://www.marriott.com/en-us/hotels/chqmj-jw-marriott-crete-resort-and-spa/overview/)

Al Maha, a Luxury Collection Desert Resort & Spa, Dubai

Nestled among the lush palm groves, emerald canopies and iconic sand dunes of the Dubai Desert Conservation Reserve, this Conde Nast-endorsed hotel offers an unparalleled combination of secluded tranquility and outdoor adventure. Embark on a thrilling 4x4 drive through the dunes or experience the magic of the desert on a camel trek before unwinding at your villa, complete with private pool. 

[Discover the Desert](https://www.marriott.com/luxury/explore-luxury-suites/al-maha-dubai)

![Couple enjoying hot tub from Ilma's Owners Suite.](https://cache.marriott.com/is/image/marriotts7prod/rz-irisandlightownerssuite-12691)

The Ritz-Carlton Yacht Collection

Embark on a journey unlike the rest with The Ritz-Carlton Yacht Collection, where the world’s wonders unfold in a vivid tapestry of natural splendor. From the untamed beauty of Alaska to the chic coastal havens of the Mediterranean, explore extraordinary destinations aboard elegantly designed yachts and enjoy excursions designed to foster a deeper connection to the planet’s diverse cultures. 

[Find Your Voyage](https://www.ritzcarltonyachtcollection.com/)

W Verbier

Bask in the Alps’ iconic beauty from your slopeside perch at W Verbier, where a dedicated Mountain Concierge gives you exclusive access to everything from your first snowboard lesson to a thrilling heliskiing excursion. After a day of adventure, cozy up with a cocktail in the Living Room — this is the birthplace of après-ski, after all. 

[Explore W Verbier](https://www.marriott.com/en-us/hotels/gvawh-w-verbier/overview/)

JW Marriott Masai Mara

Unrivaled access to the wonders of the wild awaits at this opulent safari camp overlooking the Talek River and the Masai Mara. Tented suites offer private decks, perfect for taking in the views, and exclusive safari experiences grant you access to one of the world’s most stunning destinations. Round it all out with a trip to the Spa by JW, where treatments are designed to restore and revitalize. 

[Begin Your Adventure](https://www.marriott.com/luxury/explore-luxury-suites/jw-marriott-masai-mara-lodge)

Rissai Valley, a Ritz-Carlton Reserve

Discover rare experiences at Rissai Valley, where nature provides a backdrop for both quiet reflection and outdoor adventure, and ancient Tibetan villages beckon those seeking something deeper. Step into the spa, where the six wonders of Jiuzhaigou — from its snow-peaked mountains to its emerald-green lakes — are transformed into well-being rituals crafted to restore peace and renew energy.

[Experience Rissai Valley](https://www.ritzcarlton.com/en/hotels/jzhrz-rissai-valley-a-ritz-carlton-reserve/overview/)

![hotel exterior with mountain backdrop](https://cache.marriott.com/is/image/marriotts7prod/lc-cuztl-resort-view-41532-92665)

Tambo del Inka, a Luxury Collection Resort & Spa

Embark on a mystical journey in the heart of Peru’s Sacred Valley from your home base along the Urubamba River. This luxe retreat offers a welcome respite for adventurers making their way to Machu Picchu. Enjoy elegantly designed suites with dramatic views of the Andes Mountains, refined dining options featuring organic ingredients and a spa that harnesses the healing power of water. 

[Discover Tambo del Inka](https://www.marriott.com/luxury/explore-luxury-suites/tambo-del-inka-valle-sagrado)

![Tilina - Sabrage](https://cache.marriott.com/is/image/marriotts7prod/xr-ejhxr-tilina---sabrage-36662)

The St. Regis Red Sea Resort

Celebrating an extraordinary natural habitat, The St. Regis Red Sea Resort brings visionary design to a treasured archipelago. Home to the fourth largest barrier reef, divers both novice and seasoned will find a world teeming with sea life. Back on dry land, a host of must-sees await, from Al Ula, the ancient Arabic oasis in Medina, to the culturally significant fishing village of Umluj. 

[Go Beyond the Guidebook](https://www.marriott.com/en-us/hotels/ejhxr-the-st-regis-red-sea-resort/overview/)

![Suspension Bridge](https://cache.marriott.com/is/image/marriotts7prod/rz-lirrz-suspension-bridge-w36854-34862)

Nekajui, a Ritz-Carlton Reserve

Nestled deep within the Peninsula Papagayo, Nekajui offers well-appointed accommodations paired with ethereal views of the Pacific Ocean and experiences designed to connect guests with Costa Rica's natural rhythms. Artisanal coffee rituals and modern Peruvian cuisine bring local ingredients to life, while wellness offerings are rooted in ancient rites and designed to provide renewed energy.

[Explore Nekajui](https://www.ritzcarlton.com/en/hotels/lirrz-nekajui-a-ritz-carlton-reserve/overview)

The St. Regis Venice

Enchantment awaits at The St. Regis Venice, where historic grandeur flawlessly merges with modern sophistication. Artfully restored, its interiors echo contemporary elegance while honoring Venetian heritage. Guests enjoy bespoke service, curated art by the likes of Gregor Hildebrandt, Jaume Plensa and Ai Weiwei, and, of course, that inimitable dolce vita. 

[Escape to Venice](https://www.marriott.com/en-us/hotels/vcexr-the-st-regis-venice/overview/)
