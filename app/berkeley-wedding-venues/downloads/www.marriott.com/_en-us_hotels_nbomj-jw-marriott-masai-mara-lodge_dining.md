# Dining at Jw Marriott Masai Mara Lodge

## Dining

Dining at JW Marriott Masai Mara Lodge is as extraordinary as the landscape itself. Enjoy authentic, locally-inspired dishes crafted with fresh ingredients from our organic JW Garden, all while soaking in stunning views of the savannah. From intimate tented dinners to alfresco meals overlooking the Masai Mara Reserve, each dish celebrates the vibrant flavors of the region. Whether it’s a casual breakfast, a sunset cocktail beneath an age-old fig tree, or a curated gourmet experience, every meal in our Masai Mara Reserve bars and restaurants connects you to the beauty of the African wilderness.

### Boma Dinners

Experience an unforgettable Boma Dinner at JW Marriott Masai Mara Lodge, where the warmth of a crackling fire meets the rich flavors of African cuisine. Indulge in expertly grilled meats (Nyama Choma), traditional dishes such as Ugali, and vibrant Masai dance performances under the starlit Mara sky—a true taste of culture and luxury.
