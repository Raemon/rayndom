###  Frequently Asked Questions

What restaurants does W Osaka have on property? 

The on-property restaurants at W Osaka are:

Oh.lala... LIVING ROOM​ Teppanyaki MYDO WET BAR & DECK Sushi UKIYO​ MIXup

Does W Osaka offer room service? 

Yes, room service is available at W Osaka.

What cuisine is offered at W Osaka? 

The cuisines offered at W Osaka are:

French at Oh.lala... Multiple cuisines at LIVING ROOM​ Pub at WET BAR & DECK Coffee House at MIXup Japanese at Teppanyaki MYDO, Sushi UKIYO​

How do I book a table at one of the restaurants at W Osaka? 

To book a table at Oh.lala... [click here](https://www.tablecheck.com/en/w-osaka-oh-lala/reserve/experiences) To book a table at LIVING ROOM​ [click here](https://www.tablecheck.com/en/shops/w-osaka-living-room/reserve) To book a table at Teppanyaki MYDO [click here](https://www.tablecheck.com/en/w-osaka-mydo/reserve/experiences) To book a table at WET BAR & DECK [click here](https://www.tablecheck.com/en/w-osaka-wet-bar-deck/reserve/experiences) To book a table at Sushi UKIYO​ [click here](https://www.tablecheck.com/en/w-osaka-sushi-ukiyo/reserve/experiences) To book a table at MIXup [click here](https://www.tablecheck.com/en/w-osaka-mixup/reserve/experiences) You can also call +81 6-6484-5355 to place a reservation.

Does W Osaka offer complimentary breakfast? 

Complimentary breakfast is not currently served at W Osaka. View on-site and nearby dining options on our [dining page](https://www.marriott.com/en-us/hotels/osaow-w-osaka/dining/#on-site-dining).
