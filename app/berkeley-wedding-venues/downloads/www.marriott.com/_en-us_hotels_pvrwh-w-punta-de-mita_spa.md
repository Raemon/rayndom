# Away Spa

## Spa

Forget the ordinary. AWAY Spa at W Punta de Mita pushes the boundaries of relaxation, offering a sanctuary unlike any other, committed to respecting its natural surroundings. Imagine stepping into a cave-like space, a serene oasis cradled by the roots of a majestic fig tree adorned with the symbolic Huichol “Eye of God”, and glistening pools fed by a natural spring. This unique setting becomes the stage for personalized pampering, whether you seek a quick polish or a transformative spa experience.

## Hikuri Journey 

Embark on a transformative journey guided by the sacred Hikuri, an ancestral tradition cherished by the Wixárika Culture for countless generations. As you walk this ancient path, allow yourself to be enveloped by the healing energy of the Hikuri, opening your heart to the whispers of the spirits and the wisdom of the land. "Immerse yourself in a scrub, body wrap, and massage healing session, a sacred union of nature and soul, may you find solace, renewal and profound connection with the eternal essence of existence.
