# Your Perfect Getaway Awaits at Our Summer House

## Accommodations

Our 301 renovated rooms and suites are the perfect place to find comfort and style, taking you to the Summer House feeling and giving you endless summer vibes that have made this property so special to our guests. Inspired by Grand Cayman’s natural environment of clear, blue waters and white, sandy beaches, the new color palette of blues, sand, and wood tones brings a sense of ease and calm. The incorporation of beautiful materials, such as white marble and pale oak, adds a touch of sophistication, contributing to an overall harmonious space.

A New Experience

### Visit Our Newly Renovated Rooms

Reflecting the tranquil beauty of Grand Cayman’s clear waters and white sand beaches, our new palette of blues, sands, and warm wood tones creates an atmosphere of inviting calm. The incorporation of beautiful materials, such as white marble and pale oak, adds a touch of sophistication, contributing to an overall harmonious space.
