# Explore Our Urban Premium Suites

## Suites

Take your Osaka experience to new heights—27 floors up—where luxury meets the city’s electrifying pulse. Designed for the modern explorer, each suite blends vibrant energy with sophisticated comfort. Immerse yourself in stunning city views through floor-to-ceiling windows, unwind in deep soaking tubs and luxury amenities, and indulge in curated minibars. With state-of-the-art tech and W's signature beds, your stay is a bold, unforgettable urban escape.

EXTREME WOW LIVING SPACE 

### Where Luxury Meets Urban Energy

Spanning five rooms, the Extreme WOW Suite features a stylish dining area for eight, an open kitchen and bar, and a spacious living room with a DJ booth and stunning city views. Enjoy a Zen-inspired room, a bedroom with textile artist Sigrid Calon’s unique artwork, and a lavish bathroom with a champagne bowl bathtub. At W Osaka, the Extreme WOW Suite offers a curated blend of luxury, design, and pure urban energy.

Penthouse Suite

###  Extreme Wow Suite

Step into a world of unrivaled luxury in this 200 sqm Penthouse Suite—where style meets sky. Perched atop the hotel, this extraordinary sanctuary boasts soaring 4.5-meter ceilings and sweeping panoramic views of Osaka that will take your breath away. With five seamlessly connected spaces, including a sleek dining area, fully stocked bar, and open kitchen, it's the ultimate setting for intimate soirées and exclusive events. Unwind in the lavish living room, complete with a state-of-the-art DJ booth and mood lighting, where the energy of Osaka flows effortlessly through every unforgettable moment.

### The Wow Experience

Unveil the extraordinary in our Extreme WOW suite, where floor-to-ceiling windows frame panoramic views of Osaka’s skyline—transforming from cozy sunsets to a city alive with exciting nightlife. It’s more than a view; it’s an experience that evolves with the rhythm of the city, offering you an ever-changing backdrop that elevates your stay.

Unwind in W Osaka’s WOW Suite, a 120-square-meter urban retreat with panoramic city views, a sleek dining area for six, and a luxurious bathtub with a rain shower. Indulge in the curated MixBar, relax in the king bed, and soak in the dynamic energy of Osaka through floor-to-ceiling windows.

Suites

###  Wow Suite

Enter a world of bold luxury in the WOW Suite. Located on the top floors, this 120-square-meter haven perfectly blends sophistication with the vibrant energy of Osaka. Enjoy a stylish six-seater dining area, open kitchen, and a spacious living room—designed for both relaxation and entertaining. With 2.7-meter ceilings offering sweeping vistas of Osaka's skyline and W’s signature playful design, every detail is thoughtfully crafted to inspire. The WOW Suite is more than just a room—it’s a statement of redefined luxury and unforgettable style.

### Suite Life

Immerse yourself in the heart of Osaka’s vibrant urban lifestyle. From stunning city views to luxe amenities, our WOW suites are crafted for those who crave both comfort and excitement. Drawing inspiration from Osaka’s vibrant energy and rich Japanese culture, each space is infused with playful, unexpected touches. At W Osaka, the suite life isn’t just lived—it’s elevated.

### Iconic Moments

Located at the corners of W Osaka, this 80-square-meter suite offers a stunning 90-degree view of the city. Enjoy an expansive living room, a spacious bathroom with a deep soaking tub, and floor-to-ceiling windows that capture breathtaking panoramic views of Osaka’s skyline.

Suites

###  Marvelous Suite

Immerse yourself in the Marvelous Suite, a refined 80sqm urban retreat designed for the modern traveler. With expansive floor-to-ceiling windows, you’ll be greeted by stunning panoramic views that bring the vibrant pulse of Osaka directly into your space. Relax in the sophisticated living room, indulge in a soak in the sleek bathtub, or rejuvenate under the soothing rain shower. The king bed offers the perfect setting for a restful night, blending luxury with contemporary style. For an added touch of indulgence, craft your signature cocktail at the MixBar. This is not just a suite—it’s your exclusive escape in the heart of the city.

### Authentic Luxury Awaits

Our Marvelous Suites offer a unique escape, redefining urban luxury. For those with a curious spirit and a taste for the extraordinary, you've arrived at the perfect destination. Experience W Osaka's authentic fusion of style and sophistication, where you can uncover the city’s rich culture, dynamic energy, and vibrant nightlife—right at your doorstep.
