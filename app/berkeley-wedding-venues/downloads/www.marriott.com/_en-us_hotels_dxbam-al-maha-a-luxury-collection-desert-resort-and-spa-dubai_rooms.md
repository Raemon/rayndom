# Reserve Your Stay in Our Luxurious Hotel Rooms in the Desert

## Villas

Retreat to a space of refined elegance and timeless charm. Our Dubai luxury accommodations reflect the authentic Arabian experience, featuring traditional furnishings, handcrafted décor, and private pools. From the Bedouin Villa to the Presidential Villa, each offers a distinctive perspective of the surrounding desert landscape — a tranquil sanctuary where comfort meets heritage. 

Tourism Fee & Group Bookings

AED 20 tourism fee per bedroom applies. Minimum age is 8. Group terms apply for 5+ villas. As an all-villa resort, Marriott Bonvoy upgrades are exempted.

### Presidential Villa

The presidential Villa at Al Maha is the epitome of luxury and sophistication, offering guests the ultimate desert escape. Offers three-bedrooms that feature custom-made super king-size beds, handcrafted furnishings, and opulent bathrooms, all designed to provide guests with the utmost comfort and relaxation. The inviting majlis creates a calming ambience, enhanced by the beautiful Arabian antiques and artifacts on display. The sound of gently running water adds to the sense of tranquility, creating a space where guests can truly unwind and escape the hustle and bustle of everyday life.
