# Hotels in Riyadh, Saudi Arabia

Riyadh is a city that seamlessly blends the modern with the traditional, offering a unique glimpse into the future while honoring its heritage. The city's skyline is punctuated by striking architectural marvels like the Kingdom Centre and the Al Faisaliah Tower, which stand alongside historic sites such as the Masmak Fortress and the National Museum. For those looking to explore the vibrant culture, the city’s bustling souks and the Riyadh Season festival provide endless opportunities to immerse yourself in local traditions and festivities. Whether you're here for business or leisure, Marriott Bonvoy’s hotels in Riyadh offer the perfect blend of comfort and luxury, ensuring your stay is as memorable as the city itself.

  


The heart of Riyadh is a hub of activity, with a wide array of dining options, from Michelin-starred restaurants to street food stalls, catering to every palate. Shopping enthusiasts will delight in the upscale malls like the Riyadh Mall and the Panorama Mall, while nature lovers can find tranquility in the lush parks and gardens, such as the Riyadh Zoo and the King Abdullah Park. Marriott Bonvoy’s strategically located hotels provide easy access to all these attractions, as well as to key business districts, making your stay both convenient and enjoyable. With amenities like complimentary Wi-Fi, state-of-the-art fitness centers, and top-notch conference facilities, every aspect of your visit is catered to with the highest level of service.
