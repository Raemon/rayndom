# Relax in Our Stylish Hotel Rooms in Midtown Manhattan

## Accommodations

Meticulously designed, the hotel rooms and suites at The St. Regis New York emphasize timeless yet modern luxury, perfectly encapsulating our status on Fifth Avenue. Each of our stylish hotel rooms in Midtown Manhattan is stunningly appointed and flawlessly furnished. Unwind in spacious two-bedroom suites or indulge in our lavish Presidential Suite. Original elements such as crystal Waterford chandeliers and elegant crown moldings are accented with large and beautiful photographs, vibrant-hued fabrics and splendid beveled mirrors. Count on our St. Regis Butler Service to meet your needs any time of day or night in Midtown Manhattan.

## Caroline Astor Suite

Inspired by Gilded Age doyenne and matriarch of St. Regis, the luxurious Caroline Astor Suite offers a serene and sophisticated escape overlooking views of Central Park. The 1,700 sq. foot suite features a cool color palette of bright whites, beige, charcoal grey, silver & pale blue tones. Both whimsical and luxurious, the suite reflects a modern aesthetic and contemporary vision within the deeply elegant walls of The St. Regis New York. A marble foyer separates the master bedroom from the living room in this residential suite, full of luxurious touches including a 55 inch curved TV and custom-designed furnishings. The living room with fireplace connects to a glamorous dining room with seating for 10 and a custom pantry. The suite offers one Italian marble parlor bathroom, adjacent to the living room, in addition to one (or two) Italian marble full bathroom(s), which include double sinks, a deep-soaking jetted tub, separate rain shower and toilet cabin, a bidet, and heated tile floors. 

![Living Room](https://cache.marriott.com/content/dam/marriott-renditions/NYCXR/nycxr-tiffany-suite-7001-hor-clsc.jpg?output-quality=70&interpolation=progressive-bilinear&downsize=1300px:*)

## Bespoke Two Bedroom Suites

Alongside our stylish Midtown Manhattan accommodation, we offer two-bedroom St. Regis, Grand, and Milano suites for an elevated and spacious suite experience. These suites are perfect for families, or those who simply want more room to spread out. Tailor your stay by selecting connecting room options that include a room with two doubles or one king bed. Our two-bedroom suite accommodation offers 1,500-1,700 square feet of space with an elegant marble hallway, a spacious living room, two separate bedrooms with private Italian marble bathrooms, timeless décor, and the hotel’s signature St. Regis butler service.
