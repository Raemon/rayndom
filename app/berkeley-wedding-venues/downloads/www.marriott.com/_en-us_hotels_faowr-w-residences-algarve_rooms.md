# W Residences

## Accommodations

Our W Residences are ready to plug and play, offering all the amenities you need for a comfortable and memorable stay in the Algarve. Designed for both relaxation and convenience, each residence is fully equipped with premium Smeg kitchen appliances, a washing and dryer machine, bathtub, and walk-in showers. Whether you’re staying for a short escape or an extended holiday, our modern spaces provide the perfect home away from home. As one of the most exclusive Albufeira apartment hotels, W Residences Algarve combines the privacy of a residence with the services of a luxury hotel, ensuring a flawless experience for families and friends. Enjoy spacious living areas, stylish design, and thoughtful details that elevate every moment of your stay. From cooking in your fully fitted kitchen to unwinding in a cozy lounge or sharing memories on your private balcony, everything has been designed to create an unforgettable stay in Albufeira. 

Live Life to the Max

### Escape Residence

Measuring 310 sq m, the palatial four-bedroom penthouse showcases a sublime open-plan living and dining area, boasting beautiful views out to the terrace and sparkling coastline beyond. Expect statement lights, bespoke furniture and high-end, high-tech fixtures.

### Three Bedroom Apartments

These elegant apartments offer 178-188 sq m of space, including a high-end kitchen and living area, a master bedroom with ocean-influenced decor, two additional bedrooms and a powder room. The duplexes come with a private pool overlooking the Atlantic Ocean. Expect open-plan living at its best: a stylish and social layout, leading to a large terrace with stunning sea views.
