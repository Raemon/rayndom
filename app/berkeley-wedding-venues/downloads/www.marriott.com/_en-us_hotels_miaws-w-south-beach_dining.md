# Dining at W South Beach

## Dining

W South Beach offers a curated mix of dining experiences to match any mood. Enjoy bold Chinese flavors at Mr Chow or unwind in the open-air elegance of The Grove, where local ingredients shine. Sip craft cocktails in the Living Room Bar or relax poolside with bites from WET Bar & Grille. Prefer to stay in? In-room dining brings the best of W South Beach straight to you. 

AMPLIFY YOUR ESCAPE

Elevate your stay at W South Beach with our unique experiences. Delight in complimentary happenings, luxurious in-room amenities, indulgent spa treatments, vibrant cabanas and more.

###  Frequently Asked Questions

What restaurants does W South Beach have on property? 

The on-property restaurants at W South Beach are:

Mr Chow WET® Bar and Grille Living Room Bar In-Room Dining The Grove

Does W South Beach offer room service? 

Yes, room service is available at W South Beach.

What cuisine is offered at W South Beach? 

The cuisines offered at W South Beach are:

American at WET® Bar and Grille, Living Room Bar, The Grove Asian at Mr Chow Other at In-Room Dining

How do I book a table at one of the restaurants at W South Beach? 

To book a table at Mr Chow [click here](http://www.opentable.com/restaurant/profile/35377/reserve?restref=35377) To book a table at WET® Bar and Grille [click here](http://www.opentable.com/restaurant/profile/1362697/reserve?restref=1362697) To book a table at The Grove [click here](https://www.opentable.com/booking/restref/availability?lang=en-US&correlationId=883ce19d-57ac-4854-9b85-428673293542&restRef=1043080&otSource=Restaurant%20website) You can also call +1 305-938-3000 to place a reservation.

Does W South Beach offer complimentary breakfast? 

Complimentary breakfast is not currently served at W South Beach. View on-site and nearby dining options on our [dining page](https://www.marriott.com/en-us/hotels/miaws-w-south-beach/dining/#on-site-dining).
