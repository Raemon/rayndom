##  The Fresco Technique 

Through a revolutionary technique in the history of art – the art of the fresco, based on water-based pigments on fresh plaster – Florentine artists became the world’s foremost exponents and experts. Standing the test of time over hundreds of years, the frescoes in Florence are considered one of the city’s greatest artistic signatures, and are best experienced up close.
