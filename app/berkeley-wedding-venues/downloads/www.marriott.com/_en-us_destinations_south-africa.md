# Hotels in South Africa

South Africa is a land of incredible beauty and diversity, offering everything from thrilling adventures to peaceful retreats. Whether you’re exploring the vibrant city streets of Cape Town or Johannesburg, the serene landscapes of the Winelands, or the wide-open spaces of the Karoo, the country has something for everyone. Our hotels in South Africa are the perfect starting point for your journey, offering a blend of comfort and luxury that will make you feel at home, wherever you are. Whether you’re here for business or pleasure, our hotels are strategically located near top attractions and offer easy access to the best the country has to offer.

  


Discover the lively spirit of South Africa’s townships, the unspoiled beaches of the Garden Route, and the untamed wilderness of Kruger National Park—all while enjoying the thoughtful amenities and impeccable service of Marriott Bonvoy hotels. Many of our properties boast on-site dining, fitness centers, and spa services, perfect for unwinding after a day of adventure. From seaside resorts to city oases, Marriott Bonvoy hotels in South Africa are designed to meet every traveler’s needs, ensuring your stay is both memorable and hassle-free.
