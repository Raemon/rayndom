# Stay in our hotel suites in Chicago

## Accommodations

Imbued with a sense of modern glamour and classic sophistication, transcend into The St. Regis Chicago’s 159 impeccable guest rooms and 33 luxurious suites. Take in the sights of the Windy City through floor-to-ceiling windows: immersive views from spacious suites and high-floor hotel rooms in Chicago reveal the most revered landmarks in the downtown area. The St. Regis Chicago boasts some of the largest hotel accommodations in the city, and our luxury Chicago suites are adorned with exquisite furnishings and bespoke amenities, ensuring a comfortable and indulgent stay. Sink into plush bedding and unwind in your private marble bathroom with rainfall showers and luxurious bath amenities, savoring moments of relaxation in your own personal oasis. 

Our Signature Commitment to Excellence

From Packing Service to Garment Pressing, the signature St. Regis Butler Service ensures each guest’s unique preferences are understood and realized, with meticulous attention to detail. The St. Regis Butler Service is accessible at any hour via guest phone, email, or text message.

## Explore Downtown Chicago

Treat yourself to a rewarding stay at The St. Regis Chicago located where the Chicago River meets Lake Michigan in downtown Chicago. Explore the city’s most revered downtown landmarks including the Magnificent Mile, Navy Pier, Grant Park, and the Chicago Riverwalk are all accessible from our hotel.

### The Two-Bedroom Presidential Suite

Indulge in the epitome of luxury and sophistication with our Presidential Suite at The St. Regis Chicago. Offering an unparalleled level of elegance and opulence, meticulously designed to exceed the expectations of the most discerning guests. Step into a 4,600 sq. foot jewel box enveloped in stunning views of downtown Chicago and Lake Michigan, serving as your backdrop. The spacious living area is adorned with lavish furnishings, exquisite artwork, and bespoke décor, creating an ambiance of timeless elegance and refinement. Partake in ultimate relaxation in the marble-clad principal bathroom overlooking Navy Pier, featuring an oversized, effervescent soaking tub, a separate rainfall shower, and premium bath amenities. Rejuvenate in your own clandestine personal fitness studio or unwind in the expansive living area, complete with a dining table for entertaining guests or enjoying intimate meals prepared in your private kitchen and bookended by your own exclusive St. Regis Butler.
