# Premium Rooms and Residences in Dubai Marina

## Residences

Discover the unparalleled luxury of Grosvenor House Residences, Dubai. Our exquisite rooms, suites or 1, 2, and 3-bedroom residences offer stunning Dubai Marina and Arabian Gulf views. Enjoy spacious living areas, and opulent bathrooms featuring premium amenities. Benefit from complimentary high-speed Wi-Fi, 42-inch flat-screen TVs, and dedicated butler service. Experience sophisticated design and impeccable five-star service, blending hotel luxury with the comforts of home for an unforgettable stay.

### The Residence

The Residence includes Premier Rooms and Suites, alongside the 216 Residences available for long-term and short-term residence. Each guest room, suite and residences many with private balconies, enjoys breathtaking panoramic views over The Arabian Gulf, Palm Jumeirah and Dubai Marina, adding yet another dimension to the Grosvenor House Dubai experience. Simply put, the rooms inspire the tone of the hotel, offering the highest levels of restrained luxury that appeal to business travelers and pleasure seekers alike.

![King Premier Junior Suite](data:image/png;base64,iVBORw0KGgoAAAANSUhEUgAAAA4AAAAKCAYAAACE2W/HAAAAFklEQVR42mN8/fpLPQMZgHFU48jWCAD/vyDle0zFdgAAAABJRU5ErkJggg==)

## Premier Suites

A discerning choice for professional or private guests, Premier Suites and Executive Premier Suites promise an elegant and effortless stay. Nestled between the fifth and tenth floors of The Residences, they overlook the sparkling Arabian Sea, Palm Jumeirah, or Dubai Marina through floor-to-ceiling windows. Select suites also offer a private balcony furnished with a table and chairs.

Premier Rooms

## Premier Rooms

Experience elevated comfort in our Premier Rooms, located on floors five through ten of The Residence. These rooms offer stunning views of the Arabian Sea, Palm Jumeirah, or Dubai Marina, with select rooms featuring a private balcony.

Inside, enjoy complimentary high-speed internet, a 47-inch flat screen while you control your environment with a touchscreen panel for lighting and air conditioning. Rest on our signature Luxury Collection beds with premium linens.

The elegant bathroom features a deep bathtub, standalone shower, and premium bath amenities. Indulge in plush bathrobes and slippers, with a personal butler at your service.

Guests booking directly with the hotel also receive complimentary Dubai International Airport transfers and exclusive access to our Executive Business Lounge, which includes breakfast, afternoon tea, and pre-dinner drinks.

## Premier Suites

Indulge in an elegant and effortless stay in our Premier and Executive Premier Suites, located on floors five to ten of The Residence. Enjoy stunning views of the Arabian Sea, Palm Jumeirah, or Dubai Marina through floor-to-ceiling windows, with select suites offering a private balcony.

Relax in the spacious lounge area featuring a 47-inch interactive TV with complimentary high-speed internet access. Executive Premier Suites also include a pantry with a refrigerator and a six-guest dining table.

The large bedroom boasts a plush king-size signature Luxury Collection bed with 400-thread-count sheets and a second television. The elegantly appointed bathroom features, deep bathtub, standalone shower, and premium bath amenities.

Guests also receive transfers to and from Dubai International Airport and exclusive access to our Executive Business Lounge, offering complimentary breakfast, pre-dinner drinks, and more.

Elevated Luxury and Unmatched Comfort

## Grosvenor and Royal Suites

Experience unparalleled luxury in our Grosvenor or Royal Suites, located on the ninth and tenth floors of The Residence. These expansive suites offer panoramic views of the Dubai Marina or Palm Jumeirah through floor-to-ceiling windows.

Each Suite features a spacious lounge area, a fully equipped kitchenette, and a dining area, perfect for entertaining.

Enjoy state-of-the-art amenities, including complimentary high-speed internet, a convenient touchscreen control panel for an effortless stay.

The master bedroom offers a plush king-size Luxury Collection bed and a private sitting area for ultimate relaxation. The en-suite master bathroom is a sanctuary with a Jacuzzi, rainforest shower, dry sauna, and premium bath amenities.

Furthermore, a 24-hour personal butler is dedicated to fulfilling your every request.

The 200-square-meter Majlis-style Chamber can be connected to the Grosvenor Suite, creating a larger three-bedroom apartment, or utilized as a spacious one-bedroom suite.

## Elevated Living, Unforgettable Views

Perched on the eleventh floor and above, The Residences offer a new standard of upscale living with one, two, and three-bedroom residences. Each home features expansive windows that frame breathtaking views of the Dubai Marina, Arabian Sea, or Palm Jumeirah.

Residences for Long Stay

###  Redefine Daily Living: Premium Long-Stay Apartments in Dubai Marina

Our Residences, featuring high ceilings and spacious living areas, are exquisitely designed in elegant neutral hues. The fully equipped kitchen, with black marble countertops, includes a refrigerator, dishwasher, crockery, and cutlery. Choose between a closed kitchen with extra cabinets or an open one with a breakfast bar. A six-guest dining table provides an intimate space for meals. The master bedroom boasts a king-size Luxury Collection bed with 400-thread-count sheets, a down comforter, and plump pillows, framed by an upholstered headboard and bedside tables. A chaise lounge, armchair, interactive flat-screen TV, and modern work desk complete the room. Additional bedrooms offer king-size or twin beds. Each bedroom features a walk-in wardrobe leading to a spacious en-suite bathroom with a brown marble vanity, deep bathtub, separate shower, exclusive amenities, plush bathrobes, and slippers. Some residences include a guest washroom and laundry/storage room.
