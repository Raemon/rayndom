###  Frequently Asked Questions

What restaurants does Oakland Marriott City Center have on property? 

The on-property restaurants at Oakland Marriott City Center are:

Iron & Oak Iron and Oak Topgolf Swing Suite M Club Mobile Dining

Does Oakland Marriott City Center offer room service? 

Yes, room service is available at Oakland Marriott City Center.

What cuisine is offered at Oakland Marriott City Center? 

The cuisines offered at Oakland Marriott City Center are:

American at Iron & Oak, M Club, Mobile Dining Pub at Iron and Oak Topgolf Swing Suite

How do I book a table at one of the restaurants at Oakland Marriott City Center? 

To book a table at Iron & Oak [click here](http://www.opentable.com/restaurant/profile/1010005/reserve?restref=1010005) To book a table at Iron and Oak Topgolf Swing Suite [click here](https://oaklandmarriottcitycenter.247activities.com/?servicename=Topgolf+Swing+Suite) You can also call +1 510-451-4000 to place a reservation.

Does Oakland Marriott City Center offer complimentary breakfast? 

Complimentary breakfast is not currently served at Oakland Marriott City Center. View on-site and nearby dining options on our [dining page](https://www.marriott.com/en-us/hotels/oakdt-oakland-marriott-city-center/dining/#on-site-dining).
