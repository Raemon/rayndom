# Explore Our Suites and Penthouse

## Suites

Enjoy laid-back luxury in The Reykjavik EDITION Suites, where comfort meets elegance. Our spacious suites feature separate living and sleeping areas, creating a serene retreat in the heart of the city. Thoughtfully designed with signature touches, including oversized soaking tubs and custom Le Labo toiletries, every detail enhances your stay. Flooded with natural light, floor-to-ceiling windows offer breathtaking panoramic views of the harbor and ocean strip, immersing you in Iceland’s beauty.

### Penthouse

A sanctuary of luxury and comfort, our one-of-a-kind 128 sqm Penthouse Suite offers an unparalleled stay in the heart of Iceland’s capital. Designed to feel like a true residence, this expansive suite blends modern elegance with warm Nordic touches, creating a refined yet inviting atmosphere.
