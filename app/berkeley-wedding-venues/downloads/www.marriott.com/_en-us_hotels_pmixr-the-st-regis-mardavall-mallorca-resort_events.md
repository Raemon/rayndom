## Ponent

Ponent is the largest salon at the resort, offering 224 m² of versatile space. It can be divided into two areas using integrated paneling and features a terrace with views of the garden and the sea, creating a sophisticated and exclusive setting for events. The design combines carpets with patterns reminiscent of the island's traditional clay floors and linen curtains, adding warmth and brightness. The color palette of olive green, wood, and clay pays homage to the Mallorcan landscape.

## Boardroom

Discover our exclusive Boardroom, an intimate setting designed for up to 14 guests. Bathed in natural light and complemented by a spacious terrace, it offers a serene and sophisticated environment. Ideal for executive gatherings, strategic sessions, or creative brainstorming, every detail has been meticulously crafted to enhance productivity and inspire excellence.

## Mar Sea Club

Celebrate your private event at Mar Sea Club, an exclusive venue nestled between the resort's lush gardens and the Mediterranean Sea, accommodating up to 85 guests. This sophisticated setting offers breathtaking sea views, ideal for executive luncheons, gala dinners, or special celebrations. With a decor inspired by the island's natural beauty and a culinary proposal blending Mediterranean flavors with Latin American influences, Mar Sea Club guarantees an unforgettable experience. Additionally, its privileged location allows access by boat, adding an exclusive touch to your event.

## Es Fum

Host your private event at Es Fum, our Michelin-starred restaurant nestled within the St. Regis Mardavall Mallorca Resort. This elegant venue offers a unique gastronomic experience, where haute cuisine meets panoramic views of the Mediterranean. Under the direction of Chef Miguel Navarro, each dish is a masterpiece that blends tradition and innovation, ensuring your event is infused with exceptional flavor.
