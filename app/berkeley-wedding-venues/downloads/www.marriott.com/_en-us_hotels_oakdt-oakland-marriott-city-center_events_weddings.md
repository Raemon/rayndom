## Events Spaces & Facilities

For an Oakland wedding experience that expertly reflects your personal style, we offer an array of indoor/outdoor spaces enhanced by breathtaking views creating the perfect wedding day. 

Skyline Room  
Following a private ceremony, our 2,700-square-foot ballroom provides sweeping views of San Francisco Bay and the perfect setting for receptions or post-wedding brunches.

Rooftop Event Area  
An intimate open-air space overlooking historic downtown Oakland, the rooftop event area allows guests to enjoy a cocktail reception or sit-down dinner while watching the sunset. Embrace the beautiful California weather with our outdoor event venue.

Iron and Oak  
Naturally lit and stylishly appointed, Iron and Oak dining room is ideal for smaller receptions, bridal showers, rehearsal dinners and wedding brunches.

## Catering Services

Making your day perfect is our specialty. From menus that delight to arrangements that innovate, we'll handle every finishing touch so you can enjoy your special day to the fullest.

Catering  
A memorable rehearsal dinner in our private dining room. An elegant sit-down meal with vegetarian and gluten free options. An intimate champagne brunch. No matter your catering needs, our award-winning chefs will work with you to create an inspired culinary experience that you and your guests will savor, even after the last bite. Once the reception is over, we'll see to it that there's a slice of wedding cake and glass of champagne waiting when you get to your room.

As your dream day nears, our catering team is delighted to offer a tasting event so that the menu not only meets your budget but also satisfies your unique tastes.  


Rehearsal Dinner  
Oakland weddings start off right when surrounded by family and friends in the well-appointed private dining room of our Level Two restaurant. Enjoy an impeccably prepared meal and expertly selected wine list, perfect for toasting your happily ever after.
