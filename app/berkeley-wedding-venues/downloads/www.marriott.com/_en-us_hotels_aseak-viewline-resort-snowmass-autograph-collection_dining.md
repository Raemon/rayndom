###  Frequently Asked Questions

What restaurants does Viewline Resort Snowmass, Autograph Collection have on property? 

The on-property restaurants at Viewline Resort Snowmass, Autograph Collection are:

Stark's Alpine Grill Lobby Bar Gorsuch Ski Cafe In-Room Dining Bunny's Social Club The Clubhouse The Flower Shop Snowmass

Does Viewline Resort Snowmass, Autograph Collection offer room service? 

Yes, room service is available at Viewline Resort Snowmass, Autograph Collection.

What cuisine is offered at Viewline Resort Snowmass, Autograph Collection? 

The cuisines offered at Viewline Resort Snowmass, Autograph Collection are:

American at Stark's Alpine Grill, Lobby Bar, In-Room Dining, Bunny's Social Club, The Flower Shop Snowmass Coffee House at Gorsuch Ski Cafe, The Clubhouse

How do I book a table at one of the restaurants at Viewline Resort Snowmass, Autograph Collection? 

To book a table at Stark's Alpine Grill [click here](http://www.opentable.com/restaurant/profile/1448263/reserve?restref=1448263) To book a table at Gorsuch Ski Cafe [click here](https://www.gorsuchskicafe.com/snowmass-menu) You can also call +1 970-923-8200 to place a reservation.

Does Viewline Resort Snowmass, Autograph Collection offer complimentary breakfast? 

Complimentary breakfast is not currently served at Viewline Resort Snowmass, Autograph Collection. View on-site and nearby dining options on our [dining page](https://www.marriott.com/en-us/hotels/aseak-viewline-resort-snowmass-autograph-collection/dining/#on-site-dining).
