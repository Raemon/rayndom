###  Frequently Asked Questions

What restaurants does Fairfield by Marriott Inn & Suites Key West have on property? 

The on-property restaurants at Fairfield by Marriott Inn & Suites Key West are:

Complimentary Daily Breakfast Poolside Tiki Bar

Does Fairfield by Marriott Inn & Suites Key West offer room service? 

No, room service is not offered at Fairfield by Marriott Inn & Suites Key West. You can view other dining options on our [dining page](https://www.marriott.com/en-us/hotels/eywfi-fairfield-inn-and-suites-key-west/dining/).

What cuisine is offered at Fairfield by Marriott Inn & Suites Key West? 

The cuisines offered at Fairfield by Marriott Inn & Suites Key West are:

American at Complimentary Daily Breakfast, Poolside Tiki Bar

Free Buffet Breakfast is available at Fairfield by Marriott Inn & Suites Key West during the following hours: Monday-Sunday 7:00 - 10:00 AM. Free Continental Breakfast is available at Fairfield by Marriott Inn & Suites Key West during the following hours: Monday-Sunday 7:00 - 10:00 AM.
