# Quiet Luxury

## Accommodations

Our Reykjavik hotel's guestrooms and suites have been designed as cozy retreats, each with beds facing floor-to-ceiling windows that frame various views of the surrounding neighborhood. Some come complete with an outdoor terrace, while all of them are an embodiment of the EDITION brand’s approach to modern luxury with a subtle local flavor. A muted palette of ash wood and pale grey oak serves as a warm foundation for a feature formwork concrete wall, Italian custom-made furniture, copper bed light sconces, faux fur rugs, and artwork and accessories from local craftsmen. Meanwhile, the monochrome bathrooms with custom handmade white ceramic tiles made in Italy are furnished with a white marble vanity alongside custom Le Labo toiletries of EDITION’s exclusive scent. From its prime corner spot on the 6th floor, the one-bedroom Penthouse Suite – with its own private terrace has magnificent harbor and mountain views that are further complemented by bright, light-filled, elegant interiors.

The Penthouse 

### Home Away From Home

With a truly residential feel, our one-of-a-kind Penthouse suite features floor to ceiling windows that offer unparalleled views of the harbor and Mount Esja. Enjoy the spacious living room with a central fireplace, dining area for 10 and an additional guest bathroom. Guests of the 128 sqm have the ability to interconnect with two additional guestrooms to create a spacious 3-bedroom suite.

## Cozy Retreats

The Reykjavik EDITION has 253 guest rooms, which include 26 spacious suites and a Penthouse with stylishly modern decor and posh detailing by Ian Schrager. Warm palettes of ash wood and pale gray oak are embellished with bespoke furniture, copper details, faux fur rugs, and artwork and accessories from local craftsmen. The monochrome bathrooms are sanctuaries of their own. Some rooms have their own outdoor terraces, while most have their beds facing floor-to-ceiling windows that frame various views of the surrounding neighborhood.
