# Unwind in the best Florence luxury hotel rooms and suites

## Accommodations

Exquisitely styled and lavishly appointed, our accommodations define refined luxury. Timeless elegance and personalized hospitality fill our spacious Florence hotel rooms and family suites. The St. Regis Florence features 80 luxuriously appointed guest rooms and 19 suites. Reflecting Florence’s rich artistic heritage, all of our luxury accommodation in Florence features individually hand carved gold leaf plaques and is dressed in one of three different color palettes - Medici, Florentine and Renaissance - with each design concept offering a unique selection of custom designed furniture, paintings, frescoes and crystal chandeliers to create a sophisticated sense of relaxed opulence. In honor of Florence and its traditions, every guest room and suite bears the name of noteworthy Italian artists, benefactors and nobility.

###  In-Room Dining 

Catering to all appetites, authentic Italian cuisine is available in the privacy of your room or suite. The flavors of our in-room dining introduce palates to an enchanting Tuscan menu. In the morning, a traditional continental breakfast offers a bounty of à la carte items. For lunch or dinner, Chef Gentian Sheshi selects top restaurant specialties for the in-room dining menu. 

###  Delights Delivered 

Elevate the in-room experience. Send a meaningful amenity gift during or in advance of an upcoming stay through the thoughtfully executed Delights Delivered Program. From a fresh bouquet of flowers to a personalized cake for a special celebration, deliver a memorable amenity to be enjoyed within the comforts of our rooms and suites.

###  Room With a View 

Get inspired for your next stay at The St. Regis Florence. The art of Renaissance and the art of hospitality combined in a masterpiece of exclusive living in most of our rooms with breathtaking views of the Arno River and the city's legendary art treasures. Our elegant Arno View rooms offer guests a full immersion in the beauty of Florence.
