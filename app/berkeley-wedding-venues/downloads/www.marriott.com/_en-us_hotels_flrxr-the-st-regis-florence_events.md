### Meetings & Exquisite Spaces

The St. Regis Florence’s venues provide a magnificent setting for business events

Our noble Salone delle Feste presents a wealth of decorations beyond any other venue in Florence.

The largest of our Florence event rooms seats up to 250 guests for a reception or presentation.

Virtually explore our exquisite hotel event spaces in Florence with 360-degree 3D tours.

Overlooking the Arno River and the most iconic landmarks of the city, The St. Regis Florence is located in an historic palace designed by Brunelleschi, right in the center of Florence. Centuries of history and tradition have bequeathed Florence with a deeply felt attitude to hospitality, with rituals handed down from generation to generation reinforcing the highest aristocratic principles. 

### Meeting Venues

The hotel hosts seven meeting and banquet rooms, all equipped with technological capabilities and each reflective of the hotel’s signature graceful ambiance. The impressive Salone delle Feste represents the perfect backdrop for high profile board meetings and gala dinners, while the smaller Dante, Petrarca and Boccaccio meeting rooms are ideal for company conferences. Our personnel guarantee the highest quality service for any corporate or social event.
