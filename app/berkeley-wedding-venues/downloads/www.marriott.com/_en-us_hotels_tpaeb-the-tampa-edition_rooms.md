# Relax in luxury at our hotel suites in Tampa

## Accommodations

The Tampa EDITION features 172 guest rooms and 30 spacious suites including one penthouse with stylishly modern décor and posh detailing by Ian Schrager. Enjoy luxury amenities including floor-to-ceiling windows that feature stunning views in our Tampa accommodation.

Luxurious Retreat 

### The Penthouse

Apart from its exquisite private bedroom, the elegantly residential Penthouse Suite features a spacious separate living room and half-bath, a sophisticated dining room with an oak table, and a full kitchenette with its own service entrance. Unparalleled 180 degree views of Tampa Bay, Channelside, and the city skyline can be admired from every corner of this exquisite suite.

## Bay Suite

Appointed with fine touches and timeless design, the Bay Suite evokes the feeling of a private residence at the center of Water Street. The suite features a corner-view private bedroom, spacious living and dining room, powder room, and full bathroom with abundant natural light. Unparalleled 180 degree views of Tampa Bay, Channelside, and the city skyline can be admired from every corner of this exquisite suite.
