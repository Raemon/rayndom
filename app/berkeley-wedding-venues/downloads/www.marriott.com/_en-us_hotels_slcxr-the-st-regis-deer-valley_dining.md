## Dining

The St. Regis Deer Valley offers guests select Park City restaurants and bars serving cuisine as exquisite as the views. Savor fresh seafood or a prime cut steak at RIME at The St. Regis Deer Valley; a French comfort classic at Brasserie 7452; or an elegant cocktail at The St. Regis Bar & Lounge. Regardless of the season, experience Park City outdoor dining at the Terrace Café, located on our Mountain Terrace overlooking spectacular Deer Valley.

###  Frequently Asked Questions

What restaurants does The St. Regis Deer Valley have on property? 

The on-property restaurants at The St. Regis Deer Valley are:

RIME Brasserie 7452 The St. Regis Bar La Stellina The Vintage Room Library Cafe The Terrace Cafe

Does The St. Regis Deer Valley offer room service? 

Yes, room service is available at The St. Regis Deer Valley.

What cuisine is offered at The St. Regis Deer Valley? 

The cuisines offered at The St. Regis Deer Valley are:

Italian at La Stellina French at Brasserie 7452 American at RIME, The St. Regis Bar, The Vintage Room, Library Cafe, The Terrace Cafe

How do I book a table at one of the restaurants at The St. Regis Deer Valley? 

To book a table at RIME [click here](http://www.sevenrooms.com/reservations/rimeseafoodsteakslcxr?venues=rimeseafoodsteakslcxr,7452brasserieslcxr,lastellinaslcxr) To book a table at Brasserie 7452 [click here](http://www.sevenrooms.com/reservations/7452brasserieslcxr?venues=7452brasserieslcxr,rimeseafoodsteakslcxr,lastellinaslcxr) To book a table at La Stellina [click here](http://www.sevenrooms.com/reservations/lastellinaslcxr?venues=lastellinaslcxr,rimeseafoodsteakslcxr,7452brasserieslcxr) To book a table at The Vintage Room [click here](https://fp.sevenrooms.com/events/thevintageroomslcxr) You can also call +1 435-940-5700 to place a reservation.

Does The St. Regis Deer Valley offer complimentary breakfast? 

Complimentary breakfast is not currently served at The St. Regis Deer Valley. View on-site and nearby dining options on our [dining page](https://www.marriott.com/en-us/hotels/slcxr-the-st-regis-deer-valley/dining/#on-site-dining).
