## Dining

At Solaz, every dining experience is a celebration of Baja’s culinary legacy and natural abundance. Our restaurants and bars are inspired by the flavors, traditions, and textures of the peninsula, where the Sea of Cortez meets the fertile lands of Migriño and Miraflores. Savor exceptional cuisine that honors native ingredients and ancestral techniques—each dish crafted to delight the most discerning palates. Among our culinary highlights, Al Pairo—recently recommended by the Michelin Guide—offers an immersive journey through pre-Hispanic flavors and contemporary artistry, embodying the essence of refined Baja gastronomy.
