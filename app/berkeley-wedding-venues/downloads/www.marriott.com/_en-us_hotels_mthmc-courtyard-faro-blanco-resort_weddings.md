# Weddings in the Florida Keys

## Weddings

Let your love story unfold on our white sand beach, framed by the iconic Faro Blanco Lighthouse and the endless blue of the Florida Keys. Located in the heart of Marathon, our resort offers a stunning waterfront backdrop for every kind of celebration, from intimate elopements to grand receptions beneath the stars. Choose from a variety of open-air venues, including our seaside gazebo, lush tropical gardens, and serene beachfront. Begin your day in our elegant get-ready suite, then walk down the aisle surrounded by swaying palms and ocean breeze. Whether you're planning a destination wedding, renewing your vows at sunset, or proposing in paradise, Courtyard Faro Blanco Resort offers everything you need to make your moment unforgettable.

Special Place for Your Special Day

###  The Beach at Faro Blanco 

Say your vows at sunset at this serene beachfront venue, framed by swaying palms. As the sun dips below the horizon, the space transforms into a magical setting with twinkling lights, room for a tented dance floor, and a cozy fire pit. Dance the night away under the stars, surrounded by the enchantment of the sea.

###  Breezeway Cove 

Nestled between the marina and the resort’s tropical gardens, this open-air space blends indoor comfort with outdoor charm. Floor-to-ceiling glass doors open to panoramic views of the water and gardens, making it ideal for cocktail hours, receptions, or dinners. Guests are invited to relax and celebrate in true Keys style.

###  Event Lawn 

Surrounded by flowering foliage and bathed in golden hour light, the event lawn is a versatile canvas for elegant dinners and wedding celebrations. With space for dancing, dining, and lawn games, this blank canvas is ready to be transformed for your perfect day.
