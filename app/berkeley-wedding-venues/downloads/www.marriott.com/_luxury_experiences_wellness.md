# The Art of Wellbeing

Rooted in the healing power of nature, these tranquil spa retreats draw from ancient traditions and modern science to leave you feeling better than ever.

![Spa Day, Spa, Wellness](https://cache.marriott.com/is/image/marriotts7prod/eb-laxeb-spa-relaxation-area-34700)

The West Hollywood EDITION

A haven of serenity on the Sunset Strip, this Forbes 5-star spa offers a blend of time-honored healing modalities and cutting-edge treatments. The first luxury destination in Los Angeles to weave together wellness, mindfulness and beauty from within, services include technology-forward treatments with sound and light, along with recovery therapies to restore the mind and body. 

[Explore The West Hollywood EDITION](https://www.marriott.com/luxury/explore-luxury-suites/the-west-hollywood-edition)

![AWAY Spa - Double Treatment Room](https://cache.marriott.com/is/image/marriotts7prod/wh-klxwh-away-spa-dbl-rm-35751-91451)

W Costa Navarino

From its enviable location on the sweeping Ionian coast, Costa Navarino’s unspoiled countryside beckons adventurers with its formidable trails for hiking, biking and climbing. After a day of adrenaline, escape to the bright and airy Away Spa, which offers a multitude of treatments designed to help you relax and recover from your most active days and electrifying nights.

[Explore W Costa Navarino](https://www.marriott.com/luxury/explore-luxury-suites/w-costa-navarino)

![EDITION Spa facilities](https://cache.marriott.com/is/image/marriotts7prod/eb-rekeb-spa-facilities-31179)

The Reykjavik EDITION

Featuring bespoke treatments inspired by the Icelandic landscape, The Reykjavik EDITION spa is a place to recharge, rebalance and revitalize. Recover from active days with an invigorating spa bar creation — best enjoyed in the thermal plunge pool — followed by an invigorating body massage. All this alongside an array of facilities including a hammam, steam room and sauna.

[Explore The Reykjavik EDITION](https://www.marriott.com/luxury/explore-luxury-suites/reykjavik-edition)

![Lifestyle](https://cache.marriott.com/is/image/marriotts7prod/lc-dohla-5a9a9674-12769)

Al Messila, a Luxury Collection Resort & Spa, Doha

Built on what used to be an actual oasis, this verdant retreat marries age-old Qatari practices with modern facilities. Pair a hammam ritual with a deep-tissue massage to alleviate fatigue or ask the team for a tailor-made plan and feel your tension float away as you journey through the spa’s circuit. 

[Explore Al Messila](https://www.marriott.com/en-us/hotels/dohla-al-messila-a-luxury-collection-resort-and-spa-doha/experiences/)

![Outdoor Pool](https://cache.marriott.com/is/image/marriotts7prod/rz-sjdzr-ritzsjdzrpo-781485)

Zadún, a Ritz-Carlton Reserve

Blending Sensei’s evidence-led approach with Zadún’s rich cultural heritage, this beachside sanctuary offers curated multi-day retreats that go beyond the basics to help you truly heal — mind, body and soul. Here, spa treatments are performed in secluded settings while the fitness center, expansive pool and savasana room invite moments to hone your practices and relax in ultimate comfort. 

[Explore Zadún](https://www.marriott.com/luxury/explore-luxury-suites/ritz-carlton-reserve-zadun)

![Exterior ](https://cache.marriott.com/is/image/marriotts7prod/usmvl-secret-pond-6215)

Vana Belle, a Luxury Collection Resort, Koh Samui

Inspired by the mythical ethos of the East and mystics of the legendary Thai forest Himavanta, Vana Spa provides a haven of healing in vibrant Koh Samui. A compendium of relaxing techniques crafted in the spirit of Thailand’s rich culture, Vana Spa’s massages are corporeal lullabies for the weary. Indulge in a traditional Thai massage, herbal steam and milk bath, and feel the stress melt away. 

[Explore Vana Belle](https://www.marriott.com/en-us/hotels/usmvl-vana-belle-a-luxury-collection-resort-koh-samui/experiences/)

![Pool Image](https://cache.marriott.com/is/image/marriotts7prod/jw-dedjw-iris-and-light-2-16706)

JW Marriott Mussoorie Walnut Grove & Spa 

Settled amongst Mussoorie's glittering valleys, towering oaks and beautiful walking trails, the true-to-nature elements serve as inspiration to bring this magnificent resort to life. Harnessing the power of global traditions, this opulent spa nestled in the foothills of the Himalayas offers a blend of Swedish, Balinese and Shiatsu treatments infused with ingredients from the world over.

[Explore Mussoorie Walnut Grove](https://www.marriott.com/en-us/hotels/dedjw-jw-marriott-mussoorie-walnut-grove-resort-and-spa/experiences/)

![Peaceful Inner Bath](https://cache.marriott.com/is/image/marriotts7prod/tyonz-inner-bath-6079)

The Ritz-Carlton, Nikko

Drawing its water from the locally revered Nikko Yumoto Onsen, the pools at this exclusive onsen are known to aid in pain and fatigue relief. Immerse yourself in the beauty of the spa, where the serene garden view transforms with Japan’s changing seasons. Close your eyes and let the soothing sound of rippling water wash away your worries in this tranquil retreat. 

[Explore The Ritz-Carlton, Nikko](https://www.ritzcarlton.com/en/hotels/tyonz-the-ritz-carlton-nikko/spa/)

![Chicago Luxury Hotel With Pools](https://cache.marriott.com/is/image/marriotts7prod/xr-chixr-swimming-pool18283-70642)

The St. Regis Chicago

A modern jewel box designed for indulgence, this 12,000-square-foot spa is an enclave apart. Overlooking the Chicago River with views of Navy Pier, features include a heated indoor swimming pool, outdoor pool, yoga studio and two private fitness rooms. Relax in the Iridium Lounge or on the outdoor sundeck, then enjoy an array of treatments featuring French Sothy’s products. 

[Explore The St. Regis Chicago](https://www.marriott.com/en-us/hotels/chixr-the-st-regis-chicago/experiences/)
