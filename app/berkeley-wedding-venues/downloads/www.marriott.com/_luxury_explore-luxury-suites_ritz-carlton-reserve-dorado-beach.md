Experience luxury living at its finest in our three-bedroom residences at Dorado Beach Reserve. Secluded, these condo-style accommodations boast a furnished rooftop terrace complete with a plunge pool, offering stunning views and ultimate relaxation. Inside, you'll find two bedrooms, spacious living and dining areas, a gourmet kitchen, indoor and outdoor showers, a deep soaking tub, laundry room, and a walk-in wardrobe. Enjoy the convenience of a golf cart for easy exploration of the resort grounds. Our residences, available on the first, second, third, and fourth floors, offer a variety of features including private pools, furnished patios, a separate den with a sofa bed, and all the amenities you need for a comfortable stay, ensuring an unforgettable retreat in paradise.
