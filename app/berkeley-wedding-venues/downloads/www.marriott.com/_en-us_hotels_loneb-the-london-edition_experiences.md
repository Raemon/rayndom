# In the Heart of It All

## Experiences

Looking for luxury hotels in London near Covent Garden? Look no further. Nestled between high-end Marylebone, the energy of Soho and the grandeur of Bloomsbury, Fitzrovia is a perfect fusion of neighborhoods — and the perfect place from which to set out and explore the city. Fashion’s most coveted labels are moments away on Oxford and Regent streets, and authentic English pubs and eclectic local eateries line the neighboring streets and cozy side alleys. Surrounding areas, including Covent Garden, Mayfair, Soho and Regent’s Park, are home to iconic cultural attractions and events such as the British Museum, Liberty London, and Frieze Art Fair. Within The London EDITION's walls, guests can enjoy a carefully curated program of cultural events and happenings, alongside exclusive dining at Berners Tavern, bespoke cocktails at the Punch Room and Lobby Bar, and specially crafted experiences for the entire family to enjoy.

## What’s On at The London EDITION

From food and drink experiences in our restaurant or bars to captivating cultural happenings, there's always something exciting to discover at The London EDITION.
