Acclaimed Resorts

# Paradise, Found

Whether you're seeking adventure, relaxation or a bit of both, these sun-soaked resorts offer a transformative escape. 

![Pool](https://cache.marriott.com/is/image/marriotts7prod/jw-sxmdb-sxmdb-pool-18098-93811)

JW Marriott St. Maarten Beach Resort & Spa 

Nestled along the island’s Dawn Beach, JW Marriott St. Maarten Beach Resort & Spa is an ideal destination for a mindful family vacation or a relaxing retreat, thanks to its secluded location, rejuvenating spa and laid-back dining. Take in one of the area’s namesake sunrises from your balcony and then settle into a cabana along the white-sand beach or next to the sparkling infinity pool.

[Explore St. Maarten](https://www.marriott.com/en-us/hotels/sxmdb-jw-marriott-st-maarten-beach-resort-and-spa/overview/)

![Miami Beach resort Pool](https://cache.marriott.com/is/image/marriotts7prod/xr-miaxr-tranquilitypool-miax78538-24114)

The St. Regis Bal Harbour Resort

Nestled between fashionable Collins Avenue and a brilliant white-sand beach, The St. Regis Bal Harbour Resort resides at the intersection of art, style and design. Recognized with Forbes Five-Star and AAA Five-Diamond accolades, it’s easy to see why this iconic enclave just north of Miami Beach has long been a favorite of in-the-know globetrotters. 

[Explore Bal Harbour](https://www.marriott.com/en-us/hotels/miaxr-the-st-regis-bal-harbour-resort/overview/)

![Stylish suite with ocean view](https://cache.marriott.com/is/image/marriotts7prod/wh-pvrwh-60041)

W Punta de Mita

Inspired by local Huichol culture, W Punta de Mita fuses vibrant design with coastal luxury. Set on Mexico’s Riviera Nayarit, this beachfront escape offers bold suites, a lively social scene and world-class waves. Lounge by the ocean, indulge in spa bliss or savor modern Mexican cuisine in a setting where every detail pulses with color, spirit and style.

[Explore Punta de Mita](https://www.marriott.com/luxury/explore-luxury-suites/w-punta-de-mita)

![Infinity pool, palm trees, beach umbrellas  ](https://cache.marriott.com/is/image/marriotts7prod/lc-jmklc-santa-marina-aerial-61104-05731)

Santa Marina, a Luxury Collection Resort

Experience the mesmerizing magic of Mykonos at this sanctuary of Aegean beauty. Relax on the island’s only private sandy beach, where the golden shoreline slopes gradually into crystal-clear, shallow waters. Swaying palms, warm breezes and personalized service set a serene tone that’s carried into the beach-chic suites and villas, each of which includes a private balcony and dreamy views.

[Explore Mykonos](https://www.marriott.com/luxury/explore-luxury-suites/santa-marina-greek-isle)

![Pool Deck & Cabanas](https://cache.marriott.com/is/image/marriotts7prod/rswrz-pool-deck-0880)

The Ritz-Carlton, Naples

This celebrated resort delights with signature experiences on Florida’s Gulf Coast. Immerse yourself in the coastal luxury of well-appointed suites featuring breathtaking views of the Gulf of Mexico. Enjoy a poolside oasis in a fully enclosed, climate-controlled bungalow, or retreat to your open-air cabana to lounge the day away, complete with beverages and a dedicated butler.

[Explore Naples](https://www.marriott.com/luxury/explore-luxury-suites/ritz-carlton-naples)

![Pool with lounge chairs and umbrellas](https://cache.marriott.com/is/image/marriotts7prod/eb-syxeb-beach-club-13542)

The Sanya EDITION

A chic, contemporary retreat on China’s tropical Hainan Island, The Sanya EDITION offers unexpected experiences in a truly remarkable setting. Highlights include a spectacular ocean-like pool complete with private cabanas, a two-story spa adorned with lush greenery and a vibrant rooftop bar that beckons revelers with its infinity pool and signature cocktails. 

[Explore Sanya](https://www.marriott.com/en-us/hotels/syxeb-the-sanya-edition/overview/)
