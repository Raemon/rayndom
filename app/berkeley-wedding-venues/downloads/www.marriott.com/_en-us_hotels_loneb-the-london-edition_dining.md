# A Gastronomic Gem in London

## Dining

Under the culinary genius of Michelin-starred chef Jason Atherton, dining at The London EDITION embodies Ian Schrager’s "new kind of gathering place" ethos — the hotel's restaurant and bars near London's Soho are spaces in which to linger and indulge. Our main London restaurant, Berners Tavern, is a celebration of seasonality and a modern interpretation of British cuisine, while bespoke cocktails and conviviality are on offer in the Lobby Bar and Punch Room.

Festive 2025 Brochure

Celebrate the festive season at The London EDITION, from seasonal menus to sparkling celebrations.

[Learn More](https://online.fliphtml5.com/xzwoa/tknp/#p=1)

Experience Chef Jason Atherton's Berners Tavern

###  Berners Tavern 

Berners Tavern, led by Chef Jason Atherton, showcases the finest British flavours with an ever-evolving menu that emphasises the best seasonal ingredients from the British Isles. The restaurant also features a glowing bar where guests can enjoy cocktails alongside small plates like truffle arancini, crispy prawn rolls, and mini mac and cheese.

[ Learn More ](https://www.bernerstavern.com)

###  Private Dining Room 

Situated above Berners Tavern, the private dining room is an intimate space that seats up to 14 guests. A modern, long dark walnut table with rose-upholstered chairs sits beneath an original historic skylight with atmospheric lighting. 

[ Learn More ](http://www.bernerstavern.com/private-dining/)

###  Saturday Brunch 

Enjoy an unforgettable Saturday brunch at Berners Tavern, featuring delights like smoked salmon and scrambled eggs croissant, crispy chicken & waffles, and brioche French toast. Elevate your meal with a Bloody Mary from our rolling trolley, offering three unique twists and crafted to your liking right at your table, where you can customise the spice level and garnish for a truly personalised experience.

###  Champagne Trolley 

Sip a glass of bubbly from our Champagne Trolley which offers a range of Veuve Clicquot labels, English sparkling wine and non-alcoholic sparkling wines. 

###  Sunday Roast 

Tuck into the tradition that unites friends and family around one table once a week, the Sunday Roast. Serving premium sirloin, slow-cooked Dingley pork belly, roasted chicken breast, and Cornish BBQ sea bream. There truly is a choice for every palate. Served with all the classic trimmings and a generous boat of rich gravy.

###  Bloody Mary Trolly 

Elevate your Sunday by indulging in a Bloody Mary from our rolling trolley, which navigates through the restaurant and is expertly prepared right at your table. Tailor it to your preferences, adjusting the spice level and selecting your desired garnish for a truly personalised experience.
