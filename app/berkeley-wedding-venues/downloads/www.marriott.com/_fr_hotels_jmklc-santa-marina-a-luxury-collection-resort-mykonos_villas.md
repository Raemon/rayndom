## Santa Marina Is Built on a Vision and Our Villas Ultimately Express That

Chic design for summer living. Private swimming pools, lush gardens perched on the hillside, butlers on tap. Privacy, hill-top views, cool breeze. It’s a vision of sun-drenched holidays stretching ahead, time spent enjoying life together with family and friends. We’ve got it sorted.

### Santa Marina's Exclusive Villas 

Within the lush gardens of Santa Marina resort, with panoramic sea views of the Aegean, our exclusive villas, ranging between two to seven bedrooms, offer privacy and luxury while benefitting from full access to the resort’s services, such as the restaurants, private beach, spa, recreation amenities, concierge and security.

###  Santa Marina Private Sandy Beach 

Being located on a private peninsula, Santa Marina provides an unrivaled sanctuary where you can swim and sunbathe in blissful seclusion on the 100m private sandy shores of our beach and be pampered with attentive services.

###  Dining Experiences 

No-one does the social scene, the elegance and the buzz quite like Santa Marina. It’s in our DNA. It might be cocktails at the chic Buddha-Bar Beach or a romantic dinner on the terrace at Mykonos Social, or maybe just a seafood snack from the comfort of your daybed. Whatever you feel like, at whatever pace, we’ve got the perfect something waiting, with views to match.

###  Ginkgo Spa 

Make yourself a priority. Discover the secrets of holistic wellness. Our exclusive Wellness Rituals and Wellbeing Journeys offer specially designed treatments that pamper your body and soul with products and authentic purification therapies.

###  Reef Boutique 

Right on our private beach, Reef Boutique reflects the super-chic style of Mykonos with a selection of fashion-forward clothes, jewellery, sunglasses and accessories for women and men. The collection changes with the season, featuring airy caftans, stylish dresses, swimwear and beachwear.

Cruising experiences are tailored specifically for you.
