# Hotels in Costa Rica

Costa Rica, a breathtaking tropical oasis embraced by two oceans, is a sanctuary for those who cherish nature and those who seek adventure. Its verdant rainforests, teeming with wildlife, and magnificent national parks offer a myriad of experiences, from exhilarating zip-lining to tranquil soaks in thermal hot springs. Marriott Bonvoy’s collection of hotels in Costa Rica serve as the perfect starting point to explore these natural marvels, with many situated near iconic attractions such as Arenal Volcano and Manuel Antonio National Park. Each property is meticulously designed to provide the utmost in comfort and luxury, ensuring your stay is as rejuvenating as it is thrilling.

  


Immerse yourself in the rich culture and warm welcome of Costa Rica with a visit to its vibrant capital, San José, where colonial charm meets modern convenience. Savor local flavors at a traditional soda or indulge in international cuisine at upscale eateries. Our Marriott Bonvoy’s hotels in Costa Rica are the perfect launchpads for exploring the country's renowned eco-tourism destinations and lively coastal communities. From the sun-kissed beaches of the Pacific to the verdant mangroves of the Caribbean, our hotels offer you the best of both worlds, complete with complimentary Wi-Fi, fitness centers, and concierge services to ensure your trip is truly unforgettable.
