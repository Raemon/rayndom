# Holiday Events at W Punta de Mita

## Sunset Celebrations

Sunset Celebrations is more than a festive calendar—it’s a journey from the last rays of light to the spark of a new beginning. Inspired by Tatewari, the Huichol spirit of fire who guides transformation, we invite you to release the old, honor the now, and step into the new with intention. 

Tatewari Spirit

### Discover W Punta de Mita' Restaurants

From intimate dinners rooted in tradition to bold beachfront parties, each event is designed to evolve with the sunset—starting in vibrant hues of orange and pink, and unfolding into fiery nights of celebration.

Signature Festive Experiences at W Punta de Mita

###  Echoes of the Unknown 

A Día de Muertos-inspired four-course dinner blending ancestral flavors, live music, and a celebration of memory.

###  Thank You Feast 

A Thanksgiving spread with refined seasonal dishes and a “Grateful Tree” ritual to share your intentions.

###  Ignite the Festive Season 

A complimentary soirée at the Living Room Bar where the ancestral flame lights up the holidays with bites, beats, and golden cocktails.

###  Christmas Eve Buffet 

A lavish buffet featuring raw bar, carving stations, festive favorites, and live entertainment.

###  X-Mas Feast 

A vibrant Mexican-style Christmas celebration with street-food flair, tacos, ceviches, and holiday sweets.

###  Tatewari Buffet Dinner 

A grand New Year’s Eve buffet with live stations, music, and golden décor setting the stage for transformation.

###  Spiced by Fire Plated Dinner 

A refined four-course dinner where fire, spice, and flavor meet intention.

###  Tatewari After Party 

DJs, fireworks, fire rituals, glowing makeup, tacos al pastor, and a beachfront countdown under the stars.

###  Heat & Heal Recovery 

A Wet Deck poolside recovery party with spicy broths, fresh bites, Clamato creations, and beats to recharge for the year ahead.
