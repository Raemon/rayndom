# Hotels in Dubai, United Arab Emirates

Dubai is a city of grand visions and endless wonders, where towering skyscrapers and luxurious malls meet the ancient allure of desert dunes and vibrant souks. As a global hub for business and tourism, it offers a unique blend of modern sophistication and traditional charm. Whether you're shopping for designer goods at the Dubai Mall, marveling at the views from the Burj Khalifa, or relaxing on the pristine beaches of Jumeirah, the city has something to captivate every visitor. Marriott Bonvoy’s hotels in Dubai are strategically located to provide easy access to these iconic landmarks, ensuring a comfortable and convenient stay.

  


Beyond the city’s famous attractions, Dubai is a playground for adventure and relaxation. Explore the Dubai Desert Conservation Reserve for a thrilling 4x4 safari, or unwind with a rejuvenating spa day at one of the city’s world-class resorts. Marriott Bonvoy’s properties in Dubai offer a range of amenities, from state-of-the-art fitness centers to gourmet dining options, making your stay both enjoyable and memorable. Whether you’re here for business or leisure, the city’s vibrant energy and our exceptional service will guarantee an unforgettable experience.

Dubai, a city famed for its modernity and opulence, harbors a treasure trove of natural marvels waiting to be discovered. The Dubai Desert Conservation Reserve is a sanctuary of pristine beauty, offering heart-pounding activities such as dune bashing, hot air ballooning, and camel trekking. With its diverse wildlife, including the majestic Arabian oryx and graceful gazelles, it's the ideal spot for a one-of-a-kind desert safari. For those seeking a more tranquil experience, the Al Qudra Lakes beckon with their man-made serenity, providing a peaceful backdrop for cycling, jogging, and bird watching, particularly during the cooler months when migratory birds grace the area with their presence.

If your preference is for the coast, Dubai’s beaches offer a wealth of opportunities to engage with the natural world. Jumeirah Beach, with its soft white sand and clear blue waters, is the perfect place to relax, swim, or enjoy a range of water sports. The city’s mangrove forests, which can be explored on various eco-tours, offer a more peaceful and educational experience. Here, you can paddle through the unique ecosystem on a kayak or stand-up paddleboard. Whether you’re in the mood for the wide-open spaces of the desert or the tranquility of the coast, Dubai’s natural landscapes provide the perfect contrast to the city’s urban buzz.

The culture in Dubai is a fascinating tapestry of traditional Arab heritage and a modern, cosmopolitan vibe. When you choose to stay at one of our hotels in Dubai, you position yourself at the intersection of these two worlds. Imagine wandering through ancient souks and lively markets, catching a glimpse of the city's storied past, then looking up to marvel at the iconic Burj Khalifa and the city's futuristic skyline. Our hotels are the perfect springboard for exploring the city's cultural landmarks, such as the Dubai Museum, nestled in the historic Al Fahidi Fort, offering a window into the city's transformation from a humble fishing village to a global hub. After a day of discovery, return to the comfort of your Marriott Bonvoy oasis, complete with lavish amenities and breathtaking views.

Dubai’s arts scene is as dynamic as its skyline, with numerous galleries and cultural districts like Al Serkal Avenue and the Dubai Design District (d3) showcasing a diverse range of contemporary art and design. The city also hosts major events like the Dubai Art Week and the Dubai Design Week, which attract artists and designers from around the globe. For a more immersive experience, visit the Dubai Opera, where world-class performances from opera to ballet and concerts are held, or catch a show at the Madinat Jumeirah Theatre. Our hotels in Dubai ensure you are never far from the city’s cultural heartbeat, providing easy access to these and other enriching experiences.

With its desert climate, Dubai offers long, hot summers and mild winters, making it a year-round destination for warmth and sunshine. From June to August, temperatures typically soar above 100°F (38°C), with humidity adding to the heat. This is the perfect time to enjoy the city’s indoor attractions, like the Dubai Mall and its ice rink, or to take a dip in the hotel’s pool. Despite the high temperatures, the city's modern infrastructure ensures that you stay comfortable with air-conditioned facilities in all Marriott Bonvoy hotels.

Winters, from December to February, offer a more temperate climate with average temperatures ranging from the 70s to 80s°F (21-27°C), making it ideal for outdoor activities. This season sees clear, sunny skies and cooler evenings, perfect for exploring the city’s vibrant markets, scenic beaches, and bustling streets. Spring and fall, from March to May and September to November, bring transitional weather, with temperatures in the 80s to 90s°F (27-32°C), providing a pleasant balance for both relaxation and exploration. Whether you’re visiting for a beach getaway or a cultural experience, Dubai’s climate ensures that you can enjoy the city’s diverse offerings comfortably.

Dubai is a city with a wealth of transportation options, making it easy to get to and from your hotel when you stay with Marriott Bonvoy. Most travelers arrive at Dubai International Airport, one of the busiest airports in the world. From there, you can easily take a taxi, limousine, or ride-sharing service like Careem or Uber to your hotel, no matter if you’re staying in the heart of downtown or in a more relaxed coastal area. Some Marriott Bonvoy hotels even offer complimentary airport shuttle service to make your trip even easier. If you’re driving to Dubai, the city is well connected by highways and you can easily drive in from the other emirates.

Once you arrive in Dubai, you’ll find getting around the city is a breeze. The Dubai Metro is a quick, clean, modern and air-conditioned rail system that connects many of the city’s major areas, including the Dubai Mall, Burj Khalifa and Dubai Marina. The metro is complemented by a comprehensive bus system, operated by the Roads and Transport Authority (RTA), which services more remote locations and is an affordable way to see the city. For a more scenic and luxurious experience, consider renting a car from one of the many rental agencies located throughout the city, or opt for a chauffeur-driven limousine. Dubai is also known for its innovative transportation solutions, such as the Water Taxi and the Dubai Ferry, which provide a unique perspective of the city’s coastline and landmarks. Whether you choose to drive, ride or walk, Dubai offers a diverse array of options to ensure your exploration is as smooth and enjoyable as possible.

Dubai is a playground of wonder for children, with a myriad of attractions designed to delight young adventurers. Take a trip to the Dubai Aquarium & Underwater Zoo, where your little ones can gaze in awe at the world's largest indoor aquarium and get up close to over 140,000 aquatic animals. For a day of pure excitement, venture to the Dubai Parks and Resorts, a vast entertainment hub that includes motiongate Dubai, Bollywood Parks, and Legoland, each offering a distinct and joyous experience. The interactive shows, rides, and attractions are certain to captivate the entire family for hours on end.

If your family has a taste for adventure, Dubai is ready to serve. The Dubai Miracle Garden is a sight to behold, with over 45 million flowers in captivating arrangements and patterns. The Dubai Safari Park is another gem, offering children the chance to see giraffes, lions, and monkeys in a natural environment. After a day of excitement, relax at one of our family-friendly hotels, where the little ones can splash in the pool, have fun in the play areas, or join kids’ clubs for more adventures.
