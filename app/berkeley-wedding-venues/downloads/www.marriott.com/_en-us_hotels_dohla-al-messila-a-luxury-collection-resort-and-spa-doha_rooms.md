# Luxury Doha accommodation

## Accommodations

A rare gem, Al Messila Resort & Spa is an exclusive destination inviting guests to unwind in 5-star Doha hotel rooms and luxurious family suites with balcony. The spacious rooms and suites feature king and twin beds according to your preference. Unlock the resort's full potential by choosing a room or suite with pool view and make the most of this genuine oasis.

Kids Club

From March 2nd until the first day of Eid, the Kids Club will operate from 5:30 PM to 8:00 PM, offering access to the Arts & Crafts area and Cinema. This service is complimentary for guests visiting the Ramadan Garden during Iftar.

Luxury Pool Villas in Doha

### Luxury Private Villas

The 30 custom-designed private pool villas are your true home away from home with natural light flooding every single room, enhancing every detail of the ornamental mashrabiya patterns. Immerse yourself in a genuinely indigenous experience as you step outside and bask in the sun by the pool in your private backyard enlivened by the radiant flora one can only find in a natural oasis.

Sprinkled throughout the resort grounds, the villas vary in size from one- to four-bedrooms and are thoughtfully appointed with a state-of-the-art kitchen, private garden and plunge pool.

## An Enchanting Family Playground

Let the little ones explore the resort grounds and spark their creativity at the Little Explorers' Club while allowing them to have their own space in a connecting room. One child under 12 stays for free in the existing bedding and one extra bed per room can be provided, at an extra cost of QR 250.
