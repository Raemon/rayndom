Thinking Beyond the Conference Room

###  Great Room 1 

Receive your guests in style and wow them with Art Nouveau details on the walls and views of the lively Wenceslas Square—a room as a conversation starter for your meetings or dinners. Add to the agenda.

###  Great Room 2 

This room, the twin of Great Room 1, impresses with its interior and views of Wenceslas Square. Both rooms can be connected for larger events and are next to Le Petit Beefbar at Grand Café — perfect for quick coffee breaks or lunch plans.

###  Great Room 3 

Stretched in length, this room is perfect for rows of seats and accommodates larger gatherings. In 1912, the hotel gained fame when author Franz Kafka held his only public reading within these very four walls.
