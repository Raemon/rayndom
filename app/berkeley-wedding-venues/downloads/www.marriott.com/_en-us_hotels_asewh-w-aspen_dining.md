# Bites + Sips at W Aspen

## Dining

Embark on a culinary journey at W Aspen, a destination that offers dining experiences around the clock. Each meal is a unique blend of creativity, flavor, and style. Indulge in elevated Mediterranean cuisine and curated cocktails at 39 Degrees, our signature restaurant, featuring vibrant indoor and outdoor seating. Discover WET Deck, our year-round rooftop bar in Aspen, where refreshing cocktails, an energetic atmosphere, and unforgettable 360-degree views await. Unwind in Hidalgo, Aspen’s hottest agave cocktail lounge and speakeasy, fueled by DJ sets and inspired cocktail culture. End your evening in style with our around-the-clock in-room dining experience. At W Aspen, dining is truly an immersive adventure.

###  Frequently Asked Questions

What restaurants does W Aspen have on property? 

The on-property restaurants at W Aspen are:

Hidalgo - Agave Cocktail Lounge & Tequileria 39 DEGREES Rooftop In-Room Dining

Does W Aspen offer room service? 

Yes, room service is available at W Aspen.

What cuisine is offered at W Aspen? 

The cuisines offered at W Aspen are:

Eclectic at Hidalgo - Agave Cocktail Lounge & Tequileria International at 39 DEGREES, Rooftop, In-Room Dining

How do I book a table at one of the restaurants at W Aspen? 

To book a table at Hidalgo - Agave Cocktail Lounge & Tequileria [click here](http://www.opentable.com/restaurant/profile/1390612/reserve?restref=1390612) To book a table at 39 DEGREES [click here](http://www.opentable.com/restaurant/profile/1045783/reserve?restref=1045783) To book a table at Rooftop [click here](http://www.opentable.com/restaurant/profile/1387747/reserve?restref=1387747) You can also call +1 970-431-0800 to place a reservation.

Does W Aspen offer complimentary breakfast? 

Complimentary breakfast is not currently served at W Aspen. View on-site and nearby dining options on our [dining page](https://www.marriott.com/en-us/hotels/asewh-w-aspen/dining/#on-site-dining).
