## Accommodations

A residential-inspired retreat designed for business travellers and culture seekers, The St. Regis Hong Kong offers 127 exquisitely-appointed guestrooms and suites designed by André Fu. Each room features warm, inviting touches where discerning guests experience the signature St. Regis style living and butler services, combining elements of time and place that both reinforce and elevate the spirit of the brand. 

## A Refined Hong Kong Residential Mansion

Cultural influences that represent Hong Kong are apparent in every corner of our rooms. Bedrooms are spacious in serene sanctuary with a gently curved, winged headboard capped by white coloured lacquer panels reminiscent of traditional Wan Chai shop shutters, adding a sense of place and a touch of indulgent style. Bedside lamps remind us of traditional Chinese lanterns, while the oak dividers evoke Hong Kong’s traditional shop windows.
