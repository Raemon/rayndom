# Hotels in Amman, Jordan

Amman, a city of contrasts, where the ancient meets the modern. From the historic sites of the Roman Theater and the Citadel to the contemporary shopping malls and bustling markets, there is something for everyone. Marriott Bonvoy’s hotels in Amman are the perfect base from which to explore the city, offering comfort and convenience with a range of amenities to make your stay memorable. Whether you are visiting for business or leisure, our hotels are ideally located near to the city’s key attractions and business districts, allowing you to fully immerse yourself in the city’s vibrant culture.

  


Savor the flavors of traditional Jordanian cuisine or explore international fare at the city’s diverse restaurants, many of which are just a short walk from our hotels. Experience the lively ambiance of Rainbow Street, with its cafes and boutiques spilling onto the sidewalks, or find peace in the Royal Botanic Garden. With free Wi-Fi, fitness centers, and other considerate amenities, our Marriott Bonvoy hotels in Amman provide a haven for relaxation after a day of exploration or business, ensuring your stay is as comfortable as it is captivating.

Amman is a city that effortlessly merges the urban with the natural, offering a plethora of outdoor experiences for the avid nature enthusiast. Just a stone's throw from the city center is the Amman River Park, an idyllic green expanse that beckons for leisurely strolls along the river's edge and picnics beneath the boughs of ancient trees. For the more intrepid, the nearby Ajloun Forest Reserve is a haven for hikers and birdwatchers, boasting a tapestry of woodlands and a rich diversity of wildlife. With well-marked trails catering to all levels of adventurers, from the casual rambler to the ardent trekker, the reserve ensures an accessible escape to nature for all.
