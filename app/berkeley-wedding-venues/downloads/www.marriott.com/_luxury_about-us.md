OUR PROMISE

Moving people physically has never been easier — but moving them emotionally has never been more difficult. We strive to create a new standard around the high art of moving people by giving guests more to feel. More to dream about. And more ways to access their dreams.

We are the Luxury Group at Marriott International, and we are in the enviable position of having an unparalleled luxury offering with the industry’s largest luxury footprint and pipeline. It takes decades to build a diverse and extraordinary luxury brand portfolio like ours. Together, we strive to forge memorable experiences and valuable relationships with customers, owners and associates.

Together, we will make a positive impact on the luxury industry.

_—Tina Edmundson, President, Luxury Group at Marriott International_
