Observation Bar Car 

Prior to boarding you are served an Andean infusion, which you are welcome to enjoy in the Observation Bar Car while contemplating the mesmerizing views of the Sacred Valley. A variety of small snacks are offered at the bar and drinks are available to purchase throughout the journey.

Dining Car

7-Day Itinerary - Rainbow Mountains 

##### Day 1

  * \- Travel by plane from Lima to the city of Cusco.
  * \- "Valle Sur" experience: Visit Tipón, the temple dedicated to water, followed by a tour of the Huacarpay lagoon and the church of Andahuaylillas (also called the Sistine Chapel of the Andes).
  * \- Transfer to Tambo del Inka, a Luxury Collection Resort & Spa, Valle Sagrado (conveniently located in the heart of the Sacred Valley).



##### Day 2

  * \- Private guided tour through Chinchero (town frozen in time), Moray (agricultural laboratory) and Salineras de Maras (Salt Ponds).
  * \- Enjoy the local experiences of Tambo del Inka, a Luxury Collection Resort & Spa, Valle Sagrado and the Kallpa Spa.



##### Day 3

  * \- Visit the ruins of Pisac, a great city that will show you the fusion between the original Andean cultures and those from Europe.
  * \- Visit to Ollantaytambo, and its monumental pre-Hispanic architecture.



##### Day 4 Full-Day Machu Picchu Tour

  * \- Departure from Tambo del Inka, a Luxury Collection Resort & Spa, Valle Sagrado to the town of Aguas Calientes (Machu Picchu town).
  * \- Travel by bus to the citadel of Machu Picchu.
  * \- Guided tour of the citadel plus buffet lunch in a restaurant at the mountains.
  * \- Transfer to Aguas Calientes.
  * \- Transfer to Tambo del Inka, a Luxury Collection Resort & Spa, Valle Sagrado.



##### Day 5

  * \- Transfer to Palacio del Inka, a Luxury Collection Hotel, Cusco.
  * \- Experience "Encounter of Two Worlds" Cusco city tour: Visit the San Pedro Market plus walk through the most important streets of the city and the emblematic Mansion of Cuatro Bustos, part of the hotel.
  * \- Visit the ruins of Sacsayhuaman, Qenko and Puca Pucara.



##### Day 6

  * \- Tour to the Seven Colors Mountain ( 5:00 AM – 5:00 PM)
  * \- Enjoy Peruvian gastronomy that offers the Inti Raymi restaurant.



##### Day 7

  * \- Transfer to Cusco airport to Lima international airport and final destination.



5-Day Itinerary │ Machu Picchu and Encounter of Two Worlds 

##### Day 1

  * \- Travel by plane from Lima to the city of Cusco.
  * \- Tour four ruins: Sacsayhuaman, Qenko, Tambomachay, Puca Pucara.
  * \- Arrival of Tambo del lnka, a Luxury Collection Resort & Spa, Valle Sagrado. (It is recommended to rest and acclimatize to the height.)



##### Day 2

  * \- Exploration on electric bicycles (bicycles will allow you to drive the entire route with minimal effort).
  * \- Route of the Andean Potatoes: Departure to Huayllabamba for the bike path (the Urubamba River is present on at least one third part of the route) plus tasting of different kinds of local potatoes.
  * \- Private guided tour through Moray (agricultural laboratory) and Salineras de Maras (Salt Ponds).



##### Day 3

  * \- Departure from Tambo del Inka, a Luxury Collection Resort & Spa, Valle Sagrado to the town of Aguas Calientes (Machu Picchu town).
  * \- Travel by bus to the citadel of Machu Picchu.
  * \- Guided tour of the citadel plus buffet lunch in a restaurant at the mountains.
  * \- Transfer to Aguas Calientes.
  * \- Transfer to Tambo del Inka, a Luxury Collection Resort & Spa, Valle Sagrado.



##### Day 4

  * \- Zipline in the Sacred Valley. Rappel descent plus lunch at the Huayoccari ranch.
  * \- Enjoy the local experiences of Tambo del Inka, a Luxury Collection Resort & Spa, Valle Sagrado and the Kallpa Spa



##### Day 5

  * \- Transfer to Palacio del Inka, a Luxury Collection Hotel, Cusco.
  * \- Experience "Encounter of Two Worlds" Cusco city tour: Visit the San Pedro Market plus walk through the most important streets of the city and the emblematic Mansion of Cuatro Bustos, part of the hotel.
  * \- Transfer to the Cusco airport.



5-Day Itinerary Ollantaytambo and Chincheros 

##### Day 1

  * \- Travel by plane from Lima to the city of Cusco.
  * \- Transfer 1.5 hours to Tambo del lnka, a Luxury Collection Resort & Spa, Valle Sagrado (located in the Sacred Valley, at a lower altitude, it is the perfect place to acclimatize).
  * \- Take a private guided tour through Chincheros (town frozen in time), Moray (agricultural laboratory) and Maras salt ponds.



##### Day 2

  * \- Visit the ruins of Pisac, a great city that will show you the fusion between the original Andean cultures and those from Europe.
  * \- Visit to Ollantaytambo and its monumental pre-Hispanic architecture.



##### Day 3

  * \- Departure from Tambo del Inka, a Luxury Collection Resort & Spa, Valle Sagrado to the town of Aguas Calientes (Machu Picchu town).
  * \- Travel by bus to the citadel of Machu Picchu.
  * \- Guided tour of the citadel plus buffet lunch in a restaurant at the mountains.
  * \- Transfer to Aguas Calientes.
  * \- Transfer to Tambo del Inka, a Luxury Collection Resort & Spa, Valle Sagrado.



##### Day 4

  * \- Transfer to Palacio del lnka, a Luxury Collection Hotel, Cusco.
  * \- Experience "Encounter of Two Worlds" Cusco city tour: Visit the San Pedro Market, the main square of the city, San Blas neighborhood and the Four Busts Mansion.
  * \- Visit the ruins of Sacsayhuaman, Qenko and Puca Pucara.
  * \- Relax in the Andes Spirit Spa, the only spa with a thermal circuit in the city.



##### Day 5

  * \- Transfer to Cusco airport
