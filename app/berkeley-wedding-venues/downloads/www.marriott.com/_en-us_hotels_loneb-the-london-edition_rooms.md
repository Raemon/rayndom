# Subtle Sophistication

## Accommodations

The London EDITION's luxurious rooms and suites are serene havens in the heart of London's Fitzrovia neighborhood. Stylish in their simplicity, yet decadent in their details. The rooms and suites near Soho, provide a home away from home feel while still feeling elevated and luxurious. In the rooms, oak floors and wood-paneled walls surround guests in warmth, while distinct design touches like tufted George Smith lounge chairs, cantilevered desks, gold-framed Hendrik Kerstens photography, and plush throws invite guests to unwind in style. In the suites, airy layouts and abundant natural light create a feeling of openness; several suites take that further by offering furnished terraces with sweeping city views.

Premier Suites

## Home Away From Home

Our luxury suites offer airy, spacious layouts and thoughtful design touches, combined with the hotel’s signature services like fresh flowers and evening turndown. A large living area features George Smith furniture and gold-framed photography by Hendrik Kerstens. The Penthouse and Terrace suites feature oversized, furnished terraces with expansive views of the city below.

###  Penthouse 

The Penthouse at The London EDITION is a stunning oasis of luxury and sophistication, offering breathtaking views of the city skyline. With its elegant design, featuring contemporary furnishings and rich textures, the space exudes a timeless charm. Guests can enjoy a spacious living area, a private terrace for al fresco dining, and state-of-the-art amenities, ensuring an unforgettable experience.

###  Berners Suite 

The Berners Suite invites an abundance of natural light through its expansive windows. This beautifully designed space combines contemporary elegance with comfort, creating a serene atmosphere perfect for relaxation. The suite’s elegant furnishings and thoughtful details, make it a delightful retreat in the heart of the city.

Rooms

### A Warm Welcome

With oak floors, walnut-paneled walls, and rich, neutral textures, The London EDITION's cabin-like rooms evoke the interior of a luxury yacht. Bespoke furniture and evocative photography pique interest, while oversized bathtubs and rainforest showers soothe the senses. Choose from rooms with open-plan sitting areas, private outdoor terraces, and more.
