## Accommodations

Recharge and refresh during your stay at The Westin Reserva Conchal, an All-Inclusive Golf Resort & Spa. Located near Playa Conchal, our all-inclusive hotel's airy villas, guest rooms and suites sit on 2,400 acres of lush coastal property in Costa Rica. Relax in thoughtful accommodations designed to help you feel completely balanced. Enjoy dazzling views of the Playa Conchal beaches or the dynamic Costa Rican landscape from your suite's private balcony or terrace. Sleep soundly on our plush signature mattresses with down pillows and crisp cotton linens. Our Westin White Tea Heavenly™ bath amenities and waterfall showerheads give you a spa-like experience in the privacy of your own room. Your all-inclusive package includes 24-hour room service and a well-stocked minibar of soda, bottled water, liquor and beer. Stay connected with high-speed internet access and a flat screen LCD TV. Breathe easy in Costa Rica - all of our hotel rooms and suites are smoke free.

### Westin Club Guest Rooms

For elegant accommodations in a pristine tropical setting, choose a Westin Club Room. Access the exclusive Westin Club private pool and lounge. As soon as you arrive, you’ll feel invited to settle in and relax. Take a complimentary Costa Rican coffee to the balcony, featuring an outdoor soaking tub along with refreshing garden or pool views.
