A Miami Beach escape ideal for entertaining or seaside relaxation. The Penthouse offers spacious indoor and outdoor living rooms, a full kitchen, private rooftop pool, multiple balconies, and floor-to-ceiling windows, boasting views of the ocean and Miami cityscape.
