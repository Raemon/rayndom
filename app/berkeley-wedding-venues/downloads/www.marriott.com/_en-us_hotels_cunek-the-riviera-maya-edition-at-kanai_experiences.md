# Discover a Sanctuary of Unparalleled Serenity

## Experiences

Nestled within the breathtaking landscape of the Yucatán Peninsula, The Riviera Maya EDITION at Kanai offers an immersive experience where nature and elegance intertwine. From the celestial-inspired Yoga Deck, designed to echo the region’s sacred cenotes, to the tranquil central pool, a lush oasis surrounded by floating cabanas and sun-drenched loungers, every space invites relaxation. Stroll along a palm-lined boardwalk to the SO’OL Beach Club, where an infinity pool and open-air grill embrace Tulum’s effortless allure. Bask in the serenity of a pristine white-sand beach, retreat to a private cabana, or rejuvenate at The Spa, a cenote-inspired sanctuary blending ancient wellness rituals with EDITION’s signature hospitality.

### A Sanctuary of Indulgence

EDITION Spas celebrates vibrant living, seamlessly fusing global healing traditions with a local flare at every location and showcasing top-tier wellness treatments and beauty services that merge timeless philosophies with cutting-edge methods and emerging trends.

###  Spa 

At the heart of the holistic experience of The Riviera Maya EDITION at Kanai is The Spa. The cenote-inspired sanctuary features hydrotherapy pools, a private outdoor pool and garden, a Turkish Hamman and a steam and chromotherapy treatment bed alongside six private indoor-outdoor treatment rooms.

###  LUX Tennis Club 

Level up your game at LUX Tennis Club, Kanai's upscale racquet complex in a natural reserve.

## Beach Pool

Accessible by a picturesque palm tree-lined sand boardwalk from the Lagoon to the ocean, the SO’OL Beach Club embraces the Tulum aesthetic and features an infinity pool, dining and lounge areas along the beach, and a central outdoor grill, allowing guests to witness the chef's culinary artistry.
