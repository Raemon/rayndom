## Dining

Refresh your palate and your soul at one of the many restaurants at The Westin Grand Cayman Seven Mile Beach Resort. From an airy Caribbean setting for breakfast at Ferdinand's restaurant to a themed night on the beach at Tortugas to a AAA 4 Diamond experience in Beach House to locally inspired cuisine fresh from the sea at woto, you'll find a variety of flavors with a Cayman Islands influence.

## Introducing Chef Andre Blasczak

Chef Andre boasts a career enriched by diverse culinary experiences spanning multiple continents. With a passion for infusing traditional recipes with modern flair, his innovative approach has earned acclaim in renowned kitchens around the world. His mastery of flavors and techniques reflects a deep commitment to culinary excellence, evident in every dish he creates. Chef Blasczak celebrates the bold flavors and rich culture of the island, using sustainable, locally sourced, and seasonal ingredients.

###  Frequently Asked Questions

What restaurants does The Westin Grand Cayman Seven Mile Beach Resort & Spa have on property? 

The on-property restaurants at The Westin Grand Cayman Seven Mile Beach Resort & Spa are:

Beach House Ferdinand's Tortuga Beach Grill & Bar Cayman Coffee Exchange Toes in the Sand by Beach House Woto Catboat Bar

Does The Westin Grand Cayman Seven Mile Beach Resort & Spa offer room service? 

Yes, room service is available at The Westin Grand Cayman Seven Mile Beach Resort & Spa.

What cuisine is offered at The Westin Grand Cayman Seven Mile Beach Resort & Spa? 

The cuisines offered at The Westin Grand Cayman Seven Mile Beach Resort & Spa are:

Grill at Tortuga Beach Grill & Bar Multiple cuisines at Woto Tapas at Catboat Bar Seafood at Beach House, Toes in the Sand by Beach House Coffee House at Cayman Coffee Exchange International at Ferdinand's

How do I book a table at one of the restaurants at The Westin Grand Cayman Seven Mile Beach Resort & Spa? 

To book a table at Beach House [click here](http://www.opentable.com/restaurant/profile/107215/reserve?restref=107215) You can also call +1 345-945-3800 to place a reservation.

Does The Westin Grand Cayman Seven Mile Beach Resort & Spa offer complimentary breakfast? 

Complimentary breakfast is not currently served at The Westin Grand Cayman Seven Mile Beach Resort & Spa. View on-site and nearby dining options on our [dining page](https://www.marriott.com/en-us/hotels/gcmmi-the-westin-grand-cayman-seven-mile-beach-resort-and-spa/dining/#on-site-dining).
