# Arabella Golf Courses

## Golf

Just 15 minutes away by car from The St. Regis Mardavall Mallorca Resort, our distinguished guests enjoy preferred tee times and exclusive caddy services at the nearby Son Vida, Son Mutaner and Son Quint golf courses. Private shuttle service and luxury transfers can also be arranged by the St. Regis Butler, who will be pleased to organize an indulgent day on the greens complete with lunch, beverages and a post-golf massage. 

## Green Fee Rates and Promotions

Guests at The St. Regis Mardavall Mallorca are entitled to enjoy special green fee rates at the golf courses. Prices depend on the golf course, number of holes and season. Take advantage and improve your golf skills while staying at The St. Regis Mardavall Mallorca Resort.

## Golf Academy

The Arabella Golf Academy offers our guests and customers everything they need to improve their game. Top level trained P.G.A. Golf Professionals with experience in teaching and coaching to match your individual needs and wishes, comprehensive program of classes, a gifted club fitting with Trackman for swing analysis, a junior academy for children and teenagers, a covered floodlit driving range in winter, and a 9-hole par 3 for children. For reservations and further information, please contact +34 971 704 870 or junioracademy@naturalgolfmanagement.com

## Golf Shops

All golf courses feature an Arabella Golf Mallorca Pro Shop, open 365 days a year. Each store has its own identity, with different brands and different styles, according to the personality of the course, where you will find everything necessary for playing golf. Contact Info: Golf Son Muntaner +34 971 783 000 Golf Son Vida +34 971 791 210 Golf Son Quint +34 971 606 177

Arabella Golf Courses

![Golf Son Muntaner](data:image/png;base64,iVBORw0KGgoAAAANSUhEUgAAAA4AAAAKCAYAAACE2W/HAAAAFklEQVR42mN8/fpLPQMZgHFU48jWCAD/vyDle0zFdgAAAABJRU5ErkJggg==)

Son Muntaner 

Proud host of DP World Tour’s Mallorca Golf Open 2022. Golf Son Muntaner is a course designed to exceed your expectations. In 2016 we carried out an ambitious project by completely seeding new varieties of grass on the course. The Bermuda Celebration was sown on every fairway & Agrostis V8 was chosen for its extended greens. With this process, Golf Son Muntaner becomes a reference golf course for any golf lover.

[ Book Now ](https://arabella.golfmanager.com/consumer/ebookings)

![Golf Son Vida](data:image/png;base64,iVBORw0KGgoAAAANSUhEUgAAAA4AAAAKCAYAAACE2W/HAAAAFklEQVR42mN8/fpLPQMZgHFU48jWCAD/vyDle0zFdgAAAABJRU5ErkJggg==)

Son Vida 

Golf Son Vida is the most traditional and first course in Mallorca and a legend among players from all over Europe. This prestigious course has hosted on two occasions the “Open de Baleares”, belonging to the European Tour. The first time in 1990 and the second time in 1994.

[ Book Now ](https://arabella.golfmanager.com/consumer/ebookings)

![Golf Son Quint](data:image/png;base64,iVBORw0KGgoAAAANSUhEUgAAAA4AAAAKCAYAAACE2W/HAAAAFklEQVR42mN8/fpLPQMZgHFU48jWCAD/vyDle0zFdgAAAABJRU5ErkJggg==)

Son Quint 

Son Quint is the youngest golf course of the island, inaugurated in 2007. The long and open course allows a more extensive game, which makes members and beginners more than happy. Perched high on the hillside the course provides you with splendid views. From tee 8, you see the Bay of Palma.

[ Book Now ](https://arabella.golfmanager.com/consumer/ebookings)

![Golf Son Quint Pitch + Putt](data:image/png;base64,iVBORw0KGgoAAAANSUhEUgAAAA4AAAAKCAYAAACE2W/HAAAAFklEQVR42mN8/fpLPQMZgHFU48jWCAD/vyDle0zFdgAAAABJRU5ErkJggg==)

Son Quint Pitch & Putt 

Golf Son Quint Pitch & Putt is a 9-hole course with all PAR 3 holes, the best way to learn golf. It is the only Pitch & Putt on the island, the design of holes 4, 6 and 7 will mean a real challenge for the short game of any golf player.
