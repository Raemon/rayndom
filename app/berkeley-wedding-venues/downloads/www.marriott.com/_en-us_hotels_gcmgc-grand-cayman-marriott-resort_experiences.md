# Reconnect With Yourself, Family & Friends

## Experiences

Imagine waking up and start your day with a delicious cup of freshly brewed coffee on the deck, gazing at the turquoise ocean, or enjoying a gentle morning yoga session by the sea, breathing in the salty breeze and taking in the surrounding nature. At our Summer House there is always something delightful to experience. 

## Endless Summer Pool

Dive into endless turquoise at Marriott's shimmering pool. Sun-kissed waters beckon, inviting you to bask on poolside chaises or splash into refreshing bliss. Sip frozen cocktails beneath swaying palms, savor light bites between dips, and watch the sun paint the sky with fiery hues.

## Complimentary Beach Shuttle

To enhance your experience, we offer a courtesy shuttle running daily from 9 am to 5 pm to Public Beach and Governor's Beach, departing every hour so you can enjoy your vacation to the fullest. Here, summer isn’t just a season; for us, it’s a way of life. Travel time to nearby beaches is convenient, with Governor's Beach just a 5-minute ride away and Public Beach an 8-minute ride away. 

![Couple at beach with white van](https://cache.marriott.com/is/image/marriotts7prod/mc-gcmgc-summer-house-37152-38832:Classic-Hor?wid=1300&fit=constrain)
