## Junior Suite

###### Florentine nobility

Regal color motifs and antique furniture reminiscent of Florentine nobility give the 50-square-meter Junior Suites incomparable grandeur. Wall frescos and 15th Century-style artwork communicate the city's creative history, while large windows allow guests a view of busting Via Montebello or the scenic Arno River and surrounding vineyard covered hills.  
  
[Discover more](https://my.matterport.com/show/?m=KrhQWB3dKwG)

##  Sitting Area 

Each suite boasts a comfortable sitting area or a separate living room furnished with an inviting sofa and work desk, providing plenty of space for out-of-the-office communication via a direct-dial telephone and High Speed Internet Access.

##  Stanford Suite 

Relaxation comes courtesy of the 42-inch LCD television with DVD player. Additionally, a Bose sound system with iPod docking station enables guests to select their ideal soundtracks as they recline with an in-room aperitif from the refreshment center. The smooth marble bathroom further reflects sophisticated Florentine and Medici style, including a deep soaking bathtub, large vanity and double basins.

[ 3D Tour Open in New Tab ](https://my.matterport.com/show/?m=cMTe7sQFejZ)

##  Michelangelo Suite 

Select suites also come with a separate shower while all are equipped with a second television, integrated into the mirror. Premier Acqua di Parma bath amenities, plush bathrobes and slipper complete the picture of repose. Bespoke personalized service by the St. Regis Butler is always on hand to attend to additional desires, creating the finest experience with no details overlooked. One additional bedroom can be connected to the "Michelangelo" and the "Stanford" Suite.
