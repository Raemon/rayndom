# Hotels in Singapore

Singapore, a city-state that is both modern and steeped in tradition, is a destination that has something for everyone. From the iconic Marina Bay Sands and Gardens by the Bay to the charming streets of Chinatown and Little India, Singapore is a city of contrasts that is sure to captivate you. Marriott Bonvoy’s hotels in Singapore are the perfect base from which to explore this vibrant city, offering luxurious amenities and prime locations that make your stay comfortable and convenient. Whether you are visiting for business or pleasure, our hotels will ensure that you are well-connected to the city center and all of its world-class shopping, dining, and entertainment options.

  


Singapore is a city that’s not just about the urban jungle, but also about real jungles. The Singapore Zoo, Jurong Bird Park, and the Singapore Botanic Gardens are just a few of the green spots where you can relax and reconnect with nature. Our hotels in Singapore often have their own tranquil spaces, including rooftop pools and spas, perfect for unwinding after a day of exploration. With a wide range of deals and packages, we make it easy to enjoy the best of Singapore, from its bustling markets to its serene beaches, ensuring your visit is nothing short of extraordinary.
