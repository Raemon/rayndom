## Santa Marina Is Built on a Vision and Our Villas Ultimately Express That

Chic design for summer living. Private swimming pools, lush gardens perched on the hillside, butlers on tap. Privacy, hill-top views, cool breeze. It’s a vision of sun-drenched holidays stretching ahead, time spent enjoying life together with family and friends. We’ve got it sorted.

### Santa Marina's Exclusive Villas 

Within the lush gardens of Santa Marina resort, with panoramic sea views of the Aegean, our exclusive villas, ranging between two to seven bedrooms, offer privacy and luxury while benefitting from full access to the resort’s services, such as the restaurants, private beach, spa, recreation amenities, concierge and security.
