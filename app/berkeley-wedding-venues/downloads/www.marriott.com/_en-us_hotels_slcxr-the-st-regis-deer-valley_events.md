# Event Venues in Park City

## Events

With the stunning Wasatch Mountains as an elevated backdrop, The St. Regis Deer Valley is the ideal location for your next business meeting or event. We offer nearly 10,000 feet of indoor event space comprising 11 versatile venues. The Astor Ballroom, our largest space, accommodates up to 295 attendees for a grand event. Our smaller event venues in Park City are perfect for more intimate gatherings or a secluded wedding reception. For a truly remarkable event, utilize our stunning outdoor space and embrace the beauty of our natural surroundings. Our event planners anticipate every need and arrange every detail ensuring that each meeting is thoughtfully organized and flawlessly executed.
