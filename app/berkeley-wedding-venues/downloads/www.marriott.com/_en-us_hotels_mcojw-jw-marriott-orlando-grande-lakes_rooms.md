### Family Suites

When you stay in one of the 15 family suites at our hotel in Orlando, our expert hospitality team provides itinerary and reservation planning as well as a VIP check-in experience. With children’s ages and interests accounted for, enjoy specifically chosen amenities for the whole family each night of your stay. From bathtub toys and mess-free markers to projectors and stuffed rocket ships, this exclusive offer is fun for children of all ages. We also coordinate your reservations, dining experiences, and activities so you can relax and make the most of your getaway.

### The Presidential Suites 

Our newly renovated presidential suites in Orlando provide you with a home away from home. They feature a full dining area, a one-bedroom suite with 1.5 bathrooms, a living parlor, and much more. Resolving business needs will not feel like working in a separate office that boasts an ergonomic desk and high-speed WI-FI. Entertaining guests is also a breeze with rich furnishings and plenty of space to lounge while enjoying the resort’s breathtaking views, encompassing 500 acres of stunning natural habitats, serene lakes, and the vibrant headwaters of the Everglades.
