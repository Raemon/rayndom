## M Club at Oakland Marriott City Center

M Club at Oakland Marriott City Center features complimentary breakfast, desserts, hors-d'oeuvres, Wi-Fi and so much more. It's an upscale, inviting lounge where hotel guests can work or relax during their stay in California.

## M Club at Oakland Marriott City Center

Our hotel's recently renovated M Club looks out over Oakland from the second floor. This luxury lounge provides the ideal venue to work, recharge, connect and refuel during your California stay, with the convenience of snacks and delicious food options. It's a place to work that inspires you.

M Club guests enjoy the following services:

  * Daily choice of fresh breakfast items. Cold and hot breakfast choices available.
  * Hot and cold hors d'oeuvres offered Monday through Thursday of each week.
  * Snacks and beverages available throughout the day, 7 days a week.

  * Express check-out

ELITE STATUS

## Treat Yourself to M Club Access

There are three ways to receive access to the M Club:

  * Marriott Bonvoy Platinum Elite and above members enjoy complimentary access, plus (1) one guest, as part of their elite status. Additional guest access may be purchased for $25.
  * Upgraded access may be purchased with your room reservation (at hotel check-in) for a fee that is per day, per person.
  * Meeting planners may purchase access for their VIP guests.
