**Pre-Function Space and More**

Set the scene for impressive meetings in our Oakland Convention Center hotel's over 100,000 square feet of versatile venues. 

The artfully designed, modern rooms, overlooking downtown Oakland, are designed to inspire you and keep your attendees engaged and productive. Our convenient location lets your meeting guests explore the city's vibrant downtown district, just a short walk away.

Our hotel boasts open hallways and light, airy conference rooms to ensure optimal flow between events. Highlights of our venues include our 37 flexible meeting rooms, with capacities ranging from 6 to 7,900.

  * Versatile pre-function spaces
  * Spectacular Bayfront views in Skyline meeting room
  * Sophisticated Boardrooms for intimate meetings
  * Outdoor spaces for receptions along the Oakland skyline
