# Explore Our Premium Rooms and Suites

## Signature Accommodations

Discover our rare collection of premium rooms and suites, a harmonious fusion of contemporary design, native artistry, and the untamed beauty of the Baja Peninsula. Thoughtfully curated for the discerning traveler, these luminous spaces feature bespoke details, expansive terraces, and sweeping ocean views. From intimate escapes to spacious sanctuaries, every suite is an immersive expression of place — refined, restorative, and distinctively Solaz.

### Wellness Suite

A sanctuary for renewal and reconnection.  
Created for those who seek inner peace, mindful luxury, and serene design, this suite invites you to slow down and be present. Surrounded by subtle aromas, restorative silence, and endless ocean views, it offers an immersive experience of deep rest.  
A curated space for balance: personalized training sessions, aromatherapy rituals, elevated amenities, and wellness tools designed to revitalize body, mind, and spirit.

### Villa Solaz Four Bedrooms

Baja’s most exclusive ocean view villa.  
A private sanctuary designed for meaningful moments that endure. With four bedrooms, expansive terrace, and two family pools, this residence is ideal for extended stays, intimate gatherings, or multigenerational escapes.  
It offers absolute privacy, generous living spaces, and an ambiance that harmonizes contemporary luxury with the authenticity of the surrounding landscape, envisioned by renowned landscape designer Jerónimo Gabayet.  
Live the privilege of thoughtful details: a private chef, dedicated artisan butler, and curated experiences that celebrate the art of living Baja with intention, depth, and beauty.
