# Serene Stays

Whether celebrating a milestone anniversary or escaping on an impromptu weekend jaunt, these romantic destinations offer unforgettable experiences.

![Romantic Maldives Getaway](https://cache.marriott.com/is/image/marriotts7prod/rz-mlera-romantic-maldives-getaway-14246)

The Ritz-Carlton Maldives, Fari Islands

The Maldives may be one of the world’s most romantic locales, and this resort is no exception. Experience meaningful moments together as you enjoy dinners on one of the world’s best beaches, spa treatments with ocean views and epic adventures at sea — or simply soak in the sunset views from your overwater villa complete with an infinity pool, a sundeck and the attention of a personal butler. 

[Escape to the Maldives](https://www.marriott.com/luxury/explore-luxury-suites/ritz-carlton-maldives)

![One-Bedroom Suite Living Area](https://cache.marriott.com/is/image/marriotts7prod/loneb-living-0016)

The London EDITION

Situated near London’s iconic Soho neighborhood, this chic hotel provides ample opportunity for cozying up together. Come evening, make your way to the intimate Punch Room, a refined reinterpretation of London’s 19th-century private clubs. Concealed behind a wood-paneled wall, the bar's menu hearkens back to the communal libations enjoyed in a bygone era. 

[Escape to London](https://www.marriott.com/luxury/explore-luxury-suites/london-edition)

![Mystery Villa - Private Pool](https://cache.marriott.com/is/image/marriotts7prod/jtrml-mystery-villapool-5286)

Mystique, a Luxury Collection Hotel, Santorini

Carved into Santorini’s rugged caldera cliffs, Mystique’s location alone is enough to make it a showstopper, but its Cycladic design, impeccable service and inspired dining options are what truly set it apart. Linked by a network of cobbled pathways, Mystique’s suites, villas, swimming pools and spa all boast panoramic sea views across to the volcano that shaped this storied island. 

[Escape to Santorini](https://www.marriott.com/en-us/hotels/jtrml-mystique-a-luxury-collection-hotel-santorini/overview/)

![W Lounge](https://cache.marriott.com/is/image/marriotts7prod/wh-romwv-w-lounge-99470)

W Rome

Rome, with its ancient streets and sunset-lit piazzas, is a city where romance feels as eternal as its history. Set across two 19th-century palazzos along Via Liguria and near the Trevi Fountain and Spanish Steps, W Rome embodies the city’s spirit. Come sundown, take to the sixth-floor terrace for an apertivo hour that comes complete with stunning views of the Eternal City. 

[Escape to Rome](https://www.marriott.com/en-us/hotels/romwv-w-rome/overview/)

![Signature Suite Skyline View ](https://cache.marriott.com/is/image/marriotts7prod/jw-madjw-terrace-junior-suit-25911-94970)

JW Marriott Hotel Madrid 

Moments of connection come easily at JW Marriott Hotel Madrid, where storied elegance meets modern style. Once a historic perfumery, the hotel blends warm woods, soft lighting and curated art to create a mindful retreat, just steps from the Royal Palace and Madrid’s vibrant tapas scene. 

[Escape to Madrid](https://www.marriott.com/en-us/hotels/madjw-jw-marriott-hotel-madrid/overview/)

![ritzcarlton_portland_meadowrue](https://cache.marriott.com/is/image/marriotts7prod/rz-pdxrz-portland-meadowrue-3-30583)

The Ritz-Carlton, Portland

From rustic logs bound with copper to an infinity pool inspired by a serene mountain lake, this immersive Portland hotel showcases the abundance of natural beauty that only the Pacific Northwest can provide. Located near the heart of the city center and only blocks from iconic natural features, couples can enjoy cultural city activities, creative culinary experiences and outdoor adventures.

[Escape to Portland](https://www.ritzcarlton.com/en/hotels/pdxrz-the-ritz-carlton-portland/overview/)

![Premier Suite Ginza](https://cache.marriott.com/is/image/marriotts7prod/eb-tyoeb-premier-suite-60830323-02850)

The Tokyo EDITION, Ginza

Tokyo’s mix of neon lights, chic cafés and peaceful, hidden corners make it a unique setting for falling in love all over again. For a sublimely romantic dinner, reserve a table at Sophie, the 14th-floor restaurant inside the EDITION, where the menu pays homage to Japan's abundant seasonal ingredients and rich culinary traditions through the lens of a modern brasserie. 

[Escape to Tokyo](https://www.marriott.com/en-us/hotels/tyoeb-the-tokyo-edition-ginza/overview/)

![Beachside bar and restaurant at sunset](https://cache.marriott.com/is/image/marriotts7prod/rz-gcmrz-trc-grand-cayman-39917-08972)

The Ritz-Carlton, Grand Cayman

Discover new passions together at this Caribbean resort, where watersports, golf and tennis are all at your fingertips. The thoughtful design of this Grand Cayman hotel truly enlivens the senses with its elegant design, reimagined culinary experiences and the unmatched beauty of Seven Mile Beach. Come sundown, enjoy dinner at Blue by Eric Ripert, the Caribbean’s only Forbes Five-Star restaurant.

[Escape to Grand Cayman](https://www.marriott.com/luxury/explore-luxury-suites/ritz-carlton-grand-cayman)

![Suite Balcony - Lifestyle](https://cache.marriott.com/is/image/marriotts7prod/wh-dpswh-suite-balcony---lifestyle-02052)

W Bali - Seminyak

Adventurous couples take note: A world of excitement awaits at this Bali resort. Go paragliding over Kuta Beach, tap into your creative side in the private recording studio, and dance the night away at Woobar, where the world’s best DJs create an electrifying after-hours experience. Wind down in the spa — massages are available from midnight to 6 am — before retreating to your beachside villa.

[Escape to Bali](https://www.marriott.com/en-us/hotels/dpswh-w-bali-seminyak/overview/)

![Couple walking across stepping stone](https://cache.marriott.com/is/image/marriotts7prod/xr-cunxr-kanailifestyle78-38654)

The St. Regis Kanai Resort

Standing tall above the shores of the Yucatan Peninsula, this sanctuary merges striking architecture with a lush natural landscape. Inspired by the Pleiades constellation, the resort's avant-garde exterior floats above the canopy of the mangrove trees below. From curated culinary journeys to the tranquil St. Regis Spa, indulge in an array of bespoke experiences guaranteed to ignite a spark.

[Escape to Kanai](https://www.marriott.com/en-us/hotels/cunxr-the-st-regis-kanai-resort-riviera-maya/overview/)
