# Guanacaste resort restaurants

## Dining

From detox-worthy grain bowls to trending mixology, W Costa Rica - Reserva Conchal is all about fresh ingredients and bold flavors. Start your day in Guanacaste with homemade pancakes and fresh juice at Cocina de Mercado. Soak up the sun on Playa Conchal, then eat something light poolside on the WET® Deck or beachfront at Zona Azul. Gear up for an evening out with modern cocktails and small bites in the Living Room. Or make a night of it at Latitud 10° Norte, serving up modern, elevated international dishes. Each of our resort restaurants in Guanacaste reinterprets classics with a modern edge, remixing Latin with international inspiration.

## The Pulse of the Escape

A chill atmosphere made for mingling, the Living Room is where it all happens. Kick back with a fresh fruit concoction to take in the sunset - no filter needed. Snack on bocas (the Costa Rican answer to tapas) before dinner, or keep your energy up after a night out with the small bites. Not a typical swanky hour, join our mixology on a trip to exclusive cocktail destinations with changing daily specials. Lounge seating, communal tables, and a buzzing bar scene let you find your own slice of paradise.
