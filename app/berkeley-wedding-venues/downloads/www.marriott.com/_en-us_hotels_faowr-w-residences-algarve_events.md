###  Garden Fountain 

Adjacent to Market Kitchen terrace and extending for 700 square meters, the sparkling Garden Fountain sets the stage for funky cocktail receptions and moonlight events. The W Lounge Garden is a fantastic choice for interactive events with live stations and music entertainment. An ideal venue for larger wedding parties.
