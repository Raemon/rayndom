# Marin Airporter

415-461-4222  
[www.marinairporter.com](https://marinairporter.com/)  
**_Locally Owned & Community-Minded Since 1975!_**

One of the the Bay Area’s premier transportation companies, founded in 1975, Marin Airporter is based in San Rafael, California. We are an employee-owned company providing scheduled bus service between [seven Marin County bus stops](http://marinairporter.com/stops-schedules/?fbclid=IwAR0xDrsy7HMZ1PXYmFcy4oD6RD-G6mQ89JGQ4kMqjH4YmioTLugVxsx56GQ) and [San Francisco International Airport](http://marinairporter.com/stops-schedules/sf-airport-sfo/?fbclid=IwAR0ymzWB-d53eiGWkX0qX979PAsJe9U5bCUqsrKg7-UkBUpcawrXTk822fA) (SFO), as well as [charter buses](https://l.facebook.com/l.php?u=http%3A%2F%2Fmarinairporter.com%2Fcharters%2F%3Ffbclid%3DIwAR0Un4HOHlGaDGaX9ipVP6IYvdZ-D19NVAPLfPLkSZBT1x_bf-j_k1vqR9I&h=AT21oT1xGnkbE6PYwXqefaYMHaUew_8z7d3DxzgFhyEMzhpdPj_cdXMZEka9r--q3dm_abg7A11z6GHK8pMhPsvs1wVydXlYL7hiog4RrwSFBPbv45zPC94cdN-RVOyITE2jMjratr1yY4Bp6cB0VQ) and [local transit](https://l.facebook.com/l.php?u=http%3A%2F%2Fmarinairporter.com%2Fmarin-community-airporter%2F%3Ffbclid%3DIwAR1oPNS8uHIA4D2quUwzRFco9vwU3OQ2Yn_CJW_HDp76xa6tMq_8H2gDaF4&h=AT27pvjkkLkFB7YXQPv7mVxSdQ-6BSTLBhWzRNMdFS8PXscIsQjrAYWgHHvj-1zXR468YCYsqUF76Dbz2N4TDSin0cATj6PH4AB_Pr8xBpwh05nPqsv65DqDocNx0fJ58jpn4qR9HxJVXh7Q0c2MJw).

All of our vehicles are meticulously maintained by our full-time mechanics using state-of-the-art testing and safety inspections. Our charter drivers and vehicles are SPAB-certified for carrying school groups to field trips and sporting events.

When you’re organizing a big group outing—whether it’s a wedding weekend, a company off-site, or a class field trip—transportation tends to be one of those details that sneaks up on you. Here’s the good news: Marin Airporter makes it easy.

We’ve been helping Bay Area groups get where they need to go for nearly 50 years. Our****[charter bus service](https://marinairporter.com/charters) is built on the same reliable, local, no-surprises approach we’re known for—with a fleet of full-size coaches, experienced drivers, and competitive pricing that makes sense for your budget.
