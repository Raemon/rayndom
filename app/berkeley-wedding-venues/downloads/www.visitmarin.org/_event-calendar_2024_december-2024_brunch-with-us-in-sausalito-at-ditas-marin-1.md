# Brunch with us in Sausalito at Ditas Marin

**Date** : Saturday, December 7, 2024

**Time** : Brunch Saturday and Sunday 11:00am - 2:00pm

**Phone** : 628-261-9267

[**Website**](https://ditasmarin.com/)

Ditas Marin is Sausalito’s newest culinary gem, nestled on the waterfront, offering French windows and an option to dine out on their deck overlooking the water, with stunning views of San Francisco and the bay. The restaurant boasts an extensive wine list, a full bar, and an approachable food menu crafted by award-winning chefs, designed to be comfortable, beautiful, and creative experiences.   
  
Favorite brunch dishes among locals include Quiche Lorraine, Fried Chicken & Biscuits and House-made breakfast sausage. [Menu Here](/site/assets/files/30062/brunch_menu.pdf)

Also open for dinner Wednesday - Sunday from 5:00pm - 9:00pm.

**Additional Location or Other Information**

Located above The Trident at 562 Bridgeway, Sausalito, CA
