# Nicasio Pumpkin Patch Thru October 31

**Date** : Wednesday, October 23, 2024

**Time** : Open 10:00am - 4pm Monday - Friday and 10:00am - 6:00pm Saturday - Sunday

[**Website**](https://nicasiovalleypumpkinpatch.net/)

####  Nicasio Valley Pumpkin Patch is a Traditional Pumpkin Patch Located in the Heart of Beautiful Nicasio Valley in Marin County California.

Every Day Activities includes Farm Animals, Hay Mazes, Hay Pit Picnic Areas and Educational Boards to Learn About Agriculture

No Admission Fees

**Weekends 10/1 - 10/31 Festivities:**

Big Jim's BBQ 

Hay Rides & Face Painting

Explore and Learn About Antique Tractors and Farm Equipment

Silver Shave Ice

Petting Zoo

Children's Music Sun 11am - noon

**Additional Location or Other Information**

5300 Nicasio Valley Road, Nicasio CA 94946
