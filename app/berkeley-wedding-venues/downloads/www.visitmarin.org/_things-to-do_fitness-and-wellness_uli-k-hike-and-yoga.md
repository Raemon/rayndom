# Uli K Hike and Yoga

Uli K Hike and Yoga  
(415)828-2218  
[www.ulikhikeandyoga.com](https://www.ulikhikeandyoga.com/)

Uli K Hike and Yoga offers customized and curated Hikes with or without Yoga of varying distances and difficulties in beautiful Marin County. 

Offerings include customized private, group, and corporate guided half and full-day hiking retreats, as well as yoga classes. Ideas include: Birthday Hikes with or without Yoga, Children’s Nature and Yoga, Girlfriends Day Out, Wedding Party, Anniversaries, Family or Friendship Reunions. Uli also offers one-on-one and semi-private outings and yoga sessions. [Upcoming Day Retreats Here](https://www.ulikhikeandyoga.com/offerings)

Uli’s training include running, hiking, and trekking extensively in Marin and beyond for over 17 years and spending time outdoors has become her life passions. She has trekked in Peru, Patagonia, Nepal, the High Sierra and climbed Mount Shasta. Over the past 3 years, Uli has also led several hiking trips to Yosemite, Lassen, Pinnacles National Parks, Arches and Canyonlands National Parks. Yogaworks 200 Hour Teacher Training kickstarted Uli’s Yoga education and she became a certified and Yoga Alliance registered Teacher in 2020. In 2022 Uli completed her 50- Hour Insight Yoga Teacher Training with highly acclaimed Sarah Powers, co-founder of the Insight Yoga Institute.

Combining these two passions, Uli created this business of providing hiking outings, daily retreats, and yoga practice that will help you will feel refreshed and grounded, with peace and stillness that will carry you on any life journey you may be facing.
