# Barolo Wine Dinner at Piazza D'Angelo

**Date** : Thursday, October 2, 2025

**Time** : 6:30pm

[**Website**](https://www.opentable.com/booking/experiences-availability?rid=2339&restref=2339&experienceId=547141&utm_source=external&utm_medium=referral&utm_campaign=shared&fbclid=IwY2xjawNADAhleHRuA2FlbQIxMABicmlkETFQNWFFOW9sSmtFMDQ1RXEzAR5ouhb12_kCkkTGd8zl301NN2nAz86pd22uSWHL_6x1zr29IxkDPVq1jt3kJg_aem_9RaZ90Ymbk1oY8TCXKtm6Q)

We’re delighted to invite you to a special evening celebrating the wines of Costa di Bussia, a historic Barolo estate in Monforte d’Alba, founded in 1874.

Renowned for its vineyards on the famed Bussia Hill, Costa di Bussia produces wines that beautifully express the power, elegance, and depth of Nebbiolo — a true reflection of Piemonte’s timeless character.

We’re honored to welcome Massimo Bracco from the winery, who will guide us through the estate’s story, its traditions, and a curated tasting of exceptional Barolos. To complement the wines, our chefs have created a Piemonte-inspired menu featuring seasonal ingredients and classic flavors — thoughtfully paired to highlight the structure and nuance of each wine.

We look forward to sharing an unforgettable evening of food, wine, and storytelling with you.

$90.00 per person (tax and gratuity not included). Reservations required in the above website link.
