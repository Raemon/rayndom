# Cheese & Greet Anniversary Tastings at Point Reyes Farmstead Cheese Company

**Date** : Friday, July 18, 2025

**Time** : 2:00pm

[**Website**](https://pointreyescheese.com/pages/tastings-at-the-farm)

Join us in celebrating 25 years of cheese with a festive twist on our classic tastings. Our Anniversary Cheese Tastings bring together the best of our past and present—from a bountiful grazing table piled high with our award-winning cheeses to pours from some of our favorite local beverage partners. Mingle with fellow cheese lovers, chat with the owners, and enjoy a few surprise touches we’ve added just for the occasion.

It’s a relaxed, social afternoon set against the scenic backdrop of Tomales Bay—perfect for toasting to a quarter-century of craftsmanship. Come celebrate with us!

Tickets are $55.00 per person, plus fees and includes a bountiful cheese grazing table, owner interaction, farm and family history, special local beverage partners and more. All tickets can be purchased in the above website link. 

**Additional Location or Other Information**

14700 CA Hwy. 1 Point Reyes Station, CA US 94965
