# National Charter Bus San Francisco

[](/site/assets/files/13573/national_charter_bus_1_002.jpg)




415-226-7853  
[www.nationalbuscharter.com](https://www.nationalbuscharter.com/san-francisco-charter-bus)

Streamline your travels around Marin County with National Charter Bus. Browse through a fleet of [bus rental options](https://www.nationalbuscharter.com/bus-types), including 18-passenger minibuses, 25-passenger motorcoaches and 56-passenger charter bus rentals. Whether you’re in Marin because of a [wedding](https://www.nationalbuscharter.com/san-francisco-charter-bus/san-francisco-wedding-trip-bus-rental), a [corporate event](https://www.nationalbuscharter.com/san-francisco-charter-bus/san-francisco-corporate-trip-bus-rental), a [sports gathering](https://www.nationalbuscharter.com/san-francisco-charter-bus/san-francisco-sports-bus-rental) or even a [school field trip](https://www.nationalbuscharter.com/san-francisco-charter-bus/san-francisco-school-field-trip-bus-rental), National Charter Bus will organize your round-trip for you. Contact us at 415-226-7853 for a free bus [quote](https://www.nationalbuscharter.com/charter-bus-rental-prices/)!
