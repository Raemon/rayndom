# Rancho Nicasio Restaurant & Bar

[](/site/assets/files/11072/logo.jpg)

1 Old Rancheria Road  
Nicasio, CA 94946  
415-662-2219  
[www.ranchonicasio.com](http://www.ranchonicasio.com/)

* * *




Located on the town square in Nicasio, California, this historic roadhouse was established in 1941 and situated in the geographical center of Marin County. Nicasio has retained its rural atmosphere and tranquil beauty.

Rustic ambiance serving up the best California cuisine. Local favorites include oysters, BBQ Ribs, sandwiches and burgers, and rib-eye steaks; prime rib on Saturday and Sunday.

Our five-acre site offers a beautifully manicured lawn surrounded by the rolling hills of Nicasio Valley perfect for company picnics, private parties, corporate events, weddings and other special events. [Please see our event venue page here.](https://ranchonicasio.com/weddings/)

**Hours of Operation:** Open 7 days a week with an All-Day Menu:

Monday – Friday Lunch and Dinner: 11:30 am – 9:00pm

Saturday and Sunday Brunch is 11:00am – 3:00pm and Dinner is from 5:00pm – 9:00pm
