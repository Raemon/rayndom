# Guided Tour and Cheese Tasting on Fridays and Saturdays at Nicasio Valley Cheese

**Date** : Saturday, July 19, 2025

**Time** : 10:00am - 11:30am

[**Website**](https://nicasiocheese.com/products/cheese-tasting)

Come on out and be welcomed by one of our founding family members, Rick Lafranchi, who will give you a rundown of our family ranch and creamery history. Tours are available every Friday and Saturday, April - September. The cost is $40.00 per person and tickets must be purchased in the above website link. Ticket includes a tour, a 6 cheese tasting and a non-alcoholic drink.

RANCH OVERVIEW: Then, follow Rick up to the family house for an overview and explanation of the family dairy operation.

CHEESE TASTING: Next Melisa Williams, our store manager, will lead a tasting of 6 of our award winning cheeses.

CHEESEMAKING PROCESS: Finally, Jeremy Catrambone, our head cheesemaker will share our cheesemaking process and answer any questions you may have.

Personnel may be substituted depending on availability.

**Additional Location or Other Information**

5300 Nicasio Valley Road, Nicasio, California 94946
