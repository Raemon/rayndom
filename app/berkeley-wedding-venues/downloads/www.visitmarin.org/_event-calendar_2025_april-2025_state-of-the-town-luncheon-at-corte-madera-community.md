# State of the Town Luncheon at Corte Madera Community

**Date** : Tuesday, April 22, 2025

**Time** : Lunch is at Noon and Speakers Start at 12:30pm

[**Website**](https://cortemadera.org/events/state-of-the-town-luncheon/)

Come out and learn about what is happening in the charming town of Corte Madera. This is an annual event hosted by Corte Madera Chamber of Commerce with lunch provided by Pig in a Pickle BBQ. Reservations are required for lunch; RSVP to [julie@cortemadera.org](mailto:julie@cortemader.org)

$25 for members and $30 for non-members

RSVPs are not necessary for the speaker portion of the event which starts at 12:30pm and is free to all members of the Corte Madera community. 

**Additional Location or Other Information**

498 Tamalpais Drive, Corte Madera, CA 94925
