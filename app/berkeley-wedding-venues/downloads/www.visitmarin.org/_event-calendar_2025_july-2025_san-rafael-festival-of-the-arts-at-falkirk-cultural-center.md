# San Rafael Festival of the Arts at Falkirk Cultural Center

**Date** : Saturday, July 26, 2025

**Time** : 11:00am - 6:00pm

[**Website**](https://sanrafaelfestivalofthearts.com/)

Join us on the lawn at the enchanting 11-acre Falkirk Cultural Center, a historic 1888 Queen Anne Victorian, for the San Rafael Festival of the Arts. Immerse yourself in live music, sample regional wines and local craft beers, and explore fine arts and crafts by talented artisans.

Enjoy a variety of gourmet food trucks while admiring the street painting artists as they create their stunning designs throughout the day.

Let the best local musicians elevate your experience amidst the beautiful backdrop of the Falkirk grounds and views of Mt. Tamalpais. Connect with fellow art lovers and create lasting memories at this vibrant celebration of creativity.

Admission to the Festival is free. If you would like to participate in the wine and beer tastings, early bird tickets are available for $30 from May 16th to July 25th, and $35 on the event day. Each ticket includes a wristband and a stemless tasting glass. Food purchases are separate. 

[PURCHASE EARLY BIRD TASTING TICKETS HERE.](http://sanrafaelfestivalofthearts.com/tickets/)
