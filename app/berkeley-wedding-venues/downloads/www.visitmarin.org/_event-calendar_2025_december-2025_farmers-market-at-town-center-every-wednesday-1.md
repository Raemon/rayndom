# Farmers Market at Town Center (Every Wednesday)

**Date** : Wednesday, December 10, 2025

**Time** : 12:00pm-5:00pm

**Phone** : 415-924-2961

[**Website**](https://www.shoptowncenter.com/#section-farmers)

Join us at Town Center every Wednesday for the Farmers Market! You'll find organic and specialty farmers, food purveyors, bakers and more showcasing their bounty along the Mediterranean style courtyard. Come rain or shine!

**Additional Location or Other Information**

Town Center is located just off Highway 101 at 100 Corte Madera Town Center, Corte Madera, CA 94925.
