# Nature Hike at China Camp State Park

**Date** : Saturday, July 26, 2025

**Time** : 9:00am - 11:00am

[**Website**](https://friendsofchinacamp.org/event/nature-hike-summery-walk-in-the-woods/)

As the lushness of spring slides into the golden days of mid-summer, changes happen all over the park. Join our leisurely walk into one of China Camp’s key habitats: our beautiful oak and bay woodlands. We’ll be on the lookout for a host of summer’s remarkable seeds—and the critters who depend on them—as well as plants that produce important fall berries. This hands-on wander along shady paths will open your mind to the complex and remarkable rhythm of the seasons at China Camp—a wonderful way to change the way you see the park no matter when or how you enjoy this special place.

Space is limited; registration is required. Cancellations notified via email.

**Location:** [north end of Knight Drive, San Rafael](https://maps.app.goo.gl/tyFDTEovfc4umhum7)  
**Difficulty:** moderate 2 mile walk  
**Cost:** free to FOCC members; $5 [donation](https://friendsofchinacamp.org/donate/) appreciated for non-members  
**Ages:** 8 and older; under 18 must be accompanied by a parent or responsible adult
