# Pixels Arcade & Taproom

  * [](/site/assets/files/35800/people_playing_games.900x600.png)
  *   * [](/site/assets/files/35800/pixels_party.900x600.jpg)



1137 Fourth Street  
San Rafael, CA 94901  
833-PIXELS1 (833-749-3571) or (415) 785-3125  
[www.pixelsarcadetaproom.com](http://www.pixelsarcadetaproom.com/)

* * *




Visit Marin County’s Retro Arcade Bar for All Ages Until 8:00om and 21+ Until Closing! Over 30 retro arcade games, pinball machines and a console gaming lounge are all served up in an arcade for adults with comfy seating, great music and visuals in a retro-modern atmosphere.

As of August 2025, $10 per hour / $15 for a day pass Monday – Thursday and $12 per hour / $17 for a day pass Friday – Sunday.

All games are set to free play (a few premium pinball machines take $1 more per play).

Featuring a self-pour beverage bar with a choice of local craft beers and cider. Other beverages including wine, non-alcoholic beers and soft drinks are available. In partnership with neighboring restaurants we offer wood-fired pizzas, burgers, buffalo wings, fries, nachos and salads.

Check out our [events calendar](https://pixelsarcadetaproom.com/events) for weekly events. And speaking of events, Pixels Arcade Taproom is the perfect place for a party or other special event. [Book a Party Now!](https://pixelsarcadetaproom.com/book-a-party)

Hours of Operation: Monday - Thursday: 3:00pm – 10:00pm  
Friday: 1:00pm – 11:00pm  
Saturday: 11:00pm – 11:00pm  
Sunday: 11:00pm – 10:00pm

Come to Pixels Arcade, where leisure rules!
