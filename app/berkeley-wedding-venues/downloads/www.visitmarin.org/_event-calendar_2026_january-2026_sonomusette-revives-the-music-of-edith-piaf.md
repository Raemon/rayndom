# SonoMusette Revives the Music of Edith Piaf

**Date** : Saturday, January 10, 2026

**Time** : 7pm

[**Website**](https://tickets.marincenter.org/eventperformances.asp?evt=458)

Experience the Enchantment of Paris: SonoMusette Revives the Music of Edith Piaf  
  
Close your eyes and be instantly transported to the heart of mid-20th century Paris. SonoMusette, led by the captivating, native Parisian chanteuse Mimi Pirard. SonoMusette presents an evening that channels the very essence of the city that lives vibrantly in our memories and imaginations.  
  
This concert is more than a performance; it's an intimate journey. Mimi Pirard delivers powerful, emotional interpretations of Edith Piaf’s classic songs, injecting them with a freshness and excitement that remains perfectly faithful to the spirit and tradition of the original "Little Sparrow."  
  
The formidable musicians of SonoMusette expertly weave a rich, authentic musical tapestry that provides the perfect backdrop for these enchanting songs and melodies.  
  
Mimi Pirard ~ vocalist  
Robert Lunceford ~musette accordion  
Jan Martinelli ~ piano and bass  
Isaac Vandeveer ~ guitar and bass  
Kendrick Freeman ~ drum set  
  
Marin Center Showcase Theatre  
20 Ave of the Flags  
San Rafael, CA 94903

**$50 VIP $40 General Admission (Marin Center Fees included)**
