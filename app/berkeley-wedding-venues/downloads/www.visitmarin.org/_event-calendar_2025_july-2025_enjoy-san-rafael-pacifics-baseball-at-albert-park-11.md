# Enjoy San Rafael Pacifics Baseball at Albert Park

**Date** : Saturday, July 26, 2025

**Time** : Gates Open at 5:00pm and Game Starts at 6:00pm

[**Website**](https://www.pacificsbaseball.com/pacifics.asp?page=44)

The sweet sounds of summer baseball are echoing throughout San Rafael's Albert Park. From the crack of the bat, to the pop of the glove to the smell of the grass, the San Rafael Pacifics invite you to be a part of America's Pastime.

Come see professional baseball at its finest. Playing in the Pecos League of Professional Baseball Clubs — a professional independent league — the Pacifics host games at historic Albert Park from May through July.

Typical concession food is also available such as hamburgers, hot dogs, sausages, potato chips, popcorn, peanuts, candy, sodas, water, beer / wine; cash, credit cards, and Apple Pay are acceptable forms of payment.

General Admission Tickets are $10.00 each and can be purchased in the the above website link.

**Additional Location or Other Information**

151 Andersen Drive, San Rafael, CA 94901
