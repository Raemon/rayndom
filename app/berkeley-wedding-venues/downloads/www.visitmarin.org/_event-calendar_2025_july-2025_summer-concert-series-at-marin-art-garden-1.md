# Summer Concert Series at Marin Art & Garden

**Date** : Thursday, July 17, 2025

**Time** : Gates Open at 4:00pm and Music is from 5:00pm - 6:00pm

[**Website**](https://maringarden.org/summerconcerts/)

Join us in the gardens for an expanded Summer Concert Series on Thursday evenings beginning July 10 through August 28 (excluding July 4). Hear all your favorites from rock, jazz, bluegrass, soul, French chanson, rockabilly, Brazilian, and more. There’s something for everyone!  
  
Pre-order gourmet picnic offerings or bring your own. Premium wine, beer and gelato for sale. Enjoy a KidZone hosted by [The Garden School](https://maringarden.org/garden-school/), and [Pixie Park](https://www.pixiepark.org/) will be open to all! Dine al fresco, dance and sing on beautiful summer evenings. Concerts on the Lawn and in the Redwood Amphitheater. 

Space and Admission are limited: $20 for adults pre-sale / $25 for adults at the gate; kids under 17 are free. Parking is $10 per car in advance / $15 at the gate. Private Tables on the lawn are also available for groups of 4 or 8 that include a premier location, concert tickets and parking passes. Please visit the above website for more event details, private table costs and to book your tickets now!
