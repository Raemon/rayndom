# Celebrate Dia de los Muertos on Novato Civic Green

**Date** : Saturday, November 2, 2024

**Time** : 4:00pm - 6:00pm

[**Website**](https://www.novato.org/Home/Components/Calendar/Event/23404/2527)

Join us for the City of Novato’s inaugural Día de los Muertos celebration! Come honor and celebrate loved ones who are no longer with us by contributing to our community ofrenda, festive crafts, delicious food, and vibrant music. All ages are welcome! Mark your calendars and invite your friends and family for an unforgettable cultural celebration. Activities include photo frame decorating and magnet making.⁠ Enjoy Tommy’s Salsa and live music as we celebrate together!⁠  
⁠  
Help us build a community ofrenda (altar) by bringing 3″x3″ photographs or mementos to celebrate the lives we remember. We will provide you with electronic candles, silk marigolds, and cups of water for the ofrenda. Please label items with your name and phone number. Uncollected mementos will be held at the Margaret Todd Senior Center (1560 Hill Road) through November 15 at 4 PM.

This is a free event.

**Additional Location or Other Information**

901 Sherman Avenue in Novato, California
