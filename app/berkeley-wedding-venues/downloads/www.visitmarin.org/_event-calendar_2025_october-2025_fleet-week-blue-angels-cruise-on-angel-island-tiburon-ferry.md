# Fleet Week Blue Angels Cruise on Angel Island Tiburon Ferry

**Date** : Friday, October 10, 2025

**Time** : Boarding at 2:00pm and 2:30pm Departure

**Phone** : 415-435-2131

[**Website**](https://angelislandferry.com/Fourthportfolio/fleet-week)

This year we are using the Angel Island Vessel for maximum viewing capacity!

Captain Maggie & the Angel Island Tiburon Ferry crew invite you to get ready for the experience of a lifetime on-board our Annual Blue Angels Air Show Cruises on San Francisco Bay during Fleet Week SF 2025. Boarding will begin at 2:00 pm for a 2:30 pm departure. Reservations are required as seats are limited. Online tickets automatically give you a reservation. Book your tickets in the above website link now!
