# Harvest Moon Sail on Freda B

**Date** : Sunday, October 5, 2025

**Time** : 4:15pm - 7:00pm

[**Website**](https://www.eventbrite.com/e/harvest-moon-sail-on-san-francisco-bay-october-2025-tickets-1245603336109?aff=odcleoeventsincollection)

Underway for a leisurely evening sail, take in the views of the San Francisco skyline, Golden Gate Bridge, Bay Bridge, Marin Headlands, Alcatraz and Angel Islands, all while experiencing the magic of the sunset and moonrise. Cost is $129 per person, plus fees and tickets must be purchased in the above website link.

Harvest Moon - October

October's full moon occurs right around the time when major crops are harvested: corn, pumpkins, squash, beans, and other dietary staples. Farmers used the light from this full moon to their advantage, harvesting late into the night!

Schedule:

Boarding: 4:15 pm

Departure: 4:30 pm

Moonrise: 6:01 pm

Sunset: 6:46 pm

Return: 7:00 pm

Schooner Freda B is a sea kind 80' gaff rigged coastal schooner, making her one of the San Francisco Bay's most comfortable and traditional vessels to sail. Experience the romance of yesteryear and with a local, licensed Captain and Professional Crew.
