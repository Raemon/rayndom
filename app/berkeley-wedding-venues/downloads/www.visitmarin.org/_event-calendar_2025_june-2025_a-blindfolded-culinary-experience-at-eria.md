# A Blindfolded Culinary Experience at Eria

**Date** : Friday, June 27, 2025

**Time** : 7:00pm - 10:00pm

[**Website**](https://eria.co/shop/events/a-blindfolded-culinary-experience-at-eria/)

Step beyond the ordinary. Eria invites you to an unforgettable evening designed to awaken your senses and challenge your perception.

Join us for a one-of-a-kind five-course blindfolded dinner, where taste, aroma, texture, and sound take center stage—without the distraction of sight. Each course has been thoughtfully crafted to surprise and delight, and is paired with a premium Brown-Forman spirit to elevate your experience.

What to Expect:

  * A multi-sensory culinary journey led entirely by your remaining senses
  * A carefully curated five-course menu, revealed only through taste
  * Expert pairings featuring Brown-Forman’s finest spirits
  * A warm, intimate setting with just 30 guests for the evening
  * An atmosphere of mystery, discovery, and transformation



Tickets are $140.00 per person and can be purchased in the above website link. Seating is limited to 30 seats. 

**Additional Location or Other Information**

562 Bridgeway, Sausalito, CA 94965
