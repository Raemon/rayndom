# Valentine's Day Cupcake Decorating Workshop at Marin Art & Garden Center

**Date** : Friday, February 14, 2025

**Time** : 3:00pm - 5:00pm

[**Website**](https://maringarden.org/events/valentines-day-cupcakes/?occurrence=2025-02-14&utm_source=Neon+Audience&utm_campaign=3159616d05-EMAIL_CAMPAIGN_2025_01_02_08_51_COPY_02&utm_medium=email&utm_term=0_-1d1c6a386e-1411285463)

Join us for a fun-filled cupcake decorating workshop, where kids (and adults!) can create their own sweet treats. Each decorating kit includes:

  * 3 freshly baked cupcakes
  * 2 bags of buttercream frosting
  * Heart-shaped sprinkles
  * Customizable fondant toppers



The workshop will begin with a hands-on demonstration of three frosting techniques. This is the perfect after school activity or way to get creative with friends and family. All ages are welcome!

Tickets are $30 in advance, $35 for walk-ins. More details available and online tickets can be purchased in the above website link.
