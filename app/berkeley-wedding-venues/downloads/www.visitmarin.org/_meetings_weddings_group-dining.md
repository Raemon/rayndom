# Group Dining

### The Fork at Point Reyes Farmstead Cheese Company

14700 Shoreline Highway, PointReyes Station, CA 94956  
800-591-6878  
[More Details →](https://pointreyescheese.com/pages/private-events)

At The Fork, our Culinary and Educational Center, we celebrate the rich, natural flavors of our farm through handcrafted, award-winning cheeses. Nestled above Tomales Bay, our stunning venue offers the perfect setting for intimate celebrations and unforgettable culinary experiences. Whether you're planning a rehearsal dinner, pre-wedding lunch, or day-after brunch, our culinary team will help create an exceptional, flavor-forward experience your guests will remember. From intimate gatherings to larger celebrations, collaborate with us to offer your loved ones artisan cheese and farm-fresh cuisine in a setting as beautiful as your special occasion.

### Hillside Tavern at Marin Hilltop Hotel

1010 Northgate Drive, San Rafael, CA 94903  
415-479-8800  
[More Details →](https://www.marriott.com/en-us/hotels/sfost-marin-hilltop-hotel/overview/)

Your guests are gathered to commemorate you and your loved one. But, they’re also anticipating a celebration, and this means a splendid dining experience. The food and drink are a signature element of a wedding, from bridal showers and rehearsal dinners to wedding receptions and day-after brunches. We’re here to organize a group dining event that’s both mouthwatering and memorable.

### Piazza D’Angelo Ristorante

22 Miller Avenue, Mill Valley, CA 94941  
415-388-2000  
[More Details →](https://piazzadangelo.com/mill-valley-piazza-d-angelo-private-event)

Named after the restaurant's location right on the piazza of downtown Mill Valley, Piazza D'Angelo is a family run restaurant and has provided Mill valley with authentic Italian cuisine for over 40 years. ​Quality and value are at the heart of the Piazza D'Angelo experience. We focus on sourcing the freshest ingredients from the Bay Area's wealth of resources. The result is simple and expertly crafted Italian dishes that reflect our Calabrian roots and down-to-earth personality.

Piazza D'Angelo provides complete and professional event planning. We pride ourselves on making the whole process easier and more enjoyable for you and your guests. We can help you host rehearsal dinners, weddings and receptions. Our restaurant offers three private dining spaces for groups. 

### HopMonk Tavern

224 Vintage Way  
Novato, CA 94945  
415-892-6200  
[More Details →](https://www.hopmonk.com/private-events)

Our restaurant and full bar serving American food opens up into a sun-filled beergarden under vintage oak trees, which is home to our outdoor cookout concert series and other special events. We would love to work with you to make your next event exceptional. HopMonk Tavern has hosted a wide range of events, including parties, weddings and receptions, both on-site and off-site. From brunches and luncheons to rehearsal dinners, we bring every occasion to life with exceptional service.

### Trek Winery

1026 Machin Avenue, Novato, CA 94945  
415-899-9883  
[More Details →](https://trekwines.com/private-events)

Located in downtown Novato, Trek Winery has become a premier venue location for a wedding reception, rehearsal dinner and other special gatherings for up to 120 people. The room comes with a bar and an outdoor patio area with pits. For more intimate gatherings, we have a Private Tasting Room perfect for up to 22 people seated comfortably at a long table. Both rooms can be used together for one large space or separately depending on your needs. A kitchen is available for caterers to use as well.

### Mable’s Restaurant at Lodge at Marconi

18500 Highway One, Marshall, CA 94960  
415-663-9020

Make your gatherings unforgettable at Mable’s. We specialize in rehearsal dinners, wedding celebrations, and other events hosted indoors or a soiree on our patio. Treat your guests to bayside experiences. Our team is here to create a seamless and memorable experience in an exquisite setting. 

### Mountain Home Inn Restaurant

810 Panoramic Highway, Mill Valley, CA 94941  
415-381-9000  
[More Details →](https://www.mtnhomeinn.com/private-events/)

Among towering redwoods and mountain scenery, we’ve carved out a place that feels like another world – just 20 minutes from the Golden Gate Bridge.   
Your private party can be held in our cozy Mountain View Room with its vista of the surrounding Mt. Tam mountains or in our intimate downstairs Bay View Room. Our dining areas are available for Marin weddings, cocktail reception sites, and private parties. Please note, cocktail service includes only beer and wine.

Whether it’s a wedding reception, anniversary or a holiday party, we excel at creating lifetime memories. We are fully equipped to offer state-of-the-art sound, lighting and staging systems, livestreaming, in-house catering and access to world-renowned performers of all genres.

### Buckeye Roadhouse

15 Shoreline Highway, Mill Valley, CA 94941  
415-331-2600  
[More Details →](https://buckeyeroadhouse.com/private-events)

Our beautiful Manzanita Room is perfect to host your birthday party, rehearsal dinner or special business event. We can accommodate you as a semi-private banquet and you will be seated upstairs with other diners around you, or we can seat up to 20 people on one long table. 

Our beautiful Manzanita Room overlooks the main dining room with its large fireplace and has window views of the outdoors. For a private buyout of the entire upstairs dining room for up to 50 guests for a sit-down lunch or dinner, your guests will be seated at conversation-friendly round tables.

With a deep devotion to quality, Farmshop Marin is dedicated to creating an experience that fuses together fresh ingredients from local farmers and artisans and a passion for culinary excellence.

Located in the Marin County Mart, Farmshop Marin offers the perfect setting for your next event. With its warm décor, our Restaurant, Patio, and Private Dining Room areas are ideal for various functions, from intimate gatherings to business meetings and cocktail parties. We can accommodate up to 60 seated or 100 standing guests, with customizable menus and beverages to suit your needs.

Combining these two spaces allows for a dynamic space with airy windows and French art as the backdrop for your special celebration. ### Rancho Nicasio

1 Old Rancheria Road, Nicasio, CA 94946  
[More Details →](https://weddings.ranchonicasio.com/)

Tucked away among the rolling hills of Marin County sits the quaint village of Nicasio, a unique Bay Area country venue perfect for weddings, receptions and other special events with memorable views of pastures and farmland. With its picturesque setting of green lawns, a shady grove of pines and a gazebo with an outdoor bar and decks and indoor rooms with beautiful wooden ceilings and brass trimmed fixtures. 

All event spaces can be customized with food and beverage packages from their onsite restaurant.
