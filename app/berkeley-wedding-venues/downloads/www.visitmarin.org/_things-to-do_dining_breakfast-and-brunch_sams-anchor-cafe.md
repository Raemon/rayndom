# Sam's Anchor Cafe

  * [](/site/assets/files/24727/french_toast.900x600.jpg)
  * [](/site/assets/files/24727/salad.900x600.jpg)
  * [](/site/assets/files/24727/shrimp_bruschetta.900x600.jpg)
  * [](/site/assets/files/24727/bloody_marys_and_boats.900x600.jpg)



27 Main Street  
Tiburon, CA 94920  
415-435-4527  
[www.samscafe.com](http://www.samscafe.com)

* * *




Located in Marin County on San Francisco Bay in the charming town of Tiburon, Sam’s has long been famous for fresh seasonal seafood, a fun atmosphere and breathtaking views of Angel Island, Alcatraz and the San Francisco skyline. Known for the best seafood in Marin County! [Menu Here](https://samscafe.com/menu)

Join us for outdoor dining on our deck complete with heat lamps and heated benches. Indoor dining is also available. You can even sail up to our dock and we will deliver to you aboard. Enjoy Sam’s-to-Go with quick and safe pickup or delivery. We welcome guests on a first-come, first-serve basis; However, for a group of 10-20 people you can make a reservation with a food and beverage minimum. We can even set up some pre-order items to be ready for you upon arrival. [More information here.](https://samscafe.com/reservations)

We are also the perfect location for your private celebration. We are all about birthdays, anniversaries, rehearsal dinners, cocktail parties, business luncheons, group brunches, wedding receptions, and teambuilding and corporate retreats. 

**Hours of Operation:** Monday – Thursday Noon – 9:00pm  
Friday Noon - 9:30pm  
Saturday 10:00am – 9:30pm  
Sunday 10:00am - 9:00pm
