# University of Redlands - Marin Campus Walking Tour

[](/site/assets/files/15563/seminary_at_dusk_small.jpeg)

105 Seminary Road  
San Anselmo, CA 94960  
415-451-2800  
[www.redlands.edu/marin](https://www.redlands.edu/meet-redlands/regional-campus-locations/marin-campus/)  
[www.redlands.edu/marin-events](https://www.redlands.edu/marin-events/)

* * *




We invite you to take a walk on our incredible Marin campus—home to graduate programs in theology, education, and business. The campus provides access to educational, professional, cultural, and recreational opportunities on an exceptionally beautiful site. Our educational programs are the result of a 2019 merger between the University of Redlands and San Francisco Theological Seminary, which built on the two institutions’ combined 250 years of history and common commitment to students, scholarship, and service. [PDF Walking Tour Here](/site/assets/files/15563/gst-19-052_marin_walking_tour_r7_resize.pdf)

Enjoy the grounds and consider timing it around a sunset or sunrise on Geneva Terrace. You’ll soon discover why our campus is a hidden gem!
