### [Grown in Marin - UC Agriculture and Natural Resources](https://www.google.com/url?sa=t&rct=j&q=&esrc=s&source=web&cd=1&cad=rja&uact=8&ved=0CB4QFjAA&url=http%3A%2F%2Fucanr.edu%2Fsites%2FGrown_in_Marin&ei=2qMdVaXDJYOrogTAu4GICQ&usg=AFQjCNEcmNPCcrefURZGqFx_l8HkUI5hTw&sig2=ymtI216uhz4-xo_M7YMR_w&bvm=bv.89947451,d.cGU)

 _Grown in Marin is a comprehensive resource for Marin agriculture serving farmers, educators, and the public. This website hosts a complete listing of Marin producers and where to find their goods, current articles and press relating to sustainable agriculture, historical profiles of Marin County agriculture, archives of "Grown in Marin" newsletters, workshop listings, and a variety of other useful resources._
