# "A Celebration of Contrasts", a Juried Show Featuring Bay Area Artists at Falkirk Cultural Center

**Date** : Tuesday, February 3, 2026

**Time** : Tuesday - Friday, 1:00pm – 4:30pm & Saturday, 10:00am – 2:00pm

**Phone** : (415) 485-3328

[**Website**](https://www.cityofsanrafael.org/falkirk-art-galleries/)

This juried exhibition showcases the work of 83 local Bay Area artists, highlighting a wide range of artistic approaches and perspectives. Falkirk’s three galleries will be filled with paintings, sculptures, ceramics, multi-media works, and more, offering visitors the opportunity to experience the diversity of contemporary art being created throughout the region.

The exhibition will be featured Tuesday through Saturday, from January 9 to February 20, 2026.

Free admission and parking.

**Additional Location or Other Information**

1400 Fifth Avenue in San Rafael, CA 94901
