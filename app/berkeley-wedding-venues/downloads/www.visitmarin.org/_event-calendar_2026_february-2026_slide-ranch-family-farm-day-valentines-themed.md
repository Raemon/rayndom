# Slide Ranch Family Farm Day: Valentine's Themed

**Date** : Saturday, February 14, 2026

**Time** : 9:30am - 1:00pm

[**Website**](https://secure.slideranch.org/np/clients/slideranch/event.jsp?event=1670)

Come SHARE THE LOVE with a heartwarming day of nature, crafting, and farmyard fun for the whole family! Activities may include: Bake Valentine's cookies, themed wool crafts (felted hearts or friendship bracelets), salve making, and more. All materials provided by Slide Ranch. Extend your visit by adding a night of [camping](https://www.hipcamp.com/en-US/land/california-slide-ranch-mxvhkp6n?adults=1&children=0&srid=d549fc5a-4616-4f85-91ea-d81236ae7e94) under the stars! **Appropriate for all ages.**

**[REGISTER HERE!](https://secure.slideranch.org/np/clients/slideranch/eventRegistration.jsp?event=1670)**

**COST:** Attendees 3+ $45 per person, Children up to 36 mos FREE

**Additional Location or Other Information**

2025 Shoreline Highway Muir Beach, CA 94965
