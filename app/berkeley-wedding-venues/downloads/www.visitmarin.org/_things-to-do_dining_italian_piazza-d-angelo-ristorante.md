# Piazza D’Angelo Ristorante

  * [](/site/assets/files/7653/food_variety.900x600.jpg)
  * [](/site/assets/files/7653/burrata_and_beet_salad.900x600.jpg)
  * [](/site/assets/files/7653/pasta.900x600.jpg)
  * [](/site/assets/files/7653/pizza.900x600.jpg)



22 Miller Avenue  
Mill Valley, CA 94941  
415-388-2000  
[www.piazzadangelo.com](http://www.piazzadangelo.com)

* * *




Named after the restaurant's location right on the piazza of downtown Mill Valley, Piazza D'Angelo is a family run restaurant and has provided Mill valley with authentic Italian cuisine for over 40 years.

​Enjoy a variety of fresh house made pastas, wood-fired pizzas, antipasti, as well as fresh fish, prime meats and our signature tiramisu! 

Quality and value are at the heart of the Piazza D'Angelo experience. We focus on sourcing the freshest ingredients from the Bay Area's wealth of resources. The result is simple and expertly crafted Italian dishes that reflect our Calabrian roots and down-to-earth personality.

Piazza D'Angelo also provides complete and professional event planning. We pride ourselves on making the whole process easier and more enjoyable for you and your guests. Planning on hosting a rehearsal dinner, cocktail party or company holiday event? Piazza D'Angelo offers three private dining spaces for groups. For more information, contact us at [events@piazzadangelo.com](mailto:catering@pizzadangelo.com).

**Hours of Operation:** Open Daily 11:30am - 9:00pm
