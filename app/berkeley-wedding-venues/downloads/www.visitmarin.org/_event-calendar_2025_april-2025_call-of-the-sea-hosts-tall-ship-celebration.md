# Call of the Sea Hosts Tall Ship Celebration

**Date** : Saturday, April 26, 2025

**Time** : 10:00am - 2:00pm

[**Website**](https://events.humanitix.com/call-of-the-sea-tall-ship-celebration-2025)

Join us for a fun filled day of family fun on the waterfront supporting a good cause! Call of the Sea is celebrating 40 years of on-the-water educational programs. This is a fundraiser for scholarships which provide life changing on the water experiences to under served youth. In addition to the many free activities, you will have a chance to support Call of the Sea with purchases of logo shirts and hats, food and beverages, sailing tickets and donations towards field trips for under-served schools.

Activities:

  * 10 am-1 pm: Tour the ship
  * 10 am-1 pm: Tie knots, make rope and learn about the art of sailing
  * 11 am-2 pm Learn about the science of sailing, waves and much more
  * 11:30 am-1:30 pm: Enjoy paella, ice cream and beverages available for purchase
  * 12 pm-2 pm Hear old time tunes by the Waterfront Pickers
  * 1:30-3:30 pm, [Join a sail on Schooner Seaward,](https://events.humanitix.com/san-francisco-bay-sail-on-schooner-seaward) advance ticket purchase required
  * 2 pm-5 pm [Join a sail on Matthew Turner](https://events.humanitix.com/san-francisco-bay-eco-sail-on-brigantine-matthew-turner?_gl=1*1rgnkuu*_ga*MTM5NDUwNzIwNC4xNjk5NTQ4MjU3*_ga_LHKW5FR9N6*MTcwNDQwMDQ0My4xNi4xLjE3MDQ0MDExNDQuMTYuMC4w), advance ticket purchase required



In addition to touring the tall ship, brigantine MATTHEW TURNER, attendees can enjoy a tour of the Bay Model, free educational activities and old time tunes by local favorites the Waterfront Pickers, a youth choir singing sea shanties in pirate costumes, and fun educational activities by the Sailing Science Center.

This is a free event, however RSVPs are recommended in the above website link.
