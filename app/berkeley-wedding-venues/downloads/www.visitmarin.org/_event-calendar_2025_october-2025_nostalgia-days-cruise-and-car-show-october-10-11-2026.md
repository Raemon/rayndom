# Nostalgia Days Cruise and Car Show, October 10 - 11, 2025

**Date** : Saturday, October 11, 2025

**Time** : October 10 at 4:00pm - October 11 4:00pm

[**Website**](https://www.nostalgiadaysnovato.com/)

Rev up your engines for the 29th Annual Nostalgia Days Cruise + Car Show, rolling into Downtown Novato on October 10 & 11, 2025. This cornerstone event, presented by the Downtown Novato Business Association, transforms seven blocks of beautifully tree-lined Grant Avenue into a classic car lover’s paradise. On Friday evening, join hundreds of local families to cheer on the police-escorted Hot Rod Cruise through Downtown. Then on Saturday, immerse yourself in the full-day extravaganza featuring hundreds of vehicles, from timeless classics and hot rods to custom trucks and motorcycles.

As classic rock ‘n’ roll and live Americana music fill the air, thousands of visitors can enjoy free admission to this spectacular showcase. The event culminates with an awards ceremony honoring the best in over 20 categories, including the coveted Best in Show and People’s Choice Awards. Whether you’re showcasing your prized vehicle or simply soaking in the nostalgia, this event promises a memorable journey back in time for all ages. Don’t miss this chance to experience the chrome, curves, and cool vibes of Novato’s premier automotive celebration!

The Car Show takes place on Grant Ave between Redwood Blvd and 7th St in beautiful Downtown Novato, California. To view the full schedule for this event click [here](https://www.nostalgiadaysnovato.com/).

Day 1: 4:00pm: Shine Up and Line Up  
5:15pm: Get Ready to Cruise

Day 2: 10:00am: Car Show Opens to the Public
