# Valentine's Charcuterie Workshop at Marin Art & Garden Center

**Date** : Saturday, February 7, 2026

**Time** : 2:00pm - 4:00pm

[**Website**](https://www.eventbrite.com/e/valentines-charcuterie-workshop-with-mill-valley-boards-registration-1980598432227?aff=oddtdtcreator)

The experts at Mill Valley Boards will provide all you need to create your very own spread of cheeses and charcuterie to share.

Get ready for Valentine’s Day with a hands-on experience that’s equal parts creative, delicious, and fun. In this special Valentine’s–themed workshop, you’ll design and assemble your own stunning charcuterie board—perfect for sharing with someone you love (or enjoying all to yourself!).

Guided by the experts, you’ll learn how to artfully arrange cheeses, cured meats, and festive accompaniments into a board that’s as beautiful as it is crave-worthy. Along the way, you’ll pick up tips on pairing flavors, creating visual balance, and adding romantic touches that make your board feel truly special.

Whether you’re planning a date night, a Galentine’s outing with friends, or simply treating yourself to something joyful, this workshop offers a relaxed, social atmosphere and plenty of inspiration. You’ll leave with a gorgeous, ready-to-enjoy charcuterie board—and the confidence to recreate the magic at home.

What's Included:

  * 10 inch compostable bamboo board
  * Cheese and charcuterie
  * Light Bites and a Beverage



Just bring your creativity (and your appetite).

Tickets are $161.90. More details available and online tickets can be purchased in the above website link.
