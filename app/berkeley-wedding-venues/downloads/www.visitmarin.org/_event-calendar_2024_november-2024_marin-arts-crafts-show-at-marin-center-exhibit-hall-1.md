# Marin Arts & Crafts Show at Marin Center Exhibit Hall

**Date** : Saturday, November 2, 2024

**Time** : 10:00am - 6:00pm

[**Website**](https://www.artsandcraftsshow.com/)

Marin Arts & Crafts Show presents the primary components of artisan handcraft. Celebrating the handcraft of design, it brings together the traditional techniques, natural colors and sustainable materials that give design its remarkable strength. Exhibitors are carefully-curated and chosen for their handcraft skills and rejection of mass-produced products. The products include textiles, stained glass, furniture, ceramics, metalwork, jewelry, recovered architectural objects, repurposed wood and iron, clothing, artifacts, estate finds, photography, paintings and sculpture.

Over 200 artisan exhibits at the Marin Center Exhibit Hall and inspired by the Arts & Crafts movement, the show brings focus to handcraft in all forms. Browse the vibrant showcasing of handcrafted wares, fine art, jewelry, ceramics, and specialty foods. Enjoy complimentary wine tastings, take part in painting workshops, and so much more at the Marin Arts & Crafts Show.

Free Admission.

**Additional Location or Other Information**

10 Ave Of The Flags, San Rafael, CA 94903
