# Miwok Acquatic & Fitness Center at College of Marin, Indian Valley Campus

[](/site/assets/files/29542/miwok-aquatic-ivc.jpg)

College of Marin – Indian Valley Campus   
1800 Ignacio Boulevard   
Novato, CA 94949   
628-234-8107 or (415) 884-3124  
[Website](https://marin.edu/news/community-program-lap-swim-and-fitness-center)  


* * *




The Miwok Aquatic and Fitness Center is a state-of-the-art aquatic and fitness center including an Olympic-size swimming pool, six-lane warming pool, dive pool and dive tower, as well as a full fitness center for cardio and weights, locker rooms and showers…all surrounded by scenic landscapes and available for a variety of classes, programs, and community events that cater to diverse interests and needs, while providing a safe and inviting atmosphere for swimmers and divers to train, exercise, and learn. 

In addition to serving as a hub for the College’s athletic program, outside organizations can rent the pools when they are not being used for College purposes. The pools draw athletes from the West Coast and beyond for swimming and diving, water polo, and synchronized swimming meets and tournaments. 

Year-round community adult lap swimming is available on Tuesday, Wednesday, and Thursday, from 1 p.m. to 3 p.m. through June 13, 2024. 

Summer Swim runs June 17 through August 9, Monday through Friday from 11:30 a.m. to 4 p.m. 

Purchase swimming passes in the lobby: 

  * Individual Daily Drop-In: $10; $9 Seniors (over 55) and Juniors (under 18)
  * 10-Day Discount Pass: $90: $80 Seniors (over 55) and Juniors (under 18) 
  * 10-day Combined Discount Pass for Lap-Swim and Fitness Gym $150 / $140 Seniors (over 55)
