# Nicasio Pumpkin Patch Open Daily, September 26 - November 2, 2025

**Date** : Thursday, October 23, 2025

**Time** : Open 10:00am - 4pm Monday - Friday and 10:00am - 6:00pm Saturday - Sunday

[**Website**](https://nicasiovalleypumpkinpatch.net/)

####  Nicasio Valley Pumpkin Patch is a Traditional Pumpkin Patch Located in the Heart of Beautiful Nicasio Valley in Marin County California.

We offer a wide variety of pumpkins ranging from your standard Halloween jack-o-lantern types, multicolored and bumpy knuckleheads, greenish-blue Jardale, "Cinderella" style squash, gourds, mini pumpkins and cornstalks. Pumpkins range in price from $7 to $43. 

Cash accepted and there is an ATM available on site. Credits cards also accepted.

There is no charge for parking or admission.   
  
**Weekdays, Monday - Friday Festivities:**

  * Farm animals (calves, pigs, chickens, goats) 

  * Hay mazes (two different sizes- one for younger and older children)

  * Hay pit with bouncy animals 

  * Shaded picnic areas

  * Interactive agricultural educational boards 

  * Scavenger hunt

  * Tetherballs

  * Corn hole
