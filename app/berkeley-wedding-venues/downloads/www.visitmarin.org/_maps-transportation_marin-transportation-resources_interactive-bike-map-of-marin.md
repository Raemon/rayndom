# Interactive Bike Map of Marin by TAM

[](/site/assets/files/19407/tam_logo_for_bike_map.jpg)

<https://www.tam.ca.gov/bikepedmap/>

* * *




The map depicts the existing and proposed bike and pedestrian network as adopted in Marin County’s jurisdictions [Bicycle and Pedestrian plans](https://www.tam.ca.gov/planning/bicycle-pedestrian-plans-2/). The map generalizes the classifications of bike and pedestrian facilities within adopted plans for ease of use of the map. Users should refer to adopted local plans for any questions related to specific network segments. While the data has been reviewed for general accuracy through Marin Map, TAM is not responsible for the accuracy of the data and the map may not reflect updated conditions.

This map is for informational purposes only and should not be used for bicycling directions. For bicycling directions, visit [511 Bicycling](https://511.org/biking), refer to your local bike map, or search an online map service that provides bicycling directions.

Questions or comments should be sent to [info@tam.ca.gov](mailto:info@tam.ca.gov).
