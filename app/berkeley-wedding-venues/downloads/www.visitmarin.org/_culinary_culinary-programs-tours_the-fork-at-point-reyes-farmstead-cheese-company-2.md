# The Fork at Point Reyes Farmstead Cheese Company

  * [](/site/assets/files/23458/cows_and_bay.900x600.jpg)
  * [](/site/assets/files/23458/aerial_view.900x600.jpg)

14700 Highway 1  
Point Reyes, CA 94956  
415-663-8880  
800-591-6878  
[www.pointreyescheese.com/thefork](https://www.pointreyescheese.com/fork)

**_Get the Ultimate Farm-to-Table Experience at our Culinary and Education Center!_**

* * *




Custom built on our pastoral family dairy, The Fork was designed with education and entertainment in mind. Learn about our history, sustainable farming practices and artisan cheesemaking by joining us for one of many event offerings for groups of all sizes (reservations required).   
  
On our patio overlooking bucolic hillsides and Tomales Bay, we offer abundant cheese tastings with optional walking farm tours, additional bites or meals. Your personal cheese concierge will host your group, walk you through your cheese selection, introduce you to members of our herd and give you the inside scoop on our operation. 

Visit our Marketplace (open Monday - Saturday 11am-4pm) to pick up gifts, locally-made goodies or a pre-made artisan snack pack to take along for a local hike or picnic.
