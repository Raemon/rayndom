# Daily Lunch, Olive Olive and Wine Tastings at McEvoy Ranch

**Date** : Friday, October 17, 2025

**Time** : 11:00am - 5:00pm

**Phone** : (707) 778-2307

[**Website**](https://www.mcevoyranch.com/pages/experience-the-ranch)

Our seasonal lunch menu features a selection of fresh, nutritious entrees and decadent desserts, each showcasing the exceptional flavors of our estate-produced olive oils and culinary offerings. Pre-order your meal through the reservation process at least 48-hrs in advance of your arrival to ensure availability. Our selection of award-winning wines and other beverages are available for purchase upon arrival. [Lunch Menu](https://www.mcevoyranch.com/pages/lunch)

Or, sip, savor and indulge in our award winning olive oils and small lot wines. The choice is yours and perfect for a getaway with a girlfriend or just some alone time to enjoy fresh air and beautiful hills of McEvoy Ranch! Olive Oil tastings are $35.00 per person and Wine Flights are $38 - $48 per person depending on varietals. 

Reservations are required in the above website link.
