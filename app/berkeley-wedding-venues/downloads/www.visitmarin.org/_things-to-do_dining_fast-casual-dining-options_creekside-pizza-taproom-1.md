# Creekside Pizza to Go

[](/site/assets/files/14783/all_on_one_table.jpg)




638 San Anselmo Avenue  
San Anselmo, CA 94960  
415-785-4450  
[www.creeksideSA.com](http://www.creeksidesa.com/)

Creekside Pizza & Taproom features hand-crafted artisan pizzas, deli-inspired sandwiches, crisp salads, hearty pastas and shareable appetizers, plus many gluten-free, vegetarian & vegan options. Open every day, 7-days a week from 11AM with full-service indoor dining or outdoors in San Anselmo’s public parklet and picnic areas in Creek Park and Redwood Grove.

Creekside‘s iconic copper 40 tap handle taproom is home to the finest craft beer selection in Marin. Pints, flights and growlers plus craft cans & bottles, plus a selection of over 40 curated California wines. Enjoy our daily Happy Hour at 4PM, 7 days a week.

Warm, friendly & welcoming, Creekside is committed to quality, comfort and a memorable experience. Marin County’s consistent Winner for Best Pizza, Craft Beer, Service and Happy Hour, we look forward to serving you here in the Heart of Marin.

We also provide “Creekside to Go” ~ Call for fast pick-up or mobile on UberEats. Our pizzas come in 100% compostable plant-based box-rounds... the newest in eco-sustainability. 

**Open Daily: 11:00AM to 9:00PM**
