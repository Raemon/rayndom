# The Marshall Store Oyster Bar and Smokehouse

  * [](/site/assets/files/23777/oysters_and_crab.900x600.jpg)
  * [](/site/assets/files/23777/oyster_rockefellers.900x600.jpg)



19225 State Highway One  
Marshall, CA 94940  
415-663-1339  
[www.themarshallstore.com](http://www.themarshallstore.com/)

* * *




Take in Tomales Bay views while enjoying classic dishes we create from scratch and harvest daily, from oysters, clam chowder, fish tacos, sandwiches, smoked meats, local cheeses and more. We use seasonal and local ingredients in all of our dishes. We have wonderful sandwiches served on bread from our very own bakery in Tomales, Route One Bakery and Kitchen! For desert, we have soft serve gelato from Double 8 Dairy, and gigantic chocolate chip cookies from Route One Bakery and Kitchen. Beer and Wine available for purchase as well! [Menu Here](https://themarshallstore.com/the-menu)

**Hours of Operation:** Open Thursday from 11:00am - 3:00pm and on Friday - Monday from 11:00am - 4:00pm on a first come, first serve basis.
