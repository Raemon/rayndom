# Left Bank Brasserie

  * [](/site/assets/files/21833/egg_salmon_hash.900x600.jpg)
  * [](/site/assets/files/21833/oysters.900x600.jpg)
  * [](/site/assets/files/21833/nicoise_salad.900x600.jpg)

507 Magnolia Avenue  
Larkspur, CA 94939  
415-927-3331  
[www.leftbank.com](http://www.leftbank.com)

**_Fun & French!_**

* * *




Left Bank Brasserie is an authentic Parisian-style brasserie located in the historic Blue Rock Inn in charming downtown Larkspur. Built in 1895 during the railroad era, The Blue Rock Inn was a favorite weekend spot for San Franciscans to dance and picnic under the stars. 

Open since 1994, Left Bank continues to be a popular dining destination for people from all over the Bay Area. Guests enjoy a taste of the Parisian good life on the sunlit terrace, indulge in elegant wines and handcrafted cocktails at the lively bar, and cozy up to the stone fireplace in cooler months. The restaurant’s emanating warmth and vibrancy captures the true feeling of the Rive Gauche in Paris.

Left Bank Brasserie offers an often changing, seasonal brasserie menu to highlight the best of local bounties. Rustic, French inspired dishes feature a diverse selection of natural meats, sustainable seafood, fresh local vegetables, hearty salads, and sumptuous desserts. [Menus Here](https://www.leftbank.com/larkspur-menu)

For larger parties and events, please visit our private dining section [here.](https://www.leftbank.com/private-dining-larkspur)

Saturday – Sunday 10:00am – 9:00pm
