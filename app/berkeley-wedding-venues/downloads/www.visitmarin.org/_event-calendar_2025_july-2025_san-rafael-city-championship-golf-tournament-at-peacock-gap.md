# San Rafael City Championship Golf Tournament at Peacock Gap

**Date** : Saturday, July 19, 2025

**Phone** : 415-453-4940

[**Website**](https://2025-san-rafael-city-championship-ykc96.tripleseattickets.com/registration/select?utm_campaign=191f327d39-EMAIL_CAMPAIGN_2025_05_14_09_05&utm_medium=email&utm_source=Peacock%20Gap%20Golf%20Club&utm_term=0_-191f327d39-291132807)

Peacock Gap Golf Club will hold its 4th Annual San Rafael City Championship on July 19-20, 2025. This two-day, 36-hole stroke play tournament will feature a competitive field, a generous prize fund, and a course in peak condition. 

**Flight Divisions:**

Championship: Black Tees (Gross Score, Index < 5.9)  
Senior Championship (55+): Blue Tees (Gross Score, Index < 9.9)  
Net Flight: Blue Tees (Net Score, Index < 18.0)  
Senior Net (55+): White Tees (Net Score, Index < 18.0)  
Women’s Flight: Red Tees (Net Score, Index < 22.0)

Mark your calendar, work on your swing, and don’t forget to practice those short putts. 

Entry Fee: $260 includes greens fees for both days, range balls, prize fund, box lunch, and welcome tee prize. Cart fees extra. For more details and to register, please visit the above website link.
