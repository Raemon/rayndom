# Cruise Richardson Bay on a Cycle Boat with Sausalito Boat Rentals

**Date** : Friday, October 24, 2025

**Time** : Time Slots Available From 10:00am - 6:00pm

**Phone** : 415-448-6605

[**Website**](https://go.theflybook.com/book/1607/ListView/0#/base/1607/2a42c4de-f2c8-4871-860d-9206663f3d60/config/39853/37926/activity)

Join us for an exciting adventure on our family-friendly 16-person cycle boat in Sausalito Wednesday - Sunday! With 12 pedaling stations, guests can pedal at their leisure while enjoying stunning views, all under the guidance of a professional captain. Perfect for friends, family, and corporate groups, this unique experience promises fun and memorable moments on the water. Book your seats now for an unforgettable day!

Duration is 90 minutes and Cost is $50 for Adults (18+) and $30 (Kids Under 18) plus service fees. More details and to purchase tickets, please visit the above website link. 

**Additional Location or Other Information**

2320 Marinship Way, Sausalito, CA 94965
