# Vacation Bike Rentals

[](/site/assets/files/29631/bike_on_bridge_reduced_and_cropped.jpg)

Contact: Dave McLaughin  
415.900.7305 or Email: [info@vacationbikerentals.com](mailto:info@vacationbikerentals.com)  
[www.vacationbikerentals.com](http://www.vacationbikerentals.com)

* * *




Vacation Bike Rentals is a local business based in Sausalito providing turn-key bike & e-bike rental and delivery services to hotels and vacation properties in Marin. Our top-of-the-line equipment offers you the unique experience of exploring Marin’s stunning beauty by bike. 

Our [extensive fleet ](https://www.vacationbikerentals.com/rental-bikes)consists of the best quality, European-made, Class-1, pedal assist e-bikes (20mph limit/no throttle) and premium, European-made (non-electric) [Time carbon fiber road and gravel ](https://www.vacationbikerentals.com/rental-bikes-1-1)bikes. Brand name helmets and locks are included with every rental. Our bikes are clean and professionally-maintained and delivered on demand. (Pick up in Sausalito is also an option.) 

We have all 5 star Google and Yelp reviews and have suggested [self-guided tour options](https://www.vacationbikerentals.com/rides-and-tours) in Marin and across the Golden Gate Bridge. We also offer personalized ride planning and guide services for private groups, including [family and corporate team-building excursions](https://www.vacationbikerentals.com/groups-1)
