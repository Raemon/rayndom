AvantSpace, a premium network of high-tech, boutique co-working spaces located in Downtown San Rafael. The membership-based workspace offers a solution for local professionals who no longer want to work at home but are reluctant to return to long commutes and traditional office environments. 

Featuring 17 private offices, 22 dedicated desks, 2 conference rooms, and 34 floating desks. The workspace, with a growing waitlist, is set to accommodate over 130 local working professionals. AvantSpace provides a variety of membership options that cater to those interested in having a designated office or dropping in a few days a month.
