# Goat Yoga at Charlie's Acres Farm Animal Sanctuary

**Date** : Saturday, April 19, 2025

**Time** : 10:00am

[**Website**](https://www.charliesacres.org/visit/)

Enjoy the phenomenon of goat yoga with the rescued animal residents of Charlie's Acres Farm Animal Sanctuary. Our yoga instructors will instruct you on poses and posture with the occasional goat distraction. This yoga class is intended to be fun and for all skill levels so no need to take it too seriously. Every class will be different and dependent on how our friendly goats (or sheep) decide to participate. Offering April - October. 

We'll provide yoga mats and will position all participants 6+ feet apart. Please bring comfortable clothes and a water bottle. Ages 14+.

Please arrive 15 minutes early to check-in, set up, and get acquainted with our rescued animal residents. The yoga class will be 1 hour but plan to stick around about 30 min after class for a meet and greet with more rescued animal residents.

Goat yoga is exclusively an outdoor activity. In the event of inclement weather, Goat Yoga will be rescheduled.

The cost is $60.00 per person, plus fees. All ticket can be purchased in the above website link.
