# Boca Pizzeria at The Village Corte Madera

[](/site/assets/files/13940/boca_pizza.jpg)

1544 Redwood Highway  
Corte Madera, CA 94925  
415-924-3021  
[www.bocapizzeria.com](https://www.bocapizzeria.com/corte-madera/)

* * *




Boca Pizzeria is an Italian inspired Pizzeria utilizing the abundance of Northern California’s seasonal ingredients, while showcasing local micro breweries and wine country’s boutique varietals.

Our pizzas are authentic Neapolitan style, which cooks for no more than 2 minutes in a 900 degree Italian wood burning oven, made from double milled 00 Caputo flour. Our sauce is made of the finest San Marzano tomatoes imported from Italy. We make house-made mozzarella cheese daily. [View our menus here](https://www.bocapizzeria.com/menus-italian-pasta/).

Boca Pizzeria serves lunch and dinner seven days a week. Happy Hour: Monday-Friday 3:00pm-6:00pm (exclusive to bar only)

**Hours of Operation:** Open Daily 11:30 am to 9:00 pm
