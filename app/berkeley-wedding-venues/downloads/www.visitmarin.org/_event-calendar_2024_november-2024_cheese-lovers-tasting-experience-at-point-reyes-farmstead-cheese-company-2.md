# Cheese Lovers Tasting Experience at Point Reyes Farmstead Cheese Company

**Date** : Saturday, November 30, 2024

**Time** : 11:00am and 1:30pm

[**Website**](https://fareharbor.com/embeds/book/pointreyescheese/items/307125/calendar/2023/06/?full-items=yes)

Please join us for a tasting of our award-winning cheeses on the gorgeous Fork patio overlooking our farm and Tomales Bay! You and your guests will enjoy an abundant cheeseboard, including perfectly selected and house-made accompaniments. Additional bites from the kitchen can be ordered to round out your tasting experience (see sample menu below). A few of our bovine ladies will be nearby to meet and greet -- petting and photo opportunities will be plentiful! 

Duration is 90 minutes  
Cost is $51.41 a person and must make a reservation in the above website link. 

Come hungry, leave cheesy! Your party will enjoy a plentiful cheeseboard at your reserved table, including an assortment of our cheeses and perfectly selected house-made accompaniments. In addition to your cheeseboard, delicious, seasonal offerings from The Fork kitchen will be available for purchase. A menu of the day’s offerings will be provided at each table. Options include local charcuterie, unique grilled cheeses, ooey-gooey mac & cheese and YES - our world-famous, house-made ice cream sandwiches!

Cheeseboard Includes:  
• Toma  
• TomaTruffle  
• TomaProvence  
• TomaRashi  
• Gouda  
• Bay Blue  
• Original Blue  
• Accompaniments: House-made Maple Pepper Almonds, Fresh and dried fruit, Rustic Bakery Artisan Crackers, (Gluten-free crackers available) Fresh baked bread, TCHO Chocolate, Olives.

**Additional Location or Other Information**

14700 Highway One, Point Reyes Station, CA 94956
