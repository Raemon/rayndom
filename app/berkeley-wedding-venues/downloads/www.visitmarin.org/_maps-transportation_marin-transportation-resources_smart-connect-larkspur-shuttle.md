# SMART Connect Larkspur Shuttle

  * [](/site/assets/files/29820/larkspurshuttlebutton.900x600.png)
  * [](/site/assets/files/29820/larkspur_train.900x600.jpg)



[Ride Pingo App](http://www.ridepingo.com/) or call 800-727-0279 for shuttle reservations as space is limited. 

<https://sonomamarintrain.org/LarkspurShuttle>

* * *




### **Catching the SMART Connect Larkspur shuttle at the SMART train station**

The shuttle meets every southbound train at the SMART Larkspur train station within 5 minutes. You can find the bright orange shuttle waiting as you exit the train at the drop off area to the south of the platform. Reserve a seat to ride by booking using the [Ride Pingo App](http://www.ridepingo.com/) or by calling 800-727-0279. We recommend making a reservation, as seating is limited. However, walk-ons are also welcome, if space is available.

### **Catching the SMART Connect Larkspur shuttle at the Larkspur Ferry Terminal**

The shuttle meets every northbound ferry within 5 minutes. The shuttle stops at the bus stop area outside of the ferry terminal. Reserve a seat to ride by booking using the [Ride Pingo App](http://www.ridepingo.com/) or by calling 800-727-0279. We recommend making a reservation, as seating is limited. However, walk-ons are also welcome, if space is available.

### **Hours of Service for Shuttle:**

  * Thursday – Friday: 7:00 AM – 12:30 PM and 1:30 PM – 6:00 PM
  * Saturday – Sunday: 8:30 AM – 11:30 AM and 12:30 – 7:30 PM



One-way fare is $1.50 for adults and $0.75 for people with disabilities. The service is free for riders younger than 19 and older than 64. The shuttle seats 11 riders and has room for two wheelchair users. [More Shuttle Ride Details Here](https://www.sonomamarintrain.org/connect).

**Weekday Service Area:**

The shuttle’s weekday service area centers around the SMART Larkspur Station, the Golden Gate Ferry Larkspur Terminal, and the surrounding Larkspur Landing area.

**Weekend Service Area:**

The shuttle has an expanded service area on Saturdays and Sundays serving The Village and Town Center shopping malls of Corte Madera. The shuttle will meet you at predetermined pickup and drop-off spots, visible in the Ride Pingo app.
