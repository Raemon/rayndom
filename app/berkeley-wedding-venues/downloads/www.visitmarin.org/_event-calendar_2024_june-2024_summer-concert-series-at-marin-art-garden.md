# Summer Concert Series at Marin Art & Garden

**Date** : Thursday, June 27, 2024

**Time** : Gates Open at 4:30pm and Music is from 5:00pm - 6:00pm

[**Website**](https://maringarden.org/summerconcerts/)

Join us in the gardens for an expanded Summer Concert Series on Thursday evenings beginning June 27 through August 29, 5-7 pm (excluding July 4). Hear all your favorites from rock, jazz, bluegrass, soul, French chanson, rockabilly, Brazilian, and more. There’s something for everyone!  
  
Pre-order gourmet picnic offerings or bring your own. Premium wine, beer and gelato for sale. Enjoy a KidZone hosted by [The Garden School](https://maringarden.org/garden-school/), and [Pixie Park](https://www.pixiepark.org/) will be open to all! Dine al fresco, dance and sing on beautiful summer evenings. Concerts on the Lawn and in the Redwood Amphitheater.

**Featured Music is A Family Concert with music by Megan Schoenbohm, co-presented with[Pixie Park](https://www.pixiepark.org/).**

Space and Admission are limited: $20 for adults and kids under 17 are free. Parking is $10 per car. Please visit the above website link to book your tickets now!
