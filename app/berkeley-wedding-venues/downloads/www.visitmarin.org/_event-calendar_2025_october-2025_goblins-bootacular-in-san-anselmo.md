# Goblins Bootacular in San Anselmo

**Date** : Friday, October 24, 2025

**Time** : 5:00pm - 7:00pm

[**Website**](https://www.sananselmo.gov/787/Goblins-BOOtacular)

The 2025 Goblins' BOOtacular is set for Friday, October 24th starting at 5:00pm on San Anselmo Avenue in spooky downtown San Anselmo. It is a fun, town-wide trick-or-treating event for those ages 11 years old and younger.

Dress in your most boo-tiful Halloween costume and join us for some traditional small town fun. Merchants and volunteers will be standing along San Anselmo Avenue between Woodland and Tamalpais to hand out goodies. We have some non-sugar goodies too!
