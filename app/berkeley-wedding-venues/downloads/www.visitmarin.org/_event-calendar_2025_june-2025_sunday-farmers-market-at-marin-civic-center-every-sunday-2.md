# Sunday Farmers Market at Marin Civic Center

**Date** : Sunday, June 8, 2025

**Time** : 8:00am-1:00pm

**Phone** : 415-472-6100

[**Website**](https://agriculturalinstitute.org/directory/listing-details/sunday-san-rafael-market)

Come join us, every Sunday, rain or shine, and see why the Sunday Civic Center market  _is_ one of the largest local gatherings in the state! The Sunday Marin Farmers Market first opened in 1987, and has grown to become the third largest farmers market in California. Despite gaining recognition throughout the country, the Sunday Civic Center market still maintains its Marin community roots. The majority of patrons are local eaters who visit the market religiously each week to take home the freshest foods of the season. The New York Times listed the Sunday Market as one of the top places to visit in Marin. 

The Sunday Civic Center market features nearly 200 local farmers, specialty food purveyors, and artisans selling:

  * Locally grown and organic produce
  * Local seafood
  * Grass fed meats
  * Pastured eggs
  * Milk and cheese
  * Nuts and nut butters
  * Honey
  * Rice and grains
  * Breads and baked goods
  * Plants and fresh-cut flowers
  * Coffee
  * Pre-packed and hot foods
  * Artisanal crafts and jewelry
  * Soap and personal care items
  * Pet food



**Additional Location or Other Information**

The Sunday Farmers Market is held at 3501 Civic Center Drive, San Rafael, CA 94903. The Sunday farmers market in Marin offers an abundance of free parking.
