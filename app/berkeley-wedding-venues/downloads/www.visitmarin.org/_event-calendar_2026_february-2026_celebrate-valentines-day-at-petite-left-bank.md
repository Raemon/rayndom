# Celebrate Valentine's Day at Petite Left Bank

**Date** : Saturday, February 14, 2026

**Time** : After 4:00pm

[**Website**](https://www.petiteleftbanktiburon.com/events/valentines-day-2026)

Celebrate Valentine's Day at Petite Left Bank with an evening designed for lingering and indulging. 

We will be open for regular hours on the holiday, offering a Valentine's Day inspired three course prix fixe for $95 per person, available after 4 pm. Optional wine pairings, hand selected by our Wine Director and Sommelier, are available with each course to elevate the experience.

Treat your loved one to a meal at Petite this Valentine's Day by reserving your table online today!
