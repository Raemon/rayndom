# Wildlife Tour of San Francisco Bay

**Date** : Sunday, October 5, 2025

**Time** : 1:30pm and 3:30pm

**Phone** : 415-435-2131

[**Website**](https://fareharbor.com/embeds/book/angelislandferry/items/85891/?ref=MarinCVB&full-items=yes&flow=105203)

Join us aboard the Tamalpais and experience a journey through the Bay and just outside of the Golden Gate in search of all kinds of majestic marine mammals! This adventure will have you anticipating the views of the Bay's wildlife and in awe of the nature found in the area. You'll get to see sights like Alcatraz, the Golden Gate Bridge and San Francisco.

**Rates:**  
Adults (13+): $54.50  
Child (6-12): $43.60  
Small Child (3-5): $16.45

**Available Times:**  
First Sunday of the month beginning in June through October. Two Available Times: 1:30pm and 3:30pm. Plan to board 10 minutes before departure at 1:20pm and 3:20pm. 

This cruise is 90 minutes long. Online tickets automatically give you a reservation. Reservations are required for this cruise. More details and tickets can be purchased in the above website link.

**Additional Location or Other Information**

21 Main Street, Tiburon, CA 9492
