# World Wrapps at Town Center

[](/site/assets/files/7612/world_wrapps_salad.jpg)

429 Corte Madera Town Center  
Corte Madera, CA 94925  
415-927-3663  
[www.worldwrapps.com](http://www.worldwrapps.com/)

* * *




The first World Wrapps was opened in 1996 in San Francisco, CA by a group of friends who wanted a restaurant that didn’t yet exist: one that served delicious food that was both better tasting and better for you than traditional “fast-food,” yet still fit into their fast-paced on the go lifestyles.

The result – real gourmet “meals” wrapped up in flavorful tortillas that can be eaten anywhere. With the incredible success that soon followed, the founders quickly learned they weren’t the only ones looking for exactly this kind of place.

**Hours of Operation:** Monday – Saturday 10:30am – 8:00pm, Sunday 10:30am – 6:00pm
