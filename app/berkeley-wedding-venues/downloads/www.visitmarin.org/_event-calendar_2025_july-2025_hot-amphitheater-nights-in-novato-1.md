# Hot Amphitheater Nights in Novato

**Date** : Saturday, July 26, 2025

**Time** : 5:00pm - 7:00pm

[**Website**](https://www.novato.org/government/parks-recreation-community-services/events)

Please join Novato Parks, Recreation & Community Services for our 2025 Hot Amphitheater Nights series! Live concerts at Hamilton Amphitheater are back, on select Saturdays! Free Admission. 

Feel free to bring your own picnic blankets and lawn chairs. Outside food and drink are welcome. Please note that we kindly ask you to leave your furry friends at home. 

June 28 - Carnaval (Santana Tribute Band)

July 26 - Pop Fiction (70s, 80s, 90s, 2000s)

Aug 30 - The Time Bandits (Pop, Soul, R&B, Swing, Jazz & More)  


**Additional Location or Other Information**

Hamilton Amphitheater, 601 N. Hamilton Parkway, Novato
