# Cool Cool Cool with Floratura at Sweetwater Music Hall

**Date** : Thursday, October 9, 2025

**Time** : Doors Open at 7:00pm and Show Starts at 8:00pm

[**Website**](https://sweetwatermusichall.org/event/cool-cool-cool-with-floratura/sweetwater-music-hall/mill-valley-california/)

**Cool Cool Cool** blends the best of Funk, House, and R&B to create a sound that is truly unique. With dynamic female-led vocals, a tight horn section, swirling synths, and a rhythm section that lays down a wicked groove, this band knows how to deliver an electrifying performance. When you see Cool Cool Cool in person, you’ll be swept up by the energy of their music and unforgettable live show.

**Floratura** , a Neo-Soul, R&B jam band from the Bay Area, has shared the stage with The California Honeydrops, SAY SHE SHE and performed at the iconic Santa Cruz Mountain Sol Festival 2024 with the likes of Michael Franti & Spearhead, Tower Of Power, Ghost-Note, Jackie Venson & more. Floratura invites listeners to float down stream and melt into an oasis of wonder and discovery. Seamlessly blending soaring jam-band vibes and bouncy neo-soul. Floratura’s electrifying live shows combine uplifting narratives with intricate layers of grooves and textures. Drawing from a variety of influences, the California-bred band explores genres ranging from jazz-rock and funk to electronica, utilizing colorful harmonies, spicy, catchy melodies, and extensive improvisation. The psychedelic atmosphere creates an oasis for listening, dancing and feeling welcome.

General Admission Tickets are $34.51 per person in advance and $39.66 on day of show. All tickets can be purcased in the above website link.
