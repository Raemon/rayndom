# 101 Surf Sports

  *   * [](/site/assets/files/20665/paddleboarding.900x600.jpg)



115 Third Street  
San Rafael, CA 94901  
415-524-8492  
[www.101surfsports.com](http://www.101surfsports.com)

* * *




** _The water is right behind our shop so come on down!_**

Recently named "3rd Best Water Sports Shop in the Country", 101 Surf Sports is a full service Water Sports Shop specializing in Stand Up Paddleboarding (SUP), Windsurfing, Foiling, Surfing, and Kayaking on Marin County waters as well as the San Francisco Bay. We also offer a full range of lessons, gear rentals, board repair, kayak storage, guided tours, a retail shop, and much more!

Our location has all the SUP, windsurfing, kiteboarding, surfing and kayaking gear. We offer both Stand Up Paddle and Kayak tours year round and beginning in Spring we start up our Windsurfing and Kiteboarding downwind runs.

**Operation Fall Hours** : Thursday - Monday: 10:00am - 5:00pm
