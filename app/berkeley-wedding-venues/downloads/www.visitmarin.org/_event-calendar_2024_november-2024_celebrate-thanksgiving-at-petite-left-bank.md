# Celebrate Thanksgiving at Petite Left Bank

**Date** : Thursday, November 28, 2024

**Time** : 12:00pm - 6:00pm

[**Website**](https://www.petiteleftbanktiburon.com/events/thanksgiving-2024)

This Thanksgiving, relax and enjoy a French twist on the classic turkey dinner by allowing our chefs to do all the cooking!   
  
On Thanksgiving Day, we are serving a 4 Course Prix Fixe Dinner for $79.00 per person. A special kids menu will also be available to those 12 and under - a 3 Course Prix Fixe for $24.00. Full Menu found in above website link.
