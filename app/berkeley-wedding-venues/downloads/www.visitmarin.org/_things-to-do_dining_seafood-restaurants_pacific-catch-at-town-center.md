# Pacific Catch at Town Center

  * [](/site/assets/files/7668/0200_pacificcatch030922_group_shot.900x600.jpg)
  * [](/site/assets/files/7668/0033_pacificcatch030922_sushi_tacos.900x600.jpg)
  * [](/site/assets/files/7668/0055_pacificcatch030922_korean_bbq_bowl.900x600.jpg)
  * [](/site/assets/files/7668/0167_pacificcatch030922_taco_platter.900x600.jpg)



133 Corte Madera Town Center  
Corte Madera, CA 94925  
415-927-3474  
[www.pacificcatch.com](http://www.pacificcatch.com/)

* * *




Pacific Catch is a West Coast Fish House dedicated to delivering high quality sustainable seafood with a unique perspective. From the day we opened we've been mindful about where our ingredients come from and how to bring to life the vibrant flavors and feeling of the Pacific. It's our commitment to our guests to keep things interesting, innovative, wholesome and thoughtfully sourced. That's why we've formalized our relationship with the Monterey Bay Seafood Aquarium's Seafood Watch, James Beard Foundation's Smart Catch and the Surfrider Foundation by becoming a partner and bringing on a director of sustainability to help steward these efforts.

Our menu explores a variety of Fresh Catch specials, shareables, sushi rolls, bowls and tacos. [Menus Here](https://pacificcatch.com/menus/)

We offer an indoor/outdoor patio space that allows the sunshine and cool breeze to pour in, transporting guests to a relaxed vacation spot.

**Hours of Operation:** Sunday - Thursday 11:00am - 9:00pm  
Friday and Saturday 11:00am - 10:00pm
