# Fairfax Winter Market

**Date** : Saturday, December 7, 2024

**Time** : 5:00pm - 8:00pm

[**Website**](https://fairfaxchamberca.com/)

Join us and experience the magic of the Fairfax Winter Market. Shop for unique gifts, savor warm soups and local flavors, and enjoy the festive atmosphere of Fairfax. Explore the dazzling storefront lights, participate in the cake walk raffle, and enjoy live entertainment.   
  
Join us for this family-friendly celebration in the heart of Fairfax on Bolinas Road between Broadway and Elsie Lane. Here’s a glimpse of what you can expect:  
  
• Photos with Santa: Capture the joy of the season with a visit from Santa! Bring your family for a festive photo opportunity to cherish.  
• Shopping Specials: Explore unique holiday gift options from local businesses. Support your community by shopping local for that perfect present for your loved ones. Shops along Bolinas, Broadway, and Sir Frances Drake will be open with special offers.  
• Warm Soup, Burgers & Local Dining: Stay cozy with warm drinks, delicious hot soup, savory burgers, and mouthwatering dishes from local restaurants. It’s the perfect way to enjoy the festive atmosphere while supporting Fairfax’s dining establishments.  
• Cake Walk Raffle: Local businesses are donating delectable cakes to be raffled off during the event. Join the fun for a chance to win a sweet holiday treat!  
• Entertainment by Local Talent: Enjoy festive performances by students from local schools and special appearances by Fairfax Town Council members, adding a personal and lively touch to the evening.  
• Storefront Lights: The streets of Fairfax will come alive with twinkling lights and festive decorations, creating an enchanting holiday ambiance.
