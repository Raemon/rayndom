Choosing wedding invitations can be daunting while you juggle all your wedding planning tasks. These invitations are a vital part of your special day that give your guests all the important details about your celebration.

Beautiful options from traditional stationers exist, but wedding invitation websites now match high-end custom stationery at much lower prices. Most couples spend between $300 and $700 for invitations and envelopes. Production times can range from a few days to several weeks based on your choice of provider. Timing is significant – wedding experts suggest ordering invitations at least five to seven months before your wedding date.

Today’s best wedding invitation websites cater to every style and budget. Cards cost between $1.79 and $3.09 each, and eco-friendly options have saved more than 990,000 pounds of paper. We tested 23 beautiful options and recommend Joy, a platform that combines digital and printed solutions to make your invitation process smooth.
