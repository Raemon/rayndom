Travis Zane is a cross-genre artist focused on written and visual storytelling, the founder and editor of Revolukin Magazine, and a creative consultant who develops meaningful editorial and content strategies. As a member of the queer, BIPOC+, and AAPI communities, Zane's intention is to celebrate human goodness and explore the dynamic nature of our individual and interconnected identities. He lives in between New York and California, although you can often find him in random places around the world. Say hello on Instagram, Facebook, Twitter, YouTube, TikTok and more!
