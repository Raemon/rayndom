# The Best Sonoma Wedding Venues

by [Allison Hata](https://withjoy.com/blog/author/allison-hata/) April 8, 2024

written by [Allison Hata](https://withjoy.com/blog/author/allison-hata/) April 8, 2024

[ ![The Best Sonoma Wedding Venues](https://rehearsaluploads.withjoy.com/uploads/2018/07/trent-erwin-335921-unsplash-e1533773443110.jpg) ](https://rehearsaluploads.withjoy.com/uploads/2018/07/trent-erwin-335921-unsplash-e1533773443110.jpg)

Last Updated on April 8, 2024 by [Joy Editors](https://withjoy.com)

Sonoma County, the birthplace of California winemaking, is one of the most popular places to get married in the Northern California for a reason. Sonoma Valley boasts amazing wineries, of course, but that’s just the beginning of the spectacular venues the area has to offer. There are also historic homes, world-class spas, and scenic ranches, just to name a few. The wine, the food, and the views all attract couples from the Bay Area and around the world. To help you find just the right place for your big day, here are our favorite Sonoma wedding venues!

## [General’s Daughter](https://www.thegeneralsdaughter.com/)

![general's daughter](https://rehearsaluploads.withjoy.com/uploads/uploads/generals-daughter-1024x614.jpg)![general's daughter](data:image/svg+xml,%3Csvg%20xmlns=%22http://www.w3.org/2000/svg%22%20viewBox=%220%200%201024%20614%22%3E%3C/svg%3E)

Photo via www.thegeneralsdaughter.com

Located in downtown Sonoma, the [General’s Daughter](https://www.thegeneralsdaughter.com/) has deep connections to California history. The beautiful Victorian home was built in 1864 for the daughter of Mexican general Mariano Vallejo upon her marriage to the son of Agoston Haraszthy, the founder of California’s first winery. In the century-and-a-half since, the house and its grounds have become one of the premier spots in Sonoma to host a wedding ceremony and reception with its amazing indoor and outdoor venues. The vine-covered garden patio is great for an outdoor reception, while the charming Great Room is an ideal location for an indoor event.

**Capacity:** 120

**Typical cost:** $1,000–$5,500 rental fee

**Bonus:** The acclaimed Ramekins culinary school is right next door, and they will provide your reception with their signature farm-to-table catering.

Follow them on [Facebook](https://www.facebook.com/thegeneralsdaughtersonoma/) and [Instagram](https://www.instagram.com/generalsdaughtersonoma/).

## [Cornerstone Sonoma](https://www.cornerstonesonoma.com/)

![cornerstone sonoma](https://rehearsaluploads.withjoy.com/uploads/uploads/cornerstone-sonoma.jpg)![cornerstone sonoma](data:image/svg+xml,%3Csvg%20xmlns=%22http://www.w3.org/2000/svg%22%20viewBox=%220%200%20960%20640%22%3E%3C/svg%3E)

Photo via www.cornerstonesonoma.com

[Cornerstone Sonoma](https://www.cornerstonesonoma.com/) features breathtaking gardens designed by world-renowned landscape architects. The Vineyard Lawn which borders on the pinot noir vineyards and features seasonally-blooming water lilies, is perfect for large events. The Garden Barn provides amazing views of the gardens and has a fire pit. The Wedding Garden is a secluded area with a half acre or lawn and includes a 50-foot tent.

**Capacity:** The three venues have max capacities ranging from 120 (seated)–450 (standing)

**Typical cost:** $3,500–$11,500

**Bonus:** There is a two-bedroom cottage available to rent on site, so you can prolong your stay at these beautiful gardens.

Follow them on [Facebook](https://www.facebook.com/CornerstoneSonoma/), [Twitter](https://twitter.com/csonoma), and [Instagram](https://www.instagram.com/cornerstonesonoma/).

## [Flying Cloud](https://flyingcloudfarm.com/)

![](https://rehearsaluploads.withjoy.com/uploads/uploads/Flying-Cloud1_90035397-5056-a36a-075e7bb9c23099fc.jpg)![](data:image/svg+xml,%3Csvg%20xmlns=%22http://www.w3.org/2000/svg%22%20viewBox=%220%200%20600%20275%22%3E%3C/svg%3E)

Photo via www.sonomacounty.com/weddings

Nestled in the foothills of Sonoma Mountain, this [wine country estate](https://flyingcloudfarm.com/) near Petaluma offers the quintessential Sonoma wedding. With its picturesque gardens and courtyards, great indoor locations, and a charming barn, you’ll get the full wine country wedding experience while enjoying a relaxed pace and a private setting.

**Capacity:** 100-200 depending on venue

**Typical Cost:** $1,900–$7,200

**Bonus:** Flying Cloud is also an equestrian center, and you’ll likely be able to see horses training throughout the day.

Follow them on [Facebook](https://www.facebook.com/flyingcloudweddings/) and [Instagram](https://www.instagram.com/flyingcloudfarm/).

## [Depot Hotel](https://www.depotsonoma.com/)

![depot sonoma](https://rehearsaluploads.withjoy.com/uploads/uploads/depot-sonoma-1024x683.jpeg)![depot sonoma](data:image/svg+xml,%3Csvg%20xmlns=%22http://www.w3.org/2000/svg%22%20viewBox=%220%200%201024%20683%22%3E%3C/svg%3E)

Photo via www.depotsonoma.com

[Depot Hotel Restaurant and Garden](https://www.depotsonoma.com/) is located in a beautifully restored 1870 train depot. The restaurant is perfect for receptions. The outdoor seating area by the pool, provides a romantic for an outdoor dinner, while the indoor areas have rustic European feel. The garden likewise provides a wonderful location for your ceremony. Of course a big draw for the Depot Hotel is its world-class cuisine. Their tiramisu is rightfully famous! Couples also sing the praises of the wedding coordinator and her staff.

**Capacity:** 100-150

**Typical cost:** $0–$3,000 site fee

**Bonus:** Parking won’t be a problem—wedding packages include use of a lot with space for 500 vehicles.

Follow them on [Facebook](https://www.facebook.com/DepotSonoma/).

## [Villa Chanticleer](https://www.villachanticleer.com/)

![](https://rehearsaluploads.withjoy.com/uploads/uploads/villa-chanticleer.jpg)![](data:image/svg+xml,%3Csvg%20xmlns=%22http://www.w3.org/2000/svg%22%20viewBox=%220%200%20600%20401%22%3E%3C/svg%3E)

Photo via www.facebook.com/villachanticleerevents/

For a rustic wedding venue with plenty of space for your guests, check out [Villa Chanticleer](https://www.villachanticleer.com/). This Healdsburg gem is surrounded by a peaceful oak forest and includes gardens, a gazebo, and plenty of space for great fun and dancing. And you can rest assured that the friendly staff will be there to help your guests with all of their needs on the big day.

**Capacity:** 250

**Typical cost:** $1,700–$7,500

**Bonus:** For animal lovers, the adjacent forest is home to lots of friendly deer!

Follow them on [Facebook](https://www.facebook.com/villachanticleerevents/) and [Instagram](https://www.instagram.com/villa.chanticleer.healdsburg/).

## [Beltane Ranch](https://beltaneranch.com/)

![beltane ranch wedding venue](https://withjoy.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2020/05/josevilla_Beltane_wedding-1-3.jpg)![beltane ranch wedding venue](data:image/svg+xml,%3Csvg%20xmlns=%22http://www.w3.org/2000/svg%22%20viewBox=%220%200%20875%20600%22%3E%3C/svg%3E)

Photo via https://beltaneranch.com/

Located in Glen Ellen, Beltane Ranch provides a unique wedding experience with stunning vineyard views. Perfect for an outdoor event, this historic ranch offers a charming bed and breakfast inn, beautiful gardens and olive orchard. In addition to all the natural beauty of its surroundings offers wedding packages that offer everything you need for the perfect wine country wedding. You can also choose from a list of excellent local caterers including Girl & the Fig, Sage, and Ramekins, among others!

**Capacity:** 200

**Typical cost:** $2,500-$7,400 for events with 50 guests or fewer, $20,000-$35,000 for full wedding package

**Bonus:** Some wedding packages include a rehearsal dinner and farewell brunch.

Follow them on [Facebook](https://www.facebook.com/beltaneranch/).

## [DeTurk Round Barn](https://srcity.org/656/DeTurk-Round-Barn)

![deturk round barn santa rosa wedding venue](https://withjoy.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2018/07/deturk.jpg)![deturk round barn santa rosa wedding venue](data:image/svg+xml,%3Csvg%20xmlns=%22http://www.w3.org/2000/svg%22%20viewBox=%220%200%20564%20564%22%3E%3C/svg%3E)

Photo via https://srcity.org/

This historical landmark in Santa Rosa is a surprisingly affordable way to realize your dream of a rustic yet elegant wedding. The spacious interior of the barn provides ample space for all your wedding day activities, at an unbeatable price. 

**Capacity:** 200

**Typical cost:** $138-$377 per hour

**Bonus:** The center of the barn makes for an awesome dance floor!

## [Kenwood Inn and Spa](https://www.kenwoodinn.com/wedding/)

![kenwood inn and spa wedding venue](https://withjoy.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2018/07/kenwood.jpg)![kenwood inn and spa wedding venue](data:image/svg+xml,%3Csvg%20xmlns=%22http://www.w3.org/2000/svg%22%20viewBox=%220%200%201104%20564%22%3E%3C/svg%3E)

Photo via https://www.kenwoodinn.com/

Located in the Valley of the Moon among rolling hills reminiscent of Tuscany, Kenwood Inn and Spa is rightfully considered a premier destination for a Sonoma county wedding. Built in a Mediterranean style, the inn provides excellent accommodations for you and your guests during a luxurious wedding weekend, while the world-renowned spa will make your experience all the more relaxing!

**Capacity:** 100

**Typical cost:** $10,000 site fee, $5,000 spa minimum, hotel buyout required ($4079 for elopement package)

**Bonus:** If you’re looking to have a more intimate affair, their elopement package includes a coordinator, officiant, and one hour of professional photography.

Follow them on [Facebook](https://www.facebook.com/KenwoodInnAndSpa/).
