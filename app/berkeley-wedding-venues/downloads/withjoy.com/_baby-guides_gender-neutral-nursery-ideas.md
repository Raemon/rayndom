## **1\. Black and White**

![Baby's nursery with black and white decor](https://withjoy.com/baby-guides/wp-content/uploads/2023/10/black-and-white-nursery-1.jpg)

Go for a true classic with a black-and-white neutral nursery for your baby. This sophisticated and timeless color scheme exudes elegance. Go for a nursery that’s both stylish and versatile by incorporating monochrome bedding, striped wall decals, and black-and-white animal prints.

## **2\. Jungle Animals**

Embark on a wild jungle adventure with colorful jungle animals and exotic botanicals in your baby’s nursery. Fill the room with colorful jungle animal prints, lush greenery, and playful wall decals featuring friendly lions, giraffes, and elephants, transforming the nursery into a lively and fun space that sparks curiosity.

## **3\. Mountain Retreat**

Embrace the beauty of nature with a subtle outdoorsy feel in your baby’s mountain-themed nursery. Incorporate earthy tones, snow-capped mountain wall decals, and woodland-inspired decor to evoke a sense of natural serenity, bringing the beauty of the mountains indoors. 

## **4\. Bright Citrus**

Infuse your baby’s gender-neutral nursery with the vibrant hues of colorful citrus fruit like oranges and lemons. This cheerful and lively theme adds a burst of positivity. Choose bright yellow accents, fruity wall decals, and fun patterned bedding to create a sunny and refreshing atmosphere that brings joy to the nursery.

## **5\. Among the Clouds**

Create a serene and whimsical atmosphere for your baby’s gender-neutral nursery with cloud-themed decor like a cloud mobile over the crib and dreamy artwork. Floating among the clouds in a room filled with pastel blue and white tones, your little one will experience a sense of calm and wonder, and hopefully, you will, too.

## **6\. Geometric Greatness**

Transform your baby’s nursery with the dynamic appeal of different geometric shapes and lines. This modern, artistic theme adds a touch of contemporary edge. Use bold geometric patterned textiles, modern wall art, and angular furniture to make the nursery a visually intriguing space.

## **FAQ About Neutral Nurseries**

#### **How can I decorate my nursery without knowing my baby’s gender?**

By choosing versatile colors, such as soothing pastels or calming neutrals, you can create a warm and welcoming space. Incorporating timeless themes like nature, animals, or even playful patterns ensures a nursery that’s perfect for any baby. Don’t forget to add personalized touches like family photos or artwork to make it uniquely yours.

#### **What are the best gender-neutral nursery colors?**

Soft pastels like soothing mint, gentle lavender, or pale yellow create a calming atmosphere. Neutrals such as warm grays, soft beiges, or creamy whites provide a serene backdrop that you can easily accent with playful decor. Earthy tones like gentle greens or subtle blues evoke a natural and inviting feel. Ultimately, think about timeless and versatile options that work for both boys and girls.

#### **Does gender-neutral mean no color in the nursery?**

Gender-neutral doesn’t mean sacrificing color in the nursery! In fact, it’s all about embracing a broader palette that suits all genders. You can definitely incorporate hues like soft yellows, soothing greens, or gentle grays. It’s about finding the right balance, using colors that create a warm, inviting, and versatile space for your baby. 

#### **Should I not use blue or pink in a gender-neutral nursery?**

You can absolutely use blue or pink in a gender-neutral nursery, especially if you love those colors! The key is to balance them with other neutral shades and complementary tones to create a harmonious and inclusive space. When combined with whites, grays, or other earthy tones, soft pastel blues or pinks can add a lovely touch to a gender-neutral nursery. It’s all about personal preference and creating a cozy, welcoming environment that resonates with you and your baby.
