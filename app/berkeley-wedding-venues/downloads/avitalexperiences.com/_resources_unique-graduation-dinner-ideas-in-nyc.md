#  Unique Graduation Dinner Ideas in NYC 

May 14, 2019 

It‚Äôs easy to make any celebration feel special here in NYC, from 40th birthday parties to baby showers. Having a graduation dinner in the city leaves you endless options for a delicious meal, and since this may be the only time you‚Äôre graduating, why not make it one to really remember? Unique graduation dinner ideas in NYC are easier to find thanks to this list of some of our favorites. 

## **Choose the mood at Tokyo Record Bar.**

[![](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/5/2019/05/Unique-Graduation-Dinner-Ideas-in-NYC-Tokyo-Record-Bar.jpg)](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/5/2019/05/Unique-Graduation-Dinner-Ideas-in-NYC-Tokyo-Record-Bar.jpg)

If you‚Äôve got a family who loves music or perhaps you‚Äôre a music major yourself, [Tokyo Record Bar](https://tokyorecordbar.com/) lets guests pick out the playlist for dinner. This Japanese-style izakaya has two seatings and features a seven-course meal. When you receive your menus, you‚Äôll also get the song list. Select a few of your favorite vinyl favorites to keep the good vibes coming. If you‚Äôve got a big group but still want music to be a big part of your night, consider [Turntable LP Bar](https://turntablenyc.com/lp-bar) or [Insa](https://www.insabrooklyn.com/) for some good food and karaoke. 

## **Go classic at some of New York City‚Äôs oldest restaurants.**

[![](data:image/svg+xml,%3Csvg%20xmlns='http://www.w3.org/2000/svg'%20viewBox='0%200%20640%20480'%3E%3C/svg%3E)![](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/5/2019/05/Unique-Graduation-Dinner-Ideas-in-NYC-go-classic.jpg)](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/5/2019/05/Unique-Graduation-Dinner-Ideas-in-NYC-go-classic.jpg)

Before you head back into reality, take a step back in time. Restaurants like [Peter Luger Steak House](https://peterluger.com/) and [Keens Steakhouse ](http://www.keens.com/)will transport you back into a time of indulgence with some great service. Juicy steaks, creamed spinach, shrimp cocktail- what more do you need? It‚Äôs a fun throwback to the past and it‚Äôs hard for a fancy steak dinner to not feel special. Large groups can be accommodated and there‚Äôs something for everyone on the menu. There‚Äôs definitely something very New York about enjoying a steak in an old-fashioned restaurant. It‚Äôs a unique graduation dinner idea that you‚Äôre not going to find in many other places! 

## **Book some special treatment on a food tour.**

[![](data:image/svg+xml,%3Csvg%20xmlns='http://www.w3.org/2000/svg'%20viewBox='0%200%20800%20533'%3E%3C/svg%3E)![](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/5/2019/05/Unique-Graduation-Dinner-Ideas-in-NYC-take-a-food-tour.jpeg)](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/5/2019/05/Unique-Graduation-Dinner-Ideas-in-NYC-take-a-food-tour.jpeg)Ever wanted to dine in a speakeasy, learn how to make signature cocktails, or talk to the chefs about their inspirations? Booking [a food tour](http://avitaltours.com/nyc-food-tours/) can give you all that while making your graduation celebration in NYC easy to plan. A private food tour with your favorite people will give you a unique view of the city as you go to restaurants offer great food and a great story. Adding on a demo from the chefs and bartenders will really make the experience extra special. And when all you want to think about is the sweet freedom graduation brings, having someone else plan your graduation dinner plans alleviates at least one thing off your to-do list.

## **Take in the views on one of NYC‚Äôs rooftops.**

[![](data:image/svg+xml,%3Csvg%20xmlns='http://www.w3.org/2000/svg'%20viewBox='0%200%20640%20427'%3E%3C/svg%3E)![](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/5/2019/05/Unique-Graduation-Dinner-Ideas-in-NYC-take-in-the-views.jpg)](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/5/2019/05/Unique-Graduation-Dinner-Ideas-in-NYC-take-in-the-views.jpg)

If you‚Äôre planning a graduation dinner in NYC, one of the best things you can do is find a great view of the city. When the weather is good, there‚Äôs nothing more magical than hanging out on a New York City rooftop as the sunsets. There are lots of good restaurants to choose from, like [Eataly‚Äôs Serra](https://www.eataly.com/us_en/stores/nyc-flatiron/serra-by-birreria/), [Manhatta](https://www.manhattarestaurant.com/) in the Financial District, or [Top of the Standard](http://www.standardhotels.com/new-york/properties/high-line) in the Meatpacking District. For a view of Manhattan, head to Fornino at Brooklyn Bridge Park or [Alma](http://www.almarestaurant.com/) in Cobble Hill. The ambiance and views will certainly take your breath away. The food and drinks are great, too! A rooftop meal with a view of the NYC skyline is a unique graduation dinner idea that won‚Äôt be forgotten.
