## How to Get to the Mission District

The Mission District is bordered by SoMa in the North, Potrero Hill to the East, Bernal Heights to the South and the Castro and Noe Valley to the West.

**If you are coming via BART:** The closest bart station is the 16th Street Mission Station. Once you exit the station continue west on 16th street. Turn left on Valencia street to head south and walk to your meeting location.

**If you are coming via Muni:** The closest muni stop is at the corner of 18th Street and Church Street on the J Church Muni line. From the muni stop, walk east on 18th street toward the heart of the Mission.

**If you are coming via car:** Please see our recommended parking garage information below.

**If you are not sure how to get there and are running late:** San Francisco is the original home of Uber and Lyft. Download their apps to your smartphone. Use Code AVITAL for $20 off your first Lyft ride.
