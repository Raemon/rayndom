#  The Ultimate Birthday Celebration for Foodies in NYC 

January 4, 2018 

Does your day/week/month/year revolve around food? Do your friends constantly talk about how much you love to eat and what a ‚Äúfoodie‚Äù you are? Well, it‚Äôs obvious that you should spend your special day surrounded by good food. Here are five ideas for the ultimate birthday celebration for foodies.

## Plan a Food Crawl

[![plan a hot dog food crawl or another fun food: The Ultimate Birthday Celebration for Foodies](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/5/2014/02/nyc-food-tours-east-village-food-tour.jpg)](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/5/2014/02/nyc-food-tours-east-village-food-tour.jpg)

If you have a favorite type of food, plan a whole birthday celebration around it. From a [dumpling crawl](https://www.google.com/maps/d/u/0/viewer?mid=17Pufvgl9o4N3qM98Vm7QfLSngJg&hl=en_US&ll=40.7163352931574%2C-73.99463989999998&z=16) in Chinatown to a city-wide hot dog hunt to a pizza party, it‚Äôs fun to pick one food and try the best versions around the city. Plan for some walking and breaks in between so you can make the event last without everyone filling up too fast (or falling asleep!). Stumped on what to include? Ask guests to make suggestions beforehand. Then make a loose map and schedule so latecomers can catch up with you later.

## Go Somewhere You‚Äôve Been Dying to Eat

[![splurge on your birthday: The Ultimate Birthday Celebration for Foodies](data:image/svg+xml,%3Csvg%20xmlns='http://www.w3.org/2000/svg'%20viewBox='0%200%20600%20400'%3E%3C/svg%3E)![splurge on your birthday: The Ultimate Birthday Celebration for Foodies](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/5/2017/11/29HUNGRY-TITORADS-slide-AAA9-articleLarge.jpg)](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/5/2017/11/29HUNGRY-TITORADS-slide-AAA9-articleLarge.jpg)

We all have our bucket list restaurants-the ones that are always in the top 10 lists, or that our friends tell us to go to, but we hesitate in splurging on. Well, your birthday is the perfect time to say, ‚ÄúYES!‚Äù. If you feel guilty asking friends to splurge too, go alone. Really, eating alone is totally awesome‚Ä¶and then you don‚Äôt have to share. (Plus, if you don‚Äôt have a reservation, there‚Äôs almost always a seat for one available, even at the busiest restaurants.) If you‚Äôre planning to treat yourself and have a spot in mind already, make a [reservation](https://www.opentable.com/) as soon as possible so you commit to your splurge.

## **Take a Tour**

[![nyc tour on The Ultimate Birthday Celebration for Foodies](data:image/svg+xml,%3Csvg%20xmlns='http://www.w3.org/2000/svg'%20viewBox='0%200%202800%201866'%3E%3C/svg%3E)![nyc tour on The Ultimate Birthday Celebration for Foodies](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/5/2014/02/Corporate-Team-Building-in-NYC-guests-cheersing-with-cocktails.jpeg)](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/5/2014/02/Corporate-Team-Building-in-NYC-guests-cheersing-with-cocktails.jpeg)

Let someone else take the reins and have a guide take you and your birthday crew around New York City on a food tour. Skip a regular birthday dinner and instead have a [progressive culinary experience](http://avitaltours.com/nyc-food-tours/) with four courses at four different restaurants. You‚Äôll get to learn about the culture and history of the neighborhood through food, and it‚Äôs all planned out so your only job is to eat, drink, and enjoy. (And you‚Äôll even get to chat with some of the chefs and owners of the restaurants so you can nerd out even more about food.)

## Hire a Chef

[![hire a chef: The Ultimate Birthday Celebration for Foodies](data:image/svg+xml,%3Csvg%20xmlns='http://www.w3.org/2000/svg'%20viewBox='0%200%201024%20681'%3E%3C/svg%3E)![hire a chef: The Ultimate Birthday Celebration for Foodies](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/5/2017/12/5649643694_5439cee9ff_b.jpg)](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/5/2017/12/5649643694_5439cee9ff_b.jpg)

Want to have a dinner party at home, but do none of the cooking? It‚Äôs possible. There are many services to [hire a chef](http://www.bigcitychefs.com/newyork.html) to do all the work for you. They‚Äôll help you come up with a menu and budget. Then you can be the host(ess) with the most and have a low-stress, high-quality dinner, with a glass of wine in hand, of course.

## **Host a Cook-Off**

[![host a meatball of other cookoff at part of The Ultimate Birthday Celebration for Foodies](data:image/svg+xml,%3Csvg%20xmlns='http://www.w3.org/2000/svg'%20viewBox='0%200%201024%20681'%3E%3C/svg%3E)![host a meatball of other cookoff at part of The Ultimate Birthday Celebration for Foodies](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/5/2017/12/4345540512_d2c936ed9c_b.jpg)](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/5/2017/12/4345540512_d2c936ed9c_b.jpg)

If you want to use your birthday to show off your cooking skills, host a cook-off. Pick a food that people can prep beforehand, like meatballs or chili, and send out invites well in advance so people can carefully plan their recipes. If you want, vote and award prizes. (And yes, you can declare yourself the winner because it‚Äôs your birthday).
