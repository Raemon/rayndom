[![get some noodles nearby at Tabata Ramen: 7 Great Restaurants in Walking Distance of the Javits Center: ](data:image/svg+xml,%3Csvg%20xmlns='http://www.w3.org/2000/svg'%20viewBox='0%200%20640%20427'%3E%3C/svg%3E)![get some noodles nearby at Tabata Ramen: 7 Great Restaurants in Walking Distance of the Javits Center: ](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/5/2018/02/12286664704_822240d62c_z.jpg)](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/5/2018/02/12286664704_822240d62c_z.jpg)

Seeking great ramen less than a 15-minute walk? Get your noodle fix at Tabata. Start with the Wasabi Shumai to spice things up and then choose from 13 kinds of ramen. (Yes, 13!) # 5\. The Marshal

[![the marshall is 7 Great Restaurants in Walking Distance of the Javits Center](data:image/svg+xml,%3Csvg%20xmlns='http://www.w3.org/2000/svg'%20viewBox='0%200%20640%20427'%3E%3C/svg%3E)![the marshall is 7 Great Restaurants in Walking Distance of the Javits Center](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/5/2018/02/8637635298_fc26576c40_z.jpg)](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/5/2018/02/8637635298_fc26576c40_z.jpg)

15 minutes away from the madness of the Javits Center is the oasis of The Marshal. Here you can dig into wood-fired mussels, homemade French loaves, goat cheese-stuffed chicken, and braised lamb shanks. Local farmers market dishes and wine will give you a taste of the farm (and vineyard), right in the city. The Marshal is all about sustainability-in addition to using local food, they burn no gas and cook only with replanted apple and cherry wood from local orchards and buy wind power for their electricity needs.

[_www.the-marshal.com  
_](http://www.the-marshal.com)_628 10th Ave., New York, NY 10036_

But don‚Äôt leave without the chicken.

_[www.piopio.com  
](http://www.piopio.com)__604 10th Ave, New York, NY 10036_
