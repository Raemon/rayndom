#  3 NYC Team Building Events Your Staff Will Love 

April 26, 2018 

It‚Äôs your turn to book the department offsite and you‚Äôre out of ideas. We‚Äôve got some for you. Here are 3 team building events your staff will love.

## Share A Table (Or 4!) On A Food Tour

[![take a food tour as part of Team Building Events Your Staff Will Love](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/5/2018/04/Max-fletow.-Williamsburg-Food-Tour.-Guests-eating-at-Communal-Table-together-at-Egg-copy.jpeg)](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/5/2018/04/Max-fletow.-Williamsburg-Food-Tour.-Guests-eating-at-Communal-Table-together-at-Egg-copy.jpeg)

One of the best ways to build rapport with someone is to share a meal. During a four-course culinary experience, your team will have a chance to share a progressive meal that takes place at four restaurants. The private experience is aimed at both educating your team on the stories of food in [a](http://avitaltours.com/nyc-food-tours/)[n NYC neighborhood](http://avitaltours.com/nyc-food-tours/) and also helping them learn about each other. Your guide will facilitate some team building activities during the tour of [Williamsburg](http://avitaltours.com/nyc-food-tours/tour/tourwilliamsburg-food-tour/), [Flatiron](http://avitaltours.com/nyc-food-tours/tour/flatiron-food-tour/), or [East Village](http://avitaltours.com/nyc-food-tours/tour/east-village-food-tour/), all while tasting something new together and bonding over a love of local food, wine, and cocktails.

## Take Them To Camp

[![go camping! Team Building Events Your Staff Will Love](data:image/svg+xml,%3Csvg%20xmlns='http://www.w3.org/2000/svg'%20viewBox='0%200%202048%201356'%3E%3C/svg%3E)![go camping! Team Building Events Your Staff Will Love](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/5/2018/04/9337229921_4c464858db_k.jpg)](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/5/2018/04/9337229921_4c464858db_k.jpg)

Throw it back to the days of sleepaway camp and host a summer camp for your company, complete with tug-of-war and Color Wars. Companies like [Custom Camps ](https://customcamps.com/why-a-camp-retreat/)can plan your whole event, or reach out to local camps for hosting or big cabin or [campsite](https://newyorkstateparks.reserveamerica.com/) rentals and do the planning yourself. There‚Äôs a ton of opportunity for staff to bond over silly competition and through sharing bunk beds for a night. Plus, getting eyes away from computers and instead engaged in eye contact is a great way for teams to bond.

## Improvise

[![for Team Building Events Your Staff Will Love, try an improv class at UCB](data:image/svg+xml,%3Csvg%20xmlns='http://www.w3.org/2000/svg'%20viewBox='0%200%20600%20400'%3E%3C/svg%3E)![for Team Building Events Your Staff Will Love, try an improv class at UCB](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/5/2018/04/COMEDY.UCBlogo.jpg)](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/5/2018/04/COMEDY.UCBlogo.jpg)

Improvisation is not just a skill needed to succeed in business; it‚Äôs also a fun way to spend an afternoon of team building. New York City is lucky to be home to United Citizens Brigade ([UCB](https://east.ucbtheatre.com/)), one of the best improv theatres and schools in the country. The school teaches [corporate classes ](https://newyork.ucbtrainingcenter.com/)for companies to bond through groupthink, fun, creativity, and lots of laughs. Want to see a show, too? Get some tickets for an evening performance, seven days a week.
