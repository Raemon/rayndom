#  10 Things Everyone Should Do In Los Angeles Before They Die 

November 15, 2017 

![medium_large](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/07/Four-Fun-Day-Trips-From-Los-Angeles-ojai.jpg)

![medium_large](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/07/The-Best-Birthday-Party-Places-Los-Angeles-Has-To-Offer-wine-safari.jpg)

![medium_large](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/07/Four-Fun-Day-Trips-From-Los-Angeles-laguna-beach.jpg)

Whether you‚Äôre an LA native or just visiting, here are 10 things everyone should do in LA before they die. How many have you checked off?

## 1\. Go on a San Gabriel Valley dumpling crawl

[![10 Things Everyone Should Do In LA Before They Die: dumpling crawl](data:image/svg+xml,%3Csvg%20xmlns='http://www.w3.org/2000/svg'%20viewBox='0%200%201024%20768'%3E%3C/svg%3E)![10 Things Everyone Should Do In LA Before They Die: dumpling crawl](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/2/2017/10/4287355688_0969e5f7b1_b.jpg)](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/2/2017/10/4287355688_0969e5f7b1_b.jpg)

If you‚Äôre going to go all the way east for the best Chinese food in LA, you better make an event of it. There are plenty of lists of the best [dumpling](https://la.eater.com/maps/best-dumpling-restaurants-los-angeles-map-guide) places and even already curated [dumpling crawls](https://www.chowhound.com/post/dumpling-crawl-las-san-gabriel-valley-lucky-peach-1022598), so bring a group of friends and make a day out of it. Pro tip: bring cash. Many restaurants in SGV don‚Äôt accept credit cards and you don‚Äôt want anything to hinder your eating adventure.

## 2\. Go aboard the Queen Mary

[![Queen Mary in Long Beach: 10 Things Everyone Should Do In LA Before They Die](data:image/svg+xml,%3Csvg%20xmlns='http://www.w3.org/2000/svg'%20viewBox='0%200%20640%20480'%3E%3C/svg%3E)![Queen Mary in Long Beach: 10 Things Everyone Should Do In LA Before They Die](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/2/2017/10/5372447616_f82ab089ea_z.jpg)](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/2/2017/10/5372447616_f82ab089ea_z.jpg)

Visiting the Queen Mary is the closest we‚Äôll ever get to experience setting sail on the Titanic. The boat was the grandest ocean liner in the world in the 1930‚Äôs and is now docked in Long Beach. Go on a guided tour to get the full history of the ship. And if the haunted stories don‚Äôt scare you, you can even stay overnight in the ship‚Äôs hotel.

_[www.queenmary.com/](http://www.queenmary.com/)_

_1126 Queens Hwy, Long Beach, CA 90802_

## 3\. Visit Catalina Island

[![Boat to Catalina Island:10 Things Everyone Should Do In LA Before They Die](data:image/svg+xml,%3Csvg%20xmlns='http://www.w3.org/2000/svg'%20viewBox='0%200%20640%20427'%3E%3C/svg%3E)![Boat to Catalina Island:10 Things Everyone Should Do In LA Before They Die](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/2/2017/10/36115854855_f920700c1e_z.jpg)](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/2/2017/10/36115854855_f920700c1e_z.jpg)

It‚Äôs free on your birthday to take the ferry to and from Catalina Island, but it‚Äôs worth the trip 365 days a year. Stroll around Avalon, go hiking, camp, go sailing, and explore many of Catalina‚Äôs private coves. Expect to see lots of fish, especially if you SCUBA or snorkel. And you might even run into a bison.

[_www.visitcatalinaisland.com_](https://www.visitcatalinaisland.com/)

## 4\. Stroll the Venice Canals[![Venice canals made the list of 10 Things Everyone Should Do In LA Before They Die](data:image/svg+xml,%3Csvg%20xmlns='http://www.w3.org/2000/svg'%20viewBox='0%200%20715%20401'%3E%3C/svg%3E)![Venice canals made the list of 10 Things Everyone Should Do In LA Before They Die](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/2/2017/10/5862393574_1e58310dcf_z.jpg)](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/2/2017/10/5862393574_1e58310dcf_z.jpg)

There are not many places in Venice that look like Abbot Kinney‚Äôs original vision for Venice. The Venice Canals are some of the remaining dreams of ‚ÄúVenice of America‚Äù. Stroll around and look at beautiful homes or boat or paddleboard around and see the sights from the water. While you‚Äôre in the neighborhood head to nearby Abbot Kinney Blvd. and taste some of our [favorite local eats](http://avitaltours.com/los-angeles/tour/venice-beach-food-tour/).

[_www.alltrails.com/trail/us/california/venice-canals-trail_](http://www.alltrails.com/trail/us/california/venice-canals-trail)

_Venice, CA 90292_

## 5\. Go on a cheesy Star Tour

[![10 Things Everyone Should Do In LA Before They Die: Hollywood tour](data:image/svg+xml,%3Csvg%20xmlns='http://www.w3.org/2000/svg'%20viewBox='0%200%20640%20512'%3E%3C/svg%3E)![10 Things Everyone Should Do In LA Before They Die: Hollywood tour](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/2/2017/10/5949320491_83270fa6ef_z.jpg)](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/2/2017/10/5949320491_83270fa6ef_z.jpg)

Is it tacky? Definitely. Is it a tourist trap? For sure. But even locals should do all of the ridiculous Hollywood stuff once. After all, if you live in city of movie making, you should at least see all of those locations and star‚Äôs (alleged) homes in a silly three-hour-long bus ride.

[www.starlinetours.com/](https://www.starlinetours.com/)

## 6\. Hike up to Griffith Observatory and go to the planetarium

[![Griffith observatory is on our list of 10 Things Everyone Should Do In LA Before They Die](data:image/svg+xml,%3Csvg%20xmlns='http://www.w3.org/2000/svg'%20viewBox='0%200%20500%20375'%3E%3C/svg%3E)![Griffith observatory is on our list of 10 Things Everyone Should Do In LA Before They Die](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/2/2017/10/4326918930_4e8430a437_z.jpg)](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/2/2017/10/4326918930_4e8430a437_z.jpg)

If La La Land didn‚Äôt convince you to get up to the Girrifth Observatory planetarium, let us help. The hike up to the observatory is beautiful on its own, and then the views make the hike even more worth it. But while you‚Äôre up there, you really should see a star show. The 290-seat planetarium has live shows eight to ten times a day for just a few bucks. It‚Äôs a great date, a fun family activity, and definitely one of 10 things everyone should do in LA before they die.

_[griffithobservatory.org/programs/soplanetarium.html](http://griffithobservatory.org/programs/soplanetarium.html)_

_2800 E Observatory Rd, Los Angeles, CA 90027_

## 7\. Be on a TV show or in a movie

[![10 Things Everyone Should Do In LA Before They Die: movie](data:image/svg+xml,%3Csvg%20xmlns='http://www.w3.org/2000/svg'%20viewBox='0%200%202048%201365'%3E%3C/svg%3E)![10 Things Everyone Should Do In LA Before They Die: movie](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/2/2017/10/15820238637_f2d2d5e454_k.jpg)](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/2/2017/10/15820238637_f2d2d5e454_k.jpg)

Even if you didn‚Äôt come to LA to ‚Äúmake it‚Äù you can still get in front of the camera as an extra. The work is usually paid a daily rate and you might see yourself on the big screen. Or, if you just want to attend a television show and make a cameo in the audience, there‚Äôs an opportunity for that, too. From Ellen to Jeopardy to the Voice, there are always [free tickets](http://1iota.com/) to attend tapings. After all, LA is the entertainment capital of the world and you should see something or be seen in something before you die.

## 8\. Visit the Getty Museum AND Villa

[![10 Things Everyone Should Do In LA Before They Die: getty vlla](data:image/svg+xml,%3Csvg%20xmlns='http://www.w3.org/2000/svg'%20viewBox='0%200%201024%20768'%3E%3C/svg%3E)![10 Things Everyone Should Do In LA Before They Die: getty vlla](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/2/2017/10/8818442456_d99fa6ae67_b.jpg)](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/2/2017/10/8818442456_d99fa6ae67_b.jpg)

The Getty Villa and Museum at the Getty Center aren‚Äôt in the same place, but you should put both of them on your bucket list. The museum in west LA has paintings, sculptures, drawings, photography, and more art from Europe. The villa in Pacific Palisades houses arts and Greek and Roman antiques. Both offer beautiful views of LA and the Malibu villa is near the ocean. The Getty Center is free to visit, so there‚Äôs really no excuse not to put this on your list of 10 things everyone should do In LA before they die.

[www.getty.edu/visit/](http://www.getty.edu/visit/)

_Museum: 1200 Getty Center Dr, Los Angeles, CA 90049_

## 9\. Have a drink at the Beverly Hills Hilton

[![10 Things Everyone Should Do In LA Before They Die: beverly hills hilton](data:image/svg+xml,%3Csvg%20xmlns='http://www.w3.org/2000/svg'%20viewBox='0%200%20500%20375'%3E%3C/svg%3E)![10 Things Everyone Should Do In LA Before They Die: beverly hills hilton](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/2/2017/10/213249023_69205e0225_z.jpg)](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/2/2017/10/213249023_69205e0225_z.jpg)

A lot of Hollywood history has taken place in the Beverly Hills Hilton since its 1955 opening. You  _could_ stay there, or you can still get a feel for the ambiance and sneak a peek at who is coming and going by having a drink by the pool. You can also rent a chair at the pool for the day and pretend you‚Äôre on a staycation.

## 10\. See a hockey or basketball game at the Staples Center

[![see a game at staples center: 10 Things Everyone Should Do In LA Before They Die](data:image/svg+xml,%3Csvg%20xmlns='http://www.w3.org/2000/svg'%20viewBox='0%200%20640%20360'%3E%3C/svg%3E)![see a game at staples center: 10 Things Everyone Should Do In LA Before They Die](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/2/2017/10/4891055319_3ae2a3c6ae_z.jpg)](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/2/2017/10/4891055319_3ae2a3c6ae_z.jpg)

Go cheer on the Lakers or the Kings at Downtown LA‚Äôs Staples Center. Lakers games are always star-studded and you‚Äôll never leave a Kings‚Äô game without seeing a proper hockey fight. The stadium is easy to get to on the train so you drink as many overpriced beers as you can handle, or go to dinner [Downtown](http://avitaltours.com/los-angeles/tour/downtown-food-tour/) before or after the game.

_[www.staplescenter.com](http://www.staplescenter.com)_
