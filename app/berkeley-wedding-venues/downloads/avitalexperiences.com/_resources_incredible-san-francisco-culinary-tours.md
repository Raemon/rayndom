## **San Francisco Culinary Tour vs. Food Tour?**

You may be wondering, what exactly is a San Francisco culinary tour, and how does it differ from a food tour? In general, the terms can be used interchangeably. However, a food tour typically focuses on tasting great dishes, while a culinary tour often provides a deeper dive into the food and drink culture, including history, techniques, and unique experiences.
