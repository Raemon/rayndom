[![best food tours in san francisco includes busy streets of chinatown](data:image/svg+xml,%3Csvg%20xmlns='http://www.w3.org/2000/svg'%20viewBox='0%200%20300%20225'%3E%3C/svg%3E)![best food tours in san francisco includes busy streets of chinatown](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/3/2019/11/san-fan-chinatown-best-food-tours-in-san-francisco-300x225.jpg)](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/3/2019/11/san-fan-chinatown-best-food-tours-in-san-francisco.jpg)Photo Credit ‚Äì Andreas Wulff via Flickr
