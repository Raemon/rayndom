#  6 Super Fun Things To Do And See In San Francisco 

December 20, 2019 

When visiting a new city it can be fun to explore under-the-radar activities that only locals typically experience. But sometimes you just want to embrace your inner tourist and check off all the city‚Äôs must-see attractions. This desire becomes especially apparent in San Francisco, a city with the second-highest population density in the county and so much diversity. In San Francisco, there‚Äôs no shortage of adventurous things to do, see, and, of course, eat. And with so many options, it can be tricky to navigate and get the best bang for your buck. Don‚Äôt fret. We‚Äôve navigated the city for you and compiled the most rewarding things to do and see in San Francisco!

## **Have Tea At The Oldest Japanese Tea Garden**

[![enjoying tea at tea garden things to do and see in san francisco](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/3/2019/12/sf-japanese-tea-house-things-to-do-and-see-in-san-francisco-300x200.jpg)](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/3/2019/12/sf-japanese-tea-house-things-to-do-and-see-in-san-francisco-scaled.jpg)The [San Francisco‚Äôs Japanese Tea Garden](https://www.japaneseteagardensf.com) is a relaxing oasis in the city. This meditative garden is the oldest public Japanese garden in the country. Purchase a warm cup of hot tea and a sweet treat at the Japanese Tea House while you roam the captivating grounds. Don‚Äôt forget to stop by the Zen garden where you can watch a pool of koi fish swimming around.

## **Visit Mickey Mouse At The Walt Disney Family Museum**

[![things to do and see in san francisco includes meeting mickey mouse](data:image/svg+xml,%3Csvg%20xmlns='http://www.w3.org/2000/svg'%20viewBox='0%200%20700%20467'%3E%3C/svg%3E)![things to do and see in san francisco includes meeting mickey mouse](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/3/2019/12/walt-disney-world-museum-things-to-do-and-see-in-san-francisco-300x200.jpg)](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/3/2019/12/walt-disney-world-museum-things-to-do-and-see-in-san-francisco-scaled.jpg)[The Walt Disney Family Museum](https://www.waltdisney.org) is a must-see for every Disney movie lover. This historic museum showcases the inspirational figures and ideas that brought Walt Disney‚Äôs imagination to life. Don‚Äôt leave without a walk through the ‚Äú _Mickey Mouse: From Walt to the World‚Äù_ exhibit. It chronicles Mickey‚Äôs impact and influence on art and entertainment over the past nine decades. Come learn the story and origin of the mouse that transformed animation forever.

## **Swim In Sprinkles At The Museum Of Ice Cream**

[![swim in a pool of sprinkles things to do and see in san francisco](data:image/svg+xml,%3Csvg%20xmlns='http://www.w3.org/2000/svg'%20viewBox='0%200%20700%20467'%3E%3C/svg%3E)![swim in a pool of sprinkles things to do and see in san francisco](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/3/2019/12/museu-of-ice-cream-things-to-do-and-see-in-san-francisco-300x200.jpg)](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/3/2019/12/museu-of-ice-cream-things-to-do-and-see-in-san-francisco-scaled.jpg)[The Museum Of Ice Cream](https://www.museumoficecream.com) is a globally-acclaimed experience that has sold out across locations nationwide. It now has a permanent home in San Francisco where you are invited to enter a realm where anything is possible. Hop on a cookie carousel, take a picture with a unicorn, and indulge in ice cream at their retro diner. This bright, tasty museum is perfect for families or a day date. Just make sure you don‚Äôt leave without a slide into their famous Sprinkle Pool, which holds over 100 million custom-designed sprinkles!

## **Grab Some Samples At The Ferry Building**

[![eating ice cream cone at ferry building things to do and see in san francisco](data:image/svg+xml,%3Csvg%20xmlns='http://www.w3.org/2000/svg'%20viewBox='0%200%20700%20467'%3E%3C/svg%3E)![eating ice cream cone at ferry building things to do and see in san francisco](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/3/2019/12/ferry-building-ice-cream-cone-things-to-do-and-see-in-san-francisco-300x200.jpg)](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/3/2019/12/ferry-building-ice-cream-cone-things-to-do-and-see-in-san-francisco-scaled.jpg)Located at the center of the city‚Äôs financial, banking, and transportation district, [The Ferry Building](https://www.ferrybuildingmarketplace.com/farmers-market/) is San Francisco‚Äôs most famous landmark. Its dramatic clock tower has been the icon of the San Francisco waterfront for more than a 100 years. These days, it‚Äôs also a world-class public food market. Stop by the Farmers Market and indulge in free samples. They have everything from freshly caught fish to artisan cheeses, and organic produce. It‚Äôs one of the most diverse farmers markets in the country! We guarantee you won‚Äôt leave hungry.

## **Get A New Perspective Of San Francisco At The Hamon**

[![things to do and see in san francisco watch sunset over the city](data:image/svg+xml,%3Csvg%20xmlns='http://www.w3.org/2000/svg'%20viewBox='0%200%20700%20467'%3E%3C/svg%3E)![things to do and see in san francisco watch sunset over the city](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/3/2019/12/view-of-san-fran-sunset-things-to-do-and-see-in-san-francisco-300x200.jpg)](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/3/2019/12/view-of-san-fran-sunset-things-to-do-and-see-in-san-francisco-scaled.jpg)The Golden Gate Bridge is the most desired photo spot in San Francisco, but why not branch out and get a different view of the city? Like a 360 degree panoramic view! For no cost at all, you can go up to [The Hamon Observation Tower](https://deyoung.famsf.org/hamon-observation-tower-0). Enjoy a panoramic view of downtown San Francisco, Golden Gate Park, the Bay, and the Marin headlands. The tower is on the top floor of the [De Young Museum](https://deyoung.famsf.org). When you‚Äôre done enjoying the view, go and explore the paintings of the De Young Museum, just a floor below.

[![things to do and see in san francisco food tour in tendernob neighborhood](data:image/svg+xml,%3Csvg%20xmlns='http://www.w3.org/2000/svg'%20viewBox='0%200%20700%20467'%3E%3C/svg%3E)![things to do and see in san francisco food tour in tendernob neighborhood](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/3/2019/12/avital-tendernob-tour-things-to-do-and-see-in-san-francisco-300x200.jpg)](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/3/2019/12/avital-tendernob-tour-things-to-do-and-see-in-san-francisco.jpg)Food tours are great. But have you ever been on a culinary experience? [Avital Tours](http://avitaltours.com/san-francisco/) takes the typical food tour to the next level. Their [Tendernob Food Tour ](http://avitaltours.com/san-francisco/tour/tendernob-food-tour/)explores San Francisco‚Äôs newest and hottest food scene through a 4-course, progressive meal experience. During the experience, you‚Äôll enjoy two appetizers, one entree, and one dessert, each at a different restaurant, with a sprinkling of culinary history and stories in between. The perfect way to explore a new city.
