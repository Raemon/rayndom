Use this holiday icebreaker question to let imaginations run wild with ideas about dream party guests, from movie stars to historical figures.

### 7\. **What‚Äôs the most unusual holiday tradition you‚Äôve heard of (or participated in)?**

This fun holiday icebreaker question often leads to surprising and amusing stories, giving everyone insight into different cultural traditions.

### 8\. **Do you prefer a snowy holiday season or a warm, tropical one?**

A holiday-themed icebreaker question that taps into holiday preferences and may even inspire ideas for future vacation plans.

### 9\. **What‚Äôs one holiday food or drink you think is totally overrated?**
