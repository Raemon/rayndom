## How it Looks 

The Savory S‚Äômores Bar is a hosted station where guests choose their s‚Äômore ingredients from a variety of crackers, cheeses, meats, and, finally a selection of condiments, like fig jam and hot honey. Once the guest has their ingredients, they‚Äôll head to the roasting station (usually hosted outdoors), complete with several table top fires and roasting sticks. This is where the guests will roast their cheese, construct their s‚Äômore, and indulge. Each guest typically spends 10-15 minutes at the bar, making it a high-energy, quick-interaction experience.

For a better understanding of how our Interactive Bars fit into your event space and flow, check out the [Interactive Bar: How It Works](https://avitalexperiences.com/interactive-bars/) section.

**Note:** Venue must have refrigeration and allow an open flame on premises.

## The Essentials

##### Availability

This private experience is available any day of the week, at any time! However, the venue must have refrigeration and allow an open flame on premises. 

##### Group Size

This private experience does not have a minimum or maximum number of people, but it works best for large groups, conferences, and corporate event entertainment. Groups of 100-300 is the sweet spot! 

##### Pricing

This Activation Bar starts at $7,500. We encourage you to bundle several bars together for a better experience (and saving!) Start your booking to a get a custom quote for your event.

There is no gratuity necessary. All taxes and fees are included in the flat rate pricing.
