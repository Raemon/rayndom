#  5 Places to Get Caffeine Before Your Venice Beach Food Tour 

February 6, 2018 

We promise our tours will keep you awake, but you still might need a coffee fix before spending three hours eating and drinking your way around the neighborhood. Here are 5 places to get caffeine before your Venice tour. Venice‚Äôs funky neighborhood is filled with independent coffee and tea shops, which are favorites among locals.

## Menotti‚Äôs Coffee Stop

[![5 Places to Get Caffeine Before Your Venice Tour: Menotti's](data:image/svg+xml,%3Csvg%20xmlns='http://www.w3.org/2000/svg'%20viewBox='0%200%201024%20768'%3E%3C/svg%3E)![5 Places to Get Caffeine Before Your Venice Tour: Menotti's](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/2/2018/01/3199740319_94a33a9b81_b.jpg)](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/2/2018/01/3199740319_94a33a9b81_b.jpg)

Menotti‚Äôs mission is ‚Äúinspiring the neighborhood together, one cup at a time.‚Äù The drinks here will definitely inspire you to wake up and explore all Venice has to offer. (Especially since all shots of Espresso are a double at Menotti‚Äôs.) Grab your Spanish Latte, Chai, Cold Brew, or Cappuccino to go and head for a walk on the neighboring Venice Boardwalk. Their latte-art is award-winning, so you‚Äôll get coffee that‚Äôs both beautiful and delicious.

_[menottiscoffeeveniceca.com/  
](http://www.menottiscoffeeveniceca.com/)__56 Windward Ave, Venice, CA 90291_

## Shuhari Matcha Caf√© __

_[![Shuhari Matcha Cafe: 5 Places to Get Caffeine Before Your Venice Tour](data:image/svg+xml,%3Csvg%20xmlns='http://www.w3.org/2000/svg'%20viewBox='0%200%201024%20768'%3E%3C/svg%3E)![Shuhari Matcha Cafe: 5 Places to Get Caffeine Before Your Venice Tour](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/2/2018/01/33169643311_3edfc16d76_b.jpg)](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/2/2018/01/33169643311_3edfc16d76_b.jpg)_ Sweet dreams are made of green at Shuhai Matcha Cafe. The Japanese green tea caf√© imports its teas from Japan and serves them up on Abbot Kinney Boulevard. They focus on classic green teas as well as reinterpreting and updating green tea traditions. The most popular drink is the Matcha Latte. Go old school with a Ceremonial Matcha or try something new like a Yuzu Matcha Sparkler. Matcha is also incorporated into baked goods, milkshakes, and ice cream.

_[shuharicafe.com/  
](https://shuharicafe.com/)__1522 Abbot Kinney Blvd, Venice, CA 90291_

## Blue Bottle

[![Blue Bottle" 5 Places to Get Caffeine Before Your Venice Tour](data:image/svg+xml,%3Csvg%20xmlns='http://www.w3.org/2000/svg'%20viewBox='0%200%201000%20661'%3E%3C/svg%3E)![Blue Bottle" 5 Places to Get Caffeine Before Your Venice Tour](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/2/2018/01/Blue-Bottle.jpg)](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/2/2018/01/Blue-Bottle.jpg)

Though Blue Bottle is not just a Venice coffee shop, locals still love it, as show by the constant lines. Blue Bottle fans are loyal, and the central Abbot Kinney location is perfect for strolling around with a coffee. The experts behind the counter know a LOT about coffee-from when whole beans are at their peak flavor to when you should brew your coffee. But if you just want a simple Macchiato, they‚Äôll help you out with that, too.

_[bluebottlecoffee.com/cafes/abbot-kinney  
](http://bluebottlecoffee.com/cafes/abbot-kinney)__1103 Abbot Kinney Boulevard, Venice, CA 90291_

##  Groundwork Coffee

[![5 Places to Get Caffeine Before Your Venice Tour: Groundwork](data:image/svg+xml,%3Csvg%20xmlns='http://www.w3.org/2000/svg'%20viewBox='0%200%201024%20768'%3E%3C/svg%3E)![5 Places to Get Caffeine Before Your Venice Tour: Groundwork](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/2/2018/01/2305876203_8193947fc3_b.jpg)](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/2/2018/01/2305876203_8193947fc3_b.jpg)

Groundwork is popular enough in Venice to have both a boardwalk location and a Rose Avenue location, so it‚Äôs clear Venice locals and visitors are big fans. It makes sense that Groundwork was started right here in Venice. Their hot drinks cover the bases of coffee selection, and most can be ordered cold as well, including an Espresso Milkshake, Horchata Cold Brew, and Vietnamese Iced Coffee. The organic tea selection includes many offerings of herbal, green, black, and rare.

_[www.groundworkcoffee.com  
](http://www.groundworkcoffee.com)__671 Rose Ave, Venice, CA 90291 AND 3 Westminster Ave, Venice, CA 90291._

## Intelligentsia Coffee

[![head to Intelligentsia Coffee as part of 5 Places to Get Caffeine Before Your Venice Tour](data:image/svg+xml,%3Csvg%20xmlns='http://www.w3.org/2000/svg'%20viewBox='0%200%201024%20768'%3E%3C/svg%3E)![head to Intelligentsia Coffee as part of 5 Places to Get Caffeine Before Your Venice Tour](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/2/2018/01/4433845079_d660976930_b.jpg)](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/2/2018/01/4433845079_d660976930_b.jpg)

At Intelligentsia Coffee‚Äôs Venice location, you‚Äôll find locals working on laptops at the counter, catching up at table‚Äôs, and enjoying some of LA‚Äôs best coffee. Their single-origin coffees are sourced from small farms so you can feel good about where your drink is coming from. The menu is a classic coffee menu that also has a large variety of teas and a few chocolate drinks.

[_www.intelligentsiacoffee.com/venice-coffeebar  
_](https://www.intelligentsiacoffee.com/venice-coffeebar)_1331 Abbot Kinney Blvd, Venice, CA 90291_
