## How it Looks 

The Hands-On Cocktail Bar is a hosted station where guests move through each step, starting with adding spirits and other ingredients into their shaker (shaken cocktail) or mixing beaker (stirred cocktails). Then they‚Äôll shake or stir their cocktail with expert guidance before pouring into a drinking glass and garnishing. Each guest typically spends 5-10 minutes at the bar, making it a high-energy, quick-interaction experience.

For a better understanding of how our Interactive Bars fit into your event space and flow, check out the [Interactive Bar: How It Works](https://avitalexperiences.com/product/custom-events/) section.
