#  10 Things Everyone Should Do In SF Before They Die 

October 25, 2017 

Whether you‚Äôre a San Francisco native or just visiting, here are 10 things everyone should do in SF before they die. How many have you checked off?

## 1\. Eat at Swan Oyster Depot

[![eating at swan oyster depot is on our list of 10 Things Everyone Should Do In SF Before They Die](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/3/2017/10/1356691373_157a9f72fc_b.jpg)](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/3/2017/10/1356691373_157a9f72fc_b.jpg)

There‚Äôs a reason Anthony Bourdain raves about Swan Oyster Depot and that there‚Äôs a line even on Tuesday mornings. Swan Oyster Depot has some of the freshest fish and most personable service in the city, which is probably why the line takes so long. Call in sick and go on a weekday morning for opening, when the line is a _little_ bit shorter than usual. Go alone if you want to score a faster seat (there are just a handful at the counter) or go with a friend if you want to order all the all-stars like the combination seafood salad, the half-cracked crab, scallop sashimi, smoked salmon, oysters, and a couple of Anchor Steam beers. The Sicilian sashimi isn‚Äôt on the menu but you should still order it. Trust us: you don‚Äôt want to die without trying it.

_[www.swanoysterdepot.us/](http://swanoysterdepot.us/)_

_1517 Polk St San Francisco, CA 94109_

## 2\. Watch the Hunky Jesus contest

[![Hunky Jesus is one of the 10 Things Everyone Should Do In SF Before They Die: ](data:image/svg+xml,%3Csvg%20xmlns='http://www.w3.org/2000/svg'%20viewBox='0%200%20640%20426'%3E%3C/svg%3E)![Hunky Jesus is one of the 10 Things Everyone Should Do In SF Before They Die: ](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/3/2017/10/6917606284_277f6f1b6f_z.jpg)](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/3/2017/10/6917606284_277f6f1b6f_z.jpg)

Skip the Easter egg hunt and head to the Hunky Jesus contest hosted by the [Sisters of Perpetual Indulgence. ](https://www.thesisters.org/)The event crowns one SF local as Hunky Jesus and another as Foxy Mary. The audience sits in Golden Gate Park and enjoys their Easter picnic brunch with a bit of debauchery, hooting, and hollering. It‚Äôs weird, it‚Äôs wild, and it‚Äôs oh so San Francisco.

_[www.thesisters.org/](https://www.thesisters.org/)_

## 3\. Drink at the Tonga Room

[![Tonga Room: 10 Things Everyone Should Do In SF Before They Die](data:image/svg+xml,%3Csvg%20xmlns='http://www.w3.org/2000/svg'%20viewBox='0%200%20640%20426'%3E%3C/svg%3E)![Tonga Room: 10 Things Everyone Should Do In SF Before They Die](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/3/2016/12/One-The-Best-Happy-Hours-in-Union-Square-is-at-the-Tonga-Room.jpg)](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/3/2016/12/One-The-Best-Happy-Hours-in-Union-Square-is-at-the-Tonga-Room.jpg)

We‚Äôve written about the Tonga Room before. We think it‚Äôs one of the best places to have a [birthday party](http://avitaltours.com/san-francisco/40th-birthday-party-ideas-in-san-francisco), it has a rocking [happy hour](http://avitaltours.com/san-francisco/best-happy-hours-in-union-square), and even has a [buffet](http://avitaltours.com/san-francisco/san-franciscos-10-best-buffets/). The point is: go here. It‚Äôs one of San Francisco‚Äôs quirkiest spots and the only place in the city with scheduled thunderstorms on top of a cover band. The drinks are fun and fruity, the music is great, and it‚Äôs worth the walk uphill. Don‚Äôt leave SF without a pina colada and some wild Tonga Room stories.

_[www.tongaroom.com/](http://www.tongaroom.com/)_

_950 Mason St, San Francisco, CA 94108_

## 4\. Participate in Bay to Breakers

[![10 Things Everyone Should Do In SF Before They Die: run bay to breakers](data:image/svg+xml,%3Csvg%20xmlns='http://www.w3.org/2000/svg'%20viewBox='0%200%20640%20427'%3E%3C/svg%3E)![10 Things Everyone Should Do In SF Before They Die: run bay to breakers](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/3/2017/10/7236753598_3397cf9e1a_z.jpg)](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/3/2017/10/7236753598_3397cf9e1a_z.jpg)

Bay to Breakers is one of the wackiest of the 10 things everyone should do in SF before they die. No, you don‚Äôt have to run this annual footrace, but you do have to don your wackiest costume if you want to fit in. The race has been yelled since 1912. Runners start earlier and efficiently make their way across the city, while the rest of the crowd trails behind and parties all day long. (With many breaks for snacks, drinks, and resting in parks.) You can try to make it all the way to the Pacific Ocean, but what really matters is celebrating this annual can‚Äôt-miss SF tradition.

_[www.baytobreakers.com/](https://baytobreakers.com/)_

## 5\. Go to the top of Mount Tam

[![the view from mt tam is one of 10 Things Everyone Should Do In SF Before They Die](data:image/svg+xml,%3Csvg%20xmlns='http://www.w3.org/2000/svg'%20viewBox='0%200%20640%20425'%3E%3C/svg%3E)![the view from mt tam is one of 10 Things Everyone Should Do In SF Before They Die](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/3/2017/10/14480678700_91c1f8ff1a_z.jpg)](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/3/2017/10/14480678700_91c1f8ff1a_z.jpg)

For one of the best views of the Bay, head north to Mount Tamalpais (Mount Tam for short). You can spend a day (or days) exploring the redwoods forests, hiking trails, biking areas, and sneaking peeks of animals. Make a stay-cation of it and spend the night of one of many campsites or lodges. The [West Point Inn](https://www.westpointinn.com/) has rooms in its main inn, but the better views come with the cabins, some of which have outdoor showers with a view. They also host pancake breakfasts that hikers and guests are welcome to join. If you‚Äôre feeling ambitious, hike to Stinson Beach for a swim and an ice cream. You can even [paraglide](http://www.paragliding-lessons.com/mt-tamalpais/) down the mountain or see a [theatrical](https://mountainplay.org/) performance up top.

_[www.parks.ca.gov/](https://www.parks.ca.gov/?page_id=471)_

## 6\. Take part in the Big Wheel Race

[![10 Things Everyone Should Do In SF Before They Die: Big Wheel Race](data:image/svg+xml,%3Csvg%20xmlns='http://www.w3.org/2000/svg'%20viewBox='0%200%20800%20600'%3E%3C/svg%3E)![10 Things Everyone Should Do In SF Before They Die: Big Wheel Race](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/3/2017/08/129810870_47ef21e257_o.jpg)](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/3/2017/08/129810870_47ef21e257_o.jpg)

One of SF‚Äôs most popular (and quirky) traditions is the [Bring Your Own Big Wheel Race](http://bringyourownbigwheel.com/). It only happens once a year on Easter Sunday, so make sure you‚Äôre around to knock it off your bucket list. A few weeks before the event, scour thrift stores and the Internet for a kid‚Äôs sized big wheel bike so you can spend a few hours racing down Potrero Hill‚Äôs winding Vermont Street. The street is closed off to traffic and lined with hay bales just on this day, so make sure you‚Äôre there.

_[www.bringyourownbigwheel.com/](http://bringyourownbigwheel.com/)_

## 7\. Have tea at the Palace Hotel

[![Tea at the Palace Hotel: 10 Things Everyone Should Do In SF Before They Die](data:image/svg+xml,%3Csvg%20xmlns='http://www.w3.org/2000/svg'%20viewBox='0%200%201024%20768'%3E%3C/svg%3E)![Tea at the Palace Hotel: 10 Things Everyone Should Do In SF Before They Die](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/3/2017/10/2757607880_ed955e3599_b.jpg)](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/3/2017/10/2757607880_ed955e3599_b.jpg)

Live your best and most posh life while partaking in one of SF‚Äôs best traditions-tea at the Palace Hotel. The Garden Court is a stunning place to spend the afternoon eating tiny sandwiches and scones, as locals have been doing since the early 1900‚Äôs. Forgo your regular brunch spot to cross this one of the list. Just remember to make a reservation and wear your most elegant attire.

_[www.sfpalace.com/signature-tea-san-francisco/](https://www.sfpalace.com/signature-tea-san-francisco/)_

_2 New Montgomery St, San Francisco, CA 94105_

## 8\. Eat at one of San Francisco‚Äôs best restaurants.

[![10 Things Everyone Should Do In SF Before They Die: sf restaurants](data:image/svg+xml,%3Csvg%20xmlns='http://www.w3.org/2000/svg'%20viewBox='0%200%201024%20693'%3E%3C/svg%3E)![10 Things Everyone Should Do In SF Before They Die: sf restaurants](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/3/2017/01/mission-style-burrito-lunch-and-learn-culinary-history-in-san-francisco.jpg)](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/3/2017/01/mission-style-burrito-lunch-and-learn-culinary-history-in-san-francisco.jpg)

There are lots of lists that list SF‚Äôs best eats, from [7√ó7](http://www.7x7.com/big-eat-sf-2017-2322286078.html) to [Eater‚Äôs](http://projects.sfchronicle.com/2017/top-100-restaurants/) Essentials. The [SF Chronicle](http://projects.sfchronicle.com/2017/top-100-restaurants/) also publishes a yearly ‚Äúbest of‚Äù list. While the amount of great food can be overwhelming, there‚Äôs a lot of overlap and repeats. A lot of SF‚Äôs classics like Zuni Cafe and Tartine Bakery are recurring members on this list. Treat yourself and choose at least one great restaurant to indulge. Or, make a day of it and hit up a few of the city‚Äôs most celebrated eats on our [culinary experience](http://avitaltours.com/).

## 9\. Watch the sunset in The Sunset

[![Sunset: 10 Things Everyone Should Do In SF Before They Die:](data:image/svg+xml,%3Csvg%20xmlns='http://www.w3.org/2000/svg'%20viewBox='0%200%20640%20427'%3E%3C/svg%3E)![Sunset: 10 Things Everyone Should Do In SF Before They Die:](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/3/2017/10/10739646133_e336bdf67a_z.jpg)](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/3/2017/10/10739646133_e336bdf67a_z.jpg)

Go explore one of the most underrated neighborhoods in San Francisco, where on a clear day, the sunset is spectacular. The beach is dog-friendly, so bring your pup for a run on the beach. Then, watch some surfers sneak in their last waves, have a bonfire at Ocean Beach, get a snack at [Java Beach Cafe](http://www.javabeachcafe.com/), or go for late night dumplings at [Kingdom of Dumpling.](http://www.kingofchinesedumpling.com/english/contact.htm)

_Ocean Beach, San Francisco_

## 10\. Drive down Lombard Street

[![10 Things Everyone Should Do In SF Before They Die: drive down lombard](data:image/svg+xml,%3Csvg%20xmlns='http://www.w3.org/2000/svg'%20viewBox='0%200%20640%20427'%3E%3C/svg%3E)![10 Things Everyone Should Do In SF Before They Die: drive down lombard](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/3/2017/10/7442365504_e6d71b1dd6_z.jpg)](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/3/2017/10/7442365504_e6d71b1dd6_z.jpg)

Is it touristy? Definitely. Is it worth waiting in the line of cars? Maybe. But if you haven‚Äôt done it, have you really ever been to San Francisco? Just drive down SF‚Äôs most crooked street to say you have‚Ä¶plus, it‚Äôs pretty fun.

_Lombard Street, San Francisco_
