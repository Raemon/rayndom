#  10 Fun and Frugal Things To Do in Manhattan 

March 9, 2018 

When the cost of city living (or visiting) gets prohibitive, it‚Äôs important to find fun and frugal things to do in Manhattan. From parades to pizza crawls, we have 10 ways to keep having fun on the cheap.

## 1\. Catch A Parade

[![watch a parade: 10 Fun and Frugal Things To Do in Manhattan](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/5/2018/02/6363063729_41be5344b2_b.jpg)](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/5/2018/02/6363063729_41be5344b2_b.jpg)

From the Macy‚Äôs Day Parade to the Puerto Rican Day celebration to the Tompkins Square Dog Parade, Manhattan hosts cultural celebrations all year round. All [parades](https://www.tripsavvy.com/most-famous-nyc-parades-2286681) are free to attend and the streets turn into an outdoor party. Find what [events](http://www1.nyc.gov/events/index.page) are coming up and plan a morning of an afternoon watching.

## 2\. See Shakespeare, For Free

[![see a play in the park: 10 Fun and Frugal Things To Do in Manhattan](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/5/2018/02/hero-image_SITP15.jpg)](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/5/2018/02/hero-image_SITP15.jpg)

[Shakespeare in the Park](https://www.publictheater.org/en/Programs--Events/Shakespeare-in-the-Park/) is one of the most fun free cultural events in Manhattan. Put on by The Public Theater, free tickets are distributed on performance dates by waiting in line or entering a digital lottery. This summer the theater is putting on  _Othello_ and _Twelfth Night._

## 3\. Have A Brooklyn Bridge Photo Shoot

[![brooklyn bridge shoot: 10 Fun and Frugal Things To Do in Manhattan](data:image/svg+xml,%3Csvg%20xmlns='http://www.w3.org/2000/svg'%20viewBox='0%200%202048%201382'%3E%3C/svg%3E)![brooklyn bridge shoot: 10 Fun and Frugal Things To Do in Manhattan](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/5/2018/02/34886739313_bd933623f6_k.jpg)](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/5/2018/02/34886739313_bd933623f6_k.jpg)

Could you pay a lot of money for a copy of your family photo on top of the Empire State Building? Sure. But for the cost of exactly $0, you can have your own photoshoot with one of the best views of the city that doesn‚Äôt require an entrance free-the Brooklyn Bridge. (Bonus points if you catch sunrise or sunset.) The Manhattan skyline is the perfect backdrop for your Instagram photos, holiday cards, and new profile pics. Wear something fabulous and make it a real event. Or make it a bit more fun by hiring someone to snap your pictures. NYC has many art schools where you can find a student photographer for a fraction of the cost as a professional.

## 4\. Attend A Free Concert

[![musical festival and concerts are some of :10 Fun and Frugal Things To Do in Manhattan](data:image/svg+xml,%3Csvg%20xmlns='http://www.w3.org/2000/svg'%20viewBox='0%200%201280%20960'%3E%3C/svg%3E)![musical festival and concerts are some of :10 Fun and Frugal Things To Do in Manhattan](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/5/2018/02/Concert-Festival-Music-Entertainment-Crowd-1421932.jpg)](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/5/2018/02/Concert-Festival-Music-Entertainment-Crowd-1421932.jpg)

NYC Parks hosts free summer concerts in the park, which attract hundreds of people. They also host some more low-key [year-round](https://www.nycgovparks.org/events/concerts) events, like swing dancing nights and opera events. Some events are free, but others have a minimal ticket cost. [Central Park](https://www.centralpark.com/things-to-do/concerts) is home to many free concerts, some sponsored by  _Good Morning America_ or the [Philharmonic](https://www.centralpark.com/things-to-do/concerts/new-york-philharmonic/).

## 5\. Plan A Pizza Crawl

[![Plan a pizza crawl: 10 Fun and Frugal Things To Do in Manhattan](data:image/svg+xml,%3Csvg%20xmlns='http://www.w3.org/2000/svg'%20viewBox='0%200%201024%20683'%3E%3C/svg%3E)![Plan a pizza crawl: 10 Fun and Frugal Things To Do in Manhattan](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/5/2018/02/6211931329_6e56b443de_b.jpg)](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/5/2018/02/6211931329_6e56b443de_b.jpg)

NYC is said to have some of the best pizza in the country (or world, depending on which New Yorker you ask). Grab some friends and go on a pizza crawl. Pick 4-5 spots and get a slice at each (some are as low as a dollar!), or if you have enough of a crew, split a pizza at each spot. Of course, if you‚Äôre really hungry, you can visit more than 5. Come up with a rating system and track each slice. Here are [some ideas](https://www.thrillist.com/eat/new-york/the-best-pizza-in-new-york-city) for creating your pizza plan.

## 6\. Take A Dance Class

[![dance class: 10 Fun and Frugal Things To Do in Manhattan](data:image/svg+xml,%3Csvg%20xmlns='http://www.w3.org/2000/svg'%20viewBox='0%200%201600%201064'%3E%3C/svg%3E)![dance class: 10 Fun and Frugal Things To Do in Manhattan](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/5/2018/02/30320700342_1c8c2b0a4f_h.jpg)](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/5/2018/02/30320700342_1c8c2b0a4f_h.jpg)

Get groovin‚Äô in a Manhattan dance class. You can search [Groupon](https://www.groupon.com/local/new-york-city/dance-classes) for cheap classes or head to a number of studios for beginners around the city. Take anything from [Bollywood & Bhangra](http://ajnadance.com/adult-classes/) (drop-in classes are just $20 or five for $75) to [Jazz and Ballet](http://broadwaydancecenter.com/schedule) to [Hip Hop](http://broadwaydancecenter.com/special-event/hip-hop-foundation-series) ($22 for a 90-minute class).

## 7\. Soak It Up

[![take a soak: 10 Fun and Frugal Things To Do in Manhattan: ](data:image/svg+xml,%3Csvg%20xmlns='http://www.w3.org/2000/svg'%20viewBox='0%200%202048%201536'%3E%3C/svg%3E)![take a soak: 10 Fun and Frugal Things To Do in Manhattan: ](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/5/2018/02/8017983823_2324b18b1d_k.jpg)](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/5/2018/02/8017983823_2324b18b1d_k.jpg)

Spas trips aren‚Äôt something that can usually be done on the cheap, but at the Russian and Turkish Baths in the East Village, you can use the five saunas and steam rooms, cold plunge pool, and roof deck all day for $48. Of course, if you want to splurge you can add on massage services and other treatments, but if you‚Äôre just looking to relax and soak, the day pass is a great deal. Make sure you [check the calendar](http://www.russianturkishbaths.com/hours) before heading over; some days are female only, some are male only, and others are co-ed.

## 8\. Take A Bike Ride

[![take a bike ride: 10 Fun and Frugal Things To Do in Manhattan](data:image/svg+xml,%3Csvg%20xmlns='http://www.w3.org/2000/svg'%20viewBox='0%200%202048%201365'%3E%3C/svg%3E)![take a bike ride: 10 Fun and Frugal Things To Do in Manhattan](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/5/2018/02/32718227774_af16598ccc_k.jpg)](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/5/2018/02/32718227774_af16598ccc_k.jpg)

What‚Äôs cheaper than traveling around by rideshare, taxi, bus, or Subway? Biking. (Well, yes, walking, too‚Ä¶but biking is faster and more fun.) There are plenty of[ bike trails and places to ride ](https://www.nycgovparks.org/facilities/bikeways)in Manhattan (and the other boroughs). Head to the Hudson River Greenway which stretches from Battery Park to Fort Tryon Park. On the way, you‚Äôll pass the East Coast Memorial, Intrepid Sea, Air, and Space Museum, Soldiers‚Äô and Sailors‚Äô Monument, Little Red Lighthouse, and Heather Garden. Don‚Äôt have your own bike? No problem. Snag a [Citi Bike](https://www.citibikenyc.com/), or visit [BikeRent NYC](https://bikerent.nyc/).

## 9\. Learn Something New

[![take a free yoga class from Club Free Time: 10 Fun and Frugal Things To Do in Manhattan](data:image/svg+xml,%3Csvg%20xmlns='http://www.w3.org/2000/svg'%20viewBox='0%200%202048%201365'%3E%3C/svg%3E)![take a free yoga class from Club Free Time: 10 Fun and Frugal Things To Do in Manhattan](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/5/2018/02/7355248000_0dd5e2cf88_k.jpg)](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/5/2018/02/7355248000_0dd5e2cf88_k.jpg)

[Club Free Time ](https://www.clubfreetime.com/new-york-city-nyc/free-workshops) lists free and donation-based classes all around the city. From French classes to meditation to public speaking workshops, there‚Äôs always something offered. Join the club and find something new to do. Looking for more? The [New York Public Library](https://www.nypl.org/events/classes/calendar) also lists free classes and events daily.

## 10\. Learn To Sail

[![learn to sail in NYC! 10 Fun and Frugal Things To Do in Manhattan](data:image/svg+xml,%3Csvg%20xmlns='http://www.w3.org/2000/svg'%20viewBox='0%200%201024%20866'%3E%3C/svg%3E)![learn to sail in NYC! 10 Fun and Frugal Things To Do in Manhattan](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/5/2018/02/4682609487_007a305680_b.jpg)](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/5/2018/02/4682609487_007a305680_b.jpg)

Owning a boat is expensive. Learning to sail doesn‚Äôt have to be. An introductory sailing class from [Hudson Sailing](https://hudsonsailing.org/sailing/lessons/) is just $95 for three hours in Chelsea. You‚Äôll get brief on-land safety and theoretical lesson and then hit the seas for hands-on learning. If you just want to  _go_ for a sail as a passenger, thats an option, too.
