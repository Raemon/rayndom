## **What are Corporate Team Building Events?**

Corporate team building events are when a group of colleagues are brought together for an event or activity that extends beyond standard office setting and tasks. The purpose of team building is to create a stronger connection and strengthen relationships between coworkers. Team building activities come in many forms, but all aim to create connection and improve communication, which typically result in more productivity and cooperation back in the office.

Avital specializes in corporate team building through interactive food and drinks experiences. We believe sharing food and drink is the ultimate way to connect in an authentic, inclusive, and fun way.
