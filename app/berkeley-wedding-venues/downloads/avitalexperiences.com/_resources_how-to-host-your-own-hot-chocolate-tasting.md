## How to Host Your Own Hot Chocolate Tasting

![medium](data:image/svg+xml,%3Csvg%20xmlns='http://www.w3.org/2000/svg'%20viewBox='0%200%20150%20150'%3E%3C/svg%3E)![medium](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/2025/12/several-hot-chocolate-boxes-sitting-on-table-for-hot-chocolate-tasting-150x150.png)

##### **01: Pick Your Hot Chocolates**

Check out our [Amazon list](https://www.amazon.com/hz/wishlist/ls/2D2OIWRNIRA2F?ref_=wl_share) to make shopping easy.

[Shop Amazon](https://www.amazon.com/hz/wishlist/ls/2D2OIWRNIRA2F?ref_=wl_share)

![medium](data:image/svg+xml,%3Csvg%20xmlns='http://www.w3.org/2000/svg'%20viewBox='0%200%20150%20150'%3E%3C/svg%3E)![medium](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/2025/12/mixing-hot-chocolate-for-hot-chocolate-tasting-150x150.png)

##### **02: Sort by Category & Prepare**

Group hot chocolates into categories; we recommend 3-4. Then prepare each hot chocolate the same way so you can compare them fairly (i.e. use only milk or only water, not both).

![medium](data:image/svg+xml,%3Csvg%20xmlns='http://www.w3.org/2000/svg'%20viewBox='0%200%20150%20150'%3E%3C/svg%3E)![medium](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/2025/12/yasmin-sipping-on-hot-chocolate-150x150.png)

##### **03: Observe, Taste, and Smell**

Start by tasting all the hot chocolates in one category before moving on to the next.

![medium](data:image/svg+xml,%3Csvg%20xmlns='http://www.w3.org/2000/svg'%20viewBox='0%200%20150%20150'%3E%3C/svg%3E)![medium](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/2024/07/tinned-fish-tasting-notes-card-on-table-top-150x150.jpg)

##### **04: Take Notes**

Jot down your impressions of each hot chocolate. Notice the differences in flavor, but also smell and texture. Download our tasting notes template to get started.

[Download PDF](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/2025/08/Mustard-Tasting-Notes.pdf)

![medium](data:image/svg+xml,%3Csvg%20xmlns='http://www.w3.org/2000/svg'%20viewBox='0%200%20150%20150'%3E%3C/svg%3E)![medium](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/2025/12/prepared-hot-chocolate-with-packaging-150x150.png)

##### **05: Choose Winners**

When you‚Äôre done, crown a winner in each category. You can also choose an ultimate winner!
