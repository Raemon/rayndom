#  Guide to Corporate Team Building in NYC 

December 25, 2017 

Your team works hard, So it‚Äôs important that you provide intentional team building opportunities to keep the momentum going. Team building activities can be a reward, a thank you, and a way to bond and recharge. Our 2018 guide to corporate team building in NYC has some ideas of how to do that.

## Get Offsite

[![2018 Guide to Corporate Team Building in NYC: get offsite](data:image/svg+xml,%3Csvg%20xmlns='http://www.w3.org/2000/svg'%20viewBox='0%200%202800%201866'%3E%3C/svg%3E)![2018 Guide to Corporate Team Building in NYC: get offsite](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/5/2014/02/Corporate-Team-Building-in-NYC-guests-drinking-wine-at-trattoria.jpeg)](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/5/2014/02/Corporate-Team-Building-in-NYC-guests-drinking-wine-at-trattoria.jpeg)

Working in New York City means that your backyard is one of the most exciting places in the world. So why would you stay in your conference room to do team building? Get out and have a change of scenery. Sit around a table in a restaurant, do teambuilding on an improv comedy stage, get out of an Escape Room. Whatever you do, make sure you‚Äôre doing it somewhere different than the norm.

## Feed Your Team

[![behind the scenes on a corporate team building nyc food tour in the kitchen eating ice cream](data:image/svg+xml,%3Csvg%20xmlns='http://www.w3.org/2000/svg'%20viewBox='0%200%202200%201467'%3E%3C/svg%3E)![behind the scenes on a corporate team building nyc food tour in the kitchen eating ice cream](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/5/2014/02/Corporate-Team-Building-in-NYC-in-the-kitchen.jpeg)](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/5/2014/02/Corporate-Team-Building-in-NYC-in-the-kitchen.jpeg)

We promise that your colleagues will be much more engaged in team building if they‚Äôre well fed. Get something catered, or go a step further and plan a food-centered event. Yes, your team can bond around food. A cooking class is a great way to get everyone working together toward a common goal. Or a [team building food tour](http://avitaltours.com/nyc-food-tours/corporate-team-building-food-tours/) incorporates get-to-know-you activities and a lot of time sitting around tables learning about each other.

## Make It Fun, Not Forced

[![avital's guide to 2018 Guide to Corporate Team Building in NYC](data:image/svg+xml,%3Csvg%20xmlns='http://www.w3.org/2000/svg'%20viewBox='0%200%20640%20427'%3E%3C/svg%3E)![avital's guide to 2018 Guide to Corporate Team Building in NYC](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/5/2017/12/6352364339_874efd25d0_z.jpg)](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/5/2017/12/6352364339_874efd25d0_z.jpg)

Some people dread team building because it conjures up memories of group games and forced hanging out. Plan something fun that doesn‚Äôt make anyone on your team feel like they‚Äôre being forced into spending extra time with colleagues. Make sure the team building is either on company time, or people are being compensated for their extra time. And make sure that no one is forced to do anything they are uncomfortable with. (Of course, this is different than stepping a bit outside of their comfort zone.)

## Make Sure Everyone Participates

[![2018 Guide to Corporate Team Building in NYC: get everyone on board](data:image/svg+xml,%3Csvg%20xmlns='http://www.w3.org/2000/svg'%20viewBox='0%200%201024%20520'%3E%3C/svg%3E)![2018 Guide to Corporate Team Building in NYC: get everyone on board](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/5/2017/12/3834339399_32c1361b95_b.jpg)](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/5/2017/12/3834339399_32c1361b95_b.jpg)

People of all corporate levels on the same team should be equally involved in team building. It‚Äôs important that everyone from senior staffers to new hires participate. Even if some members of the team don‚Äôt work together very much, a corporate team building is a great opportunity to learn more about each other. The inclusion will show that bonding is valuable for the entire team. The more the merrier, right?
