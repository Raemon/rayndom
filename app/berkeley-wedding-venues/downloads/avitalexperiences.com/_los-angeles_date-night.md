## **What is a LA Date Night?**

[Date Nights](https://avitalexperiences.com/date-night/) in Los Angeles are arranged outings or events with your partner. Typical date nights include dinner and a movie, drinks at a new bar, taking a hike, or even making dinner at home. Date nights are an opportunity to connect with your partner in a relaxed, fun environment that you both enjoy. 

When it comes to strengthening your relationship, there are many date night ideas to choose from.**We believe sharing food and drink is the ultimate way to connect with your partner in Los Angeles.**
