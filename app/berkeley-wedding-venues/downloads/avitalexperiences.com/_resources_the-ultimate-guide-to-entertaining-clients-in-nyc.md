#  The Ultimate Guide To Entertaining Clients in NYC 

February 24, 2020 

Entertaining clients in NYC doesn‚Äôt just have to be dinner and drinks. After the meetings are over and it‚Äôs time to take your best clients out, getting creative can help seal the deal, maintain a solid business relationship, and show that you put in a little extra effort to plan something special. Our ultimate guide to entertaining clients in NYC will help you put together an itinerary that‚Äôs sure to impress. 

## **Put a little extra ‚Äúhappy‚Äù in happy hour with cocktail-making and games**

[![hands on cocktail class is best way of entertaining clients in NYC](data:image/svg+xml,%3Csvg%20xmlns='http://www.w3.org/2000/svg'%20viewBox='0%200%20300%20200'%3E%3C/svg%3E)![hands on cocktail class is best way of entertaining clients in NYC](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/5/2020/02/hands-on-cocktails-entertaining-clients-in-NYC-300x200.jpg)](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/5/2020/02/hands-on-cocktails-entertaining-clients-in-NYC.jpg)

We don‚Äôt mean just pulling out the giant jenga, although that would be fun too! Get your clients to show off their mixology skills as you learn how to make a signature cocktail‚Äî an event that can be done in-person, virtually, or even hybrid style. Impress your clients with [a personal lesson from a professional mixologist](http://avitaltours.com/virtual-happy-hour/). Getting attention from a bartender can be hard enough in bar, but there‚Äôs no fighting for attention in a private mixology class! Plus, the knowledge and tips your clients will gain are invaluable. How often do clients get to leave an event with skills they are proud of? At the very least they will have a tasty drink in their hands! 

## **Get outdoorsy**

[![walk in the park for entertaining clients in NYC](data:image/svg+xml,%3Csvg%20xmlns='http://www.w3.org/2000/svg'%20viewBox='0%200%20300%20199'%3E%3C/svg%3E)![walk in the park for entertaining clients in NYC](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/5/2020/02/outdoor-walk-entertaining-clients-in-NYC-300x199.jpg)](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/5/2020/02/outdoor-walk-entertaining-clients-in-NYC.jpg)

If your clients are the adventurous type or are in town for the weekend, try spending some time outdoors. There are plenty of outdoor options for entertaining clients in NYC, ranging from strolling through one of the city‚Äôs many parks to jet skiing. Central Park has [lots of tours](https://www.centralparkdiscovery.com/?nt=g&kw=central%20park%20tours&mt=e&gclid=Cj0KCQiAkKnyBRDwARIsALtxe7iccZK5WGWKfN-0io1se2noqObTN68f5Qams71jTGcDAgU2_zdijDMaAqWOEALw_wcB) to choose from, but a simple picnic will also do the trick. If your group is a little less inclined to spend time outdoors, the Brooklyn Botanic Garden is just the right amount of nature. You can‚Äôt deny NYC is full of buildings, but there‚Äôs still a lot of natural beauty to be seen. And be sure to check out [our guide](http://avitaltours.com/nyc-food-tours/create-your-own-nyc-scavenger-hunt/) to creating your own NYC scavenger hunt for an outdoor activity and some friendly competition. 

## **Leave the city for a day trip**

[![wine tasting for entertaining clients ](data:image/svg+xml,%3Csvg%20xmlns='http://www.w3.org/2000/svg'%20viewBox='0%200%20300%20200'%3E%3C/svg%3E)![wine tasting for entertaining clients ](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/5/2020/02/trip-to-wine-country-entertaining-clients-in-NYC-300x200.jpg)](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/5/2020/02/trip-to-wine-country-entertaining-clients-in-NYC.jpg)

Who says you have to stay in NYC to entertain your clients? Even if it‚Äôs booking a restaurant with a view of Lower Manhattan in New Jersey, give your clients a little break from the hustle. For example, visiting the [Storm King Art Center](https://stormking.org/) is a great way to take in some culture, fresh air, and do entertaining in a creative space. If you need to get work done, consider booking an offsite meeting near a winery, and head there afterwards. There‚Äôs a lot to appreciate about New York state and the surrounding areas, and your clients will appreciate you thinking outside of the box to do something fun. 

## **Dine in a hidden restaurant**

[![hidden restaurant for entertaining clients in NYC](data:image/svg+xml,%3Csvg%20xmlns='http://www.w3.org/2000/svg'%20viewBox='0%200%20300%20199'%3E%3C/svg%3E)![hidden restaurant for entertaining clients in NYC](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/5/2020/02/secret-bar-entertaining-clients-in-NYC-300x199.jpg)](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/5/2020/02/secret-bar-entertaining-clients-in-NYC.jpg)

Just like there are hidden bars and speakeasies, hidden restaurants are also out there waiting to be discovered. Hidden restaurants feel exclusive, and offer a unique experience not everyone knows about it. Some hidden restaurants like Bohemian have a very difficult to book reservation system, while others like [Frevo](http://frevonyc.com/) or [Chef‚Äôs Table at Brooklyn Fare](https://www.brooklynfare.com/pages/chefs-table) are easier. Chances are your clients aren‚Äôt looking for the secrets the city has to offer, but that‚Äôs where you come in ‚Äî show off the NYC they aren‚Äôt necessarily looking for and give them something to rave about back home. 

## Connect with clients on a food tour

[![entertaining-clients-in-nyc-with-food-tours](data:image/svg+xml,%3Csvg%20xmlns='http://www.w3.org/2000/svg'%20viewBox='0%200%20700%20467'%3E%3C/svg%3E)![entertaining-clients-in-nyc-with-food-tours](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/5/2021/07/guests-eating-dinner-together.jpg)](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/5/2021/07/guests-eating-dinner-together.jpg)

Entertaining clients in NYC could mean doing dinner and calling it a night, sure. It‚Äôs a satisfactory option everyone is used to! But if you want to show your clients a little more than satisfactory, why not take them on a food tour? Don‚Äôt go to one restaurant, go to four! And listen to history and quirky stories in between. On a progressive food tour, your clients will get the VIP treatment with a professional host to lead the way. There‚Äôs food tours for every vibe you want to curate, from unique to Michelin-starred restaurants to really impress.

## **Relax with games at Chelsea Piers**

[![bowling and game for entertaining clients in NYC](data:image/svg+xml,%3Csvg%20xmlns='http://www.w3.org/2000/svg'%20viewBox='0%200%20300%20179'%3E%3C/svg%3E)![bowling and game for entertaining clients in NYC](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/5/2020/02/bowling-entertaining-clients-in-NYC-300x179.jpg)](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/5/2020/02/bowling-entertaining-clients-in-NYC.jpg)

[Chelsea Piers](https://www.chelseapiers.com/facilities/) has it all ‚Äî it‚Äôs your one stop destination for entertaining clients in NYC. With activities like bowling, year-round ice skating, and golf, you won‚Äôt run out of ideas for things to do. There are also event spaces and yachts with dinner cruises. It‚Äôs places like Chelsea Piers that make building positive experiences for your clients easy. 

## Try something unique with a water tasting

[![try-virtual-water-tasting-to-entertain-clients](data:image/svg+xml,%3Csvg%20xmlns='http://www.w3.org/2000/svg'%20viewBox='0%200%20700%20467'%3E%3C/svg%3E)![try-virtual-water-tasting-to-entertain-clients](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/5/2021/07/team-bonding-virtually-and-in-person.jpg)](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/5/2021/07/team-bonding-virtually-and-in-person.jpg)

You‚Äôve done a wine tasting with your clients, but have you ever done a water tasting? Before you keep scrolling, it‚Äôs a real thing‚Äî and there‚Äôs a lot to tasting water that you‚Äôve probably never realized before or taken for granted. The terroir, for example, is just as important in quality water. Another key in water tasting? The smell, of course! When it comes to entertaining in NYC, you‚Äôve got a plethora of options but we guarantee your clients have never done a water tasting. Treat them to something unique.

## Head out for a day at the ballpark

[![entertaining-clients-in-nyc-baseball](data:image/svg+xml,%3Csvg%20xmlns='http://www.w3.org/2000/svg'%20viewBox='0%200%20700%20467'%3E%3C/svg%3E)![entertaining-clients-in-nyc-baseball](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/5/2020/02/head-to-professional-sports-to-entertain-clients.jpg)](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/5/2020/02/head-to-professional-sports-to-entertain-clients.jpg)

Or basketball court, or other professional sporting event. While sports might not be for everyone, treating your clients to a VIP experience is something they won‚Äôt forget! Spring for some special seats or box for a private venue. Order food and drinks, grab some gear, and start chanting. No matter who you‚Äôre rooting for, your clients will be the ones leaving with a win!
