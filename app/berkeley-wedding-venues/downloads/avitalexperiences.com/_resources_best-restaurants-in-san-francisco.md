### 1\. Daeho Kalbijjim & Beef Soup

[Website](https://mandalaysf.com)

[Open in Google Maps](https://maps.app.goo.gl/4hNcnr18z8Nxy5VW9)

Kalbijjim is a Korean braised short rib that was served only to the Royal families of Korea for over 400 years. It is now available to San Francisco locals at Daeho Kalbijjim & Beef Soup in Japantown. **Best for: Bringing a big group and indulging.**
