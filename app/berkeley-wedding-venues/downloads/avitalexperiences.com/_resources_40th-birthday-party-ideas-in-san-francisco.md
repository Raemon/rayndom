[Paint the Town](https://paintthetown.com/party-packages/?utm_source=google_ads&utm_medium=ppc&utm_campaign=PTT_Onsite&utm_term=in%20person%20paint%20and%20sip&utm_adgroup=In%20Person_Phrase&gad_source=1&gad_campaignid=12915136035&gbraid=0AAAAABjTo-ZBqybkRfxVRBUD8AbHlFzXX&gclid=CjwKCAjwtrXFBhBiEiwAEKen10YXEPbKqU0AstVMIWvZ4JtJpCkLkm4INW9i8FR6wUfaA7uDjhN9ghoCvQ8QAvD_BwE) offers private Paint and Sip classes and has many packages that can help you fit this experience into your party budget. ## 40th Birthday Party Ideas for Men

![](data:image/svg+xml,%3Csvg%20xmlns='http://www.w3.org/2000/svg'%20viewBox='0%200%201024%20600'%3E%3C/svg%3E)![](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/2016/08/Screenshot-2025-08-31-at-12.29.31-AM-1024x600.png)Photo Credit: https://rusticales.com

### 1\. Take a Trip across the Bay for the Best Breweries

If you and your buds consider yourself beer aficionados (or at the very least, big fans of the drink), a bar crawl through the best breweries in the Bay Area might just be the perfect party idea! SF proper has a few good breweries, and if you want to stay local, we‚Äôll point you right to House of Pizza (by Cellarmaker) in the Mission District. Neighborhood: Oakland  
Price: $$+

## Best Party Themes for an SF 30th Birthday Party

![](data:image/svg+xml,%3Csvg%20xmlns='http://www.w3.org/2000/svg'%20viewBox='0%200%201024%20683'%3E%3C/svg%3E)![](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/2016/08/T1-pexels.com-stock-photo-1024x683.jpeg)Photo Credit: Pexel User Andrea Piacquadio

### 1\. Masquerade

All this theme requires is for your guests to don a glamorous or hilarious mask, put on their finest dresses and suits, and bring their dancing shoes! It‚Äôs a theme that balances being fancy with being quirky and giving guests room to play. The photo ops at a Masquerade party are countless and will be incredible keepsakes to look back on for years to come. This is a great theme if you are taking your friends to a venue with a dance floor, because looking around at a sea of people getting down to Pitbull while looking 18th-century chic is a highly entertaining experience!

![](data:image/svg+xml,%3Csvg%20xmlns='http://www.w3.org/2000/svg'%20viewBox='0%200%201024%20585'%3E%3C/svg%3E)![](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/09/Screenshot-2025-08-31-at-12.19.09-AM-1024x585.png)Photo Credit: Pexel User Nadin Sh

### 2\. Murder Mystery

This theme takes a little planning, but we promise it‚Äôs worth the extra effort! When you send out your birthday party invites, include in each a character description for what role your friends will be playing at your murder mystery theme party. You can keep it basic and assign them whether they‚Äôre a guest or the murderer, or you can create elaborate character profiles for everyone. Or it could be fun to base it on the game Clue and assign everyone a familiar character! No matter which route you go, a murder mystery theme is one of the most engrossing interactive party ideas that will keep your guests entertained for hours.

![](data:image/svg+xml,%3Csvg%20xmlns='http://www.w3.org/2000/svg'%20viewBox='0%200%201024%20683'%3E%3C/svg%3E)![](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/2020/05/pexels-rachel-claire-5863515-1024x683.jpg)Photo Credit: Pexel User Rachel Claire

### 3\. Tropical

Bring Island vibes to your birthday party when you choose a Tropical theme! San Francisco has beautiful sunny days and gorgeous beaches, so why not take advantage and throw this party outside? A tiki bar is a great touch, as well as having a few tiki torches scattered about. Set up a blender so you can keep plying guests with icy drinks like pi√±a coladas and daiquiris all night long. Dress code is bathing suits, and Hawaiian shirts‚Äîand grab a few packs of Leis from Party City for the finishing touch!

![](data:image/svg+xml,%3Csvg%20xmlns='http://www.w3.org/2000/svg'%20viewBox='0%200%201024%20644'%3E%3C/svg%3E)![](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/09/5.-Pexels.com-stock-images-1024x644.jpg)Photo Credit: Pexel User Drew Rae

### 4\. Casino

Turn your Home into a casino for one night only to celebrate your 40th birthday! All you need to do is set up a few fold-out tables, some packs of cards, poker chips, and keep the alcohol flowing. No real money needs to be involved to make this night a hit! You could either play with something like wrapped candy, start everyone out with about 25 pieces, and allow them to take home their winnings at the end of the night. Or give out 100 poker chips per person, and for every 50 each person has won/kept by the end of the party, give them one lottery ticket. End the party with a drawing to see who wins the silly or special items you pick out as prizes!

![](data:image/svg+xml,%3Csvg%20xmlns='http://www.w3.org/2000/svg'%20viewBox='0%200%201024%20683'%3E%3C/svg%3E)![](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/2016/08/T5-pexels.com-stock-photo-1024x683.jpg)Photo Credit: Pexel User cottonbro studio

### 5\. Oscar Party

Embrace Hollywood Glamour with an Oscars-themed birthday party! Turn your home or venue of choice into a VIP Oscars afterparty for one night only. It doesn‚Äôt even have to coincide with Oscar‚Äôs season. This theme is all about dressing chic, embracing your cinephile side, and sipping your drink of choice out of champagne flutes (which can totally be plastic, we won‚Äôt tell). Put your favorite movie posters up on the wall, some old black and white film on the TV as set decoration, and roll out the red carpet for your guests to walk in on. Bonus points if you acquire a few of those life-size cardboard celebrities for hilarious photo ops with your guests!
