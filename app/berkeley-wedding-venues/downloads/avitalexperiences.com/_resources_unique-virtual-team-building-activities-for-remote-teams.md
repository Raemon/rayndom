The classic improv game where you ‚Äúgo around the circle‚Äù and each person adds in one sentence while you start to build a story. There are many ways you can go about this so just go with it and have fun! It‚Äôs a great way to build communication skills and effectively think on your feet. Double whammy!

## [![pencils-and-paper-for-virtual-trivia-game-teambuilding-activity](data:image/svg+xml,%3Csvg%20xmlns='http://www.w3.org/2000/svg'%20viewBox='0%200%20700%20467'%3E%3C/svg%3E)![pencils-and-paper-for-virtual-trivia-game-teambuilding-activity](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/3/2021/07/virtual-trivia-for-team-building.jpg)](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/3/2021/07/virtual-trivia-for-team-building.jpg)

There‚Äôs a reason Jeopardy has been on TV since the 80s‚Ä¶people love trivia! Pick your platform, get your questions together and randomly select teams. Enjoy the magic of miracle berries as you alter your tastebuds and experience flavor in a completely new way in a[ virtual flavor tripping](https://avitaltours.com/virtual-flavor-tripping/) event. An Avital flavor host will take you through incredible experiments where one miracle berry will make sour foods, like lemon and kombucha, taste deliciously sweet. Flavor tripping kits are delivered to guests making the experience seamless and easy! You and your team will enjoy this journey ‚Äì don‚Äôt worry, it‚Äôs a totally legal trip!

## **9\. It‚Äôs the Five Finger Showdown (da-na-na-nahh, da na na na nahhh)**

*to the tune of The Final Countdown. Put five fingers up and get ready to learn about your co-workers. You might find your long lost soul family! The idea is to go around the group and have each person mention one unusual life experience. ## [![sign-truth-lie-virtual-truth-or-dare-game](data:image/svg+xml,%3Csvg%20xmlns='http://www.w3.org/2000/svg'%20viewBox='0%200%20700%20467'%3E%3C/svg%3E)![sign-truth-lie-virtual-truth-or-dare-game](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/3/2021/07/virtual-game-of-truth-or-dare.jpg)](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/3/2021/07/virtual-game-of-truth-or-dare.jpg)

## **10\. Challenge your team to a game of Truth or Dare.**

Let‚Äôs take things up a notch, shall we, with a virtual game of Truth or Dare. Everyone starts with their cameras on. The facilitator states a truth or dare, such as, show your phone background. Players keep their cameras on to indicate they are willing to reply. The host then asks a few players to complete the truth or dare. The successful player leads the next prompt. May the odds be ever in your favor!

## **11\. Psych!** **Find out how your team members would react in certain situations.**

This game will really get your creative juices flowing. To play, one person enters each team member‚Äôs name into Psych. The game then generates customized questions, like how would Judy fight off a team of bears? (Poor Judy‚Ä¶). Each player will then write a witty response and at the end of the round everyone votes on who had the best answer. Points are rewarded by ranking. The goal is to have the most points at the end of the game. 

## [![virtual-game-of-telephone-for-teambuilding-remote](data:image/svg+xml,%3Csvg%20xmlns='http://www.w3.org/2000/svg'%20viewBox='0%200%20700%20467'%3E%3C/svg%3E)![virtual-game-of-telephone-for-teambuilding-remote](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/3/2021/07/virtual-game-of-broken-telephone.jpg)](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/3/2021/07/virtual-game-of-broken-telephone.jpg)

## **12\. Build communication skills with See What I Mean?**

Put your communication skills to the test with this classic game of Telephone, but with a twist ‚Äì art. One participant draws a picture using only basic shapes. A selected team member will then try to explain the picture to the rest of the team. Everyone will attempt to replicate the picture without ever having seen it. The goal is to recreate the same picture as the original.

## [![girl-singing-karaoke-virtual-jukebox-game-teambuilding](data:image/svg+xml,%3Csvg%20xmlns='http://www.w3.org/2000/svg'%20viewBox='0%200%20700%20467'%3E%3C/svg%3E)![girl-singing-karaoke-virtual-jukebox-game-teambuilding](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/3/2021/07/virtual-karaoke-singing.jpg)](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/3/2021/07/virtual-karaoke-singing.jpg)

## **13\. Take center stage with Jukebox**

Warm up those vocal chords and think of your favorite song! Go around the group and take turns humming or singing a part of the song. Have your co-workers try to guess the artist and song name. Not only do you come out with a new, fun playlist, but belly laughs are guaranteed to ensue as you find out who would not make it to the finals in American Idol.

Do you and your team talk about the good ‚Äòole days? Do you ever wonder what your colleague was like in college? Well, look no further. Have a college night!
