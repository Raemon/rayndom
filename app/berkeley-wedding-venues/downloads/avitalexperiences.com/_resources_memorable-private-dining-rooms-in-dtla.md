#  Memorable Private Dining Rooms In DTLA 

June 12, 2019 

Life is a journey full of milestones: weddings, graduations, and birthdays. This is all the more reason to rent out a private dining room in Downtown Los Angeles to celebrate. DTLA is now one of the most desired places to live in Los Angeles. The restaurant scene is explosive, providing more memorable private spaces to rent out for your next big bash. Check out our list below of the most desired private dining rooms in DTLA.

## **Drago Centro**

[![](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/2/2019/06/private-dining-rooms-in-DTLA-drago-centro.jpg)](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/2/2019/06/private-dining-rooms-in-DTLA-drago-centro.jpg)

[**Drago Centro**](https://dragocentro.com)**,** located in the heart of Downtown Los Angeles is an Italian restaurant with a contemporary flair situated inside the former vault of City National Bank. They have four private dining rooms to choose from. At Drago Centro, host a more intimate dinner in their 16-person [**Flower Room**](https://dragocentro.com/private-events/)**,** surrounded by elegant velvet walls, or go for [**The Garden Room**](https://dragocentro.com/private-events/)**,** an all glass dining space surrounded by lush olive trees with a serene water fountain that can hold up to 110 guests. The menu features handmade pasta dishes with a killer wine list. With their exceptional service and clean, inviting atmosphere you can‚Äôt go wrong renting out any room here.

## **Redbird**

[![](data:image/svg+xml,%3Csvg%20xmlns='http://www.w3.org/2000/svg'%20viewBox='0%200%201599%201126'%3E%3C/svg%3E)![](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/2/2019/06/private-dining-rooms-in-DTLA-redbird.jpg)](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/2/2019/06/private-dining-rooms-in-DTLA-redbird.jpg)

After dining at [**Redbird**](https://redbird.la) in downtown Los Angeles, your dining experience will never be the same. Redbird is an architectural gem, carved inside the space of a former cathedral with a massive, beautiful open seating area. The restaurant is known for its modern American cuisine with approachable dishes designed to be shared. They have eight different private dining rooms to choose from with [The Vibiana](https://redbird.la/private-dining/), their biggest space accommodating up to 550 people. They are the place to be for an outdoor dining experience. [**The Garden**](https://redbird.la/private-dining/) is our personal favorite surrounded by a lively herb and vegetable garden with a personal pizza oven. Seating up to 100 people, it‚Äôs the perfect place for a relaxed, down-to-earth outdoor dining experience.

## **Dekkadance**

[![](data:image/svg+xml,%3Csvg%20xmlns='http://www.w3.org/2000/svg'%20viewBox='0%200%20640%20427'%3E%3C/svg%3E)![](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/2/2019/06/private-dining-rooms-in-DTLA-Dekkadance-.jpg)](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/2/2019/06/private-dining-rooms-in-DTLA-Dekkadance-.jpg)

If you‚Äôre looking for private dining space with an out of this world view, you must go to [**Dekkadance**](https://dekkadance.com)**.** Located on the 69thfloor in the heart of downtown Los Angeles, their private dining rooms are the perfect place to stock up on some photos for the gram. They have four different dining room options that can seat between 20 to 50 guests. Book the [**LA or IROSKY**](https://dekkadance.com/private-dining/) room and you get a flat screen TV with your private dining space, making this a desirable spot for sports fans. Dekkadance is known for their globally inspired international dishes, but we think their brunch buffet is where it‚Äôs at. Book a private room with their brunch buffet and an unlimited mimosa bar, and you can‚Äôt go wrong here.

## **Patina**

[![](data:image/svg+xml,%3Csvg%20xmlns='http://www.w3.org/2000/svg'%20viewBox='0%200%201600%201050'%3E%3C/svg%3E)![](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/2/2019/06/private-dining-rooms-in-DTLA-patina.jpg)](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/2/2019/06/private-dining-rooms-in-DTLA-patina.jpg)

You can‚Äôt book a private dining room in downtown Los Angeles without first checking out [**Patina**](https://www.patinagroup.com/patina-restaurant)**.** This sophisticated restaurant is located inside The Walt Disney Concert Hall. They have two private dining spaces, one for larger parties, and one for smaller groups. Our favorite is [**The Chef‚Äôs Table**](https://www.patinagroup.com/patina-restaurant/private-events) , a smaller room located at the entrance of the restaurant that makes a perfect place to experience this exceptional dining experience. Patina has gained a strong reputation for their excellent, hospitable, attentive service. If you want to impress your guests with an attention to detail staff and an award winning wine list you must come to Patina.
