#  Here‚Äôs Your NYC Patio Brunch Guide 

April 2, 2018 

Though Spring is in full swing, it‚Äôs finally getting warm enough to eat outside. So take an extra long lunch break or book a Sunday morning reservation and enjoy the weather with a side of mimosas. Here‚Äôs your NYC patio brunch guide.

## Bricolage

[![bricolage serves vietnamese brunch. Here's Your NYC Patio Brunch Guide](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/5/2018/03/Screen-Shot-2018-03-30-at-12.38.45-PM.png)](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/5/2018/03/Screen-Shot-2018-03-30-at-12.38.45-PM.png)

In San Francisco, we love [Slanted Door,](http://www.slanteddoor.com/) so it makes sense that we love [Bricolage](http://bricolage.nyc/), which was started by some of its alums. Brunch is served 11 am- 3 pm on weekends. The Vietnamese inspired menu includes unique items such as the Breakfast ‚Äúbanh xeo‚Äù Crepe, Pork Belly Hash, and Fried Chicken & Waffle with siracha butter. Enjoy them all (along with a Thai Basil Gimlet) on the back patio.

_ 162 5th Ave, Brooklyn, NY 11217  
__[www.bricolage.nyc](http://bricolage.nyc/)_

## Loosie‚Äôs Kitchen

[![loosies-kitchen-outdoor-seating \(1\)](data:image/svg+xml,%3Csvg%20xmlns='http://www.w3.org/2000/svg'%20viewBox='0%200%20640%20300'%3E%3C/svg%3E)![loosies-kitchen-outdoor-seating \(1\)](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/5/2018/03/loosies-kitchen-outdoor-seating-1.png)](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/5/2018/03/loosies-kitchen-outdoor-seating-1.png)

The website of[ Loosie‚Äôs Kitchen](https://www.loosieskitchen.com/) reads, ‚ÄúWelcome Home‚Äù and while your NYC apartment probably doesn‚Äôt have the same hip (or Instagram-worthy) backyard as Loosie‚Äôs, it really is easy to make yourself at home here. They vow to ‚Äúcure hangovers at the cafe from 7 am, and then help make new ones at lunch‚Äù. Visit between 11 am and 3 pm on weekends to score Nutella beignets, baked eggs, and cornbread with bacon jam.

_91a S 6th St, Brooklyn, NY 11249  
__[www.loosieskitchen.com](https://www.loosieskitchen.com/)_

## The Butcher‚Äôs Daughter

[![the butchers daughter: Here's Your NYC Patio Brunch Guide](data:image/svg+xml,%3Csvg%20xmlns='http://www.w3.org/2000/svg'%20viewBox='0%200%20597%20602'%3E%3C/svg%3E)![the butchers daughter: Here's Your NYC Patio Brunch Guide](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/5/2018/03/Nolita_15.png)](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/5/2018/03/Nolita_15.png)

Our LA guides regularly visit the Venice location of [The Butcher‚Äôs Daughter ](https://www.thebutchersdaughter.com/)(where you can sit outside all year long) and our New York crew waits until the weather is great to snag outside seats at the Nolita or West Village locations. Sip on one of the seven different kinds of mimosas (from blood orange to watermelon, lime, and lavender) and order one of our favorites: the Pomegranate Porridge, Coconut Yogurt Parfait, or Weekend Waffles.

_19 Kenmare St, New York, NY 10012 or_ _581 Hudson St, New York, NY 10014_

_[www.thebutchersdaughter.com](https://www.thebutchersdaughter.com/)_

## Narcissa

[![Here's Your NYC Patio Brunch Guide Narcissa](data:image/svg+xml,%3Csvg%20xmlns='http://www.w3.org/2000/svg'%20viewBox='0%200%20600%20399'%3E%3C/svg%3E)![Here's Your NYC Patio Brunch Guide Narcissa](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/5/2018/03/NarcissaGarden-1.jpg)](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/5/2018/03/NarcissaGarden-1.jpg)

Head to the East Village for a backyard getaway. (Or if you really want to get away, The Standard Hotel is right next door.) The patio at [Narcissa](http://www.narcissarestaurant.com) is a dreamy spot where even dreamier food is served. Go for a mix of sweet and savory with the French Toast Bites, Carrot Fries, Tomato Benedict, Lobster Soft Scramble, and Iced Cinnamon Buns.

_25 Cooper Sq, New York, NY 10003  
__[www.narcissarestaurant.com](http://www.narcissarestaurant.com/)_

## Sunday in Brooklyn[![Your NYC Patio Brunch Guide-head to willaimsburg for sundays in brooklyn](data:image/svg+xml,%3Csvg%20xmlns='http://www.w3.org/2000/svg'%20viewBox='0%200%201125%20750'%3E%3C/svg%3E)![Your NYC Patio Brunch Guide-head to willaimsburg for sundays in brooklyn](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/5/2018/03/4362-d0tx2e-8.jpg)](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/5/2018/03/4362-d0tx2e-8.jpg)

Is there a more pleasant restaurant name than [Sunday in Brooklyn](http://www.sundayinbrooklyn.com)? Even more joyful is that brunch is served every day. On weekdays you can order brunch from 10 am ‚Äì 4 pm, and on weekends things start earlier at 9:30 am. During warm days eat a variety of breakfast sandwiches on the rooftop garden or take your Malted Pancakes with hazelnut maple praline and brown butter to the sidewalk cafe.

_348 Wythe Ave, Brooklyn, NY 11211  
__[www.sundayinbrooklyn.com](https://www.sundayinbrooklyn.com/)_
