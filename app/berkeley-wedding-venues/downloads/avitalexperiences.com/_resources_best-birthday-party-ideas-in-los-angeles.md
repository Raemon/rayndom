#  The Best Birthday Party Ideas In Los Angeles 

December 13, 2018 

Make this year the best one yet. Here is our list of the best birthday party ideas in Los Angeles so you can plan a great celebration.

## Roll It Out

[![roller skate in style. The Best Birthday Party Ideas In Los Angeles](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/2/2018/12/The-Best-Birthday-Party-Ideas-In-Los-Angeles-roller-skate-at-Moonlight-Rollerway.jpg)](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/2/2018/12/The-Best-Birthday-Party-Ideas-In-Los-Angeles-roller-skate-at-Moonlight-Rollerway.jpg)

Roller skating parties were probably popular in your younger days (middle school roller discos anyone?) but they‚Äôre back! [Moonlight Rollerway](https://moonlightrollerway.com/) and [World of Wheels](https://wowskatela.com/) have been hosting parties for celebrities Beyonce, Kristen Bell, and Jessica Biel. You deserve a party just as fabulous. Each rink rents skates and has food, but you should bring your own sparkly outfit to shine on the floor. Some even offer lessons so you can perfect your moves before showing off at your party.

## Rooftop Pool Party

[![Rooftop Pool Party-The Best Birthday Party Ideas In Los Angeles](data:image/svg+xml,%3Csvg%20xmlns='http://www.w3.org/2000/svg'%20viewBox='0%200%20640%20428'%3E%3C/svg%3E)![Rooftop Pool Party-The Best Birthday Party Ideas In Los Angeles](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/2/2018/12/The-Best-Birthday-Party-Ideas-In-Los-Angeles-rooftop-pool-party.jpg)](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/2/2018/12/The-Best-Birthday-Party-Ideas-In-Los-Angeles-rooftop-pool-party.jpg)

Downtown LA is the home of great food, lots of museums, and more importantly for your party purposes, great hotel rooftop pools. From the[ Ace Hotel](https://www.acehotel.com/losangeles/events-and-spaces/event-spaces/rooftop-pool/) to the [Nomad](https://www.thenomadhotel.com/los-angeles), to The [Standard](http://www.standardhotels.com/la/features/the-pool-deck), to the hip [Freehand](https://freehandhotels.com/los-angeles/broken-shaker/), you can hang by the pool with a handful of friends and a handful of cocktails. Drinking carrot daiquiris by the pool at the Broken Shaker is a pretty good way to bring in a new year. If it‚Äôs too cold to swim, get a sunset cocktail and dip your feet in.

## Eat. Drink. Repeat.

[![The Best Birthday Party Ideas in Los Angeles-take a group of friends and explore the city through food](data:image/svg+xml,%3Csvg%20xmlns='http://www.w3.org/2000/svg'%20viewBox='0%200%202200%201467'%3E%3C/svg%3E)![The Best Birthday Party Ideas in Los Angeles-take a group of friends and explore the city through food](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/2/2016/07/Fun-Things-to-Do-in-Venice-Beach-guests-cheersing-on-Avital-Tours.jpeg)](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/2/2016/07/Fun-Things-to-Do-in-Venice-Beach-guests-cheersing-on-Avital-Tours.jpeg)

Have the best birthday meal ever, without the planning and awkward bill-splitting. Spend three hours eating and drinking around LA. A local guide will plan and lead the experience in [Downtown](http://avitaltours.com/los-angeles/tour/downtown-food-tour/), [Koreatown](http://avitaltours.com/los-angeles/koreatown-food-tour), or [Venice](http://avitaltours.com/los-angeles/tour/venice-beach-food-tour/). Your only job is to gather some friends, and then eat, drink, and enjoy. If you‚Äôre interested in booze more than food, the [Hollywood Cocktail Tour ](http://avitaltours.com/los-angeles/tour/hollywood-cocktail-tour/)is a great start to a big night out.

## Spa Day

[![Spa Day! The Best Birthday Party Ideas In Los Angeles](data:image/svg+xml,%3Csvg%20xmlns='http://www.w3.org/2000/svg'%20viewBox='0%200%20640%20426'%3E%3C/svg%3E)![Spa Day! The Best Birthday Party Ideas In Los Angeles](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/2/2018/12/The-Best-Birthday-Party-Ideas-In-Los-Angeles-spa-day.jpg)](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/2/2018/12/The-Best-Birthday-Party-Ideas-In-Los-Angeles-spa-day.jpg)

Los Angeles is thriving when it comes to spas. You can glam it up in an old-school Beverly Hills spa, or get a spa treatment literally any time of the day in a 24-hour Korean spa. Spots like [Wi Spa](https://www.wispausa.com/) have plenty of room for all of your guests to spend hours in the half-dozen types of saunas, and the hot and cold baths. (Note: in the baths, you can go in in your birthday suit.) You can even have your birthday dinner inside at the Korean restaurant and take a nap on the heated floors or rooftop.
