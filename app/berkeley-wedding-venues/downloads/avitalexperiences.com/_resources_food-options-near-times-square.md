#  5 Solid Food Options Near Times Square 

February 28, 2018 

When it comes to seeking out the city‚Äôs best food neighborhood, no one is running to Times Square. But if you happen to be in that part of the city, there are still places to get a good meal. Here are 5 solid food options near Times Square.

## Ivan Ramen Slurp Shop

[![Ivan ramen is one of 5 Solid Food Options Near Times Square](data:image/svg+xml,%3Csvg%20xmlns='http://www.w3.org/2000/svg'%20viewBox='0%200%202048%201536'%3E%3C/svg%3E)![Ivan ramen is one of 5 Solid Food Options Near Times Square](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/5/2018/01/13994989608_f189926f9b_k.jpg)](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/5/2018/01/13994989608_f189926f9b_k.jpg)

Solid ramen is just a few blocks from the madness of Times Square. Ivan Ramen is a ramen shop opened by a Jewish man from Long Island who opened his first shop in Tokyo. He now has New York locations including this one in the [Gotham West Market](https://gothamwestmarket.com/) in Hell‚Äôs Kitchen. The menu includes traditional shio and shoyu ramen, Slurp Shop originals, steamed buns, and vegetables. Escape from the bright lights of Times Square and escape into a steaming bowl of noodles.

_[www.ivanramen.com/en/slurp-shop/  
](https://www.ivanramen.com/en/slurp-shop/)__600 11th Ave, New York, NY 10036_

## Shake Shack

[![Shake Shack is one of Solid Food Options Near Times Square](data:image/svg+xml,%3Csvg%20xmlns='http://www.w3.org/2000/svg'%20viewBox='0%200%202048%201365'%3E%3C/svg%3E)![Shake Shack is one of Solid Food Options Near Times Square](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/5/2018/01/14365781387_571f0d654d_k.jpg)](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/5/2018/01/14365781387_571f0d654d_k.jpg)

Yes, there are Shake Shacks all around the city, and for good reason. It‚Äôs always a solid choice for burgers, hot dogs, fries, and frozen custard. If you‚Äôre looking for a reliable spot within spitting distance of Times Square, head to their Theater District location. After surviving the crazy crowds, you deserve a ‚ÄòShroom Burger and milkshake.

_691 8th Ave, New York, NY 10036  
__[www.shakeshack.com/location/theater-district/](https://www.shakeshack.com/location/theater-district/)_

## Danji

[![Danji is one of 5 Solid Food Options Near Times Square](data:image/svg+xml,%3Csvg%20xmlns='http://www.w3.org/2000/svg'%20viewBox='0%200%20640%20426'%3E%3C/svg%3E)![Danji is one of 5 Solid Food Options Near Times Square](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/5/2018/01/5378502800_a13d10f9fe_z.jpg)](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/5/2018/01/5378502800_a13d10f9fe_z.jpg)

Just a few blocks from the costumed characters wandering around 42nd Street is Danji, a small-plates Korean restaurant. It‚Äôs worth walking past the hot dog stands to eat kimchi poutine, Korean fried chicken, and bulgogi beef sliders. And you might need some soju to relax after braving the square‚Äôs bright lights.

_346 W. 52nd St, New York, NY 10019  
__[www.danjinyc.com/](http://www.danjinyc.com/)_

## Esca

[![Esca: 5 Solid Food Options Near Times Square](data:image/svg+xml,%3Csvg%20xmlns='http://www.w3.org/2000/svg'%20viewBox='0%200%20640%20480'%3E%3C/svg%3E)![Esca: 5 Solid Food Options Near Times Square](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/5/2018/01/5149795891_188d2e9964_z.jpg)](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/5/2018/01/5149795891_188d2e9964_z.jpg)

Escape to Italy‚Ä¶sort of. Great Italian food is just a stone‚Äôs throw from Times Square. if you‚Äôre looking for a long meal, have the two-hour long tasting dinner. But if you‚Äôre in a rush to get to the theatre, the avanti teatro menu moves faster. Or order from a large menu of a la carte seafood and pasta.

_402 W. 43rd St, New York, NY 10036  
__[www.esca-nyc.com/](http://esca-nyc.com/)_

## Toloache

[![Toloache: 5 Solid Food Options Near Times Square](data:image/svg+xml,%3Csvg%20xmlns='http://www.w3.org/2000/svg'%20viewBox='0%200%202048%201326'%3E%3C/svg%3E)![Toloache: 5 Solid Food Options Near Times Square](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/5/2018/01/34545243824_e4aa34321a_k.jpg)](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/5/2018/01/34545243824_e4aa34321a_k.jpg)

Take a Times Square taco break. From ceviche to quesadillas to tacos to guacamole, the menu covers Mexican classics. Toloache is even recommended by Sam Sifton from the [New York Times](http://www.nytimes.com/2010/08/18/dining/reviews/18rest.html). Make sure to make a reservation-the Toloache gets crowded before and after Broadway shows.

_251 W 50th St, New York, NY 10019  
_[_toloachenyc.com/mobile/home.html_](http://toloachenyc.com/mobile/home.html)
