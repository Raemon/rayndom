#  What To Eat At WoodSpoon, Our Favorite Brazilian Restaurant Los Angeles 

May 5, 2018 

[![Visit WoodSpoon, Our Favorite Brazilian Restaurant, Los Angeles](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/2/2018/04/download.png)](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/2/2018/04/download.png)If you‚Äôve been on our Downtown LA tour you know how much we love [WoodSpoon](http://www.woodspoonla.com/). It‚Äôs our favorite Brazilian restaurant Los Angeles! Chef Natalia Pereira owns the restaurant and also runs [A Bite For Peace](https://avitaltours.us2.list-manage.com/track/click?u=df147b48cf6574c2a27434f52&id=d0873da52f&e=aea7d265a5), a traveling, family-style dining experience that promotes peace and builds communities across the globe through the sharing a home-cooked meal. We also loved the mismatched decor, chairs, and place settings. From the cinnamon-spiced sangria to crispy croquettes, here‚Äôs what you should eat when you visit.

## Croquettes

[![Try the croquettes at What To Eat At WoodSpoon, Our Favorite Brazilian Restaurant Los Angeles](data:image/svg+xml,%3Csvg%20xmlns='http://www.w3.org/2000/svg'%20viewBox='0%200%201024%20683'%3E%3C/svg%3E)![Try the croquettes at What To Eat At WoodSpoon, Our Favorite Brazilian Restaurant Los Angeles](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/2/2018/04/13706464915_582338d4b1_b.jpg)](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/2/2018/04/13706464915_582338d4b1_b.jpg)

When it comes to croquettes at WoodSpoon, it‚Äôs hard to pick a favorite. Luckily you don‚Äôt have to. Order the Mix Plate and you‚Äôll get to try the Coxinha (a street food made with chicken), Potato Croquette, Portuguese Croquette, Pastel Portuguese (dumpling stuffed with shrimp served with coconut sauce), and Kibe (bulgar wheat, mint, and ground beef). Do you have room for more? Try the Bolinho De Bacalhau, a cod croquette.

## Sangria

[![What To Eat At WoodSpoon, Our Favorite Brazilian Restaurant Los Angeles: get the sangria!](data:image/svg+xml,%3Csvg%20xmlns='http://www.w3.org/2000/svg'%20viewBox='0%200%201024%20824'%3E%3C/svg%3E)![What To Eat At WoodSpoon, Our Favorite Brazilian Restaurant Los Angeles: get the sangria!](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/2/2018/04/3456827554_2f6045ae13_b.jpg)](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/2/2018/04/3456827554_2f6045ae13_b.jpg)

May choosing ‚Äúred‚Äù or ‚Äúwhite‚Äù be the hardest part of your visit. We‚Äôre partial to the Red Cabernet which is filled with cinnamon and berries. (The water at WoodSpoon is also tinted with cinnamon sticks.) But there‚Äôs also the White Chardonnay that‚Äôs flavored with passion fruit and orange. You can‚Äôt go wrong, so don‚Äôt stress, and if you are stressed, this sangria will calm you down after one glass.

## Chicken Pot Pie

[![try the chicken pot pie at Woodspoon, , Our Favorite Brazilian Restaurant Los Angeles](data:image/svg+xml,%3Csvg%20xmlns='http://www.w3.org/2000/svg'%20viewBox='0%200%201000%20750'%3E%3C/svg%3E)![try the chicken pot pie at Woodspoon, , Our Favorite Brazilian Restaurant Los Angeles](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/2/2018/04/o.jpg)](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/2/2018/04/o.jpg)

This is the ultimate comfort food with a Brazillian twist. This pot pie is filled with chicken, hearts of palm, olives, and roasted corn. Just trust us and order it. (You can thank us later.)

## Stew

[![Try one of the stews at WoodSpoon, Our Favorite Brazilian Restaurant Los Angeles:](data:image/svg+xml,%3Csvg%20xmlns='http://www.w3.org/2000/svg'%20viewBox='0%200%20800%20450'%3E%3C/svg%3E)![Try one of the stews at WoodSpoon, Our Favorite Brazilian Restaurant Los Angeles:](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/2/2018/04/Moqueca_de_peixe.jpg)](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/2/2018/04/Moqueca_de_peixe.jpg)

Warm up with one of WoodSpoon‚Äôs stews. Our favorites are the Moqueca and the Frango Com Quiabo. The Moqueca is a traditional seafood stew with black cod, coconut sauce, rice, and cilantro. Prefer chicken? The Frango Com Quiabo is a Brazillian chicken stew with okra served with polenta, and black beans. There are no wrong choices here.

_WoodSpoon LA:__<http://www.woodspoonla.com/>, _ _107 West 9th Street, Los Angeles, CA 90015_
