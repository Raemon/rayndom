## Best Restaurants Near the LA Convention Center

Attending a conference or event at the LA Convention Center? Here are our top picks whether you‚Äôre grabbing a quick bite with colleagues or treating clients to dinner. 

![thumbnail](data:image/svg+xml,%3Csvg%20xmlns='http://www.w3.org/2000/svg'%20viewBox='0%200%20150%20150'%3E%3C/svg%3E)![thumbnail](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/2025/09/restaurant-interior-at-caldo-verde-in-dtla-image-via-@caldoverde_dtlap-150x150.jpg)

##### Caldo Verde

[ Website ](https://www.properhotel.com/downtown-la/restaurants-bars/caldo-verde/) ¬∑ [ Open in Google Maps ](https://maps.app.goo.gl/CisguoKhsQLHwKK38)

Best spot grab breakfast in DTLA. Rich carnitas and sweet potato hash, elevated BLTA with wagyu bacon and creamy aioli, steak and eggs splashed with a zingy pistachio pisto. Plus great morning views. 

![thumbnail](data:image/svg+xml,%3Csvg%20xmlns='http://www.w3.org/2000/svg'%20viewBox='0%200%20150%20150'%3E%3C/svg%3E)![thumbnail](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/2025/09/restaurant-interior-at-pine-and-crane-in-dtla-image-via-www.pineandcrane.com_-150x150.webp)

##### Pine and Crane

[ Website ](https://www.pineandcrane.com/dtla) ¬∑ [ Open in Google Maps ](https://maps.app.goo.gl/GmvBTjCd93GTijoz7)

Michelin Bib Gourmand Taiwanese food. Get the pig ear salad and Taiwanese sausage to start. Then the three-cup jidori chicken, where the dark meat is cooked in a clay pot, glazed, and sprinkled with green onion, chiles, and whole garlic.

![thumbnail](data:image/svg+xml,%3Csvg%20xmlns='http://www.w3.org/2000/svg'%20viewBox='0%200%20150%20150'%3E%3C/svg%3E)![thumbnail](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/2025/09/restaurant-interior-at-shekarchi-in-dtla-image-via-shekarchirestaurant.com_-150x150.jpg)

##### Shekarchi

[ Website ](https://shekarchirestaurant.com) ¬∑ [ Open in Google Maps ](https://maps.app.goo.gl/jgrFeFUKDa9yBMPTA)

The best Persian food in LA since 1985! You cannot go wrong ordering any dish off their grill menu. Grab a rack of lamb hot off the grill with a side of shirazi salad and tzatziki to be transported to Persian-food heaven!
