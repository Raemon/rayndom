#  The Best Cocktail Bars of Union Square 

September 24, 2015 

_**Tired of Tourists? Take back the ‚Äòhood with these top 5 best cocktail bars of Union Square where San Francisco locals love to imbibe.**_

[![Burritt Room and Tavern cocktail bar in Union Square](data:image/svg+xml,%3Csvg%20xmlns='http://www.w3.org/2000/svg'%20viewBox='0%200%200%200'%3E%3C/svg%3E)![Burritt Room and Tavern cocktail bar in Union Square](http://avitaltours.com/wordpress/wp-content/uploads/2015/09/Best-Coctail-Bars-in-Union-Square-Burritt-Room-and-Tavern.jpg)](http://avitaltours.com/wordpress/wp-content/uploads/2015/09/Best-Coctail-Bars-in-Union-Square-Burritt-Room-and-Tavern.jpg)

1\. [Burritt Room](http://www.charliepalmer.com/burritt-room-and-tavern/)

Burritt Room, neatly nestled inside the Mystic Hotel, takes its name from The Maltese Falcon, after the alley where Sam Spade‚Äôs partner Miles is gunned down. The decor takes its cues from film noir as well, with dim lighting and curtains making for an intimate setting. The drinks on the other hand have a decidedly modern twist. The Poquito Picante (that‚Äôs ‚Äúa little spicy‚Äù for all you gringos out there) kicks things up a notch (Bam!) by slipping a serrano tincture in alongside tequila, aperol, and lime. Too adventurous for you? Try the Mass Appeal, with your choice of vodka or gin, elderflower ginger, lime, and grapefruit bitters. In the mood for something a little more classic? The Mark Twain #2 subs out the gin in a Bee‚Äôs Knees for Scotch for a smoky-sweet treat.  _417 Stockton St,__(415) 400-0561_

[![Tradition Cocktail Bar in Union Square](data:image/svg+xml,%3Csvg%20xmlns='http://www.w3.org/2000/svg'%20viewBox='0%200%200%200'%3E%3C/svg%3E)![Tradition Cocktail Bar in Union Square](http://avitaltours.com/wordpress/wp-content/uploads/2015/09/Best-Cocktail-Bars-in-Union-Square-Tradition.jpg)](http://avitaltours.com/wordpress/wp-content/uploads/2015/09/Best-Cocktail-Bars-in-Union-Square-Tradition.jpg)

2\. [Tradition](http://tradbar.com/)

Oh Tradition! It‚Äôs a lofty goal to try to reclaim an American bar tradition in a country barely old enough to have real traditions of its own, but somehow, it succeeds. Like the national American identity, Tradition is a melting pot, drawing influences from all corners of the globe and throughout history. When was the last time you saw a Singapore Sling look so comfortable on a menu alongside the Sazerac and Shrunken Skull? As a matter of fact, when was the last time you ever saw those three on a menu together at all? Just don‚Äôt try to tackle all three in a night, or your hangover will leave you feeling like it‚Äôs your skull that‚Äôs been shrunk.  _441 Jones St_ _(415) 474-2284_

[![bartender pouring cocktails at Cantina in Union Square](data:image/svg+xml,%3Csvg%20xmlns='http://www.w3.org/2000/svg'%20viewBox='0%200%200%200'%3E%3C/svg%3E)![bartender pouring cocktails at Cantina in Union Square](http://avitaltours.com/wordpress/wp-content/uploads/2015/09/Marc-Fiorito-of-Gamma-Nine.-Avital-CocktailTour.-Pouring-Pisco-Punch-at-Cantina.jpg)](http://avitaltours.com/wordpress/wp-content/uploads/2015/09/Marc-Fiorito-of-Gamma-Nine.-Avital-CocktailTour.-Pouring-Pisco-Punch-at-Cantina.jpg)

3\. [Cantina](http://www.cantinasf.com/)

If you like margaritas, come to Cantina. If you don‚Äôt like margaritas, come to Cantina. Get a regular one, or try the five-spice version, by the glass, or by the pitcher. You got options, man. I can‚Äôt palate even the thought of a tequila shot, but they‚Äôre both good enough to trick me into thinking that I‚Äôm a tequila drinker. If you still don‚Äôt want to hop on the tequila train, never fear. Caipirinhas, Hemingway daquiris, and pisco punch are here! Each as deliciously deadly as the last. You‚Äôll be grooving along to the DJ in no time.  _580 Sutter St_ _(415) 398-0195_

[![Best Cocktail Bars in Union Square](data:image/svg+xml,%3Csvg%20xmlns='http://www.w3.org/2000/svg'%20viewBox='0%200%200%200'%3E%3C/svg%3E)![Best Cocktail Bars in Union Square](http://avitaltours.com/wordpress/wp-content/uploads/2015/09/Best-Coctail-Bars-in-Union-Square-Library-Bar.jpg)](http://avitaltours.com/wordpress/wp-content/uploads/2015/09/Best-Coctail-Bars-in-Union-Square-Library-Bar.jpg)

4\. [Library Bar](http://www.jdvhotels.com/restaurants/hotel-rex/the-library-bar/)

For those of you who feel that the Library at Bourbon and Branch doesn‚Äôt take the theme far enough, there‚Äôs Library Bar inside the Hotel Rex. It has the calm quiet of a library without the bother of all those old librarians walking up to your table to shush you. It‚Äôs a cute little spot right next to Cantina, great for those with more introverted tendencies, or for catching up with old friends over a G&T.  _562 Sutter St (415) 915-9865_

[![lounge at Hawthorn Cocktail Bar in Union Square](data:image/svg+xml,%3Csvg%20xmlns='http://www.w3.org/2000/svg'%20viewBox='0%200%200%200'%3E%3C/svg%3E)![lounge at Hawthorn Cocktail Bar in Union Square](http://avitaltours.com/wordpress/wp-content/uploads/2015/09/Best-Cocktail-Bars-in-Union-Square-Hawthorn.jpg)](http://avitaltours.com/wordpress/wp-content/uploads/2015/09/Best-Cocktail-Bars-in-Union-Square-Hawthorn.jpg)

5\. [Hawthorn](http://hawthornsf.com/)

Hawthorn earns some points in my book, just because I‚Äôm a fan of anywhere that has the chutzpah to put an Aviation on their menu. It‚Äôs not an especially complicated drink to make, nor is it even my favorite. But when a bartender can deliver on the promise of gin, lemon, and maraschino liqueur, with just the right amount of the beautifully purple creme de violette, she wins me over, having sufficiently assured me of her skills behind the bar. For those of you wondering what I‚Äôm going on about, grab a Spinning Wheel (apple brandy plus champagne equals YUM) or a Firebird (tequila plus ancho liqueur plus port equals ALSO YUM) and then hit the dance floor and shake what ya mama gave ya.  _46 Geary St_ _(415) 693-9255_
