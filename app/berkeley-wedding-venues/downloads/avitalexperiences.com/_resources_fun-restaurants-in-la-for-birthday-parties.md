#  Los Angeles Fun Restaurants in LA for Birthday Parties 

January 10, 2018

## **Clifton‚Äôs**

[![Clifton's is Los Angeles Fun Restaurants in LA for Birthday Parties](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/2/2014/02/Downtown-Food-Tour-Outside-Cliftons-300x225.jpg)](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/2/2014/02/Downtown-Food-Tour-Outside-Cliftons.jpg)

There are lots of fun restaurants in LA for birthday parties, but Clifton‚Äôs is the closest thing to experiencing a meal at Disneyland or the Natural History Museum outside without paying an entrance fee. The ‚ÄúCabinet of Curiosities‚Äù including a life-sized bison, a giant meteorite, and waterfalls is a fun setting for a birthday celebration. ## **Button Mash**

[![Button Mash Los Angeles Fun Restaurants in LA for Birthday Parties](data:image/svg+xml,%3Csvg%20xmlns='http://www.w3.org/2000/svg'%20viewBox='0%200%20640%20480'%3E%3C/svg%3E)![Button Mash Los Angeles Fun Restaurants in LA for Birthday Parties](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/2/2017/01/5588208090_aeea92d7fe_z.jpg)](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/2/2017/01/5588208090_aeea92d7fe_z.jpg)

Go old school with an arcade birthday party. But unlike the arcades of your fifth birthday party, this spot serves more than greasy pizza and fries. Button Mash offers Asian food and snacks such as Double Fried Tamarind Chicken Wings, Vietnamese Egg Rolls, Cheese Corn, and Dan Dan Noodles. After eating grab another beer and some tokens to play some pinball, Ms. Pac Man, and Mortal Kombat, The restaurant kicks the kids out after 9 pm so time your reservation (for parties of 10 or more) accordingly. 

_<http://buttonmashla.com/>_

_1391 Sunset Blvd,__Los Angeles, CA 90026_

## **The Venue**

[![SIng Karaoke at Los Angeles Fun Restaurants in LA for Birthday Parties](data:image/svg+xml,%3Csvg%20xmlns='http://www.w3.org/2000/svg'%20viewBox='0%200%201298%20872'%3E%3C/svg%3E)![SIng Karaoke at Los Angeles Fun Restaurants in LA for Birthday Parties](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/2/2017/01/Screen-Shot-2017-01-30-at-12.47.34-PM.png)](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/2/2017/01/Screen-Shot-2017-01-30-at-12.47.34-PM.png)

You could have dinner and then drag your friends to karaoke afterward, or you could visit The Venue, which is a full-service restaurant and karaoke lounge that is opening this February. Don‚Äôt expect only Korean fare here. You will find chicken and steaks with Korean flavors, but you‚Äôll also be able to eat raw fish, fried macaroni and cheese and beet and goat cheese salad. Each of the 13 karaoke rooms can hold 50 people, so your guest list can include all of the talented and tone deaf singers in your life. The Venue offers a full bar with signature cocktails from experienced LA bartenders, and you can also get a bar cart in your private room. If want to skip the sit-down dinner, a smaller menu is available for delivery right to your singing suite. 

https://www.thevenuela.com/ 

_3470 Wilshire Blvd.,__Los Angeles CA, 90010_
