#  5 Beautiful Restaurants In Los Angeles 

October 1, 2018 

Lots of the best restaurants in Los Angeles happen to be stuffed into strip malls. And as Jonathan Gold showed us, many of them are delicious, despite not being the most beautiful. But LA is filled with its share of beautiful restaurants as well. Here are five beautiful restaurants in Los Angeles.

## The Wolves

[![5 Beautiful Restaurants In Los Angeles-the wolves](data:image/svg+xml,%3Csvg%20xmlns='http://www.w3.org/2000/svg'%20viewBox='0%200%20800%20533'%3E%3C/svg%3E)![5 Beautiful Restaurants In Los Angeles-the wolves](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/2/2018/10/5-Beautiful-Restaurants-In-Los-Angeles-the-wolves.jpg)](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/2/2018/10/5-Beautiful-Restaurants-In-Los-Angeles-the-wolves.jpg)

_image credit: The Wolves_

Okay, we know it‚Äôs primarily a bar, but forgive us because[ The Wolves](https://thewolvesdtla.com/) is stunning. This DTLA spot opened in the summer of 2018 but already has become a downtown destination. And The Wolves does serve food, but everyone is talking about their cocktail menu which is characterized by categories such as ‚ÄúDelicate & Subtle‚Äù, ‚ÄúStrong & Robust‚Äù, ‚ÄúOld Fashioned‚Äù, and ‚ÄúVermouth Cocktails‚Äù. Guests can drink cocktails surrounded by mahogany, marble, and stained glass. It‚Äôs the kind of place you‚Äôll go for a date‚Ä¶ or an Instagram photo session.

## Bottega Louie

[![5 Beautiful Restaurants In Los Angeles-bottega louie](data:image/svg+xml,%3Csvg%20xmlns='http://www.w3.org/2000/svg'%20viewBox='0%200%202048%201365'%3E%3C/svg%3E)![5 Beautiful Restaurants In Los Angeles-bottega louie](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/2/2018/02/26089838692_251106db29_k.jpg)](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/2/2018/02/26089838692_251106db29_k.jpg)

_image credit: Bottega Louie_

This 225-seat downtown restaurant and patisserie is in a former Brooks Brothers. Bottega Louie contributed to a to a revival on 7th Street, also known as ‚ÄúRestaurant Row.‚Äù Now instead of clothes, [Bottega Louie](https://www.bottegalouie.com) is filled with a rainbow of macaroons. The restaurant is big, bright, and white (a great contrast to the colorful pastries) It has a very ‚ÄúBreakfast at Tiffany‚Äôs‚Äù feel to it and is the perfect spot for a picture-perfect brunch.

## Felix Trattoria

[![5 Beautiful Restaurants In Los Angeles-felix](data:image/svg+xml,%3Csvg%20xmlns='http://www.w3.org/2000/svg'%20viewBox='0%200%201500%201000'%3E%3C/svg%3E)![5 Beautiful Restaurants In Los Angeles-felix](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/2/2018/10/5-Beautiful-Restaurants-In-Los-Angeles-felix.jpg)](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/2/2018/10/5-Beautiful-Restaurants-In-Los-Angeles-felix.jpg)

_image credit: Felix Trattoria_

The entirety of [Felix](http://felixla.com/) is inviting and photogenic. The floral green wallpaper makes a great backdrop, but the star is the pasta room. The giant glass windows are perfect for people who want to watch their pasta being made. And luckily, the pasta is just as beautiful as the restaurant. The restaurant sits on the corner of Abbot Kinney in Venice so it‚Äôs easy to watch pedestrians walking by, but more likely any passerby is going to be looking into Felix.

## Redbird

[![](data:image/svg+xml,%3Csvg%20xmlns='http://www.w3.org/2000/svg'%20viewBox='0%200%20845%20480'%3E%3C/svg%3E)![](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/2/2018/10/vibiana-at-Redbird-5-Beautiful-Restaurants-In-Los-Angeles.jpg)](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/2/2018/10/vibiana-at-Redbird-5-Beautiful-Restaurants-In-Los-Angeles.jpg)

The public dining areas at [Redbird](https://redbird.la/about/) is beautiful itself, but the private dining rooms are above and beyond. While the Cardinal‚Äôs Quarters and the Bishop‚Äôs Perch are perfect for small events, the most incredible of the spaces is the [Vibiana](http://vibiana.com/), Los Angeles‚Äô first Archdiocese Catholic Cathedral. The cathedral-turned-wedding, performing arts and event venue can fit up to 800 people for an event. If you go to Redbird for dinner, ask a staff member if you can peek behind-the-scenes at the garden and Vibiana‚Ä¶or host your own event there.

## Wabi Venice

[![Wabi Vence. 5 Beautiful Restaurants In Los Angeles](data:image/svg+xml,%3Csvg%20xmlns='http://www.w3.org/2000/svg'%20viewBox='0%200%201200%20801'%3E%3C/svg%3E)![Wabi Vence. 5 Beautiful Restaurants In Los Angeles](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/2/2018/10/5-Beautiful-Restaurants-In-Los-Angeles-wabi-venice.jpg)](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/2/2018/10/5-Beautiful-Restaurants-In-Los-Angeles-wabi-venice.jpg)

Exit Abbot Kinney and enter a plant-filled oasis at [Wabi Venice.](https://www.wabivenice.com/) The front room holds a sleek bar and a brick wall. The center room is filled with giant round lights and hanging plants. And the back room has walls covered with bold, floral wallpaper and Edison bulbs. No matter where you sit, the food is as good as the backdrop.
