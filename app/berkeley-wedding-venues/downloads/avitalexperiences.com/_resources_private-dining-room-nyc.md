### **Group Reservation**

Reserve tables within a restaurant for your group without exclusivity. This is a budget-friendly option for smaller, casual gatherings. While it‚Äôs cost-effective, it lacks the privacy of a dedicated space.

Semi-private spaces are partitioned areas within a restaurant that offer partial exclusivity. They‚Äôre ideal for medium-sized events where privacy isn‚Äôt a top priority but a bit of separation is appreciated.

### **Restaurant Buyout**

A restaurant buyout gives you exclusive access to the entire venue. It‚Äôs perfect for large-scale events like weddings, company-wide celebrations, or high-profile gatherings. With a buyout, you have full control over the setup, menu, and ambiance.

### **Event Space Rental**

### **Catering at Your Location**

Hosting your event at home, in the office, or another private location? Catering services bring restaurant-quality meals to you. This option is great for those seeking complete control over the environment while still enjoying delicious food.
