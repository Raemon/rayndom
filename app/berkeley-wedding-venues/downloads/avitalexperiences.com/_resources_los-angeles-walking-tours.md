#  Yes, We Walk Here. 5 Los Angeles Walking Tours To Explore The City 

June 16, 2018 

You‚Äôve probably heard someone say, ‚ÄúNo one walks in LA.‚Äù We want to make sure you know that‚Äôs not true. Plenty of people are walking around neighborhoods like Venice, Downtown, and Santa Monica. Sure, the car is the most common way of travel, but there is a lot to see on foot. Los Angeles walking tours provide a new perspective on the city that can‚Äôt be seen from the front seat. Here are five of our favorites.

## Los Angeles Conservancy Walking Tours

[![Take a walk with the LA Conservatory: Yes, We Walk Here. 5 Los Angeles Walking Tours To Take.](data:image/svg+xml,%3Csvg%20xmlns='http://www.w3.org/2000/svg'%20viewBox='0%200%20506%20310'%3E%3C/svg%3E)![Take a walk with the LA Conservatory: Yes, We Walk Here. 5 Los Angeles Walking Tours To Take.](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/2/2018/06/lac-40th-web-logo.png)](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/2/2018/06/lac-40th-web-logo.png)

The LA Conservatory offers[ self-guided tours](https://www.laconservancy.org/events-tours/self-guided-tours) as well as multiple [walking tours](https://www.laconservancy.org/tours) each week. Choose your own adventure from the Victorian neighborhood of Angelino Heights to a guided journey in Union Station. Private [group tours](https://www.laconservancy.org/group-tours) are also available. You‚Äôll learn about architecture, art, culture, and social history.

## Avital Tours

[![venice beach walking tour Yes, We Walk Here. 5 Los Angeles Walking Tours To Take.](data:image/svg+xml,%3Csvg%20xmlns='http://www.w3.org/2000/svg'%20viewBox='0%200%202200%201467'%3E%3C/svg%3E)![venice beach walking tour Yes, We Walk Here. 5 Los Angeles Walking Tours To Take.](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/2/2018/06/Katie-Edwards.-Venice-Beach.-Introduction-on-Beach.jpg)](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/2/2018/06/Katie-Edwards.-Venice-Beach.-Introduction-on-Beach.jpg)

On this walking tour, you‚Äôll get to eat. Walk between courses in [Venice](http://avitaltours.com/los-angeles/tour/venice-beach-food-tour/), [Koreatown](http://avitaltours.com/los-angeles/koreatown-food-tour), or [Downtown Los Angeles](http://avitaltours.com/los-angeles/tour/downtown-food-tour/). The three-hour and four-restaurant [tour](http://avitaltours.com/los-angeles/) includes two appetizer portions, an entree, and a dessert. On your walk, your guide will share the history of the neighborhood and the restaurants you visit.

## GPSMyCity

[![Yes, We Walk Here. 5 Los Angeles Walking Tours To Take.-Use this app to get you out and about](data:image/svg+xml,%3Csvg%20xmlns='http://www.w3.org/2000/svg'%20viewBox='0%200%20498%20492'%3E%3C/svg%3E)![Yes, We Walk Here. 5 Los Angeles Walking Tours To Take.-Use this app to get you out and about](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/2/2018/06/Screen-Shot-2018-06-01-at-6.10.00-PM.png)](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/2/2018/06/Screen-Shot-2018-06-01-at-6.10.00-PM.png)

These walks are s[elf-guided](https://www.gpsmycity.com/gps-tour-guides/los-angeles-631.html) so you can head out whenever you want to explore. With 21 walks to choose from, it‚Äôs worth downloading the [Los Angeles Map and Walks](https://www.gpsmycity.com/user/index.php?do=click_log&url=https%3A%2F%2Fitunes.apple.com%2Fus%2Fapp%2Flos-angeles-map-walking-tours%2Fid367828282%3Fmt%3D8%26at%3D1010l9wd%26ct%3Dwebsite) app to keep in your back pocket (literally). Pop culture fans might like the [Live Music Venues Walking Tour ](https://www.gpsmycity.com/tours/live-music-venues-walking-tour-in-los-angeles-2879.html)or [Movie Studios Walking Tour](https://www.gpsmycity.com/tours/movie-studios-walking-tour-in-los-angeles-3079.html), and foodies can stop for snacks on the [Little Tokyo Walk](https://www.gpsmycity.com/discovery/little-tokyo-walk-390.html). The app will navigate you on your journey and provide context for what you‚Äôre seeing.

## LA Art Tours

[![Tours led by artists-LA Art Tours Yes, We Walk Here. 5 Los Angeles Walking Tours To Explore The City](data:image/svg+xml,%3Csvg%20xmlns='http://www.w3.org/2000/svg'%20viewBox='0%200%201226%20296'%3E%3C/svg%3E)![Tours led by artists-LA Art Tours Yes, We Walk Here. 5 Los Angeles Walking Tours To Explore The City](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/2/2018/06/Screen-Shot-2018-06-01-at-6.22.13-PM.png)](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/2/2018/06/Screen-Shot-2018-06-01-at-6.22.13-PM.png)

In a city full of creatives, it makes perfect sense that there is an art tour. The guides at [LA Art Tours](http://laarttours.com/) are all artists themselves, so they are intimately familiar with the art scene in LA. Explore the city in new ways and go on the [Downtown LA Graffiti/Mural Tour](http://laarttours.com/graffititour/), [Craft Beer and Urban Art](http://laarttours.com/urban-art-and-craft-beer-tour/) Tour, [The Brewery Art Complex Tour](http://laarttours.com/brewerytour/), [Santa Fe Art Colony Tour](http://laarttours.com/santafeartcolony/), or hop on two wheels for the [Graffiti Bike Tour](http://laarttours.com/biketour/).

## Atlas Obscura

[![Atlas Obscura-Yes, We Walk Here. 5 Los Angeles Walking Tours To Explore The City](data:image/svg+xml,%3Csvg%20xmlns='http://www.w3.org/2000/svg'%20viewBox='0%200%20506%20124'%3E%3C/svg%3E)![Atlas Obscura-Yes, We Walk Here. 5 Los Angeles Walking Tours To Explore The City](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/2/2018/06/Screen-Shot-2018-06-01-at-6.29.45-PM.png)](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/2/2018/06/Screen-Shot-2018-06-01-at-6.29.45-PM.png)

We love to #embracequirky, so we appreciate the strange events put on by [Atlas Obscura](https://www.atlasobscura.com/events). Their events are always changing and sell out pretty quickly, but keep your eyes peeled for walking tours among them. One weekend they might lead a tour of a cemetery and another an adventure in an abandoned zoo. A recent event was a 2.5 mile Los Angeles River Adventure. Subscribe to their email list to stay updated.
