**Liquor Liability Insurance** : If you‚Äôre hiring **alcohol vendors for events** , they‚Äôll likely need this coverage. Make sure they‚Äôre licensed and insured to serve.

**Host or Event Insurance** : Even if your vendors are covered, you may still need **event insurance** as the organizer‚Äîespecially for large or public events.

**Venue Requirements** : Always check with your venue in advance. Some may require certificates of insurance (COIs) naming them as an additional insured.
