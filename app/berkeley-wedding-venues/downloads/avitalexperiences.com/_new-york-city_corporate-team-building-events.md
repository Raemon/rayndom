## **What are Corporate Team Building Events in NYC?**

NYC [corporate team building events](https://avitalexperiences.com/corporate-team-building/) bring teams together outside of the standard office environment. The goal of these events are to allow colleagues an opportunity to get to know one another better so they can form bonds and ultimately work better together back in the office. There are many different types of team building services and experiences to choose from, from [food tours](https://avitalexperiences.com/new-york-city/walking-food-tours/) and [interactive meals](https://avitalexperiences.com/experiences/nyc-interactive-meal/) or art classes and scavenger hunts.
