![person-eating-food-during-michelin-tour-experience-gifts-nyc](data:image/svg+xml,%3Csvg%20xmlns='http://www.w3.org/2000/svg'%20viewBox='0%200%20700%20467'%3E%3C/svg%3E)![person-eating-food-during-michelin-tour-experience-gifts-nyc](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/5/2022/05/person-eating-food-during-michelin-tour-experience-gifts-nyc.jpg)

Available Thursdays, Fridays and Saturdays at 6pm. [Book your semi-private experience here](https://avitalexperiences.com/experiences/new-york-city-michelin-food-tour/).

## **Have The Roller Disco Of Your Dreams**

[![group-roller-blading-birthday](data:image/svg+xml,%3Csvg%20xmlns='http://www.w3.org/2000/svg'%20viewBox='0%200%20700%20467'%3E%3C/svg%3E)![group-roller-blading-birthday](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/5/2017/12/roller-disco-30th-birthday-idea.jpg)](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/5/2017/12/roller-disco-30th-birthday-idea.jpg)Put on your shiniest outfit and head to [Dreamland Roller Rink](http://www.dreamlandrollerrink.com/) for their weekly roller disco. ## Plan A Pool or Hot Tub Party

[![luxury-pool-birthday-party](data:image/svg+xml,%3Csvg%20xmlns='http://www.w3.org/2000/svg'%20viewBox='0%200%20700%20467'%3E%3C/svg%3E)![luxury-pool-birthday-party](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/5/2017/12/pool-and-hot-tub-party-30th-birthday.jpg)](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/5/2017/12/pool-and-hot-tub-party-30th-birthday.jpg)Not all birthday parties have to be active. Lay around and sit in a water all day to relax on your special day. If your birthday is in the summer months, there are [lots of rooftop pools](https://www.cntraveler.com/galleries/2016-06-08/10-best-rooftop-bars-in-new-york-city) to take over for a day of splashing around. During NYC‚Äôs colder months, stay inside and relax in some hot baths. From the [Russian Turkish Baths](http://www.russianturkishbaths.com/) to the hip [Premier 57](https://premier57.com/) to Brookyln‚Äôs[ Seagate Baths,](http://www.seagatebaths.com/) there are plenty of places for you and your guests soak in the five boroughs.

[![nyc-food-tour-birthday-idea](data:image/svg+xml,%3Csvg%20xmlns='http://www.w3.org/2000/svg'%20viewBox='0%200%20700%20467'%3E%3C/svg%3E)![nyc-food-tour-birthday-idea](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/5/2017/12/east-village-food-tour-30th-birthday.jpg)](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/5/2017/12/east-village-food-tour-30th-birthday.jpg)The East Village is one of the coolest neighborhoods in NYC ‚Äì in fact, it‚Äôs known for its role as the birthplace of punk and artist hangouts. Taste a bite of Little Ukraine, and other ethnic cuisines from immigrants who have made the East Village one of the most diverse eating grounds in NYC. Let‚Äôs eat and drink through this cultural neighborhood!

[![sailing-with-friends-birthday-idea](data:image/svg+xml,%3Csvg%20xmlns='http://www.w3.org/2000/svg'%20viewBox='0%200%20700%20467'%3E%3C/svg%3E)![sailing-with-friends-birthday-idea](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/5/2017/12/sailing-30th-birthday-idea.jpg)](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/5/2017/12/sailing-30th-birthday-idea.jpg)Wouldn‚Äôt it be great to charter a yacht and head to the Bahamas for a week? [![night-at-the-museum-with-friends](data:image/svg+xml,%3Csvg%20xmlns='http://www.w3.org/2000/svg'%20viewBox='0%200%20700%20467'%3E%3C/svg%3E)![night-at-the-museum-with-friends](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/5/2017/12/museum-slumber-party-30th-birthday-idea.jpg)](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/5/2017/12/museum-slumber-party-30th-birthday-idea.jpg)Did you think sleepovers were left behind in your teenage years? [![fun-night-bowling-with-friends](data:image/svg+xml,%3Csvg%20xmlns='http://www.w3.org/2000/svg'%20viewBox='0%200%20700%20467'%3E%3C/svg%3E)![fun-night-bowling-with-friends](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/5/2017/12/bowling-for-30th-birthday-idea.jpg)](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/5/2017/12/bowling-for-30th-birthday-idea.jpg)If you want to go big, rent out a whole bowling alley. [![picnic-with-friends-birthday-idea](data:image/svg+xml,%3Csvg%20xmlns='http://www.w3.org/2000/svg'%20viewBox='0%200%20700%20467'%3E%3C/svg%3E)![picnic-with-friends-birthday-idea](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/5/2017/12/parkside-picnic-30th-birthday-idea.jpg)](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/5/2017/12/parkside-picnic-30th-birthday-idea.jpg)One way to ensure you can fit everyone you love into your party?
