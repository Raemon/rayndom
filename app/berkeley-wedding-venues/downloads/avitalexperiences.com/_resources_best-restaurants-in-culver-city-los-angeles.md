Michelin-Recommended restaurant serving West Coast seafood with classic French cooking techniques. For a fancy night out, order ‚ÄúLe Omakase‚Äù six-course menu.

![thumbnail](data:image/svg+xml,%3Csvg%20xmlns='http://www.w3.org/2000/svg'%20viewBox='0%200%20150%20150'%3E%3C/svg%3E)![thumbnail](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/2025/09/mensho.com_-150x150.jpg)

##### Vespertine

[ Website ](https://vespertine.la) ¬∑ [ Open in Google Maps ](https://maps.app.goo.gl/FehXP8ZChUfqURbz7)

two-Michelin-star restaurant that has helped put Culver City on the map as a food destination in Los Angeles. They are also awarded a green star by Michelin for their sustainability practices, so it‚Äôs a restaurant you can feel good about supporting.

Dedicated vegetarian kitchen makes it great for dietary restrictions. 

![thumbnail](data:image/svg+xml,%3Csvg%20xmlns='http://www.w3.org/2000/svg'%20viewBox='0%200%20150%20150'%3E%3C/svg%3E)![thumbnail](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/2025/09/8.-@kusano_culver_city-150x150.jpg)

##### Kusano (MR)

[ Website ](http://resy.com/link?venue_id=84689) ¬∑ [ Open in Google Maps ](https://maps.app.goo.gl/3sFRPJSTPXNkeY4w5)

A Michelin-guide, one-man operation, delivering a high-quality omakase experience to only sixteen people a night. Chef Yasu Kusano does everything, from preparing every perfect dish to pouring your drink to washing the dishes at the end of the night. 

![thumbnail](data:image/svg+xml,%3Csvg%20xmlns='http://www.w3.org/2000/svg'%20viewBox='0%200%20150%20150'%3E%3C/svg%3E)![thumbnail](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/2025/09/9cafe-brasil.com_-150x150.jpg)

![thumbnail](data:image/svg+xml,%3Csvg%20xmlns='http://www.w3.org/2000/svg'%20viewBox='0%200%20150%20150'%3E%3C/svg%3E)![thumbnail](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/2025/09/pastasisters.com_-150x150.jpeg)

##### Pasta Sisters

[ Website ](https://www.pastasisters.com) ¬∑ [ Open in Google Maps ](https://maps.app.goo.gl/6nW9ZVci29eX3hq56)

A family-run restaurant with rich history and even richer pastas, made in-house every day. The arrabiata is delightfully spicy, and if you add the burrata, it becomes a creamy, zingy masterpiece. The indulgent porcini mushroom pasta with cream and parsley is also a menu highlight.

Watering hole dating back to the 1960s. Dine on a phenomenal steak or lobster thermidor, while you sip on a Vesper or Hemingway, and find yourself transported to the days when Frank Sinatra played the piano in the corner.

![thumbnail](data:image/svg+xml,%3Csvg%20xmlns='http://www.w3.org/2000/svg'%20viewBox='0%200%20150%20150'%3E%3C/svg%3E)![thumbnail](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/2025/09/robertaspizza.com_culver-city-150x150.jpeg)
