#  Food Tours: The Ultimate Team Building Icebreaker 

April 11, 2018 

You‚Äôve done trust falls and group games and scavenger hunts, but nothing has quite nailed breaking in new members of your team. We think eating and exploring together is a great way to break the ice. So we‚Äôre recommending food tours: the ultimate team building icebreaker. Here are five reasons why.

## 1\. You‚Äôll bond over food stories.

![sharing a table is special, Food Tours: The Ultimate Team Building Icebreaker](data:image/svg+xml,%3Csvg%20xmlns='http://www.w3.org/2000/svg'%20viewBox='0%200%202200%201467'%3E%3C/svg%3E)![sharing a table is special, Food Tours: The Ultimate Team Building Icebreaker](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/5/2018/04/Katie-Edwards.-Venice-Beach.-Guests-cheersing-with-wine-at-Plant-Food-and-Wine-1.jpg)

Sometimes the best way to get to know someone is to sit and share a meal together. After all, sitting around a restaurant table is much more fun than sitting around a boardroom table. Do you know the foods your colleagues grew up eating? Do you know the food they can‚Äôt stand, or that they eat over and over every day? We‚Äôll help you share your food stories to get you to know your team better.

## 2\. Your teammates will learn about each others‚Äô backgrounds.

[![share food stories durng Food Tours: The Ultimate Team Building Icebreaker](data:image/svg+xml,%3Csvg%20xmlns='http://www.w3.org/2000/svg'%20viewBox='0%200%20800%20532'%3E%3C/svg%3E)![share food stories durng Food Tours: The Ultimate Team Building Icebreaker](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/5/2018/04/Max-fletow.-Williamsburg-Food-Tour.-XLB-Soup-Dumplings-and-eating-at-Birds-of-a-Feather.jpeg)](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/5/2018/04/Max-fletow.-Williamsburg-Food-Tour.-XLB-Soup-Dumplings-and-eating-at-Birds-of-a-Feather.jpeg)

Maybe a lobster roll brings up memories of New England summers or maybe a dumpling stop helps your colleague recall their days stuffing gyoza with grandma. Our tours bring up a lot of nostalgia that turns into great bonding among teams. You‚Äôll be able to learn about each other in ways that wouldn‚Äôt come up at the office.

## 3\. You‚Äôll share an experience.

[![Food Tours: The Ultimate Team Building Icebreaker: hanging at blue star donuts](data:image/svg+xml,%3Csvg%20xmlns='http://www.w3.org/2000/svg'%20viewBox='0%200%20800%20414'%3E%3C/svg%3E)![Food Tours: The Ultimate Team Building Icebreaker: hanging at blue star donuts](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/5/2018/04/Los-Angeles-Corporate-Team-Building-Activity-Group-at-Blue-Star-Donuts.jpg)](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/5/2018/04/Los-Angeles-Corporate-Team-Building-Activity-Group-at-Blue-Star-Donuts.jpg)

Sharing an experience is a surefire way to help break the ice faster. When your team sees each other in the office they‚Äôll be able to recall the fun they had blow torching sushi or making dessert together. This a way to bond that happy hour just doesn‚Äôt provide.

## 4\. Someone else is in charge of facilitation so your team can sit back and enjoy.

[![someone will facilitate your experinece : Food Tours: The Ultimate Team Building Icebreaker](data:image/svg+xml,%3Csvg%20xmlns='http://www.w3.org/2000/svg'%20viewBox='0%200%20800%20533'%3E%3C/svg%3E)![someone will facilitate your experinece : Food Tours: The Ultimate Team Building Icebreaker](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/5/2018/04/Dacia-Pierson.-East-Village-Food-Tour.-Avital-closeup-of-Your-Culinary-Experience-Begins-Here-sign.jpeg)](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/5/2018/04/Dacia-Pierson.-East-Village-Food-Tour.-Avital-closeup-of-Your-Culinary-Experience-Begins-Here-sign.jpeg)

Once you book a culinary experience, your job is over. For the duration of the tour, you get to sit back, relax, eat, and drink while your guide is in charge of the logistics. That way everyone on your team can focus on team building and not worry about checking on reservations.

## 5\. You‚Äôll get to walk and talk.

[![walk and talk on your food tour; Food Tours: The Ultimate Team Building Icebreaker: ](data:image/svg+xml,%3Csvg%20xmlns='http://www.w3.org/2000/svg'%20viewBox='0%200%20800%20533'%3E%3C/svg%3E)![walk and talk on your food tour; Food Tours: The Ultimate Team Building Icebreaker: ](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/5/2018/04/IMG_3415.jpg)](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/5/2018/04/IMG_3415.jpg)

At a traditional sit-down dinner, you can chat with the people next to you and across from you‚Ä¶and that‚Äôs it. During a food tour you‚Äôll walk between each course so you can meet someone new, and at the next restaurant, the seating arrangements will change. The format of a food tour is a great way to keep shaking things up without forcing it.
