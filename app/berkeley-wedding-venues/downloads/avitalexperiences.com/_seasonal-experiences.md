## **What are Seasonal Experiences?**

Seasonal events are special occasions and celebrations that occur at specific times of the year, often tied to changes in seasons or holidays. These events bring a sense of anticipation and excitement as they offer a chance to break away from routine and immerse oneself in a unique atmosphere. From traditional holidays like Christmas, Diwali, and Thanksgiving to cultural festivals, like Cinco de Mayo, St. Patrick‚Äôs Day, and the the Kentucky Derby, seasonal experiences hold a distinct place in our lives.

They often involve customs, decorations, and activities that reflect the spirit of the season, fostering a sense of unity and shared experience among communities.
