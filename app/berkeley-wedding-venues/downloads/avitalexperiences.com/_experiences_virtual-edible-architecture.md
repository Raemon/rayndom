## What Is A Charcuterie Chalet?

The Charcuterie Chalet is a savory gingerbread house made from items found on charcuterie and cheese boards. This [viral TikTok trend](https://www.tiktok.com/search?q=charcuterie%20chalet&t=1696345793900) has appeared year after year and continues to grow in excitement.
