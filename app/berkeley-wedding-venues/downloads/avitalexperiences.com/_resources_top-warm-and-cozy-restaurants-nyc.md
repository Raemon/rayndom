## 1\. **Maison Premiere**

[Reserve a Table](https://resy.com/cities/ny/maison-premiere?date=2024-02-12&seats=2)

[View Website](https://maisonpremiere.com/)

Maison Premiere is all about ambiance. As soon as you step inside the small restaurant, you‚Äôre immediately transported to the French Quarter in New Orleans or a cozy bar in Paris. From the antique liquor bottles behind the bar, marble countertops, and flickering candles on tiny tables, you‚Äôll soon find yourself warmed up with an absinthe cocktail in your hands. Presentation is key at Maison Premiere, and whether you‚Äôre enjoying oysters from the raw bar or a bowl of seafood gumbo, you‚Äôll feel far away from the cold, if only for a little while.

[View Website](https://pauliegee.com/restaurant/greenpoint/)

[Open in Google Maps](https://maps.app.goo.gl/cfcdRzpnCpQucuNi9)

## 3\. **Ruffian**

[Reserve a Table](https://resy.com/cities/ny/ruffian?seats=2&date=2024-02-12)

[View Website](https://ruffiannyc.com/)

[Open in Google Maps](https://maps.app.goo.gl/zUjo48D97rkw6jZBA)

[Ruffian](http://ruffiannyc.com/) uncorks some of the best bottles of wine and serves up small plates with loads of flavor. This wine bar is practically the definition of cozy, with a 19-seat bar and an open kitchen. Prepare to sit in close quarters to your fellow wine lovers, and also be ready to try wines from Northern France, Austria, and the Republic of Georgia. A glass or two will help you brave the walk to the subway, or at least keep you warm while you wait for your Uber.

[Reserve a Table](https://resy.com/cities/ny/il-buco?date=2024-02-12&seats=2)

[View Website](https://ilbuco.com/)

[Open in Google Maps](https://maps.app.goo.gl/keyuPfTSYVmnFvWM9)

This Soho mainstay started as an antique shop and eventually turned into one of the best places to enjoy a comforting bowl of _zuppa,_ perfect ribbons of housemade _tagliatelle_ , and delicious, creamy risotto. Everything about Il Buco will make you happy you went out for dinner and didn‚Äôt decide to stay in your apartment all winter.

[View Website](https://www.supperrestaurant.com/)

[View Website](https://pylosrestaurant.com/)

[Open in Google Maps](https://maps.app.goo.gl/U2eGLA6wP7X1XFxa6)
