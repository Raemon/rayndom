## 1\. A Food Tour

[![take a food tour-5 Los Angeles Team Building Activities Your Coworkers Won't Hate](data:image/svg+xml,%3Csvg%20xmlns='http://www.w3.org/2000/svg'%20viewBox='0%200%202200%201467'%3E%3C/svg%3E)![take a food tour-5 Los Angeles Team Building Activities Your Coworkers Won't Hate](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/2/2018/06/Katie-Edwards.-Downtown-LA.-Guests-listening-behind-th-scenes-at-Peddlers.jpg)](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/2/2018/06/Katie-Edwards.-Downtown-LA.-Guests-listening-behind-th-scenes-at-Peddlers.jpg)

We don‚Äôt think your colleagues will hate you if you take them out to eat, especially if it‚Äôs a four-course progressive meal. We can customize a culinary experience to help your team bond while exploring a new neighborhood. Your guide will share the history of the neighborhood and the restaurants you visit. Your team can partake in some team building activities and have plenty of unstructured time to enjoy their meal.

## 2\. A Baseball Game

[![go to a baseball game: 5 Los Angeles Team Building Activities Your Coworkers Won't Hate](data:image/svg+xml,%3Csvg%20xmlns='http://www.w3.org/2000/svg'%20viewBox='0%200%20640%20468'%3E%3C/svg%3E)![go to a baseball game: 5 Los Angeles Team Building Activities Your Coworkers Won't Hate](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/2/2018/06/4607113301_0464d29648_z.jpg)](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/2/2018/06/4607113301_0464d29648_z.jpg)

Even if your coworkers can‚Äôt agree on a sports team, a day at the stadium is still a great way to hang. If not all of your colleagues are big sports fans, we promise they still won‚Äôt hate getting to leave the office to spend time outside eating hot dogs and drinking beers. Use [this guide of best ](https://la.eater.com/2018/3/29/17175484/where-to-eat-dodger-stadium-best-food)[food at the stadium ](https://la.eater.com/2018/3/29/17175484/where-to-eat-dodger-stadium-best-food)if you want to skip a Dodger Dog and eat more gourmet.

## 3\. A Scavenger Hunt Or Field Day

[![scavenger hunt: 5 Los Angeles Team Building Activities Your Coworkers Won't Hate:](data:image/svg+xml,%3Csvg%20xmlns='http://www.w3.org/2000/svg'%20viewBox='0%200%20640%20512'%3E%3C/svg%3E)![scavenger hunt: 5 Los Angeles Team Building Activities Your Coworkers Won't Hate:](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/2/2018/06/10268837315_da32f5ab7b_z.jpg)](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/2/2018/06/10268837315_da32f5ab7b_z.jpg)

Get everyone out of the office and exploring the city. Your coworkers run around, search for clues, solve silly challenges, and compete for prizes. Companies like [The Go Game ](http://thegogame.com/team-building-games/)and [Watson Adventures](https://www.watsonadventures.com/public/location/los-angeles/) customize competitions for your company with personalized trivia and puzzles. You can also have them organize lawn games to mimic the field days of elementary school, or a game show in your office. It‚Äôs less corny than the oft-dreaded team building activities, and it will create friendly competition among even the most skeptical teammates.

## 4\. A Cocktail Tour

[![take them on a cocktail tour. 5 Los Angeles Team Building Activities Your Coworkers Won't Hate](data:image/svg+xml,%3Csvg%20xmlns='http://www.w3.org/2000/svg'%20viewBox='0%200%203000%202250'%3E%3C/svg%3E)![take them on a cocktail tour. 5 Los Angeles Team Building Activities Your Coworkers Won't Hate](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/2/2018/06/IMG_1758.JPG-2.jpeg)](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/2/2018/06/IMG_1758.JPG-2.jpeg)

Think of it as a roving happy hour. Book a [Hollywood](http://avitaltours.com/los-angeles/tour/hollywood-cocktail-tour/) cocktail tour and your team will visit three bars in two hours to sample drinks from historic Hollywood watering holes. They‚Äôll also learn the history of cocktails, and explore Hollywood. Three hours of drinking and exploring is pretty hard to hate, so we think this team bonding event is a win.

## 5\. A Silly Sport

[![play a silly sport like laser tag. 5 Los Angeles Team Building Activities Your Coworkers Won't Hate](data:image/svg+xml,%3Csvg%20xmlns='http://www.w3.org/2000/svg'%20viewBox='0%200%20480%20640'%3E%3C/svg%3E)![play a silly sport like laser tag. 5 Los Angeles Team Building Activities Your Coworkers Won't Hate](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/2/2018/06/7110386249_af27501a28_z.jpg)](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/2/2018/06/7110386249_af27501a28_z.jpg)

Let your team know that it‚Äôs okay to relax by having everyone play together just for fun. [Trampoline Dodgeball ](https://www.skyzone.com/torrance/attractions/ultimate-dodgeball)is like your gym class but played while bouncing. If your team is inspired by the winter Olympics, take a [curling](https://www.hollywoodcurling.org/) class together in Hollywood. Or head back to your arcade days and play [laser tag. ](http://www.zonehead.com/)
