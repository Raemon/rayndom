#  Where to Find the Best Lobster in Los Angeles 

May 2, 2017 

Though it‚Äôs a seaside city, Los Angeles isn‚Äôt known for lobster. Despite being thousands of miles from Maine, however, it‚Äôs easy to find great lobster in LA. Local chefs have put their own twist on lobster rolls, paid homage to LA food with lobster tacos, baked the seafood in pot pies, and have also kept it classic. Here‚Äôs where to find the best lobster in Los Angeles.

## The Anchor

[![Lobster Mac and Cheese from the Anchor, Where to Find the Best Lobster in Los Angeles](data:image/svg+xml,%3Csvg%20xmlns='http://www.w3.org/2000/svg'%20viewBox='0%200%202048%201366'%3E%3C/svg%3E)![Lobster Mac and Cheese from the Anchor, Where to Find the Best Lobster in Los Angeles](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/2/2017/03/13951918741_5b1296e791_k.jpg)](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/2/2017/03/13951918741_5b1296e791_k.jpg)

**Get:** The Lobster Roll and Lobster Mac and Cheese

It‚Äôs hard to discuss where to find the best lobster in Los Angeles without bringing up The Anchor. The only two items on the ‚ÄúA Must‚Äù section of The Anchor‚Äôs menu are the lobster roll with Maine lobster, shaved black truffle, and sea salt, and the lobster mac and cheese, with cheese fondue and truffle. Does a lobster roll really need to made more decadent with black truffle on top? Yes.

_<http://theanchorvenice.squarespace.com/>[  
](http://theanchorvenice.squarespace.com/)__235 Main St, Venice, CA 90291_

## Plan Check Santa Monica

[![Lobster Pot Pie at Plan Check Where to Find the Best Lobster in Los Angeles](data:image/svg+xml,%3Csvg%20xmlns='http://www.w3.org/2000/svg'%20viewBox='0%200%20640%20640'%3E%3C/svg%3E)![Lobster Pot Pie at Plan Check Where to Find the Best Lobster in Los Angeles](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/2/2017/03/19186953751_478a2f3a0c_z.jpg)](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/2/2017/03/19186953751_478a2f3a0c_z.jpg)

**Get** : Lobster Pot Pie

Plan Check has an impressive seafood platter and a lobster roll on its menu as well, but the standout is the lobster pot pie with curried lobster bisque, beets, green beans, corn, carrots, and potatoes. Chicken pot pie just got a major upgrade.

[_http://www.plancheck.com/  
_](http://www.plancheck.com/)_1401 Ocean Avenue, Santa Monica_

## Hinoki & The Bird

[![Hinoki and the Bird Lobster Rolls: Where to Find the Best Lobster in Los Angeles:](data:image/svg+xml,%3Csvg%20xmlns='http://www.w3.org/2000/svg'%20viewBox='0%200%202048%201536'%3E%3C/svg%3E)![Hinoki and the Bird Lobster Rolls: Where to Find the Best Lobster in Los Angeles:](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/2/2017/03/8431280065_dd606a2633_k-1.jpg)](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/2/2017/03/8431280065_dd606a2633_k-1.jpg)

**Get** : Lobster Roll with Green Curry and Thai Basil

Hinoki & The Bird‚Äôs lobster roll is known citywide. The roll is black, which might be confusing until you find out it‚Äôs Japanese charcoal bread. The roll was inspired by Chef David Myer‚Äôs travels through Southeast Asia. Also on the menu is the butter lobster ramen. There‚Äôs lots of great ramen in Los Angeles, but this is special.

_[www.hinokiandthebird.com/  
](http://www.hinokiandthebird.com/)__10 W. Century Drive, Los Angeles, CA 90067_

## Cousins Maine Lobster [![Cousins Maine Lobster is Where to Find the Best Lobster in Los Angeles](data:image/svg+xml,%3Csvg%20xmlns='http://www.w3.org/2000/svg'%20viewBox='0%200%202048%201365'%3E%3C/svg%3E)![Cousins Maine Lobster is Where to Find the Best Lobster in Los Angeles](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/2/2017/03/8137902893_0aaa569495_k.jpg)](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/2/2017/03/8137902893_0aaa569495_k.jpg)

**Get:** Anything on the menu with lobsterOn the menu, Cousins Maine Lobster declares that ‚ÄúLobster+Butter=Heaven‚Äù. In that spirit, the food truck-turned-restaurant has many lobster offerings on the menu including lobster BLT, lobster grilled cheese, lobster quesadilla, lobster tater tots and two kinds of lobster rolls. If you like chilled rolls with mayonnaise, go for the Maine roll. If you prefer a warm roll with butter and lemon, order the Connecticut roll instead.

_[ww.cousinsmainelobster.com  
](http://www.cousinsmainelobster.com)__8593 Santa Monica Blvd, West Hollywood, CA 90069_

## Blue Plate Oysterette

[![Blue Plate Oysterette is Where to Find the Best Lobster in Los Angeles](data:image/svg+xml,%3Csvg%20xmlns='http://www.w3.org/2000/svg'%20viewBox='0%200%201024%20768'%3E%3C/svg%3E)![Blue Plate Oysterette is Where to Find the Best Lobster in Los Angeles](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/2/2017/03/6933955751_8eef8a624e_b.jpg)](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/2/2017/03/6933955751_8eef8a624e_b.jpg)

**Get:** Seafood Tower

Blue Plate Oysterette is right by the ocean in Santa Monica. The food is as good as the view and the lobster offerings are extensive. The lobster mac and cheese is topped with parmesan and truffle oil. Truffle oil also tops the lobster tacos. The steamed lobster comes with sides of potatoes, corn, and salad. And of course, there‚Äôs the obligatory lobster roll. But if you want to really live up the beachside meal, order the seafood tower which comes with lobster, oysters, clams, mussels, prawns, and king crab.

_[www.blueplateoysterette.com  
](http://www.blueplateoysterette.com)__1355 Ocean Ave, Santa Monica, CA 90401_
