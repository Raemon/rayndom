## **What Are Los Angeles Food Tours?**

LA [Food Tours](https://avitalexperiences.com/food-tours/) are organized events that take guests to several restaurants in one of the city‚Äôs vibrant neighborhoods. The value of taking a food tour versus visiting the restaurants yourself is that food tours are typically guided by a LA local that will show you hidden gems and provide rich context and stories about the place you visit. Los Angeles food tours are great for visitors who want to experience a lot in a short period of time, but also for locals to experience their city through a new perspective. 

**Avital‚Äôs Food Tours** are prix fixe, progressive meals with appetizers, entree, dessert, and drinks, each served seated at a different restaurant. Our [LA Walking Food Tours](https://avitalexperiences.com/los-angeles/walking-food-tours/) focus on quality over quantity. But we promise, you won‚Äôt leave hungry! Our goal is for you to enjoy the highest quality ingredients ‚Äì small plates with huge flavor and rich stories. We strive to shift your perspective and create a sense of connection with the neighborhood, its history, and the people.
