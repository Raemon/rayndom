#  Cute Date Ideas for Wintertime in San Francisco 

December 5, 2017 

It might not snow in the Bay Area, but it definitely cools down during the winter. These cute date ideas for wintertime in San Francisco make the best of a season of early sunsets and cold weather.

##  Walk and Look at Holiday Lights

[![Cute Date Ideas for Wintertime in San Francisco: holiday lights](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/3/2017/11/8974091960_67ad1b6442_k.jpg)](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/3/2017/11/8974091960_67ad1b6442_k.jpg)

People watching and looking at SF‚Äôs architecture is already fun, but add some festive lights to the mix and you have a great date. Grab hot cocoa to go (we love [Dandelion Chocolate‚Äôs](https://www.dandelionchocolate.com/) version) and take a walk through one of the city‚Äôs more suburban feeling neighborhoods. Some suggestions: Sea Cliff, Presidio Heights, or West Portal. Or head to any neighborhood with a lot of holiday lights strung up. Do some research to find a great restaurant or bakery to grab dessert after your walk.

## Snuggle by a Fireplace

[![food tour: Cute Date Ideas for Wintertime in San Francisco](data:image/svg+xml,%3Csvg%20xmlns='http://www.w3.org/2000/svg'%20viewBox='0%200%201200%20800'%3E%3C/svg%3E)![food tour: Cute Date Ideas for Wintertime in San Francisco](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/3/2014/02/Churros-on-san-francisco-food-tours.jpg)](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/3/2014/02/Churros-on-san-francisco-food-tours.jpg)

For a city that doesn‚Äôt get that cold, San Francisco has a lot of [bars and restaurants with fireplaces.](https://sf.eater.com/maps/san-francisco-restaurants-with-fireplaces) So if you don‚Äôt have your own at home, get to know new neighborhoods by seeking a fireplace. Or you can make a crawl of it and go from fire to fire. Holiday time means a lot of restaurants and bars are emptier than normal, so you might be able to swing bringing a board game or a book and hanging out for a while.

## Warm Up on a Food Tour

[![take a food tour as part of Cute Date Ideas for Wintertime in San Francisco](data:image/svg+xml,%3Csvg%20xmlns='http://www.w3.org/2000/svg'%20viewBox='0%200%201024%20682'%3E%3C/svg%3E)![take a food tour as part of Cute Date Ideas for Wintertime in San Francisco](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/3/2017/02/San-Francisco-Walking-Food-Tours-Mission-District.jpg)](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/3/2017/02/San-Francisco-Walking-Food-Tours-Mission-District.jpg)

What better place for a date then a variety of SF‚Äôs best restaurants? Go on a culinary experience through __[Mission](http://avitaltours.com/san-francisco/mission-district-food-tour-faq) or [North Beach](http://avitaltours.com/san-francisco/tour/north-beach-food-tour/) and you‚Äôll visit four restaurants on your date. Plus the experience is already curated to take off the pressure of picking the perfect restaurant. It‚Äôs a great winter date for impressing a fellow foodie.

## Laugh It Off

## [![see comedy as part of Cute Date Ideas for Wintertime in San Francisco](data:image/svg+xml,%3Csvg%20xmlns='http://www.w3.org/2000/svg'%20viewBox='0%200%202048%201360'%3E%3C/svg%3E)![see comedy as part of Cute Date Ideas for Wintertime in San Francisco](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/3/2017/11/14613608550_58f4fb94db_k.jpg)](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/3/2017/11/14613608550_58f4fb94db_k.jpg)

Early nights and cold weather can be a little sad. And the holidays can be stressful. Take a break and laugh all night long at a comedy show. [Doc Rickett‚Äôs](http://docrickettssf.com/) basement venue Doc‚Äôs Lab in North Beach has entertainment almost every night of the month. (And their food is amazing; our guides love their roasted chicken.) Or head to [Cobb‚Äôs ](http://www.cobbscomedy.com/) for more evening comedy shows.
