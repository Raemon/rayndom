## Trivia & Games Entertainment Options for Corporate Events

Got gamers in your group? Games and trivia can be a great way to get groups to engage with one another naturally. Plus a little competition can lead to more fun and lasting memories. 

### Themed Trivia

If your event already has a theme it can be fun to rope it into your trivia game. For example, if your event has a film theme you might look for fun questions about the film‚Äôs characters, actors, and directors.

### Pop Culture

Pop Culture Trivia is a great go-to if you‚Äôre unsure what specific theme your guests will all enjoy. This is also an easy category to find pre-made trivia sheets for online and it can lead to fun topical discussions between your guests.

### Company-Specific

If you‚Äôre entertaining a fully in-house event it can be fun to do trivia themed around the company, or even different people on your team. You can ask questions about the year the company was founded, who was the first CEO, or how big the team was the first year. Or you can ask your team for fun facts and make a trivia game about matching the facts to the person, which is a fun way to get to know each other better.

### Employee Talent Show

If you‚Äôve got a group of outgoing employees, encourage them to show off their talents. Plan ahead so people have time to practice and prepare. They can even sign up as teams for music, dance, and other performances. Have spectators vote for the winner, but make sure everyone is awarded for their participation. 

### Who‚Äôs That Baby?

A classic for a reason! Ask everyone to bring in a photo of themselves as a baby and pin them up on the wall at the event. Each guest can pass by at their leisure and try to match the baby to the name. It‚Äôs not only adorable but strikes up friendly discussion and event stories from childhood.

One of our favorite games at Avital Experiences is the ‚ÄúEmbrace Quirky Game.‚Äù Essentially everyone writes a quirky fact about themselves on a card. Then all of the cards are read and everyone has to try and guess whose quirky fact it is.
