[Birthday party food tours](https://avitalexperiences.com/birthday-party/) in Los Angeles are culinary experiences where groups celebrate a birthday while exploring local food and flavors. Led by LA locals and knowledgeable guides, guests visit a series of restaurants and indulge in delicious dishes, snacks, and/or beverages.
