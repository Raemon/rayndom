#  Top 10 Restaurants Near LAX 

March 7, 2017 

Airport food is rarely impressive, so it‚Äôs important to know where to grab a meal before or after a flight. Our top 10 restaurants near LAX are places we love to sit down for a meal or get some takeout food to bring on board with us. It‚Äôs good to have a knowledge of close restaurants to wait out Los Angeles airport traffic. Or use this list to bring guests who have just landed and are hungry without a long drive. You‚Äôll be a hero.

## 1\. ASAP Phorage

[![ASAP Phorage is one of Top 10 Restaurants Near LAX](data:image/svg+xml,%3Csvg%20xmlns='http://www.w3.org/2000/svg'%20viewBox='0%200%202048%201536'%3E%3C/svg%3E)![ASAP Phorage is one of Top 10 Restaurants Near LAX](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/2/2017/02/9522595780_bc078a99a3_k.jpg)](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/2/2017/02/9522595780_bc078a99a3_k.jpg)

This convenience store and pho counter in Playa Del Ray is just five miles from the airport. The Washugyu beef pho probably won‚Äôt travel well through security, but it‚Äôs a great comfort food before takeoff or after landing. Or bring one of the nine varieties of banh mi on board that will put any airport sandwich to shame. Show up early for breakfast between 7:30 and 9:30 am for the banh mi burrito, dutch pancakes, or beef hash. ASAP stands for ‚ÄúAsian Sandwiches And Pho‚Äù but it also describes the amount of time until you‚Äôll want to return for your next bowl of soup. This isn‚Äôt just one of the top 10 restaurants near LAX, it‚Äôs one of the best bowls of pho in the city.

[_http://www.asap.phoragela.com/  
_](http://www.asap.phoragela.com/)_303 Culver Blvd, Playa Del Rey, CA 90293_

## 2\. Dinah‚Äôs Fried Chicken

[![Dinah's Fried Chicken: Top 10 Restaurants Near LAX](data:image/svg+xml,%3Csvg%20xmlns='http://www.w3.org/2000/svg'%20viewBox='0%200%202048%201536'%3E%3C/svg%3E)![Dinah's Fried Chicken: Top 10 Restaurants Near LAX](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/2/2017/02/15343576577_6d6f4e8949_k.jpg)](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/2/2017/02/15343576577_6d6f4e8949_k.jpg)

For a pre-flight breakfast, it‚Äôs hard to beat chicken and waffles from nearby Dinah‚Äôs. Or have late-night fried chicken with mac and cheese as your first meal back on the ground. Of course, you could get something else from their giant menu, but the chicken has been famous since 1959, so we recommend having at least one piece. (Or if you really want to make friends with your neighbors on the flight, bring along a bucket.) Dinah‚Äôs is located just three and a half miles from the airport, so it‚Äôs less than a 15 minutes drive.

_<http://www.dinahsrestaurant.com/>[  
](http://www.dinahsrestaurant.com/)__6521 S Sepulveda Blvd, Los Angeles, CA 90045_

## **3\. Sugarfish**

[![Sugarfish Marina del Ray is one of Top 10 Restaurants Near LAX](data:image/svg+xml,%3Csvg%20xmlns='http://www.w3.org/2000/svg'%20viewBox='0%200%202048%20910'%3E%3C/svg%3E)![Sugarfish Marina del Ray is one of Top 10 Restaurants Near LAX](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/2/2017/02/12738937575_f1da7e92e8_k.jpg)](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/2/2017/02/12738937575_f1da7e92e8_k.jpg)

If you‚Äôre homesick for some of Los Angeles‚Äôs best sushi, Sugarfish in Marina Del Ray should be your first stop after picking up your luggage. You can be eating from Sugarfish‚Äôs famous ‚ÄúTrust Me‚Äù menu after a short five-mile drive from the airport. Sure, airport sushi is available in most terminals, but Kazunori Nozawa is known across the country for a reason, so we‚Äôd wait the extra 15 minutes.

[_http://sugarfishsushi.com/  
_](http://sugarfishsushi.com/)_4722 Admiralty Way, Marina Del Rey, CA 90292_

## 4\. Al-Noor

[![Top 10 Restaurants Near LAX: Al Noor](data:image/svg+xml,%3Csvg%20xmlns='http://www.w3.org/2000/svg'%20viewBox='0%200%20640%20480'%3E%3C/svg%3E)![Top 10 Restaurants Near LAX: Al Noor](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/2/2016/12/Get-Garlic-Naan-at-Al-Noor-some-of-The-Best-Indian-Food-in-Los-Angeles.jpg)](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/2/2016/12/Get-Garlic-Naan-at-Al-Noor-some-of-The-Best-Indian-Food-in-Los-Angeles.jpg)

We featured Al-Noor on our list of [The Best Indian Food in Los Angeles.](http://avitaltours.com/los-angeles/best-indian-food-los-angeles/) Luckily for travelers, it‚Äôs close to the airport in Lawndale. Al-Noor is a favorite of LA Times food critic [Jonathan Gold](http://www.alnoor-restaurant.net/Reviews.html). Stop by for garlic naan, which was featured by Eric Greenspan on a garlic-themed episode of ‚ÄúThe Best Thing I Ever Ate‚Äù.

_<http://www.alnoor-restaurant.net/>[  
](http://www.alnoor-restaurant.net/)__15112 Inglewood Ave,__Lawndale, CA 90260_

## 5\. Coni‚ÄôSeafood

[![Top 10 Restaurants Near LAX: Coni'Seafood](data:image/svg+xml,%3Csvg%20xmlns='http://www.w3.org/2000/svg'%20viewBox='0%200%202048%201536'%3E%3C/svg%3E)![Top 10 Restaurants Near LAX: Coni'Seafood](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/2/2017/02/19009178489_f3ec39834b_k.jpg)](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/2/2017/02/19009178489_f3ec39834b_k.jpg)

Get your fix of Mexican seafood in Inglewood. Coni‚ÄôSeafood is known for their many varieties of shrimp, marlin tacos, and their ceviche. The best-known at Coni is the pescado zarandeado (grilled snook). It has fans throughout the city who trek all the way to the airport area for the dish. Angelenos doing the dreaded drive toward LAX without a flight reservation? It must be good.

_No website  
__3544 W Imperial Hwy, Inglewood, CA 90303_

## **6\. Manhattan Beach Post**

[![Manhattan Beach Post is one of the Top 10 Restaurants Near LAX](data:image/svg+xml,%3Csvg%20xmlns='http://www.w3.org/2000/svg'%20viewBox='0%200%202048%201536'%3E%3C/svg%3E)![Manhattan Beach Post is one of the Top 10 Restaurants Near LAX](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/2/2017/02/32469528930_5b4d7d99b0_k.jpg)](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/2/2017/02/32469528930_5b4d7d99b0_k.jpg)

Less than five miles from the airport is Manhattan Beach-a much calmer vibe with much better food. Manhattan Beach Post is a 1960‚Äôs post-office-turned-restaurant that still has some mailroom decor. An order of bacon cheddar buttermilk biscuits, a charcuterie plate, grilled octopus, and some handcrafted cocktails are enough to recover after even the most stressful flights.

[_http://eatmbpost.com/  
_](http://eatmbpost.com/)_1142 Manhattan Ave, Manhattan Beach, CA 90266_

## 7\. Little Sister

[![Top 10 Restaurants Near LAX: Little Sister](data:image/svg+xml,%3Csvg%20xmlns='http://www.w3.org/2000/svg'%20viewBox='0%200%202048%201536'%3E%3C/svg%3E)![Top 10 Restaurants Near LAX: Little Sister](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/2/2017/02/16417130313_5347ae1558_k.jpg)](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/2/2017/02/16417130313_5347ae1558_k.jpg)

We love the downtown location of Little Sister, and this Manhattan Beach location is close enough to the airport to justify a trip before or after dropping off visitors. The Vietnamese menu is much more extensive and upscale than your neighborhood pho spot. Try to get there for ‚ÄúSpice Hour‚Äù (happy hour) for small bites between 4:30 and 6:30‚Ä¶also an excellent time to hide from traffic on the 405.

[_http://littlesistermb.com/  
_](http://littlesistermb.com/)_1131 Manhattan Ave, Manhattan Beach, CA 90266_

## 8\. Superba Food and Bread

[![Superba Food and Bread: Top 10 Restaurants Near LAX](data:image/svg+xml,%3Csvg%20xmlns='http://www.w3.org/2000/svg'%20viewBox='0%200%20640%20480'%3E%3C/svg%3E)![Superba Food and Bread: Top 10 Restaurants Near LAX](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/2/2017/02/15700761432_1f972c039b_z.jpg)](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/2/2017/02/15700761432_1f972c039b_z.jpg)

We frequently visit Superba‚Äôs Venice Beach location and the El Segundo location is even more convenient to the airport. Hip brunch items like avocado toast and chia seed pudding dominate the morning menus. Later in the day, you‚Äôll find lamb ribs, rotisserie chicken, salmon, and other staples. (Of course, don‚Äôt forget the namesake bread.)

[_http://www.superbafoodandbread.com  
_](http://www.superbafoodandbread.com)_830 S Sepulveda Suite 100, El Segundo, CA 90245_

## 9\. Fishing With Dynamite[![Fishing with Dynamite: Top 10 Restaurants Near LAX](data:image/svg+xml,%3Csvg%20xmlns='http://www.w3.org/2000/svg'%20viewBox='0%200%202048%201536'%3E%3C/svg%3E)![Fishing with Dynamite: Top 10 Restaurants Near LAX](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/2/2017/02/10433688924_bb8756c52c_k.jpg)](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/2/2017/02/10433688924_bb8756c52c_k.jpg)

As a small seafood restaurant with just 35 seats, Fishing with Dynamite usually has a wait. But if you‚Äôre in the area, make the few mile trek from the airport and slide up to the raw bar for some shrimp, scallops, mussels, clams, and lobster. Or drip into a bowl of clam chowder, a plate of octopus, or Cape Cod squash rolls. It‚Äôs hard to make a bad choice here, especially if you end dinner with the Key lime pie.

[_http://www.eatfwd.com/  
_](http://www.eatfwd.com/)_1148 Manhattan Ave, Manhattan Beach, CA 90266_

## 10\. The Tripel

[![The Tripel: Top 10 Restaurants Near LAX](data:image/svg+xml,%3Csvg%20xmlns='http://www.w3.org/2000/svg'%20viewBox='0%200%202048%201151'%3E%3C/svg%3E)![The Tripel: Top 10 Restaurants Near LAX](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/2/2017/02/17323618152_6998163579_k.jpg)](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/2/2017/02/17323618152_6998163579_k.jpg)

After braving airport traffic, you deserve a drink. Luckily The Tripel in Playa del Ray is close enough that it‚Äôs an easy drive, even with traffic. The Tripel has an extensive beer list, but even larger is their food menu. This is the kind of place you can have an Oreo ice cream cake paired with a rib-eye pretzel burger. Or crispy pig ear salad, sweet potato tots, and cookies and bourbon milk. You‚Äôve got lots of options, and they‚Äôre all delicious.

[_http://www.thetripel.com/  
_](http://www.thetripel.com/)_333 Culver Blvd, Playa del Rey, CA 90293_
