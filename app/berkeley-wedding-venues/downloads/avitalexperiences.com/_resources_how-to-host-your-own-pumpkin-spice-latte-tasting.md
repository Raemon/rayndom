## Sweaters, falling leaves, and that unmistakable scent of cinnamon in the air.

Whether you‚Äôre team yay or nay, pumpkin spice season has undoubtably arrived. But with PSLs everywhere you turn, which one actually lives up to the hype? 

Sure, the best Pumpkin Spice Latte is probably the one made by your local barista. But for those mornings when you‚Äôre still in slippers, we went on a quest to find the best at-home, easy, and convenient PSLs.

For this month‚Äôs**Find Your Fave Club** , Our Sales Manager Noelle tasted six different pumpkin spice lattes in one sitting. And yes, and she‚Äôs still coming down from the caffeine high. ü§™

## ****Noelle‚Äôs Pumpkin Spiced Latte Tasting****

Fueled by fall spirit (and way too much caffeine), Noelle compared two categories of Pumpkin Spice Lattes.

**Pre-Made PSLs:**

  * Starbucks Cold Brew Concentrate Pumpkin Spice
  * Pop & Bottle Organic Pumpkin Spice Almond Milk Latte
  * La Colombe Coffee Draft Latte Pumpkin Spice



**Powders to Mix:**

  * Maud‚Äôs Pumpkin Spice Latte Instant Coffee
  * Organic Traditions Pumpkin Spice Latte Instant Coffee
  * Blume Superfood Pumpkin Spice Latte



Which latte reigned supreme? You‚Äôll have to watch the video to find out.

## How to Host Your Own Pumpkin Spice Latte Tasting

##### **01: Pick Your PSLs**

Choose a mix of pre-made and powders. Check out our [_Amazon list_](https://www.amazon.com/hz/wishlist/ls/3NLMAGN33ORBZ?ref_=wl_share) to make shopping easy.

[Shop Amazon](https://www.amazon.com/hz/wishlist/ls/106C3GCTYSWOH?ref_=wl_share)

##### **02: Sort by Category**

Group lattes into categories by either preparation method or milk variety. If you‚Äôre not tasting too many, you can also just do one big category.

##### **03: Observe, Taste, and Smell**

Start with tasting all the PSLs in one category before moving on to the next. We recommend tasting small sips so you don‚Äôt get overly caffeinated!

##### **04: Take Notes**

Jot down your impressions of each latte. Notice the differences in flavor, but also smell and texture. Download our tasting notes template to get started.

[Download PDF](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/2025/08/Mustard-Tasting-Notes.pdf)

##### **05: Choose Winners**

When you‚Äôre done, crown a winner in each category. You can also choose an ultimate winner!
