Harness your inner leprechaun as you enjoy an afternoon of mischief. This creative team building activity is perfect for artistic and unartistic groups alike. The folks at [Canva n Cup](https://www.canvasncup.com/virtual-paint-parties) will lead guests through the painting, from start to finish.
