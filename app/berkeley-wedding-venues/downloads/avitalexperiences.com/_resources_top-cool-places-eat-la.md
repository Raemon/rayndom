#  The Top 5 Cool Places to Eat in LA 

June 19, 2017 

Los Angeles has a strong culinary scene and the hot new restaurant changes constantly. It can be hard to keep up with the newest trend and score a reservation. These top 5 cool places to eat in LA are interesting, delicious and fun. We promise any potential waits are worth it. (Though if you want to eat at some other great spots and skip the line, you can always join us on a [culinary experience.](http://avitaltours.com/los-angeles/))

## 1\. Clifton‚Äôs Cafeteria

[![Clifton's: Top 5 Cool places to eat in LA](data:image/svg+xml,%3Csvg%20xmlns='http://www.w3.org/2000/svg'%20viewBox='0%200%202048%201365'%3E%3C/svg%3E)![Clifton's: Top 5 Cool places to eat in LA](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/2/2017/05/17158574169_990dbc5b05_k.jpg)](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/2/2017/05/17158574169_990dbc5b05_k.jpg)

A cafeteria might not be a place that you think of as ‚Äúcool‚Äù, but we promise you‚Äôve never seen a cafeteria like this. On the bottom floor, pick up a tray and pile it high with meatloaf, pot pie, jello molds, and other Americana favorite. Once you pay for your food, you‚Äôll notice the decor that makes Clifton‚Äôs special. A giant tree goes up the middle of the building five stories. There are taxidermied animals, tree seats, and even a meteorite on display. It‚Äôs like a natural history museum. Look around for a bit and then bring your food upstairs and visit the Monarch Bar or the Gothic Bar for cocktails and at happy hour it‚Äôs buy-one-get-one for 35 cents. (Yes, cents.) Way up top is Clifton‚Äôs tiki bar. Visit on the weekends for free swing dancing and Sunday jazz brunch, which helps keep it on our list of top 5 cool places to eat in LA. We promise you won‚Äôt be bored here.

_648 S Broadway,__Los Angeles, CA 90014  
_[_cliftonsla.com_](http://www.cliftonsla.com/)

## 2\. Bestia

[![Top 5 Cool places to eat in LA: Bestia](data:image/svg+xml,%3Csvg%20xmlns='http://www.w3.org/2000/svg'%20viewBox='0%200%202048%201536'%3E%3C/svg%3E)![Top 5 Cool places to eat in LA: Bestia](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/2/2017/05/22229659902_9d4b336efc_k-1.jpg)](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/2/2017/05/22229659902_9d4b336efc_k-1.jpg)

In the up and coming Arts District squeezed between warehouses is Bestia. It means ‚Äúbeast‚Äù in Italian and that‚Äôs what these chefs are when it comes to Italian cooking. Expect reservations that are hard to get and 45 minute minimum wait times for walk-ins. Despite being ‚Äúout there‚Äù this restaurant is definitely ‚Äúin‚Äù. It‚Äôs loud, crowded, and hip. And it‚Äôs delicious. It‚Äôs hard to make a wrong choice on Bestia‚Äôs menu, but if you want to make the best choices order the roasted bone marrow, the cavatelli alla norcina (ricotta dumplings, housemade pork sausage, black truffles), lamb ragu with saffron, and the chocolate budino tart.

_2121 E. 7th Pl.__Los Angeles, CA 90021  
__<http://bestiala.com/>_

## 3\. **Night + Market Song**

[![Night and Market Song: Top 5 Cool places to eat in LA](data:image/svg+xml,%3Csvg%20xmlns='http://www.w3.org/2000/svg'%20viewBox='0%200%202048%20957'%3E%3C/svg%3E)![Night and Market Song: Top 5 Cool places to eat in LA](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/2/2017/05/24627001685_868498b7ca_k-1.jpg)](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/2/2017/05/24627001685_868498b7ca_k-1.jpg)

This is one of two Night + Market locations, the other is in West Hollywood. The Night + Market Song location is a bit more raucous than other Thai restaurants in the city, though it‚Äôs missing the beer towers of the WeHo location. Owner and chef Kris Yenbamroong wants the vibe to be fun. He says their mission statement (if they had one) would be, ‚ÄúWe aim to make the most delicious and authentic Thai food to facilitate drinking and fun-having amongst friends.‚Äù The food is on point and it‚Äôs in the Silver Lake neighborhood, so the crowd is full of fun-having hipsters eating underneath the Cindy Crawford poster. Order the larb (spicy minced meat salad) so you can buy and wear the ‚ÄúLarb King‚Äù hat with confidence.

_3322 W Sunset Blvd,__Los Angeles, CA 90026  
__<http://www.nightmarketsong.com/nm-song>_

## 4\. **Sqirl**

[![Squirl is one of the Top 5 Cool places to eat in LA](data:image/svg+xml,%3Csvg%20xmlns='http://www.w3.org/2000/svg'%20viewBox='0%200%202048%201536'%3E%3C/svg%3E)![Squirl is one of the Top 5 Cool places to eat in LA](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/2/2017/05/11850309414_33f54f90b7_k-1.jpg)](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/2/2017/05/11850309414_33f54f90b7_k-1.jpg)

We‚Äôve said before that Squirl is the kind of place locals wait in line for, and then wait a little longer to Instagram their beautiful meal before eating.Started by [Jessica Koslow](http://avitaltours.com/los-angeles/five-los-angeles-chefs-you-should-know-about/), Sqirl might be best known for their jams sold around LA that are made from local fruit. They‚Äôre also known for their breakfast and lunches served out of a tiny 800-square-foot space. Try the Sorrel Pesto Rice Kokuho bowl, savory porridges, Savory Sweet Green Eggs and Jam, and the rotating daily jams. Don‚Äôt forget to take a picture so everyone knows you actually waited in the line to eat.

_720 N Virgil Ave #4,__Los Angeles, CA 90029  
__[sqirlla.com](http://sqirlla.com)_

## 5\. King‚Äôs Burgers And Got Sushi

[![King Sushi is one of Top 5 Cool places to eat in LA](data:image/svg+xml,%3Csvg%20xmlns='http://www.w3.org/2000/svg'%20viewBox='0%200%20500%20333'%3E%3C/svg%3E)![King Sushi is one of Top 5 Cool places to eat in LA](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/2/2017/05/2831687503_4c1f5e6f24_z.jpg)](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/2/2017/05/2831687503_4c1f5e6f24_z.jpg)

Yes, this restaurant is a little bit outside the city, but it‚Äôs worth the drive and we still consider it one of the top 5 cool places to eat in LA. Many first time diners think they‚Äôre in the wrong place when they arrive at King‚Äôs Burgers and Got Sushi. The aesthetic is a mediocrely renovated fast food restaurant. But what makes this spot cool is that despite the fact that it‚Äôs primarily a burger joint that also strangely has a lot of breakfast options, the sushi is incredible. And it‚Äôs not expensive. The staff will tell you that some people are afraid to eat sushi out of the same kitchen that serves burgers, but ignore all that is rational and dive into the offerings that range from three piece uni samplers, truffle-topped yellowtail, and albacore belly handrolls. And if you were wondering, yes, the burgers are great, too.

_9345 Reseda Blvd,__Northridge, CA 91324  
__<https://www.yelp.com/biz/got-sushi-and-kings-burgers-northridge>_
