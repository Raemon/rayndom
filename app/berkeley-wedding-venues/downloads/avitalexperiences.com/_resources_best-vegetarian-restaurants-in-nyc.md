#  Best Vegetarian Restaurants in NYC for Special Occasions 

January 24, 2020 

While growing public interest in wellness, environment, and animal rights has helped to build New York City‚Äôs vegetarian food scene, it is the food itself that has made NYC one of the world‚Äôs top vegetarian destinations. As varied as it is vibrant, New York‚Äôs plethora of vegetarian and veg-friendly restaurants deliciously reflects the diversity, and creativity of the city that never sleeps. So whether you‚Äôre celebrating a birthday or an anniversary, or whether you‚Äôre vegetarian, flexitarian, vegan, vege-curious, or tired of labels and just want to eat some mind-bogglingly good food, here are four of the best vegetarian restaurants in NYC. 

## [Honeybee‚Äôs](https://www.honeybeesnyc.com/home)

[![bbq and bourbon at best vegetarian restaurants in nyc honeybees ](data:image/svg+xml,%3Csvg%20xmlns='http://www.w3.org/2000/svg'%20viewBox='0%200%20300%20200'%3E%3C/svg%3E)![bbq and bourbon at best vegetarian restaurants in nyc honeybees ](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/5/2020/01/honeybees-best-vegetarian-restaurants-in-nyc-300x200.jpg)](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/5/2020/01/honeybees-best-vegetarian-restaurants-in-nyc.jpg)Photo via @honeybeesnyc

Honeybee‚Äôs proves that upscale, vegan barbecue is not a contradiction, but a revelation. This elegant East Village saloon is the perfect place to celebrate a birthday, bachelor/ette party, or any occasion that calls for the classiest kind of tipsiness. Sother Teague, mastermind behind the renowned bitters bar Amor Y Amargo, curates a rye and bourbon-focused cocktail menu. It pairs perfectly with smoky, plant-based barbecue. You can email or fill out a form on their website to book reservations or plan events for groups of 6 or more. 

## [Hangawi ](http://hangawirestaurant.com/)

[![tofu in leaves at best vegetarian restaurants in nyc hangawi](data:image/svg+xml,%3Csvg%20xmlns='http://www.w3.org/2000/svg'%20viewBox='0%200%20300%20300'%3E%3C/svg%3E)![tofu in leaves at best vegetarian restaurants in nyc hangawi](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/5/2020/01/hangawi-best-vegetarian-restaurants-in-nyc-300x300.jpg)](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/5/2020/01/hangawi-best-vegetarian-restaurants-in-nyc.jpg)Photo via @hangawi

Those who look at Hangawi from the outside may think they‚Äôre looking at a room in K-Town. But all who step inside understand that the restaurant lives in a time and place far away from the city hustle. Gentle string music greets visitors at the doorstep, who are invited to remove their shoes before sitting upon silken pillows. This plant-based Korean restaurant coaxes complexity and nuance out of the seemingly simplest ingredients: a two-ingredient soup of purple yams and homemade soymilk is layers of sweet, creamy, nutty, and savory all at once. The sophisticated simplicity is what makes Hangawi one of the best vegetarian restaurants in NYC.

## [Modern Love Brooklyn](http://www.modernlovebrooklyn.com/)

[![tofu pasta at best vegetarian restaurants in nyc modern love](data:image/svg+xml,%3Csvg%20xmlns='http://www.w3.org/2000/svg'%20viewBox='0%200%20300%20240'%3E%3C/svg%3E)![tofu pasta at best vegetarian restaurants in nyc modern love](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/5/2020/01/modernlove-best-vegetarian-restaurants-in-nyc-300x240.jpg)](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/5/2020/01/modernlove-best-vegetarian-restaurants-in-nyc.jpg)Photo via @modernlovebrooklyn

Modern Love Omaha introduced Isa Chandra‚Äôs vegan comfort food to the national stage in 2014. The award-winning chef and author opened its younger Brooklyn sister two years later. It continues to draw nightly crowds for a plant-based menu that is as elegant as it is unpretentious. Tuck into a generous bowl of the city‚Äôs best vegan mac and cheese while you sip on a seasonal, artisanal cocktail. You can make reservations of 9 or more at [_[email protected]_](/cdn-cgi/l/email-protection#d0b8b5bcbcbf90bdbfb4b5a2bebcbfa6b5b2a2bfbfbbbca9befeb3bfbd). 

## [Dirt Candy](https://www.dirtcandynyc.com/)

[![stacked greens at best vegetarian restaurants in nyc dirt candy](data:image/svg+xml,%3Csvg%20xmlns='http://www.w3.org/2000/svg'%20viewBox='0%200%20300%20200'%3E%3C/svg%3E)![stacked greens at best vegetarian restaurants in nyc dirt candy](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/5/2020/01/dirt-candy-best-vegetarian-restaurants-in-nyc-300x200.jpg)](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/5/2020/01/dirt-candy-best-vegetarian-restaurants-in-nyc.jpg)Photo via dirtcandynyc.com

You‚Äôll need a reservation for this whimsical Lower East Side hotspot. Chef Amanda Cohen experiments with texture, flavor, and color to create dishes of oh-so-edible art. Everything is vegetarian and can be made vegan, from the portobello mousse to the popcorn pudding. The molecular gastronomy and plated pointillism make for a one-of-a-kind, celebratory gastronomic experience that is more playful than pretentious. Be sure to pick up a copy of Cohen‚Äôs hilariously comic-book style cookbook on your way out.
