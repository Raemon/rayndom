After a quick check in and orientation get ready to be like the birds and take flight. The number of flights a person gets to participate in will depend on the package purchased. **Why We Love It:** They say the best gifts are ones that someone wouldn‚Äôt buy themselves‚Ä¶ this is definitely an experience most people don‚Äôt go out of their way to search for‚Ä¶ but end up having the most amazing time when they do.

[![these-women-are-on-a-east-village-food-tour-which-is-better-than-virgin-experience-gifts](data:image/svg+xml,%3Csvg%20xmlns='http://www.w3.org/2000/svg'%20viewBox='0%200%20700%20467'%3E%3C/svg%3E)![these-women-are-on-a-east-village-food-tour-which-is-better-than-virgin-experience-gifts](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/5/2022/07/women-on-east-village-food-tour-experience-gifts-nyc.jpg)](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/5/2022/07/women-on-east-village-food-tour-experience-gifts-nyc.jpg)

Each stop is a different dish ‚Äì you‚Äôll get two appetizers, and entree, and a dessert at four different restaurants. This tour definitely goes above and beyond compared to what you might find on Virgin Experience Gifts. Be sure to upgrade to include an alcohol pairing ‚Äì because why wouldn‚Äôt you?! [Book your East Village food tour](https://avitalexperiences.com/experiences/new-york-city-east-village-food-tour/) today!

**Why We Love It:** Not only do you get to eat _amazing_ food, you learn about the histories and stories of a neighborhood that makes New York City what it is today.

## NYC Skyline Helicopter Ride

[![helicopter-over-new-york-is-a-better-gift-than-a-virign-experience-gift](data:image/svg+xml,%3Csvg%20xmlns='http://www.w3.org/2000/svg'%20viewBox='0%200%20700%20467'%3E%3C/svg%3E)![helicopter-over-new-york-is-a-better-gift-than-a-virign-experience-gift](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/5/2022/08/virign-experience-gift-helicopter-nyc-skyline.jpg)](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/5/2022/08/virign-experience-gift-helicopter-nyc-skyline.jpg)

Give the amazing gift of taking flight over the cities of all cities ‚Äì New York City. Choose from watching the city light up taking a [skyline helicopter ride](https://heliny.com/tours/) after the sun sets, or choose to be sent off during the day to see as much detail of the city as possible. HeliNy is a top rated sightseeing company that offers helicopter rides and charters. They take their guests to see iconic landmarks from a completely new perspective (Ellis Island, Central Park, and the Empire State Building just to name a few). HeliNY offers multiple experiences that you can choose from depending on your schedule and budget. 

**Why We Love It:** See New York like never before with aerial views that will knock anyone‚Äôs socks off!

The Guerlain Spa won the best New York Spa Award 2021 and reflects the tradition and technique that the House of Guerlain is so well known for. The Warren-Tricomi Salon and its master stylist Warren Tricomi are known across the globe for being the authority on hair. Together they are at the absolute height of elegance. Make a reservation to treat your loved one to a day that will leave them glowing. 

**Why We Love It:** The Plaza Hotel screams elegance and this spa day will give your giftee a tranquil day in the life of the rich and famous.
