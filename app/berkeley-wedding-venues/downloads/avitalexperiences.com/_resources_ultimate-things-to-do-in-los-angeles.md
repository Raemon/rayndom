#  5 Ultimate Things to Do in Los Angeles 

April 18, 2018 

We‚Äôve got beaches and baseball, Hollywood and The Hobbit House, Griffith Observatory and Grand Central Market. LA is filled with activities around the clock, and it can be overwhelming. Let us narrow it down for you. Here are 5 ultimate things to do in Los Angeles.

## 1\. Dance at The Abbey

[![5 Ultimate Things to Do in Los Angeles: Abbey](data:image/svg+xml,%3Csvg%20xmlns='http://www.w3.org/2000/svg'%20viewBox='0%200%20640%20435'%3E%3C/svg%3E)![5 Ultimate Things to Do in Los Angeles: Abbey](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/2/2017/10/7344024270_aca1fd8fa2_z.jpg)](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/2/2017/10/7344024270_aca1fd8fa2_z.jpg)

The Abbey is one of West Hollywood, and LA‚Äôs, best bars. It‚Äôs decorated like a church, but the Abbey is more of a place to spend Saturday night than Sunday morning. (Though the Sunday Funday brunch is famous.) The bar is notorious for wild, late-night dancing and is regularly voted one of the country‚Äôs best gay bars. Spending the night here is an LA tradition which makes it one of the 5 ultimate things to do in Los Angeles.

_692 N Robertson Blvd, West Hollywood, CA 90069  
__[www.theabbeyweho.com](https://www.theabbeyweho.com/)_

## 2\. See a Game at Dodger‚Äôs Stadium

[![8214194616_8bcba3f7f8_k](data:image/svg+xml,%3Csvg%20xmlns='http://www.w3.org/2000/svg'%20viewBox='0%200%202048%201246'%3E%3C/svg%3E)![8214194616_8bcba3f7f8_k](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/2/2017/10/8214194616_8bcba3f7f8_k.jpg)](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/2/2017/10/8214194616_8bcba3f7f8_k.jpg)

Take yourself out to the ball game and cheer on the Dodgers. The stadium is sandwiched between downtown LA and the San Gabriel mountains, with views of each. If you plan to come hungry, buy a ticket for the all-you-can-eat section. The ticket comes with unlimited Dodger Dogs, nachos, popcorn, peanuts, and beverages. Pro tip: take the free shuttle from Union Station so you don‚Äôt have to deal with parking.

_ 1000 Vin Scully Ave, Los Angeles, CA 90012  
__[www.losangeles.dodgers.mlb.com/la/ballpark/](http://losangeles.dodgers.mlb.com/la/ballpark/)_

## 3\. Explore Downtown

## [![5 Ultimate Things to Do in Los Angeles: downtown](data:image/svg+xml,%3Csvg%20xmlns='http://www.w3.org/2000/svg'%20viewBox='0%200%20720%20480'%3E%3C/svg%3E)![5 Ultimate Things to Do in Los Angeles: downtown](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/2/2017/01/downtown-los-angeles-sweets-tour-bottega-louie.jpg)](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/2/2017/01/downtown-los-angeles-sweets-tour-bottega-louie.jpg)

Downtown Los Angeles is going through big changes. Of course, historical classics such as [Cole‚Äôs](http://213hospitality.com/coles/) French Dip and[ Clifton‚Äôs ](http://www.cliftonsla.com/) remain. But lots of construction and development means that new restaurants and bars are opening all the time. And increased train lines like the Expo Line to Santa Monica mean it‚Äôs easier than ever to get downtown.

We know that even locals don‚Äôt spend a lot of time downtown. If you haven‚Äôt been to [Grand Central Market](http://www.grandcentralmarket.com/) since the reopening, make a visit. Or spend the day exploring the gems of downtown, from the rainbow macarons at [Bottega Louie](https://www.bottegalouie.com/) to the rooftop bar on top of the [Ace Hote](https://www.acehotel.com/losangeles)l to [The Last Bookstore](http://lastbookstorela.com/). Not sure where to start? We can show you around town. Join us for a three-hour food tour and we‚Äôll fill you in on what‚Äôs happening downtown.

_Downtown Los Angeles  
__[www.avitaltours.com/los-angeles/tour/downtown-food-tour/](http://avitaltours.com/los-angeles/tour/downtown-food-tour/)_

## 4\. Get into the Magic Castle

[![5 Ultimate Things to Do in Los Angeles" Magic Castle](data:image/svg+xml,%3Csvg%20xmlns='http://www.w3.org/2000/svg'%20viewBox='0%200%201600%201200'%3E%3C/svg%3E)![5 Ultimate Things to Do in Los Angeles" Magic Castle](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/2/2017/10/The_Magic_Castle_in_Hollywood_6244896017.jpg)](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/2/2017/10/The_Magic_Castle_in_Hollywood_6244896017.jpg)

Getting into the Magic Castle doesn‚Äôt require magic, but it does require connections. Since it‚Äôs a private club, to visit, you either have to be invited by a magician, be invited by a member, or be a member. But LA is a city about knowing people who know people, so we trust you‚Äôll be able to get in (and then invite us!). Once you‚Äôre in the door, you‚Äôll be treated to a variety of magic shows from visiting magicians who definitely won‚Äôt reveal their secrets. The dress code is strictly enforced, so come looking your best and most magical.

_7001 Franklin Ave, Los Angeles, CA 90028  
__[www.magiccastle.com/](http://www.magiccastle.com/)_

## 5\. Go for a Hike in the Hollywood Hills

[![Hollywood hike: 5 Ultimate Things to Do in Los Angeles](data:image/svg+xml,%3Csvg%20xmlns='http://www.w3.org/2000/svg'%20viewBox='0%200%201024%20768'%3E%3C/svg%3E)![Hollywood hike: 5 Ultimate Things to Do in Los Angeles](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/2/2017/10/5660148506_830305a5f1_b.jpg)](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/2/2017/10/5660148506_830305a5f1_b.jpg)

LA‚Äôs nature is talked about less than its freeways, but once you get off of the 101, there‚Äôs lots of hiking opportunity. Runyon Canyon Park is one of the most popular hiking trails in Los Angeles. It‚Äôs an easy trail that‚Äôs filled with city views and celebrities walking their dogs. There are also great views of the giant homes that make these hills famous. And yes, you‚Äôll be able to see the Hollywood sign from here. Parking is hard to find on weekends, so if you can make a weekday hike we recommend it.

_2000 N Fuller Ave, Los Angeles, CA 90046  
__[www.lamountains.com/parks.asp?parkid=122](http://www.lamountains.com/parks.asp?parkid=122)_
