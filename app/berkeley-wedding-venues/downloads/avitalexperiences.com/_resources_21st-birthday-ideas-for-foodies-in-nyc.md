## **Taste some of the best artisan cocktails in NYC**

[![21st Birthday Ideas in NYC for Foodies-get artisan cocktails](data:image/svg+xml,%3Csvg%20xmlns='http://www.w3.org/2000/svg'%20viewBox='0%200%20640%20428'%3E%3C/svg%3E)![21st Birthday Ideas in NYC for Foodies-get artisan cocktails](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/5/2019/01/21st-Birthday-Ideas-in-NYC-for-Foodies-cocktail.jpg)](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/5/2019/01/21st-Birthday-Ideas-in-NYC-for-Foodies-cocktail.jpg)

Before the shots start pouring, start out the night by tasting some of the best cocktails NYC has to offer. [Death & Company](https://www.deathandcompany.com/) has a whole book of creative cocktails with all the alcohol you can think of, and some delicious small plates to share as well. [Amor y Amargo](https://www.amoryamargony.com/) is also not too far away; it‚Äôs an amaro bar that puts an interesting twist on each drink. We also love the absinthe colada at Maison Premiere. What‚Äôs more decadent and special than spending your 21st birthday drinking absinthe? Do that first, then start taking the pickleback shots. 

## **Do a group cooking class**

[![21st Birthday Ideas in NYC for Foodies-do a cooking class](data:image/svg+xml,%3Csvg%20xmlns='http://www.w3.org/2000/svg'%20viewBox='0%200%20640%20426'%3E%3C/svg%3E)![21st Birthday Ideas in NYC for Foodies-do a cooking class](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/5/2019/01/21st-Birthday-Ideas-in-NYC-for-Foodies-1.jpg)](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/5/2019/01/21st-Birthday-Ideas-in-NYC-for-Foodies-1.jpg)

Since NYC is full of diverse cuisines, you can learn to cook almost anything you‚Äôve ever wanted. There are cooking classes all over the city that range from dumpling-making to classic Italian. Book a private class with [Rustico Cooking Class](https://www.rusticocooking.com/cookingclasses.htm) to learn about the flavors of Tuscany, or if you‚Äôre a fan of [Milk Bar](https://milkbarstore.com/events/classes/), learn how to make their infamous birthday cake. The special ingredient? The feeling of accomplishment you‚Äôll get when you take a bite of your masterpiece. If you‚Äôd rather enjoy the food at home, [Home Cooking NY](https://homecookingny.com/cooking-parties/) can also send a private instructor to your house. 

## **Book a private food tour**

[![book a private tour! 21st Birthday Ideas in NYC for Foodies-](data:image/svg+xml,%3Csvg%20xmlns='http://www.w3.org/2000/svg'%20viewBox='0%200%20800%20532'%3E%3C/svg%3E)![book a private tour! 21st Birthday Ideas in NYC for Foodies-](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/5/2019/01/21st-Birthday-Ideas-in-NYC-for-Foodies-private-food-tour.jpeg)](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/5/2019/01/21st-Birthday-Ideas-in-NYC-for-Foodies-private-food-tour.jpeg)

If you‚Äôre more of a taster rather than a chef, a food tour is a perfect option. After all, it is your birthday! You should save the work for opening presents. Just like there is almost any cuisine here in NYC, there‚Äôs also a food tour for just about anything. [Scott‚Äôs Pizza Tour](http://www.scottspizzatours.com/) has been around for a while and will take you to all the best pizza spots in the city. And since this is a 21st birthday party, a speakeasy tour like Take Me Out will have the mood set right. [Avital Tours](http://avitaltours.com/) also runs private tours, and since we take you on a progressive meal in some of NYC‚Äôs coolest neighborhoods, you‚Äôll have no trouble finding a bar for afterward. Don‚Äôt worry about planning- somebody else has come up with all the 21st birthday ideas for you. 

## **Rent out an ice cream parlor**

[![21st Birthday Ideas in NYC for Foodies-rent an ice cream parlor](data:image/svg+xml,%3Csvg%20xmlns='http://www.w3.org/2000/svg'%20viewBox='0%200%20600%20400'%3E%3C/svg%3E)![21st Birthday Ideas in NYC for Foodies-rent an ice cream parlor](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/5/2019/01/21st-Birthday-Ideas-in-NYC-for-Foodies-ice-cream.jpg)](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/5/2019/01/21st-Birthday-Ideas-in-NYC-for-Foodies-ice-cream.jpg)

Is it just me or does ice cream taste better on your birthday? With all of the excellent ice cream shops to choose from, having a 21st birthday party at one of them isn‚Äôt a bad idea. This isn‚Äôt Baskin Robbins we‚Äôre talking about; NYC is full of gourmet ice cream shops to take a traditional ice cream party to the next level. [Tipsy Scoop](https://www.tipsyscoop.com/) is an excellent option for those who want to enjoy their alcohol in ice cream form, with flavors like cake batter vodka martini and maple bacon bourbon. If you‚Äôd rather stick to non-alcoholic ice cream, [OddFellows](https://www.oddfellowsnyc.com/) is another great NYC option.
