#  Things To Do Around LA For Food Lovers 

December 26, 2018

## Cook With Grandmas

[![Things To Do Around LA For Food Lovers-league of kitchens cooking class, cook with grandmas](data:image/svg+xml,%3Csvg%20xmlns='http://www.w3.org/2000/svg'%20viewBox='0%200%20400%20400'%3E%3C/svg%3E)![Things To Do Around LA For Food Lovers-league of kitchens cooking class, cook with grandmas](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/2/2018/12/Things-To-Do-Around-LA-For-Food-Lovers-league-of-kitchens-cooking-class.jpg)](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/2/2018/12/Things-To-Do-Around-LA-For-Food-Lovers-league-of-kitchens-cooking-class.jpg)

Even if your grandma isn‚Äôt in LA, there are ways to learn from a grandma chef. [The League of Kitchens ](https://www.leagueofkitchens.com/how-it-works)offers small cooking classes with ‚Äúan immigrant who is an exceptional home cook and inspiring teacher‚Äù. [Classes](https://www.leagueofkitchens.com/cooking-classes-and-tours?kind=classes) range from Indian Immersion Classes, Armenian Workshops, and whatever else grandmothers around LA are offering. There are also shopping tours so you can learn to navigate the supermarkets of different cultures and learn about new ingredients.

## Find A Secret Dinner Party

[![Things To Do Around LA For Food Lovers-join a secret dinner club](data:image/svg+xml,%3Csvg%20xmlns='http://www.w3.org/2000/svg'%20viewBox='0%200%202048%201178'%3E%3C/svg%3E)![Things To Do Around LA For Food Lovers-join a secret dinner club](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/2/2017/10/35088591670_047996f7e2_k.jpg)](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/2/2017/10/35088591670_047996f7e2_k.jpg)

LA is full of hard-to-find dinner parties. [Sharksteeth](http://wolvesmouth.com/sharksteeth), from the people behind Wolvesmouth is a seafood-centric second secret supper club. You won‚Äôt know the menu or location until after you book, so this is best for adventurous eaters. [The Disco Dining Club i](http://www.discodiningclub.com/)s less secret to find, but what happens there is a mystery. It‚Äôs a multicourse costumed party that‚Äôs ‚Äúa culinary excursion steeped in all the excess, debauchery & hedonism of disco‚Äù. [Los Angeles Eats Itself](http://losangeleseatsitself.com/) is even more abstract. They ‚Äúreinterpret and introduce guests to their surroundings, even if many of LA‚Äôs most notorious moments border on the tragic, catastrophic and macabre.‚Äù Each dinner also comes with a lecture about how the menu was created and how it ties into LA history.

## Visit Grand Central Market

[![Things To Do Around LA For Food Lovers-visit grand central market](data:image/svg+xml,%3Csvg%20xmlns='http://www.w3.org/2000/svg'%20viewBox='0%200%201280%20960'%3E%3C/svg%3E)![Things To Do Around LA For Food Lovers-visit grand central market](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/2/2017/01/2273489260_4ce5aff6ff_o.jpg)](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/2/2017/01/2273489260_4ce5aff6ff_o.jpg)

Grand Central Market is one of the best food halls in Los Angeles. You can spend hours there sampling beers, tacos, noodles, and more. Without guidance, the market can be a bit overwhelming. Use [our self-guided tour](http://avitaltours.com/los-angeles/grand-central-market-food-tour-downtown-los-angeles) to ensure you don‚Äôt miss the best spots. One of our newest obsessions is the Buko Pie at[ Sari Sari Store.](http://sarisaristorela.com/menu/) Bring some friends so you can share dishes and try as much as possible. Then walk it off around downtown.

## Eat At Smorgasburg

[![smorgasburg: Things To Do Around LA For Food Lovers](data:image/svg+xml,%3Csvg%20xmlns='http://www.w3.org/2000/svg'%20viewBox='0%200%20640%20480'%3E%3C/svg%3E)![smorgasburg: Things To Do Around LA For Food Lovers](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/2/2018/04/26102411838_673e4a614c_z.jpg)](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/2/2018/04/26102411838_673e4a614c_z.jpg)

Each Sunday from 11 am-4 pm dozens of vendors pop up for [Smorgasburg](http://la.smorgasburg.com/), a weekly food festival that started in Brooklyn. The downtown Alameda Produce Market turns into a marketplace of rainbow dumplings, Instagram-worthy sandwiches, lobster nachos, giant mozzarella sticks, and more. The best move is to divide and conquer so everyone in your group can wait in one line and meet up to share. Some weeks at Smorgasburg are themed so keep your eyes out for music flea markets, holiday markets, and more.

## Join A Progressive Culinary Experience

[![go on a food tour: Things To Do Around LA For Food Lovers](data:image/svg+xml,%3Csvg%20xmlns='http://www.w3.org/2000/svg'%20viewBox='0%200%20800%20600'%3E%3C/svg%3E)![go on a food tour: Things To Do Around LA For Food Lovers](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/2/2016/07/Group-Activities-Los-Angeles-Cheersing-at-plant-food-and-wine.jpeg)](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/2/2016/07/Group-Activities-Los-Angeles-Cheersing-at-plant-food-and-wine.jpeg)

Explore the food scene in [Venice](http://avitaltours.com/los-angeles/tour/venice-beach-food-tour/), [Koreatown](http://avitaltours.com/los-angeles/koreatown-food-tour), or [Downtown Los Angeles](http://avitaltours.com/los-angeles/tour/downtown-food-tour/). We‚Äôll take you on a culinary experience curated by expert local tour guides who will also share the history of the neighborhood and the restaurants. It‚Äôs the best way to find under-the-radar spots in LA that have both a great history and great food. The three-hour four-restaurant [tour](http://avitaltours.com/los-angeles/) includes two appetizers, an entree, and a dessert with short walks in between courses.
