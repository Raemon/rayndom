#  5 More Things to Do in LA Before You Die 

February 15, 2018 

In this massive city, there‚Äôs a lot to sort through to figure out what to do, what to eat, and what to see. We‚Äôve simplified it for you. Here are 5 things to do in LA before you die.

## 1\. Go skiing and surfing in one day.

[![Ski and surf in one day: 5 Things to Do in LA Before You Die](data:image/svg+xml,%3Csvg%20xmlns='http://www.w3.org/2000/svg'%20viewBox='0%200%20500%20374'%3E%3C/svg%3E)![Ski and surf in one day: 5 Things to Do in LA Before You Die](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/2/2017/10/236691465_1c72f195b6_z.jpg)](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/2/2017/10/236691465_1c72f195b6_z.jpg)

One of the best parts of living in LA is that you can do a morning surf in Malibu, and then drive up to Big Bear for an afternoon of skiing. Of course, if you were feeling really motivated, you could even end your day in the desert by the pool in Palms Springs. Take advantage of all the climates Southern California has to offer and throw both your wetsuit and ski boots into the car for a grand adventure.

## 2\. Play Drag Queen Bingo at Hamburger Mary‚Äôs.

[![5 Things to Do in LA Before You Die: play Drag Queen Bingo at Hamburger Mary's](data:image/svg+xml,%3Csvg%20xmlns='http://www.w3.org/2000/svg'%20viewBox='0%200%20640%20426'%3E%3C/svg%3E)![5 Things to Do in LA Before You Die: play Drag Queen Bingo at Hamburger Mary's](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/2/2017/10/3711242567_7a2f9e6f13_z.jpg)](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/2/2017/10/3711242567_7a2f9e6f13_z.jpg)

Test your luck at Hamburger Mary‚Äôs in West Hollywood. Each Sunday, Thursday, and Wednesday, the WeHo establishment hosts Legendary Bingo that benefits a different local, national and international charities each week. Bingo is hosted by drag queens, so expect a loud, raunchy, and theatrical performance. Bingo night is also attended by a rotating celebrity ball caller. You will win prizes, but also expect that if you win, the audience will throw trash at you as you walk up to claim your prize.

_[www.hamburgermarys.com/weho/bingo/](http://www.hamburgermarys.com/weho/bingo/)_

_ 8288 Santa Monica Blvd, Los Angeles, CA 90046_

## 3\. Race turtles at Brennan‚Äôs.

[![race turtles as a part of 5 Things to Do in LA Before You Die](data:image/svg+xml,%3Csvg%20xmlns='http://www.w3.org/2000/svg'%20viewBox='0%200%201024%20681'%3E%3C/svg%3E)![race turtles as a part of 5 Things to Do in LA Before You Die](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/2/2017/10/3013281296_1ec3798e79_b.jpg)](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/2/2017/10/3013281296_1ec3798e79_b.jpg)

Head to the west side to watch the slow and steady win the race. Brennan‚Äôs in Marina Del Rey hosts turtle racing on Thursday nights. Make sure you know the rules before participating. If you point at the turtles during the race, you might be banned from the game‚Ä¶really. Where else in LA can you hang out with reptiles while sipping on a beer?

_[www.brennansla.com/](https://www.brennansla.com/)_

_ 4089 Lincoln Blvd, Marina Del Rey, CA 90292_

## 4\. Choose your French Dipped loyalty.

[![ 5 Things to Do in LA Before You Die: choose cole's or phillipe's](data:image/svg+xml,%3Csvg%20xmlns='http://www.w3.org/2000/svg'%20viewBox='0%200%201024%20768'%3E%3C/svg%3E)![ 5 Things to Do in LA Before You Die: choose cole's or phillipe's](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/2/2017/10/4951896887_2494aed443_b.jpg)](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/2/2017/10/4951896887_2494aed443_b.jpg)

Cole‚Äôs or Phillipe‚Äôs? It‚Äôs a choice LA residents and visitors must make. Both restaurants claim to be the creator of the French Dipped sandwich and also claim to have the best in town. The best way to decide? Go eat both of them. Cole‚Äôs is downtown and Phillipe‚Äôs is nearby Dodger‚Äôs Stadium, so they‚Äôre within close enough proximity to split them both for lunch. (Don‚Äôt forget the spicy garlic fries at Cole‚Äôs!)

## 5\. Spend 24 hours at a Korean Spa

[![korean spa: 5 Things to Do in LA Before You Die](data:image/svg+xml,%3Csvg%20xmlns='http://www.w3.org/2000/svg'%20viewBox='0%200%20960%20640'%3E%3C/svg%3E)![korean spa: 5 Things to Do in LA Before You Die](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/2/2017/10/bath-1317997_960_720.jpg)](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/2/2017/10/bath-1317997_960_720.jpg)

Okay, you don‚Äôt actually have to sit in a steam room for 24 hours, but know that you can at Korean spas like Wi Spa. For around $25 dollars, you‚Äôll get access to steam rooms, saunas, a room to relax with heated floors, a cold plunge, hot tubs, and a roof deck. There‚Äôs also a restaurant upstairs so you really never have to leave. For additional costs, you can get massages, a serious body scrub, or facial.

_[www.wispausa.com/](https://www.wispausa.com/)_

_2700 Wilshire Blvd, Los Angeles, CA 90057_
