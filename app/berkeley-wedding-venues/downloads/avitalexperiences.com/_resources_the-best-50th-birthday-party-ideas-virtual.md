### 1\. Indulge in Chinese food on a Chinatown Food Tour

Did you know that the Chinatown in San Francisco is the oldest one in North America? This historic district is home to some of the best eats in the whole city! Join us for a pampered journey to 3-4 of the best restaurants in the area, on our [Chinatown Food Tour](https://avitalexperiences.com/experiences/san-francisco-chinatown-food-tour/). Skip all the lines and tuck into dishes like scallion and beef dumplings, avocado salmon egg rolls, or chocolate xiao long bao, on this three-hour odyssey through Chinatown.

Neighborhood: Chinatown  
Price: $$+

![](data:image/svg+xml,%3Csvg%20xmlns='http://www.w3.org/2000/svg'%20viewBox='0%200%201024%20644'%3E%3C/svg%3E)![](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/09/5.-Pexels.com-stock-images-1024x644.jpg)Photo Credit: Pexels User Drew Rae

### Have a Game Night

Turn your Home into a casino for one night only to celebrate your 40th birthday! All you need to do is set up a few fold-out tables, some packs of cards, poker chips, and keep the alcohol flowing. No real money needs to be involved to make this night a hit! You could either play with something like wrapped candy, start everyone out with about 25 pieces, and allow them to take home their winnings at the end of the night. Or give out 100 poker chips per person, and for every 50 each person has won/kept by the end of the party, give them one lottery ticket. End the party with a drawing to see who wins the silly or special items you pick out as prizes!

Neighborhood: All  
Price: $+

![](data:image/svg+xml,%3Csvg%20xmlns='http://www.w3.org/2000/svg'%20viewBox='0%200%201024%20683'%3E%3C/svg%3E)![](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/2020/05/pexels-luftschnitzel-100582-1024x683.jpg)Photo Credit: Pexels User Philipp M

The Golden Gate Bridge is one of the best bike rides SF has to offer, between the views and the fact that if you elect to bike it on the weekend or weekdays after 3:30, you can enjoy the entire west side of the bridge, which is closed off from all vehicles and reserved for bikers and pedestrians to enjoy. You can either[ book a guided tour](https://www.blazingsaddles.com/san-francisco/routes-and-tours/bike-the-bridge) of the bridge and surrounding parks or [rent a bike](https://sfbiketours.com/bike-rentals/) and do it yourself. Head over to [Golden Gate Park](https://pickleballsf.com/golden-gate-park-tennis-complex/) for an epic Pickleball tournament with your boys. Pair everyone up in teams of two and have a few games going with the winners going on to play the winners until one team is victorious! The park offers courts at incredibly reasonable rates, just be sure to reserve far in advance of the big day because these courts fill up quickly. After you‚Äôve worked up a sweat you can head over to the Little Shamrock Bar right at the edge of the park for a couple of cold pints and free popcorn.

Where: Golden Gate Park  
Price: $

![](data:image/svg+xml,%3Csvg%20xmlns='http://www.w3.org/2000/svg'%20viewBox='0%200%20676%201013'%3E%3C/svg%3E)![](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/2016/08/M1-https___rusticales.com_.jpg)Photo Credit: https://rusticales.com

### 2\. Take a Trip across the Bay for the Best Breweries

If you and your buds consider yourself beer aficionados (or at the very least, big fans of the drink), a bar crawl through the best breweries in the Bay Area might just be the perfect party idea! SF proper has a few good breweries, and if you want to stay local, we‚Äôll point you right to House of Pizza (by Cellarmaker) in the Mission District. But true hop-heads know the best beer is in the East Bay, and it‚Äôs only a train ride away! Start at [Original Pattern Brewing](https://www.originalpatternbeer.com/), then walk to [Sante Adairius Rustic Ales](https://rusticales.com/), which is right next door to [Cellarmaker House of Pizza](https://cellarmakerbrewing.com/). After grabbing a few slices to sober up, pile into an Uber, and start heading towards the beach, hitting [Almanac Adventureland and Brewery](https://almanacbeer.com/) on the way, and ending the night at [Faction Brewing](https://www.factionbrewing.com/) with views of the Bay!

Neighborhood: Oakland  
Price: $$+

### 3\. Take the Boys Out to the Giants Game

If your birthday takes place between April and October, celebrate with the eight-time World Series champions, the San Francisco Giants. Games take place often, and there are different tiers of seating options. Book a box for you and your friends to hang out i,n or get a bunch of bleacher seats and have your buddies meet you out behind center left field. Before the game, sip on beers and nosh on bar snacks at [Public House](http://www.publichousesf.com/) or [21st Amendment](http://21st-amendment.com/). After the game, go to [Momo‚Äôs](http://www.sfmomos.com/) to keep the party going.

Neighborhood: SoMa  
Price: $$+

## Best Party Themes for an SF 50th Birthday Party

![](data:image/svg+xml,%3Csvg%20xmlns='http://www.w3.org/2000/svg'%20viewBox='0%200%201024%20605'%3E%3C/svg%3E)![](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/2020/05/Screenshot-2025-08-31-at-12.00.45-AM-1024x605.png)Photo Credit: Pexels User Mart Production

### 1\. Decades

Invite your guests to dress in the style of their favorite decade to celebrate your 50th! For the ‚Äô70s you can embrace flower power with long dresses and peace sign jewelry. Or you could put on spandex and crop tops for an ‚Äô80s jazzercise look. Your guests can get grungy with plaid flannels and ripped jeans to embody the ‚Äô90s. Or go all in on Y2K and dress like you‚Äôre straight out of Mean Girls or a Britney Spears music video. This is a great theme because it allows your guests to customize their look and pick the best costume accoutrement from their closet.

![](data:image/svg+xml,%3Csvg%20xmlns='http://www.w3.org/2000/svg'%20viewBox='0%200%201024%20683'%3E%3C/svg%3E)![](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/2016/08/T5-pexels.com-stock-photo-1024x683.jpg)Photo Credit: Pexel User cottonbro studio

### 2\. Oscar Party

Embrace Hollywood Glamour with an Oscars-themed birthday party! Turn your home or venue of choice into a VIP Oscars afterparty for one night only. It doesn‚Äôt even have to coincide with Oscar‚Äôs season. This theme is all about dressing chic, embracing your cinephile side, and sipping your drink of choice out of champagne flutes (which can totally be plastic, we won‚Äôt tell). Put your favorite movie posters up on the wall, some old black and white film on the TV as set decoration, and roll out the red carpet for your guests to walk in on. Bonus points if you acquire a few of those life-size cardboard celebrities for hilarious photo ops with your guests!

![](data:image/svg+xml,%3Csvg%20xmlns='http://www.w3.org/2000/svg'%20viewBox='0%200%201024%20683'%3E%3C/svg%3E)![](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/2020/05/pexels-kevinbidwell-2386728-1024x683.jpg)Photo Credit: Pexels User Kecin Bidwell

### 3\. Wild West

Who doesn‚Äôt love the Wild West! Don on a cowboy hat and neckerchief while you pump country music from the speakers. Get the grill out and serve barbecued meats and heaping bowls of cheesy chili to turn your house into the prairie! After you eat, take the party for a nightcap at Westwood in the Marina District where you can ride one of the only mechanical bulls left in the Bay Area. They serve delicious whiskey drinks and tex-mex snacks until 1:30am so the party can keep going all night long!

![](data:image/svg+xml,%3Csvg%20xmlns='http://www.w3.org/2000/svg'%20viewBox='0%200%201024%20782'%3E%3C/svg%3E)![](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/2020/05/Screenshot-2025-08-31-at-12.06.16-AM-1024x782.png)Photo Credit: Pexel User Kindel Media

### 4\. Tropical

Bring Island vibes to your birthday party when you choose a Tropical theme! San Francisco has beautiful sunny days and gorgeous beaches, so why not take advantage and throw this party outside? A tiki bar is a great touch, as well as having a few tiki torches scattered about. Set up a blender so you can keep plying guests with icy drinks like pi√±a coladas and daiquiris all night long. Dress code is bathing suits, and Hawaiian shirts‚Äîand grab a few packs of Leis from Party City for the finishing touch!
