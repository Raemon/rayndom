#  5 Restaurants with Breathtaking Views in Los Angeles 

March 23, 2017 

Los Angeles is a city with lots of opportunity for views. On a clear day, you can see snowcapped mountains in the distance. Tall downtown buildings let you view DTLA‚Äôs architectural gems. And of course, the west side of the city overlooks the Pacific Ocean and stellar sunsets. These 5 restaurants with breathtaking views in Los Angeles look over all the city has to offer.

## 1\. Nobu in Malibu

[![Nobu in Malibu is one of 5 Restaurants with Breathtaking Views in Los Angeles](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/2/2017/03/6278390183_6e5b5e8f32_z.jpg)](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/2/2017/03/6278390183_6e5b5e8f32_z.jpg)

Your experience here will probably include viewing a celebrity, but the real view is out back. Nobu is located right off the Pacific Coast Highway (PCH), so you can eat your sushi by the ocean. The back patio is perfect for LA‚Äôs best sunny days. But if you can‚Äôt score a table outside, get a first come, first served seat at the bar with floor to ceiling windows. This PCH real estate secures Nobu on any list of restaurants with breathtaking views in Los Angeles.

_<http://www.noburestaurants.com/malibu/>[  
](http://www.noburestaurants.com/malibu/)__22706 Pacific Coast Highway, Malibu, CA 90265_

## 2\. Mariasol[![5 Restaurants with Breathtaking Views in Los Angeles: Mariasol on the SantaMonica Pier](data:image/svg+xml,%3Csvg%20xmlns='http://www.w3.org/2000/svg'%20viewBox='0%200%202048%201536'%3E%3C/svg%3E)![5 Restaurants with Breathtaking Views in Los Angeles: Mariasol on the SantaMonica Pier](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/2/2017/03/7919251784_0e40989066_k.jpg)](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/2/2017/03/7919251784_0e40989066_k.jpg)

If you‚Äôre looking for a restaurant on the water, it‚Äôs hard to get more literal than Mariasol. The Mexican spot is located at the end of the Santa Monica pier, so you‚Äôll have a clear view of the sunset and the Santa Monica, Venice, and Malibu beaches. This isn‚Äôt the kind of place that LA locals frequent, but it‚Äôs a great place to sip a margarita and stare at the ocean while you‚Äôre on vacation (or staycation). Warning: if you‚Äôre sitting outside (which you should be) don‚Äôt let the seagulls steal your food!

[_http://www.mariasol.com/  
_](http://www.mariasol.com/)_401 Santa Monica Pier, Santa Monica, CA 90401_

## 3.Upstairs at the Ace Hotel

![The Ace Hotel is one of 5 Restaurants with Breathtaking Views in Los Angeles](data:image/svg+xml,%3Csvg%20xmlns='http://www.w3.org/2000/svg'%20viewBox='0%200%201074%20848'%3E%3C/svg%3E)![The Ace Hotel is one of 5 Restaurants with Breathtaking Views in Los Angeles](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/2/2017/01/Screen-Shot-2017-01-30-at-12.31.21-PM.png)The bar and restaurant on top of The Ace Hotel has one of the best views of downtown. You can walk around the entire rooftop bar and pool deck and see views from all sides of the building. On one side you‚Äôll see ‚ÄúDowntown‚Äôs Turquoise Gem‚Äù the Eastern Columbia Building. The building is the greatest example of Art Deco east of the Mississippi. Keep walking around the rest of the deck to get the layout of downtown.

_https://www.acehotel.com/losangeles/upstairs  
__929 S. Broadway, Los Angeles, CA 90015 _

## 4\. The Restaurant at The Getty Center

[![The Restaurant at the Getty Center is one of 5 Restaurants with Breathtaking Views in Los Angeles](data:image/svg+xml,%3Csvg%20xmlns='http://www.w3.org/2000/svg'%20viewBox='0%200%202048%201345'%3E%3C/svg%3E)![The Restaurant at the Getty Center is one of 5 Restaurants with Breathtaking Views in Los Angeles](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/2/2017/03/14315018588_c18c72f4da_k.jpg)](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/2/2017/03/14315018588_c18c72f4da_k.jpg)

The Getty is a great art museum in LA. Because it‚Äôs perched up high, it also has great views. Museum food isn‚Äôt something that‚Äôs usually very exciting, but most museum food doesn‚Äôt come with a view of the Santa Monica Mountains and the ocean. Stop in for lunch, dinner, Sunday brunch, or just dessert and wine. The menu changes seasonally, but the backdrop is a staple all year round.

[_http://www.getty.edu/visit/center/eat.html  
_](http://www.getty.edu/visit/center/eat.html)_1200 Getty Center Dr, Los Angeles, CA 90049_

## 5\. The Castaway

[![Castaway is one of 5 Restaurants with Breathtaking Views in Los Angeles:](data:image/svg+xml,%3Csvg%20xmlns='http://www.w3.org/2000/svg'%20viewBox='0%200%201024%20685'%3E%3C/svg%3E)![Castaway is one of 5 Restaurants with Breathtaking Views in Los Angeles:](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/2/2017/03/5198631524_d167853a73_b.jpg)](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/2/2017/03/5198631524_d167853a73_b.jpg)

Schelp out to Burbank for a meal? If you want a great view of the Verdugo Mountains, yes. The back patio sticks out from a hillside and is covered with fire pits. There‚Äôs also koi ponds and a waterfall if the view doesn‚Äôt bring you enough inner peace. The Castaway serves a weekend brunch buffet, lunch, dinner, and a special ‚ÄúSunset Menu‚Äù, which you should probably drink a glass of wine next to a firepit.

[_http://www.castawayrestaurant.com/home  
_](http://www.castawayrestaurant.com/home)_1250 E Harvard Rd, Burbank, CA 91501_
