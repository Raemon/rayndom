#  5 Summer Food Events In SF We‚Äôre Looking Forward To 

June 10, 2018 

Summer is quickly arriving in San Francisco, and although the ‚Äúsummer‚Äù weather doesn‚Äôt always lend itself to being outside, we‚Äôre still excited. Here are five summer food events in SF and around the city that we‚Äôre looking forward to. (Note: the [SF Street Food Festival ](https://www.sfstreetfoodfest.com/)is coming in October, just after summer, but we want to give you a heads up anyway.)

## Gilroy Garlic Festival

[![Join other garlic lovers for the gilroy garlic festival-5 Summer Food Events In SF We're Looking Forward To](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/3/2018/06/Garlic_WebsiteHeader_2018-4.png)](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/3/2018/06/Garlic_WebsiteHeader_2018-4.png)

Garlic lovers, unite! Head south to Gilroy for their annual [garlic festival](https://gilroygarlicfestival.com/) that celebrates one of our favorite ingredients. The three-day event includes contests, cooking workshops, music, and of course, lots of food. Check out Gourmet Alley and fill up on zesty fried garlic calamari, Gilroy garlic shrimp, garlic fries, garlic bread, and more.

##  Outside Lands

[![Outside Lands has great food! 5 Summer Food Events In SF We're Looking Forward To](data:image/svg+xml,%3Csvg%20xmlns='http://www.w3.org/2000/svg'%20viewBox='0%200%20875%20531'%3E%3C/svg%3E)![Outside Lands has great food! 5 Summer Food Events In SF We're Looking Forward To](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/3/2018/06/ol18-outside-lands-logo-2018-large.png)](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/3/2018/06/ol18-outside-lands-logo-2018-large.png)

Yes, [Outside Lands ](http://www.sfoutsidelands.com)is technically a music festival, but the[ food selection](https://www.sfoutsidelands.com/taste/) is incredible. In the past years, restaurants such as [Rich Table ](https://www.richtablesf.com/)had booths right in Golden Gate Park. This year the list of [restaurant partners, trucks, and carts](https://www.sfoutsidelands.com/taste/) will make your mouth water. If you‚Äôre looking for both beats and eats, this is the music festival you can‚Äôt miss. (And pick us up an ice cream taco and Tartine grilled cheese!)

## Northern California Soy And Tofu Festival

[![Northern California Soy And Tofu Festival 5 Summer Food Events In SF We're Looking Forward To](data:image/svg+xml,%3Csvg%20xmlns='http://www.w3.org/2000/svg'%20viewBox='0%200%201206%20965'%3E%3C/svg%3E)![Northern California Soy And Tofu Festival 5 Summer Food Events In SF We're Looking Forward To](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/3/2018/06/stf_logo_new-transparent.png)](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/3/2018/06/stf_logo_new-transparent.png)

How many dishes can you make with soy and tofu? You‚Äôre about to find out. [The Northern California Soy and Tofu Festival](https://soyandtofufest.org/) is coming to SF on June 30th. Food vendors and food trucks will be serving up soy and tofu dishes. There will also be cooking demonstrations, [a dessert competition](https://soyandtofufest.org/tofu-dessert-competition/), samples, live entertainment, and soy education. Bring along your favorite vegetarian.

## **Ghirardelli Chocolate Festival**

[![5 Summer Food Events In SF We're Looking Forward ToGhirardelli Chocolate Festival](data:image/svg+xml,%3Csvg%20xmlns='http://www.w3.org/2000/svg'%20viewBox='0%200%20800%20400'%3E%3C/svg%3E)![5 Summer Food Events In SF We're Looking Forward ToGhirardelli Chocolate Festival](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/3/2018/06/https_2F2Fcdn.evbuc_.com2Fimages2F417601092F273400731612F12Foriginal.jpg)](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/3/2018/06/https_2F2Fcdn.evbuc_.com2Fimages2F417601092F273400731612F12Foriginal.jpg)

Chocolate lovers will rush to Ghiradelli Square for the annual [Ghirardelli Chocolate Festival](https://www.eventbrite.com/e/23rd-annual-ghirardelli-chocolate-festival-tickets-43728782967). Each September for 23 years, the festival has put on chocolate-themed events and activities like dessert demonstrations by chefs and a Ghirardelli Chocolate Master. Feeling competitive? Enter the Earthquake Ice Cream Eating Contest. We‚Äôre cheering you on.

## Nihonmachi Street Fair

[![Eat Japanese food at the Nihonmachi Street Fair Food Fest - 5 Summer Food Events In SF We're Looking Forward To](data:image/svg+xml,%3Csvg%20xmlns='http://www.w3.org/2000/svg'%20viewBox='0%200%20975%20246'%3E%3C/svg%3E)![Eat Japanese food at the Nihonmachi Street Fair Food Fest - 5 Summer Food Events In SF We're Looking Forward To](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/3/2018/06/nsf_masthead.jpg)](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/3/2018/06/nsf_masthead.jpg)

Japantown is one of San Francisco‚Äôs most delicious neighborhoods. The [Nihonmachi Street Fair Food Fest](http://www.nihonmachistreetfair.org/nsf_food_fest.htm) is the perfect place to try lots of food in one place while celebrating Asian-Pacific American life. Expect a variety of noodles, curries, teriyaki burgers, shaved ice, and more.
