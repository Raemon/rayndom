## Tips for Organizing Team Building Activities 

  * **Come in with a Plan:** A little bit of structure goes a long way! Map out the order of activities you want to accomplish with time estimates beforehand.
  * **Keep it Inclusive:** Pick activities that cater to all skill and ability levels. And don‚Äôt forget about dietary restrictions if serving food or drink. This is meant to bring your team closer, not single anyone out.
  * **Don‚Äôt Forget the Group Photo:** You‚Äôre going to want to remember this day! Make sure to get some pictures.
  * **Pick teams at random** : If the activity involves teams, find a random way to generate them rather than having people pick their own teams. This prevents anyone from being left out or ‚Äúpicked last‚Äù.
