## **When to Book an Interactive Bar?**

The bars are designed for large groups and reception-style events, hosted at your venue or event space‚Äîwhether that‚Äôs a restaurant, conference center, or your own office.

  * **Corporate Event Entertainment:** Ditch the magician for something new, different, and crowd-pleasing
  * **Conference Receptions:** Keep guests engaged and mingling after or between sessions
  * **Client Entertainment Events:** Impress clients with a unique, hands-on experience
  * **Team Building Events:** Get coworkers collaborating over cocktails or culinary creations
  * **Office Parties & Holiday Celebrations**: Elevate happy hour or add an interactive twist to your event
  * **Networking** : Break the ice with a shared activity.
  * **Product Launches** : Create a memorable brand experience with engaging stations

## How It Works

Interactive Bars are designed for large, reception-style events, perfect for groups of 100 to 300 guests. Guests rotate organically between stations, each offering a quick, engaging activity‚Äîlike mixing a cocktail or mocktail, crafting a custom rim blend, or a unique tasting experience. Guests stay engaged with quick, organized activities without the long, instructional format of a class or performance.
