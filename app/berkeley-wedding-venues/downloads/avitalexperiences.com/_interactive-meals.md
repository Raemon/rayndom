## **What Are Interactive Meals?**

Our Interactive Meal is lighter and easier than a cooking class, yet more fun and interactive than just booking a [private dining](https://avitalexperiences.com/private-dining/) room. Guests are welcomed with a delicious drink and snacks as they mix and mingle. Next, each guest will craft their own cocktail with appetizers along the way. Then, guests will be seated together for the seasonal entr√©e and another beverage pairing before teaming up for food and drink trivia with prizes. After a delicious dessert and more time for authentic engagement, the experience ends with a special, perspective-shifting flavor finale!
