#  5 Ways To Embrace Your Inner-Tourist in NYC 

February 20, 2018 

Sometimes when you live in a great city like New York, you forget to do the things that all tourists experience during their visit. While we don‚Äôt recommend spending the whole day waiting in the long line to go to the top of the Empire State Building, it is fun to embrace your inner-tourist in NYC. Here are 5 ways to be a tourist in your own city.

## 1\. Go See The Sights

[![The met is a great place to visit if you're looking for 5 Ways To Embrace Your Inner-Tourist in NYC](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/5/2018/02/16971334541_a3bb97eac3_z.jpg)](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/5/2018/02/16971334541_a3bb97eac3_z.jpg)

When is the last time you went to [Ellis Island](https://www.nps.gov/elis/planyourvisit/hours.htm) or the Statue of Liberty? Have you been to [The Met ](https://www.metmuseum.org/)or the [Museum of Natural History](https://www.amnh.org/)? What about the [Central Park Zoo](https://centralparkzoo.com/)? Pick an NYC cultural institution or landmark and visit alone or with a group of local friends. Before you go, check which museums have [f](https://www.nyc-arts.org/collections/35/free-museum-days-or-pay-what-you-wish)[ree or discounted](https://www.nyc-arts.org/collections/35/free-museum-days-or-pay-what-you-wish) days.

## 2\. Let Someone Else Be Your (Food) Tour Guide

[![take a food tour as part of 5 Ways To Embrace Your Inner-Tourist in NYC](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/5/2014/02/Corporate-Team-Building-in-NYC-guests-cheersing-with-cocktails.jpeg)](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/5/2014/02/Corporate-Team-Building-in-NYC-guests-cheersing-with-cocktails.jpeg)

Even if you‚Äôre a local, you might not be in the loop when it comes to the best restaurants in the city. Let a guide take you around New York City on a food tour. Our tour is for locals, by locals, so you‚Äôll learn something new about an NYC neighborhood. The [progressive culinary experience](http://avitaltours.com/nyc-food-tours/) includes four courses at four different restaurants. In between, you‚Äôll learn about the culture and history of the neighborhood through food. You‚Äôll even get to chat with some of the chefs and owners of the restaurants who are your NYC neighbors.

## 3\. Eat Something That‚Äôs ‚Äú _So New York‚Äù_

[![Head to Coney Island and go to Nathan's 5 Ways To Embrace Your Inner-Tourist in NYC](data:image/svg+xml,%3Csvg%20xmlns='http://www.w3.org/2000/svg'%20viewBox='0%200%202048%201365'%3E%3C/svg%3E)![Head to Coney Island and go to Nathan's 5 Ways To Embrace Your Inner-Tourist in NYC](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/5/2018/02/7834821302_398bf0ed30_k.jpg)](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/5/2018/02/7834821302_398bf0ed30_k.jpg)

Tourists come to New York in search of the original [Ray‚Äôs Pizza](http://www.rayspizza.com/), [Junior‚Äôs Cheesecake](https://www.juniorscheesecake.com/), [Magnolia Bakery](https://www.magnoliabakery.com/), and [Katz‚Äôs Deli](https://www.katzsdelicatessen.com/), usually, because they‚Äôve seen one of these spots in a movie or tv show. Eat a meal at one of these NYC classics, and know that yes, they will be filled with tourists. That doesn‚Äôt mean these spots are bad, though. You can get a great hot dog at [Gray‚Äôs Papaya](http://grayspapayanyc.com/) or [Nathan‚Äôs](https://nathansfamous.com/). (Bonus tourist points if you trek to Coney Island for your hot dog.)

## 4\. See NYC By Boat

[![take a boat ride: 5 Ways To Embrace Your Inner-Tourist in NYC](data:image/svg+xml,%3Csvg%20xmlns='http://www.w3.org/2000/svg'%20viewBox='0%200%20640%20480'%3E%3C/svg%3E)![take a boat ride: 5 Ways To Embrace Your Inner-Tourist in NYC](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/5/2018/02/1253354246_02402f1378_z.jpg)](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/5/2018/02/1253354246_02402f1378_z.jpg)

Unless you‚Äôre commuting on the Staten Island Ferry, you‚Äôre probably not taking a boat around the city very often. One of the most fun ways to see the city is from the water. There are a ton of sightseeing [cruise options](https://www.circleline.com/) from daytime to twilight, as well as [yacht](https://nycsailingcharters.com/) charters. Or, of course, you can hop aboard the [Staten Island Ferry](http://www.siferry.com/faqtips.html).

## 5\. See A Broadway Show

[![go to the theatre: 5 Ways To Embrace Your Inner-Tourist in NYC](data:image/svg+xml,%3Csvg%20xmlns='http://www.w3.org/2000/svg'%20viewBox='0%200%20640%20480'%3E%3C/svg%3E)![go to the theatre: 5 Ways To Embrace Your Inner-Tourist in NYC](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/5/2018/02/96905377_8b32107779_z.jpg)](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/5/2018/02/96905377_8b32107779_z.jpg)

Theatre shouldn‚Äôt be reserved for visitors. Head down to Times Square (the ultimate tourist destination) and see a show. (Yes, Wicked is  _still_ playing.) There are a few ways to find discounted tickets. The [TKTS](https://www.tdf.org/nyc/81/TKTS-Live) booth has two locations, the [TodayTix](https://www.todaytix.com/) app has lottery tickets and discounted tickets, and some theaters have an in-person and mobile [l](http://www.nytix.com/Links/Broadway/lotteryschedule.html)[ottery and rush tickets ](http://www.nytix.com/Links/Broadway/lotteryschedule.html)available day-of.
