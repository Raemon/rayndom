#  5 Luxurious Activities to Entertain Clients Virtually 

July 23, 2020 

Looking to add a little luxury to your virtual entertaining game? Whether you‚Äôve got a high profile client you want to impress or simply want to make your clients feel special, we‚Äôve got five luxurious activities to entertain clients virtually. 

## **Learn from a world-class chef**

Treat your clients to a private cooking class with an award-winning chef. A [Virtual Chefinar](http://avitaltours.com/san-francisco/virtual-chefinars/) experience puts the experts right in your clients‚Äô homes and teaches them how to make a delicious meal. Have ingredient boxes sent straight to their doors, and voila‚Äî all they have to do is turn on Zoom. If a happy hour is more of your clients‚Äô style, a [virtual mixology lesson](http://avitaltours.com/san-francisco/virtual-mixologist/) from an expert bartender is also available. 

[![award winning chef pouring sauce to entertain clients virtually](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/5/2020/07/entertain-clients-virtually-with-cooking-class.jpg)](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/5/2020/07/entertain-clients-virtually-with-cooking-class.jpg)

## **Book a private concert**

Without the crowds and the noise, a private concert feels much more intimate. Plus, your clients get to interact with the musician, an option that‚Äôs not always available at your standard concert. Ask your clients to dress up and send them a bottle of wine beforehand for a VIP event. 

[![person playing guitar to entertain clients virtually](data:image/svg+xml,%3Csvg%20xmlns='http://www.w3.org/2000/svg'%20viewBox='0%200%20700%20467'%3E%3C/svg%3E)![person playing guitar to entertain clients virtually](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/5/2020/07/entertain-clients-virtually-with-private-concert.jpg)](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/5/2020/07/entertain-clients-virtually-with-private-concert.jpg)

## **Hire an industry thought leader for a Q &A**

The key to adding a little luxury to entertaining clients virtually is through planning activities that will really make them feel special. Just like booking a private concert, hiring an industry thought leader for a Q&A gives them access to highly-lauded professionals. This might not be the typical way you wine-and-dine your clients, but an intimate Q&A will feel exclusive. Your clients will definitely appreciate it! 

[![industry leader to entertain clients virtually](data:image/svg+xml,%3Csvg%20xmlns='http://www.w3.org/2000/svg'%20viewBox='0%200%20700%20467'%3E%3C/svg%3E)![industry leader to entertain clients virtually](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/5/2020/07/entertain-clients-virtually-with-amazing-speaker.jpg)](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/5/2020/07/entertain-clients-virtually-with-amazing-speaker.jpg)

## **Taste great wines with a private sommelier.**

If you‚Äôve got a big budget, hiring a private sommelier for a virtual wine tasting is classy, unique, and fun. [Sommelier Company](https://www.sommeliercompany.com/virtual-wine-tasting-with-sommelier-online-beer-whiskey-scotch-bourbon-tequila) will send three bottles of wine to each of your clients and set up a Zoom call to walk you through a tasting. You can also opt for a whiskey tasting, if that‚Äôs something your clients would be interested in. It will transport your clients to the vineyards of France or Italy without leaving home. 

[![wine tasting to entertain clients virtually](data:image/svg+xml,%3Csvg%20xmlns='http://www.w3.org/2000/svg'%20viewBox='0%200%20700%20467'%3E%3C/svg%3E)![wine tasting to entertain clients virtually](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/5/2020/07/entertain-clients-virtually-with-wine-tasting.jpg)](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/5/2020/07/entertain-clients-virtually-with-wine-tasting.jpg)

## **Plan a night out at the opera**

At home, of course! Since COVID-19, the Metropolitan Opera has moved performances online. [Ticketed options](https://metstarslive.brightcove-services.com/) are available, giving you access to stunning performances from all over the world. Book some tickets for your clients and watch together. The stream also stays on-demand for 12 days after the event, so you can share with anyone who wasn‚Äôt able to make it. 

[![night at the opera to entertain clients virtually](data:image/svg+xml,%3Csvg%20xmlns='http://www.w3.org/2000/svg'%20viewBox='0%200%20700%20467'%3E%3C/svg%3E)![night at the opera to entertain clients virtually](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/5/2020/07/entertain-clients-virtually-with-opera-performance.jpg)](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/5/2020/07/entertain-clients-virtually-with-opera-performance.jpg)

Planning to entertain clients virtually? Book a [Chefinar](http://avitaltours.com/san-francisco/virtual-chefinars/) or [Virtual Mixology](http://avitaltours.com/san-francisco/virtual-mixologist/) experience! Have ingredients sent straight to clients‚Äô doors and learn from award-winning mixologists and chefs.
