#  5 Ultimate Things to Do in San Francisco 

October 18, 2017 

For a small city, San Francisco packs a lot into its 49 square miles. From brewery tours to quirky festivals to some of the country‚Äôs best eating, there‚Äôs so much to do in SF that can be overwhelming. Let us narrow it down for you. Here are 5 ultimate things to do in San Francisco.

## 1\. Bike to Sausalito and Ferry Back

[![5 Ultimate Things to Do in San Francisco: bike over the bridge](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/3/2017/10/36994952531_a9e4fe9d08_k.jpg)](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/3/2017/10/36994952531_a9e4fe9d08_k.jpg)

Biking over the Golden Gate Bridge and taking the ferry back is one of the ultimate San Francisco experiences. The red bridge is iconic and so is the SF Bay‚Ä¶and boating back also a great excuse to rest your tired legs. The trip comes with views and a great workout, and if you‚Äôre on our page a great meal in Sausalito and a beer on the ferry.

_[www.goldengateferry.org/schedules/Sausalito.php](http://goldengateferry.org/schedules/Sausalito.php)_

## 2\. Spend a Lazy Day at Dolores Park

[![Dolores Park is one of the Ultimate Things to Do in San Francisco:](data:image/svg+xml,%3Csvg%20xmlns='http://www.w3.org/2000/svg'%20viewBox='0%200%201024%20768'%3E%3C/svg%3E)![Dolores Park is one of the Ultimate Things to Do in San Francisco:](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/3/2017/10/37313545486_fa6c484282_b.jpg)](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/3/2017/10/37313545486_fa6c484282_b.jpg)

If you‚Äôre looking for a laid-back Sunday in San Francisco, head to the heart of the Mission. It‚Äôs where many of SF‚Äôs residents are picnicking, drinking mimosas, and nursing hangovers in Mission Dolores Park. The Mission is THE neighborhood for burritos, so get yours to go and bring it to the park. Adjacent to the park is [Bi-Rite Creamery](http://biritecreamery.com/) where you should get a scoop of salted caramel and its sister market [Bi-Rite Market](http://www.biritemarket.com/) where picnic supplies are in abundance. The view of the city is just as good as the seriously good people watching. When you‚Äôve had your fill of sitting, explore the Mission‚Äôs great shops and restaurants, or join us on a[ food tour.](http://avitaltours.com/san-francisco/tour/mission-district-food-tour/)

_Dolores St & 19th St, San Francisco, CA 94114  
__[www.sfrecpark.org/destination/mission-dolores-park/](http://www.sfrecpark.org/destination/mission-dolores-park/)_

## 3\. Go to Alcatraz at Night

[![5 Ultimate Things to Do in San Francisco: Alcatraz](data:image/svg+xml,%3Csvg%20xmlns='http://www.w3.org/2000/svg'%20viewBox='0%200%201024%20681'%3E%3C/svg%3E)![5 Ultimate Things to Do in San Francisco: Alcatraz](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/3/2017/10/6838272547_15d646fdd6_b.jpg)](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/3/2017/10/6838272547_15d646fdd6_b.jpg)

Visiting an abandoned (and maybe haunted!) jail is already creepy, but make it even scarier by going after dark. (Bonus: it‚Äôs also less crowded after dark, so you‚Äôll have more opportunity to explore and more intimate guided tours!) Alcatraz is a place locals often neglect, but it‚Äôs full of history and myth about its famous inmates like Al Capone. Want to make your adventure even tastier? We suggest [combining your trip ](http://avitaltours.com/san-francisco/alcatraz-tickets-package)to Alcatraz with some Italian eats in nearby North Beach.

_Alcatraz Island  
__[www.nps.gov/alca/index.htm](https://www.nps.gov/alca/index.htm)_

## 4\. Explore Golden Gate Park and Surrounding Neighborhoods

![Going to Golden Gate Park is on our list of 5 Ultimate Things to Do in San Francisco](data:image/svg+xml,%3Csvg%20xmlns='http://www.w3.org/2000/svg'%20viewBox='0%200%202048%201356'%3E%3C/svg%3E)![Going to Golden Gate Park is on our list of 5 Ultimate Things to Do in San Francisco](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/3/2017/10/37524970232_b412ad9bb4_k.jpg)

San Francisco is surrounded by beautiful nature, but it also has gems within city limits. Golden Gate Park is the most robust of SF‚Äôs natural gems and spending an afternoon here can make you forget you‚Äôre even in a city. Rowing boats on [Stow Lake](http://stowlakeboathouse.com/) . Stroll around the [Japanese Tea Garden](http://japaneseteagardensf.com/)s. Visit the albino alligator at the [California Academy of Sciences](https://www.calacademy.org/). Lots of SF‚Äôs ultimate experiences are right in this park. Some of SF‚Äôs quirkiest events happen right in the park, like the yearly Hunky Jesus contest and the free Hardly Strictly Bluegrass Festival. And the surrounding neighborhoods of the Inner Richmond and Inner Sunset are underrated neighborhoods for both shopping (hello, [Green Apple](http://www.greenapplebooks.com/) bookstore!) and Chinese food. Head to dumpling spot [Shanghai Dumpling King](http://www.shanghaidumplingkingsf.com/), city favorite [Burma Superstar](http://www.burmasuperstar.com/), or Nopa‚Äôs Mexican restaurant [Nopalito](http://www.nopalitosf.com/) for good eats.

_Golden Gate Park  
__[www.goldengatepark.com/](https://goldengatepark.com/)_

##  5\. See a Baseball Game at AT&T Park

[![baseball game is on the list of 5 Ultimate Things to Do in San Francisco](data:image/svg+xml,%3Csvg%20xmlns='http://www.w3.org/2000/svg'%20viewBox='0%200%201024%20683'%3E%3C/svg%3E)![baseball game is on the list of 5 Ultimate Things to Do in San Francisco](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/3/2017/10/8207743761_858fc884bb_b.jpg)](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/3/2017/10/8207743761_858fc884bb_b.jpg)

Don black and orange and head downtown to a baseball game. You can kayak outside of AT&T Park and hope a foul ball comes toward you, but the real action is inside the stadium. That‚Äôs also where the good food is. It makes sense that San Francisco, a food-focused city, is serving up great food in the ballpark. [Rich Table](http://richtablesf.com/) slings fried porcini donuts with raclette cheese, North Beach‚Äôs [Tony‚Äôs Pizza Napoletana](http://tonyspizzanapoletana.com/) is serving up slices, [CREAM](http://creamnation.com/) has custom ice cream cookie sandwiches, and the smell of garlic fries is impossible to ignore. Luckily baseball is a slow moving sport, so there‚Äôs lots of time for snacks in between home runs.

_ 24 Willie Mays Plaza, San Francisco, CA 94107_

_[www.sanfrancisco.giants.mlb.com/sf/ballpark/](http://sanfrancisco.giants.mlb.com/sf/ballpark/)_
