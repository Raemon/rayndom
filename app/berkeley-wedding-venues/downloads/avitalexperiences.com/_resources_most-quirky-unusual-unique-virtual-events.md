We‚Äôve scoured the internet to bring you these amazing novelty experiences that are sure to keep your team entertained and engaged.   * [Virtual Flavor Tripping](http://avitaltours.com/virtual-flavor-tripping/)

You won‚Äôt find any overused icebreakers, virtual trust falls, or long boring presentations on this list. Keep reading for the most quirky, unusual, and unique virtual events! The kind that will make your team excited to log onto the event early! 

## **[![flavor kit delivered during virtual flavor tripping event](data:image/svg+xml,%3Csvg%20xmlns='http://www.w3.org/2000/svg'%20viewBox='0%200%20700%20467'%3E%3C/svg%3E)![flavor kit delivered during virtual flavor tripping event](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/2/2021/02/virtual-flavor-tripping-setup-unique-virtual-event.jpg)](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/2/2021/02/virtual-flavor-tripping-setup-unique-virtual-event.jpg)  
1\. Trick your senses with flavor tripping**

[Flavor tripping is a unique, sensory experience](https://avitalexperiences.com/experiences/virtual-tongue-twister-tasting/) all about changing your perception of flavor. Using Miracle Berries, little red berries from Africa, change anything sour into something deliciously sweet. While you learn about flavor, you also learn about how it impacts everyone differently, as well as getting a chance to know each other. It‚Äôs a unique, mind-bending experience with great team-building results.

![team in zoom during unique virtual event](data:image/svg+xml,%3Csvg%20xmlns='http://www.w3.org/2000/svg'%20viewBox='0%200%20700%20467'%3E%3C/svg%3E)![team in zoom during unique virtual event](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/2/2021/02/kindness-quest-for-unique-virtual-events.jpg)Image via teambuilding.com

## **2\. Go on a Kindness Quest**

Practice gratitude and appreciation with your team on a [Kindness Quest](https://teambuilding.com/events/kindness-quest). You‚Äôll play games and activities that inspire kindness, in addition to contributing to the community.
