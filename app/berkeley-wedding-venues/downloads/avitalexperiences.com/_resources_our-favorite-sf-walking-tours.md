## **Walking Tours vs. Other Tours**

Despite a few (okay, many) hills, San Francisco is meant to be explored on foot. Instead of whizzing by in a bus, bike, or car, you get to experience the city at a slower pace and see the nuances of daily life. You can window shop at cute boutiques, see the fishermen bringing in the fresh catch of the day, and smell the coffee beans roasting at one of SF‚Äôs fantastic cafes. 

The only downside of walking tours is that you‚Äôre limited by how much ground you can cover in a day. You can‚Äôt explore the entirety of San Francisco in a day, but you can get a really good understanding of one of our incredible neighborhoods in a day.
