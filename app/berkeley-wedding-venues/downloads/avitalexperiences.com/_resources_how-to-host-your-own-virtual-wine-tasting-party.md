#  How To Host Your Own Virtual Wine Tasting Party 

April 5, 2021 

Bring the best of wineries to your event with a virtual wine tasting party! Even if your goal is to simply have a good time, you and your guests will learn the nuances of wine and what they like (and don‚Äôt like!), all while spending time together. It‚Äôs much easier than it sounds, and we‚Äôve put together a guide on how to host a virtual wine tasting in just 5 steps. 

  1. Choose a platform
  2. Pick your wines 
  3. Create tasting cards
  4. Set the mood 
  5. Add some snacks 



## **[![choose-video-platform-for-virtual-wine-tasting](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/3/2021/04/pick-your-platform-virtual-wine-tasting-party.jpg)](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/3/2021/04/pick-your-platform-virtual-wine-tasting-party.jpg)  
Step 1. Choose a platform.**

While Zoom might have defined 2020 and become a standard, it isn‚Äôt the only option for your virtual wine tasting party. Microsoft Teams or Google Hangouts are other great choices, and even FaceTime or Discord will work well. Whatever platform you choose, make sure to give your guests instructions on how to download it, just in case.

## **[![pick-out-wines-and-theme](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/3/2021/04/choose-your-wines-for-virtual-wine-tasting-party.jpg)](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/3/2021/04/choose-your-wines-for-virtual-wine-tasting-party.jpg)  
Step 2. Pick your wines.**

Red or white? French or Chilean? In the world of wine, there‚Äôs a multitude of delicious options each offering their own flavor and history. You can travel through tasting, taking your guests around the world just by drinking Sauvignon Blanc. Or try your [hand at tasting and then _making_ your own fortified wine](https://avitaltours.com/virtual-wine-tasting/) for an even more unique experience with an expert.

## **[![make-card-to-record-notes-during-for-wine-tasting](data:image/svg+xml,%3Csvg%20xmlns='http://www.w3.org/2000/svg'%20viewBox='0%200%20700%20467'%3E%3C/svg%3E)![make-card-to-record-notes-during-for-wine-tasting](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/3/2021/04/create-a-tasting-card-virtual-wine-tasting-party.jpg)](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/3/2021/04/create-a-tasting-card-virtual-wine-tasting-party.jpg)  
Step 3. Create tasting cards.**

Use something as simple as a Google sheet for something more collaborative or [a template](https://www.pinterest.co.kr/pin/30469734950419662/) as a tasting card. This is a place for guests to record their impressions and rate the wines. Include information about each wine you‚Äôre tasting such as where it‚Äôs from, what year, and what flavor notes they should be picking up.

## **[![vineyard-background-for-virtual-wine-tasting-party](data:image/svg+xml,%3Csvg%20xmlns='http://www.w3.org/2000/svg'%20viewBox='0%200%20700%20467'%3E%3C/svg%3E)![vineyard-background-for-virtual-wine-tasting-party](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/3/2021/04/use-backgrounds-during-virtual-wine-tasting-party.jpg)](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/3/2021/04/use-backgrounds-during-virtual-wine-tasting-party.jpg)  
Step 4. Set the mood!**

A virtual wine tasting party needs the right vibes‚Äî that means music and backgrounds. Based on your wines, you can ask guests to use backgrounds of Italian or Australian vineyards, or the wine caves of Portugal. The wine region can also help with music choice. Spanish guitar for a virtual Spanish wine tasting? Count us in!

## **[![cheese-and-fruit-snack-options-for-virtual-party](data:image/svg+xml,%3Csvg%20xmlns='http://www.w3.org/2000/svg'%20viewBox='0%200%20700%20467'%3E%3C/svg%3E)![cheese-and-fruit-snack-options-for-virtual-party](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/3/2021/04/send-snack-options-virtual-wine-tasting-party.jpg)](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/3/2021/04/send-snack-options-virtual-wine-tasting-party.jpg)  
Step 5. Add some snacks.**

It‚Äôs not necessary, but what‚Äôs a good party without snacks? Turn your virtual wine tasting into a wine and cheese pairing by choosing some selections to snack on as you drink. Olives, salami, fruit, and even chocolate all taste great with wine! 

There you have it‚Äî everything you need to throw the best virtual wine tasting party. It doesn‚Äôt matter if you‚Äôre drinking red, white, or ros√©, you‚Äôre going to have a good time!
