#  San Francisco Tourist Spots That Locals Love 

May 1, 2017 

This 7X7 city boasts many gems to explore and taste. There‚Äôs a reason why locals stick through the rising rent prices and overcrowding. From the unique food, the beautiful views, to the quirky characters ‚Äì there‚Äôs never a dull moment in this melting pot. If you‚Äôre looking to hit all of the must-do‚Äôs and must-see‚Äôs while in the city, but still want a locals perspective, check out our guide to the San Francisco tourist spots that locals love.

## sip a beer at the park

[![Dolores Park - san francisco tourist spots that locals love](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/3/2017/03/Dolores-Park.jpg)](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/3/2017/03/Dolores-Park.jpg)

Ask a local what their plans are for a hot and sunny day in the city, and many will mention gathering with their friends at a park. We recommend grabbing a beer and heading to Dolores Park to soak up the sun. All aspects of San Francisco are pretty much encompassed in this park from the stunning skyline view, the outlandish characters, and the cannabis infused edibles. Some of our other favorites include Golden Gate Park and Great Meadow Park at Fort Mason. 

## try a mission style burrito 

[![el farolito - san francisco tourist spots that locals love](data:image/svg+xml,%3Csvg%20xmlns='http://www.w3.org/2000/svg'%20viewBox='0%200%20590%20590'%3E%3C/svg%3E)![el farolito - san francisco tourist spots that locals love](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/3/2017/03/el-farolito.jpg)](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/3/2017/03/el-farolito.jpg)

In California, one of the most popular cuisines is Mexican food. Specifically in San Francisco, we hold the hat for the Mission Style burrito which was created by [Taqueria La Cumbre](http://www.taquerialacumbre.com/). These delicious burritos can be distinguished by their larger size and inclusion of extra rice or other ingredients. We recommend heading over to [El Farolito](https://www.yelp.com/biz/el-farolito-san-francisco-2) or [La Taqueria](https://www.yelp.com/biz/la-taqueria-san-francisco-2) and ordering a super burrito. Though it is up for debate that these spots host the best burritos in town‚Ä¶

## enjoy a beautiful view of the bridge 

[![crissy fields - san francisco tourist spots that locals love](data:image/svg+xml,%3Csvg%20xmlns='http://www.w3.org/2000/svg'%20viewBox='0%200%20620%20390'%3E%3C/svg%3E)![crissy fields - san francisco tourist spots that locals love](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/3/2017/03/crissyfieldpicnicarea.jpg)](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/3/2017/03/crissyfieldpicnicarea.jpg)

The best perspective of the city‚Äôs iconic bridge is not while you‚Äôre on it, but rather from a distance. To experience one of the most beautiful sights this city has to offer, head over to Crissy Field. Here you can take in a stunning view of the bay accompanied by San Francisco‚Äôs number one must see, the Golden Gate Bridge.

## Visit a Bakery

[![tartine bakery - san francisco tourist spots that locals love](data:image/svg+xml,%3Csvg%20xmlns='http://www.w3.org/2000/svg'%20viewBox='0%200%203008%202000'%3E%3C/svg%3E)![tartine bakery - san francisco tourist spots that locals love](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/3/2017/03/tartine-bakery.jpg)](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/3/2017/03/tartine-bakery.jpg)

San Francisco hosts a plethora of amazing bakeries. Tartine, having won a James Beard award in 2008, is unarguably one of the top bakeries in the city. Travelers and locals alike flock to this award winning bakery curious of its delicious pastries and freshly made bread. Don‚Äôt let that long line turn you away though, it‚Äôs definitely worth the wait. Some other favorites include [Craftsman and Wolves](http://www.craftsman-wolves.com/), [b. patisserie](http://bpatisserie.com/), and the insta-worthy [Mr. Holmes Bakehouse](http://mrholmesbakehouse.com).

## take a stroll around the ferry building

[![Ferry Building - san francisco tourist spots that locals love](data:image/svg+xml,%3Csvg%20xmlns='http://www.w3.org/2000/svg'%20viewBox='0%200%201000%20465'%3E%3C/svg%3E)![Ferry Building - san francisco tourist spots that locals love](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/3/2017/03/FerryBuilding.jpg)](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/3/2017/03/FerryBuilding.jpg)

Not only is the Ferry Building an iconic landmark for travelers, but it‚Äôs also a go to for many locals when it comes to dining and supporting local food producers. Here you can have your own progressive meal sampling produce, grabbing cheese from [Cowgirl Creamery](https://www.cowgirlcreamery.com/), and indulging in [Acme Bread](http://www.acmebread.com/). Check out our [self guided Ferry Building walking tour](http://avitaltours.com/san-francisco/self-guided-ferry-building-walking-food-tour) too! If you happen to be in the city on a Tuesday, Thursday, or Saturday morning, the ferry building Farmers Market is a must, bustling with vendors selling locally grown produce and products. 

## visit a museum

[![sf mama - san francisco tourist spots that locals love](data:image/svg+xml,%3Csvg%20xmlns='http://www.w3.org/2000/svg'%20viewBox='0%200%201200%20630'%3E%3C/svg%3E)![sf mama - san francisco tourist spots that locals love](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/3/2017/03/sf-moma.jpg)](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/3/2017/03/sf-moma.jpg)

Into the arts? Check out the SFMOMA for a profusion of creative exhibits featuring modern and contemporary art. The museum just reopened after 3 years of renovations and is filled with many pieces that will remind you of this beautiful city. If you start to get hungry, stumble into [In Situ](http://insitu.sfmoma.org/), the museums Michelin three-star restaurant. The [Museum of African Diaspora](https://www.moadsf.org/) and the [Mus√©e M√©canique](http://www.museemechanique.org/) are some other great and unique museums to check out. 

## taste san francisco‚Äôs favorite ice cream

[![bi rite creamery - san francisco tourist spots that locals love](data:image/svg+xml,%3Csvg%20xmlns='http://www.w3.org/2000/svg'%20viewBox='0%200%20700%20499'%3E%3C/svg%3E)![bi rite creamery - san francisco tourist spots that locals love](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/3/2017/03/bi-rite-creamery.jpg)](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/3/2017/03/bi-rite-creamery.jpg)

Just look for the long line on the corner of Dolores and 18th, and you‚Äôll know you‚Äôre at the right spot. Bi-Rite Creamery serves up homemade organic ice cream made by hand in small batches. Try one of their many unique flavors such as salted caramel or honey lavender with sonoma honey. If you‚Äôre more of the soft-serve type of person, skip the line and head to the window next door.

## enjoy the sunset over glistening waters

[![sutro baths - san francisco tourist spots that locals love](data:image/svg+xml,%3Csvg%20xmlns='http://www.w3.org/2000/svg'%20viewBox='0%200%201024%20683'%3E%3C/svg%3E)![sutro baths - san francisco tourist spots that locals love](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/3/2017/03/sutro-baths.jpg)](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/3/2017/03/sutro-baths.jpg)

One of our favorite spots to watch the sun set is at the top of the stairs at Sutro Baths. Head over to the westside coastline of the city and discover modern ruins of a bath facility. It can get chilly near the water, so make sure to bring a jacket or blanket!

## explore the culinary side of a famous neighborhood

[![avital tours - san francisco tourist spots that locals love](data:image/svg+xml,%3Csvg%20xmlns='http://www.w3.org/2000/svg'%20viewBox='0%200%20500%20333'%3E%3C/svg%3E)![avital tours - san francisco tourist spots that locals love](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/3/2017/03/avital-tours.jpg)](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/3/2017/03/avital-tours.jpg)

Looking to eat your way through North Beach like a local? Go on a 4 course progressive meal with Avital Tours. On these culinary experiences you will go behind the scenes to meet the chefs and food curators, as well as hear stories you won‚Äôt usually find online. This is a great activity to experience a famous San Francisco neighborhood from a locals perspective. 

What other commodities do locals love about San Francisco? Take a day trip to Napa or Sonoma, ride a bike through Golden Gate Park, celebrate Sunday with brunch and mimosas, the list of San Francisco tourist spots that locals love goes on and on. Of course there are many more elements that make this city great, but hopefully these gems will begin to make you fall in love with the city by the bay too.
