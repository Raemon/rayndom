#  Fun Things To Do With Your Parents in Flatiron 

November 13, 2018 

After a long day of work, playing tour guide is the last thing on your mind. For those who work in the Flatiron district, knowing a good happy hour spot may not satisfy your parents‚Äô touristy needs. So after they‚Äôve had their fill of the Flatiron Building, here are fun things to do with your parents in Flatiron. 

## **Catch a comedy show.**

[![Fun Things To Do With Your Parents in Flatiron-go see a comedy show](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/5/2018/11/Fun-Things-To-Do-With-Your-Parents-in-Flatiron-go-see-a-comedy-show.jpg)](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/5/2018/11/Fun-Things-To-Do-With-Your-Parents-in-Flatiron-go-see-a-comedy-show.jpg)

When you‚Äôre finished checking out the first location of Shake Shack and roaming through Madison Square Park, head over to the [Gotham Comedy Club](http://gothamcomedyclub.com) on West 23rd for shows from upcoming and established comedians. Let the professionals handle the entertainment as you enjoy a show with the fam! There shows almost every day for a pretty reasonable price. Just be aware that most shows start a bit later and have a 2-drink minimum per person. 

## **Eat at one of the many awesome restaurants.**

[![A full table on a Flatiron Food Tour. Fun Things To Do With Your Parents in Flatiron](data:image/svg+xml,%3Csvg%20xmlns='http://www.w3.org/2000/svg'%20viewBox='0%200%20800%20533'%3E%3C/svg%3E)![A full table on a Flatiron Food Tour. Fun Things To Do With Your Parents in Flatiron](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/5/2018/06/Dacia-Pierson.-Flatiron-Food-Tour.-Guests-serving-and-eating-at-Junoon.jpeg)](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/5/2018/06/Dacia-Pierson.-Flatiron-Food-Tour.-Guests-serving-and-eating-at-Junoon.jpeg)

If you‚Äôve been dying to eat at [Eleven Madison Park](https://www.elevenmadisonpark.com), now‚Äôs your chance! Tell your parents to break out the credit card! Just kidding, though this esteemed restaurant should totally be on your bucket list, if it isn‚Äôt already. However, there are plenty of places in Flatiron that just as worthy for a visit, and half the price. For contemporary Korean barbeque, [Cote](https://www.cotenyc.com) is a great option. Indian with a modern twist? Check out [Pondicheri](https://www.pondicheri.com). Comfort food? Roam through [Eataly](https://www.eataly.com) and then dine on the roof. From fine dining to affordable, Flatiron has some great options. Food is something we consider really fun, so you know we had to include it on our ‚Äúfun things to do with your parents in Flatiron‚Äù list. 

## **Play some ping-pong and have a drink.**

[![Fun Things To Do With Your Parents in Flatiron=go to spin and play ping pong](data:image/svg+xml,%3Csvg%20xmlns='http://www.w3.org/2000/svg'%20viewBox='0%200%20640%20427'%3E%3C/svg%3E)![Fun Things To Do With Your Parents in Flatiron=go to spin and play ping pong](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/5/2018/11/Fun-Things-To-Do-With-Your-Parents-in-Flatirongo-to-spin-and-play-ping-pong.jpg)](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/5/2018/11/Fun-Things-To-Do-With-Your-Parents-in-Flatirongo-to-spin-and-play-ping-pong.jpg)

Yes, this is a real suggestion. At [Spin](https://wearespin.com/location/new-york/) in Flatiron, you‚Äôre right by Madison Square Park and just a hair away from the Flatiron Building. Being a tour guide for your parents is tough, so why not relax a little with a friendly game of ping pong? If your parents like games and a drink or two, Spin is the ping pong bar of your dreams. If your parents want to meet your friends, coworkers, and/or significant other, this is also a good spot for that. In addition to drinks, there‚Äôs also food on the menu in case your parents are getting a little hangry (or if you are, for that matter.)

## **Stroll around the Rizzoli Bookstore.**

[![GO check out some books at Rizzoli. Fun Things To Do With Your Parents in Flatiron](data:image/svg+xml,%3Csvg%20xmlns='http://www.w3.org/2000/svg'%20viewBox='0%200%20640%20480'%3E%3C/svg%3E)![GO check out some books at Rizzoli. Fun Things To Do With Your Parents in Flatiron](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/5/2018/11/1383429716_ab057f8666_z.jpg)](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/5/2018/11/1383429716_ab057f8666_z.jpg)

[Rizzoli Bookstore](https://www.rizzolibookstore.com/welcome-most-beautiful-bookstore-new-york) may have only moved to Flatiron in 2015, but it‚Äôs got a lot of history. The appearance may have changed, but the commitment to great service and books has not. Enjoy your coffee and snack before going to Rizzoli Bookstore, as you won‚Äôt find a cafe in here. But what you will find is stacks of books about design, architecture, fashion, art and more in an elegant space. Read up on the history to impress your parents with before heading over there and earn your tour guide stripes.
