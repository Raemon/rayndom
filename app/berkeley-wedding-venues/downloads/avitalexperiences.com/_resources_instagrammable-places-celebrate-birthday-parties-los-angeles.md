## 1\. Enjoy Lush Gardens At The Huntington

The Huntington offers a rich art collection, a beautiful botanical garden, and multiple venues for a perfect place to celebrate birthday parties of any size. [The Huntington](https://www.huntington-hospitality.com/venues) is great for milestone birthdays as the venues are luxe and upscale. Choose an an outdoor garden surrounded by verdant trees, a sun-drenched courtyard, or gorgeous terrace. But if you‚Äôre looking for something more on the casual side, join one of their Summer Evening Strolls with friends, walk around the Japanese Botanical Garden, or pose in the museum. The birthday pictures are going to be art themselves!

[![republique-dinner-or-brunch-birthday-party](data:image/svg+xml,%3Csvg%20xmlns='http://www.w3.org/2000/svg'%20viewBox='0%200%20700%20467'%3E%3C/svg%3E)![republique-dinner-or-brunch-birthday-party](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/2/2021/07/have-a-french-birthday-party-in-los-angeles.jpg)](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/2/2021/07/have-a-french-birthday-party-in-los-angeles.jpg)Image via R√©pulique

The French California cuisine or the cafe and bakery will instantly transport you to some of the best dining in France. And it‚Äôs incredibly Instagrammable to boot! [R√©publique‚Äôs](https://republiquela.com/private-dining/) reputation exists for a reason, so don‚Äôt think it‚Äôs all views‚Äî the food is excellent. You can opt to book a private dining experience at their romantic alcove, enjoy a cozy enclave in the bakery, or watch the action going on in the open kitchen. The pictures will be great, but the memories will be even better.

[![rooftop-bar-at-upstairs-ace-hotel](data:image/svg+xml,%3Csvg%20xmlns='http://www.w3.org/2000/svg'%20viewBox='0%200%20700%20467'%3E%3C/svg%3E)![rooftop-bar-at-upstairs-ace-hotel](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/2/2021/07/cool-rooftop-bar-places-to-celebrate-birthday.jpg)](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/2/2021/07/cool-rooftop-bar-places-to-celebrate-birthday.jpg)Image via Upstairs Ace Hotel Downtown Los Angeles

## 3\. Get Great Views At Upstairs

Vibes are key if you‚Äôre looking for Instagrammable places to celebrate birthday parties. [Upstairs at Ace Hotel Downtown Los Angeles](https://www.acehotel.com/losangeles/food-and-drink/upstairs/fireplace/) is the kind of rooftop bar that brings the vibes. With its chic, bespoke design, open space, and great views, you‚Äôll be celebrating your birthday in style. Enjoy an all day menu with appetizers and tacos, or head over for a birthday brunch. [![places-to-celebrate-birthday-party-at-venice-canals](data:image/svg+xml,%3Csvg%20xmlns='http://www.w3.org/2000/svg'%20viewBox='0%200%20700%20467'%3E%3C/svg%3E)![places-to-celebrate-birthday-party-at-venice-canals](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/2/2021/07/venice-canals-best-places-to-celebrate-birthday.jpg)](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/2/2021/07/venice-canals-best-places-to-celebrate-birthday.jpg)Image via California Through My Lens

## 4\. Celebrate Near The Venice Canals

Another LA destination that brings you to Europe without having to get on a plane. [The Venice Canals](https://californiathroughmylens.com/venice-canals-near-venice-beach) might not be in Venice, Italy but they still bring all the charm. Stop by first for a stroll and a photoshoot with your friends, and then head to a restaurant nearby for a fun dinner. This dreamy hidden gem will look great in the golden glow of evening sun. Add in your loved ones? Picture perfect.

[![graffiti-tour-on-birthday](data:image/svg+xml,%3Csvg%20xmlns='http://www.w3.org/2000/svg'%20viewBox='0%200%20700%20467'%3E%3C/svg%3E)![graffiti-tour-on-birthday](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/2/2021/07/street-art-tour-for-la-birthday.jpg)](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/2/2021/07/street-art-tour-for-la-birthday.jpg)Image via LA Art Tour

## 5\. Take Pics In Front Of The Coolest Street Art

Whether you live in LA or you‚Äôre visiting, it‚Äôs always fun to play tourist to learn and see something new. A street art tour will bring you to some of the coolest, colorful graffiti with great stories behind them. [Join a street art tour](https://laarttours.com/graffititour/) for your birthday to learn about the culture, history, and techniques behind urban art. If you‚Äôre looking for an Instagram worthy place to celebrate birthday festivities, a street art tour couldn‚Äôt be better. Get ready for vibrant art from local and global artists.

[![birthday-beach-party-at-point-dume](data:image/svg+xml,%3Csvg%20xmlns='http://www.w3.org/2000/svg'%20viewBox='0%200%20700%20467'%3E%3C/svg%3E)![birthday-beach-party-at-point-dume](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/2/2021/07/beach-birthday-party-point-dume.jpg)](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/2/2021/07/beach-birthday-party-point-dume.jpg)Image via Department of Beaches & Harbors

## 6\. Head To Point Dume For A Day In The Sun

There‚Äôs no shortage of great beaches and trails in and near Los Angeles. [Point Dume](https://beaches.lacounty.gov/point-dume-beach/) is one of the most picturesque places to celebrate birthday parties. The drive down alone is enough to make you want to stop at every vantage point and take pictures. A beach birthday picnic is easy to plan, fun, and of course, a lovely location. And for the after party, head to a restaurant or bar nearby. It‚Äôs a day well-spent and a birthday party worth taking lots of pictures of!

## 7\. Lean Into Nostalgia At Player One Arcade

[Player One](https://www.playeronearcadebar.com/) gives us serious 80s vibes, from the games to the aesthetics. That makes it the perfect place to throw an 80s birthday party, complete with neon colors and big hair. Or pretend you‚Äôre in Hawkins with a Stranger Things theme. Regardless of the theme you choose, you‚Äôll have fun playing a wide variety of arcade games and even a few rare ones. Enjoy wine, beer, and even Lunchables. Yes, Lunchables. You‚Äôll get a high score for this birthday party.

Why keep your birthday to one day when you can celebrate the whole weekend? LA has plenty of places to celebrate birthday parties that doesn‚Äôt limit the fun to just one day.
