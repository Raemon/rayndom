#  Where to Get Caffeine Before Your North Beach Food Tour 

October 4, 2017 

If you need a coffee break before joining our food tour, North Beach is a perfect place to stop. Outdoor cafes are abundant in the neighborhood and we encourage sitting down and getting some rest before spending three hours eating and drinking your way around the neighborhood. Here‚Äôs where to get caffeine before your North Beach food tour.

## Hole in the Wall Coffee

[![hole in the wall coffee: Where to Get Caffeine Before Your North Beach Food Tour](data:image/svg+xml,%3Csvg%20xmlns='http://www.w3.org/2000/svg'%20viewBox='0%200%20640%20500'%3E%3C/svg%3E)![hole in the wall coffee: Where to Get Caffeine Before Your North Beach Food Tour](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/3/2017/09/7417277818_24db95a92e_z.jpg)](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/3/2017/09/7417277818_24db95a92e_z.jpg)

The people behind Hole in the Wall call themselves coffee raconteurs, so if you want to talk coffee, these are your people. The menu is small, simple, and skips out on a lot of the fluff. The offerings have to be straightforward, as Hole in the Wall is just the size you imagine it to be.

_524 Union St,__San Francisco, CA 94133  
__[www.hitwcoffee.com](https://hitwcoffee.com/)_

## Caffe Trieste[![6629516493_f2111bba66_z](data:image/svg+xml,%3Csvg%20xmlns='http://www.w3.org/2000/svg'%20viewBox='0%200%20638%20425'%3E%3C/svg%3E)![6629516493_f2111bba66_z](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/3/2017/09/6629516493_f2111bba66_z.jpg)](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/3/2017/09/6629516493_f2111bba66_z.jpg)

Fans of classic movies will be excited to learn that Francis Ford Coppola wrote much of the screenplay for  _The Godfather_ inside of Caffe Trieste. The walls don‚Äôt talk, but the baristas do, so ask them to tell you about some of the Beat movement histories here, including visits from Jack Kerouac, and Allen Ginsberg. Sit down with your cappuccino and see if any creative inspiration strikes.

_601 Vallejo Street,__San Francisco, CA 94133  
_[_www.caffetrieste.com_](http://www.caffetrieste.com/)

## Reveille Coffee Co.

[![Reveille Coffee: Where to Get Caffeine Before Your North Beach Food Tour](data:image/svg+xml,%3Csvg%20xmlns='http://www.w3.org/2000/svg'%20viewBox='0%200%20640%20396'%3E%3C/svg%3E)![Reveille Coffee: Where to Get Caffeine Before Your North Beach Food Tour](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/3/2017/09/12592779443_189b6bc00d_z.jpg)](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/3/2017/09/12592779443_189b6bc00d_z.jpg)

Reveille is situated on a perfect corner for looking out on North Beach‚Äôs famous Zoetrope building and the best bookstore in the neighborhood-City Lights. There are places to sit inside the bright cafe, but you can also sit outside and watch North Beach in action.

_200 Columbus Ave,__San Francisco, CA 94133  
__[www.reveillecoffee.com](http://www.reveillecoffee.com/)_

## Mario‚Äôs Bohemian Cigar Store

[![Marios is Where to Get Caffeine Before Your North Beach Food Tour](data:image/svg+xml,%3Csvg%20xmlns='http://www.w3.org/2000/svg'%20viewBox='0%200%20640%20427'%3E%3C/svg%3E)![Marios is Where to Get Caffeine Before Your North Beach Food Tour](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/3/2017/09/6102183373_d8e8ed6508_z.jpg)](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/3/2017/09/6102183373_d8e8ed6508_z.jpg)

Mario‚Äôs has been opened since 1971 and is family owned, right in the center of North Beach. Cigars aren‚Äôt for sale anymore, but food like their famous sandwiches and yes, coffee, are. Expect to see a lot of regular locals at Mario‚Äôs, which should vouch for the quality. Sit inside and enjoy the unique scene. The staff won‚Äôt hurry you and encourages patrons to treat Mario‚Äôs like their living room.

_566 Columbus Ave,__San Francisco, CA 94133  
_[_Yelp Page_](https://www.yelp.com/biz/marios-bohemian-cigar-store-cafe-san-francisco)
