Is this a full meal?

What if we have dietary or alcohol restrictions? 

It‚Äôs okay! We can accommodate dietary restrictions and requests for non-alcoholic substitute beverages. We‚Äôll ask for your requests and restrictions during booking. 

Can we order drinks once we get to the restaurants? 

We strongly discourage it. Everything in your pre-booked tour is ordered and prepared in advance to be ready on arrival at each restaurant. Is this a ‚Äúfood tour?‚Äù

Do we pick the restaurants? 

That‚Äôs our job! We‚Äôve spent years testing out all of the city‚Äôs awesome restaurants, finding those quirky characters, and building a culinary experience with story and purpose (that‚Äôs also delicious!) Trust us, that‚Äôs why you came here.

We‚Äôll send your confirmed meeting address prior to your tour date. How much walking is there? 

Our Downtown LA Tour is a flat route with about 1 ¬Ω miles of walking.

Our public tours sell out fast so we recommend purchasing your tickets at least 2 weeks in advance. For private tours, we also require a 2 week lead time.
