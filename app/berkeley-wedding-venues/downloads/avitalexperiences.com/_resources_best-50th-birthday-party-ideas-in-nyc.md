#  The Best 50th Birthday Party Ideas In NYC 

December 1, 2019 

If you‚Äôre thinking about doing it up for the big 5-0, NYC has you covered. Choose from classy dinners with beautiful views, live music, boat rides, and much more. For the best 50th birthday party ideas in NYC, we‚Äôve got a list that‚Äôs got something for everyone. 

## **Book a cruise for a 50th birthday party on the water**

[![boat cruise makes for one of the best 50th birthday party ideas in nyc](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/5/2019/11/best-50th-birthday-party-ideas-in-nyc-boat-tour-overlooking-freedom-tower-under-blue-sky-e1573278882718.jpeg)](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/5/2019/11/best-50th-birthday-party-ideas-in-nyc-boat-tour-overlooking-freedom-tower-under-blue-sky-e1573278882718.jpeg)

A birthday cruise around New York City will deliver great views, child-friendly entertainment, and a unique venue for a 50th birthday party. Book a dinner cruise for a group or opt to rent the entire boat for your celebration. Many companies have theme nights and can accommodate group reservations. [Hornblower](https://www.hornblower.com/new-york/birthday-party-cruise/), for example, has a Sunday Jazz Brunch Cruise, among other events. For private events, [Empire Cruises](https://empirecruises.com/packages/birthday-cruises/) has various packages and offers experienced staff, catering, and a DJ if you need one.

## **Go glamping on Governors Island**

[![best 50th birthday party ideas in nyc includes camping in style](data:image/svg+xml,%3Csvg%20xmlns='http://www.w3.org/2000/svg'%20viewBox='0%200%20700%20466'%3E%3C/svg%3E)![best 50th birthday party ideas in nyc includes camping in style](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/5/2019/11/best-50th-birthday-party-ideas-in-nyc-woman-drinking-coffee-in-glamping-tent-e1573278973400.jpeg)](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/5/2019/11/best-50th-birthday-party-ideas-in-nyc-woman-drinking-coffee-in-glamping-tent-e1573278973400.jpeg)

Out of all the best 50th birthday party ideas in NYC, a vacation might just be the most ideal. But we get it ‚Äî it‚Äôs not always possible to go away for your birthday. That‚Äôs why we like the idea of glamping on Governors Island with [Collective Retreats](https://www.collectiveretreats.com/retreat/collective-governors-island/). You can stay for a night or for the whole weekend (our recommendation, personally) and celebrate with Manhattan as your backdrop. It‚Äôs perfect for an intimate getaway with your partner or a unique outing with family and friends. Collective Retreats offers lots of activities like sightseeing and boat tours, in addition to gourmet meals and cocktails. You won‚Äôt have to go far to feel like you got away.

## **Throw a birthday bash at a billiards room**

[![playing pool in fancy billiards hall for best 50th birthday party ideas in nyc](data:image/svg+xml,%3Csvg%20xmlns='http://www.w3.org/2000/svg'%20viewBox='0%200%20700%20467'%3E%3C/svg%3E)![playing pool in fancy billiards hall for best 50th birthday party ideas in nyc](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/5/2019/11/best-50th-birthday-party-ideas-in-nyc-person-playing-billiards-with-bokeh-background-e1573279066848.jpeg)](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/5/2019/11/best-50th-birthday-party-ideas-in-nyc-person-playing-billiards-with-bokeh-background-e1573279066848.jpeg)

Playing pool, darts, and ping pong with your friends and family is one way to have a memorable 50th birthday party in NYC. At a billiards room, your guests can opt to play games or stand by and chat. It‚Äôs easy entertainment, and you don‚Äôt have to clean up! [Amsterdam Billiards](https://amsterdambilliards.com/) offers catered parties, and is located near the East Village if you feel like keeping the party going after you‚Äôve had your fill of games. If you want to add some extra flair, consider asking your guests to wear cocktail attire or go with a theme like the ‚ÄúRoaring ‚Äò20s.‚Äù It‚Äôs a tribute to old New York ‚Äî a bit of glamour and gangster, if you will.

## **Spend a day eating everything**

[![people eating at luxury food tour celebrate best 50th birthday party ideas in nyc](data:image/svg+xml,%3Csvg%20xmlns='http://www.w3.org/2000/svg'%20viewBox='0%200%201200%20800'%3E%3C/svg%3E)![people eating at luxury food tour celebrate best 50th birthday party ideas in nyc](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/5/2019/11/best-50th-birthday-party-ideas-in-nyc-flatiron-food-tour-guests-serving-and-eating-at-junoon-1-e1573280184247.jpg)](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/5/2019/11/best-50th-birthday-party-ideas-in-nyc-flatiron-food-tour-guests-serving-and-eating-at-junoon-1-e1573280184247.jpg)

Calories don‚Äôt count on your birthday, right? And New York has no shortage of restaurants and bars to indulge in. However, finding a restaurant that takes reservations, is big enough for your group, AND is within your budget can be a challenge, to say the least. To take the stress out of your birthday, book a [food tour](http://avitaltours.com/nyc-food-tours/). All the details are planned for you, from where to eat and what to order. If you decide to make it a [private event](http://avitaltours.com/nyc-food-tours/private-events/nyc-private-food-tours/), you can also book hands-on experiences, like cocktail making to customize the experience. A 50th birthday party in NYC never felt so easy.

## **Head to the theater or opera, then dine at iconic NYC restaurants**

[![classy night out at the opera for best 50th birthday party ideas in nyc](data:image/svg+xml,%3Csvg%20xmlns='http://www.w3.org/2000/svg'%20viewBox='0%200%20700%20473'%3E%3C/svg%3E)![classy night out at the opera for best 50th birthday party ideas in nyc](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/5/2019/11/best-50th-birthday-party-ideas-in-nyc-crowd-standing-and-clapping-in-growded-theater-e1573279128493.jpeg)](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/5/2019/11/best-50th-birthday-party-ideas-in-nyc-crowd-standing-and-clapping-in-growded-theater-e1573279128493.jpeg)

If you‚Äôre the type that loves dinner and a show, this option is for you. New York has plenty of good theater, so splurge on tickets or get glammed up and go to the opera. Near the theater district you‚Äôll find restaurants like [Keens](http://www.keens.com/), known for steaks and mutton since 1885, and [Feinstein‚Äôs/54 Below](https://54below.com/private-events/), described as a ‚Äú1920s New York supper club.‚Äù The best part is the option for live music if you‚Äôre hosting a private event. You can have a jazz trio, 1970s disco band, pianist, and more! With talent like this, you may just want to skip the show and head straight to Feinstein‚Äôs/54 Below instead. 

## CROSS OFF A BUCKET LIST ITEM

[![helicopter-ride-50th-birthday-idea-nyc](data:image/svg+xml,%3Csvg%20xmlns='http://www.w3.org/2000/svg'%20viewBox='0%200%20700%20467'%3E%3C/svg%3E)![helicopter-ride-50th-birthday-idea-nyc](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/5/2019/12/51.jpg)](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/5/2019/12/51.jpg)

Time to whip out the bucket list that you‚Äôve spent years creating and cross one off! Drive your dream luxury car for the weekend, go parachuting with your favourite person or maybe take a [helicopter ride](https://www.virginexperiencegifts.com/new-york/ultimate-helicopter-tour) around the city! Tour Manhattan‚Äôs iconic skyline from approximately 1000 feet in the air. See the Empire State Building, Central Park or Lady Liberty like you‚Äôve never seen before. Bonus: if you have a fear of heights, your 50th is also the perfect time to get over a fear!

## TREAT YOURSELF TO A SPA DAY

[![spa-day-for-50th-birthday-idea-in-nyc](data:image/svg+xml,%3Csvg%20xmlns='http://www.w3.org/2000/svg'%20viewBox='0%200%20700%20467'%3E%3C/svg%3E)![spa-day-for-50th-birthday-idea-in-nyc](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/5/2019/12/52.jpg)](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/5/2019/12/52.jpg)

This one needs no explanation because plain and simple ‚Äì you deserve it! The big 5-0 is a monumental birthday, and you have earned yourself a day of relaxation. Whether you choose to go before or after a night of festivities ‚Äì heck, even if this is all you do for your day ‚Äì you‚Äôre guaranteed to start your new decade on a blissful note. Now the real question is, what kind of spa day are you looking for? Because in New York, there is an endless array of options for your day of Zen. Were you looking for a traditional massage and facial? Or are you thinking going to go more all out with a day doing water therapy. Check out [this list](https://www.harpersbazaar.com/culture/travel-dining/g12141458/best-spa-nyc/) to find a spa for your perfect day.

## GO BACK IN TIME WITH A ROLLER DISCO

[![roller-skates-for-50th-birthday-idea-in-nyc](data:image/svg+xml,%3Csvg%20xmlns='http://www.w3.org/2000/svg'%20viewBox='0%200%20700%20467'%3E%3C/svg%3E)![roller-skates-for-50th-birthday-idea-in-nyc](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/5/2019/12/53.jpg)](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/5/2019/12/53.jpg)

Let‚Äôs go back to the beginning, with a roller disco! Now you may think, you only have one or two options when it comes to roller skating but think again, my friend. Roller skating is increasing in popularity with roller rinks popping up all over the place. The latest and greatest has you skating on a tarmac at JFK. Though [Runway Rink](https://www.twahotel.com/runway-rink) is more 50s-60s inspired, why not dress up for a decade you weren‚Äôt even around for? If this era isn‚Äôt really your thing, you can also check our [Pier 2 Roller Rink](https://www.brooklynbridgeskating.com/) in Brooklyn for a 1980s throwback skate or boy band night.

## FEEL ON TOP OF THE WORLD AT A ROOFTOP BAR

[![rooftop-bar-in-nyc-for-50th-birthday-idea](data:image/svg+xml,%3Csvg%20xmlns='http://www.w3.org/2000/svg'%20viewBox='0%200%20700%20467'%3E%3C/svg%3E)![rooftop-bar-in-nyc-for-50th-birthday-idea](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/5/2019/12/54.jpg)](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/5/2019/12/54.jpg)

Going to a rooftop bar in NYC is a rite of passage, whether you‚Äôre a tourist or a New York native. Spend your 50th in style by sipping on classy craft cocktails cocktails while taking in 360-degree views of the city. My two recommendations would be [Westlight](http://www.thewilliamvale.com/dining/westlight/) in Greenpoint on top of The William Vale Hotel or [The Crown](https://www.jdvhotels.com/hotels/new-york/nyc/hotel-50-bowery-nyc/restaurants/the-crown) in Chinatown atop Hotel 50 Bowery. These two rooftop bars remind you how beautiful the NYC skyline is and can make you fall in love with the city all over again.
