## **What is an NYC Walking Food Tour?**

First things first, what is a NYC walking food tour? An NYC walking food tour, clearly, takes place within New York City. A walking[ food tour](https://avitalexperiences.com/food-tours/) is a tour that uses walking as the form of transport (you see a city the best this way!). And, you guessed it, the tour centers around the food in the particular location you are walking in. This can include going to restaurants, bakeries, bars, kitchens, food trucks, and more. The tour guide or company you sign up with will determine the food, the setting, and the vibe.

Check out our hand selected list of NYC walking food tours below ‚Äì we‚Äôre pretty sure you‚Äôre going to find something you‚Äôre excited about.

## **1\. Experience Punk and Multicultural Roots in this East Village Food Tour**

![avital walking with tour guests during best nyc walking food tours ](data:image/svg+xml,%3Csvg%20xmlns='http://www.w3.org/2000/svg'%20viewBox='0%200%201024%20683'%3E%3C/svg%3E)![avital walking with tour guests during best nyc walking food tours ](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/07/nyc-scavenger-hunt-stop-east-village-ale-house-1024x683.jpg)

The East Village neighborhood in Manhattan is known as being one of the centers of the American punk scene and a neighborhood that has supported artists for over half a century. This walking tour combines amazing food with engaging storytelling. You‚Äôll be taken to four different restaurants to enjoy two appetizers, a main, and a dessert. In between each restaurant you‚Äôll walk around the neighborhood and learn some amazing history and stories of the neighborhood from your experienced tour guide. Don‚Äôt forget to upgrade to enjoy an alcohol pairing with each stop!

**Cost:** Starting at $99 per person  
**Where to sign up:** Book this [East Village Food Tour](https://avitalexperiences.com/experiences/new-york-city-east-village-food-tour/) with Avital Experiences

[![woman-walking-the-highline-on-walking-food-tour](data:image/svg+xml,%3Csvg%20xmlns='http://www.w3.org/2000/svg'%20viewBox='0%200%20700%20467'%3E%3C/svg%3E)![woman-walking-the-highline-on-walking-food-tour](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/5/2022/10/nyc-walking-food-tour-highline.jpg)](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/5/2022/10/nyc-walking-food-tour-highline.jpg)

That was a mouthful! This walking tour takes about 3 hours and tours you through a particular part of Manhattan that includes the High Line, Hudson Yards, and the iconic Chelsea Market. You‚Äôll sample savory and sweet treats from at least 5 different establishments.

**Cost:** $68 per adult, $58 per child, children under 3 are free  
**Where to sign up:** Book this [Chelsea Market Food Tour](https://www.likealocaltours.com/manhattan-tours/chelsea-market-food-history-tour/) with Like a Local Tours

## **3\. Eat Around the World in One Neighborhood: Hell‚Äôs Kitchen**

[![meat-shop-on-walking-food-tour](data:image/svg+xml,%3Csvg%20xmlns='http://www.w3.org/2000/svg'%20viewBox='0%200%20700%20467'%3E%3C/svg%3E)![meat-shop-on-walking-food-tour](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/5/2022/10/nyc-walking-food-tour-hells-kitchen.jpg)](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/5/2022/10/nyc-walking-food-tour-hells-kitchen.jpg)

This tour focuses on the Hell‚Äôs Kitchen neighborhood in Manhattan. Hell‚Äôs Kitchen has changed a lot over the years. The neighborhood is known for its gritty past (showcased in the musical West Side Story), but as it became a more affluent district, a hip and modern restaurant scene was born. On this tour you‚Äôll walk around this iconic neighborhood while stopping at some delicious, institutional and new eateries. Your guide will educate you on the area‚Äôs immigrant history, how Hell‚Äôs Kitchen got its name, and more as you taste foods from restaurants offering Caribbean, Chinese, French, German, Ethiopian, Mexican, Turkish, and Indian foods. Taste a trip around the world in one neighborhood!

**Cost:** $99 per person  
**Where to sign up:** Book this [Hell‚Äôs Kitchen tour](https://www.getyourguide.com/new-york-city-l59/new-york-hell-s-kitchen-food-tour-t43105/?partner=true) with GetYourGuide

![dramatic lighting on restaurant table during michelin food tour](data:image/svg+xml,%3Csvg%20xmlns='http://www.w3.org/2000/svg'%20viewBox='0%200%20900%20600'%3E%3C/svg%3E)![dramatic lighting on restaurant table during michelin food tour](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/08/dramatic-lighting-on-restaurant-table-during-michelin-food-tour-in-nyc.jpg)

This walking food tour takes you to some of New York‚Äôs hottest restaurants, including Michelin-starred establishments. You‚Äôll be taken to three different restaurants for an appetizer, entree, and dessert while you are led by an experienced tour guide. And what‚Äôs an NYC walking food tour without walking? ## **5\. Create Your Own NYC Walking Food Tour**

[![people-hanging-on-wall-during-food-tour](data:image/svg+xml,%3Csvg%20xmlns='http://www.w3.org/2000/svg'%20viewBox='0%200%20700%20467'%3E%3C/svg%3E)![people-hanging-on-wall-during-food-tour](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/5/2022/10/self-guided-nyc-walking-food-tour.jpg)](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/5/2022/10/self-guided-nyc-walking-food-tour.jpg)

Maybe in your world, the best NYC walking food tours are a bit more DIY? There‚Äôs no reason why you can‚Äôt create your own! ## **6\. Tour and Eat in NYC‚Äôs Most Picturesque Neighborhood: Greenwich Village**

[![restaurant-on-nyc-food-tour](data:image/svg+xml,%3Csvg%20xmlns='http://www.w3.org/2000/svg'%20viewBox='0%200%20700%20467'%3E%3C/svg%3E)![restaurant-on-nyc-food-tour](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/5/2022/10/nyc-walking-food-tour-greenwich-village.jpg)](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/5/2022/10/nyc-walking-food-tour-greenwich-village.jpg)

This iconic neighborhood, known for the center of the counterculture movement in New York in the 1960s, is one of the most expensive neighborhoods to live in today. This neighborhood has lived many lives and has been the keeper of many stories. It also holds some of the best restaurants in the city. This NYC food walking tour will focus on classic New York dishes like the quintessential bagel with shmear, the perfect slice of New York pizza, and among other things, the tour always surprises you with a secret dish. How tantalizing ‚Äì we love a surprise!

**Where to sign up:** Book your [Greenwich Village Food Tour ](https://www.secretfoodtours.com/new-york/food-tours-new-york/)with Secret Food Tours

## **7\. Where Two Neighborhoods Meet: The Chinatown and Little Italy Food Fest**

[![little-italy-on-walking-nyc-food-tour](data:image/svg+xml,%3Csvg%20xmlns='http://www.w3.org/2000/svg'%20viewBox='0%200%20700%20467'%3E%3C/svg%3E)![little-italy-on-walking-nyc-food-tour](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/5/2022/10/nyc-walking-food-tour-little-italy.jpg)](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/5/2022/10/nyc-walking-food-tour-little-italy.jpg)

Over the three-hour tour, you‚Äôll try a classic Chinese pastry, an authentic Chinese dumpling, and others while visiting family-owned establishments that have been around for over 100 years. **Cost:** $125 per person  
**Where to sign up:** Book this [Chinatown and Little Italy Food Tour](https://www.viator.com/tours/New-York-City/Chinatown-and-Little-Italy-Food-Fest/d687-22264P1) with Viator
