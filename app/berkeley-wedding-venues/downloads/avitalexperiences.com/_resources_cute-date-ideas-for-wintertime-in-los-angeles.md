[Cinespia](http://cinespia.org/), the organization behind the movies at Hollywood Forever Cemetery, does a winter series of favorite films. For the rare chilly days Los Angeles gets, we recommend getting a drink to warm up. ## Go Ice Skating

[![Cute Date Ideas for Wintertime in Los Angeles:ice skate](data:image/svg+xml,%3Csvg%20xmlns='http://www.w3.org/2000/svg'%20viewBox='0%200%201024%20683'%3E%3C/svg%3E)![Cute Date Ideas for Wintertime in Los Angeles:ice skate](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/2/2017/11/6625045137_4b5aee79c8_b.jpg)](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/2/2017/11/6625045137_4b5aee79c8_b.jpg)

LA has a surprising number of ice skating rinks. On the west side, head to the [outdoor rink](http://www.downtownsm.com/ice-at-santa-monica) at Santa Monica‚Äôs Promenade. Downtown Los Angeles residents can skate at [Pershing Square](http://holidayicerinkdowntownla.com/) or outside of [L.A. Live](https://www.lalive.com/events-calendar/holidayice). Or head further out of the city to Long Beach‚Äôs Queen Mary rink or Downtown Burbank. Somehow, these rinks stay solid even on warm winter days.
