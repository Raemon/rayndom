## **What is Private Dining?**

Private dining refers to an exclusive dining experience that takes place in a separate and often specially designed private dining space, away from the main area of a restaurant. It offers groups a more intimate and personalized environment to enjoy their meal, whether it‚Äôs a social gathering, a business meeting, or a special celebration. These private dinner parties allows guests to enjoy their food and engage in conversations without the distractions of a busy restaurant. Most of the time, your private dining event space will be equipped with distinct d√©cor, audio visual facilities, and tailored menus, enhancing the overall experience.

##### Budget

Consider your budget carefully to ensure that the cost of the private dining room aligns with your financial plan for the event.

![thumbnail](data:image/svg+xml,%3Csvg%20xmlns='http://www.w3.org/2000/svg'%20viewBox='0%200%20100%20100'%3E%3C/svg%3E)![thumbnail](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/2024/05/group-size-icon.svg)

##### Group Size

Understand the capacity of the private dining room to ensure it comfortably accommodates your group size without necessitating a costly buyout for larger parties.

![thumbnail](data:image/svg+xml,%3Csvg%20xmlns='http://www.w3.org/2000/svg'%20viewBox='0%200%20100%20100'%3E%3C/svg%3E)![thumbnail](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/2024/05/fork-and-knife-icon.svg)

##### Cuisine & Dietary Restrictions

Confirm that the restaurant offers a cuisine type that suits your preferences and inquire about their ability to accommodate dietary restrictions or preferences among your guests. Psst! Avital accommodates all dietary restrictions!

![thumbnail](data:image/svg+xml,%3Csvg%20xmlns='http://www.w3.org/2000/svg'%20viewBox='0%200%20100%20100'%3E%3C/svg%3E)![thumbnail](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/2024/05/location-icon.svg)

##### Location

Evaluate the location of the private dining room in relation to the convenience and accessibility for your guests, considering factors such as parking availability and proximity to transportation hubs.

![thumbnail](data:image/svg+xml,%3Csvg%20xmlns='http://www.w3.org/2000/svg'%20viewBox='0%200%20100%20100'%3E%3C/svg%3E)![thumbnail](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/2024/05/verified-icon.svg)

Research the restaurant's reputation for both the quality of its food and the excellence of its service to ensure a memorable dining experience for your event.

Assess the responsiveness of the private dining room manager or coordinator, as effective communication and prompt assistance are essential for a smooth booking process and successful event execution.

## How to Book a Private Dining Room

Booking a private dining room usually requires some back-and-forth with the restaurant‚Äôs private dining manager to clarify details, finalize the menu, and more.

![medium](data:image/svg+xml,%3Csvg%20xmlns='http://www.w3.org/2000/svg'%20viewBox='0%200%20150%20150'%3E%3C/svg%3E)![medium](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/2024/05/submitting-private-dining-restaurant-forms-150x150.jpg)

#### **1. Submit Inquiry on Restaurant Websites**

Visit the individual restaurant websites and submit an inquiry to check availability for your desired date and group size.

![medium](data:image/svg+xml,%3Csvg%20xmlns='http://www.w3.org/2000/svg'%20viewBox='0%200%20150%20150'%3E%3C/svg%3E)![medium](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/2024/05/woman-typing-on-computer-150x150.jpg)

#### **2. Fill Out Forms**

Fill out up to 10 forms on restaurant websites to receive information back regarding availability and other details about private dining options.

![medium](data:image/svg+xml,%3Csvg%20xmlns='http://www.w3.org/2000/svg'%20viewBox='0%200%20150%20150'%3E%3C/svg%3E)![medium](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/2024/05/private-dining-restaurant-manager-on-computer-150x150.jpg)

#### **3. Receive Email from PDR Manager**

Expect an email back from the Private Dining Room (PDR) manager with information such as availability, room capacity, venue packet, menu options, and details about minimum spend and fees.

![medium](data:image/svg+xml,%3Csvg%20xmlns='http://www.w3.org/2000/svg'%20viewBox='0%200%20150%20150'%3E%3C/svg%3E)![medium](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/2024/05/woman-talking-on-phone-with-computer-in-front-of-her-150x150.jpg)

#### **4. Ask Venue-Specific Questions**

Take the opportunity to ask any venue-specific questions you may have regarding the private dining experience, such as customization options, audiovisual capabilities, or special accommodations.

![medium](data:image/svg+xml,%3Csvg%20xmlns='http://www.w3.org/2000/svg'%20viewBox='0%200%20150%20150'%3E%3C/svg%3E)![medium](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/2024/05/woman-holding-credit-card-to-pay-for-private-dining-room-150x150.jpg)

#### **5. Book, Pay Deposit, and Sign Contract**

Once satisfied, proceed to book the private dining room by paying a deposit and signing a contract. Ensure to review cancellation terms and any extra fees before finalizing the booking.

If you book with Avital we‚Äôll find you the perfect venue for your group from among our restaurant partner network ‚Äì saves you time and headache!

Private dining is a service provided by restaurants and venues where guests can enjoy a meal in a secluded area reserved specifically for their group. This can range from small family gatherings to large corporate events. Private dining typically involves:
