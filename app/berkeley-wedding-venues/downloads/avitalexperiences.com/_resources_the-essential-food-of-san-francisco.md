#  Essential Food Of San Francisco 

January 5, 2018 

With more great restaurants than the average person can keep up with, figuring out where to eat in San Francisco can be intimidating. But you have to get the essential food of San Francisco so you don‚Äôt leave without covering the basics. Here‚Äôs what you need to eat. Try these foods.

## Mission-Style Burritos

## [![Eat a Mission Style burrito-The Essential Food Of San Francisco](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/3/2018/12/The-Essential-Food-Of-San-Francisco-mission-burrito.jpg)](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/3/2018/12/The-Essential-Food-Of-San-Francisco-mission-burrito.jpg)

You can‚Äôt leave San Francisco without eating a burrito, and you should try one of the SF staples, a Mission-style burrito. It‚Äôs a flour tortilla filled with rice, beans, salsa, and meat. Basic? Yes, and delicious. Of course, you can add cheese, sour cream, guacamole, and lettuce if you want. You‚Äôll hear different people claim their favorite spot. Some of our favorites are [El Farolito](https://www.yelp.com/biz/el-farolito-san-francisco-2), [La Taqueria](https://www.yelp.com/biz/la-taqueria-san-francisco-2), and [La Cumbre](http://www.taquerialacumbre.com/).

## Cioppino

[![cioppino-The Essential Food Of San Francisco](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/3/2018/12/10-Things-To-Eat-In-North-Beach-Sotto-Mare-Cioppino-bowl-close-up.jpg)](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/3/2018/12/10-Things-To-Eat-In-North-Beach-Sotto-Mare-Cioppino-bowl-close-up.jpg)

The tomato-based seafood stew was invited[ right here in San Francisco](http://avitaltours.com/san-francisco/history-of-san-francisco-cioppino), not Italy as people often assume. Cioppino is an essential food of San Francisco and our favorite place to get it is [Sotto Mare](https://www.sottomaresf.com/). The stew was traditionally made in the homes of Italian fisherman who worked near North Beach. No matter where you eat your cioppino make sure you get it stuffed with crab, clams, scallops, mussels, prawns, calamari, and more.

## Bread from Tartine Bakery

## [![bread at tartine bakery - food of San Francisco. ](data:image/svg+xml,%3Csvg%20xmlns='http://www.w3.org/2000/svg'%20viewBox='0%200%203008%202000'%3E%3C/svg%3E)![bread at tartine bakery - food of San Francisco. ](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/3/2017/03/tartine-bakery.jpg)](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/3/2017/03/tartine-bakery.jpg)

It‚Äôs easy to get distracted at [Tartine](https://www.tartinebakery.com/). There are morning buns, cakes, cookies, and quiches. But focus because you‚Äôre here for the bread. Yes, there will likely be a line outside but that‚Äôs because the bakery‚Äôs bread is an essential food of San Francisco. Snag your loaf and while you eat it know you‚Äôre experiencing true San Francisco magic.

## Egg Custard Tarts at Golden Gate Bakery

[![Golden Gate Bakery: The Essential Food Of San Franciscoo](data:image/svg+xml,%3Csvg%20xmlns='http://www.w3.org/2000/svg'%20viewBox='0%200%201024%20657'%3E%3C/svg%3E)![Golden Gate Bakery: The Essential Food Of San Franciscoo](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/3/2017/06/558143243_1152fbf993_b.jpg)](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/3/2017/06/558143243_1152fbf993_b.jpg)

You can get egg custard tarts at other bakeries, but the best in San Francisco are at [Golden Gate Bakery](https://www.yelp.com/biz/golden-gate-bakery-san-francisco). They‚Äôre fresh, flaky, sweet, and creamy. Bring cash and expect a (short) wait. Leave with a few more than you think you‚Äôll need, especially if you‚Äôre meeting up with friends or family. (Pro tip: you will eat them later.)

## The Rebel Within [![The Rebel Within at Craftsman and Wolves-The Essential Food Of San Francisco](data:image/svg+xml,%3Csvg%20xmlns='http://www.w3.org/2000/svg'%20viewBox='0%200%201000%20667'%3E%3C/svg%3E)![The Rebel Within at Craftsman and Wolves-The Essential Food Of San Francisco](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/3/2014/02/San-francisco-food-tours-rebel-within2-1.jpg)](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/3/2014/02/San-francisco-food-tours-rebel-within2-1.jpg)

Instagram-favorite The Rebel Within is one of the most photographed dishes in the city. It‚Äôs a muffin filled with asiago cheese, sausage, green onion, and a soft cooked egg from [Craftsman and Wolves](https://www.craftsman-wolves.com/). (Just look at that egg!) Don‚Äôt disappoint your taste buds or followers; make sure you stop here.__

##  Oysters from Swan Oyster Depot

[![5Essential Food Of San Francisco Swan Oyster Depot](data:image/svg+xml,%3Csvg%20xmlns='http://www.w3.org/2000/svg'%20viewBox='0%200%201024%20768'%3E%3C/svg%3E)![5Essential Food Of San Francisco Swan Oyster Depot](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/3/2017/10/1356691373_157a9f72fc_b.jpg)](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/3/2017/10/1356691373_157a9f72fc_b.jpg)

Squeeze into one of the dozen seats at [Swan Oyster Depot](https://www.yelp.com/biz/swan-oyster-depot-san-francisco) and let the experts behind the counter guide your oyster ordering. Get some other dishes like the seafood salads, the half-cracked crab, and Sicilian sashimi, but remember that you‚Äôre here for the oysters. At Swan Oyster Depot you‚Äôll have a true San Francisco food experience that you can‚Äôt leave the city without.
