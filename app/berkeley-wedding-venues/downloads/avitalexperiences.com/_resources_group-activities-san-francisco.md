## Iconic Attractions for Groups in San Francisco

If your group is visiting from out of town, here are some of the top attractions and sites to take them to. 

![A cruise takes passengers to Alcatraz Island](data:image/svg+xml,%3Csvg%20xmlns='http://www.w3.org/2000/svg'%20viewBox='0%200%201024%20802'%3E%3C/svg%3E)![A cruise takes passengers to Alcatraz Island](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/2026/01/3.-cityexperiences.com_-1024x802.png)cityexperiences.com

### Alcatraz

[Alcatraz Island](https://www.nps.gov/alca/index.htm) is an essential piece of this city‚Äôs history! Take your group on a tour of the storied halls of the prison. We recommend taking a ferry there to catch the breeze off the bay on your way over.

![pier 39 in san francisco, a great place to visit with teenagers](data:image/svg+xml,%3Csvg%20xmlns='http://www.w3.org/2000/svg'%20viewBox='0%200%201024%20481'%3E%3C/svg%3E)![pier 39 in san francisco, a great place to visit with teenagers](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/2025/05/6.-pier39.com_-1024x481.jpeg)pier39.com

### Pier 39

[Pier 39](https://pier39.com) has it all! Home to iconic sea lions on the pier, circus street performers, the Aquarium of the Bay, and even a small amusement park. Here you can play, shop, and dine all day without ever having to leave the area!

![The Golden gate Bridge in San Francisco](data:image/svg+xml,%3Csvg%20xmlns='http://www.w3.org/2000/svg'%20viewBox='0%200%201024%20667'%3E%3C/svg%3E)![The Golden gate Bridge in San Francisco](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/2026/01/IMG_9106-1024x667.jpeg)goldengate.org

### Golden Gate Bridge

You have to see the architectural marvel that is the [Golden Gate Bridge](https://www.goldengate.org) before you can really say you‚Äôve experienced the city of San Francisco. Whether you take a 45-minute walk, a quick bike ride, or a car over the bridge, you have to traverse its epic archways on your SF pilgrimage.

![ghiradelli square during sunset in san francisco](data:image/svg+xml,%3Csvg%20xmlns='http://www.w3.org/2000/svg'%20viewBox='0%200%201024%20503'%3E%3C/svg%3E)![ghiradelli square during sunset in san francisco](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/2025/05/4.-Ghiradellisq.com_-1024x503.png)Ghiradellisq.com

[Ghirardelli Square](https://www.ghirardellisq.com) is iconic for being home to the OG Ghirardelli Chocolate & Ice Cream Shop. ## Outdoor Activities in San Francisco for Groups

San Francisco is a city to be explored all year long! It‚Äôs true that winters can get cold and rainy, but dress prepared and you can enjoy all of these outdoor activities. 

![Views of the Conservatory of flowers at Golden Gate Park](data:image/svg+xml,%3Csvg%20xmlns='http://www.w3.org/2000/svg'%20viewBox='0%200%20300%20300'%3E%3C/svg%3E)![Views of the Conservatory of flowers at Golden Gate Park](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/2026/01/IMG_9109.jpeg)sfrec.org

Spend a peaceful afternoon at the [Japanese Tea Garden ](https://gggp.org/japanese-tea-garden/)in Golden Gate Park. ![People shop at the Ferry Building Farmer's Market in SF](data:image/svg+xml,%3Csvg%20xmlns='http://www.w3.org/2000/svg'%20viewBox='0%200%201024%20747'%3E%3C/svg%3E)![People shop at the Ferry Building Farmer's Market in SF](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/2026/01/https_www.ferrybuildingmarketplace.com_farmers-market_-1024x747.jpg)ferrybuildingmarketplace.com

### Ferry Plaza Farmers Market

Stop by the Ferry Building Saturday morning to experience a [Farmers‚Äô Market](https://foodwise.org/markets/ferry-plaza-farmers-market/) unlike any other. With over 100 vendors and stalls hawking everything from fresh, local produce to artisan baked goods, and even lunch and brunch made to order. There‚Äôs no way anyone is leaving here hungry!

![A storefront window of a square in Union square SF](data:image/svg+xml,%3Csvg%20xmlns='http://www.w3.org/2000/svg'%20viewBox='0%200%201024%20652'%3E%3C/svg%3E)![A storefront window of a square in Union square SF](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/2026/01/IMG_9114-1024x652.jpeg)unionsquareshop.com

### Union Square

If you prefer to shop name-brand, firsthand, and luxury stores, [Union Square](https://unionsquareshop.com/) is the place you‚Äôll want to go shopping. This area has everything from Bloomingdale‚Äôs to Saks Fifth Avenue. It‚Äôs also a popular place to get accommodations, as there are great hotels and easy access to public transportation.

![Women in vintage attire walk through Valencia in San Francisco](data:image/svg+xml,%3Csvg%20xmlns='http://www.w3.org/2000/svg'%20viewBox='0%200%201024%20692'%3E%3C/svg%3E)![Women in vintage attire walk through Valencia in San Francisco](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/2026/01/https_www.valenciavintagesf.com_-1024x692.webp)valenciavintagesf.com

### Valencia Street in The Mission

[Valencia Street](https://www.sanfranciscobay.com/attractions/valencia-street-shopping/) is where mainstream and alt fashion meet. You can stop in at a Lulu Lemon or get adventurous and poke around in [Ian James](https://ianjamesmade.com/) (where you can get a custom leather piece designed for you right there and then.) This is also the best area to shop for second-hand books and records.

## Day Trips Near San Francisco for Groups

If you want to show your group even more than just San Francisco proper, consider a day trip to nearby attractions. 

![Redwoods from Muir wood tower over hiking groups](data:image/svg+xml,%3Csvg%20xmlns='http://www.w3.org/2000/svg'%20viewBox='0%200%20835%201024'%3E%3C/svg%3E)![Redwoods from Muir wood tower over hiking groups](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/2026/01/IMG_9115-835x1024.jpeg)@goldengatenps

### Muir Woods

The ancient Coastal Redwood Trees in [Muir Woods](https://www.nps.gov/muwo/index.htm) have to be seen to be believed. They tower over you at heights up to 250 feet, and some have been around for over 1,200 years. You can combine seeing the park with a trip to Sausalito with tours like [this one by Gray Line](https://graylineofsanfrancisco.com/tours/muir-woods-sausalito-tour/).

![Hills and Vineyards in Napa Valley California](data:image/svg+xml,%3Csvg%20xmlns='http://www.w3.org/2000/svg'%20viewBox='0%200%201024%20801'%3E%3C/svg%3E)![Hills and Vineyards in Napa Valley California](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/2026/01/@visitnapavalley.PNG-1024x801.jpg)@visitnapavalley.

### Napa Valley

If you‚Äôre going to spend a day in [Napa Valley](https://www.visitnapavalley.com) tasting wine and looking out at gorgeous Orchards, we recommend you work with a company like [Wine Train](https://www.winetrain.com/your-visit-to-napa/daytrippers/). They pick you up in San Francisco and bring you on a scenic ride to Napa with a visit to a winery planned out for you to enjoy.

![Two friends enjoy a meal in Sonoma Valley](data:image/svg+xml,%3Csvg%20xmlns='http://www.w3.org/2000/svg'%20viewBox='0%200%201024%20884'%3E%3C/svg%3E)![Two friends enjoy a meal in Sonoma Valley](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/2026/01/https_www.sonomavalley.com_-1024x884.jpg)sonomavalley.com

### Sonoma Valley

Your other option for phenomenal wine and scenery is sunny [Sonoma Valley](https://www.sonomavalley.com)! It‚Äôs just an hour away by car and has wonderful wineries like [Buena Vista](https://buenavistawinery.com/), [Iron Horse](https://ironhorsevineyards.com/), and [Ferrari Carano](https://www.ferrari-carano.com/), where you can do tastings and enjoy the gorgeous vineyards and fresh air.

![The water and skyline in Sausalito California](data:image/svg+xml,%3Csvg%20xmlns='http://www.w3.org/2000/svg'%20viewBox='0%200%201024%20666'%3E%3C/svg%3E)![The water and skyline in Sausalito California](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/2026/01/https_visitsausalito.org_-1024x666.png)visitsausalito.org

### Sausalito

[Sausalito](https://www.visitcalifornia.com/places-to-visit/sausalito/) is best known for its incredible views of the Golden Gate Bridge and SF skyline, the lively art scene, and its iconic houseboats. Spend the day dipping in and out of art galleries, eating food from some of the top chefs on the West Coast, and walking the waterfront.

![Beautiful mountains in Yosemite](data:image/svg+xml,%3Csvg%20xmlns='http://www.w3.org/2000/svg'%20viewBox='0%200%201024%20768'%3E%3C/svg%3E)![Beautiful mountains in Yosemite](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/2026/01/Yosemite-https_www.nps_.gov_-1024x768.webp)nps.gov

### Yosemite

If your group enjoys a rigorous hike, [Yosemite](https://www.nps.gov/yose/index.htm) is the park for you. It is home to iconic cliffs like El Capitan and the Half Dome, beautiful waterfalls, and thriving wildlife. [Extranomical](https://www.extranomical.com/tour/yosemite-one-day-tour-from-san-francisco/) offers an accessible one-day tour that shows you all the best sights in the park

## How To Get Around SF with Your Group

### The Muni SFMTA

[The MUNI system](https://www.sfmta.com/muni-transit) is composed of buses, light rail, and cable cars. This transit system caters to the most popular tourist areas and can get you to most of the places in SF on this list.

### BART (Bay Area Rapid Transit)

[The BART](https://www.bart.gov), also known as Bay Area Rapid Transit, is a train that makes express stops to places like the SF International Airport, and can quickly get you to the East Bay Area.

### Gray Line Tours

[Gray Line Tours](https://graylineofsanfrancisco.com) is a trusted bus tour company that is a great choice for day trips to places like Muir Woods, Napa Valley, Sonoma Valley, and Yosemite.

### SF Bay Ferry

The [SF Bay Ferry](https://sanfranciscobayferry.com/) can take you on a scenic ride over the Bay to nearby hotspots like Oakland, Richmond, and Downtown San Francisco.
