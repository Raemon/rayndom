**Why we love it:** This fun and sometimes heated game will bring your team closer together without the need to go off-site.

## Corporate Holiday Party FAQs

What is a Los Angeles corporate holiday party? 

A [Los Angeles Company Holiday Party](https://avitalexperiences.com/seasonal-experiences/corporate-holiday-party/) is for colleagues who work together in Los Angeles, or who are visiting LA during the holiday season for an event, retreat, or conference, and want to get into the holiday spirit while in town. We happen to think a holiday party doesn‚Äôt even have to be holiday-themed, but just has to coincide with the holiday season. It‚Äôs a great way to celebrate your peers and provide a merry send-off before heading off to personal holiday gatherings and events. 

We‚Äôve compiled corporate holiday party entertainment ideas that span the breadth of many different budgets, so you can choose to go all out or to rein in the spending! 

What to wear to a corporate holiday party? 

Dressing for a corporate holiday party typically requires finding a balance between festive and professional. The specific dress code can depend on the event‚Äôs venue and time of day, so check for any guidelines from the organizers. Here are a few options based on common dress codes:

  * **Business Casual** : Choose something polished but comfortable, like a nice sweater with tailored pants, or a blouse and skirt combo. Stick to festive colors or add a holiday accessory to keep it seasonal without going overboard.
  * **Formal or Semi-Formal** : For evening events at upscale venues, opt for cocktail attire. Men could wear a suit or a jacket and slacks, while women might choose a cocktail dress or a nice jumpsuit. A touch of holiday sparkle in accessories can add a seasonal touch without being too flashy.
  * **Themed Attire** : Some parties may have a specific theme, like ‚ÄúUgly Sweater‚Äù or ‚ÄúWinter Wonderland,‚Äù allowing for more creative or casual choices. In this case, embrace the theme to add to the holiday spirit, but remember it‚Äôs still a work function, so keep it tasteful.



Ultimately, choose an outfit that aligns with both the event‚Äôs setting and your comfort level, while reflecting a bit of holiday cheer.

When to have a corporate holiday party? 

Timing your holiday party can greatly impact attendance and engagement. Here are a few considerations:

  * **During Office Hours vs. After Hours** : For an onsite holiday luncheon or low-key celebration, holding the event during work hours ensures higher participation. For a more social evening event, after-hours on a Friday may be more fitting, allowing employees to fully unwind without workday responsibilities.
  * **Weekday vs. Weekend** : Mid-week events tend to have higher attendance, as weekends are often reserved for family time. Fridays work well for evening events, as people are typically more relaxed heading into the weekend.
  * **Holiday Season Window** : Aim for early to mid-December if possible, before employees start taking time off for personal holiday plans. Avoid the days right before major holidays when attendance might be lower.



Ultimately, select a date and time that allows your team to participate comfortably and enjoyably.

Where to have a corporate holiday party in LA? 

Choosing a venue that aligns with your party goals and fits your team‚Äôs size and preferences is essential. Here are popular options:

  * **Onsite at the Office** : Hosting an office party is cost-effective and convenient, particularly for daytime events. With a bit of decoration and a catered meal, you can transform the office into a festive space.
  * **Local Restaurant or Bar** : Reserving a private room or section in a restaurant or bar offers a lively atmosphere without the need for set-up or clean-up. It‚Äôs a popular choice for smaller teams or departments.
  * **Special Event Venues** : For a larger team or more formal event, consider event spaces like banquet halls, hotels, or unique venues like art galleries or breweries. These options allow for a customizable experience and often provide catering options as well.
  * **Outdoor Settings** : In LA, you can get away with an outdoor holiday gathering at a patio or rooftop venue. This can offer a unique twist and a festive, open-air experience.



Choosing the right location helps set the tone of the party, whether you want a cozy get-together, a professional dinner, or a lively celebration.
