## How it Looks 

The Cocktail Rim Blending Bar is a hosted station where guests move through each step, starting with a salt base, selecting spices from three distinct flavor profiles, and finishing with a shake or stir before packaging their custom blend in a jar. Each guest typically spends 5-10 minutes at the bar, making it a high-energy, quick-interaction experience.

For a better understanding of how our Interactive Bars fit into your event space and flow, check out the [Interactive Bar: How It Works](https://avitalexperiences.com/interactive-bars/) section.
