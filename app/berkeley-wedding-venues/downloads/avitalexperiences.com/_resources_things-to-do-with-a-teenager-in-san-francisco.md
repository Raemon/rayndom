## 1\. Take a Walking Food Tour in North Beach

![group standing in front of cavalli cafe during north beach food tour](data:image/svg+xml,%3Csvg%20xmlns='http://www.w3.org/2000/svg'%20viewBox='0%200%20485%201024'%3E%3C/svg%3E)![group standing in front of cavalli cafe during north beach food tour](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/05/group-standing-in-front-of-cavali-cafe-during-north-beach-food-tour-485x1024.jpg)

## 2\. Experience the Exploratorium

![eyeballs in a spiral during experiment at the exploratorium, one of the best places to visit with teens in san francisco](data:image/svg+xml,%3Csvg%20xmlns='http://www.w3.org/2000/svg'%20viewBox='0%200%20960%20916'%3E%3C/svg%3E)![eyeballs in a spiral during experiment at the exploratorium, one of the best places to visit with teens in san francisco](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/2025/05/2.-exploratorium.edu_.jpg)Image Credit: exploratorium.edu

**Contend with the Elements:** Traverse the eerie [Fog bridge](https://www.exploratorium.edu/exhibits/fog-bridge-72494). Then step into a [Tornado](https://www.exploratorium.edu/exhibits/tornado)

**Experience Mind-Boggling Optical Illusions** : with the ghostly [Bird in A Cage](https://www.exploratorium.edu/exhibits/bird-in-a-cage) exhibit. Or try to comprehend the [Cafe Wall Illusion](https://www.exploratorium.edu/exhibits/cafe-wall-illusion)

**Become a Part of the Art** : with interactive exhibits like [All Eyes on Me](https://www.exploratorium.edu/exhibits/all-eyes-on-me) or the [Shadow Box](https://www.exploratorium.edu/exhibits/shadow-box)

The Exploratorium is a museum that offers countless hours of entertainment. It is also home to a lovely restaurant called the [Seaglass Cafe](https://www.seaglassrestaurantsf.com), where you can eat after exploring the galleries.

## 3\. Tour Alcatraz and See the Bay

![boat touring alcatraz in san francisco](data:image/svg+xml,%3Csvg%20xmlns='http://www.w3.org/2000/svg'%20viewBox='0%200%201024%20802'%3E%3C/svg%3E)![boat touring alcatraz in san francisco](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/2025/05/3.-cityexperiences.com_-1024x802.png)Image Credit: cityexperiences.com

When thinking of engaging things to do in San Francisco with teenagers, visiting a historic site might not be your first thought, but a tour of Alcatraz infuses the sometimes dry subject of history with new life! 

Your teen will have heard of this storied prison in school, as well as many of its famous inmates (Al Capone! George ‚ÄúMachine Gun‚Äù Kelly!), and experiencing it in person will prove even more thrilling than they anticipate. You can [cruise the bay](https://www.cityexperiences.com/san-francisco/city-cruises/alcatraz/) on your way over to the prison and enjoy beautiful views of the placid water, as well as some great photo ops, to make a whole day out of your visit.

## 4\. Feed Your Sweet Tooth in Ghirardelli Square

![ghiradelli square during sunset in san francisco](data:image/svg+xml,%3Csvg%20xmlns='http://www.w3.org/2000/svg'%20viewBox='0%200%201024%20503'%3E%3C/svg%3E)![ghiradelli square during sunset in san francisco](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/2025/05/4.-Ghiradellisq.com_-1024x503.png)Image Credit: ghiradellisq.com

This iconic square was once the birthplace of the Ghirardelli Chocolate Company. Now it has become a local hotspot with hotels, restaurants, and great shops to explore.

[**Ghirardelli Shop**](https://www.ghirardelli.com/stores/new-york?srsltid=AfmBOooW-hRB3JOrVR5g3bcFtJI1FjZuRZOYeWs7BtNpD4AkPzJb8o30): grab dessert at the Ghirardelli Ice Cream and Chocolate Shop. The menu features special themed treats like the ‚ÄúGold Rush‚Äù, the ‚ÄúGolden Gate Bridge Banana Split‚Äù, and the ‚ÄúLand‚Äôs End‚Äù, which blend Ghirardelli‚Äôs rich chocolate with refreshing ice cream

[**Subpar Mini Golf**](https://www.subparminigolf.com/san-francisco): now that you and your teen have satiated your sweet tooth, the games can begin! Head over for 18 holes of mini golf at Subpar. Or if you‚Äôd prefer some less strenuous fun, they offer classic arcade games in the back

[**Cheese School of San Francisco**](https://thecheeseschool.com): for the lactose-tolerant, cheese class is in session! The Cheese School of San Francisco is devoted to the appreciation of cheese. You can learn how to arrange a killer charcuterie board in a [class](https://thecheeseschool.com/collections/public-classes), or just peruse the cheese selection and try some samples

Ghirardelli Square is full of life and activity 7 days a week! You and your teenager are sure to enjoy yourselves at this iconic spot.

## 5\. Cycle over the Golden Gate Bridge

![golden gate bridge in san francisco](data:image/svg+xml,%3Csvg%20xmlns='http://www.w3.org/2000/svg'%20viewBox='0%200%201024%20512'%3E%3C/svg%3E)![golden gate bridge in san francisco](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/2025/05/5.-parksconservancy.org_-1024x512.jpg)Image Credit: parksconservancy.org

The Golden Gate Bridge is one of the best feats of architecture and engineering in America. San Francisco can sometimes be stressful to traverse by car, so why not hop on a bike to enjoy the view with your teen? 

If you elect to bike it on the weekend or weekdays after 3:30, you can enjoy the entire west side of the bridge, which is closed off from all vehicles and reserved for bikers and pedestrians to enjoy. You can either[ book a guided tour](https://www.blazingsaddles.com/san-francisco/routes-and-tours/bike-the-bridge) of the bridge and surrounding parks or [rent a bike](https://sfbiketours.com/bike-rentals/) and do it yourself. 

While you‚Äôre at it, we recommend biking around the base of the bridge to take in the historic Fort Point on the south side, as well as taking a moment to give a high five to the [Hopper‚Äôs Hand sign](https://www.parksconservancy.org/gateways-article/what-is-story-behind-hoppers-hands-fort-point), a local tradition.

## 8\. Botanical Gardens After Dark

![group of people listening to piano at san francisco botanical garden](data:image/svg+xml,%3Csvg%20xmlns='http://www.w3.org/2000/svg'%20viewBox='0%200%201024%20843'%3E%3C/svg%3E)![group of people listening to piano at san francisco botanical garden](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/2025/05/8.-gggp.org_-1024x843.jpg)Image Credit: gggp.org

There is plenty to see in the Botanical Gardens of San Francisco during the day, but have you ever hung around after dark? [Flower Piano](https://gggp.org/flowerpiano/) is an event that occurs in the garden multiple times a year, and if it‚Äôs on when you‚Äôre in town, you won‚Äôt want to miss it.

Your teen will feel like a VIP at this cultivated, after-hours event, in the dark.

## 9\. Spend a Day at the Beach

![rock on beach in san franicisco](data:image/svg+xml,%3Csvg%20xmlns='http://www.w3.org/2000/svg'%20viewBox='0%200%20700%20467'%3E%3C/svg%3E)![rock on beach in san franicisco](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/07/beach-birthday-party-point-dume.jpg)

**Baker Beach** : offers large waves and dramatic views‚Äìsitting at the base of the cliffs on which the Golden Gate Bridge is erected. You can observe sea life near the shoreline and fish at this beach

**Mile Rock Beach** : if you‚Äôre looking for a beach that offers a bit of terrain, climbing down the makeshift stairs to Mile Rock Beach is an exciting experience. The beach is covered in beautiful rocks and also has sandy stretches, where you can sit and relax

**Crissy Field Beach** : provides beautiful views of the Golden Gate Bridge and Alacatraz. There are picnic tables where you and your teen can sit and share a meal, and hiking trails if you get your fill of lazing around in the sun 

You and your teen should make sure to spend an afternoon basking in the glorious sun and breathing in the ocean air.

## 10\. Catch a Game at Oracle Park

![exterior shot of oracle park in san francisco](data:image/svg+xml,%3Csvg%20xmlns='http://www.w3.org/2000/svg'%20viewBox='0%200%201024%20576'%3E%3C/svg%3E)![exterior shot of oracle park in san francisco](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/2025/05/10.-mlb.com_.jpg)Image Credit: mlb.com

![exterior of Japanese tea garden in san francisco](data:image/svg+xml,%3Csvg%20xmlns='http://www.w3.org/2000/svg'%20viewBox='0%200%20840%20550'%3E%3C/svg%3E)![exterior of Japanese tea garden in san francisco](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/2025/05/11.-japaneseteagardensf.com_.jpeg)Image Credit: japaneseteagardensf.com

## 12\. Tour Famous Movie Locations 

![family with teenager posing for picture during san francisco movie tour](data:image/svg+xml,%3Csvg%20xmlns='http://www.w3.org/2000/svg'%20viewBox='0%200%20720%20480'%3E%3C/svg%3E)![family with teenager posing for picture during san francisco movie tour](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/2025/05/12.-sanfranciscomovietours.com_.jpeg)Image Credit: sanfranciscomovietours.com

## 13\. Plan a Day Trip to a National Park

![tall trees and plants with light and mist in san francisco national park](data:image/svg+xml,%3Csvg%20xmlns='http://www.w3.org/2000/svg'%20viewBox='0%200%201024%20768'%3E%3C/svg%3E)![tall trees and plants with light and mist in san francisco national park](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/2025/05/13.-nps.gov_-1024x768.jpeg)Image Credit: nps.gov

San Francisco is in close proximity to many beautiful national parks. If your teen loves being in nature, or even if they prefer the indoors, and you‚Äôre looking for an outdoor activity impressive enough to persuade them to get some sun, these parks are so breathtaking that it will inspire gratitude and awe no matter their attitude.

**Muir Woods** : he ancient Coast Redwood Trees in Muir Woods have to be seen to be believed. They tower over you at heights up to 250 feet, and some have been around for over 1,200 years. **Yosemite** : if you and your teen enjoy a rigorous hike, Yosemite is the park for you. It is home to iconic cliffs like El Capitan and the Half Dome, beautiful waterfalls, and thriving wildlife. [Extranomical](https://www.extranomical.com/tour/yosemite-one-day-tour-from-san-francisco/) offers an accessible one-day tour that shows you all the best sights in the park

**Pinnacles** : although the lesser-known of these three parks, Pinnacles has something entirely original to offer tourists. The park is composed of steep walls and caverns created by volcanic eruptions. It‚Äôs about a 2-hour drive from the city, so if you have a car with you, this could be the perfect day trip

If you are in San Francisco for multiple days, you should definitely reserve one to see these grandiose national monuments. It will be an unforgettable experience for you and your teen, rife with unbeatable photo opportunities and the joy of experiencing the awe-inspiring natural world.
