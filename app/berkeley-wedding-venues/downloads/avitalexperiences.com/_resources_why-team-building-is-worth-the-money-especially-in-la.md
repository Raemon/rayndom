#  Why Team Building is Worth the Money (Especially in LA) 

June 5, 2018 

In a city as vast as Los Angeles, where the opportunities for employment are just as vast, it‚Äôs important that your company provide opportunities for team building as a reward, as a thank you, as a way to bond, and as a chance to recharge. Team building is worth the money (especially in LA) where there are many opportunities for unhappy employees to find a new job. Here are some reasons why your business should invest in team building.

## Getting Out Of The Office Bonds Teams

![Why Team Building is Worth the Money \(Especially in LA\): group bonding on a taco crawl](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/2/2014/02/Downtown-LA-Food-Tour-Group-at-Chicas-Tacos.jpg)

A change of setting for team building is helpful in getting your team to bond. Getting your team out of the office helps them relax and get to know each other in a way that just doesn‚Äôt happen during a meeting or training. Block off an afternoon or a day and plan an event that gets everyone off-site.

## If You Retain Staff, You Save Money

[![Get out of the office and into the donut shop! Why Team Building is Worth the Money \(Especially in LA\)](data:image/svg+xml,%3Csvg%20xmlns='http://www.w3.org/2000/svg'%20viewBox='0%200%20800%20414'%3E%3C/svg%3E)![Get out of the office and into the donut shop! Why Team Building is Worth the Money \(Especially in LA\)](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/2/2018/05/Los-Angeles-Corporate-Team-Building-Activity-Group-at-Blue-Star-Donuts2.jpg)](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/2/2018/05/Los-Angeles-Corporate-Team-Building-Activity-Group-at-Blue-Star-Donuts2.jpg)

Spending a little money now will save you lots of money later. The LA job market is competitive; businesses need to recruit the best candidates and keep them. So, money spent investing in team building is money well spent. Ensure that your company retains staff and you‚Äôll save money on hiring and onboarding costs. Plus, productivity and morale are increased when a team works well together.

## Investing In Fun Is Important

[![Venice beach team: Why Team Building is Worth the Money \(Especially in LA\)](data:image/svg+xml,%3Csvg%20xmlns='http://www.w3.org/2000/svg'%20viewBox='0%200%202048%201536'%3E%3C/svg%3E)![Venice beach team: Why Team Building is Worth the Money \(Especially in LA\)](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/2/2018/04/Team-Building-Icebreakers-team-bonding.jpeg)](https://avitalexperiences.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/2/2018/04/Team-Building-Icebreakers-team-bonding.jpeg)

In a city like LA with a more laid-back vibe than some other major metropolises, it‚Äôs important to let your team have some fun. Today‚Äôs employees aren‚Äôt looking to clock in at 9:00 am, leave at 5:00 pm and repeat. Your team is looking to work somewhere they enjoy and work with people they enjoy being around, even after 5:00. Invest in fun team building opportunities to keep your team engaged and show them that you care about their happiness and well-being.
