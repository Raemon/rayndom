## Group Dining Considerations

Not all restaurants are designed for group dining. They need to be able to provide varied menu options for different dietary needs, provide private dining spaces, and flexible payment options. Here are all of the things to consider when booking a group dinner. 

  * **Varied options** : The menu should accommodate different tastes and dietary needs.
  * **Private spaces** : Having semi-private areas or separate private dining rooms is ideal for groups who want to be louder or more private. 
  * **Flexible payment** : The restaurant should be willing to accommodate splitting bills or other payment arrangements. 
  * **Helpful staff** : Staff should be trained and equipped to handle large groups, which includes efficient service and a friendly attitude. 
  * **Customizations:** If applicable, check that the restaurant can allow for personal touches like decorations, flowers, or banners.
