## MAKE YOUR NEXT HOLIDAY PARTY A YACHT PARTY

Dazzle your employees with a memorable treat!

### UNFORGETTABLE CELEBRATIONS

Light the lights, trim the tree and ring in the New Year with a customized yacht party in the serene Marina del Rey harbor. Take your corporate holiday party to a whole new level of entertaining with cocktails, delicious eats, and a fresh ocean breeze in the air! Your staff will relish all of the sights and sounds of the holidays aboard one of our luxury yachts. We’re happy to accommodate parties ranging from 12 to 250 guests – We have pre-designed packages, or feel free to design your own ideal celebration!

### ALWAYS SMOOTH SAILING

Whether celebrating the annual Marina del Rey Boat Parade of Lights for the first time or renewing a holiday tradition, a [yacht party](https://www.fantaseayachts.com/2017/12/20/yacht-party-day-or-night/) in the harbor is a fabulous and comfortable way to celebrate the holidays. Our [party yacht](https://www.fantaseayachts.com/celebrations/) cruises travel inside the tranquil harbor of Marina del Rey amongst 8.5 miles of scenic waterway including beautiful views of Santa Monica Bay and the Los Angeles coastline, where the water is always calm.
