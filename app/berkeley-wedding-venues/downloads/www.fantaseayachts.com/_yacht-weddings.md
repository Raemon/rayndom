[vc_row][vc_column][vc_column_text]Over the past four decades, we have hosted hundreds of [yacht weddings](https://www.fantaseayachts.com/weddings/pricing-and-packages/) aboard our luxurious nautical event venues. More than just ships, every one of our vessels has been customized to serve as a self-contained, 5-star, banquet hall. Our elite food service, bartenders, wait staff, and chefs are comparable if not more impressive than lavish hotels in Marina del Rey. 

Here are some of the many reasons why throwing a yacht wedding makes things easier on everyone – your bridal party, your guests, and most importantly the happy couple! – yet multitudes more magical.

**WE ARE RESPONSIBLE FOR ALL THE SET-UP AND DETAILS**

FantaSea’s [boat wedding](https://www.fantaseayachts.com/weddings/yacht-venues/) venues are equipped with all the amenities of a luxurious ballroom. You’ll have a dedicated party planner assigned to your special day, you’ll have on-site maintenance and dining staff with you at all times, and you don’t have to lift a finger to set up anything (unless you really want to).

**YOU CAN BE MARRIED BY THE CAPTAIN**

Whether you’ve got dreams of _The Love Boat_ or _The Office_ , getting married by the Captain can be a reality! All of our Captains are ordained to perform wedding ceremonies by the Universal Church of Life.

**WHAT DO I DO WITH GIFTS BROUGHT TO THE WEDDING?**

We are happy to set-up a reception table to receive your gifts, cards, and also distribute your party favors. Your gifts will be bagged later in the evening for easy transport at the end of your [yacht party reception](https://www.fantaseayachts.com/weddings/).[/vc_column_text][vc_single_image image=”1707″ img_size=”full”][/vc_column][/vc_row][vc_row][vc_column width=”1/3″][vc_single_image image=”1703″ img_size=”full”][/vc_column][vc_column width=”2/3″][vc_column_text]**EVERY YACHT HAS A BRIDAL SUITE**

Being aboard one of our ships means you’ll have access to fantastic amenities including gorgeous bridal suites. Get ready, relax, and enjoy your own private space.

**HOW FAR IN ADVANCE SHOULD I BOOK MY DESIRED YACHT?**

Some of our yacht weddings book more than a year in advance, others book 3-6 months in advance. The sooner you book, the greater the chance of getting your ideal date selection. Our wedding planners are standing by – [feel free to call, email](/contact/), or you can live chat with us during regular business hours.[/vc_column_text][/vc_column][/vc_row][vc_row][vc_column][vc_single_image image=”1709″ img_size=”full”][/vc_column][/vc_row]
