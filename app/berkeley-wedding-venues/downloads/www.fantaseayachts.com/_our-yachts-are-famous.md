### Book a ship that radiates with Hollywood style and history

Our fleet has been featured in dozens of films, television shows, and music videos. FantaSea’s fleet has been featured in films such as Yours, Mine and Ours; You, Me and Dupree; and Sorority Boys, music videos for Too $hort and O-Town, and television shows including The Hills (Lauren’s surprise birthday in the Season 5 premiere), Six Feet Under, Dexter and Arrested Development, Bones, NCIS). All of FantaSea’s yacht charters are available for use in film, television, commercials, music video productions and photo shoots. Enjoy some of our fleet’s greatest hits!
