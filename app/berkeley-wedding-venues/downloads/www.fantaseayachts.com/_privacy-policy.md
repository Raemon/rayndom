# Privacy Policy

Privacy Policy[Fantasea Yachts](https://www.fantaseayachts.com/author/fsadmin/ "Posts by Fantasea Yachts")2025-06-03T10:20:14-07:00

At FantaSea Yachts, we value the privacy of our customers and website visitors. In general, it is our policy not to sell, rent or share any personal information about our customers with third parties. Please read below for more details about our practices. Note that you can find this policy at any time by following the link at the bottom of every page on this website.

## What information do we collect from our website visitors?

We collect basic contact information from you when you fill out one of the forms on this website, such as the [general contact form](https://www.fantaseayachts.com/contact/) or the [request a quote](https://www.fantaseayachts.com/quote/) form. We may also ask for basic information about an event that you are planning. We do not ask for credit card numbers or other sensitive personal or financial information online.

## Mobile information protection

We do not share mobile phone numbers or other mobile device information with third parties or affiliates for marketing or promotional purposes. Text messaging opt-in data and consent information are treated with heightened protection and will not be shared with any third parties under any circumstances.

## What do we use your information for?

We use the information collected in the forms to respond to, and better understand the nature of, your requests and inquiries so that we may respond to you as effectively and efficiently as possible.

If you provide a story for us to share on the website, we reserve the right to use all or part of that story online or in printed marketing materials, unless you request otherwise.

We also reserve the right to publish any photographs of events held at FantaSea Yachts online or in printed marketing materials, unless you request otherwise.

## Our commitment to data security

To prevent unauthorized access, maintain data accuracy, and ensure the correct use of information, we have put in place appropriate physical, electronic, and managerial procedures to safeguard and secure the information we collect online.

## 

## Do we disclose any information to outside parties?

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer to outside parties your personally identifiable information. This does not include trusted third parties who assist us in operating our website, conducting our business, or servicing you, so long as those parties agree to keep this information confidential. We may also release your information when we believe release is appropriate to comply with the law, enforce our site policies, or protect ours or others rights, property, or safety. However, non-personally identifiable visitor information may be provided to other parties for marketing, advertising, or other uses.

Opt-Out info: The information you provide is used to fulfill you specific request. This information is only used to fulfill your specific request, unless you give us permission to use it in another manner, for example to add you to one of our mailing lists. Your personally identifiable information is kept secure. Only authorized employees, agents and contractors (who have agreed to keep information secure and confidential) have access to this information. All emails and newsletters from this site allow you to opt out of further mailings.

Third Party Info: We may also use the information you provide to contact you via email or surveys to gain your opinions regarding previously delivered services or regarding potential new services that may be offered. In addition, FantaSea Yachts may share data with trusted partners to help us perform statistical website performance analysis; send you email or postal mail; or provide customer support. All such third parties are prohibited from using your personal information except to provide these services to FantaSea Yachts.

## 

## California Online Privacy Protection Act compliance

Because we value your privacy we have taken the necessary precautions to be in compliance with the California Online Privacy Protection Act. We therefore will not distribute your personal information to outside parties without your consent.
