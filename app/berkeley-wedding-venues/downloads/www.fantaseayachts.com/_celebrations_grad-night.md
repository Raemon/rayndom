## MAKE GRAD NIGHT A YACHT PARTY

Your last big party with all your friends from school should be a BLAST!! From DJ and dancing to casino tables to photos, our Special Event Producers can’t wait to put together the perfect Grad Night for your class.

### UNFORGETTABLE CELEBRATIONS

Soon our graduates will don the cap and gown and walk across a stage to cheers and the applause of their friends and family beaming with pride. But before that – it’s time to party! A night of dancing on a yacht is the perfect sendoff to an awesome end of your high school years. You’ll fill up your social feeds with all the memories you’ll build on this special night. Easy to customize with a theme, we have **luxury yachts** that can accommodate parties from 50 to 250.

### ALWAYS SMOOTH SAILING

With FantaSea Yachts, your yacht party will travel through the tranquil harbor of Marina del Rey amongst 8.5 miles of scenic waterway where the scenic views of the Los Angeles coastline and Santa Monica Bay are breathtaking. There is virtually no risk of seasickness or motion sickness cruising these calm waters.

Gather the committee and come down to check us out!
