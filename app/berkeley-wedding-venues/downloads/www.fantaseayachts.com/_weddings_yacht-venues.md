# UNFORGETTABLE CELEBRATIONS

FantaSea Yachts owns and operates three luxury premium yachts. Each of our yachts offers a unique experience for your guests and can be catered to the specific needs of your event. FantaSea Yachts is the premier destination for Marina del Rey boat rentals. From luxurious well appointed yachts that can host up to 250 guests to intimate luxury day trips for 12.

### Always Smooth Sailing

Our yachts cruise comfortably inside the tranquil harbor of Marina del Rey amongst 8.5 miles of scenic waterway including beautiful views of Santa Monica Bay and the Los Angeles coastline.

We are available Monday – Friday 9:00 a.m. – 6:00 p.m. and Saturday 10:00 a.m. – 5:00 p.m. by appointment. We invite you to visit our venues and tour the best boat rentals Marina del Rey has to offer.
