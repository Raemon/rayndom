####  ____How far in advance should we book our corporate event?

We recommend booking your corporate event 2-6 months in advance, especially for popular weekend dates and holiday parties (November-December), summer team building events, or year-end celebrations. However, we understand corporate schedules can be dynamic, and we’ll work to accommodate shorter notice bookings when possible. Peak season dates require more advance planning.
