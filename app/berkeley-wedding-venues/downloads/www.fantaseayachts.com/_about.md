# About Us

About Us[Fantasea Yachts](https://www.fantaseayachts.com/author/fsadmin/ "Posts by Fantasea Yachts")2023-02-27T12:55:05-08:00

FantaSea Yachts was established in 1980 so that our Southern California neighbors could host yacht parties, weddings, mitzvahs, corporate events aboard our world-class fleet of luxury yachts in beautiful Marina del Rey.

We are a special-event company that has taken [yacht charters](/yachts/) and rentals to the next level. The attention and exemplary service that we have provided to our clients for the past 40 years has earned us the reputation as a premier destination for unforgettable celebrations on the water.

Founded in 1980 by Uri Ginzburg and soon joined by son Daniel and daughter-in-law Stephanie, our team includes professional planners, designers, caterers, chefs, captains, crew members, yacht rentals associates, office staff, and more many who have worked at FantaSea for 15-20 years or more. We are 100% committed to providing you, our guest of honor, and your guests with the very best experience and the most professional service that one could ever wish for. We rely on word-of-mouth and referrals for our business and take the care to give all your guests the same incredible experience.

For nearly four decades, we have provided cherished moments, superior service and luxury for every budget. At FantaSea Yachts, we treat every guest as if they were a member of our own family.
