## STYLISH QUINCEAÑERA & DEBUTS

What will make your blossoming young lady feel like a princess as she greets her court? Hosting her Quinceañera or Debut Party aboard her own beautiful luxury yacht at FantaSea Yachts in Marina del Rey, California!

### UNFORGETTABLE CELEBRATIONS

With four different venues to choose from, including three custom-built luxury yachts, we are certain to have the perfect venue for this special day. Whether you’re inviting 50 or up to 250 of your closest friends and family, we will help you take care of all your Quinceañera and Debut party planning. From catering, bar, entertainment and decorations, our Special Event Producers and yachts for rent will work along with you to plan the perfect Party.

### ALWAYS SMOOTH SAILING

Our yachts travel through the tranquil harbor of Marina del Rey amongst 8.5 miles of scenic waterway where the scenic views of the Los Angeles coastline and Santa Monica Bay are breathtaking. There is virtually no risk of seasickness or motion sickness cruising these calm waters.
