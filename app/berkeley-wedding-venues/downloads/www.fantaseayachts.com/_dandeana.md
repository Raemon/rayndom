# Dandeana

## OUR LUXURIOUS AND INTIMATE FLAGSHIP

If you are looking to rent a yacht, Dandeana may just be the one. Our most intimate yacht, she is the flagship of the FantaSea fleet, accommodating up to 100 guests with a sleek, contemporary, custom-built tri-level design.

### OVERVIEW

Dandeana has European-design accents, and is 110 feet long with a width of 24 feet. The luxurious Main Salon has modern design accents, complete with sofas, lounge furniture and lighting effects as well as a wall-mounted plasma screen welcoming your guests in style. The second floor Bar and Party Deck are equipped with an additional wall-mounted plasma screen for your DVD presentation, and large sliding glass windows for spectacular views and intimate dining and dancing under a sparkling crystal chandelier. Level three, the open Sky Deck, offers the best views of the marina and is perfect for catching every minute of your event on the tranquil waters of Marina del Rey. In addition, she has a full walk-around deck to enjoy the sights as you cruise.

### HISTORY

Dandeana is the company’s flagship yacht, built personally by founder Uri Ginzburg and named for his children, Daniel and Deana. The Dandeana has been featured in movies, on TV, and in print media. With three levels, she can accommodate up to 100 guests in comfort and style. Dandeana has cruised the waters of the Caribbean and the Mexican Riviera. Need a dose of the good life? If you’re going to rent a yacht, consider Dandeana your personal VIP experience for friends and family.
