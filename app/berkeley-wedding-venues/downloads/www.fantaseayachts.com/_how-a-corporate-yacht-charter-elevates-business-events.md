## Benefits of Corporate Yacht Charters 

First, let’s dive into the reasons why a business of any size or industry would choose to host an event on a yacht. 

### 1\. Exquisite Ambiance and Setting 

[Marina del Rey](https://visitmdr.com/) is one of the most gorgeous places to visit in California. It provides an amazing backdrop for any event you have in mind. Marina del Rey is known for tranquil waters, natural beauty, and its proximity to Los Angeles. 

![Corporate holiday parties in Marina del Rey](https://www.fantaseayachts.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/08/corporate-holiday-parties-on-a-yacht.png)

A corporate yacht party is the perfect way to break the ice in a unique setting.

### 2\. Networking Opportunities on the Water

Networking is a vital aspect of any business event, and the intimate setting of a yacht encourages meaningful connections. 

With no crowded conference halls or noisy ballrooms, attendees engage in productive conversations while enjoying the fresh sea breeze and stunning views. Whether you’re strengthening existing partnerships or forging new ones, a corporate yacht charter sets the stage for meaningful interactions.

### 3\. Exclusivity and Privacy

Privacy is often a concern for business events. By its very nature, a corporate yacht rental is private – you have the whole yacht at your disposal. Having an entire luxury yacht dedicated to your event reinforces the importance and exclusivity of the occasion. Your guests will be impressed! 

### 4\. Unforgettable Experiences

The unique experience of being on a yacht creates memories that attendees will cherish. From watching the sunset on the deck to enjoying gourmet dining with a waterfront view, corporate yacht charters elevate events into unforgettable experiences that attendees will talk about for years to come.

### 5\. Brand Elevation

Hosting an event with a [yacht charter rental](https://www.fantaseayachts.com/) sends a powerful message about your company’s commitment to excellence and innovation. It adds a level of sophistication and grandeur that can enhance your brand’s reputation. 

Whether you’re impressing clients, investors, or employees, a yacht charter showcases your dedication to going above and beyond.

In the next section, we’ll delve deeper into the unique features that make corporate yacht charters stand out from traditional event venues.

Not only are yacht charters a welcomed change of scenery, but they have many qualities that make them a top choice for any **corporate event venue**. Here are some features that set yacht charters apart from traditional venues.

The layout can be customized according to your needs. Whether it’s a formal meeting with boardroom-style seating or a casual networking event with lounge areas. The expansive decks allow you to take full advantage of the scenic surroundings, making every moment special.

### 2\. Gourmet Dining Experience

Of course, food creates another opportunity to impress. Our chefs are experts at pairing the perfect menu for any occasion. At FantaSea yachts, we’ve hosted everything from formal banquets and cocktail receptions to grand buffets. Something memorable and fun to try: one of our clients asked for special branded cookies for their product roll-out. 

### 3\. Seamless Onboard Services

Expect top-notch service from a professional crew. From attentive waitstaff to experienced event coordinators, the crew ensures that every detail of your event is meticulously executed. This level of service contributes to the overall excellence of the yacht charter experience.

## Business Events to Host on a Corporate Yacht Charter

A yacht party rental caters to a variety of business functions and gatherings. Let’s explore types of business events that are enhanced with a corporate yacht experience. 

### 1 Board Meeting in Marina del Rey

Calm and serene, a board meeting hosted on a yacht sets a tone of importance and allows participants to focus on the meeting without distraction. The unique setting is a catalyst for creativity and focus. 

### 2\. Conferences and Seminars 

Corporate yacht charters provide a distinctive backdrop for conferences and seminars. 

The novelty of the location ensures that presentations and workshops are memorable and productive. Build in breaks for networking or strolling on the deck between speakers. 

Depending on the number of attendees you’re expecting, we found that the largest yacht in our fleet, [the FantaSea One](https://www.fantaseayachts.com/yachts/fantasea-one/), is perfect for conferences. 

### 3\. Product Launches and Showcases

Introduce a new product or service in an unforgettable venue. One of our corporate clients told us that the exclusivity of the yacht and the backdrop of Marina del Rey added a sense of anticipation and excitement to their [promotional event](https://www.fantaseayachts.com/corporate-events/promotional-events/). 

### 4\. Team-Building Escape

Solidify team relationships with the ideal team-building event on a yacht. Group games themed parties, or simply sharing a meal in an incredible location foster camaraderie and allow team members to deepen their relationships

### 5\. Client Entertainment

Strengthen business relationships with a corporate yacht outing. Outside the office or conference room setting, show clients they are valued and appreciated. Whether it’s a casual dinner cruise or a more formal event, a yacht charter adds prestige to client entertainment.

### 6\. Corporate Holiday Party

Holiday parties are a great way to celebrate organizational milestones or to simply enjoy some unstructured downtime. [Revitalize with a corporate holiday party](https://www.fantaseayachts.com/yacht-escape-revitalize-with-a-corporate-holiday-party/) to cultivate company culture and show appreciation for a job well done. 

### 7\. Intimate Executive Retreat

A yacht retreat is the perfect getaway for executives who want a private and exclusive experience. It’s the ideal environment for strategic planning sessions, leadership workshops, or executive coaching programs.

Whether you’re planning a formal business event or something more casual, a party yacht rental is a unique company outing idea.

### 1 Define Event Objectives

Understand the type of event you’re planning and what the outcome will be. Are you looking to impress clients, increase brand awareness, or promote team bonding? Understanding your goals will help to guide you on the type of yacht, itinerary, and budget. 

### 2 Choose the Right Yacht

Select which [Marina del Rey yacht charter](https://www.fantaseayachts.com/yachts/) that will accommodate the number of expected attendees. ### 3\. Work with Experienced Event Planners

Our in-house event planners have years of experience and specialize in yacht charters for corporate events. We handle logistics, catering, entertainment, setup and takedown. We will partner with you to create a professional and original experience for your guests.
