# Plan an Epic Corporate Yacht Party

21

Categories: [Yacht Party](https://www.fantaseayachts.com/category/yacht-party/)

![corporate yacht party in marina del rey](https://www.fantaseayachts.com/wp-content/uploads/2022/12/corporate-yacht-party-marina-del-rey.png)

Gone are the days of corporate parties in banquet rooms, hotel conference rooms, or at business headquarters. Today’s workforce is looking for new experiences – something different. Exciting. Exclusive. 

And that’s where a[ company party on a yacht](https://www.fantaseayachts.com/4-reasons-why-a-company-party-on-a-yacht-is-a-genius-idea) delivers.

At FantaSea Yachts, we’ve hosted hundreds (err, thousands) of corporate events. From business mixers to luncheons and sunset cocktail cruises, there is no better place than Marina del Rey to explore [yacht party rentals](https://www.fantaseayachts.com/celebrations/) and sit back, relax and toast to your teammates. 

Here are our top tips for planning a [yacht party](https://www.fantaseayachts.com/corporate-events/holiday-parties/) for your next corporate bash.

## **Create a yacht party experience**

  
One of the biggest perks to booking with FantaSea Yachts is that our yachts are a blank canvas, ready to be transformed to meet your vision.   
  
That means endless possibilities to wow your guests.

![Having fun with a yacht party rental ](https://www.fantaseayachts.com/wp-content/uploads/2022/12/yacht-party-rentals-300x220.png)

Consider a [night yacht party](https://www.fantaseayachts.com/yacht-party-day-or-night/) where you can celebrate under the twinkling stars and shimmering moonlit waters. Or have a DJ play a set that will have you dancing into the night. 

Enjoy specialty libations with a premier tasting. Wine? Tequila? Whiskey? Top it off with a food pairing to maximize your experience. 

Set the tone with live entertainment like a jazz trio or a fun cover band.

Offer a [unique team building activity](https://blog.hubspot.com/marketing/creative-team-outing-ideas). From cigar rolling stations to a friendly game of corn hole or life-size Jenga, our event space can be easily outfitted to accommodate activities. 

The sky’s the limit — especially when celebrating aboard our yacht sky decks.

## **A VIP vibe for your VIPs**

  
A corporate party is a perfect opportunity to make your team feel like the VIPs they are.

At FantaSea Yachts, we always roll out the red carpet for our events – and we mean it.

![elegant yacht party as a corporate event](https://www.fantaseayachts.com/wp-content/uploads/2022/12/elegant-company-party-on-a-yacht-300x220.png)

When boarding our luxury yacht fleet, guests are greeted by a red carpet that sets the stage for the special experience that awaits (and you might even see a colleague or two strutting their stuff!) 

One of the hallmarks of our company is that we take pride in delivering truly exceptional hospitality. Our staff is dedicated to ensuring that our guests walk away feeling like a million bucks.  
  
It’s seen in our service. In the details of our setup. In our professionalism. 

And we know that our signature hospitality will make your guests feel like the ultimate VIPs.

Isn’t that worth celebrating?

## **Customize your menu**

Food is almost always the most i[mportant element of any party.](https://www.tasteofhome.com/collection/easy-party-food-ideas/) Customize your menu to please the delectable palates of your guests – and their dietary needs.  
  
Allergies. Gluten-Free. Vegan. Vegetarian. Pescatarian. Kosher.   
  
We’ve seen and served it all. Or have made arrangements to accommodate special requests. 

When everyone feels cared for, the better the experience.   
  
That’s part of the FantaSea way.

The secret behind any great event is having [the right team](https://www.fantaseayachts.com/about/our-staff/) to plan it and execute.

  * Brainstorm creative ideas.
  * Bring your vision to life with every last detail.
  * Handle any hiccups with ease and confidence.
  * Facilitate an easy breezy set up and effortless takedown.



And at FantaSea Yachts, we do all that. And more.  
  
When you plan a corporate yacht party with us, our seasoned team of event professionals is with you every step of the way. We are at your side from your first consultation throughout the entire event planning.

We handle it all. 

This ensures that you have the best of the best (and peace of mind) – at an incredible value.

A winning combo you can’t beat. 

## **Add your signature flair**

![decorated yacht for a company event](https://www.fantaseayachts.com/wp-content/uploads/2022/12/decorated-yacht-for-a-company-event-300x220.png)

Get creative with your branding and signage. There are so many ways to put your personal stamp on your event. 

Light up the dance floor with your company logo.

Choose a take home gift that thoughtfully represents your business and brand.

Have fun at the bar with signature cocktails (and mocktails) that represent your business. Or perhaps a playful inside joke amongst the team. 

Go big with a custom branded ice sculpture.

Get flashy with neon signage. 

Consider custom napkins and t-shirts for additional promotion.

Stay true to your brand and your employees to maximize the experience.

## **Enjoy the view**

What’s a [party yacht](https://www.fantaseayachts.com/celebrations/) without a view? It’s not only a selling point for your guests, but a breathtaking ocean view is a memorable experience.

Whether you’re snapping photos for the ‘gram.

Finding solitude in the sunset.

Feeling inspired for your new goals and dreams.

Delighting in the playful dolphins or barking seals. 

Finding amusement in the yacht views and clever vessel names. 

Embracing the California magic.

Our yachts provide front-row seats to the incredible wonder of our coast.

It’s a unique and special perk of celebrating aboard our luxury yacht fleet.
