# Stephanie Ginzburg

### ![](https://www.fantaseayachts.com/wp-content/uploads/2018/01/stephanie-ginzburg-240x300.jpg) Creative Director

Stephanie Ginzburg is the Senior Event Producer and Creative Director of FantaSea Yachts. Stephanie, a Dallas native, moved to Los Angeles and joined FantaSea in 1994 when she was just twenty-one and ready to conquer the world! She quickly was promoted for her events management skills and attention to detail.

Clients fall in love with Stephanie and her attention to detail, her commitment to perfection and her willingness to go to all lengths to make the client’s occasion the best experience ever. She is an asset to each of her clients that she works with and takes the time to make each event a special memorable and original moment.

Stephanie is constantly working to improve herself because she believes that the key to success is a willingness to learn new things. She is a member of the Association of Bridal Consultants, Meeting Planners International, and the International Special Events Society and she always has a smile on her face and her thumb on the latest industry trends.

Working alongside her family everyday Stephanie sees as a bonus and attributes much of the company’s success to the familial environment. In addition to her position at FantaSea Yachts, she is kept busy with her family life. Stephanie and Daniel have four kids.
