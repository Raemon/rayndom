Are you looking for a unique way to treat your employees and colleagues to a summer office getaway? Look no further than [summer corporate event cruise](https://www.fantaseayachts.com/corporate-events/)s in Marina del Rey, CA! 

Corporate event cruises have become a popular option for companies looking to provide their employees with a unique and enjoyable experience. Given that many companies have adopted remote or hybrid workforce practices, in-person events are more important than ever. 

Marina del Rey, located in Southern California, is a top destination for corporate [party yacht rentals](https://www.fantaseayachts.com/celebrations/) and the home of the FantaSea Yachts fleet. 

50% of companies planning corporate events say that the first events they’re organizing are team building events to bring colleagues back together. 

In this post, we’ll explore why corporate event cruises are the perfect summer office getaway, and highlight the benefits of hosting your event in Marina del Rey.

## **An Exceptional Summer Yacht Rental**

Summer is the perfect time to host a corporate event cruise in Marina del Rey, and let us tell you why. The summer months offer endless opportunities for outdoor activities, providing the ideal setting for team building and networking both on and off the yacht. 

With the sun shining and the Pacific Ocean beckoning, your team can enjoy a range of exciting water-based activities such as fishing, kayaking, and paddle boarding should you choose to extend your yacht party.

![a corporate yacht party in Marina del Rey](https://www.fantaseayachts.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/04/a-corporte-yacht-party-in-marina-del-rey-300x220.png)

Summer is also the perfect time to take advantage of the beautiful Marina del Rey weather. The warm and sunny climate provides the perfect backdrop for beach bonfires and sunset cruises, allowing your team to unwind and relax while taking in the stunning views of the marina.

Furthermore, the summer season offers an opportunity to celebrate team milestones and successes in a fun and memorable way. Imagine celebrating a major project milestone with a luxury yacht party, complete with gourmet food, drinks, and breathtaking views of the Pacific Ocean.

## **Easy Access to Everything**

Marina del Rey, California – a quaint seaside community that boasts the largest man-made marina in the world. A perfect destination for those seeking a luxurious and unforgettable corporate event cruise. Located just minutes from Los Angeles International Airport, Marina del Rey offers stunning views of the Pacific Ocean and a range of activities to keep your team engaged and entertained. This isn’t your average corporate retreat. No, this is a chance to break free from the mundane and indulge in the finer things in life.

Besides, Marina del Rey is also home to a range of hotels and resorts that can accommodate your team before or after the cruise. This allows for a seamless and stress-free experience for everyone involved, as all accommodations and activities can be conveniently located in one place.

## **Networking Opportunities**

Corporate event cruises offer the perfect opportunity to network with colleagues and build professional relationships in a relaxed and enjoyable atmosphere. Networking is[ a crucial aspect ](https://www.forbes.com/sites/biancamillercole/2019/03/20/why-networking-should-be-at-the-core-of-your-career/?sh=5dbe24431300)of any corporate event, and corporate event cruises offer the perfect opportunity to build professional relationships. 

Unlike traditional networking events, [a corporate yacht party](https://www.fantaseayachts.com/6-reasons-why-a-corporate-yacht-party-is-the-perfect-team-building-experience/) provides a unique and fun-filled environment that encourages attendees to let their guard down and engage with their colleagues in a more natural way.

On a corporate event cruise in Marina del Rey, your team can mix and mingle over delicious food and drinks while taking in the breathtaking views of the marina. The laid-back atmosphere allows for organic conversations to flow, and attendees can connect with colleagues they may not have had the chance to interact with in the office.

![A corporate event on a party yacht in Marina del Rey](https://www.fantaseayachts.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/04/host-a-corporate-event-on-a-party-yacht-300x220.png)

Corporate event cruises also provide a great opportunity for team members to network with individuals from other departments or branches of the company, fostering a sense of collaboration and connection that can translate into increased productivity and success.

## **Team Building Activities**

FantaSea Yachts offers the perfect venue for unique team building activities. Our yachts offer privacy, safely, and beautifully designed areas for a fun and productive offsite adventure. 

Spending time together on a yacht outside of the work environment allows colleagues to bond in a relaxed and enjoyable setting, fostering a sense of camaraderie and teamwork that can translate into increased collaboration and productivity in the workplace.

As a matter of fact, team building activities on a yacht can provide a unique and rewarding experience for employees. From promoting teamwork to developing essential skills, these activities can have a positive impact on workplace culture and employee satisfaction.

## **Productivity Tips**

A change of scenery can boost productivity. A corporate event cruise offers a chance to break away from the office and get work done in a new setting. Maybe your corporate getaway is more of a work-cation. That’s ok. 

Certainly, a well-organized event will address the most pressing issues first. Be sure to clarify your objectives in advance. You could start by picking one process to improve or simply getting feedback from your team on systems or processes that could use some change. 

One of our clients, a small start-up company, found that the change in scenery helped team members to be less guarded. The outcome resulted in the implementation of company wide positivity goals.
