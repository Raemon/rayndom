## BEAUTIFUL CEREMONIES ON THE WATER

Saying farewell to a loved one is never easy, so we at FantaSea are sensitive to the emotional challenges of planning a memorial celebration. If you’d like to rent a yacht to pay tribute to a special person, we can help you turn a somber day into a celebration of a life well-lived.

### UNFORGETTABLE CELEBRATIONS

Life should be celebrated. We help set the stage for a celebration of life where the closest friends and family gather, stories are shared, eyes mist over, and laughter rings out. A celebration where everyone can remember the loved one as vivacious as they once were.

### ALWAYS SMOOTH SAILING

Our yachts travel through the tranquil harbor of Marina del Rey amongst 8.5 miles of scenic waterway where the scenic views of the Los Angeles coastline and Santa Monica Bay are breathtaking. There is virtually no risk of seasickness or motion sickness cruising these calm waters.

## OUR SPECIALISTS WOULD LOVE TO MEET YOU

Working with one of our professional Special Event Producers, we will help you rent a yacht for the perfect farewell event, stamped with heartfelt memories of the departed.
