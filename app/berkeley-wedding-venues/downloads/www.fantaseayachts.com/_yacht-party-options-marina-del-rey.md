Pick a yacht, any yacht. Whether you are looking to throw an intimate gathering or a large-scale bash, finding the perfect yacht is essential. With so many options in Marina del Rey, it can be difficult to determine which [yacht rental](https://www.fantaseayachts.com/celebrations/) is right for your event.

Really. At FantaSea Yachts, you have the luxury (and flexibility) of selecting a **Marina del Rey yacht** based on your event needs and vision. From spacious rooftop decks to elegant chandeliers, we’re confident our luxury yacht fleet will deliver an unforgettable venue for your celebration. 

Not sure which yacht is best for you? Let’s break it down.  
  
**FantaSea One** : Whether you’re celebrating a wedding, conducting corporate events, or just looking for the perfect sunset cruise to start off your weekend right FantaSea One will not disappoint. The largest of our fleet, [FantaSea One](https://www.fantaseayachts.com/yachts/fantasea-one/) is sure to WOW your guests. 

Her signature feature is an expansive rooftop deck which can be used as a seating area while also providing enough room on board for spectacular views all around! Three additional decks allow for different views and settings. 

This modern yacht offers unique features that allow for personalization and an elegant experience. Such features include a modern Captain’s Lounge, a formal customizable seating area, and a beautiful marble bar that will quickly become a gathering place for guests. 

The large size doesn’t stop there either; this vessel has a 300 guest capacity so don’t hesitate to pack that dance floor! Learn more below!

Learn more below!

* * *

**Dandeana** : Our Flagship yacht, [Dandeana](https://www.fantaseayachts.com/yachts/dandeana/) is a popular choice for a reason: She’s a beaut. 

Whether you’re looking for a sophisticated yacht with modern amenities and stunning views, or want an unforgettable experience that’s different from the norm—Dandeana is your go-to. Her sleek design will make it easy to find space on board! Plus guests won’t be able to resist her sparkling glass windows offering spectacular ocean sunsets as well as moonlit sky moments. 

Its deck (though smaller than FantaSea One), offers guests the opportunity to experience rooftop views of ocean sunsets and moonlit skies.

Perhaps you’re looking for a beach wedding without the muss and fuss of sand. Dandeana is the perfect alternative to a beach wedding. She can accommodate up to 130 guests and is often recognized for appearances in popular movies and TV shows. Learn more below!

* * *

**Admiral XL** : If you’re looking to grab your posses and jetset for the weekend in true **VIP yacht party** fashion, our [Admiral XL Yacht ](https://www.admiralxl.com/)is for you. The smallest of our fleet, our Admiral XL is lavishly appointed with luxurious touches. She can welcome up to twelve guests making her the perfect accommodation for an overnight cruise to Catalina or an afternoon basking along the California coast. Learn more below!

When you schedule a consultation with us, we’re happy to give you a personalized tour of our luxury yacht fleet. We offer yacht rentals for all types of events, ranging from small yacht weddings to large yacht parties. And no matter the yacht, our hope is that you can easily envision your event the moment you step onboard — whether it’s a boat wedding, milestone birthday bash, anniversary party, retirement celebration, family reunion, quinceanera, high school prom, or jet-setting yacht charter. The list (and possibilities) are endless!
