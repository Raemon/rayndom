### OVERVIEW

The FantaSea One is the largest, most luxurious yacht in Marina del Rey and is designed much like a mini cruise ship. Her four expansive decks allow for a variety of scenery and a spatial progression to your event, unparalleled both with other yachts or land-based event locations.

Your guests are welcomed aboard the Captain’s Lounge, complete with modern lounge features and a wall-mounted plasma screen. One level up, the Admiral’s Dining Deck, a formal seating space wrapped with huge bay windows, can seat guests formally for dinner. Proceeding upward, the sweeping expanse of the Party Deck welcomes your guests next, replete with a huge dance floor, a large marble bar and 360º views of the Marina through its sliding glass windows. Saving the best for last, the Sky Deck crowns FantaSea One – a beautiful open deck with the sky as its ceiling; perfect for admiring sunsets or star light, providing a stunning natural setting for your wedding or other important ceremony.

FantaSea One is a dream for both guests and party planners alike, as she offers multiple levels of creativity for your vision. [Read more about the FantaSea One](https://www.fantaseayachts.com/yachts/fantasea-one/faq/).

### HISTORY

FantaSea One is an ultra-modern yacht that can comfortably accommodate your guests in ultimate style. She is the best yacht for charter in Marina del Rey and functions much like a mini cruise ship. She features four decks, a welcoming lounge area, formal dining room, dance floor deck and a full length open sky deck. She has also been featured in many prominent movie productions as well as commercial and publicity launches.
