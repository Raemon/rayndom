When you think of a [yacht wedding](https://www.fantaseayachts.com/weddings/), your mind might immediately jump to the ceremony and reception. But why limit the maritime magic to just the big day? At FantaSea Yachts, we’ve seen countless couples leverage the allure of our yacht fleet to create unforgettable moments throughout their entire wedding journey. From the instant you say “yes” to the final farewell after your nuptials, a boat wedding experience can elevate every step of your path to “I do.”

Let’s embark on a voyage through the myriad ways you can incorporate yacht rentals into your wedding events, creating a series of [nautical-themed celebrations](https://www.bridalguide.com/planning/the-details/theme/nautical-themed-wedding-ideas) that will have your guests talking for years to come.

## The Proposal: Setting Sail on Your Journey Together

Imagine this: the gentle lapping of waves against the hull, a golden sunset painting the sky, and the love of your life dropping to one knee on the deck of a luxurious yacht. A boat proposal offers an intimate, romantic setting that’s hard to beat. Here’s how to make it unforgettable:

– Arrange a private sunset cruise

– Coordinate with our staff for champagne and roses to be ready at the perfect moment

– Have a photographer discreetly on board to capture the magical moment

– Follow up with a celebration dinner on the yacht with close family and friends

## Engagement Party: Announce Your News in Style

Once you’ve said “yes,” it’s time to share the news with your loved ones. An engagement party on a yacht sets the tone for the celebrations to come:

– Host a cocktail cruise around Marina del Rey

– Use the yacht’s [multiple decks ](https://www.fantaseayachts.com/yachts/fantasea-one/)to create different atmospheres – perhaps a dance floor on one level and a quieter area for conversations on another

– Take advantage of the picturesque backdrop for engagement photos

– Surprise your guests with fireworks over the water (permit required)

## Bachelor and Bachelorette Parties: A Nautical Last Hurrah

Why settle for a typical night out when you can have an unforgettable bachelor or bachelorette party on a yacht?

– Set up a spa day on the yacht with massages and treatments

– Host a themed party – perhaps a “Pirates of the Caribbean” costume party or a glamorous “Yacht Rock” celebration

– Arrange for a professional mixologist to create [signature cocktails](https://www.brides.com/story/summer-wedding-cocktail-ideas) for the group

## Bridal Shower: Sail into Marital Bliss

A [bridal shower](https://www.theknot.com/content/bridal-shower-themes?srsltid=AfmBOoqXjgaEcTuBAtcMx1Nu71yiaYJcD5Py9emV1OUc2CGSHqYuErqp) on a yacht adds an extra layer of sophistication to this traditional pre-wedding event:

– Plan a champagne brunch cruise

– Set up interactive stations for activities like flower crown-making or perfume blending

– Use the yacht’s entertainment systems for bridal shower games

– Offer a “tea at sea” experience with a selection of fine teas and pastries

![bridal placesetting, overhead shot, pinks and blues with gold charger](https://www.fantaseayachts.com/wp-content/uploads/2024/08/annie-gray-MO05fXiDobo-unsplash-1024x683.jpg)

Get creative with rehearsal dinner place settings and play with travel or nautical themes. Photo by Annie Gray on Unsplash.

## Rehearsal Dinner: Practice Makes Perfect

The rehearsal dinner sets the stage for your wedding day. Make it memorable with these ideas

– Host an elegant seated dinner on the yacht’s main deck

– Use the journey to practice your first dance under the stars

– Give your wedding party a sneak peek of the ceremony location if you’re having a boat wedding

– Present thank-you gifts to your wedding party in this intimate setting

## The Wedding Day: Your Dream Boat Wedding

Of course, the pinnacle of your [yacht rental](https://www.fantaseayachts.com/weddings/yacht-venues/) experience will be your actual wedding day. Here’s how to make your yacht wedding truly spectacular

– Exchange vows on the top deck with the ocean as your backdrop

– Have different decks serve different purposes – ceremony, cocktail hour, reception

– Time your first dance to coincide with the sunset

– Offer a seafood-focused menu that complements the maritime setting

## Post-Wedding Brunch: The Perfect Send-Off

Cap off your wedding weekend with a relaxing post-wedding brunch cruise:

– Invite guests to board in comfortable, casual attire

– Offer a build-your-own Bloody Mary or mimosa bar

– Set up a photo slideshow of moments from the wedding day

– Use this time for any gift-giving or final thank-yous

## Why Choose a Yacht for Your Wedding Events?

  1. Unique Experiences: Each event on a yacht offers a one-of-a-kind experience that your guests won’t forget.
  2. Stunning Backdrops: The changing scenery and beautiful water views provide perfect photo opportunities.
  3. Flexible Spaces: Yachts offer various spaces that can be customized for different types of events and group sizes.
  4. All-In-One Venue: Many yachts, like those in the FantaSea fleet, offer catering, bar service, and event planning, simplifying the process.
  5. Intimate Atmosphere: The enclosed nature of a yacht creates an intimate, exclusive feeling for your events.
  6. Weather-Proof: Many yachts offer both indoor and outdoor spaces, ensuring your event can proceed regardless of weather.



## FAQ: Yacht Rentals for Wedding Events

### Q: How far in advance should I book a yacht for my wedding events?

A: We recommend booking as early as possible, especially for peak wedding season dates. For the wedding day itself, 6-12 months in advance is ideal. For other events, 3-6 months should suffice.

### Q: Can I decorate the yacht for my events?

A: Absolutely! We welcome decorations that match your vision. However, we ask that you discuss your plans with our event coordinator to ensure all decorations are safe and appropriate for the maritime environment.

### Q: What if some of my guests get seasick?

A: Our yachts cruise in the calm waters of Marina del Rey, which significantly reduces the chance of seasickness. However, we recommend suggesting that sensitive guests take motion sickness medication before boarding.

### Q: Can we hire our own vendors for things like catering or music?

A: While we have excellent in-house catering and relationships with top-notch vendors, we’re generally open to outside vendors. Please discuss this with your event coordinator.

### Q: Is there a minimum or maximum number of guests for yacht events?

A: This varies depending on the yacht and the type of event. Our vessels can accommodate intimate gatherings of 12 to large celebrations of up to 250 guests. We’ll work with you to choose the perfect yacht for your group size.

### Q: Can we have our legal wedding ceremony on the yacht?

A: Yes! Our captains are licensed to perform legal wedding ceremonies. Alternatively, you’re welcome to bring your own officiant.

### Q: What happens if it rains on the day of our event?

A: Don’t worry! Our yachts have ample indoor space to ensure your event can proceed comfortably regardless of the weather.
