Our fleet accommodates weddings of all sizes. The Dandeana hosts up to 100 guests for an intimate celebration, while FantaSea One—the largest charter yacht in Marina del Rey—can accommodate up to 250 guests. For smaller gatherings, we can arrange yacht weddings for as few as 12 guests.

Our all-inclusive packages cover catering, full bar service, private venue space, professional event coordination, tables, chairs, linens, and a dedicated captain and crew. We also coordinate with your chosen outside vendors including florists, photographers, DJs, and live entertainment to ensure seamless execution of your vision.

####  ____Can we have both our ceremony and reception on the yacht?

Yes! One of the greatest advantages of a yacht wedding is hosting your entire celebration in one stunning location. Your guests will enjoy the ceremony on deck with ocean views, then transition seamlessly into the reception—no transportation between venues required.

####  ____What happens if the weather is bad on our wedding day?

Our yachts feature spacious enclosed interior areas with climate control, so your celebration continues comfortably regardless of weather conditions. Marina del Rey enjoys mild weather year-round, but our experienced crew monitors conditions closely and can adjust the cruising route to ensure smooth sailing.

####  ____How long does a typical yacht wedding cruise last?

Most yacht wedding charters are 4 to 5 hours, which allows ample time for the ceremony, cocktail hour, dinner, dancing, and photos. We can customize the duration based on your specific timeline and needs—just discuss your preferences with our wedding specialists.

We depart from Marina del Rey and cruise along the beautiful Southern California coastline. Your guests will enjoy stunning views of the Pacific Ocean, the marina, and the Los Angeles skyline. The captain can adjust the route based on weather and sunset timing for optimal photo opportunities.

Absolutely. While we handle all catering and bar services in-house, we welcome and coordinate with your preferred outside vendors. Our team has worked with countless photographers, florists, DJs, and officiants over our 40+ years in business and can provide recommendations if needed.

How far in advance should we book our yacht wedding?[Fantasea Yachts](https://www.fantaseayachts.com/author/fsadmin/ "Posts by Fantasea Yachts")2025-12-11T11:37:10-08:00

####  ____How far in advance should we book our yacht wedding?

We recommend booking 6 to 12 months in advance, especially for peak wedding season (spring and summer) and popular dates like Saturdays. However, we occasionally have availability for shorter timelines, so contact us even if your date is approaching—we’ll do our best to accommodate you.

Is there a backup plan if guests get seasick?[Fantasea Yachts](https://www.fantaseayachts.com/author/fsadmin/ "Posts by Fantasea Yachts")2025-12-11T11:37:10-08:00

####  ____Is there a backup plan if guests get seasick?

Our yachts cruise at a gentle pace through the protected waters of Marina del Rey and Santa Monica Bay, which minimizes motion. The vessels are large and stable, and most guests don’t experience any discomfort. For anyone who is sensitive, we recommend they stay on the lower decks where motion is least noticeable.
