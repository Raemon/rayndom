## MAKE IT AN ANNIVERSARY TO REMEMBER

We at FantaSea Yachts are proud to celebrate over 38 years of premium boat rentals in Marina del Rey. We invite you to celebrate your next milestone Anniversary with us!

### UNFORGETTABLE CELEBRATIONS

Whether it’s your first anniversary, 25th or 50th… every year that you are together certainly is special. But for some occasions you just want to go all out! FantaSea Yachts features four gorgeous yachts and limitless party options; with so many choices, we are certain to have the perfect yacht and even more perfect party for you and your special someone. From the bar to the food to the decorations, we handle it all. Need a PA system for speeches or AV set-up for a slide-show? No problem. Interested in an afternoon gathering or an evening soiree? No problem: we’ve got you covered.

### ALWAYS SMOOTH SAILING

From signature cocktails to exquisite desserts, we will be there for you every step of the way to ensure you and your guests have an incredible party. Our yacht parties cruise inside the tranquil harbor of Marina del Rey amongst 8.5 miles of scenic waterway where the scenic views of the Los Angeles coastline and Santa Monica Bay are breathtaking. There is virtually no risk of seasickness or motion sickness cruising these calm waters.
