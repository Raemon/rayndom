## CLASSY COLLEGE FORMALS

The only place in Los Angeles cool and stylish enough to host your College Formal is on your very own luxury yacht charter! Whether you have a group of 50 or a party of 250, we have the perfect venue for you here at FantaSea Yachts. Choose between one of our luxury yachts, each custom built and furnished with modern accessories and the best views.

### UNFORGETTABLE CELEBRATIONS

With over 40 years of event planning experience, one of our talented Special Event Producers will work hand-in-hand with you to create the perfect college cruise event. From custom linens and cocktails to décor and entertainment, your signature will be on every detail. Set in the perfect central location, Marina del Rey is a short drive from UCLA, Loyola Marymount, USC and Pepperdine.

### ALWAYS SMOOTH SAILING

Our yacht charter parties travel through the tranquil harbor of Marina del Rey amongst 8.5 miles of scenic waterway where the scenic views of the Los Angeles coastline and Santa Monica Bay are breathtaking. There is virtually no risk of seasickness or motion sickness cruising these calm waters.
