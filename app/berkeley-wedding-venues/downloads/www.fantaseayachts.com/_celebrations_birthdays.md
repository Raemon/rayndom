## Yacht Birthday Party FAQs

####  ____What is the capacity range for birthday parties on your yachts?

Our fleet can accommodate birthday parties from intimate celebrations to large milestone events. The Dandeana accommodates up to 100 guests, the Admira XL holds up to 12 passengers, and our flagship FantaSea One can host up to 250 guests across its four luxurious decks. Whether you’re celebrating a sweet 16 with close friends or a milestone 50th with extended family and friends, we have the perfect yacht for your guest count.

####  ____What’s included in a typical birthday party package?

Our all-inclusive birthday packages include professional event planning services, dedicated serving staff, premium linens, tables and chairs, selection of passed hors d’oeuvres, non-alcoholic beverage bar, and complete setup and cleanup services. We also provide birthday cake service and can accommodate special dietary requests. Your dedicated event coordinator will work with you to customize every detail.

How far in advance should we book our birthday cruise?[Fantasea Yachts](https://www.fantaseayachts.com/author/fsadmin/ "Posts by Fantasea Yachts")2025-09-24T11:43:56-07:00

####  ____How far in advance should we book our birthday cruise?

We recommend booking 3-6 months in advance, especially for weekend dates and during peak season (May through October). Popular dates like summer weekends and holiday seasons fill up quickly. However, we often can accommodate last-minute bookings depending on availability, so don’t hesitate to call even if your celebration is approaching.

What happens if there’s bad weather on our birthday cruise date?[Fantasea Yachts](https://www.fantaseayachts.com/author/fsadmin/ "Posts by Fantasea Yachts")2025-09-24T11:43:56-07:00

####  ____What happens if there’s bad weather on our birthday cruise date?

Marina del Rey’s protected harbor provides calm waters year-round, and our enclosed decks ensure your celebration continues comfortably regardless of weather conditions. In the rare event of severe weather conditions that could affect safety, we work with you to reschedule your event to a mutually convenient date. Your celebration and guest safety are our top priorities.
