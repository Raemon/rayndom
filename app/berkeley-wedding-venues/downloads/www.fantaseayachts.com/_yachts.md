## WELCOME ABOARD THE FANTASEA YACHT FLEET…

Our beautiful yacht charters, located in scenic Marina del Rey, California, are elegantly-appointed and designed with an incomparable cruising experience in mind.

## Dandeana

Capacity: 100 guests

The [elegant Dandeana](https://www.fantaseayachts.com/yachts/dandeana/) accommodates up to 100 guests. She has a sleek, modern design with contemporary amenities. Rooftop views from the upper deck make the space onboard seem boundless. It’s the ultimate VIP experience!

[__Learn More](/yachts/dandeana/)

## FANTASEA ONE

Capacity: 250 guests

The largest yacht in our fleet, [FantaSea One](https://www.fantaseayachts.com/yachts/fantasea-one/) sports an enormous rooftop deck (one of four expansive decks), modern Captain’s lounge, and a signature marble bar that is a popular gathering place for guests. With a 250 guest capacity, this yacht can host anything from a wedding to large corporate events.

[__Learn More](/yachts/fantasea-one/)

## ADMIRAL XL

Capacity: 12 guests

For an intimate gathering of up to 12 people, the [Admiral XL](https://www.fantaseayachts.com/yachts/admiral-xl/) is the perfect vessel for luxury cruising. The Admiral XL is extravagant and stylish – excellent for high-end cruising for a few hours or overnight.
