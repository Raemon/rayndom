# Why FantaSea?

Why FantaSea?[Fantasea Yachts](https://www.fantaseayachts.com/author/fsadmin/ "Posts by Fantasea Yachts")2023-02-27T13:03:04-08:00

We hope to be your first choice for a yacht party rental. Yes, there are others who offer yacht party rentals – but not one of them offers everything we do or as well as FantaSea Yachts:

### WE CARE…

We have been in the special event business since 1980 and love what we do! We insist on being the best – for ourselves, and for you.

### WE TREAT YOU LIKE FAMILY BECAUSE WE ARE FAMILY

You are entrusting us with your special, important life moments and we appreciate the trust that you put in our hands. At FantaSea Yachts, we are three generations of family working together with one vision: To be the best in all that we do. We treat every guest like a member of our family. We want you to have a beautiful, successful event and to exceed your expectations.

### WE OFFER INCOMPARABLE VALUE

We are dedicated to life’s special moments, providing quality, superior service and luxury for every budget. Your choices are endless! With outstanding value for over 40 years of experience, we have extensive resources and will help you find the best value for all of your event needs. We will handle every step with expert care.

### WE LISTEN TO YOUR NEEDS AND MAKE YOU SHINE!

Yes, this is  _your_ event. Yes, you have full say in what happens. Yes, we have years of experience and resources and we will happily share our expertise. We want it to be unforgettable for  _you_.

### WE ARE GREEN!

We recognize the importance of protecting the environment and using resources intelligently. That commitment to the environment is reflected throughout; from our onboard electrical propulsion systems to our in-house recycling program of glass, plastic and paper, to our catering menus utilizing sustainable and local ingredients. With the Pacific Ocean as our backdrop, we pledge our dedication to taking steps to ensure that our company leaves as small a footprint as possible on our shared earth.

### YOU WILL BE IMPRESSED

We work tirelessly to ensure that you and your guests are happy! Your continued business is our priority. We want you and your guests to return to us again and again for all of life’s special moments. Our success depends on Word of Mouth, Repeat Business and Referral of Friends and Family, therefore, we give every event the utmost attention and insist upon the special care and detail involved with once-in-a-lifetime moments.

FantaSea Yachts is Southern California’s premier destination for [yacht party](https://www.fantaseayachts.com/corporate-events/holiday-parties/) rentals. From 12 to 250 guests, we would love to host your next soiree!
