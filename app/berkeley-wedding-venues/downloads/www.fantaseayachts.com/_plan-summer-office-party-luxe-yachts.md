**This summer, up the ante with your office[yacht party in Marina Del Rey](https://www.fantaseayachts.com/2018/06/28/3-steps-amazing-marina-del-rey-yacht-charter/). Take happy hour to the next level with an incredible sunset cruise. Plan a mixer or networking reception where the ocean is just as part of the event as the attendees. Reward your team with the ultimate VIP experience that reinforces their value and the importance of their contributions–positively impacting company culture and morale. Besides, what’s summer in Los Angeles without an ocean view? ;)  
**

Our Dandeana Yacht at sunset

If you nodded along to any of the scenarios above (and we hope you did), then look no further than [FantaSea Yachts](https://www.fantaseayachts.com/). With more than 35 years of experience, we consider ourselves the best luxury yacht charter company in Los Angeles. We’ve hosted incredible events aboard our yachts–from intimate business receptions to blown-out corporate bashes. And if that’s not enough, we have three gorgeous yachts that can be easily transformed into your own custom floor plan.

  * [FantaSea One](https://www.fantaseayachts.com/yachts/fantaseaone/): Accommodating up to 300 guests, this ultra-modern mega-yacht has four levels, including an expansive sky deck, giving your guests sweeping 360 views.
  * [RegentSea](https://www.fantaseayachts.com/yachts/regentsea/): Contemporary and stylish, this tri-level yacht can accommodate 135 guests and features floor-to-ceiling windows, showing off amazing views.
  * [Dandeana](https://www.fantaseayachts.com/yachts/dandeana/): The flagship of the FantaSea fleet, our Dandeana is a sleek, custom-built tri-level mega yacht that can accommodate up to 130 guests.



One advantage to hosting your event aboard one of our yachts is that the space is exclusively yours. Each event also comes with your own dedicated event planner and our signature VIP hospitality. Our event team will help execute your vision and coordinate logistics, details and vendors (think linens, floral arrangements, entertainment and more). Dreaming up a themed yacht party in Marina Del Rey? We can help with that, too. We’ve seen and done it all from beach-themed bashes and masquerade balls to winter wonderland holiday parties and tech-focused events.

![](https://www.fantaseayachts.com/wp-content/uploads/2018/01/Smashbox-300x225.jpg)

Of course, a good yacht party isn’t complete without food and that’s where our talented culinary team comes in. Our chef (trained at the Ritz-Carlton Laguna Niguel) has served FantaSea Yachts for more than 20 years and can easily dish out distinctive bites for a [cocktail yacht party](https://www.fantaseayachts.com/celebrations/) or prepare a three-course meal for a more formal affair. Our menu is seasonal, but can also be customized to meet dietary needs.

Celebrate summer with FantaSea Yachts. Your guests will LOVE the added bonus of cruising the Marina del Rey harbor, taking in a spectacular ocean sunset, and enjoying the beautiful backdrop of the Los Angeles coastline. Summer is meant to be spent outdoors so why not enjoy it on one of our luxury yachts?
