## Frequently Asked Questions: Birthday Yacht Rentals  


### Q: Can I customize the theme and decor for my birthday party?

A: Absolutely! We encourage personalization. Whether you want a tropical paradise, a glamorous masquerade ball, or any other theme, our event planners will work with you to bring your vision to life.

### Q: What kind of food and beverages are available?

A: We offer gourmet cuisine prepared by talented chefs, tailored to your preferences and dietary requirements. A wide selection of beverages, including fine wines, top-shelf liquors, and signature cocktails, is also available.

### Q: Is there a minimum age requirement for hosting a birthday party on a yacht?

A: While there’s no strict age requirement for the host, all guests must adhere to legal drinking age laws if alcohol is served. We welcome celebrations for all ages, from sweet sixteens to milestone birthdays.

### Q: What should my guests and I wear?

A: Attire can vary depending on your party theme and personal preference. We recommend smart casual wear for most events, but you’re welcome to specify a dress code for your guests that fits your vision.

### Q: Is parking available for my guests?

A: Yes, there is convenient public parking available close to where our yachts are docked in Marina del Rey.

### Q: How far in advance should I book a birthday yacht rental?

A: We recommend booking as early as possible to ensure you get your preferred date, especially during peak seasons. However, we can often accommodate last-minute bookings as well.

### Q: Can I extend my party if we’re having a great time?

A: Subject to availability, it may be possible to extend your celebration. It’s best to discuss this possibility with your event planner in advance.
