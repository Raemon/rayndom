# Halloween Hike & Sip: Moderate

October 25, 2025 @ 5:00 pm \- 8:00 pm

[Tickets – Moderate Hike 5 PM](http://14884.blackbaudhosting.com/14884/tickets?tab=2&txobjid=efdc9f68-128f-4f1a-867e-875600085da1)

As the sun dips below the horizon and shadows stretch through the forest, the Halloween Hike begins! Join us for a moderate, 90-120 minute spine-tingling adventure through the darkened trails of the majestic Oakland Redwood forest, where ghostly tales and eerie surprises await.

Led by our expert guides, this haunted trek combines the thrill of exploration with chilling stories sure to give you goosebumps. Dare to venture into the night and uncover the secrets hidden among the trees. Come in costume to add to the spooky fun—you might even receive an extra treat! This hike is perfect for friends and family who are ready to embrace the Halloween spirit with a mix of adventure and fright.

**Details**

  * Moderate Hike: approximately 2-3 miles, ±230-410 ft gain
  * Duration: 90-120 minutes
  * Cost: $40 per person (21+ only)
  * Includes two drink tickets (beer, wine, or non-alcoholic beverages) and refreshments.
  * For a safe and enjoyable experience, wear sturdy shoes, dress in layers, and bring a flashlight to light your way.



**Important Information**

This event is for adults 21 and over. Please complete the [Hiking Waiver & Consent Form](https://chabotspace.org/wp-content/uploads/2025/01/Chabot-2025-Hiking-Waiver.pdf) prior to your arrival and bring your ticket(s) for a swift check-in.

**Hikes may be canceled due to severe weather or low enrollment. Tickets may also be rescheduled with at least 48 hours’ notice, subject to availability.**

**Group Bookings**

Looking for an exclusive Halloween adventure? Email [groupsales@chabotspace.org](mailto:groupsales@chabotspace.org) to schedule a private haunted hike.

Prepare for a night of thrills, chills, and unforgettable Halloween memories on the haunted trails of Chabot!
