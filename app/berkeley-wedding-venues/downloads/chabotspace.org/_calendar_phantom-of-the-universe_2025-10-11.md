# Phantom of the Universe

October 11, 2025 @ 3:00 pm \- 4:00 pm

### Phantom of the Universe: The Hunt For Dark Matter

**25 minutes  
Ages 8 and up**

An exciting exploration of dark matter, from the Big Bangto its anticipated discovery at the Large Hadron Collider. The show will reveal the first hints ofits existence through the eyes of Fritz Zwicky, the scientist who coined the term “dark matter”. This show is narrated by Tilda Swinton. 

_Lawrence Berkeley National Lab_
