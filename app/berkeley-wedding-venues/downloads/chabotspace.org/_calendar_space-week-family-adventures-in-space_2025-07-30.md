# Space Week: Family Adventures in Space

July 30, 2025 @ 10:00 am \- 5:00 pm

## Space Week: Family Adventures in Space

July 30 – August 1 | 10AM-5PM

**Designed for explorers ages 5–12 with an adult caregiver. All ages and abilities welcome.**

[Buy Tickets](https://14884.blackbaudhosting.com/14884/tickets?tab=3&txobjid=3d868201-c3eb-4a0c-9dd5-2ba9d2a188e4)

Get ready for a full day of epic space adventures! From astronaut training and rocket launches to rover building and habitat design, you’ll explore what it takes to live and work in space. Earn mission stamps as you complete challenges and fill your mission log to win stellar prizes!

### Mission Schedule 

**Astronaut Briefing: Theater, 10:30am-11:00am, 11:30am-12pm, 1:30pm-2:00pm**  
Step into your role as a space explorer at our live Astronaut Briefing. Get insider knowledge, prep for training, and get ready for your interplanetary mission!

**Storytime:****Lower Level** , **10:30–11:00am & 11:45am–12:15pm**  
Need a moment to unwind? Enjoy calming space-themed stories in a cozy environment — perfect for young explorers and their grown-ups.

**Habitat Building: Studio 3, Ongoing**  
Build your dream home on another planet as part of this tinkering activity

**Rocket Launches: Amphitheater, Ongoing through 4:00pm, Break from 12:30pm-1:30pm**  
Build and launch your own paper rocket!

**Build Your Own Rover: Roverscape, Ongoing**  
Design, test, and race your rover across Martian terrain!

**Astronaut Training: Lab 1, 11am–3:30pm**  
Test your skills with agility, teamwork, and Moonwalking challenges.

**Community Drawing: Windows to Tomorrow  
Skybridge, Ongoing through 4pm**  
Imagine you’re on another planet. What do you see? Add your vision to our collaborative mural.

**LifeQuest Nature Walks, Meet in Classroom 1, 11:30am–12:30pm & 3:00–4:00pm **  
Join Chabot educators to explore what defines “life” on Earth and beyond.
