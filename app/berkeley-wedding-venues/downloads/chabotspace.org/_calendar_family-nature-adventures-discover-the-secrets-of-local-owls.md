# Family Nature Adventures: Discover the Secrets of Local Owls!

September 13, 2025 @ 10:30 am \- 12:30 pm

[Buy Tickets](https://14884.blackbaudhosting.com/14884/tickets?tab=2&txobjid=f12b3fd8-ea2d-47ee-b769-dad7aad1d2f8)

**$25 Adult, $35 Youth 3-8, Members receive $10 off**

Your tickets include admission to the Center for the day – **a $19-24 value!**

Step into the magical world of owls during this month’s adventure! Dive into the habits, calls, and diets of local owls. Experience hands-on science as you dissect owl pellets to uncover the mysteries of their meals. Bring your own binoculars or use our child-sized pairs as you join a guided stroll through the redwood forest to search for signs of these majestic birds in their natural habitat. Experience the wonder of the forest and leave with a newfound appreciation for these fascinating creatures. Adventure is calling—don’t miss out!
