##  Raffle packages 

  * ###  A. Ireland’s Timeless Elegance 

Experience the ultimate in luxury with a six-night stay at three prestigious Relais & Châteaux properties: Ballyfin Demesne, Laois, Ireland; Cashel Palace, Ireland; and Park Hotel Kenmare, Ireland. Enjoy two nights at each of these breathtaking destinations, immersing yourself in the beauty and charm of Ireland as you explore its rich culture and stunning landscapes.

  * ###  B. Wine Country Escape 

Savor an unforgettable escape with a luxurious stay and breakfast for two at the Madrona Hotel in Healdsburg, Bardessono Hotel and Spa in Yountville, and Chaminade Resort in Soquel, California. Along with your stays at these exquisite hotels, you’ll be treated to beautiful moments with a curated gift basket from Ritual + Fancy and a pair of stunning earrings by Kelley Hollis Jewelry, inspired by Bay Area sunsets and Art Deco designs. Enjoy complimentary tastings at 32 Winds Winery in Healdsburg, Caymus Winery in Rutherford, Imagery Estate Winery in Glen Ellen, and Soquel Vineyards and Madson Wines in Santa Cruz County.

  * ###  C. La Dolce Vita in Tuscany 

Indulge in the ultimate adult-only Italian getaway with our exclusive raffle package featuring a one-week stay at a luxury penthouse suite in the heart of Tuscany’s wine country. Your Tuscan dream includes a seven-night stay, September 14–21, 2025. Enjoy a private three-bedroom, three-and-a-half bathroom suite in a world-renowned Belmond property, fully equipped gourmet kitchen, and elegant indoor and outdoor living spaces with breathtaking countryside views. This scenic location is just one hour from Florence, in the stunning countryside of Siena — known for its vineyards, charming villages, and rich history. Restrictions and taxes may apply.

  * ###  D. Night Out Around the Bay 

You don’t need to travel far to feel like you’re getting away! From the WNBA inaugural season game of the Golden State Valkyries at the Chase Center to music at Berkeley’s Greek Theatre, we’ve got something for everyone. Also included are award-winning performances at the Presidio Theatre and American Conservatory Theater. Pair your night out with a one-night stay at the exclusive Jay Hotel, along with dinners at the intimate Japanese-style listening bar Yokai and the Michelin-recommended Greek dishes at Evvia.  


![](data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIAAAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAAALAAAAAABAAEAAAIBRAA7) ![Photograph of Bay Bridge in the day time looking at cityscape and bay.](https://www.famsf.org/storage/images/a1af69ba-a6fd-46ce-b232-6b12e7a8e840/d-night-out-around-the-bay.jpg?crop=1594,1063,x6,y56&format=jpg&quality=80&width=1000) ![Photograph of Bay Bridge in the day time looking at cityscape and bay.](https://www.famsf.org/storage/images/a1af69ba-a6fd-46ce-b232-6b12e7a8e840/d-night-out-around-the-bay.jpg?crop=1594,1063,x6,y56&format=jpg&quality=80&width=1000)

Photograph by Erik Rivera  


  * ###  E. Marin County: Hello Sausalito! 

Known for its scenic beauty, what better location for a staycation than Sausalito? Enjoy a one-night stay at the Inn Above Tide and dinner for two at Poggio Trattoria. Dive into the local arts scene with a private tour and artist meet-and-greet at the Sausalito Center for the Arts. Relax with a massage at the spa International Orange, enjoy a meal at Tiburon’s Via Piccola, and take an exclusive behind-the-scenes tour at the Marine Mammal Center. It’s a perfect blend of relaxation, culture, and charm.

  * ###  F. Behind the Curtain 

Revel in a rare opportunity to experience the elegance and thrill of San Francisco’s premier arts venues like never before. This exclusive luxury raffle offers you the chance to witness the magic that unfolds backstage. The package includes a conservation lab tour for six people at the Fine Arts Museums of San Francisco and private backstage tours of the War Memorial Opera House and Smuin Contemporary Ballet. The package also includes tickets to see performances at San Francisco Ballet, SFJazz, San Francisco Opera, and Smuin Contemporary Ballet.

Photograph by Michael O’Neal  


  * ###  G. Taste of the Bay 

This exceptional collection of experiences is designed to tantalize your taste buds and take your dining adventures to new heights, indulging in the finest cuisine San Francisco has to offer. Savor the diverse flavors of the Bay Area with exclusive gift cards to renowned dining spots Bix, City Club of San Francisco, Fang, Harris Restaurant, Holbrook House, and Perry’s. This package also includes a hands-on bread-baking class at Josey Baker Bread.

  * ###  H. Viking European River Cruise 

Sail for eight days on one of Europe’s storied rivers on an award-winning Viking Longship on a 2025–2026 embarkation. Pamper yourself in a standard stateroom, with meals, shore excursions, and tours included. Enjoy onboard amenities such as a restaurant, bar and lounge, library, and expansive sun deck. Viking Longships also feature elegant Scandinavian design, alfresco dining on the indoor/outdoor Aquavit Terrace, and environmentally considerate features. Blackout dates, restrictions, and taxes may apply.
