###  Ticket info 

[Exhibition ticket required](https://ticketing.famsf.org/events/019862d6-11c1-bf67-51f2-f1c0a36b8577). Tours are limited; no advance reservations. Visitors are required to sign up at the coat check on lower level the day of the tour.

Learn about [_Manet & Morisot_](https://www.famsf.org/exhibitions/manet-morisot) with a museum docent.

## **Location + dates**

  * Tours meet at the exhibition entrance on the lower level

  * Every Tuesday through Sunday at 11 am, 12:30 pm, and 2 pm during the exhibition, except Thanksgiving, Christmas, and New Year’s

  * Tours are about one hour

  * Please note: from Saturday, Feb 14, 2026 through Friday, February 27, 2026, docents will be available in the galleries to answer questions, but there will be no formal tours. This is due to expected crowds during the last weeks of the exhibition.
