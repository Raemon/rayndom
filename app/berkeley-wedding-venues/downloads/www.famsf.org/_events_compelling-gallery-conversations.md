###  Ticket info 

Free for Bay Area residents. Advance tickets recommended.   


Explore the de Young’s permanent collection through in depth conversations that are welcoming, inclusive, and engaging.

Join us for a 40 minute conversation about two works of art. Connect with each other and with artworks through meaningful dialogue and guided activities, such as poetry and sketching. Learn insightful stories behind works of art and leave with lasting memories!  


> I loved it because it stimulated my creativity and reminded me of my younger school days. I think these kinds of exercises are missing from our world. — Compelling Gallery Conversations participant  
> 

## Location + dates

  * Conversations depart from Wilsey Court

  * Every Saturday, Noon + 3 pm

  * First Tuesday of the month, 10:30 am

  * No Compelling Gallery Conversations on Fourth of July
