#  Visit the de Young 

##  Location information 

###  de Young 

Golden Gate Park  
50 Hagiwara Tea Garden Drive  
San Francisco, CA 94118 

[ Directions ](/visit/getting-to-the-de-young)

###  Hours 

Tuesday – Sunday  
9:30 am – 5:15 pm 

Closed on Presidents’ Day, Indigenous Peoples’ Day, Thanksgiving, and Christmas. Hamon Tower closes at 4:30 pm daily.
