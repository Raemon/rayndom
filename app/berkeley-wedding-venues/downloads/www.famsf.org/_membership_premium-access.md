##  Benefits 

**Access**

  * Unlimited free general admission for all employees and a guest
  * Opportunity to plan two Corporate Access Days, including complimentary admission to one special exhibition for all employees and a guest
  * 150 VIP corporate admission passes, which include complimentary, priority admission to one special exhibition and can be shared at your discretion with colleagues and clients 
  * Digital corporate membership card and account login, allowing you to reserve your own tickets



**Hospitality**

  * Dedicated staff member to personalize your museum experience
  * Invitation for four guests to donor exhibition receptions throughout the year
  * Invitation for two guests to Leadership Circle events, the annual Black Tie Dinner, and the Annual Luncheon
  * Two complimentary curator-led tours or lectures at your corporate office
  * Privilege to schedule docent-led tours at the de Young and Legion of Honor
  * Opportunity to host unlimited corporate events with a 20% discount on the rental fee



**Recognition**

  * Recognition in the Annual Report, featured in _Fine Arts_ magazine, and on the Corporate Partnerships page of the Fine Arts Museums of San Francisco’s website
  * Recognition in the Generous Support category as a sponsor of an annual program of your choosing. Program recognition opportunities include: Conservation, Education, Access, or Public Programs.
  * First-time Premium Access members will have the opportunity to be recognized with a dedicated post on LinkedIn highlighting your support



**Impact**

  * Opportunity to customize your corporate membership benefits package
  * Opportunity to schedule a corporate volunteer day at the de Young or Legion of Honor



**Please note:**

  * Digital Corporate Membership cardholders receive complimentary general admission with special exhibition surcharges waived for themselves and a guest.
  * A work badge, business card, or pay stub with photo ID is required as proof of employment.
  * All corporate events are subject to rental fees and all direct costs.
  * Docent-led tours are scheduled through Group Sales and are subject to availability. Docent fee and cost of admission apply. Contact [corporategiving@famsf.org](mailto:corporategiving@famsf.org) for support scheduling.



Complimentary curator-led tours have a maximum of 20 guests each and are subject to availability. Contact [corporategiving@famsf.org](mailto:corporategiving@famsf.org) to schedule.
