Artist
    Camille Pissarro (1830-1903)
Title
    Église et ferme d'Éragny (Church and Farm at Éragny-sur-Epte)
Date
    1894–1895
Object Type
    Print
Medium
    Color etching and drypoint with aquatint
Dimensions
    Image: 6 3/16 x 9 9/16 in. (15.7 x 24.3 cm) Framed (Printing Color): 16 7/8 x 21 7/8 x 1 1/4 in. (42.863 x 55.563 x 3.175 cm)
Credit Line
    Museum Purchase, Achenbach Foundation for Graphic Arts Endowment Fund
Accession Number
    1964.142.19

  * ###  Provenance 

with Karl and Faber, where acquired, 1964. 

  * ###  Exhibition History 

San Francisco, Legion of Honor, July 7 – Sept 3, 1984. "19th century French landscapes: prints and drawings from the permanent collection."

San Francisco, Legion of Honor,__ February 21 – May 3, 1981. "Masterworks from the Achenbach Foundation for Graphic Arts."

  
San Francisco, Legion of Honor, 29 March – 15 July 1986. "Impressions of an Age: French Printmaking 1870-1890," #68.

__

San Francisco, Legion of Honor, November 11, 1995 – March 3, 1996. "Treasures of the Achenbach Foundation for Graphic Arts."

San Francisco, Legion of Honor, May 24, 2025 – January 4, 2026. "Printing Color: Chiaroscuro to Screenprint."

  * ###  Bibliography 

Delteil 96, ii/iv

Robert Flynn Johnson, Karin  
Breuer, and Joseph Goldyne,  _Treasures of the Achenbach Foundation for  
Graphic Arts_, San Francisco, 1995, p. 117, no. 131.
