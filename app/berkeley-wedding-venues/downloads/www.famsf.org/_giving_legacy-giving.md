# Legacy giving

Through a legacy gift, you can join other passionate supporters in ensuring access to the Museums’ collections, exhibitions and education programs for visitors of all ages and backgrounds for generations to come.

Legion of Honor, San Francisco, 2019. Photograph by Gary Sexton. Image courtesy of the Fine Arts Museums of San Francisco

## Make a future gift with a lasting impact

Locally rooted and internationally engaged, the Fine Arts Museums of San Francisco connect visitors with local and global art to illuminate the past, speak of the present, and shape the future. Through a legacy gift, you can join other passionate supporters in making art accessible and meaningful to the public for years to come.
