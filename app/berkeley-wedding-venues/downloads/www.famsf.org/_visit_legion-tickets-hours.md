#  Tickets + hours 

##  Jump to

[Book tickets online](https://ticketing.famsf.org/events) in advance for general admission and special exhibitions. Tickets may also be purchased at the admissions desk on a first-come, first-served basis.

##  Hours + holidays 

Monday: Closed  
Tuesday–Sunday: 9:30 am–5:15 pm

The Legion of Honor is closed on Martin Luther King Jr. Day, Labor Day, Indigenous Peoples’ Day, Thanksgiving, and Christmas Day. We’re open all other holidays.

##  Tickets 

Members: Free  
Youth (17 and under): Free  
Adults: $20  
Seniors (65+): $17  
Students (w/ valid ID): $11

The [Legion of Honor Museum Store](/shop/) and [Legion of Honor Café](/visit/legion-of-honor-cafe) do not require a ticket for entry.

At 4:30 pm we offer free admission to our permanent collection. All are welcome; no ticket required. Special exhibition prices still apply.

Prices and hours subject to change without notice. Last ticket sold one hour before closing. Discounts cannot be combined with other offers or previously purchased tickets.

[ Get tickets ](https://ticketing.famsf.org/events)

Get tickets by phone:

  * General public: 888.901.6645 (24/7/365)

  * Members: 415.750.3636 (Monday–Friday, 9 am–5 pm) or 800.777.9996 (daily, 9 am–9 pm)

  * Note: transaction fees apply




Press can request tickets by emailing [press@famsf.org](mailto:press@famsf.org). 

Enhance your visit to the museum by adding an [audio tour](https://ticketing.famsf.org/events?category=Audio%20tours) (not included in museum admission). Audio tours can be enjoyed on provided devices or your personal device.
