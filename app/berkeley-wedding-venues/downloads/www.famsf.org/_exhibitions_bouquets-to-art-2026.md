March 3 – March 8, 2026

Now in its 42nd year, Bouquets to Art is an annual exhibition featuring floral displays throughout our galleries. We are excited to announce that we will continue to include the Legion of Honor in this year’s Bouquets to Art. 

Leading floral designers will transform the galleries with innovative botanical interpretations of de Young and Legion of Honor artwork and architecture. From striking sculptural creations to extravagant floral fashion designs, inspiration waits around every corner. 

Enjoy our beautiful opening night reception or enter to win a luxury raffle package. You can also purchase a fresh arrangement from the flower cart, or take part in engaging lectures with renowned designers and authors happening throughout the week.

##  More info 

  * ###  Getting to Bouquets to Art 

Note for the de Young:

    * John F. Kennedy Drive is closed to vehicular traffic from Kezar Dr. to Transverse Dr.

    * The Music Concourse Parking Garage is expected to reach full capacity during Bouquets to Art.

    * Accessible parking is available in the parking lot behind the Music Concourse Bandshell, including four van-accessible spaces.

Note for the Legion:

    * Free parking is available in the lots in front of the museum and along the north side of the building, but is expected to reach full capacity during Bouquets to Art.

    * Accessible parking is available in the parking lot in front of the building.

We recommend planning ahead in making necessary travel arrangements. Learn more about [getting to the de Young](https://www.famsf.org/visit/getting-to-the-de-young), [getting to the Legion of Honor](https://www.famsf.org/visit/getting-to-the-legion-of-honor), and [accessible parking](https://www.famsf.org/visit/accessibility#for-visitors-with-mobility-considerations-and-accessible-parking-needs).
