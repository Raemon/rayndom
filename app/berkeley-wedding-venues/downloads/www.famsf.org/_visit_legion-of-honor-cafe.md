#  Legion of Honor Café 

**Due to a private event, the Legion of Honor Café will be closed Wednesday, February 4, through Friday, February 6.**

With a fresh interpretation of American and European classic cuisine by [McCalls Catering & Events](http://mccallssf.com/), the café features menu items crafted with seasonal ingredients from local vendors and unique menu items inspired by our special exhibitions.

## **Legion of Honor Café hours**

Tuesday–Sunday:

  * Café opens: 9:30 am

  * Lunch: 11 am–3:30 pm



  * Café closes: 4:30 pm




[Sample menu](https://www.famsf.org/storage/files/e3ed680c-0a34-43a0-9e01-1d4fed04f12b/07-21-2022_a_Legion_Menu_.pdf)  
  
(Note: menu rotates regularly)

The Legion of Honor Café is open for indoor and outdoor dining, weather permitting.

Seating is on a walk-in basis. Reservations not accepted.
