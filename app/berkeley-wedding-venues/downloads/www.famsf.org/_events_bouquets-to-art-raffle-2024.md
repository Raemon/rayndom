##  Raffle packages 

  * ###  A. Culture of Michoacán Tour 

Experience the beauty of the Central Highlands of Mexico on this handcrafted, private five-night tour for two people with Betsy McNair, owner of My Mexico Tours. You’ll enjoy the Spanish-style grandeur of Morelia’s elegant architecture and the quaint beauty of picturesque Pátzcuaro, visit ancient archaeological sites of the native Purépecha people, and meet the current masters of Mexican folk art. Accommodations will be provided by the Hotel de la Soledad in Morelia and Casa Encantada B&B in Pátzcuaro.  


![](data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIAAAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAAALAAAAAABAAEAAAIBRAA7) ![Isla Janitzio Patzcuaro](https://www.famsf.org/storage/images/d16fe1f9-f97a-43b5-9bd7-45f4d12a13ba/isla-janitzio-patzcuaro.jpeg?crop=577,768,x191,y0&format=jpg&quality=80&width=577) ![Isla Janitzio Patzcuaro](https://www.famsf.org/storage/images/d16fe1f9-f97a-43b5-9bd7-45f4d12a13ba/isla-janitzio-patzcuaro.jpeg?crop=577,768,x191,y0&format=jpg&quality=80&width=577)

Image courtesy My Mexico Tours  


  * ###  B. Taste of San Francisco 

Savor a diverse selection of San Francisco cuisine at Gary Danko for classic French fare; Fang for modern twists on Chinese staples; Spruce for California-inspired dishes; Holbrook House for elevated contemporary American cuisine and The Rotunda for lunch under the historic stained-glass dome ceiling. Enjoy a sourdough cooking class for two from Josey Baker Bread.

  * ###  C. Sporting by the Bay 

Experience all the exciting major sports that San Francisco has to offer: two 2024 tickets to one pre- season 49ers football game, one Giants baseball game with Gotham Clubhouse entry in Oracle Park, one Warriors basketball game at Chase Center; Presidio Bowl gift certificate; the Sail GP Season 4 Grand Final for four at the Golden Gate Yacht Club; and a Premier Brunch Cruise for two on City Cruises.

Two tickets to each of the following institutions: San Francisco Ballet, SF Jazz, San Francisco Opera, San Francisco Symphony, San Francisco Opera, Oberlin Dance Collective (ODC), and Opera Parallèle; dinner for two at Hayes Valley restaurant, Elephant Sushi; and one night at the Hyatt Regency San Francisco Downtown SOMA.

A one-year Family-level membership at the Fine Arts Museums of San Francisco and a behind-the-scenes Conservation Lab tour at the de Young museum; a custom jewelry piece by Alexis Bittar; lunch at the boutique caviar house Tsar Nicoulai Caviar Café in the historic Ferry Building; lunch for four at the City Club of San Francisco; a private tour at Guide Dogs for the Blind; and a gift certificate for modern Greek cuisine at Kokkari.

  * ###  G. Ultimate Napa Valley Escape and Wine Bonanza 

Spend one night in Napa Valley at the Solage, Auberge Resorts Collections luxury resort and spa; enjoy a wine-and-food experience for two at Ashes & Diamonds Winery; tasting at Far Niente Winery; experience premium wines at the Ackerman Heritage House; and take home 40 bottles of select wine donated by members of the San Francisco Auxiliary of the Fine Arts Museums in honor of Bouquets to Art’s 40th anniversary.

Image courtesy Ashes & Diamonds Winery  


  * ###  H. Viking 8-Day River Cruise 

Sail for eight days on any one of Europe’s storied rivers on an award-winning Viking Longship on a 2024–2025 embarkation. Stay in a standard stateroom, with meals, shore excursions, and tours included. Enjoy onboard amenities such as a restaurant, bar and lounge, library, and expansive sun deck. Viking Longships also feature elegant Scandinavian design, alfresco dining on the indoor/outdoor Aquavit Terrace, and environmentally considerate features. Blackout dates, restrictions, and taxes may apply.
