Title
    Yam mask, Baba Mini
Date
    20th century
Object Type
    Sculpture
Medium
    Basketry made from the stem of lygodium (a climbing vine), pigment
Dimensions
    29 x 25 x 16 cm (11 7/16 x 9 13/16 x 6 5/16 in.)
Credit Line
    Gift of Marcia and John Friede (purchased in part with funds from the Evelyn A.J. Hall Charitable Trust)
Accession Number
    2010.77.110

  * ###  Provenance
