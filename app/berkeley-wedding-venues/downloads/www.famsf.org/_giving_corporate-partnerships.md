#  Corporate Partnerships 

The Fine Arts Museums of San Francisco rely on the crucial support of the corporate community to present special exhibitions, preserve world-class collections, and offer year-round educational and public programming for more than 1.5 million visitors annually. There are many ways your company can help the Museums realize their mission of providing a meaningful and diversified experience of art and culture for Bay Area, Northern California, national, and international audiences.

##  Corporate Sponsorship 

Sponsorship of an exhibition, program, or event at the Fine Arts Museums of San Francisco not only supports the Museums but also offers focused opportunities to raise your brand recognition and awareness. With your company’s business goals in mind, our staff will work with you to create a bespoke sponsorship package with benefits for your clients, employees, and executives to enjoy. Corporate sponsorships benefits include:

### Visibility

Leverage an exhibition, program, or event of your choice as a platform for brand recognition and awareness on the local, national, and global levels. 

### Hospitality

Offer your employees, clients, and VIPs exclusive opportunities to visit our Museums after hours, interact with our curators on behind-the-scenes tours, and entertain in premier venues, such as the Hamon Observation Tower.

### Access

Enjoy an unprecedented entrée into the Museums’ community of patrons, with direct access to donors and leaders at exhibition previews, opening events, galas, and other exclusive donor events.

##  Corporate Membership 

Corporate Membership provides vital unrestricted support for everything the Fine Arts Museums of San Francisco do, from presenting a robust schedule of special exhibitions to providing free education and public programs every Saturday. As annual supporters, Corporate Members enjoy a wide array of benefits throughout the year. For more information about the membership levels and benefits, visit the [Corporate Membership](/membership/#corporate-membership) page.
