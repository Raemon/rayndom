Fine Arts Museums of San Francisco (de Young Museum), April 14, 2025–July 5, 2026. “About Place: Bay Area Artists from the Svane Gift,” no cat. no.

Thomas, Elfie, “Amir Fallah’s paintings stage a captivating collision between Iranian and American culture,” _It’s Nice That Magazine_ , April 28, 2022. https://www.itsnicethat.com/articles/amir-fallah-art-280422.
