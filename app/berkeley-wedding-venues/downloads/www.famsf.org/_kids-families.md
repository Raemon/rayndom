Be curious with us!

Explore art and creativity from around the world through hands-on programs, discovery guides, and videos that bring our collections to life. Every Saturday, we offer free drop-in programming that includes Sketching in the Galleries or Family Art Making. Visitors 17 and under always receive free admission to the permanent collection galleries. Questions? Email [families@famsf.org](mailto:families@famsf.org).

We look forward to seeing you in the galleries!
