##  Social media 

The Fine Arts Museums of San Francisco (FAMSF) manages various social media channels under the names of the de Young, Legion of Honor, and FAMSF. We aim to use these platforms as a forum that stimulates constructive community conversations by applying inclusive and equitable perspectives to our collections, exhibitions, programs, and the histories they embody. 

By posting to one of our social media channels or interacting with our content, you are agreeing to abide by our Community Guidelines. These apply in addition to the [Terms and Conditions](/about/terms-conditions) or other legal notices that are provided by us and / or the owner of the social media platform. 

We welcome you to join the FAMSF community on each platform, and seek to be welcoming and attentive hosts. We moderate social media to preserve a respectful and inclusive environment in accordance with our core institutional values, which can be found [here](/about). We endeavor to maintain safe spaces that foster participation and enrich dialogue.

**We ask that all users follow these Community Guidelines:**

  * Be respectful to all those with whom you interact within our social media communities, and when sharing your comments and ideas.
  * Refrain from inappropriate, off-topic, abusive, harassing, or profane comments.
  * Do not post information that goes against the platform’s spam policy or promotes / solicits visits to third-party sites, initiatives, or products. 



**We reserve the right to reject or remove comments that are not in keeping with our values and Guidelines; and to block or ban users / followers who do not follow these Guidelines.**

The Guidelines apply to all FAMSF social media channels.
