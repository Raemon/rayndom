###  Ticket info 

Advanced table or ticket purchase is required to attend the event.  


Join us for _A Floral Affair_ at this year’s [Bouquets to Art](/exhibitions/bouquets-to-art-2025) Opening Night! 

Wander through over 80 stunning floral displays, each celebrating the perfect fusion of art and botanicals. Savor exquisite summer-inspired cuisine by McCalls Catering as you immerse yourself in a night of art, blooms, and elegance. 

[Luxury Raffle](/events/bouquets-to-art-luxury-raffle-2025) tickets will be available for purchase. Summer cocktail attire.
