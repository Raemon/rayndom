###  Ticket 

This program is free and does not require a ticket. Museum admission tickets reserved separately.  


Join us for Sketching in the Galleries on select Saturdays from 11 am to 3 pm. This free drop-in activity provides all skill levels with a chance to experiment with materials and enjoy the collections from a creative perspective. All materials are provided, including gallery stools.

## **Dates + locations**

  * Sat, Jan 31, Gallery 26 

  * Sat, Feb 7, Gallery 16

  * Sat, Feb 14, Hamon Tower 9th floor 

  * Sat, Feb 28, Gallery 12 

  * Sat, Mar 21, Gallery 25

  * Sat, Mar 28, Gallery 30




[ Museum map ](/visit/de-young-museum-map)

## **Ticket info**

Sketching in the Galleries is free and open to the public.

Every Saturday, we offer [free general admission](/visit/free-reduced-admission) for all Bay Area residents. Visitors ages 17 and under always receive free admission to the permanent collection galleries. To ensure your preferred date, advance tickets are recommended.  


## **Contact info**

[families@famsf.org](mailto:families@famsf.org)  
415.750.3600

  


 _Please note: sketching in our permanent collection galleries outside of this event during public hours is allowed using pencil and a small pad only. Anything more requires permission and a permit. Please see our_[ _sketching, copying, and painting rules / permit application_](https://www.famsf.org/resources/sketching-permit-guidelines) _for more information. Permit application is not required for Sketching in the Galleries. Materials and gallery stools are a benefit of the event and not provided otherwise._
