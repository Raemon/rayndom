#  Visit the Legion of Honor 

##  Location information 

###  Legion of Honor 

Lincoln Park  
100 34th Avenue (at Clement Street)  
San Francisco, CA 94121 

[ Directions ](/visit/getting-to-the-legion-of-honor)

###  Hours 

Tuesday – Sunday  
9:30 am – 5:15 pm 

Closed on Martin Luther King Jr. Day, Labor Day, Indigenous Peoples’ Day, Thanksgiving, and Christmas. 

###  Tickets 

Advance reservations encouraged. 

[ Get tickets ](https://ticketing.famsf.org/events)

##  Museum closure Feb 4–6 + Salon Doré closure 

Due to a private event, the Legion of Honor will be closed Wed–Fri, Feb 4–6. We apologize for the inconvenience and look forward to welcoming you to our galleries on Sat, Feb 7. The Salon Doré is closed until further notice.

###  Visit the de Young 

Nestled in Golden Gate Park and showcasing American art, textile arts and costumes, African art, Oceanic art, arts of the Americas, and international contemporary art, the de Young has welcomed visitors for over 125 years.
