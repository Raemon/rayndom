##  Benefits 

  * Significant Support recognition on the special exhibition or program of your choosing
  * Opportunity to arrange a private tour of the conservation laboratories at the de Young or Legion of Honor
  * Plus all Leader benefits:
  * Invitations to exclusive Leadership Circle events throughout the year, including private collection visits, artist studio visits, and behind-the-scenes tours with curators and art-world luminaries
  * Invitation to annual international travel with the museum director and curators, providing access to private collections, museums, and galleries
  * Invitations to curator-led, after-hours tours of special exhibitions
  * Opportunity to arrange a private curator-led tour when the museum is closed to the public
  * VIP entry to global art fairs, when available
  * Dedicated staff member to personalize your museum experience
  * Invitation for two to our Annual Black-Tie Dinner
  * Invitation to annual domestic travel with a museum curator to visit private collections, museums, and galleries
  * Opportunity to arrange a private docent-led tour annually
  * Recognition in _Fine Arts_ magazine twice per year 
  * Invitations for two to Donor Receptions, celebrating special exhibition openings
  * Invitations for two to Patrons Circle tours of special exhibitions
  * Recognition in the Annual Report, featured in _Fine Arts_ magazine
  * Invitation to the Annual Donors’ Luncheon
  * Unlimited free admission for two members, five accompanied guests, and youths up to age 17 to the permanent collections and special exhibitions at the de Young and Legion of Honor
  * Reciprocal admission to over 1,000 museums in North America
  * Priority ticketing to special exhibitions
  * Subscription to Patrons Circle email newsletter
  * Subscription to _Fine Arts_ magazine
  * 10% off at the Museum Stores and 20% off during Members-Only Sales
