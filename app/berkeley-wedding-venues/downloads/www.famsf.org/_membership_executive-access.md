##  Benefits 

**Access**

  * 75 VIP corporate admission passes, which include complimentary, priority admission to one special exhibition and can be shared at your discretion with colleagues and clients 
  * Digital corporate membership card and account login



**Hospitality**

  * Dedicated staff member to personalize your museum experience
  * Invitation for two guests to donor exhibition receptions
  * One complimentary docent-led tour or lectures at your corporate office
  * Privilege to schedule additional docent-led tours at the de Young and Legion of Honor
  * Opportunity to host unlimited corporate events with a 10% discount on the rental fee



**Recognition**

  * Recognition in the Annual Report, featured in _Fine Arts_ magazine, and on the Corporate Partnerships page of the Fine Arts Museums of San Francisco’s website



**Please note:**

  * Digital Corporate Membership cardholders receive complimentary general admission with special exhibition surcharges waived for themselves and a guest.
  * A work badge, business card, or pay stub with photo ID is required as proof of employment.
  * All corporate events are subject to rental fees and all direct costs.
  * Docent-led tours are scheduled through Group Sales and are subject to availability. Docent fee and cost of admission apply. Contact [corporategiving@famsf.org](mailto:corporategiving@famsf.org) for support scheduling.
