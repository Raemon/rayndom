##  Additional information 

###  Follow FAMSF

In Lee Mingwei’s _Sonic Blossom_ (2013–present), a singer approaches visitors and invites them to be serenaded with a gift of song selected from Franz Schubert’s lieder (songs written for one voice). Lee Mingwei listened to these songs with his mother while caring for her after heart surgery in 2012, as she used to play them to him as a child. Listen on to experience these powerful songs for yourself.
