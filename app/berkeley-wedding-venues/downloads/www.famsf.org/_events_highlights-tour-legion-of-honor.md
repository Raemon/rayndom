###  Ticket info 

No reservation necessary. A general admission ticket is required.

Highlights tours welcome you to the museum and serve as an introduction to our collections.  


Explore the museum with a docent, either through a broad overview or by focusing on a particular collection or gallery.

At the Legion of Honor, tours begin in the rotunda.

## **Ticket info**

No reservation necessary. A general admission ticket is required.
