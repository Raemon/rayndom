##  Benefits 

  * Free admission for one member and a guest to the permanent collections and special exhibitions at the de Young and Legion of Honor
  * Priority ticketing to special exhibitions
  * Early access to special exhibitions during Member Previews 
  * 10% off at the Museum Stores and 20% off during Members-Only Sales
  * Subscription to _Fine Arts_ magazine and Member Monthly email newsletter



Discounted memberships are available to seniors, people with disabilities, out-of-state residents, teachers, students, and self-identified artists. You can purchase a discounted membership on-site at the de de Young or Legion of Honor or by calling Member Services at 415.750.3636 (Monday–Friday, 9 am–5 pm).
