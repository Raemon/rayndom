## Location + dates

  * Conversations meet in the Bowles Porcelain Gallery, Gallery 23, on the lower level of the Legion of Honor

  * Occurs every Saturday, Sunday, and [Free Tuesday](/visit/free-reduced-admission#free-tuesdays-for-all)

  * 11:30 am to 1:30 pm
