#  Resources for educators 

We strive to create resources that can be quickly adapted to your teaching needs.

The Get Smart with Art series provides grade-specific lessons aligned with California State standards in history and language arts. In collaboration with the California Academy of Sciences, we are proud to offer an Art + science series that compares and contrasts the thinking skills used by scientists and artists. If you’re preparing to visit a special exhibition, we offer in-gallery text panels and a thematic map to help in your planning.
