9:30 am–5:15 pm 

Book your Member Preview tickets to see [_Art of Manga_](https://www.famsf.org/exhibitions/art-of-manga) before it opens to the public _._ Each ticket includes entry to the exhibition, as well as _ONE PIECE ONLY: How Manga Is Made_ and all permanent collection galleries at the de Young on the day of your visit.

_Art of Manga_ spotlights artistic expression in innovative Japanese comics and graphic novels that are rapidly becoming an internationally recognized mode of communication. Drawing from both Western comics and satire and Japanese printing and art history, manga immerses readers in stories that often become forerunners of greater cultural shifts.
