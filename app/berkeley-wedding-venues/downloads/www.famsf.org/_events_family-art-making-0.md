###  Ticket info 

This program is free and does not require a ticket. Museum admission tickets reserved separately.   


The Family Art Making table offers a drop-in art project activity led by professional teaching artists on select Saturdays from 11 am to 3 pm. Artists of all ages are welcome to join.

## Dates + activities

  * Sat, Jan 24: Activity inspired by [_Art of Manga_](/exhibitions/art-of-manga), Kimball Education Gallery

  * Sat, Feb 21: Activity inspired by [_Leilah Babirye_](/exhibitions/leilah-babirye), Kimball Education Gallery

  * Sat, Mar 7: Activity inspired by [Bouquets to Art](/exhibitions/bouquets-to-art-2026), Front Lawn




## **Ticket info**

Family Art Making is free and does not require a ticket for entry. Museum admission tickets reserved separately.

Every Saturday, we offer [free general admission](https://www.famsf.org/visit/free-reduced-admission#free-saturdays-for-bay-area-residents) for all Bay Area residents. Visitors ages 17 and under always receive free admission to the permanent collection galleries.
