#  Rent the museums 

Two iconic museums. One unforgettable event.

When you host a party at the Fine Arts Museums of San Francisco, you create much more than an event. You create a truly memorable experience. Whether it’s the Legion of Honor’s neoclassical architecture and breathtaking Golden Gate views or the de Young’s state-of-the-art facility in the heart of Golden Gate Park — there is something for every event, no matter the size or style. Plus, by celebrating at the Fine Arts Museums, you are directly supporting our exhibitions, education initiatives, and public programs.
