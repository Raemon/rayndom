#  VII – The Heart (Le Coeur) from Jazz 

1947

[ Henri Matisse ](https://www.famsf.org/art-finder?artist=83)

#  Artwork Viewer 

Not on view

Matisse here draws on traditional references to the artistic process and spirit: the heart. The deep-red heart on the right side of the plate stands in opposition to the dense black space on the left. The dominating black form, thought to represent the absence of creativity, threatens to overtake the red heart, the artist’s inspiration.
