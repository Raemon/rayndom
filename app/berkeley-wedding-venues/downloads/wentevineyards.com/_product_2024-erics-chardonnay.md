# 2024 Eric’s Chardonnay

The 2024 Eric's Chardonnay is unoaked to exemplify the beautiful varietal character of Livermore Valley Chardonnay. This unoaked Chardonnay is bursting with aromas of citrus, melon, and white flowers. The wine has bright flavors of honeydew and meyer lemon with a touch of minerality.

##  Tasting Notes 

The 2024 Eric’s Chardonnay is bursting with charming aromas of crisp green apple and honeydew melon. The palate is refreshing and full-bodied with mouthwatering acidity and tropical flavors of guava and lime.

### Fermentation And Aging

The wine was fermented cold in stainless steel tanks to preserve the beautiful esters of our Eric's Chardonnay. The wine was aged for six months on lees in stainless steel tanks to preserve aromatic intensity and build mouthfeel
