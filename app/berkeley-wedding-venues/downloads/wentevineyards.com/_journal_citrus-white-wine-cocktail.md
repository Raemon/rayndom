# Citrus White Wine Cocktail Featuring Wente Vineyards Louis Mel Sauvignon Blanc

Prep Time - 10 minutes | Time To Make - 10 minutes 

At Wente Vineyards, we believe that every bottle of wine has the power to create memorable moments, bringing people together to savor life’s simple pleasures. As summer arrives in full swing, there’s nothing quite like a refreshing spritz to beat the heat and elevate your gatherings. Our Louis Mel Sauvignon Blanc is the perfect choice for crafting a delightful Citrus White Wine Batch Cocktail with its bright and invigorating flavors. The Louis Mel Sauvignon Blanc showcases our commitment to quality and tradition, offering a vibrant blend of classic grapefruit, juicy white apricot, key lime, and guava. This spritz recipe captures the essence of summer, combining the crispness of our wine with the zest of fresh citrus, creating an irresistible burst of flavor. Whether you’re hosting a brunch, a picnic, or simply relaxing on the patio, this cocktail will surely be a crowd-pleaser. 

## Ingredients

### Citrus White Wine Cocktail

1 cup freshly squeezed grapefruit juice

A few slices of grapefruit and blood oranges (recommended about 3-4 of each)

1 bottle of Louis Mel Wente Vineyards Sauvignon Blanc

Club soda to taste

![https://wentevineyards.com/wp-content/uploads/2024/06/wente-vineyards-citrus-white-wine-cocktail.png](https://wentevineyards.com/wp-content/uploads/2024/06/wente-vineyards-citrus-white-wine-cocktail.png)

## Instructions

1

Combine the freshly squeezed grapefruit juice, sliced fruit, and the bottle of Louis Mel Sauvignon Blanc in a pitcher.

2

Stir well and allow the mixture to cool in the fridge for at least 20 minutes.

3

When ready to serve, pour the chilled cocktail into a coupe or wine glass and top it with a splash of club soda.

4

Garnish each glass with a slice of blood orange or grapefruit for an added touch of elegance.
