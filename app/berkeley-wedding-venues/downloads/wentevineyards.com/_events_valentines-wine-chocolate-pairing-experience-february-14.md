## Valentine’s Wine, Food & Chocolate Pairing Experience

### This seated, relaxed experience is perfect for couples, friends, or anyone looking for a unique Valentine’s outing—offering more than a tasting, it’s a chance to slow down, connect, and savor carefully crafted pairings in an intimate setting.

### February 14th | 11am – 1pm and 3pm – 5pm

Celebrate Valentine’s Day with a guided wine, food and chocolate experience that brings together Wente Family Vineyards’ history, craftsmanship, and thoughtful food pairings.

Begin with a welcome pour and storytelling moment before settling in to enjoy **sparkling wine and Chardonnay** , paired with seasonal savory bites. The experience continues with **two red wines** , each matched with chef-curated small bites designed to highlight flavor, balance, and texture. The tasting concludes with **a final red wine paired with chocolate and a petite dessert bite** —a rich, indulgent finish.

**$65 per person | club discount applies**

This is a 21 and over experience
