# Celebrating International Women’s Day at Wente Vineyards

##  Honoring and Empowering Women Innovators

#  Celebrating Women’s Day at Wente Vineyards 

At Wente Vineyards, we believe that the future of wine is brighter with the influence of women at the forefront. On International Women’s Day, we pause to celebrate the incredible women who shape our world—from the innovators within our own family to the inspiring women-led businesses we admire in our community. Today, we honor the women who have helped shape Wente Vineyards’ legacy and those who continue to push boundaries in the wine industry.

![](https://wentevineyards.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/01/Wente-Vineyards-Women-Behind-Wente-2023-Aly-Elizabeth-Niki-Candid-scaled-1.jpg) ![](https://wentevineyards.com/wp-content/uploads/2025/02/Wente-January_2025-8-1-scaled.jpg) ![](https://wentevineyards.com/wp-content/uploads/2022/08/Wente_May2022-28.jpg)

###  Women Innovators at Wente Vineyards

Wente Vineyards has always embraced innovation, with women playing a vital role in our journey. **Christine Wente** , Chairman of the Board, has led with a strong vision, guiding Wente Vineyards toward sustainability and a bright future. **Aly Wente** , President of Marketing, brings fresh perspectives to the brand, connecting Wente with new generations of wine enthusiasts. **Niki Wente** , Director of Vineyard Operations, oversees the health and sustainability of our vineyards, ensuring we continue to produce exceptional wines. Our talented **Winemaker Elizabeth Kester** plays a crucial role in crafting wines that reflect both tradition and innovation. **Jordan Wente** contributes to our family legacy by leading brand marketing for our sister winery, Murrieta’s Well. These women represent just a small part of the many amazing women behind the scenes at Wente Vineyards, whose dedication and passion help drive our success every day. Together, they are shaping the future of our winery and the industry as a whole.

![](https://wentevineyards.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/12/Family-Owned.png)

###  Celebrating Local, Women-Led Businesses

As we celebrate the women at Wente Vineyards, we also take time to honor the amazing women-led businesses that surround us in the local community. At Wente Vineyards, we believe in supporting the businesses that contribute to the vibrancy of the Bay Area, especially those led by women. Here are just a few of the incredible local women-led businesses that we admire:

![](https://wentevineyards.com/wp-content/uploads/2025/02/ella-rose-jewelry.png)

[**Ella Rose Jewelry**](https://ellarosejewelry.com/)

A local women-owned jewelry brand that combines timeless elegance with modern design. Their beautiful collections reflect creativity, craftsmanship, and empowerment—values we share at Wente Vineyards.

**[Eliza& Fern](https://elizaandfern.com/)**

A women-owned business that creates artisanal, handcrafted candles and home fragrances with unique and captivating scents. Their dedication to quality and sustainability mirrors the values we hold dear at Wente.

![](https://wentevineyards.com/wp-content/uploads/2025/02/elizafern.png)

![](https://wentevineyards.com/wp-content/uploads/2025/02/gypsy-boutique.png)

[**Gypsy Boutique**](https://www.instagram.com/shopgypsyboutique/)

A charming women-owned boutique that offers a curated selection of stylish fashion and accessories. Gypsy Boutique’s commitment to empowering women through fashion and supporting local artists aligns with our passion for community and creativity.

[**Megha Tiwari Jewelry**](https://meghatiwarijewelry.com/)

An artisan brand known for its handcrafted, one-of-a-kind jewelry, Megha Tiwari Jewelry blends cultural influence with modern sophistication. Through her artistry, Megha empowers women while championing the values of sustainability and inclusivity.

![](https://wentevineyards.com/wp-content/uploads/2025/02/megha-tiwari.png)

As we look to the future of the wine industry and beyond, we know that women will continue to drive innovation, creativity, and leadership. At Wente Vineyards, we are committed to empowering the next generation of women in wine and ensuring that their voices are heard at every stage of the process—from vineyard to bottle, from winemaking to hospitality.

We are proud of the women who have paved the way, and we are excited to support and empower the innovators of tomorrow. Whether it’s through mentorship, support of women-led businesses, or creating opportunities for women to thrive within our organization, Wente Vineyards is dedicated to building a more inclusive and innovative future for all.

So, in honor of International Women’s Day, we raise a glass to the women who inspire us, lead us, and help shape the world of wine. Cheers to the women at Wente Vineyards, to the women innovators in our community, and to the bright future ahead!
