### Fermentation And Aging

Each Cabernet lot was fermented separately in upright stainless steel tanks with pneumatage twice a day to maximize the extraction of aromas and textures. Aged in 60% new French oak barrels and 40% second use French oak barrels. The wine was aged for 19 months prior to bottling.
