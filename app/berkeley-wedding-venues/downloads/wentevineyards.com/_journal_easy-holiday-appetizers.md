#  Effortless Elegance: Easy Holiday Appetizers 

The holiday season is a time for joy, warmth, and, of course, delicious food shared with loved ones. Whether you’re hosting a family gathering, throwing a party, or having girlfriends over for a festive evening, we understand the importance of keeping things stress-free while maintaining a touch of sophistication. We’ve curated a collection of quick and easy appetizers that not only look impressive but also take less than ten minutes to prepare, with fewer than ten ingredients for each recipe.

Cheers to quick, easy, and utterly impressive appetizers that give you more time with those that matter most!

![](https://wentevineyards.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/11/holiday-apps-images-1.png)

[**Prosciutto-wrapped Pears with Blue Cheese**](https://wentestage.wpenginepowered.com/journal/prosciutto-wrapped-pears-with-blue-cheese)

This appetizer offers a perfect balance of sweet, salty, and savory flavors. The combination of juicy pears, crispy prosciutto, creamy blue cheese, and the touch of honey makes it a delightful treat for your taste buds. Enjoy these Prosciutto-Wrapped Pears with Blue Cheese with a Wente Vineyards Chardonnay such as [Morning Fog Chardonnay, Riva Ranch Vineyard Chardonnay or nth DEGREE Chardonnay.](https://wentestage.wpenginepowered.com/shop?filter=variety&shopSlug=chardonnay)

[**Festive Mozzarella Cherry Tomato and Olive Skewers**](https://wentestage.wpenginepowered.com/journal/festive-mozzarella-cherry-tomato-olive-skewers)

These mozzarella, cherry tomatoes, and olive skewers are not only delicious but also make for a colorful and eye-catching appetizer for your holiday celebrations. They can be made ahead and stored in the refrigerator before serving. This would pair well with a [Wente Vineyards Sauvignon Blanc such as Louis Mel Sauvignon Blanc or our Limited Release Sauvignon Blanc](https://wentestage.wpenginepowered.com/shop?filter=variety&shopSlug=sauvignon-blanc).

![](https://wentevineyards.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/11/holiday-apps-images-3.png)

![](https://wentevineyards.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/11/holiday-apps-images-2.png)

[**Blackberry Jam Brie with Toasted Crostinis**](https://wentestage.wpenginepowered.com/journal/blackberry-jam-brie-toasted-crostinis)

This delightful Blackberry Jam Baked Brie with Toasted Crostinis is sure to impress your guests with its rich and gooey texture, complemented by the sweet and tart notes of blackberry jam. It pairs well with [Aly’s Pinot Noir](https://wentestage.wpenginepowered.com/product/2021-alys-pinot-noir) or [nth DEGREE Merlot](https://wentestage.wpenginepowered.com/shop?filter=variety&shopSlug=merlot).
