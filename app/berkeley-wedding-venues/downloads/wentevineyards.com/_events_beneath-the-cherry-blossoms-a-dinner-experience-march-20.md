## Beneath the Cherry Blossoms

### A Dinner Experience

### March 20 | 6pm – 9pm

Step into an evening inspired by cherry blossom season, where spring flavors, Wente wine, and live music create a vibrant, celebration at the winery.

This Japanese-inspired experience features chef-led action stations, inviting guests to wander and savor:

  * Nigiri and Sushi
  * Freshly shucked Oysters
  * BBQ Beef
  * Comforting Japanese noodles
  * Peking duck



Thoughtful wine pairings highlight the season, including Riesling, Chardonnay, Rosé, Serenity Red Blend, and Pinot Noir.

Gather around communal tables designed for connection as live music sets a relaxed yet elevated tone. From first sip to final bite, this dinner celebrates seasonality, shared moments, and the beauty of spring.

**$120 per person | Club member saving apply**

This is a 21 and over event.
