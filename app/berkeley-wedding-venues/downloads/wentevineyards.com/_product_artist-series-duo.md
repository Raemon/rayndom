# Artist Series Duo

A duo of our 2021 Artist Series Wines, Duetto Cabernet Sauvignon & GSM, adorned with original artwork created by local artist Jun Yang. 

## PACK DETAILS

![](https://images.commerce7.com/wente-family-estates/images/original/21-duetto-1726778343602.png)

#### [2021 Duetto Cabernet Sauvignon](/product/2021-duetto/)

The 2021 Duetto exhibits a measure of depth and richness that is carefully honed by a sense of refinement and elegance that is easy to appreciate. Core aromas of black cherry and red plum are framed by a judicious layering of complex oak spices highlighting notes of fresh ground cinnamon and coco powder. Properly rounded, this wine shows terrific balance between tannin structure and mid-palate richness which will help it to age elegantly through 2028 at a minimum.

![](https://images.commerce7.com/wente-family-estates/images/original/2021-gsm-artist-series-1686680293469.png)

#### [2021 GSM](/product/2021-gsm/)

Our 2021 Artist Series GSM rides the line between bright Grenache and savory Syrah with a hint of Mourvedre. Label created by Artist Jun Yang.
