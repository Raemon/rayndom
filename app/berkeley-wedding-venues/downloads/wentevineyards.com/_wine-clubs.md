#  Wine Clubs 

##  From Our Vineyard To Your Front Door

At Wente Vineyards, we want to make your wine experience enjoyable and effortless. That’s why our three different wine club options give you variety and flexibility to find a selection that suits your taste and style. As a member of our nth DEGREE or Small Lot clubs, you will be granted access to unique wines only found at the Wente Vineyards Tasting Lounge. Members are also invited to participate in private events, complimentary tastings, and exclusive savings. Each month Wente + club members enjoy special savings and your favorite Wente Vineyard wines effortlessly delivered to your door.

We invite you to join our extended Wente Vineyards family.

## Wente+

Build Your Own Subscription with Wente+. Choose from our collection of wines will and bring a taste of our vineyards directly to your doorstep.

[ Learn More ](https://wentevineyards.com/wine-clubs/build-your-own)

## Small Lot Club

For wine lovers who want a variety of wines in each shipment and first access to our small lot production wines. Shipments feature Small Lot, Artist Series and nth DEGREE wines. Four bottles per shipment.

[ Learn More ](https://wentevineyards.com/wine-clubs/small-lot-club)

## nth DEGREE 4 Bottle Club

Perfect for the wine connoisseur who likes to enjoy a bottle now or cellar for later. First access to winery-exclusive nth DEGREE wines hand-crafted by Fifth Generation Winemaker, Karl D. Wente. Four bottles per shipment.

[ Learn More ](https://wentevineyards.com/wine-clubs/nth-degree-4-bottle-club/)

## nth DEGREE 6 Bottle Club

Perfect for the wine connoisseur who likes to enjoy a bottle now or cellar for later. First access to winery-exclusive nth DEGREE wines hand-crafted by Fifth Generation Winemaker, Karl D. Wente. Six bottles per shipment.

[ Learn More ](https://wentevineyards.com/wine-clubs/nth-degree-club)
