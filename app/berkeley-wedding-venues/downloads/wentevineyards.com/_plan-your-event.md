##  Special Events

Surrounded by beautifully landscaped grounds, rolling hills and lush vineyards, Wente Vineyards Terrace Lawn, Event Center, and Vineyard Lawn offers attractive and unique facilities for special events and celebrations. Whether you are planning a birthday, anniversary, retirement, or charitable event, you can be certain that we’ll help you make it a memorable occasion.

Wente Vineyards also offers private wine tastings for groups, and customized labeling for gift programs or special party favors. We look forward to working with you to create a customized event setting.

### Featured Event Sites

## Vineyard Lawn

  * The Vineyards Lawn overlooks the sandstone hills surrounded by vineyards and olive trees
  * Market Lights strung over the lawn to create an ambiance
  * Seats up to 700 guests



## Event Center

  * Cresta Blanca Room with beautiful 15 ft. ceilings made from old wine barrels
  * Cresta Blanca Room seats up to 140 guests



## Terrace Lawn

  * Terrace Lawn is nestled between the Spanish style Event Center and Rolling Hills is the perfect backdrop for an event.
  * Terrace Lawn up to 500 guests
