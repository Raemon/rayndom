#  Corporate Events 

At Wente Family Vineyards, we pride ourselves on offering a venue like no other in the Bay Area, perfect for your meetings, private events, lunches, dinners, golf outings, or team-building activities. Whether you’re planning an intimate gathering for a team of ten or a grand event for a thousand, our venue seamlessly accommodates every size with elegance and ease. Nestled in the heart of the Livermore Valley, our venue combines the tranquility of Northern California’s wine country with the convenience of the greater Bay Area, making it the premier choice for your corporate event.

Our exceptional special events team will be your guide to organize, plan and execute your event.

## Explore Our Versatile Venue Spaces

### Wente Family Vineyards Has Facilities Available For Corporate Events Of All Sizes

## Event Center

Experience elegance in our Cresta Blanca Room, adorned with historic wine barrel decor. Seats up to 150 guests.

## Terrace & Vineyard Lawns

The Terrace Lawn is just steps from the Event Center and seats up to 450 guests. The Vineyard Lawn overlooks the sandstone hills surrounded by vineyards and olive trees. Seats up to 700 guests.

## All Your Questions Answered

### How many event spaces with capacity do you have?

  * Herman Wente Conference Room 10pp
  * Charles Wetmore Conference Room 40pp
  * Cresta Blanca Room 150pp
  * Terrace Lawn 500pp
  * Caves 40pp
  * Vineyard Lawn 800pp



### Do you provide Audio Visual equipment?

  * Herman Wente Conference Room – Large TV with HDMI adapter
  * Charles Wetmore Conference Room 40pp- Large TV with HDMI adapter
  * Cresta Blanca 150pp- two drop down screens with two projectors
  * Terrace Lawn 500pp – can be rented
  * Caves 40pp- n/a
  * Vineyard Lawn 800pp- can be rented



### Is there a noise ordinance we must follow?

Yes. For the Terrace Lawn, events must end by 11pm and 10pm for the Vineyard Lawn.

### Do club members get any discounts?

Yes, club members receive 10% off the site fee.

### Is there a food and beverage minimum? 

Yes, depending on the season.

### What is the maximum capacity on the Terrace and Vineyard Lawn? 

The Terrace Lawn can hold up to 450 guests and the Vineyard Lawn can hold up to 700 guests.
