#  Proprietor’s Reserve Club 

The Proprietor’s Reserve Membership is a great option for the golf enthusiast who is seeking to get away from the busy country clubs and enjoy a true golfer’s paradise. There are no food and beverage minimums, no cart fees, and no monthly dues. This is an annual, non-equity membership that allows you to play golf for the entire year, enjoy members only events and tournaments, discounted guest fees, along with all the benefits listed below.

Membership Benefits:

  * Unlimited golf with advanced reservations, or as available with no cart fees
  * Unlimited use of the practice facilities
  * Discounted guest fees
  * Optional Membership in the Wente Vineyards Wine Club
  * 20% savings on all merchandise in our golf shop (excluding golf clubs).
  * 20% savings on food and beverage.
  * Annual NCGA Membership
  * Access to exclusive members-only events and tournaments
  * Book tee times up to 60 days in advance



We offer three different levels of memberships:

  1. Individual Memberships
  2. Family Memberships
  3. Corporate Memberships



Please contact our golf professional, Mark Vanbuskirk for more information at [mark.vanbuskirk@wentevineyards.com](mailto:mark.vanbuskirk@wentevineyards.com) or at 925-456-2479.
