## Wente Vineyards Tasting Lounge FAQ

### When are you open?

**Tasting Room Hours:**

Monday – Wednesday: 12:00pm – 5:30pm (l _ast tasting begins at 4:00pm)_

Thursday – Sunday: 12:00pm – 6:00pm (l _ast tasting begins at 4:30pm)_

**Special Hours:**

January 28: Closed

### Can we bring outside food or a picnic?

No, we do not allow outside food.

### Can we bring our pets?

Pets allowed on our outdoor lawn and select outdoor patio areas only. Trained ADA service animals are allowed indoors.

### Can we bring our children or infants?

Yes, Wente Vineyards welcomes families to our Cabernet Lawn and select outdoor patio areas. Indoor tastings are 21 years and older. Children must be supervised by their parent at all times.

### Are reservations required?

Reservations are encouraged to guarantee a seated tasting but are not required. We will do our best to accommodate walk-in wine tastings.

### Are club member tastings complimentary?

Yes, club members can enjoy complimentary wine tastings for up to four guests. Club members also receive discounts on experiences and additional tastings.

### Is there still a restaurant at Wente Vineyards?

The Restaurant at Wente Vineyards has transitioned to the Wente Vineyards Tasting Lounge. We do not offer food to order at the Tasting Lounge. For a morning or afternoon meal, visit The Grill from 7 am to 6 pm.

### Will there be an upcoming concert series at Wente Vineyards?

Our event program has evolved over the last few years. Though we no longer host large concerts at Wente Vineyards, we do have music events throughout the year. Be sure to join our mailing list to be the first to know about our upcoming events. 2025 Summer Live Music Series is from May – October.
