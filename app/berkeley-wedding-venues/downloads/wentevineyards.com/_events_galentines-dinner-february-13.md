## Will You Be My Galentine? 

### Featuring Aly Wente! Grab your best gals and join us for a fun, Galentine’s celebration at Wente.

### February 12 | 7pm- 9pm

A fashionable evening of Wente’s delectable wines poured by Aly Wente and heavy Hors D’oeuvres paired to the beautiful spring collection of [Bay Area’s own creative clothing designer, Lesley Evers](https://lesleyevers.com/).

TICKET INCLUDES:

  * A fun-filled Fashion Show
  * Wine tasting with Aly Wente
  * Heavy hors d’oeuvres and desserts
  * Special Gift for all who attend ($60 value)
  * $100 coupon for any dress over $300



**_$125 per person | Tickets sold through Lesley Evers website, click the link below to buy tickets!_**
