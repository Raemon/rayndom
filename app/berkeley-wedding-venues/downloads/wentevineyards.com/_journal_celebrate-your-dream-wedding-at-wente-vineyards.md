# Celebrate Your Dream Wedding 

If you’re envisioning a romantic, picture-perfect California wedding, Wente Vineyards offers an extraordinary setting filled with history, charm, and breathtaking beauty. As the country’s longest, continuously family-owned winery since 1883, Wente Vineyards combines tradition with warm hospitality, creating an unparalleled wedding experience in the heart of Livermore Valley.

Just a short drive from San Francisco, the vineyard unfolds in a landscape framed by rolling hills, iconic mission-style architecture, and picturesque vineyards that evoke the magic of old California. The venue’s diversity in ceremony and reception spaces caters to weddings of all sizes, from intimate gatherings to grand celebrations. Couples can choose from several beautiful settings, including the serene Vineyard Lawn, the elegant Event Center, and the lush Terrace Lawn, all embraced by olive trees and expansive vineyard views.

![](https://wentevineyards.com/wp-content/uploads/2025/01/4.png) ![](https://wentevineyards.com/wp-content/uploads/2025/01/3.png) ![](https://wentevineyards.com/wp-content/uploads/2025/01/11.png)

**Exquisite Culinary Experiences Paired with Award-Winning Wines**

No wedding at Wente Vineyards would be complete without the artistry of fine wine and food. The venue’s renowned catering team specializes in creating seasonal menus thoughtfully paired with award-winning wines, ensuring a memorable culinary experience. From tables and chairs to fine china, silverware, glassware, and elegant linens, Wente Vineyards provides a comprehensive range of amenities designed to make your day effortlessly beautiful. The dedicated service staff and bartenders add the finishing touch, coordinating every detail so you can focus on savoring each moment.

![](https://wentevineyards.com/wp-content/uploads/2025/01/1.png)

![](https://wentevineyards.com/wp-content/uploads/2024/04/DSC04936-2_1400x932.jpg)

**A Destination with Endless Activities for You and Your Guests**

Beyond the ceremony and reception, Wente Vineyards offers your guests a multitude of experiences to enjoy during their stay. From wine tastings and cellar tours to a scenic 18-hole championship golf course located in the vineyards, Livermore Valley and Wente Vineyards captures the essence of a memorable wine experience and invites guests to celebrate amidst the legacy of a historic estate. Known as the American birthplace of both Chardonnay and Sauvignon Blanc, Wente Vineyards allows you and your guests to be part of a storied tradition while making new memories together.

**Convenient and Accessible for Bay Area and Out-of-Town Guests**

Located within easy reach of the Bay Area’s major airports, Wente Vineyards is a convenient option for guests traveling from afar. Whether your friends and family are local or coming in from out of state, they’ll find the journey to Wente Vineyards seamless, setting the stage for a warm and welcoming celebration.

For couples who seek elegance, history, and an authentic wine country ambiance, Wente Vineyards is a wedding venue unlike any other. We invite you to explore our offerings and envision your big day in one of California’s most iconic and celebrated vineyards.
