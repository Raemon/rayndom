##  Tasting Notes 

The 2024 Wente Family Vineyards Limited Release Sauvignon Blanc presents an enticing medley of green melon, passion fruit, and key lime, elegantly accented with a hint of subtle oak spice. On the palate, these aromas unfold into a vibrant expression of grapefruit and citrus, delivering both depth and purity of fruit. The wine is beautifully rounded, with a well-balanced acidity that lends freshness and energy to the lingering finish.

### Fermentation And Aging

The wine was fermented in 10% new and 90% neutral French oak with a variety of yeast strains for depth, texture, and balanced oak character. Aged sur lie for four months in 10% new and 90% neutral French oak, with monthly lees stirring to build mouthfeel and complexity
