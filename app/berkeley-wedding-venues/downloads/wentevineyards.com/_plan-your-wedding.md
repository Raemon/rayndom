## Event Center & Terrace Lawn

Experience elegance in our Cresta Blanca Room, adorned with historic wine barrel decor, and the adjoining Terrace Lawn, offering a scenic outdoor ambiance.

  * Cresta Blanca Room with beautiful 15 ft. ceilings made from old wine barrels
  * Terrace Lawn is nestled between the Spanish-style event center and rolling hills is the perfect backdrop for an event.
  * Cresta Blanca Room seats up to 140 guests
  * Terrace Lawn up to 500 guests

## All Your Questions Answered

### May we bring an outside caterer?

If we are unable to accomodate your cuisine you may bring in an insured caterer for an extra charge.

### May we bring our own alcohol?

No, beverages and alcohol will be provided by Wente Vineyards catering department.

### Is there a noise ordinance we must follow?

Yes. For the Terrace Lawn, events must end by 11pm and 10pm for the Vineyard Lawn.

### Do club members get any discounts?

Yes, club members receive 10% off the site fee.

### Is there a food and beverage minimum? 

Yes, depending on the season.

### What is the maximum capacity on the Terrace and Vineyard Lawn? 

The Terrace Lawn can hold up to 450 guests and the Vineyard Lawn can hold up to 700 guests.

Wente
