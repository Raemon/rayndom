# eGift Card

Digital Gift Cards make for the perfect gift, even last minute! We’ll send your digital Gift Card by email immediately – including your personal message, the dollar amount, and a unique redemption code to be entered at checkout. Promotional offers and discounts may not be applied toward the purchase of gift cards. Gift cards are not redeemable for cash. Gift cards have no fees, are non-refundable, and do not expire. Redeemable for online wine purchases only, excludes Wente Golf & The Grill.
