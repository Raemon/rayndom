##  Tasting Notes 

Our 2021 Serenity Red Wine is a proprietary blend of Syrah, Petite Sirah, Grenache, and Mourvedre that impresses with its exotic substance, riveting depth, and engaging complexity. There is plenty of sweet oak that showcases baked cinnamon and vanilla bean which perfectly compliments the abundant candied red cherry and dried fruit flavors. 

## Wine Notes

Syrah 43% 

Petite Sirah 26% 

Grenache 26% 

Mourvedre 5% 

### Release Date

August 2023 

### Alcohol %

15% 

### Fermentation And Aging

All varietals were fermented in small stainless steel tanks with pneumatage twice a day to gently extract all the aromatics and flavors. Aged for 18 months in 80% new French oak and 20% second use French Oak barrels.
