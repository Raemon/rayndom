#  The Grill 

##  The Grill Is Open To The Public

The Grill offers a great place to relax and enjoy a glass of wine with the exceptional view of the 18th green and surrounding vineyards. Guests can choose from several delicious menu items and enjoy a selection of beverages on our patio. **View our menus below.**

_**Join us for Sunday Brunch!** Details below._

To order for pick-up, please call [925-456-2481](tel:925-456-2481)

Please Note: The Grill does not accept cash payments.

## Hours of Operation

### 

The Grill is open daily from 7:30am – 6:30pm

  * Breakfast: 7:30am- 11:00am
  * Lunch: 11:00am – 5:30pm
  * Bar: 7:00am – 6:00pm
  * Sunday Brunch: All Day

## FAQs When Visiting The Grill

### Are reservations required?

Reservations are recommended, but not required. Reserve your table [here.](https://www.opentable.com/r/the-grill-at-wente-vineyards-livermore)

### What forms of payment are accepted?

Apple Pay, Google Pay, Visa, Master Card, Amax, Discover, and Wente Vineyards Gift Cards.

*Cash not accepted.

### Are we able to walk or picnic on the golf course? 

We do not allow walks or picnics on our golf course.
