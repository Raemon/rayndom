# Mulled Wine Recipe with Mount Diablo Highlands Red Blend

Nothing captures the warmth of the holiday season like the cozy aroma of mulled wine simmering on the stove. This easy-to-make recipe combines the robust flavors of Wente Vineyards’ Mount Diablo Highlands Red Blend with classic holiday spices for a cocktail that’s perfect for gatherings or quiet nights by the fire. With its medium body and well-balanced profile, this red blend shines as the base for a drink you’ll enjoy all season long. Let the rich notes of cinnamon, citrus, and star anise transport you to a winter wonderland, one sip at a time. Warm up your holidays with this simple yet flavorful mulled wine recipe. It’s perfect for festive evenings, dinner parties, or even a cozy night at home. 

## Ingredients

1 bottle of Wente Vineyards Mount Diablo Highlands Red Blend 

1/4 cup brandy

1/4 cup maple syrup

1 orange, thinly sliced 

1 lemon, thinly sliced 

1/4 teaspoon ground nutmeg 

3 cinnamon sticks 

4 whole cloves 

1 star anise 

(Optional) 1 cup water to dilute, if desired

![https://wentevineyards.com/wp-content/uploads/2024/12/Wente-Vineyards-Mt-Diablo-Highlands-Mulled-WIne-Vertical-1.png](https://wentevineyards.com/wp-content/uploads/2024/12/Wente-Vineyards-Mt-Diablo-Highlands-Mulled-WIne-Vertical-1.png)

## Instructions

1

In a large saucepan or slow cooker, combine the red wine, brandy, and maple syrup. 

2

Add the orange slices, lemon slices, ground nutmeg, cinnamon sticks, cloves, and star anise to the mixture. 

3

Gently heat the mixture over low heat, stirring occasionally. o Pro Tip: Avoid boiling the mixture to preserve the alcohol content and maintain the delicate balance of flavors.

4

Simmer for approximately 20 minutes, allowing the spices to infuse into the wine.

5

Taste and adjust the sweetness or spice levels as needed.

6

Serve in heatproof glasses or mugs.

7

Garnish with an orange wedge, a cinnamon stick, and a star anise for a festive touch.
