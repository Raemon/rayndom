# Family Collection

A complete collection of our family-inspired wines. From the crisp elegance of Chardonnay to the delicate charm of Rosé and the silky depth of Pinot Noir, this collection captures the spirit of sharing and togetherness at the holiday table. 

## PACK DETAILS

![](https://images.commerce7.com/wente-family-estates/images/original/2024-nikis-rose-1738347942671.png)

#### [2024 Niki's Rosé](/product/2024-nikis-rose/)

The 2024 Niki's Rose opens with aromatics of melon, strawberry and rose petal. Delivers on the palate tropical flavors of mango and passion fruit with a complexing touch of minerality. The wine finishes with mouth watering acidity that makes is perfect for food pairing or a hot summer day.

![](https://images.commerce7.com/wente-family-estates/images/original/alys-web-1749167714511.png)

#### [2023 Aly's Pinot Noir](/product/alys-pinot-noir-2023/)

Our 2023 Aly’s Pinot Noir was crafted to showcase the Arroyo Seco AVA terroir. The wine has expressive aromas of cherry, cranberry, rose petal, forest floor and cinnamon. Bright flavors of red cherry and vanilla are rounded out

![](https://images.commerce7.com/wente-family-estates/images/original/erics-chard-nv-1747678697260.png)

#### [2024 Eric's Chardonnay](/product/2024-erics-chardonnay/)

The 2024 Eric's Chardonnay is unoaked to exemplify the beautiful varietal character of Livermore Valley Chardonnay. This unoaked Chardonnay is bursting with aromas of citrus, melon, and white flowers. The wine has bright flavors of honeydew and meyer lemon with a touch of minerality.
