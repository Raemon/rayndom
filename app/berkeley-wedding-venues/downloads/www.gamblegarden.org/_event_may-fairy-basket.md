# A Tisket, A Tasket, A May Fairy Basket

Date:

## Saturday, May 18, 2024 @ 9:30 am \- 11:30 am

Location:

[ 1431 Waverley Street   
Palo Alto, CA 94301 United States ]()

[ Register Now ](https://app.etapestry.com/cart/ElizabethFGambleGarden/cart5/item.php?ref=350.0.171039769)

Magic is in the air this spring as instructor Annie Carl shows students how to create their own Fairy Garden in a basket. These whimsical works of art are easy to carry, move around, and place anywhere for a festive occasion. Gardening enthusiast or not, do come and join us for this fun class. What a delightful way to enjoy May and have a keepsake for your self or giveaway as a gift. See you among the Fairies!

This class is suitable for teens and adults. Each registered student will receive supplies **good for one Fairy Garden** : one basket, plastic liner, soil, fairy, and garden items to place among the plants used. Each registered student is welcome to bring a guest: one friend or one child under 13 to help assemble their basket. Guests do not receive additional supplies; if additional Fairy Gardens are desired, they must register separately.

**Price**

$45 Members / $60 Non-members

Gamble Garden members enjoy a 20% discount! Members, enter promotion code **IRIS** at checkout to receive your discount. Registration lists are checked with member lists. You may be asked to confirm your membership in a follow up email.

_sample Fairy Garden, in-class supplies and results may differ_

### Instructor Bio

Annie Carl has been a garden liaison for a big Community Garden in Palo Alto, California since 2015. Her love of gardening and design are fused together in her work as a Graphic Designer, Landscape Designer, and the owner of online store ‘[The Garden Liaison](https://thegardenliaison.com)‘ . Annie loves to grow all kinds of vegetables, berries and flowers, and she loves to create Fairy gardens!
