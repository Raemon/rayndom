# Halloween 2023: Fun For the Entire Family

Date:

## Saturday, October 28, 2023 @ 10:00 am \- 11:00 am

Location:

[ 1431 Waverley Street   
Palo Alto, CA 94301 United States ]()

#### This event is sold out! To join the waitlist, [click here](https://forms.gle/ks9qquTqwSMRGu8s9)

Don your capes, round glasses, magical hats and fly yourselves to Gamble Garden this Halloween. We will be celebrating with a display of confectionery, Butterbeer, balloon art, fun crafts and much more. The Palo Alto Junior Museum & Zoo will showcase live owls for our learning astonishment! Write a letter and see if these owls will carry your messages to the recipient? Come to this Halloween event at Gamble Garden and find out!

### Activities

Palo Alto Junior Museum & Zoo will present a demonstration with two live owls  
Balloon twister extraordinaire Leo Kargin  
Face painting  
An Array of Confections  
Harry Potter Trivia & Prizes  
Arts & Crafts  
Butterbeer! (A&W cream soda with whipped cream)  
Golden Egg decorating  
Write letters to be delivered by “Owl Post”  
Palo Alto Police Department’s Animal Control Team with animal education and crafts
