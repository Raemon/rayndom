# Winter Festival Marketplace

Date:

## Saturday, December 2, 2023 @ 10:00 am \- 4:00 pm

Location:

[ 1431 Waverley Street   
Palo Alto, CA 94301 United States ]()

[ Register Now ](https://tickets.gamblegarden.org/event/winter-festival-marketplace)

**Friday and Saturday, December 1 & 2 @ 10:00 am – 4:00pm**

_Holiday Gifts ~ Hostess Gifts ~ Unique Gifts!_

Ring in the Season at Gamble Garden’s Winter Festival! Enjoy one-stop shopping for holiday gifts, greenery, and décor in the festive Gamble Garden atmosphere.

Activities include:

• A Festive Marketplace with vendors in the Gamble house and outdoors.  
• Over The Garden Fence – Winter Edition. Gamble’s signature sale that always draws crowds to purchase previously loved and vintage treasures at remarkable prices.  
• Free entry to win a beautifully decorated tree! Purchase additional tickets at 1 for $5 or 5 for $20  
• Bid for holiday gift baskets, exclusive experiences at more at our Silent Auction. Can’t wait? Skip the bidding and select Buy It Now

### Tickets: $5

All ages welcome. Children age 2 and under FREE (no ticket necessary).
