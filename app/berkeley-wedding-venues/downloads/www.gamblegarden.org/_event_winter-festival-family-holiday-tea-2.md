# Winter Festival Family Holiday Tea

Date:

## Saturday, December 2, 2023 @ 2:00 pm \- 3:00 pm

Location:

[ ]()

## *SOLD OUT*

Gather with friends and family for a very special holiday tea at Gamble Garden. Reserve your table for 11:00 am – 12:00 pm, or 2:00 pm – 3:00 pm.

### Menu

Variety of teas and cider  
Tea sandwiches: cucumber, egg salad and cheese  
Ginger current scone with cream and jam  
Fresh Fruit  
Petit fours  
Holiday cookies  
Optional: passed parmesan pastry pups

### Activities

Before or after your tea, wander around and enjoy all that Winter Festival has to offer. Browse for holiday gifts, greenery, and décor in the festival atmosphere. Activities include:

• A Festive Marketplace with vendors in the Gamble house and outdoors.  
• Over The Garden Fence – Winter Edition. Gamble’s signature sale that always draws crowds to purchase previously loved and vintage treasures at remarkable prices.  
• Purchase raffle tickets to win a beautifully decorated tree! 1 for $5 or 5 for $20  
• Bid for holiday gift baskets, exclusive experiences at more at our Silent Auction. Can’t wait? Skip the bidding and select Buy It Now!

### Price

per person: $50 Members / $65 Non-members. Suitable for ages 6 and up. All guests will be seated at communal tables.

**Reservations open to the public on October 1st. Member Advance Reservations begin September 5th with a link in your Member Newsletter.**
