# November Tour of Elizabeth Gamble House & Garden

Date:

## Tuesday, November 21, 2023 @ 1:30 pm \- 2:30 pm

Location:

[ 1431 Waverley Street   
Palo Alto, CA 94301 United States ]()

Step back in time to explore the life of Elizabeth Gamble and her historic house and gardens. This 60-minute guided tour starts on the front steps of the house at 1431 Waverley Street in Palo Alto.

Our November tour will put the spotlight on the soul of Gamble Garden, its soil and seeds.
