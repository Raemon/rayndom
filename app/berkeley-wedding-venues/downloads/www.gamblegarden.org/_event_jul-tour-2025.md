# July Tour of Elizabeth Gamble House and Garden

Date:

## Tuesday, July 15, 2025 @ 1:30 pm \- 3:00 pm

Location:

[ 1431 Waverley Street   
Palo Alto, CA 94301 United States ]()

[ Register Now ](https://www.eventbrite.com/e/july-tour-of-elizabeth-gamble-house-and-garden-tickets-1146548229619)

Step back in time to explore the life of Elizabeth Gamble and her historic house and gardens. This 90-minute guided tour starts on the front steps of the house at 1431 Waverley Street in Palo Alto.

Our July tour will put the spotlight on native bees. Tour attendees will be climbing 8 steps and walking about a half mile.

This tour is appropriate for adults and children age 10 and up. If you have any questions, please call us at 650-329-1356.

### Registration

Members: FREE  
Non-members: $15 adults, $5 under 18.

Gamble Garden Members can attend this tour for FREE! Members, click on “Enter Promo Code” below and enter the code IRIS to sign up for free tickets. Registration lists are checked with member lists. You may be asked to confirm your membership in a follow up email.

Registration fees are nonrefundable. Gamble reserves the right to cancel this class or event due to unforeseen events such as extreme weather or a change in Covid protocol. In the event of a cancellation your payment will be reimbursed , net of service charge if applicable.
