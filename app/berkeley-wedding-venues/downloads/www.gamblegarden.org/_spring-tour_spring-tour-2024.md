# Celebrate the best of spring

Join us for Spring Tour on April 24-25, 2026

Tour five private [gardens](https://www.gamblegarden.org/spring-tour/gardens/) and be inspired by their beauty on April 24 & 25, 10 am-4 pm.

Before or after your self-guided tour, visit Gamble Garden for an unparalleled shopping experience at the [Marketplace](https://www.gamblegarden.org/spring-tour/marketplace/) featuring local vendors, our signature [Over The Garden Fence](https://www.gamblegarden.org/spring-tour/otgf/) sale of vintage items, and a Gamble-grown [plant sale](https://www.gamblegarden.org/spring-tour/spring-plant-sale/). Bid on unique items at our [Silent Auction](https://www.gamblegarden.org/spring-tour/silent-auction/). Shop for unique garden-related gifts and get advice from horticultural resources. Enjoy refreshments while live music serenades you.

All activities at Gamble Garden are free; tickets are required to tour the private gardens.

# Parking

  * Parking is limited to neighborhood streets at the private gardens.
  * One disabled person placard parking space is reserved outside each private garden.
  * Gamble Garden has two parking lots. One is accessible from Waverley Street, the other from Churchill Ave. There is street parking surrounding Gamble. 



# Accessibility

  * All private gardens on the tour are suitable for people who use walkers, but none are suitable for wheelchairs. All Spring Tour activities at Gamble Garden are fully accessible.
  * There is one space reserved for disabled person parking placards at each private garden. 
  * At Gamble Garden there are two disabled person parking placard spaces, one in each lot. One lot is located off Waverley St near Embarcadero and the other off Churchill Ave. 



# Enjoy Lunch at Gamble Garden

Spend the day and enjoy a meal in the garden.

  * Tables and chairs will be available at Gamble Garden.
