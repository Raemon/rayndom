# Spring Tour

Date:

## Friday, April 25, 2025 @ 10:00 am \- 4:00 pm

Location:

[ 1431 Waverley Street   
Palo Alto, CA 94301 United States ]()

[ Register Now ](https://www.gamblegarden.org/springtour#tickets)

Don’t miss Gamble Garden’s popular annual Spring Tour, featuring five beautiful homes and gardens, carefully curated to inspire and delight.

Before or after your tour, visit Gamble Garden to shop for unique gift items at our Marketplace, and find gently used treasures at Over The Garden Fence sale. Get advice from horticultural resources, enjoy lunch and refreshments, while live music serenades you throughout the day. Don’t miss the event of the season!

A Spring Tour ticket gives the holder exclusive access to each of the five private gardens on the tour. All private gardens on the tour are suitable for people who use walkers, but none are suitable for wheelchairs. All Spring Tour activities at Gamble Garden are fully accessible. Activities at Gamble Garden are free and open to all.
