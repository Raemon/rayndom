# Mocktails at Gamble Garden

Date:

## Friday, August 30, 2024 @ 4:00 pm \- 6:00 pm

Location:

[ 1431 Waverley Street   
Palo Alto, CA 94301 United States ]()

[ Register Now ](https://app.etapestry.com/cart/ElizabethFGambleGarden/cart5/item.php?ref=350.0.210396215)

Celebrate summer’s end at Gamble Garden’s Mocktails Mixer! We will have a variety of syrups, bitters, garnishes, seltzers, herbs — all derived from our garden. Learn to make flavorings to mix your favorite cocktails and then recreate them later using herbs from your own home garden. Whether it’s a mojito or Roy Rogers, or you simply want to try something new, you’ll be able to experiment and create your own mocktail all evening long. Pair your drinks with homemade pastries and hor d’oeuvres. Bring your unsolved gardening issues because Gamble’s Horticulture Team will be available to answer questions. Suitable for ages 13 and up. Secure your spot at this summer’s coolest party now!

### Price

$90/person (Non-Member); $75/person (Gamble Member)
