# Robin Chapman In Conversation with Susan Woodman

Date:

## Thursday, January 23, 2025 @ 4:00 pm \- 5:30 pm

Location:

[ 1431 Waverley Street   
Palo Alto, CA 94301 United States ]()

[ Register Now ](https://app.etapestry.com/cart/ElizabethFGambleGarden/default/item.php?ref=350.0.355736629)

Join fellow Gamble Garden Members as journalist and historian, Robin Chapman, moderates programs with a focus on local history, especially with connections to the Gamble Family and Gamble Garden.

On Thursday, January 23, 2025, Long-time Gamble volunteer and current Board member Susan discusses the making of the book, _Landscape of Optimism, The Elizabeth F. Gamble Garden_ , from concept to publishing. Susan is the author of _Landscape of Optimism, The Elizabeth F. Gamble Garden_.

This is a Gamble Member Event. Registration lists are checked with member lists. You may be asked to confirm your membership in a follow up email.

Members are encouraged to invite a friend to the presentation.
