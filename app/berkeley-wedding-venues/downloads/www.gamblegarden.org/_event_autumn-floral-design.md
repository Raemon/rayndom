# Autumn Floral Design

Date:

## Saturday, October 11, 2025 @ 9:30 am \- 11:30 am

Location:

[ 1431 Waverley Street   
Palo Alto, CA 94301 United States ]()

### *Class Full*

Celebrate the Beauty of Autumn with a Floral Design Class at Gamble!

As the garden bursts into a tapestry of fall color—think vibrant mums, cheerful zinnias, fragrant herbs, and graceful grasses—join us for a hands-on class where nature’s bounty becomes your creative palette. Together, we’ll craft stunning floral arrangements that capture the essence of the season and bring its warmth indoors.

Guided by Katherine, you’ll learn practical tips and inspiring techniques to design with confidence and style. Whether you’re a seasoned arranger or just beginning, enjoy a morning in the garden designing arrangements filled with loads of texture and color.

Come for the blooms—leave with a beautiful arrangement and some fresh inspiration!

Please bring your floral clippers; all other materials included. This class is suitable for adults and teens.

**Please register by Monday October 6 to allow time for class planning and materials purchase.**

### Instructor Bio

Katherine Glazier is an instructor and floral arranger at Gamble Garden and she co-led the floral design program at Filoli for eight years. She holds a European Master Certification (EMC) and a Teacher’s Diploma in the Sogetsu school of Ikebana. Katherine exhibits at Bouquets to Art at the deYoung Museum and she conducts public floral demonstrations. Seasonal and locally grown material, with an emphasis on the beauty of California floriculture, is showcased in her designs. She also enjoys incorporating eastern and western design philosophies in her work. For more information please refer to: www.kjglazierdesign.com
