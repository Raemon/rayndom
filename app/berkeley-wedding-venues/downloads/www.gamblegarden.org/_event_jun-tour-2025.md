# June Tour of Elizabeth Gamble Garden

Date:

## Tuesday, June 17, 2025 @ 1:30 pm \- 3:00 pm

Location:

[ 1431 Waverley Street   
Palo Alto, CA 94301 United States ]()

[ Register Now ](https://www.eventbrite.com/e/june-tour-of-elizabeth-gamble-house-and-garden-tickets-774167585087)

Step back in time to explore the life of Elizabeth Gamble and her historic gardens. This 90-minute guided tour starts on the front steps of the house at 1431 Waverley Street in Palo Alto.

Our June tour will put the spotlight on that special connection between sun and leaf.

This tour is appropriate for adults and children age 10 and up. Tour attendees will be climbing 8 steps and walking about a half mile.

### Registration

Registration is required. Please register online or at the gate.

Non member pricing: $15 adults, $5 under 18.

Gamble Garden Members can attend this tour for FREE! Members, click on “Enter promotional code” below and enter the code IRIS to sign up for free tickets. Registration lists are checked with member lists. You may be asked to confirm your membership in a follow up email.

Registration fees are nonrefundable. Gamble reserves the right to cancel this class or event due to unforeseen events such as extreme weather or a change in Covid protocol. In the event of a cancellation your payment will be reimbursed , net of service charge if applicable.
