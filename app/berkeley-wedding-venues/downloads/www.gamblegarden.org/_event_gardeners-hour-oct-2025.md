# Gardeners’ Hour: October

Date:

## Monday, October 13, 2025 @ 5:30 pm \- 6:30 pm

Location:

[ 1431 Waverley Street   
Palo Alto, CA 94301 United States ]()

[ Register Now ](https://www.eventbrite.com/e/gardeners-hour-october-tickets-1364961288959)

Are you a gardener? Or interested in gardening? Come to Gamble Garden to meet others who share your interests and discuss your gardens. This **FREE** event repeats monthly on every Second Monday of the month.

All ages welcome. Please register in advance and check in when you arrive at the Gamble Garden Tea House.

### Plant Swap

Our October Gardeners’ Hour features a plant swap! We encourage all attendees to bring plants, seeds or cuttings to share with your neighbors. Extras after the event will be given away to the community.
