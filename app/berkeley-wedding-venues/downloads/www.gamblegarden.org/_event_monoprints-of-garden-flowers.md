# Monoprints of Garden Flowers

Date:

## Saturday, July 20, 2024 @ 9:30 am \- 11:30 am

Location:

[ 1431 Waverley Street   
Palo Alto, CA 94301 United States ]()

### *Class Full*

Create flower mono prints of garden flowers using a gel plate you paint. No two prints are alike . Art work is printed on thick cotton paper . Easy, simple technique and fun! All materials included. This class is suitable for adults and teens.

Enrollment is limited so that the instructor may assist attendees as necessary. The registration period has been extended but you are encouraged to sign up as soon as possible as space is limited.

### Price

$56 Member (use code IRIS) / $70 Non-member

Gamble Garden members enjoy a $5 discount! Members, enter promo code **IRIS** to receive your discount. Registration lists are checked with member lists. You may be asked to confirm your membership in a follow up email.

### Instructor Bio

Kathryn Hilton gave a very successful class last summer at Gamble. She is a California Native and lives in the San Francisco Bay Area. She is an artist, designer, and author. Much of her work is inspired by nature and travel. She creates art that is vibrant and full of joy. Original work can be seen at Gallery at 143 Main Street, Los Altos.
