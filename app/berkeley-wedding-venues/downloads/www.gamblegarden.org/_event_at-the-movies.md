# At the Movies: Popular Cinematic Songs

Date:

## Sunday, October 20, 2024 @ 10:00 am \- 11:00 am

Location:

[ 1431 Waverley Street   
Palo Alto, CA 94301 United States ]()

[ Register Now ](https://app.etapestry.com/cart/ElizabethFGambleGarden/cart3/category.php?ref=350.0.240197653)

Performed by Bay Area Youth Chamber Musicians, Harmony Ensemble

Spend a beautiful Sunday morning at Gamble Garden enjoying a medley of tunes from popular movies. Performed by Bay Area musical youth group, Harmony Ensemble, this one-hour concert features songs from movies such as “La La Land,” “Cinema Paradiso,” Breakfast at Tiffany’s,” “Les Miserables,” and more. Experience Gamble Garden in this new and exciting way!

Harmony Ensemble is a student-run program dedicated to providing young, devoted musicians with the valuable experience of collaborating with others. To learn more about Harmony Ensemble, visit [www.harmonyensemblecba.org](https://www.harmonyensemblecba.org).
