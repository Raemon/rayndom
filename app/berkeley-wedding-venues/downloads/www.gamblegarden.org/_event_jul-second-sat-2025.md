# July Second Saturday: Pollinators

Date:

## Saturday, July 12, 2025 @ 10:00 am \- 12:00 pm

Location:

[ 1431 Waverley Street   
Palo Alto, CA 94301 United States ]()

[ Register Now ](https://www.eventbrite.com/e/july-second-second-saturday-tickets-1390519353819)

All ages are welcome to spend a beautiful July morning at Gamble Garden when the garden will be literally buzzing with activity!

### FREE ACTIVITIES

**Guided Tours** – Explore the historic home and garden on an informative tour around the property. Tours meet in front of the house, and our expert guides will point out seasonal highlights and thematic points of interest along with explaining the origins of the estate. Take a scavenger hunt with you and see what you can find along the way! Garden tours begin at 10:15, 10:45, and 11:30 am.

**Education Stations** – Examine different pollinators under a microscope, and learn about pollinator diversity.

**Themed Storytime**

**Build-A-Bee Crafts Station**

**Visit Elizabeth’s House** – Walk through Elizabeth’s house, built in 1902, and get a look into the early days of Palo Alto. Our docents will be on hand to answer questions and share stories of old.

### REGISTRATION

Second Saturdays are FREE and open to all. Register online in advance, or at the door. Choose from 10:00am, 10:30am, or 11:00 am entry times. All Second Saturday guests, when you arrive, please check in at one of our two Welcome Tables in order to enjoy the Second Saturday tours and special activities.
