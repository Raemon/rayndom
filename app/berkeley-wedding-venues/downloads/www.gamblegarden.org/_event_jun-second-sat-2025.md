# June Second Saturday

Date:

## Saturday, June 14, 2025 @ 10:00 am \- 12:00 pm

Location:

[ 1431 Waverley Street   
Palo Alto, CA 94301 United States ]()

[ Register Now ](https://www.eventbrite.com/e/june-second-saturday-summer-solstice-birthday-celebration-tickets-1349201902209)

Celebrate the summer solstice and Gamble Garden’s 40th birthday at our June Second Saturday event! Second Saturdays are FREE and open to all.

### Free Activities

**Gamble Garden Birthday Celebration**  
Gamble Garden is turning 40 and you are invited! We’ll have 40 birthday cakes to celebrate Gamble’s 40th birthday party.

**Guided Tours**  
Explore the historic home and garden on an informative tour around the property. Tours meet in front of the house, and our expert guides will point out seasonal highlights and thematic points of interest along with explaining the origins of the estate. Take a scavenger hunt with you and see what you can find along the way! Garden tours begin at 10:15, 10:45, and 11:30 am.

**Sun and Photosynthesis Exploration**  
Visit kid-friendly education and craft stations around the garden that highlight the summer solstice and the sun. We are delighted to welcome the San Mateo County Astronomical Society as a partner this month!

**Craft Station**  
Make a translucent suncatcher to take home.

**Storytime**  
Listen to stories about the sun at our sun-themed storytime with YCS reader Aubrey

**Happy Birthday Gamble Garden!**  
Enjoy birthday cake at 11:00 on the Carriage House Patio!

**Visit Elizabeth’s House**  
Walk through Elizabeth’s house, built in 1902, and get a look into the early days of Palo Alto. Our docents will be on hand to answer questions and share stories of old.

### Registration

Second Saturdays are FREE and open to all. Register online in advance, or at the door. Choose from 10:00am, 10:30am, or 11:00 am entry times. All Second Saturday guests, when you arrive, please check in at one of our two Welcome Tables in order to enjoy the Second Saturday tours and special activities.
