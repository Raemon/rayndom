# iPhone Flower Photography

Date:

## Tuesday, May 14, 2024 @ 10:00 am \- 12:00 pm

Location:

[ 1431 Waverley Street   
Palo Alto, CA 94301 United States ]()

[ Register Now ](https://app.etapestry.com/cart/ElizabethFGambleGarden/cart5/item.php?ref=350.0.135014679)

Tom Upton will teach you how to combine your love of flowers and the new found love of your iPhone camera. First, he will guide you through the main features we all should know about for a solid starting point; then he will demonstrate some quite marvelous, hidden features. Finally he will address color and composition of flower photography and take you out in the garden. The class size will be limited to 15 participants to allow Tom time to help and coach everyone.

### Price

$35 Members / $45 Non-members

Gamble Garden members enjoy a 20% discount! Members, enter promotion code **IRIS** at checkout to receive your discount. Registration lists are checked with member lists. You may be asked to confirm your membership in a follow up email.

### Instructor Bio

Tom Upton is an award-winning professional photographer and “Photo-trainer”, owner of Tom Upton Studios in Mountain View. A “Photogeneralist”, he has been creating portraits, landscapes, still life, natural subjects and commercial work For over 40 years he has “taught countless people how to love their cameras and find their innate creative center”.
