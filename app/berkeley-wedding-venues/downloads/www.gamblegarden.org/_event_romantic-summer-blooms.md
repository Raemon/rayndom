# Romantic Summer Blooms

Date:

## Saturday, July 15, 2023 @ 9:30 am \- 11:30 am

Location:

[ 1431 Waverley Street   
Palo Alto, CA 94301 United States ]()

**REGISTRATION IS NOW CLOSED.** [Sign up now for our next Flower Arranging Class](https://www.gamblegarden.org/event/changing-seasons-in-the-garden/) _Changing Seasons in the Garden_ on September 16, 2023

Spend a morning at Gamble and let the summer garden inspire you. Learn how to create a dreamy and seasonal design filled with glorious flowers such as Roses, Lisianthus, Veronica and Queen Anne’s Lace. Discover how to enhance your designs with the thoughtful use of color, harmony and texture. Learn some helpful tips on how to best purchase and care for these special summer blossoms for maximum enjoyment. You will leave class with a luxurious arrangement and some new found floral design skills. This class is suitable for adults and teens.

### Cost

$119 Member (use code IRIS) / $149 Non-member

Gamble Garden members enjoy a 20% discount! Members, enter promotion code IRIS at checkout to receive your discount. Registration lists are checked with member lists. You may be asked to confirm your membership in a follow up email.

**Please register by Monday July 10th to allow time for class planning and materials purchase.**

### Instructor Bio

Katherine Glazier is an instructor and floral arranger at Gamble Garden and she co-led the floral design program at Filoli for eight years. She holds a European Master Certification (EMC) and a Teacher’s Diploma in the Sogetsu school of Ikebana. Katherine exhibits at Bouquets to Art at the deYoung Museum and she conducts public floral demonstrations. Seasonal and locally grown material, with an emphasis on the beauty of California floriculture, is showcased in her designs. She also enjoys incorporating eastern and western design philosophies in her work. For more information please refer to: www.kjglazierdesign.com
