# Easter Egg Hunt

Date:

## Sunday, March 24, 2024 @ 9:30 am \- 1:30 pm

Location:

[ 1431 Waverley Street   
Palo Alto, CA 94301 United States ]()

The Easter Egg Hunt is SOLD OUT. Please join us next year. 

Mark your calendars for Gamble’s popular Easter Egg Hunt! Fabergé eggs, petting zoo, treats, toddler area, arts & crafts, and a beautiful setting for a fun egg hunt. Sign up for 1-hr egg hunts starting at 9:30am, 11:00am, or 12:30pm. This popular event is sure to sell out, so get your tickets ASAP.

### Price

• Infants 1 year and below: FREE  
• Children age 2-17: $45 Members / $55 Non-members  
• Adults 18 and up: $40 Members / $50 Non-members

### Terms and Conditions

• All Sales are Final.  
• Tickets are non-refundable.  
• The time slot you choose is final and cannot be exchanged for a different time slot.  
• All persons (child and adult) must have a ticket to enter.  
• All infants and children must be accompanied by a ticketed adult. If no adult ticket is purchased with an infant ticket, that infant ticket will be voided.  
• This event will proceed, rain or shine.
