# Monoprints of Flowers

Date:

## Saturday, June 24, 2023 @ 9:30 am \- 11:30 am

Location:

[ 1431 Waverley Street   
Palo Alto, CA 94301 United States ]()

[ Register Now ](https://app.etapestry.com/cart/ElizabethFGambleGarden/cart5/item.php?ref=1462.0.1073513520)

Create mono prints of garden flowers using a gel plate that you paint. The prints are pressed onto thick cotton paper. No two prints are alike. They are frameable or made into note cards.

This class is suitable for adults and teens. Limit 12 students. Students should bring a pencil and pen to class. All other materials are provided.

### Price

$56 Member (use code IRIS) / $70 Non-member

Gamble Garden members enjoy a 20% discount! Members, enter promo code IRIS to receive your discount. Registration lists are checked with member lists. You may be asked to confirm your membership in a follow up email.

No refunds or exchanges.

### Instructor Bio

Kathryn Hilton is a California Native living in the Bay Area. She is an artist, designer and author. One of her books is called: IN THE. GARDEN An Artist’s View. Much of her work is inspired by nature and by travel. She creates art that is vibrant and full of joy.

Using a gel plate on thick cotton paper, she begins a layering process. Layers are added using acrylic paint and then finished with watercolor/gouache and pastels.

Original Artwork can been seen and is available at Gallery 9, 143 Main St. Los Altos.
