# September Second Saturday: Fall Equinox

Date:

## Saturday, September 13, 2025 @ 10:00 am \- 12:00 pm

Location:

[ 1431 Waverley Street   
Palo Alto, CA 94301 United States ]()

[ Register Now ](https://www.eventbrite.com/e/september-second-saturday-fall-equinox-tickets-1390540767869)

All ages are invited to join a guided tour of the historic house and gardens on a beautiful September morning. This month we will be celebrating the Fall Equinox!

### FREE ACTIVITIES

More details coming soon!

**•** Guided Tours  
**•** Education Stations  
**•** Themed Storytime  
**•** Crafts Station  
**•** Visit Elizabeth’s House  
**•** Plant Sale, 10:00am – 4:00pm. Fall is a great time to plant! Come to Gamble Garden’s fall equinox plant sale to stock up on all kinds of ornamentals, vegetables, herbs, natives, perennial plants, and more.

### REGISTRATION

Second Saturday is free and appropriate for all ages. Register in advance or at the door. All Second Saturday guests, when you arrive, please check in at one of our two Welcome Tables in order to enjoy the Second Saturday tours and special activities.
