# Winter Festival Grand Opening Party

Date:

## Thursday, November 30, 2023 @ 5:30 pm \- 7:30 pm

Location:

[ 1431 Waverley Street   
Palo Alto, CA 94301 United States ]()

[ Register Now ](https://tickets.gamblegarden.org/event/winter-festival-grand-opening-party)

Tickets on sale now for Gamble Garden Winter Festival’s Opening Night event! Gamble Garden will be beautifully decorated and illuminated for the season, perfect for a date night, girls night out, or fun with friends.

Be the first to enjoy Gamble’s holiday delights: twinkling holiday lights, tree lighting ceremony, one-stop shopping for holiday gifts, potted plants and greenery created by our garden staff, home décor in the festive Gamble Garden atmosphere.

• A Festive Marketplace with over 20 vendors in the Gamble house and outdoors under a canopy of twinkling lights.  
• Enjoy carolers and holiday music throughout the event.  
• Over The Garden Fence ~ Winter Edition. Gamble’s signature sale always draws crowds! Purchase previously loved and vintage treasures at remarkable prices.  
• Bid for holiday gift baskets, exclusive experiences at more at our Silent Auction. Can’t wait? Skip the bidding and select Buy It Now!

### Tickets

$75 Member / $95 Non-Member. This is an adults-only event.
