# May Second Saturday: Fun With Flowers

Date:

## Saturday, May 10, 2025 @ 10:00 am \- 12:00 pm

Location:

[ 1431 Waverley Street   
Palo Alto, CA 94301 United States ]()

Join us for a beautiful spring morning in the garden! **Second Saturdays are free, family-friendly, and open to all.**

Celebrate the season of flowers with hands-on activities, guided tours, and floral inspiration throughout the garden. This month’s program is presented in partnership with the **Garden Club of Palo Alto** , **Palo Alto City Library** , **Youth Community Service (YCS)** , and the **Master Gardeners**.

### Free Activities

**Guided Garden Tours**  
Discover the beauty and history of Gamble Garden on a docent-led tour. Tours depart from the front of the house at 10:15, 10:45, and 11:30 AM. Grab a scavenger hunt and explore along the way!

**Arrange a Bouquet for Mom**  
Create a small posey bouquet from freshly cut flowers—just in time for Mother’s Day. Offered in partnership with the [Garden Club of Palo Alto](https://gardenclubofpaloalto.org/).

**Flower Science Stations**  
Get curious about blooms! Learn the parts of a flower and take one apart at our flower dissection table.

**Storytime**  
Enjoy spring-themed stories in the library or outdoors. This month’s storytime is brought to you by [Youth Community Service (YCS)](https://www.youthcommunityservice.org/) and [Palo Alto City Library](https://library.cityofpaloalto.org/).

**Visit Elizabeth’s House**  
Step inside Elizabeth Gamble’s 1902 home and enjoy floral arrangements made with blooms from our garden. Docents will be on hand to share stories and answer questions.

**Master Gardeners Table**  
Bring your plant questions to the [Master Gardeners table](https://ucanr.edu/site/uc-master-gardeners-santa-clara-county)—they’re ready to help!

### Reserve Your Spot

Second Saturdays are free, but advance registration is encouraged.  
All guests, please check in at the Welcome Desk upon arrival, located in the driveway of the Gamble House, near the intersection of Waverley Street and Churchill Ave.  
_Activities subject to change._
