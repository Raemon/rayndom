# Hand-Tied Bouquets

Date:

## Saturday, July 26, 2025 @ 9:30 am \- 11:30 am

Location:

[ 1431 Waverley Street   
Palo Alto, CA 94301 United States ]()

### Registration is closed. If you would like to sign up for another floral class, visit [gamblegarden.org/floral](https://www.gamblegarden.org/events/category/floral/)

Hand-tied bouquets are a lovely way to celebrate a special occasion—or simply brighten someone’s day, including your own! Summer is the perfect season to explore some simple, approachable techniques for crafting those loose, airy arrangements everyone loves.

In this workshop, you’ll work with fresh, seasonal blooms like Roses, Veronica, Queen Anne’s lace, and delicate vines such as Jasmine. Learn how to design bouquets full of natural movement and charm. Instructor Katherine Glazier will also share some tips on how to wrap your bouquet for a stunning final touch.

Join us at Gamble Garden and experience the joy of bouquet making!

Please bring your floral clippers. Flowers and other materials are included. This class is suitable for adults and teens.

**Please register by Monday, July 21, to allow time for class planning and materials purchase.**

### Instructor Bio

Katherine Glazier is an instructor and floral arranger at Gamble Garden and she co-led the floral design program at Filoli for eight years. She holds a European Master Certification (EMC) and a Teacher’s Diploma in the Sogetsu school of Ikebana. Katherine exhibits at Bouquets to Art at the deYoung Museum and she conducts public floral demonstrations. Seasonal and locally grown material, with an emphasis on the beauty of California floriculture, is showcased in her designs. She also enjoys incorporating eastern and western design philosophies in her work. For more information please refer to: www.kjglazierdesign.com
