### Discover the Joy of Volunteering at Gamble Garden

Join us for a special event to explore the many volunteer opportunities at Gamble Garden! Whether you’re a seasoned gardener, an event enthusiast, or simply eager to give back to the community, there’s a place for you in our vibrant volunteer family.

What to Expect:  
– Meet Our Committees: Connect with current volunteers and staff at outdoor tables showcasing different aspects of our organization. Learn about roles in gardening, events, administration, and more.  
– Guided Garden Tours: Enjoy a stroll through our beautiful grounds led by members of the Garden Volunteer Committee.  
– Refreshments: Savor light refreshments as you mingle with fellow volunteers and discover how you can make an impact.

Come and see how you can contribute to maintaining and enhancing this treasured community resource. We look forward to welcoming you to Gamble Garden!
