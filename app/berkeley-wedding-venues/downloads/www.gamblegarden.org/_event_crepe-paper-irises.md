# Crepe Paper Irises

Date:

## Wednesday, July 2, 2025 @ 10:30 am \- 12:00 pm

Location:

[ 1431 Waverley Street   
Palo Alto, CA 94301 United States ]()

[ Register Now ](https://app.etapestry.com/cart/ElizabethFGambleGarden/cart3/category.php?ref=350.0.434496565)

Make the iconic Gamble Garden iris. Use exquisite, high-quality, crepe paper and a few simple supplies to create a beautiful iris perfect for any indoor space or as a gift to a special friend. This is a hands-on class with detailed step-by-step instructions.

All materials provided. No experience required. Suitable for ages 13 and up.

Bring a friend or family member to this fun summer activity. This special class happens only once as a tribute to Gamble’s 40th Anniversary.

### Price

$64 Members (use code IRIS) / $80 Non-members  
Gamble Garden members enjoy a 20% discount! Members, enter promotion code **IRIS** at checkout to receive your discount. Registration lists are checked with member lists. You may be asked to confirm your membership in a follow up email.

### Instructor Bio

Angela Tsang is a Bay Area artist who turns paper into flowers as a reminder that anything can be transformed into something beautiful. Visit her gallery www.wondersoflulu.com
