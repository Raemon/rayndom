# Robin Chapman In Conversation with Gloria Hom

Date:

## Thursday, October 24, 2024 @ 4:00 pm \- 5:30 pm

Location:

[ 1431 Waverley Street   
Palo Alto, CA 94301 United States ]()

[ Register Now ](https://app.etapestry.com/cart/ElizabethFGambleGarden/default/item.php?ref=350.0.280521759)

Join fellow Gamble Garden Members as journalist and historian, Robin Chapman, moderates programs with a focus on local history, especially with connections to the Gamble Family and Gamble Garden.

On October 24, 2024, Gamble Garden Member and Volunteer Gloria Hom discusses Cannery magnate, Thomas Foon Chew: His Achievements & Legacy. Gloria Hom, EdD, is a fourth-generation Californian and long-time Palo Altan whose grandfather established Bayside Cannery. Gloria taught economics and political science for 35 years at Foothill College, De Anza College and Mission Community College.

This is a Gamble Member Event. Registration lists are checked with member lists. You may be asked to confirm your membership in a follow up email.
