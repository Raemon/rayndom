# Winter Festival Holiday Night with Santa

Date:

## Friday, December 1, 2023 @ 5:30 pm \- 7:30 pm

Location:

[ ]()

[ Register Now ](https://tickets.gamblegarden.org/event/holiday-night-with-santa)

Get into the holiday spirit with Gamble Garden as we light up for the Winter Festival. Mr. and Mrs. Klaus look forward to meeting you! Plenty of photo opportunities with Santa and his sleigh, a beautiful ballet performance, holiday shopping, and more. Other activities include:

• A Festive Marketplace with vendors in the Gamble house and outdoors under a canopy of twinkling lights.  
• Over The Garden Fence ~ Winter Edition. Gamble’s signature sale always draws crowds! Purchase previously loved and vintage treasures at remarkable prices.  
• Holiday Tree Lighting Ceremony  
• Ballet performance with dancers from Bayer Ballet  
• Lighted Fairy stilt walker  
• Sweet and Savory Food for sale  
• Purchase raffle tickets to win a beautifully decorated tree! 1 ticket for $5 or 5 for $20  
• Bid for holiday gift baskets, exclusive experiences at more at our Silent Auction. Can’t wait? Skip the bidding and select Buy It Now!

### Tickets

$40 Members / $50 Non-members. All ages welcome. Children age 2 and under FREE (no ticket necessary).
