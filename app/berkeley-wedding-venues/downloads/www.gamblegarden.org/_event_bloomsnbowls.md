# Blooms & Bowls: Neighborhood Block Party

Date:

## Tuesday, August 5, 2025 @ 5:00 pm \- 8:00 pm

Location:

[ 1431 Waverley Street   
Palo Alto, CA 94301 United States ]()

**_Neighbors, save the date— August 5, 2025— for an evening of free, fun activities at Gamble Garden! We are celebrating[National Night Out](https://www.cityofpaloalto.org/Departments/Police/Community-Involvement/National-Night-Out) (NNO) along with communities across the nation and we are looking forward to connecting with the community._**

Amble over to Gamble Garden and stroll to Lawn Bowls for a neighborhood gathering in Old Palo Alto celebrating National Night Out on Tuesday, August 5, 5-8pm. We’ll have the full block open for neighborhood enjoyment, with activities for the whole family. Pack a picnic dinner and mingle with neighbors. Try your hand at Lawn Bowling. Kids activities, garden scavenger hunt, and more!

Co-hosted by Gamble Garden, the Palo Alto Lawn Bowls Club, and the Old Palo Alto Neighborhood Association (OPANA).
