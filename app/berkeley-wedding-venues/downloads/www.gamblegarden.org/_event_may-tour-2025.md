# May Tour of Elizabeth Gamble Garden

Date:

## Tuesday, May 20, 2025 @ 1:30 pm \- 3:00 pm

Location:

[ 1431 Waverley Street   
Palo Alto, CA 94301 United States ]()

[ Register Now ](https://www.eventbrite.com/e/may-tour-of-elizabeth-gamble-house-and-garden-tickets-774166311277)

Step back in time to explore the life of Elizabeth Gamble and her historic gardens. This 90-minute guided tour starts on the front steps of the house at 1431 Waverley Street in Palo Alto.

Our May tour will put the spotlight on Elizabeth Gamble and the flowers that she loved.

This tour is appropriate for adults and children age 10 and up. If you have any questions, please call us at 650-329-1356.

### Registration

Registration is required. Please register online or at the gate.

Non member pricing: $15 adults, $5 under 18.

Gamble Garden Members can attend this tour for FREE! Members, click on “Enter promotional code” below, enter the code IRIS, then select “Member Tickets” to sign up for free tickets. Registration lists are checked with member lists. You may be asked to confirm your membership in a follow up email.
