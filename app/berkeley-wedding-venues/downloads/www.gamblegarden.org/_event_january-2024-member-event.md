# January Member Event: Building Along Waverley in 1902

Date:

## Thursday, January 18, 2024 @ 4:30 pm \- 5:30 pm

Location:

[ 1431 Waverley Street   
Palo Alto, CA 94301 United States ]()

Registration is closed. This event is at capacity. 

In 1902, when the Gamble house was completed, Elizabeth Gamble was 14, Stanford University was 11, and Palo Alto was 8 years old. Catharine Garber, Founder & Partner at Fergus Garber, will give us a glimpse of what life was like on Waverley Street when the Gambles moved to Palo Alto!

### FREE for Gamble Garden Members!

This is a Gamble Member Event. Registration lists are checked with member lists. You may be asked to confirm your membership in a follow up email.

Members are encouraged to invite a friend to the presentation.
