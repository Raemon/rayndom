# Yoga In The Garden – July

Date:

## Saturday, July 19, 2025 @ 9:00 am \- 10:00 am

Location:

[ 1431 Waverley Street   
Palo Alto, CA 94301 United States ]()

[ Register Now ](https://www.eventbrite.com/e/july-yoga-in-the-garden-tickets-1320501839549)

Come and enjoy yoga in the garden with us. This class is open to all levels of practice and beginner friendly. Explore yoga alignment, movement, and balance through a full range of poses. You can expect a challenging and fun yet relaxing class that will leave you feeling more connected to your breath and in tune with yourself.

Meet at the front lawn by the canary palm tree. Please bring your own yoga mat. This class is suitable for adults and teens.

### Class Dates

This class meets at 9:00am – 10:00am on multiple dates, one Saturday each month from April till September. Click on each date below to register.

• [Saturday, May 17](https://www.gamblegarden.org/event/yoga-may-2025/)

*Dates are not transferrable — you must attend the date you register for*

### INSTRUCTOR BIO

Stephanie Le started practicing yoga 15 years ago as a way to help manage her chronic back pain. Her love of yoga quickly grew as her back pain decreased and allowed her to get deeper into her practice. She has completed her 500 hour certificate.
