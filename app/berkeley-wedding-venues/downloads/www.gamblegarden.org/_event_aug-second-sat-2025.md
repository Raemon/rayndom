# August Second Saturday: Harvest

Date:

## Saturday, August 9, 2025 @ 10:00 am \- 12:00 pm

Location:

[ 1431 Waverley Street   
Palo Alto, CA 94301 United States ]()

[ Register Now ](https://www.eventbrite.com/e/august-second-saturday-harvest-tickets-1390523997709)

All ages are invited to spend a beautiful August morning at Gamble Garden. Our focus this month will be the abundant harvest from our Edible Garden. Join us for garden tours, family nature hunts, kid friendly hands-on activities, a look inside our historic house, and more. UC Master Gardeners will be on hand to answer your home gardening questions.

### FREE ACTIVITIES

**Guided Tours** – Explore the historic home and garden on an informative tour around the property. Tours meet in front of the house, and our expert guides will point out seasonal highlights and thematic points of interest along with explaining the origins of the estate. Take a scavenger hunt with you and see what you can find along the way! Garden tours begin at 10:15, 10:45, and 11:30 am.

**Edible Garden Tasting** – Try a sample of the produce from Gamble’s Edible Garden!

**Education Stations** – Learn about harvesting and distributing excess food in our community.

**Themed Storytime** – Join us in the library at 10:30am and 11:00am for storytime with the Palo Alto Library.

**Harvest Basket Crafts Station** – Make a harvest basket and fruits out of paper.

**Visit Elizabeth’s House** – Walk through Elizabeth’s house, built in 1902, and get a look into the early days of Palo Alto. Our docents will be on hand to answer questions and share stories of old.

### REGISTRATION

Second Saturdays are FREE and open to all. Register online in advance, or at the door. Choose from 10:00am, 10:30am, or 11:00 am tour times.
