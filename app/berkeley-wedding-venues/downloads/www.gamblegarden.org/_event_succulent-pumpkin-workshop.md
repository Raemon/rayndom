# Succulent Pumpkin Decorating Workshop

Date:

## Saturday, October 5, 2024 @ 9:30 am \- 11:30 am

Location:

[ 1431 Waverley Street   
Palo Alto, CA 94301 United States ]()

[ Register Now ](https://app.etapestry.com/cart/ElizabethFGambleGarden/cart5/item.php?ref=350.0.268140983)

Unleash your creativity and join us for a workshop where we combine the charm of autumn with the beauty of succulents. In this hands-on experience, you’ll have the opportunity to decorate pumpkins with lush succulent arrangements, creating stunning seasonal decor that will bring a touch of nature to your space.

Materials will be provided but feel free to bring some of your own collection of berries, seed pods, leaves etc. to customize your pumpkin or to share with the group. Please bring pruning shears or heavy scissors to class.

This workshop is perfect for all skill levels, and is suitable for adults and children ages 8 and up. Registered adults may bring one child, age 8 and up. One pumpkin and decoration set per registered student. Minors must be accompanied by an adult.

Hot glue guns will be used, and attendees will need to sign a waiver before completing registration.

This is a fantastic way to celebrate the fall season while creating a unique piece of art that can adorn your home, porch, or autumn celebration table.

### Price

$48 Member (use code IRIS) / $60 Non-member

Gamble Garden members enjoy a $12 discount! Members, enter promo code IRIS to receive your discount. Registration lists are checked with member lists. You may be asked to confirm your membership in a follow up email.

### Instructor Bio

Kathy Coutré is a gardening enthusiast whose Stanford Campus garden has graced the pages of Sunset and Garden Gate magazines. Kathy’s green thumb and creativity shine through in her annual tradition of crafting succulent pumpkins. She transforms ordinary pumpkins into stunning botanical works of art. Kathy’s passion for gardening and her talent for creating succulent masterpieces make her the perfect instructor to guide you in embracing the beauty of nature while decorating pumpkins.
