# Grazing Board for Holiday Entertaining

Date:

## Sunday, November 19, 2023 @ 4:00 pm \- 5:30 pm

Location:

[ 1431 Waverley Street   
Palo Alto, CA 94301 United States ]()

[ Register Now ](https://app.etapestry.com/cart/ElizabethFGambleGarden/cart5/item.php?ref=350.0.38577175)

Join Erin Gleeson, well known local Artist and Author of The Forest Feast Cookbooks, for a delicious and artistic, and colorful Demonstration on how to put together a Masterpiece for your upcoming Holiday entertaining! 

Bring a Friend or two and join us in sharing the Masterpiece using your own provided cheese board with a glass of wine or nonalcoholic beverage, while shopping for Erin’s Cookbooks, Grazing Boards, and Tablecloths!

To provide a festive location, the class will be held in the beautiful Gamble Living room and Library.

This is a wonderful opportunity to to allow you to **indulge yourself** before the busy holiday season .

This event is suitable for adults. Enrollment will be limited.

### Price

$45 Member (use code IRIS) / $57 Non-member

### Instructor Bio

Author, Photographer and Illustrator of The Forest Feast Cookbook Series, Erin Gleeson is the New York Times bestselling cookbook author and artist behind the lifestyle brand The Forest Feast. After working in New York as a food photographer, she left the city for a cabin in the woods which took her work in a whole new direction. Inspired by local California produce, she has now created 5 vegetarian cookbooks and a line of products that showcase her unique blend of photography overlaid with watercolor illustrations and hand lettering. Erin’s goal is to inspire people to be creative at home, to cook, set a beautiful table, entertain, and share time together.
