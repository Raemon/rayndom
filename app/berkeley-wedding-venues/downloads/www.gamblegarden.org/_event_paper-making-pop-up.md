# Papermaking Pop-Up

Date:

## Saturday, June 1, 2024 @ 10:00 am \- 12:00 pm

Location:

[ 1431 Waverley Street   
Palo Alto, CA 94301 United States ]()

[ Register Now ](https://app.etapestry.com/cart/ElizabethFGambleGarden/cart3/category.php?ref=350.0.193255507)

Learn to make your own artisan paper at this fun hands-on workshop. Use floral petals, native plants and other horticultural elements to create unique and sustainable stationery. Amazing artist and instructor, [BeBe Jacobs](https://www.linkedin.com/in/bebejacobs/) from [Pacific Art League](https://www.pacificartleague.org), will guide you through the process from pulp to paper. Learn the history of papermaking, the different types of paper that can be made by hand, and the environmental benefits of making your own paper. Join us for a glorious morning of creativity and joviality as we design one-of-a-kind stationery using techniques by local artist, [Catherine Lucky](https://www.catherinelucky.com).

  * Please sign up for one time slot. This class will meet in the Gamble Garden Carriage House.
  * [10:00am – 11:00 am](https://app.etapestry.com/cart/ElizabethFGambleGarden/cart3/category.php?ref=350.0.193339449)
  * [11:00am – 12:00pm](https://app.etapestry.com/cart/ElizabethFGambleGarden/cart3/category.php?ref=350.0.193340471)



Perfect for ages 10 and up. All materials provided. No experience required. Everyone attending must have a paid ticket. Event held outdoors, so consider wearing clothes and shoes that may get wet. This pop-up is hosted by Gamble Garden and Pacific Art League.
