# Mocktails Mixer

Date:

## Friday, August 22, 2025 @ 5:00 pm \- 7:00 pm

Location:

[ 1431 Waverley Street   
Palo Alto, CA 94301 United States ]()

[ Register Now ](https://app.etapestry.com/cart/ElizabethFGambleGarden/cart5/item.php?ref=2731.0.8706073)

Celebrate summer at Gamble Garden’s Mocktails Mixer! Sample from a menu of custom mocktails crafted by our horticultural staff, featuring ingredients from our garden. Pair your drinks with a delectable selection of charcuterie complete with vegan and gluten free options. Our horticulture team will be on hand to talk about the recipes and ingredients used as well as any other gardening question our guest may have. Come join us for this wonderful evening in the garden!

All materials & ingredients provided. Suitable for adults. This event will be led by Gamble Garden’s horticultural staff.
