# March Second Saturday: Captivating Camellia, the Queen of Winter Flowers

Date:

## Saturday, March 8, 2025 @ 10:00 am \- 12:00 pm

Location:

[ 1431 Waverley Street   
Palo Alto, CA 94301 United States ]()

[ Register Now ](https://www.eventbrite.com/e/march-second-saturday-camellias-tickets-1243065565569)

Did you know that Gamble Garden is part of the American Camellia Trail? Join us for a free, family-friendly event celebrating these stunning winter bloomers! Explore guided tours of our camellia collection, engage with interactive learning stations, enjoy hands-on arts activities, and delight in story time for children. There’s something for everyone to discover in the garden!

### FREE ACTIVITIES

• Guided adult tours and family nature hunts at 10:00 am, 10:30 am, and 11:00 am  
• Story time at 10:30 am (library), 10:45 am (picnic tables), 11:15 am (library), and 11:30 am (picnic tables)

### REGISTRATION

Second Saturday is free and appropriate for all ages. Register for timed entry at 10:00 am, 10:30 am, and 11:30 am, in advance or at the door. All Second Saturday guests, when you arrive, please check in at one of our two Welcome Tables in order to enjoy the Second Saturday tours and special activities.

Please support Second Saturday with an optional donation when you register. We look forward to seeing you on Second Saturday!
