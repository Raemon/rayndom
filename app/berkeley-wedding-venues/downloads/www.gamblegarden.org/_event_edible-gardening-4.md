# Edible Gardening, Session Four: Fall

Date:

## Saturday, October 18, 2025 @ 10:00 am \- 11:30 am

Location:

[ 1431 Waverley Street   
Palo Alto, CA 94301 United States ]()

[ Register Now ](https://app.etapestry.com/cart/ElizabethFGambleGarden/cart5/category.php?ref=350.0.318401883)

Join Gamble Garden’s horticultural staff to learn everything you need to know about how to create an aesthetically pleasing and productive edible garden. In this series we will explore a wide variety of common, and not so common edible plants that are well suited to our climate. You will learn how to apply traditional design principles to your vegetable garden, as well as how to intermingle edible plants into an existing landscape. We will discuss techniques used at Gamble Garden to maintain plant and soil health year-round, including plant protection, pest management, irrigation, and general maintenance.

**Our October 18th class (Session Fall) will focus on edible plants for Fall.**

This interactive, garden-based class is suitable for adults and teens, and it is perfect for everyone from novices to advanced gardener. Suitable for adults and teens.
