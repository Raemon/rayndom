# All About Berries: Quarterly Zoom with Garden Director Corey Andrikopoulos

Date:

## Thursday, June 15, 2023 @ 4:00 pm \- 5:00 pm

Location:

[ 1431 Waverley Street   
Palo Alto, CA 94301 United States ]()

[ Register Now ](https://app.etapestry.com/cart/ElizabethFGambleGarden/default/item.php?ref=1462.0.1051679834)

Did you know that raspberries can be red, purple, gold, or black? And did you know that black raspberries are different from blackberries? Join us for a “Berry” interesting Zoom event with Gamble Garden Director Corey Andrikopoulos to learn more about the different varieties of these delicious summer fruits. Learn the difference between a raspberry and blackberry, what it means for a cane to be trailing or erect, how to select the correct variety for your garden, how cropping system differ between primocane and floricane varieties, and how to maintain your plants for years of enjoyment.
