# September Tour of Elizabeth Gamble House and Garden

Date:

## Tuesday, September 16, 2025 @ 1:30 pm \- 3:00 pm

Location:

[ 1431 Waverley Street   
Palo Alto, CA 94301 United States ]()

[ Register Now ](https://www.eventbrite.com/e/september-tour-of-elizabeth-gamble-house-and-garden-tickets-1373801038889)

Step back in time to explore the life of Elizabeth Gamble and her historic house and gardens. This 90-minute guided tour starts on the front steps of the house at 1431 Waverley Street in Palo Alto. Each month’s tour presents a unique focus.

Our September tour will put the spotlight on Gamble’s bountiful edible garden.

This tour is appropriate for adults and children age 10 and up. Tour attendees will be climbing 8 steps and walking about a half mile.
