# 2019 Wedding Spot Award Winners

_Couples from all across the country have helped us choose the best places to get married in 2019! Featuring idyllic beaches, rustic-chic barns, elegant ballrooms, historic estates, and so much more, this year’s winners will make you excited to tie the knot. With venues suited to every style and budget, you’re sure to find the perfect place to say “I do!“_

## Winners By Region

Check out our regional winners! From chic New York hotels to rural Texan estates to tropical Hawaiian beaches, our favorite venues offer a truly unrivaled setting for your special day. Planning the wedding of your dreams will be easy at any one of these spectacular venues!
