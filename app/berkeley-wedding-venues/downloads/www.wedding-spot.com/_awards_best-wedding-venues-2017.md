# 2017 Wedding Spot Award Winners

_Coast to coast, we asked our couples for their favorite picks and they responded! From rustic barns to magical mansions, private gardens and snow peaked mountains, our 2017 winners will leave you inspired and dazzled! Whether you prefer a modern event or a timeless soiree, couples beware-you’ll be sure to fall in love!_
