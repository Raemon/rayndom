#  How Wedding Spot Works 

Wedding Spot is the first online site to enable users to search, price, and compare wedding venues side-by-side. We work closely with venues and their vendors to gather options and pricing details to allow engaged couples to "build" their ideal wedding at each venue and then book an appointment if it fits their budget and preferences. Below is a step-by-step tutorial of how Wedding Spot can be helpful to you.
