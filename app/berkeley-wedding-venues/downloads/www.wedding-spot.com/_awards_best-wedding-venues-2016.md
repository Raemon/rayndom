# 2016 Wedding Spot Award Winners

_From the glowing beaches of California to the towering skyscrapers of New York City, we looked to our brides to bring you an impressive selection of the most romantic, breathtaking wedding venues in some of our most popular regions. These wedding venues are bursting with inspiration-prepare to be swept away!_
