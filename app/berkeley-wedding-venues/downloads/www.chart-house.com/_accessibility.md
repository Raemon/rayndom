# Accessibility Statement

  
This Accessibility Statement applies to: [**www.chart-house.com**](https://www.chart-house.com/)

  


![accessibility logos](https://images.getbento.com/accounts/19fcad6c1fd057f294461dacf26ebe27/media/images/AudioEye_AccessibilityStatement_Graphics_ADA-evolution.png?w=1000&fit=max&auto=compress,format&h=1000)

## We Value Digital Inclusion

In our effort to provide a fully accessible and optimized user experience for all site visitors, **Chart House** has taken careful measure to ensure an excellent user experience, regardless of the assistive technology being used to access this site or the specific abilities of those individuals seeking access to this site.

**Chart House** website is monitored and tested regularly as part of a fully managed accessibility compliance solution provided by [**AudioEye**](https://www.audioeye.com), a third-party provider of Web Accessibility testing, remediation, and monitoring services dedicated to eradicating digital access barriers ([**join the movement**](https://www.audioeye.com)). As issues of accessibility are identified, results of automated and manual testing are managed through the AudioEye’s proprietary audit ecosystem. As new solutions are discovered to improve the user experience, remediation is tracked through the AudioEye system and fixes are implemented to improve the website user experience.

## AudioEye Accessibility Certification

The [**AudioEye Trusted Certification**](https://audioeye.zendesk.com/hc/en-us/articles/360035930571-What-is-the-AudioEye-Trusted-Certification-) represents a commitment to accessibility and digital inclusion.

## Web Accessibility Guidelines

**Chart House** has leveraged the [**Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.1**](https://audioeye.zendesk.com/hc/en-us/articles/360035336012-What-is-WCAG-compliance-) as a reference to ensure the web content made available from this site is more accessible for individuals with disabilities and user friendly for everyone.

These globally recognized best practices (as recommended by the World Wide Web Consortium) consist of three levels of accessibility measurement (A, AA, and AAA). To the greatest extent feasible, **Chart House** has elected to conform to Level AA of these guidelines.

**Related Links:**

[**Web Accessibility Initiative (WAI)**](https://www.w3.org/WAI/)  
Design guidelines for electronic and information technology

## Feedback

Dedicated web accessibility experts are available to assist site visitors with any issues accessing content and the various services and functions made available across **Chart House** website. Site visitors may choose to engage with the Help Desk to report and troubleshoot web accessibility related issues.

If you encounter issues with any page on our site that presents a challenge for individuals with disabilities, please submit your **feedback**.
