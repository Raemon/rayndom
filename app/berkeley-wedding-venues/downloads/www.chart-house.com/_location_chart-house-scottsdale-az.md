## Seafood Dining in the Desert

Seated on the former 4,200-acre McCormick Ranch, Chart House brings some of the best seafood in Arizona, as well as incredible steaks and prime rib, to the heart of the Sonoran desert. In one of the West's most Western towns, Chart House seafood restaurant has made a name for itself with impeccable service, creative dishes and a happy hour that's to die for. Chart House was even voted one of the Top 10 Seafood Restaurants in the Scottsdale and Phoenix areas.

As one of the best restaurants in Scottsdale with a view, bask in the sights of Camelback Mountain and the gorgeous Camelback Lake, all while enjoying some of our legendary menu items, including Wicked Tuna, Mac Nut Mahi, Alaskan King Crab, Prime Rib and our original Hot Chocolate Lava Cake. Sit back and partake in our happy hour specials. Just minutes from Downtown Scottsdale and its legendary nightlife, arts and activities, Chart House is the perfect dining decision.
