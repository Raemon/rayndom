Chart House was voted **Best Local Fine Dining**   
in the Daytona Beach Visitors' Choice Awards.

With the nickname “The world's most famous beach,” Daytona Beach certainly deserves world-famous dining. Located at Marina Point, Chart House is one of the most celebrated seafood restaurants in Daytona Beach. We've also got the area's greatest happy hour and the best Prime Rib Daytona has tasted. Featuring unique architecture, Chart House offers a number of floor-to-ceiling windows, perfect for observing passing boats and yachts on the scenic Halifax River. From our dazzling dining room, catch a view of dolphins and even manatees as they play in the water.

Just as famous as the waterfront views, Chart House's menu is what keeps Daytona Beach diners coming back for more. A beloved vacation spot, Chart House serves a number of seafood specialties that offer creative twists on old favorites, as well as seasonal preparations, featuring local ingredients. Enjoy delicious entrees, such as Mac Nut Mahi, Snapper Hemingway, or dive into our wonderful original Hot Chocolate Lava Cake.
