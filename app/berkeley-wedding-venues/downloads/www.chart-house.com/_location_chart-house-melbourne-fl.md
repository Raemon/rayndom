## Melbourne - Melbourne's Best Waterfront Seafood

You can't find a better seafood restaurant in the Cocoa area than Chart House Melbourne. Situated on the stunning banks of the Indian River Lagoon, Chart House delivers seafood with style, delighting guests with one-of-a-kind cuisine, attentive service and a touch of class. Famous for its Early Bird and Happy Hour specials, Chart House is conveniently located near some of Melbourne's hottest nightlife spots along New Haven Avenue, and only minutes away from Melbourne Beach, one of Florida's best beaches.

Whether you're in the mood for succulent seafood dishes like Mac Nut Mahi, Savory Grouper or juicy beef dishes such as our slow-roasted Prime Rib, Chart House knows just how to satisfy. Enjoy cocktails while the sun goes down over Melbourne Harbor or simply try one of our famous desserts. Visit Chart House for a taste of truly great steak and seafood Melbourne style.
