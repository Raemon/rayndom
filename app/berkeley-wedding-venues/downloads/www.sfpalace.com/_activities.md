## Shops at the Palace

### Flatiron Wine and Spirits

With a focus on small producers, families and artisans that reflect the finest winemaking terroirs and traditions, Flatiron Wine & Spirits offers an exceptional selection of wines and spirits from the world’s greatest regions and producers.

### Ghirardelli® Ice Cream and Chocolate Shop

The perfect destination to experience decadent chocolate & World Famous Hot Fudge Sundaes topped with freshly homemade hot fudge. While at Ghirardelli, you can also indulge in unforgettable fountain treats including shakes, floats, malts and other delectable creations.

### Moleskine Store

The first West Coast location of the iconic Italian notebook. Moleskine features planners, diaries, sketchbooks, albums, bags, reading glasses, pens, pencils, cases for digital devices, booklights and reading stands.

### The Rafael’s | For the Fine Art of Fashion

Known as “a gallery of wearable art,” this high-end women’s fashion store features unique collections from 50 artists from 7 countries around the world. Clothing, jewelry, purses and other accessories can be found at this one-of-a-kind local store.

### See

Voted “Best Eyewear” over 80 times in readers’ polls in major publications from coast to coast, SEE’s eclectic prescription and non-prescription eyeglasses and sunglasses are handmade in limited quantities for maximum exclusivity. SEE’s original collection starts at $229 including single vision plastic or polycarbonate lenses.
