## The Pied Piper Mural

Commissioned in the aftermath of The Great 1906 Earthquake, this Maxfield Parrish masterpiece has been hanging in its namesake bar since the Palace Hotel reopened in 1909. As an icon of San Francisco’s cultural heritage, bar patrons have the opportunity to experience The Pied Piper in its intended setting, a rare treat for art lovers and epicureans alike.
