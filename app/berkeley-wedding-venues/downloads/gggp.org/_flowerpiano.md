**I‚Äôm a San Francisco resident, do I need to reserve a ticket in advance? Will it get me in faster? How do I prove I live in San Francisco? Is there a dedicated line for residents?**

  * Reserving a ticket in advance is helpful so we know you are coming, we can send event specific information to you via email, and it will make your check in process slightly quicker, but advanced resident tickets are not required.

  * There is not a dedicated line for U.S. Military/Veterans. Want expedited entry? [Become a member today!](https://www.gggp.org/membership) or buy tickets for the [Flower Piano Lounge](https://gggp.my.salesforce-sites.com/ticket/#/events/a0SWP000002JXAP2A4)
