## Lounge FAQs

### Where is the Lounge?

The Lounge is in the Celebration Garden at San Francisco Botanical Garden.

Enter via the Member & Lounge entrance at the Main Gate (1199 9th Avenue) for expedited entry, then follow the Lounge signs to the nearby Celebration Garden.

### When is the Lounge open?

The Lounge is open:

Friday, September 12, 4–7:30pm

Saturday, September 13 – Sunday, September 14, 12–7:30pm

Friday, September 19, 4–7:30pm

Saturday, September 20 – Sunday, September 21, 12–7:30pm

Last call at 7pm.

### How do I get tickets?

We highly recommend purchasing your tickets in advance online.

[Purchase a single day Lounge Pass or a Lounge Weekend All Access Pass here.](https://gggp.my.salesforce-sites.com/ticket/#/events/a0SWP000002JXAP2A4)

### Are kids allowed?

Yes! The Lounge is a family-friendly space and is open to visitors of all ages.

Tickets are required for children ages 5 and up. [Purchase Lounge Passes here.](https://gggp.my.salesforce-sites.com/ticket/#/events/a0SWP000002JXAP2A4)

### Do Lounge passes include General Admission to Flower Piano?

Yes!

### Do Members get a discount?

Yes, Members enjoy 20% off of Lounge tickets. [Become a Member today!](https://gggp.org/membership/)

The discount will automatically be applied when you sign in to your Member Portal.

### What kind of seating is available?

There will be a combination of chairs, picnic tables, and comfortable lawn seating available. You may bring a blanket or your own camping chair to enjoy the lawn, as provided chairs are first come, first serve.

No outside food or beverages are permitted.

### Can I bring in food and beverages?

No outside food or beverages are permitted.

### Can I transfer or refund my ticket?

Ticketed events are not transferable or refundable. SF Botanical Garden is open rain or shine and does not issue refunds due to weather.
