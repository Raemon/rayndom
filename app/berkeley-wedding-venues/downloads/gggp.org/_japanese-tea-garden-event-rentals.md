# Venue: Japanese Tea Garden

Experience the beauty of our newest offering, the Japanese Tea Garden, for your next event. Perfect for intimate after-hours ceremonies or standing cocktail parties, the beautifully renovated Pagoda Plaza can accommodate up to 50 people.

![](https://gggp.org/wp-content/uploads/2025/05/holt_901_0673-scaled.jpg) **Watch**

×

## Event Rentals

Celebrate your special day in one of San Francisco’s most enchanting hidden gems—the Japanese Tea Garden. The Japanese Tea Garden’s newly renovated Pagoda Plaza provides a unique and stunning backdrop for intimate after-hours ceremonies and standing cocktail receptions, surrounded by vibrant seasonal blooms and traditional Japanese architecture. Whether you’re exchanging vows beneath the towering pagoda or sharing a toast beneath the twilight sky, your celebration will be nothing short of magical.

**Vendors**  
We work with a pre-approved list of outside caterers, lighting, and other event specialists.

**Availability**  
After-hours only from April through the 1st Saturday of November, beginning after we close to the public.

**Pricing**  
For a quote, please complete [the event inquiry form](https://sfbg.tripleseat.com/party_request/43459%22%3EEvent). To confirm your reservation, a site visit is recommended, and a signed rental contract, and a 50% deposit is required.

[Event Inquiry Form](https://sfbg.tripleseat.com/party_request/43459)

![](https://gggp.org/wp-content/uploads/2025/05/resized-holt_901_0581-scaled.jpg)

#### 

The Japanese Tea Garden’s newly renovated Pagoda Plaza provides a unique and stunning backdrop for intimate after-hours ceremonies and standing cocktail receptions. Capacity is up to 50 people in the Pagoda Plaza for a seated ceremony and/or a standing reception. 

## Event Rental Options

### 1 Hour Ceremony 

**During daylight hours**.

Up to 50 people in the Pagoda Plaza. Includes chairs and setup, and time for photos. No food or beverage is permitted. Available April-September from 6:30pm-7:30pm. 

### 2 Hour Ceremony and/or Standing Cocktail Reception

**During daylight hours.**

Up to 50 people in the Pagoda Plaza for a seated ceremony and/or a standing reception. Includes up to 50 chairs and a limited number of tables. Available May-August from 6:30-8:30pm

### 3 Hour Ceremony and/or Standing Cocktail Reception

Up to 50 people in the Pagoda Plaza for a seated ceremony and/or a standing reception. Includes up to 50 chairs, a limited number of tables, and lighting. Full access to the Garden is not included after sunset. Available May through the 1st Saturday of November from 6:30pm to 9:30pm

## Japanese Tea Garden FAQ

### Will my event location be private/closed to the public?

The Japanese Tea Garden will be closed to the public during private event rentals.

### Do you have WiFi?

There is no dedicated WiFi within the Garden. Depending on your phone carrier, cell service is accessible in most of the Garden.

### Do you have parking?

Limited street parking is available on Martin Luther King Jr. Drive and surrounding streets. The Music Concourse Parking Garage is within walking distance of the Garden. For the hours and for additional information [goldengateparking.com](http://goldengateparking.com/)

### Are you LGBTQIA+ friendly?

Yes!

### Is smoking allowed?

No. Smoking is not allowed at any of the Gardens.
