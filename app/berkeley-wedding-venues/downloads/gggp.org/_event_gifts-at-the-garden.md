# Gifts at the Garden

December 14, 2024 \- December 15, 2024 Free

[RSVP](https://gggp.my.salesforce-sites.com/ticket/#/events/a0S6Q00000UEX2FUAX)

  * This event has passed.



Gifts at the Garden is a free-to-attend curated market of sustainable, local, and fair-trade gifts alongside exclusive Gardens of Golden Gate Park merchandise, books, and art. The market will host free activities and a donation-based wrapping station. Proceeds benefit the Gardens of Golden Gate Park. Garden members can shop an hour early. Beat the crowds and shop the best selection, [become a member today](https://www.sfbg.org/mem-fp)!

Plus, reserve a spot in our annual [Wreath Making Workshop](https://gggp.org/event/botanical-wreath-making/) on Saturday morning, 10am-12pm, and continue your festive day in the gift market!

**Date:**  
Saturday, December 14 – Sunday, December 15

**Times:**  
Saturday Members Morning: 10-11am  
Saturday General Public Hours: 11am-5pm  
Sunday General Public Hours: 10am-4pm

**Location:**  
County Fair Building next to San Francisco Botanical Garden

**Featured Programs:******

> [**San Francisco Bay Area Moms**](https://sanfranciscomoms.com/) – Kick off the holiday season with a visit to Mrs. Claus’ Village presented by Stanford Medicine Children’s Health @ Gifts at the Garden! For all ages, join Mrs. Claus and partake in winter celebrations across the world, with festive arts & crafts, storytime, LEGO play, goodies, photo opportunities, and so much more. 
> 
> _A Special Mrs. Clause Experience takes place from 10am-12pm on both days:_[Purchase your tickets](https://ticketstripe.com/events/7439106242668909) to guarantee entry before all the spots fill up! The exclusive experience per family includes: Meet & Greet Session with Mrs. Claus, Free Mini Polaroid with Frame, DIY Photo Frame, Winterlicious Treats, Holly Jolly Plushie Pal from the North Pole, Make-Your-Own Winter Characters, and More. Additionally, your ticket enables a new mom in need to receive a festive care pack!
> 
> **The Helen Crocker Russell Library of Horticulture** (Saturday Only) – Visit our very own library team as they pop-up amongst the market with discount books!
> 
> **Gardens Photo Station –** Take some festive holiday photos at our free Gardens of Golden Gate Park photo station! Holiday items included for pictures.
> 
> **[Free Family Fun Zone](https://gggp.org/learn/family-community/bean-sprouts-family-days/) – **Join our Garden team with free to make holiday crafts, featuring greens from our very own Gardens, hands-on games, and more!  
> 
> 
> [**The Mexican Museum**](https://www.mexicanmuseum.org/education) (Sunday Only) – Visit our partners from The Mexican Museum in our Family Fun Zone!
> 
> [**BIBLIOBICICLETA**](https://www.instagram.com/bibliobicicleta/) (Sunday Only) – Independent bike library giving away free books until supplies run out!
