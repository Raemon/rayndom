# Our Partners

![](https://gggp.org/wp-content/uploads/2025/04/our-partners.png)

### We would like to sincerely thank our sponsors and donors. Without their financial support many of the wonderful activities and collections at the Gardens would not be possible. We’d also like to thank our community partners who we collaborate with to bring the Gardens alive with special programming.

  
Recognized as one of the Top Ten Gardens in the United States, the Gardens bring joy, peace, awe, wonder, and inspiration to more than 1.2 million people each year. We are a major highlight of trips to San Francisco and a source of civic pride and connection for the entire Bay Area.

All donations are tax-deductible to the fullest extent allowed by law.

_Because of our public/private partnership agreement, we must post the names of donors who give money, goods or services worth more than $100 in aggregate and disclose any financial interest that the donor has involving San Francisco City government._
