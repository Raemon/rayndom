## Digital Membership

Access your membership card on your smartphone.

#### **Renewing members**

Your digital membership on the app will automatically update after renewing. Please note the digital membership card that was added to your e-wallet doesn’t automatically update and will show up as expired pass. 

To update the digital membership card to your e-wallet: 

  1. Delete the old digital membership card currently in your wallet 


  2. Open the “eMembership Card” app from Membership Anywhere on your mobile phone. 
     * If you need to re-install the app, please follow the steps listed below for **New Members** and “Step #1” to download the app.
  3. Your Gardens membership should appear under “My Memberships”. 
     * If you don’t see your Gardens membership, please follow the Step #2 for **New Members** to activate your digital membership.


  3. 

  4. Select “Show My Membership Cards”.
  5. Select “Add to Wallet” to save the renewed card.



#### **New Members**

How to Access Your Gardens of Golden Gate Park Digital Membership Card

  1. **Download App**


  * Visit the [Google Play Store](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.inficare.membershipdemo) or the [Apple App Store](https://apps.apple.com/us/app/emembership-card/id1161384548) and download the free “eMembership Card” app from Membership Anywhere.



2\. **Activate Membership Card**

  * Open the app, search ‘Gardens of Golden Gate Park’, and select the tile from the list of institutions. 
  * Select “Find My Membership Cards”. 
  * Enter your Membership ID# from the confirmation email and the last name of the primary member. 
  * Select “Download my card” 



3\. **View Membership Benefits**

  * Select “Membership Benefits” to see all the benefits of your membership, including the list of reciprocal admission at more than 360 gardens nationwide through American Horticultural Society (AHS) Reciprocal Garden Network. 
  * You also have the option to push your membership cards to your smartphone wallet. Select the “Add to wallet” at the top of your card. 



#### **Digital Guest Pass**

Members at the Garden Lover and above can access their digital guest passes that they can share with their family and friends. The guest passes are for one-time use, and guests may come on their own. 

  1. Open the app and select “Show My Membership Cards” to make sure your membership is added to the app 


  2. Go to the “Membership Benefits” section. 


  3. Click on the “Guest Passes” to view your digital guest pass 


  4. Prefer a paper guest pass? Email [membership@gggp.org](mailto:membership@gggp.org)



#### **FAQ**

**Can’t find your digital membership on the app, or have trouble downloading the app?**

  * Email [membership@gggp.org](mailto:membership@gggp.org?subject=Digital%20Membership%20Issue). We’re here to help. 



**My name is spelt incorrectly or my second member’s name was not listed on the membership.**

  * Email [membership@gggp.org](mailto:membership@gggp.org?subject=Digital%20Membership%20Issue) to have your information updated in the membership database. 



**I’d prefer not to use a digital membership card, or I do not have a mobile phone.**

  * Email [membership@gggp.org](mailto:membership@gggp.org?subject=Digital%20Membership%20Issue) or call 415-661-1316 x302 to request your paper membership card(s) to be sent by mail. 



**How will my reciprocal benefits work when visiting other reciprocal sites?**

  * Simply show your membership card at the participating gardens to receive the reciprocal benefits. Please note that reciprocal benefits at each garden may vary and some restrictions apply. Contact the garden you plan to visit for more information. 



**How can I update my contact information or check my membership status?**

  * The easiest way to check your membership status or update your contact information is to email [membership@gggp.org](mailto:membership@gggp.org), call 415-661-1316 x302. 



**Questions?**

  * Email [membership@gggp.org](mailto:membership@gggp.org), call 415-661-1316 x302, or stop by one of the Gardens’ admission kiosks during your next visit to learn more about membership.
