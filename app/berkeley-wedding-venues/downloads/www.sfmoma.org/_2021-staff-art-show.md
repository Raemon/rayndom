# Created by Colleagues: Works from the 2021 Staff Art Show

From the galleries to the administrative floors, art is everywhere at SFMOMA. Take a virtual tour of our 2021–2022 Staff Art Show, which celebrates the ingenuity and talent of our many artist-colleagues. Click each tile to learn more about the work and the staff member who created it.  
  
_Organized by Sienna Freeman, Tanya Gayer, and Christo Oropeza._
