#  Unfnshed 

### Related Exhibition [Get in the Game: Sports, Art, Culture](https://www.sfmoma.org/exhibition/get-in-the-game/)

#### Friday, Feb 14–Sunday, Feb 16, 2025  
10 a.m.–5 p.m.

####  Floor 4, Gina and Stuart Peterson White Box 

Free with museum admission

  * [](https://www.facebook.com/sharer/sharer.php?u=https%3A%2F%2Fwww.sfmoma.org%2Fevent%2Funfnshed%2F&quote=Unfnshed)
  * [](https://twitter.com/intent/tweet?text=Unfnshed+https%3A%2F%2Fwww.sfmoma.org%2Fevent%2Funfnshed%2F)
  * ##### share




Experience the intersection of identity, creativity, storytelling, and wellness through the eyes of NBA stars. Step into **Unfnshed** , an installation hosted by **NBA Player Development** , an organization that partners with players to amplify their voices. 

Open for one weekend only, this presentation invites you to explore the shared human journey of self-discovery and inspiration through the stories of NBA players. **Unfnshed** provides a platform for players to share their journeys and invites visitors to reflect on their own. While each of our paths is unique, we are all united by the need to explore and nurture our own identities.
