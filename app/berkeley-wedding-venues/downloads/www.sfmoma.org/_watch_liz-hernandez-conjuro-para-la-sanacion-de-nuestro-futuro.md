# Making a Mural with Liz Hernández

  * [](https://www.facebook.com/sharer/sharer.php?u=https%3A%2F%2Fwww.sfmoma.org%2Fwatch%2Fliz-hernandez-conjuro-para-la-sanacion-de-nuestro-futuro%2F&quote=Making+a+Mural+with+Liz+Hern%C3%A1ndez)
  * [](https://twitter.com/intent/tweet?text=Making+a+Mural+with+Liz+Hern%C3%A1ndez+https%3A%2F%2Fwww.sfmoma.org%2Fwatch%2Fliz-hernandez-conjuro-para-la-sanacion-de-nuestro-futuro%2F)
  * ##### share




Mexico City-born artist Liz Hernández reveals the symbolism and intention behind her mural _Conjuro para la sanación de nuestro futuro (A spell for the healing of our future)_ , commissioned by SFMOMA. The Oakland-based artist draws inspiration from her upbringing—her grandmother’s supernaturalism, the vibrant hand-painted signs of Mexico City, and the rituals of the Catholic Church—to make an offering to a higher power for our collective healing.
