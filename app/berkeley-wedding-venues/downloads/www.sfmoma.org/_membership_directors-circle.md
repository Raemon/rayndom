# Director’s Circle

  
  
Engage with SFMOMA at the highest level and empower the museum’s boldest visions. You’ll enjoy invitations to join SFMOMA’s director and head curators on international art trips, private exhibition tours, visits to local private collections, and opportunities to hear from artists and luminaries from the art world and beyond.

* * *

### Become a Director-Level Member: $20,000–$49,999

**Access and Engagement**

  * Curator-led exhibition tours with fellow Director’s Circle donors
  * Bay Area private collection visits
  * Annual invitation to join SFMOMA’s Director on an international trip
  * Opportunities to hear from art world luminaries through dialogue series
  * Invitation to our annual Director’s Dinner
  * Private exhibition tour with an SFMOMA curator (upon request)
  * A dedicated staff liaison to customize your SFMOMA engagement 



**Learning and Enrichment**

  * Invitation to join Collectors’ Forum and Contemporaries to enhance your understanding and enjoyment of collecting modern and contemporary art



**More to Enjoy**  
All the benefits of [Curators’ Circle](/get-involved/membership/curators-circle) membership.

[Join Director’s Circle](/accounts/membership/join/D1)

* * *

### Become a President-Level Member: $50,000–$99,999

**Everything above, plus:**

  * Opportunity to arrange a custom tour and lunch with the SFMOMA Director



[Join Director’s Circle](https://tickets.sfmoma.org/accounts/membership/join/D2)

* * *

### Become a Chairman-Level Member: $100,000+

**Everything above, plus:**

  * Complimentary mailed copies of SFMOMA publications



[Join Director’s Circle](https://tickets.sfmoma.org/accounts/membership/join/D3)

* * *

### Request Information

To request information about Director’s Circle at SFMOMA, email [directorscircle@sfmoma.org](mailto:directorscircle@sfmoma.org).

Memberships are nontransferable. All prices and benefits are subject to change.
