How do contemporary artists use sports to interrogate power and performance? This webinar will explore the ways in which artists play with and subvert power dynamics in athletic performance to examine the role of gender and race in sports. During this workshop, we will consider how sports influence contemporary artists like Paul Pfeiffer, Collier Schorr, and Shaun Leonardo.

Co-presented by SFMOMA and Art21, this workshop is part of a series exploring two different aspects of the question, “What is contemporary art?” Each workshop encourages educators to think and work like artists, using thematic, inquiry-driven processes and strategies.
