The [UC Botanical Garden at Berkeley](https://botanicalgarden.berkeley.edu/) is a 34-acre oasis featuring one of the world’s most diverse plant collections. Rain or shine, you’ll love exploring this living museum filled with more than 10,000 kinds of plants, including many rare and endangered species. There is something new to see with every season. Don’t miss the three indoor glass houses including the Tropical House and the Orchid, Fern, and Carnivorous Plant House, featuring fascinating carnivores and flamboyant flowers in bloom!

You’ll receive free admission for up to two adults and four children (18 and younger) upon presentation of your SFMOMA membership card or your membership confirmation email and an ID. Offer reserved for SFMOMA members on Saturday, February 14, and Sunday, February 15. [Plan your visit](https://nam12.safelinks.protection.outlook.com/?url=https%3A%2F%2Fbotanicalgarden.berkeley.edu%2Fvisit%2Fplan-your-visit%2F&data=05%7C02%7Cjbravard%40sfmoma.org%7C38f4fd3c5d034746e90908de3f3288ff%7C038d95c6264f4455a11bb276013a6058%7C0%7C0%7C639017683528005310%7CUnknown%7CTWFpbGZsb3d8eyJFbXB0eU1hcGkiOnRydWUsIlYiOiIwLjAuMDAwMCIsIlAiOiJXaW4zMiIsIkFOIjoiTWFpbCIsIldUIjoyfQ%3D%3D%7C0%7C%7C%7C&sdata=WmuAEDBr1zf%2FzkbhTRIvXn9e%2BhDsEOHzQwmnKm9dVlY%3D&reserved=0).

[Plan your visit](https://botanicalgarden.berkeley.edu/visit/plan-your-visit/) or prebook tickets (not required) through the following options: [Saturday](https://41780.blackbaudhosting.com/41780/page.aspx?pid=196&tab=2&txobjid=2f40ace7-6be3-4ade-b1d5-7d97292b3381) visits; [Sunday](https://41780.blackbaudhosting.com/41780/page.aspx?pid=196&tab=2&txobjid=222a7b1b-e942-4fe1-859e-3b3169553686) visits.
