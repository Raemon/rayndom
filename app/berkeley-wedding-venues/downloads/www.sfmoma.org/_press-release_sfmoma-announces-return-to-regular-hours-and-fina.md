# SFMOMA Announces Return To Regular Hours And Final Chance To See _magritte_ On Free Tuesday

As part of the Free Tuesday program, Tuesday, September 5, 2000, the first Tuesday of the month, is a free day at the San Francisco Museum of Modern Art (SFMOMA). September 5 is also the final day to view the popular _Magritte_ exhibition. The Museum will extend its summer hours (10 a.m.–6 p.m.) for one day to accommodate visitors who wish to see this exhibition one last time.

On Thursday, September 7, 2000 SFMOMA will return to regular operating hours, opening to the public at 11 a.m. daily (except Wednesdays when the Museum is closed).  
  
**Free Tuesday and final day of _Magritte_** : 

> Tuesday, September 5, 2000  
>  Galleries open 10 a.m.–6 p.m.  
>  Free admission
