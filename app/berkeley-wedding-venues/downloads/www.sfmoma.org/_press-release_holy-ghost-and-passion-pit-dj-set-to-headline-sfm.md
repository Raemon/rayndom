# Holy Ghost! and Passion Pit (DJ Set) to Headline SFMOMA's Modern Ball

###

SFMOMA is pleased to announce Holy Ghost! and Passion Pit (DJ Set) as the co-headliners for [The Modern Ball](/exhib_events/modern_ball/post_modern_party), taking place on Wednesday, April 30. The museum’s biennial fund-raising gala will be held outside SFMOMA’s building for the first time this year, transforming Yerba Buena Gardens into a spectacular party space with surprises around every corner. The evening brings together three sensational experiences, all designed by Stanlee Gatti: the Gala Dinner, Supper Club, and Post-Modern Party. The Modern Ball supports SFMOMA’s California-wide exhibitions and innovative education programs serving more than 60,000 students, teachers, and families each year.

A live performance by [Holy Ghost!](https://holyghostnyc.net/), a synthpop duo from Brooklyn, and a DJ set by [Passion Pit](https://passionpitmusic.com/), an indietronica festival favorite, will light up City View at Metreon as the late-night Post-Modern Party gets under way. The party will continue late into the night with entertainment throughout the gardens, including a silent disco tent.

Read more about [The Modern Ball](/about/press/press_events/releases/975).

[Purchase tickets on Eventbrite.](https://www.eventbrite.com/e/the-post-modern-party-at-the-modern-ball-2014-tickets-7563178671)
