# Ways to Give

Your donation makes thousands of art interactions possible.

### Support the museum

Help SFMOMA create art experiences that foster inspiration, enjoyment, and discovery. Your tax-deductible gift provides general operating support for exhibitions, events, and education programs. Whether it’s to honor or memorialize a family member or friend, or to celebrate a special occasion, this donation has a direct impact on our goal of sharing art with the widest possible audience.

Log in or create an online account to complete your donation.

SFMOMA’s EIN/federal tax ID number is 94-1156300.

[Donate Now](https://tickets.sfmoma.org/accounts/donate)
