#  Free Family Day: People Make This Place 

### Related Exhibition [People Make This Place: SFAI Stories](https://www.sfmoma.org/exhibition/people-make-this-place-sfai-stories/)

#### Sunday, Sep 14, 2025

####  10 a.m.–4 p.m. 

####  Museumwide 

Free with RSVP.

  * [](https://www.facebook.com/sharer/sharer.php?u=https%3A%2F%2Fwww.sfmoma.org%2Fevent%2Ffree-family-day-people-make-this-place%2F&quote=Free+Family+Day%3A+People+Make+This+Place)

We are kicking off our fall programming with a Free Family Day all about the exhibition [_People Make This Place: SFAI Stories,_](https://www.sfmoma.org/exhibition/people-make-this-place-sfai-stories/) celebrating the West Coast’s oldest fine art school, which closed in 2022. We will have art making and activities inspired by this unique, creative place.

### Event Schedule

All activities **10 a.m.–4 p.m.** unless otherwise stated.

  * _Floor 1, Evelyn and Walter Haas, Jr. Atrium_
    * Welcome table with giveaway kits (while supplies last!)
    * Stroller parking
  * __Floor 2, Koret Education Center__
    * Grab It game
    * Book raffle
    * Zine-making activity with SCRAP
    * Scavenger hunt prize pickup
  * __Floor 2, People Make This Place: SFAI Stories exhibition__
    * Scavenger hunt
  * __Floor 4, Gina and Stuart Peterson White Box__
    * “What is art?” collaborative floor-drawing activity
  * _Floor 1, Phyllis Wattis Theater_
    * Storytime with the San Francisco Public Library 
      * **11–11:30 a.m.**
      * **1–1:30 p.m.**
