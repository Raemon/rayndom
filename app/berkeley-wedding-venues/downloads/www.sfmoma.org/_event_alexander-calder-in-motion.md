#  Alexander Calder in Motion 

### Related Exhibition [Alexander Calder: Dissonant Harmony](https://www.sfmoma.org/exhibition/alexander-calder-dissonant-harmony/)

#### Ongoing – See schedule below for details

####  Floor 3, Alexander Calder: Dissonant Harmony 

Free with museum admission.

  * [](https://www.facebook.com/sharer/sharer.php?u=https%3A%2F%2Fwww.sfmoma.org%2Fevent%2Falexander-calder-in-motion%2F&quote=Alexander+Calder+in+Motion)
  * [](https://twitter.com/intent/tweet?text=Alexander+Calder+in+Motion+https%3A%2F%2Fwww.sfmoma.org%2Fevent%2Falexander-calder-in-motion%2F)
  * ##### share




Come see how Alexander Calder’s revolutionary sculptures interact with their surroundings and incorporate different kinds of movement and sounds. Specially trained museum staff will set a selection of exhibition works in motion as part of an in-gallery presentation. This program will take place every third Friday of every other month; see schedule below for dates.

#### Schedule:

October 21, 2022, 1:15 p.m.  
December 16, 2022, 1:15 p.m.  
February 17, 2023, 1:15 p.m.  
April 21, 2023, 1:15 p.m.
