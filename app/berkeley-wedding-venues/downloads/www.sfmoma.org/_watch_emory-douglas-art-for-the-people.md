# Emory Douglas: Art for the People

  * [](https://www.facebook.com/sharer/sharer.php?u=https%3A%2F%2Fwww.sfmoma.org%2Fwatch%2Femory-douglas-art-for-the-people%2F&quote=Emory+Douglas%3A+Art+for+the+People)
  * [](https://twitter.com/intent/tweet?text=Emory+Douglas%3A+Art+for+the+People+https%3A%2F%2Fwww.sfmoma.org%2Fwatch%2Femory-douglas-art-for-the-people%2F)
  * ##### share




Artist Emory Douglas recalls his early years in the Bay Area, leading us on the journey that resulted in his appointment as Minister of Culture and Revolutionary Artist of the Black Panther Party. Footage and photos from the rich archives of the [African American Museum and Library at Oakland](https://oaklandlibrary.org/aamlo/) and the [San Francisco Public Library](https://sfpl.org/locations/main-library/sf-history-center/digital-collections) further illustrate the local history shared by Douglas in this video.
