SFMOMA’s annual report, _On the Go: A Look at SFMOMA, July 1, 2012–June 30, 2014_ , is now available [here](https://www.sfmoma.org/about/annualreports). Covering the 2013 and 2014 fiscal years, this report features a photo retrospective of key exhibitions and events prior to and after the museum’s close in 2013 for expansion. Significant highlights include the Countdown Celebration, construction milestones and a diverse range of On the Go exhibitions. Acquisitions, publications, supporters, public programs and financial data are also shared in this comprehensive overview of SFMOMA’s previous two years.
