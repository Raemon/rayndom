# Suzanne Jackson: What is Love

Join Suzanne Jackson as she takes us on a journey through her lifelong career as a multidisciplinary artist. Through painting, dance, sculpture, and installation, Jackson has expressed herself and reflected her natural surroundings across the US, including periods spent in San Francisco; Fairbanks, Alaska; Los Angeles; and now Savannah, Georgia. From her home studio, Suzanne displays her ever-growing body of work, culminating in her upcoming SFMOMA retrospective, _What Is Love_.
