Members get special access to _Amy Sherald: American Sublime_ before it closes! Don’t miss a final chance to see this captivating mid-career survey of one of the most prominent active artists in the United States, featuring iconic portraits of Michelle Obama and Breonna Taylor, incisive and moving figure paintings, and entirely new works.

This exhibition requires timed tickets (free for members). We recommend reserving tickets in advance by visiting [tickets.sfmoma.org](https://tickets.sfmoma.org/tickets?month=2025-03-01&type=ga) or by contacting our Member Services team at 415.357.4135.

Not a member? Visit [sfmoma.org/membership](https://www.sfmoma.org/membership/) to join today.
