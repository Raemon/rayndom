Edited by Linde B. Lehtinen and Nancy Lim

Foreword by Neal Benezra; introduction and essays by Linde B. Lehtinen and Nancy Lim; interviews by Alison Saar, Ruby Neri, Alicia McCarthy, Lawrence Rinder, and Andrés Cisneros-Galindo

32 pages, 7 ½ x 10 inches, softcover

Published in 2019

Kenyatta A.C. Hinkle, Sahar Khoury, and Marlon Mullen are the 2019 recipients of the SECA (Society for the Encouragement of Contemporary Art) Art Award that has served as a unique platform for emerging or under-recognized Bay Area artists since its inception in 1967. This catalogue, published in conjunction with the SFMOMA award exhibition, offers distinctive creative visions that reflect the diverse character of the local art landscape. Featuring compelling essays, conversations with the awardees’ close friends and mentors, and full-color reproductions, this publication provides an illuminating glimpse into the originality, rigor, and ingenuity shaping the future of art in the Bay Area. 

Published on the occasion of the exhibition _2019 SECA Art Award: Kenyatta A.C. Hinkle, Sahar Khoury, Marlon Mullen,_ held at the San Francisco Museum of Modern Art (November 16, 2019–September 2020)
