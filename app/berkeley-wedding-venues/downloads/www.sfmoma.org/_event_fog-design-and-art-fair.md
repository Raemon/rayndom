#  FOG Design+Art Fair 

#### Thursday, January 22–Sunday, January 25, 2026

####  Fort Mason Center for Arts & Culture 

[Visit the FOG Fair Website](https://www.fogfair.com/tickets/)

  * [](https://www.facebook.com/sharer/sharer.php?u=https%3A%2F%2Fwww.sfmoma.org%2Fevent%2Ffog-design-and-art-fair%2F&quote=FOG+Design%2BArt+Fair)
  * [](https://twitter.com/intent/tweet?text=FOG+Design%2BArt+Fair+https%3A%2F%2Fwww.sfmoma.org%2Fevent%2Ffog-design-and-art-fair%2F)
  * ##### share




Celebrating today’s leading contributors to the worlds of design and visual arts, FOG Design+Art brings together international art galleries, prominent 20th-century and contemporary design dealers, and a weekend of exciting programs.

**Hours:**  
**Thursday, January 22–Saturday, January 24, 2026: 11 a.m.–7 p.m.**  
**Sunday, January 25, 2026: 11 a.m.–5 p.m.**

FOG Design+Art offers two general-admission ticket options: a single-day ticket or a four-day fair pass. Tickets to the fair include entry to both FOG Design+Art and FOG FOCUS, as well as access to FOG Talks, which feature dynamic conversations with leaders across the fields of art, design, and technology.

**Single-day tickets are $35 in advance and $40 after January 21, 2026 (plus fees).  
Four-day tickets are $95 in advance and $100 after January 21, 2026 (plus fees).**

Tickets will be available for purchase online and at the door. Children 12 and under are free.

**To purchase FOG Fair tickets, visit:** [fogfair.com/tickets](https://www.fogfair.com/tickets/).  
  
For a full list of [Exhibitors](https://www.fogfair.com/exhibitors/) and [FOG Talks schedule](https://www.fogfair.com/programming/), please visit [fogfair.com](http://fogfair.com/). For more information, contact [fog@fogfair.com](mailto:fog@fogfair.com).  
  
**_Want early access to the fair? Join us for the[FOG Design+Art Preview Gala](https://www.sfmoma.org/?p=793471&post_type=event&preview=1&_ppp=1b1646da7f) on Wednesday, January 21._**  
As a benefit of a gala ticket purchase, you receive return access to the fair. Bronze level and above receive multiday access to the fair. Supporter level receives single-day access to the fair.
