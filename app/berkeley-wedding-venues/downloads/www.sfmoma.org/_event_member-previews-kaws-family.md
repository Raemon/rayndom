Discover the playful and poignant artistic universe created by KAWS. Marking his first major museum exhibition on the West Coast, _KAWS: FAMILY_ traces the artist’s output over the past three decades through its keen ability to connect to shared emotions and culture. 

**Members see it first and can bring a guest for free!** This exhibition requires timed tickets; we recommend reserving in advance to secure your spot. Reserve your free tickets by following the ticket link above and logging into your member account.

Not a member? Visit [sfmoma.org/membership](https://www.sfmoma.org/membership/) to join today.
