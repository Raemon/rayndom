Welcome to SFMOMA’s virtual Second Sundays, a monthly series that includes a reading with a librarian from the San Francisco Public Library, followed by a guided art activity you can do at home. In this installment, we’ll be focusing on pioneering pop artist **Andy Warhol!**

### Art Project: Print and Repeat Print

Now that you know a little bit about Andy Warhol, it’s time to make a project inspired by him! We’re taking a cue from his “National Velvet” (1963) and adding a few more pops of color, just for fun! Watch the video to see the project come to life, and browse the written instructions and materials list below.
