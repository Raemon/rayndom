# Corporate Membership

Become a Corporate Member today and inspire creativity in your work, strengthen your connection as a team, and experience new perspectives through the most compelling and exciting art of our time.

Corporate Members benefit from special access to a variety of experiences at SFMOMA:

  * Happy hours and lunch in the outdoor Sculpture Garden at Cafe 5
  * Holiday parties, client receptions, and other private events
  * Family visits (visitors 18 and younger are always free)
  * Coffee breaks at Steps Coffee
  * Walking meetings through the galleries
  * Discounted gifts from the Museum Store and rentals of art for your offices



[List of Current Corporate Members](https://www.sfmoma.org/ways-to-give/partner-listing/)

### Benefits

[List of Corporate Benefits For Printing and Sharing](https://d1hhug17qm51in.cloudfront.net/www-media/2022/08/29160901/SFMOMA-Corporate-Membership-Benefits-3.pdf)

**All corporate members receive:  
**

  * Access to SFMOMA’s facility rental program for private company events
  * Listing on sfmoma.org
  * Subscription to SFMOMA’s Member Magazine



### $25,000

  * Free admission for all employees, plus one guest each*
  * 50 free admission passes*
  * 15 Corporate Cards**
  * 10 tickets to Member Party



### $15,000 _(for companies with fewer than 200 employees)_

  * Free admission for all employees, plus one guest each*
  * 25 free admission passes*
  * 12 Corporate Cards**
  * 8 tickets to Member Party  




### $10,000

  * 100 free admission passes*
  * 8 Corporate Cards**
  * 6 tickets to Member Party



### $5,000

  * 50 free admission passes*
  * 4 Corporate Cards**
  * 4 tickets to Member Party



### $2,500

  * 25 free admission passes*
  * 2 Corporate Cards**
  * 2 tickets to Member Party



*Admission for Corporate Members is accessible at the Membership Desk and is available on a first-come, first-served basis; tickets cannot be reserved online prior to visit. Surcharge fee for special exhibitions not included, except for visitors 18 and younger, who are always free. Each free admission pass admits one. Corporate Members should be prepared to show proof of employment (company ID, business card, pay stub, or email signature accompanied by a government issued ID) when they arrive at the Membership Desk. SFMOMA reserves the right to review membership fee based on usage.

**Corporate Cards are transferable within the company and reusable within one year, and allow free admission for cardholder plus one guest, waived surcharge fee for special exhibitions, and 10% discount on purchases at Cafe 5, Steps Coffee, and the Museum Store.
