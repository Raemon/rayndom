#  Last Look for Members — Kunié Suguira: Photopainting 

### Related Exhibition [Kunié Sugiura: Photopainting](https://www.sfmoma.org/exhibition/kunie-sugiura-photopainting/)

#### Saturday, Sep 13 + Sunday, Sep 14, 2025  
10 a.m.–Noon (both days)

####  Floor 3

Don’t miss a final chance to see the first-ever US survey of the unique work of Kunié Sugiura. Immerse yourself in works spanning the artist’s sixty-plus-year career, blending mediums like photography, painting, and sculpture, and expressing her bicultural identity as a Japanese American artist. Whether you are a first-time visitor to this exhibition or want to see it one more time before it closes, members can enjoy exclusive viewing hours during the morning on the closing weekend!

This exhibition is included with a general admission ticket; members do not need to reserve special tickets to attend Last Look for Members.

Not a member? Visit [sfmoma.org/membership](https://www.sfmoma.org/membership/) to join today.
