# SFMOMA's Countdown to 2023 Calendar

### 31 Days of Art This Holiday Season

Every day this December, open a door and unveil a Rivera-related artwork on view at SFMOMA, a mural in the San Francisco Mission District, or an item from the Museum Store. On select dates we’ll ask you to snap a picture of yourself in front of the selected artwork and tag us on [Instagram](https://www.instagram.com/sfmoma/) with the hashtag #SFMOMACountdown for a chance to win some fun prizes! (Be sure to follow us on [Instagram](https://www.instagram.com/sfmoma/) to see what you might win.)
