# 2010 SECA Art Award: Mauricio Ancalmo, Colter Jacobsen, Ruth Laskey, Kamau Amu Patton

![2010 SECA Art Award publication pages 6-7](https://d1hhug17qm51in.cloudfront.net/www-media/2018/08/03102922/2010_SECA_Art_Award_pps_6_7_edit-1024x762.jpg)

![2010 SECA Art Award publication pages 12-13](https://d1hhug17qm51in.cloudfront.net/www-media/2018/08/03102929/2010_SECA_Art_Award_pps_12_13_edit-1024x795.jpg)

![2010 SECA Art Award publication pages 18-19](https://d1hhug17qm51in.cloudfront.net/www-media/2018/08/03102935/2010_SECA_Art_Award_pps_18_19_edit-1024x751.jpg)

![2010 SECA Art Award publication pages 23-24](https://d1hhug17qm51in.cloudfront.net/www-media/2018/08/03102941/2010_SECA_Art_Award_pps_23_24_edit-1024x770.jpg)

Foreword by Neal Benezra; texts and interviews with the artists by Apsara DiQuinzio and Tanya Zimbardo

36 pages, 7 ½ x 10 inches, hardcover with slipcase

Published in 2011

This publication highlights work by the recipients of the 2010 SECA Art Award, SFMOMA’s biennial award program honoring Bay Area artists. The four winners of the 2010 award — Mauricio Ancalmo, Colter Jacobsen, Ruth Laskey, and Kamau Amu Patton — utilize diverse media to explore themes ranging from human partnership to memory. Along with illustrations of selected pieces included in the award exhibition and previous works by the artists, the catalogue features interviews and texts by curators Apsara DiQuinzio and Tanya Zimbardo.

Published on the occasion of the exhibition _2010 SECA Art Award: Mauricio Ancalmo, Colter Jacobsen, Ruth Laskey, Kamau Amu Patton,_ held at the San Francisco Museum of Modern Art (December 9, 2011–April 3, 2012)

ISBN 9780918471888 (hardcover with slipcase)
