Kunié Sugiura is a boundary-defying artist who embraces a hybrid approach that blends mediums and expresses her bicultural identity. Sugiura makes artworks that “break with conventions and traditions of both painting and photography,” to create dynamic, original forms. Learn more about how balancing duality —Japanese/American, organic/human-made, and painting/photography — defines her work.
