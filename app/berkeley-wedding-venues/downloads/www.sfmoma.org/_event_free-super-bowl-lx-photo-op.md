######  Special Event 

#  Free Super Bowl LX Photo Op 

#### January 29–February 8, 2026*

####  Floor 1, Evelyn & Walter Haas, Jr. Atrium 

Free; museum admission not required.

**Museum Hours**  
Monday–Tuesday: 10 a.m.–5 p.m.  
Wednesday: Closed  
Thursday: Noon–8 p.m.  
Friday*–Sunday: 10 a.m.–5 p.m.

*SFMOMA will be closed to the public on Friday, February 6, 2026.

  * [](https://www.facebook.com/sharer/sharer.php?u=https%3A%2F%2Fwww.sfmoma.org%2Fevent%2Ffree-super-bowl-lx-photo-op%2F&quote=Free+Super+Bowl+LX+Photo+Op)
  * [](https://twitter.com/intent/tweet?text=Free+Super+Bowl+LX+Photo+Op+https%3A%2F%2Fwww.sfmoma.org%2Fevent%2Ffree-super-bowl-lx-photo-op%2F)

Visitors and locals: enjoy a photo opportunity with the official Super Bowl LX Roman Numerals, on view for free in SFMOMA’s Evelyn and Walter Haas, Jr. Atrium from January 29–February 8, 2026.

To celebrate the NFL Championship Game and spotlight what makes the Bay Area a beacon in the fields of art, culture, technology, and sports, SFMOMA will also offer a range of programs, events, and products in partnership with the Bay Area Host Committee (BAHC).

### Featured Events

**Feb 5, 5–8 p.m.** | [Building Universes](https://www.sfmoma.org/event/building-universes/)  
**Feb 7, 2–4 p.m.** | [History of the Bay: Street Art and Graffiti](www.sfmoma.org/event/history-of-the-bay-street-art-and-graffiti/)  
**Feb 8, 10 a.m.–1 p.m.** | [Family Studio: Sports Pennants](https://www.sfmoma.org/event/family-studio-sports-pennants/)  


* * *

This program is presented in collaboration with SFMOMA’s Community Partner, the Bay Area Host Committee.
