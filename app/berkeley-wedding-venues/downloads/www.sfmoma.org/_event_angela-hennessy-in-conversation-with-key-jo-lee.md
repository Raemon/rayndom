#  Angela Hennessy in Conversation with Key Jo Lee 

### Related Exhibition [2024 SECA Art Award: Rose D'Amato, Angela Hennessy, Rupy C. Tut](https://www.sfmoma.org/exhibition/2024-seca-art-award-exhibition/)

#### Thursday, Apr 3, 2025

####  6 p.m. 

####  Floor 4, Gina and Stuart Peterson White Box 

Free with museum admission. This event takes place on a [**First Thursday**](https://www.sfmoma.org/event/first-thursdays-at-sfmoma-free-admission-for-bay-area-residents/) when the museum is free to Bay Area residents.

Since 1967, the [SECA Art Award](https://www.sfmoma.org/membership/seca/seca-art-award/) has honored more than ninety Bay Area artists with an exhibition at SFMOMA. Take a deeper dive into the artworks and motivations of one of the 2024 SECA Art Award recipients, Angela Hennessy. Hennessy will be in conversation with Key Jo Lee, chief of curatorial affairs and public programs at the Museum of the African Diaspora.
