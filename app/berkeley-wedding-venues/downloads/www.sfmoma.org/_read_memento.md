# _Memento_

### Kerry James Marshall

October 2022 

![](https://d1hhug17qm51in.cloudfront.net/www-media/2018/08/25083752/99.577_01_H02-Large-TIFF_4000-pixels-long-1024x709.jpg) Kerry James Marshall, _Memento,_ 1997; collection SFMOMA, Ruth and Moses Lasky Fund purchase;  
© Kerry James Marshall

  1. What is going on in this work? What do you see that makes you say that?
  2. What things do you recognize in this image? What things seem new to you?
  3. Describe the colors in this work. What color/s do you see most of? What color is used the least? Why do you think these colors were used?
  4. What do you think you’ll remember most about this artwork?
