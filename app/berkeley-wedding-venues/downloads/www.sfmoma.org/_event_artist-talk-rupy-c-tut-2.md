#  Artist Talk: Rupy C. Tut 

### Related Exhibition [2024 SECA Art Award: Rose D'Amato, Angela Hennessy, Rupy C. Tut](https://www.sfmoma.org/exhibition/2024-seca-art-award-exhibition/)

#### Saturday, May 17, 2025

####  10:30–11 a.m. 

####  Floor 2, SECA Gallery 

Free and open to the public; museum admission is not required.

  * [](https://www.facebook.com/sharer/sharer.php?u=https%3A%2F%2Fwww.sfmoma.org%2Fevent%2Fartist-talk-rupy-c-tut-2%2F&quote=Artist+Talk%3A+Rupy+C.+Tut)
  * [](https://twitter.com/intent/tweet?text=Artist+Talk%3A+Rupy+C.+Tut+https%3A%2F%2Fwww.sfmoma.org%2Fevent%2Fartist-talk-rupy-c-tut-2%2F)
  * ##### share




Join Rupy C. Tut for an engaging, bilingual (English and Punjabi) artist talk in the gallery as she shares the techniques and inspirations behind her paintings in _2024 SECA Art Award: Rose D’Amato, Angela Hennessy, Rupy C. Tut_. Reflecting on how dreams and memories shape her work, she will highlight new elements she has explored and discuss how the audience became part of the creative process.
