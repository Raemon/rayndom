# Reciprocal Benefits

### SFMOMA members at the Supporter level and above can gain access to museums across the U.S. and Canada

[Join Today](https://tickets.sfmoma.org/accounts/membership/join/M35)

**Whitney Museum of American Art**

Through a special agreement with the [Whitney Museum of American Art](https://whitney.org/) in New York, SFMOMA members at the [Supporter](/get-involved/membership/supporter) level and higher receive complimentary admission for two people (does not include admission to member previews or discounts at the museum store).

**Museum Alliance Reciprocal Program**

Through the Museum Alliance Reciprocal Program, SFMOMA members at the [Supporter](/get-involved/membership/supporter) level and higher receive complimentary admission and other benefits at more than thirty museums in the United States and Canada. Reciprocal benefits include:

  * Free admission at all times during museum hours*
  * Discounts on purchases made at participating museums’ stores



*Reciprocal privileges may be restricted for special ticketed exhibitions.
