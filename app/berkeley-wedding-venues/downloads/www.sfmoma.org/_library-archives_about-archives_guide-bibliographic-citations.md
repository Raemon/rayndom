# Guide to Bibliographic Citations

### Correct citation format for SFMOMA Exhibition Records

[Item description including date], folder title including exhibition dates, Exhibition Records, San Francisco Museum of Modern Art Archives. 

Exhibition:

  * _Mark Rothko  
_ August 13–September 8, 1946
  * Mark Rothko to Grace L. McCann Morley, August 20, 1946; _Mark Rothko_ , August 13–September 8; Exhibition Records, San Francisco Museum of Modern Art Archives



* * *

### Citation format for SFMOMA Administrative Records

[Item description including date], box year and folder title, series title, Administrative Records, San Francisco Museum of Modern Art Archives. 

Example (correspondence) from the Director’s Office series of the Administrative records:

  * Ansel Adams to Grace McCann Morley, 11 July 1943, 1943 Artist’s Correspondence, Office of the
  * Director 1935–1956, Administrative Records, San Francisco Museum of Modern Art Archives.



  


* * *

### Citation format for Unprocessed Materials

[Item description including date], Unprocessed Material, [series title if known], Administrative Records, San Francisco Museum of Modern Art Archives.  
-or-  
[Item description including date], Unprocessed Material, [exhibition title and exhibition dates], Exhibition Records, San Francisco Museum of Modern Art Archives. 

Examples of item descriptions:

  * Correspondence – please identify specifically as in examples above. You do not need to specify format for letters, but please specify other forms of correspondence (e.g. telegrams, memoranda).
  * Check-list, date
  * Press release, date
  * Shipping document–[to/from whom] shipping document, date
  * Loan document–[to/from whom] loan document, date
  * Exhibition poster–[name of exhibition] poster, date
  * Exhibition brochure–[name of exhibition] brochure, date



* * *

### Citation format for Photographs Collection

Title including description, date, Call number, Photographs Collection, San Francisco Museum of Modern Art Archives. 

Example:

  * Folk Arts of the Far East, Installation, 1949, ARCH.PIC.1949.01.012.002, Photographs Collection, San Francisco Museum of Modern Art Archives.
  * Symphony Luncheon, December 14, 1958, ARCH.PIC.1958.03.004.001, Photographs Collection, San Francisco Museum of Modern Art Archives.



* Bibliographic citation only — not to be used for credit line. Credit line should include photographer as copyright holder and/or creator.
