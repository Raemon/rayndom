By Marion Deuchars

Activities and illustrations by Deuchars

256 pages, 8 ¼ x 11 ½ inches, hardcover with slipcase

Published in 2016

Conceived in collaboration with award-winning author/illustrator Marion Deuchars (_Let’s Make Some Great Art_ , 2011; _Draw Paint Print Like the Great Artists_ , 2014), this kids’ activity book takes iconic artists from SFMOMA’s collection as inspiration for a range of fun, unexpected, and open-ended activities for kids ages 6 to 9.

Published in association with Laurence King Publishing

ISBN 9781780679273 (hardcover with slipcase)
