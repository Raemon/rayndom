# Countdown to 2021: One Day, One Work of Art

Are you ready for 2020 to be over? Count down the days with us here. Every day in December, you can open a door and discover an artwork from SFMOMA’s collection, then click on the revealed image to learn more about it. You might find a new favorite! Thank you for spending the last days of this momentous year with us, and for joining us as we bid _good riddance_ to 2020.
