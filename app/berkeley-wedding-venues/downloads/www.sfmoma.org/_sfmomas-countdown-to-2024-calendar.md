# SFMOMA's Countdown to 2024 Calendar

### 31 Days of Art and Audio This Holiday Season

It’s that time of year again when we look to the past as we countdown to the future! This year we [relaunched our audio guide](https://www.sfmoma.org/read/tune-in-at-sfmoma/), moving it from an app to an improved web experience. To commemorate the launch, every day in December, come back to this calendar and unveil an artwork, then input the corresponding audio stop number into our audio guide keypad (<https://www.sfmoma.org/audio/>) and discover cool audio stories as we journey into 2024. Happy New Year from SFMOMA!
