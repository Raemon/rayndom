# Matching Gifts

#### Double Your Impact with a Matching Gift

Did you know your donation to SFMOMA could be doubled — or even tripled — at no extra cost to you? Many employers offer matching gift programs to support the causes their employees care about.

By taking advantage of your company’s matching gift program, you can significantly amplify your support for SFMOMA’s mission. Your generosity helps us:

  * **Curate groundbreaking exhibitions** and enrich our permanent collection.
  * **Host education programs** that inspire creativity in art lovers of all ages.
  * **Showcase the work of emerging artists** , often in their first major museum exhibition.
  * **Offer free admission** to children 18 and younger and provide 47,000 square feet of public spaces filled with art.



#### It’s Easy to Maximize Your Contribution:

  1. **Check with your Human Resources department** to see if your employer offers a matching gift program.
  2. **Obtain and complete the necessary forms** — either online or in print.
  3. **Send the completed form to us** at [matchinggifts@sfmoma.org](mailto:matchinggifts@sfmoma.org) or mail it to:



SFMOMA Matching Gifts  
151 Third Street  
San Francisco, CA 94103

#### Need Assistance?

We’re here to help. If you have any questions or need guidance, please contact us at **415.357.4135** or **[matchinggifts@sfmoma.org](mailto:matchinggifts@sfmoma.org)**.

#### Tax Information

For your reference, SFMOMA’s EIN/federal tax ID number is **94-1156300**.
