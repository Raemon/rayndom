# KAWS: FAMILY FAQ

###  How do I purchase tickets for _KAWS: FAMILY_? 

Expand details

_KAWS: FAMILY_ opens on November 15. _KAWS: FAMILY_ is a surcharged presentation that requires a timed ticket for all visitors. All tickets will be available for purchase online. 

[Members](https://www.sfmoma.org/membership/) get free access and special viewing hours.

###  Why do I need to book tickets online in advance? 

Expand details

To help ensure a smooth and enjoyable visit, we require _KAWS: FAMILY_ attendees to purchase a ticket online in advance. 

###  How much do tickets cost? 

Expand details

There is a $10 surcharge ($12 on weekends and holidays) for this special exhibition. [Members](https://www.sfmoma.org/membership/) and visitors 18 and younger can attend for free. _All visitors must have a museum ticket and a timed ticket to attend _KAWS: FAMILY_._

[Click here to learn more ways to visit](https://www.sfmoma.org/deals-discounts/).

###  Why do I need a timed ticket for entry? 

Expand details

The exhibition has limited capacity. To ensure as many people as possible can safely experience it, timed tickets are required. 

Timed tickets provide a 30-minute entry window. Ticket holders must check in within that timeframe. If you miss your entry window, please speak with staff at our Ticketing or Membership Desk to reschedule.

For a limited time from December 20, 2025- January 4, 2026 you can purchase a KAWS Untimed Ticket. Learn more by reading our [KAWS Untimed Ticket FAQ](https://www.sfmoma.org/kaws-untimed-ticket-faq/).

###  Do members need to book a timed ticket? 

Expand details

Yes, all members must book a timed ticket for entry. Members can book tickets online; please make sure you are logged into your account.

###  I purchased a general admission museum ticket from a third party — how do I add _KAWS: FAMILY_? 

Expand details

A limited number of same-day tickets may be available on-site on a first-come, first-served basis. Please ask about availability at the Floor 2 Ticketing Desk on the day of your visit. 

###  Can I return or exchange my timed ticket? 

Expand details

Tickets are nontransferable, nonrefundable, and cannot be resold.

###  Can I enter multiple times during my visit? 

Expand details

Re-entry to _KAWS: FAMILY_ will not be allowed to ensure that as many people as possible can experience the exhibition.

However, your tickets are valid all day to visit the rest of the museum before or after your timed entry to _KAWS: FAMILY_.  
[See what’s on view](https://www.sfmoma.org/exhibitions/).

###  Is there an exhibition store for _KAWS: FAMILY_? 

Expand details

Yes. The _KAWS: FAMILY_ exhibition store is located inside the gallery and can only be accessed through the exhibition.
