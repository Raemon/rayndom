###### Discussion Questions

# _Untitled_

### Richard Misrach

October 2022 

![](https://d1hhug17qm51in.cloudfront.net/www-media/2022/05/21035109/2010.222.69_01_b02-Artsy-JPEG_4000-pixels-long-1024x767.jpg) Richard Misrach, _Untitled,_ 2005, printed 2010; collection SFMOMA, gift of the artist and Fraenkel Gallery, San Francisco;  
© Richard Misrach

  1. What words come to mind when you look at this photograph?
  2. How would you describe the mood of this photograph? What do you see that makes you say that?
  3. What strikes you most about this image? Why do you think that is?
  4. What or who is the subject of this photograph? What aspects of the work helped you arrive at that idea?
  5. Is this photograph timely? Does it have a timeless quality? What do you see in the work that supports your opinion?
