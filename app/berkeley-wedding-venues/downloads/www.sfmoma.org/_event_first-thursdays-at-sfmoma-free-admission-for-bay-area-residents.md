_The Free First Thursday program is temporarily paused. Visitors can enjoy a range of other[access programs](https://www.sfmoma.org/deals-discounts/) including Free Community Day, Museums for All, Discover and Go, as well as free admission for visitors 18 and younger._  


* * *

On the first Thursday of every month, all residents of the nine Bay Area counties are invited to spend an evening at the museum on us! **[View upcoming First Thursday dates and learn more about planned programming.](https://www.sfmoma.org/free-days/#first-thursday-programs)**

  * General admission is free from 4–8 p.m. Surcharged exhibitions require separate paid tickets.
  * Tickets are available approximately two weeks in advance. Reserving online is highly encouraged.
  * Limit two adult tickets per reservation.
  * Qualifying counties: Alameda, Contra Costa, Marin, Napa, San Francisco, San Mateo, Santa Clara, Solano, and Sonoma.
