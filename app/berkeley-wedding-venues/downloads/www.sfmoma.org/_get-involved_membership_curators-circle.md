# Curators’ Circle

  
  
Join our community of art lovers and philanthropists who directly support SFMOMA’s mission to bring unparalleled, awe-inspiring exhibitions and educational programs to the Bay Area. Enjoy first access to special exhibitions, bespoke events, and get to know SFMOMA’s innovative curators on trips to art destinations across the country.

* * *

### Become an Enthusiast-Level Member: $2,500–$4,999

_Interested in paying in installments? Contact[curatorscircle@sfmoma.org](mailto:curatorscircle@sfmoma.org)._

**Benefits include:**

  * Special-access talks, curator-led tours, signature events, and panels with arts luminaries
  * Celebratory exhibition openings and toasts 
  * Exclusive curator-led travel opportunities to exciting art destinations, highlighting contemporary art and culture 
  * Opportunity to join [Contemporaries](https://www.sfmoma.org/membership/contemporaries/), a collecting-focused group that offers innovative programs fostering conversation, education, and connections related to today’s contemporary art world
  * Unlimited free tickets to exhibitions requiring timed tickets and charged entry, pending availability



**Plus all**[**Benefactor**](https://www.sfmoma.org/get-involved/membership/benefactor)**-level benefits.**

[Join Curators’ Circle](https://tickets.sfmoma.org/accounts/membership/join/CC1)

* * *

### Become an Advocate-Level Member: $5,000–$9,999

_Interested in paying in installments? Contact[curatorscircle@sfmoma.org](mailto:curatorscircle@sfmoma.org)._

**Everything above, plus:**

  * Recognition annually in the SFMOMA Member Magazine
  * VIP passes to domestic and international art fairs upon request, when available



[Join Curators’ Circle](https://tickets.sfmoma.org/accounts/membership/join/CC2)

* * *

### Become a Patron-Level Member: $10,000–$19,999

_Interested in paying in installments? Contact[curatorscircle@sfmoma.org](mailto:curatorscircle@sfmoma.org)._

**Everything above, plus:**

  * A private curator-led tour of an exhibition or installation, upon request
  * Invitation to one [Director’s Circle](https://www.sfmoma.org/membership/directors-circle) event



[Join Curators’ Circle](https://tickets.sfmoma.org/accounts/membership/join/CC3)

* * *

Memberships are nontransferable. All prices and benefits are subject to change. To join by mail, [download an order form](https://d1hhug17qm51in.cloudfront.net/www-media/2025/12/02151828/FY26_-Blank-Curators-Circle-Contribution-Form-for-website_FINAL.pdf).
