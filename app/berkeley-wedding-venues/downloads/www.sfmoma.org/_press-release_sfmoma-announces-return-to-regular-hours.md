# SFMOMA Announces Return To Regular Hours

###

On Thursday, November 6, 2003 the San Francisco Museum of Modern Art (SFMOMA) returns to regular operating hours, opening to the public at 11 a.m. daily (except Wednesdays when the Museum is closed). The MuseumStore will continue to open early at 10 a.m. through the holiday season but will open at 10:30 a.m. starting Friday, January 2.

Regular hours:

Museum Galleries  
Open daily (except Wednesdays): 11 a.m.–5:45 p.m.  
Open late Thursdays: 11 a.m.–8:45 p.m.  
Closed Wednesdays

MuseumStore  
  
Open daily: 10:30 a.m.–6:30 p.m.  
Open late Thursdays: until 9:30 p.m.

Caffè Museo  
  
Open daily (except Wednesdays): 10 a.m.–6 p.m.  
Open late Thursdays: until 9 p.m.  
Closed Wednesdays

SFMOMA galleries, MuseumStore and Caffè Museo are closed on the following public holidays: Thanksgiving, Christmas, and New Year’s Day.
