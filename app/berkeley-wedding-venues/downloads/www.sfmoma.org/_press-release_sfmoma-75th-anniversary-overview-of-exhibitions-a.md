# SFMOMA 75th Anniversary Overview of Exhibitions and Events

###

January 18, 2010, marks the 75th anniversary of the San Francisco Museum of Modern Art. Throughout the anniversary season, SFMOMA will present a series of exhibitions and programs under the heading **_75 Years of Looking Forward_** illustrating the story of the artists, collectors, cultural mavericks, and San Francisco leaders who founded, built, and have animated the museum. In honor of this special occasion, the museum will also present a [**free weekend celebration**](https://www.sfmoma.org/press/releases/exhibitions/821)from Saturday, January 16 through Monday, January 18, 2010. Join us for the celebration.

Please click the following links for the press releases and images of the various events and exhibitions:

[**The Anniversary Show** December 19, 2009–Jan. 16, 2011](https://www.sfmoma.org/press/releases/exhibitions/820)

[**Focus on Artists** January 16–June 6, 2010](https://www.sfmoma.org/press/releases/exhibitions/819)

[**The View From Here** January 16–June 20, 2010](https://www.sfmoma.org/press/releases/exhibitions/818)

[**Dispatches from the Archives** November 7, 2009–July 6, 2010](https://www.sfmoma.org/press/releases/exhibitions/817)

[**Ewan Gibbs: San Francisco** January 16–June 20, 2010](https://www.sfmoma.org/press/releases/exhibitions/816)

[**Long Play: Bruce Conner and the Singles Collection** January 16–June 6, 2010](https://www.sfmoma.org/press/releases/exhibitions/815)
