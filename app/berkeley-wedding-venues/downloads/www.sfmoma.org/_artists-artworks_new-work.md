# New Work

Since 1987, SFMOMA’s _New Work_ series has provided a platform for experimentation: a space for artists to develop or premiere a body of work or present existing work in a new context. Initiated with the support of SFMOMA’s Collectors Forum, the series focuses on the innovative ideas and visions of national and international artists, and has played a key role in shaping the breadth and character of the museum’s collection and programming. Through _New Work_ , SFMOMA has organized early exhibitions with artists such as Matthew Barney, Marilyn Minter, Kara Walker, and Christopher Wool, all of whom received their first solo museum shows through the _New Work_ series.

For more information, [view](https://d1hhug17qm51in.cloudfront.net/media/uploads/files/DiQuinzio_Surveying_New_Work_SFMOMA_75_Years_2009.pdf) an essay from _San Francisco Museum of Modern Art: 75 Years of Looking Forward_ about the history of the _New Work_ series or [download](https://d1hhug17qm51in.cloudfront.net/media/uploads/files/sfmoma_new_work_exhibition_list.pdf) a list of past exhibitions.
