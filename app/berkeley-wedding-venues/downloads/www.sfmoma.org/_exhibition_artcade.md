# Artcade

October 20, 2024–March 23, 2025 

Floor 2, Koret Education Center

Play sports-themed video games in SFMOMA’s community-designed Artcade. Three proposals were selected from an open call, and the winning teams spent the summer designing and building their game. Presented alongside [_Get in the Game: Sports, Art, Culture_](https://www.sfmoma.org/exhibition/get-in-the-game/), the selected games reflect on the expansive nature of play, spectatorship, and the close relationship between competition and collaboration.

### About the Artists

#### Grass Stains

**[Aaron Freedman](https://aef.itch.io/)** is a game designer, developer, and professor based in Brooklyn. His most recent game is _Open Roads_ , a road trip adventure game that explores family secrets. He is currently working on a narrative documentary game, _Normandie_. He has taught game design at both New York University and The New School. 

**[Nina Freeman](http://ninasays.so/)** is a game designer and streamer based in Maryland. She is best known for her work designing and writing autobiographical games, including _Cibele_ and _Last Call_. She is currently working on an indie horror game called _Size Zero_ and is the narrative designer for the upcoming game _Lost Records: Bloom & Rage_. 

**[Diego Garcia](https://radstronomical.com/)** is a game developer, animator, and professor based in Brooklyn. His most recent game release is _Casual Birder_ — a game about birdwatching imposter syndrome. He also designed _Ball Breaker_ , a game for the television series _The Bear_. He currently teaches Game Development and Animation for Games at NYU. 

#### Pongalong

**[Celeste Clark Jewett](https://www.pntgrm.com/celeste)** is a game designer and composer based in Los Angeles. She has provided music and sound design for various movies, theater projects, and games, including the interactive theater project _You Are the Hero_ (2015) and the short film _Unholy Guacamole_ (2018). She is a designer on the WATERBOX game _The Price of Flight_ (2025) which was a finalist in the 2023 Indiecade Festival and won the Excellence in System Design award at the Independent Games Festival 2023. 

**[Jared Pettitt](https://www.pntgrm.com/jared)** is a game designer and visual artist based in Santa Cruz. His work is inspired by and in response to media-poisoned and self-destructive culture. He is a PhD candidate in UC Santa Cruz’s Computational Media Department, focusing on researching and designing game creation tools. Pettitt is currently teaching Game Systems Design at UC Santa Cruz. 

#### Super Ultra Racing Xtreme

**[Tyler Knowlton](https://ambiguous.name/)** is a programmer and game designer. Knowlton is currently an MA student in Scientific Computing and Applied Math at UC Santa Cruz. 

**[Annalivia Martin-Straw](https://annalivia.xyz/)** is a game designer and artist based in Los Angeles. She graduated in 2023 from UC Santa Cruz with a BA in Art. 

Knowlton and Martin-Straw have collaborated on several works previously, including _are you the artist_? and _Spider-880_. 

Additional thank you to our community advisors Greg Rice, Shem Nguyen, Susie Kameny, and Theresa Nguyen.
