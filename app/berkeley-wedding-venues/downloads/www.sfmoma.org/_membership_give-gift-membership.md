# Gift Membership

Gift an SFMOMA membership to spread joy and inspiration all year long! Click on a membership level below to view the full description of benefits and give your gift today. Your purchase of a gift membership provides invaluable support to our museum and helps us to engage and give back to the Bay Area. Thank you for your support!

Purchase five or more in one order and receive a 20% discount. Call Member Services at [415.357.4135](tel:415.357.4135) to receive the discount. [Current members](https://tickets.sfmoma.org/accounts/login) receive 10% off gift membership purchases.

Complimentary guest ticket each visit, special previews, discounts, and more. 

[ Purchase ](https://tickets.sfmoma.org/accounts/membership/gift/M17)

Expand details

**Benefits include:**

  * Free admission to the museum and surcharged exhibitions for member and accompanied guest (children 18 and younger are always free) _(Based on availability of tickets at the time of sale.)_
  * One personalized member card
  * Members-only previews, last looks, and parties
  * 10% off at Cafe 5 and Steps Coffee
  * 10% off Museum Store purchases (20% on your first purchase, and on member sale days)
  * 10% off gift memberships
  * 25% off parking in the SFMOMA garage
  * First access to Art Bash tickets
  * Priority ticketing for special exhibitions and select programs
  * Discounted tickets to Rooftop Radio events
  * Subscription to our quarterly member calendar and biannual member magazine
  * Option to enroll in our Automatic Renewal Program



_Memberships are nontransferable. They become effective immediately and expire after one year on the last day of the month of purchase._

### Gift Membership Details

  * Don’t have your recipient’s contact information? Consider purchasing a gift certificate by phone or on-site (good for memberships or ticket purchases).
  * As gift purchaser, you will receive an acknowledgment of your gift from SFMOMA with our thanks and tax-deductibility details.
  * Gift memberships can also be purchased on-site or by phone.
  * Gift materials may be sent digitally for last-minute gifts. Please contact Member Services at [membership@sfmoma.org](mailto:membership@sfmoma.org) or [415.357.4135](tel:+1-415-357-4135).
