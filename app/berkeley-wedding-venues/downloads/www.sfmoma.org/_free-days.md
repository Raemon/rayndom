# Free Days at SFMOMA

Thanks to the generosity of our funders, SFMOMA offers regularly scheduled free days and guests 18 and younger always enjoy free admission to the museum. We regularly update this page with upcoming dates and activities, but you can also [sign up for our newsletter](https://sfmoma.prospect2.com/f/4) to be notified when new events, exhibitions, and activities are announced.

### Free Family Days at SFMOMA

Enjoy a day of art and family fun at SFMOMA! These special days include art-making activities for the whole family in our Koret Education Center, located on Floor 2, plus additional performances, activations, and more. General admission to the museum is free for up to two adults accompanying one child or teen (18 and younger), and tickets are available two weeks in advance. **Surcharged exhibitions are not included and require separate paid tickets.** For more information on Free Family Day, see our [FAQ](https://www.sfmoma.org/visit/free-family-day-faq/).

### Free Community Days

Free Community Days offer free museum-wide admission to all visitors. Combined with a wide range of immersive activities and performances inspired by the latest SFMOMA exhibition, these Free Community Days highlight the extraordinary creativity of local artists and community organizations. Mark your calendar for the next one, and enjoy a unique opportunity to learn, create, and connect with fellow community members!

### Program Temporarily Paused — First Thursdays at SFMOMA

_The Free First Thursday program is temporarily paused. Visitors can enjoy a range of other[access programs](https://www.sfmoma.org/deals-discounts/) including Free Community Day, Museums for All, Discover and Go, as well as free admission for visitors 18 and younger._  


* * *

On the first Thursday of every month, all residents of the nine Bay Area counties are invited to spend an evening at the museum on us!

  * General admission is free from 4–8 p.m. Surcharged exhibitions require separate paid tickets.
  * Tickets are available approximately two weeks in advance. Reserving online is highly encouraged.
  * Limit two adult tickets per reservation.
  * Qualifying counties: Alameda, Contra Costa, Marin, Napa, San Francisco, San Mateo, Santa Clara, Solano, and Sonoma.
