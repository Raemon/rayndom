### Nancy "Pili" Hernández Oral History

#### San Francisco, July 6, 2021

##### 

This is an oral history interview with artist and activist Nancy “Pili” Hernández. She details her foray into murals and street art and also discusses her more recent work as an activist, especially related to her activism with the Resist banner in Washington, DC, after the 2016 US presidential election.
