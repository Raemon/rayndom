# Twin Walls Mural Company:  _Our Ancestors’ Wildest Dreams_

Elaine Chu and Marina Perez-Wong—together known as Twin Walls Mural Company—offer a behind-the-scenes look at their latest work _Our Ancestor’s Wildest Dreams_ , commissioned by SFMOMA. The San Francisco–based artists discuss their process and how this vibrant mural manifests healing and resiliency in the face of trauma, injustice, and oppression. (Music: Diana Gameros, “Amor A Todo”; recorded and mixed by Diana Gameros at Casita Fulton studio.)
