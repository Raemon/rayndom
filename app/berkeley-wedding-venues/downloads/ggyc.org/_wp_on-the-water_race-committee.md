### Interested in joining the Race Committee?

Contact the Race Office by email at [**raceoffice@ggyc.com**](mailto:raceoffice@ggyc.com?subject=Interested%20in%20joining%20RC)

You can also drop by the club before a race to observe and express your interest in joining. New Race Committee members are always welcome regardless of experience. The experienced members will provide both orientation and on-the-job training to new committee members.

Neil Motteram, Race Director
