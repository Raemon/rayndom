![Jogging.jpg](data:image/png;base64,iVBORw0KGgoAAAANSUhEUgAAAAQAAAADCAQAAAAe/WZNAAAAEElEQVR42mM8U88ABowYDABAxQPltt5zqAAAAABJRU5ErkJggg==)

Let’s be honest — around the holidays, we’re all a little sentimental. Maybe it’s the smell of pine needles, the twinkle of lights, or the sight of your dog trying (and failing) to unwrap their own present. Whatever it is, this season reminds us what really matters: connection, compassion, and yes, joy. 

[ __Learn More Today](https://joybound.org/?utm_source=DiabloMag&utm_medium=Sponsored+Content&utm_campaign=AndAlso_Phase2)

**At Joybound People & Pets** in Walnut Creek, that joy is our mission 365 days a year. We bring people and pets together, nurture their bond, and build happier, healthier communities in the process. And this holiday season, when you give to Joybound, you’re not just donating, you’re unleashing a ripple of good that touches thousands of lives. 

* * *

![Movie.jpg](data:image/png;base64,iVBORw0KGgoAAAANSUhEUgAAAAQAAAADCAQAAAAe/WZNAAAAEElEQVR42mM8U88ABowYDABAxQPltt5zqAAAAABJRU5ErkJggg==)

#### **A Gift That Doubles the Joy**

Thanks to a generous matching donor, every gift this holiday season will be **doubled** — meaning double the veterinary care and services for pet families, double the human-animal bond connections, and double the comfort for people who need it most. Imagine helping a once-homeless pup find a warm bed _and_ empowering a veteran to find purpose through training that same dog as a service animal. That’s the kind of full-circle magic your gift makes possible. 

* * *

#### **Lives Transformed — Two at a Time**

In the past year alone, Joybound facilitated over **2,000 pet adoptions** — that’s 2,000 new families formed, 2,000 lives changed, and 2,000 reasons to believe in second chances. But adoption is just the beginning. Through our **Community Solutions program** , we help pet guardians keep their animals through tough times by providing food, veterinary care, and training resources. Because we believe love shouldn’t depend on your bank account. 

Then there’s our **Engelstad Grooming Academy** , where aspiring groomers train alongside shelter animals earning job skills while giving pets the TLC they deserve. Talk about a win-win: every fluffy blowout is a step toward a brighter future for both student and pup. 

And let’s not forget our **Shelter to Service program** , training and pairing shelter dogs with people managing psychiatric or emotional challenges. Together, they learn, heal, and grow in ways that science can measure—but hearts simply feel. 

* * *

![Kitchen.jpg](data:image/png;base64,iVBORw0KGgoAAAANSUhEUgAAAAQAAAADCAQAAAAe/WZNAAAAEElEQVR42mM8U88ABowYDABAxQPltt5zqAAAAABJRU5ErkJggg==)

#### **Why Joybound, and Why Now?**

Sure, there are plenty of good causes out there. But Joybound is unique because it’s not just about animals — it’s about people too. It’s about the power of companionship, the science of empathy, and the belief that joy shared is joy multiplied. 

So this holiday season, while you’re sipping cocoa and scrolling through gift guides, consider giving something that outlasts the season. A gift to Joybound People & Pets doesn’t just bring happiness for a day — it builds a future where _every_ pet has a loving home and _every_ person who needs one has a loyal friend by their side. 

Double your joy. Double your impact. Give to Joybound People & Pets. 

And who knows? You might just find that the best gift you give this year has fur, four paws, and a heart full of gratitude.
