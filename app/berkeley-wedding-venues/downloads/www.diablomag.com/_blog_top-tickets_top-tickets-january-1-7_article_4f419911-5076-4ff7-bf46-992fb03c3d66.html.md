World Tree of Hope

Rainbow World Fund

#### Holiday

**20th Annual RWF World Tree of Hope**

**Through 1/9** Start off the new year on a hopeful note with a visit to the Rainbow World Fund’s World Tree of Hope at San Francisco’s Grace Cathedral. On display through 1/9, the tree is decorated with more than 20,000 origami stars and cranes featuring wishes for the global community. _[worldtreeofhope.org](http://worldtreeofhope.org)._

#### Museum

** _Greetings from Everywhere: A Treasures of the Tri-Valley Exhibit_**

**Through 1/10** The _Greetings from Everywhere_ show at Pleasanton’s Museum on Main is a true team effort. Community members have come together to showcase mementos of their travels around the world—from rocks and seashells to artworks that serve as reminders of globe-trotting adventures. _[museumonmain.org](http://museumonmain.org)._

_An Evening with David Sedaris_

Anne Fishbein

#### Comedy

** _An Evening with David Sedaris_**

**1/3-1/11** Each featuring sneak peeks at his forthcoming essay collection, a Q&A session, and a book signing before and after the main event, David Sedaris’s appearances at Berkeley Rep are a must for fans. Kick off the new year with some hilarity while getting a glimpse of the humorist’s creative process. _[berkeleyrep.org](http://berkeleyrep.org)._

#### Theater

** _Dog Man: The Musical_**

**1/3-1/4** Bring the kids to the Henry J. Kaiser Center for the Arts in Oakland for _Dog Man: The Musical,_ based on the iconic graphic novels by Dav Pilkey. Audiences are sure to love watching Dog Man—who has a policeman’s body and a dog’s head—take on crime. _[hjkarts.org](http://hjkarts.org)._

#### Music

** _100 Years of Hank Williams_**

**1/4** Jason Petty’s portrayal of Hank Williams at Livermore Valley Arts’ Bankhead Theater is not to be missed. Honoring classic songs like “Lovesick Blues,” Petty won an Obie Award for his performances as Williams and last appeared at the Bankhead in 2022. _[livermorearts.org](http://livermorearts.org)._
