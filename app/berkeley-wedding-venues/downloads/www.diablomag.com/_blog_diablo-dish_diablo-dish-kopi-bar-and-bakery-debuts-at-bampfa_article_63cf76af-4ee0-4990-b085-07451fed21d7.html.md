Kopi Bar

Sajia Bidar

**Nora Haron** officially debuted her [**Kopi Bar and Bakery**](https://thekopibar.com) over the weekend within the unique second-story café space in the Berkeley Art Museum and Pacific Film Archive (BAMPFA). It marks a speedy turnaround for the lauded chef, who earlier this year shuttered her contemporary Indonesian restaurant SanDai Nusantara Californian after a two-year run in downtown Walnut Creek (still no word on what, if anything, will replace it on North Main Street). The new Kopi actually represents an expanded version of the daytime coffee bar that she operated adjacent to SanDai. It will feature a similar range of creative espresso and cold brew beverages such as Haron’s popular avocado coffee, as well homemade pastries, many of them Asian themed, including pandan and coconut brioche swirls and makrut lime shortbread. The Berkeley café will also offer salads, soups, and snacks with _kaya_ toast brûlée with coddled eggs and _bubur_ ayam (Indonesian-style chicken congee) with soy sauce sambal among the new menu items. Haron also plans to work with curators to create seasonal offerings artistically inspired by specific museum exhibitions and film programs.

“Kopi Bar and Bakery at BAMPFA is meant to be more than just a café. It’s a continuation of the museum experience,” said Haron. ”I want people to feel like they can come to the café space to slow down, take it all in, and enjoy something delicious that also sparks curiosity and reflection.”

### Duck Donuts Coming to Walnut Creek

Piping hot made-to-order doughnuts are coming soon to Walnut Creek. Cult East Coast chain [**Duck Donuts**](https://www.duckdonuts.com/walnut-creek) will be opening its first Bay Area location near the Pleasant Hill/Contra Costa Centre BART station at 2920 North Main Street, where “coming soon” signage is already on display (the website lists it as “coming 2026”). The North Carolina–founded operator has built a rabid following (and nationwide presence) with doughnuts that are fried to order in front of customers, who can then choose custom coatings, drizzles, and toppings (bacon included!). Duck also offers coffee and espresso drinks, milkshakes, and doughnut ice cream and breakfast sandwiches.

### Jo’s Modern Thai and the Peach Enlist New Chefs

[**Jo’s Modern Thai**](https://josmodernthai.com%20) and sister brunch spin-off the [**Peach**](https://thepeachbrunch.com) have brought in a pedigreed new culinary team to oversee its Oakland restaurants. Jo’s new executive chef **Nui Surach** brings experience from acclaimed upscale Thai restaurants Nari and Kin Khao in San Francisco. New menu items from the Bangkok-bred former _Iron Chef Thailand_ winner include _khao soi_ curry noodle soup, charcoal clay pot pork jowl, and tempura corn fritters with cucumber plum sauce. Jo’s will also introduce new tasting menus and weekend lunch. Meanwhile, the Peach’s new lead, **Harold Villarosa,** sports a culinary résumé that includes stints at Michelin-starred restaurants Noma and Per Se. The Filipino-born, Bronx-raised chef’s new additions to the Asian-inspired brunch menu include a bodega sandwich, curry shrimp and congee, and golden pancakes with house-made triple berry compote. Villarosa and owner **Kao Saelee** also plan to launch the Peach at Night later this month with an evening menu headlined by skewers cooked over an open fire and served with a variety of creative sauces. The moves come a little less than a year after the departure of Jo’s head chef **Intu-on Kornnawong** following reports of staff discontent over working conditions.
