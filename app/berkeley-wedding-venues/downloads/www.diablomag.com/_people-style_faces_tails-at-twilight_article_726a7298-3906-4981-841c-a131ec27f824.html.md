Valley Humane Society’s Tails at Twilight black-and-white ball at Casa Real at Ruby Hill Winery in April was attended by over 300 guests eager to make a difference in the lives of local animals. Proceedings began with a champagne reception before moving into appetizers, dinner, auctions, and animal meet-and-greets. Based in Pleasanton, Valley Humane has been connecting dogs, cats, and people since the 1980s. The next Tails at Twilight gala is slated for March 21, 2026.
