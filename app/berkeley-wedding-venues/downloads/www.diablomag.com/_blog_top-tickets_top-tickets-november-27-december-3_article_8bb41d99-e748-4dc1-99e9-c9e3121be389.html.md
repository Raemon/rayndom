#### Visual Art

** _Observations of a Still Life_**

**Through 12/14** Bedford Gallery’s latest exhibit in Walnut Creek highlights the long-popular genre of the still life, particularly as a method of capturing the times in which each is created. The show aims to shift attention from society’s information overload and draw eyes to the day-to-day. [_bedfordgallery.org_](http://bedfordgallery.org) _._

#### Theater

** _Sunday in the Park with George_**

**Through 12/30** This iconic musical—with a book by James Lapine and music and lyrics by Stephen Sondheim—is being performed at Berkeley’s Ashby Stage thanks to the Shotgun Players. The beloved show captures painter Georges Seurat’s life and professional career. [_shotgunplayers.org_](http://shotgunplayers.org) _._

#### Holiday

**Walnut Creek on Ice**

**Through 1/16** Get into the winter spirit with a 90-minute ice-skating session at this fan-favorite rink at Civic Park in Walnut Creek. If you’re looking for a family activity on the Thanksgiving, Christmas, and New Year’s holidays, this is the place for you. [_walnutcreekonice.com_](http://walnutcreekonice.com) _._

#### Holiday

**Bishop Ranch Turkey Trot**

**11/27** Start your Thanksgiving at City Center Bishop Ranch in San Ramon with a 5K (or a Tiny Turkey fun run for kids younger than 5). Athletes of all ages—and furry friends—are invited to participate, and pre-registrants can give back to the Food Bank of Contra Costa and Solano. [_citycenterbishopranch.com_](http://citycenterbishopranch.com) _._

#### Theme Park

**WinterFest**

**11/28-1/4** Six Flags Discovery Kingdom in Vallejo is currently decked out in stunning seasonal decor and glittering holiday lights. Stop by during the evening for live winter-themed entertainment, meet and greets with Santa Claus, specialty eats, and access to the park’s attractions. [_sixflags.com/discoverykingdom._](https://sixflags.com/discoverykingdom)
