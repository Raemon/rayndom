Spend the first weekend in June in Napa Valley at the world-famous [Napa Valley Barrel Auction](https://www.collectivenapavalley.org/events/2025-barrel-auction/).

You and your friends will enjoy world-class wines, culinary delights from the best restaurants and a chance to bid on special wine and experiences from winemakers, all while raising money for the health and well-being of children and teens in Napa Valley.

The Napa Valley vintner community is excited to showcase the best of Napa Valley with you.

#### [Napa Valley Barrel Auction](https://www.collectivenapavalley.org/events/2025-barrel-auction/)

**Friday, June 6—12:00 to 4:00 PM**

Kick off the weekend with the return of the ever-popular Napa Valley Barrel Auction at the historic Louis M. Martini Winery, where you will taste small-production wines from 100 esteemed Napa Valley wineries. Only here can you enjoy and be the first to acquire so many incredible wines from the renowned 2023 vintage.
