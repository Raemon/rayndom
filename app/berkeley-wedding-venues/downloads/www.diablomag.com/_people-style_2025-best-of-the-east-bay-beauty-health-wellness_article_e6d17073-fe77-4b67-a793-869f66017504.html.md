#### BEST HAIR REPLACEMENT PROFESSIONAL

**HELEN OWENS, HER SECRET HAIR, ALAMO AND LIVERMORE**

No matter if clients are confronting hair loss due to chemotherapy side effects, trichotillomania, or another hair- or scalp-related condition, Helen Owens can help find customized solutions that will have them feeling confident. After experiencing hair loss in her youth due to alopecia, Owens published a memoir titled _Finding Our She-Compass: 15 Life Lessons for Women Voyaging Toward Restoration, Reconstruction, and Renewal_ in 2017 and subsequently produced more than 50 episodes of _The She-Compass Show_ podcast. At Her Secret Hair, Owens, who has been highlighted on CNBC, CNN, and _USA Today,_ continues to help East Bay women feel empowered. _[hersecrethairextensions.com](http://hersecrethairextensions.com)._

#### BEST ECO-FRIENDLY PRODUCTS

**SUSTAINABAR, ALBANY**

Sustainabar’s ethos is “Saving the Earth, one bar at a time.” Since founding the company in 2019 as middle and high school students, young CEOs Tessa and Davia Rose-Scheeres, with their mom, Julia, as COO, have certainly stayed true to that goal, developing body soap, shampoo, conditioner, lotion, and dish soap bars and shaving pucks that are plant-based and zero-waste. The family has been highlighted in _Forbes_ and _The Washington Post,_ and they estimate that they’ve been able to remove more than 20,000 plastic bottles from circulation. You can find their products at Oakland’s Bay Made, Berkeley’s FillGood and the Filling Station, and other places across the Bay. _[sustainabar.net](http://sustainabar.net)._

* * *

#### BEST INCLUSIVE BARBER

**SLICK AND DAPPER AND HAIRPIN AND STROP, OAKLAND**

For about 13 years, Oakland’s Slick and Dapper has helped LGBTQ+ folks to receive gender-affirming haircuts in a safe, welcoming space. The trans-owned salon provides hair and beard trims and shaves of various durations and styles, with special rates for children and seniors. A local favorite, Slick and Dapper also reopened its revitalized Broadway location, Hairpin and Strop salon and gallery, this spring to reach an even broader clientele with full-service offerings. Slick and Dapper and Hairpin and Strop celebrate each individual’s identity, taste, and experiences—and help clients feel like their most authentic selves. _[slickanddapper.com](http://slickanddapper.com), [hairpinandstrop.com](http://hairpinandstrop.com)._
