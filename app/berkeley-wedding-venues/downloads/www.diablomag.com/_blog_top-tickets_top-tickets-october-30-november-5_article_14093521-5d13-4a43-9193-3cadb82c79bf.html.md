#### Visual Art

 _**Connections**_

**Through 11/8** Don’t miss the last few days of _Connections,_ the latest exhibit at Valley Art Gallery in Walnut Creek. The show—which is all about making personal connections with the viewer, and whose works are included in the gallery’s rental program—features about 300 pieces in various mediums. _[valleyartgallery.org](https://valleyartgallery.org/)._

#### Day Trip

**Fright NightLife and Behind-the-Screams Tour**

**10/30, Through 11/2** California Academy of Sciences’ Halloween NightLife installment promises pop-up entertainment, interactive activities, and more fun, all with a spooky bent. Before the event, stop by the Behind-the-Screams Tour, which will introduce you to lots of eerie specimens that make up the academy’s collections. _[calacademy.org](https://calacademy.org/)._

#### Music

**Wild Halloween Fun**

**10/31** Head to Berkeley’s Gilman Brewing Company for an unforgettable Halloween celebration with rousing performances from a pair of local bands, Dirty Cello and Shake It Booty. Guests are encouraged to attend wearing costumes and enjoy the best of Gilman’s beer. _[gilmanbrew.com](https://gilmanbrew.com/)._

#### Film

**KCSM’s 60th Anniversary Jazz Documentary Screening**

**11/1** Join the California Historical Radio Society at the Alameda Theatre and Cineplex on Saturday to watch a new documentary looking back at six decades of KCSM radio. The event will also include a trip to the Radio Central museum and a private reception. _[californiahistoricalradio.com](https://californiahistoricalradio.com/)._

#### Speaker Series

 _**Elevate Every Voice: Advocacy in Action**_

**11/1** Activist Nicole Adler will speak about her life and her mission as a disability and LGBTQ+ rights advocate at Las Trampas in Lafayette. The event, focused on how love and compassion can be harnessed to bring about societal change, is part of the organization’s Elevate Every Voice speaker series. _[lastrampas.org](https://lastrampas.org/), [elevateeveryvoice.com](https://elevateeveryvoice.com/)._
