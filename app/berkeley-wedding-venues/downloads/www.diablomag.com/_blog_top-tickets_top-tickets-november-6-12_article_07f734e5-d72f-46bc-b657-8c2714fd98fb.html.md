#### Literature

**Hayley Kiyoko’s _Where There’s Room For Us_ Special Event**

**11/6** Singer and actress Hayley Kiyoko is stopping by UC Berkeley’s International House as part of an evening hosted by Mrs. Dalloway’s Literary and Garden Arts. The event, geared at teens, celebrates Kiyoko’s new novel, which captures a version of 1800s England free of social restrictions around love. _[mrsdalloways.com](https://mrsdalloways.com/)._

#### Dance

 _**The Nutcracker Suite**_

**11/7-11/8** Diablo Ballet’s rendition of the winter favorite, choreographed by Julia Adam and staged at the Lesher Center for the Arts in Walnut Creek, sees Clara gifted the nutcracker at San Francisco’s Fairmont Hotel. _The Nutcracker Suite_ marks the kickoff of Diablo Ballet’s latest season. _[diabloballet.org](https://diabloballet.org/)._

#### Holiday

**Kristi Yamaguchi Holiday Ice Rink Ceremony**

**11/7** The Kristi Yamaguchi Holiday Ice Rink is returning to San Ramon’s City Center Bishop Ranch for the 2025 winter season. The lineup for the opening ceremony includes a ribbon cutting led by the Olympian, carol singing, and skating showcases. The rink will be open through 1/4. _[citycenterbishopranch.com](https://www.citycenterbishopranch.com/)._

#### Holiday

**Veterans Day Breakfast and Open House**

**11/11** The Veterans Memorial Building of San Ramon Valley in Danville is starting its Veterans Day tribute with a breakfast for former and active-duty servicemembers and their families. Members of the public will also be able to attend a ceremony, museum open house, and Mount Diablo beacon lighting at sunset. _[vmbsrv.org](https://vmbsrv.org/)._

#### Film

**San Francisco Silent Film Festival**

**11/12-11/16** Guests at this silent movie festival—typically held at San Francisco’s Castro Theatre—can enjoy screenings of favorite and newly restored films in the classic 1940s art deco Orinda Theatre, including a showing of Charlie Chaplin's _The Gold Rush_ honoring its 100th anniversary. _[silentfilm.org](https://silentfilm.org/)._
