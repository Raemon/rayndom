#### Visual Art

**60th Annual Holiday Collection for Artful Giving**

**Through 12/30** Get your holiday shopping done while supporting local artisans at Valley Art Gallery in Walnut Creek. This year’s available art pieces include jewelry, sculptures, paintings, holiday decor, and more. Check the gallery’s website for additional information about trunk shows throughout the month. _[valleyartgallery.org](http://valleyartgallery.org)._

#### Fundraiser

** _An Evening with_ Phantom _Legend Franc D’Ambrosio_**

**12/5** Join actor Franc D’Ambrosio, the World’s Longest-Running Phantom in _The Phantom of the Opera,_ for a benefit performance at the Bankhead Theater in support of Livermore Valley Opera. _The Phantom of the Opera_ has been beloved by Broadway fans for four decades. _[livermorevalleyopera.com](http://livermorevalleyopera.com)._

#### Day Trip

**Gardens Aglow**

**12/5-12/21** The Sonoma Botanical Garden is shining with glittering lights just in time for the winter holidays. Head to Wine Country to enjoy seasonal tunes, arts and crafts, hot chocolate and wine, face painting, and additional activities while taking in the festive display. _[sonomabg.org](http://sonomabg.org)._

#### Music

**John Legend:_Get Lifted_ 20th Anniversary Tour**

**12/9** Singer John Legend and his band—and maybe even special guests—will appear at Paramount Theatre in Oakland to take a look back at his album _Get Lifted._ The tour will celebrate the singer’s biggest hits and is a must for longtime Legend fans. _[paramountoakland.org](http://paramountoakland.org)._

#### Theater

** _A Christmas Carol_**

**12/10-12/21** Center Rep is teaming up with Axis Dance Company for the 2025 production of _A Christmas Carol_ at Walnut Creek’s Lesher Center for the Arts. Revisit the timeless tale of Ebenezer Scrooge, who learns important lessons about generosity and empathy from three ghosts just in time for Christmas. _[centerrep.org](http://centerrep.org)._
