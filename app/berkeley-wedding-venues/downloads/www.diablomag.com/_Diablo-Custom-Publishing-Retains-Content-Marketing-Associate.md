August 22, 2014

**Danielle Sensley has been hired by Diablo Custom Publishing as its Content Marketing Associate, responsible for developing effective communications to its audience.**

Diablo Custom Publishing (DCP), a leading custom publishing agency helping businesses increase customer retention, grow revenue and build brand loyalty, is pleased to announce the recent hire of Danielle Sensley as Content Marketing Associate. Sensley will play a pivotal role in the delivery and execution of a wide range of content marketing campaigns and strategies. She will be responsible for content marketing through DCP’s blogs, social media, website properties, email and other forms of outbound communications.

Sensley brings more than five years of marketing experience to DCP and holds a bachelor of science degree in business administration with an emphasis on marketing. In her previous roles, she worked in a content marketing capacity for Bank of America, SEIU-UHW Education Fund and The Apollo Group.

"I've admired DCP for many years, and couldn’t be more excited to be joining such a talented, genuine and creative group of professionals," says Sensley. "I look forward to contributing to the team’s continued success in my new role. I am very excited to join DCP during this period of expansion, and I look forward to working with our custom publishing team to further the company’s development and support for our clients."

"We are very excited to bring Ms. Sensley to our team," states Dave Reik, Director of Marketing for Diablo Publications. "Danielle’s expertise in content marketing will assist us with our mission of utilizing custom content to tell our story about how we can help companies create highly effective, branded content to meet their goals."

ABOUT DIABLO CUSTOM PUBLISHING

Diablo Custom Publishing (DCP), a leading custom publisher based in the San Francisco area, provides complete print, online and mobile custom publishing services to a diverse group of clients, including top-level organizations in the healthcare and higher education industries, as well as in arts and entertainment, sports, and technology. With over 30 years of experience and an award-winning creative team, DCP develops integrated marketing solutions that help companies communicate brand value, deepen customer loyalty and increase revenue.
