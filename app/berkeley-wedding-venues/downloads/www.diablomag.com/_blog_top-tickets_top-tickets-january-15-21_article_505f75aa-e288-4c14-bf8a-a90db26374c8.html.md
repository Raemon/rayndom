#### Theater

**Formerly Incarcerated People’s Performance Project**

**1/15-1/18** Berkeley Rep is hosting a four-day festival of solo performance pieces at Roda Theatre from the artists at the Formerly Incarcerated People’s Performance Project. The festival acts as a conclusion to the group’s residency—the first of its kind at Berkeley Rep—over the last three years. _[fippp.org](http://fippp.org), [berkeleyrep.org](http://berkeleyrep.org)._

#### Comedy

** _Improvised Stephen King_**

**1/15-1/25** Synergy Theater is blending horror and hilarity at the Lesher Center for the Arts in Walnut Creek with _Improvised Stephen King._ Synergy improvises full-length productions on the spot inspired by audience suggestions. Guests can expect eerie children, graveyards, haunted houses, and more signature King elements. _[lesherartscenter.org](http://lesherartscenter.org), [synergytheater.com](http://synergytheater.com)._

#### Music

**Petty Theft—San Francisco Tribute to Tom Petty and the Heartbreakers**

**1/17** From “Free Fallin’” to “I Won’t Back Down” to “Wildflowers,” the iconic songs of Tom Petty and the Heartbreakers are instantly recognizable. Relive the band’s greatest hits with the acclaimed cover act Petty Theft at Almost Famous Wine Company in Livermore. _almostfamous.wine._

#### Theater

** _The Addams Family Musical_**

**1/17-2/1** Tri-Valley Theatre Company’s newest show at the Bankhead Theater in Livermore reintroduces audiences to a beloved creepy and kooky family. In this musical, Wednesday is dating a “normal” boy—causing distress among her parents and loved ones. _[trivalleytheatre.org](http://trivalleytheatre.org)._

#### Theater

** _Tina: The Tina Turner Musical_**

**1/21-1/22** Penned by Pulitzer Prize winner Katori Hall, this musical focuses on the life and work of 12-time Grammy awardee and rock ‘n’ roll icon Tina Turner. Catch the show at the Henry J. Kaiser Center for the Arts in Oakland. _[hjkarts.org](http://hjkarts.org)._
