In July, patrons of the Danville-based Have a Ball Foundation came together at Crow Canyon Country Club for fun and fundraising. Have a Ball expects to raise $315,000 by the end of 2025 for cancer treatment support, part of more than $5 million donated over the last two decades to local beneficiaries such as Walnut Creek’s Cancer Support Community San Francisco Bay Area and John Muir Health.
