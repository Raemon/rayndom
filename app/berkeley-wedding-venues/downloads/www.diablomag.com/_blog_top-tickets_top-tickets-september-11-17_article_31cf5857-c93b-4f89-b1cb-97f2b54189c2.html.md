#### Visual Art

 _**Fresh Works Juniors**_

**Through 10/11** The Harrington Gallery at Pleasanton’s Firehouse Arts Center has debuted a new type of exhibit. _Fresh Works Juniors_ showcases 90 pieces— sculptures, paintings, and various other mediums—from student artists from the Class of 2025 and below hailing from around the Bay Area. _[firehousearts.org](http://firehousearts.org)._

#### Theater

 _**The Reservoir**_

**9/5-10/12** Don’t miss this comedic show at Berkeley Repertory Theatre written by Jake Brasch and directed by Mike Donahue. In the play, presented at Peet’s Theatre, a young LGBTQ+ man hauls his four grandparents through self-improving activities as he tries to get sober. _[berkeleyrep.org](http://berkeleyrep.org)._

#### Comedy

**Taylor Tomlinson: Save Me Tour**

**9/12-9/13** You may recognize Taylor Tomlinson’s name from her Netflix specials _Look At You_ and _Have It All_ or her stint as host of _After Midnight_ on late-night TV. The comedian is coming to Oakland to perform three shows at Paramount Theatre. _[paramountoakland.org](http://paramountoakland.org)._

#### Visual Art

** _Echoes of Yosemite_ and _Gustavo Ramos Rivera_**

**9/17-12/14** In _Echoes of Yosemite,_ William Keith’s landscapes can be viewed among works by the California Art Club. Gustavo Ramos Rivera’s solo exhibition merges pre-Columbian symbolism from Mexico with abstract expressionism from San Francisco. Catch both at Saint Mary’s College Museum of Art in Moraga. _[stmarys-ca.edu](http://stmarys-ca.edu)._

#### Music

**Berkeley Old-Time Music Convention**

**9/17-9/21** The Freight will host two nights of the Berkeley Old-Time Music Convention, bringing together artists and bands such as Bruce Molsky, Jake Blount, and the Ozark Highballers. Other concerts will be held around Berkeley at the Starry Plough, Berkeley Public Library, and more spots. _[thefreight.org](http://thefreight.org), [berkeleyoldtimemusic.org](http://berkeleyoldtimemusic.org)._
