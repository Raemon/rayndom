Join us Memorial Day weekend at San Ramon Central Park on Sunday and Monday, May 25–26, from 11 a.m. to 6 p.m.—a free, family-friendly celebration of creativity, community, and high-flying fun!

Discover San Ramon is proud to be the presenting sponsor of the [Art & Wind Festival](https://discoversanramonca.com/summer2025/?utm_source=DiabloBuzz1&utm_medium=LocalGetaways&utm_campaign=DSR-Summer2025), a colorful celebration where professional kite flyers take to the skies with choreographed demonstrations, and kids can craft their own creations in the free kite-making workshop.

* * *

#### But there’s much more to enjoy:

● Professional choreographed kite flying demos and free kite making workshop

● Fine arts and crafts from more than 100 talented artists

● Specialty and gourmet food vendors

● International music, party bands, and a variety of performers

● Puppet shows, interactive kids’ areas, rides, and family amusement zones

● Coast Guard helicopter landing and sensory zone

* * *

#### Discover More of San Ramon & Why Everyone Loves it Here

But that’s just the beginning—San Ramon is packed with scenic hiking and biking trails, lush parks, world-class golf courses, and lively outdoor dining spots. There’s no better place to relax, explore, and soak up the summer sun.

Book a San Ramon hotel stay between May 25 and June 30, 2025, and receive 15% off your stay and a $50 LB Steak San Ramon restaurant certificate—savor the best dining in town!
