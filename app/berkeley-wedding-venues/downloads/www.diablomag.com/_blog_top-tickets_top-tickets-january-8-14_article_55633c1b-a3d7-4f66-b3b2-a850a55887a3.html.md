#### Festival

**ThursDates at OMCA Kick Off**

**1/8** OMCA’s Thursday evening event series in Oakland is back for the winter season as ThursDates at OMCA, featuring performances, games, art sessions, and additional activities for the 18+ crowd, as well as food from Town Fare and signature cocktails. Check the website for more dates through March. _[museumca.org](http://museumca.org)._

#### Music

** _Front Porch Music Presents_**

**1/9** Front Porch Music returns to Lafayette’s Town Hall Theatre Co. for the latest installment of their quarterly music series. The concert will offer a deep dive into the work of up-and-coming members of the regional music community. Don’t miss the happy hour held before the show. _[townhalltheatre.com](http://townhalltheatre.com)._

#### Visual Art

** _Viola Frey: Foundations_**

**1/10-4/5** Examine the work of the late Bay Area artist Viola Frey with this new exhibition at Walnut Creek’s Bedford Gallery. The exhibit showcases Frey’s lesser-known works, including drawings, paintings, photos, and more. The gallery will also hold a Cocktails and Conversation session tied to the show on 3/12. _[bedfordgallery.org](http://bedfordgallery.org)._

#### Holiday

**Three Kings Day Fiesta**

**1/10** Honor Día de los Reyes Magos at Oakland’s Peralta Hacienda Historical Park. Held with the intention of celebrating togetherness and good will, the lunchtime festivities will include arts and crafts and, of course, Mexican hot chocolate and Rosca de Reyes cake to wrap up the holidays. _[peraltahacienda.org](http://peraltahacienda.org)._

#### Visual Art

** _UCBG Florilegium_**

**1/14-2/2** Head to _Florilegium_ at the University of California Botanical Garden at Berkeley to see a juried exhibit of breathtaking botanical illustrations. Admission to the show is free with a ticket to the garden, and guests can attend an artist open house on 1/17. _[botanicalgarden.berkeley.edu](http://botanicalgarden.berkeley.edu)._
