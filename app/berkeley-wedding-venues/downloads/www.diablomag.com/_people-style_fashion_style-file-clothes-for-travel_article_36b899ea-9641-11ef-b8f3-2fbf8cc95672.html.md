#### Haute Contrast

The patchwork trench by Sloan, available at Oakland’s McMullen, effortlessly elevates any jeans and top combination. A twill fabrication keeps things wrinkle-resistant. Side pockets, adjustable sleeve tabs, and an optional self-tie belt add to its practicality. _$475,[shopmcmullen.com](http://shopmcmullen.com)._
