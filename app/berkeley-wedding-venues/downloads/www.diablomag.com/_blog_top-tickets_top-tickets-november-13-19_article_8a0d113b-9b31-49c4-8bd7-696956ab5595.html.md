#### Comedy

 _**Thanksgiving Unscripted**_

**Through 11/16** Synergy Theater’s latest production at Walnut Creek’s Lesher Center for the Arts offers a laugh-out-loud way to kick off your Thanksgiving celebrations. The comedy group, which improvises full-length plays, takes audiences to a madcap seasonal holiday party. _[lesherartscenter.org](https://www.lesherartscenter.org/)._

#### Visual Art

**Pleasanton Art League’s 18th Annual Fall Members’ Show**

**Through 12/20** At Firehouse Arts Center’s Harrington Gallery, the Pleasanton Art League has returned for its 18th annual exhibition. The 75-or-so highlighted artworks span all mediums, and several live artist demonstrations are scheduled for 11/15 and 12/20. _[firehousearts.org](https://www.firehousearts.org/)._

#### Music

**Wreckless Strangers**

**11/15** Wreckless Strangers’ sound blends rock, R&B, blues, and Americana styles—and honors local music history. On Saturday night, they will take the stage for a free performance at Lucca’s beer garden in Benicia. The band is fresh off a tour with shows up and down the West Coast and in the southern United States. _[luccabar.com](https://www.luccabar.com/)._

#### Music

 _**Beethoven’s Eroica**_

**11/15-11/16** California’s Symphony’s newest Walnut Creek show features works from three masters—Ludwig van Beethoven, Wolfgang Amadeus Mozart, and modern composer Jessie Montgomery. Pianist Robert Thies will also appear during the concert as a guest pianist for Mozart’s “Piano Concerto No. 21.” _[californiasymphony.org](https://www.californiasymphony.org/)._

#### Music

 _**Worlds Beyond**_

**11/16** Berkeley Symphony is examining transitions with its latest concert program, including through a co-commissioned piece by Juan Pablo Contreras. The concert will be held at the First Congregational Church of Berkeley—and guests are also welcome to attend an open rehearsal on 11/15. _[berkeleysymphony.org](https://www.berkeleysymphony.org/)._
