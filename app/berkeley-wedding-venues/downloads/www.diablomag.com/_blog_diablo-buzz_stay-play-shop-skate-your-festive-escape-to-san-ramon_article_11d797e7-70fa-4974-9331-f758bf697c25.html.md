Whether you’re planning a weekend getaway or a family outing, San Ramon makes it easy to [Stay. Play. Shop. Skate.](https://discoversanramonca.com/winter2025/?utm_source=BuzzBlog1&utm_medium=%20DiabloMag&utm_campaign=DSR-Winter2025) all season long. Discover San Ramon this winter — where twinkling lights, open-air shopping, cozy dining, and holiday fun come together.

**Enjoy 15% off your stay** at select San Ramon hotels between November 7, 2025 – January 4, 2026 and receive two complimentary ice skating tickets to the Kristi Yamaguchi Holiday Ice Rink at City Center Bishop Ranch.

The Kristi Yamaguchi Holiday Ice Rink returns with all its winter charm—glide under glowing lights, sip hot cocoa, and soak in the holiday spirit at the heart of City Center. Enjoy special events at the ice skating rink from Disney character nights, elf character, to a Kristi Yamaguchi Skating Clinic, and more.

Make the most of your visit with a comfortable stay at one of San Ramon’s conveniently located hotels—just steps from parks, dining, shopping, and seasonal entertainment. Enjoy 15% off your stay and receive two complimentary ice skating tickets to the Kristi Yamaguchi Holiday Ice Rink.
