Enjoy 15% off select San Ramon hotels now through January 4, 2026 and receive two complimentary ice skating tickets to the Kristi Yamaguchi Holiday Ice Rink.

Feel the magic of winter as you glide across the ice at City Center Bishop Ranch. Gather friends and family, sip hot cocoa, and create new traditions in the heart of San Ramon. Enjoy special events at the ice skating rink from Disney character nights, elf character, to a Kristi Yamaguchi Skating Clinic, and more.

Ice skating sessions are offered daily at 1 p.m., 3 p.m., 5 p.m., and 7 p.m. The rink opens Friday-Sunday at 11 a.m., and 9 p.m. sessions are available Fridays and Saturdays.

Make it a getaway and enjoy more time on the ice, in the shops, and around the table. San Ramon’s comfortable, convenient hotels put you steps away from holiday activities, shopping, and entertainment. Enjoy 15% off your stay and receive two complimentary ice skating tickets to the Kristi Yamaguchi Holiday Ice Rink.
