Earlier this year, local representatives from multiple faith traditions came together at JMC Cellars to engage in a centuries-old blessing of the grapes to kick off this fall’s harvest in Livermore wine country. First Presbyterian Church of Livermore’s Pastor Steve Wilde, Father Augustine Joseph from St. Charles Borromeo Catholic Church, and Rabbi Laurence Elis Milder from Congregation Beth Emek were all on hand to provide guiding words for the new season.
