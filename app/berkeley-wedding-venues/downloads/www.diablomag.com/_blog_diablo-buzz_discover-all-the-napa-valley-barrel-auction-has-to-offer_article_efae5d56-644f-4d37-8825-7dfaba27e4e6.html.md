Spend a weekend in Napa Valley with the beauty of this world-famous wine growing region as your backdrop. You and your friends will enjoy world-class wines, culinary delights from the best restaurants and a chance to bid on special wine and experiences from winemakers, all while raising money for the health and well-being of children and teens in Napa Valley. The All-Access [ticket package](https://www.collectivenapavalley.org/events/) includes the complete schedule of events: Napa Valley Barrel Auction, Vintner-Hosted Dinners, Napa Valley Your Way, and the finale of the weekend, the Live Auction Celebration.

Our vintner community is excited to showcase the best of Napa Valley with you.

* * *

#### Napa Valley Barrel Auction

**Friday, June 6—12:00 to 4:00 PM**

Kick off the weekend with the return of the ever-popular Napa Valley Barrel Auction at the historic Louis M. Martini Winery, where you will taste small-production wines from 100 esteemed Napa Valley wineries. Only here can you enjoy and be the first to acquire so many incredible wines from the renowned 2023 vintage.

* * *

#### Vintner-Hosted Dinners

**Friday, June 6—6:00 PM**

There is no better way to experience a Napa Valley evening than being surrounded by friends new and old, overlooking rows of vineyards and enjoying the extensive bounty of Napa Valley. Join one of our vintners as they open their home or cellar and host an intimate winemaker dinner with exclusive wines, a delicious meal and unmatched hospitality.

* * *

#### Napa Valley Your Way

**Saturday, June 7—11:00 AM to 2:00 PM**

This is your time to explore Napa Valley at your own pace. Get up close and personal with a Napa Valley winery of your choice as they host you for an afternoon of wine tasting, education and a behind-the-scenes look into the inner working of their winery.

* * *

#### Dinner & Live Auction Celebration

**Saturday, June 7—5:00 PM**

Cap off this extraordinary weekend with a night to remember at Chandon for the return of Auction Napa Valley. Join us for a sparkling greeting to start the evening, joining some of the most well-known vintners and winemakers for a seated dinner curated by Michelin-starred Chef Christopher Kostow.

After dinner, guests make their way to the live auction. A wild ride of feverish paddle waving awaits at vintner-hosted tables, cheering bidders as they vie for Napa Valley’s finest wines, and only-in-your-dreams experiences. All this merriment will culminate into supporting the health and education of children and teens in the Napa Valley community.

Keep the celebrations coming with live music, late-night bites and Napa Valley’s finest wines. Enjoy delightful surprises along the way as you experience what makes Napa Valley one of the world’s greatest wine and food destinations.

_Hosted by Chandon_
