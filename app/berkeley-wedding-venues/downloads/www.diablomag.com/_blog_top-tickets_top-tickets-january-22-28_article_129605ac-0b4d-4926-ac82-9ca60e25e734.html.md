#### Literature

** _Choosing Wholeness Over Goodness:_ A Workshop with Elise Loehnen**

**1/22** Spend the evening at Book Society in Berkeley for a workshop with Elise Loehnen that will take a look at cultural expectations surrounding behavior, particularly the idea of “being good.” Loehnen is the author behind _On Our Best Behavior: The Seven Deadly Sins and the Price Women Pay to Be Good._ [_booksociety.social_](https://www.booksociety.social/) _._

#### Theater

** _How Shakespeare Saved My Life_**

**1/23-3/1** Head to Berkeley Rep’s Peet’s Theatre for Jacob Ming-Trent’s self-written one-man show exploring the impact that poetry has had on his life. Directed by former artistic director Tony Taccone, the production examines Ming-Trent’s wide-ranging influences, from Shakespeare to Tupac Shakur. _[berkeleyrep.org](http://berkeleyrep.org)._

#### Sports

**Giants FanFest**

**1/24** East Bay Giants fans can now enjoy all the fun of the preseason FanFest closer to home. City Center Bishop Ranch in San Ramon will welcome baseball aficionados for autograph sessions, games, and more, with Jung Hoo Lee, Landen Roupp, and Tony Vitello all slated to appear. _[citycenterbishopranch.com](http://citycenterbishopranch.com)._

#### Music

** _Schubert in Vienna_**

**1/24-1/25** California Symphony’s latest concert at Walnut Creek’s Lesher Center for the Arts highlights pieces by Wolfgang Amadeus Mozart, Friedrich Gulda, and Franz Schubert. The program explores woodwind instrumentals and features Bay Area native and soloist Nathan Chan for Gulda’s piece. _[californiasymphony.org](http://californiasymphony.org), [lesherartscenter.org](http://lesherartscenter.org)._

#### Film

** _The Man Who Saves the World?_**

**1/28** At the Grand Lake Theatre in Oakland, filmmaker Gabe Polsky and subject Patrick McCollum will introduce their new movie, _The Man Who Saves the World?_ The film follows McCollum, a pacifist and spiritual leader who becomes connected to an ancient indigenous prophecy. _[renaissancerialto.com](http://renaissancerialto.com)._
