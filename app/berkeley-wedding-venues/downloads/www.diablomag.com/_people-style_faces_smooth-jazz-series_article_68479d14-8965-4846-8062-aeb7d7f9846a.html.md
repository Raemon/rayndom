On three Saturdays in July, music lovers congregated in Alexander Square at San Ramon’s City Center Bishop Ranch to take in the jazz stylings of artists like guitarist Jonathan Butler, piano-saxophone duo Brian Simpson and Jackiem Joyner, and trumpet player Rick Braun. Guests at the free concert series were encouraged to picnic on the lawn, tuck in to a delicious meal on the patio at one of the eateries on the square, or reserve prime seats in proximity to the stage.
