Food lovers, mark your calendars—Oakland Restaurant Week is back from March 20–30, 2025, inviting you to experience the bold flavors and diverse culinary creativity that make Oakland a top food destination. Fresh offbeing named the #1 Best Food City in the U.S. by _Condé Nast Traveler_ readers in 2024 and #2 Best Food City by _Travel + Leisure_ , Oakland continues to cement its reputation as a must-visit haven for food enthusiasts. 

Since its launch in 2010, Oakland Restaurant Week—presented by Visit Oakland—has been a much-anticipated 10-day celebration, designed to spotlight the city’s vibrant dining scene while supporting local restaurants. This year, the event is taking things to the next level with the introduction of the Oakland Restaurant Week "Idea Guide"—a fresh approach encouraging participating restaurants to think outside the box. Expect DJ-hosted dinners, interactive art activations, sip-and-shop events with local retailers, and exclusive ORW signature cocktails—all crafted to elevate the dining experience. 

Diners can explore participating restaurants by neighborhood, cuisine, meal type, and price point, making it easier than ever to plan their culinary journey. Each restaurant will offer a special menu, deal, or exclusive experience, with prix fixe menus available at six price tiers: 

● **Lunch:** $15, $25, $35 

● **Dinner:** $35, $45, $65 

This is your chance to indulge in unique menus, one-of-a-kind food experiences, and exciting activations that celebrate Oakland’s culture, creativity, and global flavors. Don’t miss out on this incredible 10-day feast in one of America’s top food cities! 

Learn more and start planning your Restaurant Week experience: [oaklandrestaurantweek.org ](http://oaklandrestaurantweek.org%20)
