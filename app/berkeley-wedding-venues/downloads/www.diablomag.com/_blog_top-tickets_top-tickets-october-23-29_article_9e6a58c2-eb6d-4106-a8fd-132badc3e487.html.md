#### Opera

 _**La Traviata**_

**10/24, 10/26** This classic Giuseppe Verdi tale tells of a courtesan whose passionate affair with a nobleman tumbles toward tragedy. Watch it come to life at the Lesher Center for the Arts in Walnut Creek thanks to Festival Opera and the Diablo Symphony Orchestra. _[festivalopera.org](https://www.festivalopera.org/)._

#### Comedy

**Diablo Improv Festival**

**10/24-10/26** Explore improvised comedy during the debut of this new festival in Walnut Creek. The weekend will feature workshops, networking, live entertainment, and even an appearance by the acclaimed Groundlings from Los Angeles. _[diabloimprovfestival.org](https://diabloimprovfestival.org/)._

#### Food and Drink

**Halloween Brew Crawl**

**10/25** Show off your favorite Halloween costumes during this beer crawl through 20 participating businesses in downtown Pleasanton. Sample high-quality brews, enjoy spooky decor and activities, rock out to live musical performances, and take home a commemorative glass as a souvenir. _[pleasantondowntown.net](https://www.pleasantondowntown.net/)._

#### Music

**Lila Downs:_Día de los Muertos_**

**10/25** Grammy-winning singer Lila Downs performs standards and new songs written in English, Spanish, and Indigenous Oaxacan languages. She will honor loved ones who have passed during a Day of the Dead show at Cal Performances’ Zellerbach Hall. _[calperformances.org](https://calperformances.org/)._

#### Holiday

**Howl-O-Ween and Bishop Ranch Beer City Run**

**10/26** This Sunday will be a busy time at City Center Bishop Ranch in San Ramon. Start your day with the Bishop Ranch Beer City Run, featuring 5K, 10K, and half-marathon courses and celebrating local beers, before heading to the Howl-O-Ween costumed pet parade in Alexander Square. _[citycenterbishopranch.com](https://www.citycenterbishopranch.com/)._
