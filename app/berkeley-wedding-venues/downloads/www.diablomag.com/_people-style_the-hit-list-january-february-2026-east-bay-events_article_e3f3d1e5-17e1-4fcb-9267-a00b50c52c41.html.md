#### _Theater_

### ** _The Addams Family Musical_**

**1/17–2/1**

Spend time with everybody’s favorite creepy and kooky family during the latest production from Tri-Valley Theatre Company at Livermore’s **Bankhead Theater.** [_trivalleytheatre.org_](http://trivalleytheatre.org) _._

* * *

#### _Theater_

### ** _Tina: The Tina Turner Musical_**

**1/21–1/22**

Revisit the songs of rock ‘n’ roll legend Tina Turner at Oakland’s **Henry J. Kaiser Center for the Arts.** The musical takes a look at the 12-time Grammy winner’s path to becoming one of the most successful artists in history. [_hjkarts.org_](http://hjkarts.org) _._

* * *

#### _Theater_

### ** _How Shakespeare Saved My Life_**

**1/23–3/1**

Join Jacob Ming-Trent for an intimate, autobiographical reflection on how poetry—from Shakespeare to Tupac Shakur—has shaped his life and search for belonging. The world-premiere show, directed by former artistic director Tony Taccone, takes place at Berkeley Rep’s **Peet’s Theatre.** [_berkeleyrep.org_](http://berkeleyrep.org) _._

* * *

#### _Music_

### ** _Enchanted Dreams: The Unofficial Tribute to the Music of Taylor Swift_**

**2/1**

Swifties, save the date for Elizabeth Scarborough Burnett’s Taylor Swift tribute concert at the **Hofmann Theatre** at Walnut Creek’s Lesher Center for the Arts. The show features multimedia elements and lots of costumes in Swift’s signature style. [_lesherartscenter.org_](http://lesherartscenter.org) _._

* * *

#### _Festival_

### **San Ramon Culture in the Community Celebration**

**2/1**

Festival attendees can enjoy multicultural art, entertainment, learning booths, and more at **Dougherty Station Community Arts Center** in San Ramon. Two stages will be set up for live music and other performances, and kids can take advantage of a variety of games and crafts. [_sanramon​.ca.gov_](http://ca.govhttps//sanramon.ca.gov/) _._

#### Dance

### **Martha Graham Dance Company:_Graham100_**

**2/14–2/15**

The New York–based Martha Graham Dance Company, founded by the eponymous trailblazer in the 1920s, comes to **Zellerbach Hall** in Berkeley. Each of the two performances will spotlight unique pieces in addition to a core program. [_calperformances.org_](http://calperformances.org) _._

* * *

#### _Music_

### **Clipping**

**2/27**

Clipping—the experimental hip-hop group featuring Daveed Diggs of Oakland and Albany native William Hutson, along with Jonathan Snipes—takes the stage at Berkeley’s **UC Theatre Taube Family Music Hall.** The trio is known for their science fiction and horror concept albums. [_theuctheatre.org_](http://theuctheatre.org) _._

* * *

#### _Music_

### ** _Deep Roots, Bold Blues: A Celebration of Black Heritage_**

**2/28**

Conclude Black History Month at Hayward’s **Douglas Morrisson Theatre,** when the West Coast Blues Society provides a musical exploration of Black history via the blues. The festivities will honor the work of blues greats and highlight the genre’s impact on music today. [_haywardrec.org_](http://haywardrec.org) _._

* * *

#### _Conference_

### **East Bay Women’s Conference 2026**

**3/3**

The East Bay Women’s Conference brings women to the **Lesher Center** for speakers, networking, and exhibitors. This year’s guest speakers include empathy coach Vera Jones, KR Method founder Korey Rowe, the Joy Project CEO Amanda Gore, and Jennifer Ashton, president and founder of Ajenda. [_walnut-creek.com_](http://walnut-creek.com) _._
