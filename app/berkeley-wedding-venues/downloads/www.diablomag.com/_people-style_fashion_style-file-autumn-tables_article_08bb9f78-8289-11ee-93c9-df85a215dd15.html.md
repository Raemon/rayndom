#### Tray Chic

Kick off a meal—or a cocktail hour—with a charcuterie spread atop an ash driftwood square tray from Danville’s Whim House. It’s sustainably made and features a leather loop so you can hang it for easy storage. _$135,[whimhouse.com](http://whimhouse.com)._
