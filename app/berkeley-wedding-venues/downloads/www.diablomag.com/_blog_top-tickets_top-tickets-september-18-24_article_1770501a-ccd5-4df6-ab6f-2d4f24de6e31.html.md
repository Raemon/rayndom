#### Theater

 _**Laura Benanti: Nobody Cares**_

**Through 9/25** Don’t miss the chance to see Broadway star Laura Benanti during her special engagement at Berkeley Rep. The Tony Award recipient is performing her one-woman comedy show—which features original music—at the Roda Theatre after runs in Edinburgh and off-Broadway. _[berkeleyrep.org](https://www.berkeleyrep.org/)._

#### Visual Art

 _**Lifted**_

**Through 12/11** Danville’s Kriste Michelini Gallery prioritizes giving a platform to local female artists. Works from five creative minds—Janet Yano, Jenny Phillips, Susan McKinney, Lisa Fleming, and Kim Cardoso—are currently on display at the Railroad Avenue gallery, each examining literal and metaphorical elevation. _[kristemichelini.com](https://kristemichelini.com/)._

#### Food and Drink

**Taste of City Center, Cocktail Competition, and September Sips**

**9/19-9/28** Head to San Ramon for Taste of City Center, City Center Bishop Ranch’s restaurant week. Guests can sample the wide variety of cuisines represented by the center’s eateries—and save the date for a Cocktail Competition (featuring _Diablo_ ’s own Ethan Fletcher) on 9/21 and wine tasting at September Sips on 9/28. _[citycenterbishopranch.com](https://www.citycenterbishopranch.com/)._

#### Day Trip

** _Last West: Dorothea Lange’s California Revisited_**

**9/20-1/4** The Sonoma Valley Museum of Art is highlighting a one-of-a-kind visual and theater art experience that showcases the photographs of Dorothea Lange. The exhibit, which also features pieces by contemporary artists, explores topics such as climate change and migrancy. An opening reception will be held on 9/27. _[svma.org](https://svma.org/)._

#### Festival

**Blues, Brews and BBQ**

**9/21** Relish the last few days of warmer weather at Washington Park in Alameda, as Blues, Brews and BBQ returns for its eighth year. Festivities over the course of the afternoon are expected to include live music, drinks, a family area, and plenty of barbecue. _[westendartsdistrict.org/blues-brews-and-bbq](https://www.westendartsdistrict.org/blues-brews-and-bbq)._
