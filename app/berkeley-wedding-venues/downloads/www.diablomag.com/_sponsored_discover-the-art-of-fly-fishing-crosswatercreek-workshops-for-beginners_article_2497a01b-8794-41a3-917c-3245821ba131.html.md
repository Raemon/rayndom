There’s something timeless about standing knee-deep in a quiet stream, rod in hand, as the world slows down around you. At CrossWaterCreek Outfitters, we believe fly fishing is more than just a sport—it’s a gateway to connect with nature.

Whether you’re brand new to fly fishing or simply looking to sharpen your skills, our hands-on workshops are the perfect place to start. Designed with aspiring anglers in mind, these sessions take you from curiosity to confidence—no experience required.

* * *

#### Learn the Basics from Our Experienced Instructors

Our workshops provide a strong, practical foundation in fly fishing. You’ll cover the essential items needed to get started—from understanding gear and fly selection to basic knots and terminology. We also introduce the fundamentals of fly casting, helping you develop technique and control from the start.

Led by our knowledgeable and approachable team, each session offers guidance from experienced anglers who are passionate about teaching and sharing the joy of fly fishing. Our team creates an inviting, no-pressure environment where curiosity is encouraged and every skill level is supported. We’ve found that when people are given the right tools and encouragement, they not only learn faster—they fall in love with the process.

* * *

#### More Than a Lesson—It’s an Invitation

At CrossWaterCreek, our mission is to inspire and equip anglers of all levels to connect with nature through the art of fly fishing. But for us, education isn’t just about technique; it’s about cultivating curiosity and a sense of stewardship for the waters we love.

That’s why conservation is part of every workshop. We provide guidance on responsible fishing practices and the importance of protecting our local ecosystems. We don’t just fish these waters—we protect them for the next generation.

* * *

#### A Brand Built on Community

As a family-owned business, CrossWaterCreek was born from a passion for fly fishing and a desire to share it with others. We know it can be intimidating to get started, which is why we make it a point to welcome every person who walks through our doors.

Whether you're learning from one of our experienced instructors or simply connecting with a fellow angler in the shop, you’ll find a community built on trust, shared stories, and a love for the outdoors.

* * *

#### Why It Matters

At CrossWaterCreek, we offer premium gear, curated travel experiences, and expert guidance—but at the heart of it all is education. Because when someone learns to cast a line, they’re not just learning how to catch fish—they’re learning patience, building focus, gaining presence, and discovering a new way to experience the world around them.

Our hope is that a single workshop sparks a lifelong connection—to fly fishing, to wild places, and to a community that values conservation, craftsmanship, and time outside.

* * *

#### Ready to Give It a Try?

Whether you’re coming solo or bringing a friend, CrossWaterCreek workshops are a great way to try something new. You don’t need a closet full of gear—just a little curiosity and a love for nature.

Workshops are now open for registration at our Downtown Lafayette location. We look forward to welcoming you to our storefront.
