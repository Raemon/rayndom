Walnut Creek’s Festival Opera hosted its yearly fundraiser in May to benefit their upcoming season. The more than 60 guests in attendance at the Piedmont Center for the Arts appreciated opera performances, silent and live auctions, fine wine, and food from Siren Sisters Kitchen in Oakland. Mark your calendar for the company’s Salon Series, which kicks off July 27, and _La Traviata,_ which runs October 24 and 26 at the Lesher Center for the Arts in conjunction with the Diablo Symphony Orchestra.
