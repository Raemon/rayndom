#### Theater

** _Charles Dickens’ A Christmas Carol: A Ghost Story Told by Jefferson Mays_**

**Through 12/21** Revisit the story of Ebenezer Scrooge and his three ghostly guests with this one-of-a-kind staging at Berkeley Rep’s Roda Theatre. Tony awardee Jefferson Mays presents the novella as a limited-engagement one-man show, during which he portrays more than 50 characters. _[berkeleyrep.org](http://berkeleyrep.org)._

Oakland Zoo

#### Family

**Glowfari**

**Through 1/25** The Oakland Zoo is alight with hundreds of animal lanterns during Glowfari this winter season. This year’s exhibits focus on Oakland wildlife, the African savanna, coral reefs, and other aspects of the animal kingdom. Guests can also visit a rides area and grab dinner within the zoo. _[oaklandzoo.org](http://oaklandzoo.org)._

#### Music

**San Francisco Gay Men’s Chorus:_Holiday Spectacular 2025!_**

**12/20** The San Francisco Gay Men’s Chorus crosses the bay to Berkeley’s Zellerbach Hall to showcase its seasonal spectacular. The chorus will don ugly Christmas sweaters and elf costumes for a blend of holiday carols, popular music hits, and more during the pageant. _[calperformances.org](http://calperformances.org)._

#### Dance

**Graham Lustig’s _The Nutcracker_**

**12/20-12/21** Audiences of all ages love following the story of Marie and the Nutcracker Prince as they battle the Rat King and explore the Land of Sweets. Catch the Oakland Ballet’s iteration of the iconic tale at the Paramount Theatre, hosted in collaboration with the Oakland Symphony and the Piedmont East Bay Children’s Choir. _[oaklandballet.org](http://oaklandballet.org)._

#### Music

** _Campana Sobre Campana: Christmas in Mexico_**

**12/22** At the Bankhead Theater in Livermore, Ballet Folklorico del Rio Grande and Mariachi Garibaldi de Jaime Cuéllar will tell the Christmas story with classic dance, a soundtrack of mariachi music, piñatas, traditions from the celebration of Las Posadas, and more. The performance is named in honor of a Christmas carol from Spain. _[livermorearts.org](http://livermorearts.org)._
