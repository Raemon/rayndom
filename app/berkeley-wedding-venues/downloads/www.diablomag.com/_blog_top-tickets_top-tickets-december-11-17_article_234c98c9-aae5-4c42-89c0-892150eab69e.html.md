#### Theater

** _Snowbound: A Holiday Tale_**

**Through 12/20** Town Hall Theatre Co.’s production of _Snowbound: A Holiday Tale_ in Lafayette transports audiences back to 1992. A team of high schoolers are marooned at an abandoned house during a snowstorm and find themselves confronting with their relationships with one another and the holiday season. _[townhalltheatre.com](http://townhalltheatre.com)._

![0ff54dba-b562-bb1b-a698-2f759a735b43.gif](data:image/png;base64,iVBORw0KGgoAAAANSUhEUgAAAAQAAAADCAQAAAAe/WZNAAAAEElEQVR42mM8U88ABowYDABAxQPltt5zqAAAAABJRU5ErkJggg==)

Michele Selene Ang in the world premiere of Jessica Huang’s _Mother of Exiles,_ performing at Berkeley Repertory Theatre.

Kevin Berne

#### Theater

** _Mother of Exiles_**

**Through 12/21** At Berkeley Rep, theatergoers have the opportunity to explore immigration through three generations of the same family—from Angel Island in the late 1800s to a climate-change-ravaged landscape in 2063. The play is a product of Berkeley Rep’s Ground Floor new works development program. _[berkeleyrep.org](http://berkeleyrep.org)._

#### Visual Art

** _Little Gems—December 2025_**

**Through 12/31** From the mixed-media pieces by Jen Garrido to the oil paintings of Hannah Franco to kiln-formed glass pigment work from Jenn Shifflet, there’s something for everyone to enjoy in Slate Contemporary Gallery’s _Little Gems_ in Oakland. The show highlights small-scale artworks by member artists. _[slatecontemporary.com](http://slatecontemporary.com)._

#### Food and Drink

**Spirit and Sweater Stroll**

**12/13** Participants in Pleasanton’s Spirit and Sweater Stroll will have the chance to taste cocktails at more than 15 businesses throughout the downtown area. Check-in is at Museum on Main, select small bites will be available, and seasonal sweaters are encouraged. _[pleasantondowntown.net](http://pleasantondowntown.net)._

#### Music

** _Let Us Break Bread Together: A Tribute to Whitney Houston_**

**12/14** Led by Kedrick Armstrong, the Oakland Symphony, Oakland Symphony Chorus, Oakland Interfaith Gospel choirs, and locally based soloists and musicians are coming together for a yearly winter celebration. This year’s programming honors the work of the iconic Whitney Houston. _[oaklandsymphony.org](http://oaklandsymphony.org)._
