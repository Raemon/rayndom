#### For the Spirits Connoisseur

Head to Mare Island’s Savage and Cooke for an hour-long docent-led tour through the distillery, followed by a tasting of whiskey, bourbon, and rye that’s made in-house, as well as a rotating selection of specialty offerings. _$50 per person,[savageandcooke.com](http://savageandcooke.com)._

Courtesy of Pacific States Aviation

#### For the Aerophile

Give him wings with a Discovery Flight from Concord’s Pacific States Aviation flight academy. After an hour of ground training, he (and a certified instructor) will fly a Cessna 172. _$285,[psaflight.com](http://psaflight.com)._

#### For the DIY Guy

Let him learn the art of welding—away from your house in a safe, guided setting—at the Crucible in Oakland. If he’s never tried his hand at welding before, consider the two-day Exploring Welding class, which allows participants to check out various types. _$325,[thecrucible.org](http://thecrucible.org)._
