#### Love in Bloom

Treat your significant other to an artistic floral arrangement, such as the gorgeous selection of David Austin roses, hellebore, abelia, ninebark, ranunculus, and anemones pictured here. #### All Aboard!

Treat your valentine to a one-of-a-kind romantic tour aboard the Livermore Wine Trolley. Enjoy a glass of bubbly, followed by visits to Longevity Wines and Sons of Liberty Alehouse for an evening of fine wine and dining (offered February 14 and 15). _Price on request.[livermorewinetrolley.com](http://livermorewinetrolley.com)._

#### Sweet Indulgences

The XOXO Box from San Francisco’s Recchiuti is a special Valentine’s-themed collection of exquisite artisanal chocolates. Enjoy burnt caramel truffles, rosemary olive oil truffles, and more. Get yours at Recchiuti’s flagship shop in San Francisco’s Ferry Building or through the Recchiuti website. _Available in boxes of 16 pieces, 32 pieces, and 88 pieces for $56, $98, and $230 respectively.[recchiuti.com](http://recchiuti.com)._

#### Massage for Two
