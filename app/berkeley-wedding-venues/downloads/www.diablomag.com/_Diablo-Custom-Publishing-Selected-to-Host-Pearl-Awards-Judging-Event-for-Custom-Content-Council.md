# Diablo Custom Publishing Selected to Host Pearl Awards’ Judging Event for Custom Content Council

##  _Pearl Award entries will be judged by a panel of marketing executives, academics, journalists,  
designers, corporate strategists and publishers in three locations across the United States._

Walnut Creek, CA–Diablo Custom Publishing (DCP) has been selected by the Custom Content Council to host the West Coast judging of the 2013 Pearl Awards. The Pearl Awards celebrate the best in custom media, design, editorial and strategic initiatives for B2B and B2C publications.

The Pearl Awards are the continent’s first award program devoted exclusively to custom publishing. Custom content providers—whether small or large, B2B or B2C—represent clients in a wide range of industries and produce magazines, newsletters, and digital publications.

Entries will be judged in October 2013 in Walnut Creek, Chicago and New York by a panel of marketing executives, academics, journalists, designers, corporate strategists and publishers. Finalists will be notified in late October, and official designation of Gold, Silver and Bronze award winners will be announced at the Pearl Awards ceremony November 12 in New York.

"It’s an honor for DCP to participate in the annual Pearl Awards program both as a competition entrant and as host representing the West Coast custom publishing industry. A qualified panel of local judges has been recruited, and it is ready to take an active role in support of the Custom Content Council," notes Dave Reik, Marketing Director for DCP.

[ABOUT DIABLO CUSTOM PUBLISHING](http://www.dcpubs.com)

Diablo Custom Publishing, the Bay Area’s leading custom publisher, provides complete print, online and mobile custom publishing services to a diverse group of clients, including the Oakland Museum of California, Chevron, University of California Hastings College of the Law, the Oakland Athletics, and the San Francisco Giants. With over 30 years of experience and an award-winning creative team, DCP develops integrated marketing solutions that help companies communicate brand value, deepen customer loyalty and increase revenue. For more information, visit [dcpubs.com](http://dcpubs.com).

About the Custom Content Council and the Pearl Awards

The Custom Content Council (CCC) is the leading association for the custom publishing industry in the United States, and is focused on promoting the growth and vitality of this dynamic marketing discipline. Marketers across the country, the media, and other interested constituencies rely on the CCC as the authoritative source of industry news, data and trends, information on the effectiveness of custom publishing, and referrals to the top custom publishers in North America. In 2004, to herald the commitment to excellence and the growing success of custom publishing, the CCC launched the continent’s first award program devoted exclusively to custom publishing.
