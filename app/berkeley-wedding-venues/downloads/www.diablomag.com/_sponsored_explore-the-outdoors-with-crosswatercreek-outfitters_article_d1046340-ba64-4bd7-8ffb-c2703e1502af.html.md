At CrossWaterCreek Outfitters, their mission is to inspire and equip anglers of all skill levels to connect with nature through fly fishing. Shop the collection at their storefront in Lafayette or online at [crosswatercreek.com](http://crosswatercreek.com).

#### **Q: Why open a shop focused on fly fishing and adventure gear in the heart of Lafayette?**

**A:** Community is at the heart of everything we do. Our downtown Lafayette shop offers a space to share our passion for the timeless art of fly fishing while connecting with others who value adventure, quality, and family. We provide hands-on learning through workshops, presentations, and our new fly tying classes starting in January. Beyond experiences, we offer curated products for everyone on your list—fly fishing–themed chocolates, sunglasses you can wear around town or out on the water, and men’s and women’s apparel and adventure wear. — _Pete McCabe_

#### **Q: Why do hands-on workshops or classes make a difference compared to shopping online?**

**A:** Being in-store connects you to our team’s expertise. We answer questions, make gear and travel recommendations, and introduce you to local fishing clubs—sharing knowledge, stories, and experiences that can’t be replicated online. — _Pat McCabe_

#### **Q: Are there gift bundles or workshop experiences that make great holiday presents?**

**A:** Our [Intro to Fly Fishing workshops](https://crosswatercreek.com/pages/workshops-at-crosswatercreek) are perfect for beginners or anyone sharpening skills. Pair a session with curated gear or specialty items for a thoughtful gift. If you’re interested in giving the gift of an experience, we can also help plan trips near and far through our partnership with Hatch Travel. Shop in-store or online at [crosswatercreek.com](http://crosswatercreek.com) and follow us @crosswatercreek for more ideas! — _Chuck Walls_
