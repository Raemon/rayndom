January 30, 2015

**As Advertising Director, Tighe will be responsible for advertising sales management and advertising operations for Diablo magazine and other related publications.**

Walnut Creek, CA — Diablo Publications is proud to announce the promotion of Toni Tighe to the newly created position of Advertising Director. Tighe will manage the advertising sales staff of Diablo Publications, which includes: Diablo magazine, the Diablo City Book Series, Diablo Weddings, Diablo Arts, Concierge East Bay and the Tri-Valley California Visitors Guide. Tighe will manage print sales, planning, and ad operations; play a key role in overall advertising strategy; and drive the implementation of new advertising technologies on behalf of Diablo Publications and its digital platforms.​

Tighe brings 25 years of sales experience to this new position. Prior to her role as Director of Advertising, Tighe was a senior account executive responsible for print and online media sales for Diablo Publications. Previous to Diablo, she was an account executive for Parents’ Press, a monthly parenting newspaper. Prior to her time at Parents’ Press, Tighe was an account executive at Corporate Report Minnesota and CN Publishing.

"I am so pleased that Toni has been promoted to this new role," says Barney Fonzi, President, Diablo Publications. "Toni brings a tremendous amount of experience, talent, customer service, and creativity to our team, and we are confident that she will help us take all our magazines to new levels of excellence. She has exhibited a very strong passion, commitment, and work ethic over her tenure at Diablo Publications, and it is rewarding to see her take on these new responsibilities."

"I’m thrilled and honored to take on this new role during this period of growth. I look forward to working closely with the sales and marketing teams to further the company’s growth and support for our advertisers," states Tighe. "I have always been impressed with the quality and professionalism of Diablo Publications, and am looking forward to this new management role."

About Diablo Publications

For 35 years, Diablo Publications has been creating award-winning publications, including Diablo magazine, Napa [Sonoma magazine,](http://www.prweb.net/Redirect.aspx?id=aHR0cDovL3d3dy5uYXBhc29ub21hbWFnLmNvbQ==) Diablo Weddings, the Diablo City Book Series, Diablo Arts, Concierge East Bay and the Tri-Valley California Visitors Guide. Covering travel, theater, lifestyle, and home design, Diablo Publications celebrates the people, places, and pleasures of the East Bay and North Bay. Diablo Publications’ custom content division, Diablo Custom Publishing (DCP), provides complete print and online marketing communications and customer publishing services for corporate clients nationwide. For more information, visit [diablomag.com](http://diablomag.com) or [dcpubs.com](http://www.prweb.net/Redirect.aspx?id=aHR0cDovL3d3dy5kY3B1YnMuY29t).
