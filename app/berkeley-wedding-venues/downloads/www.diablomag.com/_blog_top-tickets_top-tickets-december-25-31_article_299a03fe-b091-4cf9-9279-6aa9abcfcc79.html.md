#### Holiday

**Garden of D’Lights**

**Through 1/11** The Ruth Bancroft Garden and Nursery in Walnut Creek celebrates the holiday season every year with a glittering display of lights and lasers throughout its 3.5 acres. Take a self-guided tour (with set entry times), shop for waterwise plants, and sip spiced wine and cocoa. _[ruthbancroftgarden.org](http://ruthbancroftgarden.org)._

#### Museum

** _Good Fire: Tending Native Lands_**

**Through 5/31** OMCA’s _Good Fire_ exhibition in Oakland takes a look at fire by shining a light on how Native American communities have traditionally used controlled burns for environmental and cultural purposes. While you’re there, explore the museum’s other galleries and _Ancestral Visions,_ an art installation from Chelsea Ryoko Wong only accessible through 2/1. _[museumca.org](http://museumca.org)._

#### Music

**Primus**

**12/30-12/31** Featuring longtime bandmates Les Claypool and Larry LaLonde and newcomer John Hoffman, Primus got its start right here in the East Bay in El Sobrante. The band will perform a pair of shows at Oakland’s Fox Theater, providing a one-of-a-kind way to ring in the new year. _[thefoxoakland.com](http://thefoxoakland.com)._

#### Holiday

**25th Anniversary Balloon Drop**

**12/31** For a quarter of a century, the Chabot Space and Science Center in Oakland has given families an avenue to celebrate New Year’s Eve without staying up until midnight. Balloon drops will take place at the center over the course of 12/31 as 2026 arrives in cities around the world. _[chabotspace.org](http://chabotspace.org)._

#### Holiday

**East Bay Regional Park District New Year’s Eve Celebrations**

**12/31** Put your best foot forward for 2026 with the help of the East Bay Regional Park District. Hang out with wildlife at the Elsie Roemer Bird Sanctuary at Crown Memorial State Beach in Alameda, enjoy a rejuvenating hike in the morning at Oakley’s Big Break Regional Shoreline, or spend time reflecting and watching the sunset at Del Valle Regional Park in Livermore. _[ebparks.org](http://ebparks.org)._
