# Diablo Publications Launches  
 _The Livermore/Pleasanton Book,_ the Fourth in _Diablo_ Magazine’s City Series

##  _The Livermore/Pleasanton Book will provide a comprehensive overview of the Livermore and Pleasanton areas.  
The book will highlight these vibrant cities with information about wineries, culinary options, recreation, festivals, and historic venues._

Walnut Creek, CA–Diablo Publications announced today the release of _The Livermore/Pleasanton Book,_ the fourth in a series of publications highlighting the cities and towns of the East Bay. The publication will be distributed to more than 13,000 readers in the San Francisco East Bay area. Produced by _Diablo_ magazine, _The Livermore/Pleasanton Book_ provides a comprehensive overview of these vibrant cities.

Editorial coverage will include food and drink, wineries, shopping, parks, recreation, city life, and more. _The Livermore/Pleasanton Book_ will detail each city’s unique offerings as well as some of their common characteristics. The Livermore/Pleasanton area features everything from white tablecloth restaurants and museums to performance venues such as the Bankhead Theater and the Livermore Shakespeare Festival, featuring productions among the vineyards each summer.

"The two cities offer so many interesting things to see and do: The area is growing and has so many new restaurants, wineries, entertainment venues, and shopping destinations including the recently opened premium outlets, making the locale a unique and interesting place to visit, dine, and shop," states Barney Fonzi, Group Publisher for Diablo Publications.

A must-have resource, _The Livermore/Pleasanton Book_ will be available at local travel information venues, hotels, advertiser locations, Chamber of Commerce offices, and at various events throughout the region. It also will be mailed to select homes with _Diablo_ magazine.

About Diablo Publications

For more than 34 years, Diablo Publications has been creating award-winning publications, including _Diablo_ magazine, _Napa Sonoma_ magazine, _Diablo_ City Book Series, _Diablo Arts,_ and the _Tri-Valley California Visitors Guide._ Covering travel, theater, lifestyle, dining, and home design, Diablo Publications celebrates the people, places, and pleasures of the East Bay and North Bay. Diablo Publications’ custom content division, Diablo Custom Publishing (DCP), provides complete print, digital and mobile marketing communications and customer publishing services for corporate clients nationwide. For more information, visit [diablomag.com](http://diablomag.com) or [dcpubs.com](http://dcpubs.com).
