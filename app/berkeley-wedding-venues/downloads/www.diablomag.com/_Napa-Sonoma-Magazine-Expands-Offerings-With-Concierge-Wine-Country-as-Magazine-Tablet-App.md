April 01, 2015

**Napa Sonoma Magazine Expands Offerings With Concierge Wine Country as Magazine Tablet App**

Napa Sonoma will now have four issues available through the Apple newsstand with the addition of Concierge Wine Country.

![Napa Sonoma Magazine Expands Offerings With Concierge Wine Country as Magazine Tablet App](data:image/png;base64,iVBORw0KGgoAAAANSUhEUgAAAAQAAAADCAQAAAAe/WZNAAAAEElEQVR42mM8U88ABowYDABAxQPltt5zqAAAAABJRU5ErkJggg==)

Diablo Publications today announced that it is offering Concierge Wine Country as a magazine tablet app available through the Apple Newsstand. As part of the expansion, [Napa Sonoma magazine](http://apple.co/1IK9c0D) has increased the frequency of its tablet subscription to four times per year with the addition of Concierge Wine Country. The Napa Sonoma tablet app includes content from the print publication as well as bonus interactive content.

The Concierge app directs visitors to arts and entertainment venues, clothing boutiques, specialty gift stores, and trendy home and accessory emporiums found in the region’s surrounding towns and shopping districts. Concierge’s listings and menu guides help visitors sort out the expansive variety of food and beverage options in the region, including some of the world’s [finest wineries](http://bit.ly/1IK9owN) and five-star restaurants.

For visitors planning on making a trip to the Napa Sonoma region, Concierge Wine Country is available as a single edition for $4.99 or an annual subscription (three editions of Napa Sonoma magazine and the Concierge Wine Country edition) for $12.99. Readers can also subscribe to the print and tablet version of the magazines at [napasonomamagazine.com/subscribenapa](https://www.napasonomamagazine.com/subscribenapa/index.php).

"It’s the ideal time to launch our expanded Napa Sonoma offerings," states Barney Fonzi, President of Diablo Publications. "Expanding our editorial and distribution channels will allow us to reach tens of thousands of additional readers, and give our advertisers several new options to reach their target audience. Because we’ve had so much positive reader response, we are now planning to re-launch a Concierge book for the San Francisco East Bay with a companion app."

About Napa Sonoma magazine

Napa Sonoma shows visitors how to experience Wine Country like a local through its editorial coverage; listings of restaurants, accommodations, spas, outside activities, and cultural events; and expansive winery guide. Napa Sonoma magazine guides readers off the beaten path, with insightful stories about what makes the region such a spectacular destination. For more information visit [napasonomamag.com](http://bit.ly/1IK9owN).

About Diablo Publications

For 35 years, Diablo Publications has been creating award-winning publications, including [Diablo magazine](http://www.diablomag.com/), Napa Sonoma magazine, Diablo Weddings, the Diablo City Book Series, Diablo Arts, Concierge East Bay, and the Tri-Valley California Visitors Guide. Covering travel, theater, lifestyle, and home design, Diablo Publications celebrates the people, places, and pleasures of the East Bay and North Bay. Diablo Publications’ custom content division, Diablo Custom Publishing (DCP), provides complete print and online marketing communications and customer publishing services for corporate clients nationwide.
