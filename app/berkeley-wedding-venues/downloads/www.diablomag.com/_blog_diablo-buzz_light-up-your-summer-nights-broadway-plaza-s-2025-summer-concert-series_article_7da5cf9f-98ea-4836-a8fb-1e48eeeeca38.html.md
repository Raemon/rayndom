Join us this summer for [Broadway Plaza’s Summer Concert Series](https://www.broadwayplaza.com/Events?utm_source=broadwayplaza&utm_medium=email&utm_campaign=concerts&utm_content=cmd_diablobuzz7.29&utm_term=), transforming Broadway Plaza Street into Walnut Creek’s premier live music destination. This free, family-friendly event features outstanding tribute bands and a lively, community-focused atmosphere.

* * *

#### Event Schedule

**July 31** – _Swift Nation,_ a high-energy tribute to Taylor Swift’s greatest hits.

**August 7** – _RagDolls,_ the only all-female tribute to Aerosmith.

**August 14** – _3 Queens of Motown,_ celebrating the music of Tina Turner, Diana Ross, and Aretha Franklin.

* * *

Guests are encouraged to arrive early to visit local vendors, grab a bite at one of your favorite Broadway Plaza restaurants, and enjoy beverages sold by the _Walnut Creek Downtown Association._ Music starts at 7 p.m. and runs through 8:30 p.m.

Limited chairs will be available on a first-come, first-served basis, and attendees are welcome to bring their own chairs or blankets for added comfort.

Additionally, Broadway Plaza will be promoting its 2025 Back to School Donation Drive at each concert, encouraging guests to consider making a monetary contribution to the _Walnut Creek Education Foundation,_ which supports over 5,100 TK–12 students by funding programs in art, music, science, libraries, wellness, and college and career readiness—helping every student reach their full potential.

[The Broadway Plaza concert series](https://www.broadwayplaza.com/Events?utm_source=broadwayplaza&utm_medium=email&utm_campaign=concerts&utm_content=cmd_diablobuzz7.29&utm_term=) is proudly sponsored by _Visit Walnut Creek, ParkSmart, Walnut Creek Marriott,_ and _Zebra Tattoo & Body Piercing._

Come celebrate music and community on July 31, August 7, and August 14! Enjoy live music, vibrant energy, and the unmatched charm of downtown Walnut Creek—all at Broadway Plaza.
