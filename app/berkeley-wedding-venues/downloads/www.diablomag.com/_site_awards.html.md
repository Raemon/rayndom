# Diablo Publications Awards History

![Responsive image](https://bloximages.newyork1.vip.townnews.com/diablomag.com/content/tncms/assets/v3/staticpages/1/9f/19f43a7c-c431-11e9-9488-2b41f8f967ef/5d5e992a16024.image-ebb1244e0bf549e5661d914368cc5187.jpg)

### CRMA Award Description

The CRMA (City and Regional Magazine Association) Annual Awards program recognizes outstanding performance by city and regional magazines in general excellence, design, and editorial categories. Diablo magazine has been awarded with the following CRMA awards:

### Diablo Magazine CRMA Awards:

2008 Silver Award, “The Food Issue” Nov-07

2007 Bronze Award, Best Cover Nov-06

2007 Silver Award, Reader Service, "Sushi 101" Nov-06

2004 Silver Award, General Excellence

2004 Gold Award, Special Issue, "Sex and the Suburbs" Feb-04

2003 Silver Award, Overall Design

2002 Bronze Award, General Excellence

2002 Silver Award, Designer of the Year Andrew Barton

2000 Gold Award, Writer of the Year Mark MacNamara

2000 Silver Award, Best Cover Nov-99

2000 Silver Award, Special Interest Publication

![Responsive image](https://bloximages.newyork1.vip.townnews.com/diablomag.com/content/tncms/assets/v3/staticpages/1/9f/19f43a7c-c431-11e9-9488-2b41f8f967ef/5d5e992a16295.image-63945d478e28a4658fede898efdc35dd.jpg)

### Maggie Award Description 

Maggie Awards, presented by the Western Publications Association, honor editorial and design excellence in consumer and trade magazine publishing in the western United States. Diablo Publications is a 22-time Maggie Award-winner, including:

### Diablo Magazine Maggie Awards:

2020 Best Theme Issue/Consumer

2017 Best Special Theme Issue

2016 Best Special Theme Issue

2015 Best Regional and State Magazine

2015 Best Overall Publication

2015 Best Special Theme Issue 

2012 Best Overall Publication Design

2012 Best Article Series

2012 Best Series Article, Section, Column 

2011 Best Special Theme Issue

2011 Best Special Food Issue

2008 Best Special Theme Issue

2007 Best Single Editorial Illustration

2007 Best Special Theme Issue

2007 Best Corporate or In-house

2007 Best Visitor's Guide

### Napa Sonoma magazine

2007 Best Visitors' Guide, Consumer spring/summer 2006

2005 Best Visitors' Guide, Consumer winter/spring 2004

2004 Best Visitors' Guide, Consumer winter/spring 2003

2003 Best Visitors' Guide, Consumer winter/spring 2002

2002 Best Visitors' Guide, Consumer November

2002 Best New Publication Winter/Spring 2001
