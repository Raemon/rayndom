Too often pet adoption focuses on animal rescue. Does that sound odd? It doesn’t to Joybound People & Pets. In their opinion, pleading eyes and a sad song shouldn’t be the reason a person takes a pet home.

Don’t get them wrong. Pets need saving. But people do too. So while evoking compassion may work for some, Joybound prefers to concentrate on the feeling found in the bond between people and their furry friends.

Fittingly, that feeling is joy.

Your dog zooming around the yard, a cat snuggling up when you need it most, those masterful knots tied around a tree with their leash—it’s simple levity in everyday living.

* * *

![Supporting Image \(Yoga\).jpg](data:image/png;base64,iVBORw0KGgoAAAANSUhEUgAAAAQAAAADCAQAAAAe/WZNAAAAEElEQVR42mM8U88ABowYDABAxQPltt5zqAAAAABJRU5ErkJggg==)

#### Bonus Features

However, us humans don’t always do what’s in our best interest. So Joybound is here to remind the East Bay that each of their pets come with additional benefits in hopes that people will choose to benefit themselves.

Consider the dog. A dog is a welcome home ready and waiting AND ALSO there for your next big adventure. Watch as Joybound’s amazing pups ride in the passenger seat as the two of you find what’s out there, together.

Now consider the cat. Here you’ll find countless adorable features AND ALSO the ability to block out the morning rays as you sleep with the world’s fluffiest sleepmask.

Joybound believes these features, or AND ALSOs, are endless. And as you embark on a lifelong friendship, you’ll find yourself continually surprised by the joy of pets with such astonishing features.

* * *

![Supporting Image \(Laundry\).jpg](data:image/png;base64,iVBORw0KGgoAAAANSUhEUgAAAAQAAAADCAQAAAAe/WZNAAAAEElEQVR42mM8U88ABowYDABAxQPltt5zqAAAAABJRU5ErkJggg==)

#### Taking a cue from our pets

It’s clear cats and dogs are more than just pets. AND ALSO it’s clear that Joybound is more than just a pet adoption center.

Just like the four-legged, multi-purpose residents taking a temporary stay on the Joybound Campus, Joyound itself offers a variety of functions to the citizens of Contra Costa County.

In addition to pet adoption, they offer a Shelter-to-Service program that trains emotional support dogs for veterans. Englestad Grooming Academy helps those with barriers to employment gain valuable experience in the petcare industry. Through their FoodShare pantry, Joybound (partnering with Purina and individual donors) provides pet food to qualifying pet guardians in Contra Costa County. And while Joybound People & Pets serves the community, it’s also a place for the community to call their own with events like movie and trivia nights.

The list of Joybound AND ALSOs goes on and on.

And on.

So we’ll stop here. Which provides the perfect break in the action for you to come down to the Joybound campus in Walnut Creek where you’ll discover more than pet adoptions as well as some AND ALSO’s ready to surprise you with joy.
