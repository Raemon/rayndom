#### Literature

** _Give Me a Year_ Book Launch**

**10/10** East Bay entrepreneur Shonda Scott is headed to Oakland’s Uptown Station Community Hub for the launch of her new book, _Give Me A Year: 12 New Things to Embrace Change and Live Your Best Life._ The evening will include a cocktail hour and a dialogue between Scott and Maya Harris (former vice president Kamala Harris’s sister). _[12newthings.com](https://www.12newthings.com/)._

#### Festival

**ArtWalk**

**10/11** There will be something for everyone at the 2025 ArtWalk in Livermore, with over 200 artists on hand displaying textiles, jewelry, and pottery, among works in other genres. ArtWalk will take place between Maple Street and South Livermore Avenue near the Bankhead Theater and Plaza. _[livermorearts.org](https://livermorearts.org/)._

#### Holiday

**Ghost Walk 2025**

**10/11-10/12, 10/18-10/19** Kick off your Halloween celebrations with a two-hour guided walking tour down Pleasanton’s Main Street that highlights the ghostly presences and vibrant stories in the city’s past. Run by Museum on Main, each Ghost Walk offers an unforgettable look at local history. _[museumonmain.org](https://www.museumonmain.org/)._

#### Visual Art

**Fall Family Day**

**10/11** Fall Family Day at the Richmond Art Center will double as an Indigenous Peoples’ Day celebration. Guests will be able to listen to artist Luis Garcia and watch Danza Azteca Teokalli’s performance, in addition to enjoying a variety of interactive art opportunities. _[richmondartcenter.org](https://richmondartcenter.org/)._

#### Music

**Jazz Festival in the Vineyards**

**10/12** Jazz music aficionados are sure to love this concert day at Wente Family Vineyards in Livermore, which will feature performances by Roger Smith, Paul Taylor, Chris Standring, and Ruben Studdard. Attendees can also buy food, wine, cocktails, and beer, or opt for a VIP table with a buffet lunch. _[wentevineyards.com](https://wentevineyards.com/)._
