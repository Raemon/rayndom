#### Theater

 _**The Soiled Dove**_

**Through 11/1** The company of _The Soiled Dove,_ a circus-inspired dinner-theater show from Vau de Vire Society and Work of Art Catering, now features music icon Joan Baez. Set in the 1890s, the production is being staged at Big Top at Radium Runway in Alameda. _[thesoileddove.com](http://thesoileddove.com)._

#### Family

** _Disney on Ice Presents_ Frozen _and_ Encanto**

**10/16-10/19** Beloved characters Anna, Elsa, Mirabel, Luisa, and Isabela bring their stories to life at seven _Disney on Ice_ shows at the Oakland Arena. Mickey and Minnie Mouse emcee the ice-skating performances, which are sure to be a welcome trip down memory lane for Disney fans. _[theoaklandarena.com](http://theoaklandarena.com)._

#### Food and Drink

**Oktoberfest and SmokeFest**

**10/17-10/18, 10/19** Pleasanton’s SideTrack Bar and Grill is hosting a pair of events that will be full of food and fun this weekend. Visitors can enjoy two days of German-themed games, decor, entertainment, drinks, and eats during the restaurant’s Oktoberfest festivities before returning on Sunday for SmokeFest’s barbecue extravaganza. _[sidetrackeats.com](http://sidetrackeats.com)._

#### Music

**Djo: Another Bite Tour**

**10/18** Fans may recognize Joe Keery from his role on _Stranger Things_ —but this week, he stops by the Greek Theatre at UC Berkeley as musician Djo. His latest album, _The Crux,_ dropped earlier this year. Post Animal will open the concert. _[thegreekberkeley.com](http://thegreekberkeley.com)._

#### Literature

** _Coming Up Short:_ A Conversation with Robert Reich and Michael Lewis**

**10/21** Don’t miss your chance to see two local literary leaders—former secretary of labor Robert Reich, author of _Coming Up Short,_ and journalist Michael Lewis of _Who Is Government?_ —in conversation. Held as part of the Democracy Book Club series at the Goldman School of Public Policy, the dialogue will be hosted at the Freight in Berkeley. _[gspp.berkeley.edu](http://gspp.berkeley.edu)._
