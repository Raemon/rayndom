# Diablo Women Comedy Night 2021




[ ![Comedy Night](data:image/png;base64,iVBORw0KGgoAAAANSUhEUgAAAAQAAAADCAQAAAAe/WZNAAAAEElEQVR42mM8U88ABowYDABAxQPltt5zqAAAAABJRU5ErkJggg==) ](/comedy-night/image_2d2e9eec-be40-11eb-88f3-5376b06f6cb1.html)

Each year,  _Diablo_ magazine celebrates women and their contributions to the community by dedicating editorial coverage to topics important to women. The Diablo Women event gives Diablo Women advertisers, the female community, and business leaders the opportunity to learn something new and to be inspired. On May 13, 2021, _Diablo_ hosted a Comedy Night, featuring Laurie Kilmartin, Amy Miller, and Zahra Noorbakhsh.

## **Comedy Night Cocktail Kickoff:**

Presenting Sponsor Contra Costa Oncology and  _Diablo_ magazine's President and Publisher Barney Fonzi hosted the Comedy Night Cocktail Kickoff. Grab the ingredients to make a Pink Warrior cocktail and follow along in the recording above to shake things up. 

**Ingredients** : 

\- Your choice of spirit

\- St. Germain Elderflower Liqueur

\- Strawberries

\- Mint

\- Topped with club soda and a lime + strawberry wedge
