We're showing one of the great Hollywood thrillers as this month's free classic movie: Alfred Hitchcock's _North By Northwest._

Released in 1959, _North By Northwest_ features so many touchstones of Hollywood's Golden Age—huge stars (Cary Grant, Eva Marie Saint, James Mason), lavish locations (the climax on Mount Rushmore is breathtaking), and one of the great directors at the top of his game. Seeing this on the big screen will make a huge difference.

_North By Northwest_ screens on Thursday, November 9 at 7 p.m. in the historic [Orinda Theatre.](http://lamorindatheatres.com) Seating is first come, first serve—make sure to arrive early to secure parking and seating. As always, we will give away cupcakes from [Republic of Cake](http://republicofcake.com) as trivia prizes before the film,

See you at the movies!
