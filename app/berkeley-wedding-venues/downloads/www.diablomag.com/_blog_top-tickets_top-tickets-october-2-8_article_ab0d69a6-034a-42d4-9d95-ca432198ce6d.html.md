#### Opera

 _**La Traviata**_

**Through 10/5** Catch the final two performances of the Livermore Valley Opera’s _La Traviata_ on stage at the Bankhead Theater. The story, written by Giuseppe Verdi, follows a headstrong courtesan in upper-class French society who falls in love with a middle-class man. _[livermorevalleyopera.com](https://livermorevalleyopera.com/), [livermorearts.org](https://livermorearts.org/)._

Julien Benhamou

#### Dance

**Paris Opera Ballet:_Red Carpet_**

**10/2-10/4** Stop by Cal Performances’ Zellerbach Hall to catch one of just two U.S. stops of the Paris Opera Ballet’s _Red Carpet._ Featuring a live soundtrack, the Berkeley performances will serve as the piece's the North American premiere. _[calperformances.org](https://calperformances.org/)._

#### Theater

**DMT Presents:_Murder for Two_**

**10/3-10/5** Fans of Agatha Christie and the _Knives Out_ franchise will likely love _Murder for Two,_ performed in conjunction with Hillbarn Theatre and Conservatory at Hayward’s Douglas Morrisson Theatre. The show features two actors playing an ensemble of parts. _[haywardrec.org](https://haywardrec.org/)._

![Louis-Bryant-III_09142025_R3-BSO-SS1_1019.gif](data:image/png;base64,iVBORw0KGgoAAAANSUhEUgAAAAQAAAADCAQAAAAe/WZNAAAAEElEQVR42mM8U88ABowYDABAxQPltt5zqAAAAABJRU5ErkJggg==)

Louis Bryant III

#### Music

 _**Refracted Light**_

**10/5** Join the Berkeley Symphony for _Refracted Light,_ with the San Francisco Conservatory of Music’s Edwin Outwater as guest conductor. The concert, held at the First Congregational Church of Berkeley, is expected to feature compositions by Samuel Adams, Yaz Lancaster, and Franz Joseph Haydn. _[berkeleysymphony.org](https://www.berkeleysymphony.org/)._

#### Music

 _**The World of Hans Zimmer: A New Dimension**_

**10/7** Composer Hans Zimmer’s name is associated with countless beloved film soundtracks, from  _Pirates of the Caribbean_ to James Bond movie  _No Time to Die_ to  _Dune._ Fans can experience a collection of music curated by Zimmer himself on Sunday at the Oakland Arena. _[theoaklandarena.com](https://www.theoaklandarena.com/)._
