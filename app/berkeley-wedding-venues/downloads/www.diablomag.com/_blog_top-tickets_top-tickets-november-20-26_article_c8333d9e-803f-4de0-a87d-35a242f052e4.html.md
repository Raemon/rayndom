#### Music

 _**Over the River and Into the Woods**_

**11/21, 11/23** The Diablo Women’s Chorale, led by director Erin Fishler and accompanied by a selection of expert musicians, are exploring love and nature’s beauty in their autumnal program. The concerts will take place at the Lafayette-Orinda Presbyterian Church. _[diablowomenschorale.org](https://www.diablowomenschorale.org/)._

#### Festival

**Patchwork Show Makers Market and Community Festival**

**11/22** The Patchwork Show highlights local makers at each of their festival locales. Stop by Jack London Square to support East Bay artisans and engage in hands-on activities—and settle in for a meal at one of the neighborhood’s eateries near the waterfront. _[patchworkshow.com](https://www.patchworkshow.com/)._

Courtesy of the Manual Cinema

#### Theater

**Manual Cinema:_The 4th Witch_**

**11/22** This one-of-a-kind performance of _The 4th Witch,_ rooted in _Macbeth_ by William Shakespeare, blends silent actors, sound design and music, and shadow puppetry. The tale centers on an orphan girl who encounters the three witches from the original play. Catch it at Cal Performances’ Zellerbach Hall. _[calperformances.org](https://calperformances.org/)._

#### Comedy

**John Mulaney: Mister Whatever**

**11/23** John Mulaney is headed to Paramount Theatre in Oakland while on his latest stand-up comedy tour. The host of the _Everybody’s Live with John Mulaney_ talk show, the comedian has received Writers Guild of America, Critics Choice, and Emmy awards. _[paramountoakland.org](https://www.paramountoakland.org/)._

#### Film

** _The Princess Bride:_ An Inconceivable Evening with Cary Elwes**

**11/28** Between the seemingly star-crossed lovers, the giant and prince, and the rodents of unusual size, _The Princess Bride_ has delighted movie fans for nearly four decades. Westley himself, Cary Elwes, offers a look back at the film and behind-the-scenes insights into its production at Livermore’s Bankhead Theater. _[livermorearts.org](https://livermorearts.org/)._
