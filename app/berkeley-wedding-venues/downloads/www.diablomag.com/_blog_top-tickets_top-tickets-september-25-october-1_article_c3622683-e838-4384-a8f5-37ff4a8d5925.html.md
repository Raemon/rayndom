#### Holiday

**Pirates of Emerson Haunted Theme Park**

**9/26-11/2** Pirates of Emerson is back for its 35th year at Pleasanton’s Alameda County Fairgrounds, offering Halloween fun, festivity, and most of all, scares. The attraction includes four haunted houses, as well as games, live music, and food. _[piratesofemerson.com](http://www.piratesofemerson.com/)._

#### Food and Drink

**Walnut Creek Oktoberfest**

**9/26-9/27** Don your dirndl dresses and lederhosen, and head to Civic Park in Walnut Creek for Oktoberfest. The annual family-friendly event will include a biergarten, vendor booths, dancing, music, and more. _[walnutcreekdowntown.com](https://walnutcreekdowntown.com/)._

#### Music

 _**Pictures from Paris**_

**9/27-9/28** Take a trip around the world with the California Symphony, which is bringing its latest concert lineup to the Lesher Center for the Arts in Walnut Creek. The performances will feature compositions by Maurice Ravel, George Gershwin, and Modest Mussorgsky. _[californiasymphony.org](https://www.californiasymphony.org/), [lesherartscenter.org](https://www.lesherartscenter.org/)._

#### Fundraiser

**Autumn Celebration 2025**

**9/27** San Leandro’s George Mark Children’s House is holding its annual fundraiser, featuring eye-catching auction lots, multiple musical performances, and more, with KTVU’s Betty Yu as the evening’s host. Funds raised will support the pediatric palliative care efforts at George Mark. _[georgemark.org](https://georgemark.org/)._

#### Music

**Haley Reinhart**

**9/28** Singer and YouTube star Haley Reinhart is visiting Oakland’s Yoshi’s. Known for “Shook,” “Oh My!,” and a popular “Can’t Help Falling in Love” cover, Reinhart is a favorite for her work with Postmodern Jukebox and on _American Idol.[yoshis.com](https://yoshis.com/)._
