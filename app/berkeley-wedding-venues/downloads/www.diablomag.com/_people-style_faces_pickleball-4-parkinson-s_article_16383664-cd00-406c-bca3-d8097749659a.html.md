The 2025 Pickleball 4 Parkinson’s tournament at Rossmoor Walnut Creek in June brought in $112,000 for the Michael J. Fox Foundation (MJFF)’s efforts in developing a cure for Parkinson’s disease. Over two days of gameplay, the event saw more than 600 attendees. Pickleball 4 Parkinson’s, founded in 2023 by Rossmoor’s Nancy Munson, has donated $250,000 in the last three years to MJFF.
