March 12, 2015

**Diablo magazine invites readers and community to attend event**

Join _Diablo_ magazine and Dr. Michelle J. Place on Thursday, March 26 from 5:30-7:30 as she celebrates a year in her new San Ramon location and unveils the latest technology for laser liposuction and other aesthetics.

"We are thrilled to be celebrating our anniversary in San Ramon with our clients and _Diablo_ magazine readers, and we’re really looking forward to a fun and informative event," said Dr. Place.

The event will be held at Dr. Place’s facility, located at 100 Park Place, Suite 240 in San Ramon. It will include wine, hors d’oeuvres and raffle prizes. Guests will also have the opportunity to watch live demonstrations of CoolSculpting, miraDry, and Juvashape, and Dr. Place will offer special event pricing on CoolSculpting, Juvederm, Botox, and Voluma.

Raffle prizes include CoolSculpting, JuVaShape, Juvederm, and miraDry treatments. Guests who bring a friend receive a bonus entry in the raffle.

All are welcome to RSVP by visiting [diablomag.com/](http://www.diablomag.com/DrPlace)[DrPlace](http://www.diablomag.com/drplace). The first 75 responses will receive a swag bag at the door.

About Diablo Publications

For 35 years, Diablo Publications has been creating award-winning publications, including Diablo magazine, Napa Sonoma magazine, Diablo Weddings, the Diablo City Book Series, Diablo Arts, Concierge East Bay and the Tri-Valley California Visitors Guide. Covering travel, theater, lifestyle, and home design, Diablo Publications celebrates the people, places, and pleasures of the East Bay and North Bay. Diablo Publications’ custom content division, Diablo Custom Publishing (DCP), provides complete print and online marketing communications and customer publishing services for corporate clients nationwide. For more information, visit [diablomag.com](http://diablomag.com) or [dcpubs.com](http://dcpubs.com).

About Michelle J. Place, M.D.

Dr. Place specializes in Aesthetic or Cosmetic Surgery of the face, breast, and body. She has developed her practice over the last 18 years to provide a complete patient care environment with experienced and friendly staff. Dr. Place and the Aesthetic Skin Care Center provide a complete service starting with conditioning treatments for the skin, laser and intense pulsed light (IPL) treatments, skin care products, (Obagi Skin Program) and extending to Botox, Juvederm, Restylane and Sculptra. Surgical procedures include facial rejuvenation; facelifts, eyelid lifts, brow lifts; body contouring; liposuction, tummy tucks, arm lifts, thigh lifts; and breast surgery; augmentation, reduction, and lifts.
