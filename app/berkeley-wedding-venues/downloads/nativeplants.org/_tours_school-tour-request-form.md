## School Tour Request Form

Our trained docents lead tours through the Garden for school classes from preschools to 12th grade. The request must be made at least 4 weeks in advance. There is a maximum size of 30 students for school tours. You can choose one of our theme tours or tell us what you would like.

Group size is limited to 30 max and a required ratio of 1 adult chaperone for every 5 children. Transportation is not provided with this program. 

You will hear back from the Garden Office within 5 business days concerning your tour request.
