# Bulbs from Dry Summer Climates

  * Saturday, December 14, 2024
  * 10:30 AM 12:00 PM


  * Regional Parks Botanic Garden 1550 Wildcat Canyon Road Berkeley, CA, 94708 United States [(map)](http://maps.google.com?q=1550 Wildcat Canyon Road Berkeley, CA, 94708 United States)


  * [Google Calendar](http://www.google.com/calendar/event?action=TEMPLATE&text=Bulbs%20from%20Dry%20Summer%20Climates&dates=20241214T183000Z/20241214T200000Z&location=1550%20Wildcat%20Canyon%20Road%2C%20Berkeley%2C%20CA%2C%2094708%2C%20United%20States) [ICS](/events-and-classes/events/wr-ben-anderson?format=ical)



Ben Anderson, Botanic Garden gardener, will present a diverse assemblage of geophytes from the world's Mediterranean regions. We will look at bulb and bulb-like plants from families like Iridaceae, Amaryllidaceae, Alliaceae, Liliaceae, Asparagaceae, and beyond. The Bay Area is a great place to try growing many of these fun, often ephemeral charmers.
