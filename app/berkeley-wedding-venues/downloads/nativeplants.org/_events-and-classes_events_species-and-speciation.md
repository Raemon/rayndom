# Wayne Roderick Lecture: The Path to Speciation

  * Saturday, January 24, 2026
  * 10:30 AM 12:00 PM


  * Regional Parks Botanic Garden 1550 Wildcat Canyon Road Berkeley, CA, 94708 United States [(map)](http://maps.google.com?q=1550 Wildcat Canyon Road Berkeley, CA, 94708 United States)


  * [Google Calendar](http://www.google.com/calendar/event?action=TEMPLATE&text=Wayne%20Roderick%20Lecture%3A%20The%20Path%20to%20Speciation&dates=20260124T183000Z/20260124T200000Z&location=1550%20Wildcat%20Canyon%20Road%2C%20Berkeley%2C%20CA%2C%2094708%2C%20United%20States) [ICS](/events-and-classes/events/species-and-speciation?format=ical)



Ben Anderson, RPBG Gardener, will discuss the array of mechanisms by which plant lineages diverge from one another, and how that ties back into botanical diversity.
