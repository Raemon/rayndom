## For Kids

![](https://images.squarespace-cdn.com/content/v1/666115df3fa3b24521c127ed/1198490d-7eda-4ac8-a44d-5c888be718e4/ispy.png)

The Botanic Garden is a great place for kids. Here are some activities:

  * I Spy games - find plants, animals, and objects in the Garden.

    * [First ISpy](/s/First-ISpy.pdf)

    * [Second ISpy](/s/Second-ISpy.pdf)

  * Treasure hunt - follow a [map](/s/Treasure-Hunt.pdf) to find and learn about plants and objects in the Garden.




Click the links for games to print out and bring to the Garden. You can also pick up games at the Garden Greeter table on weekends and holidays.

The Garden has family-friendly tours every quarter. [Sign up for the newsletter](/publications/newsletter) to get notified when the tours take place.

![](https://images.squarespace-cdn.com/content/v1/666115df3fa3b24521c127ed/4fab6be4-4a6c-41ab-8016-0107b1b67cac/banana-slug-close.jpg)

The Garden is not just home to plants. Find out about the many different types of [animals](/guides/animal-guides) that live in or visit the Garden.
