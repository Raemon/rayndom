# August Butterfly Walks

  * Saturday, August 23, 2025
  * 1:00 PM 2:15 PM


  * Regional Parks Botanic Garden 1550 Wildcat Canyon Road Berkeley, CA, 94708 United States [(map)](http://maps.google.com?q=1550 Wildcat Canyon Road Berkeley, CA, 94708 United States)


  * [Google Calendar](http://www.google.com/calendar/event?action=TEMPLATE&text=August%20Butterfly%20Walks&dates=20250823T200000Z/20250823T211500Z&location=1550%20Wildcat%20Canyon%20Road%2C%20Berkeley%2C%20CA%2C%2094708%2C%20United%20States) [ICS](/events-and-classes/events/august-butterfly-walks?format=ical)



August butterfly walks with Sarab Seth, the butterfly guy! These are free, guided walks around the Regional Parks Botanic Garden. [Sign up](https://www.signupgenius.com/go/30E0549AFA72CABFE3-57205934-butterfly) for a walk.  
  
We normally offer a pair of weekend walks each month. Only for July and August (peak butterfly spotting season), we are adding a pair of Wednesday walks, as well. The August schedule is:

Wed, Aug 20, 2025: 1-2 PM and 2:15-3:15 PM  
Sat, Aug 23, 2025: 1-2 PM and 2:15-3:15 PM
