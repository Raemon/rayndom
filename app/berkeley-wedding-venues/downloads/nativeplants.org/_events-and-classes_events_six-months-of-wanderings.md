# Six Months of Wanderings in CA & Beyond

  * Saturday, January 18, 2025
  * 10:30 AM 12:00 PM


  * Regional Parks Botanic Garden 1550 Wildcat Canyon Road Berkeley, CA, 94708 United States [(map)](http://maps.google.com?q=1550 Wildcat Canyon Road Berkeley, CA, 94708 United States)


  * [Google Calendar](http://www.google.com/calendar/event?action=TEMPLATE&text=Six%20Months%20of%20Wanderings%20in%20CA%20%26amp%3B%20Beyond&dates=20250118T183000Z/20250118T200000Z&location=1550%20Wildcat%20Canyon%20Road%2C%20Berkeley%2C%20CA%2C%2094708%2C%20United%20States) [ICS](/events-and-classes/events/six-months-of-wanderings?format=ical)



In 2024 Liz Bittner took a six month leave and traveled to Turkey, Cyprus, and Japan, and around California. She'll share some photos of amazing plants from her travels.

Liz Bittner has been working at the Regional Parks Botanic Garden for 16 years, first as a Gardener, and now as the Garden Supervisor.
