### Native Plant Information

#### [California Native Plant Society](http://www.cnps.org/)

Organization dedicated to protecting California's native plant heritage and preserving it for future generations. Local chapters include:

  * [East Bay CNPS](http://ebcnps.org/)

  * [Marin CNPS](http://www.cnpsmarin.org/)

  * [San Francisco CNPS](http://www.cnps-yerbabuena.org/)

  * [Sonoma County CNPS](http://milobaker.cnps.org/)

  * [Napa Valley CNPS](http://www.napavalleycnps.org/)

  * [Santa Clara Valley CNPS](http://www.cnps-scv.org/)

  * [Santa Cruz CNPS](http://www.cruzcnps.org/)

  * [Solano County CNPS](http://jepson.cnps.org/)




#### Websites about California Native Plants

  * [East Bay Regional Parks District](https://www.ebparks.org/natural-resources/biodiversity/plants-guide) District-wide wild plant photo guides.

  * [CalPhotos](https://calphotos.berkeley.edu/) Photos of California native plants.

  * [Calflora](http://www.calflora.org/) Information on wild California plants for conservation, education, and appreciation.

  * [Calscape](https://calscape.org/) California's hub for California native plants and native plant gardening, created by the California Native Plant Society.

  * [U.C. Jepson Manual](http://ucjeps.berkeley.edu/IJM.html) Taxonomic treatments and identification keys for native and naturalized plants in California.

  * [USDA Plants Database](https://plants.sc.egov.usda.gov/home) Provides standardized information about the vascular plants, mosses, liverworts, hornworts, and lichens of the U.S. and its territories.

  * [Fire Effects Information System (FEIS)](https://www.feis-crs.org/feis/) Online collection of reviews of the scientific literature about fire effects on plants and animals.




#### Finding Native Plants

  * [California Plant Link Exchange](http://www.cnplx.info/) Helps you select local native plants for any location in the state, and find out who sells them.

  * [SelecTree](https://selectree.calpoly.edu/) Helps you select California native trees.




#### Books on Gardening with California Native Plants

  * [California Native Plants for the Garden](https://www.nativeson.com/product/california-native-plants-garden-book), Bornstein, Fross, O'Brien

  * [California Native Gardening: A Month-by-Month Guide](https://www.ucpress.edu/books/california-native-gardening/paper), Popper

  * [Designing California Native Gardens](https://www.ucpress.edu/book/9780520251106/designing-california-native-gardens), Keator, Middlebrook

  * [Growing California Native Plants, Second Edition](https://www.ucpress.edu/book/9780520266698/growing-california-native-plants-second-edition), Schmidt, Greenberg

  * [Native Treasures](https://larnerseeds.com/products/native-treasures), Smith




### Gardening with Native Plants

#### Local Nurseries that Sell California Native Plants and Offer Discounts to Friends Members

  * [California Flora Nursery](http://www.calfloranursery.com/) Retail native plants.

  * [Central Coast Wilds](http://www.centralcoastwilds.com/) Native plant nursery and landscape contractor.

  * [East Bay Nursery](http://www.eastbaynursery.com/) Retail includes natives.

  * [East Bay Wilds Native Plants Nursery](http://www.eastbaywilds.com/) Retail natives.

  * [Larner Seeds](http://www.larnerseeds.com/) Native seeds, plants, books and landscaping.

  * [Mostly Natives Nursery](http://www.mostlynatives.com) Retail nursery with native and drought tolerant plants.

  * [Native Revival Nursery](http://www.nativerevival.com) Retail and wholesale native plants.

  * [Oaktown Native Plant Nursery](https://oaktownnursery.com/) Tree, shrub, perennial, and grass species of the San Francisco Bay Area.

  * [The Watershed Nursery](https://www.watershednursery.com/) Retail, wholesale, and contract-grown Bay Area native plants with a focus on site-specific gene stock; wild land restoration and habitat enhancement services.
