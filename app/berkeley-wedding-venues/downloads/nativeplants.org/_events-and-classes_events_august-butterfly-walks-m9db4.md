# September Butterfly Walks

  * Sunday, September 28, 2025
  * 1:00 PM 3:15 PM


  * Regional Parks Botanic Garden 1550 Wildcat Canyon Road Berkeley, CA, 94708 United States [(map)](http://maps.google.com?q=1550 Wildcat Canyon Road Berkeley, CA, 94708 United States)


  * [Google Calendar](http://www.google.com/calendar/event?action=TEMPLATE&text=September%20Butterfly%20Walks&dates=20250928T200000Z/20250928T221500Z&location=1550%20Wildcat%20Canyon%20Road%2C%20Berkeley%2C%20CA%2C%2094708%2C%20United%20States) [ICS](/events-and-classes/events/august-butterfly-walks-m9db4?format=ical)



Join Sarab Seth, the butterfly guy, for free, guided walks around the Regional Parks Botanic Garden looking for butterflies. The walks are 1:00-2:00 PM and 2:15-3:15 PM. Sign up for the walks [HERE!](https://www.signupgenius.com/go/30E0549AFA72CABFE3-58355470-butterfly)
