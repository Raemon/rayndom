# Pollination of California Native Plants

  * Sunday, May 4, 2025
  * 2:00 PM 3:00 PM


  * Regional Parks Botanic Garden 1550 Wildcat Canyon Road Berkeley, CA, 94708 United States [(map)](http://maps.google.com?q=1550 Wildcat Canyon Road Berkeley, CA, 94708 United States)


  * [Google Calendar](http://www.google.com/calendar/event?action=TEMPLATE&text=Pollination%20of%20California%20Native%20Plants&dates=20250504T210000Z/20250504T220000Z&location=1550%20Wildcat%20Canyon%20Road%2C%20Berkeley%2C%20CA%2C%2094708%2C%20United%20States) [ICS](/events-and-classes/events/s8y0s1huyxcbxf2tscj5bz0awtjukl?format=ical)



Join us for a special free, family-friendly tour. On this tour you will dissect a California poppy, watch pollinators at work, and learn how seeds disperse. Our Garden docents will lead this tour, which is suitable for all ages.
