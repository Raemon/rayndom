**Echo Azure _(Celastrina echo)_** is a small butterfly, with a wingspan of 0.75 - 1.25 inches. It can be seen in our local area from March to mid-July. Numbers peak in June, but are also high in March. Host plants include Ceanothus, California Buckeye (_Aesculus californica_), Dogwood (_Cornus_), and Blackberries (_Rubus_).

It is found in a wide belt along the west coast from British Columbia to Baja California.
