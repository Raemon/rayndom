## Manzanita Quarterly

[Friends of the Regional Parks Botanic Garden](/support/become-a-friend) receive the quarterly publication _Manzanita_ as a benefit of [membership](/support/become-a-friend). Regular features include articles about the Botanic Garden and aspects of California plant life written by botanists, horticulturists, and other prominent native plant experts. 

_Manzanita_ has been published since 1997. Issues from 2014 onward are available as free downloads (see list below), except for the four most recent issues, which are only available to current members of the [Friends](/support/friends-of-the-garden) organization. To review specific back issues at the Botanic Garden, you can make an appointment by contacting [BGarden@ebparks.org](mailto:BGarden@ebparks.org). Many back issues of _Manzanita_ are available for purchase. Contact [BGarden@ebparks.org](mailto:BGarden@ebparks.org) for more information.
