## Wayne Roderick Lectures

Join us November through March for our popular Wayne Roderick Lectures, a series of public lectures on a broad array of topics related to plants and natural history. These presentations are enjoyable for beginners and professionals alike. 

Named in honor of its founder, a former director of the Garden, the Wayne Roderick Lectures take place on Sat 10:30 AM - 12:00 PM and begin at the Garden’s Visitor Center auditorium. 

All lectures are free and open to the public. For scheduled lectures, go to the [Events](/events-and-classes/events) page.
