## Birds in the Garden

### Bird Walks

The [Golden Gate Bird Alliance](https://goldengatebirdalliance.org/) occasionally conducts bird walks in the Garden. To search and register for walks, go to [GGBA Field Trips](https://goldengatebirdalliance.app.neoncrm.com/np/clients/goldengatebirdalliance/eventList.jsp?anotherEvent=&categoryIds=22).

### Bird Survey

Botanic Garden [docents](https://teal-mouse-b3hd.squarespace.com/volunteer/become-a-docent) have [surveyed birds in the Garden](https://baynature.org/article/birding-the-botanic-garden/). Their observations are summarized in the [Botanic Garden Checklist](/s/Bird-Checklist-2021.pdf) of 90 bird species seen in the Garden, which includes nesting species, monthly sighting frequency, and habitat prevalence.

### Gardening for Birds

  * [Excel spreadsheet of birds’ plant use in the Garden](/s/PlantUse.xlsx), download and sort the data by relevant terms, which can help you plan your own bird-friendly garden or plot an occurrence graph of migrating birds.

  * [Native Plants That Attract Birds to Your Garden](/s/attractbirds.pdf), a guide compiled by the late Es Anderson, longtime Botanic Garden volunteer.

  * [Beneficial Bird Native Plant Chart](/s/Beneficial-Bird-Native-Plant-Chart.pdf), a guide developed by the [Wild Farm Alliance](https://www.wildfarmalliance.org/) _._ Identifies plants that provide floral resources and other plant food for birds to consume throughout the year.
