## Propagate Plants

![](https://images.squarespace-cdn.com/content/v1/666115df3fa3b24521c127ed/c7f21b4e-94e9-4564-832e-ddd2c094ed28/plantswagon.jpg)

Sale plants ready 

Plant sale volunteers propagate and maintain plants offered for sale to the public at the Botanic Garden's [plant sales](/visit/plant-sales). This friendly group of volunteers meets at the Garden in the Potting Shed area of the Juniper Lodge building year-round.

Many of the volunteers are experienced horticulturists but experience isn't necessary; volunteering to propagate for the plant sale is an excellent way to learn about native plants.

Please contact Garden Staff to inquire about volunteer openings at [bgarden@ebparks.org ](mailto:bgarden@ebparks.org)or call the office at (510) 544-3169.
