## Weekend & Holiday Tours

![](https://images.squarespace-cdn.com/content/v1/666115df3fa3b24521c127ed/54dd9269-6456-4eae-8dc6-32627b436d69/tour3.jpg)

Tour group in Botanic Garden  
Photo by Carla Koop

Join our knowledgeable docents for free guided tours of the Garden on 

  * Sat - 2:00 PM

  * Sun - 11:00 AM and 2:00 PM

  * Holidays - 2:00 PM (Martin Luther King, Jr. Day, Lincoln’s Birthday, Washington’s Birthday, César Chávez’s Birthday (Mar 31), Memorial Day, Independence Day, Labor Day, Admission Day (Sep 9), and Veterans Day)




The Garden is closed New Years Day, Thanksgiving Day, and Christmas Day. 

Meet at the Visitor Center. Tours usually last about an hour and are appropriate for both adults and children. You can leave a tour at any time. No reservations are needed. Tours are limited to a maximum of 10 people.
