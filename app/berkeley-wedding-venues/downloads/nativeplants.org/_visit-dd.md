## Hours & Admission

![](https://images.squarespace-cdn.com/content/v1/666115df3fa3b24521c127ed/1717694442660-IHILU4FN8XT4PXGODHD7/1-007BG.jpg)

Bench beneath main bridge over Wildcat Creek

From October 1 to May 31, the Garden is open for visitors every day from 8:30 AM - 5:00 PM. From June 1 to September 30 the Garden is open from 8:30 AM - 5:30 PM.

Both the Anza View Road and Wildcat Canyon Road gates will be open.

Admission to the Garden is always free!

The Garden is closed New Year's Day, Thanksgiving Day, and Christmas Day.
