## Guidelines for Visitors

![](https://images.squarespace-cdn.com/content/v1/666115df3fa3b24521c127ed/d047f850-3f3f-4262-a023-9624e5f2b4e5/silk+moth.jpg)

A silk moth emerges from its chrysalis at the garden   
Photo Rosie Andrews

The Regional Parks Botanic Garden is a beautiful living museum. Please respect its mission by:

  * Walking on paths or lawns, not in plant beds.

  * Staying off rocks.

  * Providing adult supervision for children at all times.




The following activities are prohibited: 

  * Removing plant parts or specimens.

  * Removing gravel, rocks, or soil.

  * Bringing dogs (except service dogs) into the garden.

  * Riding bicycles. Do park and lock your bike at the bike stand next to the parking lot.

  * Climbing trees or shrubs.

  * Throwing frisbees, balls, etc.

  * Playing loud music.

  * Running.
