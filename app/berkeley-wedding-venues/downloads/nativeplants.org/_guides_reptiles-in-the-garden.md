## Reptiles in the Garden

This page has information about lizards and other reptiles that visit the Garden.

  * [EBRPD reptile videos](https://www.ebparks.org/programs/digital-learning/naturalist-videos/plants-animals/reptiles)

  * [Common Snakes in the EBRPD](https://www.ebparks.org/sites/default/files/common-snakes-brochure-20180927.pdf)




The western fence lizard (_Sceloporus occidentalis_) can be seen sunning, or doing push-ups, on the rocks on warm, sunny days in the [Southern California](/southern-california) and [Sierran](/sierran) sections of the Garden.
