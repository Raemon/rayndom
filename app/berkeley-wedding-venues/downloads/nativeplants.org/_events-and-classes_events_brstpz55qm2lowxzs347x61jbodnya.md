# Botanizing California

  * Sunday, December 1, 2024
  * 10:00 AM 12:00 PM


  * Regional Parks Botanic Garden 1550 Wildcat Canyon Road Berkeley, CA, 94708 United States [(map)](http://maps.google.com?q=1550 Wildcat Canyon Road Berkeley, CA, 94708 United States)


  * [Google Calendar](http://www.google.com/calendar/event?action=TEMPLATE&text=Botanizing%20California&dates=20241201T180000Z/20241201T200000Z&location=1550%20Wildcat%20Canyon%20Road%2C%20Berkeley%2C%20CA%2C%2094708%2C%20United%20States) [ICS](/events-and-classes/events/brstpz55qm2lowxzs347x61jbodnya?format=ical)



Join Glenn Keator for a free talk on areas in California for observing Spring wildflowers. This will be an introduction to a series of fee-based talks Dr. Keator will present in Spring 2025. 

Dr. Keator has authored numerous books on California native plants.
