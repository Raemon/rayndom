## Assist the Gardeners

![](https://images.squarespace-cdn.com/content/v1/666115df3fa3b24521c127ed/37b54b19-0abe-4429-acd7-af78128603a8/plantingbulbs.jpg)

Planting bulb bed   
Photo by Stephen W Edwards

If you can donate your time on a regular basis (once a week, twice a month, etc.) and have some gardening experience, you can volunteer to assist the Gardeners doing light tasks such as planting, weeding, raking, or nursery work. 

If you are interested, call 510-544-3169 or email [bgarden@ebparks.org](mailto:bgarden@ebparks.org).
