# Lichen Walk

  * Saturday, February 15, 2025
  * 1:00 PM 3:00 PM


  * Regional Parks Botanic Garden 1550 Wildcat Canyon Road Berkeley, CA, 94708 United States [(map)](http://maps.google.com?q=1550 Wildcat Canyon Road Berkeley, CA, 94708 United States)


  * [Google Calendar](http://www.google.com/calendar/event?action=TEMPLATE&text=Lichen%20Walk&dates=20250215T210000Z/20250215T230000Z&location=1550%20Wildcat%20Canyon%20Road%2C%20Berkeley%2C%20CA%2C%2094708%2C%20United%20States) [ICS](/events-and-classes/events/lichen-walk?format=ical)



Join Curator of Lichenology, Dr. Klara Scharnagl, as she leads a walk through the Tilden Regional Parks Botanic Garden. She will point out where lichens can be found, and introduce visitors to the incredible forms and diversity of lichens in the Garden.
