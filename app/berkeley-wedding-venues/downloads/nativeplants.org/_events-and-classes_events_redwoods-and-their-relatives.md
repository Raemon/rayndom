# Redwoods and Their Relatives

  * Saturday, December 7, 2024
  * 10:30 AM 12:00 PM


  * Regional Parks Botanic Garden 1550 Wildcat Canyon Road Berkeley, CA, 94708 United States [(map)](http://maps.google.com?q=1550 Wildcat Canyon Road Berkeley, CA, 94708 United States)


  * [Google Calendar](http://www.google.com/calendar/event?action=TEMPLATE&text=Redwoods%20and%20Their%20Relatives&dates=20241207T183000Z/20241207T200000Z&location=1550%20Wildcat%20Canyon%20Road%2C%20Berkeley%2C%20CA%2C%2094708%2C%20United%20States) [ICS](/events-and-classes/events/redwoods-and-their-relatives?format=ical)



Coast redwoods and giant sequoias are both members of the cypress (_Cupressaceae)_ family. Glenn Keator will discuss the “family tree” of these famous giants, which is far more extended than just these two species.
