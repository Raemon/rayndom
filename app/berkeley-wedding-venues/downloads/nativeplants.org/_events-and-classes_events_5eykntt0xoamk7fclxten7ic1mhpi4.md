# Butterfly Walk

  * Saturday, June 21, 2025
  * 1:00 PM 2:15 PM


  * Regional Parks Botanic Garden 1550 Wildcat Canyon Road Berkeley, CA, 94708 United States [(map)](http://maps.google.com?q=1550 Wildcat Canyon Road Berkeley, CA, 94708 United States)


  * [Google Calendar](http://www.google.com/calendar/event?action=TEMPLATE&text=Butterfly%20Walk&dates=20250621T200000Z/20250621T211500Z&location=1550%20Wildcat%20Canyon%20Road%2C%20Berkeley%2C%20CA%2C%2094708%2C%20United%20States) [ICS](/events-and-classes/events/5eykntt0xoamk7fclxten7ic1mhpi4?format=ical)



June Butterfly Walk with Sarab Seth, the butterfly guy! This is a free, guided walk around the Regional Parks Botanic Garden. To sign up and view more information, use this [link](https://www.signupgenius.com/go/30E0549AFA72CABFE3-57205934-butterfly#/).

We will be adding additional midweek Butterfly Walks in July and August during peak butterfly spotting season! Check back here for more updates and signups.
