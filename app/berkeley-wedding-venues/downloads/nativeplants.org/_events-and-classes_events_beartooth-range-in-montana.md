# Beartooth Range in Montana

  * Saturday, February 8, 2025
  * 10:30 AM 12:00 PM


  * Regional Parks Botanic Garden 1550 Wildcat Canyon Road Berkeley, CA, 94708 United States [(map)](http://maps.google.com?q=1550 Wildcat Canyon Road Berkeley, CA, 94708 United States)


  * [Google Calendar](http://www.google.com/calendar/event?action=TEMPLATE&text=Beartooth%20Range%20in%20Montana&dates=20250208T183000Z/20250208T200000Z&location=1550%20Wildcat%20Canyon%20Road%2C%20Berkeley%2C%20CA%2C%2094708%2C%20United%20States) [ICS](/events-and-classes/events/beartooth-range-in-montana?format=ical)



Bob Case talks about the Beartooth Range, home to many beautiful and horticulturally desirable alpine plants. 

Bob Case is a long-time member of the Friends of the Garden and a frequent speaker for the Wayne Roderick Lecture series.
