# CA Cultivars Selected by Carol Bornstein

  * Saturday, January 4, 2025
  * 10:30 AM 12:00 PM


  * Regional Parks Botanic Garden 1550 Wildcat Canyon Road Berkeley, CA, 94708 United States [(map)](http://maps.google.com?q=1550 Wildcat Canyon Road Berkeley, CA, 94708 United States)


  * [Google Calendar](http://www.google.com/calendar/event?action=TEMPLATE&text=CA%20Cultivars%20Selected%20by%20Carol%20Bornstein&dates=20250104T183000Z/20250104T200000Z&location=1550%20Wildcat%20Canyon%20Road%2C%20Berkeley%2C%20CA%2C%2094708%2C%20United%20States) [ICS](/events-and-classes/events/ca-cultivars-carol-bornstein?format=ical)



Carol Bornstein will provide an overview of the California native plant cultivars she has selected and/or been involved with over the course of her career. This lecture will be via ZOOM only.

After you have successfully registered for the talk, you will receive an email from [BGarden@ebparks.org](mailto:BGarden@ebparks.org) shortly before the talk with the ZOOM link. Please do not share this ZOOM link as space for this talk is limited!

The entire talk will be posted on the Garden [YouTube account](https://youtube.com/@friendsrpbg?feature=shared) a week after the talk.
