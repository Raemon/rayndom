# Wayne Roderick Lecture: Flora of Western Australia 

  * Saturday, December 6, 2025
  * 10:30 AM 12:00 PM


  * Regional Parks Botanic Garden 1550 Wildcat Canyon Road Berkeley, CA, 94708 United States [(map)](http://maps.google.com?q=1550 Wildcat Canyon Road Berkeley, CA, 94708 United States)


  * [Google Calendar](http://www.google.com/calendar/event?action=TEMPLATE&text=Wayne%20Roderick%20Lecture%3A%20Flora%20of%20Western%20Australia%20&dates=20251206T183000Z/20251206T200000Z&location=1550%20Wildcat%20Canyon%20Road%2C%20Berkeley%2C%20CA%2C%2094708%2C%20United%20States) [ICS](/events-and-classes/events/wayne-roderick-lecture-flora-of-western-australia?format=ical)



Glenn Keator will speak on the flora of western Australia.

Dr. Keator has taught at Merritt College, SF Botanical Garden, College of Marin, and California Academy of Sciences, among others. His primary focus is on California native plants; he initiated the docent training program at the East Bay Regional Parks Botanic Garden, and has been the principal teacher for all the docent training classes.
