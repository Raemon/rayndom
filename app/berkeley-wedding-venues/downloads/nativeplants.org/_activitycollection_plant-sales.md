# Plant Sales

Sep 1

Written By [Friends of the Regional Parks Botanic Garden](/activitycollection?author=667d75fe381147300fc6231d)

![](https://images.squarespace-cdn.com/content/v1/666115df3fa3b24521c127ed/7688ed3b-67ad-4986-b55e-97ddb44a1b65/plants.jpg)

Plant sales are on Wednesday 10:00 AM - 12:00 PM and the first Saturday of the month 10:00 AM - 2:00 PM. Here is the current [inventory](/s/RPBG-Plant-Sale-Inventory.pdf).

**Note** : In the event of heavy rain, the plant sale is cancelled.

To receive email notices of plant sales and other garden events, [join our email list.](/publications/newsletter/)
