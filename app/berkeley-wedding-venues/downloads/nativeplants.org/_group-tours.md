## Group Tours

![](https://images.squarespace-cdn.com/content/v1/666115df3fa3b24521c127ed/54dd9269-6456-4eae-8dc6-32627b436d69/tour3.jpg)

You can request a free docent-led group tour. Here is a [description of the types of tours offered](/tours "Tours"). 

Please request your tour at least 4 weeks in advance by completing the [Group Tour Request Form](/tours/group-tour-request-form).

After your tour, please fill out this short [online evaluation form](/tours/tour-evaluation-form). Your honest evaluation will help us improve future tours.
