## Full-Service Catering & Event Planning 

Mavericks House offers a full-service, in-house catering team featuring fresh, locally grown ingredients and sustainable seafood, providing you with the highest quality menu options for your celebration. We are pleased to offer brunch, lunch, tapas appetizers, or full dinner menus, in styles ranging from buffet to plated service. 

**Included with the space**

2,000 square foot ballroom · 4,000 square foot landscaped garden · 700 square foot outdoor deck with gas fire pit, coastal-inspired furniture and standing heaters · 5 foot round banquet tables, 6 feet rectangle tables, cocktail tables, or bistro tables · Two large restrooms · Complimentary on-site parking · In-house music system with wire-less microphone · Two 70’ Monitors available · Standard plates, flatware, and glassware · Classic white banquet chairs · Dedicated staff members on site for the entirety of your event · Standard ivory table linens and napkins · Customized room set-up to reflect your style

**Capacities**

INDOOR  
Standing Reception - 100 guests  
Seated Dinner - 90 guests  
  
INDOOR & OUTDOOR DECK (WEATHER PERMITTING)   
Standing Reception - 140 guests  
Seated Dinner - 130 guests  
 _A tent can be rented for the deck in case weather is a concern, please ask for a quote._

**Details & Reservations**

To reserve the space there is a food and beverage minimum plus a rental fee, please ask for a custom quote. All menus incur a service charge and state sales tax. 

To reserve the date we require a $1,000 deposit and a signed contract. Dates are reserved on a first-come, first-served basis. Please inquire about a custom quote and availability. We look forward to working with you this holiday season!
