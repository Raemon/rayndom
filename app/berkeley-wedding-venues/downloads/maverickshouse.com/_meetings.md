# Meetings

## A Waterfront Location for your Meeting, Team Building Retreat or Corporate Event.

* * *

![](https://images.squarespace-cdn.com/content/v1/5cf6de85b6e2240001bd85c7/1574298257487-O4TILEZ6W7VL4ZIRIQ7G/Mavericks-House-waterfront-meeting-space.jpg)

Treat your staff to a day on the San Mateo Coast. Mavericks House, a waterfront meeting space situated on a dramatic bluff in Half Moon Bay, is a stunning location for your meeting or corporate event. Enjoy panoramic views of Pillar Point Harbor from our indoor space and expansive bluff-top deck and garden.

![](https://images.squarespace-cdn.com/content/v1/5cf6de85b6e2240001bd85c7/1572462637395-A31SOEDEXBVX209WOFG9/Mavericks-House-Ocean-View-Meeting-Space.jpg)

Mavericks House is the ideal location for business meetings, team building, off-site cooking classes, corporate retreats, company parties and picnics, and more! With our dedicated staff and full service in- house catering team, we will create an environment where you can focus on your agenda while we take care of your needs.
