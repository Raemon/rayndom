## Mavericks House

Half Moon Bay

![](https://images.squarespace-cdn.com/content/v1/5cf6de85b6e2240001bd85c7/1715026018359-ZMR0GGPWSDUOBP6CAR8Y/Mavericks+House+2.jpg)

Mavericks House is a beautiful stand-alone venue overlooking picture perfect Pillar Point Harbor, Mavericks House is a 2,000 square foot indoor reception space featuring 180-degree ocean views, a spacious upstairs pre-wedding bridal suite, large deck overlooking the harbor, private seaside ceremony garden, private self-service parking, and fully equipped kitchen - all serviced, catered and managed by Mavericks House. Our in-house catering team specializes in fresh California coastal cuisine. Also enjoy our award-winning, fresh-on-tap beers, and numerous amenities, including outdoor lounge furniture, firepit and standing heaters.
