#  Events

![](https://images.squarespace-cdn.com/content/v1/5cf6de85b6e2240001bd85c7/1569420210782-BSRDX61W0OMQ6D2T5VBJ/Mavericks-House-waterfront-event-space.jpg)

Mavericks House, a waterfront venue situated on a bluff in Half Moon Bay, is the perfect location for your event. With stunning views of Pillar Point Harbor, an in-house catering team, and freshly brewed award-winning local beers on tap, Mavericks House is perfect for your next event or celebration.

![](https://images.squarespace-cdn.com/content/v1/5cf6de85b6e2240001bd85c7/1569420260711-T0ZEWFLZB9C162VGBMTI/Mavericks-House-ocean-view-event-space.jpg)

Our charming seaside facility is the ideal location for rehearsal dinners, family reunions, birthday parties, celebrations of life, company parties or picnics, and more. Our event team will assist in planning an unforgettable experience, taking care of all the details so you can relax and enjoy your celebration.
