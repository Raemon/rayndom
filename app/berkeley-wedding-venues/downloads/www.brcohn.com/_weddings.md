## Open Air Barn 

![](https://www.brcohn.com/wp-content/uploads/2024/10/Path_4.png)

Our open air barn offers an idyllic panoramic wine country scene with incredible views of our estate vineyards. Rustic meets elegant with wood beam ceilings that allow for unlimited customizations, from lighting to florals and everything in between. Dine and dance the night away, rain or shine.

## Olive Grove 

![](https://www.brcohn.com/wp-content/uploads/2024/10/Path_4.png)

Featuring a manicured lawn, our olive grove is lined with 160-year-old French Picholine olive trees. Each of the trees is decorated with white lights, adding a touch of elegance to your celebration and creating a spectacular and intimate setting for your cocktail hour or reception. This serene space is also an ideal location for a romantic first look!

## Amphitheater

![](https://www.brcohn.com/wp-content/uploads/2024/10/Path_4.png)

Our amphitheater is a breathtaking ceremony or cocktail reception site. The large stage and terraced lawn are framed by rolling Cabernet Sauvignon vineyards and the Sonoma Mountain range.

## WEDDING COTTAGE + GREEN ROOM

![](https://www.brcohn.com/wp-content/uploads/2024/10/Path_4.png)

**Wedding Cottage**

Brides, grooms, and their parties are invited to spend the day preparing in our thoughtfully designed wedding cottage, complete with:

  * Full-length mirror
  * Comfortable couch
  * Dining table with seating
  * Private full bathroom
  * Mini fridge



**The Green Room**

Brides, grooms, and their parties can enjoy a relaxed space for final touches and downtime before the ceremony begins — a laid-back retreat accented with signature rock ’n’ roll memorabilia.

## Wedding Pricing + Availability

![](https://www.brcohn.com/wp-content/uploads/2024/10/Path_4.png)

**Ceremony + Reception**

  * $10,000 Fridays
  * $12,000-$15,000 Saturdays
  * $8,000 Sundays–Thursdays



The site fee includes:

  * Capacity of up to 200 guests
  * Reserved event space from 4-10 p.m.
  * 60″ round tables for up to 150 people
  * Up to 150 white ceremony chairs
  * One-hour ceremony rehearsal space
  * VIP wine tasting for wine selection (up to four guests)
  * Wedding Cottage and Green Room
