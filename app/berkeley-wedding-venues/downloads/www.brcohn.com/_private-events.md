## Open Air Barn

![](https://www.brcohn.com/wp-content/uploads/2024/10/Path_4.png)

Our open air barn offers an idyllic panoramic wine country scene with incredible views of our estate vineyards. Rustic meets elegant with wood beam ceilings that allow for an unlimited amount of customizations, from lighting to florals and everything in between. Dine and dance the night away, rain or shine.

## Olive Grove 

![](https://www.brcohn.com/wp-content/uploads/2024/10/Path_4.png)

Featuring a manicured lawn, our olive grove is lined with 160-year-old French Picholine olive trees. Each of the trees is decorated with white lights, adding a touch of elegance to any evening occasion and creating a spectacular and intimate setting for your private event.

## Amphitheater

![](https://www.brcohn.com/wp-content/uploads/2024/10/Path_4.png)

Our amphitheater is a breathtaking special event site. The large stage and terraced lawn are framed by rolling Cabernet Sauvignon vineyards and the Sonoma Mountain range.
