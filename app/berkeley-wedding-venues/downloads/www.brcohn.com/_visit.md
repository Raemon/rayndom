## Area Arts Collection

![](https://www.brcohn.com/wp-content/uploads/2024/10/Path_4.png)

Our tasting room is currently hosting a temporary gallery by [Area Arts](https://www.areaarts.com/), showcasing an eclectic collection of works by renowned artists Grace Slick, Patrick Nagel, Richard Duardo, Bernie Taupin, Tom Everhart and John Lennon. This unique exhibition blends music, pop culture and fine art, offering guests a rare opportunity to experience pieces from these iconic creators while enjoying our wines.
