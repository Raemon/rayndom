## Estate Moke Tour & Tasting

![](https://www.brcohn.com/wp-content/uploads/2024/10/Path_4.png)

_Approximately 90 minutes  
Sunday • Monday • Tuesday • Thursday • Friday  
_

Climb aboard our new Moke electric vehicle for an intimate, open-air journey through our historic Olive Hill Estate! With just two guests per ride, this is your chance to explore B.R. Cohn up close and personal.

Frequently Asked Questions 

____

Can we bring our dog?

We would love to meet your well-behaved four-legged friend! We just request that you keep your dog on a leash and be prepared to clean up any messes.

Are children allowed on property?

We are a family-friendly winery and welcome guests of all ages.

Do you offer a military discount?

We offer a 10% discount on all wine purchases.

Can we smoke on property?

We do not permit smoking of any kind on property including cigarettes, vapes or cigars.

Do you host weddings?

Congratulations on your engagement! Yes, we are permitted to host weddings and would love to be part of your special day. [Learn more about weddings →](https://www.brcohn.com/weddings/)

Can I host a private event at the tasting room?

We have a variety of locations on property where we can host private events for up to 250 people. [Learn more about private events →](https://www.brcohn.com/private-events/)

Can we walk through the vineyards or take pictures in the vineyards?

For your safety, we kindly ask that you do not enter the vineyards on your own as there can be dangerous wildlife.

Can we bring in food?

Unfortunately, outside food is not permitted on the property. However, a small selection of snacks is available for purchase.
