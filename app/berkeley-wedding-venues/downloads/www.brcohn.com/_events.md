## Valentine's Day Weekend

![](https://www.brcohn.com/wp-content/uploads/2024/10/Path_4.png)

**Saturday + Sunday, February 14 + 15  
Live Music: Noon–3 p.m.**

Love is in the air at B.R. Cohn, and we’re excited to share it with you! Soulful jazz guitarist Jim Adams will play live both days to set the mood.

Our indulgent wine and chocolate pairing experience will be available every hour all weekend long. Our winemaker has curated five standout wines, each matched with handcrafted chocolates that amplify every note.

General: $85 per person  
Wine Club: $68 per person

For wine club pricing, log into Tock with the email address associated with your membership.

## Sip 'n' Paint

![](https://www.brcohn.com/wp-content/uploads/2024/10/Path_4.png)

**March**

Enjoy a guided painting experience set against the backdrop of our scenic vineyards, paired with B.R. Cohn wines. The 90-minute session includes all painting materials, plus a complimentary first glass of wine. Additional wines will be available for purchase.

More details coming soon!

For wine club pricing, log into Tock with the email address associated with your membership.

## Easter Brunch & Egg Hunt

![](https://www.brcohn.com/wp-content/uploads/2024/10/Path_4.png)

**Sunday, April 5**

Bring the whole family to the estate for a festive Easter brunch, featuring a kids’ egg hunt in our olive grove, family-friendly photo opportunities, and a Wine Country-style brunch paired with Sonoma Valley wines.

More details coming soon!

For wine club pricing, log into Tock with the email address associated with your membership.

## Line Dancing

![](https://www.brcohn.com/wp-content/uploads/2024/10/Path_4.png)

Saturday, April 25 | 5:30-8 p.m.

Put on your boots and get ready to line dance at one of the most beautiful venues in Sonoma Valley. Enjoy instructor-led dancing by Grapevine Dance under our covered barrel building, with wines and food available for purchase separately.

Tickets will be available soon!

General Admission

**$30** per person

Wine Club

**$24** per person (max 4)
