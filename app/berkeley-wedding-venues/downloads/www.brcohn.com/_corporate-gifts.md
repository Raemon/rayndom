# Customized Corporate Gifts

Give the ultimate “thank you” gift — a personalized selection of B.R. Cohn wines, vinegars and olive oils.

## CURATE YOUR SPECIAL GIFT

![](https://www.brcohn.com/wp-content/uploads/2024/10/Path_4.png)

Make your corporate or holiday gifts extra special with B.R. Cohn premium wines, handcrafted olive oils and specialty vinegars, all elegantly packaged and ready for giving. Perfect for client gifts, special occasions or events, our knowledgeable team will assist you in creating a customized gift that includes your own special touch.

__ Get Started

## WINES FOR EVERY PALATE 

![](https://www.brcohn.com/wp-content/uploads/2024/10/Path_4.png)

We know your clients have great taste! From elegantly oaked Chardonnay to bold and tannic Cabernet Sauvignon, we have a wine that fits every palate and price point. Our handcrafted and estate-grown wines range from $25 to $100+ per bottle. With multiple gift boxes to choose from, your gift will be sophisticated and personalized.

## Olive Oils & Vinegars

![](https://www.brcohn.com/wp-content/uploads/2024/10/Path_4.png)

From our estate-grown olive oil to wine-flavored and aged vinegars, we produce unique, high-quality gourmet products you can’t find anywhere else. Simply select your favorites and we’ll help you personalize them, then package them in an elegant wood box and ship them on your behalf.

## MIX, MATCH & PERSONALIZE 

![](https://www.brcohn.com/wp-content/uploads/2024/10/Path_4.png)

Our corporate gifting team specializes in creating assortments to match your budget and showcase your brand! Let us make this season a memorable one for those you want to honor most.
