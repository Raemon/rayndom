## Backstage Club

  * 3 allocations per year (March, September, November), 6 bottles each — fully customizable
  * Save 20% at B.R. Cohn, including wine, merchandise, food, elevated tasting experiences and more
  * Complimentary tastings at B.R. Cohn for 2 guests
  * Discounted flat rate shipping



[ __ Join Now ](https://shop.adairfamilywines.com/clubs/BRC-Backstage-Club)

Terms + Conditions 

To join our wine club, you must be at least 21 years of age and authorize the winery to charge your credit card on file for ongoing wine allocations. The average allocation price for the Backstage membership tier is $300, but the price will vary depending on the wines selected.

Membership requires a minimum one-year commitment (three allocations) upon enrollment and continues until a formal cancellation request is submitted. Early cancellations are subject to a fee equal to the average allocation price for your membership tier.

Cancellations must be made by phone at (707) 938-4064, by email at [wineclub@brcohn.com](mailto:wineclub@brcohn.com?subject=Wine%20Club%20Cancellation), or via our [online cancellation form](https://shop.adairfamilywines.com/Wine-Club/BR-Cohn-Club-Cancellation). If a cancellation request is received while a shipment is in transit, the shipment will be processed and charged as scheduled.

All wine shipments require an adult (21+) signature at the time of delivery. We strongly recommend using a business address for shipping. Wine cannot be delivered to a P.O. box. Allocations will be held for 30 days for members who choose to pick up at the winery. After 30 days, unclaimed allocations will be shipped to the address on file and standard shipping rates will apply.

By providing your email address and phone number, you consent to receive club-related communications via email and SMS. Standard message and data rates may apply.

The winery reserves the right to make changes to membership benefits, terms, or shipment schedules at any time. Members will be notified of any changes in advance.

Frequently Asked Questions 

____

**How long does a wine club membership last?**

Membership requires a minimum one-year commitment (three allocations) upon enrollment and continues until a formal cancellation request is submitted. The winery reserves the right to make changes to membership benefits, terms, or shipment schedules at any time. Members will be notified of any changes in advance.

**Can I skip a shipment or pause my membership?**

After you fulfill your initial one-year commitment, you are welcome to skip a shipment or pause your membership for up to 6 months per year. While your membership is paused, you may still attend club-exclusive events and purchase wine at member pricing, but you must be an active member to enjoy complimentary tastings. Contact us to request a skip or pause.

**What if I cancel my membership early?**

Early cancellations are subject to a fee equal to the average allocation price for your membership tier: $550 for the Legends tier, $400 for the Encore tier, $300 for the Backstage tier and $175 for the Discovery tier.

**How do I cancel my wine club membership?**

To cancel your membership, call us at (707) 938-4064, email us at [wineclub@brcohn.com](mailto:wineclub@brcohn.com?subject=Wine%20Club%20Cancellation) or [request to cancel online](https://shop.adairfamilywines.com/Wine-Club/BR-Cohn-Club-Cancellation). If a cancellation request is received while a shipment is in transit, the shipment will be processed and charged as scheduled.

**When will I receive my wine club allocations?**

B.R. Cohn wine club allocations are released in March, September and November. We’ll send emails and texts to your contact info on file about two weeks before your next allocation is released. During this time, you are welcome to customize the wines in your allocation online via your member portal or by contacting our team.

**Where can my wine be delivered?**

All wine shipments require an adult (21+) signature at the time of delivery. If you are not normally home during regular business hours, we recommend shipping to a business address, such as a FedEx or UPS location. Wine cannot be delivered to a P.O. box.

**Can I pick up my wine at the tasting room?**

Discovery tier allocations must be shipped. Members of all other tiers can choose the “Pickup” option when signing up or contact our team before your next ship date. Once released, your wine will be held for 30 days; after 30 days, unclaimed allocations will be shipped to the address on file and standard shipping rates will apply.
