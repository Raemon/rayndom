### PRICES: _What's this going to cost?_

###### Rental Fee

  * **$1,500–3,900/event**
  * Rental fee varies depending on: 
    * Day of week
    * Time of day
    * Time of year
  * _The rental fee includes:_

    * Dressing suites
    * Exclusive garden access, plus access to the Sculpture Park and Gallery
    * Projector, wireless mic, and speaker system
    * Prep kitchen, ice machine, and fridge
    * Tables, chairs, easels, linens, and centerpiece options
    * Event host and setup & cleanup

  * **$350/event**
  * The ceremony fee includes __ a 1-hour rehearsal, chair setup & teardown, and staff to play music.

  * **$24–/person**
