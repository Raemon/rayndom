  * **Built in 1917 as part of a World War I munitions complex,** this special spot has been thoughtfully renovated with an eye toward preserving its historic charm and providing top-notch amenities for modern celebrations.
  * **Soaring vaulted ceilings with exposed steel-beam rafters** create loft-style vibes, while oversized arched windows ensure the space is filled with tons of natural light. Exposed brick walls and poured concrete flooring round out the industrial-chic aesthetic.
  * **Large roll-up doors open onto a fantastic outdoor area,** a seasonal 30x30 tent with string lighting makes it perfect for cocktail hour or overflow seating. Not to mention, there's space for food trucks!
