### PRICES: _What's this going to cost?_

###### Package Prices

  * **$6,200–17,685/event**
  * Package prices vary depending on: 
    * Guest count
    * Package chosen
  * **The Day Package** ranges $6,200–9,900 and includes:

    * 10am–11pm rental of the entire event house and all amenities on the wedding day (except Sundays which are 11am–11pm)
    * On-property rehearsal and access by appointment for photos
    * Reception pavilion with lighting, dance floor, bar, DJ booth, and prep area
    * Tables, chairs, linens in color of choice, and use of in-house bars, props, and décor 
    * Event coordinator and staffing

**The All Inclusive Package** ranges $9,900–17,685 and includes everything in the _Day Package_ PLUS:

    * Fresh floral aisle markers, gazebo spray, and table arrangements
    * Bartender and bar setup, including cups and napkins
    * Coffee & hot chocolate service
    * DJ with sound and lighting equipment
    * Wedding cake

 _Note:_ Guest counts over 180 require a tent rental, which is not included in pricing.
