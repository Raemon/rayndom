## Why You'll Love This Place

##### A Here Comes The Guide Wedding Venue Review

Read More Show Less

  * **Say "I do" as you roll along the Mississippi River.** Your private charter departs from Bohemian Flats park and treats guests to a wonderful cruise through downtown Minneapolis.
  * **Choose between the sleek** _**Paradise Destiny II** ,_ a luxe yacht with two decks of floor-to-ceiling windows, a grand center staircase, and a large open-air aft patio, or the _Minneapolis Queen,_ an intimate picture-perfect, traditional paddle-wheel riverboat.
  * **You can also exchange vows in Bohemian Flats Gazebo** , a large covered pavilion in the pretty waterfront park, before embarking on the ship of your choice for an unforgettable reception.



### PRICES: _What's this going to cost?_

###### Rental Fee

  * **$1,275/event and up**
  * Rental fee varies depending on: 
    * Day of week
    * Time of year
    * Space reserved
    * Event duration
  * Private charter rates range $425–850/hour, with a 3-hour minimum evenings & weekends.

_Each boat in the fleet offers:_

    * On-board or on land ceremony area
    * Private boarding dock
    * Complimentary dry snacks
    * Climate controlled salons
    * Complete sound system, satellite stereo, flatscreen TVs, and Bluetooth
    * Dancing area
    * Tables, chairs, and linens
    * Uniformed Captain and crew and personal event planner



###### Meals (when priced separately)

  * **$55–100/person**
  * Menu pricing includes lunch or dinner entrées, butler-passed dessert, and a soda package with coffee (some options also include an appetizer). Hors d'oeuvres are priced separately.

Bar packages range $9.50–14.50/person per hour, with options for cash and consumption bars.
