### PRICES: _What's this going to cost?_

###### Rental Fee

  * **$1,500–4,000/event**
  * Rental fee varies depending on: 
    * Day of week
    * Event duration
  *     * **Full-day rentals** (9am–midnight) are $2,500 Monday–Thursday and $4,000 Friday–Sunday. 
    * **Half-day rentals**(7 consecutive hours) are $1,500 Monday–Thursday and $2,500 Friday & Sunday (not available Saturday). 

All rental times are inclusive of setup, decorating, and cleanup. Tables, chairs, chafers, heat lamps, and serviceware may be added on for $500.




###### Food & Beverage Minimum

  * **$3,000/event**



###### Meals (when priced separately)

  * **$20–65/person**
  * Plated, buffet, and stations-style dinners are available. Hors d'oeuvres are priced separately.

Hosted bar service requires a $150 fee for a 4-hour event, plus $225 for bartender and server. Beverage packages range $25–40/person.
