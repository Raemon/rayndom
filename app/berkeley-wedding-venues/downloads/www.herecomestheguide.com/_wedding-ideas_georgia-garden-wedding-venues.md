# 10 Breathtaking Georgia Garden Wedding Venues

## 

**If you're dreaming of an outdoor wedding among blossoms, vines, and shade trees, Georgia is a great place to get married.** Thanks to its temperate weather and a Southern knack for making all things beautiful, the Peach State is home to some of the country's most glorious gardens. Here’s a selection of our favorites, and we're sure you’ll be smitten with one (or all!) of them!
