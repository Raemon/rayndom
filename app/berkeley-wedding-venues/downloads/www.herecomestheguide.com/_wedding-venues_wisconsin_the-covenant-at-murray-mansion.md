### PRICES: _What's this going to cost?_

###### Rental Fee

  * **$3,000–7,500/event**
  * Rental fee varies depending on: 
    * Day of week
    * Time of year
  * _The rental fee includes:_

    * Access to the property, including the church and adjacent Murray Mansion
    * Two dressing suites in Murray Mansion, stocked with water, champagne, orange juice, and coffee
    * Changeover of venue from ceremony to reception
    * Dedicated event specialist and day-of coordinator
    * Pipe organ back bar
    * Cake cutting and dessert handling
    * Banquet and wood harvest tables, wood cross-back chairs, linens, napkins, glassware, and tableware
    * Setup and takedown of all tables, chairs, linens, décor, etc.
