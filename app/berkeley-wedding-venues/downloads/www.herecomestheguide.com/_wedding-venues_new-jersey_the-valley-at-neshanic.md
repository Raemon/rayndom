## Why You'll Love This Place

##### A Here Comes The Guide Wedding Venue Review

Read More Show Less

  * **The impressive manor home at the heart of this unique venue** is a modern twist on the classic Colonial Revival. Sitting at the edge of Neshanic Valley Golf Course, it offers breathtaking views of surrounding Somerset County.
  * **After your garden gazebo ceremony (complete with a dreamy mountain backdrop),** dine and dance in the Neshanic Ballroom, a classically elegant space with white wainscoting and enormous windows overlooking the golf course's rolling greens. 
  * **Their menus are fun, creative, and flexible,** designed to pamper your guests with a delightful dining experience, whether you opt for a formal plated dinner, a buffet with dynamic action stations, or a festive cocktail-style celebration.
