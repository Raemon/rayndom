## Why You'll Love This Place

##### A Here Comes The Guide Wedding Venue Review

Read More Show Less

  * **Once home to Providence's main gas company,** this entire site has been beautifully and thoughtfully renovated—it now features chic apartment homes, entertainment, dining, and a truly epic event space. 
  * **The ballroom is all about classic elegance,** with stately pillars, glittering chandeliers, arched windows, and the option to include dramatic uplighting. 
  * **Need a ceremony or unique cocktail hour area, too?** Consider The Rooftop, where you'll find crystal fire pits, inviting lounge furnishings, and epic skyline views from every angle.
