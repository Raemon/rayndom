# San Antonio River Walk Wedding Venues

## 

**Your perfect San Antonio wedding venue may just be along the city's famous River Walk.** This urban park, which enlivens the banks of the San Antonio River, is home to many unique shops, celebrated restaurants, and numerous historic sites—like the Alamo Mission and The St. Anthony Hotel—and it provides a scenic and dynamic backdrop for a celebration with your closest friends and family! Here's a handful of River Walk wedding venues worth checking out.

* * *
