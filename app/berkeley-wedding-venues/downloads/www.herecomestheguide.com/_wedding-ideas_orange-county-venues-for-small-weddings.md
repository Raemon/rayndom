# Planning a small wedding? 8 Orange County venues that are perfect for an intimate gathering.

## 

**Think a small wedding can't be just as much fun as a large one? Think again!** Small weddings can be a blast, and whether you want to celebrate at a chic hotel, in a private garden, next to the ocean—or even on it, these Orange County wedding venues offer an interesting mix of settings for your intimate event.
