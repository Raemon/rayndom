**The Blue Heron at Blackthorn offers micro-wedding packages!**  
_Micro-weddings cost $60 per person and include:_

  * Ceremony at the white pergola and reception at the patio
  * Tables & chairs
  * Charcuterie board
  * 1.5-hour open bar
  * Fresh floral arrangements for tables
  * Small cutting cake and cake table with cutting knife
  * Cupcake and coffee station
  * And more!
