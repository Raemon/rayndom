## Why You'll Love This Place

##### A Here Comes The Guide Wedding Venue Review

Read More Show Less

  * **A heritage hotel at the heart of historic Amherst,** this landmark inn boasts gracious New England style blended with updated modern conveniences. Its location offers an excellent base from which to explore the Western Massachusetts and the Five Colleges area, and is within walking distance of the Amherst’s countless charms. 
  * **There's a pretty rooftop terrace** perfect for intimate celebrations and elopements, and the classically elegant Dickinson Ballroom is just right for grand affairs. If an all-outdoor event is what you’ve been picturing—opt for the seasonal garden tent, which boasts brick flooring, sparkling chandeliers, and retractable siding. 
  * **The Inn’s 49 guest rooms ensure there’s space for all your loved ones,** who’ll overnight in luxe comfort with indulgent amenities like pillow-top mattresses and high-end linens. _30 Boltwood_ , the Inn’s inviting farm-to-table restaurant, offers unique private dining experiences to delight even the most discerning foodies in your group.
