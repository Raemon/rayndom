# No Passport Required: 55 Top Destination Wedding Venues in the U.S.A.

## 

**Dreaming of a destination wedding in a vibrant city, beachfront paradise, or luxe resorts, but don't want to learn a foreign language or take a 12-hour flight to get there?** No problem! Some of the best destination wedding venues are right here in the U.S., like a secluded ranch in CALIFORNIA, a mountaintop lodge in COLORADO, a rustic retreat in TEXAS, a chic beachfront resort in FLORIDA, and everything in between. The places we’ve included below are not just unique settings to tie the knot, they’re all perfect for kicking back and spending time with your nearest and dearest... and destinations they'll love visiting too.
