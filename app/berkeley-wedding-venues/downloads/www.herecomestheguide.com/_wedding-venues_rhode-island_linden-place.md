## Why You'll Love This Place

##### A Here Comes The Guide Wedding Venue Review

Read More Show Less

  * **Nestled in the heart of Bristol, this exquisite mansion exudes a stately grandeur.** High ceilings, hardwood floors, and carriage house doors in the climate-controlled Ballroom lend elegance and style to your celebration.
  * **The garden is a lovely place for your outdoor ceremony** , surrounded by manicured blooms and grand sculptures; cocktails follow in the adjacent lighted courtyard. Be sure to take some time for photos set against the dramatic facade of the 1810 Federal-style manor!
  * **Seasonal, sustainable cuisine is provided by the experts at Easy Entertaining, Inc.,** the Ocean State's premier catering collaborative. Menus are carefully customized to your tastes and preferences, with the fresh flavors matched by exceptional hospitality.
