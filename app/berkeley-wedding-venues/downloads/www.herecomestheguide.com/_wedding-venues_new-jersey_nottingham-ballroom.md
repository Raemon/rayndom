## Why You'll Love This Place

##### A Here Comes The Guide Wedding Venue Review

Read More Show Less

  * **One of the largest gathering halls in Mercer County, New Jersey,** this newly renovated ballroom sits nestled in the heart of Hamilton Square, and is both blissfully affordable and ultra-accessible from the New York and Philadelphia metro areas. 
  * **Its beautiful wood floors just beg to be filled with dancers,** and there’s an elevated stage to keep the music front and center. Essential amenities like prep kitchen and dedicated bar area ensure easy party prep and keep your vendors happy.
  * **Sayen Botanical Gardens can be used for your ceremony and/or photos,** with additional arrangements through the city. Located directly adjacent to the ballroom, the gardens feature walking trails, ponds, gazebos, and even a charming arched footbridge.
