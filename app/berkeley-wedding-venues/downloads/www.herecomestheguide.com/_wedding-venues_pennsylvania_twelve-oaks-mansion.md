## Why You'll Love This Place

##### A Here Comes The Guide Wedding Venue Review

  * **The venue offers a charmingly timeless backdrop** for your celebration. This stately mansion located 30 minutes north of Pittsburgh sits on top of a hill overlooking the surrounding wooded vistas. A romantic gazebo on the meticulously landscaped grounds provides a lovely photo op.
  * **The Grand Ballroom features glowing chandeliers** , a spacious dance floor, and an adjacent outdoor patio for extra room to mingle. There's also a library, a private dining room, and an entry with a picturesque winding stairway available for your use.
  * **Your wedding menu will be customized according to your tastes** and preferences by the talented in-house catering staff. Packages include a cocktail hour with hors d'oeuvres, a served first course, an entrée course or buffet, and a four-hour bar.
