### PRICES: _What's this going to cost?_

###### Rental Fee

  * **$3,500–15,000/event**
  * Rental fee varies depending on: 
    * Day of week
    * Time of year
    * Package chosen
    * Type of event
  * _Rental fees include:_

    * Use of the large event space
    * Dressing loft suites (dressing and touch-ups only; no on-site hair or makeup artists permitted)
    * Covered pavilion and grounds with garden furniture and yard games
    * Access to the Tasting Room bar with bartenders
    * In-house décor, including silk flowers, runners, centerpieces, easels, lanterns, and more!
    * Tables, chairs, custom arbor, and tablecloths
    * Venue coordinator
    * Access to the Tasting Room bar with bartenders
    * Ample parking with the option to leave cars overnight if needed

**A Night to Remember** | $9,500–11,500  
Reception-only rental (1–11pm) with all décor and coordination included. Access to the lofts is for touch-ups only.

**A Wedding Day Affair** | $13,000–15,000  
Ceremony and reception rental (noon–11pm) with all décor and coordination included.

**DIY Options** __ | $3,500–8,000  
Indoor-only ceremony and reception rental (10am–midnight) for couples who bring their own coordinator and decorate themselves. Additional options, like a day-before rental or outdoor ceremony/cocktail hour are available for $1,500.

**Add-on option** | full venue buy-out ranges from $2,500–3,500 and allows exclusive use of the entire venue without the concern of the public in the Tasting Room or on the grounds during the ceremony.
