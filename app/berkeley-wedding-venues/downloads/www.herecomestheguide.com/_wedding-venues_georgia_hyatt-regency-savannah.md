##### A Here Comes The Guide Wedding Venue Review

  * **Perched on a lovely spot along the Savannah River in the city's historic district,** this full-service hotel offers a host of flexible venue spaces and makes a great home base for exploring the city. 
  * **Hold your ceremony on the open-air terrace overlooking the river and Talmadge Bridge,** then follow with a reception in one of their opulent ballrooms, all of which feature arched floor-to-ceiling windows so you won't miss a second of the glorious view.
