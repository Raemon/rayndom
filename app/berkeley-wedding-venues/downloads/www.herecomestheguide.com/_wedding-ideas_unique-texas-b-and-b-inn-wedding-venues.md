# 8 Unique Texas B&Bs: Combine Your Wedding Venue And Lodging!

## 

**Bed & Breakfast Inns not only serve as unique wedding venues, they also provide overnight accommodations. **Texas is rich in B&Bs, and the ones featured here offer a variety of event settings, from rustic-chic barns to a luxury lakeside inn. Of course, one of their major attractions is that you and your nearest and dearest can prolong your stay in lodging that includes charming guest rooms & suites, cozy cabins, and historic houses.
