###### Rental Fee

  * **$600–800/event**
  * Rental fee varies depending on: 
    * Day of week
  * Reception rentals range $600–800 and include:

    * 3-hour event time (additional time is $250/hour)
    * Wedding coordinator and private waiter for couple and parents
    * Champagne toast for wedding party
    * Knife and server set, plus cutting and service
    * Buffet boxes for the couple and parents upon departure, and a wedding gift for the couple
    * Tables, chairs, linens, china, flatware, glassware, and setup & cleanup
    * Security guards (additional fee)

Minimum catering requirements range $4,000–8,000++.

  * **$400–450/event**
  * Ceremony fee varies depending on: 
    * Day of week
  * Ceremony fees include an additional half-hour for the ceremony, two changing rooms set with fruit, cheese, soft drinks, and water, a rehearsal, and a coordinator.




###### Meals (when priced separately)

  * **$40–80/person**
  * Menu pricing includes passed hors d'oeuvres and dinner. Bar packages range $14–24/person for the first three hours.
