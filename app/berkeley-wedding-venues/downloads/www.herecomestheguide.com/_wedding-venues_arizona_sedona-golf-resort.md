##### A Here Comes The Guide Wedding Venue Review

  * **With its utterly stunning location** **surrounded by Sedona’s breathtaking red rock formations,** this unique resort offers unforgettable views and affordable, flexible packages to meet a wide range of needs.
  * **Towering Cathedral Rock and Bell Rock** frame your outdoor ceremony on the lushly landscaped grounds. There’s a lovely patio for your cocktail hour, and the gorgeous dining room features a wall of floor-to-ceiling windows so you won’t miss a second of the scenery.
