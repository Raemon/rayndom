## Why You'll Love This Place

##### A Here Comes The Guide Wedding Venue Review

Read More Show Less

  * **This gracious country club** **exudes Southern charm**. The historic clubhouse's old brick exterior, tall archways, second-story veranda, and trees wrapped in Spanish moss, make this a picturesque favorite of wedding couples.
  * **The beautiful property presents several scenic ceremony sites** , like under a grove of spreading oaks with a glistening lake as a backdrop; on the lush, landscaped golf course; or in a "secret garden" behind a high privacy hedge.
  * **Inside, the romantic main room has soft lighting and French doors** that overlook the grounds. Their experienced staff, including a team of culinary artists, can help you customize your event with a huge selection of dishes and packages.
