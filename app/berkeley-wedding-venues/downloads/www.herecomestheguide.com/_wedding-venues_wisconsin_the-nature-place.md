## Why You'll Love This Place

##### A Here Comes The Guide Wedding Venue Review

Read More Show Less

  * **Set on five acres along a Mississippi River inlet,** this nature-focused community center in Myrick Park spans five acres of unspoiled watershed ecosystem, along with interactive experiences and unique indoor and outdoor gathering spaces.
  * **In addition to its meadows, marshland, trails, and bluffs,** the property features a stunning contemporary building with a soaring atrium and floor-to-ceiling window walls that allow all-season views of the park. You may choose to rent one room, or the entire facility for a large celebration. 
  * **You’ll be supporting the Nature Place’s awesome environmental education programs,** day camps, and community outreach projects with your rental fees…and you can even add on a special visit from a few of the center's resident native critters!
