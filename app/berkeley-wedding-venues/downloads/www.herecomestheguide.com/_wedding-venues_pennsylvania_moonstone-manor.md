## Why You'll Love This Place

##### A Here Comes The Guide Wedding Venue Review

Read More Show Less

  * **This handsome 1700s country home in lush Lancaster County** occupies a charming spot along Conewago Creek. It’s been beautifully restored—now operating as a wonderful B&B—and is surrounded by acres of mature, artfully designed landscaping. 
  * **Choose between several picture-perfect ceremony sites** throughout the property and gardens, including one featuring a dome-topped gazebo of intricately scrolled wrought iron. Receptions are held beneath the grand, fabric-draped pavilion whose peaked ceiling is hung with crystal chandeliers. 
  * **Intimate celebrations may use the Manor’s first floor,** which boasts an elegant collection of rooms that include the whimsical “Alice in Wonderland Foyer”, two formal dining rooms, a stately study, and a gracious, sun-filled parlor.
