# Serene Escapes: 12 of the Best Weekend Wedding Venues Within a 3-Hour Drive of Chicago

## 

**Planning a Chicago-area wedding, but want to escape the hustle and bustle of the city?** A short drive will have you surrounded by rolling vineyards, serene farmland dotted with rustic barns, and forested hillsides. From a luxurious log cabin surrounded by trees to a family-owned estate winery, we've gathered a handful of serene venues where you can just kick back and enjoy all that Mother Nature has to offer.

### Rockton, Illinois

[ ](https://www.herecomestheguide.com/wedding-venues/illinois/the-pavilion-at-orchard-ridge-farms-inspired-by-henricis)

**Catering:** In-house
