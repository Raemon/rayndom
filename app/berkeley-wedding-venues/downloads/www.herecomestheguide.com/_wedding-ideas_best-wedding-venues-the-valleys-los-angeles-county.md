# The 8 Best Outdoor Wedding Venues in the Valley

## 

**Looking for the best outdoor wedding venues away from the hustle and bustle of downtown L.A. or the Westside?** Surrounded by beautiful hills and mountains, the San Gabriel, Santa Clarita and San Fernando Valleys offer Los Angeles County's most scenic settings—and best values. Want to exchange vows in a secluded garden, at a chic Canyon Country bistro, or a historic Old Town Pasadena landmark? What about a unique equestrian estate or an all-inclusive country club? The Valleys have it all and more, along with fresh air and gorgeous views. Here are our top picks for memorable Valley weddings...
