# 6 Gorgeous Nashville Garden Wedding Venues

## 

**If you're dreaming of an outdoor wedding among blossoms, vines, and shade trees, Nashville is a great place to get married.** From a gorgeous greenhouse accented with stringed market lights to a charming Victorian mansion surrounded by manicured lawns and meandering paths, you’ll find all of these things and more among Music City's verdant landscape.

## [CJ's Off The Square](https://www.herecomestheguide.com/wedding-venues/tennessee/cjs-off-the-square)

### Franklin, TN

[ ](https://www.herecomestheguide.com/wedding-venues/tennessee/cjs-off-the-square)

**Fab Features:** gracious bastion of Southern charm with 3 delightful venues • secluded garden oasis with gazebo, pond, waterfall, stone walls and lots of leafy trees • 1911 manor with space for indoor events and a verdant lawn for tenting

**Catering:** In-house

## [Belle Air Mansion](https://www.herecomestheguide.com/wedding-venues/tennessee/belle-air-mansion)

### Nashville, TN

[ ](https://www.herecomestheguide.com/wedding-venues/tennessee/belle-air-mansion)

**Fab Features** : elegant 19th-century Greek Revival mansion boasting ornate antique furnishings and gorgeous architecture details, like a dramatic spiral staircase and hand-carved fireplaces • 16 beautifully appointed rooms sleep up to 40 guests, with easy access to all that downtown Nashville has to offer • picturesque 6-acre estate grounds include a fragrant rose garden, 200-year-old trees, and endless charming ceremony areas surrounded by lush landscaping

**Catering:** In-house, Preferred list, BYO   
**Price:** $ • $$ • $$$

**Catering:** Preferred list, BYO   
**Price:** $ • $$
