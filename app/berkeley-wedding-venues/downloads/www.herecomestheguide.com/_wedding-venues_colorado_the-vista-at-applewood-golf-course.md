## Why You'll Love This Place

##### A Here Comes The Guide Wedding Venue Review

  * **Blending rustic panoramas and scenic fairways** , this beautiful golf club, nestled in the foothills of the Rocky Mountains, is a picturesque, romantic wedding spot. Their garden ceremony site, flanked by a gentle stream and lush vegetation, looks out to a stunning backdrop of the Table Mountains. 
  * **The Vista Ballroom, featuring floor-to-ceiling windows** , showcases the sweeping panoramas, and the adjacent Event Deck is perfect for cocktails at sunset or a nightcap under the stars.
  * **Select from a variety of packages that let you choose the menu** , décor, and services that best fit your style and budget. And definitely take advantage of the photo ops around the property, including a bridge over the lake, or just a quiet embrace on a shaded path.
