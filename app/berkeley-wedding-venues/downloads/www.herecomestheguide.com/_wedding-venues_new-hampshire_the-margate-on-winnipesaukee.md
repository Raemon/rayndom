## Why You'll Love This Place

##### A Here Comes The Guide Wedding Venue Review

Read More Show Less

  * **Just two hours north of Boston,** this wonderful full-service resort sits at the edge of Lake Winnipesaukee in New Hampshire's beloved Lakes Region. 
  * **Say "I do" at the edge of Paugus Bay,** followed by a lavish reception beneath a clear-walled tent overlooking the pristine waters. Finish off the day with a cozy campfire, or a spectacular fireworks display over the lake. 
  * **Guests can choose from an array of great accommodations,** and there's more than enough fun right on the grounds to fill a wedding weekend, whether it's boating on the bay or basking on the private beach.
