## Why You'll Love This Place

##### A Here Comes The Guide Wedding Venue Review

Read More Show Less

  * **Style maven + stationer extraordinaire Elicia Castaldi has worked her designer magic on this mid-century modern gem, transforming it into an ultra-chic Palm Springs hideaway** – just the place to gather with your favorite people and celebrate in complete privacy. 
  * **A spacious green lawn can serve as a scenic ceremony site, with iconic palm trees and a serene mountain vista** creating a postcard backdrop. Host a poolside soiree, or a sit-down reception in one of the sweet garden enclaves, which are lavished in greenery and pink bougainvillea. Guests mingle around the life-size chess set, firepit or bocce court; or wander inside the exquisite light-filled home to take in every perfectly appointed detail. (Note the framed art prints of Castaldi’s popular cards!) 
  * **The plush movie star digs can also host** **intimate luncheons in the sleek dining room, or a welcome party on one of the landscaped patios** where everyone can chillax beneath the sunny-or-starry sky. The whole Knife House experience is swoon-worthy, just like your special occasion should be!
