###### Rental Fee

  * **$1,995/event**
  * Rental includes a sprawling central lawn & 1,600 square foot patio with a custom tent, including chandelier and café-style string lights, tables and chairs, and lounge seating, plus restrooms and parking.

Only one wedding is booked per weekend.  





###### Ceremony Fee

  * **$395/event**



###### Package Prices

  * **$125/person and up**
  * Packages typically include a cocktail hour grazing table, buffet dinner featuring wood-smoked barbecue with your selection of salads and sides, nonalcoholic beverages, and a cofee and tea station.

Bar pricing is separate; you may book a recommended bar vendor or stock your own bar. An in-house bartender and basic bar setup will be provided for an additional fee.




###### Food & Beverage Minimum

  * **$5,000/event and up**

  * **20%**
