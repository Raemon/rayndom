### PRICES: _What's this going to cost?_

###### Rental Fee

  * **$2,500–5,000/event**
  * Rental fee varies depending on: 
    * Day of week
    * Event duration
  * The rental fee includes access to the space, tables, chairs, trash removal, and an on-site manager.




###### Package Prices

  * **$11,500–25,500/event**
  * Package prices vary depending on: 
    * Day of week
    * Package chosen
  * The _DIY Wedding Package_ ranges $9,500–11,500 and includes:

    * 2-day rental
    * Farmhouse tables & dark brown folding chairs
    * Tablecloths, cloth napkins, chargers, centerpieces, and acrylic place settings
    * Catered buffet (meal & drinks)
    * 3-tier wedding cake
    * 10% off additional rental items from _Cari & Co._

The _Classic Wedding Package_ is $15,500 and includes everything in the _DIY Wedding Package_ PLUS:

    * Wedding day coordinator
    * Exclusive planning packet
    * DJ
    * Arch, ceremony décor, and upgraded centerpieces
    * Setup, cleanup, and table bussing

The _Premium Wedding Package_ is $18,500 and includes everything in the _Classic Wedding Package_ PLUS:

    * Full wedding planning
    * Fresh flower package
    * Use of the Clubhouse on the wedding day
    * Photo backdrop or lounge area
    * Upgraded china plates, flatware, and glasses
    * Sparkler send-off

The _Everything But The Dress_ package is $25,500 and includes everything in the _Premium Wedding Package_ PLUS:

    * Two-day use of the Clubhouse
    * Photography package
    * Larger fresh flower package
    * Appetizers served before dinner
    * Photo backdrop & two lounge areas

_Package pricing is based on 100 guests; each guest after 100 ranges $30–45/person depending on the package chosen._
