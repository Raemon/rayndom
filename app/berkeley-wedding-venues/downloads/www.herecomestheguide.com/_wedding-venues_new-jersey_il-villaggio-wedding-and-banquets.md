## Why You'll Love This Place

##### A Here Comes The Guide Wedding Venue Review

Read More Show Less

  * **There’s a reason this has become one of the most popular banquet halls in Northern New Jersey:** It’s a winning combo of perfectly planned event spaces, ultra-convenient location (it’s less than an hour from central Manhattan), and white-glove service from start to finish. 
  * **The Grand Ballroom’s design was inspired by the villas of southern Italy,** with an imported marble dance floor, romantic Juliet balcony framed by scrolled ironwork, and 25-foot ceilings hung with eight opulent crystal chandeliers. 
  * **Several beautifully appointed smaller rooms are available for intimate celebrations,** and there’s a quaint courtyard for romantic photos. No need to get ready at an off-site hotel…a sumptuous dressing suite is ready to host you and your wedding party.
