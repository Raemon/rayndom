# 7 Stunning Cape Ann Wedding Venues

## 

**Dreaming of a seaside wedding with breathtaking scenery and coastal charm?** While Cape Cod often steals the spotlight, its northern neighbor, Cape Ann, offers a compelling alternative with its laid-back beaches, and historic charm. Whether you envision a romantic ceremony in the sand on Half Moon Beach, a chic celebration in a bluffside Inn in Rockport, or onboard an iconic Wejack yacht cruising the Gloucester Harbor, check out these amazing locations perfect for your New England seaside soiree.
