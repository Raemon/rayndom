## Why You'll Love This Place

##### A Here Comes The Guide Wedding Venue Review

Read More Show Less

  * **Towering pines and peaceful meadows** surround this charming private event venue, which is nestled at the heart of historic Camp Grace. In addition to its ultra-cool, purpose-build gathering spaces, it’s outfitted with all the fun of traditional summer camp, including a fire pit, ropes course, and tons of group-friendly games. 
  * **The renovated mid-century event hall** boasts a stylish, rustic-modern design, with a lofty ceiling vaulted by exposed beams and pine panels that were hewn at nearby McNary Mill. Outside, you’ll find artfully maintained grounds that feature gardens, a waterfall, and both open and covered patios. 
  * **On-site overnight accommodations are available at Sleepy Grace.** House up to 150 guests in the cozy, eclectically designed guest lodgings, which include glamping tents, remodeled farmhouses, historic cabins, and bunkhouses.
