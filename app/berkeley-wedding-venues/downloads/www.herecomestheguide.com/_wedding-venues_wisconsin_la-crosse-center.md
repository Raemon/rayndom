## Why You'll Love This Place

##### A Here Comes The Guide Wedding Venue Review

Read More Show Less

  * **Set in the heart of La Crosse along the Mississippi River,** this sprawling convention center offers a nearly endless array of party spaces in an ultra-convenient location—just fifteen minutes from the city’s regional airport and within walking distance of 50 restaurants and over 1500 hotel rooms. 
  * **Sleek, contemporary event options range from grand reception halls to intimate, loft-style rooms,** and many have layouts that can host all phases of your celebration. For seasonal outdoor gatherings, the fourth level boasts an airy rooftop terrace, which overlooks the gently rolling river. 
  * **Packages have been expressly designed to take the stress out of planning,** whether you’re local or organizing from afar. Everything you need—coordination, catering, bar service, audio/visual setup, and so much more—is included…just show up and enjoy!
