### PRICES: _What's this going to cost?_

###### Rental Fee

  * **$3,200–5,000/event**
  * Rental fee varies depending on: 
    * Guest count
    * Event duration
  * The _Your Choice_ rental option ranges $3,200–4,600 for up to 150 guests and includes: 

    * Use of the venue for 6 hours (ending by 11pm)
    * Tables, chairs, linens, and setup & teardown
    * Color LED outdoor lighting
    * 2 vintage Airstream trailers
    * Prep kitchen
    * Pet-friendly!

    * Additional 3-hour rental time (2–11pm)
    * Ceremony set with chairs, plus setup & takedown
    * Additional linens for dining and cocktail tables

Add-on options include extended rental time, patio heaters, projection package, sound system, a hydration station, and more. The chandelier & sheers package is $2,400 (draping and chandeliers may also be rented separately).
