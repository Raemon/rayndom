### PRICES: _What's this going to cost?_

###### Ceremony Fee

  * **$0–1,000/event**
  * Ceremony fee varies depending on: 
    * Day of week
    * Space reserved
  * If the ceremony is hosted in the same space as the reception, there is no fee. Ceremonies held in a banquet room, on the patio, or in the theater have a fee that ranges $500–1,000.




###### Package Prices

  * **$1,615–7,500/event**
  * Package prices vary depending on: 
    * Time of year
    * Space reserved
    * Guest count
    * Package chosen
  * _All reception packages include:_

    * Access to the venue 8am–midnight
    * Use of the outdoor patio
    * Tables, chairs, linens & skirting
    * On-site event staff
    * Setup & teardown of venue-provided items

Upgraded packages may also include use of the dressing suite, a customizable dance floor, drapery, staging, and uplighting.
