### PRICES: _What's this going to cost?_

###### Package Prices

  * **$120–160/person**
  * Package prices vary depending on: 
    * Day of week
    * Time of year
    * Guest count
    * Package chosen
  * _All-Inclusive wedding packages include:_

    * Beachfront wedding ceremony and space for the reception
    * Exclusive use of the 10-acre facility
    * White or ivory table linens and your choice of napkin color
    * Passed hors d'oeuvres and complimentary fruit and cheese tray
    * Boat rides on the pontoon boat for the couple and guests
    * Catered meal
    * Wedding coordinators, waitstaff, and in-house chef
    * And more!

_Peak season (mid-May–October) pricing is based on a 100 adult minimum for Fridays and Saturdays. Off-season pricing is based on an 80 adult minimum for all dates November–mid-May and November._

**Overnight packages** include accommodations for a minimum of 30 guests and are an additional $120/person. Overnight packages include an after-party at the fire pit, overnight accommodation, and a full breakfast the following morning.
