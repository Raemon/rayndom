##### A Here Comes The Guide Wedding Venue Review

  * **Occupying a charming spot in downtown Raleigh,** this light-filled neighborhood favorite restaurant offers graceful event spaces and a fabulous menu filled with Tuscan-inspired delights.
  * **The oversized windows of the vintage storefront make a wonderfully romantic ceremony backdrop.** Continue the celebration here, in the restaurant’s Main Room, or opt to open the entire space—including the pretty Palm and Tuscan Rooms—for a grand reception.
