# 9 Of The Best Places To Get Married In and Around Fort Collins

## 

**For the Northern Colorado couple looking for the perfect place to say ‘I do’, you'll find plenty of lovely locations in and around Fort Collins.** From rustic Rocky Mountain retreats to super chic courtyards and majestic mansions, there are sweet spots that suit any and every wedding style here.
