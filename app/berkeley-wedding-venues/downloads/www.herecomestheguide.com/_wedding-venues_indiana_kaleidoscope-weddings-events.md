    * Time of year

    * Bridal Suite
    * Grooms Lounge
    * Tables, chairs, and black table linens
    * On-site venue manager and one venue planning consultation
    * Audio guest book
    * Warming kitchen

  * **$1,999/event**

    * In-house charger inventory

  * **$1,250/event**
