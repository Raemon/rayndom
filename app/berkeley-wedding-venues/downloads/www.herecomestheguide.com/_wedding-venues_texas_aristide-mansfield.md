## Why You'll Love This Place

##### A Here Comes The Guide Wedding Venue Review

Read More Show Less

  * **It was custom-built with weddings in mind.** Modeled after an 18th-century Italian villa, Aristide has all you’ll need for a smooth, memorable event. Romantic architecture and grounds, modern technology and amenities, ample parking, a state-of-the-art catering kitchen—they’ve thought of everything, so you don’t have to.
  * **Outdoor ceremonies are held in some amazing places.** You can exchange vows at the base of the grand staircase leading to the villa, in the lush gardens, under the covered loggia, or in front of a cozy fireplace on a secluded patio. 
  * **The Grand Ballroom is incredible.** This gorgeous room is hung with six-foot Tuscan chandeliers, and features Old World details, like intricate flooring and stacked-stone pillars. It also has a second-story balcony lined with arched alcoves that may be uplit in the color of your choice.
