# 11 Irresistibly Rustic Wedding Venues in Massachusetts

## 

****Massachusetts offers some exceptional—and scenic!—rustic wedding venues.** **Many include barns, which are very cool in their own right, and most are located on farms so you get the added benefit of a beautiful, natural setting. Whether you envision a casual celebration or a black-tie affair, you'll find a rustic wedding venue that fits the style of your event.
