###### Rental Fee

  * **$1,500–3,900/event**
  * Rental fee varies depending on: 
    * Time of year
    * Type of event
  * _Ceremony + reception rentals range $2,900–3,900 and include:_

    * 3.5- to 4-hour event time for ceremony & reception
    * Day-of coordinator and planning services with the event team
    * Use of the dressing suite 1.5 hours prior to the ceremony
    * Ceremony space with seating, tables, and a brick inlay runner
    * Rehearsal, plus access to the ceremony site for photos 1.5 hours prior to the event
    * Lavalier microphone, speaker system, and DJ/band setup
    * Dining tables, Chiavari chairs, linens, napkins, and votives
    * Setup & breakdown
    * Assorted décor, including chandeliers and twinkle lights, cake cutting sets, easels, potted plants, and more

_Ceremony-only rentals_ range $1,500–1,900 and include everything above EXCEPT dining tables & chairs. Use of the dressing suite for 1.5 hours prior is included.
