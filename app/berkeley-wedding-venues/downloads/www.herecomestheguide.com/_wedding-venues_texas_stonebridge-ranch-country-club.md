## Why You'll Love This Place

##### A Here Comes The Guide Wedding Venue Review

Read More Show Less

  * **From Victorian elegance to Texas chic** , this country club in the heart of McKinney offers two luxurious clubhouses with options for both indoor and outdoor ceremonies, as well as views of the lake and surrounding golf course.
  * **The spacious and classically modern Stonebridge Clubhouse** features a beautiful ballroom and airy covered terrace, while the comfortable Ranch Clubhouse includes vaulted ceilings with exposed beams and a sprawling patio with fire pit.
  * **On-site private event directors ensure a seamless day** , and the extensive wedding packages provide delicious in-house catering, linens and centerpieces, tables and chairs, a bartender, a wooden dance floor, cake cutting, and much more.
