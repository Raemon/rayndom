###  [ Lake Life is Back! Austin's 10 Best Lakeview Wedding Venues ](https://www.herecomestheguide.com/wedding-ideas/austin-lake-view-wedding-venues)

**Some of Texas’ most beautiful waterfront wedding venues aren’t on the Gulf coast—they’re on a lake!** Just a short drive from Austin, the lakes and surrounding areas offer a natural environment with woods, hills, and prairie land, not to mention plenty of outdoor recreation options (fishing, boating, swimming, scuba diving, picnicking, camping, and zip lining). Host your wedding at any of these Austin lakeview venues, and you can easily turn your celebration into a vacation!

* * *

[ ](https://www.herecomestheguide.com/wedding-ideas/outdoor-wedding-venues-in-austin)

###  [ Under the Big Texas Sky: 15 Outdoor Wedding Venues in Austin ](https://www.herecomestheguide.com/wedding-ideas/outdoor-wedding-venues-in-austin)

**Austin’s a great place for an outdoor wedding.** It offers a variety of venues and settings, and whether you celebrate at a Victorian Mansion, a resort, or a Sculpture Garden and Museum, nature will enhance your event.

* * *

[ ](https://www.herecomestheguide.com/wedding-ideas/austins-best-wedding-venues)

###  [ 11 of Austin's Best Wedding Venues ... and Why They Stand Out! ](https://www.herecomestheguide.com/wedding-ideas/austins-best-wedding-venues)

**Austin gets high rankings for so many things:** its outdoor attractions (parks, lakes and trails), fine restaurants, support of the arts, and overall quality of life. After checking out the city’s wedding venues, we’d have to say it’s a great place to get married, too. Here’s a selection of some of Austin’s best spots to tie the knot.

* * *

[ ](https://www.herecomestheguide.com/wedding-ideas/texas-unique-austin-wedding-venues)

###  [ 7 of Austin's Most Eclectic Wedding Venues ](https://www.herecomestheguide.com/wedding-ideas/texas-unique-austin-wedding-venues)

**Planning an Austin wedding and want to keep it a little weird?** From a former turn-of-the-century train station to a trendy boutique hotel on South Congress to a rustic-chic, eco-friendly ranch just outside the city limits, we've rounded up some of the most unique spaces this eclectic city has to offer.

* * *

[ ](https://www.herecomestheguide.com/wedding-ideas/texas-hill-country-wedding-venues)

###  [ 12 Texas Hill Country Wedding Venues with Rustic Flair - Boots, Barns, and Beauty ](https://www.herecomestheguide.com/wedding-ideas/texas-hill-country-wedding-venues)

**The Hill Country surrounding Austin may be known for its gorgeous scenery and thriving wine industry, but it’s also a mecca for rustic weddings.** Whether you’re planning a down-home celebration with a casual barbecue or a cowgirl-chic bash with a silver-and-crystal reception, you’ll find the natural beauty of the region on display at all of these venues.  


* * *

[ ](https://www.herecomestheguide.com/wedding-ideas/austin-mansion-wedding-venues)

###  [ 8 Austin Private Estate Wedding Venues Oozing With Southern Charm ](https://www.herecomestheguide.com/wedding-ideas/austin-mansion-wedding-venues)

**Elegant, romantic and very photogenic, Austin’s mansions are popular wedding venues for good reason.** There’s definitely something special about saying “I do” beneath a 100-year-old oak or sipping champagne on a shady veranda. Host your celebration at one of these historic places and you and your guests will be transported back to a more genteel time.
