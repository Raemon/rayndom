## Why You'll Love This Place

##### A Here Comes The Guide Wedding Venue Review

Read More Show Less

  * **Great Moose Lake surrounds nearly all of this lush, 100-acre estate** , whose open fields offer gorgeous views of the water, two private islands, and the distant mountains. It’s locally owned and operated, offers charming on-site accommodations, and there are plenty of vacation rental options nearby, too. 
  * **Wed at the lake’s edge,** then grab a drink from the adorable Anchor Bar before leading guests to stunning Wedding Barn. With its towering picture windows, vaulted ceiling, and huge sliding doors, it’s an impressive (and ultra-fun!) space for the ultimate party. 
  * **Many of your guests can be housed on the property,** between the main house, cottages, and two dressing suites that each boast sleeping quarters. The sweet Rehearsal Barn is available for intimate receptions, or for pre/post-wedding events.



### DEALS: _What discounts, special offers, or packages are available here?_

**Have a romantic, lakeside elopement at Grace on the Lake!****The Elopement package is $4,495 for up to 15 guests** and includes:

  * 24-hour access to the venue (noon–noon)
  * 1-night stay in the Farm House (up to six overnight guests)
  * Choice of a lakeside ceremony with a custom-built arbor or a ceremony on the Grand Barn Patio with views of the lake
  * Table & chairs
  * Outdoor firepit area with wood, lakeside hammocks, and a picnic area
  * Access to the activities building with kayaks, canoes, and paddleboards, as well as lawn games like corn hole, horseshoe, and giant jenga
  * Access to the beach, dock area, and walking trails



 _Elopements are available Sunday–Wednesday. Packages can be customized to fit a couple's specific needs. Please contact the venue for more details._
