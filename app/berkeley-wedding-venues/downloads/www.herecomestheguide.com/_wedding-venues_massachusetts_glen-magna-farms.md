### PRICES: _What's this going to cost?_

###### Rental Fee

  * **$6,000–9,800/event**
  * Rental fee varies depending on: 
    * Day of week
  * _The rental fee includes:_

    * Exclusive access to the grounds, and the mansion's first floor (including the foyer, drawing room, dining room, and veranda) and second floor (3 changing rooms)
    * Access to the venue 2 hours prior for wedding party photos
    * Ceremony rehearsal (if needed)
    * Sailcloth tent with clear side walls, lighting, dance floor, and heating as needed
    * Tables and fruitwood chairs

Venue fees are $6,000 Mondays–Thursdays, $8,200 Fridays & Sundays, and $9,800 Saturdays and holidays.

_A Family Membership to Danvers Historical Society required to rent the venue._




###### Meals (when priced separately)

  * **$88–125/person**
  * Meal pricing includes plated, buffet, or stations-style entrées, cake cutting and service, chocolate dipped fruits and fresh berries with cream, coffee & tea, and all tableware, linens, décor, and staffing.

Bar packages are additional, starting at $40/person for 4-hour service. Consumption and cash options available.
