## Why You'll Love This Place

##### A Here Comes The Guide Wedding Venue Review

Read More Show Less

  * **Elegant, contemporary, and sophisticated, this country club** provides picture-perfect backdrops for your celebration inside and out. The 36-hole golf club includes the Founders and Legacy courses as well as a state-of-the-art golf learning center.
  * **The pristine grounds feature sparkling water features** and gently rolling green hills. A patio at the rear of the clubhouse overlooks signature hole 18 and makes a gorgeous open-air space for a cocktail hour or for guests to mingle in the evening.
  * **Delicious menus showcasing local specialties and international choices** are offered with impeccable service. You can select from a variety of plated dinner, buffet, and action station options, plus hors d'oeuvres and artful reception table displays.
