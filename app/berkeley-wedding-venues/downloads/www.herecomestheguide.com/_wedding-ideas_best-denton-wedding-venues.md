# 4 Best Denton Wedding Venues

## 

**Denton, TX is a welcoming, innovative, and creative community with a friendly, small town atmosphere just a short drive from the big city.** Along with a vibrant entertainment culture—performing arts venues, museums, and music and film festivals, Denton boasts a multitude of outdoor activities-a water park, recreation centers, 30 parks, nearby lakes, and plenty of hike and bike trails. Check out these wedding venues if you’re planning your wedding in Denton, TX!
