# Bewitching Beauties: 6 Uniquely Salem Wedding Venues

## 

**For the couple searching for an extraordinary setting for their New England wedding, Salem is the perfect destination.** With its unique blend of history, charm, and mystique, you’ll find a bewitching array of venues fit for any style. From a beautifully preserved centuries-old church to a 350-year-old seaside cottage bordered by lush gardens, we’ve gathered some of the most distinctive locations that capture the magic and allure of this enchanting city.

## [The Coach House, Salem MA](https://www.coachhousesalem.com/)

[ ](https://www.coachhousesalem.com/)

This lovingly preserved 1879 former sea-captains home has been beautifully restored into a chic boutique hotel flush with modern amenities. Located less than a mile from the heart of Salem, [**The Coach House**](https://www.coachhousesalem.com/) is a charming hotel that offers 11 contemporary rooms that seamlessly blend historical charm with luxe furnishings and edgy design elements. Booking also comes with access to a personal concierge who can answer any questions regarding the hotel's amenities and offer suggestions on where to eat and explore!
