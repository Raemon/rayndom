### PRICES: _What's this going to cost?_

###### Rental Fee

  * **$7,200–11,700/event**
  * _The rental fee varies depending on the day of the week and includes:_

    * 12-hour exclusive access to the venue and property
    * Two dressing suites on different levels
    * Large catering prep kitchen
    * Event setup, breakdown, and cleaning
    * White or fruitwood chairs, table rounds, and cocktail tables
    * China, silverware, glass water goblets, and chargers in your color of choice
    * Linens (over 80 colors, in polyester and lamour satin) and napkins in color of choice
    * Use of the facility for a 1-hour rehearsal and portraits
    * On-site venue manager
    * And MORE!

There is an in-house rental department offering plenty of design & décor items for additional cost, including venue draping, tenting, archways and string lighting, centerpieces, mobile stages, and more!

Other add-ons include a photo booth, event coordination, staffing, and more!




###### Additional Info

  * Bar packages are an additional fee, ranging $20–26/person for 3 hour service. There is an additional 21% service charge.
