## Why You'll Love This Place

##### A Here Comes The Guide Wedding Venue Review

  * **For a dreamy Southern coastal wedding** , this country club located on the north side of Hilton Head Island n provides classic Southern grandeur. The majestic Old English-style clubhouse and verdant fairways are surrounded by lush pines and kissed with ocean breezes.
  * **Exchange vows on the sands of Dolphin Head Beach** or on the lawn beneath picturesque magnolia and palmetto trees. For your reception, you can choose from a number of elegant event rooms overlooking the gently rolling hills of the golf course, or opt for an outdoor location.
  * **A team of dedicated private event directors** will make the planning process easy and enjoyable. They’ll work with you to develop a distinctive custom menu, including from-scratch recipes and creative cocktails served by personal bartenders.
