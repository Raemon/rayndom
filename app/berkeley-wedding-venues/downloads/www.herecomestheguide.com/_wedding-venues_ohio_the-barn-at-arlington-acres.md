### PRICES: _What's this going to cost?_

###### Rental Fee

  * **$4,500–6,000/event**
  * _The rental fee includes:_

    * Space for the ceremony & reception
    * Exclusive acess to the full 120-acre property, including the barn and lawn by the silo
    * Use of two large, climate-controlled dressing suites
    * Venue management, on-site events team, and setup & cleanup
    * Ample, easily accessed outdoor parking and golf cart for guest transportation
    * Use of the venue for on-site engagement photos
    * Round, banquet, and cocktail tables, white ceremony chairs and mahogany reception chairs, cedar arbor, whiskey barrels, and a recycled wood bar with drink coolers
    * And more!
