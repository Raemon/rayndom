## Why You'll Love This Place

##### A Here Comes The Guide Wedding Venue Review

Read More Show Less

  * **Originally built in 1787,** this unique inn has undergone lots of growth throughout its 230-plus year lifespan. Now a designated historic landmark, its lovely rooms and grounds have become one of the area’s most beloved places to celebrate. 
  * **There are several dreamy spots for your ceremony,** and their indoor reception rooms all offer pretty views of the Lake Meone and the Inn’s surrounding gardens. Smaller (but equally charming) dining spaces are available for intimate affairs. 
  * **You’ll capture wonderful photos****throughout the property** as you stroll beside the lake, explore historic outbuildings, cobblestone paths, and gardens…there’s even a vintage carousel!
