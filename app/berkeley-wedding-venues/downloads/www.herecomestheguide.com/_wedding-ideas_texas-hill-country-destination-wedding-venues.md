# 12 Fabulous Destinations for your Hill Country Wedding Weekend

## 

**The Texas Hill Country is the perfect destination for a weekend wedding.** With its gorgeous scenery, a thriving wine industry (wine tasting, anyone?), and lots of other local attractions that will keep you and your guests entertained for days on end. From a luxurious waterfront resort with its own "Party Yacht" to a charming B&B surrounded by ancient oaks, these fabulous locations offer all the amenities you'll need for your extended celebration!
