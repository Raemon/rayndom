# 8 Awesome Wedding Venues In and Around Boulder

## 

**With picturesque mountain ranges and forests in every direction and a vibrant city at its heart, Boulder is an ideal location for a fabulous wedding..** Whether you want to exchange vows in a hidden oasis beneath towering redwoods, on the landscaped lawn of a historic mansion, or in an eclectic reimagined warehouse, you won’t have to look hard to find a stunning setting for an amazing celebration here.
