## Why You'll Love This Place

##### A Here Comes The Guide Wedding Venue Review

Read More Show Less

  * **Modeled after Old World stone architecture,** this charming chapel and its accompanying reception hall will have your guests feeling as if they’ve been transported to the heart of a Spanish villa.
  * **Guests approach the chapel’s little “island” via footbridge.** Once inside, they’ll take in views of the surrounding water and cascading fountain features through floor-to-ceiling arched cathedral windows. 
  * **Step across a courtyard to your stately two-story reception hall,** which boasts a bright, whitewashed interior and bountiful natural light. A covered portico and open-air terrace are perfect for cocktail hour.
