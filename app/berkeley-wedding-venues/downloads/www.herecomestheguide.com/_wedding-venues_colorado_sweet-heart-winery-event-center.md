## Why You'll Love This Place

##### A Here Comes The Guide Wedding Venue Review

Read More Show Less

  * **This family-run winery sits on eight pretty acres,** dotted with mature trees and boasting postcard-worthy panoramas of the the surrounding countryside. 
  * **The outdoor ceremony site is perfection,** with a stacked stone wall serving as your nature-inspired “altar.” A grove of towering trees provides dappled light and welcome shade. 
  * **Receptions are wonderful in the Barrel Room—** here, you’ll find a cozy, rustic-industrial vibe, with a soaring, vaulted ceiling, exposed rafters and ductwork, and racks of wine barrels along the walls.
