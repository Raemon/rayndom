### PRICES: _What's this going to cost?_

###### Rental Fee

  * **$3,000/event**
  * _The rental fee includes:_

    * Access to _The Parker Education Center, Private Outside Terrace,_ and _The Atrium_ 9am–midnight
    * Dressing suite
    * Use of, setup, and teardown of tables & chairs
    * Dance floor area
    * Screen & ceiling-mounted projector
    * Bar setup & teardown with licensed bartender
    * Wifi & parking

 _One reception permitted per day._




###### Ceremony Fee

  * **$250–700/event**
  * Outdoor ceremony rentals include a 2-hour time block, a 1-hour rehearsal, and coolers of bottled water for guests. Use of dressing rooms may be added for $100/room; chairs must be arranged with an outside vendor.

Seven unique gardens are available for rental. An indoor weather backup is only included if also renting the reception venue.

  * **20%**
