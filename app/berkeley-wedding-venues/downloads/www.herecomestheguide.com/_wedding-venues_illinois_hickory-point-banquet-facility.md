## Why You'll Love This Place

##### A Here Comes The Guide Wedding Venue Review

Read More Show Less

  * **This well-appointed municipally-run venue** features artfully landscaped grounds and views of the Hickory Point Golf Course. Guests will have no trouble finding lodging in nearby Decatur. 
  * **The ballroom boasts a soaring cathedral ceiling,** large windows that let in tons of natural light, and a flexible floor plan that can suit events of any size. 
  * **We absolutely love the Pavilion** —this fully-covered space features a gently peaked roof, brick accents, and "walls" made from weather-resistant screening that create an awesome indoor/outdoor feel.
