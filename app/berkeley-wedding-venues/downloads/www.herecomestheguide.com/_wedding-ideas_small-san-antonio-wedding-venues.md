# 4 San Antonio Wedding Venues Perfect for Micro Weddings

## 

**Intimate weddings can be just as fun, or more, than a larger celebration!** Whether you’re looking for a quaint venue on the River Walk or a romantic garden venue, San Antonio has a variety of wedding venues for your micro wedding. And as this list proves, a shorter guest list doesn’t mean having to compromise your vision for your perfect wedding day!

* * *

## [Sovereignty Ranch](https://www.herecomestheguide.com/wedding-venues/texas/sovereignty-ranch)

### Bandera, TX

[ ](https://www.herecomestheguide.com/wedding-venues/texas/sovereignty-ranch)

**Fab Features** : gorgeous, sun-kissed Hill Country ranch retreat on 200 acres that encompass farmland, lawns and stone patios, and a burbling creek • farm-to-table cuisine features ingredients directly sourced from the on-site regenerative farm and ranch • spend the weekend enjoying the mineral pond, sauna, farm restaurant, and unique, modern lodging in tiny homes, glamping tents, farmhouses, and more!

**Catering:** In-house, BYO   
**Price:** $ • $$

## [Mokara Hotel and Spa](https://www.herecomestheguide.com/wedding-venues/texas/mokara-hotel-spa)

### San Antonio, TX

[ ](https://www.herecomestheguide.com/wedding-venues/texas/mokara-hotel-spa)

**Fab Features:** luxurious hotel alongside San Antonio’s historic River Walk • ceremonies on the Rooftop Terrace with sweeping views of the sparkling cityscape, followed by receptions in the sophisticated Saddlery Ballroom • incredible culinary team with cuisine inspired by the region’s culture

**Catering:** In-house   
**Price:** $ • $$ 

[ Click Here for More Info](https://www.herecomestheguide.com/wedding-venues/texas/mokara-hotel-spa)

### San Antonio, TX

**Fab Features:** exchange vows beneath the branches of a sprawling ancient oak or under a lovely grapevine-and ivy-covered gazebo • enjoy dinner al fresco under the open-raftered ceiling, hung with a delicate crystal chandelier and fairy lights • intimate events are wonderful in the on-site restaurant, which also boasts a spacious bridal suite and cozy wine cellar for wedding party prep

**Catering:** In-house   
**Price:** $ • $$
