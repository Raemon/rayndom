## Why You'll Love This Place

##### A Here Comes The Guide Wedding Venue Review

Read More Show Less

  * **Set in a sweeping valley near the Paonia Reservoir** at the base of Ragged Mountain, its iconic namesake, this family-owned working cattle ranch occupies some of the prettiest land in western Colorado. 
  * **Arrive by horse-drawn carriage to your meadow ceremony spot,** where views stretch for miles in every direction, and there’s a spacious, rustic-elegant barn with a stage and bar for the reception. 
  * **Guests are encouraged to dry camp on the beautiful property,** and there are cozy lodgings in the barn, as well. More accommodations, both hotels and vacation rentals, are available in nearby Marble and Paonia.
