## Why You'll Love This Place

##### A Here Comes The Guide Wedding Venue Review

Read More Show Less

  * **Centrally located in the heart of downtown** , this venerable club dating back to 1883 offers a sophisticated and welcoming atmosphere for celebrations both large and small. You'll be delighted by the attentive, personable staff and charmingly European ambiance.
  * **The flexible spaces can accommodate a variety of uses.** The grand Main Dining Room offers a lovely backdrop for a formal dinner, while the spacious Main Lounge is perfect for a ceremony, cocktail hour, or dance reception.
  * **Luxe accommodations are available on site.** The boutique guest rooms and suites feature upscale furnishings and amenities such as access to the Athletic & Wellness Center, which includes an Olympic-sized pool, yoga, Pilates, massage services, and more.
