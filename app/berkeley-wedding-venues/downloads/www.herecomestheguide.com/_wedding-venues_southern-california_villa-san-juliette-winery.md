### PRICES: _What's this going to cost?_

###### Rental Fee

  * **$8,500–10,500/event**
  * Rental fee varies depending on: 
    * Day of week
    * Time of year
  * _Rental fees include:_

    * Use of the Farmhouse for wedding party prep
    * 1-hour ceremony rehearsal
    * Site manager and tasting room staff
    * Access to the tasting room, front lawn & patio, and barrel room
    * Photo access to the vineyard and upstairs lounge
    * Catering kitchen and food prep area
    * Tables and natural wood folding chairs  


May–October, the rental fee is $10,500; November–April, the rental fee is $8,500. All Friday & Sunday dates are $8,500.

Overnight lodging is available in the Farmhouse for an additional fee.
