## Why You'll Love This Place

##### A Here Comes The Guide Wedding Venue Review

Read More Show Less

  * **Constructed between 1887 and 1906 in the Richardsonian Romanesque style** , this landmark building is exudes stately elegance. Enjoy bird's-eye views from the mezzanine, and snap photos among the soaring marble columns.


  * **Nothing beats the size and splendor of the six-story Rotunda** , adorned with 37 stained-glass windows, a magnificent "Father of Waters" marble statue, and a sweeping staircase for ceremonies. 


  * **The downtown location offers easy access to hotels, parking and transit.** You get exclusive use of the space on the day of your event, a private changing room, and striking photo backdrops. Plus, you can choose from several catering/menu options.
