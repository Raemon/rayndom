## Why You'll Love This Place

##### A Here Comes The Guide Wedding Venue Review

  * **Just two miles from Wells Beach, this coastal Maine venue** combines championship golf course beauty with elegant modern amenities, offering lovely spaces set amid rolling fairways and easy guest access from Portland or Boston.
  * **The elegant Webhannet Ballroom** serves as your main celebration space, with floor-to-ceiling windows showcasing the verdant greens and picturesque sunsets. Al fresco ceremonies are lovely in the nearby Wedding Garden, a secluded spot surrounded by mature trees and natural foliage.
  * **All-inclusive packages** **eliminate guesswork** thanks to transparent pricing that covers everything from delicious in-house catering to dedicated planning support. Want to stay and play? Their sister property Stage House Inn nearby makes it easy for guests to extend their Maine weekend.
