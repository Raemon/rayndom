# Intimate Affairs: 19 Colorado Venues Perfect For Your Micro Wedding

## 

**If you’re dreaming of an intimate affair amongst****mountain views, alpine lakes, and wildflower meadows, Colorado may be the perfect place for your micro wedding or elopement.** With unique locations spanning across the state, you’re sure to find just what you’re envisioning for your special day. So ditch the extravagant bash and celebrate your love story surrounded by just those who matter most at one of the beautiful venues Colorado has to offer!
