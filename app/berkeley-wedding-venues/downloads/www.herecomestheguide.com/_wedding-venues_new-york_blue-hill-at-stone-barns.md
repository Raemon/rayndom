### PRICES: _What's this going to cost?_

###### Rental Fee

  * **$16,000–55,000/event**
  * Rental fee varies depending on: 
    * Day of week
    * Time of year
    * Guest count
  * For parties of 175 guests or fewer, the venue fee ranges $16,000–19,000.

For larger weddings, the venue fee ranges $40,000–55,000 and includes a buyout of the restaurant. Saturdays from May–October and certain holiday weekend Sundays are reserved for these larger format events.




###### Food & Beverage Minimum

  * **$45,000–85,000/event**
  * Food & Beverage minimum varies depending on: 
    * Day of week
    * Time of year
    * Guest count
  * F&B minimums for events under 175 guests range $45,000–55,000.

F&B minimums for events over 175 guests range $60,000–85,000.




###### Meals (when priced separately)

  * **$445–495/person**
