## Why You'll Love This Place

##### A Here Comes The Guide Wedding Venue Review

Read More Show Less

  * **Nestled in the heart of charming and historic Anoka,** this Victorian-style theater is located on the second floor of the Old Milk Factory, right along the banks of the Rum River. 
  * **The interior has the feel of a turn-of-the-century Victorian home,** with vintage wallpaper, tapestries, rich hardwood floors, a balcony, and even a fireplace right on the stage. 
  * **Your rental also includes use of the cozy Side Room,** with its dazzling pressed tin ceiling, The Studio, an awesome spot for an intimate cocktail lounge, and The Bridal Room, which boasts luxe furnishings and windows overlooking the river.
