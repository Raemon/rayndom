# 8 Historic Maryland Wedding Venues That Will Sweep You Off Your Feet

## 

**Searching for a historic landmark or memorable estate in Maryland is like hitting the mother lode.** And lucky for you, many of these treasures—some dating as far back as colonial times—are available for special events. So check out our list and have fun as you explore these great wedding venues.

* * *

## [Historic Worsell Manor](https://www.herecomestheguide.com/wedding-venues/maryland/historic-worsell-manor)

### Warwick, MD

[ ](https://www.herecomestheguide.com/wedding-venues/maryland/historic-worsell-manor)

**Fab Features** : this 300-year-old former stomping ground of George Washington is an exquisite example of Georgian architecture • say "I do" on a green pasture overlooking the woodlands, or on the front lawn with the stately brick manor behind • move to the Barn for your reception, with its soaring, open-raftered ceiling and large doors that open to the grounds

**Catering:** Preferred list   
**Price:** $ • $$ • $$$

## [The George Peabody Library](https://www.herecomestheguide.com/wedding-venues/maryland/the-george-peabody-library)

### Baltimore, MD

[ [ Photography by Brea ](https://bybrea.com/) ](https://www.herecomestheguide.com/wedding-venues/maryland/the-george-peabody-library)

**Fab Features** : considered one of the world's most beautiful libraries, with over 300,000 volumes of books to create a truly storied setting • the Reading Room is encircled with six tiers of stately cast-iron columns and ornate, gold leaf-embellished railings • cocktails in the adjoining Exhibit Gallery, which hosts rotating exhibitions in its warm, elegant space

**Catering:** Preferred list   
**Price:** $$ • $$$ 

[ Click Here for More Info](https://www.herecomestheguide.com/wedding-venues/maryland/the-george-peabody-library)

## [Antrim 1844](https://www.herecomestheguide.com/wedding-venues/maryland/antrim-1844)

### Taneytown, Maryland

[ [ Dani Leigh Photography ](https://danileighphotography.com/) ](https://www.herecomestheguide.com/wedding-venues/maryland/antrim-1844)

**Fab Features** : grand, 1844 mansion and agrarian estate nestled in the foothills of the Catoctin Mountains • exquisite al fresco ceremony options include a streamside meadow, a formal English Tea Garden, and even a glass-enclosed Garden Room that affords a view of the grounds • versatile packages with hands-on coordination make planning easy, and delectable menus boast locally farmed Maryland and Pennsylvania ingredients  


**Catering:** In-house   
**Price:** $ • $$ 

[ Click Here for More Info](https://www.herecomestheguide.com/wedding-venues/maryland/antrim-1844)

## [Milton Ridge](https://www.herecomestheguide.com/wedding-venues/maryland/milton-ridge-historic-chapel-and-reception-hall)

### Clarksburg, MD

[ [ Leigh Anne Brader Photography ](https://www.leighannebraderphotography.com/) ](https://www.herecomestheguide.com/wedding-venues/maryland/milton-ridge-historic-chapel-and-reception-hall)

**Fab Features** : charming ceremonies in the Wedding Chapel or in the landscaped gardens • this full-service wedding venue offers everything from minister to makeup artist available for hire • the fully restored 1875 log cabin serves as a peaceful Bridal Cottage for getting ready  


**Catering:** In-house   
**Price:** $ • $$

**Fab Features** : 18th-century Georgian mansion in the heart of Annapolis • stunning ceremonies in front of the charming summer house, on the Chippendale-style bridge, or beside the sparkling pond • 2-acre oasis of lovely gardens and manicured lawns bounded by brick walls and lush trees

**Catering:** Preferred list, BYO

[ Mary Bell Photography  ](https://www.herecomestheguide.com/wedding-venues/maryland/whitehouse-caterers-at-overhills-mansion-circa-1897)

**Fab Features** : fully restored Victorian landmark originally built as a wedding gift for a beloved son • say your vows under a simple wood trellis embellished with flowers, then retire for dinner and dancing in the Grand Ballroom • plan your perfect reception menu with the culinary experts at Whitehouse Caterers

**Catering:** In-house

### Pasadena, MD

[ [ Sara Vars Photography ](https://www.saravars.com/) ](https://www.herecomestheguide.com/wedding-venues/maryland/knollview-house)

**Fab Features** : lovely, private estate with spectacular, unparalleled views across Chesapeake Bay • exchange vows on the patio or the waterfront lawn, followed by cocktails and lawn games or an intimate indoor dinner • stunning photo ops around the Dutch-style manor home with its crisp, whitewashed siding and impressive gambrel roof

**Price:** $$ • $$$
