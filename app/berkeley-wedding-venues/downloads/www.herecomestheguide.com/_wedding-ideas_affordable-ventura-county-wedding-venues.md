# Big Day Savings: 5 Affordable Ventura County Wedding Venues

## 

**With bustling neighborhoods bordered by both wooded mountain countryside and fun-in-the-sun coastline, Ventura County embraces the best of the California lifestyle.** SoCal wedding couples have discovered this diverse destination that's much easier on the budget than those pricier neighbors to the North and South (we're looking at you, Malibu and Santa Barbara!). Here are some of our fave Ventura County affordable wedding venues!
