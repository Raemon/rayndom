## Why You'll Love This Place

##### A Here Comes The Guide Wedding Venue Review

Read More Show Less

  * **The quintessential Seattle waterfront destination** just blocks from downtown, Bell Harbor offers unparalleled views of some of Seattle’s most notable sights: Puget Sound and Elliott Bay, the Olympic Mountains, Mt. Rainier, and the city’s gorgeous skyline. It's an unforgettable setting that can accommodate 25 to 300 lucky guests for indoor & outdoor weddings and other special events, like rehearsal dinners, tea ceremonies, and grand cultural celebrations.
  * **Their cuisine features great regional ingredients.** The chefs at Bell Harbor believe that food is love; they excel in executing custom menus that accommodate the dietary preferences and restrictions of the wedding couple and their guests, all showcasing fresh, local, and sustainable ingredients.
  * **Experienced wedding specialists will connect you with a network of industry experts**. The team here is dedicated to ensuring your special day is stress-free and truly memorable; whether it's exploring various layout options, offering vendor recommendations, or customizing their generous packages, coordinators will work to ensure your day reflects your wishes.
