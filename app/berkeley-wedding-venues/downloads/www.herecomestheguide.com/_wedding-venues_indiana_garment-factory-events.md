### PRICES: _What's this going to cost?_

###### Rental Fee

  * **$2,750–7,500/event**
  * Rental fee varies depending on: 
    * Day of week
    * Time of year
    * Space reserved
  * _Rental fees include:_

    * All-day access to the venue from 10am–midnight (or 10am–10pm on Sunday)
    * Two on-site suites, available for the entirety of the rental
    * 1-hour access for ceremony rehearsal, and 1-hour access for engagement photos
    * Complimentary tasting for four
    * Bar setup for reception and Lounge Bar (bartender is $150 additional)
    * Tables, banquet chairs, brown resin chairs, linens, and napkins in your choice of color
    * Staffing, plus setup & teardown
    * Pet-friendly!

**Full venue rentals** include exclusive use of the entire venue, and range $4,000–7,500. Holidays range $8,500–10,000.

**Partial venue rentals** include the first floor only, for fewer than 100 guests, and range $2,750–4,750.

  * **$500/event**
  * Outdoor ceremonies for 150 guests or less are available for an additional fee of $500.




###### Service Charge or Gratuity

  * **22%**
