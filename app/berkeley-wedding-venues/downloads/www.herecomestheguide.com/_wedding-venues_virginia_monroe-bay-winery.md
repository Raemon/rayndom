## Why You'll Love This Place

##### A Here Comes The Guide Wedding Venue Review

Read More Show Less

  * **Perched on the edge of Monroe Bay,** this quaint winery and cidery is surrounded by six lush acres, and affords fabulous photo opportunities along the water and around the grounds.
  * **Celebrations are a blast in the vintage strawbale barn,** with its lofty, wood-paneled ceiling and ample natural light. Its unique wall accents, made with recycled wine bottles, are a crowd favorite. 
  * **Pair your dinner menu with some of their fantastic wines and ciders—** not only are they lovingly and expertly crafted, the bottles themselves are works of art that will add a colorful pop to any tablescape.
