### DEALS: _What discounts, special offers, or packages are available here?_

**Catey Heritage Farm is a working flower farm!** There are a number of unique benefits to this:

  * Overnight stays in a cabin beside a picturesque wildflower field
  * Packages that include florals sourced from the farm
  * Affordable DIY flower options



**Are you A-OK with DIY? Inquire about a "flower party"** — buy buckets of flowers from the farm, and the day prior to the wedding, work with the owner to create simple arrangements & bouquets.

### PRICES: _What's this going to cost?_

  * **$2,500/event and up**
  * _The rental fee includes:_

    * Lawn games and assorted décor (vases, candles, table runners, etc.)
    * Event coordinator to assist with timelines and day-of management
    * Staff to assist with parking, setup, and teardown

Micro-weddings for up to 75 guests start at $1,800. Other events, like showers and parties, start at $100/hour.
