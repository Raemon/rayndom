### DEALS: _What discounts, special offers, or packages are available here?_

****Intimate Wedding Package** | $2,400**  
This special elopement or vow renewal package is priced for up to 10 guests; additional guests are $35/person up to 30 total. It includes:  


  * Private ceremony space at a signature sunset viewing location
  * Non-denominational officiant
  * 6-inch cake and sparkling wine toast
  * Signature amenity in guest room
  * $100 dining credit (perfect for breakfast in bed the next day!)



_The booking of a 1-night stay is required with this package, and is an additional cost. Not available Saturdays or holidays. Inquire for details!_

### PRICES: _What's this going to cost?_

###### Rental Fee

  * **$5,500/event**
  * _Rental fees include_ :

    * Ceremony set with white chairs and a fruited water station
    * Reception set with round tables, wood dining chairs, and a teakwood dance floor
    * Linens, napkins, silver flatware, china, and crystal stemware
    * Custom menu planning with the catering team
    * Complimentary casita for the newlyweds on the wedding night
    * Discounted room rate for guests
    * Complimentary valet parking
    * 15% discount on the Resort's Adventure Center and 10% discount on spa treatments



###### Package Prices

  * **$175–320/person**
  * _All packages include:_

    * Cocktail hour with passed hors d'oeuvres and 1-hour unlimited bar
    * Plated dinner service
    * Cake cutting and garnish
    * Sparkling wine toast and wine service with dinner

Some packages may also include passed signature cocktails during cocktail hour, late night snacks, wine pairings, flatbreads, and more!  
  
In packages where bar is not included, beverage pricing ranges $20–28/person per hour.

  * There is a F&B minimum of $18,000 for events held May–October.
