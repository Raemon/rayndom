## Why You'll Love This Place

##### A Here Comes The Guide Wedding Venue Review

Read More Show Less

  * **With its ideal location in South Charlotte,** this classic, welcoming club is loaded with traditional charm and has a top-notch, caring events team dedicated to flawless celebrations. 
  * **There’s a sweet, rustic-chic ceremony gazebo** on the clubhouse back lawn, surrounded by flowering foliage and offering views of the lush surrounding grounds. 
  * **The lodge-inspired clubhouse**(and its gorgeous ballroom) boasts tons of stylish design details, like a stacked stone fireplace, rich wood trusses, and glass-doored wine cases.



### DEALS: _What discounts, special offers, or packages are available here?_

****Intimate wedding packages** for up to 50 people ****are available Fridays and Sundays, starting at $6,000**. _Packages include:_

  * 7–10 hours of venue access for the ceremony + reception
  * Floor length linens
  * Gold or silver chargers and votives
  * Passed or stationary hors d'oeuvres
  * Buffet or plated dinner
  * Beer & wine or house bar
