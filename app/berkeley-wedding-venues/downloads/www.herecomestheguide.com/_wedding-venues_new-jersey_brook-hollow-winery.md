## Why You'll Love This Place

##### A Here Comes The Guide Wedding Venue Review

Read More Show Less

  * **This family-run winery sits on a beautiful piece of property** at the heart of the Delaware Water Gap. It offers sweeping views of the surrounding hills and farmland, but is an easy 90-minute drive from New York City. 
  * **You can see the vineyard's 7,000 grape vines from your ceremony spot** beneath a romantic wooden arbor. A nearby covered porch and surrounding lawn is a great place for cocktails and games. 
  * **Your reception hall is especially special:** Traditional sliding doors open to reveal the fabulous barn, with its all-wood interior, romantic lighting, and hand-built farm tables.
