### PRICES: _What's this going to cost?_

###### Rental Fee

  * **$6,000–7,000/event**
  * Rental fee varies depending on: 
    * Day of week
  * _The rental fee includes:_

    * Exclusive access to the venue
    * Setup & cleanup
    * Use of their _Something Borrowed_ room of décor
    * Tables & Chiavari chairs
    * Dressing suite
    * Day-of coordinator & on-site team members to assist the night of the wedding
    * Outside bar area with a licensed & insured bartender
    * And more!



###### Package Prices

  * **$20,900–23,595/event**
  * The _All-Inclusive Option A_ package is $20,900 and includes:

    * Signature buffet dinner & appetizers
    * Bar & beverage station (couples may choose three wines & three beers from the venue list)
    * Cake
    * Florals for the wedding party & parents of the wedding couple
    * Floral table centerpieces & fresh arrangements for the ceremony aisle
    * Wedding DJ/C
    * Photographer
    * 1-hour wedding rehearsal
    * Wedding planner/day-of coordinator
    * Table linens & dinnerware
    * Champagne pouring & cake cutting

The _All-Inclusive Luxe_ package is $23,595 and includes everything in _Option A_ PLUS upgrades to photography, cake, bar & beverage station, dinner, and florals.
