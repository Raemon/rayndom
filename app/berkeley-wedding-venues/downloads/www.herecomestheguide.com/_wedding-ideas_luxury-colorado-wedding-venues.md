# 6 Luxurious Colorado Wedding Venues

**If you're dreaming of a Colorado wedding with more of the "chic" and less of the "rustic," you need a venue with high style and ample amenities—and we've got you covered.** From five-star resorts with a ranch-chic vibe to European-style hotels with award-winning design packages to exclusive mountaintop retreats with thousands of acres to explore, these venues marry Colorado's jaw-dropping surroundings with world-class service and offer a uniquely luxurious setting for your nuptials.
