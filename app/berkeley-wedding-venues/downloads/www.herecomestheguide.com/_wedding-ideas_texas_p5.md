###  [ 3 Gorgeous East Texas Barn, Ranch, and Farm Wedding Venues ](https://www.herecomestheguide.com/wedding-ideas/ranch-barn-farm-east-texas-wedding-venues)

**Ahhhh…it’s amazing how a little bit of country can make your wedding positively enchanting.** You'll usually find beautiful grounds and charming indoor spaces. And barns are a major attraction, be they weathered and historic or updated with all the modern amenities you could want. These East Texas venues offer a variety of ranch/farm settings, and many boast other assets like a gazebo, pond, or outdoor chapel. Whether you envision a place that's full-on rustic or one that leans more towards elegant, one of these is sure to fit you perfectly.

**With picturesque countryside in every direction, East Texas is an ideal location for an outdoor wedding.** From a ranch and vineyard to a charming retreat and private estate, East Texas has some of the state's best al fresco wedding venues.

**Want a one-of-a-kind setting for a trendy, eclectic wedding?** Houston is home to some fabulous wedding venues that combine industrial spaces with chic, modern amenities. They’re often historic and always filled with interesting elements, like original columns, brick walls, exposed ductwork, industrial lighting…even great views and cool artwork.

**San Antonio boasts fabulous outdoor wedding venues for every taste.** Whether you envision exchanging vows on lush landscaped grounds surrounding an Italian-inspired villa, amidst the artful ambiance of an urban sculpture garden on the Riverwalk, or on a serene ranch overlooking rolling hills, you're sure to find your dream al fresco setting among these picturesque places.

###  [ Say Goodbye to DIY! 7 All-Inclusive Wedding Packages: San Antonio TX Wedding Venues ](https://www.herecomestheguide.com/wedding-ideas/san-antonio-all-inclusive-wedding-venues)

**If you don't feel like handling most of the wedding details yourself, choose a place that will take care of many of them for you.** San Antonio TX is home to a variety of wedding venues that are all-inclusive, from a century-old stone chapel and Victorian hotel to a pavilion in the vineyards and historic train depot.

* * *

[ ](https://www.herecomestheguide.com/wedding-ideas/unique-houston-wedding-venues)

###  [ Dare To Be Different: 8 Unique Houston Wedding Venues ](https://www.herecomestheguide.com/wedding-ideas/unique-houston-wedding-venues)

**Nicknamed “Space City”, Houston is not only the largest city in Texas, it's also big on out-of-the-ordinary wedding venues.** From a historic theatre to an aviation museum, it's home to unique places that will appeal to couples looking for something completely different.

* * *

[ ](https://www.herecomestheguide.com/wedding-ideas/all-inclusive-houston-wedding-venues)

**Not into DIY? These all-inclusive Houston wedding venues will de-stress your big day by taking care of just about everything for you!** You'll have plenty of event space options, from a garden or elegant ballroom to something completely different—a Wild West setting.

**Whether you want to get married against a backdrop of grapevines, on the shore of a lake, or in a fragrant herb garden, you'll find these outdoor wedding options and more in and around Dallas.**

###  [ 17 Nonprofit Wedding Venues Where Your Big Day Can Benefit Others ](https://www.herecomestheguide.com/wedding-ideas/nonprofit-wedding-venues)

**It's great when your wedding can do some good long after your Big Day.** If you choose to host your celebration at one of these beautiful nonprofit venues, your rental fees will also benefit and empower others in a variety of ways, from supporting underprivileged and at-risk kids to saving animals to building up your own community. So say "I do" to a good cause and let your special day leave a lasting impression on more than just your wedding guests.

###  [ 12 Fabulous Destinations for your Hill Country Wedding Weekend ](https://www.herecomestheguide.com/wedding-ideas/texas-hill-country-destination-wedding-venues)

From a luxurious waterfront resort with its own "Party Yacht" to a charming B&B surrounded by ancient oaks, these fabulous locations offer all the amenities you'll need for your extended celebration!

Their options include world-class museums, great regional dining, music, dancing, rodeo, Nascar, and even learning about the city's history.
