# Outdoor Weddings

**Ahhhh... the Great Outdoors.** Is there a more perfect backdrop for your I DOs than the beauty and splendor of Mother Nature?

Fortunately, you don't have to rough it to have a spectacular alfresco event. Below you'll find lists of the [best outdoor wedding venues](https://www.herecomestheguide.com/wedding-venues/search/outdoor) across the U.S., offering all the conveniences of an indoor affair. And we've got tons of inspiration and ideas to help you pull off an incredible open-air celebration, too.

* * *

[ ](https://www.herecomestheguide.com/wedding-ideas/outdoor-sacramento-wedding-venues)

###  [ Celebrating Al Fresco: 16 Outdoor Wedding Venues In and Around Sacramento ](https://www.herecomestheguide.com/wedding-ideas/outdoor-sacramento-wedding-venues)

**Whether you want to get married in the secluded wilderness or a hidden urban garden, you'll find these outdoor wedding options and more in and around Sacramento Gold Country.** Wineries, flower-adorned inns, tranquil courtyards, elegant estates—they're all here and within an easy drive of the Sacramento airport and the Greater Bay Area.

* * *

[ ](https://www.herecomestheguide.com/wedding-ideas/orange-county-outdoor-wedding-venues)

###  [ Celebrating Al Fresco in Orange County: 13 Cool Places for an Outdoor Wedding ](https://www.herecomestheguide.com/wedding-ideas/orange-county-outdoor-wedding-venues)

**Blessed with fabulous weather, proximity to the coast, cool ocean breezes, and an abundance of large estates with gorgeous grounds** , Orange County is a natural for outdoor weddings. So whether you want to celebrate right on the beach, up in the hills, or even in the middle of the water, here are some great venues to check out.

* * *

[ ](https://www.herecomestheguide.com/wedding-ideas/new-york-rustic-wedding-barns-farms-historic-homes)

###  [ New York's Rustic Wedding Gems: 18 Captivating Barns, Farms, and Historic Homes ](https://www.herecomestheguide.com/wedding-ideas/new-york-rustic-wedding-barns-farms-historic-homes)

**With picturesque sites pre-dating the Revolutionary War, upstate New York is a treasure trove of historic farms, barns, and country manor homes.** Surrounded by the awesome natural beauty of the Hudson River, crystalline lakes, and Catskill and Adirondack Mountain peaks, it's a four-season wonderland that's trending as a destination getaway and mecca for great organic food, wine, and sightseeing.

* * *

[ ](https://www.herecomestheguide.com/wedding-ideas/columbia-gorge-wedding-venues)

###  [ 4 Columbia River Gorge Wedding Venues With Spectacular Mountain, River, and Forest Views ](https://www.herecomestheguide.com/wedding-ideas/columbia-gorge-wedding-venues)

**Designated a National Scenic Area—the largest in America!—The Columbia River Gorge is also one of the 7 Wonders of the Pacific Northwest.** In this spectacular canyon, the Columbia River cuts through the Cascade mountains, forming a boundary between Washington and Oregon while creating amazing cliffs, as well as mountain, river, and forest views everywhere you look. All this natural beauty is on display at the following wedding venues, where you won't need much in the way of decorations.  


* * *

[ ](https://www.herecomestheguide.com/wedding-ideas/rustic-colorado-wedding-venues)

###  [ Country Glamour: 15 Rustic Colorado Wedding Venues ](https://www.herecomestheguide.com/wedding-ideas/rustic-colorado-wedding-venues)

**With snow-capped mountain peaks, lush Aspen and evergreen forests, and sweeping valley vistas, Colorado certainly lives up to its reputation as the jewel of the American West.** And for couples seeking a rustic, outdoorsy wedding venue, there's no better place to start than the Rocky Mountain state. Quintessential barns, ranches, and farms are located in every corner of Colorado, making it easy to find a perfect fit...the only tricky part is choosing just one! 

* * *

[ ](https://www.herecomestheguide.com/wedding-ideas/outstanding-outdoor-san-diego-wedding-venues)

###  [ 18 Outstanding Outdoor San Diego Wedding Venues ](https://www.herecomestheguide.com/wedding-ideas/outstanding-outdoor-san-diego-wedding-venues)

**San Diego is one of the top wedding destinations in the entire country.** Its incredible weather, beaches, ocean vistas, and lush gardens make it hard to resist, especially for an al fresco celebration. 

* * *

[ ](https://www.herecomestheguide.com/wedding-ideas/woodsy-northern-california-wedding-venues)

###  [ 26 Woodsy Wedding Venues in Northern California ](https://www.herecomestheguide.com/wedding-ideas/woodsy-northern-california-wedding-venues)

**If you want nature, in the form of glorious trees, to play a role in your wedding, these venues could be just what you're looking for.** From a cathedral in the forest created by a grove of redwoods to a canopy of spreading oaks aglow with bistro lights at night, they'll all add a woodsy beauty to your celebration.

* * *

[ ](https://www.herecomestheguide.com/wedding-ideas/woodsy-southern-california-wedding-venues)

###  [ 27 Woodsy Wedding Venues in Southern California ](https://www.herecomestheguide.com/wedding-ideas/woodsy-southern-california-wedding-venues)

**Whether it's a single spreading oak, a dappled forest, or a majestic grove of redwoods, trees impart an irresistible beauty to a wedding.** All of the Southern California venues we've highlighted below showcase Mother Nature's arborial handiwork, and any one of them might have just the outdoor setting you're looking for.

* * *

[ ](https://www.herecomestheguide.com/wedding-ideas/oregon-willamette-wedding-venues)

###  [ 8 Gorgeous Outdoor Willamette Wedding Venues ](https://www.herecomestheguide.com/wedding-ideas/oregon-willamette-wedding-venues)

**Oregon's Willamette region offers every type of outdoor wedding setting imaginable, all within an easy drive of Portland, Salem, and the coast.** There are creeks, forests, vineyards, farms, ranches, hilltops and valleys—if you're looking to get married surrounded by nature's bounty, you're certain to find lots to love at these romantic Willamette Valley spots.

* * *

[ ](https://www.herecomestheguide.com/wedding-ideas/colorado-aspen-wedding-venues)

###  [ Rocky Mountain High... 8 Aspen Wedding Venues You'll Love! ](https://www.herecomestheguide.com/wedding-ideas/colorado-aspen-wedding-venues)

**Part history, part fantasy, this old mining town-turned-posh resort and celebrity magnet has earned Aspen a reputation as the ultimate Rocky Mountain high.** Combined with the city’s richly diverse arts, culture and dining scene, it’s ideal for a destination wedding. Here are some Aspen venues we think you should check out!

* * *

[ ](https://www.herecomestheguide.com/wedding-ideas/jersey-shore-wedding-venues-waterfront)

###  [ Bay Breezes and Boardwalk Views: 16 Jersey Shore Waterfront Wedding Venues ](https://www.herecomestheguide.com/wedding-ideas/jersey-shore-wedding-venues-waterfront)

**The Jersey Shore, with its long history as the East Coast's favorite playground, is home to a wealth of fantastic places to celebrate...especially if you're looking to wed on the waterfront.** Choices include both quaint, historic spots that highlight the area's serene natural landscapes, and modern spaces that tap the Shore's vibrant culture and nightlife. Here's a selection of some of the region’s best venues.

* * *

[ ](https://www.herecomestheguide.com/wedding-ideas/affordable-wedding-venues-in-wine-country)

###  [ Affordable Wine Country Weddings: 12 Napa and Sonoma Venues We Love! ](https://www.herecomestheguide.com/wedding-ideas/affordable-wedding-venues-in-wine-country)

**The Napa/Sonoma Wine Country is famous for its wines and gorgeous scenery, but it’s also renowned as a wedding mecca.** And although hosting your Big Day here can be pricey, you’ll be happy to know that the region is also home to more affordable venues. Here’s a list of places that are easier on your wallet!

* * *

**If nature is an essential element of your wedding vision, start your search by exploring these terrific Houston venues.** From rustic and quaint to Shabby Chic and Old World Spanish, each one has its own distinct personality.

* * *

[ ](https://www.herecomestheguide.com/wedding-ideas/georgia-garden-wedding-venues)

###  [ 10 Breathtaking Georgia Garden Wedding Venues ](https://www.herecomestheguide.com/wedding-ideas/georgia-garden-wedding-venues)

**If you're dreaming of an outdoor wedding among blossoms, vines, and shade trees, Georgia is a great place to get married.** Thanks to its temperate weather and a Southern knack for making all things beautiful, the Peach State is home to some of the country's most glorious gardens.

* * *

[ ](https://www.herecomestheguide.com/wedding-ideas/northern-virginia-country-barn-farm-wedding-venues)

**It’s amazing how a little bit of country can make your wedding positively enchanting.** Whether it’s on a farm, in a barn, or at a secluded private estate, these scenic Northern Virginia venues set a glorious stage for your celebration.

* * *

[ ](https://www.herecomestheguide.com/wedding-ideas/best-outdoor-virginia-wedding-venues)

###  [ Mother Nature Showin' Off: 14 of the Best Outdoor Virginia Wedding Venues ](https://www.herecomestheguide.com/wedding-ideas/best-outdoor-virginia-wedding-venues)

**If you want to be one with nature on your wedding day, it couldn’t be easier!** Winery…resort…historic estate…botanical garden—here’s an irresistible handful of Virginia’s best outdoor wedding venues.

* * *

[ ](https://www.herecomestheguide.com/wedding-ideas/best-virginia-wedding-venues)

###  [ From Barns to Ballrooms: 11 of the Best Virginia Wedding Venues ](https://www.herecomestheguide.com/wedding-ideas/best-virginia-wedding-venues)

**Virginia is home to a wide range of exceptional wedding venues, from botanical gardens and historic landmarks to luxury resorts and fabulous farms.** We’ve selected some of our favorite places that showcase the state’s wealth of event locations, and you just might want to check them ALL out!

###  [ Under the Big Texas Sky: 15 Outdoor Wedding Venues in Austin ](https://www.herecomestheguide.com/wedding-ideas/outdoor-wedding-venues-in-austin)

**Austin’s a great place for an outdoor wedding.** It offers a variety of venues and settings, and whether you celebrate at a Victorian Mansion, a resort, or a Sculpture Garden and Museum, nature will enhance your event.

* * *

[ ](https://www.herecomestheguide.com/wedding-ideas/connecticut-waterfront-wedding-venues)

###  [ 16 Cool Connecticut Waterfront Wedding Venues ](https://www.herecomestheguide.com/wedding-ideas/connecticut-waterfront-wedding-venues)

**Connecticut's diverse waterfront venues, spanning its captivating coastline, tranquil lakes, and scenic rivers, offer an array of irresistible backdrops for your special event.** Imagine exchanging vows at a charming riverfront museum, celebrating at a historic lakeside inn, or hosting a grand reception at a stunning 1900 mansion overlooking Long Island Sound – these waterfront wedding venues each have their own style, but possess the unique quality that being on the water bestows.

**If you have your heart set on getting married in the great outdoors, then you’ll want to check out these Washington wedding venues!** Whether you want to celebrate on the water, overlooking majestic mountains, in a garden, or on a ranch or farm— Mother Nature is definitely front and center at all of these glorious locations.
