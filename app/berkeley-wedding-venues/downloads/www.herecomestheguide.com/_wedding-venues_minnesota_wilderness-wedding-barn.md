### DEALS: _What discounts, special offers, or packages are available here?_

**$500 discount off****full-service wedding/reception package** if either of the wedding couple is an active duty law enforcement officer, firefighter, military personnel, or any honorably discharged vet.

* * *

**Ceremony-only rentals** start at $2,500, which includes 4-hour use of the dressing rooms and ceremony site of choice (excludes the barn). _Available Sunday–Thursday._

**Reception-only rentals** start at $4,500, which includes 8-hour rental of the barn and outdoor cocktail area, plus décor, staffing, and rentals (excludes dressing suites).

### PRICES: _What's this going to cost?_

###### Rental Fee

  * **$5,500–13,500/event**
  * Rental fee varies depending on: 
    * Event duration
    * Type of event
  * _The rental fee for a ceremony & reception is $5,500, which includes:_

    * Venue access from 8am–midnight (or until 11pm Sunday–Thursday)
    * Two dressing suites: one set with mimosas & charcuterie board and the other with whiskey & cigars
    * Wedding garden with gazebo and unique floral swing
    * Ceremony & design coordinators, plus décor setup & teardown
    * Indoor/outdoor grain bar and furnished cocktail patio
    * Outdoor photo booth area, yard games, and staff-maintained bonfire
    * Reception barn with draping and string lighting
    * Antique furnishings, harvest tables, Chiavari chairs, and table décor & design pieces
    * On-site staff, parking attendants, and end-of-event cleanup

 _Weekend-long rentals are $13,500,_ which includes access Friday 4–10pm, Saturday 8am–midnight, and Sunday 9am–noon, with the ability to host rehearsal dinner/farewell brunch on-site.
