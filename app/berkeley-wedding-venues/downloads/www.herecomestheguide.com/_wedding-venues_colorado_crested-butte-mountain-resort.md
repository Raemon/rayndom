##### A Here Comes The Guide Wedding Venue Review

  * **The backdrops are so stunning, your photos are sure to go viral.** Spectacular views combine with unique reception venues, professional staff, and first-class lodging. Whether you're hosting 50 or 500 for a formal or casual celebration, the panoramas of the Rocky Mountains, covered in lush scenery or crowned in snow, are truly inspiring.
  * **Their site Ten Peaks, offers 360-degree vistas** surrounded by meadows of wildflowers and a tiered, ceremony amphitheater. An adjacent patio is perfect for cocktails and a tented dinner reception. For a small fee, add in the Umbrella Bar, where floor-to-ceiling windows and a deck overlook Mt. Crested Butte's 12,000-foot peak.
  * **To get a true alpine experience, exchange vows at the "Red Lady"** , followed by an easy hike, shuttle or chairlift ride to the newly renovated Uley's Cabin for an intimate dinner...or host a larger gathering on the lawn. Need more space? Go big at the Mountaineer Square Ballroom, complete with a lounge and expansive patio.
