  * **Beautifully transformed from a working mill to a stunning event venue** , The Room has honored its history and retained original details like classic brickwork and original floor, but it's been updated with a chic metropolitan flair.
  * **The owners' unique approach to coordinating all facets of your wedding** ensures that it will be highly personalized. A design consultant creates an inspiration board to customize color, look, and flow just for you.
