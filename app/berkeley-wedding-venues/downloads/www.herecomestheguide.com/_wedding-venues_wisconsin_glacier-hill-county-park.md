### PRICES: _What's this going to cost?_

###### Rental Fee

  * **$350–5,700/event**
  * Rental fee varies depending on: 
    * Day of week
    * Time of year
    * Space reserved
  * **Weekend Rentals**  
Weekend rentals run 10am Friday–10am Sunday, and include use of both the Hall and Chapel. Pricing ranges $3,500–5,700 depending on the time of year.

**Single Day Rentals**  
From Sunday–Thursday, the Hall and Chapel are rented separately. The Chapel ranges $350–400 and the Hall ranges $900–1,600 for single-day use.

* * *

_The rental fee includes:_

    * Exclusive access to the Hall, Chapel, dressing area, and select outdoor spaces
    * Free park entrance for you and your guests, including all park features and public amenities
    * Patio area with picnic tables, grill, and fire pit
    * Climate control, dimmable lighting, and two TVs with HDMI in the Hall
    * Bar area, catering prep area, walk-in cooler, and chest freezer
    * Ample parking
    * On-call park staff



###### Additional Info

  * Overnight accommodations are available in adorable on-site cabins and houses!

_Cabins_ sleep 2–6 guests, and range $75–90/night; the _Ranger House_ sleeps 10-12 guests and ranges $175–300/night. There is a 2-night minimum.

_The weekend overnight package_ is available May–October, and includes a 3-day, 2-night stay for 30–40 guests in 9 cabins, 1 house, and 1 additional shelter, with full access to trails, Friess Lake, and all park amenities.
