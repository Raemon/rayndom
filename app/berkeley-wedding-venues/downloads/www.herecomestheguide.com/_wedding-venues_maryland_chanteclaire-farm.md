### PRICES: _What's this going to cost?_

###### Package Prices

  * **$175/person and up**
  * * * *

**Packages start at $175/person** with a 125-guest minimum.

* * *

_Packages include:_

    * Exclusive use of the venue from Friday morning–Sunday morning
    * Fully decorated event spaces, plus access to 4,000+ décor and centerpiece items
    * Catering services, including artisan cheese & fruit hors d'oeuvres, canoe salad bar, rolls, and entrées with starch & vegetables
    * Customized wedding cake
    * Iced water, iced tea, coffee & tea service, soft bar, and mixers
    * Driver & golf cart, jumbo lawn games, selfie station, and bonfire after party
    * DJ and emcee services
    * Planning services, to include day-of coordination, tasting & planning events, multiple meetings, unlimited contact access, access to the online Chanteclaire Planning Hub, and more!
    * Tables, Chiavari and ceremony chairs, floor-length linens, napkins, uplighting, dinnerware, glassware, silverware, heaters, and lighting throughout

* * *
