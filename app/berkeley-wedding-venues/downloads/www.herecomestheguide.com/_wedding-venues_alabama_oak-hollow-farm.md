### PRICES: _What's this going to cost?_

###### Rental Fee

  * **$2,250–4,500/event**
  * _Barn and Lodge rentals include:_

    * Exclusive use of the selected venue starting at 8am on the wedding day
    * Continuous food & beverage service during the reception
    * Tables, chairs, linens, and acrylicware for the meal
    * Burlap table runners or puddle pieces, lanterns, and wood pieces
    * Access to the grounds for bridal and engagement photos
    * Tubs & ice for alcohol
    * Setup & teardown

**The Lodge** | 60-guest maximum | $2,250 + tax  
**The Barn** | 75-guest minimum | $4,500 + tax




###### Ceremony Fee

  * **$1,000/event**
  * Ceremony fees include benches for guest seating, an arbor of your choice, and a rehearsal (weather permitting). 




###### Meals (when priced separately)

  * **$30/person and up**
  * Menu pricing includes appetizers, salad, entrée, sides, bread, and water, lemonade, coffee, and sweet & unsweet tea. 

Bar setup & bartender fees are additional, starting at $125/bartender.




###### Additional Info

  * There is an 18% service fee on catering, and an 8% tax on the final balance (excluding gratuity).
