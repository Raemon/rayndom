### PRICES: _What's this going to cost?_

###### Rental Fee

  * **$1,395–2,995/event**
  * Rental fee varies depending on: 
    * Day of week
    * Time of year
  * _Rental fees include:_

    * 4-hour reception time
    * Venue coordinator 
    * Tables, white Chiavari chairs, black or white linens & napkins, dinnerware, glassware, and silverware
    * Room setup & cleanup
    * Access to the grounds for photos

Add-on a cocktail hour and/or additional reception time for $300/half-hour. Upgrade options include linens, centerpieces, uplighting, arbors, wall décor, cake cutting, sparkler send-off, and in-house DJ/MC services.

* * *

Two complimentary get-ready rooms are available for free starting at 10am; inquire for day-of brunch and beverage packages (additional get-ready time before 10am is $100/hour).  





###### Ceremony Fee

  * **$895/event**
  * Outdoor ceremony chair rental is $3/chair; rehearsal and day-of coordination is $295.




###### Package Prices

  * _The All-Inclusive Reception Package_ is an add-on to the Rental Fee to make planning easy. Pricing starts at $13,695 for 150 guests and includes:

    * Appetizer station and buffet dinner
    * Gold bar package (beer, wine, and liquor)
    * Cake cutting service
    * Bottle of champagne
    * Servers and bartenders
    * Gold, silver, or black chargers, centerpieces, silk florals, and room uplighting
    * DJ and light show

Additional guests are $80/person over 21 and $60/person under 21. Pricing does not include the ceremony fee or the rental fee.




###### Meals (when priced separately)

  * **$28/person and up**
  * Buffet-style entrées start at $28/person, and are served with salad, two sides, and fresh rolls. Passed hors d'oeuvres start at $11/person; open bar packages range $18–25/person for 4-hour service. Bartender and server fees are additional.
