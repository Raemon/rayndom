## Why You'll Love This Place

##### A Here Comes The Guide Wedding Venue Review

Read More Show Less

  * **This former Brooklyn factory has been transformed into a unique event venue** that exudes both a minimalist and luxury feel. Centrally located in Williamsburg, just steps away from picturesque McCarren Park, it offers indoor and outdoor spaces and stunning Manhattan skyline views.
  * **The Main Space is filled with light thanks to three large skylights.** Dining and dancing take place on the polished concrete floor beneath pendant lights and mini chandeliers hanging from the 22-foot ceiling. You'll also appreciate the state-of-the-art Bose Surround Sound System.
  * **You can exchange vows on the landscaped Rooftop Terrace** that overlooks the glittering Manhattan skyline. During the festivities, guests can also enjoy some fresh air in the secluded courtyard with its 15-foot-high walls and vertical garden. If they prefer to relax indoors, there's a private mezzanine lounge with floor-to-ceiling windows that let them observe all the action on the first floor.
