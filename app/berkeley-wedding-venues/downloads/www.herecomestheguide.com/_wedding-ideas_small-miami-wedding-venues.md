# 9 Stunning Venues For Your Small Miami Wedding

## 

**Miami has so much to offer when it comes to planning an intimate wedding** —wonderful hotels, tropical gardens, secluded beaches, and an overall feel that's a bit more serene than its bustling neighbors. Whether you're hoping for al fresco vows by the water, in a luxurious ballroom, or something in between, these exceptional venues have smaller wedding spaces that are big on style.
