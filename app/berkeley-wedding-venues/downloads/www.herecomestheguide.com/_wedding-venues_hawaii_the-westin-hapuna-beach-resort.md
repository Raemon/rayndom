## Why You'll Love This Place

##### A Here Comes The Guide Wedding Venue Review

  * **Breathtaking views of the Kohala coastline and the Pacific Ocean** are part of wedding ceremonies on the grassy lava bluff called Turtle Point.
  * **Indoors, the rich native woods and high ceiling of the spacious Hapuna Ballroom** give receptions a refined elegance.
  * **The themes of welcome and renewal at the resort have historic origins.** The Westin Hapuna Beach Resort is set on land blessed with underground springs. In ancient times they bubbled to the surface after heavy rains, creating natural artesian fountains that brought respite and relief to weary travelers. _Hapuna,_ which means "spring of life," is a word that often resonates with newlyweds starting their new life together.
