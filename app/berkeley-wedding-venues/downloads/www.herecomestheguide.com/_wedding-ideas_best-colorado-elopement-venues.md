# Road Trip Weddings: 14 Of The Best Elopement Destinations In Colorado

## 

**Searching for a gorgeous place to elope, but don't want to get on a plane or travel far from home?** Thanks to Colorado's picturesque landscape you can exchange vows with a majestic Rocky Mountain backdrop, surrounded by towering trees in a National Forest, on the shores of an impossibly blue alpine lake, among sandstone-capped spires, or in a historic mansion-turned-B&B. Whatever your wedding day style is, adventure awaits at these perfect local hotspots!

* * *

## 

* * *

## COLORADO SPRINGS

* * *

_Located at the base of the Rocky Mountains, this popular destination is known for it's beautiful and unique scenery, like the glacier-carved Pikes Peak, the iconic red-sandstone rock formations in the Garden of the Gods Park, and _Pike's National Forest.__

* * *

## [Garden of the Gods Resort and Club](https://www.herecomestheguide.com/wedding-venues/colorado/garden-of-the-gods-collection)

### Colorado Springs, CO

[ ](https://www.herecomestheguide.com/wedding-venues/colorado/garden-of-the-gods-collection)

**Fab Features:** extraordinary resort with unparalleled mountain views • variety of on-site accommodations from basic rooms to three-bedroom casitas • fantastic amenities including a decadent wellness spa

**Catering:** In-house   
**Price:** $ • $$ • $$$ 

[ Click Here for More Info](https://www.herecomestheguide.com/wedding-venues/colorado/garden-of-the-gods-collection)

Photography | [Moose and Bear](https://www.mooseandbearphotography.com/blog/2018/6/20/paint-mines-desert-elopement/)

## ROCKY MOUNTAIN NATIONAL PARK

* * *

_One of the U.S.'s most visited national parks, this 415-square-mile natural playground boasts gorgeous mountains, forests, and alpine tundra. For summer elopements plan between June - September when the snow is mostly melted._

**Catering:** In-house, Preferred list   
**Price:** $ • $$
