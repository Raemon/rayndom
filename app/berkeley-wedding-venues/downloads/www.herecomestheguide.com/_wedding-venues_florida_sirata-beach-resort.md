## Why You'll Love This Place

##### A Here Comes The Guide Wedding Venue Review

  * **The white sands of St. Pete Beach are visible from virtually every inch of this classic,** family-friendly resort, which features ample accommodation options, tons of activities, and easy access to all the area’s top destinations.
  * **Weddings of all sizes and styles can be accommodated throughout the resort’s generous group of venues,** from private ceremonies on the sand to grand affairs in the Royal Palm Ballroom or beachside Coconut Palm Pavilion.
