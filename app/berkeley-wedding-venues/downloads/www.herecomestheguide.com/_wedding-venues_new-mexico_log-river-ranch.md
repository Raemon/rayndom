### PRICES: _What's this going to cost?_

###### Rental Fee

  * **$4,600–7,900/event**
  * Rental fee varies depending on: 
    * Day of week
    * Guest count
  * _Rental fees include:_

    * 12-hour venue rental
    * Bridal Barn dressing suite
    * Choice of ceremony site and barn reception space
    * Tables, chairs, linens, napkins, and choice of dishware (classic white china or mismatched vintage china)



###### Package Prices

  * **$8,500/event and up**
  * Package prices vary depending on: 
    * Day of week
    * Guest count
    * Package chosen
  * _Micro wedding pricing shown is for up to 50 guests and includes:_

    * Everything included in venue rental fees, PLUS:
    * Officiant  

    * Photographer
    * Bouquet & boutonniere as well as centerpiece florals
    * Small wedding cake
    * Coordination services

Customization is available: add lodging $2,500/night (2-night minimum); catering which starts at $50/person (buffet style, two appetizers, salad, entrée with two sides); and/or upgrade florals.




###### Additional Info

  * _Optional Add-Ons:_

    * Cocktail hour by the river
    * Beverage station, barware, and snack & food presentation (food & alcohol not included)
    * Assorted ceremony and reception décor
    * Overnight lodging in the 4-bedroom cabin for 10–14 people
    * Coordination, partial, or full-service planning packages
