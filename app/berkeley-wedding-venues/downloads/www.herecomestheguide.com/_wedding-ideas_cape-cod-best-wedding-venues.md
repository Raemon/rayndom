# 6 Of The Best Places To Get Married in Cape Cod

## 

**With its pristine beaches, grassy dunes, shimmering water, and verdant countryside, Cape Cod is actually a series of quaint villages, each with its own unique personality.** Collectively they offer a variety of settings, from luxury resorts to quiet getaways to sandy shores, so there’s something for every couple to love here!

Weekend Weddings

**Catering:** In-house
