## Why You'll Love This Place

##### A Here Comes The Guide Wedding Venue Review

Read More Show Less

  * **Sweet rustic elegance:** Enjoy the peaceful country club atmosphere and vintage-chic styling at this ballroom and garden adjacent to the Timber Creek Golf Course. Planning is seamless with their award-winning catering team and the state-of-the-art amenities.
  * **Charming outdoor ceremony site:** The Rose Garden Arbor features bright splashes of colorful flowers and lush greenery. A pillared portico shades the seated guests and aisle, which leads to a wooden ceremony arch. There are also dressing areas nearby for both wedding parties.
  * **Spacious and versatile ballroom:** With a neutral palette, large dance floor, and band/DJ staging, the ballroom has what you need to get the party started. Everything from the tables to the linens and silverware is included, and the uniformed staff will take care of the details.

  * **$250–5,000/event**
  * The rental fee ranges $250/hour Sunday–Friday to $400/hour Saturday, both with a 5-hour maximum. Saturday rentals also require a 100-guest minimum.

All rentals include tables, chairs, trained & uniformed serving staff, and more.

  * **$800–1,000/event**
  * _The ceremony fee includes:_

    * Easel for an engagement photo
    * Ceremony rehearsal time
    * Dressing areas for the wedding couple

  * **$36–46/person**
  * Menu pricing includes a salad, buffet or plated entrées with chef's choice of starch & vegetables, warm sourdough rolls with butter, and coffee & tea.

Bar pricing is additional, and a $500 minimum is required.
