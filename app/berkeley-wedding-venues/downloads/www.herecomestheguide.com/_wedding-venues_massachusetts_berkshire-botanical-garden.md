## Why You'll Love This Place

##### A Here Comes The Guide Wedding Venue Review

Read More Show Less

  * **Spanning twenty-four acres and housing dozens of unique areas,** this beautifully curated horticultural destination boasts walking paths, ponds, trees, meadows, and an ever-changing array of colorful, romantic gardens. 
  * **Choose your favorite backdrop for vows** from among so many stunning, lush, outdoor spaces, then celebrate beneath a grand tent on the lawn. Intimate affairs can be accommodated in the historic 18th-century Center House.
  * **Your rental fees help support the garden.** What a cool way to celebrate the start of your lives together—knowing your wedding helped to keep the BBG's mission of education and preservation alive for future generations of garden lovers!
