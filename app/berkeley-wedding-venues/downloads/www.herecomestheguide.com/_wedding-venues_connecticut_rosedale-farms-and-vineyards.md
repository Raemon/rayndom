### PRICES: _What's this going to cost?_

###### Rental Fee

  * **$7,500/event**
  * _Rental is for a Friday, Saturday, or Sunday and includes:_

    * The venue from 4pm to midnight.
    * Use of the 60' x 90' tent (with sides if needed) with perimeter bistro lighting
    * The vineyard for your ceremony
    * The use of lawn area for cocktail hour as well as any games you’d like to set up
    * All tables (60" round, 8' banquet, sweetheart, high tops for cocktail hour) and 200+ chairs
    * Prep tent, lighting, and tables for your caterer
    * Electricity with backup generators
    * Two upscale handicapped-accessible port-a-toilets
    * Two flatbed ’35 & ’47 trucks, cast iron clawfoot tub, and five oak wine barrels
    * Two fire pits that the venue will start and tend (firewood included)
    * Designated parking area
    * Two staff members always on site



###### Additional Info

  * A minimum purchase of $500 of the venue's wine, flowers, or produce is required.
