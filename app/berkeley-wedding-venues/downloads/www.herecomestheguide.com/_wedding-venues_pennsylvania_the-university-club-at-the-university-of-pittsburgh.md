## Why You'll Love This Place

##### A Here Comes The Guide Wedding Venue Review

Read More Show Less

  * **Located in the heart of the campus** and just 10 minutes from downtown Pittsburgh, this university social club is housed in a classically styled Hornbostel limestone building that was built in 1923 and thoroughly renovated.
  * **The spacious Rooftop Terrace provides gorgeous views** of the city's skyline and landmarks. Inside, the opulent grand ballroom features brilliant crystal chandeliers and an intricately designed 20-foot ceiling.
  * **A dedicated on-site venue coordinator** will make sure everything proceeds flawlessly. Elegant and convenient wedding package amenities include ivory linens and napkins, coffee and tea service, cake cutting and plating, and a private wedding party refresher room.
