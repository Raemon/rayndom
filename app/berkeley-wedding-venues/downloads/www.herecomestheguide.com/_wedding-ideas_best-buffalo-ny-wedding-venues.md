# Best of Buffalo: 4 Beautiful Wedding Venues In Western New York

## 

**On the hunt for the perfect wedding venue in Buffalo?** As the second largest city in the state of New York, this bustling metropolis is home to a stunning downtown riverfront, award-winning restaurants, famous parks and landmarks, and a ton of unique wedding venues**.** From rustic-style shindigs beneath the stars to elegant receptions in a historic hotel and everything in between, you’re bound to find your perfect backdrop for your big day in and near Buffalo.

## [The Annex](https://www.herecomestheguide.com/wedding-venues/new-york/the-annex)

### Chaffee, NY

[ ](https://www.herecomestheguide.com/wedding-venues/new-york/the-annex)

**Fab Features** : chic, modern farmhouse-inspired event center outfitted with gleaming cement floors, a stylish built-in-bar, white shiplap walls, and a shady canopied pavilion • ceremonies beside the pretty pond, on a picturesque patio, before a lovely fieldstone fireplace, or anywhere else on the sprawling property • fabulous inclusions like sparkling water features, indoor & outdoor fireplaces, lounge furniture, lit pathways, and much more

**Catering:** Preferred list, BYO   
**Price:** $ • $$
