### PRICES: _What's this going to cost?_

###### Rental Fee

  * **$3,500/event and up**
  * Rental fee varies depending on: 
    * Day of week
    * Space reserved
    * Guest count
    * Event duration
  * Rental fees start at $3,500 for a 6-hour event with 100 guests, and increases by $1,000 per 50 guests, up to 400 guests at $9,500. Discounts may be available for weekend events.

_Venue rentals include_ banquet tables, gold Chiavari chairs, ivory house linens and napkins, wine glasses, and a bartender & busser.




###### Food & Beverage Minimum

  * **$25/person and up**
  * Food & Beverage minimum varies depending on: 
    * Guest count
    * Package chosen
    * Wine or hard liquor upgrades

  * F&B minimums start at $25/person for guests age 21+, and $12/person for guests under 21.
