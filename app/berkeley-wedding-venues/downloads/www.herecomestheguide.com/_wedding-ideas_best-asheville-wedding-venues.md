# 9 of Asheville's Best Wedding Venues

## 

**From majestic mountains to a vibrant downtown full of historic architecture and charm, Asheville is full of fabulous spaces to tie the knot.** So whether you're dreaming of a swoon-worthy soiree surrounded by nature or a boho-chic bash in a converted warehouse, we've got the perfect site to match your wedding day style here!

* * *

## [Twisty Maple Events](https://www.herecomestheguide.com/wedding-venues/north-carolina/twisty-maple-events)

### Fletcher, NC

[ [ Jen Yuson Photography ](https://www.jenyuson.com/) ](https://www.herecomestheguide.com/wedding-venues/north-carolina/twisty-maple-events)

**Fab Features** : idyllic, woodsy setting perched on a hill just outside of Asheville, with magnificent views of the seasonally-changing leaves and mountains beyond • receptions in an expansive, clear-top tent for all-weather celebrations • overnight lodging for two nights in the cozy, fully furnished farmhouse is included in all wedding packages

**Catering:** Preferred list, BYO   
**Price:** $ • $$ 

[ Click Here for More Info](https://www.herecomestheguide.com/wedding-venues/north-carolina/twisty-maple-events)

## [The Greenhouse at Ross Farm](https://www.herecomestheguide.com/wedding-venues/north-carolina/the-greenhouse-at-ross-farm)

### Candler, NC

[ ](https://www.herecomestheguide.com/wedding-venues/north-carolina/the-greenhouse-at-ross-farm)

**Fab Features** : beautiful, locally owned and sustainable farm with 22 greenhouses that boast the largest variety of plants in WNC • intimate gatherings with lush tropical plants as your built-in décor... year-round! • snap photos among the plants, in the charming boho-style swing chairs, beside the picturesque ponds, and amid the working farm's pretty landscape

**Catering:** BYO   
**Price:** $ 

[ Click Here for More Info](https://www.herecomestheguide.com/wedding-venues/north-carolina/the-greenhouse-at-ross-farm)

## [JuneBug Retro Resort](https://www.herecomestheguide.com/wedding-venues/north-carolina/junebug-retro-resort)

### Weaverville, NC

[ ](https://www.herecomestheguide.com/wedding-venues/north-carolina/junebug-retro-resort)

**Fab Features:** 50-acre lush woodland resort just 12 minutes north of Asheville • creekside ceremonies beneath a canopy of walnut trees, or something more intimate in the dreamy treehouse • guests can rent one of 10 restored 1950s campers and spend the weekend hiking, exploring, or lounging in the hammocks

**Catering:** In-house, BYO   
**Price:** $ • $$ 

[ Click Here for More Info](https://www.herecomestheguide.com/wedding-venues/north-carolina/junebug-retro-resort)

### Cashiers, NC

[ ](https://www.herecomestheguide.com/wedding-venues/north-carolina/the-village-green-of-cashiers)

**Fab Features:** spectacular 13-acre nonprofit park facility • the rustic-chic Lewis Hall is designed with a 30-foot ceiling, a stained concrete floor, two garden courtyards, and massive hand-carved beams connected with mortise and tenon joinery • gorgeous al fresco celebrations on the expansive event lawn backed by the stunning Blue Ridge Mountains 

**Catering:** Preferred list   
**Price:** $ • $$

**Fab Features:** European-style resort with dramatic mountain vistas • event spaces include an all-white wedding garden, an apple orchard, and a rustic trout pond with fountain and bridal bridge • the Orchard House is designed with a soaring pine wood ceiling and massive glass doors that open to lush English-style gardens
