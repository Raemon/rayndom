## Why You'll Love This Place

  * **The simple, natural beauty of this special spot will shine throughout the day,** with 360 degree unobstructed views of Lake San Cristobal and Sleeping Ute Peak from its home on the lush valley floor. 
  * **Host a ceremony overlooking the water,** with cocktails, dinner, and dancing in a meadow under the stars. Should the weather take an unexpected turn, a spacious covered area will let you keep the celebration going. 
  * **An on-site collection of curated accessories and embellishments (altars, wine barrels, rustic chairs, and more) are at your disposal,** as is a dressing suite large enough to accommodate your entire bridal party.
