### PRICES: _What's this going to cost?_

###### Ceremony Fee

  * **$350–575/event**
  * Ceremony fee varies depending on: 
    * Whether room flip is needed

  * The fee includes 90-minute ceremony rehearsal, ceremony, and chair setup.




###### Package Prices

  * **$1,325–22,069/event**
  * Package prices vary depending on: 
    * Day of week
    * Time of year
    * Guest count
    * Package chosen
  * * * *

**Venue + Décor packages range $1,325–5,795** and include:  


    * Upper Ballroom, Lower Ballroom, or the Castle (additional fee of $395)
    * 6-hour time block between 4 and 11pm
    * Two dressing areas
    * Décor package, including centerpieces, backdrop, sparklers/bubbles/bells for exit, and more
    * Private event planning meetings and coordination
    * Use of all grounds and buildings for photography
    * Tables, Chiavari chairs, warming kitchen, house sound system, and wireless mic

**All-Inclusive packages range $4,543–22,069** and include everything in the Venue + Décor package PLUS:

    * Full service catering (buffet-style lunch or dinner, or floating reception)
    * Floral package, to include bouquets, boutonnières, and corsages
    * 3-tier wedding cake
    * Full-service event planning



###### Additional Info

  * Add-on options include wedding planning packages, DJ/MC services, photo strip stations, live music, and alcohol/bar packages (starting at $480 for beer & wine, and $530 for full bar).
