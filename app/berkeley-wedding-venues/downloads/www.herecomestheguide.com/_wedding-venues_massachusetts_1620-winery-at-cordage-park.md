## Why You'll Love This Place

##### A Here Comes The Guide Wedding Venue Review

Read More Show Less

  * **Once a rope factory that produced rigging for sailing ships,** this stunning brick building has been artfully transformed into a working winery and event space. Tucked in historic Cordage Park on Plymouth’s waterfront, it offers a taste of history with a stylish rustic twist. 
  * **In the building’s Grand Ballroom,****you’ll find a soaring ceiling,** dramatic crystal chandeliers, dark wood plank flooring, and a huge corner fireplace. Just off the Ballroom, you’ll find the Main Bar, with its custom braided rope detailing and inviting fireside seating. 
  * **The Barrel Room is a wonderful spot for your ceremony,** with its backdrop of stacked barrels surrounding a cozy fire feature. This room also has its own dedicated bar, making it a great option for intimate receptions or rehearsal dinners.
