### DEALS: _What discounts, special offers, or packages are available here?_

**Lavender Shores Farm offers Mini Reception Celebrations!**  
**Mini receptions for up to 100 guests** start at $2,000 and include access to the barn noon–6pm, tables, chairs, and linens. _Additional time can be added at $300/hour. Contact the venue for more details._  


### PRICES: _What's this going to cost?_

###### Rental Fee

  * **$4,000/event**
  * The site fee for a weekend wedding is $4,000, which includes use of the barn, tables, and chairs from 9am–midnight.
