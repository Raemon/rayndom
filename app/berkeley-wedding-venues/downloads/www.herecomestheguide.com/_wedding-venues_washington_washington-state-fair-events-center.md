## Why You'll Love This Place

  * **You'll have a blast capturing photos around these classic fairgrounds** , where you'll find fountains, vintage signage, adorable fairway concessions booths, and even a beautiful antique carousel. 
  * **Exchange vows in the lush Planting Patch beneath a towering ivy-covered arch** , then move to the Fair Farm, a traditional red barn with an open-beamed ceiling, quaint shuttered windows, and glittering chandeliers. 
  * **If you're looking for an all-indoor space, consider the elegant Fair View Club** , with its delicate wood millwork, stone fireplace, and picture windows overlooking the fairgrounds.
