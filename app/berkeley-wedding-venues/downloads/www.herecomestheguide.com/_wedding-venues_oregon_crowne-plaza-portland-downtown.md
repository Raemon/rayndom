## Why You'll Love This Place

##### A Here Comes The Guide Wedding Venue Review

Read More Show Less

**• Two Ballrooms to choose from give you options for both your ceremony and reception.** Both happily feature high ceilings, crystal chandeliers and upscale appointments. Plus, there’s a Fireside Plaza if you prefer to celebrate outdoors.

**• Northwest-inspired dishes reflect the flavors of Portland.** It’s fitting that in a city known for a wide range of tastes, the on-site catering team has put together a particularly diverse and flexible menu.

**• Access to local attractions is a breeze.** With public buses and shuttles nearby, it’s easy to get to the trendy, Pearl District…or just go exploring on one of the complimentary bikes the hotel keeps for its guests.
