## Why You'll Love This Place

##### A Here Comes The Guide Wedding Venue Review

Read More Show Less

  * **With an enchantingly lush southern Vermont location** and buildings that span from the 1700s to today, this historic resort features a host of distinctive venues, a full range of spa, dining, and golf services, and ample lodging to house even the longest of guest lists.
  * **Warm weather weddings are a dream at the Meadow House,** whose spacious lawn and custom, peaked tent overlook a lily pond and panorama of the Green Mountains. The 1811 Lawn also boasts a sweeping mountain view, and is situated near the home of Abraham Lincoln’s granddaughter. 
  * **Crystal chandeliers and floor-to-ceiling windows fill the opulent Colonnade Ballroom with beautiful light,** while the Pond Pavilion, with its open rafters and wraparound window walls, sets a romantic scene for intimate Manchester, Vermont celebrations.
