## Why You'll Love This Place

##### A Here Comes The Guide Wedding Venue Review

Read More Show Less

  * **You can book a Maine destination wedding here year-round.** The staff at this arts center located in the state's western mountains can help you plan a sparkly winter gathering, a spring fling, a lush summer soirée, or a golden fall celebration.
  * **It has two charmingly vintage timber-frame barns.** The Maine Room, with towering windows offering forest views, can accommodate both your ceremony and dinner. And the 200-year-old Quisisana Barn, featuring twinkle lights and a bar, is ready for your cocktail hour and dancing.
  * **A wedding planner is included in all of their packages** , as are tables, chairs, and linens, whether you go with just the basics or the "We Do It All" option. You and your guests will also enjoy gourmet comfort food—buffet or family-style—plus appetizers.



### DEALS: _What discounts, special offers, or packages are available here?_

**Early Bird Special: Receive a 10% discount when you pay half the estimated cost of your wedding at the time of booking.** Offer applies to any package or date. 

**Spring Fling Package: Host your wedding anytime in March or April and receive a special $175/person price** that includes use of the two barns, great food, table linens and centerpieces, sound system, DJ, the cake, and open bar (beer and wine) all night!

_All of these specials apply to new bookings only. Packages require a 100-guest minimum._
