# 10 Unique Tennessee Wedding Venues

## 

**If an "outside the box" celebration is on your list of must-haves, check out the following locations.** You'll find a restored 19th-century train station, a wildlife sanctuary, a pink palace, and a vintage theater among this selection of Tennessee’s unique and inspiring wedding venues.

[ ](https://www.herecomestheguide.com/wedding-venues/tennessee/capitol-theatre)

## [International Storytelling Center](https://www.herecomestheguide.com/wedding-venues/tennessee/international-storytelling-center)

### Jonesborough, TN

[ [ AJ Photography ](https://www.ajphotography.org/) ](https://www.herecomestheguide.com/wedding-venues/tennessee/international-storytelling-center)

**Fab Features** : nestled in a three acre park, lush with gardens, shady groves, and scenic walking trails • say "I do" on the gracious brick-lined Courtyard, then dine & dance in the second floor parlor, with its large windows and private balcony framing pretty city views • prime location in the heart of charming Jonesborough's historic Main Street, with plenty of activities and lodging within walking distance

**Catering:** BYO   
**Price:** $ • $$

[ ](https://www.herecomestheguide.com/wedding-venues/tennessee/dogwood-meadows)

**Fab Features:** stunning ranch-style venue situated on the Cumberland Plateau • exchange vows in the expansive meadows, set with a charming barn-door entry and rustic wooden benches • affordable packages include exclusive use of the property for 2 days

**Catering:** BYO   
**Price:** $ • $$ 

[ Click Here for More Info](https://www.herecomestheguide.com/wedding-venues/tennessee/dogwood-meadows)

### Erwin, TN

[ ](https://www.herecomestheguide.com/wedding-venues/tennessee/glamping-retro)

**Catering:** Preferred list, BYO   
**Price:** $ • $$
