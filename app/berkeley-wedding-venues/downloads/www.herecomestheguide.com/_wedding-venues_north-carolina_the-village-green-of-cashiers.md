## Why You'll Love This Place

##### A Here Comes The Guide Wedding Venue Review

Read More Show Less

  * **A thirteen-acre park at the heart of the historic village of Cashiers,** this special spot boasts beautiful photo opportunities throughout its strolling paths, wetlands, lawns, forest glades, and views of the Blue Ridge Mountains. 
  * **Lewis Hall is an extraordinary space for celebrations,** with its 30-foot timber frame cathedral ceiling, and towering stone fireplace. It’s equipped with a full catering kitchen and dressing rooms, setting you up for easy hosting. 
  * **The park is a non-profit organization,** serving the community with not just a wonderful green space, but cultural events, ecological education programs, and more—your rental fees help support this treasured local resource.
