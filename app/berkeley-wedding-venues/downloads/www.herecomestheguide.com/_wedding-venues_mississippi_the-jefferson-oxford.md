### PRICES: _What's this going to cost?_

###### Rental Fee

  * **$3,500–13,000/event**
  * Rental fee varies depending on: 
    * Day of week
    * Space reserved
    * Guest count
    * Type of event
  * _The rental fee includes:_

    * Exclusive 15-hour access to the room(s) rented on your wedding day
    * Use of the Faulkner Room, covered porch, and waterfront walkway
    * Two dressing rooms
    * Catering prep space, 3 rolling bars, rolling ice bins, and ice machine
    * Gas fireplace during winter months
    * Tables, chairs, and black polyester linens
    * Bar glassware (if using in-house bartending services)
    * Portable sound system, including speakers and mics, and SmartTV
    * Jefferson team member on-site, security officer, and setup & teardown
    * Ample parking
    * Discounted pricing for additional events/days (rehearsal dinner, setup, etc.)

  * There is a $750 fee for an outdoor ceremony (waived if the entire venue is rented).
