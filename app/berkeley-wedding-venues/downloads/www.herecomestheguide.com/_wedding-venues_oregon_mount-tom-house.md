### DEALS: _What discounts, special offers, or packages are available here?_

**Mount Tom House offers****elopement packages for $3,995 for up to 20 guests!**  
_Elopements include:_

  * More flexibility with your choice of event locations, with indoor and outdoor options available
  * Lodging for up to 12 adults for two nights
  * Breakfast each morning for all overnight guests
  * Tables & chairs with setup & cleanup
  * Tableware & drinkware options
