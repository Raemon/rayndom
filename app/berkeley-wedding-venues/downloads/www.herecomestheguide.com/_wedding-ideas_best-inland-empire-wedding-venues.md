# Inland Empire Wedding Venues: 12 Best Spots in Riverside & San Bernardino

## 

**From Riverside and Temecula to Palm Springs, Big Bear, and beyond, California’s Inland Empire is packed with gorgeous wedding locations.** This sun-drenched region east of Los Angeles has everything from chic desert resorts and vineyards to rustic ranches and mountaintop retreats. If you’re searching for Inland Empire wedding venues in Riverside and San Bernardino counties, start with these 13 favorites.
