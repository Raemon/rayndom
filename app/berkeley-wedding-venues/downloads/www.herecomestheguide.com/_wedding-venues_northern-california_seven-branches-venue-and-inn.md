## Why You'll Love This Place

##### A Here Comes The Guide Wedding Venue Review

Read More Show Less

  * **Seven Branches Venue and Inn is a picturesque wine country wedding venue** located in Sonoma, CA. Surrounded by beautiful parklands, this setting offers a stunning backdrop for your day's festivities. It's just minutes from Sonoma's town square, making it easily accessible for any out-of-town guests.
  * **Seven Branches Venue and Inn showcases the area's Spanish roots,** with a tiled roof, balconies, and patios. Your milestone day can begin with a reservation in one of our comfortable suites, where you'll get to relax with your wedding party. Exchange your vows in the courtyard, with ivy-covered walls adding a touch of rustic charm. This property also houses a classic ballroom, with a grand fireplace, custom-made marble-top bar, and antique French chandeliers. The space is fitted with comfortable lounge seating, perfect for sitting as you catch up with loved ones. Celebrate out of doors by hosting a reception on the patio. You'll be shaded by a canopy while still enjoying the glorious wine country sunshine.
  * **The team at Seven Branches Venue and Inn offers a range of services** to help streamline your wedding planning process. This includes catering, from their exclusive partner _Folktable,_ bartending, and desserts, with drinks and dishes to delight even the most discerning palates. The venue's own staff will remain onsite to help you and your guests for a seamless celebration. The events team, meanwhile, will work closely with you to help make your wedding day vision a reality.
