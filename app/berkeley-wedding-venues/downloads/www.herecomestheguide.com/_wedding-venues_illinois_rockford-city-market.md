### PRICES: _What's this going to cost?_

###### Rental Fee

  * **$4,000–7,000/event**
  * Rental fee varies depending on: 
    * Day of week
    * Type of event
  * **Reception-only pricing** is $4,000–5,000, and includes:

    * 9am–11am exclusive use of Bowtruss Room and Market Hall
    * Tables & chairs
    * Catering prep suite
    * On-site security

**Ceremony and reception pricing** is $6,000–7,000 and includes everything listed above, PLUS:

    * All-day use of two separate dressing suites
    * Rectangular ceremony arch and white garden chairs
    * On-site coordinator



###### Additional Info

  * All food & beverage must be provided by one of the preferred caterers. Alternately, you may select a separate bar package from Rockford City Market, which ranges $5–7/person per hour, or a cash bar.
