## Why You'll Love This Place

##### A Here Comes The Guide Wedding Venue Review

Read More Show Less

  * **Perched atop Red Mountain with spectacular views of the city** , this park/museum venue celebrates the history of Birmingham and its metalworking industries (the impressive Vulcan towering above the grounds is the world’s largest cast iron statue).
  * **Both indoor and outdoor event spaces are available** for your ceremony and reception. The City Overlook offers splendid vistas of the downtown skyline and the mountainside, and the Electra Event Room has floor-to-ceiling windows along with topnotch AV capabilities and tables and chairs configured to your needs.
  * **Thoughtful wedding package amenities include** guest access to the museum and observation tower, a designated day-of event professional, and a select group of experienced caterers to choose from.
