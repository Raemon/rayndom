# Georgia Gems: 5 Unique Savannah Wedding Venues

## 

**Savannah is known for so many things: fabulous architecture, natural beauty (who doesn't love those gorgeous Spanish moss-draped trees?), great food, and balmy weather even in winter!** It's not surprising that movies like _Midnight in the Garden of Good and Evil_ and _Forrest Gump_ were filmed there…or that this city is home to quite a few unique wedding venues. Check out the ones we've highlighted!

## [Georgia State Railroad Museum](https://www.herecomestheguide.com/wedding-venues/georgia/georgia-state-railroad-museum)

### Savannah, GA

[ ](https://www.herecomestheguide.com/wedding-venues/georgia/georgia-state-railroad-museum)

**Venue Superpowers:**

Instagrammable

Indoor/Outdoor Flow

Affordable

**Fab Features:** unique historic venue set in downtown Savannah • the Blacksmith Shop impresses with soaring exposed brick walls and massive wood trusses, while the Roundhouse and Overnight Shed boast a permanent stage and cool indoor-outdoor flow • guests can stroll through the intimate garden with a bubbling fountain, antique train cars, and atmospheric architectural ruins

**Catering:** Preferred list, BYO   
**Price:** $ • $$
