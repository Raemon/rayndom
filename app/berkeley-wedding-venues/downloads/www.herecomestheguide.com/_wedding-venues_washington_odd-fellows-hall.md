## Why You'll Love This Place

##### A Here Comes The Guide Wedding Venue Review

Read More Show Less

  * **Its fabulous location on Orcas Island’s Madrona Point offers up spectacular sunsets.** Situated among Madrona trees, evergreens and an array of colorful wildflowers, this popular community gathering place is both charming and affordable!
  * **The Hall includes a waterfront deck overlooking the beautiful sound and village.** Inside, you’ll find maple floors, a high ceiling and French doors leading to the deck, plus a patio for the perfect indoor/outdoor celebration.
  * **This historic locale comes equipped with the amenities you’ll need most.** From a full commercial kitchen to tables, chairs, a variety of audiovisual systems and a long list of great vendors, they have you covered so you can relax and have fun.
