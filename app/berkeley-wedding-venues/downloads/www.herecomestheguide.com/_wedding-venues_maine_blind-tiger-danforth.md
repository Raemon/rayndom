## Why You'll Love This Place

##### A Here Comes The Guide Wedding Venue Review

Read More Show Less

  * **This expansive, artfully restored 19th-century home is now a welcoming boutique hotel,** tucked in the heart of Portland’s West End. It provides the perfect backdrop for small weddings of up to 35 guests… and serves as a stunning home base for a weekend-long getaway with your favorite people.
  * **The layout is tailor-made for intimate events, with nine chic guest rooms** and a gracious collection of gathering spaces, including a speakeasy billiards room, a light-filled sun porch, a rooftop cupola, lush gardens, and artfully designed living and dining rooms. A built-in whole home speaker system lets you make the party sound just right, no matter where you’re celebrating.
  * **Their curated list of trusted local chefs and caterers gives you plenty of flexibility** to create your perfect menu, with in-house enhancements like welcome gifts, snack trays, and a fully stocked pantry available to further customize the experience.
