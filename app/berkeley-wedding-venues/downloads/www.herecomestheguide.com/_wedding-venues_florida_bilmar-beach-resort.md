### PRICES: _What's this going to cost?_

###### Ceremony Fee

  * **$1,500/event and up**
  * The ceremony fee includes white chairs, your choice of arch, specialty table, and shepherd's hooks with lanterns.

A day-of coordinator is an additional $850 fee.




###### Package Prices

  * **$45–70/person**
  * Package prices vary depending on: 
    * Package chosen
  * _All packages include:_

    * Venue space (tent required for some venues and guest counts)
    * Plated or buffet dinner, plus salad, rolls & butter, and coffee
    * Bartender, staff, and cake cutting and service
    * Tables, chairs, linens, centerpieces, and napkins in your color of choice
    * Complimentary overnight stay in a PSQ plus 50% off an additional 1 or 2 nights in a suite or penthouse
    * Negotiated discounted group room rates
    * Valet for parking (50+ guests)

Bar service is priced separately, except for the all-inclusive buffet package, which is $140/person and includes a 4-hour open bar and champagne toast.




###### Service Charge or Gratuity

  * **24%**
