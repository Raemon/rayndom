## Hawaii Marriage License | What you need to know (with official links)

Apply online, then meet an authorized license agent in person to pick up your license. The license is valid only in Hawaiʻi, and your ceremony must occur within 30 days of pick-up; there’s no waiting period once issued.

Start here: [Hawaiʻi Dept. of Health — Marriage & Civil Union Licenses](https://health.hawaii.gov/vitalrecords/marriage-licenses/) (official requirements, fee, validity, and agent list).
