## Why You'll Love This Place

##### A Here Comes The Guide Wedding Venue Review

Read More Show Less

  * **Though it's set on a pristine plot of lush Indiana countryside,** this charming family (and dog!)-friendly winery is just a quick drive northeast of Indianapolis. Surprise perk: the adorable, colorful labels on their wine bottles will add a super-festive pop to your table settings!
  * **The vines themselves provide a naturally gorgeous backdrop** at your dedicated outdoor ceremony space, which also boasts some seriously magnificent sunsets. A spacious, rustic-modern event hall, complete with a dazzling multi-tiered crystal chandelier, offers ample room for dining and dancing.
  * **There’s an excellently curated collection of in-house rentals** that includes all the extras you’ll need, like wedding arches, wine barrel cocktail tables, lounge furnishings, moveable greenery walls, signage, and table décor. 



### PRICES: _What's this going to cost?_

###### Rental Fee

  * **$500–8,750/event**
  * Rental fee varies depending on: 
    * Day of week
    * Time of year
    * Space reserved
  * The **Upper Ballroom** ranges $3,500–8,750 and includes:

    * 9-hour rental time
    * Dressing suite, lounge, and balcony
    * Gold Chiavari chairs and round tables
    * Hue lighting system, projector, screen, house audio, and microphone
    * Parking and security
    * Option to rent Lower Ballroom at discounted rate

The **Lower Ballroom** rental ranges $500–5,000 for 5 hours, and includes farm tables, chairs, soft seating, and AV system.

The **Outdoor Stage Area** rental ranges $1,000–5,000 and includes use of the Vineyard Terrace, covered pergola, metal tables & chairs, electrical hookups, and string lighting.




###### Ceremony Fee

  * **$1,000/event**
  * Rental of the **Ceremony Pad** includes 200 white resin garden chairs, a wooden ceremony arch, electrical hookup for sound, and a weather backup.
