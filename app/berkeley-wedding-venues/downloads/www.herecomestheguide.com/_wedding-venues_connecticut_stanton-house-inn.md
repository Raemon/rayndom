## Why You'll Love This Place

##### A Here Comes The Guide Wedding Venue Review

Read More Show Less

  * **Built in 1843, this classic Connecticut B &B** is only steps from downtown Greenwich’s charming historic district, and is surrounded by lush grounds that include a tranquil seasonal pool and patio. 
  * **In the warmer months, you can wed in the romantic walled garden,** which can also host al fresco cocktails. As the weather grows cooler, events can move into the mansion’s graceful dining room and intimate library. 
  * **Twenty-one inviting guest rooms** ensure the majority of your attendees can stay right on site, and boast cozy amenities like private baths, whirlpool tubs, and balconies.
