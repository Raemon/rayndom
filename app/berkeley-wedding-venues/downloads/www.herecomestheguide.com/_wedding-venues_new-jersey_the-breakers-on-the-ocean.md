  * **The Breakers on the Ocean is a waterfront treasure** and the anchor of Spring Lake, a one-of-a-kind Jersey Shore destination. The hotel offers a family legacy of remarkable hospitality coupled with historic charm and timeless style.
  * **Host your ceremony on the Ocean Terrace** , which provides a sweeping view of the Atlantic. Then, move the celebration into either the Seashell or Crystal Ballroom, both of which also boast spectacular ocean vistas.
  * **Your guests will love the modern, comfortable accommodations** and their beautiful ocean views. The spacious, luxury rooms have great added amenities like gas fireplaces and oversized whirlpool baths—and they're reasonably priced, too!
