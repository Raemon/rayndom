## 10–12 Months To Go…

[ ](https://www.herecomestheguide.com/wedding-ideas/top-destination-wedding-venues-united-states)

[Castle Ladyhawke](https://www.herecomestheguide.com/wedding-ideas/top-destination-wedding-venues-united-states "Castle Ladyhawke")

  * [Work out your budget ](https://www.herecomestheguide.com/wedding-party-ideas/detail/6-simple-steps-to-setting-a-wedding-budget)and establish your top priorities—where to save/where to splurge.  

  * Get your creative juices flowing. Start browsing our [Wedding Ideas](https://www.herecomestheguide.com/wedding-ideas), [Real Weddings](https://www.herecomestheguide.com/real-wedding), and [Pinterest](https://www.pinterest.com/hctg/) as well as wedding blogs and magazines to [identify your wedding style](https://www.herecomestheguide.com/wedding-party-ideas/detail/how-to-find-your-wedding-style) and color palette.  

  * Compile your preliminary[ guest list ](https://www.herecomestheguide.com/wedding-party-ideas/detail/tips-wedding-guest-list)(you'll need that guest count!).  

  * Choose your wedding party—who do you want by your side at the altar?  

  * Sign up for [MyGuide](https://www.herecomestheguide.com/myguide). We'll send you useful—and sassy!—wedding planning emails.  

  * [Find a venue ](https://www.herecomestheguide.com/wedding-venues)for your ceremony and reception, and reserve your date. [Know what questions to ask when evaluating a wedding venue.](https://www.herecomestheguide.com/wedding-party-ideas/detail/questions-to-ask-your-wedding-venue/)



[ ](https://www.herecomestheguide.com/wedding-venues/southern-california/flora)

[Flora The Venue](https://www.herecomestheguide.com/wedding-venues/southern-california/flora "Flora The Venue") | [Let's Frolic Together](https://letsfrolictogether.com/ "Let's Frolic Together")

  * Do you need wedding insurance? It’s something to think about. Check with your venue about Liability Insurance and consider other options, like cancellation insurance.  

  * Now that you have a date, tell everyone to save it! For [destination weddings](https://www.herecomestheguide.com/wedding-ideas/category/destination) or weddings around a holiday, consider sending out Save-the-Date cards or emails. Or [create your own wedding website](https://www.herecomestheguide.com/wedding-ideas/ultimate-guide-to-creating-your-wedding-website), and let your invitees know about it.  

  * Say yes to your [wedding dress ](https://www.herecomestheguide.com/wedding-ideas/wedding-dress-shopping-101)(or tux!) and begin assembling the perfect accessories. Need inspiration? Attend a trunk show or wedding expo.  

  * Already feeling overwhelmed? Consider hiring a Wedding Planner.  

  * [Find your vendors](https://www.herecomestheguide.com/wedding-ideas/the-ultimate-guide-to-choosing-your-wedding-vendors). Assemble an all-star vendor team. We'd start with:
    * Caterer
    * Photographer / Videographer
    * Officiant
  * When you hire a vendor, [get all the details in writing](https://www.herecomestheguide.com/wedding-party-ideas/detail/protect-your-wedding-get-it-in-writing)! Another way to minimize stress: Start dreaming up your honeymoon…

## 6–9 Months To Go…

[ ](https://www.herecomestheguide.com/wedding-venues/new-jersey/fiddlers-elbow-country-club)

[Fiddler's Elbow Country Club](https://www.herecomestheguide.com/wedding-venues/new-jersey/fiddlers-elbow-country-club "Fiddler's Elbow Country Club") | [Cassi Claire Photography](https://www.cassiclaire.com/ "Cassi Claire Photography")

  * Continue researching, interviewing and booking vendors:
    * Decide on arrangements with your Floral Designer.
    * Do a tasting and choose your wedding cake with your Cake Designer.
    * Hire the DJ/Entertainment for your ceremony, cocktail hour, and reception.
    * Discuss the style and wording of your wedding invitations with a Stationer or online invitation company.  

  * Create your [gift registry](https://www.herecomestheguide.com/wedding-ideas/items-that-should-be-on-your-wedding-registry) (and don't forget to update your wedding website!).  

  * Arrange hotel room blocks for out-of-town guests and book your own suite for the wedding night.  

  * Shop for bridesmaid/flower girl dresses and/or groomsmen suits, and give your attendants clear instructions on how to place their orders.  

  * Arrange and book any necessary transportation.  

  * Go over shower and/or bach party details, as well as the guest list with the person(s) hosting the party.  

  * Consider premarital counseling. Let's face it: Planning a wedding is stressful. Make sure you and your fiancé are rock-solid through this journey, and prepared to face married life together.



* * *

## 3–5 Months To Go…

[ ](https://www.herecomestheguide.com/wedding-venues/florida/hilton-clearwater-beach-resort-and-spa)

[Hilton Clearwater Beach Resort and Spa](https://www.herecomestheguide.com/wedding-venues/florida/hilton-clearwater-beach-resort-and-spa "Hilton Clearwater Beach Resort and Spa") | [K & K Photography](https://kandkphotography.com/ "K & K Photography")

  * Book the rehearsal and rehearsal dinner location(s). If you're including entertainment or specialty details like a groom's cake, now's the time to lock in these elements.  

  * Put together your rehearsal dinner guest list.  

  * Make childcare arrangements and/or plan wedding entertainment for all relevant kiddos.  

  * Reserve all necessary party rentals and linens.  

  * Order [wedding favors](https://www.herecomestheguide.com/wedding-party-ideas/detail/10-wedding-favors-under-1-dollar) for your guests.  

  * Shop for and reserve any additional formalwear (like suits or rental tuxes).  

  * Concentrate on finalizing the:
    * Guest list. Get everyone's mailing address.
    * Invitation wording. Confirm your invitation text with the Stationer, and consider additional stationery (programs, menu cards, place cards, thank-you cards, etc.).
    * Schedule a pickup or delivery date for your invites.
    * Ceremony readings and vows.
    * Menu, beverage, and catering details.
    * [Timeline of the wedding day](https://www.herecomestheguide.com/wedding-ideas/how-to-create-your-wedding-timeline) and reception formalities.  

  * Do a makeup & hair trial and book your stylists. While you're at it, come up with your own [beauty and fitness plan](https://www.herecomestheguide.com/wedding-ideas/wedding-beauty-timeline) to ensure you're confident for the big day.  

  * Shop for and purchase your [wedding rings](https://www.herecomestheguide.com/wedding-ideas/engagement-rings-101). Order engravings if desired.  

  * Finalize honeymoon plans and obtain all necessary documents (are you sure your passports haven't expired?).



* * *

## 6–8 Weeks To Go…

[Minted Weddings](https://www.minted.com/ "Minted Weddings")

  * You're getting close…. Mail out those invitations! Have a game plan for [recording the RSVPs](https://www.herecomestheguide.com/wedding-party-ideas/detail/a-wedding-invitation-secret-number/) and meal choices.  

  * Consider a contingency plan. _Especially_ if you're hosting an outdoor wedding. Talk to your venue coordinator about a potential Plan B. You can never be too careful—or too prepared!  

  * Touch base with your vendors to confirm date, deposits, and details.  

  * Start researching marriage license requirements and name-change paperwork, if applicable.  

  * Begin your dress/attire fittings. Be sure to buy the appropriate undergarments beforehand.  

  * So you think you can't dance? Consider taking a dance lesson with your fiancé—a good way to break in your wedding shoes!  

  * Give the wedding party a nudge—make sure they've ordered all necessary attire.  

  * [Write thank-you cards ](https://www.herecomestheguide.com/wedding-ideas/wedding-thank-you-note-etiquette)for shower gifts and any early wedding gifts received.  

  * Are you assembling welcome bags for your guests? If so, now’s the time to get those together.



* * *

## 3–5 Weeks To Go…

[ ](https://www.herecomestheguide.com/wedding-venues/colorado/the-st-vrain)

[The St Vrain](https://www.herecomestheguide.com/wedding-venues/colorado/the-st-vrain "The St Vrain") | [Stephanie Parsley Photography](https://www.stephanieparsley.com/ "Stephanie Parsley Photography")

  * Send out rehearsal dinner invitations. If your get-together will be informal, feel free to send an Evite.  

  * Finalize and confirm:
    * Wedding vows and readings with your Officiant.
    * Shot list with your Photographer and/or Videographer.
    * Song list for ceremony, cocktail hour, and reception with your DJ and/or Band/Musicians.
    * [Timeline for the reception](https://www.herecomestheguide.com/wedding-ideas/how-to-create-your-wedding-timeline) and who's giving the toasts.
    * Wedding night and honeymoon accommodations.  

  * Obtain marriage license and complete name-change documents, if applicable.  

  * Pick up your wedding rings and proofread any engraving!  

  * If you're the traditional type, do you have something old, new, borrowed, and blue?  

  * Purchase your guest book, toasting flutes, cake servers, unity candle, and all that good stuff.  

  * [Buy gifts ](https://www.herecomestheguide.com/wedding-party-ideas/detail/9-bridal-party-gifts-that-give-back)(optional) for the wedding party and parents of the wedding couple.  

  * Have your final dress/suit fitting. Bring your shoes and accessories for the full impact.  

  * Sigh. Hunt down whoever hasn't RSVP'd yet.



* * *

## 1–2 Weeks To Go…

[ ](https://www.herecomestheguide.com/wedding-venues/washington/fireseed-catering-and-wedding-venue)

[Fireseed Catering and Wedding Venue](https://www.herecomestheguide.com/wedding-venues/washington/fireseed-catering-and-wedding-venue "Fireseed Catering and Wedding Venue") | [Kody Ng Photography](https://www.kodyngstudios.com/ "Kody Ng Photography")

  * Give your caterer/venue the final guest count.  

  * Arrange seating and create the seating chart and/or place cards.  

  * Pick up your gown/suit. Swoon.  

  * Confirm arrival times and [finalize the wedding timeline](https://www.herecomestheguide.com/wedding-ideas/how-to-create-your-wedding-timeline) with vendors and the wedding party—make sure your MOH or someone you trust has a copy, too.  

  * Go over pickup times and locations with your limousine/transportation vendors.  

  * Finalize reception tables. Create the seating chart and/or place cards if you're having seating arrangements.  

  * Hand over wedding favors, décor, paper, and ceremony items to your on-site coordinator or planner. Include any special set-up instructions.  

  * Put together your own [Wedding Emergency Kit.](https://www.herecomestheguide.com/blog/detail/wedding-day-emergency-fashion-kit/)  

  * Speaking of emergencies: Check the weather report, and if things look iffy contact your venue to go over the contingency plan.  

  * Start packing for your honeymoon. (See "weather report" above.)  

  * In desperate need of a facial or massage? Now's the time to squeeze one in.  

  * Want more "Week Before" guidance? Check out our super-thorough[ Week-Of Wedding Checklist ](https://www.herecomestheguide.com/wedding-ideas/week-of-wedding-checklist)for all the TO-DOs leading up to your I DOs.



* * *

## The Day Before…

[ ](https://www.herecomestheguide.com/wedding-venues/georgia/meadows-at-mossy-creek)

[Meadows At Mossy Creek](https://www.herecomestheguide.com/wedding-venues/georgia/meadows-at-mossy-creek "Meadows At Mossy Creek") | [Tay Tesvich Photo](https://www.taytesvichphoto.com/ "Tay Tesvich Photo")

  * Make sure all wedding-day items are packed/laid out and ready to go! *_Don't forget the rings and marriage license!_  

  * [Figure out tips](https://www.herecomestheguide.com/wedding-party-ideas/detail/tip-wedding-vendors) and final payments for vendors. Put them in clearly marked envelopes and give them to the Best Man or another person you trust to hand out at the reception.  

  * Assign someone to pack up your gifts/belongings after the reception (don't forget the top tier of your cake!).  

  * Thank your BFF for agreeing to return the tuxedos and other rental items the day after the wedding.  

  * Wrap any gifts you'll be giving to your future spouse, family, or wedding party. Or simply write them heartfelt notes to open on the day-of.  

  * Drop off welcome bags to guest hotels, if you're doing them.  

  * Enjoy a mani-pedi.  

  * Attend the rehearsal and dinner. Now's the time to give out wedding party gifts.  

  * Try to go to bed early…you need your beauty sleep tonight!

## Wedding-Day Advice

[ ](https://www.herecomestheguide.com/wedding-venues/florida/nova-535)

[NOVA 535](https://www.herecomestheguide.com/wedding-venues/florida/nova-535 "NOVA 535") | [Sabrina Autumn Photography](https://smautumnphoto.com/ "Sabrina Autumn Photography")

  * Allow plenty of time to get ready.  

  * Do the rounds at your wedding—greet everyone and thank them for coming.

## After the Honeymoon / Back to Reality…
