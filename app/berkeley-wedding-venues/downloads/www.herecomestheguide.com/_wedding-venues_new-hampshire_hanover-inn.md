## Why You'll Love This Place

##### A Here Comes The Guide Wedding Venue Review

Read More Show Less

  * **With its distinctive New England charm and cool location** (it’s the only boutique hotel on the Dartmouth College campus), this historic property features stately vintage vibes blended with posh modern amenities. 
  * **Their Grand Ballroom is New Hampshire’s largest,** and its simple, timeless design blends seamlessly with a wide range of colors and styles. For more intimate affairs, there’s the Hayward Room, which boasts an outdoor terrace overlooking the Dartmouth Green. 
  * **Menus focus on creative, locally inspired cuisine,** but the catering team is always happy to customize based on your chosen themes or dietary/cultural preferences. Added perk alert!: Thursday through Sunday, guests can show their Hanover Inn room key to a receive a discount on admission to select museum and performance venues.
