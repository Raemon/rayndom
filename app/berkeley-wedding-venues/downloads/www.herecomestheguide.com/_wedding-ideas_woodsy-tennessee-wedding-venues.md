# 11 Woodsy Wedding Venues in Tennessee

## 

**Tennessee is brimming with wonderful and woodsy places to exchange vows!** Whether you're looking for an intimate ceremony under century-old oaks, or host a grand soirée surrounded by flowering trees at a botanical garden, we have handpicked our favorite venues that provide the perfect outdoor setting for your forest festivities.
