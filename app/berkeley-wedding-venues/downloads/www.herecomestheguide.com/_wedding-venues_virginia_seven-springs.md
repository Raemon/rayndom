  * **$8,500–16,000/event**
  * Package prices vary depending on: 
    * Time of year
    * Event duration
  * The _Full Day Celebrations_ package __ ranges $8,500–10,500 and includes:

    * Access to the venue 10am–10pm
    * Access to the property for multiple planning visits
    * Floor plan creation
    * Photoshoots for clients – engagement, maternity, anniversary, family, etc offered for life
    * Ceremony chairs (set up by venue staff), farmhouse cross-back chairs for reception, and tables
    * 1-hour ceremony rehearsal
    * Options for on-site rehearsal dinners
    * Indoor entertaining options __ in the _Reception Pavilion_ & _Carriage House_
    * Several outdoor options on the _Back Lawn_ , bistro-lit side lawn, and wildflower field
    * Parking attendant with parking for up to 200 guests
    * Golf cart service for your elderly guests
    * Bonfire with firewood (perfect for s'mores!)
    * Circular driveway in front of _Historic Manor_ for bus/shuttle drop-off
    * Venue manager & assistant on-site during the event
    * Wedding planning services offered through the venue
    * Two getting-ready spaces
