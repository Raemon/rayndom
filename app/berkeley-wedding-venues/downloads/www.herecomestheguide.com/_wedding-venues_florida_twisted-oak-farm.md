    * Day of week
  * _The rental fee includes:_

    * Dressing suite
    * Farm tables & chairs
    * Use of the property for engagement photoshoot
    * Decorations from the _Décor Galore_ room
    * Yard games
    * On-site parking & parking attendant
    * Staff day-of coordinator/facilitator
    * And more!



###### Package Prices

  * **$450–11,000/event**
  * Package prices vary depending on: 
    * Package chosen
    * Additional add-ons selected

  * _The All-inclusive Package is $11,000 and includes:_

    * 9-hour use of the venue, noon–9pm (extra time available for surcharge)
    * Use of the dressing suite to get ready
    * Professional photographer for 2.5 hours (200 edited photos)
    * Officiant
    * Flowers (bouquet, boutonniere, and tablescapes)
    * Decorating service
    * Self-serve drink station (water, sweet tea, and lemonade)
    * Appetizer reception
    * Catered dinner
    * Day-of coordinator & venue staff on-site
    * Professional DJ
    * Wedding cake
    * Set up, tear down, and garbage removal service

 _This package is not available on Saturdays._
