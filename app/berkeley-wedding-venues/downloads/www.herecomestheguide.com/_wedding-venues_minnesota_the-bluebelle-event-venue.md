## Why You'll Love This Place

  * **Minnesota’s lush Northwoods surround this picturesque family-run venue,** set in the historic town of Bemidji. They’re proud of being one of the more affordable options in the area, and are happy to help you make the most of your budget.
  * **A lushly forested clearing with a wooden arbor creates a lovely spot for vows** , and wide-open meadows are perfect for cocktails. For your reception, there’s a fantastic, rustic-modern event room with an attached covered patio overlooking the grounds. 
  * **Two dressing suites are included in your rental,** and both are wonderfully appointed, boasting plenty of natural light and comfy furnishings. Packages can also include awesome perks like use of in-house décor pieces and late night shuttle service.
