## Why You'll Love This Place

##### A Here Comes The Guide Wedding Venue Review

  * **The historic mountain lodge is nestled on 33 acres of pristine Catskills woodland.** Built in the 1880s, this “Stick Style” mansion has an indoor gathering space for receptions, a deep wraparound porch that’s perfect for a cocktail hour or late-night mingling, and eight guest rooms. 
  * **Outdoor celebrations can be held in several pretty spots around the grounds** , including a meadow with a fire ring, picnic tables, and stage, and a cleared forest glade whose stone walls create a “cathedral in the woods.” 
  * **An imaginative events team will help craft your wedding** , and take care of all the details to bring it to life. They’ll arrange everything from signature cocktails to incredible feasts to fabulous entertainment—they can even plan fun group activities, like cooking, music, or movement classes.
