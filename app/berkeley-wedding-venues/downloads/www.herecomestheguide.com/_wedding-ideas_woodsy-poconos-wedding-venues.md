# 4 Woodsy Wedding Venues in the Poconos

## 

**Want to get married amongst the trees? The Poconos has a wealth of forested wedding venues where Mother Nature reigns supreme.** From an authentic Adirondack-style summer camp nestled on the shores of Fairview Lake to a secluded retreat overlooking the Appalachian Trail ridge, these locations provide the perfect outdoor setting for your woodsy celebration.
