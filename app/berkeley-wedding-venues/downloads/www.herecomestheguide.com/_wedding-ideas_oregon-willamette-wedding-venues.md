# 8 Gorgeous Outdoor Willamette Wedding Venues

## 

**Oregon's Willamette region offers every type of outdoor wedding setting imaginable, all within an easy drive of Portland, Salem, and the coast.** There are creeks, forests, vineyards, farms, ranches, hilltops and valleys—if you're looking to get married surrounded by nature's bounty, you're certain to find lots to love at these romantic Willamette Valley spots.

* * *

## [Abbey Road Farm](https://www.herecomestheguide.com/wedding-venues/oregon/abbey-road-farm)

### Carlton, OR

[ ](https://www.herecomestheguide.com/wedding-venues/oregon/abbey-road-farm)

**Fab Features:** picturesque working farm in Oregon's Wine Country, surrounded by a dreamy landscape of gardens, meadows, and rolling hills • gorgeous event spaces include the rustic-industrial Silo Garden and the Wedding Triangle, which overlooks the Trappist Abbey • on-site bed & breakfast suites located in unique grain silos

**Catering:** Preferred list   
**Price:** $$ 

[ Click Here for More Info](https://www.herecomestheguide.com/wedding-venues/oregon/abbey-road-farm)

## [Deepwood Museum & Gardens](https://www.herecomestheguide.com/wedding-venues/oregon/deepwood-museum-gardens)

### Salem, OR

[ [ Emily Gigoux Studios ](https://emilygigoux.com/) ](https://www.herecomestheguide.com/wedding-venues/oregon/deepwood-museum-gardens)

**Fab Features:** Queen Anne-style Victorian house in the heart of Salem's historic district • surrounding 5-acre park filled with nature trails and accented by a greenhouse and original carriage house • al fresco event spaces include romantic formal gardens, ivy-covered arbors and gazebos, and an open-air tennis court

**Catering:** BYO   
**Price:** $ 

[ Click Here for More Info](https://www.herecomestheguide.com/wedding-venues/oregon/deepwood-museum-gardens)

## [Gardens at Riverside](https://www.herecomestheguide.com/wedding-venues/oregon/gardens-at-riverside)

### Albany, OR

[ [ Hailee Rawson Photo ](https://www.haileerawsonphoto.com/) ](https://www.herecomestheguide.com/wedding-venues/oregon/gardens-at-riverside)

**Fab Features** : lush venue with four distinct garden settings, each with stunning photo backdrops • the Japanese Garden boasts a quaint tea house overlooking tranquil water features, and the Gazebo Garden's charming structure overlooks a pretty pond • larger events in the Cherry Blossom Garden (especially lovely in spring) or the romantic English Garden

**Catering:** Preferred list, BYO   
**Price:** $$ 

[ Click Here for More Info](https://www.herecomestheguide.com/wedding-venues/oregon/gardens-at-riverside)
