## Why You'll Love This Place

##### A Here Comes The Guide Wedding Venue Review

  * **Set on a plateau just west of downtown Walla Walla** , this golf club and event center offers a breathtaking backdrop for your celebration. The panoramic vistas include the bright green slopes of the golf course and the Blue Mountains on the horizon.
  * **The open-air Valley View Pavilion** features adjustable curtains and sliding panels that quickly transform it into an indoor party space. A surrounding one-acre lawn overlooks the Walla Walla Valley, making it an excellent outdoor ceremony option.
  * **Amenities such as decorative wine barrels** , tables and chairs, a sound system, a dressing suite, and a gas fire pit are included with the venue rental. You can also add on catering services and bartending, with a signature cocktail designed just for you!
