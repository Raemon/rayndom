### DEALS: _What discounts, special offers, or packages are available here?_

**Elopements****start at $1,350** for up to 10 guests, and include:

  * 2-hour venue rental
  * Pre-ceremony guidance
  * Two dressing suites
  * Ceremony site set with arch and cherry resin chairs
  * First dance and father/daughter and/or mother/son dances
  * Private newlywed picnic



**Micro-weddings****start at $5,000** for up to 50 guests, and include:

  * 6-hour venue rental
  * Pre-ceremony guidance and day-of coordination
  * Two dressing suites
  * Ceremony site set with arch and cherry resin chairs
  * First dance and father/daughter and/or mother/son dances
  * Full dinner setup with your desired layout



Both options may be booked up to 6 months in advance.
