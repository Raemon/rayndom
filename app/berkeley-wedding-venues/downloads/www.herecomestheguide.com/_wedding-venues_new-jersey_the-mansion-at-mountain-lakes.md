## Why You'll Love This Place

##### A Here Comes The Guide Wedding Venue Review

Read More Show Less

  * **Combining rustic and elegant architectural details with gorgeous outdoor areas** , this beautifully updated venue offers a variety of spaces for your ceremony and reception. The grounds are especially lovely, thanks to an abundance of trees, lawns, seasonal flowers, and a cascading rock waterfall—think fabulous wedding photos!
  * **The Grand Ballroom impresses with its hand-painted ceiling and crystal chandeliers** , while state-of-the-art lighting lets you use colored uplights, pin lasers, and more to create just the right ambiance. Two private cocktail rooms open to landscaped sitting areas, a dressing suite, and an outdoor garden for ceremonies.
  * **Your event will be tailored to your needs. Menus can be customized** and feature award-winning food accompanied by the impeccable service of their professional wedding team. One more thing: Your guests will appreciate the Mansion's easy accessibility from Northern and Central New Jersey.
