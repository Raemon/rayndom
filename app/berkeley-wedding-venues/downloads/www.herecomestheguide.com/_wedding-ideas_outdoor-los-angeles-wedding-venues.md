# 12 Awesome Outdoor Los Angeles County Wedding Venues

## 

**Los Angeles County may be a bustling metropolitan region, but it's also home to some awe-inspiring outdoor wedding venues.** These pretty places can be found in a variety of settings—on a bluff overlooking the Pacific, on a rooftop garden taking in the city lights, or even nestled in a forest beneath towering trees. And thanks to Southern California's agreeable climate, you can host an unforgettable al fresco celebration pretty much all year-round.
