  * **Located on the second floor of Irish Eyes Pub & Restaurant,** this fun, relaxed venue offers unobstructed views of Lewes Harbor from its perch above the water. 
  * **The space flows beautifully—** the main banquet hall boasts floor-to-ceiling windows and glass doors that open onto the sunroom, where you'll find rustic wood flooring and wraparound windows with panoramic views of lovely Lewes Harbor.
  * **In addition to tasty traditional menu offerings,** you have the option to include fun upgrades, like a pulled pork slider station or mashed potato bar with all the fixings.
