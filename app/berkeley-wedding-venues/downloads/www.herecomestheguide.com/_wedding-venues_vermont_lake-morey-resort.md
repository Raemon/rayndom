  * **It’s an all-season gem, perched above the Connecticut River** in the lush Vermont hills. Offering gorgeous views of Lake Morey and Echo Mountain, this is your ideal wedding weekend spot—when they’re not celebrating, guests can boat, swim, golf, hike, ice skate, and more. 
  * **From terraces, gardens, lawns and patios****to traditional ballrooms and intimate dining rooms,** the resort boasts a stellar collection of indoor and outdoor spaces, each of which has been purpose built for effortless gatherings.
