# 11 Outstanding Outdoor Charlotte Wedding Venues

## 

**Charlotte is more than a thriving metropolis with fantastic sports teams, NASCAR, and a hopping university scene.** This charming town is also home to some beautiful and tranquil outdoor venues that can make your wedding day dreams come true. From rustic receptions to swanky soirees, here's a selection of stunning estates, posh resorts, and historic farmhouses perfect for an al fresco celebration.
