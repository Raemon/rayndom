Day-of rentals at Camp Thorpe starts at $500 for a 5-hour weekday rental in May, or $750 for a 5-hour May weekend rental. Fall weekday/weekend day-of rentals start at $1,500. _Day-of rentals do not include cabins or kitchen use._
