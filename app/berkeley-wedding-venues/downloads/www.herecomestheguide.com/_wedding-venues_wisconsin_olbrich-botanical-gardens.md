###### Rental Fee

  * **$3,700–4,600/event**
  * Rental fee varies depending on: 
    * Day of week
    * Time of year
    * Space reserved
    * Type of event
  * _Rental fees include:_

    * Use of Evjue Commons and Lussier Terrace from noon–11pm
    * Use of the Atrium for your indoor ceremony from noon–7pm
    * Between May & September your outdoor ceremony may be held in the Event Garden (weather permitting)
    * Private changing room
    * Tables & chairs
    * 1-hour ceremony rehearsal
    * Photo opportunities throughout the gardens during public garden hours
    * One year Garden Family Membership
    * Reduced ticket rate for Olbrich's GLEAM: Art in a New Light exhibition for your guests (September & October)

Individual sites may be reserved for ceremony-only or reception-only between November 1–March 31.
