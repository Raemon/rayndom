## Why You'll Love This Place

##### A Here Comes The Guide Wedding Venue Review

Read More Show Less

  * **Perched on a bluff overlooking the Mississippi River** , this venerated country club founded in the 19th century is just 10 minutes from the downtown areas of both Twin Cities. The pristine 18-hole golf course provides a lovely backdrop year-round.
  * **Floor-to-ceiling windows in the classically designed River Room** offer gorgeous views of the Minneapolis skyline. This ballroom also features six chandeliers, Tudor décor, an impressive stone fireplace, mahogany pillars, and a built-in wood dance floor.
  * **You can choose from an extensive array of menus** designed by the accomplished executive chef and prepared by the experienced staff. Presentation options include creative buffets, beautifully plated entrées, and action stations.



### PRICES: _What's this going to cost?_

###### Rental Fee

  * **$2,000/event**
  * The rental fee includes access to the River Room starting at noon, tables & chairs, linens, standard rentals, and staffing.

From May–October, outdoor events are possible: add-on $1,500 for an outdoor ceremony and $1,000 for an outdoor cocktail reception.




###### Package Prices

  * **$125/person and up**
  * _The Premiere Package includes:_

    * Passed hors d'oeuvres
    * Choice of soup or salad and selection of entrées
    * Late night snack
    * 1-hour hosted bar (additional time is $37–51/person for 3-hour service)
    * Champagne toast

À la carte options and enhancements are also available.




###### Food & Beverage Minimum

  * **$10,000/event and up**
  * Minimums are based on food only, not beverages or rental fees.




###### Service Charge or Gratuity

  * **22%**
