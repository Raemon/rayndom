###### Package Prices

**Ceremony-only packages** start at $1,950 + tax for a 2-hour rental, and include:

    * 2-hour venue rental
    * Licensed wedding officiant, wedding certificate, and wood wedding arbor
    * On-site ceremony attendant
    * Dressing suite with 2 bathrooms, pool & ocean lanais, kitchen, living, garden, and dining areas
    * Water & lemonade station and 24 white folding chairs
    * Roundtrip shuttle from Wakiki and town area for 18 guests
    * Setup & breakdown, plus trash cans and removal
    * House sound system with microphone and iPhone hookup

**Ceremony + reception packages** for up to 50 guests start at $8,950 + tax, and include:

    * _Everything in the Ceremony Only package,_ PLUS:
    * Kaimea Estates concierge
    * Round trip shuttle from Wakiki, Kahala, and the town area for 42 guests
    * Self-service coffee station
    * Tables, chairs, linens, napkins, and basic china, flatware, water goblets, and flutes for toasting
    * Additional guests up to 70 for additional cost
