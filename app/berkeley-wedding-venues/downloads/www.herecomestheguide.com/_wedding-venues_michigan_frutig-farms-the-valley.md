### DEALS: _What discounts, special offers, or packages are available here?_

**Frutig Farms Offers Elopement Packages!**  
_Elopement packages are for 4–20 guests and include:_

  * Two hours of exclusive access to wedding party getting-ready suites
  * Five hours of venue access for ceremony, cocktail hour, appetizers, and dinner
  * Wedding officiant
  * Boutonniere and bouquet for the wedding couple
  * Two hours of professional photography (ceremony through dinner)
  * Three-course family style or plated meal served in the historic upper Barn
  * China, flatware, and glassware
  * Post-dinner coffee & tea
  * Locally sourced single tier wedding cake (three flavor choices; including gluten-free)



_Elopements can booked Monday—Sunday in April—December._ _Please contact the venue for more details._
