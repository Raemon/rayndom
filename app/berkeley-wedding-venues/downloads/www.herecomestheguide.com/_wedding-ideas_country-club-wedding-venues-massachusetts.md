# Top Flight: 6 Country Club Wedding Venues in Massachusetts

## 

**Country clubs offer amazing versatility with landscaped grounds, terraces with a view, private dining rooms, and elegant ballrooms.** But what makes these Massachusetts venues really stand out are their great locations, from Boston-close to Cape Cod or the spectacular Berkshires…and the variety of styles, like classic Colonial, reimagined New England barns—even a mansion that feels like a castle.

## [The Villa](https://www.herecomestheguide.com/wedding-venues/massachusetts/the-villa)

### East Bridgewater, Massachusetts

[ [ Zhaun Frias Photography ](https://zhaunfriasphotography.com/) ](https://www.herecomestheguide.com/wedding-venues/massachusetts/the-villa)

**Fab Features:** exchange vows under a wrought-iron or white gazebo, surrounded by posh, manicured gardens, velvety lawns, and a cool 250-year old converted dairy barn • opt for traditional glamour in the Grand Ballroom, designed with gold fixtures and a chic 14-foot linear fireplace, Old World-style romance in the French-inspired Madera Ballroom, or celebrate al fresco in the airy Tent • generous packages are paird with expert coordination provided by Saphire Event Group

**Catering:** In-house   
**Price:** $ • $$
