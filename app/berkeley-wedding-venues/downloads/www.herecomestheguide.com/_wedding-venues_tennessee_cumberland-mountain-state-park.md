## Why You'll Love This Place

##### A Here Comes The Guide Wedding Venue Review

Read More Show Less

  * **This tranquil state park nestled beside scenic Byrd Lake f** eels like a breath of fresh air. Set on over 1,700 acres in the Cumberland Plateau, you’re surrounded by trails, tall trees, and quiet water—perfect for couples who want the outdoors to be part of the story.
  * **Intimate ceremonies might unfold on a stunning waterfront patio,** beautifully crafted with native Crab Orchard stone. Just inside is the park’s beloved restaurant (also the in-house caterer for all events), where you’ll find multiple gathering spaces with floor-to-ceiling windows framing views of the lake. 
  * **The historic Recreation Center, built by the CCC in the 1930s,** is another lovely option, with warm wood paneling, a stone fireplace, and beautiful arcing trusses. After the celebration winds down, you and your guests can retire in one of the cozy on-site cabins or lodges—there are plenty of activities to be had tomorrow!
