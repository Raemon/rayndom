## Why You'll Love This Place

##### A Here Comes The Guide Wedding Venue Review

Read More Show Less

  * **This D.C.-convenient vineyard boasts wonderful views,** both of the picturesque rows of vines and the deep, surrounding woods. Several nearby hotels and B&Bs offer an added convenience to your overnight guests. 
  * **The old-world inspired Tuscan Villa can host your ceremony and reception,** and features a covered portico perfect for cocktail hour. It boasts a soaring cathedral ceiling hung with crystal chandeliers and drop-pendant lights, and floor-to-ceiling windows overlooking the pretty grounds.
  * **For something more rustic (but just as chic),** check out the Hilltop Pavilion, whose open sides ensure your guests can take in 360-degree views of the vineyard, valley, and sunset.

### DEALS: _What discounts, special offers, or packages are available here?_

**The Hilltop Pavilion** **can host up to 60 guests!**  
Pricing starts at $1,350 for 3 hours, and includes tables & chairs for up to 60. Additional time is $200–275/hour.

### PRICES: _What's this going to cost?_

###### Rental Fee

  * **$3,000–10,500/event**
  * Rental fee varies depending on: 
    * Day of week
    * Time of year
  * _The rental fee includes:_

    * 8 hours of event time (excluding setup and cleanup time)
    * Dressing suite
    * Ceremony site with white padded chairs, wine barrel décor, a pergola, a stone aisle, and a gorgeous vine tunnel
    * Cocktail hour & reception in the Tuscan Villa and portico
    * Dining & banquet tables, Chiavari chairs, and dance floor



###### Service Charge or Gratuity

  * **18%**

  * Beverage packages range $35–45/person. Cash bars are also available.
