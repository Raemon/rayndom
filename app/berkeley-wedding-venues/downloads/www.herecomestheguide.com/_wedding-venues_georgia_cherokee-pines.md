  * **This hidden middle-Georgia gem features a lush, park-like setting,** flexible event options both indoors and out, and a lovely, thoughtful staff dedicated to making each couple’s unique vision a reality. 
  * **Hold your ceremony under the sprawling oaks** or in the quaint gazebo, followed by a reception in the handsome, well-appointed banquet room, which boasts a large wooden dance floor, candelabra chandeliers, and French doors that open to an airy patio. 
  * **Their generous full-wedding package** includes access to the venue starting on Friday afternoon, allowing you ample time for setup. You also have the option to wed at the sweet vintage chapel in Forest Hill Park (just five minutes away), then follow with a reception at Cherokee Pines.
