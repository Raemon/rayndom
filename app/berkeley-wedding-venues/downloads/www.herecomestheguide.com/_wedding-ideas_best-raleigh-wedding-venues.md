# 12 of Raleigh's Best Wedding Venues

## 

**With its world-class museums, cool craft breweries, and historical neighborhoods, you're sure to find the perfect venue to fit your wedding style in Raleigh.** Whether you're dreaming of a romantic soiree in a timeless mansion, an al fresco affair among ancient oak trees, or a rustic gathering in a weathered barn, you’re guaranteed to find something spectacular in and around this beautiful city.
