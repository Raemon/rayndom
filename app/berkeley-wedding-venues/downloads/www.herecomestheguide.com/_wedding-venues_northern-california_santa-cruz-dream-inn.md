### PRICES: _What's this going to cost?_

###### Rental Fee

  * **$3,500/event and up**
  * Rental fee varies depending on: 
    * Day of week
    * Time of year
    * Space reserved
  * The venue fee includes room rental, self-parking, complimentary tasting, tables, chairs and linens, dance floor, cake cutting, venue coordination and more. 




###### Ceremony Fee

  * **$1,350–2,500/event**
  *     * The Cowell Beach Cremony Package is $2,500 +tax
    * The Oceanview Ceremony is $1,350 +tax.
    * both options include set-up and breakdown, ceremony arch or arbor. 
    * Ask about the "You, Me & The Sea" rehearsal | reception | brunch option!



###### Package Prices

  * **$95/person and up**
  * Package prices vary depending on: 
    * Package chosen
  *     * Catering packages include plated dinner or buffet, two desserts, and coffee/tea. 
    * Farewell Brunch packages start at $70/pp
    * After-Party snacks available as an add-on, too!



###### Service Charge or Gratuity

  * **23%**
