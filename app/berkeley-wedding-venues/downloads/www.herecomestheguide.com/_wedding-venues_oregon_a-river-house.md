## Why You'll Love This Place

##### A Here Comes The Guide Wedding Venue Review

Read More Show Less

  * **Sitting along the south bank of the Rogue River on an expanse of pretty forest and meadows,** this retreat offers dreamy views at every turn, intimate, romantic gathering spots, and a collection of sweet overnight accommodations.
  * **The gently moving river provides a tranquil backdrop for vows,** and the celebration continues waterside, with expansive lawns that host woodsy receptions beneath towering pines and strings of bistro lights. 
  * **With weekday, two-day, micro-wedding, elopement packages and more,** you’ll find a package to fit your vision. Add in a weekend stay at their darling vacation cottages, which sleep 14–16 guests between them, and boast charming vintage-chic décor.
