## Why You'll Love This Place

##### A Here Comes The Guide Wedding Venue Review

Read More Show Less

  * **It's got the tranquil feel of a private country estate****—** without the long drive! Not only is this dedicated event space an easy trip from both D.C. and Baltimore, there are loads of entertainment and overnight accommodation options in nearby Hagerstown. 
  * **Say "I do" in a quaint stone courtyard,** where you'll find a delicate latticework arbor and a backdrop of manicured juniper trees. In the event of rain, there's plenty of space for a tent.
