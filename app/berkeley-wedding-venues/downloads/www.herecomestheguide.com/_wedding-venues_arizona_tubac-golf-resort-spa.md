## Why You'll Love This Place

##### A Here Comes The Guide Wedding Venue Review

Read More Show Less

  * **Set on the site of historic Otero Ranch,** this dreamy oasis is surrounded by 500 sprawling acres of native plants, trees, and pastures, and features breathtaking views of the Santa Rita and Tumacacori mountain ranges.
  * **A replica of a 1700s mission-style chapel,** complete with dramatic mesquite doors and a chapel bell, sets the scene for your outdoor ceremony. Follow with a reception in the graceful Geronimo Ballroom, or in a clear-sided tent under the stars.
  * **Your guests will feel right at home in the lovely, hacienda-style accommodations,** which boast charming amenities like cozy fireplaces, lounge areas with plush leather furnishings, and private patios overlooking the grounds.
