### PRICES: _What's this going to cost?_

###### Rental Fee

  * **$1,095–3,800/event**
  * Rental fee varies depending on: 
    * Day of week
    * Space reserved
  * Full house rental fees range $1,500–3,800, and include:

    * 10-hour rental, ending at midnight
    * Use of the first floor of the main house and Carriage House and the courtyard
    * Second floor library and changing room for wedding party prep
    * Carriage House apartment for wedding party prep
    * Reception tables and chairs for up to 65 (additional chairs available to rent)
    * Two private parking lots
    * Additional days may be added at a discounted rate

Partial use of the house (inside of Main House or Carriage House Bar and Courtyard) ranges $1,095–2,850, for guest counts under 100.

A security officer is required for guest counts over 50, or any event serving alcohol.
