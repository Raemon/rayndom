**If you're dreaming of a gorgeous wedding in Texas, the state's Gulf Coast could be the answer to your prayers.** It offers a 350-mile-long stretch of sandy beaches, not to mention lots of things to do and see—and, of course, a great selection of wedding venues that are on or near the water.

###  [ 26 Texas Wedding Chapels With Exceptional Charm ](https://www.herecomestheguide.com/wedding-ideas/texas-wedding-chapels)

**The right church can transform a wedding ceremony into an uplifting and inspirational experience, especially when the place itself is special.** Texas boasts a wide variety of chapels—historic, impressive, intimate and charming, and open-air for starters—and we've highlighted a selection that offers something for everyone.

* * *

[ ](https://www.herecomestheguide.com/wedding-ideas/unique-texas-b-and-b-inn-wedding-venues)

###  [ 8 Unique Texas B&Bs: Combine Your Wedding Venue And Lodging! ](https://www.herecomestheguide.com/wedding-ideas/unique-texas-b-and-b-inn-wedding-venues)

**Bed & Breakfast Inns not only serve as unique wedding venues, they also provide overnight accommodations.** Texas is rich in B&Bs, and the ones featured here offer a variety of event settings.

* * *

[ ](https://www.herecomestheguide.com/wedding-ideas/unique-texas-and-oklahoma-wedding-venues-spotlight-on-the-springs)

###  [ Unique Texas and Oklahoma Wedding Venues: Spotlight On The Springs ](https://www.herecomestheguide.com/wedding-ideas/unique-texas-and-oklahoma-wedding-venues-spotlight-on-the-springs)

**The Springs Weddings & Events offers a variety of premier venues with a focus on natural beauty. In fact, nature is everywhere**—in the gorgeous landscaping at each location, as well as in the wood and stone elements inside their reception halls.

**If nature is an essential element of your wedding vision, start your search by exploring these terrific Houston venues.** From rustic and quaint to Shabby Chic and Old World Spanish, each one has its own distinct personality.

**If there's one thing Texans know how to do, it's throw a great party.** (Well, that, and barbecue!) And the Houston area is home to some of the Lone Star State's most incredible wedding venues. Whether it's a private estate shaded by century-old oaks, an open-air chapel, or a historic cotton gin, you'll find something to love in each of our favorite Houston wedding venues.
