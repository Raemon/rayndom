### PRICES: _What's this going to cost?_

###### Rental Fee

  * **$2,500/event**
  * _The rental fee includes:_

    * Bridal retreat suite with gilded golden ceilings 
    * Overnight suite for the newlyweds
    * Tables, chairs, ivory linens, sterling silver flatware, Bauscher china, Riedel stemware, sterling silver table stands with numbers, and votive candles
    * Cake table, silver cake cutting knife, and server
    * Dance floor
    * Vendor ‘green room’
    * A/V system
    * Venue access three hours prior to the start of the event
    * Setup & break down
    * Tasting for two



###### Ceremony Fee

  * **$1,500/event**
  * _The ceremony fee includes:_

    * Ceremony A/V
    * 1-hour rehearsal/walk-through on a pre-arranged day before the wedding
    * Ceremony chairs set theater-style, with a center aisle
    * Setup & breakdown of the ceremony room
    * Private room for the signing of the marriage documents



###### Food & Beverage Minimum

  * **$15,000/event**



###### Meals (when priced separately)

  * **$95–125/person**
  * Meal packages include plated dinner; appetizers, a starter, a choice of entrées, dessert, and late-night selections.

  * **24%**

  * Alcohol packages start at $48/person; the bartender fee is not included.
