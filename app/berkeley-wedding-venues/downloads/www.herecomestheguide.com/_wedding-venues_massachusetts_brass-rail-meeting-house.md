## Why You'll Love This Place

##### A Here Comes The Guide Wedding Venue Review

Read More Show Less

  * **With its pretty, park-like setting** and flexible indoor/outdoor event spaces, this private lakeside venue isn’t just lovely—it’s also affordable and convenient, with in-house amenities like catering, bar services, two dressing suites, and valet parking. 
  * **The outdoor deck features a charming, Victorian-inspired gazebo** to shelter your ceremony. It’s spacious enough to host a fully al fresco reception, with the gazebo doubling as the perfect spot for a band or DJ. 
  * **The main ballroom is a fantastic choice for your indoor celebration,** with its warm, inviting ambiance, and the chic lower ballroom is ideal for intimate gatherings, boasting a dramatic, contemporary design.
