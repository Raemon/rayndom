## Why You'll Love This Place

##### A Here Comes The Guide Wedding Venue Review

Read More Show Less

  * **Designed by renowned architect George Washington Maher,** this turn-of-the-century gem is a shining example of the midwest's "Prairie School" of building design. It's been lovingly preserved and maintains the majority of its original detailing.
  * **The entire first floor and expansive wraparound porch are yours for the day,** allowing you to structure your event however you choose. Guests will love mingling throughout the beautiful rooms, which are replete with period art and furnishings.
  * **Two opulent upstairs suites are available for your day-of preparations,** and the breathtaking stained glass windows in the upper landing make a fabulous backdrop for first-look photos or portraits.
