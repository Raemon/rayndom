# Moody and Magnificent: 26 Gothic Wedding Venues Across the US

## 

**Forget the white wedding—it's time to go dark.** With an architectural legacy that spans back to the 11th century, the Gothic style and its soaring spires, flying buttresses, and intricate, pointed arches, is iconic both in structure and vibe. For couples searching for a dramatic backdrop that echoes their grand love story, these enchanting, moody venues—from sprawling, turreted castles to hidden garden sanctuaries to picturesque chapels—offer an alluring Gothic romance you won't soon forget.
