### DEALS: _What discounts, special offers, or packages are available here?_

**Micro-Wedding Packages****range $2,250–3,250, and include a 4-hour rental for up to 50 guests.** _Available Monday–Thursday._

**Elopements****start at $1,500, with three options to accommodate up to 25 guests.** Elopements include padded folding chairs, a ceremony site of hoice, an officiant, bouquet + boutonnière, and on-site parking. VIP options also include professional photography.

_Available Sunday–Thursday only, and must be reserved within 60 days of the wedding date._

    * Tables, rustic cross back chairs, and white padded resin ceremony chairs, set to your specifications
    * Caterer's prep area and large bar with bartenders
    * NO additional service fees or taxes
