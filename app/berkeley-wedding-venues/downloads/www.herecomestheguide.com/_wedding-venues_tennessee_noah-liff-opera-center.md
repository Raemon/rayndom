## Why You'll Love This Place

##### A Here Comes The Guide Wedding Venue Review

Read More Show Less

  * **It’s a sleek, modern space with sparkling architecture.** The award-winning Noah Liff Opera Center features soaring skylights, suspended chandeliers, and walls comprised entirely of windows.
  * **Its chic rooms can accommodate a range of events.** From a simple cocktail party for 20 to an elaborate gala, there’s a room to suit your needs. The striking glass details and state-of-the-art lighting throughout are guaranteed to impress your guests.
  * **Packages are all-inclusive, but still flexible.** You get a generous 10 hours of rental time, plus helpful extras like the use of a dressing suite, professional event lighting, and your choice of finish on the stylish wooden chairs. They provide an extensive list of approved caterers from which to choose, and you can even bring in your own alcohol at no additional cost.
