## Why You'll Love This Place

##### A Here Comes The Guide Wedding Venue Review

Read More Show Less

  * **With its incredible Blue Ridge Mountain surroundings and quiet spot along the French Broad River,** this tranquil nature preserve encompasses 175 acres of unspoiled Western North Carolina beauty, and is just a few minutes from the galleries, shops, restaurants, and breweries of historic Brevard.
  * **Host an intimate wedding in the Coyote Lodge,** whose gracious great room boasts an impressive 1920s stone fireplace (it's even overlooked by a second floor balcony for your bouquet toss!) For large celebrations, there’s the newly built Mayes Event Center, which features attractive industrial-vintage design with rollup glass doors that open onto a patio.
  * **Charming cabin accommodations can house nearly everyone on your list;** options include quaint spots for singles and couples, and grand lodges for groups and multiple families. Full property buyouts are welcome.
