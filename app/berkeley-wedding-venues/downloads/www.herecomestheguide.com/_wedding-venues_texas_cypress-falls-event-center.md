## Why You'll Love This Place

##### A Here Comes The Guide Wedding Venue Review

Read More Show Less

  * **Located in Texas Hill Country about an hour outside of Austin** , this private event center is filled with graceful trees, picturesque fields, and even a waterfall. A shaded grove on the edge of the creek running through the property provides a dreamy rustic outdoor ceremony setting.
  * **You'll love their All-Inclusive Package option!** It includes everything in the basic wedding package, use of all on-site décor, all linens and chair ties, lounge furniture, photo booth, champagne toast for all guests, and a complimentary wedding-night stay in their Bungalow.
  * **On-site accommodations at the Lodge make it easy for out-of-town guests to stay.** Built in 1949, the 22-room vintage retreat features a swimming hole, fishing, kayaking, stand-up paddling, a pool, and tennis courts, along with a continental breakfast in the morning.
