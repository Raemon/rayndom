  * **$4,995–6,995/event**

    * All-day use of the venue and 18-acre property

    * Choice of five ceremony locations, set with an arch/altar and seating
    * Rolling beverage carts with water bottles
    * Reception hall set with tables, chairs, and linens
    * Prep kitchen and pizza oven
    * Use of golf cart for photos and wedding party transportation
    * Venue manager, staff, and security
    * Corn hole boards, foosball, and catch and release fishing in the 6-acre pond
