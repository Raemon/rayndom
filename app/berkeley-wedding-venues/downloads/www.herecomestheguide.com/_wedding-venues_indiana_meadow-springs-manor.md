### PRICES: _What's this going to cost?_

###### Package Prices

  * **$6,500–8,000/event**
  * The _Bronze_ package includes:

    * Property access 9:30am–midnight the day of the event
    * Two dressing rooms
    * Tables & chairs
    * Setup & teardown
    * Two 1-hour planning sessions
    * Shuttle to local hotels (one pickup & one drop off for up to 30 guests)
    * Use of the property for engagement photos

The _Silver_ package includes everything in _Bronze_ PLUS:

    * Additional property access Friday 10am–7pm
    * Friday night stay in the Manor

The _Gold_ package includes everything in _Silver_ PLUS:

    * Additional property access through Sunday noon the day after the wedding
    * Saturday night stay in the Manor
    * Table linens (up to 40)



###### Meals (when priced separately)

  * **$25–30/person**
  * Meal packages include buffet or plated dinner, iced tea & lemonade, plate chargers, dinner & dessert plates, stemmed water goblets, and silverware.
