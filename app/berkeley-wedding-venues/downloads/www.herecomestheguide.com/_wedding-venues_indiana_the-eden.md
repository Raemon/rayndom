###### Rental Fee

  * **$2,250–2,750/event**
  * Rental fee varies depending on: 
    * Day of week
  * The rental fee includes:

    * Access to the venue 8am–11:30pm
    * Tables, mahogany wood folding chairs, white table cloths, and choice of colored cloth napkins
    * Setup and cleanup of floor plan
    * Event attendant

An additional day's rental may be available for $1,000 (based on availability 90 days prior).




###### Ceremony Fee

  * **$1,250/event**

  * **$19–35/person**
  * Meal pricing includes buffet-style dinner, accompaniments, salad, and rolls & butter, plus all labor & service charges.
