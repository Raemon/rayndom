## Why You'll Love This Place

##### A Here Comes The Guide Wedding Venue Review

Read More Show Less

  * **If “romantic”, “rustic”, “vintage”, and “rural” are on your wedding inspo list,** you’ll definitely want to check out this sweet, family-owned lavender farm, with its rich local history, quaint charm, and boundless dreamy photo opportunities. 
  * **Intimate weddings have so many lovely backdrops to choose from,** including a meadow beside the 1950s gambrel barn, meadows of prairie grass, a weathered, whitewashed corn crib, and of course the richly colored lavender fields.
  * **They offer a collection of fun and helpful add-ons,** including spool tables and matching stools, party tents in various sizes, and tasty lavender lemonade for your cocktail hour.
