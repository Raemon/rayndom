## Why You'll Love This Place

##### A Here Comes The Guide Wedding Venue Review

Read More Show Less

  * **Originally built in the ‘20s as an Oddfellows Lodge,** this stately gem is an Oregon City treasure, having been carefully restored to update amenities and flow while preserving historic details. 
  * **The interior boasts a ballroom, grand hall, two lounges, and a gorgeous Art Deco bar,** all tied together with graceful antique elements like elegant wainscoting, vintage light fixtures, and large, original windows.
  * **In-house catering is provided by** _**The Hive Catering Co.**_**,** and guests are certain to rave about their luscious, seasonal, farm-to-table fare with a Northwest flair.
