## Why You'll Love This Place

##### A Here Comes The Guide Wedding Venue Review

Read More Show Less

  * **This pretty upstate spot is surrounded by acres of peaceful grasslands,** with its charming crimson barn at the property’s heart. You’ll find plenty of lodging options nearby, as it’s just a few miles outside Greenville.
  * **Traditional barn doors open to reveal a gorgeous interior,** boasting a soaring gambrel ceiling with dramatic exposed rafters. Polished concrete flooring adds a modern touch, and the entire space is illuminated by twinkle lights and rustic-chic wagon wheel chandeliers.
  * **There are patios on both sides of the barn,** creating excellent indoor/outdoor flow. A climate-controlled dressing suite is available the morning of your event, and the owners offer great discounts to military, veterans, and first responders.
