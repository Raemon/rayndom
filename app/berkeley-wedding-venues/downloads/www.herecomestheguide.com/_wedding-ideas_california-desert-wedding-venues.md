# 5 Hot California Wedding Venues with Cool Desert Vibes

## 

**Dreaming of sunset-colored canyons, tribal touches, and fancy flora for your big day? Then a desert wedding is for you!** These locations turn up the heat with postcard-worthy panoramas and an adventurous "middle of nowhere" vibe. From a Spanish Revival mountainside hideaway in Palm Springs to a charming adobe resort in the High Desert, these cool California venues provide a one-of-a-kind experience thanks to their bold boho-chic aesthetic and laid-back landscape.
