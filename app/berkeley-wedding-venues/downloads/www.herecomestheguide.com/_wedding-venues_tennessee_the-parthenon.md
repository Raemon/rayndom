### PRICES: _What's this going to cost?_

###### Rental Fee

  * **$5,000/event and up**
  * An Olympian Level Membership is required to hold your after-hours event at The Parthenon. Memberships start at $5,000 and range to $10,000 for holiday dates. These fees include:

    * Access to the Parthenon Naos, Treasury, art galleries, lobby, and restrooms
    * Parthenon Event Manager, museum staff, park police officer, and custodian throughout
    * Venue access from 4:30pm–midnight, including setup & teardown
    * Rehearsal prior to the event (requires a $300 staffing fee)
    * Add-on the Parthenon Plaza for $1,000
