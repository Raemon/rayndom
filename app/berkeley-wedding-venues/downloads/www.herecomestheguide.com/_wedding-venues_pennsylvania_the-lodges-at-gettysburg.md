## Why You'll Love This Place

##### A Here Comes The Guide Wedding Venue Review

Read More Show Less

  * **From its ridge-top location,** this historic retreat features a collection of charming vintage stone and wood buildings encircled by 37 acres of lush woodlands, and offers views across the Gettysburg battlefields and Hunter Lake.
  * **A horse-drawn carriage brings you to your dockside ceremony beside the lake.** Snap some photos around the majestic property while your guests sip cocktails on the lawn, then shift to the dramatic two-story reception hall for party time; it’s a true star, with its cathedral ceiling, glossy hardwood flooring, and gaslamp-style chandeliers. 
  * **Each of the 29 guest lodges has a cozy, inviting feel,** and each boasts wonderful period art pieces donated by Dale Gallon Historical Art. Fully outfitted with modern amenities, the lodges range from traditional hotel-style rooms to large single family-style cabins.
