## Why You'll Love This Place

##### A Here Comes The Guide Wedding Venue Review

Read More Show Less

  * **The natural setting: This 40-acre property with 20 acres of vineyards** produces great weddings as well as wines—and it's only two miles north of Long Island Sound!
  * **Vineyard celebrations: Romantic ceremonies and receptions are held outdoors** , with the vineyards as a beautiful backdrop. Watching the sun set makes a lovely ending to your festivities.
  * **Farm-to table cuisine: Each menu, which is customized by their chef** , features fresh, local ingredients. Family-style farm dinners are popular! And if you love wine, opt for a private wine cellar tour and tasting for your group.



### PRICES: _What's this going to cost?_

###### Rental Fee

  * **$525/event and up**
  * Rental fee varies depending on: 
    * Time of year
    * Space reserved
    * Event duration
  * Venue rental is priced hourly. 

The Barn rents for $525–575/hour. The Back Courtyard is $1,500/hour, plus a tent rental fee. A ceremony on the Lawn is included with a reception; ceremony-only rentals of the Lawn are $500/hour. 




###### Meals (when priced separately)

  * **$85–110/person**
  * Pricing above applies to a coursed family-style or plated meal; hors d'oeuvres are priced separately. Bar packages range at $17–28/person for 3 hours (Chamard Vineyards wine is priced separately, by consumption).
