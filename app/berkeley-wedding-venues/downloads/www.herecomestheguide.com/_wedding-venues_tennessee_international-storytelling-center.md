### DEALS: _What discounts, special offers, or packages are available here?_

**Micro-Weddings and Elopements**  
 _Available Monday–Thursday from 10am–8pm only._

**Micro-weddings range $1,100–1,650** and include:

  * Up to 20 people (Package 1) or 35 people (Package 2)
  * 2.5-hour venue use (Package 1) or 4-hour venue use (Package 2)
  * Officiant to perform the ceremony
  * Personal florals for the couple
  * Professional photography team
  * Single-tier or two-tier wedding cake
  * Tables, chairs, and on-site décor 
  * Catering kitchen, lobby, and balcony



**Elopements range $330–550** , and include:

  * 2- to 4-hour use of your chosen locations
  * Tables, chairs, and on-site décor 
  * Catering kitchen, lobby, and balcony
