## Why You'll Love This Place

##### A Here Comes The Guide Wedding Venue Review

Read More Show Less

  * **Tucked on the edge of Arizona’s serene White Mountains,** this beloved restaurant**** and event venue offers the perfect combination of elements for a fabulous wedding day: a cozy, rustic-chic ambiance, delicious custom-crafted cuisine, and impeccable service from a passionate, family-owned team.
  * **Celebrations unfold between the light-filled, wood-paneled Saloon,** spacious inside deck, and lower deck areas, all interconnected for an easy flow from event to event. There’s even an adorable dressing cottage—originally built in 1873, it’s now a fully outfitted prep suite, complete with full-length mirrors and ample seating for your crew.
  * **All-inclusive packages are made extra special thanks to the fabulous hand-crafted cuisine,** which showcases seasonally inspired elevated comfort food—think housemade pizzas, bourbon-forward cocktails, and a locally famous cheesecake made from scratch by Lisa, one of the venue’s welcoming owners.
