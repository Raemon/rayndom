## Why You'll Love This Place

##### A Here Comes The Guide Wedding Venue Review

Read More Show Less

  * **Historic, secluded, and completely charming,** this North Georgia B&B is surrounded by acres of meadows and perennial gardens, nestled against the lush backdrop of the Chattahoochee National Forest. 
  * **The gardens, grounds, and elegant, rustic-chic pavilion** may be used in any combination you’d like, and there are options onsite for your rehearsal dinner, as well. Secret bonus alert: It’s consistently voted one of the best dining destinations in Georgia. 
  * **Sixteen beautifully appointed rooms in the B &B—**penthouse, king and queen, and three-room suites—can accommodate up to 36 overnight guests, with ample additional lodging just down the road in Clarkesville.
