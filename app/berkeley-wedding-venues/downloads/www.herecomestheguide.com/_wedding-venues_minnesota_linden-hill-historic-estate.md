### PRICES: _What's this going to cost?_

###### Package Prices

  * **$2,250–12,000/event**
  * Package prices vary depending on: 
    * Day of week
    * Time of year
    * Package chosen
  * **Peak season** (May–October) pricing ranges $5,750–9,750 for Standard packages, and $8,000–12,000 for Deluxe packages.

**Off-peak season** (November–April) pricing ranges $2,250–4,000 for Standard packages and $4,500–6,250 for Deluxe packages.

* * *

**Standard packages** include:

    * 3-day rental time (except Monday–Wednesday, which are single day rentals)
    * Full use of the main floor and lower level of the Musser Mansion
    * The Pavilion and both upper & lower grounds _(not included in off-peak packages, November–April)_
    * Dressing suite
    * Commercial kitchen and butler's pantry access
    * A/V system, bluetooth sound system, mics, and speakers
    * Tables & chairs
    * Fully-stocked bar with choice of open, hosted, or combo bar, plus 1–2 bartenders and 5 hours of service
    * Use of spaces for rehearsal + dinner, and Musser Mansion for post-wedding brunch (_not included in weekday rentals_)

**Deluxe packages** include everything in the Standard Package, PLUS overnight lodging for up to 3 nights for 25 guests.
