## Why You'll Love This Place

##### A Here Comes The Guide Wedding Venue Review

  * **Located on the lovely Statler Wilson Commons of West Virginia University,** this stately, modern event center boasts a beautiful location in the heart of the Appalachians, and a host of stylish venues outfitted with all the essential amenities. 
  * **The Alumni Courtyard offers an expansive green space,** perfect for al fresco ceremonies and receptions. Or, opt for an indoor celebration, surrounded by the understated elegance of the Ruby Grand Hall. 
  * **Searching for something more intimate?** Consider the Corson Alma Mater room, with its deep-brown wood flooring, inviting fireplace, oversized windows, and digital grand piano.
