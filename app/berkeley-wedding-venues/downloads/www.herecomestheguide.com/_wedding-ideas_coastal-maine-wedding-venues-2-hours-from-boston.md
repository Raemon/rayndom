# 8 Charming Coastal Maine Wedding Venues Within A 2-hour Drive From Boston

## 

**Planning a Boston wedding, but keep finding yourself dreaming of a sweet seaside celebration?** Then hop in the car and head to the southern coast of Maine where you'll have no trouble finding an epic waterfront location along this romantic and rugged shoreline. From an iconic cliffside resort to an elegant private club on the beach, we've pulled some wow-worthy wedding venues all within a 2-hour drive from Boston.

* * *

## [The Colony Hotel](https://www.herecomestheguide.com/wedding-venues/maine/the-colony-hotel)

### Kennebunkport, ME

[ [ Casey Durgin Photography ](https://caseydurginphotography.com/) ](https://www.herecomestheguide.com/wedding-venues/maine/the-colony-hotel)

**Fab Features** : grand historic hotel perched on an oceanfront bluff in iconic Kennebunkport • celebrate al fresco on the lawn overlooking the waves or in the warm, beautifully appointed dining room • pet-friendly guest rooms offer classic New England charm, with pretty wallpaper, gleaming wood floors, and bright windows that frame sea views

**Catering:** In-house

### York, ME

[ ](https://www.herecomestheguide.com/wedding-venues/maine/dockside-restaurant-on-york-harbor)
