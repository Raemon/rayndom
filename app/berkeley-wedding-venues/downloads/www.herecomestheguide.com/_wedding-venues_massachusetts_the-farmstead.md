  * **Full weekend rentals include overnight lodging for 40.** Country weddings come to life at this rustic-chic farmhouse set on 25 acres of manicured lawns, pastures, and gardens. When you host a destination wedding weekend here, you get to spend a lot more quality time with friends and family!
  * **Al fresco receptions are held under a beautiful sailcloth tent** , with plenty of space for guests to explore. Ceremonies take place at a variety of romantic sites including in a forest glade, in front of an arbor flanked by hydrangeas, or on a lovely lawn bordered by stone walls.
