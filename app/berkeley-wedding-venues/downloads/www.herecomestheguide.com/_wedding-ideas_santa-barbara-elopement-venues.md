# 4 Santa Barbara Venues Perfect for Small Weddings and Elopements

## 

**From the crystal-blue shoreline to its rustic Wine Country, Santa Barbara is pure romance—and not surprisingly, a popular elopement destination.** Whether you’re on a budget or willing to splurge, you’ll find some tempting options among these venues that welcome small weddings.

* * *

## [Santa Barbara Zoo](https://www.herecomestheguide.com/wedding-venues/southern-california/santa-barbara-zoo)

### Santa Barbara, CA

[ ](https://www.herecomestheguide.com/wedding-venues/southern-california/santa-barbara-zoo)

**Fab Features:** 30 acres of lush gardens with ocean views, set between a bird sanctuary and the beach • intimate weddings in the Discovery Pavilion, with its floor to ceiling windows, built-in audio, and doors that open to an outdoor patio for cocktails • no need to rent elephants—they're already on-site, along with giraffes, lions, penguins, and more!

**Catering:** In-house   
**Price:** $ • $$
