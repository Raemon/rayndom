### DEALS: _What discounts, special offers, or packages are available here?_

**Elopement packages for under 30 guests** include 4-hour access to the venue, including event space and use of the lodge for the wedding party. _Inquire for details and pricing!_

### PRICES: _What's this going to cost?_

###### Rental Fee

  * **$7,500/event and up**
  * _All rentals include:_

    * Use of the Pavilion, Lodge, and Breezeway
    * Ceremony rehearsal and access for engagement photos
    * Use of the lodge and cabin for wedding party prep
    * Complimentary parking with shuttle service (if desired)
    * Tables, navy or white linens, rustic church pews, and chairs

Ceremony + reception rentals run Saturday 9am–11pm. Rehearsal dinners add access Friday 5–11pm; reception-only rentals begin three hours prior to event start time.




###### Additional Info

  * On-site lodging for up to 20 people in the lodge, cabin, barn, and breezeway may be booked separately; pricing ranges from $800 for one night to $1,500 for two nights.
