  * **Cincinnati’s parks, historic monuments, and glittering skyline are all on view** from this elevated event space. Tucked into the side Mt. Adams, it’s truly a jewel in the crown of the “Queen City.” 
  * **The Ballroom, Highland, and Hearth Rooms host celebrations in a range of sizes,** and all feature dramatic, contemporary-chic design, with floor-to-ceiling windows that offer unparalleled bird’s eye panoramas.
