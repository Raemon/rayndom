### PRICES: _What's this going to cost?_

###### Rental Fee

  * **$400–3,000/event**
  * Rental fee varies depending on: 
    * Day of week
    * Time of year
    * Space reserved
  * The room rental fee includes:

    * Exclusive use of the venue 10am–midnight
    * Dressing suites
    * Outdoor courtyard, backyard, and fire pit
    * Tables, Chiavari or wood cross-back chairs, and AV equipment
    * Free on-site parking
    * Name on marquee signs
    * Access to community boat docks



###### Package Prices

  * **$60–105/person**
  * Package prices vary depending on: 
    * Day of week
    * Package chosen
  * _All wedding packages include:_

    * Two dressing suites with food & drinks
    * Buffet dinner with two entrées, sides, and bread
    * Bar package (cocktails, wine, beer, seltzers, and champagne)
    * Unlimited soda and coffee
    * In-house décor options with setup & cleanup
    * Wedding planner & host, bartenders, servers, chefs, setup & cleanup team
    * Late-night snack

Upgraded packages can include dessert display(s), an expanded alcohol package, and more

Above pricing applies to up the first 100 guests; additional guests range $45–85/person.
