# Beacons of Love: 9 Romantic Lighthouse Wedding Venues in New England

## 

**Picture this: saying your vows in New England with a charming lighthouse in the background, its light shining like the love you share.** If you're looking for a wedding spot that's both romantic and rich in seafaring history, these coastal spots are perfect! Imagine making your promises with the ocean stretching out before you, sea breeze in your hair, and these iconic towers watching over your special moment. Check out our handpicked selection of the most enchanting lighthouse wedding venues across New England, where history, stunning views, and coastal magic come together to create an unforgettable beginning to your marriage.
