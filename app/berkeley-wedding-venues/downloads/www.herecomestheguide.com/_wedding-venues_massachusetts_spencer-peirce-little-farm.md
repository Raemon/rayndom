## Why You'll Love This Place

##### A Here Comes The Guide Wedding Venue Review

Read More Show Less

  * **A beautiful 230-acre site in charming Newbury,** this family-friendly farm boasts early Colonial buildings, interactive exhibits, and sweet foster farm animals who are always ready for their closeup. 
  * **There are several beautiful locations for your outdoor ceremony—** our fave is the front lawn, with the historic 1600s home as your backdrop. Host an intimate reception in the barn, or a lavish tented celebration on the grounds. 
  * **Rental fees help support Historic New England's stewardship and preservation** of this and other important properties throughout the area. Your rental comes with a "thank you" membership to the organization, which includes free tours of this spot, and dozens of others.
