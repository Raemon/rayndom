## Why You'll Love This Place

##### A Here Comes The Guide Wedding Venue Review

Read More Show Less

  * **Owned by two sisters with a flair for entertaining and event design,** this custom-built venue (named for their maternal and paternal grandmothers) has a flexible layout and ideal location, convenient to Alabama, Georgia, and Tennessee. 
  * **The Tree Deck offers an elevated, whimsical spot for your al fresco ceremony,** and you’ll follow with an equally enchanting cocktail hour in the sweet, string-lit courtyard and a reception in either the Candlelight Pavilion or the Conservatory, both of which boast airy, modern style.
  * **A wide range of packages make choosing your best match easy,** and consist of single day, multi-day, and intimate options. Weekend packages include lodging for up to 20 guests in the Cobblestone, Camellia, and Timberland Cottages.
