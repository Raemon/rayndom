# 4 Venues Perfect For Your Orange County Wedding Weekend

## 

**Want your OC wedding celebration to last all weekend long?** We’ve hand-picked our favorite Orange County venues that not only provide a gorgeous backdrop for your event but are conveniently located near fun activities for you and your guests to enjoy and some even offer on-site accommodations.
