## Why You'll Love This Place

##### A Here Comes The Guide Wedding Venue Review

Read More Show Less

  * **Located on the banks of the Cooper River with stunning views** of the Arthur Ravenel, Jr. Bridge and Waterfront Park, this Mt. Pleasant event center offers a gorgeous backdrop for your celebration, both inside and out.
  * **With intricate murals depicting Lowcountry scenes** , hand-forged iron chandeliers, Italian textured walls, and a fireplace, the Cooper River Room provides a warm and welcoming reception space. 
  * **The adjacent porch supplies an additional area for mingling** and opens onto the Crescent Lawn, which can be tented if you'd like to bring the festivities outside. The lawn can also function as a lovely ceremony location.
