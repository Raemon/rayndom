## Why You'll Love This Place

##### A Here Comes The Guide Wedding Venue Review

Read More Show Less

  * **The multi-story Great Hall is the spot for a stunning reception** , with oversized exposed beams of pumpkin pine, wood floors, and incredible chandeliers of copper and glass.
  * **Host a fun, rustic cocktail hour in the Barn Gardens**. The barn opens to reveal a custom bar and there's a wonderful flow between the barn and its surrounding gardens.
  * **The inn provides everything you'll need for your entire wedding weekend.** Besides overnight accommodations for all your guests, there's a heated salt water pool, rehearsal dinner options, and a spa offering a full range of primping and pampering (including lash and nail treatments). They'll even put together a custom wedding party package for you and your besties.
