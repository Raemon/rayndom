## Why You'll Love This Place

##### A Here Comes The Guide Wedding Venue Review

Read More Show Less

  * **The year-round beauty of the southern Green Mountains surrounds this historic inn,** which was built in the 1800s as a summer retreat. Recently fully renovated, it boasts bespoke guest accommodations, a cozy tavern, and a world-class wine cellar. 
  * **There are pretty spots for ceremonies and photos absolutely everywhere on the 112-acre property,** which boasts apple orchards, a trout pond, a covered bridge, and vibrant gardens. The Lawn and the Paddocks offer sweeping meadows for tented receptions. 
  * **Thirteen inviting rooms provide lodging for overnighters,** and options include traditionally charming inn rooms and the wonderful, four-bedroom Carriage House. Your hosts can arrange a variety of cool experiences to round out your weekend, like fly fishing, spa treatments, and private wine tastings.
