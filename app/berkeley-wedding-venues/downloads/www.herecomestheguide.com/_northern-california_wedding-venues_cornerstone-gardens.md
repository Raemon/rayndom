## Why You'll Love This Place

##### A Here Comes The Guide Wedding Venue Review

Read More Show Less

  * **Nestled into the southwestern tip of the legendary Carneros region,** straddling both Sonoma and Napa Wine Country, Cornerstone Sonoma features several distinctive private event locations in which to entertain, including several gardens, a modern barn, Sperry tent with market lights and the lovely Lily Pond Lawn. Each site offers its own unique blend of art and gardens to enhance your celebration and provide a stunning backdrop for your event.
  * **_THE LILY POND LAWN / Ceremony_** Walk through a private floral-lined pathway to The Lily Pond Lawn. Here, you and your partner will say your vows in front of a breathtaking reflective pond, covered in blooming waterlilies.
  * **_THE WEDDING GARDEN / Dinner_** Enjoy an elegant outdoor dining experience under a custom designed circular tent, surrounded by white roses and stunning perennials.
  * **_THE GARDEN BARN / Cocktail Hour + Dancing_** Dance the night away in The Garden Barn, complete with indoor string market lights and large sliding doors to let in the fresh wine country air. The Garden Barn is heated and includes fire pits, Adirondack chairs, and teak tables for your use.
