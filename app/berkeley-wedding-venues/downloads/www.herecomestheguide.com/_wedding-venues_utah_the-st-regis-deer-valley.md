### PRICES: _What's this going to cost?_

###### Rental Fee

  * **$10,000/event**
  * _The wedding fee includes:_

    * Complimentary 1-bedroom suite for the couple on the wedding night and night prior
    * Ceremony set with wood garden chairs
    * Welcome beverage station
    * Audio set for the officiant including wireless mic and speakers
    * Complimentary plated menu tasting for four
    * One breakout room on the first floor for vendor storage or green room
    * Wood dance floor, staging, and printed menu cards
    * Mahogany Chiavari chairs, tables, linens, napkins, and china, stemware, and silverware
    * Dedicated banquet captain and white glove service



###### Food & Beverage Minimum

  * **$4,000/event and up**
  * Food & Beverage minimum varies depending on: 
    * Day of week
    * Time of year
    * Space reserved
  * The Astor Ballroom requires a food & beverage minimum of $60,000; the Wine Vault requires a $4,000 food & beverage minimum. 




###### Meals (when priced separately)

  * **$225–325/person**
  * Dinner menus typically range $225–325/person, and include butler-passed hors d'oeuvres, and a 3-course plated dinner with a petite dessert display. Bar packages are additional, and typically range $95–135/person.

Brunch menus typically range $85-160/person




###### Service Charge or Gratuity

  * **26%**
