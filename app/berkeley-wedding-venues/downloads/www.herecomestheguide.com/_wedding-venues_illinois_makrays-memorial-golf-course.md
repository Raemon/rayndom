## Why You'll Love This Place

##### A Here Comes The Guide Wedding Venue Review

Read More Show Less

  * **You’ll be surrounded by natural beauty** at this 18-hole championship-caliber golf club, from the lush green fairways to a serene lake, ringed by feathery natural grasses. A picturesque bridge over the water makes a great backdrop for wedding photos.
  * **A fireplace adorned with candles offers a warm welcome** as guests enter the Ballroom Foyer at the top of the Grand Staircase. The Ballroom itself treats diners to wonderful views of the surrounding fairways and tree-covered hills through floor-to-ceiling windows.
  * **You can upgrade your wedding package with a bevy of exciting extras** , including a blizzard machine, chocolate fountain, s’mores bar, and an ice sculpture! Plus, their restaurant, the Oak Terrace Grill, is a fabulous place for a rehearsal dinner—why not keeps things simple and host everything here?
