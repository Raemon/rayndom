## Why You'll Love This Place

##### A Here Comes The Guide Wedding Venue Review

Read More Show Less

  * **The sparkling lake is at the heart of all activities at this picturesque, historic rural venue;** it can be seen from every vantage point throughout the festivities, providing a colorful and serene atmosphere for photography and celebration.
  * **A white, tier-topped ceremony gazebo sits at the water’s edge,** perfectly framing the cascading fountain at the lake's center. Just steps away, you’ll find a custom-built reception hall with a charming rustic-modern aesthetic, boasting an attached outdoor terrace and tons of windows overlooking the grounds and woodlands. 
  * **Two separate villas serve as dressing suites for wedding parties,** and they’ve been outfitted with the most fabulous amenities, including light-filled prep areas, fireplaces, and newly remodeled kitchens and bathrooms.
