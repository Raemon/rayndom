# Don’t Break The Bank: 7 Affordable Palm Springs Wedding Venues

## 

**Palm Springs and the surrounding towns of the Coachella Valley may be hot wedding destinations,** but there are still an enticing selection of beautiful + affordable wedding venues. We’ve included our favorite spots that offer a good value with lots of style points, and also deliver when it comes to stunning vistas and great amenities. 

* * *

## [The Living Desert Zoo and Gardens](https://www.herecomestheguide.com/wedding-venues/southern-california/the-living-desert-zoo)

### Palm Desert, CA

[ [ Ryan Horban ](https://www.ryanhorban.com) ](https://www.herecomestheguide.com/wedding-venues/southern-california/the-living-desert-zoo)

**Fab Features:** vibrant oasis in Palm Desert, offering garden weddings on the Lurie Patio surrounded by flourishing succulents and cacti • receptions on the Palm Garden Patio, a lush space encircled by over 50 species of towering palms • invite your guests to feed giraffes from an elevated platform while sipping on cocktails!

**Catering:** In-house   
**Price:** $ • $$ 

[ Click Here for More Info](https://www.herecomestheguide.com/wedding-venues/southern-california/the-living-desert-zoo)

* * *

## [Indian Wells Country Club](https://www.herecomestheguide.com/wedding-venues/southern-california/indian-wells-country-club)

### Indian Wells, California

[ [ Ashley Durham ](https://ashdurham.com/) ](https://www.herecomestheguide.com/wedding-venues/southern-california/indian-wells-country-club)

**Fab Features:** prestigious golf known for its impeccable grounds and white-glove service • ceremonies on the 18th hole against a rugged mountain silhouette • lavish receptions in the grand ballroom where the wall of windows brings the outside in

**Catering:** In-house   
**Price:** $ • $$ • $$$ 

[ Click Here for More Info](https://www.herecomestheguide.com/wedding-venues/southern-california/indian-wells-country-club)

## [The La Quinta Country Club](https://www.herecomestheguide.com/wedding-venues/southern-california/la-quinta-country-club)

### La Quinta, California

[ [ Alicia Mink Photography ](https://aliciamink.com/) ](https://www.herecomestheguide.com/wedding-venues/southern-california/la-quinta-country-club)

**Fab Features:** lawn ceremonies under a spreading olive tree against a backdrop of the Santa Rosa mountains • the Eisenhower Pavilion offers a great indoor/outdoor flow for events • additional enhancements include late-night snacks, an ice cream & gelato station, speciality linens, kings tables, and more

**Catering:** In-house   
**Price:** $ • $$ 

[ Click Here for More Info](https://www.herecomestheguide.com/wedding-venues/southern-california/la-quinta-country-club)

## [The Paloma Resort](https://www.herecomestheguide.com/wedding-venues/southern-california/the-paloma-resort)

### Cathedral City, CA

[ ](https://www.herecomestheguide.com/wedding-venues/southern-california/the-paloma-resort)

**Fab Features** : vibrant, mid-century modern hotel that exudes classic Coachella charm • secluded ceremonies in the Wedding Meadow, an expansive green space framed by lush hedges and soaring palms, with sweeping views of the mountains beyond • spend the weekend enjoying the on-site restaurant Sol Y Sombra, indulging in spa treatments, or whiling away the day in a poolside cabana

**Catering:** In-house   
**Price:** $ • $$ 

[ Click Here for More Info](https://www.herecomestheguide.com/wedding-venues/southern-california/the-paloma-resort)

## [Rasta Rita Cantina + Venue](https://www.herecomestheguide.com/wedding-venues/southern-california/rasta-rita-cantina-venue)

### Twentynine Palms, CA

[ ](https://www.herecomestheguide.com/wedding-venues/southern-california/rasta-rita-cantina-venue)

**Fab Features** : high-desert gem in historic Twentynine Palms, "The Gateway to Joshua Tree" • exceptional views capture desert panoramas and breathtaking sunsets • creative, affordable packages include the locally famous Rasta Rita Margarita and taco trucks  


**Catering:** In-house, BYO   
**Price:** $ • $$

## [Mission Hills Country Club](https://www.herecomestheguide.com/wedding-venues/southern-california/mission-hills-country-club)

### Rancho Mirage, California

[ [ Brianna Broyles Photography ](https://www.briannabroyles.com/) ](https://www.herecomestheguide.com/wedding-venues/southern-california/mission-hills-country-club)

**Fab Features** : stunning ceremonies at the Lakefront, overlooking the verdant 18th green, or the secluded Grove, with its fragrant citrus trees and lush hedges • spacious clubhouse veranda with views of the jagged Santa Rosa Mountains • lovely lounge and banquet areas offer understated elegance with a modern take on mid-century style

**Catering:** In-house   
**Price:** $ • $$ • $$$
