## Why You'll Love This Place

##### A Here Comes The Guide Wedding Venue Review

Read More Show Less

  * **This gracious historic home** **is mere minutes from Nashville**. It offers**** Southern charm and hospitality and a personalized approach to planning and designing your all-inclusive wedding.
  * **Their garden oasis is completely hidden from the street.** Perfect for intimate ceremonies, cocktail receptions or after-dinner strolling, this photogenic sanctuary includes a pond, waterfall, stone walls and lots of leafy trees.
  * **The 1911 house is on the National Register of Historic Places.** With its iron gates and pillars, this architectural gem is an exquisite backdrop for your photos. For al fresco events and larger gatherings, enjoy the spacious pavilion overlooking the garden.
