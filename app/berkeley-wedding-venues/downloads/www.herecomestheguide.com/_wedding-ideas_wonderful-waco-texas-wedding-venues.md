# The Texas Heartland: 11 Wonderful Waco Wedding Venues

## 

**Halfway between Dallas and Austin, Waco puts you in the Texas heartland.** And while the city's claim to fame may be Chip & Joanna Gaines from the "Fixer Upper" TV show, this college town has much more to offer! From a historic railroad station, a family-owned ranch, and even Baylor University, check out this list of our favorite venues in and around Waco Texas.
