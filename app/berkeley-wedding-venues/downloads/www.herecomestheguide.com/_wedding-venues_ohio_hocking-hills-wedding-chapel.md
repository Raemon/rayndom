## Why You'll Love This Place

##### A Here Comes The Guide Wedding Venue Review

Read More Show Less

  * **Tucked at the center of a peaceful forest,** you’ll find this dreamy year-round getaway with their own Valley View Hills winery on site. Comprised of a collection of inviting cabins and gathering spaces, it boasts tranquil walking trails and resort-style amenities, and is less than an hour from Columbus.
  * **The quaint log chapel is just right for intimate ceremonies or elopements,** with its warm wood interior, covered portico, and large windows that bring in ample natural light. You might also opt to exchange vows outside by the romantic stacked stone fireplace, with a seating area shaded by an elegant pergola.
  * **There are thirteen cabins available for your overnight guests,** ranging in size from cozy honeymoon suites to multi-room lodges for groups and families. With great amenities like hot tubs, full kitchens, and fire pits, they offer an ideal opportunity to extend the good times.
