### PRICES: _What's this going to cost?_

###### Rental Fee

  * **$800–3,550/event**
  * Rental fee varies depending on: 
    * Day of week
    * Event duration
  * _Hourly rentals_ range $200/hour Monday–Thursday to $300/hour Friday–Sunday, with a 4-hour minimum for all days.  
_All day rentals_ run from 9am–11pm on weekends and range $3,150 (Sunday) to $3,550 (Saturday)_._

The rental fee includes:_  
_

    * Use of the Pavilion, including the restrooms, catering prep kitchen, and full access to the Plaza
    * Two dressing suites
    * Assorted bistro tables, high boys, banquet tables, and white folding chairs
    * 2 industrial farmhouse wooden bars and farmhouse wooden picnic tables
    * Security, chair setup, event insurance, and a day-of coordinator
    * Discounted hotel blocks in the area
    * Seasonal fountains (generally open March/April through September/October)
