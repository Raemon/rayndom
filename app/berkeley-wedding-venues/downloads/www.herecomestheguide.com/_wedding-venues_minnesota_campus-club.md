## Why You'll Love This Place

##### A Here Comes The Guide Wedding Venue Review

Read More Show Less

  * **Overlooking the Mississippi River** , this University of Minnesota dining club provides an elegant urban setting for gorgeous indoor and outdoor venues, such as the open-air terrace that can be used for a sunny ceremony or cocktail hour. 
  * **Enjoy beautiful views of the downtown skyline** through the floor-to-ceiling windows in the West Wing Dining Room. The floor plan can be customized to your needs, and you can opt to include a dance floor, audiovisuals, and even a piano.
  * **Locally sourced and thoughtfully prepared cuisine** is provided by the in-house catering team, which can easily accommodate a range of specialized menus. Packages also include wedding planners and event staff, plus a complimentary rehearsal, cake cutting, and security.
