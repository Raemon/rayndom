# 20 Florida Wedding Venues With Tons of History & Charm!

## 

**Bring a bit of the past into your present by hosting your wedding at one of Florida's fabulous historic venues.** From a turn-of-the-century hotel and ancient monastery to private estate mansions and a waterfront museum, you have a wide variety of choices with lots of indoor and outdoor options for your event.
