## Why You'll Love This Place

##### A Here Comes The Guide Wedding Venue Review

  * **This charming building has been at the center of life and business in downtown Plano** since the late 1800s. A designated historic landmark, it’s been lovingly restored and outfitted with all the amenities needed for stellar events. 
  * **The interior boasts towering walls of exposed brick,** hardwood flooring, and vintage-style lighting. Sweet touches like a greenery-laced chandelier and wall of flowers (available as add-on rentals), create an artful blend of industrial and natural design—and create a host of cool photo backdrops. 
  * **Tiered, affordable packages give you a range of choices** to fit any budget, and include the option to bring in your own caterer and alcohol, or to take advantage of their fabulous in-house chef and bar services.
