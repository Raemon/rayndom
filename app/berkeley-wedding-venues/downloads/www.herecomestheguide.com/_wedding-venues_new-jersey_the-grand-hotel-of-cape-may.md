### PRICES: _What's this going to cost?_

###### Ceremony Fee

  * **$600/event and up**
  * Beach and sundeck ceremony are $12/person, with a $1,000 minimum. Indoor ceremonies are $600.

  * **$145–205/person**
  * Package prices vary depending on:

    * Package chosen
    * Year of event

    * Stationed and passed hors d'oeuvres
