# 8 Must See Venues in the Wedding Capital of Texas ... Dripping Springs!

## 

**Known as the "Wedding Capital of Texas", Dripping Springs offers a wealth of wedding venues that showcase the beauty and hospitality of the region.** Just minutes from Austin, this Hill Country town is close to wineries and other local attractions your guests will enjoy. Plus, the city passed a "dark sky ordinance" to help preserve visibility of the glittering canopy of stars at night. Celebrate at any of these places and let the romance begin!
