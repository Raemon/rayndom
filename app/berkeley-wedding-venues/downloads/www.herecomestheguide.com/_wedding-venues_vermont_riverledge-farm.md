## Why You'll Love This Place

##### A Here Comes The Guide Wedding Venue Review

Read More Show Less

  * **Postcard-perfect in every way,** this charming retreat boasts an inviting white farmhouse and traditional red barn (complete with a weathervane-topped cupola on the roof), nestled among 300 acres of lush meadows and woodland.
  * **Exchange vows beside the pond,** on a mountainside with sweeping Green Mountain views, or in the farmhouse’s sweet backyard. Toast the day with cocktails in the garden, followed by a reception in the refurbished barn, where you’ll find beautiful woodwork and handmade chandeliers.
  * **Settle in to the large, cozy farmhouse for the weekend.** With six bedrooms, four baths, two kitchens, and multiple common spaces, it’s the perfect spot for gathering with your favorite people.
