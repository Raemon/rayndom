## Why You'll Love This Place

##### A Here Comes The Guide Wedding Venue Review

Read More Show Less

  * **Waterfront farm-to-table charm** **meets modern elegance** at this light-and-airy venue, housed within a vintage barn right on San Diego Bay. Expansive bay views and abundant natural light create the perfect backdrop for your celebration.
  * **The intimate Pond Room and spacious Marina Room** offer flexibility for ceremonies and receptions, with the Marina Room featuring a built-in bar, multiple lounge areas, and outdoor patio space overlooking the water. You'll love how easily your day transitions from a waterside ceremony to reception festivities in these naturally lit spaces.
  * **Fresh farm-to-table cuisine and craft beverages** are provided in-house, ensuring your guests enjoy locally sourced ingredients prepared with care. The Seaport Village location means guests can explore the charming waterfront promenade before or after your celebration.
