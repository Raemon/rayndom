## Why You'll Love This Place

##### A Here Comes The Guide Wedding Venue Review

  * **It’s a truly spectacular spot and a nature lover’s dream,** set on the banks of Millinocket Lake and boasting a breathtaking panorama that includes Mount Katahdin, Maine’s highest peak. 
  * **Wed in any season,** thanks to a host of indoor and outdoor spaces that include lakeside ceremonies on “The Look”, tented lawn receptions, and cozy gatherings in the River Driver’s Restaurant.
  * **Choose from a huge array of guest accommodations.** There are cabins that range from rustic to luxe, as well as multi-room lodges, and two campgrounds that offer tent sites for both small and large groups.
