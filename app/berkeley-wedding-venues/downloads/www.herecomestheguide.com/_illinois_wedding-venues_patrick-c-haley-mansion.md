## Why You'll Love This Place

##### A Here Comes The Guide Wedding Venue Review

Read More Show Less

  * **Envisioned as a “Castle on the Hill,” this impressive 1891 limestone mansion** provides a spectacular setting for any grand occasion. Inside, you’ll find authentic Victorian décor complete with stained-glass windows, intricately detailed friezes, and oak, mahogany and cherry woodwork. 
  * **An elegant white pergola is the dramatic focal point of the gardens,** surrounded by colorful seasonal blooms, mature evergreens, and a pair of delicate waterfalls that splash into pretty ponds. For an indoor ceremony with an equally lovely feel, opt for the Conservatory, where a light-flooded skylight and walls of 12-foot French windows frame wonderful views of the grounds.
  * **The Ballroom showcases the manor's exceptional grandeur,** outfitted with crystal chandeliers, billowing fabric lining, and a high-gloss tile floor perfect for dancing the night away. Here, your guests will enjoy the venue's exceptional cuisine, which include everything from butler-passed hors d'oeuvres and a multi-course dinner to a custom-designed cake and playful late-night snacks.
