# 11 of the Best Beach Cities Wedding Venues in L.A. County

## 

**Thanks to year-round sunshine, the charming seaside towns of Redondo Beach, Manhattan Beach, Rancho Palos Verdes, Downey, and Long Beach are packed with picture-perfect wedding destinations.** Whether you want to tie the knot with your toes in the sand, inside a one-of-a-kind glass chapel, or at a gorgeous cliffside resort, you'll find no shortage of swoon-worthy locations for your grand soiree in the South Bay of Los Angeles County.
