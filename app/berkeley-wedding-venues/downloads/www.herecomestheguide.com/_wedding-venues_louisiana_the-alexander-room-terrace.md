### PRICES: _What's this going to cost?_

###### Ceremony Fee

  * **$1,000/event**
  * The ceremony fee includes:

    * Half-hour ceremony
    * Use of dressing room 2 hours prior
    * Wireless microphone, outdoor sound system, and festoon lighting
    * Setup of Chiavari chairs



###### Package Prices

  * **$75–79/person**
  * Package prices vary depending on: 
    * Guest count
    * Package chosen
  * _All packages include:_

    * Hors d'oeuvres, stations, and buffet dinner
    * Full bar package
    * Cake credit with _Swiss Confectionary_ or _Haydel's Bakery_ , plus cutting
    * On-site day-of coordinator and security
    * Dressing room
    * Attendants for the couple and parents
    * Tables, gold Chiavari chairs, linens, glassware, candles, and cake stands
    * Abundant free parking



###### Additional Info

  * There is a guest minimum of 100 year-round. Peak season Saturdays (March–June and October–December) have a 150-guest minimum.
