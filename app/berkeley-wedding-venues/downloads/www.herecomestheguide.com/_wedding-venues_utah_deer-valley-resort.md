### PRICES: _What's this going to cost?_

###### Rental Fee

  * **$2,500–5,000/event**
  * Rental fee varies depending on: 
    * Day of week
    * Time of year
    * Space reserved



###### Package Prices

  * **$200–260/person**
  * Package prices vary depending on: 
    * Day of week
    * Time of year
    * Package chosen
  * The _Classic Package_ includes:  


    * Ceremony rehearsal
    * Exclusive use of the reserved lodge & grounds
    * Ceremony sound package from _Wasatch Audio Visual_
    * Bartender service
    * Tables, chairs, and house linens
    * Dual entrée buffet
    * Round buttercream wedding cake
    * And more!

The _Signature Package_ includes everything in the _Classic Package_ but with a plated 3-course dinner to replace the dual entrée buffet.
