### PRICES: _What's this going to cost?_

###### Rental Fee

  * **$5,000/event and up**
  * Rental fee varies depending on: 
    * Day of week
    * Time of year
    * Guest count
  * _The venue fee includes:_

    * Access to the outdoor ceremony field, front yard, outdoor kitchen, pavilion area, and barn
    * Overnight accommodations in two lodges and 15 glamping tents (sleeping 54 total)
    * Optional space for rehearsal dinner (50 guests)
    * Tables, chairs, two arbors, china, flatware, mason jar glasses, and miscellaneous décor 
    * Two indoor kitchens, indoor bar, keg system, and ice machine
    * Yard games and fire pit with firewood
    * On-site representative and staffing
