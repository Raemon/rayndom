## Why You'll Love This Place

##### A Here Comes The Guide Wedding Venue Review

Read More Show Less

  * **People flock from around the world to this epic Utah resort for good reason:** With its ideal blend of natural beauty and endless adventure, it’s the perfect year-round playground. Thanks to a collection of unique indoor and outdoor venues (all managed by the pros at Vail Resorts), you can create the alpine celebration of your dreams…set in your favorite season! 
  * **Guests are treated to stellar mountain views as they ascend to 8,000 feet** in the enclosed gondola that leads to Red Pine Lodge, the resort’s largest venue. Additional options include the intimate Lookout Cabin, with its open exhibition kitchen, and Legacy Lodge, which boasts classic ski chalet vibes. 
  * **A huge array of accommodation choices** range from traditional hotel-style rooms to luxe vacation residences, and wedding guests all enjoy generous booking discounts. Your coordinator can also build seasonal activities and gatherings into your personalized package.
