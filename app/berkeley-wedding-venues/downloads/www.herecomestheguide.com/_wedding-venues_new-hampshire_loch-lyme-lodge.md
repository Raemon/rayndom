## Why You'll Love This Place

##### A Here Comes The Guide Wedding Venue Review

Read More Show Less

  * **Perched at the edge of a crystal-clear lake,** this country resort sits just outside the town of Lyme. Beloved for its traditional New England charm, the village and its surrounding area is also home to plentiful inns and vacation rentals. 
  * **Exchange vows at the water’s edge,** followed by lawn games and mingling as the sun sets behind the trees. Dine and dance beneath a spacious tent beside the barn.
  * **Twenty cute, rustic cabins** **mean many in your party can stay right on-site,** enjoying a weekend of swimming, boating, kayaking, fishing, and more—then wind things down with a lakeside campfire.
