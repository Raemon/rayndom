# Real Wedding Ideas: Rustic

**When we say "rustic wedding" you may automatically think burlap, cowboy boots, and mason jars. But rustic weddings have come a long way since they exploded onto the wedding style scene!** Nowadays, rustic refers to anything that's a bit rough around the edges, a bit more organic, and a bit more boho. Find a wealth of rustic wedding inspiration and tons of photos on our [Rustic Wedding Ideas board on Pinterest](https://www.pinterest.com/hctg/rustic-wedding-ideas/ "Pinterest Board for Rustic Wedding Ideas").
