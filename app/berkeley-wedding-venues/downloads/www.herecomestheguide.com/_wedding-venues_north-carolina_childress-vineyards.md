## Why You'll Love This Place

##### A Here Comes The Guide Wedding Venue Review

Read More Show Less

  * **This sweeping vineyard and its stunning, Old World-inspired central building** are the passion project of NASCAR team owner Richard Childress, who fell in love with the art of winemaking in his early racing days in California. 
  * **Wed beneath the sweet gazebo,** in the courtyard, or on the lush lawn, then follow with a reception in the Grand Pavilion. For your cocktail hour or an additional mingling spot, consider adding the Victory Lounge or pretty Winery Foyer. 
  * **They offer several thoughtful packages** to meed the needs of weddings large and small, with awesome perks like access to the elegant dressing suites, wine club memberships, and even a private-labeled wine just for you!
