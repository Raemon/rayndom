## Why You'll Love This Place

##### A Here Comes The Guide Wedding Venue Review

Read More Show Less

  * **This fabulous, first-class event venue is conveniently located** by all major highways and just 11 miles from DFW airport**.** Built with top-notch celebrations in mind, it's the perfect setting for hosting everything from social gatherings to grand weddings with distinction and excellence.
  * **The beautiful Ballroom boasts dazzling crystal chandeliers** hung from vaulted 31-foot ceilings and the Holly Chapel is adorned with floor-to-ceiling windows that fill the space with natural light. An adjacent patio allows guests to flow outdoors and admire the sparkling pond. Flexible enough to host parties of 100–500, it's got room for everyone while maintaining a sense of comfort and intimacy.
  * **The event proceeds go directly to the Greater Keller Women’s Club Foundation,** which uses these proceeds to fund their various community support efforts, which include college scholarships to local teens and donations to organizations that help families in need.
