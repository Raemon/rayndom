# 9 Stellar Austin Wedding Venues that offer On-site Lodging

## 

**Austin, the vibrant capital of Texas, is the perfect destination for your dream wedding.** This dynamic city offers more than just stunning scenery and a variety of eclectic wedding venues; it provides a wealth of experiences for your guests. Explore the city's vibrant food scene with award-winning brunch spots, rooftop bars, and live music venues. Enjoy outdoor adventures like kayaking on Lady Bird Lake, hiking in the Barton Creek Greenbelt, or visiting local wineries. From romantic retreats to chic hotels in the heart of downtown, we've curated a selection of the best venues in the area that offer on-site accommodations, and luxe amenities that will enchant your guests from the welcome cocktail reception to the post-bash brunch.
