# 8 Unique Connecticut Wedding Venues

## 

**Connecticut offers a range of venues that can make your celebration anything but ordinary.** From a riverfront museum and dramatic performing arts center to a historic mansion & gardens or former lace factory, here's a selection of unique Connecticut wedding venues well worth checking out.

## [The Garden House at Keeler Tavern Museum & History Center](https://www.herecomestheguide.com/wedding-venues/connecticut/the-garden-house-at-keeler-tavern-museum-history-center)

### Ridgefield, CT

[ ](https://www.herecomestheguide.com/wedding-venues/connecticut/the-garden-house-at-keeler-tavern-museum-history-center)

**Venue Superpowers:**

Garden Romance

European Vibes

Fees Support the Museum

**Fab Features** : beautifully restored collection of historic buildings in the heart of Ridgefield • variety of lovely event spaces from the bricked wall garden with a reflecting pool surrounded by lush landscape to the Garden House with French doors leading to a quaint terrace • intimate receptions beneath a white tent or inside the charming Garden House with ornate crown molding and antique chandeliers

**Catering:** Preferred list   
**Price:** $ • $$

**Venue Superpowers:**

Contemporary Design

Interactive Galleries

Animal Photo Ops

****Fab Features** : **stunning interactive indoor & outdoor spaces will awe your guests (and your fee goes towards funding further research and preservation) • say "I do" alongside the extraordinary belugas, then join your guests to explore the gorgeous galleries, filled with colorful fish and fascinating exhibits • enjoy delectable, curated menus from on-site caterer _Lancer Hospitality_

## [The Carousel Museum](https://www.herecomestheguide.com/wedding-venues/connecticut/the-carousel-museum)

### Bristol, CT

[ [ Caroline Talbot Studios ](https://www.carolinetalbot.com/) ](https://www.herecomestheguide.com/wedding-venues/connecticut/the-carousel-museum)

**Venue Superpowers:**

Fascinating Museum

19th-Century Factory

Affordable

****Fab Features** :** marvel at over 100 years of carousel art and history at this museum housed in a quintessential 19th-century New England factory • two remarkable event spaces boast a treasure trove of antique horses, gleaming wood floors, a Venetian crystal chandelier, and even a working carousel! • fees help support the Museum's mission to preserve and restore these unique works of art 

**Catering:** BYO   
**Price:** $

Vintage Vibes

Train Photo Ops
