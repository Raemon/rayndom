## Why You'll Love This Place

##### A Here Comes The Guide Wedding Venue Review

Read More Show Less

  * **It's a secluded country ranch at the heart of a nature preserve.** The 200-acre property boasts peaceful ponds, bountiful native wildflowers, and views of cattle grazing across the prairies. 
  * **The private chapel can host your ceremony** (it comes complete with a traditional church organ), or you can opt to exchange vows beneath a peaked, wrought-iron gazebo a the edge of a pond.
  * **Celebrate in the generously sized reception hall** , which features a glossy Italian marble floor and large windows overlooking the scenic grounds.
