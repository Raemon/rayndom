# Charming Romance: 7 Beautiful Berkshires Wedding Venues

## 

**With its breathtaking mountain vistas, idyllic blue lakes, alluring lush woodlands, and historic charm, there aren't many more romantic places to say ‘I do’ than the Berkshires.** Whether you’re picturing an intimate garden ceremony or a grand celebration amongst sweeping mountain backdrops, there’s beauty around every corner. Explore our curated list below and begin planning your dreamy Berkshires celebration.
