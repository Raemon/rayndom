# Serene Escapes: 6 Gorgeous Getaways within a 4-hour drive of Atlanta

## 

**Planning an Atlanta wedding, but want to escape the hustle and bustle of the city?** Thanks to its close proximity to National Parks and pristine beaches, you won't have to travel very far to find a relaxing retreat for your big day. From an Old World Spanish-style mansion to a turn-of-the-century cotton mill on the banks of a sweeping river, we've gathered a handful of serene venues all within a 4-hour drive of Atlanta.

* * *

## 

* * *

## EAST OF ATLANTA: GEORGIA COAST & SAVANNAH

* * *

In just under a 4-hour drive (or a 1-hour flight!) you can reach Georgia's picturesque islands and the Atlantic coast dotted with luxury oceanside resorts, charming seaside towns, and sandy dunes. 

* * *

## [Sea Island](https://www.herecomestheguide.com/wedding-venues/georgia/sea-island)

### Sea Island, Georgia

[ ](https://www.herecomestheguide.com/wedding-venues/georgia/sea-island)

**Fab Features:** Forbes Five Star-rated luxury resort and spa situated in the Golden Isles • say "I do" beneath a canopy of moss-covered oaks or inside the enchanting Cloister Chapel • the grounds boast over 5 miles of pristine, private coastline surrounded by ancient oaks, picturesque marshland, and three championship 18-hole golf courses

**Catering:** In-house
