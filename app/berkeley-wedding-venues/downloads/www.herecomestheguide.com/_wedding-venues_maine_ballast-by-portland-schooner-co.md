  * **Intimate and utterly romantic,** this turn-of-the-century brick gem is set at the heart of Portland’s Old Port neighborhood, and is overflowing with charming vintage details.
  * **Hardwood flooring, a gracefully arched doorway of exposed brick,** and delicately scrolled chandeliers all lend an air of grace and history to the space, and oversized industrial-style windows fill its rooms with ample natural light.
  * **For a uniquely unforgettable “land and sea” celebration,** consider adding a privately chartered cruise around the harbor on one of Portland Schooner Co.’s historic, handcrafted sailing vessels.
