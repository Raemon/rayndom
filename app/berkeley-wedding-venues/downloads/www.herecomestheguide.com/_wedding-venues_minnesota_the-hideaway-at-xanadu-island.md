### PRICES: _What's this going to cost?_

###### Package Prices

  * **$1,500–10,250/event**
  * Package prices vary depending on: 
    * Time of year
    * Package chosen
    * Type of event
  * The _Say I Do at Xanadu: Just The Wedding_ package is $1,500 and includes access to the entire island & barn 8am–midnight. Available mid-October–mid-May.

* * *

The _Stay at Xanadu: A Weekend Event_ package is $7,150 and includes the entire island, barn, cabins, and historic Lake House Friday noon–Sunday noon. Available mid-May–mid-October.

* * *

The _Destination Xanadu: A Week To Remember Forever_ package is $10,250 and includes the entire island, barn, cabins, and historic lake for a week (Friday noon–Friday noon)_._ Available mid-May–mid-October.

* * *

Tables & chairs are included with every package. Wedding coordination can be added to any package for a fee.




###### Additional Info

  * _All packages include access to activities beyond the celebration such as:_ fishing boats, canoes, kayaks, stand-up paddle boards, a firepit with stocked firewood, books/puzzles/crafts in each cabin, a playground, fishing & floating docks, a vintage paddle boat, and more.
