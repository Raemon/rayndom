### PRICES: _What's this going to cost?_

###### Package Prices

  * **$7,000–8,000/event**
  * _The signature Club at Snoqualmie Ridge Wedding Package includes:_

    * 6-hour event, plus a 1-hour ceremony rehearsal
    * All-day access to bride's dressing room, groom's room, and the venue for setup
    * Customizable food & beverage service and cake cutting, with a complimentary tasting for 4 (for weddings over 100)
    * Event planning guide and custom floor plan
    * Ceremony orchestration, dedicated banquet captain, servers and staff, and setup & cleanup
    * House tables, chairs, linens, votive candles, dance floor, AV, and standard rentals
    * Golf cart escort to romantic photography locations on the property
