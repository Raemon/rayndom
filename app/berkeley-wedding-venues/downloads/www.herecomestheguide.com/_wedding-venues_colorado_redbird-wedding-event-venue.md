##### A Here Comes The Guide Wedding Venue Review

  * **Walk a pretty forest path and cross a footbridge** to your ceremony site, where you’ll wed with your choice of stunning natural backdrops. Mingle with guests on the lawn before moving to the lovely, modern farmhouse-style reception hall, which boasts a shady covered patio.
