**Central Texas is a region of contrasts, and the wedding venues here reflect that.** We've put together a selection that showcases a variety of settings, from charmingly rustic to country chic.

###  [ 8 College Station/Bryan Wedding Venues with Country Charm & Southern Hospitality ](https://www.herecomestheguide.com/wedding-ideas/college-station-wedding-venues)

**Affectionately known as Aggieland, College Station is home to Texas A &M****, one of the state's most beloved institutions** and the premier education, research, cultural, and sports center of the area. The surrounding Brazos Valley boasts river views, hiking, pre-Civil War sites, sprawling ranches and, of course, great wedding venues that blend country charm with southern hospitality.

###  [ 9 of the Best North Texas Wedding Venues ](https://www.herecomestheguide.com/wedding-ideas/north-texas-wedding-venues)

**North Texas, conveniently located near DFW airport, offer a wonderfully diverse choice of neighborhoods, landscapes, and wedding venues.** Is your ideal setting a country spread with a rustic barn? Or perhaps it's a country club on the shores of a lake? What about a historic mansion, hillside vineyard, or industrial-chic place in the downtown square? Whatever you're looking for, there's plenty to explore here.

If you're looking for your own little oasis for a day, these garden wedding venues in and around Forth Worth do not disappoint.

###  [ 8 Must See Venues in the Wedding Capital of Texas ... Dripping Springs! ](https://www.herecomestheguide.com/wedding-ideas/dripping-springs-wedding-venues)

**Known as the "Wedding Capital of Texas", Dripping Springs offers a wealth of wedding venues that showcase the beauty and hospitality of the region.** Just minutes from Austin, this Hill Country town is close to wineries and other local attractions your guests will enjoy. Plus, the city passed a “dark sky ordinance” to help preserve visibility of the glittering canopy of stars at night.

**A wedding in Austin can be so much fun—especially if you choose a venue that’s a great home base for exploring this world-famous city's attractions.** Lots of places are within an easy drive to plenty of daytime adventures, like hikes in the Hill Country, paddle boarding on Lady Bird Lake, or checking out Zilker Park. And, since they're nestled in Austin's bustling downtown, they’re within steps of restaurants, galleries, shopping, and legendary music-infused nightlife (hello, 6th Street!).

Explore the city's vibrant food scene with award-winning brunch spots, rooftop bars, and live music venues. Enjoy outdoor adventures like kayaking on Lady Bird Lake, hiking in the Barton Creek Greenbelt, or visiting local wineries. From romantic retreats to chic hotels in the heart of downtown, we've curated a selection of the best venues in the area that offer on-site accommodations, and luxe amenities that will enchant your guests from the welcome cocktail reception to the post-bash brunch.
