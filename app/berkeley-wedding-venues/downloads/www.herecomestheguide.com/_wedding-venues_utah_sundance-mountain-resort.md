### PRICES: _What's this going to cost?_

###### Ceremony Fee

  * The Mandan Summit is available for summer ceremonies, with a site fee of $4,000 (which includes chairs and a sound system) and a fee of $1,640 for use of the Outlaw Express Lift.




###### Package Prices

  * **$205/person and up**
  * **Special pricing is available for bookings within 6 months of the event** (this is an ideal option for Utah locals who don't need guest lodging). _Inquire with the venue for details._

* * *

**The Destination Wedding Package** _ranges $205–225++/person and includes:_

    * Cocktail hour reception with passed hors d'oeuvres
    * Buffet or plated dinner, including salad, entrées, rolls, and desserts
    * 5-hour bartender fees
    * Cake cutting and service
    * Full range sound system, tables & chairs, linens, and standard rentals, plus setup & teardown
    * 1 complimentary room night for the wedding couple, plus a welcome amenity
    * 50-minute Sundance Signature Massage for the couple
    * Discounted group lodging rates and complimentary self-parking

 _A minimum of 100 guests is required._ For guest counts under that number, a room rental fee of $2,000–8,000 will apply.

_A minimum lodging reservation is also required_ for destination weddings:__ the required number is **35 rooms for 2 nights**. Pricing varies depending on the season and guest count.
