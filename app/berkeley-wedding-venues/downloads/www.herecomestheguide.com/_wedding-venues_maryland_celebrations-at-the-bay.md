## Why You'll Love This Place

##### A Here Comes The Guide Wedding Venue Review

Read More Show Less

  * **Conveniently located between Baltimore and Annapolis** , this waterfront venue overlooks Chesapeake Bay, providing a gorgeous backdrop for your wedding.
  * **The Chesapeake Ballroom boasts a 32-foot-high ceiling** and balcony, as well as a waterside deck that brings you and your guests close to the sea. You can also celebrate in the climate-controlled Tented Vista Ballroom, with its parquet floor, elegant chandeliers, and silk-lined ceiling. More intimate celebrations can be held in the Knollview House, with a charming wraparound porch overlooking lush lawns and the beautiful bay.
  * **All of the details will be handled by _Catering by Uptown_** , a company with 30 years of experience producing special events. In addition to full coordination services, they offer a wide array of food and beverage choices to accommodate your vision and your budget.
