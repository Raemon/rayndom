## Why You'll Love This Place

##### A Here Comes The Guide Wedding Venue Review

Read More Show Less

  * **Located along the Tuscaloosa Riverwalk with panoramic waterfront views** from the outdoor patio, this farmers market destination transforms into a modern and easily customizable event space that will wow your guests.
  * **Lined with glass roll-up doors that allow in tons of natural light** , the main hall is airy and spacious. The large white brick stage provides an excellent backdrop for your band or DJ, and the exposed ductwork ceiling lends a contemporary vibe. 
  * **Convenient inclusions and add-ons take some of the stress out of planning.** Tables and black Chiavari chairs are provided with your rental, and you can also choose from a delightful variety of linens.
