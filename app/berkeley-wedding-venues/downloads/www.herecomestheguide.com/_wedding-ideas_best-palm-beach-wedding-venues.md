# 9 of the Best Palm Beach Wedding Venues

**From luxurious oceanfront resorts to swanky Spanish-style private estates, Palm Beach is bursting with jaw-dropping wedding venues.** So whether you want to go full-on glam at a posh hotel or keep it casual with a ceremony in the sand, check out our picks for some of the best locations to tie the knot in this stunning South Florida hotspot.
