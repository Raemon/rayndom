## Why You'll Love This Place

##### A Here Comes The Guide Wedding Venue Review

Read More Show Less

  * **These 140 pristine acres** are operated by the Southern Appalachian Highlands Conservancy (SAHC). Just a short drive from Asheville, the farm boasts sweeping views of Mt. Pisgah and the Newfoundland Mountains. 
  * **Relax in the refurbished farmhouse** before your hilltop ceremony overlooking the valley and mountains. Follow with a tented reception in an open meadow as the fireflies twinkle above the grass. 
  * **Warm fuzzies guaranteed:** Your rental fees go toward helping SAHC fulfill its “forever commitment” to the stewardship of the irreplaceable lands of western North Carolina and eastern Tennessee.
