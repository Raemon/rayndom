### PRICES: _What's this going to cost?_

###### Package Prices

  * **$3,500/event and up**
  * The Orpheum & the Oaks package is for up to 100 guests and includes:

    * 15-hour rental
    * Access to the entire venue, including the main downstairs ballroom and upstairs mezzanine
    * Two private getting-ready areas
    * Caterer's prep kitchen

Bar, floral design, stationary/signage, and day-of or month-of coordination packages may be added on for an additional charge.
