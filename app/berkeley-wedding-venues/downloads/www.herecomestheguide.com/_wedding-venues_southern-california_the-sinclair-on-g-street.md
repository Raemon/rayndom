### PRICES: _What's this going to cost?_

###### Rental Fee

  * **$1,500–4,000/event**
  * Rental fee varies depending on: 
    * Day of week
    * Time of year
    * Event duration
    * Type of event
  * _The rental fee includes:_

    * 12-hour exclusive access to the venue (2 levels)
    * 1-hour ceremony rehearsal
    * On-site venue manager for the entirety of the event
    * Furnished dressing lounges for both wedding parties
    * Four 8ft custom bars
    * Cleaning fee, WiFi, and all taxes

Weekend rentals range $3,000–4,000; weekday rentals range $1,500–2,500. Hourly rentals for shorter events may be available.




###### Ceremony Fee

  * Included in venue rental.

  * **$1,820–6,575/event**
  * Packages can include furniture from _Cedar & Pine_ and month-of-coordination from partnered coordination team. See more information about package options below.
