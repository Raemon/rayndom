## Why You'll Love This Place

##### A Here Comes The Guide Wedding Venue Review

Read More Show Less

  * **This 1910 Beaux Arts gem has been reimagined as a one-of-a-kind events space,** retaining its historic glamour while adding plenty of modern flourishes throughout its nearly 16,000 square feet of unobstructed space.
  * **At the heart of the venue is a remarkable, soaring glass atrium** with walls and floors clad in rich marble, classical arched alcoves, a stylish raised stage to host your DJ or band, and high-tech, customizable architectural lighting to bring your vision to life.
  * **Its prime downtown location makes guest access a breeze,** while its light-flooded spaces offer a uniquely airy vibe and unmatched versatility. Surrounded in every direction by world class lodging, restaurants, and cultural attractions, this is a truly vibrant setting for your City celebration.
