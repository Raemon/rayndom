## Why You'll Love This Place

##### A Here Comes The Guide Wedding Venue Review

Read More Show Less

  * **This pretty campus boasts lush grounds,** unique architecture, and a huge range of celebration choices. It’s just over an hour from downtown Chicago and features an on-site hotel, making it extra-appealing for traveling guests. 
  * **Ranging from sleek and modern to timelessly classic,** your venue options include gorgeous historic _Altgeld Hall_ , with its dramatic auditorium and grand staircase, the cool, contemporary _Barsema Alumni_ _and_ _Visitors Center_ , and a huge variety of indoor and outdoor spaces. 
  * **They offer several comprehensive packages,** all at refreshingly affordable prices. Every package includes the assistance of their experienced event coordination team, as well as a complimentary wedding night stay at Holmes Hotel.
