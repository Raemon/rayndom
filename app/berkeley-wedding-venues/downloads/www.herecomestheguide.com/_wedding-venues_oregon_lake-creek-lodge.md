## Why You'll Love This Place

##### A Here Comes The Guide Wedding Venue Review

Read More Show Less

  * **Get back to nature at this unique destination wedding venue.** Imagine gathering friends and family on 42 tree-packed acres that are exclusively reserved for you. Guests can further enjoy the feeling of a retreat in the woods by staying in one of the lodge’s beautiful cabins, some of which are pet-friendly.
  * **It’s the perfect location for a wedding with a rustic flair.** Your ceremony, reception, and even rehearsal dinner can be held outside, either under the towering pine trees or in a heated tent. Indoors, the charming dining room seats up to 40 and can be expanded to an adjoining deck that may also be tented and heated.
  * **The cuisine is as natural as the setting.** They offer a wide selection of menu options, prepared with only the finest locally sourced ingredients. Need something special? The chef will try to accommodate your wishes!

###### Rental Fee

  * **$7,000–18,000/event**
  * Rental fee varies depending on: 
    * Time of year
  * _The rental fee includes:_

    * Semi-exclusive use of the _Lake Creek Lodge_ campus Friday noon–Sunday noon
    * Space for the ceremony, reception, rehearsal dinner, and/or welcome festivities
    * Tables
    * Bar plus bartenders & barware for up to four hours of service on Friday & up to five hours of service on Saturday
    * 2-night stay in a 2-bedroom historic cabin during the off-season to help the couple plan their upcoming wedding
    * On-site liaison
    * Venue amenities such as fly fishing pond, firepits (when allowed), seasonal swimming pool, sport courts, children's play area, seasonal snowshoes, and more!
