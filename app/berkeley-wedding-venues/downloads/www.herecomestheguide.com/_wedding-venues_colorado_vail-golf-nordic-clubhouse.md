## Why You'll Love This Place

##### A Here Comes The Guide Wedding Venue Review

Read More Show Less

  * **Nestled in a stunning valley amid the Gore Range Mountains,** this wonderful club boasts mature trees, a pristine lake, and sweeping views of the area's unparalleled scenery. 
  * **Walk across an arched wooden footbridge** to your ceremony spot on the Wedding Island, perched at the lake's center and offering an unforgettable spot to say "I do."
  * **Follow your vows with a reception in the Gore Range Room,** with its soaring vaulted ceiling and wall of windows looking out across the golf course, woods, and mountains.



### PRICES: _What's this going to cost?_

###### Rental Fee

  * **$2,900–9,900/event**
  * Rental fee varies depending on: 
    * Day of week
    * Time of year
    * Package chosen
  * Rental of the _Gore Range Room_ ranges $2,900–8,400, and includes:

    * 10-hour venue access
    * Use of the banquet room, reception room, patio, and ceremony lawn
    * Access to the green room/dressing suite
    * Tables, chairs, white table linens, and napkins
    * 8 on-site parking spots

Fees for the _Wedding Package_ range $5,900–9,900, and include everything in the Gore Range Room rental, PLUS:

    * 12-hour venue access
    * Use of the Wedding Island _(not available in winter)_
    * Golf cart transportation to/from the Wedding Island for the wedding party
