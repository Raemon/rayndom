# 6 Of The Best Barn Wedding Venues In The Chicago Area

## 

**Want to have a barn wedding close to Chicago? It's totally doable! You'll find quite a selection of barns, all within a couple of hours' drive of The Windy City.** Naturally, most are located on a farm so you'll also benefit from gorgeous natural surroundings—many that can be dressed up or down, depending on the style you're going for. Here are a handful of Chicago-area barn wedding venues worth checking out.

* * *

## [Engelbrecht Farmstead](https://www.herecomestheguide.com/wedding-venues/illinois/engelbrecht-farmstead)

### Paxton, IL

[ [ Now and Then Photography ](https://photographynowandthen.com/) ](https://www.herecomestheguide.com/wedding-venues/illinois/engelbrecht-farmstead)

**Fab Features** : century-old farm surrounded by wide-open panoramas, fresh country breezes, and plenty of picturesque photo ops • charming white barn with two floors, sky-high ceilings, and plenty of rustic wood details • all rentals include 3 days of access, including overnight lodgings in the authentic farmhouse

**Catering:** Preferred list, BYO   
**Price:** $ • $$
