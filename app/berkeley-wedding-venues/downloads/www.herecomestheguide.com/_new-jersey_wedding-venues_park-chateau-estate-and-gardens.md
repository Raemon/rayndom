## Why You'll Love This Place

##### A Here Comes The Guide Wedding Venue Review

Read More Show Less

  * **The manor and grounds are reminiscent of a classic French countryside estate.** Lush gardens, inspired by those at Versailles, completely surround the chateau and create a sense of secluded peace…all within easy reach from anywhere in the tri-state area. 
  * **Ceremonies can be held in a private chapel nestled among the gardens** , or overlooking a graceful lily pond encircled by flowers. Follow your vows with a cocktail hour on the flagstone terrace.
  * **Your reception in the 17th century-inspired ballroom will be exquisite.** Opulent chandeliers hang from an elaborately embellished recessed ceiling, and rows of arched windows line the walls, letting in natural light and views of the verdant property.
