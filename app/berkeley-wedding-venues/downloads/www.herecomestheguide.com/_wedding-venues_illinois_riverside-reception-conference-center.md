## Why You'll Love This Place

##### A Here Comes The Guide Wedding Venue Review

Read More Show Less

  * **Set at the edge of the Fox River** , the Riverside Receptions & Conference Center is housed in a historic building constructed of natural stone and brick. 
  * **The interior is warmed by several fireplaces** , while lace curtains and antique memorabilia add charm. Outdoors, a brick courtyard makes a lovely ceremony site. 
  * **Complimentary parking is provided** , and the center is just steps away from the Herrington Inn (great for your overnight guests) and the renowned Geneva shopping district.
