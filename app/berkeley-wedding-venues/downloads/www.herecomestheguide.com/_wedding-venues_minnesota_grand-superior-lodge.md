### PRICES: _What's this going to cost?_

###### Rental Fee

  * **$2,500–4,000/event**
  * Rental fee varies depending on: 
    * Day of week
    * Time of year
  * _Rental fees include:_

    * Use of the Star Harbor Ballroom & Lounge for the reception
    * Use of the brick patio for ceremony & cocktail hour
    * Hand-carved arch, ceremony chairs, cornhole games, bistro lighting, fire pit, and setup & teardown
    * Tables & chairs, linens, table skirting, china, votives, and dance floor
    * Full bar setup, bartenders, and serving staff
    * Complimentary menu tasting

_May–October_ | $3,000 Friday – $4,000 Saturday  
 _November–April_ | $2,500 Friday – $3,000 Saturday




###### Food & Beverage Minimum

  * **$3,000–6,000/event**
  * Food & Beverage minimum varies depending on: 
    * Day of week
    * Time of year
  * _May–October_ | $4,000 Friday – $6,000 Saturday  
 _November–April_ | $3,000 Friday – $4,000 Saturday




###### Meals (when priced separately)

  * **$22–45/person**
  * Buffet, stations-style, plated meals, and carving stations are available. Hosted and cash bars are additional.




###### Service Charge or Gratuity

  * **20%**



###### Additional Info

  * Saturday weddings require the filling of 12 guest rooms in the main lodge. Two-night minimums apply to lodging for both Friday & Saturday weddings.
