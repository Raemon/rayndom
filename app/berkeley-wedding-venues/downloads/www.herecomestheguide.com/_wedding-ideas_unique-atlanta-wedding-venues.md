# Distinctively Different: 13 Unique Wedding Venues in Atlanta

## 

**In a city as richly diverse as Atlanta, you’ll find a wealth of out-of-the-ordinary settings.** Gravitating towards lush gardens or something more rustic? How about a hip downtown locale with a dazzling skyline, or an opportunity to incorporate Atlanta’s arts and culture? If you’re looking for a unique venue for your celebration, Atlanta’s got you covered.
