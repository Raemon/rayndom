# 5 Affordable Asheville Wedding Venues

## 

**Searching for a stylish wedding venue in Asheville that won’t break the bank?** We’ve got you covered! From a rustic-chic farmhouse just outside the city limits, to a loft-style event space with a cool speakeasy vibe, check out these lovely locations that offer champagne style on a beer budget.

## [Delaney Ridge](https://www.herecomestheguide.com/wedding-venues/north-carolina/delaney-ridge)

**Fab Features:** secluded retreat tucked on over 90 acres of woodlands, meadows, and gardens • upscale, industrial-inspired reception space brings the outside in, with a wraparound patio and glass garage-style doors that open onto the lush grounds • several packages are available to meet every taste and budget

**Catering:** Preferred list, BYO

## [Nantahala Weddings & Events](https://www.herecomestheguide.com/wedding-venues/north-carolina/nantahala-weddings-and-events)

### Topton, NC

[ ](https://www.herecomestheguide.com/wedding-venues/north-carolina/nantahala-weddings-and-events)

**Fab Features** : exquisite destination venue set beside the sparkling Nantahala Lake, with nature as your altar at the Chapel in the Sky • cozy tiny homes offer overnight lodging for up to 14 people for weekend-long soirees (plus, no end times!) • host a yoga session with your guests, or enjoy hiking, rafting, and river adventures with a guide, making your weekend a true experience!

**Catering:** Preferred list, BYO   
**Price:** $ • $$

**Fab Features** : historic farm only 12 minutes from Asheville but secluded among acres of pastoral meadows, oaks, and rolling hills • ceremonies set against the mountains with receptions in the classic red barn or under the stars on the expansive deck • cozy lodgings in the modern farmhouse
