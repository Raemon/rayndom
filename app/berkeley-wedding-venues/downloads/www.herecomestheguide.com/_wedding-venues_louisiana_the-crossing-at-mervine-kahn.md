### PRICES: _What's this going to cost?_

###### Rental Fee

  * **$1,500/event and up**
  * Rental fee varies depending on: 
    * Space reserved
    * Guest count
  * **The Mervine Kahn** rental fee is $2,800 for up to 299 guests and $3,000 for 300+ guests, plus $500 per 100 guests thereafter.

**The Bank Facility** is $1,500 for a reception only.

* * *

_Additional spaces available to rent:_

The Bridal Suite/Salon is $500 and includes a sandwich & fruit tray, champagne for toasting, a stocked mini-fridge, and a coffee station

The Kids Room is $400, and includes movies, coloring, and an on-site staff member

The Guest House is $500/night and sleeps 10 people.




###### Ceremony Fee

  * **$200–1,250/event**
  * Ceremony fee varies depending on: 
    * Space reserved
    * Guest count
  * The Ceremony Room is $250 for up to 160 guests. 

A ceremony held in the Bank Facility is $200 when added to a reception in the same space; when used in conjunction with a reception in Mervine Kahn, the fee is $1,250 for 170+ guests.




###### Meals (when priced separately)

  * **$25/person and up**
  * Food is priced according to menu choices on a per-person basis, depending on food selection. Plastic dinnerware is included, but may be upgraded for a fee.

Beverage packages are additional, and range $15–24.50/person.
