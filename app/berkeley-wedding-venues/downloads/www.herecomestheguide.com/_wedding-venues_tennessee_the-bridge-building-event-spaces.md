## Why You'll Love This Place

##### A Here Comes The Guide Wedding Venue Review

  * **Originally built in 1908, this gorgeously renovated venue** **is now ranked the "greenest" building in the world.** Nestled on the downtown riverfront, the Bridge Building preserves the original architecture of a Nashville icon while integrating a contemporary sensibility with sustainable eco-design.
  * **The rooftop patio offers stunning views of the cityscape** , making a memorable backdrop for a ceremony or reception under the stars. The sleekly modern first floor features exposed industrial beams, and it opens onto the riverfront walk for additional mingling space.
  * **Infinity Hospitality offers in-house event planning services** , connecting couples with an experienced event specialist who can help customize lighting, décor, audiovisuals, staging, draping, and more to bring your vision to life.
