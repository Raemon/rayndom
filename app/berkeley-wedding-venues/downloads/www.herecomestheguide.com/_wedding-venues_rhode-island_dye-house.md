## Why You'll Love This Place

##### A Here Comes The Guide Wedding Venue Review

Read More Show Less

  * **Once part of a large weaving and fabric-dyeing operation,** this turn-of-the-century building has been artfully converted into several guest suites and event spaces, and is tucked in the heart of Providence’s historic Olneyville section. 
  * **In the Studio, you’ll find a 20-foot gabled ceiling,** original red brick flooring, whitewashed walls, and tons of natural light pouring in through dozens of oversized windows. 
  * **Hosting an ultra-intimate affair? Consider The Loft,** which boasts—in addition to luxe overnight accommodations—soaring ceilings, a lounge area, and a fully-equipped outdoor kitchen.
