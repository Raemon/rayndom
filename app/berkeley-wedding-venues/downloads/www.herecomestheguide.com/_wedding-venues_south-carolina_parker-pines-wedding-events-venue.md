### PRICES: _What's this going to cost?_

###### Rental Fee

  * **$3,000–8,500/event**
  * Rental fee varies depending on: 
    * Day of week
    * Time of year
    * Event duration
  * _The rental fee includes:_

    * Space for the ceremony & reception
    * Dressing suites
    * Tables & chairs with setup & breakdown
    * Access to the property for engagement and/or wedding photography
    * Golf cart service for the wedding party for outdoor ceremonies and couples portraits 

SIngle-day events range $3,000–4,800.

Three-day events cost $8,500 and include access Friday 8am–Sunday 1pm, plus five on-site cabins, use of the _Old Barn_ for a rehearsal dinner, 15 farm tables, and white linens.

Additional days for setup or breakdown may be added for $1,200/day.
