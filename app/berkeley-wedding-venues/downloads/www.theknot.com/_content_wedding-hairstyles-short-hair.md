## What Are the Best Wedding Hairstyles for Short Hair?

Whether you're already a short hair bride or debating a big chop, you may be wondering how to style short hair for a wedding. The good news is that the options are virtually endless. Many people prefer short hairstyles for weddings because they take less time to style. With hair that sits at the shoulder or above, you can achieve a classic curly or straight style in a matter of minutes. These simple looks are some of the best short hair wedding styles since they save you stress on your wedding morning.

Some people fear that cutting their hair will limit their options when it comes to wearing it up, but we're here to assure you that there are actually plenty of stylish [bridal updos](https://www.theknot.com/content/updos-for-long-hair) for short hair. Some of our favorite short hair updos for weddings include slicked-back buns and braided updos. In most cases, these styles can be achieved with shoulder-length hair. However, if your hair is a bit shorter, your stylist can easily add some clip-in extensions or a hair piece. 

To determine which short hairstyles for weddings are best for your [hair type](https://www.theknot.com/content/wedding-hair-type-tips), we recommend booking a [hair trial](https://www.theknot.com/content/prep-for-hair-and-makeup-trial) with a [wedding hairstylist](https://www.theknot.com/marketplace/beauty-services). **Psst:** You can use [The Knot Vendor Marketplace](https://www.theknot.com/marketplace/beauty-services) to find a trusted hair professional in your area.
