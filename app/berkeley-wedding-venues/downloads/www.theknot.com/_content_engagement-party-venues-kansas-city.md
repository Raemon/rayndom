## Johnnie's Jazz Bar & Grill in the Power and Light District 

![Johnnie's Jazz Bar & Grill engagement party venue in the Power and Light District ](https://media-api.xogrp.com/images/5ca302f2-0d17-4252-82b4-021469816a8b~rs_768.h-cr_0.0.1280.960)

_[Photo: Johnnie's Jazz Bar & Grill](https://www.theknot.com/marketplace/johnnies-jazz-bar-and-grille-power-and-light-district-kansas-city-mo-2056076)_

Opt for some unique and fun with [Johnnie's Jazz Bar & Grill](https://www.theknot.com/marketplace/johnnies-jazz-bar-and-grille-power-and-light-district-kansas-city-mo-2056076). This features notable 1920s decor, a theater-style space with velvet furnishings and plenty of room to eat, drink and relax. A private balcony on the upper level is ideal for a private dinner with a small group, but overall, this engagement party place in Kansas City can hold up to 200 people. The retro feel is really what makes this one stand out. 

[See This Venue](https://www.theknot.com/marketplace/johnnies-jazz-bar-and-grille-power-and-light-district-kansas-city-mo-2056076)

## Voo Lounge in the Central Business District 

![Voo Lounge engagement party venue in the Central Business District ](https://media-api.xogrp.com/images/14ec2c9e-1152-46e9-a51b-5e955567271e~rs_768.h-cr_0.0.1423.1067)

_[Photo: Voo Lounge](https://www.theknot.com/marketplace/voo-lounge-kansas-city-mo-2085046)_

For couples looking for something both relaxed and chic, the stylish [Voo Lounge](https://www.theknot.com/marketplace/voo-lounge-kansas-city-mo-2085046) is a nice option. A mix of both vintage-inspired and modern decor makes this spot alluring and unique, with a darker ambiance, a large bar and comfy velvet chairs. Its impressive list of handcrafted cocktails makes this a nice party spot for couples who enjoy a good drink, and it has other fun features, like a hidden room made with cute photo ops in mind. 

[See This Venue](https://www.theknot.com/marketplace/voo-lounge-kansas-city-mo-2085046)
