### About this venue

The Oshman Family JCC located in Palo Alto, California and is one of the most beautiful venue locations on the Peninsula. This premier location offers several unique event spaces totaling 20,000 sq. feet. Flexible planning options make this an ideal place for weddings, bar/bat mitzvahs, corporate meetings, engagement parties, BNI groups, Holiday Parties, birthday parties and more. We offer an array of versatile venues to accommodate any event, from 70 to 300 people. Room rates depend on the space used and the number of guests at your event. Room rates include tables and chairs set to client specification, access to our free parking garage (while available), free Wi-Fi, housekeeping and an event attendant who will be available throughout your event to answer questions and assist with any last-minute needs or changes. We are proud to offer preferred pricing with a number of local vendors, including catering, linens, event décor, entertainment and more. We also have an array of audio/visual offerings, including virtual event options such as onsite streaming and recording, offsite streaming and recording and editing. Our Virtual event options allow you to include loved ones near and far on your special day.
