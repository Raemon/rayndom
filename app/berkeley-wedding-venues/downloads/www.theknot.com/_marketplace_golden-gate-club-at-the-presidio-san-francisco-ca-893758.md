This elegant event venue at the Presidio's Main Post combines historical and classic styling with Spanish Colonial revival architecture for a remarkable setting. Its convenient location in San Francisco provides a scenic escape in the city. \- Choose from three different banquet rooms: the Ventana Ballroom, Cypress Room, or the Hawthorn Room \- Enjoy breathtaking Bay Area sights and peekaboo views of the Golden Gate Bridge \- Minutes down the road from Fisherman's Wharf and San Francisco's Financial District \- Entertain guests in the scenic front courtyard, ideal for cocktail hour and lawn games Located in San Francisco, CA, Golden Gate Club at the Presidio is situated on the island's northwestern corner, offering stunning views of the San Francisco Bay, the Presidio forest, the Golden Gate Bridge, and Alcatraz. Golden Gate Club was initially constructed in 1949 and served as a service club for enlisted men and a locale where historical treaties were signed. This building is now a venue for various celebrations and features seven indoor event spaces and an outdoor balcony and courtyard. The Presidio Chapel sits just up the hill from the Golden Gate Club and is an apt site for your ceremony. If you'd prefer to stay at the Golden Gate Club, ceremonies delight in the alfresco courtyard or Cypress and Hawthorn ballrooms. After your ceremony, guests can enjoy lawn games in the courtyard and head to the Cypress or Hawthorn rooms for appetizers and cocktails before heading into the Ventana ballroom for dinner. Make a grand entrance into your reception with a stunning sparkler show. The Ventana ballroom is a popular choice for receptions with its spacious floor plan, vaulted ceilings with white exposed wood beams, and floor-to-ceiling windows offering premium Bay views. Additionally, you can host a live band with the ballroom's built-in stage. Guests can cozy up next to the fireplace in the back of the room or head to the dance floor for a night of dancing. Golden Gate Club is managed by Wedgewood Weddings, who will work with you to create the celebration you envision. The spaces in this venue can accommodate up to 300 guests for your festivities. With the team by Wedgewood Weddings onsite, you'll receive a custom tour and planning experience that will give you more time to enjoy your engagement and stress-free wedding coordination. Book your personalized tour of this stunning and historic site today. Golden Gate Club at the Presidio is ideal for couples that want to experience historic elegance with a magnificent view.
