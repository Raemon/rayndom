About this vendor

La Donna Bridal Atelier is a family-own wedding dress retailer located in San Jose, CA. Offering custom couture garments and a made-to-order service, this atelier can create a bespoke outfit that really stands out. The team of professional dressmakers uses the finest of tailoring methods and techniques to produce a one-off garment that is truly unique to you. Madina is the manager and lead style consultant at La Donna Bridal Atelier. Using her industry knowledge and expert visual eye, she is dedicated to helping you find the perfect outfit for your milestone occasion. Madina loves keeping up to date with evolving trends in the world of wedding attire. Whether you favor a luxury embellished gown or a simple silhouette, Madina and the team will be there to guide you. By paying special attention to your preferences, they strive to deliver that sought-after wow moment. La Donna Bridal Atelier offers a variety of tailoring and dress-making services. For a fully personalized garment, you can design your wedding gown with the help of an expert stylist. The team will work with you to accentuate your body shape and reflect your style. Before they assemble your garment, they can offer you a private appointment to take your measurements. When the item is ready, the team can arrange a final fitting to make any adjustments for a seamless fit. Plus-size gowns, sample dresses, and custom-made tuxedos are also available at the atelier.
