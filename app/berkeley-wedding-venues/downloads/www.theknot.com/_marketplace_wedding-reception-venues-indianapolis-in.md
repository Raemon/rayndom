### FAQs

#### Can I host a micro wedding in Indianapolis?

Yes, you can host a micro wedding here. With a broad range of venues throughout the city, finding the perfect place for an intimate celebration is easy.

#### What season is the best for a wedding in Indianapolis?

Fall is considered the best and most popular wedding season, with peak autumnal foliage in mid to late October.

#### How do I get married in Indianapolis?

To get married in Indianapolis, you must first receive a marriage license from a clerk's office in the Indiana county where you reside. It will be valid for 60 days, and couples must host their wedding ceremony before their license expires.
