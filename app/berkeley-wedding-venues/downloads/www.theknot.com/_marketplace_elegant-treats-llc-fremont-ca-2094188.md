Elegant Treats LLC is a unique wedding catering company based in Fremont, CA. This team specializes in gourmet dessert bars and spreads, adding a touch of sweetness to your special occasion. The dedicated staff is committed to providing exceptional services that exceed your expectations. A family-run business, Elegant Treats LLC was established in 2025 with a vision of sweetening each client's special day. The team is driven by its passion for delicious desserts made with high-quality, locally sourced ingredients. They believe food is an experience, and they adore creating unique dishes that delight the senses and leave a lasting impression. Elegant Treats LLC takes pride in providing tailored services to match each couple's unique vision. It offers a range of beautifully designed dessert bars, stations, and spreads that can be set up at your cocktail hour or reception. Options include a mini pancake or waffle bar, a churro bar, cotton candy and popcorn stations, an ice cream sundae bar, an acai bowl bar, and much more. Delivery, setup, and service are included throughout your celebration, offering an impeccable experience from start to finish.

Monica Shaheed is the passionate founder and owner of Elegant Treats LLC, a mobile catering company specializing in upscale dessert and snack bars for weddings, events, and corporate gatherings. With a heart rooted in family and creativity, Monica was inspired to launch her business as a way to create more flexibility and fulfillment beyond the traditional 9-to-5 lifestyle. As a dedicated wife and proud mother of seven in a blended family, Monica brings warmth, hustle, and a deep sense of purpose into everything she does. From gourmet churros and mini pancake bars to beautifully curated charcuterie and fruit carts, her goal is to deliver not just treats — but memorable experiences. When she’s not catering events across California, Monica enjoys exploring beaches and mountains with her family and making magical memories at Disneyland.
