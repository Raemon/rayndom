## Napa on Providence in Charlotte

![Napa on Providence engagement party venue in Charlotte](https://media-api.xogrp.com/images/b3bdb464-01f7-4457-90b0-e436237de5cd~rs_768.h-cr_0.0.1037.778)

_[Photo: Napa on Providence](https://www.theknot.com/marketplace/napa-on-providence-charlotte-nc-986648)_

The perfect setting for couples seeking indoor/outdoor engagement party venues in Charlotte to enjoy the region's wonderful weather, [Napa on Providence](https://www.theknot.com/marketplace/napa-on-providence-charlotte-nc-986648) is a great option. Neutral decor throughout works with your choice of [engagement party decor](https://www.theknot.com/content/engagement-party-decorations). With two different event spaces to choose from, depending on the size of your gathering (The Rutherford Room can hold 12 to 20 people, while the Oakville Room is suitable for as many as 40 people), along with an outside terrace reminiscent of Napa Valley, this beautiful engagement party venue is a top choice among local couples. 

[See This Venue](https://www.theknot.com/marketplace/napa-on-providence-charlotte-nc-986648)

## The Mercantile in Rock Hill

![The Mercantile engagement party venue in Rock Hill](https://media-api.xogrp.com/images/f0626048-558b-458b-bdee-9b3b6c5394fd~rs_768.h-cr_0.53.790.1106)

_[Photo: The Mercantile](https://www.theknot.com/marketplace/the-mercantile-rock-hill-sc-2078898)_

Located in the Charlotte suburb of Rock Hill, South Carolina, [The Mercantile](https://www.theknot.com/marketplace/the-mercantile-rock-hill-sc-2078898) is a beautiful, historic engagement party venue that's also a popular [bridal shower location near Charlotte](https://www.theknot.com/content/bridal-shower-venues-charlotte-nc). This 1900s-era warehouse was used for Coca-Cola bottling, but it's been turned into a lovely space, with original wood beams and white-washed brick walls. Whether you fancy an intimate engagement party with up to 50 guests or a large party with up to 200 of your acquaintances, The Mercantile offers not only a beautiful space but on-site catering, an in-house florist, full bar service and even a dedicated event coordinator. 

[See This Venue](https://www.theknot.com/marketplace/the-mercantile-rock-hill-sc-2078898)
