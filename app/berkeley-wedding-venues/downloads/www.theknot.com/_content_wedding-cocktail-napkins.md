## How Many Cocktail Napkins to Buy for a Wedding? 

Now for the tricky part: How many cocktail napkins per guest? When you buy disposable napkins for weddings, ensuring you have more than enough for each guest is a must, so a good rule of thumb is around three to four napkins each. Here's how many you should consider based on your guest count.

  * **How many cocktail napkins for 50 guests?** 150–200
  * **How many cocktail napkins for 100 guests?** 200–300
  * **How many cocktail napkins for 150 guests?** 300–450
  * **How many cocktail napkins for 200 guests?** 600–800
