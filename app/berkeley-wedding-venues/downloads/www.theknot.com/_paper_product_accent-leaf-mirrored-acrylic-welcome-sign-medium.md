Details

Simplicity has never looked so sophisticated! This welcome sign features a leaf motif in the upper left-hand corner and a warm welcoming message and your names in a blend serif cursive typography. Your wedding date and venue details add a touch of personalization, with a handwritten invitation to "lets party!" completing this playful look. **Materials** Our wedding signs are available in a curated selection of materials designed to complement your setting, from grand entrances to intimate tabletops. Each option is thoughtfully selected for its visual impact, ease of use, and styling versatility.  
**Indoor Foam Core** Smooth and lightweight, foam core is ideal for indoor use where ease and elegance meet. It’s a great option for welcome signs, seating charts, and other easel displays.  
**Outdoor Corrugated** Made from white corrugated plastic and built to withstand the elements, this option is perfect for outdoor signage. Each sign includes an aluminum metal H-stake for easy setup in grass or soil   
**Plastic** A smooth, sturdy material ideal for tabletop signage. Designed to be freestanding, lightweight, and easy to reposition—perfect for dynamic displays like bars, counters, or dessert tables.  
**Acrylic** Minimal and modern, acrylic brings an elevated transparency to any display. Each sign arrives with a protective film to keep it scratch-free until you're ready to style.  
**Mirrored Acrylic** Crafted from durable, lightweight acrylic with a flawless mirror finish, this material offers the luxe look of glass without the worry. Each sign features slightly beveled edges for a smooth, safe finish and a clean, crisp reflection that photographs beautifully.  
**Please note:** Signage will ship separately from any non-signage items in your order. Due to the custom nature of these products, signage is not eligible for reprints. Easels and display stands are not included.
