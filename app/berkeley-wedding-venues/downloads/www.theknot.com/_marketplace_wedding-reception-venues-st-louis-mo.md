## Wedding Reception Venues in St. Louis

Famed for its music scene, barbeque ribs, passionate sports fanbase, and iconic Gateway Arch, St. Louis, MO will be a memorable place to host your special day. This historical settlement is vibrant, charming, and cultural with incredible landmarks to visit and exciting things to do during your city wedding.

### Finding Your St. Louis Wedding Venue

Midtown and the West End have some fabulous venues if you're looking for grand, sophisticated spaces. Picture elevated ceilings, huge archways, and white walls that make for a blank canvas. There are also plenty of contemporary, industrial venues surrounding Midtown and The Gate. The exposed brick and freestanding beams that make these venues shine might stand out to couples looking for a more modern vibe.

### Amenities in St. Louis

Brimming with historical landmarks, incredible cuisine, and soulful music, your stay in St. Louis has the potential to be an enlightening experience. A hub for American history, the city has plenty of museums and centuries-old structures to explore. Continue your journey into the past and find live blues, bluegrass, and jazz music playing in many classic bars and taverns.

### FAQs

#### Can you have weddings on Sundays in St. Louis?

Getting married on a Sunday will depend on the availability of your place of worship or desired venue and officiant, so it's possible. Some courthouses perform civil marriage ceremonies on Wednesdays only.

#### What is the best time of year to get married in St. Louis?

May, June, September, and October are the most popular months for a wedding. Since St. Louis gets several inches of snow each winter, expect a white wedding in December, January, and February.

#### How do I plan an affordable wedding in St. Louis?

Try comparing weekday rates with weekend prices across various venues, and keep an eye out for inclusive wedding packages that offer equipment hire. Vendors with pre-loved wedding decor and florists that use in-season flowers for their arrangements will help save you money while achieving your vision.
