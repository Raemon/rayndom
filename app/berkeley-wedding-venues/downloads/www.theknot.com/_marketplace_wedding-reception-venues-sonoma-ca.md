The first thing to come to mind is likely the vibrant vineyards and iconic wineries that enrich this gorgeous county. ### Finding The Best Wedding Venues in Sonoma

The perfect setting for a wine country wedding, acres upon acres of lush vineyards await couples seeking a dreamy, verdant backdrop. ### Things to do in Sonoma

One of the main activities you can fill your days with during your wedding celebration is, of course, wine tours. From tasting the region's exquisite wines to exploring the lush vineyards where the grape-growing magic happens, Sonoma is a haven for wine lovers. That's not all this stunning county offers. Couples and loved ones can embark on historical tours and nature excursions, dine on delicious local delicacies, and even take to the skies in a hot air balloon or helicopter.

### FAQs

#### Can you have a winery wedding in Sonoma?

Yes, many wineries can host weddings on their grounds. Imagine the beautiful photos with a stunning vineyard as your backdrop!

#### What do I need to get married in Sonoma?

Before your wedding, you must apply for a marriage license at the County Clerk-Recorder Office.

#### What time of year is best for a wedding in Sonoma?

Harvest season in the fall is the most popular time of year, with summer also favored for its long days and sunny weather.
