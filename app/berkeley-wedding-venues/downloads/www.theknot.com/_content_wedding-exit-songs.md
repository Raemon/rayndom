[](https://www.theknot.com/content/wedding-after-party-songs)If you're looking to make a grand departure at your reception, you'll need a standout wedding exit song to play while you and your spouse leave as newlyweds. Whether you plan your departure under a bridge of sparklers or you envision something more subtle, your reception exit should be memorable and romantic. 

[READ MORE Find a Local Wedding DJ Who Can Match Yiour Vibe ](https://www.theknot.com/marketplace/wedding-djs)

Couples are going out of their way to make their reception exits uniquely personal. According to [The Knot 2025 Real Weddings Study](https://www.theknot.com/content/wedding-data-insights/real-weddings-study), which surveyed nearly 17,000 couples who got married in 2024, 43% of Gen Z respondents and 26% of Millennials had a "grand" reception exit. From fireworks to [flashy getaway cars](https://www.theknot.com/content/creative-wedding-exit-ideas) and even [confetti tosses](https://www.theknot.com/content/18-creative-exit-toss-ideas), there are so many ways to give your departure a personalized stamp—especially through your music. Making your [reception playlist](https://www.theknot.com/content/billboard-most-popular-wedding-songs) is one of the most exciting aspects of planning your nuptials. (These are the [best wedding songs](https://www.theknot.com/content/best-wedding-songs) that define your love story, after all.) So when it comes to selecting a tune to accompany your newlywed send-off, you'll want to pick something that's unforgettable.

Below, we've rounded up all of our favorite wedding exit songs to help you leave in style. From modern chart-toppers to classic wedding hits that everyone knows, these would all make excellent options for your reception departure. Want even more ideas? Your [wedding DJ](https://www.theknot.com/marketplace/wedding-djs) might have a few showstoppers up their sleeve too.
