BartendingChris, based out of Oakland, CA, offers mobile bartending services for weddings and other special occasions. Serving the Bay and Sacramento areas, they deliver a customized hospitality experience for your big day. Founder Chris Navarro is a licensed Bartender who brings a full-service bar experience directly to your event. He works closely with you to transform your vision for your special day into a carefully tailored experience. With his help, you can create a signature beverage menu that reflects your unique style and love story. BartendingChris creates custom packages based on your specific needs, taking into account your location, venue style, hours of service, and number of guests. He can bring the entire bar setup so you enjoy a great experience no matter the setting, or deliver bartending services at any venue with an existing setup. Services begin with three hours of event coverage, which can extend to eight or more.
