Accessorize your wedding with an abundance of blooming florals with 3bcosmos, a wedding florist located in San Carlos, CA. Team of this beautiful Flower Studio believe that flowers can tell a story that words cannot. Their gorgeous colors and sensational fragrances can touch the hearts of couples and guests alike. 3bcosmos uses the freshest florals with the most vibrant colors for the weddings they style. They collect these from local farms to ensure their quality and to support the businesses around them. This results in beautiful setups, happy couples, and booming companies. 3Bcosmos Team will create an array of different arrangements you can pick to enhance your wedding day, including stunning center pieces, gorgeous bouquets and matching breathtaking boutonnieres. This can be done in whatever style you would like with any arrangement of florals you wish. Work closely with them and they’ll make sure your visions are brought to life.
