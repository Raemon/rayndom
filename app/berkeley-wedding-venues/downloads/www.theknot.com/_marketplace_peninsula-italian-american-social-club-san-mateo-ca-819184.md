Nestled in the heart of San Mateo, CA, the Peninsula Italian American Social Club offers couples a timeless setting for their wedding celebration. Since 1934, our club has been a gathering place for family, friends, and community, and today, it continues to provide the perfect blend of tradition, elegance, and hospitality. Our spacious ballroom accommodates up to 375 guests, with high ceilings, classic charm, and plenty of room for dining and dancing. Couples may also take advantage of our intimate outdoor courtyard, ideal for romantic ceremonies or cocktail hours under the California sky. With in-house catering inspired by authentic Italian flavors, your guests will enjoy handcrafted menus featuring family-style service, seasonal dishes, and fine wines. Our dedicated team takes pride in handling every detail, from setup to service, so you can relax and enjoy your special day. Whether you’re envisioning a grand celebration or an intimate gathering, the Peninsula Italian American Social Club provides the warm atmosphere, exceptional cuisine, and personalized care to bring your wedding vision to life.
