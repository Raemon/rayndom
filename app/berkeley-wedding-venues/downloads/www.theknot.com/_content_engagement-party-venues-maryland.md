## good neighbor in Baltimore, Maryland 

![good neighbor engagement party venue in Baltimore, Maryland ](https://media-api.xogrp.com/images/830b75c6-5ced-4495-8bcf-dbe164407334~rs_768.h-cr_0.0.1212.909)

_[Photo: good neighbor](https://www.theknot.com/marketplace/good-neighbor-baltimore-md-2089691)_

A unique venue in the middle of Baltimore, [good neighbor](https://www.theknot.com/marketplace/good-neighbor-baltimore-md-2089691) is a coffee shop, gift shop, art space and venue all in one. This versatile location offers a few different types of spaces. The coffee shop space makes for a good intimate gathering, their outdoor patio is great for nice weather, and the design garage is an open-concept space that feels modern and creative. 

[See This Venue](https://www.theknot.com/marketplace/good-neighbor-baltimore-md-2089691)

## Kharis Lavender Fields in Gaithersburg, Maryland 

![Kharis Lavender Fields engagement party venue in Gaithersburg, Maryland ](https://media-api.xogrp.com/images/38099d73-289e-4103-96cf-6d1fd67be59c~rs_768.h-cr_17.0.1908.1418)

_[Photo: Kharis Lavender Fields](https://www.theknot.com/marketplace/kharis-lavender-fields-gaithersburg-md-2074796)_

Romantic and dreamy, [Kharis Lavender Fields](https://www.theknot.com/marketplace/kharis-lavender-fields-gaithersburg-md-2074796) is the perfect Maryland engagement party location for the outdoorsy couple who wants to take advantage of warm weather in Maryland. Lavender fields offer a picturesque and colorful background for your party (and photos), and the Lavender Barn on-site is great for smaller parties. The inside features chic rustic decor and a beautiful patio space, and you know the entire venue will smell fantastic. 

[See This Venue](https://www.theknot.com/marketplace/kharis-lavender-fields-gaithersburg-md-2074796)

## Bushel and a Peck Kitchen & Bar in Clarksville, Maryland 

![Bushel and a Peck Kitchen & Bar engagement party venue in Clarksville, Maryland ](https://media-api.xogrp.com/images/b2cb830f-b48d-40dd-be25-28c043fbe3b3~rs_768.h-cr_0.0.1212.909)

_[Photo: Bushel and a Peck Kitchen & Bar](https://www.theknot.com/marketplace/bushel-and-a-peck-kitchen-and-bar-clarksville-md-2068795)_

Nicely decorated with both indoor and outdoor space, [Bushel and a Peck Kitchen & Bar](https://www.theknot.com/marketplace/bushel-and-a-peck-kitchen-and-bar-clarksville-md-2068795) is a classic Maryland restaurant that serves the kind of fresh seafood the state is known for. This also happens to be a popular [wedding shower location in Maryland](https://www.theknot.com/content/bridal-shower-venues-maryland) because there's plenty of space and they offer locally sourced and seasonally appropriate menu items. 

[See This Venue](https://www.theknot.com/marketplace/bushel-and-a-peck-kitchen-and-bar-clarksville-md-2068795)

## The Bluebird in Baltimore, Maryland 

![The Bluebird engagement party venue in Baltimore, Maryland ](https://media-api.xogrp.com/images/a2d69806-cbbb-40bc-8693-a8fcebcfa384~rs_768.h-cr_64.0.1131.800)

_[Photo: The Bluebird](https://www.theknot.com/marketplace/the-bluebird-baltimore-md-2071327)_

With stylish decor and unique, vintage-inspired accents, [The Bluebird](https://www.theknot.com/marketplace/the-bluebird-baltimore-md-2071327) is a very chic restaurant to host an engagement party in Maryland. The Green Room is perfect for a cozy, intimate party, with a bar and seated dining options, while The Pub is smaller and makes for a great cocktail event. For couples who want to keep things small, this is a beautiful choice with great food.

_[Photo: Port Annapolis Marina](https://www.theknot.com/marketplace/port-annapolis-marina-annapolis-md-2073924)_

## Deep Blue at Kitty Knight in Galena, Maryland
