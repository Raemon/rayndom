### About this vendor

Marigold is an Indian outfit rental company based out of San Ramon, CA and shipping nationwide. We offer a curated collection of lehenga rentals, sarees, shararas, Indo-Western outfits, and sherwanis for both men and women- with jewelry and accessories to complete the look. Whether you're the bride, groom, part of the wedding party, or a guest, Marigold helps you look stunning at every event without the stress of shopping or the cost of buying. We offer a sustainable fashion option for Indian outfits. What sets us apart? Our concierge styling service makes outfit planning easy and enjoyable. We work closely with you to coordinate looks for every event—mehndi, sangeet, haldi, wedding, and reception. Our exclusive bridesmaid and groomsmen collections ensure your wedding party looks cohesive and camera-ready. With our fit assurance, you'll have peace of mind knowing your outfit will arrive ready to wear, with adjustments made based on your sizing and preferences before the big day.
