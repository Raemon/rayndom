About this vendor

Some say that the wedding band sets the tone for the entire wedding. Ruby band believes that and makes sure your wedding will be remembered forever. Performing throughout Northern California, The Ruby Band is based out of Pleasanton, CA, and focuses on getting your guests onto the dance floor! This band has a diverse song repertoire that appeals especially to the age group of the Bride & Groom, and their parents. The Ruby Band plays weddings in San Francisco, the East Bay, Silicon Valley, San Jose, Napa Valley, Sacramento, Lake Tahoe, and Santa Cruz/Monterey. Headlined by recording artists Andreea Onica Keller and Phillip Andrew Sullivan, the group is known for their upbeat dance songs and lively and engaging Master of Ceremonies (Mr Sullivan) who, if your wish, will "officiate" over all the specialized parts of the proceeding: Bride+Groom grand entrance; Bride+Groom first dance; Bride+Dad dance; Introducions of the toasters; and so on. The talented musicians who comprise The Ruby Band have more than 15 years of experience playing at weddings across the Bay Area. Together, they have performed at over 100 venues in Northern California. In addition, the M.C. Phillip Sullivan has presided over at least 200 weddings on both the East and West Coasts of the U.S. He knows how to work with you and your planner smoothly and seamlessly to ensure a spectacular wedding party that flows without a hitch. The Ruby Band prides itself on engaging its audiences in the songs and getting them up and dancing - and participating. Their repertoire includes: \- current pop tunes (2000-2021) \- dance, rock, R&B songs from the '80s and '90s. \- classic rock songs from the '70s and '80s \- modern Country; and Latin flavor \- dinner and cocktail hour music \- instrumental music during ceremony These musicians can also customize their playlist to suit the mood and themes of your ceremony, cocktail hour, luncheon or dinner, and reception party. They will work closely with you and your wedding planner to fit seamlessly into your wedding program and run of show.
