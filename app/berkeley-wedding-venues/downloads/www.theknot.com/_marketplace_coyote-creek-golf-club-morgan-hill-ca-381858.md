Coyote Creek Golf Club is a stunning golf club and countryside event venue in Morgan Hill, California. From the rolling hills to the lake and mountain views, Coyote Creek Golf Club offers a striking backdrop for you and your loved ones to celebrate. No matter what you’re looking for on your wedding day, this golf club is the perfect place to enjoy one of the best days of your life. Coyote Creek Golf Club offers enough space for significant events for family and friends. If you are looking for an intimate setting, we also have the best setting for you. The golf club offers a craftsman-style clubhouse with dramatic fireplaces and comfortable furnishings, as well as a charming dining room, a bar area, and a spacious patio for warm-weather celebrations. Services Offered Coyote Creek Golf Club is staffed by a team who will go above and beyond to help you make your big day a stunning and unforgettable celebration. Couples can rent various event items, such as chairs, dance floors, tables, barware, china, flatware, glassware, and linens. Some of the services that they offer include: Set up and clean up Event planning Outside vendors Pet-friendly spaces WiFi Coyote Creek Golf Club is also home to 12 Mile Station, where couples and their guests can enjoy a menu with diverse mouthwatering dishes. No matter your unique catering needs, 12 Mile Station can create a dining experience unlike any other.
