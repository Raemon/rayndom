Updated Dec 29, 2025

Along with the fresh feeling of new beginnings, springtime also provides an abundance of in-season blooms perfect for any wedding celebration. Whether it's bright white tulips, soft blush peonies or brightly hued ranunculus, choose from a plethora of flowers for your spring wedding centerpieces. No matter what motif you select for your[ spring wedding](https://www.theknot.com/content/spring-wedding-planning-tips), work with your [florist](https://www.theknot.com/marketplace/florists) and [decor](https://www.theknot.com/marketplace/wedding-decor-shops) pro (who you found on [The Knot Vendor Marketplace](https://www.theknot.com/marketplace)) to bring your vision board to life.

[READ MORE Find (and Book!) a Creative Florist on The Knot  ](https://www.theknot.com/marketplace/florists)

We chatted with expert florist Tara L Folker, owner and lead designer at[ Splints & Daisies Floral Design](https://www.theknot.com/marketplace/splints-and-daisies-lancaster-pa-334443) out of Lancaster, Pennsylvania, for spring wedding reception centerpiece ideas. Overall, her favorite spring combos are "anything that is blooming." A few notable options include hyacinth, [tulips](https://www.theknot.com/content/tulip-bouquet-ideas), [anemones](https://www.theknot.com/content/anemone-bouquet), [ranunculus](https://www.theknot.com/content/ranunculus-wedding-bouquets), quince, daffodils, pansies, muscari and poppies. 

[READ MORE Find (and Book!) a Creative Florist on The Knot  ](https://www.theknot.com/marketplace/florists)

"There are such amazing varieties that bloom in the spring, that I have a hard time picking favorites," she said. "Just as the leaves are emerging from the trees, my flower farmers have goodies beyond dreams. The combinations that can be put together by having an open mind to what nature is currently providing are always my favorite." From romantic arrangements to chic and modern creations, gain inspiration for your tablescape with these[ wedding centerpiece](https://www.theknot.com/content/centerpieces-we-love) ideas for spring. And if you see something you really like, be sure to add your faves to your [Pinterest board](https://www.pinterest.com/theknot/).

## Rustic Spring Wedding Centerpieces

Folker described rustic centerpieces as "more carefree, garden style" and sometimes "messy." Look for a design that looks "as though it was picked from the garden [and] overflowing with abundance."

## Modern Spring Wedding Centerpieces

A modern centerpiece is often eye-catching and unexpected. Folker sees her modern centerpieces as a creative form of "floral art." "My version of modern would be arranged more artistically, with a unique shape and thoughtful placement," she said. "I love to combine the principles of various design styles."

### 15\. Modern Spring Centerpiece with Tulips and Roses

![Modern Spring Centerpiece with White Tulips and Roses](https://media-api.xogrp.com/images/415a7edf-be83-462f-b75d-03a724291b4d~rs_768.h-cr_0.355.1458.1813)
