Nick Foxer is a wedding musician in Santa Rosa, CA. With 88 keys and all the feel-good iconic hits, Nick Foxer is a lively alternative to a full wedding band. Featuring midi percussion loops, harmonies, and maybe even a harmonica, he brings high energy and maximum danceability to every event. Think one-person band. He's also a great addition when background/ambient live piano music is preferred, performing without percussion or even instrumentally. Nick's been a full-time live entertainer in Northern California for 5+ years, with experience playing indoor and outdoor weddings of all types. He's a fantastic budget-friendly option for upbeat live music, as well as times when space may be limited.

Owner/Entertainer

I was born and raised in the vineyards of Sebastopol, CA. After venturing out to the Midwest for music school, and spending many years in the LA and Nashville music scenes, I’m thrilled to be back home in the wine country doing what I love. I specialize in classic pop and rock songs from various decades: Eagles, Elton John, Oasis, Tom Petty, etc. …maybe even some country.
