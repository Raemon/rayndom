Take in the breathtaking views of Newport and Narragansett Bay when you host your engagement party aboard [Coastal Queen Cruises](https://www.theknot.com/marketplace/coastal-queen-cruises-and-jamestown-newport-ferry-jamestown-ri-2036028) out of Jamestown, Rhode Island.
