Meraki Wreath and Floral Company is a wedding and event florist in Clayton, CA. With their specialty in custom, nature-inspired floral designs, this team is happy to provide clients with centerpieces, bouquets, and other arrangements to complete their nuptial theme. Co-founders Thelma and Ashley have many years of experience in floral designs and love to give each couple's special day a unique, fresh appearance. Their company name Meraki comes from the Greek word that refers to the heart and soul a person puts into their work. Thelma and Ashley live by this phrase, with their close attention to detail and dedication to their clients. Meraki Wreath and Floral Company works with fresh, dried, and silk florals to create any arrangements you want. Your vision will be their main priority as they customize a design plan that fits your budget, wedding colors, and venue type. On your wedding day, they will handle delivery and setup to ensure each petal leaves a lasting impression on your guests.
