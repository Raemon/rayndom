Details

The Bridgerton x The Knot collection speaks to couples planning a wedding inspired by the Regency era, down to every last elegant flourish. Find a design that reflects your epic romance only on The Knot.   
Let your loved ones know that you're eagerly waiting for them to accept your invitation. These wedding response cards display a simple color scheme, giving the minimalist design a fitting backdrop. The elegant layout is rich with antique charm, matching the Mayfair-inspired elegance of the Bridgerton style. The words "Kindly Reply" loop across the top of the card in a regal cursive script. A motif of garden flowers and leaves blooms in the center of the response card for an added touch of beauty.
