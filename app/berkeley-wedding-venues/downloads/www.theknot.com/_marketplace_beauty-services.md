## Finding a Beauty Salon

Advice From The Knot

### Start With Your Style

Searching for wedding hair and makeup in your area?

Look at wedding beauty photos to get inspired. Also check out pros’ portfolios to see what looks they specialize in.

### Understand the Options

There's some key info you'll want to consider before reaching out to beauty artists.

Think about how many artists you’ll need, if you’d like to have a pre-wedding trial, and if you’d like them to work onsite.

### Start a Conversation

Know what you're looking for? Reach out!

The Knot's "Schedule Appointment" feature helps you connect with pros so you can see if a beauty artist is the right fit.
