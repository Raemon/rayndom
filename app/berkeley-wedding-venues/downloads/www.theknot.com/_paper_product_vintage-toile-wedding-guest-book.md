Details

The classic toile style paints the front of your wedding guest book, which is adorned with the vintage French pattern. Birds swoop through the scene to perch on branches while grapes hang heavy with juice. The pastoral meets the elegant on the cover and invites your guests to leave their own refined, romantic thoughts on the pages within.  
  
This book has a casebound cover with matte lamination, and 100 blank pages (50 sheets) of 70# opaque text paper. Includes a 0.25" white grosgrain bookmark ribbon.
