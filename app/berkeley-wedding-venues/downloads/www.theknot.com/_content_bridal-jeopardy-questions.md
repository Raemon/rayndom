## What is Bridal Shower Jeopardy?

If you're a fan of the TV show _Jeopardy!_ (or even the more recent _Pop Culture Jeopardy!_), then you'll be familiar with this classic game. However, many brides-to-be choose this as a fun [bridal shower game](https://www.theknot.com/content/a-complete-guide-to-bridal-shower-games) or [bachelorette party game](https://www.theknot.com/content/bachelorette-party-game-ideas), especially with a naughty twist. Bridal Jeopardy is a chance for your friends, family and loved ones to test their knowledge of the happy couple for some special prizes. You can even split people off into teams and offer a Double Jeopardy round to squeeze in more bridal Jeopardy questions and answers. Let's look at what you need to know to play this trivia-heavy game.

## How to Play Bridal Jeopardy

For bridal shower Jeopardy, you'll have to consider the rules of the typical game. Categories are split into different points, starting from 100. Each topic gives you an answer, and you must answer in the form of a question. You can also sprinkle in a Daily Double or two in the game, where guests can wager twice the points they have. For the Final Jeopardy question, players will have 30 seconds to fill in their answers.

And if you're looking for a ready-made bridal Jeopardy board, you can find both physical items and cute bridal shower Jeopardy templates on [Etsy](https://www.awin1.com/cread.php?awinmid=6220&awinaffid=565019&campaign=&clickref=TK-bridal-jeopardy-questions&ued=https%3A%2F%2Fwww.etsy.com%2Fsearch%3Fq%3Dbridal%2Bjeopardy&platform=pl). If you want to create your own, consider Canva and use a poster board or digital PowerPoint during the shower. As for coming up with Jeopardy questions for bridal showers, we've got a few ideas to help out.

## Bridal Jeopardy Questions

Get your Jeopardy music ready for this bridal shower game (no cash prize included). To help get the ball rolling, we rounded up some bridal Jeopardy categories and questions. From nice to naughty and everything in between, there's a category for everyone to have a chance to win. Here are some bridal Jeopardy questions, ideas and topics to get you started on planning this fun game.

**Bridal Jeopardy Questions:** About the Bride | Funny | Dirty | About the Fiancé | About the Couple | Famous Couples | Wedding Traditions | Love Songs | Name That Movie | Final Question

### Questions About the Bride

  * This famous star is the bride's celebrity crush.
  * This sweet treat is the bride's favorite dessert.
  * You can catch the bride at any event with a glass of this, her favorite drink.
  * Back in the day, this was the bride's first job.
  * On karaoke night, the bride is most likely to pick this song to sing.
  * You'll find the bride binging this, her favorite TV show.
  * Before saying yes to the dress, the bride tried on this many wedding dresses.
  * These are the bride's sun, moon and rising signs.
  * This is the bride's go-to coffee order.
  * This was the first concert the bride attended.
  * The bride is most likely to indulge in this self-care activity.
  * This is the bride's favorite book.



### Funny Bridal Jeopardy Questions

  * This is the most embarrassing thing that happened on the couple's first date.
  * The couple can quote this movie word-for-word.
  * This song has been added to the wedding do-not-play list.
  * Of the two, this person is most likely to fall asleep watching TV.
  * Despite their best efforts, this person is the messiest.
  * For the couple, these are their biggest pet peeves.
  * This is who actually said "I love you" first.
  * These are the couple's weirdest phobias.



### Dirty Bridal Jeopardy Questions

  * This person initiated the first move in the relationship.
  * This city is the most popular bachelorette party destination. (What is Las Vegas?)
  * This luxury destination was voted the top honeymoon spot. (What is Mauritius?)
  * This popular drink might also be a honeymoon activity. (What is sex on the beach?)
  * This is the bride's preferred sex toy.
  * This is when the bride lost her V-card.
  * This is who is on the couple's "hall pass" list.



### Questions About the Fiancé

  * Growing up, this was (fiancé)'s favorite pastime. (Can be a sport/hobby.)
  * This is the fiancé's middle name.
  * This city is where (fiancé) grew up.
  * This is (fiancé)'s zodiac sign.
  * This is how many siblings (fiancé) has.
  * This is (fiancé)'s favorite kind of food.
  * This is (fiancé)'s favorite video/board game.
  * On pizza night, you'll find this topping on (fiancé)'s slice.
  * This is (fiancé)'s dream car.
  * This person was (fiancé)'s childhood hero.
  * On Halloween, (fiancé) is most likely to dress up as this character.



### Questions About the Couple

  * This city is where the couple first met.
  * This spot is where the couple had their first date.
  * You can usually find the couple at this place, aka their favorite restaurant.
  * (Name) and (name) have marked this place as their dream travel destination.
  * This is the name(s) of the couple's pet(s).
  * This special date is when the two got engaged.
  * These are the couple's nicknames for each other.
  * This is the spot where the couple took their first vacation.



### Questions About Famous Couples

  * In 2018, this American actress married Prince Harry in a royal ceremony. (Who is Meghan Markle?)
  * This _Game of Thrones_ couple took their on-screen romance into real life, marrying in 2018. (Who are Kit Harington and Rose Leslie?)
  * When Beyoncé married Jay Z, she had 70,000 of these at the wedding, her favorite flower. (What is a white orchid?)
  * This Hollywood star became Princess of Monaco when she married Prince Rainier III in 1956. (Who is Grace Kelly?)
  * This famous couple threw a wedding for two of their bulldogs in 2015. (Who are Chrissy Teigen and John Legend?)
  * This Hollywood couple starred together in _A Quiet Place_ and married in 2010. (Who are Emily Blunt and John Krasinski?)
  * In 1981, this British royal walked down the aisle in a 25-foot-long train. (Who is Princess Diana?)
  * This _Modern Family_ star has been married to painter Justin Mikita since 2013. (Who is Jesse Tyler Ferguson?)



### Questions About Wedding Traditions

  * This metal is commonly used to make wedding bands because of its durability and symbolism of eternal love. (What is gold?)
  * Brides in India often wear this color instead of white. (What is red?)
  * These "somethings" are the four things a bride needs on the wedding day. (What is something old, something new, something borrowed and something blue?)
  * This sweet treat is traditionally cut by the newlyweds together at the reception. (What is cake?)
  * This British monarch originated the tradition of white wedding dresses. (Who is Queen Victoria?)
  * Couples bury this alcoholic drink to keep the rain away on their wedding day. (What is bourbon?)
  * In a traditional Hindu wedding, the couple walks around this sacred object seven times. (What is a fire?)
  * Guests throw this at the newlyweds when they exit to symbolize prosperity and fertility. (What is rice?)
  * This ancient ceremony is where the phrase "tie the knot" comes from. (What is hand-fasting?)
  * Brides usually toss this to predict who may be getting married next. (What is a bouquet?)
  * This small metal object, often shaped like a ring, is thrown at newlyweds in Ireland instead of rice. (What are coins?)



### Questions About Love Songs

  * "Bridal Chorus" by Richard Wagner is also known by this name. (What is "Here Comes the Bride?")
  * John Legend wrote this now-popular wedding song for his own wife, Chrissy Teigen. (What is "All of Me?")
  * This Whitney Houston ballad, originally sung by Dolly Parton, is one of the best-selling love songs of all time. (What is "I Will Always Love You?")
  * According to Billboard, this song by Diana Ross and Lionel Richie is the most successful love song of all time. (What is "Endless Love?")
  * Paul McCartney dedicated this song to his wife, a track that appeared on his first solo album. (What is "Maybe I'm Amazed?")
  * This artist, who went on to write the music and lyrics for the musical _Waitress_ , had her first big hit with "Love Song." (Who is Sara Bareilles?)
  * Rihanna and Calvin Harris collaborated on this popular 2011 song. (What is "We Found Love?")



### Name That Movie

  * "​​I came here tonight because when you realize you want to spend the rest of your life with somebody, you want the rest of your life to start as soon as possible." (What is _When Harry Met Sally_?)
  * "You have bewitched me body and soul, and I love, I love, I love you." (What is _Pride and Prejudice_?)
  * "In another life, I would have really liked just doing laundry and taxes with you." (What is _Everything Everywhere All at Once_?)
  * "I'll play you."… "For what?"... "Your heart." (What is _Love & Basketball_?)
  * "Thanks for the adventure—now go have a new one." (What is _Up_?)
  * "I would rather spend one lifetime with you than face all the ages of this world alone." (What is _The Lord of the Rings_?)
  * "As you wish." (What is _The Princess Bride_?)
  * "I'm just a girl, standing in front of a boy, asking him to love her." (What is _Notting Hill_?)



### Final Question Ideas

  * These two months are tied for the least popular months for weddings. (What are January and February?)
  * This is the most popular day of the week to get married, with 70% of couples choosing this day. (What is Saturday?)
  * These hues will be the official color palette of the wedding.
  * This will be the first song the couple dances to at their wedding.
  * This is the designer of the bride's wedding dress.
  * The bride's engagement ring is in this cut.
  * This is how many states/countries the couple has visited together.
  * This destination is where the couple will journey after the wedding.
