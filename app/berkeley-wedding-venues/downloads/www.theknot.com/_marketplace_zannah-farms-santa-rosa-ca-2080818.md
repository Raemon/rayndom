Zannah Florals is a wedding florist in Santa Rosa, CA, serving the surrounding areas. Ashley wants to provide you and your partner with natural beauty, creativity, and a soulful connection through flowers on your special day. Zannah Farms opened its doors in 2020 with a beautiful vision for its two acres of rich earth. The skilled team cultivates a diverse array of flowers, lavender, and herbs, with over 20,000 blooms lovingly hand-seeded. Over 150 varieties of plants are growing at Zannah Farms on any given day as they nurture more than 1,000 Californian plants and shrubs. You can further enrich your special day with farm-to-event flowers for every occasion. Zannah Farms offers a range of florals for centerpiece arrangements, bouquets, aisle pieces, wreaths, and so much more. You can customize your order, allowing you to fill your DIY bucket with a selection of your favorite blooms. Zannah Farms has everything you need for a stunning wedding day visual.
