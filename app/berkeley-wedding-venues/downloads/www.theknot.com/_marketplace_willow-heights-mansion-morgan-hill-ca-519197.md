### About this venue

![2026 Best of Weddings Winner](https://media-api.xogrp.com/images/a8d5af98-7fba-4287-a568-695c3d6436e1~rs_90.90.fit)

Thanks to recommendations from couples on The Knot

Located in Morgan Hill, CA, Willow Heights Mansion is nestled on 26 acres of rolling hills and spectacular vineyards. The mansion offers a stunning location for all types of weddings and private events, and offers exclusive access to the property for your event day in order to enjoy an intimate celebration with your loved ones. The stunning private estate boasts 16,000-square-feet of space and can accommodate up to 300 guests. You can exchange your vows at the breathtaking outside ceremony site with rows of rolling hills in the distance. The mansion itself is classically beautiful, offering a number of locations for timeless wedding photos. With marble fireplaces, wrought iron staircases, and a stunning water feature at the entrance. The reception hall is exquisitely elegant, with polished wooden floors and draped ceilings. Wide windows surround the room, allowing light to flood the space and providing an airy ambiance. Getting-ready rooms are available on-site, with plenty of amenities to provide wedding parties with everything they require to prepare themselves for the big day. Overnight accommodations are also available for up to 12 guests. Willow Heights Mansion strives to offer a truly effortless experience for newlyweds-to-be. You'll have access to our wedding planning portal and concierge service throughout your wedding planning process. About a month before your event, one of our Concierge Service Associates will be assigned as your Day-Of Coordinator, and will remain your on-site source of support until the last guest leaves. The coordinator will be on-hand throughout both the rehearsal and wedding, ensuring that everything flows flawlessly. Catering will be provided by our in-house culinary team, who offers seasonal menus made with fresh produce. From organizing linens and glassware to flower arches and centerpieces, our talented LUXE Design & Decor team will ensure your wedding vision comes to life! A one-stop-boutique for all your wedding needs, we also include an in-house DJ for all of your ceremony and reception music and microphone needs. Willow Heights Mansion prides itself on its expertise and expansive knowledge of cultural weddings. From Baraats, to Sofreh Tables, to Tea Ceremonies, there is no limit to what Willow Heights Mansion can organize to give you your dream day.
