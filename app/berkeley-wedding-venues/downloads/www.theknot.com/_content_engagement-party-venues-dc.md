## Taberna del Alabardero in Washington, DC

![Taberna del Alabardero engagement party venue in Washington, DC](https://media-api.xogrp.com/images/8074972f-39a5-4be4-8930-9f476daadbcb~rs_768.h-cr_0.0.1173.880)

The first Spanish restaurant in DC and a fully authentic experience, [Taberna del Alabardero](https://www.theknot.com/marketplace/taberna-del-alabardero-washington-dc-2050150) is a popular DC engagement party location for groups of all sizes. Choose from an intimate library setting for up to eight people or host a large event for up to 140 in the main dining area. In any case, you and your guests will nosh on authentic Spanish cuisine like tapas, paella and sangria.
