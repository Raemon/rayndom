About this vendor

Wedding and Event Florals By Litsa is a wedding florist company based in San Francisco, CA. Owner and lead designer Litsa Siegel takes great joy in serving nearlyweds as they celebrate their joyous day. She and her team craft elegant arrangements using fresh flowers of vibrant hues. Lista Siegel has been cultivating her passion for floral design from a young age. For over a decade, she has specialized in wedding floristry, working closely with couples to help bring their dream events to life. She works diligently to craft one-of-a-kind arrangements that honor her clients’ wishes, ensuring that every detail is to their satisfaction. Wedding and Event Florals By Litsa provides clients with a wide range of offerings to choose from. Products available range from bouquets, boutonnieres, corsages to table centerpieces, altar designs, and aisle decor. Both delivery and setup services will be provided, ensuring that every last petal is carefully placed.
