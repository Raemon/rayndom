ABC Ranch is a wedding venue in Thermal, CA, offering a space where comfort meets convenience. It will provide you and your partner with all the amenities of home as you surround yourselves with luxury. Located 30 minutes from PSP International Airport, ABC Ranch is ideal for long-distance travelers and out-of-town guests. Whether planning a smaller, more intimate family gathering or a lavish, grandiose affair, this peaceful retreat can accommodate up to 600 people. You can choose from several flexible event spaces for your celebration, inside and outside. The venue also boasts comfortable onsite accommodations, so you can stay and explore the area. On the big day, exchange vows on the expansive grassy lawn. While you and your beloved pose for photos, guests can mingle and sip cocktails at the built-in bar by the pool. This space also features a wood deck dance floor and DJ booth for your reception. The award-winning ABC Ranch is celebrated for its exceptional hospitality and unforgettable experiences. Its location allows you and your wedding party to explore some of the nearby attractions, specifically the renowned music festival Coachella. The staff at ABC Ranch will welcome your wedding guests as they arrive and will be by your side throughout the rehearsal dinner or reception, offering their advice and answering any questions you may have. Services include state-of-the-art audiovisual tech, professional event coordination, and a commercial-grade kitchen for onsite catering. At ABC Ranch, you'll have all the indoor and outdoor event space you need for a seamless celebration. Be sure to check out the gallery for an exclusive 360° Virtual Tour!
