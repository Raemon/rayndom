## Rome + Fig in Tampa 

![Rome + Fig engagement party venue in Tampa ](https://media-api.xogrp.com/images/af43a8c8-0c04-4102-beaf-39ebc450c0e8~rs_768.h)

_[Photo: Rome + Fig](https://www.theknot.com/marketplace/rome-and-fig-tampa-fl-2073080)_

[Rome + Fig](https://www.theknot.com/marketplace/rome-and-fig-tampa-fl-2073080) is a beautiful bistro that makes a great spot for a more low-key affair. The restaurant is stylish with a light and airy vibe, and they serve locally sourced and globally inspired dishes—so if really good [engagement party food](https://www.theknot.com/content/engagement-party-food-ideas) is a must, this is a good pick. There's a comfortable indoor space as well as an outdoor patio outfitted with fire pits for a cozy feel and enough space for an intimate party for your closest friends and family members. With room for up to 125 guests, this is also a sweet [bridal shower location in Tampa](https://www.theknot.com/content/bridal-shower-venue-tampa). 

[See This Venue](https://www.theknot.com/marketplace/rome-and-fig-tampa-fl-2073080)

## Big Cat Habitat in Sarasota 

![Big Cat Habitat engagement party in Sarasota ](https://media-api.xogrp.com/images/8b99a8a8-27f0-45d3-86c4-ec2c3a3378a4~rs_768.h-cr_0.51.751.1052)

_[Photo: Big Cat Habitat](https://www.theknot.com/marketplace/big-cat-habitat-sarasota-fl-2073448)_

Animal lovers or couples who want something more unique will love the idea of hosting their engagement party at [Big Cat Habitat](https://www.theknot.com/marketplace/big-cat-habitat-sarasota-fl-2073448). This is a non-profit organization that's home to more than 300 exotic and domestic animals. They also have a beautiful tented outdoor event space for a variety of different events, including engagement parties, and your guests can enjoy the party while also seeing some really cool animals. On top of that, all of the funds from your venue rental go directly to supporting the animals housed there, which is nice to think about. 

[See This Venue](https://www.theknot.com/marketplace/big-cat-habitat-sarasota-fl-2073448)

## Urban Brew and BBQ in Saint Petersburg

There are indoor and outdoor seating options for up to 150 guests (think picnic tables for a really low-key vibe), as well as an area with shuffleboard courts for some fun [engagement party games](https://www.theknot.com/content/engagement-party-games). ## Wild Rover Brewing Company in Tampa

![Wild Rover Brewing Company engagement party venue in Tampa](https://media-api.xogrp.com/images/7c8a6928-9504-4cbb-a775-4a08f09395dd~rs_768.h-cr_89.0.1512.1067)

_[Photo: Wild Rover Brewing Company](https://www.theknot.com/marketplace/wild-rover-brewing-company-tampa-fl-2065776)_

A laid-back vibe and industrial-style decor make up the [Wild Rover Brewing Company,](https://www.theknot.com/marketplace/wild-rover-brewing-company-tampa-fl-2065776) which is a great engagement party place in Tampa for couples who want a nice event that stands out without feeling too formal. The space is a mix of both indoor and outdoor elements, with string lights for a magical feel, games to keep people entertained and a large bar for some craft beers. The brewery production room is great for larger parties of up to 120 guests, while the covered outdoor spaces are better for more intimate events. 

[See This Venue](https://www.theknot.com/marketplace/wild-rover-brewing-company-tampa-fl-2065776)

## GrillSmith in Carrollwood 

![GrillSmith engagement party venue in Carrollwood ](https://media-api.xogrp.com/images/f2ce24c9-9110-4cca-9944-367753f1a802~rs_768.h-cr_0.0.1041.781)

Slightly casual but with a polished vibe, [GrillSmith](https://www.theknot.com/marketplace/grillsmith-clearwater-fl-2085111) has a few locations in Florida and serves up delicious American food. ## Aspirations Winery in Clearwater 

[Aspirations Winery](https://www.theknot.com/marketplace/aspirations-winery-clearwater-fl-2077996) can be found in the heart of Clearwater and has a nice private event space, along with good food and, of course, drinks. It's a functioning winery with local beers and Florida wines—they can even create custom labels for your event for a more personalized feel. You're able to provide your own catering and entertainment if you want to as well, which is nice for those who want to take more control over the planning. 

[See This Venue](https://www.theknot.com/marketplace/aspirations-winery-clearwater-fl-2077996)

## The Vault in Tampa 

![The Vault in Tampa engagement party venue ](https://media-api.xogrp.com/images/1033370b-37fb-44ac-93fa-6795f6bb223b~rs_768.h-cr_0.0.1172.879)

_Photo: The Vault_

Elegant and historical, [The Vault](https://vaulttampacatering.com/tampa-venue-for-wedding/) could be considered a [luxury wedding venue in Tampa](https://www.theknot.com/content/luxury-wedding-venues-florida), but is also a good Tampa engagement party location. It was built in 1919 as the Exchange National Bank and has been redone, featuring 35-foot ceilings, a stunning second-floor mezzanine looking over an open gallery and huge floor-to-ceiling windows for views of the Tampa Bay skyline. You can also rent the outdoor space if you're interested.

![Cross Creek Ranch engagement party venue in Dover](https://media-api.xogrp.com/images/5e0d3ba0-bc0d-4249-95f6-ce2a2685cbe0~rs_768.h-cr_124.0.1831.1280)

Two different event spaces are available: a large barn with rustic-chic decor and a vintage seating area that includes access to a garden and forest, or a smaller hall with greenery-wrapped lighting and wood accents.
