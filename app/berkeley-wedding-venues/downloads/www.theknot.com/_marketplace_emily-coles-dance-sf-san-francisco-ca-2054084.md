Emily Coles

Owner

My dance career began at the age of 5, prancing on stage with little girls in tu-tus. I loved the stage, but not the tu-tu . For 20 years I pranced on stages, searching for the "right dance". The search included modern, jazz, Afro-Cuban, and finally salsa. To salsa I added a full range of ballroom, Latin and social dances, which I have now taught for over 15 years. Anything that creates joy has a positive impact on our complicated world, and that positive impact is important to me. We need more dancing. We need more music, more singing, more laughing. We need more dorking around :)
