Dance Arts Ballroom + Fitness Studio offers dance lessons for weddings and other special events in San Rafael, California. Their dance specialists aim to ensure you feel confident and prepared for your first dance as newlyweds. Dance Arts Ballroom + Fitness Studio features a team of some of the best dance instructors in the industry. They are passionate about the power of dance to bring people together and help make happy everlasting memories. They specialize in various dance styles, including Ballroom, Salsa, Swing, and Tango. They offer a range of bespoke wedding packages that include choreography, coaching, and practice to ensure you nail your first dance. Packages include an initial consultation, detailed private lessons, and practice sessions. The number of lessons and practice sessions varies depending on which package you choose, but all will help you feel like a superstar when you step on the dance floor. However you envision your first dance, they'll work tirelessly to make you and your partner shine.
