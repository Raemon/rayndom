The bridal shower is a time-honored tradition to signify the beginning of a new chapter. From the venue and the guest list to [decorations](https://www.theknot.com/content/bridal-shower-decorations), [food](https://www.theknot.com/content/bridal-shower-food-ideas) and [game ideas](https://www.theknot.com/content/a-complete-guide-to-bridal-shower-games), much care, love and consideration goes into planning this special day. But the sweetest item on your [to-do list](https://www.theknot.com/content/a-bridal-shower-planning-checklist) might be choosing a [baker](https://www.theknot.com/marketplace/wedding-cake-bakeries) to whip up a [beautiful (and yummy) dessert](https://www.theknot.com/content/bridal-shower-cakes). Though you might find it easy to pick flavors, toppings and designs, you might struggle with what to write on a bridal shower cake to wow your bride-to-be. 

[READ MORE The Best and Most Unique Bridal Shower Ideas ](https://www.theknot.com/content/bridal-shower-ideas-we-love)

After all, a bridal shower cake is a chance to make a statement about the collective sentiment endorsing her lifelong union with her mate, says Sarah Chianese, the owner and executive chef at [Mangia and Enjoy!](https://www.mangiaandenjoy.com/)—and the written words on top should also reflect her. "The written cake topper can be humorous or more classic and must be thoughtful and memorable," she says. "The decoration can also reflect and enhance the writing, so get creative with the baker and [devise a visual theme](https://www.theknot.com/content/bridal-shower-themes)."

[READ MORE The Best and Most Unique Bridal Shower Ideas ](https://www.theknot.com/content/bridal-shower-ideas-we-love)

Not sure what to put on a bridal shower cake? No worries! Here, we've rounded up the best bridal shower cake sentiments—for the sweet to the simple, no-fuss bride—that will make her feel seen and loved. Chianese and bridal expert, Lilia Shatnaya, the owner and designer of [Plume and Stone Invitation Studio](https://plumeandstone.com/), recommend these wedding shower cake quotes.

## Simple Bridal Shower Cake Wording

If she doesn't like a lot of fuss about celebrating her love—where you might even call her the opposite of a so-called bridezilla—consider these bridal shower cake messages, perfect for the simple bride who prefers to keep everything harmonious and beautiful.

## Funny Bridal Shower Cake Wording

When the bride was single and dating, she somehow found the humor in even the worst dates. She's the first to cheer up her friends—and always has the last laugh. Now, you can return some of the puns and fun with these hilarious bridal shower cake sayings.

  1. You've Been Promoted From Bridesmaid to Bride
  2. Off the Market! ✔️
  3. S*** Just Got Real!
  4. Who's Going to Be Our Wingwoman Now?
  5. [Bride's Partner's Name] Put a Ring On It
  6. To the Girl Who Keeps Us Laughing (And Loving)
  7. [Bride's Name] Has a Type & It's [Bride's Partner's Name]
  8. Team OMG She's a Bride!
  9. About Damn Time
  10. The One Where [Bride's Name] Gets Married

## Sweet Bridal Shower Cake Wording

What do you write on a bridal shower cake for a bride who's been dreaming of her wedding long before she met her partner? Someone who, along the way, has celebrated everyone else in her life with kindness, thoughtfulness and love. Make her [fairytale visions](https://www.theknot.com/content/disney-bridal-shower) come true with these bridal cake messages that are sweeter than frosting.

  1. [Bride's Name] Is in Love—and It Shows
  2. Happily Ever After
  3. Your Love Is Sweeter Than This Cake
  4. Sweet Beginnings for the Sweetest Love
  5. Dreams Do Come True
  6. Your Happily Ever After Starts Now
  7. Love Is in the Air
  8. Forever and a Day
  9. Forever and Ever



## Classic Bridal Shower Cake Wording

Is your bride following all of the traditions—with her own spin—and keeping everything polished and elegant? These classic sayings for a bridal shower cake will make her feel like every detail was well-thought-out, from the flavor to the sentiment and theme.

  2. [Bride's Name] Is a BRIDE!
  3. Two Hearts, One Spirit
