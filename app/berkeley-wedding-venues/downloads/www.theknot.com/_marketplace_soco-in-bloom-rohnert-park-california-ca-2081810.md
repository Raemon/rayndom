SoCo In Bloom is a full-service florist in Sonoma County CA, catering to weddings and other special events in the Sonoma, Mendocino and Lake Counties. Owner and lead floral designer Tina Chiotti-Stewart is passionate about flowers, weddings, and sourcing products from local farms whenever possible. With over 25 years of wedding and event experience, SoCo In Bloom has provided exceptional customer service to each one of its unique clients. SoCo In Bloom will show you and your partner why floristry is the art of the heart as you choose your perfect wedding day arrangements. Designing with Sonoma County-grown flowers whenever possible, in-season blooms last longer and come in beautiful summertime soft, bright, and lively colors to the warm hues of fall.
