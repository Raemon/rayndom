### About this vendor

Skilled Songwriter Songs By Faith is a wedding musician and songwriter based in Hercules, CA. Faith Luster has been featured on numerous recordings from coast to coast and would be delighted to offer her services to make your big day even more special. Captivating Jazz Style Faith minored in jazz voice at the illustrious Howard University in Washington, DC, where she honed her talents and marked the start of her writing career. Having shared the stage with some of the industry's most iconic voices, from Diana Ross to Anita Baker, she can create an unforgettable ambiance that elevates any occasion. You can see her deep appreciation for jazz in her work, and she draws inspiration from many legendary artists like Sarah Vaughn, John Coltrane, Betty Carter, and Ella Fitzgerald. Immerse Yourself in the Magic of Music Songs By Faith adapts a unique style that skillfully fuses the emotive rhythms of Gospel with the soulful grace of jazz. Faith's aim is to curate a musical experience for those who want one that both soothes and captivates. Reach out today for more information about Faith's wedding services!
