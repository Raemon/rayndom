## Mother-of-the-Groom Dress Shopping Tips

As the mother of the groom, finding the perfect dress for your child's wedding can be a bit overwhelming at times. You want to look your stylish best while still being comfortable enough to celebrate the occasion. Keep reading to see Rockmore's best tips for finding a dress you're sure to love. ### Color Yourself Gorgeous

While you can't go wrong with classic mother-of-the-groom dress colors like navy, black or burgundy, don't feel restricted by these choices. "When shopping for your special dress, look for your favorite color, so you always look fabulous and feel confident wearing it," Rockmore suggests. "Find a color that complements your eyes so that minimal makeup is required. Happy tears are coming."

### Consider Your Shoes

"Wear comfortable shoes so you can dance a lot and enjoy yourself!" Rockmore says. Just ensure your mother-of-the-groom shoes balance style with comfort, especially if your son is getting married outdoors or in more "rugged" terrain (think: ranches, mountain venues or barns). "When shopping for heels, always look for a platform to ensure comfort. There's also nothing wrong with wearing flats," she adds.
