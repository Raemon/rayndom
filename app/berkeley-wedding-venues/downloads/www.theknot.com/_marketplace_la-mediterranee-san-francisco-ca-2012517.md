About this vendor

Catering to Bay Area couples since 1979, La Mediterranee is proud to be known as the top Mediterranean caterer in the San Francisco Bay Area. Our three restaurants & catering kitchen have consistently won "Best Mediterranean" in both the SF Weekly & SF Examiner Reader's Poll year after year. What draws our clients to us is our delicious food. What impresses them is our exceptional service! From planning to execution, we dedicate ourselves to making your special day flawless & beautiful! Whether your celebration is an intimate backyard gathering or an impressive family style meal for 200, La Mediterranee has the experience, capability, and expertise to truly impress you and your guests through great food, friendly service, & beautiful presentation! Anoush Ella! (May it be Sweet)
