# The Best Mother-Daughter Songs to Celebrate Your Special Bond

From '70s classics to modern hits from Beyoncé and Taylor Swift, these songs showcase the special bond between mom and daughter.

Of all the dances at your wedding, the one with your mom carries its own kind of magic, right alongside the moments you'll share with your new spouse, your dad and other beloved friends and family members. With that in mind, it's safe to say that the best mother-daughter songs don't just set the tempo. Like any of the most popular wedding tunes, they set the mood, spark old memories (and create new ones) and showcase the special bond you share.

## 1\. "Growin' Up Raising You," by Gabby Barrett 

**Lyrics you'll love:** "Hand me the world, wrapped in a pink blanket/The world falls asleep on my chest/I'm so in love even though we just met/Got no clue what I should do next/We'll get it wrong, we'll get it right/Whole lotta tears we both have to cry/You'll learn to run, I'll learn to let go/Years'll go fast, nights'll go slow/Lord knows the best thing that I'll ever do/Will be growin' up raisin' you"

"Growin' Up Raising You" flips the traditional parent-child narrative by portraying motherhood as a two-way street where both mom and daughter learn and grow alongside each other. The raw storytelling and heartfelt vocals, paired with Barrett's emotional delivery, give the song an authenticity that makes it especially meaningful for a mother-daughter dance at your wedding.

## 2\. "Mama Liked the Roses," by Elvis Presley 

**Lyrics you'll love:** "Oh, mama liked the roses/but most of all she cared/About the way we learned to live/And if we said our prayers"

One of the most underrated[ Elvis songs for a wedding](https://www.theknot.com/content/elvis-wedding-songs), "Mama Liked the Roses," makes a poignant pick for a tender mom and daughter dance. Its heartfelt blend of nostalgia and love naturally lends itself to special memories made on the dance floor.

## 3\. "Mother," by Sugarland

**Lyrics you'll love:** She's your mother, you love her/There won't be another/place like her again that you call home/She stands here to help you/ there's nothing she won't do/As long as she's alive/you're not alone/you've got each other/That's your mother

A classic country ballad, "Mother" by Sugarland, uses heartfelt vocals and gentle instrumentals to emphasize the bond between mother and child. It's an emotional tribute that resonates beautifully in a wedding setting.

## 4\. "Sweet Child O' Mine," by Sheryl Crow 

**Lyrics you'll love:** "Sweet child o' mine"

Sheryl Crow's cover of the Guns N' Roses classic "Sweet Child O' Mine" transforms the rock anthem into a mellow, acoustic rendition with her signature soulful vocals. The stripped-down arrangement gives the song a nostalgic, tender quality.

## 5\. "Stand by Me," by Ben E. King

**Lyrics you'll love:** "So darlin', darlin'/Stand by me"

This time-honored [top wedding song](https://www.theknot.com/content/billboard-most-popular-wedding-songs) pairs soulful vocals with a steady groove for swaying away on the dance floor. Meanwhile, its enduring message of support and togetherness makes it especially fitting for a memorable mother-daughter moment.

## 6\. "In My Life," by The Beatles

**Lyrics you'll love:** "In my life, I love you more"

While not a traditional mom-daughter song, "In My Life" by The Beatles carries a reflective tone that honors the people who shape us most—including, of course, our mothers. Its gentle melody and nostalgic lyrics turn the dance into a profoundly personal moment, and we can almost guarantee there won't be a dry eye in the house once the chorus plays.

## 7\. "Kind and Generous," by Natalie Merchant

**Lyrics you'll love:** "You've been so kind and generous/I don't know how you keep on giving/For your kindness I'm in debt to you/And I never could have come this far without you/For everything you've done, you know I'm bound/I'm bound to thank you for it"

As you pick out [wedding songs for your whole event](https://www.theknot.com/content/best-wedding-songs), consider Natalie Merchant's "Kind and Generous" for a mother-daughter dance that feels just as joyful as it is sentimental. With its infectious energy and words of appreciation, it's a fun way to pay homage to everything Mom has—and continues to—do, making it one of the most upbeat mother-daughter songs on this list.

## 8\. "Child of Mine," by Carole King

**Lyrics you'll love:** "Oh, yes, sweet darling/So glad you are a child of mine"

Carole King's "Child of Mine" pairs tender verses with a catchy, singable chorus that feels instantly familiar. Overall, it's a sweet, reassuring song for celebrating the bond between mother and daughter.

## 9\. "You Are the Sunshine of My Life," by Stevie Wonder

**Lyrics you'll love:** "You are the sunshine of my life/That's why I'll always be around/You are the apple of my eye/Forever you'll stay in my heart"

A 1970s classic, Stevie Wonder's "You Are the Sunshine of My Life" practically bursts with joyful energy, carried by Wonder's hallmark soulful vocals. Its upbeat melody and sincere lyrics make it a sunny, feel-good song choice.

## 10\. "I'll Stand by You," by The Pretenders

**Lyrics you'll love:** "Nothing you confess/Could make me love you less/I'll stand by you/I'll stand by you/Won't let nobody hurt you/I'll stand by you"

Released back in the '90s, "I'll Stand by You" by The Pretenders quickly became a wedding favorite. Decades later, its message of unconditional love and simply being there for one another remains a go-to choice for a mother and daughter song.

## 11\. "My Girl," by the Temptations

**Lyrics you'll love:** "I've got sunshine on a cloudy day/When it's cold outside/I've got the month of May/I guess you'd say/What can make me feel this way?/My girl (my girl, my girl)"

A Motown staple for a reason, you can't help but dance along to "My Girl." With its easy groove and lyrics everyone knows by heart, it's a fun, laid-back pick for a mother-daughter dance.

## 12\. "Stand by You," by Rachel Platten 

**Lyrics you'll love:** "I'm gonna stand by you"

Not to be confused with The Pretenders' classic mentioned earlier, Rachel Platten's "Stand by You" has a catchy pop sound that's hard not to sing along with. It's a great choice if you want your mother-daughter dance to feel upbeat and playful, rather than overly emotional and tear-jerking.

## 13\. "Count on Me," by Bruno Mars

**Lyrics you'll love:** "You can count on me like one, two, three, I'll be there"

Bruno Mars' "Count on Me" is an easygoing song that works well for moms and daughters who share more of a best-friends vibe. The ukulele instrumentals and catchy chorus keep things unfussy and relaxed so that you can truly let loose and enjoy the special moment.

## 14\. "Never Grow Up," by Taylor Swift

**Lyrics you'll love:** "Your little hand's wrapped around my finger/And it's so quiet in the world tonight/Your little eyelids flutter 'cause you're dreamin'/So I tuck you in, turn on your favorite night light/To you, everything's funny/You got nothing to regret/I'd give all I have honey/If you could stay like that"

Taylor Swift always knows how to tug at the heartstrings. Her song, "Never Grow Up," is a soft, acoustic ballad that captures the bittersweet feeling of watching a child grow. Its tender lyrics and stripped-down sound make it an emotional choice for a dance song for a mother and daughter.

## 15\. "Protector," by Beyoncé

**Lyrics you'll love:** "And I will lead you down that road if you lose your way/Born to be your protector, 'tor/Even though I know someday you're gonna shine on your own/I will be your protector"

"Protector" by Beyoncé has an incredibly intimate feel, with stripped-back production that puts the focus on her voice and the words. The song even opens with her daughter Rumi asking for a lullaby, which makes its promise of lifelong love and protection feel especially fitting for a mother-daughter dance.

## 16\. "When I Look at You," by Miley Cyrus

**Lyrics you'll love:** "Yeah, when my world is falling apart/When there's no light to break up the dark/That's when I, I/I look at you"

Talk about a blast from the past: You may remember this emotional Miley Cyrus track from her 2010 movie, _The Last Song,_ which gives it a nostalgic pull for anyone who grew up in the late 2000s. Its poetic lyrics, which convey finding strength and light in dark moments, make it an even more powerful choice for a mother-daughter dance.

## 17\. "Your Song," by Elton John 

**Lyrics you'll love:** "I hope you don't mind, I hope you don't mind/That I put down in words/How wonderful life is/While you're in the world"

Elton John's "Your Song" is a 1970s classic that has been captivating listeners across generations. It feels personal enough to be anyone's song—yes, really—and the piano melody paired with tender lyrics gives it a natural warmth that makes it a lovely choice for the[ mother of the bride](https://www.theknot.com/content/mother-of-the-bride-duties) and her daughter.

## 18\. "Bridge Over Troubled Water," by Simon & Garfunkel

**Lyrics you'll love:** "Like a bridge over troubled water/I will lay me down/Like a bridge over troubled water/I will lay me down"

Released in 1970, Simon & Garfunkel's "Bridge Over Troubled Water" is a sweeping ballad with gospel influences and one of the duo's most iconic vocal performances. Its message of unconditional support makes it a less traditional—albeit just as exceptional—choice for a mother-daughter dance.

## 19\. "A Mother Like You," by JJ Heller 

**Lyrics you'll love: "** You gave me the best of your heart/And you carried my rocks in your pocket/Flowers and feathers and butterfly wings/You had an answer for all of my questions/You showed me wonderful things/I know that it hasn't been easy/I hope that I love like you do/'Cause I know that this world would be better/If everyone had a mother like you"

When it comes to sentimental songs for a mother-daughter dance, few strike the perfect chord like "Mother Like You." This tender, heartfelt ballad beautifully honors the love, guidance and sacrifices a mother makes for her child(ren). Together, these qualities make it a touching choice for a mother-daughter dance at a wedding.

## 20\. "I Hope You Dance," by Lee Ann Womack

**Lyrics you'll love** : "I hope you never fear those mountains in the distance/Never settle for the path of least resistance/Livin' might mean takin' chances, but they're worth takin'"

This Lee Ann Womack song—which has since become an iconic country-pop smash hit—has remained a wedding playlist staple since its 2000 release. When it comes to wedding reception songs, this one perfectly conveys a mother's wishes for her child by encouraging them to take risks, embrace love and live life to the fullest.

## 21\. "In My Daughter's Eyes," by Martina McBride

**Lyrics you'll love** : "'I've seen the light/It's in my daughter's eyes

Martina McBride's "In My Daughter's Eyes" is a country music song from 2003. Throughout the track, McBride sings sweetly about the strong, everlasting bond between a mother and a daughter.

## 22\. "Forever Young," by Rod Stewart

**Lyrics you'll love:** "And when you finally fly away, I'll be hoping that I served you well/For all the wisdom of a lifetime, no one can ever tell/But whatever road you choose, I'm right behind you win or lose"

## 23\. "I'll Be There," by The Jackson Five

**Lyrics you'll love** : "I'll reach out my hand to you/I'll have faith in all you do/Just call my name, and I'll be there"

This 1970 number combines pop, Motown and soul flavors. Its lyrics, meanwhile, convey unconditional love and support, making it a good mother-daughter song.

## 24\. "Like My Mother Does," by Lauren Alaina

**Lyrics you'll love:** "When I'm weak and unpretty/I know I'm beautiful and strong/Because I see myself like my mother does"

"Like My Mother Does" was released in 2011 by country singer Lauren Alaina. The tune is a tear-jerker thanks to its uplifting lyrics, in which Alaina sings about how much her mother inspires her (i.e., "She's a rock/She is grace/She's an angel/She's my heart and soul").

## 25\. "Mom," by Meghan Trainor (feat. Kelli Trainor)

**Lyrics you'll love** : "Her love's 'til the end, she's my best friend/Ain't nobody got a mom like mine"

This cheery number features a phone call between Meghan Trainor and her mother, Kelli— hence why she is listed as a featured artist in the song. According to Trainor, her producer advised her to call her mom and record the conversation. The result? A cheerful tune, complete with sentimental lyrics to match.

## 26\. "Slipping Through My Fingers," by ABBA

**Lyrics you'll love:** "Sometimes I wish that I could freeze the picture/And save it from the funny tricks of time/Slipping through my fingers"

When it comes to mother-daughter wedding songs, "Slipping Through My Fingers" by ABBA is sure to evoke emotion with its heart-tugging lyrics, in which a mother reminisces about her daughter's earlier years while also acknowledging how quickly time is passing. Additionally, the song was performed by Meryl Streep and Amanda Seyfried in _Mamma Mia!_ (2008). Just make sure to talk repertoire with your[ wedding musicians](https://www.theknot.com/marketplace/wedding-soloists-ensembles) before making your selection!

## 27\. "Because You Loved Me" by Celine Dion

**Lyrics you'll love:** "You were my strength when I was weak/You were my voice when I couldn't speak/You were my eyes when I couldn't see/You saw the best there was in me/Lifted me up when I couldn't reach/You gave me faith 'cause you believed/I'm everything I am/Because you loved me"

This song beautifully describes a mom who supports her daughter through good times and bad (and your wedding-planning journey!). If these lyrics ring true to your relationship with Mom, this Celine Dion classic is an ideal choice as a mother-daughter wedding song.

## 28\. "The Best Day," by Taylor Swift

**Lyrics you'll love:** "Now I know why all the trees change in the fall/I know you were on my side/Even when I was wrong/And I love you for giving me your eyes/For staying back and watching me shine and I didn't know if you knew/So I'm taking this chance to say/That I had the best day with you today"

This nostalgia-inducing Taylor Swift track is sure to make your mother smile. In the song, Swift recounts multiple moments throughout her early childhood and adolescent years in which her mother has loved, helped and supported her. 

## 29\. "Wind Beneath My Wings," by Bette Midler

**Lyrics you'll love:** "Did you ever know that you're my hero/And everything I would like to be?/I can fly higher than an eagle/For you are the wind beneath my wings"

"Wind Beneath My Wings" was first written and recorded in 1982, but it wasn't until 1989, when Bette Midler's version was released, that the song truly skyrocketed in popularity. Midler's heartfelt rendition offers a distinct pop flavor and a sweet, melodic sound. The lyrics work perfectly as a mother-daughter dance song thanks to its lovely message.

## 30\. "Where You Lead, I Will Follow," by Carole King

**Lyrics you'll love:** "If you need, you need me to be with you/I will follow where you lead/If you're out on the road/Feeling lonely, and so cold/All you have to do is call my name/And I'll be there on the next train"

Carole King wrote "Where You Lead, I Will Follow" back in 1970. The song was featured on her 1971 album, _Tapestry_. Fittingly enough, it also serves as the theme song for _Gilmore Girls_. (For the TV show, King rerecorded the song with her own daughter, and it makes a beautiful duet.) When it comes to sentimental mother-daughter dance songs, this one takes the cake—especially if you're a fan of the series.

## 31\. "A Song For Mama," by Boyz II Men

**Lyrics you'll love:** "You were there for me to love and care for me/When skies were gray/Whenever I was down/You were always there to comfort me"

Released in 1997, "A Song For Mama" is one of Boyz II Men's most popular songs. The R&B single boasts poetic lyrics, such as "Mama, you're the queen of my heart," making it a great pick for your mom-daughter wedding song. It's also a popular choice for a[ mother-son dance song](https://www.theknot.com/content/mother-son-wedding-songs).

## 32\. "You're My Best Friend," by Queen

**Lyrics you'll love:** "Ooh, you make me live/Whenever this world is cruel to me/I got you to help me forgive"

If you think of your mom as your best friend, this Queen song serves as a fitting tribute. Its catchy melody and enduring lyrics make it a timeless song choice for your mother-daughter dance.

## 33\. "Mother Like Mine," by The Band Perry

**Lyrics you'll love:** "We'd walk on grass that's greener/And the dishes would all be cleaner/If the world had a mother like mine"

"Mother Like Mine" is an endearing, emotional mother-daughter country song by The Band Perry, comprised of siblings Kimberly, Reid and Neil. Kimberly told[ _Billboard_](https://www.billboard.com/articles/columns/the-615/1554915/the-band-perry-mother-like-mine-exclusive-song-premiere) it was "the most honest song" that the trio wrote for their 2013 album, _Pioneer_. She continued, "Our mother is our personal hero. We are her labor of love. It's our honor to our mother on _Pioneer_."

## 34\. "Somebody's Hero," by Jamie O'Neal

**Lyrics you'll love:** "For twenty years there at home/Until the day her girl was grown/Giving all her love to her was her life's ambition/But now her baby's movin' on and she'll soon be missin' her/But not today/Those are tears of joy runnin' down her face"

Jamie O'Neal wrote and recorded "Somebody's Hero" after being inspired by her own little girl. The song is a moving and emotional ode to all mothers and maternal figures, who—as we already know—are superheroes in their own right.

## 35\. "Mama's Song," by Carrie Underwood

**Lyrics you'll love:** "You've given me everything that I will need/To make it through this crazy thing called life"

In the ballad, Underwood thanks her mother for all she has done for her. It's also perfect for your wedding playlist, as Underwood reassures her mother that she will be okay as she continues to plan for her future and (eventually) gets married. "Giving me away is not goodbye," she sings, before continuing: "As you watch me walk down to my future/I hope tears of joy are in your eyes."

## 36\. "Mama," by Spice Girls

**Lyrics you'll love** : "Back then, I didn't know why/Why you were misunderstood/So now, I see through your eyes/All that you did was love"

Who doesn't love the Spice Girls? The girl group's empowering "Mama"—a pop ballad featuring keyboards, guitar and string instrumentals—chronicles the emotional yet beautiful bond between mothers and daughters.

## 37\. "Isn't She Lovely?" by Stevie Wonder

**Lyrics you'll love:** "Isn't she lovely?/Isn't she wonderful?/Isn't she precious?"

You may think of this Stevie Wonder hit as your classic romantic love song, but the singer actually wrote and recorded it in celebration of the birth of his daughter, Aisha Morris. Throughout the song, Wonder gushes over his then-baby daughter, declaring "I never thought through love we'd be/Making one as lovely as she/But isn't she lovely made from love?" This is also a popular choice for a father-daughter dance song.

## 38\. "I Turn To You," by Christina Aguilera

**Lyrics you'll love** : "When I'm scared, losing ground, when my world is going crazy/You can turn it all around/And when I'm down you're there pushing me to the top/You're always there givin' me all you've got"

Christina Aguilera boosted "I Turn To You" to fame following her 2002 rendition of the emotional ballad. Its stunning lyrics, which depict mothers as a loving and rock-solid support system, make it a must-play at your wedding.

## 39\. "Sweetest Devotion," by Adele

**Lyrics you'll love** : "I'll forever be whatever you want me to be/I'll go under and all over for your clarity/When you wonder if I'm gonna lose my way home/Just remember, that come whatever, I'll be yours all alone"

Adele cowrote this song about her son, so it's a perfect choice for a mother and daughter dance for a wedding, as well. The loving lyrics exemplify the bond between parents and children.

## 40\. "There You'll Be," by Faith Hill

**Lyrics you'll love:** "When I look back on these days/I look and see your face/You were right there for me"

Faith Hill's 2001 hit is particularly fitting for a mom-daughter dance thanks to its poignant lyrics. Consider playing this tender tune in celebration of the love and support shared between you and your mother.

## 41\. "A Mother's Prayer," by Celine Dion

**Lyrics you'll love** : "Every mother's prayer/Every child knows/Lead her to a place/Guide her with your grace/To a place where she'll be safe/I pray she finds your light/And holds it in her heart/As darkness falls each night/Remind her where you are"

Celine Dion reworked her famous song, "The Prayer"—a stunning duet with Andrea Bocelli— to "A Mother's Prayer" on her 2004 album, _Miracle_. The song was adapted into a solo, featuring Dion's signature vocals. Throughout the tune, Dion sings of her hopes for her children—eternal love and peace.

## 42\. "Landslide," by Fleetwood Mac

**Lyrics you'll love:** "Well, I've been 'fraid of changin'/'Cause I've built my life around you/But time makes you bolder/Even children get older/And I'm gettin' older, too"

This song's lyrics capture the passage of time and the bittersweet nature of change, themes that resonate strongly for an emotional mother-daughter dance. The lines about building a life around someone and growing older together reflect the deep emotional bond between mother and daughter, making it a heartfelt mother and daughter wedding song to play at your wedding—not to mention it's considered one of the very best[ Fleetwood Mac love songs](https://www.theknot.com/content/fleetwood-mac-wedding-songs) of all time. 

## 43\. "II MOST WANTED," by Beyoncé, Miley Cyrus

**Lyrics you'll love:** "I'll be your shotgun rider 'til the day I die/Smoke out the window flyin' down the 405/And I'll be your backseat baby/Drivin' you crazy anytime you like"

With lyrics about being a constant companion and a "shotgun rider," this mother-daughter dance song symbolizes unwavering support and shared adventures. It's a new song from 2024 that'll create a fun and modern atmosphere for the dance, all while celebrating the special friendship and unconditional love between a mother and her daughter.
