### About this vendor

PRADO FLOWERS is a San Francisco, CA-based wedding florist that offers its services throughout the Bay Area in addition to Miami and Mexico. At Prado Flowers, we believe every love story deserves to be told in petals, color, and unforgettable beauty. Our wedding designs are not just arrangements — they’re artful expressions of the deep emotions that make your day truly one of a kind. Based in the heart of San Francisco and blooming far beyond, we create floral experiences for couples throughout the Bay Area. Whether you're exchanging vows on a windswept beach, in a historic estate, or under the stars in your own backyard, our team will be there to transform your vision into a breathtaking reality. We pour our hearts into every celebration we’re a part of, which is why we accept only a select number of weddings each season. This ensures that each couple receives our full attention, care, and the creative devotion your day so richly deserves. From your first consultation to the moment you walk down the aisle, we’re by your side — dreaming with you, designing for you, and celebrating what makes your love story uniquely yours. Now booking for the 2025 wedding season. To begin your journey with us, reach out to schedule a consultation. We’d be honored to be part of your most meaningful day.
