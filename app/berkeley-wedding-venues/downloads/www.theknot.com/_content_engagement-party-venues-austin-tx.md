If you're [just engaged](https://www.theknot.com/content/wedding-planning-tips-to-get-organized), you probably have a lot of planning you need to start thinking about. Putting together an engagement party might be at the top of your to-do list, and if you're a Texas resident, you may be looking for engagement party venues in Austin. Whether you want to celebrate your upcoming nuptials with a small and intimate event or a big and boisterous party, Austin has a venue that's going to be a good fit for you and your partner. 

[READ MORE Find the Ideal Austin Engagement Party Venue ](https://www.theknot.com/marketplace/rehearsal-dinners-bridal-showers-austin-tx)

Since Texas typically boasts pretty great weather (think lots of sunshine and warm temperatures), you may want to take advantage of that and look for outdoor [engagement party venues](https://www.theknot.com/content/engagement-party-venues) or ones that incorporate some sort of outdoor space. Austin offers an array of large lakes, and there are plenty of waterfront venues that feature gorgeous views. The city is also known for its bustling dining scene, so a spot that has great food and drinks is a must. 

[READ MORE Find the Ideal Austin Engagement Party Venue ](https://www.theknot.com/marketplace/rehearsal-dinners-bridal-showers-austin-tx)

[Engagement party planning](https://www.theknot.com/content/engagement-parties-411) can be stressful, but picking out a venue that fits your theme and vision can help make everything else feel like it's falling into place. The below list features a variety of great spots, such as breweries for something more casual, fancy [small wedding venues in Austin](https://www.theknot.com/content/small-wedding-venues-long-island) for something more formal and [romantic Austin restaurants](https://www.theknot.com/content/most-romantic-restaurants-austin) for a low-key party. Read on for some of the best spots available, and check out the [Austin engagement party venues](https://www.theknot.com/marketplace/rehearsal-dinners-bridal-showers-austin-tx) section of The Knot Vendor Marketplace for more options.

## The Rackhouse at Desert Door in Driftwood 

![The Rackhouse at Desert Door engagement party venue in Driftwood](https://media-api.xogrp.com/images/ea59cebd-5ec1-4e53-addc-4de5a55cad6f~rs_768.h-cr_0.312.2000.1812)

_[Photo: The Rackhouse at Desert Door](https://www.theknot.com/marketplace/the-rackhouse-at-desert-door-driftwood-tx-2047514)_

This popular event spot features an open, airy space with a desert-inspired feel. High, 20-foot ceilings and wood fixtures give [The Rackhouse at Desert Door](https://www.theknot.com/marketplace/the-rackhouse-at-desert-door-driftwood-tx-2047514) a modern farmhouse vibe that is great for couples looking for a more low-key, bohemian [engagement party theme](https://www.theknot.com/content/engagement-party-theme-ideas) that still feels special. It has a beautiful 28-foot-long bar, and the flexible rental options allow you to book both spaces (the main room and the patio) for a party of up to 75 guests or just one for something more intimate. 

[See This Venue](https://www.theknot.com/marketplace/the-rackhouse-at-desert-door-driftwood-tx-2047514)

## Oasis Texas Brewing Company in Lake Travis

![Oasis Texas Brewing Company engagement party venue in Lake Travis, Texas](https://media-api.xogrp.com/images/bda2d35a-ecb1-4590-8c25-b9fa4a146e63~rs_768.h-cr_0.0.1285.964)

_[Photo: Oasis Texas Brewing Company](https://www.theknot.com/marketplace/oasis-texas-brewing-company-austin-tx-2080769)_

Open, breezy and more relaxed, the [Oasis Texas Brewing Company](https://www.theknot.com/marketplace/oasis-texas-brewing-company-austin-tx-2080769) is a great choice for laid-back couples who want an engagement party in Austin that feels special but not too similar to a wedding. It's located on Lake Travis, so it offers some great views of the water, as well as a beautiful look at the sunset. Elevated bar food and an array of craft beers and cocktails are something guests will definitely enjoy, and there are three different event spaces to choose between to make the party truly your own. Even better? Pets are allowed too. 

[See This Venue](https://www.theknot.com/marketplace/oasis-texas-brewing-company-austin-tx-2080769)
