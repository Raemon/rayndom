The city of Las Vegas has captured the imagination of millions like no other. From casinos to shrimp cocktails to Elvis impersonators, this place has it all. And the ease with which you can get married here makes it a dream destination for the spontaneous couple.

### Finding Your Las Vegas Wedding Venue

As the Marriage Capital of the World, it's hard not to find what you're looking for. For starters, the chapels in the city are in a league of their own, with many of them offering themes such as Hawaiian, Star Trek, Halloween, and more for weddings. But it would be remiss if one only thought of it as the go-to place for quirky and quick weddings. Home to some of the most phenomenal national parks, including Red Rock Canyon and Antelope Canyon, this desert oasis can be an inspiring setting for your celebrations.

### Activities in Las Vegas

With casinos and resorts aplenty, the Entertainment Capital of the World will not disappoint. The nightlife is also something to experience, and you are sure to leave with hazy memories. If you are more of a nature enthusiast, there are several national parks that you can visit here. Whether you'd like to hike, kayak, or climb, there is something for everyone. You can even hire a helicopter and enjoy the sweeping vistas from the sky.

### FAQs

#### How can I apply for a marriage license in Las Vegas?

You can apply online for a marriage license in Las Vegas. Once you submit your online application, you will get a confirmation number. Bring this to the Clark County Marriage License Bureau. No appointment is necessary and it will take less than an hour to get everything sorted!

#### Can I just walk into a Las Vegas chapel to get married?

Yes, you can just walk into most Las Vegas chapels to get married! Some even permit drive-through weddings.

#### Is the Marriage License Bureau open on Sundays?

Yes, the Clark County Marriage License Bureau is open year-round, from 8 a.m. to midnight.
