## Good Neighbor in Hampden

![Outdoor event space with large art walls](https://media-api.xogrp.com/images/a5356658-1b4c-4e44-9a04-9f7707a2c51a~rs_768.h-cr_20.0.620.800)

_[Photo: Good Neighbor](https://www.theknot.com/marketplace/good-neighbor-baltimore-md-2089691)_

This unique event space in the Hampden neighborhood makes a beautiful backdrop for any type of event. Priding itself on being a neutral canvas that can accommodate just about any type of [engagement party decorations](https://www.theknot.com/content/engagement-party-decorations), [Good Neighbor](https://www.theknot.com/marketplace/good-neighbor-baltimore-md-2089691) offers several different spaces. The shop allows for intimate dining with a small gathering, while the patio offers a beautiful outdoor space that can host your event in good weather. Looking for something funkier? Reserve the design garage for a modern, industrial feel your guests won't soon forget. Regardless of the space you choose, staff will help you secure the perfect [engagement party food](https://www.theknot.com/content/engagement-party-food-ideas), drinks and rentals for your event.
