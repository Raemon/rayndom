## Finding a Florist

Advice From The Knot

### Start With Your Style

Before you look for florists, think about what kind of flowers you like. Do you prefer large centerpieces or smaller arrangements?

The best florists will help you figure out flowers and decor that fit your wedding style and are in season.

### Understand the Options

Know the style you like? Here are some things to consider before reaching out to florists.

Think about which events you’ll want floral arrangements for and if you’ll need other decor like an aisle runner or votives.

### Start a Conversation

Found florists you’re interested in? Reach out! Share your style and see if any are the right fit.

Try The Knot’s “Request Quote” feature. It helps you know what info to share with florists so you can easily get custom quotes.
