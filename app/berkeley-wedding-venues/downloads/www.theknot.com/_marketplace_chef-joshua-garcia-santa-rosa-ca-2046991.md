About this vendor

Chef Joshua Garcia is a boutique, chef-owned wedding catering business in Santa Rosa, CA, specializing in romantic and gourmet dining experiences. Based in the heart of wine country, the company’s bespoke cuisine reflects the rich and indulgent nature of its counterpart. Couples are sure to delight in the caterer’s farm-to-table approach, ensuring each dish is created with high-quality and locally sourced ingredients. After working with several high-profile culinary teams, Joshua Garcia established a private catering company, allowing him to connect with patrons more intimately. Inspired by the unique and diverse elements of the world’s cuisines, chef Joshua is skilled in French, Italian, Asian fusion, and American cooking styles. His flair for flavor is complemented by a keen eye for design and presentation, drawing in the senses from every direction. Chef Joshua Garcia offers several catering packages for intimate gatherings and wedding occasions. Couples can enjoy the luxury of gourmet cooking for brunch, cocktail hour, and reception, with both family-style serving and plated dinners available. Additional services include wine pairings and private lessons in traditional Japanese wet stone sharpening and butchery knife skills.
