### About this vendor

![Vendor](https://media.xogrp.com/images/155478bb-9dbd-451c-b1f3-b989305824ff)

Anthony Schreier

OWNER

Message Vendor

Soirée Society LLC is an elevated Bay Area mobile bar service based in San Bruno, CA, bringing curated cocktails, exceptional hospitality, and unforgettable vibes to weddings and private events. We specialize in creating a bespoke bar experience tailored to your celebration, whether it's a garden ceremony, city rooftop reception, or elegant estate affair. Our team of certified and insured bartenders not only crafts exceptional drinks but also adds style and energy to your event. With a passion for detail and guest experience, we will help you toast to forever. What sets Soirée Society apart is our obsession with quality and detail. We’re a premium dry hire bar service, meaning you provide the alcohol, and we elevate everything else, from freshly squeezed citrus and house-made syrups to signature cocktails that rival top-tier cocktail bars. Our drinks aren’t just well-made, they’re balanced, beautiful, and memorable. While others pour basic mixes, we serve thoughtful creations with style and professionalism.
