About this vendor

Hi! My name is Chelsea and I am a certified professional Makeup Artist serving Mendo, Lake and Sonoma County, CA. I come from a family of creative talent and have touched on many different artistic talents growing up before finding my passion for makeup. I've been practicing makeup for 10 years and been applying makeup professionally for over five years. I love creating unique, colorful and diverse makeup but I also have a talent for soft, elegant and natural makeup. I specialize in Bridal/Event makeup with a professional hand and a flexible and accommodating schedule. Hiring me is hiring an Artist who has been trained in proper hygiene/sanitation practices, color theory, enhancing and corrective makeup techniques and the ability to work on all skin types, tones, facial/eye shapes and age ranges.
