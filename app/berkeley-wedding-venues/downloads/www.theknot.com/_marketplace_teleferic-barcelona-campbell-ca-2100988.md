### About this vendor

Where Spanish Cuisine Comes to Life Northern California, is where you'll find Teleferic Barcelona, a restaurant with expert wedding caterers who can leave a lasting impression on all your guests. They welcome couples to experience and share the vibrant culture of Spain through exceptional food. And wait... There's more! You'll have access to a spacious primary dining area and/or two private rooms for your next event. A Taste of Tradition At Teleferic Barcelona, it's all about honoring Spain's rich culinary traditions and modern flair. Since 1992, the business has become a top dining destination for all occasions, from weddings and corporate events to private gatherings and anything in between. Tapas, Greens, Paella, and More! These California wedding caterers know exactly how to craft an unforgettable dining experience that guests will be talking about forever. The menu includes lots of traditional tapas like jamon iberico, croquetas, patatas bravas, and pan con tomate. If you feel like sharing a dish, why not go for a Spanish paella? It's a great way to connect with loved ones during a meal, and better yet, you have lots of different paellas to choose from. Teleferic Barcelona offers lobster, tomahawk, iberico pork, veggie, mixta, and negra paella. The options are really endless!
