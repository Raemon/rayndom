About this vendor

Based in San Francisco, CA, Purple Finch Fashion Design Studio creates stunning one-of-a-kind wedding dresses for its soon-to-be wed clients. This company specializes in bespoke and custom-made formal dresses and accessories, allowing couples to enjoy a completely unique look as they celebrate their long-awaited wedding day. The items created by the team at Purple Finch Fashion Design Studio are completely customized and locally made. Your designer will work closely with you to ensure that every element of your gown reflects your wishes, requirements, and personal style. Once you meet your designer, you will have the chance to try sample dresses, give your measurements, choose your favorite fabrics, and much more. Once your dress has been designed on paper, the team will create a stunning dress that can be customized for you. Fitting appointments can also be arranged closer to the wedding day, leaving you with time to make any adjustments if needed.
