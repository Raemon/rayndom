Huria's is a woodfired pizza and Mediterranean cuisine specialty vendor based in Sonoma County, CA, and the North Bay Area, catering to weddings and other events. Founded by Chef Mohaimen and Adriana Huria, this unique caterer offers original NY-inspired pizza Napoletana and delectables from North Africa and the Mediterranean coast. They provide beautiful flavor combinations, ambiance & vibe, and custom packages to suit all wedding types. With over 10 years in the industry, Chef Huria truly knows how to make a wedding meal unique. Choose Huria's to satisfy your guests and make your wedding incredible. Services Offered Huria's offers a focused, pop-up kitchen buffet-style service for weddings of all kinds. They bring a lively, woodfired set-up on-site, designed with warm vintage lighting, floral decor to match, and thoughtful touches that will showcase your selection of dishes and pizza bar in a lovely, buffet-style setup. All food is prepared onsite using an authentic Italian brick oven, so your guests can step up and experience the magic of live cooking and, most importantly, enjoy the flavor of woodfired pizza and more! Cuisine Huria's specializes in Mediterranean cuisine, drawing from North African and Southern European flavor profiles, among others, and features brick-oven Neapolitan pizza with their NYC twist that everyone will love. Using fresh herbs and vegetables, they create fantastic flavors enhanced by the brick oven, and Huria's experience will always leave you and your guests in safe hands.
