About this vendor

Based out of Northern California, Mariana's Floral Design provides bespoke blooms for weddings and other milestone occasions. Gabby, founder and lead designer was taught the art by her grandmother whom has more than 15 years of experience in the field. Growing up around flowers her entire life she now has found her passion within flowers. Mariana's Floral Design has made the dreams of many to-be-weds come true through their floral arrangements and is ready to make yours a reality. Wedding service with Mariana's Floral Design includes the delivery and setup of your blooms. Flower arrangements such as bouquets, boutonnieres, centerpieces, corsages, flower crowns, and flower girl baskets. These professionals also create decor for aisles, arches, pedestals, and more.
