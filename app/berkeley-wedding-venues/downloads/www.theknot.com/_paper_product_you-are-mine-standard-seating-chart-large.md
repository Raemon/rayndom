Details

Crowned with a message letting guests know they’re all your favorite people, these seating charts feature your names and wedding date along the top for a touch of personalization. Numbers in a serif font are neatly arranged in a grid-like composition and followed by the names of the guests at each table, set in clean, evenly spaced sans serif type to keep the layout organized and easy to read. **Materials** Our wedding signs are available in a curated selection of materials designed to complement your setting, from grand entrances to intimate tabletops. Each option is thoughtfully selected for its visual impact, ease of use, and styling versatility.  
**Indoor Foam Core** Smooth and lightweight, foam core is ideal for indoor use where ease and elegance meet. It’s a great option for welcome signs, seating charts, and other easel displays.  
**Outdoor Corrugated** Made from white corrugated plastic and built to withstand the elements, this option is perfect for outdoor signage. Each sign includes an aluminum metal H-stake for easy setup in grass or soil   
**Plastic** A smooth, sturdy material ideal for tabletop signage. Designed to be freestanding, lightweight, and easy to reposition—perfect for dynamic displays like bars, counters, or dessert tables.
