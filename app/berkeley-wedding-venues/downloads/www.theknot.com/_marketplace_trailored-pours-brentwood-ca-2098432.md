Trailored Pours is a mobile bar and beverage service catering to weddings and all types of special events in Brentwood, CA, and the Greater Bay Area. It was established by the owner, Linsay, in 2025, a product of her desire to do something different with her life. Linsay spent years working in the corporate world, but left it all behind to pursue something that would enrich her life creatively, let her connect with other people, and play an active role in the local community. Already with a background of offering quality service, Linsay's desire to create lasting memories and meaningful connections led to her opening Trailored Pours. She loves wholesome ingredients and looks forward to sharing her concoctions with you and your loved ones. Whatever the size of your reception, Trailored Pours offers a number of wedding packages to match your needs. You can reach out to Linsay and discuss your plans and aspirations for the big day. Whether a private affair or a large-scale gathering, Trailored Pours will provide a beverage experience that is tailored to your needs and trailered to your event.
