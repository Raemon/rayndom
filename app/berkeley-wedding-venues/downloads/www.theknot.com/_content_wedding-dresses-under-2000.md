## What Is Considered "Cheap" for Wedding Dresses in 2025–2026?

So, what exactly is considered "cheap" for bridal gowns? Well, according to The Knot 2025 Attire and Fashion Study, the [average wedding dress cost](https://www.theknot.com/content/average-cost-of-wedding-dress) is $2,000. Based on this data, we'd consider any dress below that pricepoint one that fits into the affordable wedding dress category.

There are various [wedding dress cost factors](https://www.theknot.com/content/why-are-wedding-dresses-so-expensive) that influence this national average. Most notably, the recent [tariff impact](https://www.theknot.com/content/financial-anxiety) has forced many wedding dress brands to increase their prices. While these brands search for new ways to navigate the wedding dress supply chain, and ultimately lower prices, many nearlyweds have been opting for budget bridal gowns in the meantime.

Of course, every couple's wedding budget is different, and therefore so is their bridal dress budget. To avoid overspending on your wedding attire, be sure to stick to the amount you've allocated toward it in your budget. **Pro tip:** [The Knot Budget Advisor](https://www.theknot.com/wedding-budget) is a free and easy way to closely monitor your spending and get clarity on various wedding costs.
