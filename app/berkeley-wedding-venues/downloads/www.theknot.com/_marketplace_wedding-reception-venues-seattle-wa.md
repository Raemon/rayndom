### FAQs

#### What time of year is best for a wedding in Seattle?

The best time of year for a wedding in Seattle is June, which offers calm and sunny weather and many local festivals and events to enjoy.

#### Who can officiate a wedding in Seattle?

Anyone who is a Washington judge or an authorized ordained member of a religious clergy can legally officiate a wedding ceremony in Seattle.

#### How do I get a marriage license in Seattle?

Apply online or in person at the King County Records and Licensing Services Division office. Both partners must be present and have a valid photo ID, and there is an application fee. There is also a mandatory waiting period of 3 days, and then the license must be used within the following 60 days.
