About this vendor

The 90s provided an enormous amount of amazing music, and Citizen Flannel is here to take you and your guests back to that incredible time in music history! When it comes to weddings, Citizen Flannel dishes out the tunes that your guests will want to hear, and will love to dance to. From the iconic bands of the era like Sublime, Third Eye Blind, No Doubt and Alanis Morissette, to superstars Green Day and the Red Hot Chili Peppers, to unforgettable one-hit wonders like Lit, Harvey Danger and Semisonic, all the musical bases are covered!
