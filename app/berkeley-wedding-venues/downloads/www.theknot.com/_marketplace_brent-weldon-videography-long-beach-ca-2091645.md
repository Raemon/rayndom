At Brent Weldon Videography, based in Long Beach, CA, we specialize in creating cinematic wedding films that beautifully capture the magic of your love story. With a keen eye for detail and a passion for storytelling, we preserve every heartfelt moment, from the first glance to the final dance. Our wedding films are designed to be timeless treasures, expertly crafted to tell the unique story of your day in a way that you’ll cherish forever. For couples looking to ensure every special moment is captured, Brent Weldon Videography offers the option of additional shooters to cover multiple perspectives. Whether you want more coverage during getting ready, the ceremony, or throughout the reception, our talented second shooters will seamlessly work alongside our primary team to deliver comprehensive, cinematic wedding footage. This added flexibility allows us to capture more details and emotions, giving you a full, multi-angle view of your unforgettable day. Whether you’re planning an intimate ceremony or a grand celebration, we’re here to create the perfect wedding film for you. Let Brent Weldon Videography help bring your vision to life with our dedicated team of filmmakers, ensuring no moment is missed. Take Your Wedding Photos to the Next Level with Underwater Photography If you're looking to add a truly unique and breathtaking element to your wedding experience, we also offer professional underwater photography and Videography services. As a professional underwater photographer and videographer, I specialize in capturing stunning, artistic shots beneath the surface. Imagine beautiful underwater portraits or action shots of you and your partner in an elegant, serene setting—creating memories that are truly one-of-a-kind. Whether it’s a romantic swim in a pool or a beachside underwater session, this creative addition will elevate your wedding album and video to a whole new level. It’s the perfect way to add a surreal, artistic touch to your wedding memories. We’ll guide you through the process to ensure comfort and safety while capturing these incredible, magical moments.
