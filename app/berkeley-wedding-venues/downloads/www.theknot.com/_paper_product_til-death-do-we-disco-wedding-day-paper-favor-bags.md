These groovy day after favor bags provide your guests with thoughtful post-party essentials in a super stylish package. Your Paper Gift Bags are printed with full-color digital designs on a choice of kraft or white paper. Photos aren't recommended for kraft paper but work well on our white paper. Each bag has an imprint area of 4.25” x 4.5” with a full art size of 5.75” x 8” (bleeds not available). Your treat bags will be lined with food-safe wax paper to keep the contents fresh.
