About this vendor

Prep2Plate Catering Co. provides professional catering services for everything from weddings to weekly meal prep in Concord, CA. Every guest is served a lovingly prepared, flavorsome, and nutritious meal catered to their preferences and dietary needs. Personal chef and caterer Keisha Aforonwa infuses her culinary creations with a classic American flair. She has been enlisted in the Top 10 of 2023's Favorite Chef Competition. Chef Keisha and her team can prepare a selection of cuisine, from American BBQ to Cajun, seafood, and Southern-style dishes. They also provide menu options for vegetarians and vegans, ensuring all your guests can enjoy wholesome, tasty food. From gnocchi bakes to cacio e pepe, lemon garlic salmon, and lobster tail, you and your guests will experience an explosion of flavors. Prep2Plate Catering Co. offers various private chef services, including daily meal prep, weekly meal prep, and dinner meal prep. They can provide customizable event catering for weddings and other noteworthy events of up to 100 people, including buffet, family style, and seated meals. The team includes delivery, setup, and cleanup duties, leaving you to simply enjoy the power of flavor during your celebrations. They also offer event planning, day-of coordination, decorations, balloon arches, photo booths, servers, and event rental services on top of their catering. Contact Chef Keisha and her team for more information on their wide range of event offerings.
