About this vendor

Operating out of the heart of San Jose, CA, Zhenned Out Eatery is proud to serve wedding cakes and dessert tables that are light and delicious! Zhenned Out Eatery specializes in Asian-inspired flavors, from soft and fluffy buttercream to fresh fruit fillings. We will work directly with you to ensure your special day is as tasty as it is memorable. We offer a variety of options for a dessert table, including cake pops, cream puffs, brownies, cupcakes, cannoli, cookies, madeleines, and more. If what you have in mind is not listed, reach out, and we will make it happen. For the rest of 2024 only, we are offering a $700 special that includes 150 dessert items in three varieties! This only applies to weddings taking place in 2024. Choices: Choose three items, max one flavor each Cakepops - Ube, Chocolate, and Vanilla Creampuffs - Matcha, Oreo, Ube Tiramisu - Vietnamese Coffee, Strawberry, Pumpkin Chai Brownies - Original, Oreo, Matcha
