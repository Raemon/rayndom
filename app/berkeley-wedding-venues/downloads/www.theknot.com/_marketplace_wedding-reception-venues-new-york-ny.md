### FAQs

#### How do I get a marriage license in New York City?

To obtain a marriage license in New York City, you and your partner need to visit the City Clerk's Office together. You'll need to provide identification and pay a fee. Although the license is issued immediately, you'll have to wait 24 hours before holding your ceremony.

#### Are there wedding planners who specialize in weddings in New York City?

New York City is home to many wedding planners who specialize in local weddings. They'll know the venues and suppliers available, the best ways to avoid traffic jams, and have an eye for design.

#### Can tourists get married in New York City?

Tourists are welcome to get married in New York City. As long as you have a local marriage license and officiant, you can tie the knot anywhere in the city.
