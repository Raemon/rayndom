### About this vendor

Graze Craze Charcuterie Boards & Boxes is a Pleasant Hill, CA-based catering company specializing in one-of-a-kind dining experiences for wedding celebrations and milestone events. The business employs a team of foodies who offer a unique and affordable bite-sized cuisine for all kinds of special occasions. With fresh ingredients, exceptional customer service, and a range of catering options, this catering company could be an excellent choice for you. At Graze Craze Charcuterie Boards & Boxes, you'll get to enjoy delicious assortments of condiments, meats, fruit, and more. Each food display is completely customizable, ensuring there's something tasty for everyone to savor. Best of all, every charcuterie board is elegantly presented, made-to-order, and bursting with colors and flavors, adding a special charm to your long-awaited festivities. When the big day comes around, you can relax knowing you'll leave a long-lasting impression on all your guests. Graze Craze Charcuterie Boards & Boxes provides farm-fresh produce, housemade ingredients, and exquisite attention to detail. Their charcuterie boards feature spiced ham, fresh-baked artisan bread, condiments, a selection of dips, and jams.
