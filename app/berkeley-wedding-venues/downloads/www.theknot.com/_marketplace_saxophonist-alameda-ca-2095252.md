Angelo Luster

SAXOPHONIST

Message Vendor

Be enchanted by the inspiring sounds of Saxophonist, a musician based in Alameda, CA, on your wedding day. Angelo Luster wishes to bring you and your guests on a one-of-a-kind spiritual journey through music. Angelo Luster trained in jazz and classical at the Young Musicians Program at the University of California, Berkeley, for six years after accepting a scholarship at 12 years old. Bill Bell and Jim Moran were his mentors, who had experience in training the likes of Benny Green, Dave Ellis, Rodney Franklin, and John "Jubu" Smith. Once he completed his studies, Angelo began performing at jazz festivals across the US and Europe. Angelo Luster, Saxophonist, will play soulfully on your big day, inspired by contemporary gospel and West Coast movements. He offers his incredible talent to all kinds of wedding festivities, from the ceremony to the cocktail hour and reception. Feel all the emotions as you celebrate your union surrounded by loved ones at the hands of Angelo.
