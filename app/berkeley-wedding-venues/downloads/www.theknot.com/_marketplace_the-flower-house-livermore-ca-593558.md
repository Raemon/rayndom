About this vendor

Based in Livermore, CA, The Flower House is a floral business that helps bring weddings and other events to life. This team of professionals is passionate about couples and their big day. Getting to the betrotheds' backstory is part of the fun, and The Flower House is happy to be inspired by this romantic narrative. The Flower House is a mother-daughter-run establishment. This team is happy to help couples across the San Francisco Bay Area, bringing their wedding visions to life. They take a contemporary approach to each occasion, aiming to tailor the event and its look to the couple being wed. In addition to curating beautiful bouquets, The Flower House can help with creating an overall cohesive wedding design. Matching colors, styles, and themes, this team gets to work crafting an occasion that reflects couples' personalities. Working with your other vendors, The Flower House will ensure that the final presentation is something truly wonderful. What You'll Love About The Flower House Whether you have a clear vision or need help defining your style, this mother-daughter duo will help transform your venue into a blooming masterpiece.
