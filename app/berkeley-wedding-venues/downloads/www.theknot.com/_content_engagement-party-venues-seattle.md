## SoDo Park in SoDo

![Inside large luxury event space with hanging foliage and lighting](https://media-api.xogrp.com/images/d31f35df-390b-438f-bf01-0c330f56c25e~rs_768.h-cr_68.0.1171.827)

_[Photo: SoDo Park](https://www.theknot.com/marketplace/sodo-park-seattle-wa-217359)_

[SoDo Park](https://www.theknot.com/marketplace/sodo-park-seattle-wa-217359) by Landmark Event Co. is a stunning venue located in SoDo. Housed in a century-old industrial building, you can expect soaring ceilings, exposed beams and an open layout. With a capacity of up to 500 guests, it's great for both smaller and larger celebrations, while its private nooks and cozy corners offer quiet moments with your loved ones. Plus, its prime location provides easy access to local restaurants, bars and hotels, making it a fantastic choice for a stylish celebration.

[See This Venue](https://www.theknot.com/marketplace/sodo-park-seattle-wa-217359)

## Buca di Beppo in South Lake Union

![Inside rustic Italian restaurant with checkered tablecloths and pictures on the wall](https://media-api.xogrp.com/images/23dec93a-08c3-4d0c-a9aa-7f0ff2472226~rs_768.h-cr_69.0.1172.827)

_[Photo: Buca di Beppo](https://www.theknot.com/marketplace/buca-di-beppo-seattle-seattle-wa-634918)_

[Buca di Beppo](https://www.theknot.com/marketplace/buca-di-beppo-seattle-seattle-wa-634918) in Seattle's South Lake Union neighborhood offers a vibrant space with eclectic [engagement party decor](https://www.theknot.com/content/engagement-party-decorations), making it a standout choice for engagement parties. The restaurant has several private and semi-private dining spaces, including the Wine Room, which can accommodate up to 35 guests, and the Pope's Room, suitable for 12 to 16 guests. For a truly intimate gathering, the Kitchen Table seats up to 6 people. Overall, this Seattle engagement party location can host events with up to 338 guests, providing flexibility for whatever kind of bash you're dreaming of.

[See This Venue](https://www.theknot.com/marketplace/buca-di-beppo-seattle-seattle-wa-634918)

## Pike Brewing Company in Pike Place Market

[Pike Brewing Company](https://www.theknot.com/marketplace/the-pike-brewing-company-seattle-wa-261643), which is adjacent to Seattle's historic Pike Place Market, offers a fun brewery vibe for your engagement party. Their private event spaces, like the Museum Room, include a private bar, personal AV system and lounge seating, accommodating up to 125 guests. For giant celebrations, the entire venue can host up to 1,200 attendees, providing flexibility to suit various party sizes. With award-winning craft beers, delicious food and a warm, welcoming atmosphere, Pike Brewing Company is a terrific local option. 

[See This Venue](https://www.theknot.com/marketplace/the-pike-brewing-company-seattle-wa-261643)

## Rhein Haus Seattle in Capitol Hill

When it comes to unique engagement party venues Seattle couples will love, look no further than [Rhein Haus](https://www.theknot.com/marketplace/rhein-haus-seattle-wa-2001183). This venue offers a Bavarian-inspired setting that stands out with its indoor bocce courts and spacious bier hall. Located in Seattle's Capitol Hill neighborhood, this venue can host up to 500 guests in total, while smaller private spaces are available for groups of approximately 20 to 100 guests. As far as [engagement party food](https://www.theknot.com/content/engagement-party-food-ideas) goes, you can expect excellent house-made sausages, pretzels and amazing Bavarian dishes. They also have 25 different beers on tap, along with a stellar cocktail program that your guests are sure to love. 

[See This Venue](https://www.theknot.com/marketplace/rhein-haus-seattle-wa-2001183)

## Alder & Ash in the Central Business District

![Inside intimate, modern event space](https://media-api.xogrp.com/images/25bcc53b-91cb-4c33-8133-415722674158~rs_768.h-cr_82.0.1185.827)

_[Photo: Alder & Ash](https://www.theknot.com/marketplace/alder-and-ash-seattle-wa-2077198)_

[Alder & Ash](https://www.theknot.com/marketplace/alder-and-ash-seattle-wa-2077198), located on Pike Street in downtown Seattle's Central Business District, offers a sophisticated setting. The venue has several private and semi-private spaces to accommodate various group sizes. The Alder Room seats up to 28 guests and can host up to 40 for a standing reception, while the cozier Ash Room is ideal for smaller gatherings with a seating capacity of 12. For larger receptions, the bar and lounge area provides a gorgeous space with a standing capacity of up to 96 guests. For grand celebrations, the entire restaurant can accommodate up to 175 guests standing or 86 seated.

[See This Venue](https://www.theknot.com/marketplace/alder-and-ash-seattle-wa-2077198)
