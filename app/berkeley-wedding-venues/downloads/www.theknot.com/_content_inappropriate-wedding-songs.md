## Inappropriate Wedding Reception Songs

Now that we've covered the ceremony, let's switch to the moment the party starts: your reception. Music is an essential component of a reception, setting the scene for your wedding party entrance, when you walk out as newlyweds, your first dance, when guests hit the dance floor and beyond. The below songs, while catchy, are best to be avoided by your [wedding DJ](https://www.theknot.com/marketplace/wedding-djs) because some guests may deem them inappropriate.
