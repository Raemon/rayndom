### About this venue

![2026 Best of Weddings Winner](https://media-api.xogrp.com/images/a8d5af98-7fba-4287-a568-695c3d6436e1~rs_90.90.fit)

Fairview Metropolitan is a wedding venue located in Oakland, CA. With over 30 years of experience in the wedding industry, their team makes it their mission to bring your wedding vision to life. They wish to make the planning process as streamlined as possible, so you can enjoy each special step on the way to the aisle. This venue accommodates both indoor and outdoor events of almost any size, complete with picturesque views that make for stunning photos and a lifetime of memories. Fairview Metropolitan can accommodate wedding celebrations from 50 to 225 guests. In the background, you'll notice a meticulously manicured golf course in front of views of the San Francisco skyline. To begin your day, you may opt for an outdoor ceremony in front of their stunning wooden arbor, saying "I do" as your loved ones look on. Shortly after walking down the aisle, you can take advantage of the venue's countless photo opportunities to take your first photos as a married couple. The property features a fully enclosed pavilion with floor-to-ceiling windows that allow floods of natural light to enter throughout the day. This is the ideal place to hold your reception, as it can be customized to reflect your style and personality. For maximum comfort, the space has wall-to-wall carpeting, and it includes a built-in dance floor to make for a lively event. This venue offers a range of packages to best suit the needs and budget of your event. With the understanding that everyone's needs are unique, their pre-designed packages can be customized to your liking. Fairview Metropolitan can lunch or dinner, appetizers, and wine on each table. You can choose from plated or buffet-style service, which includes entrees, sides, a salad, and seasonal vegetables. Making planning a breeze, their planning team can organize a DJ and a photo booth. In addition, cakes and other sweet treats can be provided by their preferred vendor Mazzetti's Bakery.
