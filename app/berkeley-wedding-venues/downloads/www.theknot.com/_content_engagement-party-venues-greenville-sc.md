## Judson Mill in Greenville

![Judson Mill engagement party venue in Greenville](https://media-api.xogrp.com/images/7bf634bf-7563-4ebc-b3c0-b1763c00cf6b~rs_768.h-cr_0.0.1152.864)

_[Photo: Judson Mill](https://www.theknot.com/marketplace/events-at-judson-mill-greenville-sc-2054439)_

Looking for a rustic, barn-type atmosphere for your engagement party place in Greenville, SC? [Judson Mill ](https://www.theknot.com/marketplace/events-at-judson-mill-greenville-sc-2054439)offers multiple indoor and outdoor spaces, bar service and comes with some key amenities, like tables and chairs. The Annex is a large space with stunning high ceilings and windows and the ability to hold up to 350 people seated. For something smaller, The Smokestack is an indoor venue with an outdoor courtyard and has a more casual feel. 

[See This Venue](https://www.theknot.com/marketplace/events-at-judson-mill-greenville-sc-2054439)

## The Venue at Cherokee Valley in Travelers West 

![The Venue at Cherokee Valley engagement party venue in Travelers West ](https://media-api.xogrp.com/images/2c48a9f9-e9d9-40ac-8530-e6ac3f9d90c9~rs_768.h-cr_0.0.1139.854)

_[Photo: The Venue at Cherokee Valley](https://www.theknot.com/marketplace/the-venue-at-cherokee-valley-travelers-rest-sc-2082657)_

Elegant and special, [The Venue at Cherokee Valley](https://www.theknot.com/marketplace/the-venue-at-cherokee-valley-travelers-rest-sc-2082657) sits at the edge of a golf course and over 170 acres of land. You can have your party indoors or outside in a garden space, but either one offers beautiful views and thoughtful amenities. This full-service venue often hosts weddings, and it's a nice choice for those who want their engagement party to be a bit more formal. 

[See This Venue](https://www.theknot.com/marketplace/the-venue-at-cherokee-valley-travelers-rest-sc-2082657)
