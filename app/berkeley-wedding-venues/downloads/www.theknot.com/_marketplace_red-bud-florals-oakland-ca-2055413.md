Tami Etziony

Owner

With a horticulture and garden design background, Tami has an organic style that incorporates complex textures and flowing lines. She uses the scents and hues of flowers and foliage as an art medium. Designs are sourced from local and organic growers to the greatest extent possible. She is committed to environmental conservation and Red Bud Florals is registered a green business in California. Tami's influences are European design and Ikebana (Japanese flower design ). She has been a floral designer for over thirty years and is inspired by nature's lines and textures.
