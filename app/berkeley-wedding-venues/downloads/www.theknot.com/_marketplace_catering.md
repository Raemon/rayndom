## Finding a Catering

Advice From The Knot

### Start With Your Style

Ready to plan your wedding food? Think about the style of your event.

Whether you're having a sit-down dinner, buffet or passed apps, your menu should reflect the style of your wedding.

### Understand the Options

Need to hire a caterer? The best caterers book up to 12 months in advance.

Catering involves more than just food. Explore menus and ask about presentation, along with what’s included in catering costs.

### Start a Conversation

Found potential caterers? Start a conversation to learn more and set up a tasting.

The Knot's "Request Quote" feature shares your key info with caterers so they can send you their best packages.
