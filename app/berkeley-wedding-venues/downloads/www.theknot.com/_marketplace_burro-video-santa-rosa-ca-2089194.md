About this vendor

Burro Video, based in Santa Rosa, CA, is a wedding video production company serving throughout Northern California and the Bay Area. Their films not only cover your wedding but also capture the essence of your and your partner's love story. You can look back on your cherished wedding film in years to come and watch your milestone moments as they are beautifully retold. Experience the joy, the love, and all the raw emotions share on the day all over again. Your wedding film will be crafted by filmmakers who understand the art of storytelling through their cinematic and authentic styles. Burro Video offers tailored packages so you and your partner can choose what suits your preferences and needs. They use professional-grade 4K cameras and advanced audio equipment to ensure every moment is captured in stunning quality. Sneak peaks will be made available in just one week when the day is over. Every film is edited to highlight the emotional highs of the event. After, you will receive your beautifully edited wedding film via a secure digital link for easy access and sharing. Contact the team for more information on the packages they offer.
