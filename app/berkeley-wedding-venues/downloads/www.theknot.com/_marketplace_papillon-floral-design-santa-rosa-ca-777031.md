About this vendor

Are We the Right Florist for You... Our clients choose to work with us because they love our models of sustainability, our commitment to sourcing local/seasonal materials, our use of color and unique palettes, and our signature style of floral design. We aim to create a look that is wild, garden-like, romantic, free, and artistic. This may not be for everyone, and that's okay! We just ask that you take a look at our Instagram first to make sure our style meshes with your dream wedding flowers. Our Full-Service Weddings... We started out as a full-service wedding florist, and we love being able to work with couples in detail as we create a truly customized, cohesive work of art for you. Full-service weddings with us start at $3,500 for a more minimal look and go upwards of $15,000 depending on number of floral pieces and other details. This service includes a lot of communication, customization, alterations, options, and delivery/setup/installation on the day of your wedding. Our A La Carte Service... For elopements, courthouse weddings, backyard weddings, or more intimate gatherings, our A La Carte service might be just right! This option is wonderful for those who don't need customization, consultations, or day-of setup/installation. You pick items off the menu, choose between 4 color/feel options, and let us do what we do best! You may choose to either pickup at our shop, or have us deliver to your wedding site (delivery fee depends on distance from our storefront and number of pieces). Those that choose our A La Carte service can expect to pay anywhere from $300 for a single bridal bouquet up to $2,000+ for an array of floral pieces for both ceremony and reception. Thank you for your interest in our wedding service! If you'd like to inquire further, please fill out our inquiry form on our website and we'll get back to you with more information.
