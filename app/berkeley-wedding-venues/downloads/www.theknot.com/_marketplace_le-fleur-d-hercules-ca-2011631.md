About this vendor

At Le Fleur D , we pride ourselves to take great care of our clients and make sure we capture their vision. Our mission is to provide you with excellent service and quality on your special day. Floral designs that will reflect your unique taste and style that you will forever cherish. Event planning is definitely our cup of tea, as we have completed different events, especially weddings, We take care of all the necessary procedures to ensure that each client has a stress-free preparation and a sophisticated celebration, from coming up with a theme and color scheme to recommending suppliers, booking them, meeting and liasing with them down to smallest details. For other floral arrangements, we offer stunning bridal bouquets, bridal entourages, bridesmaid bouquets, corsages, ceremony arrangements, table centerpieces, and candelabra flowers. We also provide flowers for cake tables and can do up arches to give you the most picture-perfect entry there ever was, to suit the needs of a modern couple. Tell us about your ideal occasion and we will manage it for you! Pls visit our website at www.lefleurd.com , www.lefleurevents.com
