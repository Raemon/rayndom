Diablo Country Club near Danville, CA, has been a premier wedding venue for over a century, renowned for its elegant hospitality. Nestled against the stunning backdrop of Mt. Diablo, it offers a serene and picturesque setting for your special day. Whether you envision an intimate gathering or a grand celebration, you can exchange vows indoors or outdoors, in a fun and relaxed or formal atmosphere. With abundant natural light, luxurious details, and exceptional cuisine, Diablo Country Club provides a truly memorable experience. Among our lush green golf course we offer a vibrant space perfect for your ceremony, cocktail hour, seated meal, and reception, all in one convenient location. Our dedicated staff at Diablo Country Club provide personalized guidance from your initial consultation, assisting with every detail to ensure your wedding day unfolds seamlessly. They'll offer expert advice and recommend the area's finest florists, bakeries, photographers, and officiants. The culinary team will collaborate with you to create a personalized menu, bringing your wedding day dreams to life.
