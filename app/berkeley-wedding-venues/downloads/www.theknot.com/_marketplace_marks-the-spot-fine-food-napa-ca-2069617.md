About this vendor

Awarded "BEST CATERING COMPANY" and "BEST WEDDING CATERER", Marks the Spot is a highly regarded, chef-owned, full-service wedding catering and event planning company based in Napa, California. Established in 2010, this full-service catering company offers custom menu planning with a range of service options. Their fleet of centerpiece-worthy vehicles are a great addition to vineyard weddings and celebrations -- large or small, casual or elegant. Their beautiful custom wood-fired oven, elegant mobile bar, and vintage food truck help to set the mood and allow various service types throughout the day - from arrival of the first guests, to the main meal service, through to late night snacks for the wedding party. “I just wanted to say thank you for making my daughter’s wedding so delicious and so wonderfully memorable. From the planning to the food, we couldn't have been more pleased.” – Karen Perry, Mother of the Bride Established in 2010, their years of experience and deep relationships with the best local vendors and service providers ensure your special day is everything you dream about. Custom curated and themed menus help your vision comes to life in every detail. Working with only the finest producers, farmers, and purveyors, the food is made from fresh, seasonal ingredients. And of course, dietary requirements are happily accommodated to ensure everyone in attendance enjoys your celebration.
