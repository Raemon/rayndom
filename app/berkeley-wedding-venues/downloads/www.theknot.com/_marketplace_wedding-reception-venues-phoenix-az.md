But if you thought Phoenix to be a place only fitting for outdoor celebrations, think again. This spectacular desert landscape has it all and allows you to celebrate your love story in scale.

If being outdoors is how you'd like to spend time, then Phoenix welcomes you with open arms. Here, you can hike, camp, swim, horseback ride, or climb to your heart's content. The Desert Botanical Garden is a unique attraction as it is one of the few public gardens in the country dedicated to desert plant life. With over 200 golf courses, you can even take a little tee time with your wedding party.
