Description

Style LE149 Ave perfectly embodies the “Sculpted Elegance” collection by Le Blanc. This wedding dress showcases clean, modern lines and sculpted beauty. Crafted from perfecting satin and charmeuse, Ave boasts a sheath silhouette, scoop neckline, 8-point boning in the bodice, and an enhanced low back that is sleek and flattering. This gown comes with detachable spaghetti straps and a cape, offering versatility and the opportunity for brides to personalize their look throughout their wedding day. Ave is the epitome of modern elegance, designed for the bride who desires a gown that is timelessly beautiful and contemporary.
