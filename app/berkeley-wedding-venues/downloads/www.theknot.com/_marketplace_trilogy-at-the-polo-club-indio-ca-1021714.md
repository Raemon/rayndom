### About this venue

Trilogy at the Polo Club is a stunning wedding reception venue in Indio, CA. Surrounded by the Santa Rosa Mountains by a picturesque waterfall and lake, the Polo Club is an ideal venue to celebrate your wedding day. The venue is perfectly suited to accommodate magnificent celebrations as well as unforgettable intimate events. It provides stunning indoor and outdoor spaces to create the wedding of your dreams. At the 2Palms Event Center, which can accommodate up to 100 people, you'll find a spacious dance floor and comfortable guest seating. State-of-the-art audiovisual equipment is available here for your use. For outdoor ceremonies in the sunshine, Santa Rosa Lawns and Reflection Lake are ideal romantic spots. These can host 150 and 200 guests, respectively. A dressing room is available for your pre-wedding use, and spaces are wheelchair accessible. What's more, you can enjoy the sophistication of exceptional Country Club service, flexibly-priced menu options, and a convenient location, making your day a success. Menus are planned and executed to perfection by our culinary team, and every detail is personally cared for by our staff, giving you the freedom and peace of mind to truly enjoy your special day. In addition to food and beverage catering, wedding packages include the use of essential furnishings and decor, as well as day-of planning services. The knowledgeable events team is happy to refer you to some of the Indio area's best wedding vendors. Trilogy at the Polo Club ensures that your beautiful wedding day goes off without a hitch.
