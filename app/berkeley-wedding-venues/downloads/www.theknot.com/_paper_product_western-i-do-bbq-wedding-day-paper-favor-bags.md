These rootin'-tootin’ thank you favor bags will send your guests home with something mighty fine like! Decorated with charming illustrations of cowboy hats, wedding rings, and clinking champagne glasses, these Western-themed bags are worth hollering about. They also feature a combination of fonts with a playful message and your names for an added touch of personalization.   
Your Paper Gift Bags are printed with full-color digital designs on a choice of kraft or white paper. Photos aren't recommended for kraft paper but work well on our white paper. Each bag has an imprint area of 4.25” x 4.5” with a full art size of 5.75” x 8” (bleeds not available). Your treat bags will be lined with food-safe wax paper to keep the contents fresh.
