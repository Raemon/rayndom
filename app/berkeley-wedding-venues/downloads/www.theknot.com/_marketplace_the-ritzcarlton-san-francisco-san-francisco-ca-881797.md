Set against the backdrop of a majestic neoclassical building in the prestigious neighborhood of Nob Hill, weddings at The Ritz-Carlton, San Francisco are as timeless as your romance. Exchange your vows in the enchanting city-view courtyard surrounded by fragrant roses, boxed hedges, and the sounds of a tranquil water fountain. Celebrate with friends and family under the sparkling Bavarian crystal chandeliers in the glamorous grand ballroom. Prepare to embark on the next chapter of your life with an unforgettable bridal afternoon tea or a celebratory bachelor cocktail night. Named the #1 hotel in San Francisco by Travel + Leisure’s World’s Best Awards 2025, The Ritz-Carlton, San Francisco presents exceptional service, expert planning, and the finest regional cuisine to ensure your wedding day is exquisite. Our dedicated wedding advisors are experts at providing the personalized touches that ensure your wedding reflects you and your partner’s tastes and preferences. We look forward to designing the wedding of your dreams in the breathtaking City by the Bay.
