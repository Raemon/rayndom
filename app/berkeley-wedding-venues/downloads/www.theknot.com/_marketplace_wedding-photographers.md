Advice From The Knot

### Start With Your Style

Starting to think about hiring a wedding photographer?

Kick off the search by looking for photographers whose photos you love. Browse portfolios and save any that stand out to you.

### Understand the Options

When considering options think about timing, how many shooters you want, who gets photo rights, and if packages are customizable.
