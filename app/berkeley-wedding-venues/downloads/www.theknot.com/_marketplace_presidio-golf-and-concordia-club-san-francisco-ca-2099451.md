### About this venue

![Vendor](https://media.xogrp.com/images/0f8cb15b-6966-4d00-b955-d7bfec596b04)

Located in San Francisco, CA, The Presidio Golf & Concordia Club is a historic venue offering couples a charming and elegant setting for their wedding day. Originally founded through the merger of two of San Francisco’s most distinguished social clubs, the venue blends over a century of tradition with modern sophistication. Boasting stunning views of the golf course and located close to the city, this property is the ideal choice for couples who want to celebrate in San Francisco. The venue features a selection of versatile event spaces to suit gatherings of all sizes. The Main Dining Room offers panoramic views of the course and city skyline, accommodating up to 120 guests for a seated dinner or 100 with a dance floor. The Lawson Little Room provides an intimate atmosphere ideal for more intimate get-togethers, while the Main Bar offers a warm and inviting setting for cocktail receptions. For larger celebrations, reserving the entire clubhouse is an option, with the space accommodating up to 250 guests for a reception-style event. Each space combines refined architecture and natural lighting to create an unforgettable, romantic ambiance. Couples hosting their wedding at the Presidio Golf & Concordia Club enjoy exceptional service from a professional and dedicated staff. The Club provides full catering and bar service, featuring exquisite cuisine known throughout San Francisco for its quality and creativity. We offer convenient paid parking in our on-site parking area, with easy access from anywhere in the Bay Area. The Presidio Golf & Concordia Club offers couples and their guests a wedding experience that they will cherish for the rest of their lives.
