## Finding a Live Wedding Band

Advice From The Knot

### Start With Your Style

Thinking about hiring a wedding band? Start by envisioning your wedding’s vibe.

Your wedding music sets the tone of your day, and your style will influence the type of band you'll want to hire.

### Understand the Options

If you’re searching for bands, consider these things.

Think about the events you’ll need them for, what songs are on your list, and try to hear the band—or see them live!—before making a decision.

### Start a Conversation

Found a wedding band whose sound you love? Reach out and share your musical taste to see if it's a good fit.

The Knot's "Request Quote" feature walks you through what to share so that your band has the info they need.
