### FAQs

#### What time of year is best for a wedding in San Antonio?

The best time of year for a wedding in San Antonio is during March and April, which offer some of the best weather. October is also a popular month to say “I do” in this city.

#### Who can officiate a wedding in San Antonio?

An ordained minister by a religious organization or a judge or justice of any recognized court can officiate a wedding in San Antonio. The officiant does not need to register with any government office as a wedding officiant in Texas.

#### How do I get a marriage license in San Antonio?

To get a marriage license in San Antonio, you and your partner must go to the County Clerk Marriage office. You'll need a valid form of ID and to pay a fee, and there is a 72-hour waiting period before the ceremony may take place. Online application forms are also available.
