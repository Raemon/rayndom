Brandon Bosch — DJ, Musician, Officiant and Wedding Host in California Brandon Bosch is an experienced DJ, live musician, and wedding host who knows how to create unforgettable celebrations across California. With over 15 years of experience, he combines professional skill with a laid back, soulful style, offering both expertly mixed DJ sets and live acoustic performances to bring your wedding to life. Brandon’s easygoing energy and thoughtful approach help couples feel relaxed and confident from their first call to the last song. Services and Style Brandon provides a full range of wedding entertainment options, covering every part of your big day: Ceremony — Live acoustic guitar and vocals or carefully curated tracks with a hidden sound system to stay out of photos Cocktail Hour and Dinner — Relaxed playlists or live acoustic sets that set the perfect mood for conversation Reception and Dancing — High energy DJ sets with ultra-high-end sound, seamless mixing and a music flow that keeps guests on the dance floor MC Services — Clear, friendly, and professional announcements to guide the night smoothly Lighting and Sound — Every lighting cue is timed to the music, creating a fun and immersive vibe without ever feeling over the top The Celebration DJ offers a "choose your own adventure" proposal system, enabling you to build the wedding service package of your dreams & you get to choose Brandon's suit Engaging Add Ons Photo booth with photostrips and secret video captures Hybrid Band and DJ setup — A DJ with live musicians like a saxophonist, drummer, or violinist for an exciting live meets DJ feel Karaoke setup for singalong fun Personalized monograms and simple visual effects Silent Disco headsets for after party vibes Party Upgrades — Add ons like Cold Sparklers, Dancing on the Clouds, Uplighting, and more to make your celebration even more memorable Musical Approach Brandon is an open format DJ with a huge range of music knowledge. Whether you want timeless classics, new hits, or something a little unexpected, he works closely with you to create a soundtrack that feels personal and true to you. Genres include: Pop, Funk, Soul, R&B, Classic Rock, EDM, Indie, Christian, Country, Hip Hop, Rap, Top 40, Metal, Punk, Bollywood, Bhangra, Americana, Jazz, House, Remixes, and more. Whatever your style, Brandon loves the challenge. The Experience Brandon only books one event per day and limits his calendar to ensure each wedding gets his full attention. Every couple receives video calls, interactive quote options, and a fun, collaborative music planning process with MUST play and DO NOT PLAY lists. Grounded in daily Tai Chi and meditation, Brandon brings a calm, positive presence to every celebration, helping you enjoy the day, the music, and the moments that matter most. Brandon Bosch is more than a DJ. He is a passionate artist who believes that great music brings people together and creates memories that last a lifetime.
