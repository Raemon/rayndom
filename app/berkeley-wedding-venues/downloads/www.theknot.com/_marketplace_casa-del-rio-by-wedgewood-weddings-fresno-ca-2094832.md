### About this venue

Casa del Rio is Fresno's newest premier wedding venue, blending natural beauty and sophisticated design for your perfect celebration. Situated in one of Fresno's most desirable neighborhoods, this newly remodeled estate seamlessly blends natural, earthy charm and modern elegance. Discover this charming log cabin with wood walls Choose from multiple scenic ceremony locations Delight in expansive meadow and pond views Enjoy the cocktail patio, complete with a cozy fireplace Dance the night away in the stunning grand hall with soaring ceilings The charming log cabin exterior with wood grain details and stone accents immediately evokes cozy warmth. Inside, the wood flows into the soaring ceilings and walls of the grand hall. Newly remodeled by an award-winning architectural team, Casa del Rio's thoughtful design pairs rugged materials with glamorous touches like crystal chandeliers and polished concrete floors. Casa del Rio sits peacefully in harmony with its surroundings. Lush greenery and cypress trees dot the expansive grounds. Massive windows perfectly frame views of the meadows and pond on the estate. Multiple outdoor ceremony locations let you customize nuptials by the waterfront or under the trees. Beyond the ceremony, Casa del Rio's spaces create an unforgettable reception. The sprawling cocktail patio boasts an outdoor bar, stone fireplace, and seamless indoor/outdoor flow when doors open to the grand hall. On-site suites make preparing for the big day a breeze. This venue truly has it all for your perfect Fresno celebration. At Casa del Rio, woodland design meets sophisticated style, bringing indoors and outdoors together beautifully. Get ready for fresh air, gorgeous scenery, and new memories that will last a lifetime. This is one wedding destination that offers an experience like no other. Wedgewood Weddings makes hosting your wedding easy with our all-inclusive packages, award-winning menus, and dedicated support. Our all-inclusive packages take care of every wedding detail so you can relax and enjoy the process. We offer fully customizable food and drink menus prepared by our expert in-house catering staff using proven crowd-pleasing recipes. You'll experience personalized wedding planning with our on-site coordinator guiding you from your first venue tour to the big day. Assistance is available seven days a week whenever you need it. With over 30 years of experience, you can have total confidence in Wedgewood Weddings to exceed your expectations for your special day. Our team can't wait to bring your wedding vision to life seamlessly and joyfully. Casa Del Rio by Wedgewood Weddings is a wedding venue located in Fresno, CA.
