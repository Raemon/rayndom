Whether it's a country club, historic mansion or waterfront resort, the best bridal shower venues in Orange County each have their own distinct charm and flair, just as our favorite[ Los Angeles bridal shower venues](https://www.theknot.com/content/bridal-shower-venues-los-angeles) do.

## The Colony House in Anaheim, California

![Outdoor event patio with palm trees and hanging bistro lighting](https://media-api.xogrp.com/images/576bfe12-10e0-4daa-a662-ce1d93f7863c~rs_768.h-cr_69.0.1172.827)

_[Photo: The Colony House](https://www.theknot.com/marketplace/the-colony-house-anaheim-ca-984917)_

[The Colony House](https://www.theknot.com/marketplace/the-colony-house-anaheim-ca-984917) offers a handful of spaces for parties of varying sizes, making it one of the most versatile venues in Orange County. The Pavilion features an expansive outdoor space, a living wall with lush greenery, sleek light fixtures, an exposed wood beam roof and open-air sidewalls, making it the perfect setting for an alfresco bridal shower. There's also the expansive Great Hall, with its stylish whitewashed brick wall, a vaulted ceiling, reclaimed wood accents and a sleek marble bar; The Loft, with its large windows and wood-panel flooring; and an open-air patio with a roaring fireplace to enhance the cozy vibes.

## NOVA Kitchen and Bar in Garden Grove, California

![Inside restaurant event space with modern decor](https://media-api.xogrp.com/images/f045fe93-5e36-4124-acb9-c50141ae6d25~rs_768.h-cr_69.0.1172.827)

[This trendy Asian fusion eatery](https://www.theknot.com/marketplace/nova-kitchen-and-bar-garden-grove-ca-2036035) has several event spaces for bridal showers, the largest being the Metal Room, aka the main dining room, with an expansive bar and contemporary decor at every turn. Additional spaces include the intimate Bamboo Room, which is equipped with striking chandeliers and abundant natural light; the Earth Room, which is centered around a large chandelier; the Fire Room, which features bold pops of red; the Whiskey Room, which has a dark color palette and a speakeasy-style vibe; and, finally, the airy patio.

## The Muckenthaler Mansion in Fullerton, California

Outdoor showers can take place in one of two lawns, the outdoor courtyard patio or the main gallery, which is equipped with a baby grand piano. Additional highlights at this party venue in Orange County include the sun-drenched solarium, a wrought-iron spiral staircase imported from Italy, stained glass windows and an ornate tiled fireplace.
