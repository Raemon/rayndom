Melo Photo + Video provides affordable wedding photography and videography in Sonoma, CA, with packages starting at $600. Couples can customize 4–10 hours photo + video coverage for under $3,000. With flexible pricing, instant online booking, and a vetted creative team, Melo is one of the most budget-friendly and stress-free ways to capture your wedding. We created Melo with one thing in mind: you and your wedding day. Local California Wedding Photographers Our local Sonoma, CA photographers and videographers specialize in natural, candid storytelling and are experienced with the area’s most popular venues and outdoor locations. Our local photographers are here to capture every candid moment: whether you’re planning a micro wedding with a handful of loved ones or an elaborate celebration with everyone you know. Customizable Packages Starting at $600 Weddings come in all shapes, sizes, and budgets. That’s why we offer cost-effective packages beginning at $600 for 4 hours of coverage. You can build your day exactly how you want: Want just four hours for an intimate elopement? Perfect. Need 6 hours for a traditional ceremony and reception? We’ve got it. Looking for 8 hours of photo and video for a big celebration? Covered. Want 10 hours for full-day storytelling? Also an option! And yes! Your photo + video package can come in under $3,000, making Melo one of the most budget-friendly wedding photography options in the area. A Stress-Free Booking Experience Our couples have always been our priority. Booking your Sonoma wedding photographer or videographer should feel simple and enjoyable, and never overwhelming. That’s why our entire process is online: Check availability for your wedding date Build a custom package that fits your timeline Book instantly from wherever you’re most comfortable Once you’re booked, we’ll create your A-Team of photographers and videographers, selected from our carefully vetted Team Melo talent group. FAQs What areas of Sonoma, CA, do you serve? We serve the entire Sonoma metro area, including Santa Rosa, Petaluma, Rohnert Park, Windsor, Healdsburg and Cloverdale. Do you use local photographers and videographers? Yes. Our team is made up of local California photographers and videographers, familiar with Sonoma’s venues, outdoor spaces, and unique lighting conditions. Do you offer elopement or micro-wedding packages? Absolutely. Our 4-hour package is perfect for courthouse weddings, micro weddings, and elopements anywhere in Sonoma, CA. How do I check availability? Visit our website, select Sonoma, CA, and enter your wedding date. Availability updates instantly so you can build and book your package online.
