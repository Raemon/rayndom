### About this vendor

Flower Icon is a luxury wedding and event florist based in San Francisco, CA, specializing in modern, high-end floral design. Known for its distinctive style and refined approach, Flower Icon creates floral experiences that feel elevated, elegant, and effortlessly impactful. With a focus on clean lines, premium blooms, and creative storytelling, Flower Icon offers everything from bespoke bridal bouquets to large-scale floral installations. The team works across a wide range of aesthetics—from minimal and contemporary to romantic and lush—ensuring each wedding is beautifully tailored to the couple’s vision. Clients begin with a collaborative planning process that includes color palette development, floral selection, and curated mood boards. Every design is customized with careful attention to seasonality, venue, and atmosphere. From initial concept through delivery and on-site installation, Flower Icon brings sophistication and precision to every step—making it the go-to florist for stylish weddings and events across San Francisco and the Bay Area.
