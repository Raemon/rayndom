About this vendor

Riki Dalal, an Israeli wedding dress designer, has had one simple mission for over 40 years - To create the dream dress for every bride coming into her shop. Her trademark style includes daring backs, tight rears, deep necklines, and intricate detail. Serving brides in over 15 Countries does not stop Riki Dalal from making sure every dress is of top-notch quality. We know as a bride, you have many designers to choose from. Riki Dalal is unique in that we can customize your dress to your wishes, and we can make your dress to your measurements. Our Made to Measure service allows us to customize the fit of your gown. If you can dream it, we can design it! Now offering 3 leading brands which can meet the budget of almost any bride. Shopping at Riki Dalal you can expect an extravagant experience with expert advice, champagne, and chocolates! Riki Dalal Couture - Custom dresses starting at $4500 NOYA Bridal by Riki Dalal is a unique line of ready-to-wear bridal gowns, with an average price $3200! Liri Revolution line – $1,100- $2,000 LiRi by Riki Dalal is a unique line of ready-to-wear bridal gowns. A favorite choice for brides around the globe - Each design is made to make you look PERFECT on your special day.
