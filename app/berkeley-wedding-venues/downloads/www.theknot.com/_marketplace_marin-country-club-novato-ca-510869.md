### About this venue

Marin Country Club is a wedding venue in Novato, CA. Situated in the Bay Area’s North Bay region, this California wedding venue is close to the San Francisco International Airport, making it a convenient location for local and out-of-town guests. At Marin Country Club, the beautiful Tuscan-style clubhouse is surrounded by olive trees and fragrant lavender, making it an idyllic setting for your special day. The exquisite architecture and the stunning grounds will leave you and up to 200 guests in awe. Three separate event spaces can be utilized for the duration of your festivities. You and your partner will choose between stunning indoor and outdoor areas, including a classic banquet hall, which creates a romantic atmosphere. You and your loved ones can enjoy various offerings from the in-house catering team for your meal. Dine on mouth-watering appetizers, fresh fish and meat entrees, and flavorful desserts. Dietary restrictions can also be accommodated, ensuring something on the menu for everyone in attendance. In addition to providing a picturesque backdrop for your special day, the team also offers several services to bring your vision to life. Between the banquet staff and event coordinator, they’ll take the time to understand your needs and make your dream wedding a reality with meticulous attention to detail and flawless execution. Whether planning a wedding shower, engagement party, ceremony, or reception, each event will be handled with a personalized touch, giving your event the care and attention it deserves.
