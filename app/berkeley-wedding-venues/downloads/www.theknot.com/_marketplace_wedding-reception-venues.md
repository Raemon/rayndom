## Finding a Wedding Venue

Advice From The Knot

### Start With Your Style

Imagine your reception. Is it indoors or outdoors? Is it a big celebration, or could you visit small wedding venues?

Search for venues that fit your vision. And remember—decor can also help transform a venue into your style.

### Understand the Options

Since every venue and package is unique, keep this info in mind when you start your venue search.

A venue’s quoted cost is specific to your guest count, the hours you need it, and any specifics included. (Not sure? Ask for more details!)

### Start a Conversation

Found a venue you’re into? Reach out! Ask about their availability and see if their capacity aligns with your guest count.

Also try The Knot’s “Request Quote” feature. It helps you decide what info to share so you can easily connect with venues.
