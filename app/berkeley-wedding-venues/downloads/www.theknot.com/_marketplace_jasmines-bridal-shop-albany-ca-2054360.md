About this vendor

Located in Fremont, CA, Jasmine's Bridal Shop designs and creates personalized wedding gowns. Whether you want to design your dream dress from scratch or there is a piece you would love to have recreated just for you, this team is there to help. They work with you from the get-go to design, create, customize, and alter gowns and accessories to suit your unique style. With quick turnaround times and budget-friendly pricing, Jasmine's Bridal Shop is there to help. Owner James has over a decade of experience assisting clients in their wedding fashion journey. Always willing to lend a hand, this skilled professional strives to send each client down the aisle in an outfit they simply adore. The one-of-a-kind pieces are made just for you with the highest quality products and the most careful attention to detail possible. From planning to production, you can work with the team to bring your wedding attire visions to life. Changes can be made throughout the production phase to ensure the end product is exactly what you have been dreaming of. Not just for the dress, veils, wedding party, and flower-child apparel can also be designed and brought to life in this incredible studio. You can book a free consultation over Zoom with James at this link: https://calendly.com/hui-lin-james/initial-consulation-for-dress-project
