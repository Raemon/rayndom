About this vendor

Based out of Sunnyvale, CA, the Magnolia Jazz Band entertains at weddings throughout the San Francisco Bay Area. They are available to provide music for your ceremony and cocktail reception, as well as for dinner and dancing. These talented musicians combine elegance with energy, creating a performance that you and your guests will remember for years to come. You'll love entertaining your guests with music that includes hits from Frank Sinatra, Louis Armstrong, and Elvis Presley. Magnolia Jazz Band specializes in classics from the 20s, 30s, 40s, 50s, and 60s. They also play Latin favorites, including music from Antonio Carlos Jobim. You can customize this band's versatile lineup, to change the number of musicians to suit the size and mood of your event. They can also act as MC to direct your celebration. Magnolia Jazz Band is led by string bassist Robbie Schlosser, who first formed the group in 1975. He has been playing music since he was in the third grade and started off learning the violin. Robbie has also played with New Orleans' Preservation Hall Jazz Band and performed on PBS with the Royal Society Jazz Orchestra. These days he plays alongside several other talented musicians, including clarinetist Gary Miliken and guitarist Andy Norblin.
