## Finding a DJ

Advice From The Knot

### Start With Your Style

Deciding on music for your wedding? The Knot recommends booking a DJ that focuses on weddings.

When searching, look for DJs who will build a playlist based on your vision (dance party, mosh-pit, or something in-between!).

### Understand the Options

Have a specific DJ in mind? Reach out 9-12 months before your wedding. Otherwise, 6-9 months out is fine.

Decide which events you want a DJ for. Also consider additional equipment, like microphones, you might need on your wedding day.

### Start a Conversation

Found a DJ you feel good about? It's time to start a conversation! Share your musical taste to see if it's a good fit.

The Knot's "Request Quote" feature walks you through what to share, so your DJ has the info they need to make a custom quote.
