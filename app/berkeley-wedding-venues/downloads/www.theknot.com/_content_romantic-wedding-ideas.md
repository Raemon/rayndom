## What Is a Romantic Wedding? 

A romantic wedding encompasses any theme that makes you feel dreamy and in love. This might include soft colors, candlelight, abundant flowers, princess-inspired details or vintage and moody designs. This theme takes shape in many ways and is different for each couple. Cascading roses, velvet tablecloths, fairy lights and blush wedding invitations may appear on your[ inspiration board](https://www.theknot.com/content/how-to-make-an-inspiration-board). Incorporate romantic wedding ideas that give you the elated feeling of being in love.
