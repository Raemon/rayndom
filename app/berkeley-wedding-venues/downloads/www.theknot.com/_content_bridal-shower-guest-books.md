Congrats! You're officially engaged, and it's time to start party planning. First things first, your family will want to throw you a wedding shower. And one major component of this party is having a bridal shower guest book to keep up with all of your loved ones. A [bridal shower](https://www.theknot.com/content/bridal-shower-basics) is the perfect way to jumpstart your wedding planning journey, and if you're wondering, "Do you have to have a guest book at a bridal shower?" We're here to convince you why it's a memorable addition to any prewedding event. You'll love having personalized messages from your favorite people, especially as the years go on. Plus, guest books no longer look like just one thing, with many alternatives taking their place for an exciting keepsake that you and your future spouse will treasure. From posters to photo books, no matter the size of your wedding shower, there are limitless choices. With all of these unique and modern wedding shower guest book ideas, we bet you'll find the right option for your day.
