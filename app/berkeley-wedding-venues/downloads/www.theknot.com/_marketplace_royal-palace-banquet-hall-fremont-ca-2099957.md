### About this venue

Royal Palace Fremont, in Fremont, CA, is a premier Bay Area wedding venue and banquet hall, offering a luxurious and versatile setting for weddings, receptions, corporate events, and special celebrations. Our venue features three beautifully designed ballrooms — the Venetian (50–150 guests), Majesty (150–220 guests), and Sultan (300–1,000 guests). Each is adorned with stunning décor, modern amenities, and elegant interiors. We pride ourselves on delivering a seamless and unforgettable event experience, from customizable catering services to ample parking and professional event support. Whether you’re planning an intimate gathering or a grand celebration, Royal Palace Fremont is the perfect location to make your dream event a reality.
