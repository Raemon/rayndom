No matter how much you love your [engagement ring](https://www.theknot.com/content/engagement-ring-trends), there are situations when you may need to temporarily take it off. Think: the gym, the beach, even a spray tan. For a stylish storage option, consider buying a decorative ring dish. This simple accessory is the most convenient way to keep your ring safe and within reach on your busiest days.

Personalize your engagement ring dish with a monogram, a meaningful message or even your favorite song lyrics, or just keep it simple and minimalist. And don't forget to snag a men's ring dish if your partner prefers something more masculine. Engagement ring holders also make great [engagement gifts](https://www.theknot.com/content/engagement-gift-ideas) for a loved one. 

Whether you're treating yourself or buying it for someone else, there's a decorative engagement ring holder or trinket dish for every aesthetic and budget on our list. From quirky and funky to a luxury ring dish that's elegant and sleek, you'll find tons of options for a dish that'll [take care of your engagement ring](https://www.theknot.com/content/how-to-care-for-your-engagement-ring) when you can't wear it. Check out our list of pretty, editor-approved ring dishes.
