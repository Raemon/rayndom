Community Congregational Church is a hidden gem wedding venue nestled in the scenic hills of Tiburon, CA. It offers panoramic views of the San Francisco Bay, Golden Gate Bridge, and Mount Tamalpais. This inclusive Bay Area venue welcomes all couples, embracing diversity and celebrating love in all its forms. With breathtaking mountain and ocean views, it’s the perfect setting for your dream wedding. Couples can exchange vows in the stunning open-air chapel on the lawn, with the Golden Gate Bridge and San Francisco Bay as a breathtaking backdrop. For a more intimate or symbolic experience, the Chartres-style labyrinth offers a unique and spiritual space for your ceremony. Indoor options include the Seminar Room, which features an open-beamed ceiling, cozy fireplace, and picturesque views of the bay and gardens—perfect not only for intimate gatherings but also as a relaxing bridal suite to prepare for your big day. The Sanctuary is ideal for a traditional church ceremony or as a backup plan for unpredictable weather. Both indoor and outdoor spaces are available for wedding ceremonies and receptions. Located just 10 miles from San Francisco, the venue provides easy access for couples seeking a nearby reception in the city while still enjoying the serene beauty of the Tiburon hills. After you say “I do,” you and your guests can stroll through the idyllic gardens or walk along the contemplative labyrinth and soak in the peaceful atmosphere before and after your special day. The venue’s experienced team will guide you through every step of your wedding planning, offering a variety of packages. They also provide recommendations for officiants and other trusted vendors to bring your vision to life. Whether you envision a grand celebration or an intimate affair, Community Congregational Church Tiburon is a Bay Area wedding venue that combines stunning views, inclusive values, and expert service to create a truly unforgettable day.
