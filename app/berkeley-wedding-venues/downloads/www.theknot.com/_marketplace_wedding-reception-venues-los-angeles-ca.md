#### Who can officiate a wedding in Los Angeles?

Authorized members of religious denominations like rabbis, priests, and ministers can officiate your wedding in California. Judges, magistrates, Members of Congress, and some local elected officials are also an option. California Constitutional Officers and state legislators can officiate wedding ceremonies, too.
