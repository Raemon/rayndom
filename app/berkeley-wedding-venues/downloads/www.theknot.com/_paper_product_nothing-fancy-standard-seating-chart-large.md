Details

Make it easy for your loved ones to find their seats with these simple, streamlined seating charts. All of your family and friends' names are neatly printed beneath numerical columns in an easy-to-read serif typeface. **Materials** Our wedding signs are available in a curated selection of materials designed to complement your setting, from grand entrances to intimate tabletops. Each option is thoughtfully selected for its visual impact, ease of use, and styling versatility.  
**Indoor Foam Core** Smooth and lightweight, foam core is ideal for indoor use where ease and elegance meet. It’s a great option for welcome signs, seating charts, and other easel displays.  
**Outdoor Corrugated** Made from white corrugated plastic and built to withstand the elements, this option is perfect for outdoor signage. Each sign includes an aluminum metal H-stake for easy setup in grass or soil   
**Plastic** A smooth, sturdy material ideal for tabletop signage. Designed to be freestanding, lightweight, and easy to reposition—perfect for dynamic displays like bars, counters, or dessert tables.  
**Please note:** Signage will ship separately from any non-signage items in your order. Due to the custom nature of these products, signage is not eligible for reprints. Easels and display stands are not included.
