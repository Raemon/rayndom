### FAQs

#### Are there any age requirements to get married in Kansas City, MO?

While you can apply for a marriage license when you are 15 years old, you also need a parent, guardian, or judge to give consent. If you're 18, you can do away with this step.

#### Who can perform your wedding ceremony in Kansas City, MO?

An authorized person, such as a judge, or an ordained religious clergy member such as a priest, minister, or rabbi can perform the ceremony.

#### Is Kansas City weather suited for outdoor weddings?

Kansas City summers are great for outdoor weddings, as it tends to be pretty warm. It is generally recommended that you avoid planning your wedding from April through June, which is tornado season here.
