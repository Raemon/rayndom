About this vendor

Please read through our pricing guidelines below before inquiring and visit our website to fill out a direct inquiry form if you like what you see! Siren Sisters Kitchen, LLC is a woman-owned wedding catering business based in Fairfax, CA. These culinary artists are passionate about creating menus that captivate, enchant, and forge connections, embodying the spirit of the Greek mythological creature, the siren. Owner Dina established this business with a group of women, mothers, and caretakers whose stories inspired her. Despite their diverse backgrounds, these women found unity in their shared experiences and collective passion for food. They see every dish they craft as an opportunity to take diners on a culinary voyage, experiencing well-loved flavors and cuisines from a new perspective. Siren Sisters Kitchen, LLC operates a cozy storefront and a thriving off-site catering kitchen in the heart of Fairfax a. With 30+ years of experience, Dina and her team know how to feed a crowd, whether it's an intimate family gathering or a 500-person banquet. Couples can work with the Siren Sisters to design a menu that fits the mood and ambiance of their big day. Buffet-style dining, family-style meals, and grazing table catering are available, giving newlyweds and their guests many ways to experience the flavors of the Siren Sisters Kitchen.
