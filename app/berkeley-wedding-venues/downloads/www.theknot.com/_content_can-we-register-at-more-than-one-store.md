## Can You Consolidate Your Wedding Registries?

Yes! Many sites let you combine wedding registries into a single convenient location. Ideally, your registry should be a highly curated collection of items you want for your space. Often, that means maintaining registries at several stores, even if each registry only contains a few items. That's highly inconvenient when it comes to sharing your registry links with guests, who often prefer to shop at one or two places for wedding gifts. 

[READ MORE The Trendiest Things to Put on Your Wedding Registry ](https://www.theknot.com/content/new-wedding-registry-trends)

Additionally, services, experiences and even cash or gift cards are becoming increasingly popular [registry gifts](https://www.theknot.com/content/how-many-gifts-to-register-for). Consider Airbnb or Expedia gift cards, Tinggly experience gifts or a lavish couples' massage for your honeymoon. Registering for these items could make things even more complicated before wedding registry consolidation became possible. Creating a [universal registry](https://www.theknot.com/content/the-knot-universal-registry) on The Knot allows you to keep all of your registry items in one convenient location.

## Benefits of Consolidating Your Wedding Registries

In a word, the biggest benefit of having a combined wedding registry is convenience! Having everything in one place makes it easier for your guests to shop for that perfect gift and easier for you to manage and keep track of your registry. Still not convinced you should combine wedding registries? We've listed some additional benefits below.

### Register at Several Different Places

If a particular store has one or two items you can't live without but not enough for a traditional wedding registry, a wedding registry that combines stores is a perfect option. 

### Combine Gifts With Services, Gift Cards and Experiences 

Hoping to stock your kitchen _and_ score a weekend getaway in the Bahamas, courtesy of your wedding guests? It's totally possible with a combined wedding registry.

You'll find everything from kitchen essentials to elevated home goods and unique items from popular brands like KitchenAid and Le Creuset. 

### 2\. Sync Gifts From Partner Sites

Choose gifts from any one of The Knot's featured partners like Target, Amazon and Anthropologie for a seamless addition to your wedding registry. This feature allows you to browse gifts and create a store registry without leaving The Knot's website. 

### 3\. Add Anything From Anywhere

If your desired store or item isn't featured anywhere else on The Knot, you can _still_ add it to your registry following these simple steps: 

  * Find your bookmark bar. If it's not visible, select "View" and navigate to "Show Bookmarks Sidebar" (or similar).
  * Drag the pink "Add to The Knot" browser bar to your bookmarks list. 
  * Go to your desired store's website. 
  * Click the item you'd like to register for. 
  * You'll be prompted to log in to The Knot if you're not yet logged in.
