One of the most important parts of curating an outfit—whether you're a bride, guest or bridesmaid—is choosing the perfect hairstyle to go with your dress. Hairstyles for [open-back dresses](https://www.theknot.com/content/open-back-wedding-dresses) are extra important, because you likely will want to choose a hairstyle that lets you show off the open back of your dress. Whether you opt for a sleek updo or loose undone tresses, we've scoured the internet for 35 of the best hairstyles to pair with [backless dresses](https://www.theknot.com/content/backless-wedding-guest-dresses). Save this page to show your hairdresser at your trial run and try a few that stand out to you so you can pick your favorite. We may be biased, but they're all winning styles.

## Half-Up Waves

![Half-up waves wedding hairstyle](https://media-api.xogrp.com/images/8d5159ff-9691-451b-a4e3-88a7bd21ad98~rs_768.h-cr_34.0.1047.1350)

_Photo: Hana Gonzalez Photography_

A [half-up style](https://www.theknot.com/content/half-up-hairstyles-for-long-hair) is perfect for sweeping your hair out of your face, but still letting your long tresses shine. Soft, undone waves make this style the perfect effortless, yet chic hairstyle.

## Textured Low Bun

![Textured low bun wedding hairstyle](https://media-api.xogrp.com/images/f10b5171-996d-4d6f-815f-e42ba565ba11~rs_768.h-cr_34.0.1047.1350)

_Photo: Aloha Tati Photography_

Sweep your hair into a messy low bun for an elegant hairstyle that pairs particularly well with an open-back dress. Whether you're the bride or a guest, this style is perfect for any occasion.

## Glam Curls

![Glam curls wedding hairstyle](https://media-api.xogrp.com/images/9842f8db-77a7-4c0b-b9c3-c48a8e9d6c35~rs_768.h-cr_34.0.1047.1350)

_Photo: Sarah Seven_

A deep, plunging backless dress is a match made in heaven with this voluminous curled hairstyle. You know what Dolly Parton says, the bigger the hair, the closer to God!

## Pretty Ponytail

## Pearlcore
