Message Vendor

Leandra Ramm, mezzo-soprano, is a professional singer based in Pittsburg, CA, in the San Francisco Bay Area, offering elegant, high-quality live vocal music for weddings and special events. With years of experience performing on major concert and opera stages, I bring the same level of artistry, preparation, and professionalism to every wedding—whether it’s an intimate ceremony or a large celebration. My goal is to create a meaningful, beautiful musical experience that feels personal and effortless for you. I work closely with each couple to select repertoire that reflects your vision, style, and values. Offerings include classical and sacred music, art song, musical theater, beloved crossover selections, Disney favorites, and tasteful popular music arranged for live voice. I am also happy to accommodate special requests whenever possible. For couples who would enjoy vocal duets, my husband Jim Coniglio—also a professional singer—may join me at no additional charge for select pieces, offering the option of beautifully blended duets for ceremonies or special moments. Clients consistently describe my voice as warm, expressive, and refined, and my approach as calm, reliable, and easy to work with. From the initial inquiry through the final note of your ceremony, you can expect clear communication, thoughtful planning, and a seamless experience that allows you to relax and enjoy your day. I am available for wedding ceremonies, cocktail hours, memorials, private celebrations, and other special occasions. Professional, punctual, and detail-oriented, I take pride in elevating each event with live music that feels intentional, moving, and unforgettable. If you are looking for live wedding music that combines emotional depth, polish, and professionalism, I would be honored to be part of your celebration.

Jim Coniglio

Vocalist/Ensemble Singer

Jim Coniglio is a seasoned vocalist with extensive performance experience throughout the San Francisco Bay Area. His stage credits include Fiddler on the Roof (Tevye), Oliver!, Joseph and the Amazing Technicolor Dreamcoat, The Music Man, Man of La Mancha, Les Misérables, Gypsy, White Christmas, and Legally Blonde, performing with companies such as Pittsburg Theatre Company, Diablo Theatre Company, Contra Costa Musical Theatre, and Lucky Penny Productions. In addition to musical theatre, Jim regularly performs in vocal ensembles and collaborative settings, including appearances with Arété Singers and local professional vocal groups. He brings a warm, reliable presence, strong musicianship, and ensemble sensitivity to weddings and special events, frequently performing duets and coordinated vocal programs tailored to each couple’s ceremony or celebration. For an additional fee, Jim is available to perform as part of a vocal duet or ensemble, adding rich harmony and expanded musical options to your ceremony or event.

  * ![Vendor](https://media.xogrp.com/images/28b4d6c8-dfd0-40b8-83b6-c56040f96308)

Matt Hidalgo

Vocalist/Ensemble Singer

Matt Hidalgo, tenor, performs regularly throughout Sacramento and Bay Area and is a familiar face with many top performing groups in the regions. For an additional fee, Matt Matt teaches voice lessons in Sacramento and is Vocal Director at Roseville High School. Along with teaching and performing, he is also a Chorus Member with the San Francisco Opera. Matt is a versed performer of oratorio, opera, art song, and experienced concert artist. Recently, Matt performed the World Premier of Sacramento native composer, Carlos Fuentes as tenor soloist in Requiem. Matt also recently performed the U.S. premiers of Gabriel Ruiz-Bernal’s Magnificat and Rowland Lee’s Requiem. He has also performed various oratorios by JS Bach, W.A. Mozart, James DeMars, Joseph Haydn, George F. Handel, Claudio Monteverdi, and Camille Saint Saëns. A competition winner, Matt won the Sacramento State Music Department’s Concerto Competition, including the opportunity to perform Benjamin Britten’s Serenade for Horn, Tenor, and Strings. Matt can also be heard as a featured soloist in SCSO’s CD recording, Lest we Forget. For an additional fee, Matt is available to perform as part of a vocal duet or ensemble, adding rich harmony and expanded musical options to your ceremony or event.

Leandra Ramm

Main Vocalist

Mezzo-soprano Leandra Ramm is praised for her “beautiful and quite moving” performances (Nordstjernan Newspaper) and "brilliant" and “stand out” artistry (San Francisco Classical Voice). Taking the stage with San Francisco Symphony, she was alto soloist in Bach’s Magnificat in 2023 under Jane Glover's baton, Bernstein’s Chichester Psalms conducted by Michael Tilson Thomas and starred in ‘Ecstasy’ in 2022, presented at Soundbox. Ramm shined in diverse recent productions, including First Witch in Dido’s Ghost and Dido and Aeneas with Philharmonia Baroque Orchestra, Third Lady in The Magic Flute with Livermore Valley Opera, Lapak in The Cunning Little Vixen with Pocket Opera, and alto soloist in Elijah with San Francisco City Chorus. She was featured in West Edge Opera’s 2025 summer festival (David and Jonathan; world premiere of Dolores) and returned to Livermore Valley Opera as Annina in La Traviata. Her repertoire showcases versatility, portraying roles such as the Squirrel/White Cat/Shepherd in L’enfant et les sortilèges with Pacific Symphony, and Serafina/Spirit Woman in La Llorona with Opera Cultura. Ramm is known for her genuine portrayals of “pants roles” and has received acclaim for Rooster and Blue Jay in The Cunning Little Vixen with West Edge Opera, and Prince Orlofksy in Die Fledermaus with Pocket Opera. Captivating audiences as Anybodys in West Side Story at Opera San Jose, her contributions to the company include Paggio in Rigoletto, Mercedes (cover) in Carmen, and Cherubino (cover) in Le Nozze di Figaro. Ms. Ramm singing regularly with San Francisco Opera Chorus.
