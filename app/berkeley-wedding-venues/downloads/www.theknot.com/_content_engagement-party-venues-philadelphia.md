## P.J. Clarke's in Center City

![Rustic restaurant venue with long leather sofas and crystal chandeliers](https://media-api.xogrp.com/images/9b160f1c-453e-49a2-863d-9c7e6b813aa1~rs_768.h-cr_0.0.1102.827)

_[Photo: P.J. Clarke's](https://www.theknot.com/marketplace/pj-clarkes-philadelphia-pa-2063596)_

While the first [P.J. Clarke's](https://www.theknot.com/marketplace/pj-clarkes-philadelphia-pa-2063596) restaurant was started in 1884 in New York City the Philadelphia location is situated in The Curtis Atrium in historic Washington Square. The space has multiple private rooms that you can book for your engagement party of up to 250 guests, including the Oyster Bar and Lounge, Curtis Dining Room, The Chapel and The Walnut Room.

[See This Venue](https://www.theknot.com/marketplace/pj-clarkes-philadelphia-pa-2063596)

## Rosalie in Wayne

![Lovely blue and white event space with hanging light fixtures and floral wallpaper](https://media-api.xogrp.com/images/f01cb6df-cb89-4d3a-9a77-c3f7d800f4a7~rs_768.h-cr_69.0.1172.827)

_[Photo: Rosalie](https://www.theknot.com/marketplace/rosalie-wayne-pa-2055044)_

Located just outside of the city, [Rosalie](https://www.theknot.com/marketplace/rosalie-wayne-pa-2055044) is a vibrant and colorful Italian restaurant located in the historic Wayne Hotel. Their thoughtfully curated menu features locally sourced seasonal ingredients and artisan craft cocktails. Their expert staff can help cater your event to your preferences and the space can accommodate up to 100 guests. 

[See This Venue](https://www.theknot.com/marketplace/rosalie-wayne-pa-2055044)
