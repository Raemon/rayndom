### About this vendor

Sol Azteca Catering & Events is a wedding catering business in East Cotati Avenue, Rohnert Park, CA. In 1976, along with their children, Ernesto and Francisca Lemus moved to Rohnert Park in California. Francisca was a fantastic chef, always cooking authentic Mexican meals for her family. She learned by watching her grandmother cook back home in Jalisco, Mexico. In 1992, Francisca opened her own restaurant, Taqueria Sol Azteca, serving delicious authentic Mexican food. The business grew over the years, and for over two decades, Francisca served a proud local community with the help of her kids. In 2014, her son Francisco "Frankie" took over full-time and helped spawn a catering business named Sol Azteca Catering & Events. The future looks brighter than the sun for Sol Azteca. Sol Azteca offers all types of catering for every imaginable celebration. They provide for private parties, corporate lunches, holiday events, and birthdays, and you can even rent their nacho cart! Please feel free to contact Frankie or a team member for more information.
