About this vendor

Based in Napa Valley, Sidewalk Coffee Company is mobile espresso and coffee cart that specializes in weddings and events. The business is owned by husband and wife Mike & Stacia, who love coffee and are passionate about providing high-quality service. Working closely with this dynamic team, you can create a custom-made menu tailored to suit the taste and theme of your wedding. Sidewalk Coffee Company offer both hot and iced coffee and espresso drinks, including lattes, teas, mochas, cold brews, frappes, fruit smoothies, affogatos, and many more. For those looking to add a personal touch, they can make a signature drink for your event. Sidewalk Coffee Company can cater for events of any size, indoors or outdoors. They are happy to take care of delivery, setup, and cleanup, so all you have to worry about is celebrating your own unique love story. Mike and Stacia would be delighted to be a part of your special day.
