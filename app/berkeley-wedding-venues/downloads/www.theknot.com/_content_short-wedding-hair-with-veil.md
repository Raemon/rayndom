When planning your [wedding day glam](https://www.theknot.com/content/things-no-one-tells-you-about-wedding-hair-and-makeup), choosing a wedding hairstyle is half the battle. For all of you short-haired brides, we're here to help by providing you inspiration for short wedding hairstyles with [veils](https://www.theknot.com/content/wedding-veil-trends). Whether you have sleek shoulder-length tresses or a daring pixie cut, your hairdresser will be able to work with you to craft your dream wedding day hairstyle for short hair. Choosing your favorite is all about trial and error, so don't be afraid to test out a few different bridal hairstyles at your [hair trial appointment(s)](https://www.theknot.com/content/prep-for-hair-and-makeup-trial) leading up to the wedding to get a feel for what you like and don't like. That Pinterest-perfect hairstyle may not always come out exactly how you envisioned IRL, so don't be afraid to try a few looks until you land on _the one_.
