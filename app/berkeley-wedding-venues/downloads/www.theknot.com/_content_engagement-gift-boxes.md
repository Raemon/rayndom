If you know a couple who's recently announced their engagement, you may want to congratulate them with something special. An engagement gift box or engagement gift basket is a lovely way to express your excitement ([getting engaged](https://www.theknot.com/content/wedding-planning-tips-to-get-organized) is a big deal, after all). These types of [engagement gifts](https://www.theknot.com/content/engagement-gift-ideas) are great because the couple will get to enjoy several presents at once that all fit a common theme, but you only have to shop for one. Another benefit of choosing an engagement gift basket: it typically comes packaged for you, with the option of adding your own [engagement wishes](https://www.theknot.com/content/engagement-wishes-what-to-write-in-an-engagement-card) for a personal touch. (Translation: no more worrying about gift wrapping or finding a suitable card.) Ready to shop? Discover our favorite engagement gift box ideas below.
