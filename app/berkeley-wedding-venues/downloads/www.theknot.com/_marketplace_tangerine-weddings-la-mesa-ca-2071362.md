About this vendor

Located in San Francisco, CA, Tangerine Weddings is a wedding videography business. With a focus on cinematic storytelling and short-form videography, the Tangerine Weddings team will be able to perfectly capture your special day. Established in 2022, this company brings together a group of creatives to share their passion for storytelling with the world. They love capturing life's most important moments, bringing their own unique perspectives along with years of industry experience. The team will be able to guide you and your partner through the entire process in a friendly and calm way. The Tangerine Wedding team's goal is to give couples an easy and amazing experience by offering photo and video bundles, so that you get the best of both worlds. The team will collaborate with you and execute your vision and style. They strive to create a product that is an accurate yet elevated version of the best day of your life, from the ceremony to the reception and everything in between.
