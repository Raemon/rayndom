About this vendor

Picture this. Your beautiful ceremony is complete. Cocktail hour has passed and the delicious dinner and heartfelt toasts are winding down... Now its time to let loose and PARTY LIKE IT'S 1999! From A sides to B sides and deep funky cuts and slow love ballads, your guests won't want to leave the dance floor, shakin' it to classics such as Little Red Corvette, 1999, I Feel for You, I Would Die 4 U, Controversy, Purple Rain, When Doves Cry, Lets Go Crazy, Raspberry Beret as well as Morris Day and The Time’s The Bird and Jungle Love. Plus, we couldn’t dare forget the Prince/Sheila E hits The Glamorous Life and A Love Bizarre and Prince/Sheena Easton’s U Got The Look! Awesome pre-recorded music on band breaks plus MC services and announcements. We'll keep your guests dancing till they drop!
