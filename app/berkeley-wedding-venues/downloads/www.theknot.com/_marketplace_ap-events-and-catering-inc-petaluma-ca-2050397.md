About this vendor

Operating out of Petaluma, CA, AP Events and Catering Inc. produces delicious food for weddings and other special occasions. This team of professional caterers is led by Chefs Ali Deniz and Alex Pena. These experienced caterers love to create delightful food that flawlessly accompanies special events such as wedding celebrations. All menus at AP Events and Catering Inc. change with the season to ensure that dishes include the freshest ingredients possible. With a keen focus on local suppliers and organic food, you can rest assured that these caterers will also use the finest produce out there. Some of their menu selections include duck confit tacos, caviar stations, garden green salad, herb and shallot encrusted steelhead trout, and so much more. On the day of your wedding, these caterers can deliver all of your cuisines to your venue. They will get ready to serve up your food however you wish. This can include stations, casual buffets, sit-down meals, and everything in between.
