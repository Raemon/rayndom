About this vendor

We are a Benicia-based business specializing in video production. Our head videographer John has been shooting and editing music shows for years, focused now on capturing weddings on video for lifetime memories. Fade-Out Videography's goal is to offer a variety of packages with more affordable wedding video options. Our video style is energetic, upbeat, and sweet with a killer wedding soundtrack. With John's music skills from his years of editing music shows, he knows how is tell your love story to a unique rhythm that's tied to your heartstrings. Fade Out Videography customizes and helps you create content for personal use as well as all of your social media accounts and platforms. With permission, we can also link together your professional wedding photographs, add captions, put them to music, and make a professional video for you. We service the entire San Francisco Bay Area, Napa, Sonoma, and Sacramento. We are proud to be a minority-owned and part-woman-owned small business. We would love to help you document one of the best days of your life!
