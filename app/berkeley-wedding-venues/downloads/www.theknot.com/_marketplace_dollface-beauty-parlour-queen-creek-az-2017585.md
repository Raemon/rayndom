About this vendor

Jessica the owner & artist behind Dollface Beauty Parlour, is a wedding hair and makeup mobile styling service previously based in Santa Rosa, CA,(wine country) relocated to beautiful Arizona, This well-established company has been assisting clients in creating their dream wedding look for over 10 years. Jessica the owner and sole artist is well-versed in an array of styles and she keeps up with the latest wedding trends to stay top of their industry. Founder and lead stylist Jessica Ambrose is a Sonoma County native and has built her career by working in many notable salons & freelanced for top wedding planners in wine country, Jessica has always had a passion for making people feel at their most beautiful, both inside and out. With this in mind, Jessica opened her own salon in order to offer her clients a dedicated one-on-one beauty experience. Jessica provides on-site hair and makeup application for wedding parties. Before the big day, a trial can be held to try out different looks. Jessica can accommodate any style although she specializes in creating breathtaking vintage looks. From boho brides to striking glamor, this artist will work with clients to create their ideal wedding style. She can also provide bridesmaid styling and groom’s beauty packages for clients’ convenience.
