Bridal MUAH is a bridal hair and makeup team based in Livermore, CA, serving the Bay Area and beyond. This team of beauty experts incorporates their creativity into designing memorable hair and makeup looks for soon-to-be newlyweds. They create a bespoke look for each client to ensure they look as radiant as they feel as they walk down the aisle. We specialize in soft glam and natural makeup looks and timeless, effortless hairstyles! Bridal MUAH was founded by Taylor Cercone, a professional hairstylist who is very passionate about weddings. Taylor has loved hairstyling since she was a teenager, and she honed her skills while working in the Los Angeles area. During her time in Southern California, she styled celebrity clients and worked on award shows before moving back to the Bay Area. She's done artistry for Jessica Chastain, Amber Valetta, Rosanna Arquette, The Oscars, Emmys, Grammys, and more. Bridal MUAH is a team of experienced hairstylists and makeup artists who are thrilled to pamper you and your wedding party. They use the highest-quality products and tools to ensure your look lasts throughout all of the happy tears and laughter on your wedding day. Your experience includes a consultation, makeup and hair preview session, a day of beauty timeline, MUAH prep forms before your preview and before your wedding day, and on-site, on-location services!
