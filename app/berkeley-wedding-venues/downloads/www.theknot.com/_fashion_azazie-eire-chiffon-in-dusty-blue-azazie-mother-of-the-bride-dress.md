Description

Elegant and flowing, the Eire dress is a floor-length chiffon A-line gown designed for the mother of the bride. It features a flattering V-neckline, 3/4 sleeves with soft ruffle cuffs, and a pleated bodice cinched with a matching pleated belt. A cascading front ruffle and leg slit add graceful movement to the skirt for a timeless yet modern look.
