About this vendor

Operating out of San Francisco, CA, Audio Ecstasy Event Sound & Lighting is a business that provides weddings with DJ entertainment and other event services. John and Dianne Roca lead these event experts to offer you premier entertainment for your special day. You can draw on their skills and passion to help realize your wedding day vision. The Audio Ecstasy Event Sound & Lighting team provides services in the Bay Area and surrounding counties. Audio Ecstasy Event Sound & Lighting offers quality DJ entertainment, sound, lighting, planning, coordination, and photo booth services for your wedding.
