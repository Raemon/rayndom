### About this vendor

Prime Time Events is a premier provider of luxury restroom trailer rentals across most of California, delivering hotel-like comfort and elegance to events of all sizes—from intimate weddings and backyard celebrations to large festivals, corporate gatherings, construction sites, and multi-day occasions. If you are in the market for a venue in the Fresno area, we have one available and it comes with these beautiful restrooms, check us out under Venue's in The Knot. With strategic locations in Fresno (our headquarters in the Central Valley), Modesto, and Palo Alto, we proudly service the Bay Area, Central Valley, Northern California, Southern California, and beyond—ensuring reliable, timely delivery and setup wherever your event takes you. Our fleet features a wide range of upscale mobile restroom trailers, including: \- Climate-controlled interiors with AC/heating for year-round comfort \- Real flushing porcelain toilets, running hot/cold water, spacious mirrors, and premium lighting/finishes \- Options from compact 1-2 stall units to high-capacity multi-stall trailers (including ADA-compliant accessible stalls) \- Dedicated shower trailers for refreshment at outdoor or extended events \- Handwash stations and additional amenities for enhanced hygiene We specialize in making "portable" feel anything but basic—our units are clean, private, and thoughtfully designed to elevate guest experiences at outdoor venues, ranches, vineyards, beaches, parks, and more. Whether it's a Fresno-area ranch wedding, a Modesto festival, or a Palo Alto corporate gala, we handle delivery, setup, discreet servicing, and pickup with professionalism and 24/7 emergency availability when needed. Woman-owned and operated since 2017, Prime Time Events combines exceptional customer service with top-rated luxury solutions to ensure your event shines from start to finish. Contact us today at (559) 495-5555 or visit www.primetimeeventsllc.com to book and bring true comfort to your next gathering!
