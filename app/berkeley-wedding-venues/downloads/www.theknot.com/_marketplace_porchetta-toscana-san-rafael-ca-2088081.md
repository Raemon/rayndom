About this vendor

Porchetta Toscana is a wedding caterer located in San Rafael, CA. With roots originating in Italy, this family-owned and operated eatery brings delicious, traditional Italian cuisine to the table. Owners Elena and Gustavo and their family aim to provide couples with a culinary experience they will never forget. Elena is originally from Tuscany and was raised in a small town called Forte dei Marmi. There, her love of good food and cooking first developed. She has now passed down her skills and passion for food to the rest of her family. All ingredients at Porchetta Toscana are fresh and locally sourced, so couples know their wedding menu will include high-quality produce. The team will happily accommodate any dietary requests and needs and offer a complete service for the big day. Here, couples and their loved ones will be treated like family and enjoy a tasty meal they'll remember for years to come.
