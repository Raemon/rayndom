About this vendor

VAMA Designs is a luxury designer wear brand in San Francisco Bay Area! We are a fusion blend of Indian glamour and western designs! Our traditional ethnic designs are crafted with perfection and precision. We have created an international presence in one of a kind high quality couture designs, vibrant colors, rich fabrics and intricate embroideries finish to an excellence! We specialize in Bridal designs and party wear Groom Wear as well as customized Jewelry. Our unique designs are inspirations from Sabyasachi, Manish Malhotra and many more leading Indian Designers. Our boutique in San Jose has over 500 ready to wear latest designs of Lehengas, Shararas, Lachaas, Anarkalis, Sarees, Sherwani, Jodhpuri, Indo Western, Suits and Jewelry!
