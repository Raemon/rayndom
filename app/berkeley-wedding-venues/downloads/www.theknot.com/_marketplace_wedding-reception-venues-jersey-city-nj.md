### FAQs

#### What time of year is best for a wedding in Jersey City?

The best time for a wedding in Jersey City, NJ, is typically during the late spring, summer, and early fall. Late spring and early fall provide milder temperatures and less humidity, while summer allows for longer days and vibrant outdoor scenery.

#### Who can officiate a wedding in Jersey City?

Ministers of every religion and various government officials can legally officiate weddings in Jersey City. There are no requirements to register with the state as a wedding officiant.

#### How do I get a marriage license in Jersey City?

To get a marriage license in Jersey City, couples must visit the county clerk's office with a photo ID. For a marriage license to be issued, it's required that at least one of the applicants is a resident of the city. There is a 72-hour waiting period, and it is valid for 30 days from the date of issuance.
