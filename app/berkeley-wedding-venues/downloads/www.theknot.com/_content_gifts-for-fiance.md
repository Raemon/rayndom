Congrats on your engagement! Now that you've officially leveled up to soon-to-be weds, you've entered a unique time in your relationship. Whether you're celebrating the holidays together, a milestone birthday or another occasion, we can help you find meaningful gifts for your fiancé.

Wondering how to choose a present that reflects your new relationship status? It's not that different to picking [gifts for your boyfriend](https://www.theknot.com/content/anniversary-gifts-for-boyfriend) or [girlfriend](https://www.theknot.com/content/gifts-for-girlfriend). Thinking about their interests, personality and the reason you're shopping is a good starting point. You can also lean into a romantic theme with gifts celebrating your engagement and upcoming wedding. Whatever your approach, you can use our list of gift suggestions to guide you. Here are the top gifts for your fiancé for the holidays, their birthday or even your [engagement anniversary](https://www.theknot.com/content/engagement-anniversary-gifts).
