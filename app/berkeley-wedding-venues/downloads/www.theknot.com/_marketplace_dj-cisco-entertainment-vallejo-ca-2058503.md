About this vendor

Their impressive portfolio boasts working with renowned clients such as The San Francisco 49ers, Red Bull, California's Great America, and San Jose State University. With such prestigious affiliations, you can trust that DJ Cisco Entertainment LLC delivers excellence in every event they cater to. DJ Cisco Entertainment LLC is a highly experienced and professional DJ service located in San Francisco Bay Area and servicing surrounding regions. With over 20 years in the industry, they have worked with a wide range of clients, including professionals, celebrities, stadiums, festivals, high-end events, and large productions. Their services include Emcee, DJ, Karaoke DJ, Latin DJ, Wedding DJ, Prom DJ, Bar Mitzvah DJ, and Sweet 16 DJ. DJ Cisco, the driving force behind the company, has been honing his skills as a DJ for more than two decades. His experience spans a variety of events, including fashion shows, weddings, corporate events, private parties, clubs, festivals, and concerts. Throughout his career, he has had the privilege of collaborating with notable celebrities such as Robin S, Dev, Luciana, Franky J, and Baby Bash. Additionally, he has worked alongside performers and entertainers like the San Francisco 49er's Gold Rush cheerleaders. With a passion for music that dates back to his childhood, DJ Cisco's dedication shines through his exceptional mixing abilities and style. He continually expands his knowledge by taking classes at the Napa School of Music and produces his own remixes. His commitment to collaboration is evident in his seamless coordination with other professionals, including DJs, performers, and sound engineers, to ensure that every event runs perfectly, smoothly, and on track. DJ Cisco Entertainment LLC utilizes only the highest quality DJ equipment and plays the finest selection of music. From turntables to CD's, DVD's, laptops, and controllers, they employ cutting-edge technology to create an unforgettable musical experience. You can explore their impressive body of work on their Instagram page, @DJCiscoEntertainment. Whether you are planning a wedding, prom, bar mitzvah, or any special occasion, DJ Cisco Entertainment LLC guarantees an extraordinary event filled with great music and unforgettable memories. Trust their expertise and professionalism to create the perfect soundtrack for your next event.
