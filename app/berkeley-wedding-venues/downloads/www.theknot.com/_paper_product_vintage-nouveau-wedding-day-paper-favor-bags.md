Express gratitude towards your guest’s presence with this vintage thank you favor bags. They feature an understated design composition focused on typography. Each bag has an imprint area of 4.25” x 4.5” with a full art size of 5.75” x 8” (bleeds not available). Your treat bags will be lined with food-safe wax paper to keep the contents fresh.
