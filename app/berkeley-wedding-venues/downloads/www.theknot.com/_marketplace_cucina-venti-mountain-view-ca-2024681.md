Located in Mountain View, CA, Cucina Venti offers an elegant and versatile setting for weddings and special celebrations. With a sophisticated atmosphere and unmatched service, this property is a premier choice for couples celebrating in the Bay Area. Its prime location, adjacent to the boutique-style Shashi Hotel, makes it convenient for out-of-town guests, while its extraordinary culinary team creates bespoke menus that are a feast for the senses. Whether you're planning an intimate gathering of your nearest and dearest or a grand celebration, the team at Cucina Venti ensures every detail of the big day exceeds your expectations. The venue provides a wide range of spaces to suit events of all sizes and styles. The main dining room accommodates up to 100 seated guests or 150 standing, while smaller groups can gather in the private boardroom, which includes screens, projectors, and microphones for presentations or toasts. The front dining space and booth section offer a warm and welcoming environment for elopements and microweddings. Outdoors, couples can host alfresco receptions on beautiful terraces adorned with gazebos, chandeliers, and fireplaces. Beyond its stunning spaces, Cucina Venti provides exceptional services to ensure every wedding is flawlessly executed. With over 30 years of experience, the event planning team works closely with couples to bring their vision to life, supported by an in-house interior designer and a talented florist. They also offer an extensive vendor recommendation list, from DJs to photographers, to help elevate your event even further. The attentive staff, refined menus, and extensive wine list finish off the venue's offerings, ensuring that every element of your big day is perfect.
