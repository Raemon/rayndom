HopMonk Catering is a full-service catering company specializing in weddings and private events throughout Novato, CA, and the surrounding areas. With a foundation in the renowned HopMonk Tavern, we offer both in-house event experiences and mobile catering services throughout the North Bay. Our culinary team delivers thoughtfully crafted menus designed to suit a range of event styles and guest preferences, while our professional staff ensures flawless execution from planning to service. Whether hosting an intimate celebration at one of our tavern locations or a large off-site reception, HopMonk delivers exceptional food, warm hospitality, and unforgettable moments.
