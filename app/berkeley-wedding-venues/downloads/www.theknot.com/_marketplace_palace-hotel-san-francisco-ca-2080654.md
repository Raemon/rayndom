### About this venue

Palace Hotel is a romantic wedding venue in San Francisco, CA, belonging to The Luxury Collection, whose doors opened in 1875. Reminiscent of a bygone era, this enchanting venue transports guests back in time while maintaining excellence with modern style and amenities. An enduring landmark whose essence is firmly rooted in the city, this iconic hotel nurtures an atmosphere ideal for opulent weddings. With almost 44,000 square feet of lavish event space for your wedding, Palace Hotel invites you to bring your vision to life. Its gorgeous interiors feature soaring ceilings, archways, and columns crafted from marble and illuminated by soft, golden light. Say "I do" in the Sunset Court, a long room crowned with a glass dome. There are three exquisite ballroom options for your reception, each ornately designed and positively breathtaking. Crystal chandeliers, sconces, and large windows create a bright and airy ambience throughout the venues, complimented by gold and neutral tones and elegant drapery. Incredibly versatile, couples can host 250 to 1,000 guests, with ample room for dining, dancing, and live entertainment. As the night draws to a close, you and your loved ones can retire to luxury accommodations for the utmost serenity on your special day. Booking Palace Hotel for your wedding venue means you'll receive unwavering support from the venue's events team. These seasoned professionals deeply understand the industry and will ensure your celebration embodies everything you envisioned. Couples can get in touch to learn more about custom packages, secure their date, and begin cultivating their dream wedding.
