### FAQs

#### What time of year is best for a wedding in Orlando?

Peak wedding season in the Sunshine State falls in March, October, and November. During this time, the weather tends to be fabulous and not overly hot; your hair and makeup will thank us later!

#### Who can officiate a wedding in Orlando?

From ordained ministers (such as priests, rabbis, and other religious leaders) to judges and justices of the peace, you have a few options to consider. The state of Florida also allows close friends or family who have been ordained online to officiate specific wedding ceremonies.

#### How do I get a marriage license in Orlando?

Make it official by visiting the Orange County Clerk of Courts (or their website) to obtain the essential forms. You'll need to bring valid identification, and pay a license fee—then you're free to tie the knot!
