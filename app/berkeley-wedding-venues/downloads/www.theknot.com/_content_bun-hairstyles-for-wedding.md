Bridal looks and buns go hand in hand, whether a good side bun, a classic bridal ballerina number, or a messy boho top knot. The true beauty of any bun hairstyle for weddings is its versatility. They can be as simple and chic as you like, intricate and over the top, soft and romantic, or t[extured](https://www.theknot.com/content/wedding-hairstyles-for-curly-hair), boho, and [braided](https://www.theknot.com/content/wedding-hairstyles-for-long-hair-with-braids). Wear your bun tight and high for a more sleek and modern vibe, or keep it loose, low, and curled if you're seeking a more [classic or whimsical](https://www.theknot.com/content/romantic-bridal-hairstyles) style. 

[READ MORE The 18 Best Bridal Hair Accessories for Every Wedding Vibe ](https://www.theknot.com/content/bridal-hair-accessories)

Another thing to love about buns? They keep hair pulled back and out of your face so you can dance the night away worry-free. Hot out? Rock a fancy top knot. Buns also look killer with an open-back dress, though you can customize your bun to work with any[ wedding neckline](https://www.theknot.com/content/hairstyles-for-high-neck-dresses) or silhouette.
