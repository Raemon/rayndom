## Every Bridal Shower Sign You Need

Unlike other prewedding parties, a wedding shower has fewer moving pieces. Be sure to add these signs to your [bridal shower checklist](https://www.theknot.com/content/a-bridal-shower-planning-checklist). Additional themes or larger events may require multiples of certain signs. As a start, here are the ones you'll likely need:

  * Welcome sign
  * Cards and gifts sign
  * Favors sign
  * Bar signs (custom drinks, mimosa bar, etc.)
  * Food signs and menus
  * Signs for games or interactive elements (like a flower bar or "grab a ring" station)
  * Table numbers/seating charts for larger showers
  * Guestbook sign
