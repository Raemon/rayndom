### Finding Your Columbus Wedding Venue

If you're looking for something a little more secluded, the surrounding riverside parks make a lovely setting for newlywed photos. Columbus is also home to multiple stately manors overlooking lush woodlands that give a similar vibe. You're sure to find your perfect match!

### FAQs

#### Which seasons are best for an outdoor wedding in Columbus?

In Columbus, late spring and early summer are the best seasons for outdoor weddings. The mild temperature is perfect, and spring brings blossoming flowers to the botanical gardens.

#### Who can officiate a wedding in Columbus?

An ordained or licensed minister of any faith can officiate a wedding in Columbus. If you want a loved one to be your officiant, they'll need to register with the Secretary of the State of Ohio, a judge, a mayor, or the Superintendent of the State School for the Deaf.

#### How quickly can you get married in Columbus?

In Columbus and the rest of Ohio, you can receive your marriage license and get married on the same day. All you need for your license is a valid ID showing you're over 18 and to pay a fee.
