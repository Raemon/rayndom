Description

The Lucienne dress is an elegant strapless A-line bridesmaid gown in stretch satin, featuring an overlapping panel detail on the neckline and the option to add spaghetti straps for extra support. Pleating in the floor-length skirt enhances movement and creates a beautifully voluminous silhouette.
