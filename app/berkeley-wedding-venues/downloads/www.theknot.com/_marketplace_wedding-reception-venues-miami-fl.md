#### Is there a waiting period to obtain a marriage license in Miami?

All Florida residents have a mandatory three-day waiting period before the marriage license is valid. Alternatively, you can waive the waiting period by attending a four-hour premarital course from a registered provider. In this case, the license will be issued immediately.

#### Who can perform a wedding in Miami?
