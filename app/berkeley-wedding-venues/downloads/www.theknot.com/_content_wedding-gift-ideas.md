## What's a Good Wedding Gift?

Anything from the couple's registry is a good wedding gift. They requested each item, so you know they'll appreciate something from their wishlist. Shopping for a couple who doesn't have a registry? No problem. "If you're looking for something off the registry, I would look to [The Knot Registry Collections](https://www.theknot.com/registry/store/collections) for inspiration," says Sam Kaplan, a senior manager of registry at The Knot. "Our team has curated an assortment of gifts for every couple's interests." Find out which gifts guests love giving, the coolest experiential presents, the latest winners of [The Knot Registry Awards](https://www.theknot.com/registry/store/collections/registry-awards) and more.

We've also found great [wedding gifts for couples who have everything](https://www.theknot.com/content/wedding-gifts-for-couples-who-have-everything), from personalized keepsakes to memorable experiences they can enjoy together. Wondering how much to spend on a wedding present? The Knot 2024 Real Guests Study found that the average wedding gift costs $150. Of course, you can spend more or less than that depending on your budget. Read up on [wedding gift etiquette](https://www.theknot.com/content/rules-of-wedding-gift-giving) and use our [wedding gift calculator](https://www.theknot.com/content/how-much-to-spend-on-wedding-gifts) to guide you.
