## Short Wedding Dresses with Sleeves

[Wedding dresses with sleeves](https://www.theknot.com/content/wedding-dresses-with-sleeves) aren't just for winter nuptials. In our books, short bridal dresses with sleeves work great for any time of year. Sleeves offer timeless style, while shorter hemlines allow for breathability. Of course, for weddings taking place in colder climates, they can always be worn with a pair of tights underneath. (BTW: White lace tight are totally in right now.) If you're looking for short style wedding dresses with sleeves, we've got you covered with plenty of shoppable options below.
