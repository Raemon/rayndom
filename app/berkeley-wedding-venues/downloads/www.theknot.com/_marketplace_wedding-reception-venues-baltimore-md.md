With magical venues lining the streets, couples will have no trouble finding their dream setting in Charm City. ### Finding Your Baltimore Wedding Venue

Consider the city's cultural and artistic district for your pick of stately homes and well-maintained parks. While North Baltimore is known for several landmark properties, South Baltimore is sure to impress with its old-world charm. Thanks to the river that runs through the city, there are waterfront venues that you can choose from as well. If the idea of a waterfront celebration really speaks to you, the city is also home to some boat wedding venues!

### Activities in Baltimore

Complete your Baltimore itinerary by heading to Inner Harbor, a seaport and tourist attraction lined with hotels, shops, and museums. Grab a bite to eat at Little Italy or Fells Point, both downtown havens for foodies. You can also go bar hopping and taste Baltimore's history of beer-making with every sip.

### FAQs

#### When is the best time of year to get married in Baltimore?

With mild temperatures and picture-perfect backgrounds, spring and fall are the best times of year for weddings in Baltimore. Specifically, April, May, September, and October are popular months.

#### What are the requirements to apply for a marriage license in Baltimore?

You must be at least 18 years old to apply for a license. Although only one person needs to appear in front of the Court of Baltimore County, they should be able to provide information regarding both parties.

#### Is there a waiting period for getting married in Baltimore?

Once you are issued your marriage license, there is a waiting period of 48 hours before you can have your ceremony.
