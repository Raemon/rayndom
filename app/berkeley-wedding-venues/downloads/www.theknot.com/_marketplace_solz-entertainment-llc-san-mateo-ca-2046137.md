About this vendor

Solz Entertainment LLC is a wedding DJ company operating out of San Mateo, CA. Owner Albert Solorio, AKA DJ Solz, is a professional entertainer who has been soundtracking celebrations of all shapes and sizes for over ten years. Passionate and personable, he is ready to give your guests an unforgettable dance floor experience full of fun and flair. Over the last decade, DJ Solz has earned a reputation throughout the Bay Area for his dedication to providing above-and-beyond customer service. A consummate professional and lifelong lover of music, he is constantly looking to improve his skills, and uses state-of-the-art equipment for superior sound and lighting. With your vision for your special day in mind, he will tailor his services to keep the dance floor full all night long. Solz Entertainment is available to provide on-location DJ services to celebrations throughout the state of California and beyond. In your initial consultation, you will be invited to discuss the details of your day and musical preferences. From there, DJ Solz will craft a fully-customized entertainment package, including personalized playlists, professional MC services, uplighting, and more.
