Advice From The Knot

### Start With Your Style

Ready to shop for a wedding dress, but not sure where to start?

Before you visit salons, look at photos and save any dresses you like. Keep your venue and body type in mind to ensure the style fits your wedding.

### Understand the Options

Once you have some style inspiration, stay open to suggestions from your bridal salon.
