Music does more than fill the room on your wedding day—it shapes the entire experience. The best Christian wedding songs add depth and meaning, from hymns that bring a sense of reverence to the ceremony to upbeat worship tracks that keep guests celebrating well into the night. The right songs tie your love story and your faith together, making the festivities feel deeply personal and sacred. Some couples love the energy of [live wedding bands](https://www.theknot.com/marketplace/live-wedding-bands), while others prefer the easy flow and infectious energy that [wedding DJs](https://www.theknot.com/marketplace/wedding-djs) bring to the table. Either way, the music you choose will stay with you long after the last dance, which is why we've gathered some of our favorite Christian wedding songs—conveniently organized by category to make planning a little easier.

## Christian Prelude Wedding Songs

The prelude is the quiet stretch of time when guests are finding their seats, and music helps set the mood. With that in mind, the best Christian [wedding prelude songs](https://www.theknot.com/content/ceremony-prelude-selections) bring a sense of peace and reverence, reminding everyone they're gathering for something sacred. In many [Christian wedding traditions](https://www.theknot.com/content/christian-wedding-traditions), it offers guests a moment to pause, reflect and prepare hearts for the ceremony ahead.

### "Great Is Thy Faithfulness" by Carrie Underwood

**Lyrics you'll love:** Great is Thy faithfulness, O God my Father/There is no shadow of turning with Thee

FEATURED VIDEO FROM THE KNOT

**When to press play:** A hymn of God's constancy and care, this is a beautiful choice for string quartet or piano arrangements. It strikes a balance between reverence and joy, making it especially fitting for a quiet prelude. Or if you opt for Carrie Underwood's version, her soaring vocals bring a modern, heartfelt touch to this timeless classic.

### "It Is Well With My Soul" by David Phelps and Guy Penrod 

**Lyrics you'll love:** When peace like a river attended my way/When sorrows like sea billows roll/Whatever my lot, Thou has taught me to say/It is well, it is well, with my soul

**When to press play:** Peaceful and grounding, this church hymn for a wedding works exceptionally well as an instrumental. Played softly, it gently reminds everyone of God's presence and peace as the ceremony begins.

### "Come Thou Fount of Every Blessing" by Chris Rice

**Lyrics you'll love:** Come Thou fount of every blessing/Tune my heart to sing Thy grace/Streams of mercy never ceasing/Call for songs of loudest praise

**When to press play:** This beautiful wedding song for a Christian wedding only enhances the sacred, faith-filled atmosphere as guests arrive and take their seats. An instrumental version, performed on piano or strings, adds an extra-serene and elegant touch.

### "Be Thou My Vision" by Audrey Assad 

**Lyrics you'll love:** Be Thou my wisdom, and Thou my true word/I ever with Thee and Thou with me, Lord

**When to press play:** This popular hymn has been reimagined for the modern age with Audrey Assad's clear, soulful voice. "Its lyrics about God guiding your life make it a deeply meaningful song for couples entering into marriage," says DJ Andrew Figueroa of [Everlasting Sounds DFW,](https://www.theknot.com/marketplace/everlasting-sounds-dfw-keller-tx-981493) a Christian-based DJ company in Keller, Texas.

### "Goodness of God" by CeCe Winans 

**Lyrics you'll love:** 'Cause all my life You have been faithful/And all my life You have been so, so good/With every breath that I am able/Oh, I will sing of the goodness of God

**When to press play:** This prelude song "testifies to God's faithfulness through life's seasons, aligning perfectly with vows that promise 'for better or worse,'" Figueroa says. It's a heartfelt way to set the tone for a ceremony rooted in faith and gratitude.

### "Bless the Broken Road" by Rascal Flatts

**Lyrics you'll love:** Every long-lost dream led me to where you are/Others who broke my heart, they were like Northern stars/Pointing me on my way into your loving arms/This much I know is true/That God blessed the broken road/That led me straight to you

**When to press play:** This country classic carries subtle Christian undertones about God's hand in bringing two people together. While you might not think of it as a prelude song right away, the instrumental version creates a warm and reflective backdrop. Ask your band or DJ to slow it down for a softer, more romantic feel.

## Christian Wedding Processional Songs

If the traditional "Wedding March" isn't your style, rest assured, there are plenty of other Christian wedding [songs for walking down the aisle](https://www.theknot.com/content/songs-to-walk-down-aisle-to). Our playlist of [Christian wedding processional songs](https://www.theknot.com/content/ceremony-processional-selections) includes soft, sweet tunes that pair perfectly with your walk down the aisle, plus two beautiful, scripture-oriented pieces if you want to send your DJ a few instrumental wedding Christian songs.

### "Unfailing Love" by Jimmy Needham 

**Lyrics you'll love:** You're my unfathomable precious unimaginable joy/And you're exceedingly excited and abundantly more than I could ask for/With your hand in mine we will pass through time and space/And every second, every minute, every hour of every day I'll say/My unfailing love for you will not be moved

**When to press play:** This Christian wedding song highlights God's steady presence in marriage. As a processional, it carries a strong sense of love, commitment and faith down the aisle.

### "The Prayer" by Andrea Bocelli and Celine Dion

**Lyrics you'll love** : Can we touch the soul of heaven/Can we unite a sacred lesson/Every child creates a skylight of beauty/Can you hear cathedrals fallin'/All the universe is callin'

**When to press play** : A soaring duet that blends pop and classical influences, "The Prayer" has become one of Bocelli's and Dion's most iconic performances. Its message of love, guidance and faith makes it an especially powerful pick for Christian wedding ceremony music. Additionally, according to Figueroa, the song is "perfect for a processional, unity candle or communion moment."

### "Pie Jesu" performed by Joanna

**Lyrics you'll love** : Instrumental

**When to press play** : Drawn from Andrew Lloyd Webber's _Requiem_ , "Pie Jesu" brings a sense of calm and reverence, making it a timeless choice for special moments like the processional or even the lighting of the unity candle. This instrumental rendition is especially versatile and works well for pretty much all parts of a Christian wedding service.

### "Soon" by Hillsong United 

**Lyrics you'll love** : I will be with the One I love/With unveiled face I'll see Him/There my soul will be satisfied/Soon and very soon

**When to press play** : This praise and worship wedding song is renowned for its tender lyrics, making it a meaningful addition to a Christian wedding. While Hillsong's version is the most widely known, there are many other renditions to choose from, affording couples the opportunity to select one that best suits their preferences.

### "When I Say I Do" by Matthew West 

**Lyrics you'll love** : There must be a God/I believe it's true/'Cause I can see His love/When I look at you/And He must have a plan/For this crazy life/'Cause He brought you here/And placed you by my side

**When to press play** : A perfect choice for your processional, this song beautifully intertwines love and faith. It's especially meaningful for couples who want to showcase the spiritual side of their relationship.

### "Alla Luce del Dio" by Mark Magnuson 

**Lyrics you'll love:** Instrumental

**When to press play** : This instrumental song sets a beautiful, peaceful tone for the ceremony, especially as you walk down the aisle. There are a few different versions out there, so you can choose the one that best matches your style—and since all are stunning, you simply can't go wrong.

### "I Will Be Here" by Steven Curtis Chapman

**Lyrics you'll love:** I will be here and you can cry on my shoulder/When the mirror tells us we're older/I will hold you and/I will be here to watch you grow in beauty/And tell you all the things you are to me/I will be here

**When to press play** : One of the most beloved contemporary Christian wedding songs, "I Will Be Here," has been a ceremony staple for decades. Its message of lifelong commitment resonates across generations, and countless covers—both vocal and instrumental—allow couples to find a version that best fits their style.

## Christian Wedding Ceremony Songs

Your wedding [ceremony music](https://www.theknot.com/content/ceremony-music-basics) should reflect both your love and your faith. Pairing these selections with a [Christian wedding ceremony script](https://www.theknot.com/content/protestant-wedding-ceremony-script) or meaningful [Bible verses about love](https://www.theknot.com/content/bible-verses-about-marriage) can make the moment even more powerful.

### "The Marriage Prayer," by John Waller

**Lyrics you'll love:** Father, I said, ''Til death do us part"/I want to mean it with all of my heart

**When to press play:** "The Marriage Prayer" is a heartfelt and aptly named choice for your wedding ceremony, with lyrics that invite God's blessing and guidance over your marriage. It's especially moving when played during the vow, unity ceremony or even as a recessional when you walk back up the aisle together. 

### "How Great Thou Art" by Carrie Underwood

**Lyrics you'll love:** Oh, Lord my God/When I in awesome wonder/Consider all the worlds/Thy hands have made/I see the stars/I hear the rolling thunder/Thy power throughout/The universe displayed

**When to press play:** Few hymns carry the weight and beauty of "How Great Thou Art," which Figueroa describes as "a classic worship hymn that reminds guests that the marriage is ultimately rooted in God's greatness." Carrie Underwood's rendition is nothing short of spectacular; her soaring vocals make it a go-to for contemporary Christian weddings. Elvis Presley also recorded his own rendition of this hymn, proving how popular it is across different generations. 

### "Love Never Fails" by Brandon Heath

**Lyrics you'll love:** 'Cause love is right here/Love is alive/Love is the way/The truth, the life/Love is the river that flows through/Love is the arms that are holding you/And love is the place you will fly to/Love never fails you

**When to press play** : "Drawn straight from 1 Corinthians 13, this song literally sings the 'love chapter' of scripture," Figueroa says. He goes on to describe the Christian marriage song as "timeless for weddings," making a beautiful choice for any moment of the ceremony. 

### "Amazing Grace" by Andrea Bocelli

**Lyrics you'll love:** Amazing grace, how sweet the sound/That saved a wretch like me/I once was lost but now I'm found/Was blind but now I see.

**When to press play:** One of the most popular wedding hymns, "Amazing Grace," takes on an even more moving quality with Andrea Bocelli's soaring vocals. Its themes of redemption and gratitude are a powerful fit for the processional, communion or a quiet moment of reflection within the ceremony.

### "Wedding Song (There is Love)" by Peter, Paul & Mary

**Lyrics you'll love:** Well a man shall leave his mother and a woman leave her home/They shall travel on to where the two shall be as one/As it was in the beginning, is now until the end/Woman draws her life from man and gives it back again/And there's love

**When to press play:** While this song was written in the early 1970s, it remains a staple at Christian weddings to this day. Its gentle folk sound and biblical imagery make it especially meaningful for exchanging vows or lighting the unity candle, highlighting the sacredness of two becoming one.

### "The Lord's Prayer" by Andrea Bocelli feat. The Mormon Tabernacle Choir

**Lyrics you'll love:** Our Father, who art in heaven/Hallowed be thy Name/Thy kingdom come/Thy will be done/ On earth as it is in Heaven

**When to press play:** Backed by the Mormon Tabernacle Choir, Andrea Bocelli's rendition of "The Lord's Prayer" is equal parts moving and majestic. It's a beautiful fit for a prayerful moment in the ceremony, especially in a church setting, making it one of the most beautiful wedding worship songs of all time. 

### "The Day Before You" by Matthew West

**Lyrics you'll love:** I had all but given up on finding the one/That I could fall into/On the day before you/I was ready to settle for less than love/And not much more/There was no such thing as a dream come true/But that was on the day before you

**When to press play:** This heartfelt ballad captures the transformative power of finding your perfect match. The romantic lyrics also make it ideal for the wedding processional or first dance.

### "I Choose You (The Wedding Song)" by Ryann Darling

**Lyrics you'll love:** And I'd say I do/For the rest of my life/With all that I have, I do/And I will/When the sky is falling/I promise you, I'm all in/No turning back/And every day, every moment/Every breath you take/I choose you

**When to press play:** Written as a modern wedding anthem, this song is full of promise and commitment. Its tender lyrics set the perfect tone for a walk down the aisle, the exchange of vows or a memorable first dance.

## Christian Wedding Recessional Songs

Amp up the fun vibes and happy mood at the end of your ceremony by opting for one of these top Christian music wedding songs when [planning your recessional](https://www.theknot.com/content/how-does-the-recessional-work). Maybe you'll choose an upbeat song with meaningful lyrics that will propel you into your fabulous reception, or maybe you prefer powerful, lilting instrumentals like "Ode to Joy" or the "Hallelujah Chorus."

### "Two Becoming One," Jonathan & Emily Martin

**Lyrics you'll love:** We are two becoming one/Here I am, here's all of me/You have all that once was mine/And here's my promise/I'm yours to hold/I'll stay here by your side

**When to press play:** This tender tune from real-life married couple Jonathan and Emil Martin celebrates unity in marriage, making it a natural fit for the recessional. Have your wedding DJ or live band play it to bring both energy and meaning as you make your official debut as a married couple.

### "City of Joy" by The Sound

**Lyrics you'll love:** Well I've heard about a place, where there's no more night/Where the lion and the lamb are sleeping side by side/Well I've heard that there are colors that no one can describe/We are bound for, we are bound for the City of Joy

**When to press play:** With lyrics that point to heaven's promise of joy and peace, this upbeat track is perfect for a celebratory recessional, as it sets a hopeful, energetic tone that carries you straight from the altar into your reception. Expect smiles and happy tears all around. 

### "Multiplied" by NEEDTOBREATHE

**Lyrics you'll love:** God of mercy sweet love of mine/I have surrendered to Your design/May this offering stretch across the skies/And these Hallelujahs be multiplied

**When to press play:** This worship anthem blends heartfelt lyrics with soaring energy, making it a powerful choice for Christian couples. Consider playing it on the walk back up the aisle when you want joy and faith to overflow.

### "Good Day for Marrying You" by Dave Barnes

**Lyrics you'll love** : I'll call my mama and daddy/Baby, you call yours too/Put on your white dress/Yeah 'cause, oh baby, it's a good day for marrying you

**When to press play:** This song is light, sweet and downright fun. It sends you and your guests out the door with laughter, smiles and energy to keep the vibes high as you enter the cocktail hour and reception.

### "Ode to Joy" by Beethoven, performed by Joanna

**Lyrics you'll love** : Instrumental

**When to press play:** With its triumphant melodies, "Ode to Joy" makes a classic, elegant recessional choice that works beautifully in any setting. The familiar opening notes set a grand, uplifting tone to signal the celebration ahead.

### "Thankful for You" by TobyMac

**Lyrics you'll love:** But I can say without a doubt/I never had it figured out/Lord you open every door I've stepped through yea?/And I try to keep my head about it

**When to press play:** This terrific track sets a thankful and celebratory tone. It's a fun option for couples who want their recessional to reflect both their joy in marriage and their gratitude toward God.

## Christian First Dance Wedding Songs

Make your first dance a gorgeous reflection of your unity with these Christian love songs for your wedding. With incredible lyrics that will make your guests swoon during your special moment, any one of these options could be the perfect first dance song for your wedding musicians to play. For more inspiration on choosing the right song and making the moment your own, don't miss our [first dance tips](https://www.theknot.com/content/first-dance-tips).

**Lyrics you'll love** : It was a day/Just like any other day/I was a boy/Just like every other boy/But when the girl/Unlike any I had seen/It's like she stepped out of a dream/And into my world

**When to press play:** This tender ballad captures the magic of finding the one. For this reason, it's an absolute must-play for couples looking to achieve a fairy-tale-worthy first dance.

**Lyrics you'll love:** Like the first man /I was cut so deep by Heaven's knife/When I awoke from my sleep/Oh my Lord, she's beautiful/She's a part of me/She's my wife

### "You Raise Me Up" by Josh Groban

**Lyrics you'll love** : You raise me up, so I can stand on mountains/You raise me up, to walk on stormy seas/I am strong, when I am on your shoulders/You raise me up to more than I can be

### "I Promise (Wedding Song)" by CeCe Winans

**Lyrics you'll love:** I will love you faithfully, forever unconditionally/And my love I promise/Everything I have is yours/You're everything I prayed and waited for/And my love I promise you

### "Loyal" by Lauren Daigle

### "With Arms Wide Open" by Creed

**Lyrics you'll love:** Well I just heard the news today/It seems my life is going to change/I close my eyes, begin to pray/Then tears of joy stream down my face

**When to press play:** While not a traditional Christian wedding song, this heartfelt rock ballad can still be a powerful choice for couples who connect with its message of love and new beginnings. Of course, it's a given for any Christian rock-loving duos! 

### "Bread and Wine" by Josh Garrels

**Lyrics you'll love:** If I fall, I fall alone, but two can help to bear the load/A threefold cord is hard to break/All I have I give to you if you will share your sorrows too/Then joy will be the crown upon our heads/My friend

**When to press play:** With lyrics about unity and shared burdens, this track reflects the depth of lifelong commitment. It's a thoughtful selection for couples who want their first dance to feel both spiritual and poetic.

### "God Gave Me You" by Blake Shelton

**Lyrics you'll love** : Half of what I could be/I can't do without you/We are stitched together/And what love has tethered/I pray we never undo

**When to press play:** This modern love ballad is "rooted in gratitude to God for your partner," Figueroa says, who goes on to describe it as "a perfect first dance option." And with Blake Shelton's signature country twang, it's especially meaningful for couples who love country music. 

### "Household of Faith" by Steve Green and Marijean Green

**"Lyrics you'll love:** We'll build a household of faith/That together we can make/And when the strong winds blow/It won't fall down/As one in Him will grow/And the whole world will know/We are a household of faith

### "I Have and Always Will" by Dave Barnes

**Lyrics you'll love** : I guess it's because I just do/Following heaven's clues/This is a big mystery/How I found you found me

**When to press play:** Straightforward and sincere, this song is a clear-eyed declaration of lifelong love. It's an easy choice for couples who want their first dance to feel genuine, rather than overly formal.

### "When God Made You" by Newsong

**Lyrics you'll love:** Oh, I wonder what God was thinking/When he created you/I wonder if he knew everything I would need/Because he made all my dreams come true/When God made You/He must have been thinking about me

**When to press play:** Few songs capture the romance of a wedding day quite like this duet. Whether you use it for the first dance or during a unity candle, it feels intimate and worshipful all at once. Think of it like a prayer wrapped up in a love song.

### "Loved You Before" by Natalie Taylor

**Lyrics you'll love** : I love you, I love you, I love you, I do/I've loved you before I even knew you/I love you, I love you, I love you, I do/I've loved you before I even knew you

**When to press play:** A modern, romantic pick, this song feels fresh and effortless, like it could've been written just for the two of you. If we had to describe it in three words, we'd say sweet, simple and totally swoon-worthy.

## Christian Mother-Son Wedding Dance Songs

It can be difficult to find great Christian [mother-son dance songs](https://www.theknot.com/content/mother-son-wedding-songs) for your wedding band or DJ to play—but we're here to help. Ahead, we've rounded up the [best wedding songs](https://www.theknot.com/content/best-wedding-songs) for a mother-son dance that honor your faith and your special bond. 

### "Difference Maker" by NEEDTOBREATHE

**Lyrics you'll love:** Yeah, isn't it amazing how a man can find himself alone/Call into the darkness for an answer that he's never known/Yeah, isn't it amazing how God can take a broken man/Yeah, let him find a fortune, let him ruin it with his own two hands

**When to press play:** Reflective and soulful, this is the kind of song that fits seamlessly into dinner or cocktail hour. It creates a thoughtful backdrop that invites guests to pause, connect and really bask in the moment.

### "Because You Loved Me" by Celine Dion

**Lyrics you'll love:** You gave me wings and made me fly/You touched my hand I could touch the sky/I lost my faith, you gave it back to me/You said no star was out of reach

**When to press play:** Celine always knows how to bring the emotion, especially in this song. The lyrics tug right at the heart of what moms do best—lifting us up and loving us unconditionally. Expect a few tears (and that's the point).
