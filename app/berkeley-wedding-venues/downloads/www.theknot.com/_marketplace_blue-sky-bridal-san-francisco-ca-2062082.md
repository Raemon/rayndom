Blue Sky Bridal is a newly opened bridal consignment store in San Francisco on Union Street. We may be new to San Francisco but we've been consigning wedding dresses since 2007 in Seattle and Portland! Our bridal store specializes in off-the-rack wedding dresses which include brand new, designer samples and once-wed gowns. All gowns are offered 15-60% below retail prices and available in sizes 0-28. Clients are welcome to shop in one of Blue Sky Bridal's stores in San Francisco, Seattle or Portland. Each store feature an ever-changing inventory curated specifically for its brides. Through a private appointment working one-on-one with a knowledgeable stylist, you can discuss dress styles and designers before selecting your gowns to try on. Our stylists don't work on commission so there's never any pressure to buy. Blue Sky Bridal offers its brides sustainable, on-trend wedding dresses in a friendly and welcoming environment with trusted stylists Once you've chosen your unique dream dress, you get to take that very same dress home that day! Alternations are not offered in-house, but brides will receive a list of trusted alterations specialist who will help ensure your gown fits perfect! If you're looking for an eco-friendly alternative when it comes to your dress, look no further than this bridal store. As well as San Francisco, this company has stores located in Seattle and Portland. We've been consigning wedding dresses since 2007! We'd love to see you! Have a wedding dress to sell? We'd love to consign your dress! We are currently accepting gowns on consignment that are 3 years and newer. Visit www.blueskybridal.com to purchase or sell your dress today!
