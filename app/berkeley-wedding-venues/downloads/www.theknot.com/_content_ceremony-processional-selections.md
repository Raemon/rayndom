## Best Wedding Processional Songs

Whether played by a [wedding DJ](https://www.theknot.com/marketplace/wedding-djs) or an instrumentalist, these well-known hits will induce happy tears before the vows. (**_Psst:_** We've included some [songs for walking down the aisle](https://www.theknot.com/content/songs-to-walk-down-aisle-to) on our list, but look here for more than 100 other tunes to choose from.)

### "You & Me" by Dave Matthews Band

**Lyrics you'll love:** "We gonna take a boat to the end of the world/All the way to the end of the world/Oh, and when the kids are old enough/We're gonna teach them to fly"

FEATURED VIDEO FROM THE KNOT

**Why to press play:** If you're a DMB super fan, having "You & Me" as a wedding processional song is a must. This upbeat tune is sure to make everyone start dancing in their seats. 

**Best for:** The couple

### "100 Years" by Five for Fighting

**Lyrics you'll love:** "I'm fifteen for a moment/Caught in between 10 and 20/And I'm just dreaming/Counting the ways to where you are"

**Why press play:** Are you and your partner high school sweethearts? Then this sentimental song about one's first love is for you.

**Best for:** The couple

### "Your Song" by Elton John

**Lyrics you'll love:** "It's a little bit funny, this feeling inside/I'm not one of those who can easily hide/I don't have much money but boy, if I did/I'd buy a big house where we both could live"

**Why press play:** This Elton John romantic classic would sound great played by an ensemble of violins and a piano. We wouldn't be surprised if the tune made guests tear up. 

**Best for:** The couple or wedding party

### "Stand by Me" by Ben E. King

**Lyrics you'll love:** "When the night has come/And the land is dark/And the moon is the only light we'll see/No, I won't be afraid /Oh, I won't be afraid/Just as long as you stand/Stand by me"

**Why press play:** "Stand by Me" is probably one of the most popular wedding processional songs on our list, but for good reason. Let everyone know how much you appreciate your parents' love and support with this 1961 tune. 

**Best for:** The parents of the couple

### "Your Everything" by Keith Urban

**Lyrics you'll love:** "I wanna be the wind that fills your sails/And be the hand that lifts your veil"

**Why press play:** Not only does the music video for this [country wedding song](https://www.theknot.com/content/country-wedding-songs) include a couple getting married, but the lyrics even talk about one of the most romantic parts of the wedding ceremony. You really can't go wrong with this pick. 

**Best for:** The couple

### "This Will Be (An Everlasting Love)" by Natalie Cole

**Lyrics you'll love:** "This will be an everlasting love/This will be the one I've waited for/This will be the first time anyone has loved me"

**Why to press play:** Looking for famous wedding processional songs that are sure to be a hit? Then this classic jam is what you're looking for. There's a reason why this song is featured in almost every [rom-com movie](https://www.theknot.com/content/best-rom-coms) wedding scene known to man. 

**Best for:** The couple, wedding party or parents

### "Turning Page" by Sleeping at Last

**Lyrics you'll love:** "I've waited a hundred years/But I'd wait a million more for you/Nothing prepared me for/What the privilege of being yours would do"

**Why press play:** [_Twilight_](https://www.theknot.com/content/twilight-weddings) fans stand up! This tear-jerking song is perfect for those having a harpist at their ceremony or who simply love the idea of processing to the same song Bella did in _The_ _Twilight Saga: Breaking Dawn: Part 1_. 

**Best for:** The couple

### "What a Wonderful World" by Louis Armstrong

**Lyrics you'll love:** "I see trees of green, red roses too/I see them bloom for me and you/And I think to myself what a wonderful world"

**Why press play:** We don't care if it's too on the nose. We think this is a great choice for the flower girl procession because of the lyrics above. 

**Best for:** The flower girl

### "Everything" by Lifehouse

**Lyrics you'll love:** "Find me here, and speak to me/I want to feel you, I need to hear you/You are the light that's leading me to the place/Where I find peace again"

**Why press play:** With religious undertones, this is one of the best songs for a wedding ceremony processional. The song has the perfect tempo for a slow walk to the altar. 

**Best for:** The couple

### "You Make Me Feel So Young" by Frank Sinatra

**Lyrics you'll love:** "You make me feel so young/You make me feel so spring has sprung/And every time I see you grin/I'm such a happy individual"

**Why press play:** Need a song that makes people feel like they're floating on air? Add this Sinatra tune to your processional music for the wedding.

**Best for:** The couple, flower girl or ring bearer

### "I Choose You" by Willie Hutch

**Lyrics you'll love:** "Oh, how can I ever thank you/Except take you home and make you my lovin' wife/So we can always be together?/Oh, ain't that nice?"

**Why press play:** Declare your love with this '70s hit. The horns and Hutch's sultry vocals are the perfect combination for a powerful ceremony entrance. 

**Best for:** The couple

### "The One" by Gary Allan

**Lyrics you'll love:** "I'll fill those canyons in your soul/Like a river lead you home/And I'll walk a step behind/In the shadows so you shine"

**Why press play:** This song talks about proving oneself worthy of loving their partner. We think this country tune is a great way to hint at what you'll discuss in your wedding vows. 

**Best for:** The couple

## Traditional Wedding Processional Songs

If you're planning a formal affair, you may want to stick with the classics for you and your [wedding party](https://www.theknot.com/content/wedding-party-glossary). These common wedding processional songs are familiar to everyone and, as a bonus, many of these tunes have crescendos that are perfect bridal processional songs for a grand entrance.

### "Clair de Lune" by Claude Debussy

**Lyrics you'll love:** Instrumental

**Why press play:** Inspired by a poem of the same name, this romantic song will take guests' breath away. All you have to do is hire the right ensemble.

**Best for:** The couple, flower girl or ring bearer

### "Canon in D" by Johann Pachelbel

**Lyrics you'll love:** Instrumental

**Why press play:** It's a bit of a mystery why the "Canon In D" is known as "the wedding song," but that doesn't take away from the fact that it's beautifully composed. Consider having this airy tune played to show guests your love for the classics. 

**Best for:** The wedding party, parents of the couple or couple

### "Flower Duet" by Leo Delibes

**Lyrics you'll love:** Instrumental

**Why press play:** Hire some of the best vocalists to pull off this famous duet. Because of the song's title, it's a great option for those who plan on having flower girls.

**Best for:** The flower girl

### "Appalachian Spring" by Aaron Copland performed by New York Philharmonic

**Lyrics you'll love:** Instrumental

**Why press play:** A song with a quick tempo, this tune is great for getting people to the altar and in their positions quickly. We think this song is ideal for those having spring weddings based on the song's title. 

**Best for:** The flower girl and ring bearer

### "Hymne" by The O'Neill Brothers

**Lyrics you'll love:** Instrumental

**Why press play:** While researching songs, we noticed that many people said they used "Hymne" instead of the "Bridal Chorus" by Richard Wagner to make their entrance. Consider following others' lead by choosing this soft and romantic tune. 

**Best for:** The parents or couple

### "Arioso" by J.S. Bach

**Lyrics you'll love:** Instrumental

**Why press play:** This song is beautiful no matter which musician plays it.. Though, one of the best live performances we've seen is with a violinist and harpist. 

**Best for:** The wedding party or couple

### "Prelude in C Major" by J.S. Bach

**Lyrics you'll love:** Instrumental

**Why press play:** We love this simple and elegant piece. You'll need to find a top-rated pianist in your wedding location to bring this song to life. 

**Best for:** The wedding party or parents

### "Trumpet Voluntary" by Jeremiah Clarke

**Lyrics you'll love:** Instrumental

**Why press play:** Also known as "The Prince of Denmark's March," this song has the perfect regal tone for those who want to feel like royalty. Fun fact: This song was played at the [wedding of Prince Charles and Princess Diana](https://www.theknot.com/content/princess-diana-prince-charles-royal-wedding-photos).

**Best for:** The wedding party or parents

### "Für Elise" by Ludwig van Beethoven

**Lyrics you'll love:** Instrumental

**Why press play:** Add lots of drama with this upbeat number. You can use this song for the procession of the majority of your VIPs, since it's about three minutes long—the ideal processional song length is 3–10 minutes. 

**Best for:** The wedding party or parents

### "Trumpet Tune" by Henry Purcell

**Lyrics you'll love:** Instrumental

**Why press play:** A very cheerful song, "Trumpet Tune" would be perfect for those having their ceremony in a venue with tall ceilings where the sound can envelope the space. Even though many people have chosen this as their wedding processional song, it's a classic that everyone will enjoy hearing. 

**Best for:** The wedding party, parents or couple

### "March" from Occasional Oratorio by George Frederick Handel

**Lyrics you'll love:** Instrumental

**Why press play:** Love the sound of an organ? Then add this track to your list of songs for the wedding processional. 

**Best for:** The wedding party or parents

### "Canon in F" by The O'Neill Brothers

**Lyrics you'll love:** Instrumental

### Find your kind of venue

From barns to ballrooms, discover reception venues that feel like you.

**Why press play:** Charming with a slow tempo, this song is ideal for people who want violins and piano played at their ceremony. If you want to add more instruments, talk to your wedding music coordinator about what options you have. 

**Best for:** The wedding party

### "The Wedding Song (There Is Love)" by Peter, Paul & Mary

**Lyrics you'll love:** "Well a man shall leave his mother and a woman leave her home/They shall travel on to where the two shall be as one"

**Why press play:** We didn't only add this song because of its title. The lyrics are also perfect for the ceremony since it talks about love and "becoming man and wife."

**Best for:** The couple

## Modern Wedding Processional Songs

Surprise your guests with tunes they didn't know could be played at the ceremony when you choose any of these picks. They're also great additions to your list of [wedding prelude songs](https://www.theknot.com/content/ceremony-prelude-selections), so keep that in mind while you listen.

### "Birds of a Feather" by Billie Eilish

**Lyrics you'll love:** "Birds of a feather, we should stick together, I know/I said I'd never think I wasn't better alone"

**Why press play:** Billie Eilish has lots of great hits, but this one is definitely one of our favorites. Your wedding party will thank you for choosing a cool song they're familiar with. 

**Best for:** The wedding party

### "I Was Married" by Tegan and Sara

**Lyrics you'll love:** "I married in the sun (tell me where tell me where)/Against the stone of buildings built before/You and I were born (start again start again)"

**Why press play:** The song is about the songwriters experiencing homophobia as queer people, but finding hope in the end. According to Sara Quin of Tegan and Sara, the song also is an acknowledgement of how far society has come and celebrates the legalization of same-sex marriage. If you and your partner are a part of the LGBTQIA+ community, consider this hopeful song for your processional. 

**Best for:** The couple

### "Salted Wound" by Sia

**Lyrics you'll love:** "Tell her all of how you feel/Give her everything she needs to hear/Give your heart, and say 'come take it'/And she will see that you're a good man"

**Why press play:** This song is about pushing oneself to be with someone despite being scared to love. For those who have trouble expressing their feelings, this is a good option. 

**Best for:** The couple

### "Sweet Disposition" by Temper Trap

**Lyrics you'll love:** "So stay there/'Cause I'll be coming over/And while our blood's still young/It's so young, it runs"

**Why press play:** Add a bit of nostalgia to your ceremony. Featured in _500 Days of Summer_ , this beautiful love song is about innocent and young love. 

**Best for:** The wedding party

### "Love You This Way" by Aly & AJ

**Lyrics you'll love:** "I love you this way/I don't care, no matter who's watching/I love you this way/Let 'em stare, we got 'em all watching"

**Why press play:** Aly & AJ haven't stopped making some of the best jams, so why not include one of them in your ceremony? We love this dance-worthy wedding processional song. 

**Best for:** The wedding party

### "Home" by Phillip Phillips

**Lyrics you'll love:** "Hold on to me as we go/As we roll down this unfamiliar road/And although this wave is stringing us along/Just know you're not alone/'Cause I'm going to make this place your home"

**Why press play:** Choose this song if you plan on having an acoustic guitar at your ceremony. The tune also has more great lyrics, like "The trouble, it might drag you down/If you get lost, you can always be found."

**Best for:** The couple or wedding party

### "Find Someone Like You" by Snoh Aalegra

**Lyrics you'll love:** "I've been waitin' my whole life to find someone like you/Find someone like you/Baby I, I want this/I want this to last forever"

**Why press play:** We love Snoh's beautiful vocals in this love song. The soulful melody proclaims how much the person loves having their partner in their life. 

**Best for:** The couple

### "Crazy in Love" by Beyoncé (feat. Jay-Z)

**Lyrics you'll love:** "I look and stare so deep in your eyes/I touch on you more and more every time/When you leave, I'm begging you not to go/Call your name two or three times in a row"

**Why press play:** Want guests to get out of their seats and dance? Then you have to choose this upbeat song every Beyoncé fan will love to hear. 

**Best for:** The wedding party

### "Marry You" by Bruno Mars

**Lyrics you'll love:** "Hey baby, I think I wanna marry you/Is it the look in your eyes, or is it this dancing juice/Who cares baby, I think I wanna marry you"

**Why press play:** Whether you include the lyrics or go the instrumental route, this is one of the best pop wedding ceremony processional songs. Our favorite part of the song is the wedding bells in the background. 

**Best for:** The wedding party or couple

### "The Only Exception" by Paramore

**Lyrics you'll love:** "And that was the day that I promised/I'd never sing of love/If it does not exist, but darlin'/You are, the only exception"

**Why press play:** This song might seem sad at first, but in reality, it's about having hope about loving someone again. In the traditional [wedding processional order](https://www.theknot.com/content/whats-the-traditional-wedding-processional-order), the flower girl is right before the to-be-wed(s)' entrance so this is a great introduction song. 

**Best for:** The flower girl and couple

### "Real Love Baby" by Father John Misty

**Lyrics you'll love:** "I'm in love, I'm alive/I belong to the stars and sky/Let's forget who we are for one night"

**Why press play:** You've probably heard this song a bunch on social media, but we still think it's a great pick. This alternative and indie folk singer has lots of other love songs to choose from too. 

**Best for:** The wedding party

### "Too Good to be True" by Kacey Musgraves

**Lyrics you'll love:** "Be good to me and I'll be good to you/But please don't be too good to be true"

**Why press play:** This dreamy song is on Kacey Musgraves' new album "Deeper Well." We think this tune is great for a wedding since it talks about the excitement and uncertainty of falling in love. 

**Best for:** The wedding party or couple

### "Can't Help Falling in Love" by Haley Reinhart

**Lyrics you'll love:** "Like a river flows surely to the sea/Darling so it goes/Some things are meant to be/Take my hand, take my whole life too"

**Why press play:** Covers are also a great option for your wedding processional music. This Elvis classic, sung by _American Idol_ finalist Haley Reinhart, is just as good as the original. 

**Best for:** The wedding party

## Fun Wedding Processional Songs

If you're a fun-loving couple looking for an [upbeat love song](https://www.theknot.com/content/upbeat-wedding-songs) to match your personality, you'll certainly find it here. Consider choreographing a dance for your wedding party using one of these wedding processional songs, and have a look at [wedding bands](https://www.theknot.com/marketplace/live-wedding-bands) to play them live while you're at it.

### "All You Need Is Love" by The Beatles

**Lyrics you'll love:** "(Love) There's nothing you can do that can't be done/(Love) Nothing you can sing that can't be sung/(Love) Nothing you can say, but you can learn how to play the game/It's easy"

**Why press play:** Considered a hippie anthem, everyone will crack a smile as they hear this song. Fun fact: There were lots of other famous artists singing the background vocals, such as Eric Clapton and Mick Jagger.

**Best for:** The wedding party or couple

### "Dance Tonight" by Paul McCartney

**Lyrics you'll love:** "Well you can come on to my place if you want to/You can do anything you want to do/Everybody gonna dance tonight/Everybody gonna feel alright"

**Why press play:** Get everyone moving in their seats and toward the altar with this McCartney hit. To keep the integrity of the song, don't forget to hire a band that has a mandolin player.

**Best for:** The wedding party or parents

### "Here Comes the Sun" by The Beatles

**Lyrics you'll love:** "Little darling, it's been a long cold lonely winter/Little darling, it feels like years since it's been here/Here comes the sun"

**Why press play:** We know there's already a song from The Beatles on our list, but we had to include this one too. Also, the song has the perfect amount of cheer for a spring or summer wedding. 

**Best for:** The couple, flower girl or ring bearer

### "Over the Rainbow" by Israel Kamakawiwo'ole

**Lyrics you'll love:** "Somewhere over the rainbow/Way up high/And the dreams that you dreamed of/Once in a lullaby"

**Why press play:** Whether you're a _The Wizard of Oz_ fan or love Kamakawiwo'ole's voice, this song is a must. This version involves a ukulele so we suggest playing this at oceanfront weddings. 

**Best for:** The flower girl or ring bearer

### "I Choose You" by Sarah Bareilles

**Lyrics you'll love:** "Tell the world that we finally got it all right/I choose you/I will become yours and you will become mine"

## Unique Wedding Processional Songs

Think outside the box for your wedding ceremony entrance songs and opt for something unexpected. We've compiled a list of unconventional wedding processional songs that aren't overplayed, but are sure to give your guests all the feels.

### "Moonshine" by Bruno Mars

**Lyrics you'll love:** "On top of the world (the world)/Is where I stand when you're back in my life (back in my life)/Life's not so bad when you're way up this high"

**Why press play:** Don't sleep on this 2012 hit from Bruno Mars. This song would sound great with vocals or as an instrumental with the right band. 

**Best for:** The wedding party

### "Wonderful Tonight" by Eric Clapton

**Lyrics you'll love:** "She puts on her makeup and brushes her long blonde hair/And then she asks me, 'Do I look all right?'/And I say, 'Yes, you look wonderful tonight'"

**Why press play:** Need a gentle tune with some great guitar? Pick this Clapton song that's all about how much he's infatuated with his partner. 

**Best for:** The wedding party

### "Hoppípolla" by Vitamin String Quartet

**Lyrics you'll love:** Instrumental

**Why press play:** This song is ideal for couples who are having a live band for the ceremony. We guarantee "Hoppípolla" is a joyful melody that everyone will love. 

**Best for:** The wedding party, parents or couple

### "Thank You for Being a Friend" by Andrew Gold

**Lyrics you'll love:** "Thank you for being a friend/Traveled down the road and back again/Your heart is true, you're a pal and a confidant"

**Why press play:** Show appreciation for your wedding party's hard work with this Andrew Gold song. Warning: You might have to worry about your VIPs and your guests singing it under their breath for the rest of the night. 

**Best for:** The wedding party
