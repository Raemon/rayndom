About this vendor

Tastee Grill Catering and Events in San Mateo, CA, we specialize in crafting unforgettable wedding experiences through our diverse and customizable culinary offerings. Our menus feature an array of cuisines, including Mediterranean, Italian, Asian, Thai, and Indian, complemented by gourmet salads and the flexibility to create bespoke dishes tailored to your preferences. Our Culinary Offerings: Mediterranean Delights: Savor dishes like grilled lamb kabobs marinated in curry yogurt with pomegranate molasses and vibrant Spartan salads combining spinach, artichokes, kalamata olives, goat cheese, and more, dressed with a refreshing lemon vinaigrette. Italian Classics: Indulge in traditional favorites such as spaghetti with marinara or meatball sauce and chicken fettuccine Alfredo, showcasing the rich and comforting flavors of Italy. Asian and Thai Flavors: Experience the zest of Asian cuisine with offerings like sesame chicken, beef and broccoli, and green beans in black bean sauce, bringing authentic tastes to your celebration. Indian Cuisine: Delight in the rich and diverse flavors of Indian dishes, with the option to customize menus to suit your taste preferences, ensuring an authentic culinary experience. Gourmet Salads: Our selection of fresh, innovative salads add elegance and healthfulness to your wedding menu, crafted to complement any main course. Customized Creations: Understanding that every couple has unique tastes, we offer personalized menu planning to ensure your wedding feast reflects your culinary desires. Why Choose Us: Our commitment to excellence is evident in our use of the freshest ingredients and our dedication to tailoring each menu to the specific needs and preferences of our clients. Whether you're envisioning a traditional sit-down dinner or a modern buffet with interactive stations, our team is equipped to bring your vision to life, ensuring a seamless and memorable dining experience for you and your guests. Partner with Tastee Grill Catering and Events to create a wedding celebration that not only meets but exceeds your culinary expectations, leaving a lasting impression on all who attend.
