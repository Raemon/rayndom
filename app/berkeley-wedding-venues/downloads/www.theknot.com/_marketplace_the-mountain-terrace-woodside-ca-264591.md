### About this venue

![Hall of Fame](https://media-api.xogrp.com/images/6738d190-bb59-4af8-bbe3-df752b1f6f51~rs_90.90.fit)

AWARD WINNER (5X)

Thanks to recommendations from couples on The Knot

It's love at First Sight. When experiencing The Mountain Terrace, the rest of the world seemingly disappears. Stunning, natural beauty. Majestic redwoods. Mature oak trees. Sprawling meadow offering breathtaking views of the San Francisco Bay and the East Bay Hills. Gorgeous redwood decks. Rustic and charming interiors. Expansive yet intimate. Perfect for romantic weddings or commitment ceremonies of any size. One visit and you'll know we're your type. Celebrate your love and enchant your guests with your wedding ceremony on the expansive grassy meadow surrounded by towering redwood trees with views of the San Francisco Bay. Then take advantage of the wide array of photo opportunities on the property while guests savor the delicious hors d'oeuvres and beverages prepared and served by the experienced on-site catering team. The Mountain Terrace also boasts a gorgeous redwood deck with a seasonal tent, ideal for enjoying a delightful dinner with your guests at any time of year. The lodge offers an alternate dining space, wood floor for dancing the night away, distinguished antique wood bar and picture windows with views of the gorgeous outdoor amenities. Don't worry, you don't have to choose between these amazing backdrops for your celebration as the facility rental includes exclusive use of the entire property. All ceremony and facility setup and clean up, as well as food and beverage service are handled by The Mountain Terrace's expert events team. Event services offered include the following: \- 8 hour rental (including 2 hours of set up, 5 hour event and 1 hour of clean up) with the option of adding overtime \- Event planning support \- Menu tasting and menu customization \- Tables, chairs and tableware for 250 wedding guests \- Wedding rentals \- Ceremony arbor \- Full bar with various package options \- Bridal suite \- Ample parking (with parking attendants to greet your guests upon arrival) \- Seasonal Tent over the decks \- Setup and cleanup \- Food and Beverage service staff \- On-site Banquet Manager \- Wedding Coordination (option) \- On-site accommodations for family and wedding party (option) The Mountain Terrace is a stunning retreat, just minutes from Silicon Valley. Conveniently located but leaving you and your guests feeling like you are worlds away.
