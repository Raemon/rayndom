Choosing the right groomsmen socks is a crucial step in curating your wedding day look. Whether you opt for sleek and classic dress socks or a quirky printed pair, groomsmen socks are a great way to inject some color and personality into [groomsmen attire](https://www.theknot.com/content/fashion-rules-for-grooms). You can also opt for a special personalized pair as a gift for your groomsmen to thank them not only for standing by your side on your wedding day, but for a lifetime of friendship and memories as well. Your groomsmen's socks can even feature a funny print or design to hint at an inside joke between the groomsmen. If you're not sure where to start, we've compiled our 25 top picks for groomsmen socks that run the gamut from dress socks to funny socks to custom socks. 

**In this article:** Where to Buy | Dress Socks | Funny | Custom
