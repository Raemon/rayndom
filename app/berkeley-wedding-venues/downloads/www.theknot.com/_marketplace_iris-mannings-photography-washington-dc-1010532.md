About this vendor

Hi there! I'm Iris, a wedding and portrait photographer for the vibrant, joyful, and romantic couples. Everyday, I'm learning what it means to truly live a full and intentional life. I believe in celebrating even the littlest of things. My desire is for you to feel cared for and genuinely celebrated. Capturing real, joyful, and romantic moments that define your lives and being able to share those celebrations is what I love most about what I do. When you combine those life moments and photographs together, your unique celebration is told in such an incredible way. I am so honored that my work has been featured in publications including ABC News, The Knot, PopSugar, Aisle Perfect, Washingtonian Bride and Groom, BellaNaija, Munaluchi Bridal and Black Bride Magazines just to name a few. I am based in the Washington DC Metropolitan area and available for travel worldwide.
