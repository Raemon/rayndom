About this vendor

Based in Concord, CA, Kosta Curries is a catering service that can offer deliciously crafted menus for your wedding celebrations. These full-service caterers create delicious Indian cuisine at an affordable price to delight your guests and their taste buds during an unforgettable celebration. Kosta Curries provides beautifully presented meals to elevate your occasion dinner. Having serviced memorable events of all sizes since 2017, this business and team of professionals have all the necessary skills and expertise to cater to your vision. The team specializes in gorgeous presentation and food decor to add something special to your dining experience. From Mehndi ceremonies to receptions, this company presents a range of menus they can customize to suit your needs and tastes. Their mobile setup means that Kosta Curries can offer their delicious food and convenient services in whatever setting or location your occasion is being held. Plenty of rental options for buffets, tableware, as well as front staffing and bartenders can also be provided to ensure a seamless experience. Live stations are offered as an alternative serving option, serving delicacies such as Pav Bhaji, Kathi Rolls, Chat, and much more. They are also happy to accommodate many dietary restrictions and preferences. Reach out to Kosta Curries for more information about their packages and services.
