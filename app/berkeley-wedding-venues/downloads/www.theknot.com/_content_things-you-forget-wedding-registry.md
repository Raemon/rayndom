There's a lot to keep straight while [planning a wedding](https://www.theknot.com/content/how-to-plan-a-wedding), but you shouldn't let that stop you from creating a well-planned wish list before the big day. After all, forgotten registry items will only become things you'll have to buy later. That's why you and your partner should do yourselves a favor and set aside time to learn the [top wedding registry tips](https://www.theknot.com/content/wedding-registry-26-top-tips) before making your own. Then, when you're ready, head to [The Knot Wedding Registry](https://www.theknot.com/registry) and start including [the best registry items](https://www.theknot.com/content/best-wedding-registry-ideas). But we guarantee while you're picking your faves, you'll feel like one or two things are missing. Check out our round-up of registry must-haves couples easily forget so you can make your newlywed life comfier and happier.
