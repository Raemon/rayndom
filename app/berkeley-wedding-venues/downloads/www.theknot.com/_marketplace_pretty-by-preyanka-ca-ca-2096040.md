Based in Rodeo, CA, Pretty By Preyanka is a beauty practice that offers wedding services. Multi-certified makeup and hair professional, Preyanka Kanda, can provide on-site styling and application in the San Francisco Bay Area and worldwide. She would love to help your wedding look come to life on your big day. Pretty By Preyanka is a California-based beauty company specializing in makeup and hair services for weddings and all your special occasions. Owner and lead artist Preyanka Kanda was born and raised in the Bay Area, originally from San Jose. As a first-generation college graduate from the University of California, Berkeley, with a degree in Sociology, Preyanka discovered her true passion in the beauty industry after graduation and decided to pursue it professionally. The goal of Pretty By Preyanka is to provide you with an exceptional beauty experience. Preyanka wants to help you look and feel confident through her professionalism, compassion, and creativity. Her makeup application and hairstyling skills will enhance your enjoyment of your special day.
