## Most Popular Wedding Flowers

These are the all-time most popular wedding flowers, according to The Knot research. Any of these [wedding flower ideas](https://www.theknot.com/content/unique-wedding-flower-ideas) would look beautiful, whether you're searching for types of flowers for wedding bouquets, boutonnieres or reception centerpieces. There's a reason these pretty wedding flowers are the most commonly used varietiesâ€”they're all absolutely stunning. Here are the wedding flower names you need to know:

### 1\. Rose

![A beautiful and romantic peach-toned rose](https://media-api.xogrp.com/images/828dcd4e-dabb-4b13-9e15-6ab5909c982f~rs_768.h-cr_0.147.1875.1553)

_Photography: Jamie Chung,__Styling: Sarah Smart,__Art Direction: Sarah Evans_

What's the most popular flower used in weddings? A romantic rose of course. Roses are beautiful, fragrant and versatile, which contributes to their status. From their use in many [different types of wedding bouquets](https://www.theknot.com/content/a-glossary-of-bouquets-and-wedding-flowers) to dramatic ceremony arches, there's room for roses in just about every part of a wedding. 

"A rose is a timelessly beautiful bloom with different shapes and qualities: standard, spray and the super-romantic garden shapes," says Hunter. "They're available in a really wide variety of colors and are fairly robust, even on warmer event days." The flower comes in myriad hues including orange, pink, purple, red, white and yellow.

[Rose Bouquets to Have & Hold](https://https://www.theknot.com/content/rose-wedding-bouquet)

### 2\. Ranunculus

![A dreamy pink ranunculus](https://media-api.xogrp.com/images/a4d96863-6c62-4529-b701-092084114207~rs_768.h-cr_0.247.1875.1653)

_Photography: Jamie Chung,__Styling: Sarah Smart,__Art Direction: Sarah Evans_

Ranunculus is one of the most beloved types of wedding flowers because of how lush the botanical's petal structure is. "I feel like I can stare at ranunculus blooms and lose track of time," says Hunter. "They're romantic, quintessentially spring-y and have a sheen to the petals that really makes their colors unique. The color options range from white and soft pastels to highly saturated hot colors, and they perform well in no-water applications like boutonnieres and corsages."

A standard ranunculus is seen here, but butterfly ranunculus, which has a more open petal structure, is another popular variety. Beyond coming in a few different shape varieties, ranunculus comes in a range of colors including burgundy, peach, pink, red, white and yellow.

[Admire Ranunculus Bouquets](https://www.theknot.com/content/ranunculus-wedding-bouquets)

### 3\. Peony

![A dreamy white peony](https://media-api.xogrp.com/images/1f33744f-ae05-46d2-8193-fc9713041902~rs_768.h-cr_0.147.1875.1553)

_Photography: Jamie Chung,__Styling: Sarah Smart,__Art Direction: Sarah Evans_

If you want a voluminous, lush flower with cloud-like petals, then the peony is your ideal type of wedding flower. Although the flower can be pricey and hard to procure because it has a limited growing season, it isn't hard to see why so many couples believe it is one of the best wedding flowers. 

"Peony is just the quintessential bridal flower. It's large and fragrant," says Hunter. An especially popular variety of peonies is the coral charm peony, identifiable by the bloom's yellow center. Peonies will work well in most of your [wedding bouquet ideas](https://www.theknot.com/content/unique-wedding-bouquet-ideas), but we also love to see them used in low reception centerpieces.

[Pretty Peony Bouquets](https://www.theknot.com/content/peony-wedding-bouquet)

### 4\. Hydrangea

![A gorgeous, cloud-like white hydrangea](https://media-api.xogrp.com/images/6d099c67-3f3c-403f-a750-28eca52be3cf~rs_768.h-cr_0.420.1875.1826)

_Photography: Jamie Chung,__Styling: Sarah Smart,__Art Direction: Sarah Evans_

Hydrangea is another popular wedding flower if you want something full and pillowy. White hydrangeas are often used as flowers for bridal bouquets, but the botanical comes in blue, pink, purple and green as well, which look stunning in flower installations, like arches and flower wall backdrops. Additionally, they're associated with New England coastal summers, so preppy couples, take note.

[Hot Hydrangea Bouquets](https://www.theknot.com/content/hydrangeas-wedding-bouquets)

### 5\. Dahlia

![Romantic pink dahlia flower](https://media-api.xogrp.com/images/633d145d-3dcd-4d56-b324-8a57e441a3a6~rs_768.h-cr_0.0.1000.750)

_[Liron Erel & Co.](https://www.theknot.com/marketplace/callie-hobbs-photography-denver-co-763698)_

It's not hard to see why dahlias are a popular wedding flower. Their lush petal structure is stunning and the amazing wedding flower type comes in a variety of colors and species to fit any couple and any wedding style. Cactus and pompon dahlias are two especially popular types of wedding flowers.

"Dahlia is my absolute favorite flower," says Hunter. "They come in a very wide range of colors, from subtle pastels to mixed color petals that seem iridescent or layered, and really richly saturated deep colors as well. There are many plant breeding enthusiasts who specialize in dahlia growing, so there are constantly more special colors and shapes of blooms becoming available. They also do not ship well, which makes them all the more special to harvest and design with as a locally grown flower."

[Dahlia Bouquets That Slay](https://https://www.theknot.com/content/dahlia-wedding-bouquet)

### 6\. Calla Lily

![Dreamy white calla lily](https://media-api.xogrp.com/images/a154f2ab-599f-4304-bd59-84bcf213b675~rs_768.h-cr_0.264.1875.1670)

_Photography: Jamie Chung,__Styling: Sarah Smart,__Art Direction: Sarah Evans_

Known for their trumpet-like shape, calla lilies are an iconic option for minimalist couples. They work especially well in bouquets for to-be-weds who want a long-stemmed option. And they come in an array of colors from maroon to pink, white and yellow.

### 7\. Gardenia

![A gardenia boutonniere on the lapel of a neutral-toned suit](https://media-api.xogrp.com/images/f03dbc78-dff4-4b6c-9f1b-e7b43e89463e~rs_768.h-cr_0.227.675.733)

_[Jillian Rose Photography](https://www.theknot.com/marketplace/jillian-rose-photography-westlake-village-ca-768116)_

Gardenias are a great choice for personal arrangements, like boutonnieres and corsages, because they smell divine. But if you have guests or loved ones who are sensitive to smell, these flowers should be used sparingly.

[Gardenia Bouquets, This Way](https://www.theknot.com/content/gardenia-wedding-bouquet)

### 8\. Orchid

![Gorgeous tangerine-colored orchid flowers](https://media-api.xogrp.com/images/8edcf33e-3adb-4105-9c87-1723b89fb8b5~rs_768.h-cr_0.347.1875.1753)

_Photography: Jamie Chung,__Styling: Sarah Smart,__Art Direction: Sarah Evans_

There are many varieties of orchids, all of which are beautiful and could be considered as flowers for wedding bouquets and arrangements. Dendrobium orchids are one of the most popular varieties of this eye-catching bloom. If your color palette includes purple, white or yellow, then consider orchids as wedding flowers.

[Just Really Nice Orchid Bouquets](https://www.theknot.com/content/orchid-wedding-bouquets)

### 9\. Protea

![A romantic and striking protea flower](https://media-api.xogrp.com/images/ae0960d5-2e87-4def-86f1-f016d717a476~rs_768.h-cr_0.157.1875.1563)

_Photography: Jamie Chung,__Styling: Sarah Smart,__Art Direction: Sarah Evans_

Protea are a fun and dramatic wedding flower that has gained popularity over the last few years. A king protea is seen here, but pincushion proteas are another popular variety of wedding flowers. Protea is a good type of wedding flower if you have a bold color palette filled with shades of orange, pink or red.

[Punchy Protea Bouquets](https://www.theknot.com/content/protea-wedding-bouquet)

### 10\. Lilac

![A purple lilac flower arrangement](https://media-api.xogrp.com/images/7089e314-4074-4cd9-b441-c91ba237b802~rs_768.h-cr_0.0.1000.750)

_[Amy & Jordan Photography](https://www.theknot.com/marketplace/amy-and-jordan-photography-scottsdale-az-564599)_

Similar in appearance to hydrangeas, lilac is a great option for purple-loving couples. Lilac is a great type of wedding flower because it adds height and structure to wedding flower arrangements. Additionally, lilac is a fragrant wedding flower and a nice option if you want to scentscape the wedding day.

[Lovely Lilac Bouquets](https://www.theknot.com/content/lilac-wedding-bouquets)

### 11\. Lily of the Valley

![A petite lily of the valley bouquet tied with a white ribbon](https://media-api.xogrp.com/images/fa6fa600-4ff3-4fd5-a5ba-40a671db1974~rs_768.h-cr_0.36.750.599)

_[Laura Gordon Photography](https://www.theknot.com/marketplace/laura-gordon-photography-richmond-va-559656)_

Also known as andromeda, this petite bloom is the preferred choice of British royals. While not technically a member of the lily family, lily of the valley is a popular wedding flower type nonetheless. In addition to being a good flower for wedding bouquets, the small blooms look great in boutonnieres as well.

### 12\. Anemone

![A stunning rosy-peach anemone flower](https://media-api.xogrp.com/images/db733198-9d68-4c49-8a5e-ffd22178e832~rs_768.h-cr_0.277.1875.1683)

_Photography: Jamie Chung,__Styling: Sarah Smart,__Art Direction: Sarah Evans_

Anemones are delicate flowers that come in a variety of colorways and are easily identifiable by their black centers. Many to-be-weds chose anemones as wedding flowers because of this dramatic and eye-catching center, which look great at modern weddings. You're probably most familiar with white anemones that have a black center, but anemones also come in berry, purple and red.

[Eye-Catching Anemone Bouquets](https://www.theknot.com/content/anemone-bouquet)

### 13\. Tulip

![A romantic pink and pale yellow tulip](https://media-api.xogrp.com/images/69289d0c-471c-4982-9af2-ccc5de9adad6~rs_768.h-cr_0.304.1875.1710)

"Tulips are wonderful because they come in a wide variety of colors," says Hunter. "They also are available in some really stunning double or peony-shape blooms for specialty work. We also sometimes will reflex their petals for a unique, ethereal effect."

### 15\. Carnation

![A hanging purple carnation garland](https://media-api.xogrp.com/images/3fbcbada-f0ba-4d67-8eed-a48b1b1d23e9~rs_768.h-cr_0.0.1000.750)

_[Lindsay Hackney Photography](https://www.theknot.com/marketplace/lindsay-hackney-photography-portsmouth-nh-994695)_

Carnations are [affordable wedding flowers](https://www.theknot.com/content/top-ten-inexpensive-flowers) that are hearty enough to be used in a variety of ways. Similar in appearance to marigolds, carnations are often included in Indian wedding decorations.

Hunter mentions that she loves carnations for their versatility: "They grow in a zillion different colors, including some really special antique-y tones that work really well for the subtle creamy or muddy tones that are so popular in wedding work. They're also super resilient to heat, so they still look great even on the most sweltering days."

[Cutie Carnation Bouquets](https://www.theknot.com/content/carnation-wedding-bouquet)

### 16\. Sweet Pea

![Sweet Pea florals](https://media-api.xogrp.com/images/a85db342-e519-41a4-a75b-cd76788d7e3d~rs_768.h-cr_0.16.1158.1560)

_Photo: Stocksy_

Sweet pea is an incredibly popular wedding bouquet flower, but because it's fragile, it needs to be treated with care. If you're considering this flower for your wedding bouquet, talk with your florist about how you can include it and ensure the arrangement is durable.

"Sweet pea is intoxicatingly fragrant, delicate and romantic, and evokes a bit of grandma's garden in the best possible way," says Hunter.

### 17\. Anthurium

![Man holding a bouquet of Anthurium flowers](https://media-api.xogrp.com/images/5dd22925-4f23-40c9-b6b4-de61b3b92502~rs_768.h-cr_0.33.1094.1492)

Want a unique and bold type of wedding flower? Anthurium is just the botanical you should consider. This popular wedding flower has a distinct look consisting of a waxy heart-shaped petal and prominent stamen. The flower is great for tropical and beach weddings.

"It's shiny, large and unique," says Hunter of the tropical bloom. "It signals luxury anywhere that it's included."

### 18\. Amaranthus

![Fresh Amaranthus bouquet](https://media-api.xogrp.com/images/397556af-6a71-4e9d-a7d4-31ef1f55310b~rs_768.h-cr_0.35.1080.1475)

If you've always dreamed about a cascading wedding bouquet or arrangement, then consider this popular wedding flower that's definitely trending. Amaranthus is made up of dense clusters of flowers on weeping vine-like stems that drape and waterfall gracefully.

"We're seeing a ton of it on Pinterest boards for this coming season and we're here for it," says Hunter. "It's textural, the green is a beautiful icy-olivey color and the rose and burgundy tones are gorgeous. It just adds drama to anything you put it in."

### 19\. Marigold

Hunter says: "Marigold seems to have been on a steady rise over the last couple of seasons. It works well in lush farm-to-vase style designs as well as mono-bloom work. They're also classically used in flower strings, which we love. They're robust and heat tolerant, and available in the most vibrant orange and yellows as well as softer, more romantic ivory."

## Additional Types of Wedding Flowers

While the flowers above are the most popular wedding flowers of all time, they aren't the only great flowers for weddings couples can consider. We're taking a look at even more trendy and popular wedding flower types. Keep these types of wedding flowers in mind while you're planning.

### Alstroemeria

![A bouquet with fresh Alstroemeria flowers](https://media-api.xogrp.com/images/c215732d-a08f-4ef0-9abe-5afd7c2f338f~rs_768.h-cr_0.0.1000.750)

_[Sarah and Rachel Photography](https://www.theknot.com/marketplace/sarah-and-rachel-photography-noblesville-in-588674)_

Also commonly called the Peruvian lily or lily of the Incas, alstroemeria is a popular wedding flower that's identifiable by the speckle marks on its petals. The most popular shades of alstroemeria, which is native to South America, are purple and red.

### Amaryllis

![A flower arrangement in a gold vessel with pink amaryllis flowers](https://media-api.xogrp.com/images/6a77bed1-dae8-4a99-a836-6e3f25d0aa87~rs_768.h-cr_0.249.750.812)

_[Awake Photography](https://www.awakephotographers.com)_

Amaryllis is a great flower for couples planning a Christmas wedding, if you're concerned with [wedding flowers by season](https://www.theknot.com/content/your-guide-to-wedding-flowers). The popular wedding flower type grows from a bulb and is native to the Western Cape region of South Africa. White, red and orange are popular colors for this wedding flower.

### Astilbe

![A bud vase wedding centerpiece with astilbe flowers](https://media-api.xogrp.com/images/3701faf2-2153-4935-b131-570d43e84a19~rs_768.h-cr_0.217.750.780)

_[Lizzie and Marco](https://lizzieandmarco.com)_

Astilbe is a great filler flower. While it's similar in color to amaranthus, its shape is the complete opposite of amaranthus. Astilbe, also referred to as false goat's beard and false spirea, has a strong stem with bright plume-like flowers.

### Astrantia

![A romantic bouquet of astrantia flowers](https://media-api.xogrp.com/images/617e9b09-ae75-4f77-aaec-0de25ae1d591~rs_768.h-cr_0.0.1000.750)

_[VUE Photography](https://www.vuephotographyonline.com)_

Astrantia, also known as masterworts, is a pincushion-like flower. It closely resembles scabiosa, and comes in a variety of shades ranging from white and blush to dark pink.

### Birds of Paradise

![Stunning tropical bouquet with bright orange bird or paradise flowers for a summer wedding](https://media-api.xogrp.com/images/a988673f-d2e0-48bd-9f6c-5825afd820ad~rs_768.h-cr_0.821.1094.1642)

_Photography: VANESSA VARGAS PHOTOGRAPHY_

Why does this popular wedding flower bear an avian moniker? Because it almost looks like a bird with its beak-like structure. If you're planning a modern tropical wedding and looking for good wedding flowers that are also a little edgy, this is the bloom for you.

### Black-Eyed Susan

![Summery and friendly black-eyed susan flowers](https://media-api.xogrp.com/images/e39fe82e-0431-472a-8719-7ed538fa6762~rs_768.h-cr_0.0.1000.750)

_[Via Imagery](https://www.theknot.com/marketplace/via-imagery-santa-barbara-ca-990231)_

If you're a to-be-wed dreaming of a wildflower wedding bouquet, then consider adding a query to your list of [questions to ask potential florists](https://www.theknot.com/content/wedding-florist-questions-before-hiring) to include this bloom in your selection. Scientifically known as rudbeckia hirta, black-eyed Susan is a flower native to North America and recognizable by its vibrant yellow leaves and black center (which resembles an eye).

### Bougainvillea

If you like marigolds and camellias, then you'll surely also like zinnias. Zinnias are a petal-rich flower that comes in a variety of bright hues ranging from pink and red to orange and yellow.

## Popular Wedding Greenery and Foliage

Now that you have a thorough understanding of the best flowers for weddings, let's look at popular wedding greenery. Though, you can also go for a monofloral look. The common varieties below are some of the most popular wedding greenery options used by couples.

### Air Plant

![Wedding reception centerpiece with cactus in terrarium and assortment of air plants](https://media-api.xogrp.com/images/46794417-e582-4501-b6d4-d23b5afbe74d~rs_768.h-cr_0.60.750.623)

_[Jenny DeMarco Photography](https://www.theknot.com/marketplace/jenny-demarco-photography-austin-tx-380666)_

Air plants paradoxically don't need dirt to thrive in the way that most popular wedding greenery varieties do. This greenery works best in centerpieces as opposed to wedding bouquets.

### Bunny's Tail

![Arrangement of bunny's tail stems used as wedding ceremony aisle decor](https://media-api.xogrp.com/images/9cbfa319-afbf-4b24-95b4-e3c377035a0f~rs_768.h-cr_0.145.750.708)

_[Katie Edwards Photography](https://www.theknot.com/marketplace/katie-edwards-photography-santa-barbara-ca-976272)_

Bunny's tail is one of the few wedding foliage options that isn't green in color. This grass is identifiable by its fluffy white ends that look like rabbit tails.

### Cactus

![A cactus in a clay pot sitting on reception table](https://media-api.xogrp.com/images/77f9bc1a-9ccd-4124-8867-10a8a040f062~rs_768.h-cr_0.151.675.657)

_[nbarrett photography](https://www.theknot.com/marketplace/nbarrett-photography-dallas-tx-598654)_

If you're planning a wedding in a hot, arid climate, like the American Southwest, then consider including cactus in your lineup of greenery for your wedding. There are many varieties of cactus to think aboutâ€”you could even get creative and decorate with prickly pear cactus and also serve prickly pear cactus margaritas as a signature cocktail.

### Dusty Miller

![Dusty miller boutonniere on lapel of a navy suit](https://media-api.xogrp.com/images/6b64dd35-f2c9-4872-a3fc-e6f5748480de~rs_768.h-cr_0.300.750.863)

_[Misty Miotto Photography](https://www.mistymiotto.com)_

Dusty miller is a popular greenery for weddings. This popular wedding greenery is also known as silver ragwort and is made of soft leaves with textured edges.

### Eucalyptus

![Engagement ring on eucalyptus branch](https://media-api.xogrp.com/images/0ad5704c-4501-4694-a8be-effa97a219da~rs_768.h-cr_0.133.750.696)

_[Callie Hobbs Photography](https://www.theknot.com/marketplace/callie-hobbs-photography-denver-co-763698)_

Eucalyptus is the most popular greenery for wedding bouquets. There are many varieties of the popular wedding greenery to consider, such as silver dollar eucalyptus, seeded eucalyptus, willow eucalyptus and true blue eucalyptus. "Its muted blue-green tones and resilient leaves are beautiful, especially in a rustic romantic context," says Hunter.

### Fern

![Cake decorated with ferns and scabiosa pods](https://media-api.xogrp.com/images/61d025b7-ddab-4290-bce1-0c2699ecbfc8~rs_768.h-cr_0.69.750.632)

_[All Bliss Photography](https://www.theknot.com/marketplace/all-bliss-photography-charlotte-nc-875286)_

Ferns are a great green foliage wedding option for rustic or woodland nuptials. Because of the greenery's large, feather-like leaves, it works well as a filler, especially if you're planning on a greenery-only foliage wedding bouquet.

### Fresh Herbs

![Fresh herbs decor](https://media-api.xogrp.com/images/aef2d428-3d10-4828-997d-c3a4e9f6fbe2~rs_768.h-cr_61.0.1033.729)

_Photo: meredith hanafi photography_

Fresh herbs are an unexpected addition to your typical slate of wedding foliage ideas. "We really love incorporating fresh herbs into our designs, like mint, oregano, sage and rosemary," says Hunter. "Their generally mild fragrance is pleasant, and they bring texture and a seasonal centering that we really like."

### Hops

![Dried hops used as wedding decor](https://media-api.xogrp.com/images/600dcf5f-205a-43a1-b930-561589af6400~rs_768.h-cr_0.219.750.782)

_[Jenn Emerling](https://jennemerlingweddings.com)_

Beer lovers, this one's for you. If you're planning to host your wedding at a brewery, why not consider decorating with dried hops to complement the space?

### Hypericum

![Groom with hypericum berry boutonniere](https://media-api.xogrp.com/images/4f886ac7-d740-46a1-84a8-62f4a2dc41dd~rs_768.h-cr_0.219.750.782)

_[Karson Photography](https://karsonphotography.smugmug.com)_

Hypericum berries are a unique way to add texture to a flower arrangement. We often see to-be-weds include these hardy berries in boutonnieres as some winter wedding greenery.

### Ivy

![Ivy covered white wedding cake](https://media-api.xogrp.com/images/8ff4d415-22d0-479a-b28f-abcb2e44bef1~rs_768.h-cr_0.45.1094.866)

_[Jen Huang Photography](https://www.theknot.com/marketplace/jen-huang-photography-new-york-ny-204000)_

If you're planning an English garden-inspired wedding then ivy may be the perfect wedding greenery option for you. Ask your florist to drape ivy vines around banisters and ceiling beams to make your venue feel like a secret garden.

### Lamb's Ear

![Lamb's Ear boutonniere](https://media-api.xogrp.com/images/799be385-d921-4404-a218-566de40d2184~rs_768.h-cr_0.1253.2333.3003)

_[Photography: Terri Baskin Photography](https://www.theknot.com/marketplace/terri-baskin-photography-fredericksburg-va-523362),__[Floral Design: BCG Events](https://www.bcgevents.com/)_

Lamb's ear is similar in appearance to dusty miller, but has rounded edges instead of textured ones. As the name suggests, the leaves of this wedding foliage are soft like a lamb.

### Lunaria

![Dried lunaria leaves in floral arrangement](https://media-api.xogrp.com/images/03937030-d190-4632-90a6-77bbd31834d8~rs_768.h-cr_0.79.750.642)

_[Jeff Brummett Visuals](https://www.theknot.com/marketplace/jeff-brummett-visuals-bedford-tx-822082)_

Lunaria is a unique plant whose dried seed pod branches are commonly included as wedding foliage in arrangements. The round seed pods have a translucent parchment-like appearance when dried that gives an ethereal feel to any arrangement that includes them.

### Magnolia

![Magnolia leaf wreath with monogram sign on vintage getaway car](https://media-api.xogrp.com/images/ac2a1cf9-61cb-4294-8893-833ce9cc4298~rs_768.h-cr_0.283.750.846)

_[Mike Cassimatis Photography](https://www.theknot.com/marketplace/mnc-photography-st-louis-mo-567165)_

Magnolia leaves are hardy and largeâ€”two great qualifications for wedding garlands and installations. Magnolia leaf wreaths and garlands are especially popular with weddings in the South.

### Monstera Leaves

![Monstera leaf bouquet](https://media-api.xogrp.com/images/d5ba048f-b5d8-4a4a-9acf-59365687b40d~rs_768.h-cr_0.394.675.900)

_[Photography: Mary Costa Photography](https://www.theknot.com/marketplace/mary-costa-photography-los-angeles-ca-391745)_

You're probably familiar with these Swiss cheese-like leaves, even if you didn't know they were known as monstera leaves. Monstera leaves are large tropical leaves that are popular at beach weddings.

### Olive Leaves

![A white wedding cake decorated with olive leaves](https://media-api.xogrp.com/images/92bcd09c-b8be-404d-9e32-626657ba5779~rs_768.h-cr_0.124.1094.945)

Olive leaves are a nice option for romantic vineyard weddings because of their soft, muted green appearance. They act as a greenery filler without being visually overbearing.

### Palm Leaves

![Boutonniere with dried palm leaf](https://media-api.xogrp.com/images/b615e562-93fb-4fae-8ae4-888d81e9d8c2~rs_768.h-cr_0.219.750.782)

_[Love and Covenant](https://www.theknot.com/marketplace/love-and-covenant-photography-inc-tampa-fl-1045419)_

Dried palm leaves are especially popular with boho weddings these days. Dried greenery is a good option if you want something that will last a long time.

### Pampas Grass

![Pampas grass round ceremony altar arch](https://media-api.xogrp.com/images/8c54d5e6-13a8-44c0-97d9-f8b792074ff1~rs_768.h-cr_0.255.750.818)

_[Katka Koncal](https://www.katkakoncal.com)_

Pampas grass had a moment in the spotlight and it's not hard to see why so many couples loved this wedding foliage. The grass adds soft texture to any design and is durable since it is used dried, as opposed to fresh.

### Rosemary

![Rosemary wreath boutonniere](https://media-api.xogrp.com/images/c6b4cca9-c8d9-4a9a-9a8a-5479c1cb5210~rs_768.h-cr_61.0.1033.729)

_[Photography: CASSANDRA ZETTA](https://www.instagram.com/cassandrazetta/)_

### Ruscus

![Bride holding bouquet with Ruscus and roses](https://media-api.xogrp.com/images/36df3fa1-bab9-4502-90dc-bc5f311837e1~rs_768.h-cr_0.262.675.768)

_[Cotton Weddings](https://www.theknot.com/marketplace/cotton-weddings-houston-tx-647642)_

Ruscus is similar to smilax and is often used as a greenery filler in arrangements. Ruscus is beloved as wedding greenery because it is simple and reliable.

### Smilax

![Smilax on tent pole of sailcloth tent](https://media-api.xogrp.com/images/d8afd5b3-9b75-4bc2-bc66-bba68e966115~rs_768.h-cr_0.108.750.671)

_[Lauern Galloway Photography](https://www.theknot.com/marketplace/lauren-galloway-photography-tampa-fl-828704)_

Smilax is one of the most popular wedding greenery varieties you've likely never heard of. When florists need a durable, generic greenery to serve as the base for a large-scale design they often use smilax.

### Succulent

![Groom with wool suit jacket and succulent boutonniere](https://media-api.xogrp.com/images/696d71e5-fcf5-438f-96b4-bfdcb5bbfb1d~rs_768.h-cr_0.0.1000.750)
