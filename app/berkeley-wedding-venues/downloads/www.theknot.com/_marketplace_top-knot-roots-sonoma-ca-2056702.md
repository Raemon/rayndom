About this vendor

Top Knot Roots is a wedding hair styling service based in Sonoma, CA. Owner Melissa is a licensed beauty professional with the experience and talent you need to create the perfect look for your wedding celebration. Top Knot Roots has serviced weddings and special events since 2015 and is licensed by the Californian State Board of Cosmetology. Whether you want tousled hair, touch-me waves, a swoon-worthy updo, or dainty floral embellishments, Melissa can style your hair to match your dress and wedding theme. Her goal is to help you look and feel your absolute best on your big day, and she can't wait to meet you and get started. Top Knot Roots offers on-site hair and tanning services for your wedding day. This team offers consultations and hair trials before your important celebration so that they can fully understand your needs and expectations. Whether you are looking for hair styling or airbrush tanning, these professionals will help make your big day even more special.
