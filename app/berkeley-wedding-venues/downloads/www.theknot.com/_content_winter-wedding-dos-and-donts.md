Hosting a winter wedding has so many advantages, from less expensive vendors to decorating with the coziness of the holidays in mind. Of course, some couples love the snow while others want a huge, rustic fireplace to curl up in front of at their reception. Whichever way you want your wedding to go, we've rounded up real winter wedding images to help you dream up your day. As you look through these winter wedding photos, remember that if you're signed in to The Knot, you can use the heart icon to choose the ones that inspire you the most, then [access your favorites](https://www.theknot.com/your-favorites/inspiration) any time.

## Winter Wedding Themes

So much of wedding planning depends on the venue and theme. Once these major decisions are made, you can figure out how all the other things fall into place, from [winter wedding invitations](https://www.theknot.com/content/winter-wedding-invitation-ideas) to reception decor.

### Vintage Burgundy and Gold Theme

![Vintage Burgundy and Gold Theme](https://media-api.xogrp.com/images/da75c36d-2e75-471b-b7dd-89584074fd66~rs_768.h-cr_0.90.1080.1530)

_[Photography: Photography Anthology](https://www.theknot.com/marketplace/photography-anthology-cincinnati-oh-2011633)_

These stunning ceremony programs were tied with burgundy silk ribbons to match the couple's custom wedding crest. Ornate, golden floral details on the bowl complemented the vintage brick background for a timeless, romantic ambiance.

### Celestial-Inspired Wedding Theme

![Celestial-Inspired Wedding Theme](https://media-api.xogrp.com/images/56b6324b-3951-40b1-aca4-0855baea8cdd~rs_768.h-cr_8.0.1072.1419)

_[Photography: Tida Svy Photography](https://www.theknot.com/marketplace/tida-svy-photography-los-angeles-ca-2016331)_

Get inspired by starry winter skies and choose a [celestial wedding theme](https://www.theknot.com/content/celestial-wedding-ideas) for your nuptials. Moon-shaped details and a metallic color palette will help you pull off the ethereal vibe. 

### Sparkling New Year Theme 

![Sparkling New Year Theme](https://media-api.xogrp.com/images/2522f099-78c3-41ee-9cc4-6f747b58b216~rs_768.h-cr_0.35.1458.1979)

_[Photography: CARTERelite](https://www.theknot.com/marketplace/carterelite-fuquay-varina-nc-540569)_

Sequined table linens were the backbone of this sparkling reception. Lush white flowers and gold-painted greenery in the center of the tables doubled down on the winter-themed wedding.

### Christmas Wedding Theme

![Christmas Wedding Theme](https://media-api.xogrp.com/images/41b6010b-d6a0-4337-956e-9d454df4e824~rs_768.h-cr_0.122.1458.2066)

_[Photography: Christy Tyler Photography](https://www.theknot.com/marketplace/christy-tyler-photography-chicago-il-1026360)_

This reception incorporated Christmas colors through classy elements like red plaid, boughs of evergreen and dainty red bows. Against the rustic wood table, a cozy holiday dinner was created.

### Winter Wonderland Wedding Theme

![Winter Wonderland Wedding Theme](https://media-api.xogrp.com/images/a6a3f587-fdae-46f3-832f-8c3a86a541b8~rs_768.h-cr_0.122.1458.2066)

_[Photography: Nadia D. Photography](https://www.theknot.com/marketplace/nadia-d-photography-atlanta-ga-393781)_

This couple created a winter wonderland wedding ceremony by decorating their chuppah with airy, white fabric and willow branches. Hanging roses and candles gave it that other-worldly feel, which could easily be done with a winter wedding arch, too. 

### Vintage Glamour Theme

![Vintage Glamour Theme](https://media-api.xogrp.com/images/912a0aeb-cc3d-404f-9b38-0e1b0193a0f0~rs_768.h-cr_0.121.1458.2065)

_[Photography: Trinity Photography](https://www.theknot.com/marketplace/trinity-photography-abbeville-sc-316822)_

Candelabras, rich red velvet and creeping greenery all tied together to create a gothic glam reception. This theme is perfect for winter weddings, where an enormous stone fireplace gives cozy castle vibes while keeping everyone warm.

### Rustic Black-and-White Theme

![Rustic Black-and-White Theme](https://media-api.xogrp.com/images/c30e8f98-6969-4f58-a6b8-832a3ebcaa44~rs_768.h-cr_0.243.1458.2187)

_[Photography: Emily Delamater Photography](https://www.theknot.com/marketplace/emily-delamater-photography-portland-me-353927)_

The epitome of rustic elegance, this black-and-white table is subtle and classy. The weathered wood table, greenery runner and jars of honey and sage create a warm welcome at any indoor winter wedding.

### Whimsical Fairy-Tale Wedding Theme

![Whimsical Fairy-Tale Wedding Theme](https://media-api.xogrp.com/images/5db0caf8-d60b-4628-bd80-949ff15d74a2~rs_768.h-cr_81.0.1377.972)

_[Photography: Abbey Moore Photography](https://www.theknot.com/marketplace/abbey-moore-photography-kentwood-mi-581408)_

A fairy-light-wrapped tree and hanging greenery help create this whimsical fairy tale. Set in a greenhouse, this winter wedding reception used tall green candles and textured florals to add depth and wow guests. 

## Winter Wedding Decorations 

From bringing in natural elements to more festive decorations, there are plenty of ways to lean into the winter aesthetic. Consider using some of these decoration ideas for a [winter wedding cake](https://www.theknot.com/content/winter-wedding-cakes), too.

### Find your kind of venue

From barns to ballrooms, discover reception venues that feel like you.

### Candle and Disco Ball Centerpieces

![Candle and Disco Ball Centerpieces](https://media-api.xogrp.com/images/d16d86d0-1ca2-499e-8ba2-b500ae81c1e0~rs_768.h-cr_0.122.1080.1562)

_[Photography: Ashley Izquierdo](https://www.theknot.com/marketplace/ashley-izquierdo-%E2%9D%A4%EF%B8%8F%F0%9F%92%8D-tampa-fl-964926)_

Tap into one of our favorite wedding decor trends by adding mini disco balls to your winter centerpieces. Velvety red rose petals and taper candles add to the rich theme. 

### Stump, Candle and Greenery Aisle Decor

![Stump, Candle and Greenery Aisle Decor](https://media-api.xogrp.com/images/9f6be092-73c9-46cd-ba09-30303642a880~rs_768.h-cr_86.0.1373.965)

_[Photography: Trinity Photography](https://www.theknot.com/marketplace/trinity-photography-abbeville-sc-316822)_

These [winter wedding flowers](https://www.theknot.com/content/winter-weddings-fresh-flower-ideas) are the perfect way to dress up a ceremony aisle for the nearlyweds. Robust greenery garlands and cut logs are pure rustic glamour. 

### Tented Greenery Tunnel

![Tented Greenery Tunnel](https://media-api.xogrp.com/images/f3db23d6-59c2-41a8-be0d-4101dff6acf9~rs_768.h-cr_0.129.1458.2073)

_[Photography: Brie Thomason Photography](https://briethomason.com/)_

When playing with different entrances to their [winter wedding venue](https://www.theknot.com/content/winter-wedding-venues), this couple landed on a tented tunnel overflowing with candles and green garlands. The rugs had a touch of gold in them to add an elevated, regal twist to the winter wedding decor.

### Christmas Tree Wedding Decor 

![Christmas Tree Wedding Decor](https://media-api.xogrp.com/images/6ebcdaf7-a323-493a-a125-1a6f366b180d~rs_768.h-cr_5.0.1453.1086)

_[Photography: Erin Wilson Photography and Films](https://www.theknot.com/marketplace/erin-wilson-photography-and-films-plano-tx-818530)_

If your venue has Christmas trees in place as decor, stage a photoshoot in front of them. Otherwise, ask about adding some of your own for the big day. It's a clever way to capture the holiday spirit in your wedding album. 

### Jingle Bell Escort Cards

![Jingle Bell Escort Cards](https://media-api.xogrp.com/images/7e1c25dd-f65a-43d1-bf3f-5e95828bb20c~rs_768.h-cr_82.0.1375.970)

_[Photography: Rising Lotus Photography](https://www.theknot.com/marketplace/rising-lotus-photography-ocala-fl-873004)_

Not only are these jingle bell escort cards a cute way to add the holiday spirit to your winter wedding decorations, but your guests will have fun ringing them throughout the reception when it's time for you and your brand-new spouse to share a kiss. 

### North Pole Photo Op

![North Pole Photo Op](https://media-api.xogrp.com/images/63bb0b80-3f4b-405e-993a-33d58f5142c8~rs_768.h-cr_140.0.1433.970)

_[Photography: Jennifer Lindberg Weddings](https://www.theknot.com/marketplace/jennifer-lindberg-weddings-lockhart-tx-211072)_

One of our favorite December wedding ideas is a Santa-inspired photo booth! Complete with cozy furnishings, silver highlights and stanchion ropes, guests were hoping they were on the nice list for this reception!

Create a seasonally-appropriate guest experience by setting up a hot chocolate station at your reception. Pair with some popular [winter wedding food](https://www.theknot.com/content/fun-winter-foods-youll-love-for-your-wedding-or-any-party) for the full experience.

### Blankets on Ceremony Benches

![Blankets on Ceremony Benches](https://media-api.xogrp.com/images/5a17a5d4-d32d-43ae-ab7c-eef7e249bc06~rs_768.h-cr_0.51.600.851)

One of the easiest ways to incorporate [winter wedding colors](https://www.theknot.com/content/winter-wedding-colors) into an outdoor ceremony is through cozy throw blankets for guests. These ivory and blue blankets were the refined touch the space needed. 

### Snowmobile Rides

If you or your guests are all about adventure, rent snowmobiles for a next-level reception. Just don't forget helmets for safety. If you really want to wow, have guides bring your guests to a remote location for a truly unique wedding experience.

### Horse-Drawn Sleigh Rides

![Horse-Drawn Sleigh Rides](https://media-api.xogrp.com/images/2a905931-9f70-44f8-bf23-34724ca63a8b~rs_768.h-cr_85.0.885.600)

_[Photography: Jamee Photography](https://www.theknot.com/marketplace/jamee-photography-littleton-co-197258)_

For a winter wedding outside, one of the most fun ways to play with the theme is through transportation. Horse-drawn sleigh rides gave guests a full tour of this Colorado ranch as part of the celebration. 

### Snow Machine Wedding Exit

![Snow Machine Wedding Exit](https://media-api.xogrp.com/images/243a088f-1c4e-4846-95b8-433f38f546c6~rs_768.h-cr_81.0.1377.972)

_Photography: Anna Routh Photography,__Event planning: Grace Leisure Events_

If you don't have real snow on your wedding day, make it! Rent a snow machine for your wedding exit to walk through a tunnel of flurries instead of sparklers or confetti.

### Space Heaters

![Space Heaters](https://media-api.xogrp.com/images/a4827106-c8f6-438e-b88b-720f656957e5~rs_768.h-cr_81.0.1377.972)

_[Photography: Suzy Goodrick Photography](https://www.theknot.com/marketplace/suzy-goodrick-photography-gilbert-az-892184)_

Even in a warm climate, temperatures can drop at night during the winter. Keep your guests comfortable with space heaters placed throughout the space.

### Mitten Wedding Favors

![Mitten Wedding Favors](https://media-api.xogrp.com/images/be991d14-8883-40cb-9e53-269221df839a~rs_768.h-cr_0.0.1080.1440)

If you're exchanging vows in cold temperatures, consider this creative [winter wedding favor idea](https://www.theknot.com/content/winter-wedding-favor-ideas). Your guests can wear these cozy mittens on the day and after the fact.

## Indoor Winter Wedding Ideas 

Your wedding theme will guide so many of your decoration choices, but here are a few fun ways to bring a winter wonderland inside.

### White Neon Sign

![White Neon Sign](https://media-api.xogrp.com/images/6449c13b-bc9f-44ba-8ed6-11606bf314e7~rs_768.h-cr_0.240.1458.2184)

_[Photography: Elisa Lopez Photography](https://www.theknot.com/marketplace/elisa-lopez-photography-indianapolis-in-2004236)_

A wood-planked wall is perfect for your rustic winter wedding backdrop, but adding a neon sign completes it with a personalized, modern touch. Sticking with a classic white light makes it even more wintery.

### Hanging Christmas Ornaments and Greenery

![Hanging Christmas Ornaments and Greenery](https://media-api.xogrp.com/images/fdb31ad6-8154-4913-96f9-a2f7e70e0492~rs_768.h-cr_0.7.1458.1951)

_[Photography: Erin Wilson Photography and Films](https://www.theknot.com/marketplace/erin-wilson-photography-and-films-plano-tx-818530)_

This glamorous [Christmas wedding](https://www.theknot.com/content/christmas-wedding-ideas) leaned into the season with a red-and-green theme. Hanging ornaments tied in the color scheme with the tables for a cohesive look.

### Silver and Charcoal Seating Chart

![Silver and Charcoal Seating Chart](https://media-api.xogrp.com/images/e77d178f-dd1b-4e02-a7f3-3bc0f8cdf7cb~rs_768.h-cr_0.125.1080.1565)

_[Photography: Tida Svy Photography](https://www.theknot.com/marketplace/tida-svy-photography-los-angeles-ca-2016331)_

When paired with other cool tones like ice blue, a silver and charcoal color palette is perfect for a winter wonderland wedding theme. This seating chart is a great example of how you can use candles and existing elements of your venue (like an exposed brick wall) to add warmth and contrast. 

### Greenery Table Runner

![winter wedding ideas rustic table setting](https://media-api.xogrp.com/images/a3f13d43-da22-4415-a942-2cbfad72dcd9~rs_768.h-cr_0.0.918.1224)

_[Photography: Ashley Caroline](https://ashley-caroline.com/)_

Your table setting doesn't have to be over-the-top to be on-theme for a winter wedding. Pair greens with red linens for a wintry, laid-back vibe.

### Wedding Chairs With Fresh Eucalyptus

![red patterned chairs at long banquet table decorated with eucalyptus garlands](https://media-api.xogrp.com/images/693979d0-3777-4f9d-a8f7-dfddfccf8cf3~rs_768.h-cr_0.32.2000.2699)

_[Photography: Laura Murray Photography](https://www.theknot.com/marketplace/laura-murray-photography-denver-co-418791)_

Greenery garlands look just as festive when added to the backs of chairs. Use them to adorn your [sweetheart chairs](https://www.theknot.com/content/wedding-sweetheart-chairs) or where your family will be seated at the VIP table. 

### Red Signature Wedding Cocktails

![winter wedding ideas red cocktails](https://media-api.xogrp.com/images/5564ad46-73e2-4290-bf41-3ef4c74076ab~rs_768.h-cr_0.0.1328.1771)

_[Photography: Amber Gress Photography](https://www.theknot.com/marketplace/amber-gress-photography-brooklyn-ny-951929)_

Looking for winter wedding cocktail ideas? Serve up your favorite red drink to celebrate your cold-weather nuptials.

### Confetti Drop on the Dance Floor 

![bride and groom standing in the middle of the dance floor with white and gold confetti falling around them](https://media-api.xogrp.com/images/a051be6f-4d0c-4566-8527-ea9715321261~rs_768.h-cr_200.0.3400.2400)

_[Photography: Jenny DeMarco](https://www.theknot.com/marketplace/jenny-demarco-photography-austin-tx-380666)_

Make your snow wedding dreams a reality without braving the cold weather. Fill the room with white confetti for an epic reception picture during the final dance. 

### Treats for Santa Buffet

![Treats for Santa Buffet](https://media-api.xogrp.com/images/83e9c7a0-7c79-4360-95df-c172b9b1b7ab~rs_768.h-cr_82.0.1375.970)

_[Photography: Jennifer Lindberg Weddings](https://www.theknot.com/marketplace/jennifer-lindberg-weddings-lockhart-tx-211072)_

When it comes to a December wedding, sometimes it's best to lean into the holiday theme if it brings you joy. This couple did just that with Santa's treats, including boozy milkshakes, snowball cookies and take-home treats.

## Rustic Winter Wedding Ideas 

From a winter ranch to a snowy mountain getaway, so many winter wedding venues lean towards a rustic aesthetic. Use that to create a jaw-dropping wedding style.

### Hot Chocolate Wedding Favors

![winter wedding ideas diy hot chocolate favors](https://media-api.xogrp.com/images/40564976-5534-42c8-bd1c-740af4196b87~rs_768.h-cr_0.0.906.1208)

_[Photography: AnyaFoto](https://www.theknot.com/marketplace/anyafoto-hackensack-nj-863528)_

Surprise your guests with hot chocolate kits as favors at each place setting. The next time they make cocoa at home, they'll remember your special day. 

### Extra Tall Candles and Greenery-Draped Iron Chandeliers

![Extra Tall Candles and Greenery-Draped Iron Chandeliers](https://media-api.xogrp.com/images/6129c557-7385-4830-a354-422863314fa4~rs_768.h-cr_0.2.1458.1096)

This couple added warmth to their cozy reception space through tall candles and greenery-draped chandeliers. These winter wedding reception ideas were a simple yet elegant touch to the space.

### Weathered Frame Photo Board

![Weathered Frame Photo Board](https://media-api.xogrp.com/images/31479472-29b3-4384-859a-a8cc52771a5e~rs_768.h-cr_80.0.1377.973)

This weathered picture frame board fits in right next to a [winter wedding bouquet](https://www.theknot.com/content/winter-wedding-bouquets) for a gorgeous, rustic display. With the rock wall and lit garland, it had all our favorite winter decorations for a wedding.

### Pine Cone Place Holders

### Crystal Branch Centerpieces

### Rustic Glam Stage Decor
