Wondering how to have the best wedding dance floor ever? Set the stage for your [first dance](https://www.theknot.com/content/classic-first-dance-songs) and an evening of celebration with one of these magical wedding dance floor ideas. Custom dance floor decals, overhead decor, lighting effects and wedding dance floor rentals make it possible to create a custom dance floor that fits your wedding style and decor. 

[READ MORE The Best First Dance Songs of All Time ](https://www.theknot.com/content/classic-first-dance-songs)

Whether you're customizing the dance floor at a wedding venue or looking for an outdoor wedding dance floor solution, we've rounded up lots of ways you can make your dance floor an exciting focal point of your event space. And if you _really_ like one of the following ideas, make sure to heart the image so you can [access your favorites](https://www.theknot.com/your-favorites/inspiration) later.

## Ideas for Wedding Dance Floor Rentals 

If your wedding reception space requires you to have a dance floor installed, don't worry. Wedding dance floor rentals have come a long way this past decade, so you have lots of looks to choose from.

[READ MORE The Best First Dance Songs of All Time ](https://www.theknot.com/content/classic-first-dance-songs)

### 1\. Checkered Black and White Dance Floor for Wedding

![Black and white checkered dance floor. ](https://media-api.xogrp.com/images/a6e6a609-0e97-47b3-b0dd-a67ad2f80a88~rs_768.h-cr_114.0.1410.972)

_[Photography: VinnyD. Photography](https://www.theknot.com/marketplace/vinnyd-photography-valley-stream-ny-964767)_

Here's a unique [boho-chic](https://www.theknot.com/content/bohemian-wedding-ideas) decor look. A classic black and white checkerboard dance floor paired with black furnishings and natural-colored decorations, including wicker and grasses and suspended fixtures over the dance floor.

### 2\. Graphic Black and White Design

![Graphic striped black and white dance floor](https://media-api.xogrp.com/images/ba9a6ee8-2b48-4dc3-bbf8-33b76e0171cd~rs_768.h-cr_113.0.1024.683)

_[Photography: Purple Tree Photography Inc.](https://www.purpletree.ca/),__[Decor: Dance Floor Decor Event Creations](https://dancefloordecor.com/)_




Mix up the traditional black and white checked dance floor with a crisp geometric design. Concentric squares draw your guests' eyes to the center of the dance floor and put you and your partner in the spotlight.

## Outdoor Wedding Dance Floor Ideas

[Outdoor weddings](https://www.theknot.com/content/outdoor-wedding-planning-guide) are certainly popular these days, and the good news, there are lots of ways to integrate an outdoor dance floor. Consider one of these five creative ideas for your wedding dance floor. 

### 6\. Rug Dance Floor for Wedding

![Outdoor wedding dance floor made with area rugs](https://media-api.xogrp.com/images/31e55ea9-66ed-471d-9980-644738396af2~rs_768.h-cr_0.156.1080.1596)

_[Photography: OK Crowe Photography](https://okcrowe.com/)_




For an eclectic wedding style, scatter area rugs over the ground for a beautiful outdoor wedding dance floor that's soft under the feet. Want the boho look? Pick out mismatched rugs as this couple did.

### 7\. Dark Wood Dance Floor Tiles on Grass

![Dark Wood Outdoor WeddingDance Floor Tiles on Grass](https://media-api.xogrp.com/images/f52dba61-834c-4bd1-bc96-8da716730fce~rs_768.h-cr_50.0.850.600)

_[Photography: Valorie Darling Photography](https://valoriedarlingphotography.com)_




This large dark wood tile dance floor for an outdoor wedding looks amazing laid on top of grass. The wood element complements the environment of the reception.

### 8\. Draped String Lights

![Draped string lights over a dance floor. ](https://media-api.xogrp.com/images/bb41aa34-a0a7-4c51-bfb1-0e444fe2b578~rs_768.h-cr_111.0.1888.1333)

_[Photography: Todd Danforth Photography](https://www.theknot.com/marketplace/todd-danforth-photography-los-angeles-ca-950140)_




The grooms decided a dance floor wasn't needed for their beach [destination wedding](https://www.theknot.com/content/domestination-wedding-definition). The newlyweds and the guests danced the night away in the sand with beautiful string lights decorations hanging over them.

## Creative Wedding Dance Floor Ideas

The following decorations (and dance floor) are a great way to put a cool focus on where you and your guests will be dancing. Here are a few of our favorite ideas.

### 12\. Multiple Disco Balls

![Wedding dance floor with disco ball decorations](https://media-api.xogrp.com/images/7a5842ac-69ee-4512-973d-f9b8ad995af1~rs_768.h-cr_60.0.1020.720)

_[Photography: Catherine Ann Photography](https://catherineannphotography.com)_




Nothing says dance party more than a disco ball! Hang several [disco balls](https://www.theknot.com/content/disco-bachelorette-party) in different sizes and colors for a dazzling effect.

### 13\. Neon Signage and Globe Lights

![Neon Signage and Globe Lights](https://media-api.xogrp.com/images/47245b05-d3c9-46ff-9406-021f64542447~rs_768.h-cr_70.0.1365.971)

_[Photography: Chaz Cruz Photographer](https://www.theknot.com/marketplace/chaz-cruz-photographer-brooklyn-ny-763260)_




Use custom [neon signs](https://www.theknot.com/content/neon-signs) and globe lights to highlight your wedding dance floor. This decoration goes well with a graphic patterned dance floor design and creates a fun party vibe.

### 14\. Multicolored Paper Lanterns

![Paper lanterns suspended over a wedding dance floor](https://media-api.xogrp.com/images/eb8d3f9f-9c93-49e6-b0a1-1ac9076cf587~rs_768.h-cr_50.0.850.600)

_[Photography: Mary + Roy](https://maryandroy.com)_




Another easy and impactful dance floor decoration is to hang paper lanterns in your wedding colors overhead. For more flair, add battery-operated tea light candles to each lantern.
