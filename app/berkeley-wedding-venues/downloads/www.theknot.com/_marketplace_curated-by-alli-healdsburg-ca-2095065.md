Curated By Alli is a Healdsburg, CA-based wedding planning company. Over the last 18 years, owner Alli Kleinke has worked on over 1,000 events and has become known for their avant-garde approach that is also original, romantic, and theme-based. Alli is proud to bring wine country weddings to life in beautiful ways. A trained Michelin chef, sommelier, designer, and event coordinator, this talented professional is creative and detail-oriented, with the ability to work to your unique needs. No matter the style, budget, theme, or size of your wedding, Alli can handle all the details using a unique skillset honed over the better part of two decades.
