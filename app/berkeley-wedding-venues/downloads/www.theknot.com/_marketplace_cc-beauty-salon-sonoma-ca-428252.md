10/13/2011 • Carol H

I booked with this salon a week before my wedding. Juanita, the hairstylist, was very easy to deal with and she booked me for a trial run that Thursday. I originally chose a simple...

bun but she said it was too simple so I got a more complicated hairstyle. The makeup artist, Rosie, didn't speak English but Juanita translated for her. I liked the final result and I only had to pay $75 for the trial run and $95 for the real thing. They're the cheapest I've found here in Sonoma and I was happy with them. I love Juanita and I'm going back to her soon for a haircut.
