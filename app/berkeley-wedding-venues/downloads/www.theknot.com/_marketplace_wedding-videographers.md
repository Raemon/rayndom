## Finding a Wedding Videographer

Advice From The Knot

### Start With Your Style

Start your videographer search by thinking about how you want your wedding video to feel - cinematic or like a documentary?

The best wedding videographers will be capturing the moments on your wedding day, and editing them to tell your story.

### Understand the Options

Know the style you want? Now consider what parts of the day you want filmed and how many hours you'll need a videographer.

Talk to videographers about other decisions too, like what type of music you want and whether you'll get to preview the footage.

### Start a Conversation

Once you've viewed some videographer portfolios, it's time to start a conversation.

The Knot's "Request Quote" feature will help you answer other key questions a videographer might have before you meet.
