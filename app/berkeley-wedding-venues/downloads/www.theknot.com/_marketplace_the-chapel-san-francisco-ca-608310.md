### About this venue

Located in San Francisco, CA, The Chapel is a restaurant wedding venue. This location is known for its history in the San Francisco music scene, playing host to many dynamic concerts over the years. Here, music-lovers can celebrate their special day with a sense of history in this one-of-a-kind venue. The Chapel once served as a mortuary, but has undergone a number of refurbishments to bring it to its current aesthetic standard. You'll find elements of ironwork, stained glass, wooden features, and more here, creating a distinct and characterful atmosphere. This location features a fully functional chapel, where you can hold your wedding ceremony. Following that, you and your guests can enjoy a cocktail reception on the airy patio. Your reception can take place in the main event space, which can be set up for banquet dining and dancing to follow. The stage makes your reception ready-made for hosting entertainment so that you can dance the night away in the company of your loved ones. This venue has a generous capacity of up to 750 guests. The Chapel provides support for all couples that wish to celebrate with them, offering them a number of useful services. These include in-house catering and beverage packages so you can work with the catering team to design a menu to fit your event. From appetizers to entrees, the staff of The Chapel's celebrated restaurant will help you Event rentals are also available, including chairs, tables, lighting, sound equipment, and more. Security can also be hired for your event, ensuring that it is a safe and enjoyable experience for all. Take a 360° Virtual Tour and enjoy an exclusive sneak peek today!
