### FAQs

#### What time of year is best for a wedding in Houston?

The best time of year for a wedding in Houston is spring and fall. The rainy season runs between May and October, and the heat and humidity during summer can get pretty high. If your heart is set on a winter wedding, you might be looking at a crisp 40 degrees, so you’ll want to find an indoor venue.

#### Who can officiate a wedding in Houston?

Licensed or ordained ministers, priests, or rabbis, as well as justices of the peace and most judges, can officiate weddings in Houston. Ceremonies can also be conducted by any officer of an authorized religious organization.

#### How do I get a marriage license in Houston?

To get a marriage license in Houston, you'll have to visit one of the 10 county clerk offices. You'll both need to show some valid personal ID, like a birth certificate, driver's license, or passport.
