About this vendor

Based in Petaluma, CA, Denise Kramer Weddings offers bespoke wedding planning services to couples hosting their milestone occasion. Whether you need someone to help you perfectly place those finishing touches or for an expert to take all the weight off your shoulders, Denise can fulfil your vision. Since 2007, Denise Kramer Weddings has been helping couples create their ideal celebration. Denise is an award-winning event planner with over two decades of experience. You can rest assured that this seasoned professional will help you design the wine-country wedding you've dreamed of. Denise Kramer Weddings would love to hear your ideas for your special day and can provide a full-service experience where you won't have to lift a finger. From civil to cultural, religious, eco-friendly, destination weddings, and more, Denise has the expertise to create an incredibly bespoke event. Couples can choose from full and partial planning packages and month-of and day-of coordination to receive the service they need for their magical occasion.
