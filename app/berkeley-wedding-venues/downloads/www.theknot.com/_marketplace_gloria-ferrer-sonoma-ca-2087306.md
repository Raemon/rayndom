### About this venue

Gloria Ferrer is a stunning wedding venue in Sonoma, CA, that invites you and your partner-to-be to host a memorable celebration. This one-of-a-kind winery venue is the perfect place to say, 'I do,' offering flexible spaces for your reception, ceremony, and dinner. Better yet, the team caters to all kinds of occasions, whether you plan an intimate gathering or a grand soiree. Gloria Ferrer features several reception and ceremony locations for wedding festivities of up to 120 people. The Tasting Room is ideal for both seated and standing cocktail hours, providing a picture-perfect backdrop for your special day. After you and your guests enjoy a glass of wine, you'll make your way to the Vista Pavilion for your much-anticipated ceremony. Here, you can exchange the most sincere vows amid breathtaking vineyard views. Afterward, loved ones will wine, dine, and dance in the Vista Terrace at your wedding reception. You can host every sparkling milestone at Gloria Ferrer, including your anniversary, engagement party, wedding shower, and bachelor/bachelorette. In addition, the team will work diligently to ensure you experience a seamless celebration from start to finish. They offer event coordination for destination weddings, as well as food and catering services. The venue also employs a dedicated bar staff with the expertise to keep you and your guests happy and hydrated all night long.
