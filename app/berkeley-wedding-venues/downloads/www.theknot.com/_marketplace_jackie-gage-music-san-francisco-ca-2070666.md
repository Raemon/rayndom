About this vendor

Jackie Gage's velvety voice provides a relaxed, soothing environment perfect for romantic wedding ceremonies, first dances, and more. With hundreds of events under her belt, and various performances and tours around the world, this bandleader understands what's needed to create the perfect moment for your unforgettable day. Jackie can perform as a solo vocalist/guitarist/pianist, in a duo with a guitar or cello, all the way up to a 7 piece band, and even a big band. Have a special song request? Or want to include any unique musicians to your big day? Customize your music needs with her "a la carte" for any performance. Whether set in intimate villas, to ocean-view escapes, you can join luxury tastemakers like Anthropologie, Domaine Chandon, Airbnb, Google and others who have trusted her services for unique, unforgettable music experiences. We are excited to bring this same caliber of art and entertainment to your celebration.
