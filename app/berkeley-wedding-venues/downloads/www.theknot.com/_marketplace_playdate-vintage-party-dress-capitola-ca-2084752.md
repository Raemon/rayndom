About this vendor

Playdate Vintage Party Dress is the first exclusively-vintage wedding salon/party dress and accessories shop in the greater Bay Area, located right here on Santa Cruz's West Side in California. We source, restore, and beautifully present a curated collection of vintage bridal and formal pieces, accessories, and various vintage oddities. Our dresses are available for purchase or rental, and we offer bridal/special event styling and vintage personal shopping. Finding vintage to fit your own style and your own body for a special occasion can be tough, so we take on the work of sifting and searching for you. We aim to carry inclusive sizes and styles, and to maintain a safe environment for all brides, partygoers, and guests. We also combat the excessive waste in the wedding/events and fashion industries by helping you to dress responsibly! In addition to vintage wedding dresses, we offer pieces for bridesmaids, mother-of-the-bride, wedding guests, and anyone looking to dress with a bit of flair! We also have a broad inventory of vintage jewelry, bags, coats, veils, hats, other accessories, and wedding or shower gifts. Come check us out in The Old Wrigley Building on Santa Cruz's fun and hip "west side." We are open on Fridays for drop-in shoppers and by appointment--see our site to schedule. Additionally, we have virtual styling and shopping availability, as well as in-home delivery and try-ons throughout the Bay Area and Central California (for an additional fee). We hope to see you for a "playdate" soon!
