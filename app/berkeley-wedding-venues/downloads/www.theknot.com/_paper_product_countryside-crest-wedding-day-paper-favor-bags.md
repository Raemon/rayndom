Details

These thank you favor bags feature a charming watercolor illustration of a countryside crest, delicately sealed with a satin-like ribbon. Wildflower blooms peek out from the crest, adding a nature-inspired touch. Your names and a heartfelt message are beautifully inscribed in a flowing, handwritten-style script, encouraging your loved ones to indulge using the contents within the bag.   
Your Paper Gift Bags are printed with full-color digital designs on a choice of kraft or white paper. Photos aren't recommended for kraft paper but work well on our white paper. Each bag has an imprint area of 4.25” x 4.5” with a full art size of 5.75” x 8” (bleeds not available). Your treat bags will be lined with food-safe wax paper to keep the contents fresh.
