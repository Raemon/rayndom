About this vendor

Based out of Oakland, CA, Culinary Excellence is a catering business that serves weddings in the Golden State and beyond. Using locally sourced ingredients to create delicious meals, their culinary team boasts decades of combined experience. They will work with you to develop a mouth-watering menu that features a selection of your best-loved dishes. Jessica Murphy, Culinary Excellence's coordinator, has been involved in the business for years. She and the team delight in helping couples and their loved ones celebrate their milestone occasions. Soon-to-be newlyweds are sure to be impressed by the business' eclectic repertoire of dishes. Their menu is inspired by a range of cuisines, including Indian, French, Italian, and Spanish. Culinary Excellence can provide delivery and setup services, leaving you to enjoy your big day without any worries. Buffets, family-style meals, and plated dinners are just a few of the available dining options. Once your meal is cooked, it will be served by their impeccable serving wait staff. Eager to clients with a one-stop shop for all of their wedding day needs, the business also offers planning, floral design, and lighting services.
