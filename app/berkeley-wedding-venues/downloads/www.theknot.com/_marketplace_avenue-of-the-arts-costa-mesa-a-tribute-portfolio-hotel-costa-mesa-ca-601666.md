Avenue of the Arts Costa Mesa Hotel is a wedding venue situated in Orange County, CA. This lovely Tribute Portfolio hotel offers an experience that goes beyond the ordinary. They celebrate and promote the best of human expression and achievement. Their eclectic arts-inspired design and warm, welcoming atmosphere come together to create a fun and romantic setting where you can be yourself. You will be greeted by an experienced and dedicated team that is ready to help you make the most of every moment. They strive for your satisfaction, and they are eager to make your wedding a wonderful and relaxing day to remember. Costa Mesa's only AAA Four-Diamond is available for wedding ceremonies, receptions, and other special events. The venue has both indoor and outdoor spaces, including the Grand Ballroom and the picturesque Lakeside Lawn, with a maximum capacity of 218 guests. Overnight accommodation is also available for you and your guests, with standard and deluxe rooms and luxury suites. Couples can enjoy a complimentary Deluxe Honeymoon suite, along with champagne, chocolate-dipped strawberries, and a post-wedding breakfast. Avenue of the Arts Costa Mesa Hotel offers five-star service and flexible packaging options. Wedding package features and options include planning assistance, event catering, shuttle service, and much more. Their team of experts can craft a package to suit any style and budget, so whatever your needs, you can be sure that your special day is in capable, caring hands. They are excited to welcome you to Avenue of the Arts and the beginning of a new, exciting chapter of your life.
