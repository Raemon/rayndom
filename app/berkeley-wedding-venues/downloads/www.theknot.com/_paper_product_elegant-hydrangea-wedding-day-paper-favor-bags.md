Show your gratitude to your guests with these elegant floral-inspired food favor bags. Your heartwarming message in cursive and serif fonts delicately sits above a blooming bouquet of watercolor hydrangeas. Your names and wedding date appear below in a classic font, adding a touch of elegance.

Each bag has an imprint area of 4.25” x 4.5” with a full art size of 5.75” x 8” (bleeds not available). Your treat bags will be lined with food-safe wax paper to keep the contents fresh.
