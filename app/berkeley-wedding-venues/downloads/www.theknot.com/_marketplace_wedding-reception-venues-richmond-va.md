### FAQs

#### How can my out-of-town guests get to Richmond?

Your out-of-town guests can travel to Richmond via D.C., Virginia Beach, or Charlotte, NC. There's also an international airport just seven miles from downtown. Transportation is very convenient in this city, so your guests can arrive with ease and in style.

#### How do I get a marriage license in Richmond?

To get your marriage license in Richmond, you and your partner will need to visit a Court Clerk's Office. Ensure you bring valid ID and other required documentation. The license is valid for up to 60 days after it's issued.

#### Who can officiate a wedding in Richmond?

Anyone can officiate a wedding in Richmond as long as they're authorized by a Virginia Circuit Court. Virginia does not accept online ordinations, so a licensed officiant may be more convenient in this city.
