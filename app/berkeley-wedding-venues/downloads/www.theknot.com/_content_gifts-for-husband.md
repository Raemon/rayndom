We get it: Shopping for gifts for your husband can sometimes feel like an unsolvable riddle. Even asking him what he wants might not help, especially if he's a [husband who has everything](https://www.theknot.com/content/gifts-for-husbands-who-dont-want-anything) already. That's where we come in. We've rounded up the best gift ideas for your husband, whether you're celebrating the holidays, his birthday, Father's Day or [just because](https://www.theknot.com/content/just-because-gifts) he's awesome. Before we dive into our list of recs, we're sharing a few tips on how to choose thoughtful presents for your husband.

First up, think about what he likes and how you want to make him feel. Appreciated? Go the romantic route and shop for something sentimental, like a piece of custom artwork. Understood and cared for? Buy him something useful that he needs, like a new wallet, cozy sweats or self-care goodies. Surprised and delighted? Honor his favorite hobby or opt for an experience you can both do together. Whatever response you're hoping for, you're sure to find a great present for your husband on our list. We've sorted our suggestions into different categories to make the process even simpler.
