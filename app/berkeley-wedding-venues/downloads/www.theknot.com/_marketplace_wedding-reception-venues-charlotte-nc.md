### FAQs

#### What time of year is best for a wedding in Charlotte, NC?

The best time of year for a wedding in Charlotte is during the spring and fall. Winters can be cold and wet, and summers can get humid and hot, so venues with climate control might be a wise choice!

#### How do I get a marriage license in Charlotte?

You can get a marriage license in Charlotte by visiting the county's Register of Deeds. You'll have to pay a fee and fill out a form. There is no wait time after you obtain the license, and it will be valid for 60 days.

#### Who can officiate a wedding in Charlotte?

A magistrate or an ordained minister of any religion can officiate a wedding in Charlotte. If you opt for a magistrate, you'll need to contact the magistrate's office in your county to check on availability.
