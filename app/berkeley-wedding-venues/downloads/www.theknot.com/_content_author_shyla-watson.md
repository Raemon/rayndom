**Intro**

Shyla was an Editor at The Knot Worldwide, where she wrote and edited content, with a particular focus on SEO optimization. She has worked in digital media for over a decade, covering celebrities, books, and other aspects of pop culture and entertainment. 

Born and raised in Los Angeles, Shyla still calls the city home, where she lives with her dog, Bailey. In her free time, she enjoys discovering new restaurants, watching K-dramas, and reading romance and mystery novels.

**Experience**

Shyla has always been captivated by storytelling. A lifelong reader, she majored in English at Lafayette College before beginning a career as an entertainment writer and red carpet reporter. After freelancing for outlets including PopSugar and InStyle, she joined BuzzFeed as a Pop Culture Writer, eventually rising to Senior Editor and Celebrity Strategist. In these roles, she interviewed numerous authors and celebrities, creating engaging stories that resonated with wide audiences.
