### About this venue

Thanks to recommendations from couples on The Knot

The University Club takes pride in being one of San Francisco, CA's oldest traditions and premier wedding venues, dating back to 1890. As one of the Bay Area's finest private city clubs, we are continuing to grow and evolve with the times, while retaining an old-world atmosphere and charm. Located on the top of Nob Hill, the University Club offers 8,000 square feet of event and meeting space that is perfect for any occasion. Our two dining rooms at the Club enjoy panoramic views of the San Francisco skyline. There are eight classical spaces ranging in size from the most intimate to ballroom settings for 200.
