# SUMMER WORK PARTY

June 27, 2025 @ 9:00 am \- 1:00 pm

![](https://sausalitowomansclub.org/wp-content/uploads/2025/05/Screen-Shot-2025-05-28-at-11.12.35-AM.png)

Clear Out, Clean Up, and Re-Organize the Front Basement Room

We have found there are too many items that do not get used. Items that are blocked from sight, even if one wanted to use them, they wouldn’t know where to find them.  
It’s time to do some Summer Cleaning! 

Please Come Join Us!  
If you have any hidden treasures down in the front basement room or items you want us to save, please let us know. 

Come for an hour or two. We’d love your participation.

Karen Wilcox & Jenny Rynders
