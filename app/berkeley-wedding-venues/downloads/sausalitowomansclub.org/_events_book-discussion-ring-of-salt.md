# BOOK DISCUSSION: Ring of Salt

January 29 @ 10:00 am \- 12:00 pm

![](https://sausalitowomansclub.org/wp-content/uploads/2025/07/ringofsalt.png)

January 29, 2025, at 10am

SPECIAL VIRTUAL BOOK EVENT: Betsy Cornwell joins us virtually to discuss her newest book, Ring of Salt. Maid meets Under the Tuscan Sun in this inspiring and lyrical memoir about a writer and mother who flees an abusive marriage and must learn to reclaim the story of her life through a search for home on Ireland’s wild, western coast. RSVP by January 7th to Elizabeth Pierce to gain virtual access to the event.
