# SAUSALITO PLAYERS PERFORMANCE

November 12, 2025 @ 7:00 pm \- 9:00 pm

![](https://sausalitowomansclub.org/wp-content/uploads/2025/07/Bullshot-Crummond-Graphic.jpg)

The Sausalito Players presents Bullshot Crummond

Written by Alan Shearman, Diz White, Ronald E. House, John Neville-Andrews & Derek Cunningham  
  
Starring Aleah Mainzer, Helen Russell, Linda Schanfein, Sean Pritchard, Steve Wilson  
  
Directed by Lewis Shireman

This parody of low-budget detective movies typifies British heroism. Teutonic villain Otto von Brunno and his evil mistress crash their plane in the English countryside and kidnap Professor Fenton, who has discovered a formula for making synthetic diamonds. Bullshot Crummond is called to the rescue.  
  
Wednesday, November 12, 2025 @ 7:00 PM  
Sausalito Woman’s Club  
120 Central Ave. Sausalito, CA  
Free admission, Light refreshments
