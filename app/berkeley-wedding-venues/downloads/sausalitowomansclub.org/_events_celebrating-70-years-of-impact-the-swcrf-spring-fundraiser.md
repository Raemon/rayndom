# Celebrating 70 Years of Impact: The SWCSRF Black and White Gala

April 18 @ 6:00 pm \- 9:00 pm

![](https://sausalitowomansclub.org/wp-content/uploads/2025/07/Screen-Shot-2025-12-26-at-9.52.37-AM.png)

“Dust off your favorite black-and-white attire and join us for an evening of glamour, stories, and shared purpose. We’ll celebrate 70 years of scholarships with food, music, and sparkling conversation—fueling even more dreams for the decades ahead.”

Proceeds benefit the Sausalito Woman’s Club Scholarship Recognition Fund (SWCSRF), 501(c)(3): 68-023-9954

_**The Evening:**_

Doors open 5:30 pm  
Delectable hors d’oeuvers  
Open bar  
Music & Dance  
Raffles and auctions

$95 per ticket, RSVP by 4/13/2026

*Limited Parking (Christ Episcopal Church Lot Available)– Carpool/Uber encouraged

[Purchase Tickets/Sponsor](https://70th-anniversary-black-and-white-gala.square.site/)

  * Visionary Sponsor: $5000
  * Community Champion: $2,500-$4,999
  * Scholar Advocate: $1,000-$2,499
  * Community Builder: $500-$999
  * Education Ally: $250-$499
  * Scholar Supporter: $100-$249
  * Friends of SWCSRF: up to $99



Sponsorship donation is fully tax deductible (ID: 68-0239954). For more information about sponsorship benefit, click [Learn More.](https://drive.google.com/file/d/1uYuDhLwPwB059fFSFs41ARCbL14yzzy9/view)
