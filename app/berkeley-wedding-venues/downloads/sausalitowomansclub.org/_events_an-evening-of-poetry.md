# AN EVENING OF POETRY

April 23 @ 6:30 pm \- 9:00 pm

**DATE CHANGED to 4/23**

Join us for a special community gathering celebrating some of the best poetry of our times

Hear readings from teachers, clergy, moms and dads, business folks, students, and award winning poets as they share words that sear, that soothe, that jostle our minds and mark our hearts. Committee: Barbara Holmes, Averel Wilson, Laurie Tandy, Karen Roark, Roberta Malloy, Maria Paterno, Cheryl Popp and Leslie Hail.

Limit 150. No charge.  
RSVP by 4/20 to Barbara Holmes.
