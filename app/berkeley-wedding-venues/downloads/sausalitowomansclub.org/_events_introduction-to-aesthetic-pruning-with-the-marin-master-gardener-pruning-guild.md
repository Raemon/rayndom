# INTRODUCTION TO AESTHETIC PRUNING WITH THE MARIN MASTER GARDENER PRUNING GUILD

April 7 @ 5:30 pm \- 7:00 pm

Wish you knew how to prune your Japanese maple or lemon tree? Join SWC member and Marin Master Gardener Sharna Brockett for an intro to aesthetic pruning. Learn its vision and philosophy, key concepts like the “What,” “Why,” “How,” and “When,” and techniques for shaping shrubs and small trees—plus resources to support your pruning journey with confidence.

Limit 20. No charge. RSVP by 4/7 to Chair Sharna Brockett.
