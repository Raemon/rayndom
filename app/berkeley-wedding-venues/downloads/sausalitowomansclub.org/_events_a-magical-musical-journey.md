# A MAGICAL MUSICAL JOURNEY

April 26 @ 3:00 pm \- 4:30 pm

25.00$

Fabulous classical violinist, Charmian Stewart, and equally fabulous classical cellist, Amanda Craver –– The Forget–Me–Knots –– perform traditional Celtic, Scandinavian, and American music on the violin, cello, Hardanger fiddle, and Nyckelharpa. Lively tunes and haunting melodies will set the Club reverberating with their fascinating performance. Light refreshments reflective of the program and wine available.

Limit 100. Cost $25.[ PURCHASE TICKET](https://square.link/u/ojMEg2O0) by 4/24  
Co–Chairs Paula Monsef, Susie Visher.
