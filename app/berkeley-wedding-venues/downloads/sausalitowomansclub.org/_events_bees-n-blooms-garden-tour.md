# BEES N BLOOMS GARDEN TOUR

May 29, 2025 @ 10:00 am \- 11:30 am

20.00$

![](https://sausalitowomansclub.org/wp-content/uploads/2025/04/labyrinthshot6-4-20-768x520-1.jpg)

_Hosted by the SWC Sustainability Committee  
_

Thursday, May 29 · 10:00 AM  
Santa Rosa, CAJoin the SWC Sustainability Committee for a serene and inspiring 1.5-hour guided tour of the beautiful Bees N Blooms Lavender Labyrinth and Flower Gardens, located at the foot of Taylor Mountain on the Santa Rosa Plain. 

• Wander through acres of CCOF-Certified Organic gardens  
• Discover the essential role of pollinators  
• Marvel at the Lavender Labyrinth in peak bloom  
• Visit the tree nursery filled with pollinator-friendly species

After the tour, enjoy a relaxing picnic under the shaded pavilion (Bring your own lunch).

Tour Cost: $20 per person  
Space is limited – RSVP by May 8 [PURCHASE ONLINE](https://square.link/u/PoVTlwiQ)  
If paying by check, email Kim Huff  
[kim@kimber.net](mailto:kim@kimber.net)

This visit is timed perfectly during Lavender Bloom Fest, when the farm is bursting with color and fragrance. In late May, the English lavender varieties in the labyrinth will be at their peak—each ring a different brilliant hue of purple, pink, or white.

Learn more about this sustainable farm: Bees N Blooms Farm Info or track the bloom progression <https://beesnblooms.com/?page_id=198>

We hope you’ll join us for this relaxing, educational, and eco-friendly outing in nature!
