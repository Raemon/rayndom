Come one, come all to the Sausalito Woman’s Club elegant extravaganza **“Preserving the Magic”** a visual feast of entertainment and carnival-style revelry.

**The silent auction is now LIVE** click [**here**](https://givebutter.com/c/SWCPS2025/auction) to start shopping for only-in-Sausalito experiences and adventures, benefitting the preservation of our beautiful club.

A sample of the auction items include:

  * A private tour of 2200 Studios for up to 10 people (formerly the legendary Record Plant)
  * An insider tour and talk with local fashion designer Rebecca Bruce
  * A bespoke written history of your pre-1900s Sausalito home
  * A table for six at Sausalito favorite Jazz and Blues by the Bay



In addition, there are getaways to Sonoma and Tahoe, massage certificates from The Ritual Spa and Wellness, ski lessons, architecture and design consultations, and much much more!!!
