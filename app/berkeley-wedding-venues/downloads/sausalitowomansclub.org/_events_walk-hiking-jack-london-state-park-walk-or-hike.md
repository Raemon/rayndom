# Walk & Hiking: Jack London State Park Walk or Hike

May 5, 2025 @ 9:00 am \- 2:00 pm

![](https://sausalitowomansclub.org/wp-content/uploads/2024/07/Screen-Shot-2025-04-05-at-2.48.11-PM.png)

**Jack London State Park Walk or Hike**  
Men are welcome but “no dogs”  
Hikers meet at 8:30 am, Walkers meet at 9:00 am to carpool from SWC $10/car entrance fee)

Explore the beauty of Jack London State Park in Glen Ellen with us! We offer two options: a walking tour and a hike. Highlights include the Wolf House, museum, winery ruins, and London Cottage. Both groups will reunite afterward for a picnic lunch at the beautiful gardens at Benziger Winery in Glen Ellen. Please let us know if you are willing to drive. (there is a $10 fee per car, if you are not a California State Park member).

RSVP by April 30th to co-leaders Nancy Curran (hikers) and Margo Merle (walkers)
