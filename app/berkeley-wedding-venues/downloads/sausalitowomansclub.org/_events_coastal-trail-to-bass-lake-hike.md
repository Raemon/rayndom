# COASTAL TRAIL TO BASS LAKE HIKE

November 13, 2025 @ 9:00 am \- 2:00 pm

![](https://sausalitowomansclub.org/wp-content/uploads/2025/07/Bass-Lake-to-Alamere-Falls-EL-photos_20-1024x589-1-1.jpeg)

Thursday, November 13, 2025 | 9:00am – Meet at SWC to carpool 

Hike along this 5.9 mile out-and-back trail in Point Reyes that follows a well-maintained coastal path to Bass Lake. Take in the gorgeous views along the way as you wind through the forest with frequent wildlife sightings. Elevation gain: 770 ft. Bring water, hat, and optional hiking sticks. Lunch to follow at Coast Café in Bolinas. RSVP by 11/10 to Co-leaders Nancy Curran and Sandy Strawbridge.
