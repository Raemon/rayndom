## Are you interested in becoming a Sausalito Woman’s Club member?

### What are the requirements to join?

1. Sausalito Resident for one year previous to applying.

2. You must have three (3) sponsors who are current members of the Sausalito Woman’s Club, one of which has known you for at least one year and the other two have known you for at least six months

### How do I apply?

Please contact Corresponding Secretary, Donna Bachle. Please send her an email at [donna_bachle@yahoo.com](mailto:donna_bachle@yahoo.com)
