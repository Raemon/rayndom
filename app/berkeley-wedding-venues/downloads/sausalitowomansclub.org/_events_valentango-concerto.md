# VALENTANGO CONCERTO

February 14 @ 3:00 pm \- 6:00 pm

40.00$

![](https://sausalitowomansclub.org/wp-content/uploads/2025/07/Screen-Shot-2025-12-26-at-9.48.14-AM.png)

Celebrate love and community at ValenTango, an intimate afternoon concert featuring live tango music by Orquesta Típica Tarareando, light Argentine-inspired treats, and a no-host bar with bubbles and other beverages. Bring a Valentine, a friend, or simply your love of music for an afternoon of heart, harmony, and gratitude.

Proceeds benefit SWCSRF’s 70-year tradition of supporting local scholars and sustaining access to higher education through community-driven support.

Limited capacity; [Purchase Tickets](https://valentango2026.square.site/)

Doors open 3:00 pm. Performance starts: 4:00 pm.  
No Host-Bar – Light Appetizers
