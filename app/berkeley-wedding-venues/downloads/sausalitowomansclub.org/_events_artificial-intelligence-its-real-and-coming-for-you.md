# ARTIFICIAL INTELLIGENCE: IT’S REAL AND COMING FOR YOU

May 27 @ 6:00 pm \- 7:30 pm

10.00$

![](https://sausalitowomansclub.org/wp-content/uploads/2025/07/AI-Image-600-x-400.jpg)

Delp, The Charles William Harrison Distinguished Professor of Electrical and Computer Engineering and Professor of Biomedical Engineering at Purdue University as he provides an overview of AI, generated media, deepfake videos and popular AI tools.
