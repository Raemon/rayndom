# Sonoma Horticultural Nursery

April 23, 2025 @ 10:00 am \- 2:00 pm

![](https://sausalitowomansclub.org/wp-content/uploads/2025/04/imengine.prod_.srp_.navigacloud.jpg)

Join us for a breathtaking trip to Sonoma Horticultural Nursery in Sebastopol during the peak bloom of azaleas and rhododendrons! This vibrant display of color and beauty is a must-see for plant enthusiasts and nature lovers alike. We’ll explore the lush gardens, soak in the fragrant blossoms, and learn about these stunning plants from the experts at the nursery.

**We’re also in need of drivers** to help get everyone there, so if you’re able to lend a hand and share the ride, it would be greatly appreciated! Don’t miss out on this special opportunity to experience the magic of spring in full bloom.

RSVP to [Mickie Lloyd](mickielloyd@gmail.com)
