# CeCe CHATS – AGING, END–OF–LIFE WISHES, AND FINDING MEANING IN LATER LIFE

June 13 @ 1:00 pm \- 3:00 pm

20.00$

Later Life Coach Cecile “Cece” O’Connor, a seasoned registered nurse with over forty years of experience, aims to shed light on the challenges that often go unaddressed, empowering older adults to find agency, purpose, and fulfillment as they age. This platform is also a stand against ageism, advocating for a society that values every stage of life.

Limit 100. Cost $20. [PURCHASE TICKET](https://square.link/u/rQqljT57) by 5/22

Chair Caroline BonN
