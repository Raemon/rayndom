# SWC TITLE IX SPORTS & LEISURE CLUB – KAYAKING EVENT

February 26 @ 9:00 am \- 11:00 am

35$

Join us for a 2–hour kayaking outing in Richardson Bay. Meet at 9am at SeaTrek in Sausalito. Kayak, paddle, life jacket, and any waterproof clothing needed will be provided. Bring your own lunch for a picnic afterwards. No limit. Parking at Bay Model.

Please make your own reservation at [seatrek.com](https://www.seatrek.com/rentals-sausalito/). Select **the 2-HOUR option. Your cost will only be $35 with a Member discount.** You will be asked to include your CC to hold the reservation, but you will not be charged until after the event. Please email Chairs, Jennifer Gridley and Kathleen Maher if you have questions and to let them know you will be joining us.
