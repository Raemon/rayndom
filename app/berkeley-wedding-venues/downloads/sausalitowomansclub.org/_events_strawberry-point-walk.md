# STRAWBERRY POINT WALK

September 12, 2025 @ 10:00 am \- 2:00 pm

![](https://sausalitowomansclub.org/wp-content/uploads/2025/07/img_2035-1.jpg)

Friday, September 12, 2025 | 10:00am – Meet at SWC to carpool 

Enjoy an easy 1mile walk along the paved shoreline path with stunning views of Tiburon, Belvedere, San Francisco, and Sausalito. The walk features charming homes and gardens. After the walk, we’ll return to Harbor Point Tennis and Swim Club for lunch on the deck. Bring a hat, water, and money for lunch. RSVP by 9/9 to Leader Margo Merle
