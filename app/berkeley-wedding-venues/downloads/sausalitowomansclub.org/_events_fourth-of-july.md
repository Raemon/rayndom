# Fourth of July

July 4, 2025 @ 10:00 am \- 12:00 pm

![](https://sausalitowomansclub.org/wp-content/uploads/2025/07/Screen-Shot-2025-07-01-at-7.42.36-AM.png)

Join the Sausalito Woman’s Club for the 4th of July Parade & Festivities! 

Celebrate Independence Day with the Sausalito Woman’s Club as we proudly take part in Sausalito’s beloved 4th of July Parade! Wear your red, white, and blue and join us for a day filled with community spirit, patriotic pride, and summer fun.

Parade Details:  
March with us through downtown Sausalito, waving flags and sharing smiles as we honor this special holiday. The parade features floats, bands, classic cars, and lots of local flair!

Bring your friends and family — and help us make this 4th of July unforgettable!
