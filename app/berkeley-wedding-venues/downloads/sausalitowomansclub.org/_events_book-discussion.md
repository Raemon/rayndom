# Book Discussion:

April 17, 2025 @ 4:00 pm \- 5:00 pm

![](https://sausalitowomansclub.org/wp-content/uploads/2024/07/The-Women-.jpg)

Carol Cotton, discussant  
**The Women, Hannah Kristin**  
A book about Frankie McGrath and her enlistment as a surgical nurse in the Vietnam War in 1966 coming from her privileged upbringing in Coronado, CA.

While focused on the experience of women in the Vietnam War, The Women reflects on how women were dismissed by history as having never served in the war.
