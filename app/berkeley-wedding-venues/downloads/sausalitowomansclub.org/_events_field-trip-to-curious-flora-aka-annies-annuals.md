# FIELD TRIP TO CURIOUS FLORA (aka ANNIE’S ANNUALS)

May 1 @ 9:30 am \- 2:00 pm

Sue Currier and Kim Huff will be your guides on the annual trip to Curious Flora’s wholesale nursery in Richmond to buy spring plants. Lunch to follow at Little Louie’s in Pt Richmond. Carpool from SWC. If it’s raining, this event will be moved to the following week, May 8. 

Limit 25. RSVP by 4/24 to Co-Chairs Sue Currier, Kim Huff.
