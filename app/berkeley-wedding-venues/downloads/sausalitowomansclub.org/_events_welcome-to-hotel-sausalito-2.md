### **Sausalito Woman’s Club Presents “Welcome to Hotel Sausalito”**  
**A Musical Comedy written by Linda Ojeda & directed by Sela Seleska**

Come one, come all for a hilarious evening of music and laughter as our local thespians regale you with stories. Loosely based on historical characters who lived and loved in Sausalito, Secrets will be revealed. This show is a fundraiser for the SWC General Fund. Writer; Linda Ojeda. Director; Sela Seleska. Hors d’oeuvres and No–Host Bar.

Doors open 6:00 pm Performance 7:00 pm  
No Host-Bar – Light Appetizers
