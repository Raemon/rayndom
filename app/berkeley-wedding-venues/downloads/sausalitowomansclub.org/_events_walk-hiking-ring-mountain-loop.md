# Walk & Hiking: Ring Mt/Tiburon Ridge Hike to Old St. Hilary’s Church

May 19, 2025 @ 8:30 am \- 3:00 pm

![Ring Mountain Loop](https://sausalitowomansclub.org/wp-content/uploads/2024/07/Screen-Shot-2024-07-14-at-3.13.33-PM.png)

Monday, May 19 – 8:30 am (carpool from SWC) hike limited to 16 hikers  
RING MT/TIBURON RIDGE HIKE TO OLD ST. HILARY’S CHURCH

A scenic 7-mile moderately challenging hike atop Tiburon Ridge open space with spectacular panoramic views. We will see vibrant spring wildflowers and intriguing historic and geographic landmarks. We will also be walking through several neighborhoods with fabulous homes and gardens. The hike ends at Old St. Hilary’s Church then down into Tiburon to Rustic Bakery for lunch.

RSVP by 5/15 to Kathy Atkins-Page cell/text 415-218-9453 more details to follow
