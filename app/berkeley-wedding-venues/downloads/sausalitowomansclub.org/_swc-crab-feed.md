**Silent Auction Rules**

**YOU DO NOT HAVE TO BE PRESENT TO WIN**

**Silent Auction Bids can be placed BY YOU at the Event OR by Barb Sutak at** the event**_pursuant to your EMAIL instructions_**. Email bids to Barb Sutak must include the following information:

  1. A) _Your full name and contact information_ including: name, email address, telephone number, and mailing address,
  2. B) _The complete description of the Item you want_ to bid for (see descriptions of the Auction items below),
  3. C) _Your Opening (starting) Bid amount_ in whole dollars,
  4. D) _Your Incremental bid amounts_ , if any, at which you wish to increase your bid on each item up to your maximum bid amount per item



EXAMPLE:

  * Auction Bid Item: “Beef Tenderloin Dinner for 8 at the Winner’s Home Prepared by Ann Arnott & Mori Fischer”
  * My starting bid for this item is $500.
  * Increase my bid at $100 increments above my competitor’s bid up to a maximum bid of $1,000).


  1. E) Your opening bid will be placed on bid list for the item prior to the start of the auction, and if other bidders are bidding against you, your authorized bid increases will be recorded for you on the bid list in increments in real time during the auction. Your bid amounts on the item will be placed up to your maximum bid, if necessary, to win. If your highest bid limit is less than your competitor’s highest bid, you will not win the item.
  2. F) Auction Items will be awarded to the highest bidder.
  3. G) If, for some reason the highest bidder does not claim their item, we will contact the next highest bidder until a winner is identified.
