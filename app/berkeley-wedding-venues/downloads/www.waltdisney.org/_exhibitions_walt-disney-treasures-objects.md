# _Walt Disney Treasures_ Objects

Cost

Free 

Date

New Object Every Two Months   
October 2024–October 2025 

Location

Awards Lobby 

To celebrate the release of our new book, _Walt Disney Treasures: Personal Art and Artifacts from The Walt Disney Family Museum_ , we are displaying rare and unique objects from the collection that are also featured in the book. Some of the artifacts showcased in the book and here have never been seen by the public. These objects will be on view for free in the museum’s Awards Lobby and will rotate periodically through 2025.

##  Lillian Disney's Tea Set 

#### On view August–October 2025.

The sixth installation in this series celebrates the significant contributions of Lillian Disney.

[Read More](https://www.waltdisney.org/blog/walt-disney-treasures-lillian-disneys-tea-set)

######  June–July 2025 

##  Harper Goff Scrapbook 

#### On view June–July 2025.

The fifth installation in this series celebrates the significant contributions of Harper Goff.

[Read More](https://www.waltdisney.org/blog/walt-disney-treasures-harper-goff-scrapbook)

######  May–June 2025 

##  Walt's Train Uniform 

#### On view May–June 2025.

The fourth installation in this series celebrates Walt's lifelong love of trains.

[Read More](https://www.waltdisney.org/blog/walt-disney-treasures-walts-train-uniform)

######  February–April 2025 

##  Heinrich Kley 

#### On view in February–April 2025.

The third installation in this series celebrates the influence of German artist Heinrich Kley on the aesthetic of Disney's early animated features.

[Read More](https://www.waltdisney.org/blog/walt-disney-treasures-heinrich-kley)

######  December 2024–January 2025 

##  Salvador Dalí 

#### On view in December 2024–January 2025.

The second installation in this series celebrates the friendship between Walt Disney and Surrealist artist Salvador Dalí.

[Read More](https://www.waltdisney.org/blog/walt-disney-treasures-salvador-dali)

######  October–November 2024 

##  Portrait of Diane Disney Miller 

#### On view in October–November 2024.

Walt commissioned portraits of both of his daughters, Diane and Sharon, upon their 16th birthdays. Illustrator and portrait painter Neysa McMein composed this image of Diane Disney Miller, holding a copy of Lewis Carroll’s Alice in Wonderland.

[Read More](https://www.waltdisney.org/blog/diane-portrait)
