# SOLD OUT   
Summer Class | Dungeons & Drawing

Cost

$725 members | $850 non-members 

Date

Mon–Fri, Jul 14–25 | 9am–3pm   
(half-day Jul 25) 

Year

2025

Location

Learning Center 

Ages

Grades 3–5 

It’s time to roll into adventure and prepare your spell slot sheets, travelers! Come join us on an incredible journey as we mix writing, visual art, and a little bit of simulation to create a captivating tale mixed with fun role-playing game elements. Explore what makes an exciting story as you dive deep into twists, turns, and timeless techniques while making choices that have impact on your own fictional expedition.

### Additional Class Details

Students who take this course will learn about how story and illustration inform each other. Students will be expected to both write and draw during the two weeks of this course.

**Please note: The last day of class will be a half day (9am–noon). Parents and family are invited to join us at noon to visit the classroom and view student work from the two-week session.**

### Scholarship Information

To ensure that all students have the opportunity to take classes at The Walt Disney Family Museum, we are pleased to offer scholarships to qualifying families and students of all ages. How to apply:

  * Send an email to [studio@wdfmuseum.org](mailto:studio@wdfmuseum.org) with the subject line “Summer Scholarship Information 2025” by April 4, 2025.
  * Include the student’s name and age, a parent/guardian’s name (if applicable), and the class name and dates.



If you have any additional questions about scholarships, please email the Studio Team at [studio@wdfmuseum.org](mailto:studio@wdfmuseum.org).

### Accessibility Information

The Walt Disney Family Museum strives to make its programs accessible to all guests. For specific accessibility information, please contact the Studio team at [studio@wdfmuseum.org](mailto:studio@wdfmuseum.org).
