# Legacy Giving

Through a legacy gift, you can join other passionate supporters in ensuring access to the museum’s collections, special exhibitions, public programming, and educational initatives for visitors of all ages and backgrounds for generations to come. Your thoughtful gift will help continue to foster the next generations of creative talent and provide access to arts education for all. 

### Give a Gift of Art

The Walt Disney Family Museum also welcomes bequests and promised gifts of art that align with our collecting priorities. Please notify us of your intention to make a promised gift of art to allow for the necessary curatorial review and approval. 

To have your donation of art considered, please complete and submit the form below. 

[Give a Gift of Art](https://www.waltdisney.org/bequest-intention-and-legacy-society-enrollment)

### Join The Walt Disney Family Museum Legacy Society

We welcome being included in your legacy giving plan, and invite you to join The Walt Disney Family Museum Legacy Society. Members of the Legacy Society are recognized in the regularly published Member Magazine, invited to special events, and receive access to exclusive opportunities at the museum throughout each year. 

If you wish to include us in your legacy giving plans and join The Walt Disney Family Museum Legacy Society, please complete and submit the form below:

[Join Our Legacy Society](https://www.waltdisney.org/bequest-intention-and-legacy-society-enrollment)
