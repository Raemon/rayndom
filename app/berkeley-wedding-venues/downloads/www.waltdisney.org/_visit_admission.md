###   
Hours

**The museum is open Thu–Sun, 10am–5:30pm (last entry at 4:30pm) and is closed Thanksgiving Day, December 25, and January 1.**

Operating hours are subject to change, please check our calendar prior to planning your visit.

For further information about our hours or admission prices, please contact Ticket Desk at 415.345.6800.

### Special Exhibitions

In addition to its main galleries, which house the museum's permanent collection, The Walt Disney Family Museum has several special exhibition spaces. In the lower level of the building, the **Theater Gallery** and **Lower Lobby Gallery** host small-scale, rotating special exhibitions; these exhibitions are typically free to the general public, or admission to them is included with museum admission. The recommended viewing time for these exhibitions is generally 30 minutes. 

Please [visit our Exhibitions page](https://www.waltdisney.org/exhibitions) for more information about the museum's current, upcoming, and past special exhibitions. 

### Ticketing Policies

We encourage all guests—including members—to reserve tickets prior to their visit. Admission to the museum is available on a timed-ticket basis with limited capacities set for each time slot. Visit our [Tickets](http://www.waltdisney.org/tix) page to purchase or reserve tickets. Members of The Walt Disney Family Museum are invited to visit our [members-only ticket reservation page](https://www.waltdisney.org/memtix) to reserve your free tickets.

Children under the age of 12 must be accompanied by an adult who shall be responsible for the child at all times.

If you purchased tickets and need to reschedule your visit, email [tickets@wdfmuseum.org](mailto:tickets@wdfmuseum.org). Although no refunds are available, our team is happy to reschedule your visit.

#### Additional Ticketing Policies for Programs in the Theater

Tickets reserved for children under the age of 5 without an accompanying adult ticket in the same order will be automatically returned. Lapsitting infants under the age of 1 do not require an additional ticket.

### Museum Store, Cafe, Awards Lobby

  * Visitors are invited to explore the Museum Store, cafe, and Awards Lobby free of charge. 



### San Francisco Museums for All

  * Proof of San Francisco residency and a valid CalFresh, EBT, or Medi-Cal card required.
  * Cardholder + three guests receive free general admission to the museum galleries.
  * Available to redeem in person at Ticket Desk.
    * The Walt Disney Family Museum participates in the San Francisco Museums for All program, which offers free or reduced admission to local museums and cultural centers. See a full list of participating institutions at [https://www.sfhsa.org/san-francisco-museums-all](http://www.sfhsa.org/san-francisco-museums-all).



### Primary Caregiver of a Person with a Disability

  * The primary caregiver receives free general admission to the museum galleries and free film admission (film admission subject to availability, check [calendar](https://www.waltdisney.org/calendar) for showtimes).
  * Available to redeem in person at Ticket Desk.



### US Military Admission 

  * Valid for active, retired, and veteran US military personnel with valid US military ID.
  * Valid for dependents with valid dependent ID. 
  * Cardholders receive free general admission to the museum galleries.



### Blue Star Museums Admission

The active-duty member does not have to be present for family members to use the program. ### Members of NARM, ROAM, or Time Travelers

  * Proof of NARM, ROAM, or Time Travelers reciprocity required.
  * Cardholders receive free general admission to the museum galleries.
  * Number of complimentary general admission tickets vary by membership level.
    * These tickets are available on a first-come, first-serve basis. Number of complimentary museum tickets vary by membership level. Please note: NARM, ROAM and Time Traveler benefits include admission to the museum only. Special exhibition admission is not included. To make a reservation prior to your visit, affiliated organizations are asked to email [tickets@wdfmuseum.org](mailto:tickets@wdfmuseum.org).



### Member VIP Tickets

Upon arrival at the museum, simply present your Member VIP ticket at the entrance to receive the next available admission time. Please note, you may experience a minimal wait and we apologize in advance for any inconvenience. Member VIP Tickets do not include special exhibitions or film screenings. To make a reservation with this ticket before your visit, please email [membership@wdfmuseum.org](mailto:membership@wdfmuseum.org). 

### WDFM Donation Certificates

Upon arrival at the museum, simply present your WDFM Donation Certificate at the main entrance to receive the next available admission time. Please note, you may experience a minimal wait and we apologize in advance for any inconvenience. WDFM Donation Certificates do not include special exhibitions or film screenings. To make a reservation with this certificate before your visit, please email [membership@wdfmuseum.org](mailto:membership@wdfmuseum.org).
