#### Talks, Virtual

# Virtual Replay | Memories of Mary Blair with Historian John Canemaker and Mary’s Niece Maggie Richardson

Cost

$5 members | $10 non-members 

Date

Fri, Aug 22–Fri, Aug 29 

Year

2025

Location

Vimeo 

Missed our sold-out July 12 event? Experience this Talk from the comfort of your own home!

In celebration of our latest special exhibition,  _Mary Blair: Mid-Century Magic_ , join us for an exclusive presentation with historian and Mary Blair expert John Canemaker and Mary Blair’s niece Maggie Richardson. Canemaker wrote two books about Blair— _The Art and Flair of Mary Blair_ (published by Disney Editions) and  _Magic Color Flair: The World of Mary Blair_ (published by Weldon Owen)—and Richardson is the caretaker of Blair’s estate.

**Please note: This is a pre-recorded event. There will be no live Q &A component, and the recording will not be offered beyond the virtual stream time. A password and link to a private Vimeo page is included with your ticket which will be active beginning Friday, August 22 at 9am. Please note: Tickets for this replay will be taken off-sale on Friday, August 22 at 9am.**
