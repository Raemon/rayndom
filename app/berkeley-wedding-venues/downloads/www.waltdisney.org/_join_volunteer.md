# Volunteering

Get inspired by creativity, innovation, and entrepreneurship. Work alongside our dedicated staff to inspire visitors of all ages and share your enthusiasm for Walt’s story, right here in San Francisco. Volunteer commitment is essential to the ongoing success of The Walt Disney Family Museum.

Volunteers assist with weekend public programs as well as private museum events. The responsibilities can include greeting, wayfinding, seating, and engaging with guests to answer questions.

### Interested in Volunteering at The Walt Disney Family Museum?

We are currently not accepting applications for volunteer positions at this time. We will be expanding our volunteer opportunities and recruitment for 2026 soon. If you have any questions, email us at [volunteer@wdfmuseum.org](mailto:volunteer@wdfmuseum.org) for more information. We look forward to hearing from you.

### Current Volunteers 

Thank you for being a volunteer! Stay tuned for emails regarding future events and volunteer opportunities. We look forward to seeing you at the museum.
