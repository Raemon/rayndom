# _Happiest Place on Earth_ Member Preview Night

Cost

Free for members with purchase of admission   
to the [Curator Talk](http://www.waltdisney.org/education/talks/curator-talk-happiest-place-earth-author-and-curator-don-hahn) program, see details below 

Date

Thu, Nov 13 | 5:30–8pm 

Year

2025

Location

Awards Lobby   
Theater   
Lower Lobby   
Theater Gallery 

**Please note: To attend this reception, members must purchase admission to[Curator Talk | _Happiest Place on Earth_ with Author and Curator Don Hahn](https://www.waltdisney.org/education/talks/curator-talk-happiest-place-earth-author-and-curator-don-hahn).**

In celebration of our newest special exhibition,  _Happiest Place on Earth: The Disneyland Story_ , we are pleased to host an exclusive Member Preview Night. Experience  _Happiest Place on Earth: The Disneyland Story_ before it opens to the public and toast to the special exhibition’s opening with bubbles and light bites.

### On-Sale Information

Members who purchase tickets to [Curator Talk |  _Happiest Place on Earth_ with Author and Curator Don Hahn](https://www.waltdisney.org/education/talks/curator-talk-happiest-place-earth-author-and-curator-don-hahn) are invited to join for  _Happiest Place on Earth_ Member Preview Night. No additional purchase required.

### Schedule of Events

  * **Reception** | 5:30–6:30pm
  * **Program** ([Curator Talk |  _Happiest Place on Earth_ with Author and Curator Don Hahn](https://www.waltdisney.org/education/talks/curator-talk-happiest-place-earth-author-and-curator-don-hahn)) | 6:30–8pm
