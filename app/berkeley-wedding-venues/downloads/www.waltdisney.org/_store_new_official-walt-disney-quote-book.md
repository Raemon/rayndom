## The Official Walt Disney Quote Book 

[New](/store/new) [Books](/store/books)

[![Share](https://www.waltdisney.org/themes/custom/wdfm/images/share.svg)](https://www.addtoany.com/share#url=https%3A%2F%2Fwww.waltdisney.org%2Fstore%2Fnew%2Fofficial-walt-disney-quote-book&title=The%20Official%20Walt%20Disney%20Quote%20Book%20)

$24.99 

Quantity

As part of The Walt Disney Company’s 100th anniversary festivities, this must-have quote book showcases insights from Walt Disney alongside rarely-seen Disney photographs. Walt Disney once said, "There is more treasure in books than in all the pirates' loot on Treasure Island and at the bottom of the Spanish Main"; never has this been truer than within these pages.

This collection of quotations from the co-founder of The Walt Disney Company ranges from the well-known to the obscure, but all are assured to entertain, enlighten, and inspire. _The Official Walt Disney Quote Book_ has been compiled for anyone eager to learn more about Walt Disney.

Hardcover.

Dimensions: 5.75” x 8.5”
