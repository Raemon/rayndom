##  Virtual Experiences 

### Explore The Walt Disney Family Museum from Anywhere

In keeping with our mission, The Walt Disney Family Museum is finding new and unique ways to connect to our audience virtually, including the innovative offerings detailed below.

##  Featured Experiences 

##  WDFM Mobile App 

Access the museum right from your phone. The streamlined, easy-to-use WDFM mobile app enhances the guest experience in a variety of ways—both for guests visiting The Walt Disney Family Museum in person and for the public to virtually enjoy from the comfort of their homes or anywhere in the world.

[Get the App](https://www.waltdisney.org/mobile-app)

##  Virtual Tour and Museum Highlights 

Anyone in the world can experience and enjoy The Walt Disney Family Museum with our Virtual Tour and Museum Highlights. Step inside the museum and enjoy this immersive tour for people of all ages who wish to visit the museum and learn about Walt’s life in a streamable format.

[Take the Tour](https://www.waltdisney.org/virtual-tour)

##  Virtual Group Experiences 

Our team has customized an inspiring array of team building activities and workshops that groups can enjoy from the comfort of their own homes. These virtual programs are intended to promote creativity, encourage team bonding, and provide an opportunity to connect with colleagues.

[Learn More](https://www.waltdisney.org/virtual-experiences/virtual-group-experiences)

##  Virtual Winter Fun 

Celebrate the season and kick off a new year with this special virtual experience for your private group. First, a live Storyteller tour guide will share heartwarming stories and festive games from Walt’s family traditions, then get creative with a museum-exclusive winter activity tutorial. 

[Learn More](https://www.waltdisney.org/virtual-winter-fun)

##  Virtual Fundraisers 

Virtual Fundraiser event proceeds support The Walt Disney Family Museum’s education programs, which provide arts and animation instruction to the next generation of creative talent. Proceeds also support the museum’s initiatives to provide scholastic opportunities to under-resourced young people, including its newest initiative, the At-Promise Youth Animation Academy, an intensive animation-focused jobs-training program for high school students experiencing economic and social disadvantages.  


[Browse Past Virtual Fundraisers](https://www.waltdisney.org/join-give/virtual-fundraisers)
