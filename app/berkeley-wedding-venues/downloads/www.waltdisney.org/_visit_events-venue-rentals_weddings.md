# Weddings

Celebrate your love story at The Walt Disney Family Museum, one of San Francisco’s most unique and enchanting wedding venues. Located in the heart of the Presidio, The Walt Disney Family Museum offers an unforgettable setting for weddings, rehearsal dinners, engagement celebrations, bridal festivities, and more! The breathtaking backdrop of the Golden Gate Bridge coupled with the natural beauty of the surroundings provides the perfect backdrop for your special day.

The Diane Disney Miller Exhibition Hall serves as the center of our wedding experiences, a spacious and elegant space perfect for both ceremonies and receptions. For couples seeking a more intimate ceremony and reception space, we also offer our _Fantasia-_ inspired theater and Awards Lobby.

We invite you to see the magic in person as we host the beginning of your happily ever after!

For additional information and pricing, please fill out [this form](https://www.waltdisney.org/wedding-form) or contact our Events team at 415.345.6837 or [events@wdfmuseum.org](mailto:events@wdfmuseum.org).
