# Enchanted Evenings

Reimagine a night at the museum with our after-hours pop-up experience, Enchanted Evenings. These exclusive events are offered periodically throughout the year and crafted for the ultimate Disney fan. Immerse yourself in a private screening of a Disney film while enjoying exclusive access to our latest special exhibition, _Disney Cats & Dogs_. Each Enchanted Evening is uniquely themed and paired with delicious food and libations. Don’t miss out on these memorable experiences—a perfect night out for romantic partners, friends, and family. 

##  Enchanted Evenings 

##  Date Night at The Walt Disney Family Museum 

Looking for a unique date night for two? The Walt Disney Family Museum is excited to announce a special after-hours pop-up experience that will give you and a guest a _bella notte_. Three pairs of guests will gain private access to the latest special exhibition _Disney Cats & Dogs_, then enjoy a private screening of Disney Animation’s _Lady and the Tramp_ (1955) over a Tony’s Restaurant-inspired Italian dinner.

[Learn More](https://www.waltdisney.org/education/special-events/enchanted-evenings-date-night-walt-disney-family-museum)

##  Enchanted Evenings | Tim Burton’s The Nightmare Before Christmas (1993) 

Dearly departed, we invite you to join us for an eerie Enchanted Evening at The Walt Disney Family Museum. 13 (un)lucky guests will immerse themselves in a private screening of  _Tim Burton’s The Nightmare Before Christmas_ (1993) while enjoying exclusive access to our latest exhibition  _Disney Cats & Dogs_. This spine-tingling soiree is paired with tantalizing treats and wickedly delightful drinks. We encourage all ghostly guests to partake in festive attire—a perfect spell-binding night for romantic partners, friends, and family.

[Learn More](https://www.waltdisney.org/education/special-events/enchanted-evenings-tim-burtons-nightmare-christmas-1993)
