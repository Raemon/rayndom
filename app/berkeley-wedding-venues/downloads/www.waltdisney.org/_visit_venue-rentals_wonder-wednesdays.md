# Wonder Wednesdays

### "I still look at the world with wonder." — Walt Disney

Immerse yourself in the story of Walt Disney with exclusive private access to the main museum galleries. This experience is only available when the museum is closed to the public.

Tailor your own custom event rental and gain a deeper perspective about the life and legacy of Walt Disney with one of our specialized tours available upon request. For an additional fee, book the theater or Awards Lobby to host the perfect post-gallery reception.

To check availability, email [groups@wdfmuseum.org](mailto:groups@wdfmuseum.org) or fill out the inquiry form.
