Join us for a very special Storytime with illustrator and art director Eliza Reisfeld, who will be reading _My Zoo: A Book of Feelings_ , a book she illustrated to help kids navigate their emotions with the help of some animal friends.

### Eliza Reisfeld

**Eliza Reisfeld** is an Illustrator and Art Director based in Oakland and represented by Bookmark Literary.  Her playful work is inspired by her passion for animals, nature, and the human connection. She captures charming personalities with a wide variety of mediums, including watercolor, gouache, colored pencil, and digital media. She i’s also a dedicated sculptor, creating characters, figures, and animals in clay. After graduating from Rhode Island School of Design (RISD) with a BFA in Illustration, Reisfeld illustrates picture books and art for animation and branding. She has worked with clients from Disney Publishing, Meta, ABC, and the American Psychological Association, to name a fewamong others. She also co-founded RogueMark Studios, a creative agency in Berkeley, CA. Her favorite animals are sloths, tigers, and her black cats Bagheera and Mowgli.
