# _The Sword in the Stone_ (1963)

Cost

FREE for members   
FREE for primary caregivers of persons with disabilities   
$10 adults, seniors, and students | $8 youth   
$5 with general admission or a member guest   
FREE for children ages 5 and under 

Date

Sat & Sun, Jan 4–26 | 2pm   
No screening Jan 18 & 25 

Year

2025

Location

Theater 

Experience a New Year’s transformation and join us in celebrating our latest special exhibition, _Directing at Disney,_ with a month-long screening of Walt Disney’s _The Sword in the Stone_ , based on T.H. White’s classic retelling of the King Arthur story _,_ directed by Disney Legend Wolfgang “Woolie” Reitherman. (Runtime: 79 minutes)

Film screenings are subject to change. If you’re unable to attend a film screening within the first 20 minutes of the film duration, please contact our Ticketing team at [tickets@wdfmuseum.org](mailto:tickets@wdfmuseum.org) for a refund.

Children under 12 years of age must be accompanied by an adult who shall be responsible for the child at all times.

### On-Sale Information

Tickets for _The Sword in the Stone_ (1963) will be available beginning **Wednesday, January 1** at **noon PT**.
