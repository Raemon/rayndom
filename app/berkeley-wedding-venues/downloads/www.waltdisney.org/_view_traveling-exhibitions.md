# Traveling Exhibitions

The Walt Disney Family Museum is dedicated to teaching present and future generations about the life and legacy of Walt Disney, and is proud to offer several of our past original exhibitions to museums and institutions nationwide. These exhibitions vary in size and scope, and can be adapted based on the borrower’s space.

For more information about hosting one of these exhibitions at your museum or institution, please contact:

  * Exhibitions | [inquiry-exhibitions@wdfmuseum.org](mailto:inquiry-exhibitions@wdfmuseum.org)



##  Traveling Exhibitions 

##  _The Walt Disney Studios and World War II_

Immerse yourself in the compelling and multifaceted story of The Walt Disney Studios’ extensive contribution to the Allies’ World War II effort through an array of original artwork, film clips, photos, literature, and more. 

[Download Exhibition Materials](/sites/default/files/2025-07/WWII_Traveling%20Pitch_6.23.25.pdf)

##  _Walt Disney’s The Jungle Book: Making a Masterpiece_

Explore the creative complexities behind _The Jungle Book_ (1967), including the use of cutting-edge animation techniques, the memorable soundtrack from the Sherman brothers and Terry Gilkyson, and the film’s enduring popularity and influence decades after its release.

[Download Exhibition Materials](/sites/default/files/2025-07/JB_Traveling%20Pitch_6.23.25.pdf)

[Browse Exhibition Videos](https://www.waltdisney.org/jungle-book)

##  _The Art of Disneyland Attraction Posters_

A curated selection of nostalgic Disneyland attraction posters from The Walt Disney Family Museum’s collection.

[Download Exhibition Materials](/sites/default/files/2025-07/DP_Traveling%20Pitch_6.23.25.pdf)

##  _Directing at Disney_

Experience classic Disney animated films from the director’s chair and go behind-the-scenes with this showcase of rare film artifacts. Explore objects and ephemera, including detailed production and story meeting notes, storyboards, concept paintings, layout drawings, original sheet music, office memos, and hand-written letters.

[Download Exhibition Materials](/sites/default/files/2025-07/DD_Traveling%20Pitch_6.23.25.pdf)

[Browse Virtual Tour](https://massinteract.com/the-walt-disney-family-museum/#media=259&yaw=1.66&pitc…)

##  _Mary Blair: Mid-Century Magic_

This exhibition—which will be available to travel in October 2025—explores the creative vision and achievements of one of Walt Disney’s most imaginative and renowned designers and art directors, Mary Blair.

[Download Exhibition Materials](/sites/default/files/2026-01/MB_Traveling%20Pitch_10.28.25.pdf)

[Browse Virtual Tour](https://massinteract.com/the-walt-disney-family-museum/#media=286&yaw=-10.52&pi…)
