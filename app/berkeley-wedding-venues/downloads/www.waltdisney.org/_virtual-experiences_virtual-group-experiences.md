# Virtual Group Experiences

##  Team Up with Us for Your Virtual Group Experience 

We are excited to welcome you to The Walt Disney Family Museum for a magical day of virtual events. 

Our team has customized an inspiring array of team building activities and workshops that groups can enjoy from the comfort of their own homes. These virtual programs are intended to promote creativity, encourage team bonding, and provide an opportunity to connect with colleagues.

**Read more on our offerings and fill out our inquiry form below to find out more and book your experience.**

Para opciones en español, puede ponerte en contacto con [events@wdfmuseum.org](mailto:events@wdfmuseum.org).

[Download Virtual Group Events Brochure](https://www.waltdisney.org/sites/default/files/2021-03/WDFM_VirtualExperiences_Deck_2021_V2%20%282%29.pdf)

### Escape Room

$50 per guest, 10 guest minimum

Race against the clock to solve puzzles using virtual art and artifacts that will “unlock” the next room. 

For more details, visit <https://escaperoom.wdfmuseum.org/>.

### Team Drawing Fun

$100 per guest, 10 guest minimum

Learn more about animation and drawing in this hands-on introduction to drawing with a live museum instructor. Our knowledgeable studio staff will teach your team how to draw an object or character of your choice.

### Team Building and Development Workshops

$50 per guest, 10 guest minimum

**Lessons from Walt |** Explore themes such as disruption, teamwork, and adapting.  
Compares the struggles during Walt’s life to the challenges of today in a discussion on the strategies and forward thinking that Walt adopted in order to overcome and find success. Part presentation, part breakout rooms for activities and discussions. 

**Wonderful Women of Disney |** Celebrate female empowerment in the workplace and beyond.   
Celebrates contributions many important women made to Walt’s life and legacy. After introducing you to a few inspiring women, we will share the struggles facing working women today, play games, and learn strategies from Walt on supporting female thought leaders and colleagues. 

**Everyone is a Storyteller |** Discover communication, inspiration, and effective storytelling.  
Get an insider’s look at Walt’s incomparable storytelling and learn how to apply it to your work. In this communication development training, hear from our certified storytellers about the techniques we have developed directly from Walt’s body of work. Not just for employees with creative positions, discover how this can improve your skills for every position, including managers, marketing, sales, HR; everyone can become a high-level storyteller! Se habla español.

### Live Virtual Tour

$50 per guest, 10 guest minimum

Make your next virtual event a standout and join our certified Storyteller Guide for a one-hour virtual journey through The Walt Disney Family Museum. In addition to a live presentation of our most popular artifacts, you will hear inspiring and astounding stories about the life of Walt Disney. Contact us for more options to tailor this experience, from family-friendly groups to happy hour crowds, we can’t wait to share Walt’s story with you.

Se habla español.

### Virtual Holiday Celebration

Cost varies per experience

Celebrate the season and kick off a new year with this special virtual experience for your private group. First, a live Storyteller tour guide will share heartwarming stories and festive games from Walt’s family traditions, then get creative with a museum-exclusive holiday activity tutorial. Review the activity tutorial descriptions on the [Virtual Holiday Celebration page](https://www.waltdisney.org/virtual-holiday-celebration) and email [events@wdfmuseum.org](mailto:events@wdfmuseum.org) for availability.

[More Info and Registration](https://www.waltdisney.org/virtual-holiday-celebration)
