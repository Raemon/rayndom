# Holiday Gift Guide

Posted on Mon, 12/01/2014 - 12:57

With the holidays quickly approaching, shopping can get stressful. Fortunately, the [museum’s online store](http://store.wdfmuseum.org) is here to spread holiday cheer and is stocked full of the perfect gifts for everyone on your list. Check out this holiday gift guide below for this season’s exclusives. Happy shopping!

[_The Lost Notebook: Herman Schultheis and the Secrets of Walt Disney’s Movie Magic_](http://store.wdfmuseum.org/featured-products/the-lost-notebook-herman-schultheis-and-the-secrets-of-walt-disney-s-movie-magic)

_The Lost Notebook: Herman Schultheis and the Secrets of Walt Disney’s Movie Magic_ is a great gift for those interested in a behind-the-scenes look at depth-defying animation techniques spearheaded by The Walt Disney Studios. The most comprehensive source available on Schultheis' lost notebooks, this glossy 291 page book tells the compelling story of the mysterious life and disappearance of Studios technician Herman Schultheis and is sure to spark any animation enthusiast’s curiosity. With this book in hand, stop by this holiday season to view our accompanying exhibition— _[The Lost Notebook: Secrets of Disney's Movie Magic](/lost-notebook)._

[Stoneware Train Mug](http://store.wdfmuseum.org/mugs/stoneware-train-mug-navy)

Coffee and tea lovers will enjoy curling up around the fireplace with a warm beverage in this custom made stoneware train mug, made exclusively for The Walt Disney Family Museum. Versatile in use (oven, dishwasher and microwave safe), the custom train design and appealing colors make this mug a holiday must have.

[The Great Locomotive Chase Commemorative Set By Lowell Smith](http://store.wdfmuseum.org/featured-products/the-great-locomotive-chase-commemorative-set-by-lowell-smith)

Train enthusiasts will treasure this limited edition _Great Locomotive Chase_ Commemorative Set By Lowell Smith, produced exclusively for The Walt Disney Family Museum. Complete with working N-scale models, art images, and a darling collector’s pin, this set comes with a certificate of authenticity and art images perfect for any railroading collector. 

[Mary Blair Boxed Pin Set](http://store.wdfmuseum.org/featured-products/mary-blair-boxed-pin-set)

Make a loved one’s holiday season whimsical by gifting them a Mary Blair boxed pin set. A Walt Disney Family Museum exclusive, this set of four pins featuring some of Blair’s most iconic illustrations is a great gift for fans of Blair’s work, or for anyone who wants to add a little color and flair to their style collection.

[The Band Concert Pin Set](http://store.wdfmuseum.org/featured-products/the-band-concert-pin-set)

Music lovers will appreciate this _Silly Symphony_ exclusive "The Band Concert" pin set that includes five pins and artwork from the joyful cartoon short.

[Camille Rose Garcia Messenger Bag](http://store.wdfmuseum.org/featured-products/camille-rose-garcia-messenger-bag)

Prepare your student or commuter for a trip down the rabbit hole with this goth chic Camille Rose Garcia exclusive, inspired by the Lewis Carroll classic. Only 50 of these messenger bags were produced, so be sure to snag it for your holiday haul before it’s gone.

[Mickey & Minnie Mouse Collectible Doll](http://store.wdfmuseum.org/featured-products/mickey-collectible-doll)

These vintage [Mickey](http://store.wdfmuseum.org/featured-products/mickey-collectible-doll) and [Minnie](http://store.wdfmuseum.org/minnie-collectible-doll) dolls are an absolute treasure just waiting to be unwrapped. Adapted from the 1930s and available for purchase together or separately, Mickey and Minnie are sure to bring holiday delight to children and adults alike. 

[Snow White Pin Set featuring the Seven Dwarfs](http://store.wdfmuseum.org/featured-products/snow-white-pin-set-featuring-the-seven-dwarfs)

Heigh-Ho, it’s off for the holidays we go! Featuring the seven dwarfs at work in the diamond mine, this _Snow White and the Seven Dwarfs_ pin set comes packaged in a custom box featuring iconic artwork from the movie poster from the full-length animated feature film.
