# Join as a Member Today and Experience Our Member Portal

The Walt Disney Family Museum is dedicated to increasing our digital presence to stay true to our mission. Member contributions ensure that we can continue opening our museum virtually to the world and so that future generations can continue to be Inspired by Walt Disney's life and legacy.

With the expansion of our digital presence, members receive exclusive access to our new Member Portal, home to all previous episodes of Happily Ever After Hours, additional virtual program videos, and a digital archive of select member magazines.

We will continue to add special content to the Member Portal, [join as a member today](https://tickets.wdfmuseum.org/webstore/shop/viewItems.aspx?cg=WDFMWS&c=MEM) to access these benefits and so much more.

Already a member? [Log in here](/user) to access our Member Portal.
