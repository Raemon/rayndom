### Santa J. Claus

**Santa J. Claus** is more than just the beloved figure of Christmas; he’s a global icon spreading joy year-round! With a dynamic and verified social media presence on TikTok, Instagram, and YouTube, Santa J. Claus has captivated his nearly five million followers with heartwarming and positive messages, resonating with people of all ages, including celebrities, influencers, and top brands. 

Beyond the screen, Santa J. Claus is a passionate advocate for children’s well-being. As an ambassador for St. Jude Children’s Research Hospital, he dedicates his time to making a real difference, supporting child advocacy groups and hospitals like Benioff Children’s Hospitals of San Francisco and Oakland, Lucile Packard Children's Hospital at Stanford, The Make-A-Wish Foundation, UNICEF, and many more. 

Santa J. Claus’s influence has extended into popular culture, with appearances on _Access Hollywood_ , the _Today Show_ , and Nickelodeon, as well as featured in Meghan Trainor and JoJo Siwa’s festive music video “I Believe in Santa.” He has also partnered with iconic brands like Got Milk?, Crumbl Cookies, See’s Candies, Lifetime TV, Kellogg’s, and others, making him a true ambassador of joy and positivity worldwide.
