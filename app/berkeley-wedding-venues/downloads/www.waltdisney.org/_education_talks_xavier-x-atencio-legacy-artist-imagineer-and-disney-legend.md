# _Xavier "X" Atencio: The Legacy of An Artist, Imagineer, and Disney Legend_

Cost

$25 members   
$45 adults | $40 seniors and students (with ID) | $35 youths   
FREE for children ages 5 and under, Registration Required 

Date

Sat, Jan 10 | 6pm 

Year

2026

Location

ILM Premier Theater   
1 Letterman Dr   
San Francisco, CA 94129 

Xavier “X” Atencio started his career at Walt Disney Productions as an assistant animator and went on to be one of the first Imagineers, writing the scripts for  _Pirates of the Caribbean_ and the  _Haunted Mansion_. Join us for a discussion of the new Disney Editions book,  _Xavier "X" Atencio: The Legacy of An Artist, Imagineer, and Disney Legend_ with authors Tori Atencio McCullough, Kelsey McCullough, and Bobbie Lucas—Atencio’s daughter, granddaughter, and grandchild-in-law, respectively—all of whom also had, or continue to have, careers at Disney. The book contains previously unpublished photographs and artwork from Atencio’s private collection and provides uniquely personal insights into his life, career, and legacy.

Children under 12 years of age must be accompanied by an adult who shall be responsible for the child at all times.

**Please note: This program is taking place at the ILM Theater located at 1 Letterman Dr.**

### About the Speakers

![](data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIAAAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAAALAAAAAABAAEAAAIBRAA7)

### Tori Atencio McCullough

**Tori Atencio McCullough** is “X” Atencio’s eldest daughter. She is the only one of his four children who followed in their father’s footsteps, having had her own forty-year career at Walt Disney Imagineering in the interior design department. She retired in 2016 and later co-authored a book about her Disney career, _Women of Walt Disney Imagineering: 12 Women Reflect on Their Trailblazing Theme Park Careers._

![](data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIAAAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAAALAAAAAABAAEAAAIBRAA7)

### Kelsey McCullough

**Kelsey McCullough** is “X” Atencio’s first granddaughter. Following in the family footsteps, she started her Disney career working in technology for Disney Parks in 2013 and has since moved over to working on technology innovation at The Walt Disney Studios.

![](data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIAAAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAAALAAAAAABAAEAAAIBRAA7)

### Bobbie Lucas

**Bobbie Lucas** is “X” Atencio’s grandchild-in-law. She is a writer, producer, and film professor in Los Angeles. In the Atencio family tradition, she spent part of her career at Disney, where she worked in television development.
