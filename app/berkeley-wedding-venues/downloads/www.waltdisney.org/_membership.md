# Membership

[Join as a Member](https://tickets.wdfmuseum.org/webstore/shop/viewItems.aspx?cg=WDFMWS&c=MEM)

[Renew as a Member](https://www.waltdisney.org/tickets/renewals)

### Our Imagination. Walt’s Legacy. Your Museum.

Our members are those who appreciate the life, work, and legacy of Walt Disney. Through their commitment to The Walt Disney Family Museum, our members shape the museum's vision by supporting [special exhibitions](https://www.waltdisney.org/exhibitions), [education initiatives](https://www.waltdisney.org/education/outreach), and conservation programs. 

In exchange for your support, as a member you will receive unlimited complimentary admission to our main galleries and select special exhibitions. Members at all levels enjoy the benefits of the [Time Travelers reciprocity program](http://timetravelers.mohistory.org/institutions/), which provides discounts and/or complimentary admission to passholders at participating institutions throughout the country. In addition, members receive advance access to in-person and virtual program tickets and special exhibitions, invitations to member-only events, on-demand access to select past virtual programs, and discounts in the [Museum Store](https://www.waltdisney.org/store/all) and on tickets for [talks](https://www.waltdisney.org/education/talks), [workshops](https://www.waltdisney.org/education/workshops), [classes](https://www.waltdisney.org/education/classes), and more, in addition to level-specific benefits. For more information about the various membership tiers and their corresponding benefits, see the listings below.

We have improved our digital presence to stay true to our mission. Your membership ensures that we can continue opening our museum virtually to the world and that future generations can continue to be Inspired by Walt Disney's life and legacy. Join today to visit our [Member Portal](https://www.waltdisney.org/members) and receive exclusive access to select Happily Ever After Hours, Virtual Workshops and Storytimes, and more!

Please note that all memberships, regardless of level, are valid for one year from date of purchase and must be renewed annually if the member wishes to retain their benefits.

##  Membership Levels 

### Join Today 

Individual Membership

### One Member + One Guest | $100

  * With your personalized membership card, you and a guest enjoy unlimited free admission to our permanent collection and select special exhibitions for a year.
  * Discounts on additional guest tickets for the main galleries.
  * Invitations to member-only events, including Member Preview Days when new exhibits open. 
  * Advance access to member pre-sale tickets as well as discounts on ticketed programs, talks, classes, and workshops. 
  * A ten percent discount in the Museum Store. 
  * Participation in the [Time Travelers](http://timetravelers.mohistory.org/institutions) reciprocity program, which provides discounts and/or complimentary admission to passholders at participating institutions. 
  * Complimentary subscription to the museum’s member magazine and enews bulletins. 



$18 of this membership is tax deductible. Amount subject to change.

Student, Senior (65+), California Educator Individual

### One Member + One Guest | $75

  * With a valid ID, you may be eligible to receive a discount on the Individual membership. The benefits for these discounted memberships are the same as the Individual level. These discounted memberships are not tax deductible. 



Dual Membership

### Two Members + One Guest | $190

**All the benefits of an Individual membership, PLUS:**

  * Total of two personalized membership cards that allow two members and one additional guest per visit to enjoy unlimited free admission to our permanent collection and select special exhibitions for a year. 
  * Participation in two additional museum reciprocity programs, [NARM](https://narmassociation.org/) and [ROAM](https://sites.google.com/site/roammuseums/home/list-of-roam-museums), which allow passholders access to more than 1,000 member institutions across North America. 



$108 of this membership is tax deductible. Amount subject to change.

Friend Membership

### Two Members + Two Guests | $500

**All the benefits of a Dual membership, PLUS:**

  * Total of two personalized membership cards that allow two members and two additional guests per visit to enjoy unlimited free admission to our permanent collection and select special exhibitions for a year.
  * Total of four one-time-use VIP tickets, so your guests can visit the main galleries “like a member” for the day. 
  * A 15% discount in the Museum Store. 
  * Invitations to special Friend-level and above member and donor events, including VIP previews and receptions and also member-only programs and talks. 
  * Advance access to tiered member pre-sale tickets and also discounts on ticketed programs, talks, classes, and workshops. 
  * Concierge assistance from dedicated membership staff. 
  * FREE membership gift upon signup, selected by member from an array merchandise and/or publications. 



$331 of this membership is tax deductible. Amount subject to change. 

Supporter Membership

### Two Members + Two Guests | $1,250

**All the benefits of a Friend membership, PLUS:**

  * Invitations to special Supporter-level and above member and donor events, including VIP previews and receptions and also member-only programs and talks. 
  * Priority access to tiered member pre-sale tickets and also discounts on ticketed programs, talks, classes, and workshops. 
  * Advance opportunity to sponsor gala seats at Supporter-level tier. 
  * Recognition in donor and member listings. 



$1,081 of this membership is tax deductible. Amount subject to change. 

### Already Purchased Museum Tickets? 

General admission tickets to the main museum may be applied toward the total cost of a membership within thirty days of ticket purchase date either in person or over the phone (415.345.6810). Applied value may not exceed total cost of membership; amount of tickets applied toward cost of membership varies with membership level.

### Matching Gifts

Did you know you may be able to double your membership contribution with no cost to you? Many employers will match your current membership contribution. The matching gift does not change your membership level, but the additional gift is a huge help to The Walt Disney Family Museum. Contact your employer’s Human Resources department directly to find out if your company has a matching gifts program.
