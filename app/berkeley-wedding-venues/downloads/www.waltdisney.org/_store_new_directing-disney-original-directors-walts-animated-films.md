## Directing at Disney: The Original Directors of Walt's Animated Films

[New](/store/new) [Special Exhibitions](/store/special-exhibitions) [Books](/store/books)

[![Share](https://www.waltdisney.org/themes/custom/wdfm/images/share.svg)](https://www.addtoany.com/share#url=https%3A%2F%2Fwww.waltdisney.org%2Fstore%2Fnew%2Fdirecting-disney-original-directors-walts-animated-films&title=Directing%20at%20Disney%3A%20The%20Original%20Directors%20of%20Walt%27s%20Animated%20Films)

$50 

Quantity

_Directing at Disney_ _The Original Directors of Walt's Animated Films_ offers the little-known history behind Walt's foundational directors, such as Wilfred Jackson, Clyde Geronimi, and Dave Hand. This untold history collection chronicles the progression of the animation director role during the 1920s through the 1970s. For students of filmmaking and management, this book serves as go-to resource for a complex and sometimes technical role that demanded precision. For all Disney fans, these personal portraits of Walt’s closest creative leaders offer a fuller picture behind the stories, characters, and magical realms in Disney’s beloved and respected animated films.

Hardcover

Dimensions: 8" x 10"
