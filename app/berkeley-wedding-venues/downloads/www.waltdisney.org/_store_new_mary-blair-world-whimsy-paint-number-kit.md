## Mary Blair World of Whimsy Paint-By-Number Kit

[New](/store/new) [Special Exhibitions](/store/special-exhibitions)

[![Share](https://www.waltdisney.org/themes/custom/wdfm/images/share.svg)](https://www.addtoany.com/share#url=https%3A%2F%2Fwww.waltdisney.org%2Fstore%2Fnew%2Fmary-blair-world-whimsy-paint-number-kit&title=Mary%20Blair%20World%20of%20Whimsy%20Paint-By-Number%20Kit)

$39.99 

Quantity

**Exclusive for the _Mary Blair:__Mid-Century Magic_ special exhibition. **World of Whimsy paint-by-number kit takes us to a magical land inspired by the mid-century art of Mary Blair, known for her imaginative, bold color pairings and joyful, whimsically stylized illustrations. This scene depicts a geometric castle decorated with flags and pennants. Colorful hot air balloons can be found floating overhead. Completing the 12-color acrylic painting can take between 12–14 hours. This paint-by-number kit is recommended for adults and teens age 13+.

Contents:

  * (1) 8x10 artist-quality, unmounted canvas with a pre-printed background
  * (12) American-made acrylic paints*
  * (2) Princeton Brush synthetic sable hair paintbrushes 
  * Instructions



You will need:

  * A cup of water to wash brushes
  * Rag or paper towel for drying brushes
  * Cotton swabs for fixing mistakes
  * Toothpicks for stirring paints (optional)
  * 8x10 frame to display your completed artwork (optional)



*Elle Crée paint is non-hazardous and conforms to the requirements of ASTM D4236
