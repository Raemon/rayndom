## Disney & Dali: Architects of the Imagination | Exhibition Catalog

[Special Exhibitions](/store/special-exhibitions) [Books](/store/books) [Sale](/store/sale)

[![Share](https://www.waltdisney.org/themes/custom/wdfm/images/share.svg)](https://www.addtoany.com/share#url=https%3A%2F%2Fwww.waltdisney.org%2Fstore%2Fspecial-exhibitions%2Fdisney-dali-architects-imagination-exhibition-catalog&title=Disney%20%26%20Dali%3A%20Architects%20of%20the%20Imagination%20%7C%20Exhibition%20Catalog)

$9.99 

Quantity

Explore the fascinating story of the unlikely alliance and unique friendship between two of the most renowned innovators of the twentieth century: brilliant eccentric Spanish Surrealist Salvador Dalí and American entertainment icon Walt Disney. This interactive exhibition is illustrated in this comprehensive catalogue, showcasing how these two remarkably different innovators were drawn to each other by their remarkable talents.

Dimensions 9” x 7”
