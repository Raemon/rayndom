# Corporate Engagement & Partnership

Since opening in 2009, The Walt Disney Family Museum has inspired more than two million visitors by sharing the life and legacy of Walt Disney. Animated by Walt Disney’s brilliance in fostering creative talent, the museum is committed to supporting the next generation of artists, innovators, and creatives, with a focus on reaching marginalized individuals and communities. Join us in creating outstanding educational programs, community events, and special exhibitions that will inspire generations of creative thinkers, storytellers, and leaders while cultivating your organization’s support of arts access for all ages, STEAM-focused education, and diversity in the arts. Partner with The Walt Disney Family Museum to inspire and connect with our community through custom sponsorships in exhibitions, programs, and education. Enhance your brand by aligning with our innovative museum and reaching millions in our global community. 

For more information on how to get involved as a corporate partner, member, or sponsor, email [evann@wdfmuseum.org](mailto:evann@wdfmuseum.org).

### Corporate Giving

For 15 years, magic at The Walt Disney Family Museum has been made possible thanks to corporate philanthropy. Your support funds our operations, education programs, special exhibitions, and museum-wide initiatives. Help us continue to support the next generation of creative talent with a tax-deductible gift or a tailored partnership. Your generosity strengthens our community and preserves art, animation, and innovation for future generations. 

![](data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIAAAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAAALAAAAAABAAEAAAIBRAA7)

### Become a Corporate Member

Inspire your employees with complimentary museum admission, film screening access, and an array of opportunities for private VIP tours, team activities, private events, and more, while aligning your organization with engaging community programming, STEAM- focused educational initiatives, and arts access for all. 

_Corporate Memberships start at $2,000 and include prominent recognition on our website and donor wall._

[Download Corporate Membership Brochure](https://www.waltdisney.org/sites/default/files/2025-09/WDFM_CorpMembership_Brochure_v3_red.pdf)

### Sponsor an Event or Program 

Sponsor an event at The Walt Disney Family Museum and put your organization's brand in sight. 

Annual Event Sponsorship opportunities include The Walt Disney Family Museum Gala, The [Walt Disney Family Museum Animation Festival](https://www.waltdisney.org/animation-festival), and more. 

![](data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIAAAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAAALAAAAAABAAEAAAIBRAA7)

### Sponsor an Exhibition

Our special exhibitions are attended by thousands of guests per year. See upcoming exhibitions available for sponsorship [here](https://www.waltdisney.org/exhibitions).

### Support Educational Initiatives and Community Programming

Our Education team makes education in the arts available and accessible to learners everywhere. Help us support the next generation of creative talent in the Bay Area and beyond. 

Your support allows us to create free STEAM Resources for classrooms and offer low- or no-cost field trips to K-12 students and Title I schools, in addition to supporting [The Walt Disney Family Museum Animation Academy](https://www.waltdisney.org/education/walt-disney-family-museum-animation-academy). 

![](data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIAAAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAAALAAAAAABAAEAAAIBRAA7)

### Provide In-Kind Support 

In-kind donations help the museum in a variety of our community-focused efforts. See if something your company is already producing can help our mission while helping your organization meet its philanthropic goals.
