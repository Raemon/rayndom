#### Special Events

# Member Appreciation Weekend

Date

Sat–Sun, Jul 26–27   
**Shop in-person:** Additional discount available 10am–5:30pm both days   
(last entry to Museum Store at 5pm)   
**Shop online:** Additional discount available Saturday 9am–Sunday 5pm PT 

Year

2025

Location

Museum Store (In-Person and [Online](http://waltdisney.org/store)) 

To show our appreciation, members will receive an additional 10% discount on in-store or online merchandise purchases made Saturday, July 26 and Sunday, July 27—see below for details. Shop exclusive merchandise from our Museum Store before attending [Walking with Giants: The Untold Story of  _Mary Poppins_ with Gregg Sherman](http://www.waltdisney.org/education/talks/walking-giants-untold-story-mary-poppins-1964-gregg-sherman) on Saturday, July 26.

Please note: This offer cannot be combined with any other promotions, discounts, or permanent markdowns. Does not apply to Fine Art. Limited to stock on hand.

### Shop at the Store

Current ways to shop during Member Appreciation Weekend:

  * In person during the museum's operating hours, Saturday-Sunday from 10am–5:30pm (last entry at 5pm).
  * Online Saturday 9am–Sunday 9pm PT through our Member Portal, with global shipping available by emailing [store@wdfmuseum.org](mailto:store@wdfmuseum.org).



For items not on our website, please email [store@wdfmuseum.org](mailto:store@wdfmuseum.org) or call 415.345.6859.
