## LIGHTS! CAMERA! GLAMOUR! THE PHOTOGRAPHY OF GEORGE HURRELL | Exhibition Catalog

[Special Exhibitions](/store/special-exhibitions) [Books](/store/books)

[![Share](https://www.waltdisney.org/themes/custom/wdfm/images/share.svg)](https://www.addtoany.com/share#url=https%3A%2F%2Fwww.waltdisney.org%2Fstore%2Fspecial-exhibitions%2Flights-camera-glamour-photography-george-hurrell-exhibition-catalog&title=LIGHTS%21%20CAMERA%21%20GLAMOUR%21%20THE%20PHOTOGRAPHY%20OF%20GEORGE%20HURRELL%20%7C%20Exhibition%20Catalog)

$7.95 

Quantity

Take the golden age of Hollywood home with you through this monograph of The Walt Disney Family Museum's special exhibition—[ _Lights! Camera! Glamour: The Photography of George Hurrell_](https://www.waltdisney.org/exhibitions/lights-camera-glamour-photography-george-hurrell). This soft-covered catalogue includes 20 pages packed with a lavish number of the black and white photographs showcased in the exhibition, accompanied by immersive text covering the life and work one of America’s finest photographers: George Hurrell. These portraits include rare and vintage prints of Jean Harlow, Clark Gable, Joan Crawford, Katharine Hepburn, Carole Lombard, and more—including Hurrell’s uncle-in-law, Walt Disney.

Paperback

Dimensions 11” x 8.5”
