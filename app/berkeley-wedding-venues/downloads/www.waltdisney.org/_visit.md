# Visit

### One of the Best Attractions in San Francisco, California

Originally constructed in 1897 as an Army barracks, the iconic building transformed into The Walt Disney Family Museum more than a century later and today houses some of the most interesting and fun museum exhibitions in the US. Explore the life story of the man behind the brand—Walt Disney.

### Looking for What to Do in San Francisco? Come Visit Us!

Whether you are a local or a tourist in San Francisco, experience Disney magic at The Walt Disney Family Museum! You will love learning about the origins of Walt Disney, Disneyland, and Mickey Mouse. Find animation, innovation, and inspiration through our interactive galleries. Great for couples and families!

### One of the Top Things to See in California

Located in the Presidio of San Francisco, come enjoy an awe-inspiring view of the Golden Gate Bridge at one of the best attractions in San Francisco. With thrilling new exhibitions launching multiple times a year, there is always something exciting at The Walt Disney Family Museum!
