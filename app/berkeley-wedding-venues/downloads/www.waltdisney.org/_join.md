# Join & Give

### Our Imagination. Walt’s Legacy. Your Museum.

There are many ways to get involved at The Walt Disney Family Museum. You can support our mission by joining our membership program, making a donation, becoming a corporate partner, or by sharing your knowledge and passion as a volunteer. Join us today. 

##  Membership 

Our members are those who appreciate the life, work, and legacy of Walt Disney. Through their commitment to The Walt Disney Family Museum, our members shape the museum's vision by supporting special exhibitions, education initiatives, and conservation programs. 

[Learn More](/join/membership)

##  Walt's Circle Donor Program 

Walt’s Circle Donor memberships**** provide significant additional benefits on top of our standard membership benefits. Generous gifts not only help donors access the museum community in a new way, but they also allow us to uphold Walt’s legacy and expand our vision. 

[Learn More](http://www.waltdisney.org/join/walts-circle)

##  Corporate Partnerships 

The support of our corporate partners ensures that future generations will continue to be inspired by the remarkable life story and legacy of Walt Disney. Join The Walt Disney Family Museum in creating outstanding educational programs, community events, and exhibitions that will inspire generations of creative thinkers, storytellers, and leaders—all while cultivating your organization’s support of arts access, STEAM-focused education, and diversity. 

[Learn More](https://www.waltdisney.org/join/corporate)

##  Donate 

When you make a tax-deductible gift to The Walt Disney Family Museum, you support Walt Disney’s legacy and the museum’s unique mission to not only inform and inspire, but carry on Walt's values as well. We offer giving programs tailored toward individuals, corporate partners, and foundations, so support the museum today—at the level that works for you. 

[Learn More](/join/donate)

##  Volunteer 

Interested in helping the museum share Walt Disney's legacy with others? As a volunteer, you will have the opportunity to create unique and lasting experiences for our visitors by sharing your passion for—and knowledge of—Walt's story.

[Learn More](https://www.waltdisney.org/join/volunteer)
