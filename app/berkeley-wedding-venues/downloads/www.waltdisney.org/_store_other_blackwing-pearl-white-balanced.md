## Blackwing Pearl White (Balanced)

[Other](/store/other)

[![Share](https://www.waltdisney.org/themes/custom/wdfm/images/share.svg)](https://www.addtoany.com/share#url=https%3A%2F%2Fwww.waltdisney.org%2Fstore%2Fother%2Fblackwing-pearl-white-balanced&title=Blackwing%20Pearl%20White%20%28Balanced%29)

$34 

Quantity

A set of twelve pencils made of the finest Japanese graphite and straight grain California cedar that sharpens quickly. The graphite in the Blackwing Pearl White is a balanced and smooth core—softer than the graphite found in the Blackwing 602, but firmer than the graphite found in the Blackwing firm dark point. The Blackwing brand has a a long history of being the preferred pencil for some of the preeminent artists in the world.
