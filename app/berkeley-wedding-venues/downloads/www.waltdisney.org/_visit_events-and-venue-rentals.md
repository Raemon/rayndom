# Events and Venue Rentals

The Walt Disney Family Museum is a premiere venue rental space in San Francisco. With exciting content and original exhibitions for all to enjoy, The Walt Disney Family Museum is happy to offer our space for celebrations and activities for all types of groups and ages.

Located in the Presidio of San Francisco, The Walt Disney Family Museum is one of the Bay Area’s most inspirational event and meeting venues. The breathtaking backdrop of the Golden Gate Bridge, coupled with the natural beauty of the surroundings, embrace this unique San Francisco event space.

For additional information and pricing, please fill out [this form](https://www.waltdisney.org/events-form) or contact our Events team at 415.345.6837 or [events@wdfmuseum.org](mailto:events@wdfmuseum.org).

##  Weddings 

Celebrate your love story at The Walt Disney Family Museum, one of San Francisco’s most unique and enchanting wedding venues. Located in the heart of the Presidio, The Walt Disney Family Museum offers an unforgettable setting for weddings, rehearsal dinners, engagement celebrations, bridal festivities, and more!

[Learn More](https://www.waltdisney.org/visit/events-venue-rentals/weddings)

##  Wonder Wednesdays 

Enjoy private group access to the Walt Disney Family Museum. Your group can experience Walt Disney’s story while the museum is closed to the public. One-hour tours are available upon request. Contact us to reserve your spot today.

##  Virtual Group Experiences For Teams 

Our team has customized an inspiring array of team building activities and workshops that groups can enjoy from the comfort of their own homes. These [virtual programs](https://www.waltdisney.org/virtual-experiences/virtual-group-experiences) are intended to promote creativity, encourage team bonding, and provide an opportunity to connect with colleagues. Some of the options for group experiences include escape rooms, team drawing, and holiday celebrations.

[Learn More](https://www.waltdisney.org/virtual-experiences/virtual-group-experiences)

##  Virtual Guest Speakers 

Experience Walt Disney’s legacy of inspiration and innovation by booking a special speaker for your next event, meeting, or team bonding experience. Our exclusive roster of industry insiders will present engaging stories and imaginative insights through topical presentations and workshops.

##  Holiday Venue Rentals 

Make your next holiday celebration magical by booking The Walt Disney Family Museum. Host your holiday party in one of our stunning venue rental spaces. [Learn More](https://www.waltdisney.org/holiday-venue-rentals)

##  Virtual Winter Fun 

Celebrate the season and kick off a new year with this special virtual experience for your private group. First, a live Storyteller tour guide will share heartwarming stories and festive games from Walt’s family traditions, then get creative with a museum-exclusive winter activity tutorial. One of the festivities features being taught by local experts in mixology and baking.

[Learn More](https://www.waltdisney.org/virtual-winter-fun)
