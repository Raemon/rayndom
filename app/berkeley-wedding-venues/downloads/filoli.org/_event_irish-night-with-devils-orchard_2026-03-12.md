#  Irish Night with Devil’s Orchard

March 12 @ 7:00 pm-8:15 pm

|Recurring Event [(See all)](https://filoli.org/event/irish-night-with-devils-orchard/all/)

An event every day that begins at 7:00 pm, repeating until March 14, 2026

Intertwined in Filoli’s history are enduring tales of Ireland. Join us in the grand Ballroom to celebrate Filoli’s Irish connection with Devil’s Orchard. Surrounded by the sweeping murals of the Bourn family’s Muckross estate, the room comes alive with haunting melodies and richly textured, tradition-rooted acoustic sound, inspired by Gaelic and Scandinavian traditional music.

[Devil’s Orchard Tickets](https://filoli.org/winter/#devils-orchard)
