#  Service Learning: Bulb Packing

October 29, 2025 @ 9:00 am-12:00 pm

  * This event has passed.



Take part in the most anticipated Service Learning program of the year with Filoli’s very own Horticulture Team. This indoor activity will have you packing bulbs to support this year’s Holidays at Filoli. In addition to participating in an invaluable program for Filoli, you will also gain valuable knowledge about when and how to plant bulbs to create your own beautiful spring display.

[Bulb Packing Tickets](https://filoli.org/youth-programs/service-learning/#bulb-packing)
