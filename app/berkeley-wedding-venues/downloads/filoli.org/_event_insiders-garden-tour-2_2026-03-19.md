#  Insider’s Garden Tour

March 19 @ 1:30 pm-3:00 pm

|Recurring Event [(See all)](https://filoli.org/event/insiders-garden-tour-2/all/)

An event every month that begins at 1:30 pm on day Third of the month, repeating until September 30, 2026

Go beyond the garden gates with Filoli’s horticulture experts. Step into the Greenhouses and Potting Shed, wander the legendary 16-acre Renaissance garden, and get up close to the plants that define its charm. Come curious—our team is ready to share the insight and inspiration behind Filoli’s living beauty.

**Experience Includes:**

  * 90-minute guided tour with a member of Filoli’s Horticulture team
  * Exclusive behind-the-scenes access
  * General admission to Filoli
  * Complimentary beverage and a special keepsake
