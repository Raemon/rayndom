Celebrate the Year of the Horse!

Through February 28, 2026

The new year is a time for cultural traditions, family gatherings, and hopes for good fortune in the coming year. The festivities will include traditional Lion Dance performances, music performances, Filoli’s collection of Asian art and decor displayed throughout the Historic House, activities with local community partners, and much more!

## Lunar New Year Family Day

February 28, 2026 | 10am–5pm

Immerse yourself in the vibrant spirit of Lunar New Year with dazzling Lion Dance performances, festive games, and engaging activities hosted by local community partners. Celebrate new beginnings and the rich traditions of this joyous holiday in a day filled with culture, connection, and the stunning beauty of the Garden.

## Celebrating Lunar New Year with Winnie Wong

February 28, 2026 | 2–3pm

Celebrate Lunar New Year with an enchanting performance by musician Winnie Wong. Guests will experience the vibrant sounds of traditional Chinese instruments and learn about the cultural significance of the holiday. Join us for a joyful celebration filled with music, storytelling, and community as we welcome a new year of luck, harmony, and renewal.

**Cost: Free with General Admission; reservation required.**  
_Members receive free admission; reservation required._

Historic House Display

As you explore the Historic House, look for vignettes celebrating the holiday, including a red envelope exchange and game of Mahjong. A special floral display inspired by Lunar New Year will accompany highlights from Filoli's Asian art collection. In the Garden House, citrus trees show off their richly colored fruit, a symbol of good luck and prosperity for the new year.
