# Summer Stage Concert Series

## Two weeks. Six bands. One unforgettable experience.

Get outside this summer and enjoy the perfect combination of live music and nature at Summer Stage. As performers take the stage in the Meadow, set against the stunning backdrop of the Historic House, this concert series delivers the ultimate outdoor music experience. Beautiful summer weather, breathtaking natural beauty, and captivating music—it doesn’t get much better than that!

## An Experience, Not Just an Event

### Live music. Golden sunsets. Garden vibes.

There are summer concerts and then there’s Summer Stage at Filoli. Between the surrounding natural beauty, the ideal summer weather and the Historic House creating a one-of-a-kind backdrop, there’s something special about Summer Stage. And that’s not even mentioning the food, drinks and world-class performances by some of the best musical acts around.

Summer Stage is perfect for anyone and any type of outing, be it family fun, a date night, a get together with friends or just someone looking for **[live music in the Bay Area](https://filoli.org/summer-stage/live-music-bay-area/)**. Located in the Meadow on Filoli’s beautiful and historic grounds, Summer Stage is the perfect combination of nature, art and history, and a celebration of the Bay Area performing arts community through live music, dance and more. 

There’s nothing quite like listening to live music with a drink in hand, the sun setting behind the mountains in the distance and Filoli’s incredible ambiance all around you — it’s truly a magical and unique experience.

## Frequently Asked Questions About Filoli Concerts

How old do you have to be to go to the Summer Stage? _Expand_

All shows during Summer Stage are family friendly and open for all ages. Tickets are required for children 2 and up.

Where will Summer Stage concerts be held? _Expand_

Summer Stage is held outdoors in Filoli’s Meadow, against the backdrop of the Historic House.

It is a 3-5 minute stroll from the ticket kiosk to the Meadow. Please allow time to check-in, walk to the Meadow, and get refreshments. In the Meadow, you will encounter various types of terrain including dirt paths, a meadow grass field, and uneven areas with bark.

How does seating work for Summer Stage? _Expand_

There will be an open area for seating in Filoli's Meadow and we encourage you to bring lawn chairs or a thick blanket to comfortably enjoy the scenic terrain. Chairs will not be provided with General Admission tickets. 

VIP Admission tickets will come with access to an adirondack chair as well as complimentary wine, beer, canned cocktails, non-alcoholic drinks and light snacks throughout the evening.

Will I be able to bring food and beverages? _Expand_

No outside food or beverages are permitted.

Beer, wine, cocktails, non-alcoholic drinks, and light snacks will be available for purchase starting 60 minutes before the performance begins.

Check "What food options are available during Summer Stage?” below for more details!

What food options are available during Summer Stage? _Expand_

Grab a bite from local food trucks between 5:30–8pm, or enjoy a sit-down meal at the Quail Café, which stays open late until 7pm.

See the Food Truck schedule below:

Will the House, Gardens, or Natural Lands be open to explore? _Expand_

The House and Gardens will be open until the start of the show at 7pm.

The Natural Lands and trails will be closed.
