#  Flora Party: Holiday Wreaths

December 9, 2025 @ 2:00 pm-4:00 pm

  * This event has passed.



Join us for a Holiday Wreath Party at Filoli! Celebrate the season with a one-of-a-kind wreath-making experience set in the heart of the holidays. Learn the art of crafting a stunning wreath as you weave together fresh greens, dried accents, and festive flair. Whether you’re a seasoned DIYer or a first-time maker, you’ll leave with a handmade masterpiece—and a bit of holiday magic.
