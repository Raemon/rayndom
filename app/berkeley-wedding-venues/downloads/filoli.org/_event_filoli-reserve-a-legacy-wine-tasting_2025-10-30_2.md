#  Filoli Reserve: A Legacy Wine Tasting

October 30, 2025 @ 2:30 pm-4:00 pm

|Recurring Event [(See all)](https://filoli.org/event/filoli-reserve-a-legacy-wine-tasting/all/)

An event every week that begins at 2:30 pm on Thursday, repeating until November 6, 2025

An event every week that begins at 2:30 pm on Thursday, repeating until November 6, 2025

One event on October 23, 2025 at 2:30 pm

  * This event has passed.



Experience the elegance of a garden afternoon at Filoli, with an exclusive wine tasting in the sun-drenched charm of the Garden House. Savor a thoughtfully curated selection from Chase Cellars, a Napa Valley winery founded by William Bowers Bourn’s father-in-law, Captain George Chase!

Whether you’re a seasoned connoisseur or simply curious, this intimate tasting invites you to slow down, take a sip, and celebrate the enduring artistry of winemaking.
