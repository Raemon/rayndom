# Ballroom Performance Series

Filoli’s first family, Agnes and William Bourn, designed the Ballroom to host intimate performances. This year, uniquely-themed performances will celebrate Valentine's Day, St. Patrick's Day, and Filoli’s popular partnership with Opera San José. Ticket sell-outs are expected—book your experiences today!

**Performances last for 70 minutes**  
_Doors open one hour prior to show_

## Valentine's Day Jazz with Stella Cole  


February 12–13 | 7pm  
February 14 | 5pm & 7:30pm

Let love take center stage this Valentine’s Day with an enchanting evening in the Ballroom, featuring the captivating Stella Cole. Immerse yourself in an unforgettable concert experience with your valentine—where timeless tunes and an intimate ambiance weave a spell of romance.

Make Valentine's Day unforgettable. Add a **Moët & Chandon or Dom Pérignon **upgrade and celebrate your love in true style.

**Cost: $85-$125**  
_Members receive 10% off_

Buy Tickets

## Irish Night with Devil's Orchard  


March 12–14 | 7pm

Intertwined in Filoli’s history are enduring tales of Ireland. Join us in the grand Ballroom to celebrate Filoli’s Irish connection, surrounded by the sweeping murals that depict the Bourn family’s idyllic Muckross estate. 

As Devil’s Orchard takes the stage, the room comes alive with haunting melodies and richly textured, tradition-rooted acoustic sound, inspired by Gaelic and Scandinavian traditional music.

**Cost: $55–$85**  
_Members receive 10% off_ __

Buy Tickets

## Opera San José  


March 17–19 | 7pm

Opera San José brings the power and passion of their world-class artistry to our historic stage. This unforgettable evening offers an exclusive preview of Opera San José's 2026–2027 season, along with stirring highlights from their upcoming production of the iconic classic, _La Traviata_. 

To make the night even more memorable, enhance your experience with a bottle of **Moët Champagne or Dom Pérignon** , available for an additional fee.

**Cost: $75–$105**  
_Members receive 10% off_
