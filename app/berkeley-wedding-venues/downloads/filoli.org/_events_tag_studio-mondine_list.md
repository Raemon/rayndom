####  [ Stories in Bloom: Ikebana with Studio Mondine ](https://filoli.org/event/stories-in-bloom-ikebana-with-studio-mondine/ "Stories in Bloom: Ikebana with Studio Mondine")

January 24 @ 2:00 pm-3:00 pm

86 Cañada Road, Woodside, CA 94062  86 Cañada Road, Woodside, CA, United States 

Experience the art of Japanese floral design as Studio Mondine brings Ikebana to life through a live demonstration inspired by Filoli’s winter gardens. Discover how balance, form, and seasonality shape each arrangement, and enjoy an intimate look at the storytelling behind this centuries-old tradition. Ikebana Tickets
