#  Merry & Bright Preview Night

November 21, 2025 @ 6:30 pm-10:00 pm

  * This event has passed.



Be among the first to experience the wonder of the season before Holidays at Filoli opens to the public. Step into a world of holiday magic as the estate sparkles to life with twinkling lights, joyful sounds of live carolers, complimentary hot drinks served in a keepsake mug, and so much more! Begin your holiday season with this exclusive experience where tradition meets elegance with just a little bit of spectacle.
