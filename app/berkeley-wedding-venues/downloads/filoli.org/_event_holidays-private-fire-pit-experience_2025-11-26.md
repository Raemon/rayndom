#  Holidays Private Fire Pit Experience

November 26, 2025 @ 4:30 pm-6:00 pm

|Recurring Event [(See all)](https://filoli.org/event/holidays-private-fire-pit-experience/all/)

An event every day that begins at 4:30 pm, repeating until January 11, 2026

An event every day that begins at 7:00 pm, repeating until January 11, 2026

  * This event has passed.



Cozy up this season with Filoli’s Private Fire Pit experience! Invite your favorite people for an intimate evening around your own private fire pit.

Surrounded by the glow of the holidays, you’ll enjoy a relaxed and festive atmosphere perfect for sipping warm drinks, sharing treats, and making memories under the stars. It’s the ultimate way to slow down, soak in the magic of the season, and stay toasty all night long.

[Fire Pit Tickets](https://filoli.org/holidays/#firepit)
