#  Flora Party: Thanksgiving Centerpieces

November 25, 2025 @ 2:00 pm-4:00 pm

|Recurring Event [(See all)](https://filoli.org/event/flora-party-thanksgiving-centerpieces/all/)

One event on November 25, 2025 at 2:00 pm

One event on November 25, 2025 at 10:00 am

  * This event has passed.



Create a show-stopping centerpiece sure to be the talk of your Thanksgiving table. Guided by Filoli’s expert instructors, you’ll craft an elegant focal point using seasonal materials thoughtfully sourced from Filoli’s estate and local vendors.
