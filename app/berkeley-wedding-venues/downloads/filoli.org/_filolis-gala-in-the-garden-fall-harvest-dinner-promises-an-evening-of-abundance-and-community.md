##  Filoli’s Gala in the Garden: Fall Harvest Dinner Promises an Evening of Abundance and Community

July 30, 2024 | [Press Releases](https://filoli.org/press-releases/)

FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASE  
July 30, 2024

**Woodside, CA** — Save the date for an unforgettable evening under the stars at Filoli’s annual _Gala in the Garden: Fall Harvest Dinner_ , taking place on Friday, September 20, 2024, at 5:30 PM. This signature event promises to celebrate community spirit and the bountiful harvest season, set against the stunning backdrop of the historic Filoli estate.

Guests will indulge in a farm-to-table dining experience meticulously curated to showcase the freshest seasonal flavors. Imagine savoring each bite amidst the beauty of Filoli’s iconic gardens, where every dish tells a story of sustainability and local partnership.

“We are thrilled to invite longtime supporters and new friends to join us for this premier fundraising event,” said Kara Newport, Filoli’s President & CEO. “The _Gala in the Garden: Fall Harvest Dinner_ celebrates the richness of our land and heritage and plays a crucial role in supporting Filoli’s mission. Funds raised will contribute directly to the preservation of our historic House, magnificent Gardens, and expansive Natural Lands, as well as nurturing the next generation’s connection to nature through our educational programs.”

The evening will feature live entertainment, curated tours of the estate, and opportunities to connect with fellow enthusiasts of art, history, and the environment. Whether mingling amidst the vibrant blooms or enjoying the crisp evening air, guests will be immersed in the essence of Filoli’s legacy of conservation and community.

Tickets for the _Gala in the Garden: Fall Harvest Dinner_ are available now, offering an exclusive chance to experience Filoli in all its seasonal splendor while contributing to its ongoing stewardship. For the sponsorship list and to secure your seat at this extraordinary event, visit: [filoli.org/gala](https://filoli.org/gala).

**About Filoli**

Filoli is a vibrant landscape of the Bay Area, situated on the unceded ancestral lands of the Ramaytush Ohlone, in Woodside, California. The estate boasts 654 acres of beauty nestled along the slopes of California’s coastal range. Originally built as a private residence in 1917, Filoli was opened to the public in 1977 as a 501(c)(3) nonprofit organization and site of the National Trust for Historic Preservation. The property is considered one of the finest remaining country estates of the 20th century, featuring a 54,000+ square-foot Georgian revival-style mansion, 16 acres of exquisite English Renaissance gardens, a 6.8-acre Gentleman’s Orchard, and hundreds of acres of Natural Lands with five distinct ecosystems and a 1-mile Estate Trail. Filoli is dedicated to connecting our rich history with a vibrant future through beauty, nature, and shared stories so that one day all people will honor nature, value unique experiences, and appreciate beauty in everyday life. For more information about Filoli, visit [filoli.org](https://filoli.org).
