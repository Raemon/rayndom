# Stories in Bloom

# Each Bloom Tells a Story

The popular Stories in Bloom series showcases storytelling of all forms, including lecture, music, and art. Through this program, we explore a range of topics and encourage guests to share their perspectives through a Q&A.

**Free with Filoli admission; Reservations required.**

## Ikebana with Studio Mondine  


January 24 | 2–3pm

Experience the art of Japanese floral design as Studio Mondine brings Ikebana to life through a live demonstration inspired by Filoli’s winter gardens. Discover how balance, form, and seasonality shape each arrangement, and enjoy an intimate look at the storytelling behind this centuries-old tradition.

[ Buy Tickets (Sold Out) ](https://51772.blackbaudhosting.com/51772/tickets?tab=2&txobjid=8d4466c6-6157-4cd9-84ba-f3eff88fce04)

## The Accidental Vineyard  


February 15 | 2–3pm

Filoli welcomes bestselling author Richard Moran, who shares behind-the-scenes stories from _The Accidental Vineyard_ , exploring passion, perseverance, and the unexpected journey of preservation and developing a Vineyard.

[ Buy Tickets ](https://51772.blackbaudhosting.com/51772/tickets?tab=2&txobjid=a5b7387f-633d-4d80-ab58-e82145778cca)

## Celebrating Lunar New Year with Winnie Wong  


February 28 | 2–3pm

Celebrate Lunar New Year with an enchanting performance by musician Winnie Wong. Guests will experience the vibrant sounds of traditional Chinese instruments and learn about the cultural significance of the holiday. Join us for a joyful celebration filled with music, storytelling, and community as we welcome a new year of luck, harmony, and renewal.
