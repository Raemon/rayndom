#  House Curatorial Tour

March 5 @ 1:30 pm-3:00 pm

|Recurring Event [(See all)](https://filoli.org/event/house-curatorial-tour-2/all/)

One event on March 5, 2026 at 1:30 pm

Step inside Filoli like never before with the Curatorial team. Tour the rarely seen second floor of the historic House, from the Family Bedrooms to the Sitting Room’s stunning views, and peek into the Curatorial Offices. Begin with sparkling wine and leave with a special keepsake.

**Experience Includes:**

  * 90-minute guided tour with a member of Filoli’s Curatorial Team
  * Exclusive behind-the-scenes access
  * General admission to Filoli
  * Sparkling wine and a special keepsake
