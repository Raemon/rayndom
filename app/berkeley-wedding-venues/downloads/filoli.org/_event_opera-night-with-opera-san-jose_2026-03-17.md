#  Opera San José

March 17 @ 7:00 pm-8:15 pm

|Recurring Event [(See all)](https://filoli.org/event/opera-night-with-opera-san-jose/all/)

An event every day that begins at 7:00 pm, repeating until March 19, 2026

Opera San José brings the power and passion of their world-class artistry to our historic stage. This unforgettable evening offers an exclusive preview of Opera San José’s 2026–2027 season, along with stirring highlights from their upcoming production of the iconic classic,  _La Traviata_.

[Opera San José Tickets](https://filoli.org/winter/#opera-san-jose)
