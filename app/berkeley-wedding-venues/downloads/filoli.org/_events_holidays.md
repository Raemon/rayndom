## Three Enchanting Experiences, One Unforgettable Holiday Season

Celebrate the season at the Bay Area’s premier holiday destination. A beloved annual tradition rivaling the nation’s most celebrated events, _Holidays at Filoli_ transforms the historic estate into a wonderland of breathtaking beauty and festive delight.

Day and night, there's something magical for everyone at Filoli. During the day, take a **Gilded** journey through the elegantly decorated House and Garden and explore **Thistlewyck** , an enchanting village in the Redwoods with [**Rose** **Wonders**](https://filoli.org/rose-wonders/) , Filoli's brand new Troll, at the heart of it all. At night, immerse yourself in the glowing wonder of **Holiday Lights**.

Each experience reflects our mission to curate unique moments and create shared stories, inviting you to celebrate the season with family, friends, and community in unforgettable style.

Members receive FREE access to our Daytime _Holidays at Filoli_ experiences. **[Become a member today!](https://filoli.org/support/membership/)**

_**Holidays at Filoli —** Presented by: _Pacific Gas and Electric Company__

_**Yuletide Woodland Court —** Sponsored by: Harrell Design + Build_

Join us for Holidays at Filoli in 2026!

After a groundbreaking season of festivities, **we'd like to give the warmest thanks to all of the wonderful guests who made this year's Holidays magic happen**.

With your support, **Thistlewyck** shimmered into our view for the first time, the historic House transformed into the premier **Gilded** experience, **Holiday Lights** brought out glowing wonder under the evening sky, and Filoli's newest Troll friend, **Rose Wonders** , is welcomed to her new home.

**We look forward to welcoming you back this year for yet another season of enchanting experiences!**
