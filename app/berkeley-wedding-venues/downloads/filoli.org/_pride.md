## Pride

Celebrate the LGBTQ+ community with a weekend of performances and experiences. Filoli bursts into rainbow color with festive decor, artwork, and live performances.

## Pride House Party

Friday, June 13, 2025 | 6–10pm

Filoli proudly presents Pride House Party—a celebratory evening to kick off Pride weekend in style. Set in and around the Historic House, this high-energy event brings queer artistry to center stage, from drag and burlesque to DJs and contortionists. Immerse yourself in bold performances, joyful moments of connection, and a vibrant tribute to the spirit of Pride. Come celebrate love, identity, and community in all its beautiful forms.

**Cost: $55**

# Pride Weekend

Saturday, June 14, 2025 | 10am–8pm  
Sunday, June 15, 2025 | 10am–5pm

Celebrate love, self-expression, and community at Filoli’s Pride Weekend! This family-friendly event blends creativity, wellness, and joyful celebration in a stunning setting. Express your creativity through art, unwind with a sound bath, dance to live performances, and connect with LGBTQIA+ community partners. Wrap up your day with an evening of Drag Bingo. Reserve your spot today and be part of the beauty of Pride.

**Click to view Pride Weekend schedule**

**Cost: Free with Admission**

## Flower Crown Making

June 14 & 15, 2025  
Saturday & Sunday | 10am-4pm

Celebrate Pride with a handmade flower crown! Stop by the Garden House and design a custom crown that reflects your unique spirit. Choose from a rainbow of fresh blooms, and craft a keepsake to remember the day. Let your creativity blossom!

All ages and skill levels are welcome.

**Cost: $20; Filoli Admission is required to attend.**

## Watch Live Performances

Enjoy a weekend of vibrant performances, from Drag Queen Story Time and Drag Bingo to colorful flagging performances and a soothing Sound Bath by Banyan Tree Women’s Collective.

## See Filoli in Color

Filoli bursts into a sea of color with rainbow decor around every corner. From the beautiful interior floral displays by Nigella SF, to the rustic rainbows sprinkled across our Natural Lands, you'll feel a sense of Pride everywhere you go!

## Meet the Community

Express yourself through art, and connect to resources with help from local LGBTQ+ organizations at their tables in the Garden and make sure to visit the Clock Tower Shop for products from queer vendors.
