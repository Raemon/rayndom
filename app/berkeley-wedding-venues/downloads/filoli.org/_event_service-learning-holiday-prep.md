#  Service Learning: Holiday Prep

October 30, 2025 @ 9:00 am-12:00 pm

  * This event has passed.



Collaborate with the Horticulture Team as they embark on turning Filoli into a holiday wonderland! Transforming the historical estate into a wintery paradise is no easy feat. This Service Learning program is focused on Holiday preparations, and will have you participating in a variety of festive decor projects. Come ready to be creative, learn a thing or two about gardening, and stay for a visit afterwards, on us!

[Holiday Prep Tickets](https://filoli.org/youth-programs/service-learning/#holiday-prep)
