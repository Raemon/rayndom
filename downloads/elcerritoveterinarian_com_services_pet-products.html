Pharmacy & Pet Products

Know exactly what you're getting.

We offer a variety of products, food, and medications to keep your pet happy and healthy. Our in-house pet pharmacy is stocked with parasite preventatives and prescription medications used to treat injuries and illnesses of all kinds.

Our top priority is your pet’s safety. At El Cerrito Veterinary Care, the prescription medications we carry and sell are purchased directly from the manufacturers. You know exactly what you’re getting, and we are available to answer any questions.

Check out our Online Pharmacy

We are also pleased to offer the convenience of purchasing prescriptions online through our trusted online pharmacy.

Written Prescriptions

We respect our clients who prefer to fill their prescriptions outside our clinic. Upon request, we will provide you with a written prescription that you can send to the third-party pharmacy of your choice.
