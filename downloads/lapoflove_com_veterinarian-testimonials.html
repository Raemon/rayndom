Dr. Alexa

We decided to use in home hospice for our 17-year-old cat Sophie. Dr. Alexa was very compassionate with us and explained everything well. Our cat got to be in her favorite place when she passed which was so comforting. The thought of taking her to an in-person vet filled us with despair. I so appreciated Dr. Alexa being kind and respectful of us and Sophie. It made a horrible experience a bit easier. I would highly recommend in home hospice care in the sad event that you need that.

Michelle
