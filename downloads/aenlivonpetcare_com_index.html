Welcome To Aenlivon Pet Care Veterinary Housecalls

Aenlivon Pet Care provides quality veterinary health care at your own home; currently we are serving Fremont, Union City, Newark, parts of Hayward, Menlo Park and Palo Alto.

At Aenlivon Pet Care we understand the stress and anxiety your pet and you have to go through while visiting a veterinary hospital for routine and unavoidable health care. Providing quality care for your pets’ at their own house is what we do. Dr. Geethanjali Hegde will provide you quality care for your pets at your home where your pets are at ease and relaxed.

Please don’t hesitate to contact us today at (510) 386-2003 to request your pet’s appointment!

New Clients

Aenlivon Pet Care offers in home veterinary services to pet families in the Tri City area. The veterinarian comes right to you and offer wellness and preventative care, sick visits, chronic disease management and at home euthanasia by Dr Hegde. A visit to the veterinary hospital can be a stressful experience and being in a familiar environment with the owner can greatly reduce a pet’s stress. Let us show you how great a personalized veterinary house call can be!

If you have any questions or comments about how we can care for your pet, please contact us today at (510) 386-2003.
