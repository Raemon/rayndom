When you book an appointment, you are holding a space on our calendar that is no longer available to other clients & patients in need.  We understand that your pet's condition can change unexpectedly, so if you need to cancel or postpone, we respectfully request a 12-HOUR NOTICE by phone and/or email.

This compensates the veterinarian for their time in addition to providing removal and transport service. Bites & State Required Rabies Testing

Please Be Advised:

If your pet bites or has bitten any person (puncturing the skin enough to bleed) less than 10 days prior to euthanasia service, we will be required by law to submit your pet's body to your local county health department for rabies testing.  

            This will incur an additional fee of $300 for complying with this legal requirement.
