Compassion Pet Hospice serves the East Bay Area of the Northern California since 2015. We provide our beloved pets with the alternative of spending the last season of their lives in the comfort and warmth of their homes. Our services include phone and online consultation, in-home customized palliative/hospice care (short-term/long-term), chronic pain management, in-home euthanasia, and online pharmacy.
