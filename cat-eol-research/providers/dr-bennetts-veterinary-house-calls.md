# Dr. Bennett’s Veterinary House Calls

Website: https://www.bennettvet.com/

## Summary

- In-home euthanasia: Yes (published pricing; serves Berkeley and surrounding East Bay)
- Private cremation: Yes (bundled option; “ashes are returned to caretaker”)
- Expected wait time: Estimates given when the appointment is made; “Emergency / Urgent fit into schedule fee” listed (implies possible short-notice; not a guarantee)
- Scheduling: Appointment arranged by phone; pricing page says estimates given when appointment is made

## Pricing breakdown (itemized)

- Euthanasia fee: Euthanasia with no cremation: <60 lbs 450; >70 lbs 475; >90 lbs 500; >120 lbs 550 (listed without $ sign; appears to be USD)
- Sedation fee: Not separately listed (likely included; confirm)
- Travel fee: “Out of territory / Additional driving” 50-250 possible; house call fees charged based on estimate
- After-hours/weekend fee: “After Hours, Nights, Holidays, Weekends” 100-200 possible
- Cremation (private): Euthanasia + private cremation (ashes returned): <20 lbs 720.00; 20-69 lbs 770.00; 70-99 lbs 845.00; 100-120 lbs based on estimate
- Cremation (communal): Euthanasia + communal cremation (no ashes returned): <20 lbs 575.00; 20-69 lbs 625.00; 70-99 lbs 725.00; 100-120 lbs based on estimate
- Body transport: Not separately itemized (bundled with cremation packages; confirm)
- Incidentals to consider: “Emergency / Urgent fit into schedule fee” 100.00 possible; “Viscous or dangerous animal” 50-250 possible; “Soiled Pet” 50-200 possible; other adverse circumstances may increase fees

## Scheduling details

Pricing page states “Estimates will be given when the appointment is made” and lists an “Emergency / Urgent fit into schedule fee”, but does not publish a specific guaranteed wait time.
