# Caring Heart Vet

Website: https://caringheartvet.com/

## Summary

- In-home euthanasia: Yes (published pricing)
- Private cremation: Yes ($350 listed on euthanasia page; ashes returned 10-14 business days)
- Expected wait time: Not explicitly stated; appointments available 7 days/week 9:30am-7:30pm; phone calls returned 8:30am-7pm
- Scheduling: Online appointment request form; phone for immediate assistance (per site footer)

## Pricing breakdown (itemized)

- Euthanasia fee: For pets 60 lb and under $550; 61-80 lb $600 (page is not cat-specific; cats are typically in the <=60 lb tier)
- Sedation fee: Not separately listed (likely included; confirm)
- Travel fee: Additional fees starting at $50-150 may apply for extended travel time (>20 min driving distance)
- After-hours/weekend fee: Additional fees starting at $50-150 may apply for evening/weekend/holiday/short notice appointments
- Cremation (private): $350 (individual cremation; includes standard urn option; ashes usually returned in 10-14 business days by USPS signature confirmation)
- Cremation (communal): $200 (ashes not returned; scattered at sea via Oceans of Love)
- Body transport: Not explicitly separated on page (cremation described as an option; confirm transport/pickup handling for cats)
- Incidentals to consider: Cancellation policy: may charge $400 if <12 hours; $500 house call fee if <6 hours; if pet dies naturally after doctor started driving/arrived then $530 house call fee plus additional fees and any aftercare fees if elected

## Scheduling details

Scheduling is via online appointment request form; site notes phone for immediate assistance. No explicit “same-day” promise; page mentions short notice appointments may carry extra fees.
