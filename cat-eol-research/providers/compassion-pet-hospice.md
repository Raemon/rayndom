# Compassion Pet Hospice

Website: https://compassionpethospice.com/

## Summary

- In-home euthanasia: Yes (published pricing)
- Private cremation: Yes (“Individual Cremation”; priced by weight)
- Expected wait time: “Same day/urgent appointment is occasionally available” (fee applies); appointment hours published
- Scheduling: Website appointment page; contact phone/email listed

## Pricing breakdown (itemized)

- Euthanasia fee: In-home euthanasia visit $530 (two-hour appointment time assigned)
- Sedation fee: Not itemized (confirm if included)
- Travel fee: “An additional non-negotiable $75 travel fee applies to the following extended areas” (list not captured in this report yet)
- After-hours/weekend fee: Additional $150 for evening/weekend/holiday appointment (site also says Dr. Ma not available for evening; weekend/holiday occasionally available with advanced arrangement)
- Cremation (private): Individual cremation: under 20 lbs $350; 20-69 lbs $375; 70-99 lbs $410
- Cremation (communal): Communal cremation: under 25 lbs $200; 25-69 lbs $225; 70-99 lbs $250
- Body transport: Not separately itemized; site states they entrust Animal Memorial Service (AMS) for aftercare
- Incidentals to consider: Same day/urgent appointment fee $100-$150; overtime (euthanasia) $100 per each additional 30 minutes beyond 2 hours; additional clay paw print $30 each; cancellation/change within 24 hours: $75 fee

## Scheduling details

Appointment hours: Tuesday through Friday 10am-4pm. Site notes same day/urgent is occasionally available (fee applies) and weekend/holiday appointments are occasionally available with advanced arrangement. Cancellation/change within 24 hours: $75 fee.
